
        BARNETT--Joseph W. Jr., ''Bear'' died peacefully on January 20th, 2008 at the age of 70. He was a graduate of Rye Country Day School, The Hotchkiss School, Yale University and University of Virginia Law School. He also attended Columbia University Graduate School. Bear served honorably as a Lieutenant in the United States Marine Corps. He was a lawyer and Partner at Badger, Fisher, Cohen and Barnett, and most recently served as a Consultant and Legal Advisor. Bear is survived by his children, Alexis Barnett and Duke Barnett and grandchildren, Charlie Howard and Reese and Tyler Barnett. We will miss him dearly. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to The Michael J. Fox Foundation For Parkinson's Research at www.michaeljfox.org. Funeral services will be on Wednesday, January 30th, 11:00am at Christ's Church in Rye, New York.
