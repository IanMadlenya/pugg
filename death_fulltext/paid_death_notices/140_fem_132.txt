
        FREEMAN-Janice M. Passed away Wednesday, September 16, 1998, after a courageous, dignified battle. One of the early women stockbrokers on Wall Street, who later was Senior Vice President at Manufacturer's Hanover Trust, she was the beloved mother of Douglas S. Freeman II and the cherished sister of Helen M. Dunn & Harold F. Miller, Jr. A memorial service will be held at St. James Church, 865 Madison Ave., (71 St.) NYC, Sun., September 27, at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers donations should be sent to The American Cancer Society New York Chapter.
