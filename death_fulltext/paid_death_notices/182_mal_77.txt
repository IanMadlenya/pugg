
        HAWLEY-Samuel W. Age 92, died on February 28, 2002 in Fairfield, CT. Mr. Hawley, a graduate of Yale and Harvard Business School, worked his entire business career at People's Bank, Bridgeport, CT, where he was the former Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer. For information contact Spear Funeral Home, Fairfield, CT. A memorial service will be held Thursday, 11:30 AM, March 7, 2002 at the Greenfield Hill Congregational Church. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Bridgeport Hospital Foundation, 267 Grant St, Bridgeport, CT. 06610, or the Greater Bridgeport Area Foundation, 940 Broad St., Bridgeport, CT. 06604, or to a charity of your choice.
