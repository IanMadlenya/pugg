
        STOUDEMIRE-Alan, M.D., 49, of Atlanta, GA, on February 2nd, 2000. It is with profound sadness that the Board of Directors of the Society for Liaison Psychiatry mourns the passing of our esteemed colleague, Alan Stoudemire, M.D., after his second valiant battle with cancer. He was a leader in our field and an example to us all. We offer our condolences to his wife, Susan, and his children, William and Anna. Donations in his memory may be sent to Children's HealthCare of Atlanta, 1680 Tullie Circle, Atlanta, GA 30329.
