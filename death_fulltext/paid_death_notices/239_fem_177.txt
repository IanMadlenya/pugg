
        KRIEGER--Eleanor. Passed away at the age of 89 in Lake Worth, Florida on December 28th, 2006. She is survived by Basil, her loving husband of 64 years, her daughter Karen and her husband George Myers, her son Robert and his wife Randi, her grandchildren Robyn and Lyle, and her great-grandchildren Cody and Cory. Before moving to Florida, she was a long term resident of Woodmere, NY where she was active in community affairs. She will be forever missed.
