
        MELTZER-George. On February 7, 1998. Adored and adoring husband of Judith (nee Stettin). Precious father of Donald Meltzer and Mindy (nee Meltzer) Ferrentino. Loved father-in-law of Debra (nee Phillips) Meltzer and Paul Wolfle. The best Pop George to Leslie, Phillip and Amanda Meltzer. A son, not a son-in-law, to Blanche Stettin. Loved brother of Harriet Cohen and Dr. Herbert Meltzer. Dear uncle, brother-in-law, relative and friend. Proud U.S. Navy veteran and member of the USS Missouri Association. His lifelong career was in the linen supply industry. A darling man who was a perennial good guy. For those who would like to remember George with a donation, please consider the Don Monti Memorial Research Foundation, North Shore University Hospital, 300 Community Drive, Manhasset, New York 11030. Visitation today 7-9 PM. Services Tuesday, 12:30 PM, at ''Gutterman's,'' 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
