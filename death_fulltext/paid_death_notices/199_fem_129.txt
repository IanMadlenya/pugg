
        FLANAGAN--Gloria. Wife of Peter Flanagan, President Emeritus of the Life Insurance Council of New York, died Friday, August 22, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Center from Cancer. She had eight children, Joseph, Elizabeth, Donna, Peter J., Jeremy, Arthur, John and Patrick. Daughters - in - law, Valerie, Jackie, Eva and Rachel and son-in-law John. She also had nine grandchildren. Gloria was a fighter and had a great sense of humor. She said her final message to her friends and family was ''eat dessert first, life is uncertain.'' She will be waked at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 81 Street and Madison Avenue on Sunday, August 24, and Monday, August 25, from 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass is at St. Patrick's Cathedral in New York City, Tuesday, 10AM. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the Center for Discovery, P.O. Box 840, Benmosche Road, Harris, NY 12742-0840, in memory of Gloria Flanagan.
