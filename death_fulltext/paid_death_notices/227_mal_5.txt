
        KELZ--Allan. Age 75, on November 30, 2005. Beloved husband of Joyce of 51 years. Dear father of Robin Rudnitsky and Stacey Kelz. Fatherin-law of Dr. Gary Rudnitsky. Cherished grandfather of Seth and Dana. Son of Sol and Diana Kelz. Graduate of NYU. Commercial real estate broker for over 30 years in the Tri-State area. He will be remembered for his brilliant sense of humor and love of his family. Funeral service today, 10:30AM, GuttermanMusicant, Hackensack, NJ. Please make all donations to American Cancer Society.
