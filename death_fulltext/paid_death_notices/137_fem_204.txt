
        SNYDER-Beatrice. On June 21, 1998. Age 74. Co-founder with her husband Harold Snyder of Biocraft Laboratories, Inc. She was a pioneer in the generic drug industry, an accomplished businesswoman and a noted philanthropist. Beloved wife of Harold, her husband of nearly 50 years. Loving mother of Beryl Snyder Trost, Brian and Jay Snyder. Adored grandmother of Evan, Brette, Ophelia, Maxwell and Jackson. Funeral services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, N.Y., today, 12:30PM. Shiva will be held at the home of Beryl Snyder Trost, 10 West 66 Street, on Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday between 5 and 9PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Beatrice Snyder Charitable Foundation, 300 Winston Dr., Apt 1711, Cliffside Park, N.J. 07010.
        SNYDER-Bea. With great sadness the Board and staff of Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc. mourn the passing of Bea Snyder, a dear friend and sweet person. She was a great supporter of our programs for children, including the Meet-the-Artist school series and Reel to Real. We extend our sympathies to her husband Harry Snyder, to her daughter Beryl, her sons and their families. Beverly Sills, Chairman Nathan Leventhal, President Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts, Inc.
