
        KARTEN--Rachel. Born in Swirz, Poland October 21st, 1913 died peacefully at home Saturday, September 30. She came to the United States in 1947 after surviving the Holocaust in which her first child was killed. Rachel died in the presence of her children, Mark and Arlene. She was predeceased by her husband of 40 years, Isak. She is mourned by her grandchildren Stephanie, Pamela, Abigail and Melissa, her son-inlaw Joe, her daughter-in-law Lorraine, and her brother Bill.
