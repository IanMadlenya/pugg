
        CLAESON-Walter W. Died at age 87 on February 27, 2000. He was a veteran of World War II in the European Theater and the Vice President of American Title Insurance Company. He was an intelligent, sensitive and special man and will be missed by his surviving wife, Ann; son, Robert; grandchildren, Matthew and Kristen and a host of friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave (at 81 St) Thursday, March 1, 2-5 & 79PM. Funeral Service Thursday 11:30AM at the Funeral Home.
