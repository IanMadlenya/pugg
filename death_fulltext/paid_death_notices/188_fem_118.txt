
        BRAUNSTEIN-Rita, 83, (nee Pogostin) on September 16, 2002 in Aventura, FL. Formerly of Woodmere, Long Island, NY. Mrs. Braunstein was predeceased by her husband Irving (1996). Graveside services will be today 11 A.M. at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York. Donations may be made to Vitas Hospice Unit of Aventura Hospital, Aventura, FL.
