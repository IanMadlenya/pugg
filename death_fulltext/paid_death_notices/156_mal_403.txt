
        SEEGER-Francis J., Rev. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the former chaplain of Mount St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, on Jan. 25. Reposing in Quinn-Fogarty Funeral Home, 162-14 Sanford Ave., Flushing, on Friday, Jan. 28, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9:30 P.M. Funeral Mass Saturday, Jan. 29, at Holy Family Church, 175-20 74th Ave., Flushing, 11 A.M. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
