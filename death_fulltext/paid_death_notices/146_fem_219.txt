
        WHYTE COTTON WRENN-Dorothy. Died on March 18, 1999 in Portland, Oregon, born in Auburn, NY, she attended The University Michigan and Columbia University. In a professional career devoted to journalism and public health education in the broadest sense, her special concern was with the rights and well-being of mothers and children. She lived and worked in New York City for many years, serving as Editor-InChief of Parents Magazine until 1970 when she moved to Oregon. She is survived by her son, Michael Whyte, daughter-in-law, Susan, and grandsons, Timothy and Zachary. At her request, there will not be a service. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Planned Parenthood.
