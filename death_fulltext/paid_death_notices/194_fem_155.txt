
        MISHKIN-Mildred ''Milli'', at 76 years of age in Berkeley, CA, passed away on March 18th. A native of Brooklyn, NY, lived in Berkeley, CA for the last 30 years. A retired Adult Education Administrator. Survived by her husband of 28 years, Paul Mishkin of Berkeley, CA and her son Jonathan Westover of Los Angeles, CA. Private services were held. For more information contact Sinai Memorial Chapel, Lafayette, CA.
