
        SMITH-Warren H., 93, of Geneva, N.Y. and formerly of New Haven, CT. Died peacefully at home on November 22, 1998. An editor, author and philanthropist, he devoted his life to editing the letters of Horace Walpole at the Yale Library and to interests in local history, libraries and Hobart and William Smith colleges in Geneva. Services will be held Wednesday, November 25th, at 10 AM at Trinity Church, Geneva, N.Y. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Geneva Free Library, 244 Main St., Geneva, N.Y. 14456
