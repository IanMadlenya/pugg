
        KENNEDY-Peter J. Died on November 3, 2001. Born March 8, 1912, Brooklyn, New York. Son of Cornelius Kennedy & Mary Reilly of Ireland and New York. A 1929 graduate of Regis HS, Manhattan, New York. Served in the U.S. Army's 77th Division in Guam, Okinawa and elsewhere in the Pacific. Awarded two Bronze Stars and a Purple Heart. Retired from J.C. Penney Company in 1969 after 40 years. Continued to work in the toy industry as a manaufacturer's representative until 1988. Married for 51 years to former Geraldine Murphy, who died in 1994. Survived by a sister, Florence Kennedy, of Fairfield, CT and children Mary Jane Bikowski of Sewickley, PA, Geraldine Biederman of Cromwell, CT, Gail Van Buren of Lunenburg, MA, Hope R. Kennedy of Port Washington, NY, Peter J. Kennedy, Jr., of Glastonbury, CT, James X. Kennedy of Geneva, NY, John P. Kennedy of Port Washington, NY and 15 grandchildren. Reposing at Austin F. Knowles, Inc. Funeral Home, 128 Main St., Port Washington. Visiting hours Tuesday 7-9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Wednesday 10:30 AM at St. Peter of Alcantara Church. Interment will follow at Nassau Knolls Cemetery. A memorial gift in his name may be sent to the Regis High School Scholarship Fund, Alumni Office, 55 E. 84th Street, New York, New York 10028.
