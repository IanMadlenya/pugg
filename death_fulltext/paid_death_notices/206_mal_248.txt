
        BERGER--Albert. A kind, sweet, ''gentle man'' died March 19, 2004 after a courageous battle with cancer in Boca Raton, FL. He was a resident of Harrison, NY. He will be missed by his loving wife Barbara, devoted daughter Jill, son Robert and adored grandson Ian. He was the son of the late Lillian and Samuel Berger. He was also a graduate of N.Y.U. and prior to his retirement, he was affiliated with Warner Communications. All of us in his dear family whom he loved and who loved him, will remember his place in our hearts. Graveside services Monday, 11am at the New Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Iselin, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name may be made to the Dickstein Cancer Center and White Plains Hospital Center.
        BERGER--Albert. The Board of Directors and members of Bocaire Country Club, Inc, record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Albert Berger, on Friday, March 19, 2004. We extend condolences to all of his family. Graveside services to be held Monday, March 22, at Mt. Lebanon Cemetery in Iselin, NJ. Kurt Merl, President
        BERGER--Albert. To a man who devoted so much of his time to the Dickstein Cancer Center, his caring and heart will never be forgotten. Rest in peace. Edie Dickstein
