
        MASLOW-Florence. Born July 4, 1910, known as ''Miss Florence,'' an internationally celebrated dancer in the 1920's and 1930's. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Harry Maslow. Her strong will and ''joie de vie'' will continue to live on in her loving children Toni Gallant and Valerie Outhred, and her adoring grandchildren Mitchell, Chad, Jennifer, Alex and Stephanie. She will be greatly missed by her family whom she loved, and who loved her, so dearly.
