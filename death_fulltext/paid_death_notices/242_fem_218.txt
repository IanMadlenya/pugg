
        RUBENSTEIN--Estelle, nee Bauman, born March 4, 1912, died peacefully in her home, March 27, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Murray Rubenstein. She was a brilliant financial advisor, and loyal colleague and friend to many. She is survived by her daughters, Judith Schaffer and Alice Brent, grandchildren Stacey, Jessica, Ava, and Alex, and great-granchildren Ella and Elsa. A longful life, well lived, a peaceful death at home with family. Rest in peace, Estelle, we love you. Service will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: Calvary Hospital Hospice at (718) 518-2465.
