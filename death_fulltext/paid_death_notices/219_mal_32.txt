
        AINSLIE--Peter G. 58, of New York City, died suddenly Saturday, April 2, 2005. Born Johnson City, TN. Writer, editor, reporter for Women's Wear Daily, Time Magazine and most recently, Time Warner Corporate. He enjoyed music, humor, cooking, adventures and making sure everyone around him was happy. He will be greatly missed by his many friends and colleagues, but mostly by his family. He is survived by his loving parents Jean and George, sister Ellen, brothers Michael and David, and many nieces and nephews, his beloved Laurie, and by his two remarkable children, Erin and Patrick, to whom he devoted his life. Friends may call on Tuesday, April 5, from 7-9 pm, at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, followed by a memorial service in celebration of his life on Wednesday, April 6, at 11:30 am, in the Campbell Chapel.
