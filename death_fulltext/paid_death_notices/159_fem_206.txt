
        ROSEN-Dr. Carol Zwick, on April 23, after a valiant struggle against cancer. Dear wife of Bernard. Loving mother of Richard and the late Rachel. Beloved grandmother of Randall, Mae and Alexander. Dear brother of Dr. Harry Zwick of San Antonio. Her life was devoted to her family and her profession of Physics. She excelled in experimental work and was the holder of several patents relating to physics of materials. Services ''Gutterman-Musicant'', Hackensack, NJ, Tuesday, 11AM.
