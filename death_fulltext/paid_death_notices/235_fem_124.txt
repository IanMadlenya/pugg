
        BLOOMGARDEN--Pearl Sisson. Born September 27, 1912. Passed away in Los Angeles on August 17, 2006. Longtime resident of Great Neck and West Palm Beach. Beloved wife, mother, grandmother, great grandmother and aunt. She built a successful career first at Lord & Taylor then at Bonwit Teller for 25 years as a Senior Buyer. Wife of the late George Sisson & Sidney Bloomgarden, she is survived by her daughter Karyl (Mark Liszt) Sisson, stepson Barry (Patricia) Bloomgarden and grandson Max (Erin) Sisson Liszt. Funeral services will be held 10:15 AM Sunday at I.J. Morris Chapels, 21 E. Deer Park Road, Dix Hills.
