
        MARSHALL-Richard M. Died on November 10, 2000 in Greenwich, CT, at the age of 75. He was a decorated Ranger in the 86th Infantry Division of the Blackhawks in World War II. A Harvard Law School graduate, class of '50, a partner in the New York law firm of Marshall & Tucker, and a partner in Marsam Realty, a family business that owns and operates real estate in NYC. He is survived by his sons Peter and James, their wives Meltem and Lorri, his stepson Anthony Averett, his granddaughter Lindsey Sage, and his wife Elinor. A memorial service will be held in December.
