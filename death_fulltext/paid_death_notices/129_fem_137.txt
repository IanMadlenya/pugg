
        KRENIK-Helen (nee Heiling). Died at age 94 in Boynton Beach, FL. Devoted wife to the late Charles Krenik. Beloved mother to Paul, Betty and the late Henri. She is also survived by her great-grandchildren Josse, Zachary, Bella and little Jeremy. She will be sorely missed. Services Sunday, Oct. 19, 10 AM, Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Tpke, Woodbury, LI.
