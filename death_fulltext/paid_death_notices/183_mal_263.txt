
        DONNELLY-James Joseph, 69, of Beach Haven Terrace, NJ formerly of Mahwah, NJ and Ridgewood, NJ died peacefully on April 20, 2002. Beloved husband of 41 years to Kay (VanWettering). Loving father and father-in-law of Jim and Susan Donnelly of Mahwah, NJ, Ken and Arlene Donnelly of Oviedo, FL, David and Susan Donnelly of Lititz, PA and Amy and Scott Donnelly Roche of Portsmouth, RI. Devoted brother of John Donnelly of North Wales, PA and Mary Warren of Coopersburg, PA. Cherished grandfather of nine grandchildren. He was a member of Saint Francis RC Church, Beach Haven, NJ, a former member of Immaculate Conception Church, Mahwah, Mount Carmel Church, Ridgewood, and Saint Peter's Church, River Edge NJ. His career in the radio broadcasting business spanned over forty years. He retired in 1992 as AM Drive Anchor for WCBS Newsradio 880, New York, a position he held for over twenty years. Prior to WCBS, Mr. Donnelly held on-air positions at WNEW AM, New York, KYW and WJMJ Radio stations in Philadelphia, and WCOJ Radio in Coatesville, Pennsylvania. He also broadcasted for Armed Forces Radio in Germany. Recently, he was a volunteer at Southern Ocean County Hospital. Raised in Philadelphia, he attended Roman Catholic High School and LaSalle University.
