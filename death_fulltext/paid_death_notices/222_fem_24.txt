
        GARCIA--Celerina. July 3, 2005. Devoted daughter of the late Sixta and Serafin Garcia. Celerina was a longtime employee of Chase Manhattan Bank. Also a Spanish professor at the New School of Social Research. She was a member of Padre Felix Varela Foundation. She was a longtime active parishioner of St. Paul the Apostle Church. Reposing at John J. Barrett & Son Funeral Home, 424 West 51st Street, NYC, 3-9 PM, on Thursday. Funeral Mass at 10 AM, Friday, St. Paul the Apostle R.C. Church, Entombment St. Raymond's Cemetery, Bronx, NY.
