
        LING--George L., Jr., 73, of Fair Lawn, NJ, died Monday, June 21. He was a lawyer and certified public accountant and a founding partner of Behan, Ling & Ruta, New York City. He was a member of the New York and New Jersey Bar associations. He was a graduate of Pace University and Fordham Law School. He was a member of the Church of the Messiah, Paterson, where he served as Treasurer and Elder. Funeral services will be held today at Vander Plaat Colonial Home, Fair Lawn, NJ.
