
        HARRIS-Howard S. A faculty member at Bronx Community College of the City University of New York for over 30 years, succumbed to cancer on April 22, 2000. Professor Harris distinguished himself as an Professor, researcher, author and Chairman of the Social Sciences Department for the past twenty years. He also created several nationally recognized programs, including the establishment of a Human Services Associate Degree curriculum and a child welfare worker certificate program. He was active in the PSC/CUNY Union and served as Chapter Chair at the Bronx campus. Professor Harris is survived by his wife Carol, his son Scott and his parents Sidney and Ruth Harris. A gregarious person by nature and a collaborative colleague, he will be missed. President Carolyn G. Williams Bronx Community College Family
