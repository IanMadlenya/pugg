
        MALLOY-Edwin A. The Board of Trustees and staff at the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our beloved friend, Edwin A. Malloy. He will be warmly remembered for his steadfast support of American artists and his dedication to furthering the welfare and progress of American art. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to his wife Susan and family. Flora M. Biddle Honorary Chairman Leonard A. Lauder Chairman Joel S. Ehrenkranz President Maxwell L. Anderson Director
