
        NICHOLS - Margaret ''Maggie.'' Born June 9, 1931, died July 1. Graduated Antioch, masters at University of New Mexico. First woman editor for ''Field and Stream'' magazine. Inspired photographer. Author of ''Wild, Wild Woman: A Complete Woman's Guide to Enjoying the Great Outdoors.'' Longtime member of the Society of Women Geographers. Graduate professor at Pace. Invaluable consultant and mentor to collegaues and literary friends. Maggie and her late husband Mike traveled extensively but loved NYC, especially Greenwich Village, their home since 1964. They actively supported the arts, especially live jazz and theater. A gathering of the Nichols tribe will be held in Maggie's honor in mid September. Friends may call 212-544-2210 for details.
