
        GLOWATSKY-Edward. Died on April 27th, in Richmond, VA. He was 77. Edward was the beloved husband of the late Toby (Fisch). He lived in East Brunswick, NJ. He was a devoted father of Andrew and Loren, and grandfather of five granddaughters. Surviving family include many loving cousins, sisters-in-law, nephews, nieces, grand-nephews and grand-nieces. A kind, warm neighbor and good friend to all.
