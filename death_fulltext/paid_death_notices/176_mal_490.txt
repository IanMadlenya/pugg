
        RUDIN-Lewis. Another pillar of our community is gone, but has left his mark forever. Friend and colleague in many endeavors, he was always ready to help a worthy cause. Our deepest sympathy to his family. The Weiler and Arnow Families
        RUDIN-Lewis. The New-York Historical Society salutes this great builder of the New York landscape and who improved the lives of its residents. His example counts most in New York today. Kenneth T. Jackson, President
        RUDIN-Lewis. The Officers and Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of a former, long-standing member, Lewis Rudin. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his family. Samuel G. Weinberg, President
