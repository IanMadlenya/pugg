
        ROSSMAN-Lena Spiegel. Passed away Aug. 20, 1997. Beloved aunt of Olga Spiegel and great aunt of Kai Ephron and Fima Ephron. She will also be missed by her many dear friends in NYC & San Diego, CA. She was a graduate of the Sorbonne in Paris and an economist. She was also a permanent consultant at the World Federation of Trade Unions at the United Nations and a social worker. We will miss her greatly. Her courage, intelligence, knowledge and wise counsel will leave a deep void in us all. May her soul rest in peace. A memorial service will be announced at a later date.
