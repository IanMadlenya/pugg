
        KAUFTHAL-Leo. The Moriah School of Englewood mourns the passing of Mr. Leo Kaufthal, father of our esteemed former president, Ilan Kaufthal. The Moriah community will forever be grateful to Ilan for his boundless commitment and visionary leadership. May the remarkable legacy of Leo Kaufthal, a Holocaust survivor, be a source of strength and inspiration to the bereaved family. We extend heartfelt condolences and pray the Kaufthal family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Harvey Silberstein Principal Rella Feldman, President
        KAUFTHAL-Leo. The Ramaz School family mourns the passing of the beloved father of its devoted trustee and alumni parent, Ilan Kaufthal, and its devoted alumni parent, Uri Kaufthal. They demonstrate the greatest honor for their father by their acts of communal support and commitment. We extend heartfelt sympathy to all the members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein, Principal David N. Kahn, Chairman Board of Trustees
