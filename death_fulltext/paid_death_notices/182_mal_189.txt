
        CASEY-James M., 61. Died March 8, at Jacobi Hospital. He was an Arbitrator for the American Arbitration Association and a selfemployed CPA. Memorial services are March 23, 11 AM at the Church of the Messiah, Rhinebeck, NY. Memorials may be made to Center for Hope (Hospice), 176 Hussa Street, Linden, NJ 07036.
