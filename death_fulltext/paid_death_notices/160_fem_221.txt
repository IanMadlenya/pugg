
        CROASDALE-Elsie Osborne. Widow of Stanley B. Croasdale. Died at her home in Englewood, CO, on May 27, at age 100. Born in Walsall, England, she moved with her family as a child to Nutley, NJ. She and her husband lived in Nutley and Madison, NJ, and Fort Lauderdale, FL. Mother of Joyce Croasdale Kappel of Denver, grandmother of Joanne, Margaret and Elizabeth, mother-in-law of Stephen J. Kappel of Financial Information, Inc. Interment at Fort Logan National Cemetery Denver.
