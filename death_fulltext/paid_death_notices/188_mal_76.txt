
        LASH-Richard M., 71, aka ''Ricardo Pestana,'' died August 28 in San Juan, PR, after long illness. Founder of two non-profit organizations and tireless advocate for people with disabilities and their families, especially in low-income communities. Influential in the development of special education and human service systems. Noted for his courage, innovation and irreverent humor. Survived by his loving wife and professional partner, Myrta Cuadra, and by seven children, four stepchildren, fifteen grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
