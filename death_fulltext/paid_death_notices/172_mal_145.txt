
        WILSON-Robert Dickerson. Born May 26, 1925 in Bronxville, N.Y. and died March 22, 2001 in Greenwich, CT. He was General Counsel of U.S. Steel and Carnegie Pension Fund and Secretary of the Pension Fund Investment Committee. He was a Director of Empire Blue Cross/Blue Shield Corp. of N.Y. and General Counsel of Colgate Palmolive International and Assistant Secretary of Colgate Palmolive Co. Survived by sister, Alice Bergan, and four sons, Robert, Jr., David, Steven, and Andrew, and five grandchildren. Memorial services will be held in Lenox, MA. on Saturday, May 19, at 3 PM at The Church on The Hill.
