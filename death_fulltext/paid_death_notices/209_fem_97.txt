
        MARTIN--Helen B. Helen Barker Martin, wife of 54 years to the late John M. Martin, died on June 9, 2004, age 94, in Bloomfield, CT. Mother of John T., deceased, William R.H. of Fair Haven, NJ and Peter B. of Summit, NJ. She is also survived by seven grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren. Services will be private. Memorials may be sent to St. John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor, Long Island, New York 11724.
