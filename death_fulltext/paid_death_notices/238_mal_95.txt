
        MURRAY--Thomson C., of Mill Neck, L.I. suddenly on November 3, 2006 age 73. Beloved husband of Roberta. Loving father of Heather White (Bill), and Thomson C. Murray Jr. Dear brother of G. Donald of Napa, CA, and Robert N. Murray of Houston, TX. Proud grandfather of Turner and Cynnie White. Worked for over 30 years at Doubleday Publishing Co. Attended Hotchkiss School. A graduate of Princeton Univ. A member of the Ivy Club. He served overseas in the Armed Forces. He was an avid fisherman, loved music, and his many friends. A service will be held at St. John's of Lattingtown, Locust Valley on Tuesday November 14, 2006 at 11am. Interment private.
