
        VAN NORDEN--Jeanie, 88, of New York, Died January 25, 2005. She was predeceased by her parents, Ottomar H. and Jeanie Duncan Van Norden and three brothers, Hoghland, Duncan and Langdon. She is survived by two nieces, Olivia Van Norden Truslow, and Susan Van Norden Humphrey and a nephew Langdon Van Norden Jr. She will be sorely missed. Funeral private.
