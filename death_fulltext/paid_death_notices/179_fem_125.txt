
        EDELSTEIN-Heather, 52, passed away peacefully on December 18, 2001 after a long and courageous fight against breast cancer. She had the heart of a lion and served as a beacon of strength to all who had the honor of knowing her. Her family and many dear friends will forever miss her dignity, passion, loyalty, love and devotion. Her infectious smile is emblazoned on our hearts for eternity. Most beloved and devoted wife of Lee, mother of Seth and Lauren, aunt of Sam, Gabe and Noah, sister of Raphael and Phyllis, daughter of Lillian. You will remain always in our hearts and minds. Services Thursday, 2:15pm at Star of David, 7701 Bailey Rd, Tamarac, FL.
