
        KELLEY-Augustus Wilkins III, of Greenwich CT. who was a former vice president of Bristol-Myers Squibb Co, died suddenly at home on August 23 at the age of 80. Mr. Kelley graduated from Choate Academy and St. John's Law School and attended Columbia University. Following his military service in World War II, he was an attorney for Prentiss Hall and Donovan & Leisure before joining Bristol-Myers Co in 1956. He was a member of the board of governors of the Greenwich Country Club and Greenwich RTM, and president of Putnam Hill Apartments, Inc. In addition, he was a member of the Union League Club of New York and a Life Master bridge player. Husband of the late Elizabeth Watson Kelley of Pittsburgh, he leaves one son, Marc Watson Kelley of Wappingers Falls, New York; two daughters, Christina Kelley Gilmartin of Milton, MA and Corrine Kelley Wagner of Scituate, MA; four grandchildren, and eight nieces and nephews. He was a brother of the late Randolph B. Kelley of Greenwich and Albert J. Kelley of Westport. The funeral service will be held at 1pm on Thursday, August 27 at Christ Church. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to the Greenwich Library.
