
        GIEGERICH-Joseph F. Of Eastchester, NY, formerly of the Bronx, retired Battalion Chief, 18th Battalion, New York City Fire Department. Husband of Mary Aileen and the late Claire Giegerich. Father of Stephen, Thomas and Joseph. Grandfather of Marianne, Elizabeth, James, Matthew, Christine, Emily and Daniel. Father by marriage to Linda Jean, Anne Marie, Joseph J. and Mary. Grandfather by marriage to Kristopher, Karen, Jeanne Marie, Keith, Amanda, Andrew, Molly, Megan and Kathleen. A veteran of the U.S. Navy, he held a Bachelor of Arts degree from John Jay School of Criminal Justice and a Master's in English from Fordham University, both obtained in retirement. He was a lover of language, literature and history, and an avid baseball fan. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville between the hours of 79 PM Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM Thursday. Mass of Christian Burial, Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, Scarsdale, on Friday, May 26th at 9:45 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Inner City Scholarship Fund (Archdiocese of NY).
