
        SLATER--Alexander Byers, life-time resident of Locust Valley, NY died February 15. Sandy was an avid Sportsman, a skier, a master angler and an excellent wing shot. He loved his time with nature. He is survived by his wife Susan, son Alexander Jr., daughters Katharyn Hart and Serena Wilson; granddaughters Audrey Alexis and McKenzie. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Dog Therapy Training Organizations, The Delta Society, 875 124th Ave., NE Bellevue, WA 98005, in honor of Therapy Dog ''James'' recent best in show at Westminster and his own Springers ''Rex'', ''Dipper'', ''Corn'' among his many beloved dogs.
