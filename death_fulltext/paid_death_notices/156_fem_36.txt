
        SNYDER-Susan. Of Scarsdale, N.Y. Died Saturday, January 1, 2000 after a long and courageous battle with breast cancer, at age 53. Loving wife of Marc A. Snyder, mother of Rachel Snyder and Michael Snyder and daughter of Lee Krupnick and the late Samuel Krupnick of Saint Louis, MO.
        SNYDER-Susan. We profoundly mourn the untimely death of our dear friend and colleague. Her loving exuberance remains strong in our hearts and minds. We pray that she may yet encounter a kinder and more gentle providence, and extend our most sincere sympathy to her beloved Mark, Michael, Rachel and the bereaved family. Lee and Ralph Kern
