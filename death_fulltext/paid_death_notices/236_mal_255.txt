
        BOSNIAK--Samuel of Philadelphia, PA passed away peacefully on Sunday September 24th at age 90. He was the husband of the late Thelma Bosniak. Loving father of Dr. Stephen Bosniak of NYC, Mark Bosniak Esq. of Philadephia and Karen Patou of Palo Alto, CA. Cherished grandfather of Jordana Bosniak, David Henry, Laura and Caroline Patou.
