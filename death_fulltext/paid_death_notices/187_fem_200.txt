
        MEIERS-Annie Dix. On August 22. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Joseph Meiers, respected educator, admired friend to many. Born in Montgomery, Alabama on October 22, 1902. She was passionate about people and politics. We loved her. We will miss her. She is remembered by her devoted sister-in-law, seven nieces and nephews, 20 grand nieces and nephews, 26 great grand nieces and nephews and many friends. A memorial service will be held in October. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Covenant House, The Southern Poverty Law Center, The St. Labre Indian School or a charity of your choice.
