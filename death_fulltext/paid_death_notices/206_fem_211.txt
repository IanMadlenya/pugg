
        ELLIOTT--Jane Rader. The Board, staff and volunteers of The New York Botanical Garden are deeply saddened by the untimely death of their esteemed friend, Jane Rader Elliott. Jane, as a volunteer, and her husband Wright, as a Board member and Advisor, served the Garden throughout the last decade. We shall miss Jane's charm, vitality, and friendship. We extend our heartfelt condolences to our colleague Wright and their sons. Wilson Nolen, Chairman Gregory Long, President Thomas J. Hubbard, Chairman Emeritus
        ELLIOTT--Jane. The Governors and staff of Lawrence Hospital Center are deeply saddened by the death of our former Governor, Jane Elliott. She was a trusted advisor and friend of the Hospital. Her concern for healthcare in our community was deeply valued and she will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband, Wright, and her entire family. The Board of Governors Lawrence Hospital Center
