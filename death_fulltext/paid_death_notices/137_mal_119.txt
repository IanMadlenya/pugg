
        TRICARICO-James Antone, Sr. Called back to God on June 11, 1998. Devoted husband of Annette. Loving father of Marie A. Kennedy, Rolinda Antone and James A., Jr. Beloved and cherished grandfather of eight. Great grandfather of one. Mr. Tricarico was a veteran of WW II. He was a newspaper reporter for the Fairchild Publications, New York City. Family will receive friends Saturday & Sunday 2-5 and 7-10 PM at the Fredericks Funeral Home, 192-15 Northern Boulevard, Flushing, NY. Mass of Christian Burial Monday 9:45AM St. Mary's Nativity, Flushing. Interment to follow at Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, NY. Donations to St. Jude's Hospital.
