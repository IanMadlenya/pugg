
        VEHLOW--Richard Otto. 62, of East Fishkill, formerly of Bayside, died on March 4, 2005. Born on July 29, 1942, in Brooklyn, he was the son of Otto and Myrtle Vehlow. He served in the US Marine Corps. On June 24, 1967, he married Linda Steinitz in Bayside. He was a science teacher for 34 years at PS 20 in Flushing until retiring in 2002. He is survived by his wife Linda and son Richard. Other survivors are his mother and father-in-law, Elizabeth and Edward Steinitz, and sister-inlaw Nancy Steinitz. Funeral services from McHoul Funeral Home, Inc., 895 Rte 82, Hopewell Junction; 845 221-2000.
