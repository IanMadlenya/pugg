
        BOBER-Irving L., age 90, of Fair Lawn, N.J. Born in Poland and formerly of Passaic, NJ. A World War II Army Veteran. Graduate of New Jersey Law School in 1927. Practicing Attorney in Passaic until 1937. Owner of Apollo Stationery Inc. in New York City, until his retirement in 1983. Survived by his children, Paula Klein and Michael; his son-in-law Stephen; sisters Gertrude Kronish and Sara Edelson; and grandchildren, David and Jennifer Klein. Services are today, 12:30 P.M., at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Route 4 East, Paramus, NJ, followed by interment at Riverside Cemetery, Rochelle Park, New Jersey.
