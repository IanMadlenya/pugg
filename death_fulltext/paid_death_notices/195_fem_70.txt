
        SPIESSBACH-Sister Joseph Dolores, beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, Convent of Mary the Queen, died Wednesday, April 9, 2003. She is the dear sister of Rosemary and Joseph Spiessbach. Reposing at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark Street, Yonkers, NY on Friday 1-8 P.M. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:30 A.M. at the convent. Interment to follow at St. Teresa's Cemetery, Summit, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Avenue, Bronx, NY 10471.
