
        MULHALL-Helene. AMIT is deeply grieved at the untimely passing of the beloved sister of our most devoted friend, advisor and supporter, Alvin Hellerstein, and cherished sister-in-law of our most dedicated National Vice President, Mildred Hellerstein. A person of many talents, Helene Mulhall lived a varied life, and through her personal Aliyah demonstrated her love for the people and State of Israel. Heartfelt condolences to her beloved mother, brother, children and the entire family. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Dir.
