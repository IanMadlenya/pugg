
        PENN-Sylvia Harris. December 10, 1914 to September 3, 2002. Beloved mother of Eileen Penn Posnick and the late Ronald Penn, grandmother of Joshua Posnick, sister of Anne Harris Chanen and the late Dr. Arnold Harris, widow of Yiddish journalist Ascher Penn, mother-inlaw of Michael Posnick, ''Bubby'' to all. Praise her, a woman of complete faith and unconditional love. Memorial donations to Project Ezra, 197 E. Broadway, NY, NY 10002.
