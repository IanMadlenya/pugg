
        DELMHORST-Margaret McGill. On February 5, 1998, in Stonington, CT, in her 92nd year. Wife of the late Berton J. Delmhorst, mother of Arthur M. Delmhorst of Riverside, CT; George B. Delmhorst of Brooklyn, NY; and John R. Delmhorst of Fairfield, CT. Also survived by a sister Georgiana McG. Hull of Bedford Village, NY, and grandchildren Dwight, Kristina, Jennifer, Robert, Frederic and James. She was President of the Alumnae Association and Trustee of The Packer Collegiate Institute, President of the Women's Auxiliary of the Brooklyn Botanic Garden, and Trustee of the Brooklyn Institute of Arts and Sciences. An avid sportswoman, gracious gardener, lover of literature and opera, she is loved by all who knew her. A memorial service will be held at the United Church in Stonington, CT, on Thursday, February 12, at 2 PM. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to Packer, 170 Joralemon Street, Brooklyn, NY 11201 or The Brooklyn Botanic Garden, 1000 Washington Avenue, Brooklyn, NY 11225.
