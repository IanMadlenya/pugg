
        GELFAND--Pauline. Born September 13, 1909, died August 9, 2006. Beloved mother, loyal friend and proud active supporter of Jewish Women International, B'nai Brith and the Anti-Defamation League. She is survived by a daugher, Carol of Manhattan and a son Thomas of Brooklyn. Services Sunday August 13, 11:30am at Nieberg's Midwood Chapel, Coney Island Ave and Ave M, Brooklyn.
