
        KEARNS-Joseph G. In his 91st year. On June 20, 2002 in Cooperstown, NY. Of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn and Breezy Point, Queens. Mr. Kearns was President of Daniel F. Young, Inc., International Freight Freight Forwarders. He was responsible for the safe transportation of Michael Angelo's ''Pieta Statue'' from Rome to New York City World's Fair in 1964. Mr. Kearns was a long time parishioner of Saint Anselm's R.C. Church as well as a long time member of Knights of Holy Sepulcher and Knights of Malta. Mr. Kearns is survived by his beloved wife of 57 years, Agnes (nee Kelly), his daughter Kristine A. Kearns and her friend Ann Walsh, daughter-in-law Marianne Hokke, grandchildren Michael and Marieke, brother-in-law William Kelly and many nieces and nephews. He is pre-deceased by his sons Michael and Stephen, and five brothers and sisters. Visiting Sunday 2-4PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 Fourth Ave, Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial Monday, 10AM, Saint Anselm's Church. In lieu of flowers, expresssions of sympathy in the form of memorial gifts may be made to Catskills Area Hospice and Palliative Care, Inc., 542 Main St, Oneonta, NY. 13820.
