
        KHADIR-Omar. On May 7, 2002. Born Nov. 29, 1937 in Casablanca. Lived in New York since 1965. Chief Chef to the Moroccan Ambassador to the UN and to other distinguished people. We will miss his warm charm and good sense of humor. He was a wonderful companion and friend. God bless. Funeral services at Campbell's, Madison Avenue and 81st Street, Friday, 11:30 AM. Philip Hahn
