
        RYALS-Juanita Opal Bettes. Died July 3 in Oklahoma City. Born in Texas, 1910, to Ellen and William Andrew Bettes. Graduate, Lexington (OK) High School, 1928; Hill's Business College, Okla. City; and was a legal secretary. Married Marlin D. Ryals (d. 1990) in 1936; became a homemaker. Children: DeLane, Dean, Kent, Marlene (White), Ted, and Randel. Her Her spirit of love continues.
