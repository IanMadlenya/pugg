
        BENNETT-Stewart. Died on September 3, 2000 in Dallas, TX after a long illness. Born January 6, 1927 Brooklyn, NY. He graduated from Harvard College and the Harvard Business School. Until he became ill some years ago Mr. Bennett was engaged in oil and gas exploration and production. He is survived by his beloved wife Ann Fisher Bennett & was the devoted father of Stewart Bennett, Jr., Martha B. Huffman, Katherine Bennett and Elizabeth B. Pascal all of Dallas as well as two grandchildren & a brother Robert Bennett of Washington, D.C. Funeral services were in Dallas. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to the American Lung Assoc, 8150 Brookriver Dr, Dallas, TX 75247
