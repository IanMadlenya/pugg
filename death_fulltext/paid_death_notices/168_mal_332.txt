
        CLAY-Joseph A. of Douglaston, NY. On Sunday, January 21. Devoted husband of Agnes; beloved father of Barbara, Mary Jane, Patricia, Kathleen, and Suzanne. Joe was a retired Sr. Vice President of TWA and President of Air LaCarte; veteran of the US Army Air Force during World War II. Avid gardener and reader, exceptional grandfather to Clay, Ben, Harry, Kaitlyn, Emma, and Luke. We will cherish memories of him always. Reposing at the Frederick Funeral Home of Flushing, corner of 193rd St. and Northern Blvd. Funeral Mass Wednesday 9:45 A.M. at St. Anastasia's R.C. Church, Douglaston. Visiting hrs Tuesday 2-5, 7-9 p.m. In lieu of flowers, donations to Ozanam Hall, 42-41 201 St., Bayside, NY would be greatly appreciated.
