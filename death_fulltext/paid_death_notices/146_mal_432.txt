
        MALINA-William. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of William Malina, devoted husband of Jean, beloved father of Robert and Michael and father-in-law of Fran and Anita. Proud and loving grandfather and great-grandfather, his charitable legacy is carried on by his family members, who have actively supported the work of UJA-Federation. His presence will be greatly missed. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec V.P.
        MALINA-William. The City Athletic Club mourns the death of our member, William Malina, and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. City Athletic Club Board of Govenors
