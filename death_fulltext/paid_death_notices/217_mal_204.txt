
        WIDDRINGTON--Peter N. T. The Board of Directors, Officers and Staff of Radiology Corporation of America deeply mourn the passing of our beloved and admired colleague, Peter Widdrington. Peter, in his responsibilities as Vice-Chairman of our Board was quick to employ his wisdom, experience and courage to our problems and our opportunities. His fearless, candid and deliberate style made him invaluable to our Company faced with both startup and new technology challenges. Peter was also a man of compassion, always concerned with those less fortunate. Our thoughts and prayers are with his devoted wife, Betty Ann and his beloved two daughters, Cindy and Stacey and grandchildren, Patrick, Peter and Jack. Monroe R. Meyerson, Chairman Allen D. McGee, President and CEO
