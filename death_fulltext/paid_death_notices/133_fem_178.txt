
        KENNEDY-Elizabeth Wright. Of New York City and Chatham, MA. On February 20. Mrs. Kennedy was in her 30th year of teaching at St. Bernard's School. She is survived by her daughter Cornelia Kennedy Atkins, son-in-law Lawrence Atkins, and grandson Robert Andrew Atkins. Memorial service 10 AM, Saturday, March 7, at Holy Trinity Episcopal Church, 316 E. 88 St. Contributions may be made to the Sarah Wright Atkins Fund, St. Bernard's School, 4 E. 98 Street, NYC or St. Christopher's Episcopal Church, Chatham, MA.
