
        URRY-Mrs. Charles F. Known as Kathe, passed away on February 10th in Locust Valley, NY at the age of 83. She was born in Prague, educated in Switzerland and London and was a graduate of the Sorbonne. She is survived by Valerie Urry, James & daughterin-law, Lieta Urry and three grandchildren, Nathalie, Katherine, Charles and a brother, Eric W. Weinmann. A funeral Service will be held at 10 A.M. on February 14th at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, at 73rd Street and Madison Avenue, New York.
