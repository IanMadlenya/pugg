
        PAASWELL-Lucille Schoenbrod, 95. Quietly on March 14th, in New York City. Wife of the late Dr. Benjamin Paaswell, sister of the late Edward and Gilbert Schoenbrod. Mother of Jean Paaswell Bailinson. Grandmother of Ann Kleinman, Frank Bailinson, Jr., and James Bailinson. Grandmother-inlaw of Jerome Kleinman, Jann Dickerson and Nancy Gibson. Great-grandmother of Jill Kleinman, Emily and Charlotte Bailinson and Peter Bailinson. She was loved by all and will be missed by all. Memorial will be held at a future date.
