
        STAUDERMAN-Rev. Albert P. Died November 3, 2002 at Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center, FL, at age 92. He touched the lives of many as the editor of ''The Lutheran Magazine'' and earlier in his ministry as Pastor of St. Paul's Lutheran Church in Teaneck, NJ. He is survived by his wife Ruth (Dimon), children Albert Jr. and Susan, and by eight grandchildren. A memorial service in celebration of his life will be at 11AM, Friday, November 8, at St. John's Lutheran Church, Lake Park, FL.
