
        JELENKO-Jesse F. Jr. On Dec. 24, 1997, after a brief illness, in Edina, MN. Born July 28, 1913. He was past president of J.F. Jelenko & Company, a leading producer of dental products. He is survived by two children and four grandchildren. Services will be held Sunday January 4, at 1 P.M., at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, New York.
