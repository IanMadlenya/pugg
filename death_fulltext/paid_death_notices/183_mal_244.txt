
        KRONES-Fred H., on April 18th, 2002. Born January 17th, 1926 in Passaic, NJ. Son of Edward and Anna. Husband of the late Rose Goodstein. He will be deeply missed by sister Amy Ross, children Patty and Michael Silver, Michael and Janet Krones, Judith Krones and David Schorr, grandchildren Jesse and Eliot Silver, Sarah, Jonathan, Ariella and Jeremy Krones, Ronit and Natanya Schorr. Formerly of attorneys, Goodstein, Mehlman & Krones and more recently, a producer of theater and film. Funeral service Sunday, 11 AM, at Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY. Contributions to UJA Federation in his memory.
