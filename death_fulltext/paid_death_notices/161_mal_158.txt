
        STONEBRIDGE-Rev. John. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired Pastor Emeritus of Sacred Heart Church, Glendale, on June 13. Reposing at Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221st St., Queens Village, N.Y., on Thursday, June 15, from 3-5 and 78 p.m. Mass of Transferral on Thursday, June 15, at 8 p.m. Funeral Mass on Friday, June 16, at Queen of Peace Residence, at 10:30 a.m. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatiorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
