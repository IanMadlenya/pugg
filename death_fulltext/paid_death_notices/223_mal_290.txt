
        SETON--Charles. It is with deep sorrow that we mourn the loss of one of the founders of our Society, a leader of the American copyright bar, a mentor to many generations of copyright lawyers and a philanthropist whose contributions will live on through his endowment of the award that bears his name in recognition of the best writing from young lawyers in the field of copyright. You shall be remembered. The Officers and Trustees of the Copyright Society of the USA
