
        JAFFE-Winnifred. After a long illness, Winnifred Jaffe passed away on July 21, 1997. She was born October 16, 1898 and spent almost half a century in education. Her educational degrees were from Teacher's College, Columbia University. Her teaching was almost entirely in Ridgefield, N.J. She is survived by her brother Dr. William J. Jaffe. Her late parents were Elias & Ida Rosensohn Jaffe. Her late brother and sister were Dr. John Jaffe and Sophie G. Jaffe. Services at Gutterman's, Wednesday 9:30 AM, 331 Amsterdam Avenue near W. 76th St, NYC.
