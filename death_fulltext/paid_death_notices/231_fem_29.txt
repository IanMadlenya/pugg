
        FLEMING--Penny. A longtime ABC News Producer, died Monday, April 3, 2006 of pancreatic cancer. Penny spent the last 15 years producing stories for 20/20. Before coming to ABC, Penny was a producer at NBC and ''The Today Show.'' She is survived by her daughters, Emma and Abby, stepsons Scott & Craig, grandchildren Colin, Zachary and Joshua. In lieu of flowers, the family request donations be made to Abby and Emma's Education Fund.
