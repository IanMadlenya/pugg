
        SCHWARTZ -- David Got tschalk, Dr. Died On April 12, 2005, at the age of 87. Beloved husband of Phyllis Kess, father of Jonathan, Douglas and Thomas. Grandfather of Gregory, Andrew and Jennifer. Devoted to his family, medicine and his many patients. Attended Townsend Harris High School, Cornell University, and Louisiana State Medical School. He was an alumnus of Mt. Sinai Hospital, an associate attending physician at North Shore and Beth Israel hospitals and an associate A.F.A.C. A captain in the Medical Corps in World War II, serving bravely in North Africa, southern France, and Italian campaigns, earning a Purple Heart. A true family physician in the highest tradition on call day and night, year-round. He loved life and did all he could do to ensure long life for all under his care. He will be truly missed by all who had the honor to know him. Services Friday, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave (81st St)
