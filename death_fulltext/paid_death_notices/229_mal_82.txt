
        EHRLICH--Harvey B. Died peacefully at age 98 after a brief illness. Beloved husband of the late Belle ''Bubbles''; father of Barbara and Jules Reich, and Jerrold and Vivian (Fenster) Ehrlich; grandfather of Mark and Insuk, Bruce and Lisa (Mirowitz), Alan and June, Randi and Alan Kahn, Philip, Jeffrey and Barbara Reich, Laurie and Keith Emmer, and Joshua. Great grandfather of Leanna, Madison, Mariel, Jason, Rebecca, Matthew, Stephanie and Justin. Harvey, an attorney since 1929, devoted his life to the service of the people. He was one of the founders of the New York State Liberal Party and served as Party Secretary for Queens County and Staff Director for the New York City Council. As one of New York's leading attorneys, he served as: Deputy Chief Assistant DA for Queens County; Head of Special Investigations Unit and Chief of the Settlement Bureau of the New York City Comptroller's Office; and Confidential Secretary to a New York State Supreme Court Justice. He worked tirelessly for the Zionist Organization of America and B'nai B'rith. After retirement, he was a founder of the American Society of Retired Attorneys and volunteered as a hearing officer in the courts and schools of Broward County, Florida. He was inducted into the Broward County Senior Volunteer Hall of Fame for his services to the community. He was deeply devoted to his family who celebrate his long and rich life and mourn his loss. Donations may be made to DOROT, 171 West 85 Street, NY NY 10024. Memorial services will be held on Tuesday, February 7 at 11:15 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave at 76 Street. Burial at Mt. Ararat Cemetery in E. Farmingdale, NY. The family will be sitting Shiva at the home of Jerry and Vivian Ehrlich, Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday, 4-9.
