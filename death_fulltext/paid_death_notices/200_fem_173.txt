
        FAROLL--Leonie. On September 18, 2003 at age 75. Beloved daughter of the late Joseph and Berenice Faroll and sister of the late Miriam Stich and survived by one nephew and two nieces. Miss Faroll was the bastion of strength of the residents and staff of 1120 Park Avenue and served as a historian and treasurer for more than 30 years. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Tuesday, September 23rd, 7-9 PM with services Wednesday, September 24th, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Linden Hill Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Wellesley College or a charity of your choice.
