
        CHARNEY-Wolfe R. Wife Rita, (Greenfield) Charney, sons Jonathan, Michael, Richard, and Robert, and 7 grandchildren mourn the death of their loving and devoted Wolfe. Graduate of Yeshiva College and Harvard Law, Attorney, generous and strong supporter of Israel, Jewish culture, Arts and Community, he died April 15, in Connecticut at age 85. Born in London to Rabbi and Mrs. Aaron Charney, raised in Bayonne, NJ, he lived in New Rochelle, Scarsdale, Manhattan, NY, and Weston, CT. He is survived by his wife, sons and sister Ruth Rubin of Haifa. In lieu of flowers, donations in his honor to NYANA, or Anti-Defamation League of B'nai Brith. Service: April 17, 1:00PM, Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, New York.
        CHARNEY-Wolfe R. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Rita and the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd Paul S. Schreiber, President
