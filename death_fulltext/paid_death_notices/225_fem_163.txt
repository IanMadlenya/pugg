
        BERGMAN--Maxine (Feinberg) of Hartsdale, N.Y. on October 24, 2005 died at the age of 77. She is survived by her husband of 56 years Melvyn H.; loving children Gene & Wendy and Keith and Jill; dear grandchildren Elijah, Hilary, and Woody; loving brother Philip and sister-inlaw Eileen. She lived her life to the fullest, traveling the world, skiing, and playing golf almost to the end. She was a selfless friend to all and will be remembered for her kindness, graciousness, positive and fun-loving outlook on life, and charitable endeavors. Funeral services to be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel 21 West Broad St, Mt. Vernon, on Thursday 10 AM. October 27.
