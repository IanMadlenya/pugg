
        KORNBERG-Frieda. On January 7th, 2001 in her 90th year. Devoted wife of the late Henry Kornberg. She is survived by her three children, Jacques Kornberg, Cecile Sheramy and Stephen Kornberg, seven grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Beloved by her family, she will be dearly missed. Memorial contributions may be made to the Margaret Teitz Nursing Home, Jamaica, Queens. Services today, 1 PM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Boulevard (76th Road), Forest Hills, NY.
