
        ROTH-A. Sidney. On Thursday, December 6, in Andover, MA; formerly of Palm Beach, FL and Massapequa, NY. Predeceased by his wife, Selma (Halpert). Survived by his children, Jonathan and Barbara of Westport, CT; Peter and Amy of Rochester, NY and Leslie and David Silverstein of Andover, MA and his grandchildren, David and Nancy; Michael and Deborah; Jamie; and Scott and Brett Silverstein. Loyal alumnus of Cornell University where he was an AllAmerican football player. Former president of the Long Island Association of Home Builders. Donations may be made in his memory to the Alzheimer's Association, 919 North Michigan Avenue, Chicago, IL 60611 or the American Diabetes Association, Memorial and Honor Program-NCC, P.O. Box 2680, North Canton, OH 44720.
