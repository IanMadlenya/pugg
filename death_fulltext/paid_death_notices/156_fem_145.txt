
        WEENOLSEN-Hebe., 93, author of ''The Last Englishman,'' ''To Keep this Oath,'' ''The Forbidden Mountain,'' and ''The Trial of Jennie Sykes,'' died on December 19 in Manhattan. She was preceded in death by her husband, Robert Weenolsen, and her son, Robert Weenolsen, Jr. Surviving are her daughter, Dr. Patricia Weenolsen, and Melissa Sirovina of Seattle, Washington; Pamela, Valerie, and Jennifer Marott of California, and Anne, Dr. Stephen, and Sean Gray of Illinois.
