
        LOWRY - John C. on January 3, 1997. A memorial service will be held on Friday, at 121:15 AM. at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        LOWRY - John C. The Officers, Directors and employees of The Buckingham Research Group deeply mourn the passing of our beloved and long time Senior Vice-President, John Lowry. His intellectual prowess, good humor, and above all, his mature wisdom and sagacity will sorely be missed by all of us. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his wife Mio and his daughters, Lisa, Kathryn and Erica, his grandchildren, Eric and Caroline, and his brother George. May the memories of his love and devotion comfort them in this trying time. Memorial services are planned for Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, on Friday, January 10 at 11:15 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Asphalt Green, The Riverdale Country Day School or favorite chairty.  The Buckingham Research Group
