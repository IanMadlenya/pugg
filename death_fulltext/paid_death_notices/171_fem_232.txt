
        AUER-Elizabeth Croker. A longtime resident of Armonk, NY, on April 27, age 81. Born in Manhattan, Mrs. Auer later moved with her family to White Plains, before moving to Cedarhurst, LI. She graduated from Our Lady of Mercy Academy, Syosset, NY, and was married for 55 years to the late George J. Auer Jr., a partner in the law firm of Brewer and Auer, White Plains, NY. She held various leadership roles in the Westchester County Women's Republican League and was a volunteer at White Plains Hospital. She is survived by her brothers Howard and Wallace Croker of Belfast, ME, her daughter Kathleen James of Mt. Kisco, NY, her sons George J. Auer III, of Medford Lakes, NJ, and James T. Auer of South Weymouth, ME, and ten grandchildren. Visiting hours will be on Monday, April 30, from 5 PM until 7 PM, at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains. Funeral mass Tuesday, 10 AM, at St. John the Evangelist Church, White Plains. Interment Middle Patent Cemetery, Bedford, NY. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be sent to White Plains Hospital.
