
        CROSS-Deming Stedman, Jr., 49, of NYC. A banker at Barclay's Bank of New York, he died August 11 at the New York University Hospital after an extended illness. He was born in Providence, RI, April 28, 1948, the son of Deming and Dorothy Cross of Wakefield, R.I. and Beaufort, S.C. He leaves a friend, Dante Bini of NYC, a sister, Dana Coes, and was predeceased by another sister, Lacy Cross. He also leaves 3 nieces and 2 nephews. He was a graduate of Pomfret School, Washington University in St. Louis, and received his law degree from St. Louis University Law School.
