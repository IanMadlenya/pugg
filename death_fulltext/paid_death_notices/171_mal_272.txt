
        FINE-Arnold, on April 19, at age 93. His illustrious 60 year career began at Interstate Department Stores, then as a Controller for Wilbur Rogers and consultant for the Insurance Department of the State of N.Y. Beloved husband of Cecile. Loving father of Richard, Paula and Jane. Cherished grandfather of Mark, Caryn and Nancy. Services will be held at 12 Noon, April 22 at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapel 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills. Contributions may be made in his name at the Forest Hills Jewish Center.
