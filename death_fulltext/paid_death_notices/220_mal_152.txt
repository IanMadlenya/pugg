
        RONKIN--Semyon. Mannes College of Music, a division of New School University, mourns the passing of Semyon Ronkin, violin teacher in the Extension and Preparatory Divisions since 1994. Mannes will miss his dedication to teaching and his devotion to his students. Philip Scaturro, Chair, Board of Trustees Bob Kerrey, President John Beerbower, Chair, Mannes Board of Governors Joel Lester, Dean Mannes College
