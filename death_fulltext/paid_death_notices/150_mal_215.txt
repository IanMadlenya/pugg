
        FISH-Herman L. -On July 7, 1999, age 80. Resident of San Francisco. Graduate of Queens College, class of '41. Clinical psychologist for NYC Bureau of Child Guidance for 23 years. Taught at Brooklyn and Queens Colleges and Pace Institute in Manhattan. Member of the American Psychological Association for 50 years. Survived by his wife of 56 years, Florence, his children Kenneth Fish of Fairport, NY, Victoria Reed of San Francisco, his sister Ida Pearl Karr of Georgetown, Texas, and four grandchildren.
