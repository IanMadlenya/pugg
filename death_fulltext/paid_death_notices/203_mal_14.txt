
        ORZACK--Louis, of Newton formerly of New York, passed away November 27, 2003. He was the beloved husband of Maressa (Hecht). Dear son of the late Morris and Jennie Orzack. Devoted father of Deborah of Worcester, Steven Orzack and Ariane Cherbuliez of Cambridge, Elizabeth and Joshua Friedman of Connecticut. Loving brother of the late Everett, Alvyn, and Beatrice. Cherished grandfather of Margot and Jacob Friedman and, Nora Cherbuliez-Orzack. A proud graduate of Townsend Harris High School, City College, Columbia University, and Indiana University. He was a professor of sociology at Rutgers University. Memorial service will be held at a time and date to be announced. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to The Townsend Harris High School Alumni Association, 149-11 Melbourne Ave., Flushing, New York 11367.
