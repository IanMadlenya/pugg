
        LOTZ-Jack (Jacob). On June 13, 1997. Beloved husband of Doris. Devoted father of Janet Robinson. Well known and respected in the men's clothing industry for over 50 years and served as office manager for Joseph H. Cohen and Sons. After he retired he became a volunteer teacher of English to foreign-speaking students. Services Monday, 1:15 PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
