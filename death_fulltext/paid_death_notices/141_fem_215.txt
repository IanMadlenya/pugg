
        REUILLARD - Pearl Schwartz. Died October 23, peacefully at home at age 90. Pearl was a retired teacher from Thomas Jefferson High School in Brooklyn. She was involved in political activities all of her life, especially with The League of Women Voters. Pearl will be missed by her many nieces, nephews and friends. Funeral services Tuesday, October 27, 1:00 PM, at ''BoulevardRiverside Chapels,'' 312 Coney Island Ave, at Prospect Park, Brooklyn. Interment to follow at Baron Hirsch Cemetery, Staten Island.
