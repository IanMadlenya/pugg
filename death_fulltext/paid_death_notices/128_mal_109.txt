
        GRUNEBAUM-Edward J. Died on September 7, 1997. Beloved husband of Helen. Devoted father of James, the late Andrew, and Robert. Loving grandfather of Jason. Family receiving Wednesday from 7-9 P.M., 5 Riverside Drive. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Jewish Guild for the Blind or to your charity.
        GRUNEBAUM-Edward. The Officers, Directors, Trustees and staff of The Jewish Guild for the Blind note with deep sorrow the passing of Edward Grunebaum, beloved husband of Helen, a Trustee of The Guild and past President of our Women's Division. Ed was a gentle man who generously supported The Guild in our efforts to improve the lives of blind and visually impaired persons. James M. Dubin, Chairman John F. Heimerdinger, President
