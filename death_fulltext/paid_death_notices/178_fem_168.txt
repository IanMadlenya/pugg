
        WALSH-Patricia Ann. Of Palisades, NY, died at home on November 16, 2001 at the age of 50, after a courageous battle with ovarian cancer. She was the daughter of the late Anne Savino Walsh and William Walsh. Ms. Walsh held a Masters in Social Work from New York University and a Masters in Media Studies from the New School for Social Research. She had been employed as a social worker by Alternative to Domestic Violence in Hackensack, New Jersey. In addition, Ms. Walsh was a long distance runner who successfully completed the New York City Marathon in 1992. She climbed Mount Kilimanjaro and other mountains; was an accomplished costume designer; and enjoyed museums, opera, and travel. Her friends described her as having a wonderful sense of humor and great compassion. She is survived by her 11 year old twin sons, Noah and Aidan Walsh, her partner Cristina Biaggi, many caring friends, and her six dogs, three birds, and cat. A funeral service will be held, Tuesday, 1 pm at the Palisades Presbyterian Church in Palisades, NY. Visiting hours will be Monday, 7-10 pm, at the Moritz Funeral Home, 98 Route 303 South, Tappan, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Alternative to Domestic Violence, 1 Bergen County Plaza, Hackensack, NJ 07601, 201-336-7575 .
