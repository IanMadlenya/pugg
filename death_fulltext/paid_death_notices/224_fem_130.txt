
        MILLER--Ida Peiken. Formerly of Rockville Centre, NY, and Lake Worth, FL, recently of Huntington Beach, CA, died Sunday, at 94. Services Tuesday, Boynton Beach, FL, Sinai Memorial Chapel. Her husband, Henry J. Miller died in Florida two years ago. She leaves behind two children, Mrs. Susan Spiritus of Newport Coast, CA, and Dr. Frederick Miller of Denver, CO, and four granddaughters, Margo and Lisa Spiritus and Joanna and Rachel Miller.
