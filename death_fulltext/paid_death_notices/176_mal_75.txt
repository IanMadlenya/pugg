
        SIEGLER-Ralph T., 79, died peacefully in his sleep September 6. Husband of Rodie Siegler; father of David, John and Ruth; husband of Corrinne Siegler, father of Adam and Katherine. Doting grandfather of Jake, Jessica, Cory, Nello, Charles, Sophia and Marlon. A full and happy life, a restless and inquisitive intellect, a loving and attentive father and grandfather. We love you & miss you. Memorial service Thursday, September 13, 3:00 PM, Riverdale Yacht Club, 718-543-0792.
