
        SHACKMAN-Nathan H, MD, retired physician, passed away Dec. 19, 1998, in Santa Barbara, California, at age 89. A graduate of NYU School of Medicine, he practiced internal medicine and cardiology in Brooklyn, New York, and Los Angeles, CA. Devoted husband, father, and physician, he is survived by his beloved wife, Dorothy, and their son, Dr. Daniel Shackman, of Santa Barbara, as well as friends, family, and generations of grateful patients.
