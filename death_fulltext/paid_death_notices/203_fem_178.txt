
        FEIGELMAN--Miriam (nee Richman), 79, of Bayside, Queens. Died on Thursday, December 18th. She was the beloved wife of Victor. Mother of Ted, Elizabeth, Susan and Paul. Mother-in-law of Katie, Dean, Cliff and Kathleen and grandmother of seven. Many will remember her as a magnetic and dynamic teacher and as a devoted friend. Also survived by her sister Esther Goldberg.
