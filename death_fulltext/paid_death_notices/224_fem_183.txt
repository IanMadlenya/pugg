
        ROSENTHAL--Jacqueline. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Jacqueline Rosenthal, who together with her beloved late husband, John Rosenthal, set an example of philanthropic leadership imbued with compassionate generosity and the spirit of klal Yisrael. Their commitment enabled us to enrich Jewish life and learning in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We extend our deepest sympathies to their children: John Paul Jr., Laura, Lisa, and Ronald; to their grandchildren; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
