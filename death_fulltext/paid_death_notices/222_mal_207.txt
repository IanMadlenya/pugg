
        FREEDBERG -- Irwin, M.D. The faculty and residents of the Department of Dermatology, Henry Ford Hospital, Detroit, Michigan, note with sadness the passing of Irwin Freedberg, M.D., Chairman of the Ronald O. Perelman Department of Dermatology at NYU. Dr. Freedberg was one of the greats in dermatology who was an excellent chairman, superb leader, invaluable mentor, trusted advisor, and dear friend. He had served on the leadership position of all major dermatologic organizations, had edited the major textbook of dermatology and the most respected investigative dermatology journal, and had made numerous contributions in advancing the scientific base of dermatology. His enthusiasm for and his service to this specialty is an inspiration to all. He has left a lasting impact on dermatology and he will be dearly missed by all. Our heartfelt condolences go to his wife Irene, his children and his loving family. Henry W. Lim, M.D., Chairman and C.S. Livingood Chair, Department of Dermatology, Henry Ford Hospital, Detroit, Michigan
        FREEDBERG--Irwin M. M.D. The Department of Medicine at the NYU School of Medicine is deeply saddened by the death of our dear colleague, Irwin M. Freedberg, M.D. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family and colleagues. Martin J. Blaser, M.D. Chair Department of Medicine NYU School of Medicine
