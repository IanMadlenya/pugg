
        BAKALAR--Alan. 83, of New York City, died peacefully on June 6, 2004. Husband, father, and grandfather, World War II Army Air Corps pilot, quiet benefactor of many personally known and unknown to him. His steady presence will be sorely missed by Ruth,his wife of 62 years; his sons Jake, Nick, and Kenny; his daughters - in - law Susan, Francine, and his grandchildren Elizabeth, Dana, and Matthew.
