
        FAISON-John Williams. On December 5, 1997, in his 80th year. Beloved husband of Grace Gray Faison. Devoted father of Sandy Faison and Jeff Stockly Faison. Dear brother of Seth Shepard Faison, Sr. Adored grandfather of Jeneve Noel Pendleton, John Richard Faison, Jeffrey Shepard Faison, Molly Remsen Brush and Lucy Gray Brush. Memorial service onTuesday, December 9, 11 AM, at Plymouth Church of The Pilgrims, Orange and Hicks Streets, Brooklyn Heights. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims, or the Brooklyn Bureau of Community Service, 285 Schermerhorn St., Brooklyn, NY 11217.
