
        AUMANN-Esther (nee Schlesinger). Age 67, wife of Robert John Aumann, passed away October 23, in Jerusalem. Buried on the Mount of Olives in Jerusalem. Esther is survived by her husband, four children, 14 grandchildren, four sisters and a brother. Esther's thirst for knowledge was insatiable, and her interests-and insights-embraced the world. The love and warmth she radiated were reciprocated by all whose lives were touched by hers.
