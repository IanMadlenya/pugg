
        KENNEDY--Otto W., 98. Born Vienna, Austria, November 14, 1905, died August 12, 2004. A lawyer, like his father, Dr. Julius Knopfmacher, Otto left Vienna in 1938 after the Nazi invasion. He fled to England, then New York City,and retired to Los Angeles, Ca. He served in the US Air Force during WWII. Married 34 years to his beloved wife, Eva Kennedy. Brilliant and charming, he will be missed by his sister-in-law Lily, nieces Lilo and Ines and their children and grandchildren.
