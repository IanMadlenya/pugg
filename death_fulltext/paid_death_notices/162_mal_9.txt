
        HAGGITT-Rodger C., M.D. The Board of Trustees, National Scientific Advisory Committee, and staff of the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America are deeply saddened by the tragic loss of our esteemed colleague and dear friend, Dr. Rodger C. Haggitt. A brilliant gastrointestinal pathologist, Dr. Haggitt had a profound impact on the care of people with Crohn's disease and ulcerative colitis. His commitment to CCFA led to the development of professional education programs of the highest quality. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family. May they be comforted by the knowledge that his contributions to medicine will continue to touch thousands of lives. Lisa H. Richardson, Chairperson of the Board Charles O. Elson, M.D., Chairperson, National Scientific Advisory Committee James V. Romano, Ph.D., President and C.E.O.
