
        CARLTON-Helen Storey. 76 years old, died on March 9, 2002, of Somers, NY, former longtime resident of South Salem, NY. Former Assistant Fashion Editor for Life Magazine in New York City. Wife of Richard K. Carlton, mother of Douglass K., & Alexander Carlton, grandmother of Scott Alexander Carlton. Memorial Service will be held at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, N.Y., Thursday at 9 A.M. Contributions in her memory may be made to: The Visiting Nurses Association, 100 South Bedford Road, 3rd Floor, Mount Kisco, New York 10549.
