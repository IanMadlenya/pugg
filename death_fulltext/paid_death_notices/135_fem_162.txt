
        PHILLIPS-Barbara T. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center (BIMC) family joins the staff of Jeanette and Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Dept in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Barbara T. Phillips, beloved wife of our dear friend and colleague, Lewis A. Phillips, cousin of Beth Israel Trustee Carol P. Green, and her brother, our generous benefactor Lawrence Phillips. To Lewis, the entire Phillips family, and all who loved her, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees, BIMC Matthew E. Sink, M.D., President, BIMC Terry Williams, Director, Jeanette and Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Dept
