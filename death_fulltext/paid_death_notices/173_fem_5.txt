
        FISCHER-Harriet Shapiro. Of Boca Raton, passed away May 30, 2001. Cherished treasure of her husband, Dr. Milton Fischer; her sons Mark and Steven Shapiro; her grandchildren Joe and Eva, and her sister Florence Justin. Her light lives on in all of us. Services Sunday, June 3, 11:30 AM, at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (near 91st St), Manhattan, New York.
