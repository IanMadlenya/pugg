
        KATZ-Eli. Age 88, passed away at home in Coconut Creek, Florida, November 18. Mr. Katz formerly of Roslyn & Great Neck, practiced law until 1994. He was a graduate of NYU and Columbia University School of Law, and was qualified to the Supreme Court of the United States in 1969. He served as Mayor of Thomaston and was active in B'nai Brith. He contributed to many Jewish philanthropies. Mr. Katz was an avid tennis player and swimmer. He enjoyed sixtythree years of married life with his wife Beulah who survives him. Also surviving, his children Lorna Lawson, and Charles Katz, and grandchildren Jason and Djahariah Katz.
