
        BENDERSKY - Max, M.D., F.A.C.C.P. Age 80 of Tamarac, Florida, formerly of Brightwaters, L.I. Beloved husband of Dorothy (nee Fryburg). Cherished father of Beatrice of Tamarac, FL., Dr. Ruby of Albuquerque, N.M., and Sarah of Portland, OR. Devoted grandfather of Kim and Ann, son of the late Tess & Sam Bendersky and brother of the late Jacob. Born in Amityville, L.I., graduated NYU in 1937 and received his medical degree from the University of Lausanne, Switzerland in 1942. He interned at Knickerbocker Hospital in New York City. He specialized in Pulmonary diseases serving residencies in Denver, CO., Syracuse, NY, and Patchoque, L.I. He entered in private practice in 1951 until his retirement in 1976 to Tamarac, Florida. He was a physician of intellect and integrity with great compassion for all people. He will be missed.
