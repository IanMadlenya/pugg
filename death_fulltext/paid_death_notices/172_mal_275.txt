
        HALL-John Jay, PhD. Physicist and fisherman on May 23, 2001 from severe bacterial infection at Maimonides Hospital following successful coronary bypass. His death is mourned by his life partner Vivian Britt, his children John MacGregor Hall, Maura Delfour Hall and Benjamin Britt Hall, his family and all who knew and loved him. Memorials may be made to the ACLU, Friends of Princeton Track, Columbia University Graduate School of Arts & Sciences, The Lawrenceville School or the charity of your choice.
