
        SAIDENBERG - Eleanore. The members of the Art Dealers Association of America mourn the passing of their colleague and friend, a founding member of ADAA. A dealer for nearly 50 years, she was devoted to art, to artists and to her family. She will be missed and remembered. Our deepest sympathy and condolences to her sons, Robert and Lawrence, and their families. Richard Gray, President Gilbert S. Edelson, Admin VP
