
        CLARKE--Beaufort James Barron Clarke was born in Tupelo, Mississippi, on September 11th, 1946, and passed away on April 30th, 2007 in London. His education included a Bachelor of Arts from George Washington University and a Juris Doctor from The University of South Carolina. He worked at Shearman & Sterling in New York and was the Chairman and CEO of ICON Capital Corp in New York, the fifth largest independent leasing and financing company in the United States. Beau was well known for his great philanthropy and contributed to the Empire State Pride Agenda, GLAAD, the Elton John Foundation and the Gay Human Rights Foundation among other charities. Surviving members include his ex-wife Linda Clarke and his sister Lauren Evans, her husband and her two children.
