
        DOUGLAS-William R. Born December 16, 1914 in Indianapolis, IN. He was a biochemist who lived between New York and Denmark. He passed at 3:05AM on December 26, 2001. He is survived by his wife Marie Salustowicz Douglas, five daughters Yvonne Hicks, Cheri, Marilyn Mazur, Mia and Cathy of Copenhagen, cousin Lawrence and five grandchildren. He was cremated. A memorial service will be held at 1:00PM Sunday, February 3, 2002 at Community Church on 35th Street, New York City.
