Survived by husband Fred; sons Neil, Mark and Jeff; sister Rita; and brother Jack. She will forever be dancin' and singin' in the rain. Funeral service Thursday 10 AM at Temple Israel Center in White Plains, NY. Interment at Sharon Gardens Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Pl, White Plains, NY 10601.
