
        LEDIEU-Sr. Salesia, M.S.C. on Oct 1, 1998, Missionary Sister of the Sacred Heart of Jesus, esteemed educator, died in Dobbs Ferry, NY at the age of 95. Native of New orleans, LA, she was a mentor to thousands at Mother Cabrini High School, NY and Cabrini College, Radnor, PA. Friends are invited to Viewing Sunday, October 4, 4PM9PM and Monday Funeral Mass, 11AM both at Mother Cabrini High School, 701 Fort Washington Ave, Manhattan. Interment will follow at West Park, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cabrini Mission Foundation, Inc, 222 East 19th Street, NY, NY 10003.
