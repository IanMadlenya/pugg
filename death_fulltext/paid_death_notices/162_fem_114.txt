
        ENRIGHT-Erica. Died of cancer at her home in Los Angeles in the early morning of Sunday, July 16. She was 52 years old. Born and raised in New York, Erica studied drama at the University of Miami and the Herbert Berghoff School, appearing off-Broadway, in films and on television. Later turning to TV news, she wrote and produced at WNEW, WABC and ''Good Morning America'' in New York and at KCBS in Los Angeles. At the time of her illness, she was researching a book about criminal tragedy in Montana. The daughter of Stella and the late Daniel Enright, Erica is survived by her mother and her brother, Don. Services on Wednesday, July 19, at 3:00 at Pierce Brothers Westwood Village Memorial Park, Los Angeles. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions can be made to The Program to End Parkinson's, 201 Wilshire Boulevard, Santa Monica, California 90401.
