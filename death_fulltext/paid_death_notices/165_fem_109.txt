
        KREMER - Rose (Boxinbaum). Passed away peacefully September 29, 2000. Beloved wife of the late David. Devoted mother of Manny (Noreen), Gloria, Rickie (Yoshua), Bryan (Myrna) and Henry. Adoring ''Boobie'' to Patrick David, Jody and Mark, Max and Davida, Devra and Shira. Great-grandmother of six. Devoted sister of Albert, and Esther. Predeceased by her brother Louie & sister Minnie. Lifelong member of B'Nai Brith and G.O.I. Ladies Auxiliary. Graveside service held at the Adas Israel Cemetery, Hamilton, Ontario, Canada.
