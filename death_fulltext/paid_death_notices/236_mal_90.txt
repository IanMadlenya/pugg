
        McKEON--Robert F. , died recently in Manhattan. He was born January 22, 1925 in Auburn, New York. Predeceased by parents William H. McKeon Sr. and Wanda McKeon, brothers William H. McKeon, Jr. and Richard McKeon, he is survived by sister Jane Dodge and brothers John McKeon and Paul Connor. Memorial Mass at Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Avenue, September 23, 2006, at 9:30 AM.
