
        GREENE-Bernard Victor. Age 72, of Thompson, PA, formerly of 239 Nichols Avenue, East New York in Brooklyn, died on Monday. From 1954 to 1982, he had his own dental practice in Audubon, PA. He resided in Brooklyn, NY from 1942 to 1950, and in Audubon, PA from 1950 to 1979, and lived for the past 19 years in Thompson, PA. He served during World War II as a medic in the US Army. He was a graduate of Muhlenberg College in 1949 and was a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Dental School in 1954. Beloved son of the late Victor Greene and the late Anna (nee Lynch) Greene. Devoted husband of Anastasia (nee Bycosky) Greene of Thompson, PA. Beloved father of Michael J. Greene of Thompson, PA. Loving brother of Valeria Parfitt of New York. Dear father-in-law of Jo-Ellen Smith Greene. Cremation was private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
