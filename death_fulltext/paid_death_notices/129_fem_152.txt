
        LEWIS-Sara Rose, died peacefully in Virginia Beach, VA., on October 17, 1997. Affectionately known as ''Rose'' to all who knew her, she was the loving wife of the late Harry Lewis. Rose is survived by her devoted daughter Margie Brotman, Margie's husband Bob, two grandsons David and Ira, their wives Lynnda and Amy, and nieces and nephews, all of whom loved her dearly. Rose served as the executive secretary to the various chairmen of the New York State Athletic Commission from 1937 to her retirement in 1983. A graveside service will be held at the Baron Hirsch Cemetery, Staten Island, New York on Monday, October 20 at 11 AM.
