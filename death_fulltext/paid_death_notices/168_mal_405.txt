
        GLADSTONE-James Zadoc. 77, died peacefully at home on January 25, 2001, after a long battle with cancer. He leaves his loving wife Nancy (Wolf), his three children, Carol, Bill, and Stephen, their spouses and four grandchildren. Graduate of Yale College and Columbia University, he lived with gusto to the end. He leaves many devoted friends, fellow duffers, skiers, Life Masters, jazz fans and philosopher kings. Memorial service at Temple Beth El, Chappaqua, NY, Sunday January 28 at 2 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions to Purchase College Foundation, Jazz Program, c/o Professor Todd Koolman, Purchase College, NY 10577 or Hospice Care in Westchester, 100 South Bedford Road, Mt. Kisco, New York 10549.
