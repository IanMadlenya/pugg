
        PLANT-Richard, Dr. died at his home in Greenwich Village on March 3, 1998 at age of 87. Dr. Plant was a prominent academic and scholar of the Holocaust and the author of ''The Pink Triangle, The Nazi War Against Homosexuals''. He is survived by his close friend and companion, Michael Sasse. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to The New York City Gay and Lesbian Anti-Violence Project, 647 Hudson St, NY NY 10014. Arrangements for a memorial service will be announced.
