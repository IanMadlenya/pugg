
        CARLSON-Carole J., 60, of New York City, died Monday, January 8, 2001 at her home. She was born January 2, 1941 in Connecticut, daughter of the late George E. Carlson & Edith V. Carlson. Carole worked as a medical secretary & was an avid painter and knitter and enjoyed fine cuisine. She is survived by two daughters, Samantha and Pamela Schwartz and son-in-law Stephen Cassidy, all of New York City.
