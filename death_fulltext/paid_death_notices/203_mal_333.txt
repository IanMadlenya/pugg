
        MASLAND--Richard L., M.D. The Faculty and Staff of The Department of Neurology at Columbia University Medical Center mourn the loss of our esteemed friend and colleague, Dr. Richard L. Masland. Dr. Masland, a former Director of The National Institute of Neurological Diseases and Blindness of The National Institutes of Health, served as Henry and Lucy Moses Professor of Neurology, Chairman of The Department of Neurology, and Director of The Neurology Service at Columbia from 1968 to 1973. Dr. Masland's gentle manner and wise counsel endeared him to everyone, and he was well loved by his students. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Mary Wootton Masland, his sons, Richard and Tom, his daughters, Frances and Sarah, and his grandchildren. He will be greatly missed. Gerald D. Fischbach, M.D., Executive Vice President for Health and Biomedical Sciences and Dean of the Faculty of Medicine, Columbia University Timothy A. Pedley, M.D., Henry and Lucy Moses Professor of Neurology and Chairman of The Department of Neurology Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons
