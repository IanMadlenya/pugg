
        BELLAK-Leopold, MD. Beloved father of Trina Bellak, Esquire of Maryland and Kerin Adams of New Jersey. Loving grandfather of Dina Adams. Devoted friend of his wife of many years, Sonya Sorel Bellak, brother of Ludwig (deceased). Died on March 24, 2000 of pneumonia. Dr. Bellak was a psychoanalyst, psychiatrist and psychologist and his contributions to all these fields will long be remembered. A Memorial Service to be announced.
        BELLAK-Leopold, M.D. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with deep sorrow the passing of our distinguished Clinical Professor Emeritus of Psychiatry. The author of some 30 books and more than 150 chapters and scientific papers, he was widely renowned for his pioneering work in schizophrenia, community and geriatric psychiatry, ego function assessment, and the development of brief psychodynamic therapy. He was greatly admired by his colleagues for his devotion to the training of generations of medical students and residents. He was a giant in the field, and he will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
        BELLAK-Leopold, M.D., The members of the Westchester Psychoanalytic Society mourn the passing of their esteemed colleague, Leopold Bellak, former President and founding member and extend their deepest sympathy to his family. Arthur Lew, M.D., President.
