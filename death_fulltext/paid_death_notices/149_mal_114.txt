
        MADISON-Albert (Big Al). Suddenly on June 4, 1999. Beloved son of Bartlett and Willa Mae Madison and brother of Carolyn Madison Burke, Al is survived by his devoted nephew, Alan B. Burke, niece, Constance J. Burke, Alan's wife Sonya, Godson Earl Davis, Jr. and ''brothers'' Earl Davis, Sr., Bob Wilson, Jr., and Richard Jennings. Al will be sadly missed, but held in the hearts of all who loved him. Contributions may be made to the United Negro College Fund, 120 Wall St., 9 Fl, NYC, NY 10005. Private interment. Memorial services in NYC held at future date.
