
        KLEEMAN--Elaine J. Of 10 Park Avenue, passed away on August 8, 2005, after a long illness. Elaine was the President of Kleeman Associates, a Founding Member of Les Dames d'Escoffier, and author. She is remembered by many friends and relatives, most notably her two cousins, Mrs. Jeanne McGrath, and Dr. Elwin Branz. Memorial service will be held on September 24 at 2:30 PM at The Community Church of New York, 40 East 35 Street.
