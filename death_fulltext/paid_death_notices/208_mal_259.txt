
        MAYNARD--Daniel S. The Port Authority of NY & NJ was saddened to lose its colleague and friend, Dan Maynard. He provided astute and sensitive guidance on many regional issues, especially regarding the Port of NY/NJ. He was a knowledgeable and respected voice for our agency. We will miss his expertise, perspective and his distinctive laugh. We extend our deepest condolences to Richard, Barbara and Ken.
