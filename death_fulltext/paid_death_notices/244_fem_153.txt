
        PHENIX--Gena Tenney, November 22, 1911 -May 7, 2007. Born New York City to Gena Branscombe, John F. Tenney. Attended Rayson, Fieldston, Barnard. Faculty, Barnard Columbia 1935-1943. Honored musician and scholar. Engaged world at large, opposing intolerance, upholding the disadvantaged. Spiritual counselor. Beloved wife of Dr. Philip H. Phenix. Deeply missed by sons Dr. Morgan Scott and Roger, wives Betty and Jo Ann, and five grandchildren, nine great-grandchildren, extended family, friends. She said, ''Rejoice in the life and work of others''.
