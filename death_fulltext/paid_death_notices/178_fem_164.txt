
        RIVERA-Carmen A. On September 11, 2001 at the World Trade Center tragedy. Beloved wife of Luis Rivera. Devoted mother of Racine, Luis, Jr. and Britney. Cherished daughter of Ada Lopez. Also survived by Sal and Angel, loving daughter-in-law of Enrique and Raquel Rivera. Carmen wore many hats, Assistant Vice President in Trusts and Estates for Fiduciary Trust. At the time she was studying for her Master's Degree at Marist University scheduled to graduate in May of 2002. A memorial service will be held at Our Lady of Mercy, 2496 Marion Avenue, Bronx between 188 Street and Fordham Road at 10AM on December 1. You will always be in our hearts and never forgotten.
