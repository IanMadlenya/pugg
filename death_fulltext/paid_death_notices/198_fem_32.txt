
        ALLAN-Rosalind (nee Metcalfe), June 26, in NYC. Born in Providence, RI, Rosalind made her career in the fashion industry & in that capacity lived in Manhattan, Paris, and Florence, Italy. An innovator, entrepreneur, and professional, she inspired many who were fortunate enough to know her. Memorial donations are suggested to American Schools Abroad, C/O Martha Siegler, PO Box 2933, Sag Harbor, NY 11963.
