
        GOLDSTEIN-Irving. On Friday, May 26, 2000. Irving Goldstein of Chevy Chase, Md., beloved husband of Susan Goldstein, loving father of Elizabeth (Betsy) Goldstein and her husband Gudmundur Audunsson and Jill Goldstein and her fiance, Todd Wernstrom. Loving brother of Arthur (Stephanie) Goldstein and Dale (Larry) McClure. Loving son of Hyman and the late Leah Goldstein.
