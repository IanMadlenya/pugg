
        BERNSTEIN--Dr. Leopold A., June 26, 1927 to February 11, 2006. MBA/CPA/Ph.D Professor of Accounting and Finance at Baruch College, Author of Financial Statement Analysis text books. Holocaustsurvivor, lover of nature and music. Devoted to his family, Husband of Cynthia, father of Dr. Deborah Bernstein MD. and Jeffrey Bernstein, father-in-law of Paul Weinstein, and Carolyn Rodney, grandfather of Jennifer and Stephanie Weinstein. We love, admire, and miss you and your zest for life, more than words can say.
