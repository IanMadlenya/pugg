
        MARCUS--Robert Allan, 72, died on March 17, 2007 in NYC. He was the son of James L. Marcus and the late Sophia Marcus, and the stepson of Helen Marcus. He is survived also by his brother and sister-in-law, Donald M. and Marianne T. Marcus, nieces Laura R. Marcus and Susan Marcus Mendoza (Jorge), and nephew James W. Marcus. He was a devoted friend to many, a doting uncle, and a brilliant man who had an unusually wide range of interests. He will be missed.
