
        MOLOSHOK-Ralph E., M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our longstanding colleague, teacher and friend. Dr. Moloshok began his association with Mount Sinai in the Department of Pediatrics in 1940. After receiving training in General Pediatrics and Pediatric Endocrinology, he devoted the next 45 years to clinical teaching and to the superb practice of medicine. He will be fondly remembered for his exceptional wisdom and expertise, his traditional bedside manner, and his compassion for his patients. The AAS salutes Dr. Moloshok's outstanding commitment to Mount Sinai and his many years of service. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Anthony Squire, M.D., President Edward Raab, 1st Vice President Michael Harris, 2nd Vice President Vicki Lopachin, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
