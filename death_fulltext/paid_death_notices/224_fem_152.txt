
        AWAD--Rose (nee Azrak). Born May 23, 1914, died peacefully on September 17, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Dewey. Beloved and loving mother of Claudette Razook, Carole Hunt, Douglas Awad and James Awad and the late Joan Elias. Cherished grandmother of 15 grandchildren and 18 great grandchildren. Rose devoted her life to her husband, family and extended family and friends, held a position of respect and was deeply loved by all. She had an extraordinary sensitivity and compassion for all those she loved, and all those loved were embraced by her kindness. Rose was an extraordinary cook and was quick to share her talents with her favorite charities, particularly the Ladies Aide Society and St. Mary's Orthodox Church in Bay Ridge where she was a prominent and devoted parishioner. She has left her impact on her progeny, who will carry on her principles, moral values and love forever. She will be sorely missed. Funeral liturgy at 10am on Wednesday, September 28, 2005 at St. Mary's Orthodox Church, 8100 Ridge Blvd, Bklyn, N.Y. Interment to follow at Green-Wood Cemetery.
