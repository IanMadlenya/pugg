
        BOAS--Margaret K. Died on December 13 at the age of 104. Widow of Raymond E. Boas and mother of the late Elizabeth Breslauer Vreeland Tompkins. Peggy was loved by her niece, Susan Hirschman, and her grandsons, Nicholas and Alexander Vreeland. She was the respected stepmother of Gilbert, Francoise and Jean Pierre Boas. She was the great grandmother of Diane Reed, Victoria, and Olivia Vreeland.
