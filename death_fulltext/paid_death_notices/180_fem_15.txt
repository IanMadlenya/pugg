
        SLOAN-Barbara G. Born January 1, 1918 in New York City, passed away on December 19, 2001-A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of Smith College, a housewife and mother. Barbara was a source of strength, a model of courage, forebearance and good humor to all. She is deeply missed. Surviving are her son David, her daughter Leslie, and her granddaughter Christina. A Memorial Service will be announced. Edward F. Carter Funeral Home, 170 Kings Ferry Road, Montrose, NY 10548. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Salvation Army.
