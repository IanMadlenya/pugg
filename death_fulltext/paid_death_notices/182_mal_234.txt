
        WHITELEY-Stark Canning. Suddenly on March 11, 2002 at age 55. A graduate of The Hill School, Columbia and Johns Hopkins Universities, Stark was a lifelong historian and teacher. He lived in New York City and Friendship Long Island, ME, & was a member of The University Club in NYC. He is the son of the late Charlotte Newberry Whiteley and George Whiteley Jr. He is survived by his sister Diane Woodward (John H.) of South Kent, CT, and brothers George Whiteley III (Bunny) of NYC and Will Whiteley of Halifax, MA. In addition, he is survived by nine nieces and nephews and 10 grand-nieces and nephews. A memorial service to celebrate his life will be held Saturday, March 16, at 3 P.M. at The Church of the Holy Trinity, 316 East 88th Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory can be made to The Hill School, Stark Whiteley Library Fund, Pottstown, PA. 19464.
