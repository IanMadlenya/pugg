
        POSNACK--Ann. A resident of Great Neck and Rye, NY, died December 4, 2005 at age 94. Born in New York to Jacob and Edith Bock, Ann worked as a social worker for the Peninsula Counseling Center. She is survived by her son Alan (his wife Anne), three grandchildren, and seven great grandchildren. Memorial donations may be made to the Peninsula Counseling Center, 124 Franklin Place, Woodmere, NY 11598.
