
        BERNSTEIN-Philip H. 95, born November 19, 1906, passed away July 26, 2002, in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Philip graduated from NYU in 1931, where he was a member of the National Championship Track Team. Philip was a major contributor to the textile industry as the originator of crushed velvet bedspreads and draperies and as the former Chairman of the Board and founder of Crown Crafts, Inc. Philip is survived by his wife, Inez, sister Edith, son Michael, daughter Elizabeth, and grandchildren Deborah, Alicia, Diana, Danielle and Sarah. We will remember Philip for his marvelous sense of humor, his strong sense of ethics, and his devotion to his family. Services Monday, July 29, 2002, at 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'' (76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue). In lieu of flowers donations to the Alzheimers Association, 800-272-3900.
