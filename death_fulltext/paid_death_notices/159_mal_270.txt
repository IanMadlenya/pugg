
        MOORE-George S. The Boards, Faculty and Staff of the New York-Presbyterian Hospital and the Joan and Sanford I. Weill Medical College of Cornell University are saddened by the passing of our friend and esteemed Life Trustee, George S. Moore. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Mr. Moore rendered nearly 40 years of distinguished service to the Board of The New York Hospital, which he joined in 1961.
