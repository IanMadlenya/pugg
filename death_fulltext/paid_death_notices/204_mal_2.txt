
        COTTON--Robert Henry, 89; December 19, 2003, Fort Myers, FL. Beloved family man, mentor and friend; food industry innovator; artist in glass and in life. Survived by his wife Cis, whom he married 56 years ago today; daughter Dorothy Kirkwood of FL; Leonard W. and Thomas C. of CT; eight grandchildren, two great-grandchildren.
