
        THOMPSON-Thomas W. A memorial service will be held for Thomas W. Thompson, of Rhinebeck, NY, at 11 AM, on Thursday, February 15, Chapel of the Brick Church, Park and 92 St. A Senior VP at Columbia Artists, over 45 years Thompson managed artists such as Yehudi Menuhin, Robert McDuffie, Birgit Nilsson, Andras Schiff and Charles Rosen, and produced regular U.S. tours for many orchestras including the RPO, the Czech Philharmonic, and the Stuttgart, Franz Liszt and Prague Chamber Orchestras. Born in Cedartown, GA, ''Tommy'' Thompson was a true southern gentleman and his passing represents the end of an era in the music industry.
