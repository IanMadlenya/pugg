
        EGE--Hans A., The Board and staff of The Bay and Paul Foundations are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed colleague Hans A. Ege and extend our sympathy and condolences to his wife, Else, and daughters, Elizabeth and Tina. Hans became a director of the foundation nearly twenty years ago and has honored us throughout this period with his wisdom, leadership, and patriotic devotion to both his adopted United States and native Norway. Synnova Bay Hayes, President Frederick Bay, Executive Director
