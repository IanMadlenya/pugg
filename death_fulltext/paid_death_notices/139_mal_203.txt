
        BUTTERFIELD - Harvey Dean, the sixth Episcopal Bishop of Vermont, died on August 10th in Burlington. Bishop Butterfield was born in 1908, he graduated from the University of Vermont in 1931, and the General Theological Seminary in 1934. He married Carolyn Whitney in 1934. Rt. Reverend Butterfield is survived by his two children, Whitney Butterfield and Deborah Galbraith. A memorial service will be held at The Cathedral Church of St. Paul in Burlington on August 22, 1998 at 10:00am.
