
        DILLMEIER-William E. On June 23, 2000, on his 89th birthday, at his home in Garden City, NY. Father of William E., Jr. of Lambertville, NJ, Robert L. of Garden City and the late David S., who died in 1967. Father-in-law of Margaret and Joy Dillmeier. Husband of the late Doris Dillmeier, who died in 1971 and the late Hazel Dillmeier, who died in 1999. Survived by his grandchildren Elizabeth Reilly, William E. III, David S., Margo and Kurt Dillmeier and three great-grandchildren Sean, Abigail and Kaitlin. He is also survived by his sister Mary Louise Connelly, of Williamsburg, VA. A gentleman of exceptional dignity, modesty and achievement. A graduate of Brooklyn Prep, Georgetown University (class of 1933) and St. John's Law School. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave. at 12th St., Garden City, NY. 2-5 and 7-9 PM Sunday. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Joseph's Church, Garden City, 10 AM Monday. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY. Contributions may be made to the Dillmeier Family Scholarship Fund, St. Michael's College, Colchester, VT 05439
