
        FLYNN--Robert Hugh. Mutual of America and its Board of Directors, together with all of its officers and employees, mourn with great sadness the loss of Robert Hugh Flynn-beloved son of our friend and Chairman William J. Flynn and his wife Peg, and brother of our colleague Jim. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Karen; their children Carris, Michael and Billy; his siblings Maureen and Bill; and to his entire family. Bobby, to all of us who knew him, was a kind and caring young man. His love will always be felt and remembered. Thomas J. Moran President and Chief Executive Officer Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
        FLYNN -- Robert Hugh. Trustees and members of The National Committee on American Foreign Policy mourn the untimely death of Robert, son of our beloved Chairman William J. Flynn. To Bill and Peg and to the Flynn family we extend our heartfelt condolences. George Schwab, President
