
        WOLF--Lydia Brewster Hubbard. Of Cornwall, CT, died March 8th, 2005, aged 92, at Sharon (CT) Hospital (where she was born in 1913). Survived by a son, Thomas Gordon Wolf, two grandsons, Matthew and Stefan Wolf, all of Cornwall, three brothers, William of Wilmington, Gordon of Tuscon and Thomas of Bronxville and Cornwall, and numerous nephews and nieces. Her sister, Elizabeth Hubbard Lansing, predeceased her. A memorial service will be held in Cornwall in the Spring. She was a woman of great compassion.
