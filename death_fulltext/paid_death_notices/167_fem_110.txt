
        BERKMAN-Alice Barrasch. On Dec. 11, 2000, in her 90th year. Beloved wife of the late I. George Berkman. Also survived by her six grandchildren Tony, Elise, Emily and her husband David Harris, Ben, Joseph Bacal and Gabrielle Berkman-Levine. Funeral was held December 12th at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue and 76th Street.
