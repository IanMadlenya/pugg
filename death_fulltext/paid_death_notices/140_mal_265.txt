
        GERETY-Pierce J. A memorial service for Pierce J. Gerety, Jr. will be held on Saturday, September 26 at 1 PM. Family and friends will gather at the home of his mother, 1133 Cedar Road, Southport, CT. A reception will follow the service. Memorial contributions may be made to the Pierce Gerety Memorial Fund at New York Community Trust, 2 Park Ave., New York, NY 10016.
