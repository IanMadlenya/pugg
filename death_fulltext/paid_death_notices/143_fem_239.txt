
        GRAHAM-Virginia. The Board, staff and members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City note with profound sorrow the passing of well-known television personality Virginia Graham. She was an eloquent advocate for individuals with cerebral palsy and related disabilities, both as a speaker for UCP's Women's Division and a frequent telethon host. We extend our condolences to her family. Leo Hausman, President Edward R. Matthews, Executive Director UCP of New York City
