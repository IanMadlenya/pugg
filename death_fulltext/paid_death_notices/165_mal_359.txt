
        LEVY-Robert I., M.D. 63, of Morristown, NJ, on October 28. Beloved husband and best friend of Ellen (Feis). Proud father of Andrew and Nina, Joanne, Karen and Michael Gooen, Patricia and Stuart Zuckerman, devoted son of Sarah and the late George, dear brother of the late Harold. Loving grandfather of Naphtali, Esther, Tsvi, Tova and Shoshana Levy and Elizabeth and Sandy Gooen. He will be remembered by numerous friends and colleagues, all of whose lives are blessed to have been touched by his warm friendship, wise counsel, and good humor. Service today at 2:15 PM at Menorah Chapel, 2950 Vaux Hall Rd., Union, NJ, 908-964-1500. Interment at B'nai Abraham Memorial Park, Union, NJ. A memorial service is planned at 11 AM on November 28 at Caspary Auditorium, Rockefeller University, York Ave. at 66th St., NYC. For further information please call 973-660-5021. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Robert I. Levy Endowment Fund at Yale University School of Medicine, 333 Cedar St., New Haven, CT 06510 to fund a professorship in preventive cardiology in his name or The Robert I. Levy Endowment at The American Heart Association, 7272 Greenville Ave., Dallas, TX 75231 for an annual lecture in his name at scientific sessions.
