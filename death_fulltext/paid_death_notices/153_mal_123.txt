
        BLACK-Edward. Age 90. Beloved husband for 63 years of Muriel Fleischman Black, wonderful father of Jo Ann and Howard Chase, adored and adoring Grandpa Eddie to Adam Chase, loving brother to five siblings and Uncle Eddie to every child he ever met. He was ''The Baron'' of the New York Journal American for many years before going into the public relations business. He served in Great Britain in World War II. Eddie was blessed with an extraordinarily outgoing personality, a great sense of humor and genuine kindness and generosity to all who crossed his path. Services were held Sunday, October 10, in Miami. Contributions in his memory may be made to ''Alzheimer's Care Committee/Notables'' of Douglas Gardens, 5200 Northeast Second Avenue, Miami, FL 33137.
