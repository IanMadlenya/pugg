
        HARDIN--Edward, photo grapher. Known professionally as Ted Hardin, died January 20th, 2007 of cancer. He was pre-deceased by his son Christopher Hardin and is survived his wife Sara and his beloved daughter Haley Hardin, his sister Diana Walker of Washington DC and his brother B. L. Hardin III of Bethesda, MD. Services will be held Wednesday January 24th at 2pm at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 90th and 5th Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Conservatory Garden, c/o The Central Park Conservancy, 830 5th Ave. NY, NY 10021
