
        GOLDEN-Bella. The West End Synogogue community mourns the loss of its oldest member. However, Bella Golden, at 101, matriarch of four generations of Reconstructionist Jews, has already passed on to and through her remarkable family her generosity of spirit, her creative talents expressed in her excellent silversmithing, ceramic work and needle arts, her innovative pathfinding, her broad-sweeping educational energy and talents and, not least, the importance of wearing a hat at just the right jaunty angle. Surely her memory will continue to be a blessing; she has already enriched us all. Rabbi Avi Winokur Cantor Kathy J. Barr Donald Shapiro, Chairperson
