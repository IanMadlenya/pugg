
        WYKER--Kenneth. 45 died on November 4th, 2006 after a 2 year illness. He resided in San Antonio and was raised in Harrison, New York. Beloved husband of Suzanne, devoted father of Eden (7) and Olivia (5), former General Counsel for Clear Channel Communications for 12 years. He is also survived by his grandmother Florence Eisner, his father and stepmother Robert and Jane Wyker and his sisters Shelley, Sharkey, Amy Campbell and his father-in-law Edward Basara. Funeral service on Tuesday November 7th, San Antonio.
