
        BISCEGLIA-Victor R., Colonel, USMC Ret. On November 23, 2001. Beloved husband of Audrey, devoted father of Victor J., John, Dalis, Michael, Nicholas and nine grandchildren. 1940 graduate of Columbia University commanded a U.S. Guerilla unit in China during W.W. II. Commander of 8th Marine Regiment and completed military career as director of First Marine District, Garden City, NY. Later worked for USO and Y & R Advertising, before forming own consulting business. Interment at Arlington National Cemetery. He was respected by all who knew him. Dad, you are in our hearts forever.
