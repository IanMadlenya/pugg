
        SILVERS--Lainie Zee. Died peacefully after a long illness on June 10th. Daughter of the late Ruth and Eugene Zee. Beloved niece of Charlotte and the late Herbert Wool. She will be missed by her aunt, friends, and many caring cousins. Services will be held Tuesday, 10:45am at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. $(6$)SILVERS--Lainie. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Lainine Silvers beloved niece of our devoted leader Charlotte Wool, cousin of our Trustee Ronald and Poppy Wurtzburger. To them and the entire family our deepest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier
