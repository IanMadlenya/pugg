
        ALMY-Richard, Jr. Age 68. Of South Dartmouth, MA. on April 3, 2001, at home after a courageous struggle with prostate cancer. Beloved husband of Anne Taylor Almy. Devoted father of Richard T. Almy of Old Greenwich, CT., Heather L. Almy of Norwalk, CT. & Gretchen H. Almy of Middleboro, MA. Also survived by a sister, Caroline A. Gerry of Locust Valley, N.Y. & sister-in-law Mrs. David Almy of Hamilton, MA. Predeceased by his younger brother David Almy in 1996. Memorial services will be held Sunday, April 8th, 3 PM at the First Congregational Church of Greenwich, Old Greenwich, CT., and on Saturday, April 14th, 11 AM at the Congregational Church of South Dartmouth, Padanarum, MA. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory for prostate cancer research to the Dana Farber Institute, 1309 Beacon St., Brookline, MA. 02446.
