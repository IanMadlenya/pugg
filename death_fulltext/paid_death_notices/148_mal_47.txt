
        STERN-Herman. The Officers, Trustees and staff of Jewish Child Care Association note with sorrow the death of Herman Stern. Mr. Stern came to Jewish Child Care Association's Edenwald Center in 1957 and retired in 1982 as the institution's Director. His devotion to troubled young people was a model for future generations. Our deepest sympathy to Mr. Stern's widow, Marian, his three children and eleven grandchildren. Joyce L. Kramer, President Irwin U. Goldberg, Board Chair Paul Gitelson, Exec. Vice Pres.
