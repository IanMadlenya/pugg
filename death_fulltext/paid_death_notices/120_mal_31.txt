
        MACEIRAS - Alfred. 62. Died January 2, 1997 after a courageous battle with cancer. He was a devoted Supervisor for NY's Bureau of Child Welfare, a loving father and loyal friend. Funeral services and burial Saturday, 10 A.M. at Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn. Maceiras is survived by his son Mark Lentczner, daughter Rachel Maceiras and companion Marsha Smithline. In lieu of flowers, donations to Ronald McDonald House.
