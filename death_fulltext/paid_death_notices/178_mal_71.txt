
        RUBIN-Kenneth. Of Ardsley, Professer of Physics at CUNY for 35 years. Husband, father, grandfather, ardent Researcher, Skiier, Skater, Trekker, International Traveler, Food afficinado and Blues enthusiast died November 3, after nine months of wordlessness and paralysis. He is soaring now. He will missed by Teel, David and Adrian, Josh, Robyn and Nisha and Judy. A future memorial service is planned.
