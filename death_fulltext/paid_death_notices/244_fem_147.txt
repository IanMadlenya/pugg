
        CAIN--Harriet F., age 86, of 1 Beechwood Dr., Waterford, formerly of New York, died May 15, 2007 at the Lawrence and Memorial Hospital in New London, CT. Miss Cain had been employed as a fashion editor for the New York Times and Conde Nast prior to her retirement. She was predeceased by her twin brother Dr. Edward A. Cain, Jr. and is survived by her sister-in-law Jeanne Cain of New York. For complete obituary please visit: neilanfuneralhome.com
