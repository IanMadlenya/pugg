
        GOLD-Abraham. Died peacefully at 96 in his Boca Raton home on February 26, 1999. He is survived by his loving daughters, Lois Schauber and Marilyn Laurie; his grandchildren Marc, Paul, Nancy, Amy and Lisa; his seven greatgrandchildren; his devoted sisters Lillie and Surie and his brother Max. Certified Public Accountant and lawyer, he was a whiz with numbers with a marvelously logical mind that was warmed by a terrific sense of humor. Dad-our lives were endlessly enriched by your love and your belief in us.
