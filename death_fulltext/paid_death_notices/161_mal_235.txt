
        HARWOOD-Paul H., M.D. of Essex, CT died on June 19 in Essex. Beloved husband of Sibyl Hine Harwood. Devoted father of Frances, Catherine & Sibyl Harwood and the late Paul H. Harwood III. He will also be fondly missed by his grandchildren; Laurie Schuur Duncan, John and Mary B. Thorne-Thomsen and Darcy and Paul Heintz. Memorial service will be Saturday June 24 at 12:00 P.M. at the Essex Congregational Church, Essex, CT.
