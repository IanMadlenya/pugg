
        MICHELSON-Elizabeth Munch, 64, of Southbury, CT died July 28, 1997. She was born in Hartford, CT and was a well known decorative arts author & curator. She leaves her children, Leslie Michelson of Cambridge, MA; John and Colleen Michelson of Enfield, CT and Keith Michelson of North Tunbridge, VT; 2 grandchildren, Robert and Katie Michelson; a sister, Judith Pinney of Avon, CT., and many devoted friends who remained caring, loving and supportive throughout her long illness. Funeral services will be held in the chapel, Temple Beth Israel, 701 Farmington Avenue, West Hartford, CT., Thursday, July 31 at 1:00 P.M. Donations may be made to the Gilda Radner FOCR, Rosewell Park Cancer Institute, Buffalo, New York 14263.
