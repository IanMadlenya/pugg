
        GIBB-Barbara Lowrie, 88, passed away, March 25, 1997, at her home in Weybridge, VT. She was born in New York City on January 7, 1909, the daughter of Isabelle Forrest Lowrie and Charles N. Lowrie. She and her husband, Senator Arthur Gibb, moved to Vermont in 1951. She is survived by her husband, Senator Arthur Gibb; five children, Dwight Gibb of Seattle, Wash., Arthur Gibb, Jr. of Annapolis, Md., Lowrie Gibb, of Westport, Conn., Barbara Gibb Otsuka, of Middlebury, and Henry Gibb, of Denver, Colo., ten grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
