
        SIMMONS-Bruce. A victim of the World Trade Center Disaster on September 11, 2001. He was a Partner with Sandler O'Neill & Partners on the 104th Floor of Tower 2. Loving husband of Kathy (nee Hillman), adored father of Meghan, Jack and Emma. Devoted son of Flora Aaroe. A Celebration Mass of Bruce's Life will be offered at 10:00 AM on Friday, October 12, 2001 at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel RC Church, Prospect and Passaic Streets, Ridgewood, NJ 201-444-2000. A time of fellowship will be held in the parish center immediately following the mass. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Bruce Simmons Memorial Scholarship C/O Student/ Sponsorship Partnership, Inc., 420 Lexington Avenue, Suite 2930, New York, New York 10017.
