
        Van CLIEF--Anthony S. The Board of Directors and staff of the Brookdale Foundation Group are deeply saddened by the loss of Anthony S. Van Clief, the husband and best friend of Mary Ann Van Clief, Vice President of the Brookdale Foundation, on Sunday September 25. Tony will forever be remembered for his charm, wit, warmth, his fabulous Italian cooking, his love of golf, and his unparalleled devotion to his wife, Mary Ann. We extend our sincerest condolences to Mary Ann, children Tami and Dite, and their spouses, brother, Elwood, and mother-in-law, Mama May Andersen. If so desired, memorials may be made to the Bright Side Manor, 300 Teaneck Rd., Teaneck, NJ . Stephen L. Schwartz, President
