
        LEAMY-James P. 87, in Clifton Park, NY, formerly of Bronx, NY, died January 2, 2000. Son of the late Patrick & Josephine Leamy, husband of the late Madeline O'Brien, Emma McBride, & Marjorie Stragfeld Leamy. He was a NYC Police officer from 1936-1956. Survived by daughter, Mary Ellen Smith, son Patrick Leamy & five grandchildren. Burial in St. Raymonds Cemetery, Bronx, NY
