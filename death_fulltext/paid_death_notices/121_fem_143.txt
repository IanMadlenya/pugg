
        GORDON-Sylvia. In loving memory. Born in New York City, August 14, 1914, wife of the late Morris Gordon, photojournalist & pioneer in photography. She leaves her daughter Diane Gordon-Sacchetti of Manhattan, her sister Barbara Okin of Florida, her nieces and nephews of California, Ohio, Florida, New York, and her friends around the world. She was retired from her importing business from Italy. Services were private.
