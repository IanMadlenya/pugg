
        CHATZOPOULOS--Nikolaos. 64, of Roselle Park, NJ, died on September 19, 2006. Born in Constantinople (Istanbul), Turkey, Mr. Chatzopoulos immigrated to the United States in 1969, and worked as a chemist in the field of precious metal refining for 37 years. Active at St. Demetrios Greek Orthodox Church in Union, NJ for 32 years, he was co-founder and past president of World Hellenism and co-founder of the Federation of Greek-American Societies of New Jersey. He was invested as Archon Aktouarios of the Ecumenical Patriarchate, awarded the Archdiocesan Medal of St. Paul, and was a member of the Metropolitan Council of New Jersey, the archdiocesan delegation to the worldwide meetings of the Council of Hellenes Abroad (SAE), the board of trustees of SAE America District #
