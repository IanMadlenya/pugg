
        ROSEN--Vicki Turk, who lived in Brooklyn, New Rochelle, and Manhattan all of her life, died April 25, 2005 at age 86 in Palisades, NY. She was the daughter of Harold and Rose (nee Maisel) Turk. She was the beloved wife of second husband, Reuben Rosen, who survives her. The widow of Saul Stein, deceased in 1972, the mother of Dr. Cathy Stein Greenblat, formerly of Princeton, NJ and NYC, and currently of Austin, TX and Nice, France, and grandmother of Leslie H. Shah (nee Greenblat) of Silver Spring, MD and Kevin Greenblat of Austin, TX. Vicki was an important presence in all of their lives.
