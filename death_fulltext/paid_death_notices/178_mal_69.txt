
        NATION-Douglas P.C. Age 59. Died Sept. 2nd, 2001 in Switzerland. He was a Senior Managing Director at Bear Stearns, NYC. A memorial service will be held at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue, at 11:00 AM, Nov. 8th. Donations to US Court Tennis Preservation Fund/Douglas Nation Fund. Mail to USCT Preservation Fund, in care of The Racquet Club of Philadelphia, 215 South 16th Street, Philadelphia, PA. 19102.
