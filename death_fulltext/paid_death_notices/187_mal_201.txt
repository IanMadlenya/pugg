
        BREENE-James Kibbe Jr. Of Warren, CT, died August 16, 2002, at the age of 74. Longtime educator, he leaves his wife of 47 years, Kathleen, brother Peter of Devon, PA, daughters Bailey, Julie and Jane, son Jamer, daughter-in-law Lee Breene, sons-in-law Larry Gendron, Townsend Ziebold and John Kenney, & his ten wonderful grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Canterbury School, New Milford, CT., or The Gunnery, Washington, CT.
