
        WELLES-James Bell, Jr. Devoted husband, father and grandfather, died Saturday, August 28 after a brief illness. Mr. Welles is survived by his beloved wife of 52 years, Ann Thom Welles, and their children Nancy W. Leahy, James B. Welles III, Thomas B. Welles and Amy B. Welles. Son William B. Welles predeceased him. Mr. Welles has nine grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Thursday, September 2nd at 11:00 a.m. at St. Mark's Church, 85 East Main St., Mt. Kisco, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to Camp Dudley, Westport, NY and the Emma Willard School, Troy, New York.
