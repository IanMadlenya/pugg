
        HARNETT--Bernard. ''Buncie'' was a lawyer. During World War II he was a Treasury Department agent. The last of his generation of Harnetts, he leaves behind his wife Sylvia, children Joan Tallan and Jeffrey Harnett, grandchildren Mitchell, Eric, Mark and Daniel Tallan, and Michael, Matthew and Andrew Harnett. He also leaves behind six great-grandchildren and many nieces and nephews. Buncie was a great father and family man. He loved Sylvia with all his heart. Buncie died peacefully at age 96, with Sylvia at his side. Graveside service at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Monday, May 16th, at 1 pm.
