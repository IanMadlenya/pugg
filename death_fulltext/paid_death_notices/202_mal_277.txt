
        WILLIAMSON--Jack, 76, died November 20, Hilton Head, SC. Founder and president, DiFranza Williamson, NYC 1955-1989. An award-winning artist and graduate of Parsons School of Design. Mr. Williamson is survived by his wife Irene, six children Arthur Williamson, Jacqueline Piro, John A. Williamson, Jennifer DeMeo, Gregory and James Williamson; sister Ruby Martin and three grandchildren.
