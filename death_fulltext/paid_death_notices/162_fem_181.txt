
        LOWY-Florette. Of Maplewood, 82, formerly of Newark and Short Hills, New Jersey, died Tuesday, July 25, 2000; beloved wife of Meyer R. Lowy; devoted mother of Peter Q. Lowy of Bradley Beach, Katherine & Martin Gurvey of Lincolnshire, IL and Susan and Lawrence Lubow of Convent Station, N.J., doting grandmother of Jill Gurvey and Matt Wray and Andrew Gurvey and Elizabeth Lubow and Jonathan and Jessica Lubow. Funeral service Thursday, July 27, at 11AM in the BernheimApter - Goldsticker Suburban Funeral Chapel, 1600 Springfield Ave, Maplewood, N.J. Period of mourning will be observed at her home through Sunday evening.
