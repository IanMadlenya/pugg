
        BRESLAU--Martin, age 84, of Delray Beach, FL; formerly of Valley Stream and Woodbury, NY; Passed on April 24, 2007 from heart failure. Devoted husband to the late Rita, beloved father of Bruce, Allen, Carol, father-in-law to Howard, Eric and Marilynn, grandfather to Blake, Spencer, and Jake, grandfather-in law to Joey, great-grandfather to Chase and friend to many. His kindness, humanity and sense of humor served as an inspiration to all. He will be missed. The Breslau & Fertig families
