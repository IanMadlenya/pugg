
        QUINTAL-Muriel S. (nee Smith) of Rye, New York and formerly of New York City. Died peacefully May 7, 2003 at age 90. Born February 28, 1913 in NYC to Martin Henry and Frances (nee Montague) Smith. Devoted mother of Richard M. Quintal (wife Catherine W.) of Darien, CT, and Barbara Q. Berglund of Pennington, New Jersey, and grandmother to Christopher W. Quintal, Barbara C. Quintal, Theodore M. Berglund, Katherine H. Berglund, and great grandmother of Lukas T. Berglund and Jack O. Berglund. Her nephew Robert M. Baylis (wife Lois W.) also survives her. Predeceased by her husband Theodore G. Quintal & sister Dorothy S. Baylis. Friends are welcome to attend the funeral service and celebration of her life, 10AM Saturday, May 10th at the Church of the Heavenly Rest, 2 East 90th Street in NYC followed by an interment at Kensico Cemetery in Valhalla, NY. Family requests donations in Muriel's name to the Church of the Heavenly Rest in NYC. For information Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 914-698-5968.
