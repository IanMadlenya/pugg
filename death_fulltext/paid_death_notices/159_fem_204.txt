
        KRAMER-Margaret S., Dr. The officers, members and staff of the American Technion Society (ATS) express profound sorrow at the loss of our esteemed friend Margaret Kramer of Palm Beach. As a Technion Guardian, she demonstrated the highest level of commitment to the Technion-Israel Institute of Technology, the ATS and Israel. A pioneer in biochemistry who developed numerous patents, Dr. Kramer understood the value of scientific education and research, and avidly supported many projects at the Technion including faculty recruitment, fellowships and student dormitories. She served most recently as vice president and member of the board of the ATS Palm Beach Chapter, and was honored for her many years of commitment with a Lifetime Achievement Award. American Society for TechnionIsrael Institute of Technology Lawrence S. Jackier, President Irving A. Shepard, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvyn H. Bloom, Executive VP Paul R.
