
        TROUT-Robert. On November 14, 2000. Distinguished journalist in both radio and television. One of the first correspondents to cover London for CBS during World War II and later for ABC radio and television. Husband and colleague of the late Catherine ''Kit'' Trout and brother-in-law of Elizabeth Crane Prucher, uncle of Elizabeth Crane Boland, Stephen and Jeffrey Crane. A Mass in memory of Robert Trout will be celebrated at his parish church, St. Joseph's in Greenwich Village (371 Sixth Avenue), on Saturday, December 2, 2000, at 3:30 PM.
