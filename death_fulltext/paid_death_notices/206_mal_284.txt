
        CONDON--Edward Gerard. On March 20, 2004. Longtime resident of Long Beach, NY. Born August 30, 1932 to Dorothy Collins and Arthur Condon in Queens, NY. Harvard University Class of 1954. His career centered in publishing as an advertising sales executive. He leaves four children, Christine, Jacqueline (Christopher) Cohane, Clifford (Dianne), Kerry (Michael) Dalton and their mother Mary, seven grandchildren, a brother Jack and two sisters, Joan Sheehan and Doris French, and dear friend Patricia Keever Wells. Church, Long Beach NY. Private cremation to follow.
