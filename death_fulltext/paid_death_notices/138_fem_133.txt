
        REICHEK-Bessie D. Died on July 20th, 1998 at age 89. Beloved wife of Max Reichek. Beloved mother of Ruth Baron and Nathaniel Reichek. Loving grandmother to Jennifer and Matthew Reichek and Rafe Baron. Devoted sister to Solomon, Joseph and Wolf Citron who predeceased her. Beloved mother-in-law of Robert Baron and Lesly Reichek and grandmother-in-law of Minnie Reichek. Teacher to generations of New York's children. She never met a child she could not teach and love. Funeral service at Bet Am Sholom Synagogue, White Plains, N.Y.
