
        SINGER--Rabbi Dr. Joseph I. The residents, Board and staff of Menorah Home & Hospital note with sorrow the passing of Rabbi Singer. He served with great distinction as a member of the Board of Directors of Menorah Home & Hospital. We will all sorely miss his wise, kind and gentle nature. Our heartfelt condolences to his loving family. Fred Abrams, President Paul Melinger, Chairman of the Board Jane Rosenthal, Chief Executive Officer
