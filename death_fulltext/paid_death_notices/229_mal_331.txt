
        PONTE--Vincent Pasciuto. A Harvard graduate and a world renowned town planner, died peacefully on February 9, 2006. He is survived by his two aunts, Ruth Mitrano and Esther Robertello. He worked with the world's most prestigious architectural firms designing concepts to minimize pedestrian traffic congestion in landmark projects in New York, Boston, Dallas, and other locations, including the subterranean pedestrian system of Place Ville Marie, in Montreal. He will be sadly missed by all who knew him.
