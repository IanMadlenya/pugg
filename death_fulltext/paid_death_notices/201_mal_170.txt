
        AGNEW--William H., 83, of Maplewood, formerly of Summit, NJ. On Saturday, October 18, 2003. He was a pioneer in the insurance business, as a Principal of Post and Kurtz Inc., NYC. He was a decorated Marine Corps Major in Okinawa, a dedicated volunteer to his alma mater Amherst College and a longtime Vestryman of Calvary Episcopal Church in Summit. Husband of the late Janet Elisabeth Gittinger Agnew. Father of P. Tomlin Agnew, Nancy E. Woodward, Susan Agnew-Huke and Elisabeth A. Thomson. Brother of Robert K.T. Agnew. Also survived by ten grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held at Calvary Episcopal Church, 31 Woodland Ave., Summit on Thursday, October 30 at 11AM. Relatives and friends are invited to attend. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Remembrance Fund-Calvary Church, Summit, New Jersey 07901.
