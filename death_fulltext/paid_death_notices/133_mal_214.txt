
        CLARK-Robert John Balfour. Age 60, of Gainesville, AL., formerly of New York, NY and Locust Valley, NY, died February 3, 1998. He was the son of the late Marjorie Ward Clark and the late John Balfour Clark. He is survived by his sister, Patricia Wing Whitaker of Bradenton, FL., and his two daughters, Alicia Clark Van Arsdall of Arlington, VA., and Melissa Ward Clark of New York, NY. Funeral services were private. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations may be sent to St. Alban's Episcopal Church, Gainesville, AL 35464, C/O Father Richard Losch.
