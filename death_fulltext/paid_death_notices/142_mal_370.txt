
        HERLING - Erwin. American Friends of Bar-Ilan University mourn the passing of Erwin Herling, a devoted member of our Presidential Founder's Society. His commitment and brilliance strengthened many Jewish communal causes in Israel, the United States, and Europe. May his beloved wife, Tova, and his many friends draw solace from inspiring memories. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, Chancellor Brig. Gen. (Res.) Yehuda Halevy, Executive Vice President Elaine Bloom, Florida Director
