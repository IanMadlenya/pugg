
        LANDEGGER-Carl M., 44. Of New York City, died at his home Tuesday of a heart attack. Born in Manila, Philippines, on May 17, 1953, he resided in New York City most of his life. Mr. Landegger was the president of The Black Clawson Company, a global supplier of capital machinery for the paper and converting industry. Through his work Mr. Landegger travelled throughout the world, including South America, Europe and Asia. Surviving him are his children Carl F. and Camila of Edison, NJ, his wife Sandra Landegger of Edison, New Jersey and his parents Carl C. Landegger and Cecilia Elizalde Landegger, both of New York City. Services will be Saturday, November 1, at 10 AM at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave. and 84th St. In lieu of flowers donations should be made to The Heart Association, 122 East 42nd St, NYC.
        LANDEGGER-Carl Michael. On October 28, 1997. Beloved father of Carl John and Camila Landegger. Loving son of Carl C. and Cecilia Landegger. Devoted brother of Christine Mroczek, Claudia Malaya, Cary Paul Landegger, Celia Landegger and Gregory Landegger. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., Friday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave at 84 St, Saturday at 10 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory may be made to the Heart Association, 122 East 42 Street, NYC 10168.
