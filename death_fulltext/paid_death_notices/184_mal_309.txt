
        LEONARD-James G. Retired investment banker, died May 21, 2002, at the age of 83. He graduated from Yale, class of 1939, and served four years in the Pacific in WWII. He was the recipient of the Silver Star while executive officer of the destroyer USS Benion in the vicinity of Okinawa on April 30, 1945. He is survived by his wife Anne Butler Leonard, two children, Victoria Hambleton and Geoffrey Leonard, three step-children, Geoffrey Henning, Alyson Henning Walker and Kimberly Henning Brack, a sister, Elizabeth Mabach, and six grandchildren. He was predeceased by his first wife Georgine Fawcett Leonard, who died in 1982. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Yale Clas of 1939, Association of Yale Alumni, attn: Janet Cappiello, 232 York St, New Haven, CT 06511, or the Scranton Memorial Library, 801 Boston Post Rd, Madison, CT 06443. There will be a memorial service, Saturday, June 1, 2002, at St. Andrew's Episcopal Church, Madison, at 11 AM.
