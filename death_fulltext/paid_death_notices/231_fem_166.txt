
        STAFFORD--Margaret Frances. Born November 17, 1912 to William and Margaret Hayes. Was welcomed to Heaven on Easter Sunday by her beloved husband John, her Mama & Papa, and brothers Howard and Georgie (Father William Hayes, O.F.M.). Beloved aunt of Mary Gloria Gillen and Eileen O'Brien, twelve grandnieces and nephews, sixteen greatgrandnieces and nephews, and five great-greatgrandnieces and nephews. Dearest friend & ''Mother'' to the O'Regan family. Devoted parishioner of Our Lady of Angels Church in Brooklyn where her mass of Christian Burial was held on April 20.
