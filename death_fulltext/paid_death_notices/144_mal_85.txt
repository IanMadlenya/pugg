
        GRENNAN-Philip. Died January 1, 1999. He was the husband of Sally. Father of Christopher, Phyllis Huffman, Christine Stiller and Matthew (deceased). A partner in the architectural firm of Poor and Swanke, he was Chief Design Engineer for all AT&T projects. The first anacoic chamber and renovation of the American Express building in Paris. He will be missed by all who knew him.
