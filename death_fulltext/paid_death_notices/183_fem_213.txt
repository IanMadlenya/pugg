
        CAUST-Jeanette. On April 19, 2002 in Houston, Texas, 84 years old. A beautiful spirit, and dearly beloved mother to Sonya and Helene and stepmother to Harvey. Treasured grandmother to Michael, Jennifer, Noah, and Sammy and proud great-grandmother to Rayven. Loving wife of the late Leon Caust and the late Samuel Dolney. Cherished aunt and great-aunt to many nieces and nephews. She will be joyously remembered forever by many friends, family, and all whose lives she touched with her smile, grace and warmth. Services Tuesday, 12:30 P.M. at Wien & Wien, Hackensack, New Jersey, 201-489-3800. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to The American Diabetes Association.
