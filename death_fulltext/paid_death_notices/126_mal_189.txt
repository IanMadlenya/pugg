
        MILONE-Salvatore A., age 89, died July 17, 1997 at Rockville Centre, NY. Born in Rockville Centre on July 19, 1907, he was the son of Dominick and Elisa (nee Coppola) Milone. In 1935 he married the former Margaret Annunziato who died in 1966. In 1969 he married the former Maria Pennisi who survives him. He was the father of Richard D. Milone, M.D. and Elissa M. Metz, children of his first marriage, father-in-law of Mary Katherine Milone and Joseph G. Metz, and the grandfather of Richard D. Milone, Jr., Esq., Margaret E. Milone (deceased), Thomas M. Milone, Joseph G. Metz, Jr. (deceased), Regina M. Metz, Esq., Mary Elissa Metz, and Edward C. Metz. He is also survived by his sisters, Caroline M. Giaquinto, Vera M. Ferme and Grace M. Caso, and his brothers, Louis J. Milone and John E. Milone. His brothers, Dominick R. Milone and Joseph R. Milone, predeceased him. Reposing at Macken Mortuary, Inc., 52 Clinton Ave., Rockville Centre, Saturday and Sunday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Monday, 11 AM, at St. Agnes Cathedral, Rockville Centre. Entombment Cemetery of the Holy Rood, Westbury, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Salvatore A. Milone Scholarship Fund at Molloy College.
