
        KOVACS-John J.. Of Amston, CT, died peacefully on Wednesday, November 24, at age 91, at the Marlborough Health Care Center, Marlborough, CT. He was born in Spuyten Duyvil in the Bronx, NY and was formerly of Point Pleasant Beach, NJ. His funeral service will be Tuesday at 11:30AM at St. Francis of Assisi Chapel at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY. His family will receive friends at the Potter Funeral Home, 456 Jackson St. (Rte 195), Willimantic on Monday from 6:00 to 8:00PM. Contributions to a charity of the donor's choice.
