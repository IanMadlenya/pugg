
        BANDER-Ingram. Age 85, formerly of Queens, NY, died on August 5, 1998, in Baton Rouge, LA. He was a senior executive with the United Jewish Appeal of Greater New York from 1963 to 1980, when he retired to Palm Springs, CA. There he served as a volunteer with the Riverside County Mental Health Department as well as on the boards of various organizations for the mentally ill, for which he received several awards. Predeceased by his wife Harriet Pomeranz Bander, and survived by his children Elaine (Michael Pearson), Yigal (Ellen) and Alan, and granddaughters Rina, Orli, Deborah, Rachel and Sarah.
