
        LAYNE-Abner A. Age 82. Died on May 1, 2002. Graduate of Cornell University, retired journalist. Beloved husband of the late Mary Elizabeth. Loving brother of Robert (Rita) and Sidney (Phyllis). Survived by sisters-in-law Roberta Rosen & Jessica Newton, brotherin-law Donald Newton (Barbara), also many nieces, nephews, grandnieces, grandnephews. He will be remembered and loved by all those he touched in his long and healthy life.
