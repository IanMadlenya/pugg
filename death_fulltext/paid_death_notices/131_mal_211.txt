
        RAFELD-Gabriel. In loving memory. Adoring husband of Ann for 51 years, devoted Dad to Rosie, Marvin and Dwight. Proud Pa to Nicole, Michael, Samantha, Marc, Daniel and Pamela. He survived the Holocaust to live life with a vital gusto. He taught us how to enjoy life and respect the rights of others. Gabriel Rafeld was loved by all who knew him. We are shocked and deeply saddened by his sudden death. We will miss him terribly. Please make donations to The American Heart Association.
