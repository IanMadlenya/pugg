
        DINCE--Bertrand A. Passed away at age 76 on November 4, 2005. Father to Adam, son to the late Louis and Evelyn Dince, he is survived by nephews David Dince, William Dince, niece Nancy Kelly, brother Paul and the many who enjoyed his music and talents as a teacher. Services to be held Sunday, November 6th, at 9 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
