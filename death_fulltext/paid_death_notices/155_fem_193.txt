
        HARRIS-Edith Gruenberg. Died peacefully in her Manhattan home on December 19, 1999. Wife of the late Stephen Matsner Harris, she is survived by her daughter Emily, her son Frederick and four grandchildren. She was 85 years old and formerly lived in New Rochelle, N.Y. A memorial service will be held at Temple Ner Tamid, 936 Broad St , Bloomfield, N.J. at 8PM on December 23. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to Natural Resources Defense Council, 40 West 20 Street, NY, NY 10011.
