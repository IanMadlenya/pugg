
        FOGARTY-Judith Irene. Age 60. Died peacefully at home in Manhattan on May 2, 1999 after a long illness. A graduate of Fordham University, and a Copy Editor at ''People'' magazine, Ms. Fogarty loved New York and loved life. She will be greatly missed by her longtime companion, Marie P. Monaco, her mother Doris F. Weckworth, sister and brother-inlaw, Virginia and Gilbert Sharp, niece Deborah Ratel, nephew James R. Hausler, stepfather Charles Weckworth, and so many friends. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice, 1st Avenue and 16th Street, New York, NY 10003.
