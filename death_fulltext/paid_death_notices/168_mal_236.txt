
        BOGIN-Henry J., 80, of Harrisburg, formerly of Brooklyn and Queens, died January 16, 2001. He was the widower of Helen C. Bogin. Surviving are a son, Matthew B., a daughter, Heidi J. Oshin; and three grandchildren. Services were held Wednesday from Beth El Cemetery Chapel, Harrisburg. Memorial contributions to the Helen and Henry Bogin Memorial Fund, Beth El Temple, 2637 North Front Street, Harrisburg, PA 17110. Condolences may be expressed at www.legacy.com.
