
        MAGNER-Max. July 27, 2002. Born March 18, 1918, Brooklyn, NY. Adoring and loving husband of Ethel. Father of Norman, Lois and Marge. Father-in-law of Ava and KiHan Kim. Grandfather of Andrew, Matthew, Stephanie and Oliver. New York City police officer for 25 years. Celebrated 60th wedding anniversary in March. Life member Lieutenants Benevolent Association, Shomrim Society and B'nai B'rith. Max will be remembered fondly by all who knew him. Funeral services at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, Long Island, Monday, July 29, 2002, at 12 noon.
