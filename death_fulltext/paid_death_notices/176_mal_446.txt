
        BROSNAN-James S. On September 24th, of cancer. Mr. Brosnan was a graduate of Phillips Exeter Academy, Yale University, attended the Harvard Law School and was a Captain in the U.S. Marine Corps and served in the Korean War. Mr. Brosnan's wide ranging intellect, wit and command of language affected and improved the lives of his friends who will miss him dearly. He is survived by two sons, James and Andrew. A service will be held on Friday, September 28th at 11:30 AM at St. Ignatius Loyola Church.
