
        HOOKER - Joan F. Died peacefully on June 26, 2002 after a prolong illness. Scholar, author, educator, traveller, mother and friend. Former wife of the late Arthur Bowles Hooker. Survived by her son Sam Hooker of Norwalk, Ct., her daughters Wendy Hooker Swift and Martina Kearney Hooker both of Brooklyn, New York, and 4 grandchildren. She will greatly missed by so many whose lives she touched. The family is making arrangements for a service.
