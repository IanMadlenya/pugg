
        BASCOM--Howard Joseph. ''Joe''. Died May 4, 2006 in New York City. Born July 6, 1924 in Columbus, OH. Joe served in the Navy during WW II, and received his bachelor of fine arts from Ohio State University. He then taught drawing, painting, and design at The Toledo Art Museum before moving on to New York to work as a textile designer and illustrator. Since 1985, he concentrated on colored pencil works, for which he received numerous awards. He is survived by his wife, Virginia (''Ginger''), son, Michael, of Portland, OR, and stepson, Robert Winther of Williamsburg, VA.
