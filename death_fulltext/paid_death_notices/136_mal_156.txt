
        PHILLIPS-Lewis A. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the staff of the Jeanette & Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Department in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Lewis A. Phillips, cousin of Beth Israel Trustee Carol P. Green, and her brother, our generous benefactor Lawrence Phillips. He will be sorely missed by all who knew and worked with him. To the entire Phillips family and all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman Board of Trustees Matthew E. Sink, M.D., President Terry Williams, Director Jeanette and Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Department
