
        PLEHN-Richard Allen. On August 14, 2000. Survived by his devoted wife, Lois Plehn, and four children, Cynthia and her husband Edward Greenwald of Los Angeles, CA, Pamela, Richard and Tina and soon-to-be son-in-law David Max, of New York City. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Wednesday, August 16, 6-9PM, with service at Frank E. Campbell Thursday, August 17, 1:30PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Department of Public Health at Cornell University Medical School.
        PLEHN-Richard Allen. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Richard Allen Plehn. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
