
        DEGENSTEIN-Lila. On February 18, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Lester. Loving mother of Lee and his wife Joan. Adoring grandmother of David and Michael. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Monday, February 21, 11:30 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society.
        DEGENSTEIN-Lila. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Lila Degenstein. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
