
        DVORIN-Gertrude. Adored wife of the late Milton. Loving mother of Umi Liens and Leonard. Grandmother to Joshua, Allie, Matthew and Ben and great-grandmother. Lifelong resident of Linden, New Jersey, 1935 graduate of Douglas College, lifelong member of Hadassah, Gert was known for her intellectual and cultural pursuits. Services December 30, 12:30 graveside Beth Israel Memorial Park, Woodbridge, New Jersey.
