
        HARRISON-Leah G. (nee Goldmann). October 14, 2001. Wife of the late Paul P. She was 91 and retired after 37 years of teaching in NYC public schools. Mother of Elaine H. (Ernest) Cohen of Upper Darby, PA. Also survived by four grandchildren and seven great grandchildren. Sister of Pearl Goldmann. Graveside services are on Tuesday at 2 P.M., Riverside Cemetery, Saddlebrook, New Jersey. Shiva at her daughter's residence. Contributions to Temple Israel of Upper Darby PA.
