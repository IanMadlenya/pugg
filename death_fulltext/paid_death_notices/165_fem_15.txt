
        FRANKLIN-Gloria J., formerly known as Beulah Allen, of Kettering, OH died Thursday, September 28, 2000. She was a member of St. Albert the Great Catholic Church. Gloria lived 42 years in New York City and retired as nurse. She was preceded in death by her father Wheeler R. Allen, brother Clinton Allen and grandparents John and Esther Coffey, and Noah and Ada Allen. Survived by her husband Alan Franklin, mother Georgia (Coffey) and step-father Willard Cozatt of Dayton, sister Wanda Hann of Dayton, niece Deborah (Hann) McClure of Dayton, stepdaughter Sally Franklin of New York City, two step-brothers Jim (Lisa) and Mike Cozatt both of Troy and their children Darin, Dane, Cameesa, Aaron and Elizabeth. Mass of Christian Burial was held Saturday at St. Albert the Great Catholic Church. Interment Memorial Park Cemetery. Friends were received Friday, at BakerHazel Funeral Home, in Dayton, OH. www.bakerhazel.com
