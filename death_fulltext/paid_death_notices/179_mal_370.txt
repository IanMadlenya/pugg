
        SELVER-Rene. Passed away at the age of 84 years on December 28, 2001. Beloved husband of Jill. Father of Paul Selver and Elsbeth Haladay and grandfather of Jesse and Eden Haladay and Adam, Max and Katelyn Selver. He served his country as a soldier in the United States Army in World War II, rising to the rank of Captain. He led Dutchess Lingerie, Inc. and Sylvester Textile Corp. for over two generations. He gave generously to his community as a participant and leader of the United Jewish Appeals Mission to Israel, as a fundraising chair for the March of Dimes, as a patient advocate at Englewood Hospital and as Chairman of The Dispute Resolutions Committee in the building where he lived. He was a gentle and honorable man. His love of his wife, his children and grandchildren knew no bounds. He lived his life to the fullest. Funeral services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St., 10 AM on Sunday December 30, 2001. Contributions in his memory may be made to either The Anti-Defamation League or the Federation of Jewish Philanthropy.
