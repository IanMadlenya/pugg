
        BLOOM-Haven Roller. Suddenly on March 24th, 1999, beloved daughter of Barry and Frances Bloom, cherished granddaughter of Betty Roller, the late James J. Roller, the late Murray Bloom and the late Fay Bloom. Dear niece of Jessica Roller and Carol Bloom Furman. She was a Class 9 student at The Chapin School, New York City.         BLOOM-Haven. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the tragic and untimely passing of its member, Haven Bloom. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Martin I. Klein, President
        BLOOM-Haven Roller. We mourn the loss of our niece and cousin who was a special child. She was the focus and treasure of her parents Fran and Barry. Her time with us was all too short. With love, Anna, Carol, Bob, Patti, Debbie, Billy, Jared and Natalie
