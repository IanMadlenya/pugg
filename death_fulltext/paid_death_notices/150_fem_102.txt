
        HEALY-Eileen. The Center for Independence of the Disabled in New York, Inc. (CIDNY) and Independent Living Services mourn the loss of our esteemed Board President for the last decade, Eileen Healy. A beloved and respected leader in the independent living movement, Eileen was a lifelong advocate for the rights of people with disabilities. We, her colleagues and friends, will remember her dedication, energy and commitment as we continue her work. We offer our heartfelt condolences to her mother and entire family.
        HEALY-Eileen. The Board of Directors and staff of Independence Care System mourns the loss of our friend, colleague and chairperson of our Consumer Advisory Board. We will miss her wisdom, humor and vision. Our sincere sympathy to her family and friends.
