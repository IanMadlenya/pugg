
        MIESSE--Mirja Paabo. Age 81. Widow of William Thomas Miesse. Passed away January 29, 2004 in Mount Shasta, CA. A family memorial service for her cremated remains to held at her gravesite at Riverside Cemetery, Rochester, N.Y. on a date to be announced. She is survived by son William, daughter Anne and her husband Marzio, three grandchildren and a great grandson.
