
        DITCHIK-Florence Leder (Dixon), 92, died peacefully at home on February 2, 1997, surrounded by loved ones. Survived and beloved by sons: Donald, Richard and Stephen Dixon; daughters Marguerite (Bunny) Franco and Pat Dixon. Daughters-in-law Gerald, Irma and Anne; dear friend Sonia Sealey; fifteen grandchildren and thirteen great grandchildren all of whom loved her and were loved by her. She was predeceased by her husband Dr. Abraham M. Ditchik and children James and Carole Dixon. Services are private.
