
        THOMSON--Mary Bush, a resident of South Salem, NY died peacefully on December 20, 2007. She was born in Lima, Peru, the daughter of Charles and Estela Bush. She was predeceased by her husband Robert Dewar and her sister Estela Bush McCoun. She is survived by her son Robert Andrew Dewar Thomson, and a sister Cora Bush Michalis. A Memorial Service will be held at St. Luke's Episcopal Church Katonah, NY on Tuesday January 29 at 11am.
