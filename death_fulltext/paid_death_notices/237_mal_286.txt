
        HANLY--Joseph M. CSP, age 80, died Monday, October 23, in San Francisco. Father Hanly was ordained in 1953 at St. Paul the Apostle Church in New York City. For seventeen years Fr. Joe was at St Paul the Apostle Church in Los Angeles, conducting Inquiry Classes for non-Catholics and marriage preparation programs. He was also an effective and powerful preacher of missions and retreats in many parts of North America. Viewing will be at Old St. Marys Church, Grant and California Street, in San Francisco, Monday, October 30, at 4:00pm. A Mass of Christian Burial will follow at 7:00pm. Burial will be at Holy Cross Cemetery in Colma, CA at 10:00am, Tuesday, October 31.
