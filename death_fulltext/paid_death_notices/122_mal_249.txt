
        GIBSON-Ronald (Rufus), died peacefully on Wednesday, March 18, 1997 after a short illness. He was affiliated with the New York office of the European Commission. He is survived by his niece, Rosemary and her three children and his sister-in-law, Madeline, all of whom reside in England. Funeral service will be held at 4PM on Monday, March 24th at the Church of the Incarnation, 209 Madison Avenue. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the above church would be appreciated.
