
        YOUNG-Alexander, MD. The medical staff and administration of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed colleague, Alexander Young, MD. Dr. Young was a distinguished member of the Dermatology Department for over four decades and served as the first Director of Dermatology at our St. Luke's Division. He was an outstanding teacher and mentor to generations of medical students and house officers. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family and all his loved ones. Laurence Huntington, Chairman Ronald Ablow, MD President Robert Campbell, MD Medical Director Richard Fried, MD President, Medical Board Vincent DeLeo, MD Director, Dermatology
