
        MERVOSH-Edward. On December 29, 1998. Beloved father of Heather; loving companion of Patty Swain; cherished friend of many. Economic journalist and editor for Business Week magazine for 14 years; New York editor for the Economist Group; editor-in-chief of International Business magazine; most recently with KPMG online publication. He will be missed greatly by all. Visiting Sunday, 2-4 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
