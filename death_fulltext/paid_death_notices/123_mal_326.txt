
        DEITCH-Morton L. On April 26, 1997. Beloved husband of the late Marion, Frances and Helen. Loving father of Kenneth. Grandfather of David and Michael and stepfather of Nance R. Turner. Devoted brother of the late Edwin. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Tuesday, 4-8 PM. Services Wednesday, April 30th, 11 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65th St. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        DEITCH-Morton. The Officers and Trustees of Jewish Child Care Association convey their deepest sympathy to the family of Morton Deitch, a dear friend and distinguished colleague, who was a devoted advocate for children and families. Since joining JCCA's Board in 1951, Mr. Deitch served as President, Chairman of the Board, and Honorary Trustee, and was instrumental in developing the agency's innovative Group Residence Program. His informed, farsighted and enlightened leadership has contributed to the preservation of our most valued human resource-our children. Irwin U. Goldberg, President Stephen Sokoloff, Chairman of the Board Paul Gitelson, DSW, Executive Vice President
        DEITCH-Morton, L. The Firm of Stroock & Stroock & Lavan mourns the passing of Morton L. Deitch, a devoted colleague and a mentor to all for more than 70 years. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family and friends.
