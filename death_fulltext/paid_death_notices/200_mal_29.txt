
        FAIRCHILD -- Julian. The Board of Trustees of The Green - Wood Cemetery mourn the passing of Julian Douglas Fairchild, Trustee and former Chairman, who served the cemetery faithfully for over 40 years. Throughout his tenure on the Board, he resourcefully guided Green-Wood with devotion and interest; wise in his judgment and clear in his thinking. In his passing, the Trustees have lost a most esteemed colleague. The Trustees extend to his wife and family our heartfelt condolences. By Order of the Board of Trustees of the Green-Wood Cemetery
