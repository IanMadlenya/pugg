
        TARLOWE-Morris Arthur ''Sonny.'' Passed away in Boca Raton, FL, on August 28, 2001, he was 89. His was a gentle soul and loving heart. He embraced life. He will be dearly missed but his spirit lives on in everyone who knew him. Contributions in Arthur's memory may be made to A.M.C., 1600 Pierce St., Denver, C0 80214, or Bialystoker 212-475-7755.
