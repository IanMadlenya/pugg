
        GREENBAUM--Irene Blaser. Of Washington D.C., Palm Beach, and New York City, died January 4, 2006 after a long illness. She is survived by her children, Constance Rubin (Carl Wisotsky) and Martin (Ronna) Blaser, of New York City, and her grand children Heather (Marc), Lisa, Daniel, Genia, and Simone, and great-grandson, Evan. She was an extraordinary woman of spirit and valor, and she will be deeply missed. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her memory to the Israel Cancer Research Fund, 1290 Avenue of the Americas, Suite 550, NY, NY 10104. Funeral Services, Thursday 12:45PM, The Riverside, 76th and Amsterdam.
        GREENBAUM--Irene. The faculty and staff of the Department of Medicine at the NYU School of Medicine are deeply saddened by the passing of Mrs. Irene Greenbaum, mother of Dr. Martin J. Blaser, Chair of the Department of Medicine. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Dr. Blaser and his family. Department of Medicine, NYU School of Medicine
