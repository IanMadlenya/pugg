
        WAGNER-Robert. Sarah Lawrence College notes with sorrow the death of Robert Wagner, brilliant literature scholar, beloved teacher, member of the college faculty, 1953-1999, and Dean of the College, 1971-1977. We extend our deepest sympathy to his brother C. Roland Wagner, his wife Anneliese, daughters Elise and Carrie, son-in-law Robin Stout, grandchildren Leah and Alice, and his companion Susan Silver. Michele Tolela Myers, President Barbara Kaplan, Dean of the College
