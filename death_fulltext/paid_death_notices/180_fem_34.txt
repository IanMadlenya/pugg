
        SALZMAN-Ida. With profound sorrow, we mourn the death of Ida Salzman, age 87, who died December 31, 2001. Adored and cherished spouse, for 63 years, of Jacob A. Salzman, mother and mother-inlaw of Richard and Sue Salzman, Anita and Myron Pulier, and Robert Salzman and Lori Brooks. Grandmother of Eric, Greg, Anne, Rachel, Talia and Aramie and great grandmother of Jake, Will, Chloe and Abe. Ida expressed pleasure and gratitude for every aspect of her life. Filled with love and true friendship, her life will forever be a source of inspiration and strength. A memorial service will be held in the Spring.
