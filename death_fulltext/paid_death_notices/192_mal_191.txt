
        BOROWSKI-Elie. A giant of a man-who lived a life of scholarship, heroism, philanthropy, and family. Elie Borowswki is a world-renowned collector and scholar of monotheism's shared values, texts, and artifacts. The Borowski Collections' are world known; with the most important works housed in his Bible Lands Museum, Jerusalem. Elie Borowski was a humanitarian, a Holocaust survivor, a classical scholar focused on the future. On behalf of the values of good, Elie Borowski battled the 'angel of death' for 90 years.
