
        STEVENS--Ella Clyde Myers, age 95, died on Monday, November 3, 2003 after a long and productive life. Born in Columbia, South Carolina, to the late Isaiah and Ola Myers. The widow of the late New York Supreme Court Justice Harold A. Stevens. A graduate of Benedict College and graduate studies in New York and Chicago, she retired as a teacher in the NYC Public School system. She is survived by a cousin, Doris Toppin, a nephew, James Q. Stevens II, two grand nephews, James III and Jeffrey, and a grand niece, Gabrielle. In addition, there is a host of friends and associates from her many years of service in her religious, charitable and social activities. Funeral services will be held at 10:00 a.m., Monday, November 10th, at St. Mark's Evangelist R.C. Church, West 138th Street between 5th Ave and Lenox. Entombment will be at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Hawthorne, New York. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, memorials may be donated in memory of Ella to Inner City Scholarship Fund or Catholic Charities. Visiting hours are from 1:00 to 3:00 pm on Sunday November 9th at the Elizabeth Smith Funeral Service, 2284 Adam Clayton Powell Blvd., at 134th St.
