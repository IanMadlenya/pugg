
        JAFFEE--Isabel, mother, grandmother, friend, and activist, died March 13, after a heroic battle with cancer. Committed to peace and social justice, Isabel's adventurous spirit, humor and sage advice will be sorely missed. She is survived by daughters, Anna and Cyrisse, sons-in-law Kevin Briody and Barry Anechiarico, and grandchildren Ashley and Jonathan JaffeeAnechiarico. A 1pm Memorial Service will be held April 15 at Samuel Field Y, Little Neck, NY. Contributions may be sent to Doctors Without Borders and Jewish Currents.
