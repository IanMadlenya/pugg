
        LEVY--Richard L. (Dick) of Sarasota, FL and Somers, NY (formerly of New York City) died on August 2, 2004 at the age of 83 of melanoma. Beloved husband of Barbara Ershler Levy, cherished father of Adam (Natalie) and Carol (Andrew) Garfinkle, adored grandfather of Madeline and Grace Levy, Julia and Alexa Garfinkle. Brother of Laurence and the late Janice Goldstein. He worked in the women's shoe manufacturing industry for 43 years, retiring in 1990. He represented Encore Shoes for 25 years. He was a Shakespeare buff and organized and led weekly reading and discussion groups in Heritage Hills in Somers and at the Selby Public Library in Sarasota. He was a gifted craftsman who made unique gold and silver jewelry, as well as varied woodworking pieces during his retirement. He was born in Boston, graduated from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania. He joined the army as an enlisted man and was discharged in 1946 as a first lieutenant, having served in the Signal Intelligence Corp in Europe and Asia. We will remember him as a true renaissance man. His integrity, strength of character, and above all, love for his family will be an inspiration to us always. Funeral services will be held at the Levine Chapel, 1 PM, August 4. In lieu of flowers, donations to Memorial Sloan Kettering Hospital, Melanoma Research, 1275 York Avenue, New York City 10021.
