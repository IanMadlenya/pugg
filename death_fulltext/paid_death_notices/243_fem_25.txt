
        KERNOCHAN--Adelaide. Beloved wife of Jack, adored mother of John, Denny, Sarah, Wayne and Rose. Author and educator, her extensive travels and tireless work for UNESCO produced UN bestseller ''World Concerns and the United Nations'', innovative model teaching units for schools worldwide, which is still in use. She will be missed and remembered for her fortitude and dynamism. Memorial at 11am, Friday, April 20, All Souls Church, 1157 Lexington Ave. Donations to UNICEF instead of flowers appreciated.
