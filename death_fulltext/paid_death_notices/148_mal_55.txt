
        PAYTON-Scott J. On May 1, 1999. Beloved son of Doris Payton-Jacobs and the late Ralph W. Payton, loving brother of Heather K. Payton, Jasmine C.S. Jacobs, adored stepson of Brian L. Jacobs. He is mourned by two nieces, aunts, uncles, cousins and numerous friends. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, Sunday, May 9, 7 to 9 PM. Service Monday, May 10, 10 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. Interment at Calverton National Cemetery.
        PAYTON-Scott James. Was born to Ralph Warrick Payton (deceased) and Doris Smith Payton in Brooklyn, N.Y., March 11, 1971. Scott was an incredibly intelligent and curious child who grew into a wonderful, adventurous young man. Scott was an avid reader who held a B.A. in English Literature. He loved his family, his friends, his climbing and his Marine Corps. He leaves to mourn a family who loved him totally and without reserve and will carry his smile in our hearts forever.
