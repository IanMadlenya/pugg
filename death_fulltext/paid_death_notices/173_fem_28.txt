
        FARRISS-Catherine Faulconer. Of New York City, died Friday, May 25, at Cabrini Hospital in New York. A memorial service will held Friday, at 5 PM, June 8, at First Presbyterian Church. The Rev. Dr. James G. Speer will conduct the service. She was the wife of the late Carter Wood Farriss. A native of Burlington, NC, Mrs. Farriss graduated from the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Her lifetime career was in television and radio at NBC, where she was a staff writer for over 30 years. In 1985, she received an Emmy award for writing the NBC documentary entitled ''Chagall's Journey.'' She was a member of First Presbyterian Church, New York City. She is survived by a sister, Mrs. Nelson K. Reid of Burlington, three nieces and one nephew.
