
        WILLIS-Everett I., of Rye Brook, New York, February 27th. Beloved husband of Margaret, loving father of Everett I. Willis, Jr. and Robert F. Willis, grandfather of Katherine E. Willis and Evan Charles Willis. Also survived by his sister, Lonna Chapman. Mr. Willis was a retired senior partner at the firm of Dewey Ballantine LLP where he became a member of the Firm in 1944. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate contributions to Rye Presbyterian Church, Rye, New York. There will be a memorial service on Wednesday, March 4, 11 A.M. at Rye Presbyterian Church, Rye, N.Y.
