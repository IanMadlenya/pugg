
        BENSTOCK-Jane, age 92, died peacefully at her home in Brightwaters, NY on May 19, 1999. Wife of the late David. Devoted mother of Mochelle Stettner of Allentown, PA and Gerald Benstock of Belleair Beach, FL. Cherished grandmother of 8 and great-grandmother of 15. Memorial contributions to the Bayshore Jewish Center.
