
        SCHINDLER--Joe P., 76, November 20th, passed away after a long and valiant fight with Multiple Myeloma. Survived by children Deborah, Rachel and Aaron, son-in-law Todd Thaler and granddaughter Emma Thaler. We will deeply miss his guidance, leadership, strength, wisdom and love. Funeral service Sunday, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Joseph Michaeli Multiple Myeloma Research Fund at the New York Presbyterian Hospital, Cornell, Att: Ruben Niesvizky, M.D. or A.J.C.
