
        MARTINSON--Paul. Manhattan School of Music and its Board of Trustees is saddened by the passing of Paul Martinson, cherished friend to the School and Trustee Emeritus. Mr. Martinson possessed a deep and abiding love for music and was a longtime supporter of the School's many programs. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife Frances and to the entire Martinson family. David A. Rahm, Chairman, Board of Trustees Marta Istomin, President
