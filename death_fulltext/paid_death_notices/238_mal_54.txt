
        McKEE--G. Ross. Educator, columnist, philanthropist, died August 19, 2006 at age 93 at his Newfoundland summer home. A native New Yorker, he attended Collegiate and Lawrenceville Schools and graduated Princeton University in 1935. He served in the Air Force in WWII. He married Marion Fountain with whom he had four children. They lived in New Canaan, CT and summered in Quogue, NY. In 1974, he moved to Key West where in 1976 he married Anne Corbett. He will be sadly missed by his widow, Anne, his four children, three stepchildren and nine grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Key West on December 21.
