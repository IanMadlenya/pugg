
        GREENSPAN--Phyllis. The staff, Trustees and Volunteer Association of the Jewish Museum mourn the loss on September 22, 2005 of our former colleague of 20 years, Phyllis Greenspan. As a teacher and docent, she engaged colleagues and visitors of all ages with her charm, knowledge and a deep commitment to Jewish culture. Our deepest sympathy to her husband Murray Greenspan and their whole family. Joan Rosenbaum, Director Leni May, Chairman Barry Alperin, President
