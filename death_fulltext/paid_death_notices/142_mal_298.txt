
        CAREY-William P. Jr., MD. The residents, staff and faculty of the Ronald O. Perelman Department of Dermatology at New York University Medical Center mourn the sudden, tragic death of our alumnus. Bill graduated from the University of Pennsylvania School of Medicine and was an outstanding member of our 1998 residency class. At the time of his accidental death he was a Fellow in Dermatopathology at the University of Pennsylvania with a brilliant career awaiting him. We share this great sorrow with Bill's wife Lisa, his parents, family and friends. Irwin M. Freedberg, M.D. MacKee Professor and Chairman
