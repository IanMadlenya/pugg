
        BERGNER-Rose (nee Grenadier). Died September 18, 1998 in La Jolla, Ca. Widow of Harold A. Bergner, M.D. Devoted mother of Elaine Schneider. Grandmother of Danielle and Jane Schneider. Great-grandmother of Rachel Eaton. Cherished sister of George and Simon Grenadier. Her family including many loving nieces and nephews will remember her kindness, generosity and humor.
