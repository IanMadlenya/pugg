
        RAAMOT-Tonis, 68, renowned civil engineer. Tonis Raamot, CEO, of Raamot Associates, P.C. died on Wednesday 26 April 2000 at New York University Medical Center. Dr. Raamot established the firm in 1969 and was a recognized authority on soil mechanics and foundation engineering in the United States and more than 20 foreign countries. Prior to establishing his own firm, Dr. Raamot was Chief Civil Engineer of the Raymond Concrete Pile Division of Raymond International Inc. His teaching experience included a professorship in soil mechanics and foundation engineering at Newark College of Engineering (now AJIT). Dr. Raamot also served on active duty in the U.S. Navy Civil Engineering Corps, retiring as a Lieutenant Commander. Dr. Raamot was also an alumnus of Columbia University (BA, BS, MS) and the University of Illinois (Ph.D.) He was a fellow of the American Society of Civil Engineers and also a member of the National Society of Professional Engineers and the Society of American Military Engineers. Dr. Raamot is survived by his wife Annette, his daughters Anne, Kristin and Liia, his brother Jaan, and his grandchildren Maggie, John and Marin.
