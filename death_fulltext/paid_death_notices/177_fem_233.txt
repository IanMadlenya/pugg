
        SAMROCK-Hyla. Died at home on Friday, October 19. She was 84. Married for nearly 60 years to Victor Samrock, who died in 1995. She is survived by her children, Carl and Ellen; grandsons Gabriel and Steven; daughter-in-law Carol, and other family members and friends. Born in Hartford, CT, Hyla (Rubin) came to New York as a teenager to study dance and join legendary modern dance pioneer Doris Humphrey and her troupe. She left the company to become a wife, mother and homemaker while continuing to follow her passionate commitment to liberal causes. Contributions to Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60th St., NYC 10022 are appreciated.
