
        ARIES-Peter L. A lifetime resident of Westchester County died Friday, January 9, 1998 at his home in Ossining, NY. He was 60. He was born May 28, 1937 in Yonkers, NY to the late Ernest J. Aries and the late Gladys Friedman Aries. He was a graduate of Riverdale Country School and Hobart College. On June 12, 1960 he married Florence Sue Lazar. Mr. Aries was a prominent member of a family well known in real estate in Westchester County dating back to the turn of the century. At the time of his death, he was the Chairman of the Board of Aries Deitch and Endelson, Inc. Hartsdale, NY, a firm specializing in shopping center development, leasing and management throughout New York State and several other states. Mr. Aries was the founder and first president of the Building Owners and Managers Association of Westchester County (BOMA). He was a member of Dunwoodie Lodge of Masons. He was a lecturer at the New York University Real Estate Institute, and was active and well known in the International Council of Shopping Centers (ICSC). He was the author of numerous articles for real estate publications. In addition to his wife, he is survived by two sons, Richard Aries of Agoura Hills, CA., and James Aries of New York City, a daughter, Nancy Aries Koff of Briarcliff Manor, NY, a sister, Linda Aries Friedman of White Plains, NY, three grandchildren: Garrett and Jamie Aries and Zoe Koff, a daughter-in-law, Elise Aries, and a son-in-law, Ronald Koff. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon on Sunday at 10AM. Interment will be at Mt. Hope Cemetery in Hastings-on-Hudson, New York. In lieu of flowers, the family would appreciate contributions to the American Cancer Society.
        ARIES-Peter. We will miss our dear friend. Our heartfelt condolences to the entire family. With much love. The Pollack Family
