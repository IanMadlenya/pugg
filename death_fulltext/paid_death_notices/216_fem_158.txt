
        PARADISE--Charlotte (nee Unger). 99, of Winchester, MA, and formerly of Cincinnati, OH, on January 16, 2005. Wife of the late James C. Paradise, she is survived by her son Robert Paradise of Bedford, MA; her daughter Ellen Paradise Fisher of Cambridge, MA; her brother Mort Unger of Oakbrook, IL; five grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. Contributions in her memory can be made either to the James C. and Charlotte Paradise Scholarship Fund at the Greater Cincinnati Foundation, 200 West Fourth St, Cincinnati, OH 45202; or the James Paradise and Maurice McCracken Fund for Civil Liberties at the ACLU, 125 Broad Street, 18th Floor, New York, NY 10004-2454.
