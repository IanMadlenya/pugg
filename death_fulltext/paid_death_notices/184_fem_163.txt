
        BAHR-Marianne (nee Wolf). Died of natural causes on Tuesday, May 21, 2002 after a long illness. Formerly of Hanover, Germany, Mrs. Bahr was a resident of New York City since 1939. She was the beloved wife of her late husband, Julius Bahr, a coffee importer. The relatives from around the world in Milan, Italy, Sao Paulo, Brazil, South Africa, Mexico, Switzerland, London and Montevideo, Uruguay all mourn her passing. She was a long-standing member of Congregation Rameth Orah. She was an active Director-Trustee of the Wolf Foundation of Tel Aviv Israel, founded by her uncle Richard Wolf, former Cuban Ambassador to Israel. She will be greatly missed by those who had the privilege of knowing this fine human being.
