
        MENDLE--Blanche. Passed away February 20, 2006, she was 91 years old. Beloved mother of Judith Konigsberg and Susan Tenser, loving grandmother to Matthew Feiner, Allison Feldman, Lyndsay Dowd and Jared Goldstein. Great grandmother to Colby Goldstein and Molly Feldman. We will miss her love and humor. $(6$)MENDLE--Blanche. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved mother of Judy Konigsberg and dear mother-in-law of Paul Konigsberg, ardent supporters of our programs in medical education and biomedical research. Judy is a member of the National Board of Einstein's Women's Division and an honorary president of its Westchester/ Fairfield Chapter. Paul serves as treasurer of our Board of Overseers and is a member of the Executive Board of our Men's Division. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Judy and Paul, and to all the members of their family. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J.
