
        BRAUN-Lev, Ph.D. Beloved husband of Micheline and dear uncle of Chaia Bekefi. Also survived by grand-nephew Ariel Bekefi and grand-niece Tamara Bekefi. Dr. Braun was a retired Professor of Political Science at Fairleigh-Dickinson University and prior to that, during W.W. II, he was a broadcaster for Radio Free Europe and the BBC. Funeral service Thursday, 1 PM, at the Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, West 72nd St, NYC.
