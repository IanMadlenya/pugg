
        LANSING-Richard. On March 29, 1997, at home. He leaves his third wife, Elizabeth Alston Lansing, 20 nieces and nephews and their children. Born in Rochester, NY on May 23, 1916, Richard is remembered for his incisive mind and wicked sense of humor. With his loden cape and great imagination, he was a special friend to children. A gathering to remember him will be announced later. Should you wish, please send a contribution in his memory to the Alzheimers Association 420 Lexington Avenue, New York, New York 10017.
