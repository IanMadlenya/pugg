
        CARMEL -- Herman, Ph.D. Scholar, Rabbi, Professor, Author. Died in NYC February 27, aged 94. Husband of Ida of blessed memory. His love, friendship, humor and dignity will be missed by his family and the granddaughters on whom he doted, Rina, Abigail, and Joanna. Donations may be sent to Magen David Adom, 866-583-8539.
