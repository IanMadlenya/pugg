
        JOSEPH-Inga. Died October 9 from injuries sustained during the September 11 attack on the World Trade Center. She leaves behind family and friends in her native Berlin and in New York City. A New Yorker for over 3 decades, she revelled in the great music of a great city. Services Saturday 7 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th and Amsterdam Ave. Donations may be made in Inga's memory the Metropolitan Opera Guild, 70 Lincoln Center Plaza, NY, NY 10023
        JOSEPH-Inga. The staff of Rohde & Liesenfeld Inc worldwide mourns the passing of our employee and dear friend, Inga Joseph. We extend our deepest condolences and heartfelt sympathy to her entire family and friends. We are deeply saddened by the passing of Inga Joseph. We treasure our memories together and deeply mourn her loss even as we celebrate her life.
