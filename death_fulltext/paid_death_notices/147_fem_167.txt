
        THROOP-Barbara Williams. Formerly of Lawrence, NY, beloved wife of the late Enos Thompson Throop IV, daughter of Thomas Resolved Williams, died on April 26, 1999 in St. Michaels, MD. Mrs. Throop graduated from St. Timothy's School in 1931. An avid squash player, she represented the United States internationally, defeating Britain's top-seated player in the Wolfe-Noel Cup Competition in 1937. She is survived by her two sons, Enos Thompson Throop V, of St. Michaels, MD and Garrow Throop of Newtonville, MA, and two grandchildren, Julia and Nicholas Throop. Services will be held at 11:00 AM on Friday, April 30, at Trinity-St. Johns Church, 1142 Broadway, Hewlett, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to the U.S. Driving for the Disabled, Inc., P.O. Box 264, Jarrettsville, MD 21084 would be appreciated.
