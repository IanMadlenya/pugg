
        KAPLANSKY-Susan. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff at the Hospital for Joint Diseases are deeply saddened by the loss of Susan Kaplansky, devoted daughter of our good friends Dr. and Mrs. Alfred D. Grant. Dr. Grant is a long standing member of our medical staff, who has served with distinction for many years. Susan leaves a grieving family and many friends who will remember her for her kindness, grace and her dedication to her family. Our heartfelt condoleneces are extended to her husband, Eyal, their children, her parents and to the entire family. William A. Perlmuth Chairman of the Board John N. Kastanis, FACHE President and CEO Victor H. Frankel, M.D. President Emeritus Joseph D. Zuckerman, M.D. Surgeon-in-Chief
        KAPLANSKY-Susan. The members of the NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery, are deeply saddened by the untimely passing of Susan Kaplansky, dear daughter of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Alfred D. Grant and his wife Ellen. Dr. Grant has been a dedicated member of the department for over forty years. Susan will always be remembered for her kind and gentle ways and as a devoted daughter, wife and mother. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her husband Eyal, their children, her parents and to the entire family. Joseph D. Zuckerman, MD Chairman NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery William Jaffe, MD, Vice-Chairman NYU-Hospital for Joint Diseases Dept. of Orthopaedic Surgery
