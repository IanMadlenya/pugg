
        LAIRD-W. Frederick. Retired Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of the Columbia Gas System, Inc., died Tuesday, Sept. 15, at Christiana Hospital in Wilmington, DE after a brief illness. He was 79. Survivors include his wife of 55 years, Jane, two sons, Frederick W. of Plainfield, NJ and John W. of NYC, & four grandchildren. There will be a memorial service at Westminster Presbyterian Church, Pennsylvania Ave. at Rodney St. in Wilmington at 11 AM, Monday, September 21. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to Delaware Hospice, 3515 Silverside Rd., Wilmington, DE. 19810 or Westminster Presbyterian Church, 1502 W. 13th Street, Wilmington, DE. 19806.
