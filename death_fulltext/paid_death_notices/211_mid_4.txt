
        KLEIN--Marshall, on August 24, 2004, at the age of 86. Beloved husband of the late Hanna. Loving father of Robert and his wife Carol, and Susan and her husband Russell. Adoring grandfather of Loren and Michael Klein, and Hanna. Dear brother of Samuel. Cherished companion of Florence Neidich. Mr. Klein was a principal of Crown Products of Yonkers, NY. Funeral service Thursday, August 26, 2004, 1 PM from ''Westchester Reform Temple'', 255 Mamaroneck Road, Scarsdale, NY.
