
        PEISER-Peter Dietrich. Adored father, admired friend; died on Friday, November 27, 1998, at home in New York City, age 73. Born in Bonn, Germany, he moved with his mother to London aged 10, where he was raised and educated. At 28 he was employed by E.R. Squibb & Sons, and remained with them for the length of his career. During his tenure he worked in London, the British West Indies, Teheran, Cairo, Beirut, New York, Paris, and in 1979, moved to the headquarters in Princeton, NJ, to found the ConvaTec Co. As President, he was instrumental in the development of DuoDerm, a transdermal skin preparation now used worldwide for multiple applications. He is survived by his daughter Caroline, whose love for him is eternal. Donations may be made to the Humane Society or the ASPCA.
