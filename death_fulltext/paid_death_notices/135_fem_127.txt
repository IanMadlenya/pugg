
        TAYLOR-Elsie Lathrop. In Tucson, AZ on April 9, 1998, age 96. Widow of W.R.K. Taylor. Formerly of NYC. Survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Elsie and Ralph Conly of Sunnyvale, CA., her son Kirk's widow, Anita Gildersleeve of New Milford, CT., her niece, Sylvia Van Sloun of Westport Point, MA., and by six grandchildren and thirteen great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. Philips-in-the-Hills, River Road and Campbell Avenue, Tucson at 3 PM, April 29. Gifts in her memory may be sent to Tucson Medical Center Auxiliary, 5301 East Grant Road, Tucson, Arizona 85733, where Mrs. Taylor was an active volunteer for many years.
