
        FITZGERALD-W. Francis.93, of West Hartford, CT and formerly of Somers, NY, died on December 13, 1999 at Avery Heights Nursing Home, West Hartford, CT. He had worked as a banking and real estate financial consultant in NYC. He is survived by his daughters, Barbara Runde, Janet and Peggy FitzGerald, a son Daniel FitzGerald, and five grandchildren. Private family funeral mass. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to: VNA Hospice, 103 Woodland Street, Hartford, CT 06105
