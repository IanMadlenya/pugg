
        LOVER-Melanie Jill. Age 43, on March 10, 1999 at her home in West Orange, NJ. Beloved wife of Jim Schlink, daughter of Vera and sister of Melissa. She will be deeply mourned by her family, her many friends and respected colleagues. Melanie received an undergraduate degree in Music Education from Kean College and an MBA from Adelphi University. Melanie, a Novartis Pharmaceuticals Executive, was Director of Business Development and Licensing with the Consumer Division. In lieu of flowers a donation may be made to the North Shore Animal League, Lewy Street, Port Washington, NY 11050 or the West Orange Animal League. Services will be held at 11:30 AM on March 12 at Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Road, Union, NJ. Interment at B'nai Abraham Memorial Park, Union, N.J.
