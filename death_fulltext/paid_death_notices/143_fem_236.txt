
        STRAUSS-Margaret L., 83, suddenly on December 25, peacefully in her sleep. Beloved wife of Charles for over fifty-seven years. Devoted mother of Leyae (deceased), Barbara, Charles, Jr., and Sherry. Adoring grandmother of Emily, Hannah and Noah. Loving sister-in-law of Barbara and Peter Strauss, and Anne Levi Knox. Memorial service on Sunday, 12 noon, at Congregation Emanuel of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Avenue, Rye, New York. A great conservationist. Donations to the National Resources Defense Council or the Life Center of Larchmont would be appreciated
