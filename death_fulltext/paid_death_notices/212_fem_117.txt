
        SNYDER--Joan D. On September 9, after a long battle with diabetes. Described by Dan Rather as ''A pioneering reporter who opened new opportunities for women at CBS News.'' Joan spent 30 years at CBS as a writer, producer and reporter, always fighting for the underdog. She later worked for the NYC Parks Dept. A memorial tribute will take place Sunday, October 3, 3PM, at Central Park Armory, 5th Ave and 64th Street.
