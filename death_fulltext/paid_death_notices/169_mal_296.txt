
        TALBOT-Robert ''Pete'' 83. Of Bloomfield, CT and Fishers Island, NY died February 26, 2001 in CT. He was the son of the late Harold Richmond Talbot and Nannie Brown Talbot. He graduated from Deerfield Academy in 1935 and from Yale University in 1939. He was retired from Pratt and Whitney Aircraft. Survivors include his wife, Caroline Allen Talbot, three sons and a daughter and eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Monday, March 5, 2001 at 2:00 P.M. at Asylum Hill Congregational Church, Hartford, CT. Donations may be made to the American Red Cross, 209 Farmington Ave., Farmington, CT 06032 or the Henry L. Ferguson Museum, Fishers Island, NY 06390.
