
        FAIRCHILD--Julian Douglas, on August 26, 2003, at age 87, of Locust Valley, New York died in Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida. Survived by his wife Alison Brewster Fairchild and sons John D. Fairchild and Peter L. Fairchild, and six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Friday, September 5, at 11:30 AM, at St. John's of Lattingtown in Locust Valley, New York. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the World Wildlife Foundation.
