
        SCHAFFER-Abraham I., M.D. The Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital express their sorrow at the loss of Abraham I. Schaffer, M.D., Honorary Associate Physician. A valued member of our Department of Medicine since 1967, Dr. Schaffer was a respected cardiologist and a devoted and inspiring teacher. We honor the memory of this outstanding physician and extend our sincere condolences to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO Stephen A. Obstbaum, M.D., President, Medical Board Michael S. Bruno, M.D. Director of Medicine
