
        GRUNZWEIG - Zigmund. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center expresses great sorrow on the passing of Zigmund Grunzweig, beloved father of Milton Grunzweig, MD, esteemed attending in the Department of Medicine. We extend our profound and heartfelt condolences to his wife Irene, daughter Marta and the entire family. Edwin L. Schulman Chairman, Board of Trustees David I. Schachne Chairman, Executive Committee Alvin I. Kahn, MD President and CEO Richard J. Fogler, MD Sr. Vice President and CMO
