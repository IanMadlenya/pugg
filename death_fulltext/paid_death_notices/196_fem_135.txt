
        SILVERSTEIN - Sadie. 91. Wife of the late Rabbi Israel Silverstein. Mother of Lorraine (Leonard) Dauber, grandmother of Paul Dauber, Jane (Michael) Lewittes, and Daniel (Dina) Dauber.
