
        OSBORN--Paul Albert, of Cedar Grove, NJ passed away at the age of 75 on February 23rd. Paul was raised in Montclair, NJ and was a veteran of the Korean War. He was a highly regarded forensic document examiner in New York City for nearly 50 years in the century old practice founded by his grandfather and now run by his eldest son. Paul leaves behind his wife of 50 years, Shirley, three sons, seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. Services will be Saturday, March 3rd at Union Congregational Church at 176 Cooper Avenue in Upper Montclair, NJ.
