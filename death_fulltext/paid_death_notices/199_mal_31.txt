
        SMITH--William G. of Manhattan, died on July 30, 2003. He was born on September 19, 1922 in Chicago. He was a graduate of the class of 1941 from Shady Side Academy. Mr. Smith joined Cartier, Inc., in 1947 and retired in 1985 as Director of Project Development for Cartier International. Private burial services will take place at Calvary Cemetery, Pittsburgh, PA. >>AD#
