
        HARRINGTON-Denis J., of East Northport, New York. Native of Droumanassig, Kenmare, County Kerry, Ireland, on April 28, 1997 in his 72nd year. Korean War veteran recipient of the Bronze Star & Purple Heart. Retired paper handler with The New York Times for 45 years. Devoted husband of Bridget ''Betty'' E. (nee Tolan). Beloved father of Elizabeth M. Dyer, Colleen A. Browning, Denis J., Jr, and Erin M. Loving grandfather of Bridget, Katy, Kerry, Chase, Christopher, Terran, and Zacky. Fond brother of Kathleen O'Sullivan, Daniel, John, James, Patrick, the late Michael, and the late Timothy. Reposing at Nolan & Taylor-Howe Funeral Home, 5 Laurel Ave, Northport. Funeral Mass, Friday, 9:45 AM, St. Anthony of Padua Church, East Northport. Visiting hours Wednesday 7-9 PM, and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9 P.M.
