
        MACAULEY - Naomi (Zemlan sky). Of Bloomfield, NJ, formerly of NYC, on July 21, 2000. She was 63 years old. The former editor of Vogue Patterns Magazine, she retired in 1973. Survived by her husband Robert G., three children, David, Robert and Susan Joy Brunetti, a granddaughter, Kira Rebecca Macauley. Funeral service Monday, 10:30 AM, at the Watchung Presbyterian Church, Bloomfield.
