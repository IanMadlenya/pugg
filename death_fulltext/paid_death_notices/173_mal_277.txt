
        CAGAN-Jack. On June 25, 2001 at age 80. Beloved husband for 55 years of the late Susan. WWII veteran, with a Purple Heart and Distinguished Flying Cross. He was a sr. tax auditor/US Treasury, then a CPA and tax attorney for over 40 years. He will be remembered as a man of great intellect, grace, wit and wisdom, which was sought by all, whether family, friends, or colleagues. He is survived by his children, Arlynn, Barbara, Edward and Eric, their spouses, his five grandchildren & his widow, Renee. We will always love him & cherish his memory forever.
