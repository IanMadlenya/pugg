
        UTTAL--Robert, 100, died April 28, 2006 at home. He is survived by daughters Barbara Clark, Palm Coast, FL, Maxine Uttal, Hilton Head, SC, Elinor Uttal, Pembroke Pines, FL, Patricia Nelson, NYC, sister Toby Cedarbaum, Tucson, AZ, 9 grandchildren, 12 great-grandchildren, 1 great-great grandchild. He was predeceased by son William and wives Ida and Edna. He owned Uttal's Luggage Store in Mt. Vernon, NY and was active in Sinai Temple, the Lion's Club and the Chamber of Commerce. He served as President of the National Luggage Dealers Association. Retired in 1981 to N. Miami Beach, FL, was active in Friends of Mount Sinai Hospital and the California Club. Graveside services by Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mt. Vernon, NY.
