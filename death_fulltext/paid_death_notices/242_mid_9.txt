
        GOLDSTEIN--Ida, of Deerfield Beach, FL, died on March 24 at 92. Adored mother of Marcia Shubitz, and her husband, Gerald, and Richard Goldstein and his wife, Nancy Lubell. Cherished grandmother of Stacey Shubitz. Beloved wife of the late Morris Goldstein. Devoted sister of the late Joseph Brown and Sylvia Epstein.
