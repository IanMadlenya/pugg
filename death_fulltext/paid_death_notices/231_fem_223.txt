
        WINICK--Mariann Pezzella. On Good Friday, April 14, 2006, after a long illness. Loving wife of Charles. Beloved mother of Laura and Raphael. Devoted sister of Regina Becker and Anthony Pezzella. A thoughtful teacher, scholar and writer. Faculty member of Lehman College and Rome's International Montessori School. This exceptional woman will be missed by those who love her, near and far.
