
        KNAFEL-Susan Rappaport. On June 7, 1998. Beloved wife of Sidney R. Knafel. Loving mother of Douglas R. and Andrew G. Knafel. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, on Tuesday, June 9th, at 1:30 P.M. Interment to follow at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, New York.
        KNAFEL-Susan. The Board and staff of F.E.G.S. deeply regret the passing of its esteemed Secretary and Board member, Susan Knafel. Susan was a Member of the Board of F.E.G.S. since 1984. She was a dedicated and committed Lay Leader, whose compassion, commitment and understanding helped F.E.G.S. to provide vital services to over 70,000 people each year. She will be sorely missed. We extend our heartelt condolences to her husband, Sydney, sons, Andrew and Douglas, and the entire Knafel family. Joseph Stein, Jr. President/Chair F.E.G.S. Alfred P. Miller Executive V.P./CEO, F.E.G.S.
        KNAFEL-Susan. The Jewish Foundation for Education of Women mourns the loss of Susan Knafel, long-time member of the Board and former Chair of the Policy Committee. Her keen insights as a member of the Scholarship Committee helped ensure that hundreds of women achieved their educational goals. We will all miss her special warmth. Our deepest condolences to her family. Susan Schatz, President
        KNAFEL-Susan. The Rogosin Institute, Board of Directors and staff members mourn the death of Susan R. Knafel, beloved wife of RI board member Sidney Knafel. The Knafel family actively supports the RI research and treatment programs. Heartfelt condolences are extended to the family and friends.
        KNAFEL-Susan R. The partners, associates and staff of Cooperman Levitt Winikoff Lester & Newman, P.C., extend their deepest sympathy to their long-time friend and client, Sidney Knafel, on the loss of his beloved wife, Susan. May the family be spared further sorrow.
