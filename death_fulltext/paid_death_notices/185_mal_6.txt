
        BIEN-Robert L. Prominent New York City mid-century architect and a distinguished gentleman, died on May 25, 2002. Our mentor and friend, the consummate professional who always had time for a story and a laugh. Mr. Bien was a walking historian of New York City and its architecture. Our deepest sympathy to his wife and our dear friend Lynne, daughter Deborah, grandchildren Jocelyn and Robert and sister Phyllis McGovern. We will forever be graced by his spirit and guidance. A public memorial service will be held Tuesday, June 4, at 12 noon at the chapel of St. George's Church, 6 Rutherford Place (between 16th and 17th Streets and 2nd and 3rd Avenues). Craig J.
