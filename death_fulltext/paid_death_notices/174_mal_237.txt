
        MARBACH-Dr. Joseph J. Adoring husband of Judith Marbach. Devoted father of Claudia and Daniel, Emily and Tim, Ezra and Cecily. Dear brother of Lee Smith and Paula Nathan. Loving grandfather of Rachel, Noah, Rebecca, Sam, Akiva, Amos and Alice. Dr. Marbach was a compassionate doctor to his patients in the area of chronic facial pain and fibromyalgia. Dr. Marbach held the Robert and Susan Carmel Professor in Algesiology at University of Medicine and Dentistry of NJ with appointments at both dental school and the Department of Psychiatry. He also held joint appointments as lecturer at Columbia University at the Mailman School of Public Health and College of Physicians and Surgeons. Donations can be made to the NY Chapter of Hadassah. Funeral will be held today, 9:30 AM, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st and Amsterdam.
