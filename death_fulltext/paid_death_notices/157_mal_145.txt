
        AYRES-C. Burgess. In Bedford, MA, formerly of Wallingford, CT, and Jaffrey, NH, February 12. Husband of Katharine (George). In lieu of flowers, gifts in his memory may be sent to the Choate Rosemary Hall School Scholarship Fund, 333 Christian Ave., Wallingford, CT 06492.
