
        MORRIS-Jean Deckinger. 90, of Flushing, NY, died February 1, 2000 at New York Hospital. Beloved wife of the late Florian, loving mother of Miriam Lastoria and husband, Michael, aunt of Susan, Alan, and Shea Meyers. She will be fondly remembered and missed by family and friends. Memorial service Thursday February 3, 2000, 10:30 A.M. at Parkside, 9860 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills, followed by interment at Mount Hope Cemetery.
