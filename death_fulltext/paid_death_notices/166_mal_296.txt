
        LeLANDAIS-William Burke, 43. Of South Egremont, MA. Died Tuesday, Nov. 21, 2000 at St. Peter's Hospital, Albany, NY following a long illness. He was born Aug. 16, 1957. Son of the late Pierre Jean LeLandais and Patricia Burke LeLandais, brother of Michelle Esposito, grandson of Jean LeLandais. Burke is the beloved husband of Sarah (Van Wagner) LeLandais of Craryville, NY. A memorial Mass will be held at Our Lady of the Valley, Sheffield, MA on Saturday, December 2, 2000 at 11 AM.
