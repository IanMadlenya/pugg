
        GOTTSCHALK-Hertha (nee Kaufmann). On January 22, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Fred. Devoted mother of Susan and Elaine. Loving sister of the late Walter Kaufmann. Cherished grandmother of Marc, David, Adam and Lisa, and a good friend to many. Services at Riverdale Temple, 246th St and Independence Ave, Riverdale, NY, 12:30 PM, Sunday, January 25, 1998. Her loving generosity, dignity and impeccable demeanor will be remembered by all who knew her. Contributions in her memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or Race for the Cure.
