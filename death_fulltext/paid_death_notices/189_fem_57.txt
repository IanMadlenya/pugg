
        NICHOLS - Nancy. The Trustees and Staff of The Museum of Modern Art note with sadness the loss of their great friend, Nancy Nichols. From her personal generosity to her hand in shaping our staff, her mark on the institution is indelible. David Rockefeller, Chairman Emeritus Agnes Gund, President Emerita Ronald S. Lauder, Chairman Robert B. Menschel, President Glenn D. Lowry, Director The Museum of Modern Art, New York
        NICHOLS-Nancy S. The City University of New York mourns the untimely passing of Nancy Nichols, managing partner of Heidrick & Struggles, beloved wife of Rodney W. Nichols, a member of the Board of The Research Foundation of The City University of New York. Her exemplary dedication to education, culture, and the arts will be long remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Matthew Goldstein, Chancellor and Chairman of the Board of The Research Foundation of The City University of New York
        NICHOLS-Nancy. The Board of Directors and the staff of the Research Foundation of the City University of New York extend their condolences to Rodney W. Nichols, a member of our board, on the untimely death of his wife, Nancy S. Nichols. May her memory be a blessing to her family and friends. Richard F. Rothbard Interim Exec Director Research Foundation/CUNY
        NICHOLS-Nancy. The Director and Board of Fellows of the Lyman Allyn Museum, New London, CT extend deep sympathy to Rod, Courtney, Lindsay, and Christopher on the sad loss of Nancy. Our friend who served the museum community thoughtfully and with distinction. Wendy Lehman Lash, Chairman
