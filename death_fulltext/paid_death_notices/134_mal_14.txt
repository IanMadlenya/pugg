
        RUBIN-Irwin S. On February 27, 1998, age 67, at home in Bear Creek, PA. A founder and former President of Trion Industries, passionate golfer and traveler, lover of life. Beloved father, grandfather, brother and friend. He is survived by his children, Jeffrey and Susan Rubin and Lita and Alan Saltzman; his grandchildren Justine, Ethan, Leigh and Kate; brother Warren Rubin; and sister Myrna Doerfler. Services Monday, March 2, 1 PM, at the Rosenberg Funeral Home, 348 South River St., Wilkes-Barre, PA. At Irwin's request, his life will be celebrated at a gathering following the services at the Woodlands Hotel. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center Fund, 622 West 168th St., New York, NY 10032.
