
        CITRON-Robert Mark. Born Newark, NJ, March 4, 1945, passed away Sunday, May 7, 2000, in his home. Survived by his mother, Hanna Citron, Pembroke Pines, FL, Michael and Rena Citron, Hartford, CT, Jonathan and Sandy Citron, Chatham, NJ, Laura and Richard Gelber, Hollywood, FL, Wendy and Panagiotis Binioris, NYC, his 11 nieces and nephews, and their friends. Memorial is planned at the Binioris residence, Tuesday May 9th from 4 to 8 P.M.
