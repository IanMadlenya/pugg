
        SALZMAN-Hal A. 93 of New York City and Miami Beach, Fl died on December 6, 2000 in Miami Beach. Mr. Salzman was a graduate of New York University. He was the owner of the Hal A. Salzman Public Relations and Advertising Firm. He and his company handled the public relations of many famous movie stars and clients. Mr. Salzman was a member of Temple Emanu-El, New York City and was a member of the New York Friars Club. Beloved husband of Irene Salzman, loving father of Judy B. Lesser, cherished grandfather of Andrea Lesser and Erik (Kate) Lesser, devoted brother of Mrs. Eve Bates and loving brother-inlaw of Mrs. Beatrice Elliott. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, December 10, 2000, 1:00 PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street at Amsterdam Avenue, New York, NY with interment to follow at Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, NJ. Memorial contributions in his memory may be made to the National Kidney Foundation at 1250 Broadway, New York, NY 10002.
        SALZMAN-Hal. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed member Friar Hal Salzman on December 6th. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held on Sunday, December 10 at 1 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street at Amsterdam. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        SALZMAN-Hal A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Hal A. Salzman. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
