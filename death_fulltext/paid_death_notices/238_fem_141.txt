
        STOCK--Eleanor Schwarz passed away at her vacation home in Sagaponack on November 22, 2006, after a long illness. She was born in Manhattan on September 15, 1924 and lived at 155 East 72 Street in Manhattan. She is survived by her husband, Richard, of 61 years, by their two children: Hilary and Alan, and by six grandchildren: Kyle and Michael Ryan, Jonah, Joe, Cal, and Nora Stock. She was a graduate of the Brearley School, Vassar College Phi Beta Kappa, and held a masters degree from Columbia University. For several years she taught at the Brearley School. She was active as a volunteer for community causes. She was a past president of the Yorkville Civic Council and the founder of the Friends of Julia Richman High School. A charming and brilliant woman, she will be missed by all who knew her. A memorial service will be announced later.
