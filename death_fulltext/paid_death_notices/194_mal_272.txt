
        CROAK-Allan. Died March 27, 2003. Beloved husband of Elizabeth. Loving father of Eileen Tucker, Kathleen Cooper & Maureen Thomas. Grandfather of six. Mr. Croak lived in Ridgeway, NJ for 32 years moving to Morristown, NJ in 1986. He was a special agent for the FBI in NYC for 25 years and Vice President of security for Manufacturers Hanover Trust in New York City for 17 years retiring in 1983. Visitation is on Sunday, 7-9 PM. Lewis Funeral Home, Moorestown, NJ. Burial Mass Monday, 11:30 AM at Our Lady of Good Counsel Church, Moorestown, NJ.
