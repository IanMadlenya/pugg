
        RADDOCK--Deborah Susan, age 64, died peacefully in her home on March 19, 2007. The cause of death was melanoma. Deborah's memory is cherished by her husband, Richard D. Raddock; her mother, Grace Charleston; her sons Daniel, Michael, and Robert; her five grandchildren; and by her many loving friends and family members. Her kindness, nurturing spirit, intelligence and sense of humor will be missed. Family and friends will gather at her home in McLean, VA, at 11 am on Friday, March 23, to share memories of Deborah. She asked that any memorial contributions be donated to the charity of your choice.
