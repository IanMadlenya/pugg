
        STEIN--Dennis. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Dennis Stein on October 23, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held Sunday, October 26, at 11 AM, Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue and 91 Street. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
