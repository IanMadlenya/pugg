
        PETTERSEN-Eleanore Kendall. Beloved friend, mentor, renowned architect, founder of the architectural firm Design Collaborative and protege of Frank Lloyd Wright died at her home in Saddle River, NJ on January 15, 2003 at the age of 86. She was the first woman to open her own architectural practice in NJ and the first female president of the NJ State Board of Architects. A memorial service will be held in early February. Contributions in memory of Eleanore Pettersen should be sent to Altrusa Club of Bergen County, 246 Woodfield Road, Township of Washington, NJ 07676. For information, contact Robert Spearing Funeral Home, Inc, 155 Kinderkamack Road, Park Ridge, New Jersey.
