
        GERBER - Evelyn C. Survived by her husband and friend of 49 years, Charles S. and her devoted and loving son, Charles Waas; his wife, the darling of her life, Silvia; her granddaughter Ashley Waas Gerber, who brought life, cheer and comfort to her through her year long illness. The daughter of the long deceased Lillian Waas and Henry Cohn, founder of the Attlee Press. Sympathy may be expressed by donations to Make a Wish Foundation. She passed away on January 2, 1997.
