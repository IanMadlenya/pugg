
        LIBMAN - Hannah (Silverman). Aged 88, on February 18, 1998. Devoted wife of the late Morris, loving mother of Jill, Kit Howard (Robert), Hillard (Elaine). Adored grandmother of Brett, Jodi (Steven), Jim, Jon (Kathleen). Greatgrandmother of Griffin and Gage. Co-founder in 1929 of Camp Hillard, Hartsdale, New York, she will be sadly missed by all who knew and loved her. Shiva will be observed at the home of Kit and Robert Howard, Sunday through Tuesday.
