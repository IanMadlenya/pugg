        LEE-John J. The employees of Tosco Corporation extend their condolences to the family of John J. Lee. John served as Tosco's President for three years from 1990 to 1993, and as a Director from 1988 to 1993. His vision, counsel, and support set the stage for Tosco's rapid growth and success. He was a leader with a rare talent to see and plan for the future while controlling day-to-day operations with style, good humor and grace. John made valuable contributions to every area of our organization. He was truly a man for all seasons. We will miss him. The Employees of Tosco
        LEE-John J. The Cancer Support Team expresses profound sorrow to our co-Founder, Gayle Lee and her family, at the loss of her beloved husband and their devoted father, John Lee.
