
        MAAS-Michael. Of Westhampton Beach, NY. On May 12, 2002 at the age of 70. Beloved husband of Jane Maas, loving father of Katherine and Jennifer Maas, adoring grandfather of Jenna Maas. Brother of the late Peter. A Fellow of the American Institute of Architects and a member of its national Board of Directors, he was former managing partner of HLW International. He graduated from Renssalaer Polytechnic Institute in 1954 and served for three years in the United States Marine Corps. Among the many projects whose design teams he led were the world headquarters for Chemical Bank, corporate headquarters for the U.S. Trust Company, and buildings for Travelers, Jaguar, the U.S. Army War College and the School of Public Health at Harvard University. He served as president of the Fifth Avenue Association, received the Silver Beaver Award from the Boy Scouts and an honorary Doctor of Laws degree from St. John's University. Funeral mass on Friday, May 17 at 10:30 A.M. at St. Thomas More, 65 East 89th Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Architectural Foundation, 1735 New York Avenue, NW, Washington, DC 20006.
