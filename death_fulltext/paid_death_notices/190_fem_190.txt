
        RAINES - Bertha Estelle Walker, age 95, died on November 23 at her home in Birmingham, Ala. She was born January 1, 1907, in Arley, Ala. Her husband, W.S. Raines, died on Oct. 6, 2002. She is survived by a daughter, Mary Jo Raines Dean of Mountain Brook, Ala.; two sons, Jerry W., of Birmingham, and Howell, of New York City; seven grandchildren; 12 great-grandchildren; and one great-great-grandson.
