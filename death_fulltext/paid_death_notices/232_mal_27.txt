
        KERNER LEIGHTON New York City Opera joins the Classical Music world in mourning the passing of our respected colleague and dear friend. As longtime classical music critic, of the Village Voice and free lance writer, he demonstrated rare and unfailing integrity and enthusiasm. His constant and passionate support of our company and of young artists, will be sorrily missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Dorita. Paul Kellogg General Director
