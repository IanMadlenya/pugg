
        TUCKER-Michael Patrick. On September 11th, 40, of Rumson, NJ was taken from his family too soon in a senseless act that changed our lives forever. He started his career in a basement of a bank to become, 12 years later, a Sr. V.P., a Partner and one of the top traders at Cantor Fitzgerald. Survived by his wife Mary Beth (nee Rozniak Tucker) and four daughters: Taylor, Gaffney, Andy and Morgan. Mother and Father: Dr. Thomas and Mrs. Joan Tucker, sisters, Susan and brotherin-law Bill Jenkins, Cathy Tucker, Maureen Tucker; brothers, John and sister-in-law Tara Tucker, Chris and sister-in-law Herta Tucker. Maternal grandmother, Dorothy Williams. Father and motherin-law; Ron and June Bulka Rozniak; brother-in-law, Ronald Rozniak; sister-in-law, Meg Chaykowsky. A memorial gathering will be held at the Navesink Country Club, Lufborrow Lane, Navesink Section of Middletown, NJ, September 30, 2001, 11-3 pm. The Memorial Mass will be held at Holy Cross Church, Rumson, NJ on Monday, October 1, 2001 at 11 am. In lieu of flowers memorial donations can be made to the Tucker Girls Education Fund, P.O. Box 46, Rumson, NJ 07760
