
        ROSENTHAL--Marvin. Age 77, died December 4. Loving husband of Elaine, devoted father of Jane Knudsen and the late Lynn Rosenthal. Father-in-law of Chris Knudsen. Devoted grandfather of Bobby, Dana and Kelley. He served in the Korean War. He was with ''The New Yorker'' magazine for 25 years prior to working as a commercial real estate broker until his death. Beloved by friends and family, he will be greatly missed. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Rogosin Institute, 505 E. 70th St, NYC 10021.
