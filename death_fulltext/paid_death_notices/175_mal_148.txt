
        BARNOUW-Erik. The Remembrance Service for Erik Barnouw, who died July 19, 2001, will be held at St. Paul's Chapel, Columbia University, on September 13, 2001 at 2:30 PM. Memorial gifts in his memory, may be made to, The Organization of American Historians, 112 North Bryan St., Bloomington, IN 47408, in support of The Erik Barnouw Award.
