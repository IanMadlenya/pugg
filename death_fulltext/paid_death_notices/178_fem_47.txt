
        DAVIS-Jean. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins the staff and volunteers of the Jeanette and Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Department in acknowledging with sadness the passing of Jean Davis, a devoted member of the Volunteer Service Board for over 10 years. To her family, and to all who loved her, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Matthew E. Fink. M.D., President and CEO, Beth Israel Theresa Rizzo-Williams, Director, Jeanette and Morris Kessel Volunteer Services Gladys Messenger, President, Volunteer Service Board
