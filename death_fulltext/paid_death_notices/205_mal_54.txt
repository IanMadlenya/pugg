
        RAISKIN--Samuel M. Age 81, of Boca Raton, Florida. Died February 1, 2004 in his son's home in Brandon, Florida, surrounded by his family. He is survived by his wife Elayne (Penny), brother Rabbi Gerald Raiskin and his three sons Jonathan, Richard and David. A devoted and extraordinary husband and father. Cherished grandfather of Sarah, Spencer, Maxwell and Alex. His memory will be cherished by his entire family, friends and all that he touched with his kindness and love. May God bless him forever.
