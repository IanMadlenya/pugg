
        COLLINS-Daniel Gerard. Died on June 16, 2002, at age 72 after a short illness. A memorial service in his honor will be held on Friday, Sept. 20, at 11 A.M. at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, New York City. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Daniel G. Collins Memorial Fund, C/O The Development Office, NYU School of Law, 161 Avenue of the Americas, New York, New York 10013.
