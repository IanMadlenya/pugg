
        USADI-May Permut, 83. Died Wednesday, September 25, 2002. She was the wife of the late Samuel Usadi for 57 years, mother of Deborah Nagler and Robert Usadi, grandmother of Benjamin Usadi, Daniel Nagler, Douglas Usadi, and Jennifer Nagler and sister of Sylvia Waltzer and Irving Permut. She and her family were proud of her strength and independence. Services and burial at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, N.Y. at 2PM on Friday, September 27, 2002. Contributions in her memory to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, N.Y. 11590, would be welcome.
