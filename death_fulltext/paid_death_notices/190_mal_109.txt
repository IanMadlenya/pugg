
        COALE-Ansley J. William Church Osborne Professor of Political Affairs Emeritus and former director of The Office of Population Research, on November 5, 2002, at Pennswood Village, Newtown, PA. He is survived by his wife Sarah and two sons, Ansley Jr. and Robert; also by three grandchildren. Memorial Service will be held in Dodds Auditorium at the Woodrow Wilson School, Princeton University, on Saturday, December 14, at 2 PM.
