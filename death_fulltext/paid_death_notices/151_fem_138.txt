
        WAGNER-Edna Jones Teller. Age 82. August 13, 1999. Wife of Victor H. Wagner, mother of Wazir David Teller and Jane Teller, stepmother of Edriecote and Irene Wagner, grandmother of Nathan Walter. Barnard College class of 1938. Retired math teacher of New Lincoln School. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Joyce Theater. Party to be held on her birthday, November 7, 1999.
