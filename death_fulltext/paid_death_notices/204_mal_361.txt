
        HIRSH--Kurt, devoted husband of Margaret, beloved father of Helen and Richard, cherished and adored grandfather of Daniel, Evelyn, Stephen, and David, trusted friend and advisor, passed away on January 26 at age 86. Kurt enjoyed an active retirement from a long career, having successfully established a family and business in America. Kurt will be remembered for his kind and generous spirit, his caring friendship, and a legacy of entrepreneurship and perseverance that lives on in his children and grandchildren. Donations to be sent in his memory to the Lung Association. Services Thursday 1pm at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY.
