
        GLENN-Gerard D., 74. Of Park Ridge, NJ on Tuesday, October 8, 2002. Beloved husband of Margaret (nee Bock). Devoted father of Thomas Glenn of No. Haledon, NJ. Dear brother of Floria Dignam of Asbury Park, NJ. Funeral Mass 9:30 Thursday at Our Lady of Mercy R.C. Church, Park Ridge, NJ. Visiting 2-4 and 7-9PM Wednesday at the Robert Spearing Funeral Home, Inc., 155 Kinderkamack Road, Park Ridge, NJ. Interment George Washington Memorial Park Paramus, NJ. In lieu of flowers kindly make donations to Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, P.O. Box 27106, NY, NY 10087. Before retiring, Gerard was a Printer for Virgil Mountain Press, NYC for many years. He was a former Governor of the NY Athletic Club, NYC and an 8 time winner of the National 3-Cushion Billiard Championship. He was a U.S. Army Veteran of World War II.
