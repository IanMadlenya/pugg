
        CLARK--Joseph P., on January 26. Beloved husband of Marian. Father of Joseph and John. Graduate of Regis H.S., Rensselear Polytechnic Institute (RPI), NYU Graduate School of Business Administration. He fought with the 89th Inf. Div. in World War II through France and Germany. Participated in the liberation of Ohrdruff Concentration Camp. Awarded Bronze Star for Valor. Served 30 years in the Army Reserve, retiring as a full colonel. Active in alumni activities at Regis and RPI. Former trustee and council member St. Jean Baptiste, Knight Commander Holy Sepulchre. Member of AUSA, New York Athletic Club, 89th Div. Assoc., and American Legion. A life of service, kind and charitable in word and deed. Always available to lend a hand, admired, respected and loved by his friends and relatives. Visiting Friday, 2-5, 7-9, at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave. at 81st St. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday, 10:30 AM, at St. Jean Baptiste. Interment Arlington National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to St. Francis Bread Line, 135 West 31st St., NYC 10001.
