
        HOWLAND - Jane Murdoch. Passed away peacefully at Mary Manning Walsh on February 23, 2002. Loving mother of Louisa Howland Miller and Grafton Dulany Howland and grandmother of Kate Howland Miller. Her daughter Kathy Murdoch Howland predeceased her. In her youth, she organized the Dolphin Dances at the Plaza Hotel and subsequently represented artists through Dolphin Enterprises. A docent at the Metropolitan Museum for many years, she helped many people and will be missed by her family and many friends. ''Psalm 23.'' In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Mary Manning Walsh, 1339 York Ave., NY, NY 10021.
