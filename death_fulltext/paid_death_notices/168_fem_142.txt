
        POST-Hariet Bottomley. Died peacefully on January 12, 2001, at Wake Robin in Shelburne, VT. The family mourns her loss and will hold services at Trinity Episcopal Church in Shelburne at 11 AM on Monday, January 15. A memorial service will be held at St. John's Church in Cold Spring Harbor, NY in spring. Mrs. Post's first husband, Shepherd Smith, died in 1955, and she married Edward Everett Post in 1962. She is survived by her husband, two sisters Susan B. Chambers and Virginia B. Colyer, her nine children, seven grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.
