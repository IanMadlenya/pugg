
        MENACHEM-Pearl Herbert, 89, of Sunny Isles, Florida, passed away April 24, 2003. Her beloved husband, Louis Menachem and son, Neil Menachem preceded her in death. Pearl is survived by her brother, Marvin Herbert, daughter - in - law, Barbara Menachem, grandchildren, Andrew, Fred, Seth Menachem and Mara MenachemKarlinsky, great-grandchildren, Jackson Menachem and Neil Karlinsky and dear friend and caretaker Wilna DesHommes. Graveside services will be held 11:00 AM Sunday, April 27, 2003 at Knollwood Park Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY.
