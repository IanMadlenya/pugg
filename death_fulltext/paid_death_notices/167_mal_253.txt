
        GOETZ-Robert Hans, MD. Retired Professor of Surgery at Albert Einstein College of Medicine, Pioneer of the Coronary Bypass Operation and Intra-aortic Balloon Pump, passed away at his home in Scarsdale, NY December 15, 2000 at the age of 90 after a battle with pancreatic cancer. Devoted husband to Dr. Verena M. Goetz, loving father to Sylvia Perle of Detroit, MI, Stephen Goetz of Los Altos Hills, CA, Lionel Goetz of Fairfield, CT, and Angela Goetz of Manhattan, NY; grandfather to Mark, Katie, Lisa, Claudia, Lionel, Kelly, Michael, and Brendan; and great grandfather to Gene Odell. He will be deeply missed by family and friends. A memorial event to celebrate his extraordinary life will be held at the Community Unitarian Church, 468 Rosedale Ave., White Plains, NY at 4:00PM on Thursday, December 21st.
