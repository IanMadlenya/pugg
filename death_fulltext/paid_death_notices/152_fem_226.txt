
        TIERNEY-Alice ''Mickey'' (nee Herchenroder) of Surfside Beach, S.C., passed away September 25, 1999. Formerly from New York and was retired from the Surety Association of America. Survived by brother Francis Herchenroder, sisters Anne Milligan & Fleurette Topalow. Predeceased by her husband Robert H. Tierney. Memorials to Leukemia Society.
