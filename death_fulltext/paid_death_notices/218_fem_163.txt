
        CANTOR -- Ysobl J. (nee Stone). Died in Teaneck, NJ, March 18, 2005, at age 93. She was the beloved widow of Herman, to whom she was married for over 50 years. She leaves her sons Gerry (Dorothy) of Westfield, Bill (Paula) of Woodcliff Lake, and Howard (Pamela) of Ramsey. She was loved and respected by her 12 grandchildren and 15 great-grandchildren. Services are to be held at Gutterman-Musicant, Hackensack, NJ, at 10:30 am, Sunday. Shiva will be at the family homes in Woodcliff Lake on Sunday and Monday, Westfield on Tuesday and Wednesday, and Ramsey on Thursday.
