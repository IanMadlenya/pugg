
        KATZ-John Zaduck. The Board of Directors and staff of the Mount Pleasant Cemetery of Hawthorne, NY, mourn the loss of our former longtime director. John Katz served with distinction for 28 years, significantly contributing to the Cemetery's preservation, financial strength, and beauty. We will not forget his sound judgement and gentle manner. Our condolences are extended to his wife Dorothy, his son John Andrew, and the family. Louis Sapir, Chairman Bruce Feld, President
