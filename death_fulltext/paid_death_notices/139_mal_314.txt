
        COHEN-Ezra. On Aug. 26, 1998. Born Brooklyn, NY May 4, 1919. Loving husband of Ray. Devoted father of Barbara, Marilyn, Jacqueline and Susan. Doting grandfather of 14 grandchildren. Also survived by 7 brothers and sisters. Formerly vice president of the Sunweave Linen Corp. Active supporter of the UJA. He will surely be missed by all who he touched. He was a man who believed that actions speak louder than words. He lived his whole life by this.
        COHEN-Ezra. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun, its Sisterhood and Couples Club mourn the passing of the beloved father of their devoted member, Jackie Marcus, and extend heartfelt condolences to all members of the bereaved family. Rabbi Haskel Lookstein Fred Distenfeld, President
