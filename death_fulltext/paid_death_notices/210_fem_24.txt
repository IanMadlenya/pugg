
        EISENBERG--Leni. On July 5th, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Sigg Eisenberg; loving mother of Fred (Tamara) and Peter (Gail); adoring grandmother of Michele (Buddy), Jason, Karen and Jascha and great-grandmother of Zachary; loving sister of Regina Silbiger who was her best friend. Leni lived her life with great dignity and strength guided by old world values and traditions. Services will be held on Wednesday, July 7th at 1PM, Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
        EISENBERG--Leni. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our devoted member, Leni Eisenberg, beloved mother of Peter and Gail, and Fred and Tamara. To them and the entire family our deepest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
