
        ALEXANDER - William. (a.k.a. Alexander Levy) Retired architect, patron of art and music, resident of Los Angeles, died June 2, 1997. Devoted and loving brother to surviving sisters Deborah Frey, Rose Goldberg, and Benjamin. A special uncle to many nieces, nephews, their children and grandchildren and to so many people. Pioneered to build Richard Haliburton home in 1937 and many more successful accomplishments in his profession. Memorial service in Los Angeles being planned.
