
        RETTINGER-Ginny, 93. Died November 26, 2001. Interred on November 27 at the New Montefiore Cemetery next to her husband of 68 years, David (March 3, 1997). A graveside service for immediate family was held. Ginny leaves behind her children Martin and Jill, Phyllis and David, Diane and Harv; nine grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. She will always be loved and missed. Donations in her memory to any charity of your choice educating inner-city children.
