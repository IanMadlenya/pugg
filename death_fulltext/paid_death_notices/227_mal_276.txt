
        BERNSTEIN--Alfred Paul. On December 26 after a courageous struggle with Parkinson's disease. Beloved brother of Pearl Kay, devoted uncle of Andrew Kay, and cherished companion of the late Robert Garrett. A gentleman of integrity, generosity and intelligence, Paul was an astute businessman and a gracious host. His goodness will be missed by his family, friends and former business associates. Services Wednesday, 12:45 PM, The Riverside, 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
