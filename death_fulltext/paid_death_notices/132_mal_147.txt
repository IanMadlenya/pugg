
        FISHBEIN-Eli. AMIT is deeply grieved by the sudden passing of the beloved husband of our devoted Regional Vice President, Myrna Fishbein. A person of warmth and compassion, Eli Fishbein contributed so much to the furtherance of the AMIT Mission of Saving the forgotten Children of Israel. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his dear wife Myrna and to his children Sarah and Steven Epstein, Elisa and Clint Greenbaum and Kalman and Susie Fishbein and the entire family. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Director
