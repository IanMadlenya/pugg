
        AMORY--Harcourt, 82. Of Vero Beach, FL. Formerly of New York City and Pound Ridge, NY, March 1, 2006. Survived by his devoted wife, Jean Moore Amory, and their children Dita Amory Nickson, Jean Trudeau Fenn, Garry Trudeau, Michelle Trudeau and six grandchildren. Previously married to Dita Naylor-Leyland. Born in Beverly, MA. He attended Brooks School and Harvard University. Served in the American Field Service, British Eighth Army, and OSS during WWII. Services on Saturday, March 11, 2006, 11 A.M. at Saint Mark's Anglican Church in Vero Beach, FL. Contributions may be sent to the Indian River Memorial Hospital Foundation, 1000 36th Street, Vero Beach, Florida 32960, or Saint Mark's Anglican Church, P.O. Box 6994, Vero Beach, Florida 32961.
