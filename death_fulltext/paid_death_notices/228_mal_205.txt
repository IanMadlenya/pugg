
        SMITH--Paul M., III. 60, died January 13 in Rancho Mirage, CA. Born in Chicago, former resident of Sharon, CT. A Georgetown graduate, he worked many years for J.P. Morgan and then founded Smith McDonnell, a private investment company. Memorial services to be announced in Rancho Mirage and Sharon. Contributions appreciated to Hotchkiss Library, Sharon, CT and Little Guild of St. Francis, West Cornwall, CT.
