
        IMMERMAN--William, ''Velvel'', age 87, of Brooklyn, and lately of West Palm Beach, FL. Died May 13, 2004. Husband of the late Anita Baron Immerman. Companion of Arline Warrence. Father of Marc, Arlene & Bob. Grandfather of nine. We will miss his wit, poetry, talent, and charm. Memorials Sunday May 16, 2PM at 4714 Okeechobee Blvd., W. Palm Beach; Tuesday May 18, 2PM, 715 Marrietta Rd., Ambler, PA.
