
        VAN DINE-Isabel. November 27, 2002. Beloved wife of Vance Van Dine. Loving mother of the late Rose M. Van Dine. Devoted sister of Thomas, Samuel and Richard Brewster. Loving aunt of numerous nieces and nephews. Born in NYC, and a graduate of Miss Hall's School, and Smith College Class of 1954. She was a dedicated volunteer at both the Children's Storefront as well as the American Museum of Natural History. Service Thursday, December 5, 10:30 AM, at St. Thomas Episcopal Church, 5th Ave at 53rd St. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Children's Storefront or the American Museum of Natural History.
