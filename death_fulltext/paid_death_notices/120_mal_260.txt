
        LIPPINCOTT-Job H. On January 21, 1997 at the Cape Cod Rehabilitation Hospital in Sandwich, MA. Age 86. Formerly of Chatham, N.J. and Nantucket, MA, he leaves his wife Rosemond, daughter Sara, son Job, two grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Mr. Lippincott, a graduate of Princeton University and Harvard Law School, was a retired partner of the New Jersey law firm of Stryker, Tams & Dill, and a well-known Nantucket artist whose works are in many private collections. The funeral will be held Saturday, Our Lady of Victory, Centerville, MA.
