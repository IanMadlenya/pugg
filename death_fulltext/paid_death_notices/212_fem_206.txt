
        MARKELL--Marcia R. Died September 27, 2004. Born January 9, 1928 in Boston. Wife of Elliot. Mother of Jonathan, Jane, Alyson and the late David Markell. Grandmother of David, James and Emily. Graveside service at Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY, on Wednesday at 1 PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society, 1311 Mamaroneck Ave., White Plains, NY 10605.
