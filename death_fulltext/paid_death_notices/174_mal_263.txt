
        FRANSIOLI-Thomas Adrian, III. Of Wenham, MA, died Monday. Born in NY on March 17, 1933, he was the son of the late Thomas and Elizabeth McVickar Fransioli. A graduate of Harvard 1955, and Harvard Business School 1960, he joined the Bank of Boston, specializing in international banking in South America, Europe and Asia, and later with a venture capital group in Boston. He is survived by his wife Mona, 2 daughters, Lena and Christina, & a granddaughter, Zoe. Memorial service will be held on Friday, August 3, at Christ Church, Hamilton, MA. at 10 AM.
