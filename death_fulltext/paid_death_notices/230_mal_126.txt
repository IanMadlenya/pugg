
        ALTER--Robert H., 77. On March 10, 2006. Founder, Past President and CEO of Cabletelevision Advertising Bureau, founder and past President of Temple Beth Shalom, Member of the Broadcasting and Cable Hall of Fame since 2003. He was a resident of Hastings - on - Hudson and Martha's Vineyard. He served on many community and professional boards. Survived by his beloved wife, Lucile, cherished daughters Deborah Alter, Amy Alter Jeffereis and Midge Alter Caparosa, five adored grandchildren, his mother-in-law Sylvia Levine. Predeceased by daughter Maureen Baron. Funeral at 11 A.M. Sunday Edwards - Dowdle Funeral Home, Dobbs Ferry, NY (Saw Mill Exit 17). Memorials to ALS Foundation, Temple Beth Shalom or Young Adult Institute, NY, NY.
