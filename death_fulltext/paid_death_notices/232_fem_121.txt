
        REISLER--Harriet. On May 18th, 2006. Beloved wife of the late New York Supreme Court Justice Ray Reisler, Loving mother of Raymond Frank and Nancy, proud grandmother of Karen, Zachary and Michael and greatgrandmother of Samantha and Allison. Master's degree in English from Adelphi University With a seminal thesis on Walt Whitman. A gracious, elegant woman who treated everyone with a friendliness and kindness that reflected her joie de vivre.
