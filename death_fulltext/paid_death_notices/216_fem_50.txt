
        GROBERG -- Marcia Black. Age 73, beloved mother of Neil, Richard and Eric, and sister of Susan Greenfield, died unexpectedly on January 5, 2005. She was the mother-in-law of Ellen Wolfson, M.D., and Aileen Groberg, and the grandmother of Rebecca, Daniel, Lhara and Delainey Groberg. She was a graduate of Midwood High School in Brooklyn where she was born, and she attended the University of New Hampshire and the University of Pennsylvannia before graduating from NY University. Funeral is today at 11:15 at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be sent to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation.
        GROBERG--Marcia. A great mom to her devoted sons Neil, Richard and Eric, a dear sister and a loving aunt. A special, beautiful, courageous, bright and witty person. You will be sadly missed. Sue and George Greenfield, Gary and Lori, Ellen and Jonathan
