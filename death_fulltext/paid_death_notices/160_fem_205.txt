
        PAYMER-Natalie M. Beloved wife of Edwin. Devoted mother of Joel and Douglas. Dear mother-inlaw of Sheryl and Amy. Cherished grandmother of Heather, Jason and Sarah. Loving sister and sister-in-law of Martin, Libby and Marcella. Devoted daughter of Beatrice and the late Joseph Gamso. Also remembered as a dear aunt, relative and friend. She was the Executive Secretary of the NY Guild of Photographic Dealers from 1971 to 1998. Donations in her memory can be made to the American Cancer Society or the Alzheimer's Association. Services Sunday, May 28, at 11:30 AM, at Gutterman's Chapel, Rockville Centre, NY.
