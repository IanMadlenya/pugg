
        DENVIR-William Cremin, age 75, of Seabrook Island, SC, formerly of New York, died on Saturday, January 11, 1997 at his residence. He was the son of the late James Peter Denvir and Grace Cremin Denvir. Mr. Denvir was an interior designer associated with B. Altman's, NY. Services will be held today in SC, with burial in New Haven, CT. Information, McAlister Funeral Home, Charleston, SC.
