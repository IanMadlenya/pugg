
        SCALA--Rose E., age 97, died Wednesday, February 16, 2005 at the Osborn Nursing Home in Rye, NY. Ms. Scala served as Principal of the Rose E. Scala School in the Bronx, NY from 1952 through 1972, previously known as PS 71. She was also a past President of the Catholic Teachers Association of the Archdiocese of New York. She is survived by her brother Anthony J. Scala Sr. of Harrison, New York and many nieces and nephews. She was predeceased by her brothers Arthur and Joseph and sisters Clotilde and Margaret. Visiting hours will be held on Friday from 2-4 and 79 at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd, Rye, NY. Mass of Christian Burial at 10AM Saturday at the Church of Resurrection in Rye, New York. Interment at Calvary Cemetery.
