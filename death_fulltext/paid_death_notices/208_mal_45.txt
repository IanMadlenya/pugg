
        GREENBERG--Michael. The faculty and staff of the NYU Cancer Institute record with sorrow the untimely death of Michael Greenberg, son of their beloved benefactor, Arnold Greenberg and his late wife Marilyn, and stepson of Roberta Greenberg, a tireless supporter of the Institute's activities. Our thoughts and prayers are with his family at this difficult time. Steven J. Burakoff, M.D., Director Rosemary W. Mackey, Exec. Dir. for Administration
