
        AMBROSE--John P., Jr. Of Annapolis, MD, formerly of Greenwich, CT, on June 4, 2004, after a valiant battle with pancreatic cancer, surrounded by his family. Loving husband to Lisa. Beloved father of John, Terese, Donna, Virginia, Anthony, Edith, Catherine, James, Meddaugh and Christopher. Adored grandfather of 30 grandchildren. Beloved brother of Aileen, Edith and Margaret Mary. Cherished brother-in-law of Michael McGrail. He served in the US Army with the 82nd Airborne Division. Businessman, yachtsman, salesman par excellence. Parishioner of St. Mary's Church in Annapolis. Benefactor to St. Mary's School and Dematha High school. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the John P. Ambrose Jr. Memorial Foundation.
