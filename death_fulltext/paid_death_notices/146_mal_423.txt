
        BULOVAS-Rev. Msgr. Frank John. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of Transfiguration Church, 64-14 Clinton Avenue, Maspeth, on March 19. Reposing at Kodis Funeral Home, 63-03 Flushing Avenue, Flushing, Tuesday, March 23, from 2 to 5 PM. Mass of Transferral Tuesday, March 23, at 7:30 PM in Transfiguration Church. Funeral Mass Wednesday, March 24, at Transfiguration Church, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
