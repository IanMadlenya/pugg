
        RAUKIND-Morty. A man of many virtues and triumphs. Born in Dallas Texas, May 29th, 1934. It was my joy to have been his friend and lover for over 30 years. Morty will be missed by all who knew him, family, friends and business associates. Morty was a special man with great compassion and forthright integrity. Loving father to Lou and Leslye, grandfather to Matthew and Jordyn. Loving son to Nell and brother to Barbara and Will. Loving uncle.
