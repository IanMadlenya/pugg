
        ENGEL--Walter F., 51, of Englewood, NJ, on Palm Sunday, April 1, 2007, after living a beautiful life full of love and music. Beloved son of Lucia and Herbert. Adored brother of Richard, William, Frank, Herbert Jr., Lucie, Mary, Elizabeth and Hans. Former husband of Jill Sagarin. Cherished uncle, friend, musician, composer, painter, and poet. He was an emblem of compassion, peace and love. Funeral Mass Wednesday April 4 at 1pm at Mt. Carmel Church, Ridgewood, NJ. Private burial. Omit flowers, donations to the Walter Engel FYP, Unitarian Society of Ridgewood, care of Dorothy Neff, 349 Graydon Terrace, Ridgewood, NJ 07450 are welcome. Services by the Trinka-Faustini Funeral Service, Maywood, NJ 201-487-3050.
