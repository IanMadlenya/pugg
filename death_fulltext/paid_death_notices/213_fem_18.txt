
        RENAUD -- Gertrude Yang, 78, passed away on September 29. Lover of Manhattan, she attended the Dalton School and Bennington College. She studied ballet under George Ballanchine and worked as an artist at Vogue Magazine. She was also a talented painter and photographer. She is survived by her daughters Jill Renaud Roosevelt and Katherine Koo, her mother Von Sung Yang, her sisters Lucy, Alvie and Annie, her brothers George and John Yang, and her grandson Nicholas Koo Weissman. She was deeply loved by her family and will be truly missed by all who knew her.
