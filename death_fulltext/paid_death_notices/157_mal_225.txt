
        BURTON-Maurice. Born December 15, 1912 in Warsaw, Poland. Passed away in Ft. Lauderdale, FL, February 17. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his wife Anna, his sons Philip and Robert, and grandchildren Emanuelle and Joshua. He was a Holocaust survivor who came the to the United States and built a new life. He always fought to overcome obstacles and loved life. He will be missed.
