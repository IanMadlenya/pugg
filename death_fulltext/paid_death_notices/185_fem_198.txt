
        BENJAMIN - Eileen. The Board of Directors and tenants of 1155 Park Avenue mourn the loss of our friend, neighbor and longtime resident, Eileen ''Nicky'' Benjamin. While serving on the Board for many years, Nicky was held in high regard for her intelligence and constructive suggestions. Always gracious and elegant, Nicky will be missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her sons, Robert and Bruce, their families and to her sister, Peggy Dubois.
        BENJAMIN-Eileen Kellner. On June 26, 2002 in New York City. Beloved wife of the late Edward, loving mother of Robert and Bruce. Cherished grandmother of Sarah, Susannah and Marguerite. Devoted mother-in-law of Ellenore and Candy. Dear sister of Peggy Ann Dubois. Services private. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Damon Runyon Cancer Research Foundation, 675 Third Ave, NY, N.Y. 10017.
