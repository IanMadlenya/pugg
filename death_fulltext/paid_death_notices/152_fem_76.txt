
        HALLENBORG-Janet, 62, of New York City, died August 21, 1999 after a ten month battle with appendicile cancer. She attended Bennington College and graduated New York University with a B.S. in Art Education. A jewelry designer, flamenco dancer and world traveler, she leaves a mother in Hawaii, numerous devoted friends, her colleagues on 47th St., and her beloved Yorkie, whose care goes to her longtime friend and companion, David. She will always be remembered for her infectious spirit. There will be a memorial gathering in late November. Donations may be made to Cabrini Hospice, 227 E. 19th St., N.Y., NY 10003.
