
        KAUFMAN-Leonard, Dr. Of Croton-On-Hudson and Hillsdale, New York. Died on Monday, July 19, 1999 at Phelps Memorial Hospital. Dr. Kaufman was in practice in Ossining, New York, and served as the Medical Director of Skyview Nursing Home and National Patent Corporation. Dr. Kaufman is survived by his wife of 45 years Joy, his children Randy Hustvedt and Peter Kaufman, his daughterin-law and son-in-law, Ann Waterfall and Eric Hustvedt, and his grandchildren Emma and Willa Kaufman. A Memorial Service will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Phelps Memorial Hospital Center in Sleepy Hollow, New York.
