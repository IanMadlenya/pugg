
        BORSDORFF--William Berle. Of Juno Beach, Florida died on February 10, 2004 at the age of 88. He was a graduate and benefactor of the Kent School, Kent, CT., Dartmouth College and the Amos Tuck School. He was awarded the U.S. Navy Silver Star and French Croix de Guerre with Bronze Star for PT boat service during WWII. A 30 year career with Johnson & Johnson culminated with his Presidency of Jelco Laboratories International. Married to Eleanor A. Linney in 1973, she predeceased him. Surviving are two stepsons, William Linney and James Linney; three nieces, Emily Kane, Dorothy Anderson and Mary Everett. A Memorial Service will be held at the Church of the Good Shepherd, Tequesta, Florida on Friday, February 20, 2004 at 10:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations to: Hospice by the Sea, Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, Florida or the Church of the Good Shepard.
