
        GOLDENBERG -- Bernard. August 12, 1912--August 9, 2005. Born in NYC, a graduate of Boys High School and CCNY, a veteran of World War II, and a dedicated teacher of mathematics at Erasmus Hall High School. He is survived by Irene, his beloved wife of 67 years, his children Robert (Nina Wacholder) and Laura (Terry Rustin) and his grandchildren Alex, Rachel, Shifra and Jacob. Donations can be sent to Alzheimer's Foundation of America, 322 8th Ave, 6th fl, NYC 10001
