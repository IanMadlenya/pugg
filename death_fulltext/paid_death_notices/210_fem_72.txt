
        HARPER--Lynn. On July 11, 2004. She is survived by husband Bruce, parents Rose and Charles Vialotti, and brother Charles Vialotti. She graduated from Marymont School, Smith College (summa cum laude, Phi Beta Kappa). She studied drama with Lee Strassberg, Harold Clurman and Stella Adler, among others but ultimately spent her brief adult life working for Kosen-Rufu (World Peace) with SGI-USA, a Buddhist organization. Family will receive friends Wednesday, 6-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Funeral service Thursday, 7 PM, at Frank E. Campbell. Private entombment to take place on Friday at Southampton Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to the Sr. Patricia Lynch Cancer Center at Holy Name Hospital, 718 Teaneck Road, Teaneck, N.J. 07666.
