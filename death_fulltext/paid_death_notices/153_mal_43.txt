
        ADAMS-David George. Born May 25, 1906. Devoted his life to working for the Episcopal Church for which he became the Comptroller of the Diocese of New York. He passed away after an extended illness. We will all miss his gentle warmth, goodness, and the Irish twinkle in his eyes. There will be a graveside service October 9, 11AM, at Christ Episcopal Church, Bronxville, N.Y.
