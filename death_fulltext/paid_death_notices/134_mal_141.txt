
        BROWNELL-George R., 83. An international corporate attorney and lifelong resident of Westfield, NJ, died March 8, 1998. A graduate of Cornell Law School, Mr. Brownell was a partner of Mudge Rose Guthrie and Alexander, NYC until 1965. Thereafter he served as special counsel and Vice President of the Legal Department at D. K. Ludwig's National Bulk Carriers. He currently served as a director of Intercat, Inc., Alfred Karcher, Inc. and Brady-Palmer Label Corp.
