
        FEYER-George, October 21, 2001 in New York City. A devoted husband of the late Judith Hoffmann Feyer (d. 1982) and of Marta Kleyman Feyer. Loving father of Robert P. Feyer and father-in-law of Marsha N. Cohen. Delighted and proud grandfather of Daniel J.C. Feyer, Stephen L.C. Feyer & Jonathan C. Feyer. An internationally acclaimed pianist, nightclub entertainer and recording artist, he brought pleasure to countless thousands of his fans with his unique style of piano and vocal entertainment. A caring and generous man, he also gave of his talents to patients at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital and other health care institutions. Private funeral services were held in San Francisco, California. A memorial to remember his life will be held at 1:00PM, Saturday, Nov. 3, 2001 at the Feszek Club, 344 E. 69th St. In lieu of flowers, the family requests contributions in his memory be made to Princeton University (P.O. Box 140, Princeton, NJ 08544) or to your favorite charity. For information, call 415-931-2201.
