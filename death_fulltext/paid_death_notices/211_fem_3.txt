
        MORI--Patricia Marguerita, passed away July 24, 2004. Cherished by her son Richard K. Bienen, grandchildren Isabelle and Ruby and sisters Pamela, Dina and Linda. Patricia was renowned in the fashion world as a high fashion model and longtime assistant to Halston. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Wednesday, August 4, at 11:00 AM.
