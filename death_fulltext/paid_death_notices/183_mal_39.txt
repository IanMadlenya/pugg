
        BROWN-Ernest H. ''Tony'', 61. Of Greenwich died on Monday, April 1. He was a loving & proud father, a loyal friend, and a devoted humanitarian. Son of Ernest and Harriet, brother of Bill and Rick, father of Adam, Ashley, and Evan. Services at First Presbyterian Church in Greenwich, CT on Saturday April 6, at 11:00 AM. Donations may be made to Kids in Crisis of Greenwich, CT. (203-622-6556).
