
        YELIN-Dr. Gabriel. Died Jan. 24 in San Francisco at 89. Formerly of South Orange, N.J., and Montreal. Devoted husband of the late Jeanette; beloved father of Louise and Edward; dear father-in-law of Robert Friedman and Abby Snay; adored grandfather of Jenny, Benjamin & William. Retired Chief of Pathology at Newark Beth Israel. His family celebrates his life and mourns his passing.
