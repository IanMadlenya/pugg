
        KRETCHMER--Alma Massetti, 58, born in Brescia, Italy, died January 10, 2007 in Palenville, New York, after a lengthy battle with breast cancer. She lived with a passion for life and beauty. She was a very special, courageous, and dynamic person, a woman of vision and valor. She worked together with her late husband, Steven Kretchmer, at Steven Kretchmer Design, and helped develop this internationally renowned jewelry design firm into a leader in the industry. She is survived by her beloved daughter, Claudia. A fund through the Dana-Farber Cancer Institute is being established in Alma's memory. Please check www.stevenkretchmer.com in the coming weeks for details.
