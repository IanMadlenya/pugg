
        BLOSVERN-Ellen Jane. An NYC teacher, succumbed Saturday, April 5. She was 50 years old. Her parents, Monroe and Miriam Blosvern, brother Peter and daughter Robin survive her. Burial will be at the Mt. Mariah Cemetery in Fairview, NJ, following services at the Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76th St, NYC, at noon, Tuesday, April 8. Shiva will be observed at 144 Clove Road, New Rochelle, NY. Contributions to Sloan-Kettering Cancer Fund.
