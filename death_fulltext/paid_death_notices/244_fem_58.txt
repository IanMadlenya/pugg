
        ESCOTT--Mary Ryan. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff, Students, Parents and Alumnae of Convent of the Sacred Heart New York City deeply mourn the passing of an exceptional colleague, dedicated Alumna and treasured friend. A valiant woman with a vibrant faith and a devoted Child of the Sacred Heart, the Sacred Heart community will cherish the memory of MiMi's loving spirit and remember her entire family in perpetual prayer.
        $(6$)ESCOTT--Mary R., on Monday May 7. Loving mother of Paul Anthony Escott and Michael James Escott. Beloved spouse of the late Michael Escott. Cherished sister of Jamie Ryan Gans, Nancy Ryan Beha and James A. Beha. Devoted aunt to nine nieces and nephews and seven greatnieces and great-nephews. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street on Wednesday and Thursday 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Mass of Christian Burial at the Church of St. Ignatius, 980 Park Avenue, on Friday 10 am.
