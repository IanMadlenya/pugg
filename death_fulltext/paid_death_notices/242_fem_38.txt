
        COHEN-PHILLIPS--Janice H., 75, of Somers, passed away March 2nd, 2007 after a courageous battle with lung cancer. A devoted wife of Lee Phillips and Gerald Cohen, she was also cherished by her children, Karen and Michael Levine and Richard Phillips. Adored by her grandchildren Matthew and Andrew Phillips and sisters-in-law Phyllis Roth and Estelle Lane. Janice will also be greatly missed by her nieces, nephews, grandnephews and grandnieces. Loved and respected by all who knew her, she will always be remembered as a best friend to many, a person of incredible generosity, humor and strength. Janice enriched the lives of many through her years of teaching, volunteering and ended her life with the same strength and dignity as she lived it.
