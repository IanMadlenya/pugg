
        DERASKA-Norma Nasso. 62 of Rumson, died at home on Saturday. Mrs. Deraska was a Library Associate for Brookdale Community College for 22 years. Born in Brooklyn, NY, she lived in Matawan for 11 years before moving to Rumson 20 years ago. Survived by her husband Donald J. Sr., son Dr. Donald J. of Winchester, MA, daugher Diane Deraska Malinowski and 3 grandchildren. Visitation Tuesday 2-4pm and 7-9pm at the Holmdel Funeral Home, 26 South Holmdel Rd, Holmdel, NJ. Funeral mass Wednesday 10am at Holy Cross Church, Rumson, NJ. Cremation private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Brookdale Community College, Library Division, attention David Murray, 765 Newman Springs Rd, Lincroft, NJ 07738 or Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, New York, NY.
