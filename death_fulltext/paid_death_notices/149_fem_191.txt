
        MISHLER-Helen. Died Friday, June 18, 1999 surrounded by her loved ones. Beloved wife of U.S. District Judge Jacob Mishler. She is survived by her daughter Gail and son-in-law Marty Unger; her son Bruce; daughter Sue and sonin-law Chuck Lubitz; Alan Mishler and Fran Mishler. Devoted grandmother to Sharyn, Debra and Rachel. Great grandmother to Tayler, Morgan, Ethan and Jagger. Her luminous, gracious and loving presence will be missed.
