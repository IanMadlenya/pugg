
        FELDBLUM--Selene. April 18, 2005. Devoted wife of the late Philip Feldblum and the late David Rosenberg. Mother of David and James Rosenberg. Mother-in-law of Deirdre Rosenberg and Janet Bly. Loving grandmother of Christopher, John, David and Katherine and great-grandmother of Olivia and Julia. She was a buyer at B. Altman & Co. in the 1940's. She was the founder and first Executive Director of the Senior Personnel Employment Committee whose mission was to obtain employment for persons over 65 years old. Contributions in her memory may be made to Senior Personnel Employment Council, 1 Summit Avenue, White Plains, N.Y. 10606.
