
        BENARDETE-Seth. On November 14, 2001. Beloved husband of Jane. Loving father of Ethan and Alexandra Emma. Dear brother of Jose and Diego. Professor of Classics at NYU and Philosophy at the Graduate Faculty of the New School for Social Research. He was the author of many articles, translations, and books, among them ''The Being of the Beautiful'', 1984, ''Socrates' Second Sailing'', 1989, and ''Plato's Laws: the Discovery of Being'', 2000. Memorial service will be held at a later date.
