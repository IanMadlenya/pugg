
        HARRIS--Jonathan of Larchmont. Died January 18, 2004 at age 60. Graduate of Columbia College 1965 and Columbia Law School 1968. He was an attorney with Swidler, Berlin, Sheareff and Friedman. Jonathan is survived by his mother Mollie, wife Myra (nee Levine), sons Arthur and Robbie and brothers David, Andrew and Benjamin. Funeral Service today, Tuesday 1PM at Westchester Jewish Center in Mamaroneck followed by an Interment at Riversville Cemetery. Donations to the UJA Israeli Emergency Fund, 701 Westchester Avenue, White Plains, NY 10601. For information call Zion Memorial Chapel 914381-1809.
