
        KANE-Louis Isaac. On Friday, June 9, of Boston and Ogunquit, ME. Husband of Katharine D. Kane. Father of Dr. Holliday Kane Rayfield and her husband Rick Rayfield, Jenny Kane and Joseph Daniels Kane. Grandfather of Sophie Alexander Rayfield. Son of George E. Kane of Boston and the late Sally (Smith) Kane. Nephew of the late Charles E. Smith. Brother of Ann Kane Collier and her husband Marvin Collier of Boston. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. He received the A.B. from Harvard College 1953 and completed service in the U.S. Marine Corps as a Captain in 1959. Mr. Kane was Chairman of Au Bon Pain Corp., Co-chairman of the Harvard College Fund and recently received the Harvard Medal. Past Chairman of the West End Boys & Girls Club and Trustee of Bentley College. Shiva at his home at 20 River Rd., Ogunquit, ME (207-646-7471) on Sunday, 7-9 PM, and continuing Monday through Thursday, 1-4 PM and 7-9 PM. Remembrances may be made to the West End House, 105 Allston St., Allston, MA 02134, Ogunquit Museum of American Art, Ogunquit, ME 03907 or Harvard College, c/o Harvard College Fund, 124 Mt. Auburn St., Cambridge, MA 02138. For information, the Levine Chapels, Brookline.
