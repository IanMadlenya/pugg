
        HART-Lynda. Of Manhattan, died on December 31, 2000. Beloved spouse of Stacey Foiles, sister of Kerry and Randy Hart. Author of Fatal Women and Between the Body and the Flesh, Hart was professor of English, University of Pennsylvania. Services Tuesday, 4-9, Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9 at Horne Dannecker O'Connor, 445 West 43 St., NYC. Memorial service January 20, call 212-998-1649 for information. In lieu of flowers contributions in her name to The Village Zendo, 200 Varick St., NYC 10013 or The Astraea National Lesbian Foundation, 16 East 16 St., NYC 10003, are appreciated.
