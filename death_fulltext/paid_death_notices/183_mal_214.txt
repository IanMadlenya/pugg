
        WURZBURGER-Dr. Walter S. Congregation Shaaray Tefila in Lawrence records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed Rabbi Emeritus Dr. Walter S. Wurzburger. Renowned scholar, author and teacher who inspired and led our congregation for more than three decades. His legacy as a communal leader, noted rabbinic figure and close disciple of Rabbi Joseph B. Soloveitchik will be cherished and remembered for years to come. We extend heartfelt condolences to his wife Naomi and to his sons Benjamin, Joshua, Myron and the entire family.
