
        LANDESMAN--Louis. Age 93, loving husband of Florence; father of Betsy Jacobs and Richard Landesman; grandfather of Lisa Jacobs, Rabbis Andrew and Cheryl Jacobs, Melissa and Joel Landesman; loving great-grandfather and uncle, passed away on November 23. Raised in the lower east side of Manhattan and Brooklyn, Louis became a successful advertising Vice President. He spent countless hours volunteering his time to improve the lives of others. Donations may be sent to Ramat Shalom Synagogue, 11301 West Broward Blvd., Plantation, Florida 33325
