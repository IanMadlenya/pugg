
        GREEN-Florence. On August 17, 2002, in Greensboro, N.C., at the age of 88. Remembered lovingly by her son, Ronald, and his wife, Susan; her grandchildren, Cynthia, Jonathan, and Anne; her grandson-in-law, Josh Jablons, and her great-grandchildren, Zachary, Jonah and Hannah Jablons. Florence will be remembered by many graduates of the Jewish Theological Seminary, where she dedicated most of her working life.
