
        MURRAY-Paul Francis, Dr. 58, died on February 24, 1997 of a heart attack. Paul earned a Ph.D in Business from GWU. He started his own company and built houses in CA. Paul retired to Osterville, MA, yet he was never at rest. He was a competitor in the 1972 Mr. America contest, a triathlete and a member of the Power Lifting Hall of Fame. He was fascinated with Russia and he published two letters in The New York Times on November 5, 1992 and November 24, 1996. He was the first American to be sent by the IESC to help Ukraine. He was passionate about education and helping children with dyslexia. His humor and charm kept us all in high spirits and we treasure his memory. He was an inspiration to us all. Dad is deeply missed and loved.
