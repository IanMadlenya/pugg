
        PRESTIN--Frieda. Age 92, of Tampa, FL, died Saturday. Mrs. Prestin was preceeded in death by her husband Henry and a daughter Rena Yannopoulos. Survived by her daughter, Lorna Michaelson, Tampa; five grandchildren, including Maia Michaelson, Manhattan, NY, and Jay Michaelson, Brooklyn, NY; great-grandson Henry Michaelson. Graveside services will be held 11 am, Tuesday, at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
