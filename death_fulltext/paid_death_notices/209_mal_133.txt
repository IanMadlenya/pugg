
        GRANVILLE--Bernard J., 80, on June 3, 2004. Born November 8, 1923, in New York City, son of Sol and Regina Greenberg. Served with distinction in the Army during WW II. Actor, singer, cabaret performer, member of AEA, SAG, AFTRA. Bernard was an insurance broker, co-founder of the Greater Gotham Business Council, and the first gay insurance broker to advertise as such. An avid reader, a lover of great food, and an indefatigable theatregoer. He was a great friend, with impeccable wit and style, and will be missed by all who knew him. We love you, Bernard.
