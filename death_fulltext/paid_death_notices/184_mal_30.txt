
        FELDMAN-Nathan. Of Belle Harbor, NY, at home on May 2, 2002, surrounded by his family. Beloved husband of Estelle, cherished father of Ira (Marilu) & Beth Kempner (Jim), adored and adoring grandfather of Will, Matthew and Lena. Dear brother of Mildred Hyman. Retired owner of A.N. Falik, Inc. He spent all of his working life in the diamond jewelry industry. A man of warmth, dignity and generosity, he was greatly loved and will be missed by all. Service today 1PM ''Parkside'' Memorial Chapel, 2576 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn, NY (opposite Kings Plaza). Interment will follow at the Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Long Island, NY.
