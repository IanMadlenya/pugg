
        DANK-Barbara Allman. On January 22, 1999. A 1938 graduate of Radcliffe College and the Harvard-Radcliffe Program in Business Administration, she was a pioneer in educating women about new and challenging roles from homemaker to business. A member of the Baldwin, LI School Board for many years, she served on the Board of the Stevens Scholarship Fund for Deserving Students at Baldwin High School. A certified financial planner, she designed and implemented the Seniors' Financial Planning Service at Family Services Association, Hempstead, LI. She retired in 1997 to develop an educational framework to include career development activities for children from kindergarten through high school. She is survived by son Harry, daughter Bonnie, daughter-in-law Shoshana, and grandchildren Micah and Avital. Services Sunday, 10:30 AM at Gutterman's, 175 Long Beach Rd., Rockville Centre, LI. Donations to the Hospice of Howard County, 5537 Twin Knolls Rd., Columbia, MD 21045.
