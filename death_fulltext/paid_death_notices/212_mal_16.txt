
        SACKS--David G. The Jewish Outreach Institute mourns the passing of its former chairman. David was instrumental in helping to solidify the Institute's role in transforming research into practice, helping to pave the way for hundreds of thousands of interfaith families and their children to find their rightful place within the bounds of the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathy to Marcella; to David's children; and to the entire Sacks and Rosen families. Rabbi Kerry M. Olitzky, Exec Director Terrence Elkes, President Eugene Grant, Chairman
        SACKS--David. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons notes with sadness the passing of David Sacks, esteemed Jewish leader, beloved husband of Marcella Rosen. We extend deepest condolences to the family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
        SACKS--David. The Board of Westchester Jewish Community Services mourns the passing of our past president, David Sacks. His leadership has enriched our agency and the greater community. We extend our sincere sympathies to the family. Robert Stavis, President Alan Trager, CEO/Exec. Dir.
