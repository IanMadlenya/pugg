
        PORT-Evelyn J. December 2, 2001, at the age of 56, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Beloved wife of the late Garrett, and loving mother of Darren and Stephanie. Adored daughter of Margot and the late Henry Gutman. Cherished sister of Joan (Marc) Mintz and Karen (Steven) Yager, and sister-in-law of Penny and Lenny Goldberg. Also survived and loved by many nieces and nephews. A strong and independent woman filled with warmth and kindess, who touched everyone around her. She will be deeply missed and loved by her family and friends.
