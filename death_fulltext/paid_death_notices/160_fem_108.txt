
        GALLIONE-John. Age 81, on May 11, 2000. Retired NYC Trial Attorney representing various trade unions and active in NYC politics. Served in WWII as Assistant Judge Advocate. Beloved husband to Dori. Father of Gale Cohen and her husband Michael, Joy Martinez and her husband Ernesto and the late Jan Gallione. Devoted brother of Joyce Binder and cherished grandfather of Grace, Jesse, Jonathan and Allison.
