Funeral services Sunday, Nov. 22 at 1:30 PM in the chapel at Cong. Beth Israel, 701 Farmington Ave., W. Hartford, CT. Burial at Fairview Cemetery, W. Hartford. In lieu of flowers, please make donation to charity of choice.
