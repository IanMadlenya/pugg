
        BAILINE--Mark, 55. Of Montville, NJ died at his home on Monday June 19, 2006 from complications of a brain tumor. Born in Jersey City, NJ, he is survived by his wife Bernice, sister and brother-inlaw Nancy & Robert Goldman, niece & nephews David, Greg, Brian & Erica Goldman and his beloved dog ''Murray'' and many friends. A graduate of Pace University, Mark and his wife worked in the corporate community until 10 years ago when they purchased the UPS Store in Jersey City, NJ. Services will take place on Tuesday June 20, 2006 at 10AM at the Sanctuary of Abraham and Sarah at the Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, NJ along with entombment. The family will receive visitors at the Bailine residence in Montville, NJ Tuesday and Wednesday as well at the Goldman residence in Rumson, NJ Wednesday and Thursday evenings. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the American Brain Tumor Foundation, 2720 River Rd, Des Plaines, IL 60018, would be appreciated.
