
        BRANIFF--John F. III. Kane Lodge No. 454, F. & A. M. announces with profound sorrow the death of Bro. John F. Braniff III on February 8, 2005. A lifelong resident of Staten Island, he was Raised in Kane Lodge on December 3, 1991. He is survived by his wife Sonia, three daughters and extended family. Active in many genealogical societies, Bro. Braniff was particularly fond of Kane Lodge and will be missed. Ernest W. Prescott III, Master Paul D. Reitz, Secretary
