
        VAN DAMM--Joseph B., 92. Formerly of Yonkers, NY, resident of Pompano Beach, FL. passed away on January 1, 2006. After serving in the Navy during WWII, Joe married his high school sweetheart, Lenore and remained in New York. Joe took over the family business and stayed proprietor of ''Salem Brokerage'' in Westchester for many years. Joe retired to Florida in 1991 after Lenore passed away. Predeceased by a brother, Ed Van Damm, Atlanta, GA. Survived by a loving son Albert of Astoria, NY; a nephew, Greg, Atlanta; niece Beth, Atlanta and all who knew and loved him. Services will be private. Donations in his name directed to the American Cancer Society or to www.NAMI.org. For information Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapels, Deerfield Beach, FL. 954-427-4700.
