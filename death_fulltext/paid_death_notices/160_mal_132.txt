
        TUTEUR-Roger. On May 9, 2000 at age 94. Adored husband of Audrey. Father of the late Henry Tuteur. Loving father of Ann. Cherished grandfather of Ottavia. Funeral services will be held at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street on Friday 11:30 A.M. Interment private. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        TUTEUR-Roger. Congregation Emanu-El of The City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Roger Tuteur, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
