
        SAMUELS-Berne R., on June 23, 2002. Beloved son, brother and uncle, he was born January 21, 1919 in NYC, son of Max and Betty (nee Lusthans). Berne was a C.P.A. in NYC for many years. Predeceased by parents and brother Daniel. Survived by sister Rhoda Anker and her husband Leonard, Dobbs Ferry, NY, nephews Mark and Warren. Funeral services were held on Monday at Mount Hebron Cemetery, Queens.
