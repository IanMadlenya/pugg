
        STOLINSKY-Irving, of Lauderhill, FL, formerly of Clifton, NJ, died August 1, age 81. Survived by wife Babs (Aufzien); children Masha (Stolinsky) and Avraham Taurog of Brooklyn, Peter and Peggi Stolinsky of Los Altos, CA, David Stolinksy of NYC, Robin Stolinsky of NYC; grandchildren Etta and Shmuel Taurog; sister Jeanne Lawrence of North Miami Beach. He was the Secretary-Treasurer of Reading Industries, refiners and manufacturers of copper products before retirement.
