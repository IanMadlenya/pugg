
        REISS-Ruth K. On April 13, 2000, at age 91. Beloved wife of the late Georges Bernard Reiss. Loving mother of Elizabeth Baecher and Jeanne-Marie Byington. Adoring grandmother of John and Edward Baecher, and great-grandmother of two. Devoted sister of Martin L. and Robert D. Kay, dear aunt and wonderful friend. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 Street, Monday, 6-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Jean Baptiste Church, Lexington Ave. at 76 St., Tuesday, 11 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to your favorite charity.
