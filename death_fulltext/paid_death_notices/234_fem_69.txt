
        FLESCHNER--Janice(nee Hubshman) died on June 24, 2006 at JFK Hospital in Lake Worth, FL. She was 85. Mrs. Fleschner grew up in New York and Milford, CT. She graduated from the Ethical Fieldston School. She also studied at The Arts Students League of New York and attended Temple University's Tyler School of Art. She was married to Malcolm King Fleschner who died in 1999. They lived in Westport, CT, New York City, Palm Beach and often summered in France. Together they were avid collectors of 20th Century American Art and donated many pieces to the Norton Museum in West Palm Beach, Florida. She is survived by her son and daughter, six grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Expressions of sympathy may take the form of donations to the National Audubon Society.
