
        ROSTENBERG--Dr. Leona. Died March 17, 2005. Beloved lifelong friend and partner of Madeleine B. Stern. Leona Rostenberg Rare Books was founded in 1944 and joined by Madeleine B. Stern in 1945. She was past president of the Antiquarian Booksellers' Association of America and a power in the world of antiquarian books. She was also the author of several books on printing history and co-author with Madeleine Stern of autobiographical memoirs. She was the daughter of Dr. Adolph and Louisa D. Rostenberg, and the sister of the late Dr. Adolph Rostenberg Jr. She was the closest relative of her beloved cousin Richard F. Koch. She will be sorely missed. Services at Campbell's, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, on Wednesday, March 23, at 1 PM.
