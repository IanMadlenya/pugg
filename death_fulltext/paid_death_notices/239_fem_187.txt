
        LOUCKS--Carolyn Bade, beloved wife of the late William Dewey Loucks, Jr. died December 22 after a long courageous battle with COPD. Her daughter was by her side. Mrs. Loucks was born in Brooklyn, NY on September 9, 1921. She attended The NY School of Fine & Applied Arts (Parsons School of Design). She was a fine artist. She enjoyed her house in Londonderry, VT working in her garden (featured in House Beautiful) or spending time with her family in Bronxville, painting, sewing or working in her art room with her husband reading by her side. She was active with The Junior League of Bronxville and is an Emeritus member. Loving mother of Karen L. Klein (Donald) of NYC, her son Thomas A. Loucks (Domique) of Greenwood Village, CO. Proud grandmother of Allison of NYC and Christopher, Averil and Alexandra of CO. A private service will be held in January. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations to 2nd Congregational Church, P.O. Box 77, Londonderry, VT 05148 or Jansen Memorial Hospice, 69 Main St, Tuckahoe, NY 10707
