
        QUINN--Kevin B. Of Somers, New York, and Bolton Landing, New York, passed away at home on February 2nd after a long and courageous battle with pancreatic cancer. He was 63 years old. Kevin worked on the trading floor as a stock specialist and partner with M.J. Meehan & Co. for 30 years. He was a proud member of the New York Stock Exchange for 18 of those years before ringing the Exchange's closing bell upon his retirement in December 1998. Kevin was born on November 7, 1942 in the Bronx to William and Elizabeth (Walker) Quinn. He served in both the U.S. Air Force (1960-1964) and U.S. Army (1964-1967) as well as U.S. Army Reserves (1974-1980). Kevin was the loving, devoted husband of Mary (Foley) for 36 years and the proud father of three children, Colleen (Petersen) and husband Joseph, Mary Beth and Kevin, Jr. He was also the loving granddad of Kyle and Shannon Petersen, born to Colleen and Joseph. Kevin is survived by four sisters and predeceased by two brothers, as well as his parents. He is also survived by numerous aunts, uncles, cousins, nieces and nephews. Kevin was a member of the Knights of Columbus, the Elks Club, and the American Legion.
