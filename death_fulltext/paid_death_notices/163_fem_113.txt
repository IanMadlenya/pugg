
        O'REILLY-Sister Mary Joseph, O. Carm. On August 13, 2000 in her 89th year. Native of Louisburg, County Mayo, Ireland. Over 50 years of devoted service with The Carmelite Sisters For The Aged And Infirm. Survived by brother, Patrick, in England, nephews, Rev. John Collins, CSP, James Collins, Rev. Michael Hunt, CSP, David Hunt, and niece, Ann C. Hunt, three grand nephews and two grand nieces. Reposing at Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Avenue (at 72nd Street) Wednesday from 10 A.M. to 9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, 9:30 A.M. at Holy Family Chapel, Mary Manning Walsh Home. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, N.Y. For further information contact the John Krtil Funeral Home at 212-744-3084.
