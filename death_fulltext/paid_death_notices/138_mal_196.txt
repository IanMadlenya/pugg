
        PROSSER-Walter R. Jr. Age 76 on July 1, 1998 of New York City and Center Moriches, NY. A beloved brother of Mary P. Kalbfleisch and Jennie P. Robinson. Uncle to Stephen A., John P. and Thomas W. Holthausen, Fred A. and Jeffrey P. Robinson. Great-uncle to Christian C. Gretchen A., Erica A. Holthausen, L. Jean Armstrong, Katherine A., Sara E., Benjamin P. and Henry W. Robinson. Mr. Prosser graduated from Poly Prep C.D. School 1939, Dartmouth College 1947. He served as an Officer US Army (OSS-Far East), DirectorNear East College Association International, final service with International Institutional Services, New York City. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Walter R. Prosser Fund & mailed payable to American Community School at Beirut, 850 Third Ave. 18th Floor, New York, NY 10022. An informal memorial service will be held Friday, August 7, 1998 from 4-7 P.M., at the Marriott Marquis Hotel, New York City.
