
        KAPLAN-Arthur. Age 83. On October 29, 1998. Devoted husband of Phyllis for 53 years. Beloved father of Joanne & father-in-law of James Handloser. Dear brother of Philip Kaplan & brother-in-law of Eleanor Kaplan. Adored uncle of four nieces and nephews. Arthur was a WWII veteran, 349th Batallion, 88th Division, serving in Italy. He was awarded the Bronze Star. May his memory live forever.
