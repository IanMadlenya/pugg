
        LIST-Vera G. The Board and Congregation of Temple Sholom, Greenwich, CT. mourn the loss of our longtime member and benefactor, Vera G. List. Vera List and her late husband Albert List were devoted and generous supporters of this institution for more than half a century. We will always be indebted to them. Our heartfelt condolences go out to the entire List family. Greg Kandel, President Mitchell Hurvitz, Rabbi Temple Sholom
