
        WEXLER-John P., M.D., Ph.D. Albert Einstein College of Medicine is profoundly shocked & saddened by the untimely death of our valued colleague and friend, Dr. John Wexler, Professor of Nuclear Medicine and of Radiology. A member of our faculty since 1973, John played an active and expansive role in the life of our community as physician, teacher and administrator. In 1996 he was named Assistant Dean for Information Technology assuming overall responsibility for coordinating the development of the medical school's Internet site. We will miss his presence and his vitality. We extend our deepest condolences to his entire family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. The Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University
        WEXLER-John P., M.D., Ph.D. The Department of Nuclear Medicine at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine & Montefiore Medical Center notes with great sadness the untimely death of Dr. John Wexler. John was a vital member of the faculty of the department for 24 years. His expertise in cardiovascular nuclear medicine, PET Imaging and computer technology was unsurpassed. He will be remembered fondly and missed greatly. Our condolences to John's wife, Marjorie and children Thomas and Elizabeth, and to all members of his family.
