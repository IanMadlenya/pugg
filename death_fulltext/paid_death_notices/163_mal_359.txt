
        SCHWARTZ-Sam passed away at Mt. Sinai Hospital in New York City on August 30, 2000 after a prolonged illness. Loving father of Sheila Paulos and grandfather to Leah Paulos and Daniel Paulos, father-in-law of John Allen Paulos. Beloved companion of Marsha Engelsher. Jean Gibenstein his wife of 56 years died of Alzheimer's Disease in 1997. Born in New York City in 1917, Mr. Schwartz was founder and president of Metropolitan Window Washing Company in White Plains, NY. He lived life passionately with great love for his family and friends. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him. Services will be held at Louis Hirsch and Sons, Inc., 1225 Jerome Avenue, Bronx, New York on Thursday, August 31st at 1:30 P.M. Interment is at Cedar Park, Paramus, New Jersey.
