
        METZGER--Aaron P. Passed away peacefully on July 8, 2003. He was 79 years old. Surviving are his wife Sally, his children Carol and Mark Schaffer, Janis and Michael Moisan and Diane and Michael Cohen. He leaves behind his most precious gift, his eight grandsons Matthew, Ian, Russell, Bobby, Jared, David, Corey and Jonathan. He is also survived by his brothers Rabbi Stanley Metzger and Melvin Metzger. As a member of the New York and Florida Bars, he practiced law for over fifty years.
