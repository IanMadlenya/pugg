
        SWAYNE--Michael E. Sr. Died Friday, January 23, in Somerville, NJ. He was 69. Survived by his wife of 44 years, the former Ida Murphy of Somerset, NJ, three sons, Michael Jr., James Sr. and John, all of Somerset, three daughters Patricia Swayne of Harmony, NJ, Maureen Swayne of Somerset, Anne Sickles of Hillsborough, NJ, and seven grandchildren. Services will be Wednesday, at 8:30 am, from the Gleason Funeral Home, 1360 Hamilton St, Somerset, followed by a 9 am Funeral Mass at St. Matthias Church in Somerset. Relatives and friends may call the funeral home on Monday and Tuesday, from 2-4 and 7-9 pm.
