
        ALI-Asad, MD. January 17, 2002. The Neurology Staff at The New York Hospital-Queens, is deeply saddened by the death of our longtime friend and colleague, Asad Ali, MD. Among his many legacies is a lifelong devotion to the care of the needy and the infirm, and a determined commitment to the health and well-being of his patients, family and friends. He will be greatly missed. Drs. J. Applebaum, A. Farkash, G. Ney, E. Oribe, N. Schaul, G. Waldman
