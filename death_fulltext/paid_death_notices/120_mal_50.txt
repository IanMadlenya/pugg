
        KOLOVSKY - Judge Harold. Of Morristown, NJ. Died on January 3, 1997. He is survived by his wife Edith Mintz Kolovsky. A funeral service was held on Sunday, January 5 in Morristown, NJ, interment Beth El Cemetery, Paramus NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Morristown Memorial Hospital and Columbia University School of Law would be appreciated.
