
        HARRIS--Jack. Age 71. Born in London, England. Died July 25, 2003 in Stamford, CT. Jack was a veteran of the U.S. Army and the British Merchant Marines. Beloved father of Mark, Gregory and Alison. Brother of Audrey and Maureen, grandfather of seven. He was larger than life, with a heart to match. He will be forever missed by the many he has known. >>AD#
