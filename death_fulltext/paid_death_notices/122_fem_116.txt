
        BEECHER-Margery. Age 86. On March 13, 1997 after a prolonged illness. Beloved wife for 63 years to Lawrence (Larry) Beecher. Loving mother of Andrew and Robert. Dear grandmother of Amy and Owen. Devoted sister of Stanton Gottlieb. Service Sunday 11AM, Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. She will always be remembered for her style, humor and love of the ballet, opera and travel. She will never be forgotten. Contributions in her memory may be made to your favorite charity.
        BEECHER-Margery. Marge, darling, run where the flowers are. All my love, Larry
        BEECHER-Margery. The Officers, Trustees and Members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to her husband Lawrence and the family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Alan Levine, Ch. of the Bd. Robert P. Antler, President
