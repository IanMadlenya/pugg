
        NELSON-John Harris. Age 63, of Manchester-by-the-Sea, MA, died June 20th, 2002. Beloved father of Andrea Abbott and Trevor Nelson. Adoring grandfather of McLeod, Conrad, Lyric, and George. A graduate of St. Mark's School, Southborough, MA, and of Harvard College. He possessed a deep passion for literature, music, philosophy, and the history of art. His was a dazzling mind and he will be forever remembered as a dear and gentle soul. A memorial service will be held at 11:30 AM on June 26th at Emmanuel Chapel, Masconomo Street, Manchester-by-the-Sea, MA.
