
        CROOKS - Edwin Smith. Member of The American Stock Exchange. On July 26, 2002. Beloved husband of Brooks Cushman. Loving father of Edwin and William. Devoted son of Marie Crooks and the late Edwin S. Crooks, Sr. Loving brother of Kathleen Ball and Michael Crooks. Cherished grandfather of Evan. Memorial service Thursday, August 1, 2002, 4:30PM at Trinity Church, Broadway at Wall Street, New York City. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Edwin S. Crooks Memorial at the Wildlife Center of Virginia, P.O. Box 1557, Waynesboro, VA. 22980.
