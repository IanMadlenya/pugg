
        QUACH--Emily Hsu Landau. On December 30, 2003. A beautiful person adored by all. Born in Laos, she graduated from Wells College and Columbia University. She is survived by her husband, two children, parents, sisters and brother. She will be missed by all whom she touched. A memorial service will be held at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue at East 51st Street in Manhattan, on Tuesday, January 6, at 10:30 am. Donations may be made in her memory to Barnes Jewish Hospital Foundation, Breast Cancer Research: 600 S. Taylor, St. Louis, MO 63011.
