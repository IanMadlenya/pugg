
        COHEN - Theodore Marks (Teddy). 75, died June 29, 2002. Beloved son of the late Schmule and Eva Marks Cohen, loving brother of Robert M. and Michael Marks and sisters-in-law Zipty and Cantor Bette, devoted uncle of Margot and Daniel, caring friend of Eileen Reichel. His sweet soul will be remembered by his many friends forever. Donations may be made to UJA/Federation.
