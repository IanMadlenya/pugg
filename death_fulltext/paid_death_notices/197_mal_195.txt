
        GROVE--John L. Greencastle, PA, renowned entrepreneur and local philanthropist, died on Monday, June 16, 2003 at his home following an extended illness. Mr. Grove was known worldwide in the equipment industry having co-founded Grove Manufacturing Company in 1946 manufacturing the first hydraulic crane. In 1969 he co-founded JLG Industries Inc, which became the world's leading producer of mobile aerial work platforms. He is survived by his wife of sixty years, Cora I. (Wagner) Grove. >>AD#
