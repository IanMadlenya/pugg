
        BRESSLER-Alfred W. On March 15, 1999 at the age of 94. A distinguished attorney in practice for more than 70 years, he is survived by many devoted friends. Service and interment private. A memorial service will be announced.
        BRESSLER-Alfred W. On March 15, 1999 at age 94. The Henry and Lucy Moses Fund mourns the loss of its President. His devotion to the practice of law was legendary and he brought the same extraordinary degree of energy, skill and tenacity to his charitable endeavors as he unstintingly provided to his clients at Moses & Singer during his long and fruitful career. The Henry and Lucy Moses Fund, Inc. Joseph L. Fishman, Director Irving Sitnick, Director
        BRESSLER-Alfred. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Alfred Bressler, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
