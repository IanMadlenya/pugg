
        GREENSTEIN - Joseph. Joseph Yussy, Don Jose Greenstein, age 78 died at home peacefully after a long illness on January 9. Master builder, jungle explorer, founder of Su Casa North and South and World War II veteran. Born in the Bronx, N.Y., son of David and Goldie Greenstein. Survived by his beloved wife of 53 years, Miriam Nates Greenstein; father of daughters Robin Wolfson of Port Chester, N.Y. and Jody Soltanoff of Woodstock, N.Y.; grandfather of granddaughters Kimberly Wolfson Lisack and Kacey Wolfson; grandsons Dr. Gregory Soltanoff and Eric Soltanoff and Joseph Lisack; sons-in-law Larry and Peter; brothers Morris and Irving Greenstein; special friends Carlene and Tyler. Those closest to him know he will soon be back at work, wearing his tool belt, fixing whatever has to be repaired and loving it. You live forever in our hearts-AdiosYour Loving Wife and Daughters Services Thursday, January 11, 1:15PM, Riverside Funeral Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, N.Y.C. Contributions in his memory may be sent to La Cruz Rojo, Isla Mujeres, Quintan A Roo Mexico 77400.
