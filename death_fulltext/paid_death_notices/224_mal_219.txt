
        SLADE--John. The World Union for Progressive Judaism records with sadness the death of John Slade, our good friend and generous benefactor. His love for the Jewish people and commitment to Jewish values was wisely recognized and admired. John was our World Union International Humanitarian Award honoree in 1992. Our heartfelt condolences to his beloved wife, Marianne, his daughtersand their families. Steven Bauman, Chairman Rabbi Uri Regev, President
