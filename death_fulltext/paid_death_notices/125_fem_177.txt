
        KELLENBERG-Elizabeth Mary, of Rockville Centre, N.Y. on June 25, 1997. Devoted daughter of the late Elizabeth and Conrad. Predeceased by her dear late brothers Conrad J., Most Rev. Walter P. Kellenberg, D.D., Msgr. Edward J., and her dear late sister Marie E. Kellenberg. Loving aunt of Conrad L., Sr. Joan Kellenberg r.c., Mary E. Coyle, and Eileen G. Evans. Also survived by fifteen grand nieces & nephews and thirteen great grand nieces and nephews. Teacher of Latin and business at Mt. St. Ursula Academy, Bronx. Professor at Mt. St. Vincent College in Riverdale and Hunter College. She was chosen as woman of the year at Molloy College in recognition of her generous contributions to the community and caregiver to her family. Reposing Macken Mortuary, Inc., 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Centre, Sunday 2-4, 7-9 pm. Funeral Mass St. Agnes Cathedral Monday 10 am. Interment Cemetery of the Gate of Heaven, Valhalla, N.Y.
