
        BLOODGOOD-George J. On March 25, 1999, of congestive heart failure. Of Rockville Centre, NY. Loving husband of Patricia (nee Dolan). Beloved father of William, Maura, Patricia, Eileen, Regina and Deirdre. Dear fatherin-law of of Christopher Cleveland, Kevin Maler, and Sidice Mutandwa. Cherished grandfather of Henry Maler and Lillian Ryan. Devoted friend of Bill W. Retired substance abuse counselor after twenty-five year career with Time, Inc. He worked his way up from the mail room of Life Magazine to become Picture Editor of Sports Illustrated. Reposing at Macken Mortuary, Inc., 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Centre, NY, Sunday and Monday, 2-4, 7-9 PM. Funeral mass Tuesday, 10:30 AM, St. Anthony's RC Church, Oceanside, NY. Interment St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, NY.
