
        VAN RIPER-Hart E. 95, formerly of New York. On November 4, 2000 at his residence in Indianapolis. During his medical career, Dr. Van Riper was Medical Director of the National Foundation for Infantile Paralysis and oversaw the research program under which the Salk vaccine was developed. He is survived by his children, MaryHart Bartley of NY, David Smith Van Riper of Berthoud, CO and Christina Van Riper of Indianapolis. Memorial contributions may be made to the March of Dimes, 233 Park Avenue So., NYC 10003.
