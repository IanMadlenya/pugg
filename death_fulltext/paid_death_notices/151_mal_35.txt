
        BURCKHARDT-Rudy. We are deeply saddened by the death of gallery artist and dear friend Rudy Burckhardt. An extraordinarily gifted photographer, painter and filmmaker, Rudy was admired and beloved by a community of artists & writers of all generations. A gentle man of great dignity, vitality and humor, his presence in our lives is missed. Our love and thoughts to Yvonne, Jacob, Tom, Yoshiko, Kathy, Hugh, Keno and Rudy's many friends. Andrew H. Arnot, Eric Brown and Philippe Alexandre Tibor de Nagy Gallery
