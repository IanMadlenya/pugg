
        FELD-Alfred L. Of Old Bridge, New Jersey, January 2. Devoted husband of Harriett. Loving father of Jay (Maryann) and Leslie (Jim). Cherished grandfather of Paul Willy. Brother of Hy (Lillian). Adored uncle and great-uncle. W.W. II veteran, garment center schlepper and executive for 40 years. Past president and relentless worker for Temple Beth Ohr. Passionate lover of his family, baseball (''wait till next year''), music, and life stories. He had a special love for children; a ''magic'' man. He enjoyed life and was able to bring happiness to others through smiles and laughter.
