
        GORMAN-Ben R. On December 12, 2000 in his 91st year. Beloved husband of Selma (Bunny), father of Peter and Janice (Weiss), adored grandfather of Jonathan, Brandon, Sophie and Allison. Beloved brother to Edna Gordon. Caring Bronx Podiatrist for over fifty years whose strong will and dedication kept him on the racquetball court until the age of 87. Cherished in our hearts forever.
