
        DAMAN--Harlan. The Young Israel of Scarsdale mourns the passing of Harlan Daman, beloved husband of Carole and devoted father of Avi and Gila. A gentle soul, Harlan was a man of quiet dignity, warmth, humor, great intellect, piety and kindness. He will be missed. Jacob Rubenstein, Rabbi Jonathan Morgenstern, Assistant Rabbi Barry Carron and Joel J. Goldschmidt, Co-Presidents
