
        FIALA-M. Susan of Pound Ridge, NY on December 7, 1999. Beloved wife and companion of Rudolph for 51 years. Loving mother of Jennifer, Christopher, Pamela, Timothy and Geoffrey. Cherished grandmother of Sarah, Andrew, Peter, Robin, Wendy, Emily, Deborah, Colleen, Marina, Thomas and Maggie. Devoted sister of William Beal of Phoenix, AZ. Born March 9, 1926 in Carthage, IL. Graduated Phi Beta Kappa, Knox College, Galesburg, IL, 1947. Celebrated music teacher and theatrical director for hundreds of students over two decades at the Rippowam Cisqua School, Bedford, NY and Pound Ridge Elementary School. Founding member of the acclaimed Tuesday Consort Baroque musical group. Choir director and organist at the Ridges Methodist Church, Stamford, CT, the Presbyterian Church, Bedford and the Pound Ridge Community Church. A celebration of her life will be held at a memorial service at the Pound Ridge Community Church in the Pound Ridge Hamlet at 3 P.M. on Friday, December 10. In lieu of flowers, contributions to: The Master Teacher's Fund, Rippowam Cisqua School, Bedford, New York 10506.
