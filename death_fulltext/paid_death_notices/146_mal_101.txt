
        SAMUELS-Nathaniel. Died peacefully at home on March 2, 1999. He was the beloved husband of Dr. Mary Hyman Samuels & devoted father of Geoffrey of NYC and Neil of Doylestown, PA. He is survived by his two cherished sisters, Gertrude of Beverly Hills, CA., and Shirley Rife of Chicago, IL., two adored grandchildren, Benjamin and Oliver, and loved nephew, Steven, and niece, Marilyn. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Maurice and Sarah Samuels Fund at Harvard University.
        SAMUELS-Nathaniel. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Nathaniel Samuels. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
