
        SHALAM-Vicky, aged 94, of Geneva, Switzerland, passed away peacefully on March 14. She was the beloved wife of the late Murad Shalam and the devoted mother of John, Jaqueline, Huguette and Claude. She was the adored grandmother and great-grandmother of 12. She was an incredible woman whose enduring spirit bridged many generations and many worlds. She will be forever cherished by all those who knew her. Memorial services will be held at 10 A.M., Sunday, March 19th, at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 8000 Jericho Tpk., Woodbury, NY.
