
        WEBER-Harold N., 98. Of Northlake Drive, Orange City, FL, died Wednesday in the John Knox Medical Center, Orange City, FL. Born in Albany, New York, he was the son of the late Adna F. and the late Mabel Norris Weber. He was a graduate of Amherst College, Amherst, MA., and a member of AARP. He was an Administrator, employed by State of New York Transportation Dept. before he retired and moved to Florida in 1970. No known survivors exist. Lankford Funeral Home in Deland, FL.
