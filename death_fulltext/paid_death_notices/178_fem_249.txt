
        ELLIOTT-Margaret K. Of Key Biscayne, FL, and Mt. Kisco, NY, died at home on Nov. 22. The wife of Michael S., and the beloved mother of Jessica and Michael C. Elliott. Margaret was the daughter of Marguerite Kirby and the late Charles F. Kirby. She is also survived by her sister Marie Brennan, brother Captain John Kirby and many loving nieces and nephews. A Memorial Mass will celebrate her life at St. Francis of Assisi Church in Mt. Kisco, Wednesday, November 28, 2001, at 11 A.M. A second Memorial Mass will be celebrated at St. Agnes Church, Key Biscayne, Friday, Dec. 7, at a time to be determined. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the Westchester Exceptional Children's School, 520 Route 22, North Salem, NY 10560, or The Vincent J. Fontana Center for Child Protection, New York Foundling Hospital, 590 6th Ave, New York, NY 10011, celebrating Margaret's love of children and her many years of work for children and their families.
