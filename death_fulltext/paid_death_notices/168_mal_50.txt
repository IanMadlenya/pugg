
        TUMELTY-Joseph P. The Alumni Association of Columbia University's Mailman School of Public Health mourns the untimely passing of a loyal alumnus of the College of Pharmaceutical Sciences, Joe Tumelty '70. Through his untiring efforts, a new entity was formed: The Alumni Association of the Mailman School of Public Health and the College of Pharmaceutical Sciences for which we are deeply indebted. We will miss having benefit of his leadership and we extend to his family and friends our heartfelt condolences. Allan Rosenfield, MD, Dean James T. Harden '83, Chair Board of Advisors LaVerne Mooney DrPH, '94 Pres. Alumni Association Howard L. Nadel 'CPPS '74, Vice President
