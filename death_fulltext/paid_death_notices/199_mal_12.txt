
        ABEL--David W. On July 30, at age 89. Devoted husband of Edith, cherished father of Andrew, fond brother of Sidney. Music educator, violinist, composer, author. His ''Three Hebraic Moods,'' was performed at Carnegie Hall in 1997. He compiled a book of contrasting quotations, ''What's That You Say?'' published in 1990, including his own quote: ''An old man is like a Strad violin, mellowed and resonant with time.'' Maestro David W. Abel will be deeply missed but not forgotten. >>AD#
