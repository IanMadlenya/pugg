
        BERG--Evelyn, Past President of the Secretary's Chapter of the UFT; Executive Committee Member of the UFT; Payroll Secretary at the Bronx High School of Science. Mrs. Berg succumbed to an undisclosed illness on August 1, 2006. Services will be held on Sunday, August 6, 11 AM, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., NYC., 212-769-4400. Interment follows at 1 PM at Beth Israel Cemetery, 1000 Woodbridge Center Dr., Woodbridge, NJ, 732-634-2100. Mrs. Berg is survived by her husband Martin, son Jeffrey, daughter-in-law Penny, and grandchildren Joshua and David Berg. Expressions of sympathy may be sent to the home of Mr. and Mrs. Jeffrey Berg, 2 Kakiak Court, New City, NY 10956. Donations may be sent to the American Cancer Society in her memory.
