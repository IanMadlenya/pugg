
        KATZ--Yetta (nee Herzlich). At 92, peacefully on November 8, 2003, at the Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale, NY. Beloved wife of the late Jonas for 57 years; both born in Dynow, Poland. Cherished mother of Sandy, Helen and Stanley, Nancy and Harold. Adored grandmother of Howard, Lori, Andrea, Lois, David, Mary, Adam, Dana, Robert, Lily, Eliza and Michelle. Great-grandmother of Anna-Lise, Casey, Ariel, Eli, Zachery and Eden. She was a gracious lady whose legacy of kindness, wisdom, strength and optimism is an inspiration to us all. Those who knew and loved her will hold her always in their hearts and thoughts. Private family service.
