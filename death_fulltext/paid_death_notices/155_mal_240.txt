
        RIGNEY-Thomas G., M.D. December 16, 1999. Physician, U.S. M.A., Westpoint, NY. Manhattan College 1941; Cornell Medical School 1944. WW II Capt. U.S. Army 10th Mountain Division. Survived by his wife Marian, daughter Alison Rigney, son T. Glenn Rigney, sister Janet Rigney. Services Tuesday, 1:30 PM, Old Cadet Chapel, Westpoint.
