
        STAFF-Julius G. (''Buddy''), died on Feb. 15, 1997. He was the son of the late Commissioner Samuel G. Staff and the late Alice Grossman Staff. He was a graduate of the Barnard School for Boys and attended Hobart College, Geneva, NY. Mr. Staff was graduated from the University of Miami, Coral Gables, FL. His father was a Commissioner of the New York State Insurance Fund under three different administrations. His maternal grandfather founded one of the most successful and prestigious shoe manufacturing and retail stores of its time-Julius Grossman Shoes and Pedemode. Mr. Staff was survived by his former wife, Toni Kahal McGowan, of Prescott, AZ. He had also been married to Jane Freeman Kahn, of Beverly Hills, CA, who predeceased him. Burial was private.
