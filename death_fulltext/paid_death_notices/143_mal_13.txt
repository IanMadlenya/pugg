
        RUSSELL-Paris Scott, Jr. 89 years old. Died in Osprey, Florida on Nov. 14, 1998. Born May 27, 1909. He was an investment banker, partner of Glore Forgan, member of Squadron A, 101st Cavalry, Manhattan Unit. Survivors include his wife Elizabeth, three daughters Jill Benedict of Short Hills, N.J., Sandra Tait of Wisconsin, Pamela Bush of Chatham, Mass., a son Scott III of Ponte Vedra Beach, Florida, nine grandchildren, seven great-grandchildren.
