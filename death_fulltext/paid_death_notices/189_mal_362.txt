
        BLIND-William Charles. Died at home in Lancaster, PA. on October 26, 2002 after a long illness. Also a resident of Kennebunkport, ME. and Beaufort, S.C. Born in Chicago May 18, 1911 to Josephine K. and William A. Blind. Graduated from the University of Notre Dame 1932 and Harvard Law School, 1935. He was a long-term partner of Kelley, Drye and Warren, New York City. He was a Lieutenant in the U.S. Navy in WWII. He was a member of The New York Yacht Club, The Mid-Ocean Club-Bermuda, Kennebunk River Club, The Cape Arundel Golf Club and The Webhannet Golf Club, Maine. He is survived by his wife of 65 years, Peggy A. Blind (nee Kauffman), his brother Robert J. Blind of Colorado, two children, William C. Blind, Jr. of New York City and Anne C. Cook, also of New York City, four grandchildren, Rebecca C. Blind, Burroughs Blind, Cutler Cook and Crosby Cook. Services will be held Saturday, November 2nd, 2PM at Saint Leo the Great Catholic Church, Lancaster, PA. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Hospice of Lancaster County, POB 4125, 685 Good Dr., Lancaster PA 17604-4125.
