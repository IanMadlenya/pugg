
        McNULTY--Virginia D. Of Rye, NY, formerly of Vero Beach, FL on January 25, 2006. Devoted wife of John to whom she was married for 61 years. Loving mother of Robin Dawson, Susan Dill and Marianne Stoupnitzky. Dear grandmother of eight grandchildren. Mrs. McNulty will be remembered as a warm, funny and loving person devoted to her family.
