
        POTTS-Jean. At age 88, died Wednesday, November 10, 1999. Jean was the recipient of The Edgar Award for ''Go Lovely Rose'' in 1954 and went on to write 14 more mysteries and nearly 40 stories. She was a native of Saint Paul, Nebraska, where there is a permanent exhibit of her works at the Gruber House, Lower Level. The exhibit known as the Jean Potts Archives was the inspiration of the Howard County Historical Society and one board member in particular, Ron Sack. Jean will be missed by her many friends and admirers
