
        RAYMOND-Josephine. February 4, 2000. Beloved wife of Dana. Loving mother of Catherine, Peter and John. Cherished grandmother of David, Nicholas, Anne, Jane, John, Kaila and Briana. Devoted sister of Gerard. Her spirit and energy will live on with us always. Memorial service Friday, February 11, 3 PM, St. Vincent Ferrer Church, Lexington Ave. at 66th St.
        RAYMOND - Josephine. The Trustees, Council members, and staff of the Archives of American Art, Smithsonian Institution, are profoundly saddened by the passing of longtime Trustee, Josephine Raymond, who served for over 35 years as an invaluable friend and tireless advocate of the Archives of American Art. To her husband, Dana, and family, we extend our deepest sympathy. Frank Martucci, Chairman Barbara G. Fleischman, President Richard J. Wattenmaker, Director
