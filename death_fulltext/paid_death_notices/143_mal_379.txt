
        ADLER-Edward, Dr., of Englewood, NJ. Died December 29, 1998 at age 82. He was a Consulting Scientist and Electrochemist. Inventor of numerous patents and founder of Copper Foil Corporation, a manufacturer of high perfermance copper foil for printed circuits. A graduate of CCNY and Columbia University. He is survived by his wife Ruth, son Peter of Darien, CT., and daughters Stephanie Adler Silverstein of Newton, Mass., and Elizabeth Adler of Williamstown, Mass. Sister Shirley Panken, and three grandchildren: Jeffrey Adler and Anna and Lily Silverstein. Services 10:30AM Thursday, December 31 at Adath Jeshurun Cemetery, West Roxbury, Mass.
