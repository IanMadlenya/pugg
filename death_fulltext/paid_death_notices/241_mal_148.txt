
        LEVINE--Kent, aka the Handsome Modest Man, erasable, unforgettable, passed away after a long illness on Cape Cod, February 4, 2007. He is lovingly remembered by his wife Wendy, children, David, Michael, Robin, Lauren and 11 grandchildren. Burial was held in Old North Cemetery, Truro MA. Memorial donations may be made to The Truro Rescue Squad, Route 6, Truro MA, 02666.
