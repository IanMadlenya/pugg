
        HOPKINS - Terence Kilbourne. Died on January 3, 1997, peacefully at Lourdes Hospital, Binghamton, NY. He was 68 years of age. The cause of death was cancer. He was professor of sociology at Binghamton University where he found its graduate program in sociology in 1970, retiring in 1995. He leaves his wife Gloria N. Hopkins, and many loving friends and former students around the world.
