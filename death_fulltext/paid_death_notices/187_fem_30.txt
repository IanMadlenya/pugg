
        SCHNEIDERMAN-Elsie (nee Brown). Born Brooklyn March 11, 1911. Died July 17, 2002, Boca Raton, FL. Wife of Murray; mother-in-law of Pat Phelps; grandmother of Charles, Robert, Brett, and Kirsten Sawyer. A smart, independent woman, she taught school, spoke French, saw the world. She left Brooklyn for CA until chased by an earthquake at 85, moved herself to FL. Into her nineties, she could devour a lobster.
