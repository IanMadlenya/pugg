
        WINDERBAUM-Harriet (nee Bender), 85. On December 24, 2001 of cancer. Beloved wife of Harold for 62 years and loving mother to Michael (Rae) and Larry (Helene). Adoring grandmother of Anna (Gary), Nomi (Jim), Elise and Eli. Devoted daughter to Nathan and Celia and sister of Shirley Leonard and the late Blanche. She was uniquely compassionate, honest and protective to all of her family and friends. She empowered us all with guidance, support, encouragement and unconditional love. Funeral services to to held today, December 25, at Plaza Funeral Home, (91st Street and Amsterdam) at 9:30AM.
