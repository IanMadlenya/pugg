
        BROWN-Helene F. The members and staff of Pryor Cashman Sherman & Flynn mourn the loss of their dear friend and colleague, Helene Brown, loving and beloved wife of Dean, mother of Elizabeth and Samantha, daughter of Shirley Roffman and sister of Susan Hyer. Her great spirit, loving heart and professional dedication will be deeply missed by us all. The family has requested that memorial donations in Helene's name be directed to The Stephen Gottlieb Memorial Fund for Cancer Research, c/o Stuart Gottlieb, 220 Brewery Road, New City, New York, 10956.
