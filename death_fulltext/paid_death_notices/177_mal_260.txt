
        WEINSCHEL-Moe. Of Belle Harbor, NY on October 16, 2001, age 84. Beloved husband of Aida for 59 years. Cherished father of Alan and Sara Ameri. Adored father-inlaw of Barbara and the late Bob Ameri. Devoted grandfather of Daniel, Sharon, Lawrence, Jennifer, Adam, Naomi, Jessica, and Abigail. Loving surrogate grandfather to many. A man of many talents, he was a former glassblower, police officer, auto mechanic, business consultant, teacher and salesman. He was also a numismatist and president of American Israel Numismatic Association; member and former officer of Jewish War Veterans; former member of the Board of Temple Beth-El, Rockaway Park; a community activist; and a good friend to all he encountered. There will be a huge gap in many lives. Services October 18, 2001, 12 noon, at Boulevard Chapels, 1895 Flatbush Ave., Brooklyn, NY.
