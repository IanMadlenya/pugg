
        UNGERLEIDER - Peter. The Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies in Jerusalem and the American Pardes Foundation mourn the passing of Peter Ungerleider who supported the study of the great classics of our tradition by the finest graduates of American universities within a context of free inquiry and diversity. As President of the Dorot Foundation, he continued the tradition of his late mother, Joy Gottesman UngerleiderMayerson. Condolences from Zion to his family. Rabbi Danny Landes, Director Moshe Werthan, Chairman Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies Selig Sacks, Chairman Rae Janvey, Executive Director The American Pardes Foundation
