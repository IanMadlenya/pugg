
        BECKER-Stephen David, 71. Of Miami, Florida passed away peacefully at the Miami Heart Institute on March 27, 1999. He fought long, hard, and well for the life he had. He is survived by his wife Mary, his three children Keir, Julie, and David, his sister Myra, & his three granddaughters, Olivia, Carly, and Maya. A Memorial Service will be held Wednesday, March 31 at 1 PM, at the Gregg L. Mason Funeral Home, 10936 NE 6th Avenue, Miami, FL. 33161.
