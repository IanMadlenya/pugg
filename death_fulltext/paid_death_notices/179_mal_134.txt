
        WHITE-Sidney. A renowned navigator and journalist, died at 77, on November 1. Mr. White served in the Navy from 1942-1946. With a Master's Degree in Journalism and Asian Studies, University of Michigan, he served for many years as a war correspondent in Southeast Asia and the Middle East. A memorial will be held on Sunday, December 16 at Japanese American United Church, 255 Seventh Avenue, New York, NY. Please call 212-242-9444.
