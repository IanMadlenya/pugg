
        AMBROSE-William G., 76. Of Irvington, VA., died March 10, 1999. He is survived by his wife, Priscilla Ambrose; one son, William G. Ambrose Jr., of Poland, ME..; one daughter, Catherine A. Smith, of Fredericksburg, VA.; and three grandchildren. Funeral services will be held 2 P.M., Saturday, March 13, in the Grace Episcopal Church in Kilmarnock, VA, with interment in the Historic Christ Church Cemetery in Irvington, VA. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Grace Episcopal Church Memorial Fund or to the Northern Neck Free Health Clinic, Kilmarnock.
