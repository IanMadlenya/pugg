        RITTENBERG-Jack. The administration, trustees, faculty, staff, students and alumni of the Fashion Institute of Technology are greatly saddened by the loss of Dean Emeritus Jack Rittenberg, a 35 year member of the FIT community. As dean for Business and Technology and later for Student Affairs, he contributed greatly to the life of the college. This teacher, mentor, and mensch was loved and respected by his colleagues and students. All will miss ''Uncle Jack''. Dr. Joyce F. Brown, President
