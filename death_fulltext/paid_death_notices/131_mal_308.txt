
        ROTHENBERG-Rabbi Emanuel. Pioneering rabbi, visionary in Jewish education and communal life in Texas, Pennsylvania and New York. He also served as Director of the Religious Department of The Jewish National Fund. To his wife, Gabriella, and children, Dina, Abe, Daniel and Kiki, his grandchildren and great grandchildren, may you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Shiva will be observed as of Tuesday at the Rothenberg home.
