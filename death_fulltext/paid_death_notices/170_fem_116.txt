
        CHOW-Tsai-Ya Lee ''Annie''. On March 14, 2001 at age 94. Beloved wife of the late Ming Cheng Chow. Cherished aunt of 24 nieces and nephews. Considered the pioneering spirit of the Lee family, she was the only daughter of six sons. She came to this country in 1934 from Shanghai China, where she practiced law. She received a J.S.D. from NYU and an M.A. from Columbia University. She became the first female stockbroker for Francis DuPont and Company 1949. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Friday 2-5Pm. Funeral service Saturday, 10AM at the Chinese Community Church of New York, 6 West 96 St at Central Park West. Interment Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, NY
