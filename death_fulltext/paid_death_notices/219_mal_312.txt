
        WILLIAMSON -- Samuel J. The NYU Faculty of Arts and Science mourns the loss of its esteemed colleague and friend, Sam Williamson. A physicist by training, his scholarly gifts and intellectual interests ranged widely, bringing him distinction as a scientist and much admiration from his colleagues. His contributions to this community of scholars will be sorely missed, as will his warm friendship. His loved ones are in our thoughts. Richard Foley Dean, NYU Faculty of Arts and Science Peter Lennie Dean for Science
