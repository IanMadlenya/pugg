
        VALYI-Emery. Of Katonah and Riverdale, on May 5, at age 86, husband of Elisabeth, father of Kathy and grandfather of Aran, Kieran and Clara. An inventor with over 200 U.S. and foreign patents who made original technical contributions to foundry technology, mass and heat transfer systems, packaging technology and automotive structures, Emery Valyi was active in his community and an early director of Wave Hill. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Elizabeth and Sandor Valyi Foundation. A memorial service is planned.
