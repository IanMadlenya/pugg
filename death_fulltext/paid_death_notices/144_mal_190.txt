
        WIND-Gerald. Died Saturday, January 9, 1999 at the age of 70 after a long battle with ALS. A founder and pillar of the Jewish community of Monsey, N.Y. and current resident of Sunny Isles, Florida. He will be remembered for his generosity, sensitivity and fine character. Former President of Langen & Wind Color Labs and Gerry Wind & Associates. Loving husband of Phyllis, beloved father of Susan, Shalom, Ira, Debra (Krim) and Robert, grandfather of 8 grandchildren, brother of Sylvia Walkenfeld and Marion Klein. May his soul be bound in the bond of eternal life. Shiva will be observed until Friday morning.
