
        PROSNITZ-Gerry. Congregation Rodeph Sholom records with sorrow the passing of our long-time member, Gerry Prosnitz, whose commitment and caring will be sorely missed by our synagogue community. Our deepest sympathies are extended to her sons Eugene and Leonard, and their families. Robert N. Levine, Sr Rabbi Jack B. Levitt, President
        PROSNITZ-Gerry. The Board of The Jewish Association of Retired Professionals mourns the passing of its founder and past President. She was a dedicated and tireless leader for many years. We extend our condolences to the family.
