
        INTRIERI--Julia. Age 93. On December 28, 2004. Born May 30, 1911 in Elizabeth, N.J. Daughter of Nicholas and Rose Caruso. Devoted wife of the late Dominick, and thelate Emil Lamanda. Beloved mother of Pasquale and Nicholas. A source of strength, love, goodness and inspiration to her family, neighbors and anyone who needed help.
