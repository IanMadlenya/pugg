
        CUSTER-Virginia Estabrook. 80. Of Naples, FL died Tuesday, January 6, 1999. Formerly of Chestertown, MD. She was a graduate of Hofstra University. Survived by three daughters, Barbara Ann Custer of Los Angeles, CA, Mimi Kaine (Jay W.) of Oak Ridge, TN, and Mary Custer of Los Angeles, CA.; one sister, Barbara Estabrook of Washington, D.C.; four grandchildren, Capt. James, Christopher, Timothy and Patrick Kaine, and a great grandson, Daniel. She was preceded in death by her husband Merton D. Custer in 1985. Graveside services will be held at St. Paul's Cemetery in Chestertown, MD. Memorials to the American Heart Association.
