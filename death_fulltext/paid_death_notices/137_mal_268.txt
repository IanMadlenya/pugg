
        LIEBERMAN-Irving. Of Bayside, NY. On June 24th at his home in Delray Beach. Beloved husband of Ruth. Devoted father of Judith, Frederic and the late Roy. Dear father-in-law of Marjorie and brother of the late Roslyn Blau. Loving grandfather of Jennifer Shane, Andrew William and Anna Bess Marissa Xin Guang. Dear brother-in-law of Norma Gross, Sylvia Jaffe and the late Frances Schneider. Services will be held at 2 P.M., on Sunday at Temple Beth Sholom, 171-39 Northern Boulevard, Flushing. Esteemed teacher and advisor to many students at Martin Van Buren High School. His family and friends will miss this good, sweet man.
