
        COSTA-Robert J. at age 34 on December 2, in Manhasset, Long Island. Cherished life companion of Allen J. Pilikian, loving son of John Costa and Alice Tighe, devoted grandson of the late William and Delfina Costa, beloved brother of John Costa Jr., Thomas Costa and Joni DiMuro. A warm and wonderful human being who will be missed by all whom he touched. His battle against his disease was a source of inspiration to all who knew him. He was deeply loved and will be sorely missed. He is with the angels now. His funeral Mass will be held at St. Anthony's Roman Catholic Church in Nanuet, N.Y. on Saturday, December 5th at 12 Noon. Interment to follow at Fern Cliff Cemetery in Hartsdale, N.Y. Contributions in his memory should be made to God's Love We Deliver 166 6 Ave. NYC, NY 10013.
