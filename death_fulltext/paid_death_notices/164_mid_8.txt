
        VATTER-Harold Goodhue. Age 89. On September 8, 2000. At his home in Portland, Oregon. Professor of American Economic History at Portland State University for 35 years. B.A. University of Wisconcin, M.A. Columbia, Ph.d University of California at Berkeley. Survived by Rita Jett of Holbrook, Arizona, Terry Vatter of Dundee, N.Y., Marc Vatter of Providence, R.I., three grandsons and Dr. Joy Spalding and her family.
