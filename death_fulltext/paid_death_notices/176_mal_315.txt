
        DUFFY-Christopher Michael. A resident of Crestwood. Died on September 11, 2001 in the attack on the World Trade Center. He was born February 19, 1978 in the Bronx to John and Kathleen Duffy. Chris grew up in Crestwood and attended Annunciation Grammar School and graduated from Fordham Preparatory School in 1996, where he captained the lacrosse team. He received his B.A. in Finance from Villanova in May 2000 and joined the investment firm Keefe, Bruyette & Woods the following month as an assistant trader on the firm's equity desk. He is survived by his parents, John and Kathleen, his brothers Brian and Kevin, his sisters Kara and Caitlin, and his grandmothers Anne Downey and Alice Langan. The family will be receiving friends on Friday, September 21, 2001 between the hours of 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM at The Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville, NY. Celebration of Life and Mass of the Resurrection Saturday, September 22, 10:30 AM at the Annunciation Church, Westchester Avenue, Crestwood. Donations in lieu of flowers should be made to The Chris Duffy Memorial Scholarship Fund, Fordham Preparatory School, East Fordham Road, Bronx, NY 10458.
