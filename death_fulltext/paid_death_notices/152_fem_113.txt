
        MURTAGH-Roberta F. Of Lake Worth, FL, died September 11, 1999, at the age of 76, with loving friends and family by her side in Gallatin, TN. She is survived by her six children Melinda, James, Robert, Richard, Kenneth, Hilary and their families. Mrs. Murtagh was born December 31, 1922, in NYC, where she spent the majority of her life. For the last 15 years Mrs. Murtagh's primary residence has been in southern Florida. Mrs. Murtagh is preceded in death by her husband of 49 years, James P. Murtagh, who died May 8, 1996, in Naples, FL. Church service will be held at a later date in New York.
