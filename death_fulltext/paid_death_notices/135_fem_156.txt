
        STEARNS-Virginia Davenport, widow of Hal H. Stearns, died April 19 in Seattle, WA. She was beloved by many friends and relatives who will greatly miss her participation in the NYC community, her special style and her wonderful sense of humor. Memorial donations may be sent to the East Side House Settlement, 337 Alexander Ave, Bronx, NY 10454 or the Manhattan Eye, Ear & Throat Hospital, 210 East 64th Street, NYC 10021. The Homrighausen, Mahon and the Woodyatt families will miss her love and friendship and extend condolences to her brothers.
