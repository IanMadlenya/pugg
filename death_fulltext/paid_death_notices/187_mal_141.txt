
        SCHATTMAN-Herbert. Died August 11. Son of the late Helen and Sam Schattman. Survived by his loving wife Edna and their children Richard and wife Ruth, Nancy-Ellen and husband Joseph, and his adored grandchildren, Joshua, Noah, Sarah, Rachel and Caitlin. He was with United Factors Corporation for 40 years, where he became Vice President, and then President and CEO of CBT Factors. Funeral private.
