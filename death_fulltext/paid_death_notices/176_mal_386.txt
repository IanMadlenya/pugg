
        MARDOVICH-Edward J. ''Mardo.'' Of Lloyd Harbor, tragically missing from the World Trade Center disaster where he was President of Euro Brokers, Inc. Forever cherished husband of Laura, loving and devoted father of Leigh (14), E.J. (12), Tori (11) and Joseph (8). Beloved son of Doris and Ed. Dear brother of Jamie and Doreen. Loving son-inlaw and brother-in-law, and adored uncle to 20 nieces and nephews. He will be terribly missed by his dear friends who are too many to mention. There will be a Remembrance Mass at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church in Centerport, New York, on Tuesday, September 25, at 6 PM. All are welcome to visit the Mardovich family at the Huntington Crescent Club, between 7 and 10 PM, on Tuesday, September 25. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the Edward J. Mardovich Memorial Fund, at Our Lady Queen of Martyrs.
