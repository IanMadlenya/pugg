
        LEVITAN--Florence, of New Rochelle, NY, died on Thursday, May 24, at her home. She was 87. Florence, daughter of Harry and Goldie Husid, was predeceased in 2006 by her beloved husband George and in 2005 by her cherished daughter Geordie Levitan. She was also predeceased by her brother Harry Husid and sister Edith Levitan. She is survived by her loving daughters Carol Smolen of Las Vegas, NV, Barbara Muller of Stamford, CT, and Joanne Levitan of Pelham, NY; and her granddaughters Jamie Kristofco of Denver, CO, Danielle Smolen and Brooke Feingold, both of Manhattan. Private funeral services were held on Friday, May 25.
