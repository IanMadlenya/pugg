
        STEINHORN-Harriet. Of Ellenville, New York and Aventura, FL., died October 2nd, 2002 in Port Chester, NY. She was the former owner and operator of the Grand Mountain Hotel in Greenfield Park, N.Y. She is survived by her loving daughter, Amy, & her husband, Brad Kreinces, one sister, Helen Haas, one brother, Howard Golden, and three adoring grandsons: Alex, Jason and Evan. Funeral and burial services were held on Thursday, October 3rd, in Ellenville, N.Y. ''She will always be in our hearts.''
