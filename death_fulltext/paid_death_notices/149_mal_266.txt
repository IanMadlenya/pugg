
        RAMSEY-Robert H., 89. Died Thursday, June 17, in Southbury, CT. He was the husband of Vera (Volkert) Ramsey of Southbury and loving father of Jill R. Edgar of Stamford, CT, grandfather of Christopher Edgar of Stamford and Timothy Edgar of New York City. He was predeceased by his son, Robert ''Sandy'' Ramsey. He was a retired executive for the Newmont Mining Company, St. Joe Minerals, and was editor-inchief of Engineering and Mining Journal.
