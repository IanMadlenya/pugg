
        WALLISON-Walter, on February 24, 2000, at the age of 93, and even then too soon. The beloved husband of Pearl, adored father of Peter and Frieda, and Jane and Hugh, revered grandpa to Ethan, Jeremy, Elizabeth and George, Rebecca, and Amanda, a gentle, loving and inspiring friend and companion to his adoring family, he chose his path to the very end, and will live always in our memories.
