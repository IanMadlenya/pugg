
        WHIPPLE--George Amiel II, 75, of NYC. Died April 16, 2005 at Cape Cod Hospital in Hyannis from complications of a stroke. He attended Andover and Massachusetts Institute of Technology. In 1949, he married the former Hope Auchincloss and they later divorced. He was a specialist on the New York Stock Exchange and an owner and operator of commercial real estate. Before his death, he was a financial advisor. Mr. Whipple was also an avid amateur pilot for many years. He leaves behind three children, Jack Van Horn Whipple II of Atlanta, GA, George A. Whipple III of Rye, NY, and Susan Whipple Wald of Los Angeles, CA, six grandchildren and Marjorie Sargent of Brewster, MA, his long time companion.
