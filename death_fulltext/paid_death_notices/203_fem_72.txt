
        BARRY--Sheila Anne. Beloved mother to Mark Weissman and Lael Kimble, grandmother and 'Ting' to Declan and Susanna, dear mother-inlaw to Lisa Ferris and Evan Kimble, passed in Santa Barbara on November 28. She died in the labor of the work she loved so deeply for over thirty years, producing and editing books for children. Her insight, wisdom and generous nature are missed. Contact Neptune Society for Memorial Service information at 805-569-1838. Contributions to UNICEF.
