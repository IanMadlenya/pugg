
        CHRISTOPHERSON-Donovan R. (''Dick''), 65, of Scottsdale, died on July 14, 1998. He was born in Ostrander, MN. on November 22, 1932. He graduated form St. John's University in Collegeville, MN in 1954, served as an officer in the U.S. Navy, and went on to earn his Master of Business Administration degree from the University of Minnesota. He worked for The Pillsbury Company & subsidiaries for twenty-three years, eventually holding the position of Chief Financial Officer of Burger King Corporation in Miami, Florida. From 1981 to 1994, he was a senior financial executive with Pepsico, Inc. in Purchase, NY. In 1994, he and his wife Mary retired to Scottsdale. They built their dream home in the desert surrounded by mountains, coyotes, javelinas and beautiful sunsets. Dick was a devoted husband, father, and grandfather. He is survived by his daughter, Mary Hoff, and her husband, Gene of Plymouth, MN; his son, Tom, and his wife, Judith of Schlossborn, Germany; his son, Jim, and his wife, Susan of Scottsdale, AZ; his daughter, Anne Christopherson of Corcoran, MN, and his grandchildren, Jan-Constantin and Nils-Frederick, both of Schlossborn, Germany, whom he dearly loved. His sister, Betty Wagner, and his brothers Robert, William, David, and Michael also survive Dick. A memorial Service will be held on Monday, July 27, 1998, at 2:00 PM at Messinger Mortuary & Chapel, 7601 E. Indian School Road, Scottsdale, AZ 85251. Memorial donations may be made to the Donovan R. Christopherson Scholarship Fund, in care of St. John's University, Collegeville, MN, 56321. Rest well, Papa.
