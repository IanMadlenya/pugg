
        SMYTH-James Valentine. On August 18, 2001, at 98 years of age. Devoted husband of Ella, with whom he is reunited, for 50 years. Beloved father, friend and counselor to James Kevin. Loving grandfather to Sara Elisabeth. Beloved brother to John, Catherine and Dorothy. Beloved uncle to three generations of Smyths, Taylors and Franklins. His wisdom, gentility and love of life will be sorely missed. Viewing Wednesday, August 22, 2-5 PM, 7-9 PM, Hugh Reid Funeral Home, 153 Greenpoint Ave., Brooklyn, NY. Funeral Mass Thursday, August 23, 8 AM, St. Anthony of Padua Church, 862 Manhattan Ave., Brooklyn, NY. Donations to Little Sisters of the Poor, Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221st St., Queens Village, NY 11429.
