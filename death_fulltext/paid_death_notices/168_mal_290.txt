
        STAVE-Bernard R. On January 19, at 96. Attorney for over 72 years in New York City. Graduate of Brooklyn Law School, in private practice for most of his life and on the legal staff of the New York State Banking Department in the 1940's. New York Secretary to Congressman Emanuel Celler, longtime Arbitrator in the Small Claims Court, former president of the Apex Society, Board Member, President and then longtime General Counsel of his beloved Southbridge Towers Housing Cooperative. For over 20 years he devoted himself tirelessly to the 1800 cooperators of Southbridge. The law was his passion and he served his clients with dignity, care and dedication. His pride and affection for his family were known to all with whom he came in contact. He was predeceased by wives Millie and Dinny. He leaves his longtime friend Tessie Wigler, two sons Howard and wife Renee and Bruce and wife Sondra, grandchildren Gregg and Chris, Marjorie and Allahyar, Channing and Sara, and three great grandchildren Elise, Yasmin and Soraya. Graveside services at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY on Sunday, January 21, at 1:00 P.M. Memorial contributions may be made to the North Shore LIJ Health System Foundation, 125 Community Drive, Great Neck, NY 11021.
        STAVE-Bernard R. We mourn the passing of Bernard R. Stave, father of our esteemed Trustee, Howard D. Stave, and extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire Stave family. Roy J.
