
        KETCHAM-Platt Vandewater, 89, formerly of Brooklyn, NY, and most recently of Charlotte, NC, died on Dec. 6, 1999, in Charlotte. He was employed for many years at the law firm of Greenbaum, Wolff & Ernst, of New York City. He is survived by two daughters, Deborah Abels, and son-in-law Wedge Abels of Charlotte, NC, and Nancy Lagmarsino and son-in-law John Lagomarsino of Emerson, NJ; and by three grandchildren. Memorial gifts may be made to The Good Shepherd Lutheran Church, General Memorial Fund, 7420 Fourth Avenue, Brooklyn, New York 11209.
