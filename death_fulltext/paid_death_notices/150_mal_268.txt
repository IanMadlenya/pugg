
        COLOZZA-Joseph, suddenly on July 14, 1999 in Florida at the age of 58. Devoted husband of Valentine, beloved father of Rosemarie and Joseph. Cherished brother of Julie Porcelli and Robert. Mr. Colozza was a member of the Board of Directors of the Downtown Athletic Club. He was Vice President of Local 1814 I.L.A. and Vice President of Longshoremans Atlantic Coast Division. Funeral Mass is on Saturday, July 17th, at 2 P.M., at Ascension Catholic Church, Boca Raton, Florida. In repose Babione Funeral Home, 1100 North Federal Highway, Boca Raton, Florida 33432.
