
        HOROWITZ--George Q on November 28, 2005. Beloved husband of Renee. Loving father of Seth, his wife Lori and Robin. Devoted son of Libby. Dear brother of Don, Linda, Ruby and Jackie. George became CEO and Chairman of Everlast Worldwide Inc., in October 2000 after spending over 30 years in the apparel business, prior to which he was an educator in the NYC school system. He was very active in charitable organizations and a board member at the American Heart Association and the S.G.M.A. He left an everlasting impression on everyone he met with his dynamic personality and warm heart. A memorial service will be held Monday, December 5, 2005, 10:00 A.M. at Temple EmanuEl, Fifth Ave at 65th St. Contributions may be made to Dr. Theodore A. Atlas Foundation, PO Box 140998, Staten Island, NY 10304 and SloanKettering Kidney Cancer Research, New York City.
