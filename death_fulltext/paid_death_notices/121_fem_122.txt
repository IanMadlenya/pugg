
        FISHMAN-Sara. Cherished wife and best friend of husband Bernard. Loving mother of Carol Beth, mother-in-law of David, and dear grandmother of Molly and Ethan. On Feb. 16, 1997. Esteemed member for 22 years of the medical social work team of Babies Hospital at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. Her insightful, tender and courageous love for her family, friends and colleagues will be profoundly missed. Service at noon on Wednesday 2/19 at the Riverside Chapel, 180 West 76 St. at Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The American Cancer Society.
