
        KELFON-Theodore Thomas. The son of Daisy Kelfon Lockley of Baltimore, Md. Died suddenly on June 11, 2000 in Goldwater Hospital in Long Island, NY. He leaves to mourn a son, Theodore Kelfon Williams of New York, a mother Daisy Kelfon Lockley, six sisters, Doris, Gloria, Eleanor, Alice, Roxann and Hazel, four brothers, Godfrey, Herman, James and Carl all of Baltimore.
