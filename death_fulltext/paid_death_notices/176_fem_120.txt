
        DALSIMER-Dorothy. Loving widow of Philip, active and devoted mother, caring and supportive grandmother, adoring great grandmother, a sister and good friend of many, died September 9, 2001 at a youthful 88. She was a cherished part of our lives. Memorial service to be held Sunday, October 21, 1:00 PM at the Unitarian Universalist Congregation for the South Fork, Water Mill, NY. Donations in her memory may be made to the Peconic Land Trust, Southampton, NY, or the Unitarian Universalist Congregation of the South Fork, Building Fund, Water Mill, NY 11976.
