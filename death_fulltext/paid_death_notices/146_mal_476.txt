
        HORTON-Clifford (Bobby). 48. Died at home of congestive heart failure on February 14. Bobby was known for his ''joie de vivre''. He loved making people smile and could always be found at Christmas playing Santa Claus for needy children. For the past ten years, he organized and funded a profuse gift giving campaign at Christmas for New York's underprivileged children. Bobby was the head of the patternmaking department at Tyme Design. Bobby is survived by his wife, Patricia, and son, Kenny. His family, many friends and colleagues will all miss him dearly. Memorial service will be held at a later date.
