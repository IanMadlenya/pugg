
        BARKLEY-Dan. Of New City, NY, died suddenly on June 7. Beloved husband of Barbara. Devoted father of Mitchell and Jill. Cherished son of Margot. Loving grandfather of Brian, Alex, Jonah and Samuel. Adored father-in-law of Amy and Ronnie. This extraordinary person of unusual accomplishment and character was taken too soon, and will be remembered by his many friends, colleagues and family for his charm, grace, honor and integrity. Services on Sunday at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ, at 11 AM.
