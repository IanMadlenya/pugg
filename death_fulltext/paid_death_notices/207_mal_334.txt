
        SOBERMAN--Robert J., M.D. Passed away on April 27, 2004. The Board of Directors and staff of the New York Organ Donor Network mourn the loss of a distinguished pioneer of organ transplantation. Dr. Soberman was a leading advocate of organ donation, a founder of our organization in 1978, and a former member of our board and medical board. His dedication helped to ensure the success of kidney and pancreas transplantation, the fair distribution of organs for transplant, and the saving of countless lives. Rocco F. Andriola, Esq. Chairperson NY Organ Donor Network
