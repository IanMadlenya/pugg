
        WEINBERGER--Caspar W. The Board of the American Friends of The Churchill Museum at the Cabinet War Rooms (London) mourns the loss of our fellow Trustee, Caspar W. Weinberger. We value the passion he devoted to raising awareness of the legacy of Sir Winston S. Churchill through the new museum in London. We extend our sympathy to Mrs. Weinberger and their family. Sir Deryck Maughan, Chair Phil Reed, Director
