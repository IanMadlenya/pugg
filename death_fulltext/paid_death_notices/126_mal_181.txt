
        TRETTER-Victor. The Board of Trustees and Administration of the New York College of Podiatric Medicine and the foot clinics of New York are saddened to learn of the passing of Mr. Victor Tretter, one of our distinguished board members. Mr. Tretter served the college and clinics for many years with distinction. Our thoughts and prayers are extended to his wife Agnes and his family. Alfred Gerosa, Chairman Louis Levine, President
