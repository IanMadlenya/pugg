
        KADISH-Henrietta. Died in her sleep at her home in Metuchen, NJ, on Monday, May 10, 1999. Born in Brooklyn, she lived in Millburn, NJ, and New Brunswick, NJ, before moving to Metuchen 32 years ago. She was the widow of Irving J. Kadish, a land developer and builder. She was a bookkeeper, a life member of Hadassah, B'Nai Brith, the sisterhood of Temple Neve Shalom, all of Metuchen, and the Senior Club of the Jewish Community Center, Edison. She was a friend of the Albert Einstein Society, the Sloan Kettering Hospital and Channel 13, Public Television. She is survived by her three children, Gerald, of New Jersey, Marcia K. Bershtein, of Edison, NJ, and Richard, of Rockville, MD. She leaves eight grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Services will be held on Tuesday, May 11th, at Goldstein Funeral Chapel, 2015 Woodbridge Avenue, Edison, New Jersey, at 1 P.M., followed by interment in Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, NJ.
