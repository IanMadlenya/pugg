
        GERNSBACK--Madelon, 98, on March 29, 2007, in Ponte Vedra Beach, FL of natural causes. Born July 19, 1909, in New York City, daughter of famed inventor, publisher and ''father of science fiction'' Hugo Gernsback and Rose Harvey Gernsback. She spent most of her adult life in Italy working for her father's publishing house covering scientific and cultural affairs throughout Europe. Beloved half-sister of Bertina Baer and Jocelyn Neichin. Dearest sister of the late M. Harvey Gernsback. She is also survived by her cherished niece and nephew Wendy Davidson and Mark Gernsback.
