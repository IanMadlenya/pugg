
        KUIPERS--Henry G. Lord, Abbett and Co. LLC extends its deepest sympathy and condolences to the family of Henry G. Kuipers, who died on April 12th, 2004. Mr. Kuipers retired from the firm in 1980 after 47 years of dedicated service, which makes him one of the longest service employees, if not THE longest service employee, to date with the firm. At the time of retirement, he was a Senior Vice President of Lord Abbett mutual funds and the head of Equity and Fixed Income Trading having worked his way up from an entry level clerical position.
