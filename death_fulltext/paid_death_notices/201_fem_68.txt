
        MANKIN--Janey. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Janey Mankin, October 13, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family. Services will be held on Wednesday, October 15th, 2003, at 11 A.M. at Temple Beth El of Great Neck, Long Island. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
