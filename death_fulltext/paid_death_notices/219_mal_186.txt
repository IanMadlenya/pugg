
        DAVEY--Bruce James. 77, on April 13, 2005. He retired as senior vice-president and treasurer of New York Life Insurance Company in 1992 after 34 years of service. Beloved father of Lynn Leahey, Laurie Mindich, Bruce Davey, Jr. and Suzanne Manelski, husband of Julia Twyford Davey, and 10 grandchildren. His integrity, loyalty and wry sense of humor will be missed. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to St. George's-by-the-River, Rumson, NJ.
