
        SILVER-David Aaron. Born February 4, 1913, in New York. Beloved husband of Claire. Loving father to Esther, Carole, Joseph, Renee and Harvey. Grandfather to Annie, Scott, Adam and Jenny. David's generosity of spirit enriched the lives of all around him, and instilled in his family the values of kindness, community, humanity and responsibility. He delighted in his family and his laughter honored us all. Services today, 1 PM, Riverside-Nassau Memorial Chapel, Great Neck.
