
        RUSSELL -- Wendy Vander Poel. The trustees, faculty and staff of Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory are deeply saddened at the passing of Wendy Vander Poel Russell. Mrs. Russell was a member of the Board of Trustees of the Laboratory since 1984, serving as Secretary from 1985-87 and 1992-97 and thereafter as an Honorary Trustee. Her grace, leadership and irrepressible enthusiasm were valued by everyone at Cold Spring Harbor. She will be greatly missed. To her daughters, family and many friends, we express our deepest sympathy. Eduardo Mestre, Chairman Bruce Stillman, President James D. Watson, Chancellor W. Dillaway Ayres, Chief Operating Officer
