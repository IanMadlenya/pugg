
        KEAGY-Ann Lavaty. Parsons School of Design, a division of New School University, mourns the loss of Ann Lavaty Keagy, who chaired the Department of Fashion Design from 1953 until 1982 when she retired. For three decades Mrs. Keagy educated two generations of the best & brightest on Seventh Avenue, including Donna Karan, Louis Dell'Olio, Jeffrey Banks and Zack Carr. While at Parsons Mrs. Keagy initiated the legendary Designer Critic program, which remains today as a lasting legacy. Marie Essex, Chair, and the Faculty of the Department of Fashion Design
