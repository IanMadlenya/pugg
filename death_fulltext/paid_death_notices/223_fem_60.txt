
        GARFINKLE -- Susan Cole man. 62. Passed away on Saturday August 6 following a two year battle with pancreatic cancer. She died on her own terms surrounded by her loving family in her home. Susan passed exactly the way she lived, with an inspirational amount of strength and dignity. Although Susan left this world far too soon, her mark will endure. She is survived by her beloved husband Ray, her sister Nancy, her children Jeffrey, Tara and Neil, her daughter-in-law Christina, and her grandchildren Maxwell and Benjamin. Services are today, 10:00 AM, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ. Donations in her memory may be made to the Lustgarten Organization for Pancreatic Cancer.
