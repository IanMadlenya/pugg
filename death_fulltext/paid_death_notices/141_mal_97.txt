
        McCABE-Eugene L. The Board of Directors and Staff of Weston United Community Renewal, Inc. deeply mourn the untimely death of our Board President and distinguished leader, Eugene L. McCabe. His compassion and profound commitment to all of those in medical need assured that the least fortunate are entitled to receive services with dignity and respect. His leadership created a new vision, beacon of hope, and inspiration to those whom we serve. Mr. McCabe will be deeply missed, but his inspirational presence will be constant and secure as we move forward in making his dream a reality. The work done by Mr. McCabe will forever speak for him. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family and to The North General Hospital community. M. Moran Weston, Founder and Pres. Emeritus Sharon Lockhart-Carter
        McCABE-Eugene L. The partners, associates and staff of Epstein Becker & Green, P.C., are saddened by the death of Eugene L. McCabe, an extraordinary leader, innovator and visionary. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Elsie, and his entire family. Epstein Becker & Green, P.C.
