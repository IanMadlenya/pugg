
        SIEGAL-Vivian. The Board of Directors and Staff of the 92nd Street Y mourn the passing of Vivian W. Siegal, our dear friend and esteemed board member for over 40 years. Her years of commitment to the 92nd Street Y are deeply appreciated. Our hearts and prayers go out to Vivian's brother Leonard Weiner, son and daughter-in-law, Frederick P. and Marta Siegal, daughter and son-inlaw, Marian and Jack Krauskopf, as well as beloved grandchildren David, Nina, Lewis and Katie. She will be missed by all who knew her. Philip Milstein, President Joseph Leff, Chairman Sol Adler, Executive Director
