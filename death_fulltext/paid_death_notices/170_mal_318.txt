
        WOLF-Harold Died on Friday, March 23, 2001. Mr. Wolf was a resident of Far Rockaway and Lawrence since 1937. He was the President of the Harold Wolf Insurance Agency. He is survived by his wife Helen of 63 years, his children Richard and Judith Wolf, and Marjorie and Walter Groden, his grandchildren Elisabeth and Chris Schmitz, Neil Groden, Cindy and Lenny Atlas, and Sherry and Lee Sustar. He is also survived by his great-grandchildren Eric and Ryan Schmitz, and Zoe Atlas. Services Monday, 11:30 AM, at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapels,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI.
