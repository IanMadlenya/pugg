
        WILSON--John Donald. Of Bronxville, NY, died June 30th at the age of 91 from Parkinsons Disease. He worked at The Chase Manhattan Bank as Chief Economist and after his retirement, wrote ''The Chase,'' the history of The Chase Manhattan Bank 1945 to 1985. He was married to Danesi Matthews Hilton, of Cincinnati, OH, and leaves four children, Nina Lihn, of Washington, DC, John Douglas Wilson, of Okemos, MI, David M. Wilson, of Lancaster, PA, and Mary Danesi Thomas, of Hudson, MA, and six grandchildren.
