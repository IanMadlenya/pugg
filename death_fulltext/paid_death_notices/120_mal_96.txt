
        MASON - Bimy, Jr. 87, on January 5, 1997, at his home in Rye, NY. Beloved husband of Elizabeth Smith (nee Brownson), father of Jerome A. of New York City, and grandfather of Herome A. Jr. and Elizabeth F. Retired Chairman of Union Carbide Corporation after nearly 40 years of service (1932-1971). Served on the Board of Directors of Bradford National Corporation, Consolidation. Coal Company, The Fidelity and Casualty Company of New York, Manufacturers Hanover Trust, Metropolitan Life Insurance Company and North American Philips Corporation. Public governor of the New York Stock Exchange from 1966 to 1969, and also served as a member of the Board of Trustees of Cornell University from 1964 to 1969. A member of the Business Council, serving as Vice-Chairman from 1966 to 1969. Chairman of the Council's Liaison committee with the Council of Economic Advisors in 1970. A member of the Economic Development Council from 1966 to 1968. A member of the National Industrial Pollution Control Council, serving as Co-Chairman of the Chemicals Sub-Council from 1970 to 1971. Received the National Brotherhood Award from the National Conference of Christians and Jews in 1969. A trustee of the Presbyterian Hospital in New York City from 1967 to 1976, United Hospital in Port Chester, NY, from 1976 to 1983, and of Cornell University from 1964 to 1969. Also a trustee of the John A. Hartford Foundation. A graduate of the Hill School in Pottstown, PA, and Cornell University, where he received a bachelor of science degree in chemical engineering in 1931. A member of the Larchmont Yacht Club, Blind Brook Club, and Apawamis Club in Rye. Services will be private.
