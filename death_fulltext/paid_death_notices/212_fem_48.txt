
        BROMANTE -- Palma. On September 4, 2004 in Norwalk CT. She was CEO of Sirmos, a corporation serving the interior design and hospitality industries. She was 74 and resided in NYC and Weston CT. Beloved mother of Karen Alvarez, Craig Corona, Kevin Corona and Wayne Corona. Cherished grandmother of ten. Dear companion of Joseph Del Guidice. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Tuesday, September 7, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Wednesday, September 8, 11AM at St. Francis of Assisi R.C. Church, 35 Norfield Rd, Weston, CT.
