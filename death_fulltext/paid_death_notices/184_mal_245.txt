
        GASPARINETTI-Luigi. The Board of Trustees and staff of WLIW21 mourn the untimely passing of our esteemed Board Member, Luigi Gasparinetti on May 16, 2002. Luigi was a dedicated member of our Board and a generous contributor of both time and money in support of our mission of bringing the finest quality public television to our audience. His vision, energy and commitment will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to his family.
        GASPARINETTI-Luigi. All of us at The Howard Gilman Foundation are saddened by the death of our fellow Director, Luigi Gasparinetti. To his family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. Isabella Rossellini, Chairman Arlene Shuler, Executive Director
