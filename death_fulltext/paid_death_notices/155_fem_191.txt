
        DOYLE-Jean P. Age 72. On December 17 after a long illness. Born in Quincy, Mass. and raised in Teaneck, N.J. Mrs. Doyle (nee Pardey) studied nursing at Muhlenberg College and Teachers College of Columbia University. Registered nurse, she served for many years on the staff of New York Hospital and the Payne Whitney Clinic before retiring to Brielle, N.J. The widow of Colonel John F. Doyle, she is survived by her daughter, Mary Jane Driscoll of Tarrytown, N.Y. and her sister, Elisabeth Fox of Springfield, Ill. Burial will be at Arlington National Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to The American Cancer Society.
