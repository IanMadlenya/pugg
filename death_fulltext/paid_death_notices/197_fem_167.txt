
        JOHNSON--Jennifer Worthington, 23, of Greenwich, CT. died on the morning of June 18th, in Jackson, WY. in a car accident. Beloved daughter of Worthington and Maria Johnson, Jennifer graduated from Greenwich Country Day School, Choate Rosemary Hall in Wallingford, CT and Colgate University with honors, where she was also a sister in Kappa Kappa Gamma. Jennifer had been living in Jackson since last September and was passionate about her life and work there. Besides her parents, she is survived by her sisters, Nina Johnson of San Francisco, Handley Johnson and Stephanie Johnson of Greenwich, her fraternal grandmother, Annemarie Cochran of Southport, CT and her maternal grandfather, James R. James, Jr. of St. Louis, MO. A celebration of her life will take place at her parent's home on Saturday, June 28th, 3:00 to 7:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to Jen's favorite cause, The Teton Valley Ranch Education Fund.
