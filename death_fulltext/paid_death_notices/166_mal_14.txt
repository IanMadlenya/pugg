
        KANE-James E., 88, of West Hartford, CT. Died October 31, 2000. Born on October 16th, 1912 in Bridgeport, CT, he served during WWII and retired in 1972 from the Veteran's Administration as an Assistant Chief Attorney. He is survived by his wife of 57 years, Alice Kane; a daughter and son-inlaw, Patricia (Kane) and W. Scott Braznell of Hamden, CT., and two grandchildren. He was predeceased by a son, John Riley Kane.
