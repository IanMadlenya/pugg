
        GURTNER--Gregory John, MD, gastroenterologist and scientist at Washington University, St. Louis, MO, August 11, 2004 at the age of 36. Born in Buffalo, NY and raised in Baltimore, MD, attended Gilman School, received his undergraduate degree at Vanderbilt, TN followed by medical school at NY Medical College, Valhalla, NY and then moved to St. Louis, MO where he received post-graduate training in Internal Medicine and Gastroenterololgy. While working with world-renowned scientists at Washington University, Dr. Gurtner became interested in understanding the fundamental mechanisms of human disease and developing novel approaches for their cure. He enjoyed traditional American tavern cuisine, St. Louis Cardinals and Rams and was an accomplished musician. Survived by his mother, Nancy (nee Cash) Gurtner, Ed.D of Ridgefield, CT, his brother Geoffrey Gurtner, MD of Manhattan, NYH. Services, Monday, 1pm, St. Andrew's Lutheran Church, Ridgefield, CT. Burial to follow. Visiting at the Kane Funeral Home, 25 Catoonah St, Ridgefield on Sunday, 4pm to 7pm. A memorial service will be held at Washington University, St. Louis, MO at a date to be announed. Contributions to the American Cancer Society or the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America would be appreciated.
