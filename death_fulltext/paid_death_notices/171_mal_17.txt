
        ROTHSCHILD-Karl. Passed away Thursday, March 29, 2001 in his 96th year. Beloved husband of the late Wally Rothschild; loved father of Herbert and Marie Rothschild, Bernette and Irwin Bernstein; adored grandfather and greatgrandfather of 14 grandchildren and fifteen great-grandchildren. He lived a long and fulfilled life. He will be missed.
