
        VIEBRANZ--Alfred C. The St. Lawrence University Board of Trustees, in official action, has honored late University benefactor and volunteer leader Alfred C. Viebranz '42. Mr. Viebranz was president of the Alumni Executive Council, chair of the Board of Trustees and at the time of his death on February 18, chairman emeritus of the Board. He received the honorary degree Doctor of Laws from St. Lawrence for his service and in recognition of his career in corporate communications. The personification of the liberal arts, Mr. Viebranz earned a degree in physics, served as a US Naval officer; worked as an engineer, business executive and historian; and claimed writing as his passion. His generosity helps ensure that St. Lawrence students benefit each year from working with a distinguished visiting writer.
