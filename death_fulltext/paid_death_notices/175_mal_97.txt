
        BRACKMAN - George Martin. With great sadness we announce his peaceful passing at home on August 6th, 2001. Composer, arranger, B.S. & M.S. Juilliard 1948. Military Order of the Purple Heart. Husband, father, and grandfather. He is survived by his wife Julia, his four children Alison, Wendy, Adam, Peter and his four grandchildren Robin, Tarah, Kyle and Ethan.
