
        ALBIZU--Olga. We mourn the loss of our beloved family member and lifelong friend who died peacefully at home in NY on July 30, 2005. A contemporary abstract artist, she was born in Ponce, P.R. on May 31, 1924. She studied painting with Esteban Vicente in Puerto Rico and Hans Hoffman in New York. Her works won critical acclaim and are in the permanent collection of several museums. A service will be held, Sat. August 6 at Funeraria J. Oliver, Ponce P.R. Carmen Matilda Vivas Edwyn Tiryakian, Edmund Tiryakian, Adele Siegal, Josefina Tiryakian, Edward Tiryakian
