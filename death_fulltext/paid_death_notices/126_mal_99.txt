
        EPSTEIN-Harold, age 94, died on July 8 at his home and garden in Larchmont, NY. Beloved husband of Esta, also survived by his daughters Laurel Loeb and Susan Cherbuliez and son-in-law Dr. Theodore Cherbuliez, his granddaughters Ariane, Nicole, Lisa, Diana, and Juliette, and Lisa's husband John Terry, and his great granddaughter Eloise Terry. Memorial services will be held on Thursday, July 10 at 4 P.M., at Temple Israel, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd., New Rochelle. Donations may be made to Wave Hill, 675 W. 252 St., Bx, NY 10471, where a Fall Celebration will be held to honor Harold's extraordinary dedication and contribution to horticulture.
        EPSTEIN-Harold. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its cherished member and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. Amiel Wohl, Rabbi
