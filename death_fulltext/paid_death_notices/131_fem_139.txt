
        SALMORE-Helen L. Beloved and devoted wife for over fifty-two years of Walter. Dear sister of Murray Lerner. Cherished, adored and loving mother of Evan and Stephen. Devoted proud and admired grandmother of Michael, Paul and Elizabeth. Adored best friend and mother-in-law of Natalie and Barbara Salmore. Those who knew her will always have fond memories of her impeccable style and taste, her honesty, her energy, her unerring common sense and her love and loyalty to family and friends. We thank her for always being there for us. Services were held at Union Field Hungarian Society Synagogue, Cypress Avenue and Cypress Hill Street, Glendale, New York.
