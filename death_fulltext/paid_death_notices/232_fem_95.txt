
        MILLER--Edith B. Of Rancho Santa Margarita, California and longtime resident of Scarsdale, New York on April 9, 2006. Born on June 16, 1920, the daughter of Frances and Joseph Blaustein. Beloved wife of the late Howard. Devoted sister of Anita. Mother of Kenneth, Margie and Robert. Grandma of Jill and Jason & great-grandmother of Morgan and McKenna. She was an accomplished artist and wonderful friend who will be missed by all who knew her. Service will be held on Friday May 19, at 3 PM at ''The Riverside''.
