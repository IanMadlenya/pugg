
        BENDER-William, 69, of Fort Pierce, Fla., died November 5, 1998. A native of New York, he came to Ft. Pierce from NY 10 years ago. He was an apartment building manager in New York City. Survivors include sister, Lillian Bender of NY; niece, Deborah Egan of Sayreville, NJ; nephews, Richard and Timothy Egan and brother in law, Mickey Egan, all of New York City, and long time companion Charles Brink of Ft. Pierce. He was predeceased by his parents William and Lillian Bender and sister Joyce Egan. HaisleyHobbs Funeral Home, Fort Pierce, Florida.
