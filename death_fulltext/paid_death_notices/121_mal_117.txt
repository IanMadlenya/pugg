
        WILCOX-Herbert B., Jr, MD. The Association of the Alumni of the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia Univesity records with sadness the death of its dedicated alumnus and director emeritus on February 5, 1997. A graduate of the Class of 1934, Dr. Wilcox distinguished himself in his service with the Second General Presbyterian Hospital during World War II and as a long-time member of the clinical faculty of the college and the medical staff of Presbyterian Hospital. We join in expressing sincere condolences to the family. Herbert Pardes, M.D. Vice President for Health Sciences Dean of the Faculty of Medicine and his many friends in the P&S Alumni Association
