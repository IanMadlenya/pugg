
        OLDEN--Marc. Novelist, on September 5 in New York City. A highly regarded crime writer with 40 published books, his book ''Poe Must Die'' is considered the definitive novel on Edgar Allan Poe. With advanced degree black belts in Japanese Karate and Aikido, he coached and mentored many members of the NYPD in Aikido. He was President of the Vedanta Society of New York. At the time of his death he was nearing completion of a circa 1939 spy thriller. Adored son of Courtenaye. Beloved companion of 30 years to Diane Crafford. His integrity, humor and wisdom will be deeply missed by his many friends.
