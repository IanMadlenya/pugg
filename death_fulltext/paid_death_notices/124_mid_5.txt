
        GOLDMAN-Muriel F. ''A woman of beauty and grace.'' Beloved wife of David. Devoted mother of William and Dr. Irene Goldman. Dear sister of Richard Friedman. Funeral services Sunday at noon at Gutterman-Musicant, Hackensack. Contributions to Wurzweiler School of Social Work of Yeshiva University, NYC, appreciated.
        GOLDMAN-Muriel. The Board of Governors of the Wurzweiler School of Social Work of Yeshiva University mourns the passing of Muriel Goldman, wife of David Goldman, our friend and fellow trustee. We extend our heartfelt condolences to David and his entire family on their tragic loss. David I. Schachne, Chairman Wurzweiler School of Social Work
        GOLDMAN-Muriel. The board of governors and membership of Edgewood Country Club record with deep sorrow the death of Mrs. Muriel Goldman, an esteemed member and extend to her beloved husband David and family its deepest sympathy. Howard Friedberg, President Eric Segal, Secretary
