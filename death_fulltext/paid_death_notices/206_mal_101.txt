
        GOTTFRIED--Ted M. Age 75. Died March 7, 2004. A lifelong New Yorker, he was the author of over 100 books ranging from racy adult fiction to young adult non-fiction, including a notable series on the Holocaust. He worked for social justice, first in the civil rights and anti-war movements, recently in the peace movement. He was an avid chess player, playing for the Stuyvesant Town Chess Club. He was a founding member of the National Writers' Union. A world wide traveler, he also enjoyed kayaking and vacationing in Vermont. He is survived by his wife Harriet, five children, Julie, Daniel, Katherine, Toby and Valerie, two stepdaughters, Melanie and Lisa, twelve grand children and many friends. He will be greatly missed. Memorial service will be held Saturday, March 13th at All Souls Unitarian Church in New York at 11AM. Memorial donations may be made to the American Civil Liberties Union.
