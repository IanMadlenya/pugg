
        KERST-Richard Nevin, age 83, of Katonah, N.Y., passed away on April 22, 1997. He was a graduate of the University of Wisconsin. Mr. Kerst served as a Lieutenant in the Navy aboard the USS Phoenix in the Pacific Theatre during World War II. He was associated with the Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center in NYC for 34 years until retiring in 1979 as Vice Chairman of the Board of Trustees. Mr. Kerst began his career in 1945 as the Assistant Comptroller, and was promoted to Comptroller, Assistant SecretaryTreasurer, Vice President of Administration, and then President from 1975-1977. He was a member of the Board of the Hecksher Foundation for Children for many years and was a member of the Board of Trustees for the Katonah Museum of Art. Beloved husband of Barbara Clark Kerst. Father of R. Duncan Kerst of Portland, OR, and Katrena K. Engstrom of Woodbridge, CT. Brother of Kenneth Kerst of Heathville, VA. Grandfather of Nicholas Engstrom, Cameron Kerst, and Allison Kerst. He was predeceased by his first wife, Annie Lee Duncan. Friends are invited to attend a memorial service on Monday, June 2, 1997, at 11 A.M. in the South Salem Presbyterian Church, South Salem, NY. Committal will be private. Memorial contributions may be made to the Katonah Museum of Art, Route 22 at Jay Street, Katonah, New York 10536.
