
        CHRISTMAS-Walter. Died January 26, 2002, at age 83. Beloved husband of June. Devoted father of Vincent, Rachel, and Gordon. Caring father-in-law of Karlton and Lisa. Adored grandfather of Shamsa, Romare, Miles, and Khari. After many years in corporate and nonprofit public relations, he retired to become a travel writer and public and independent school volunteer. His family, who will always cherish him, will hold a memorial service. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Jackie Robinson Foundation, for the Spike Lee Youth Motivation Award, 3 West 35th Street, New York, NY 10001.
