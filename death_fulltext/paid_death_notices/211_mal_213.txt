
        SPARBER--Norman. The officers, directors and staff of the Herman Goldman Foundation mourn the loss of their colleague and good friend, and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. A past president and longtime director of the foundation, he will be missed for his strength, his warmth and his ceaseless dedication to the promotion of good works.
        SPARBER--Norman H. Norman provided a lifetime of good counsel to many and support to good causes in our community. He will be sorely missed by those who knew him over so many years. His friends at Kahn Brothers & Co., Inc.
