
        WEIN - Professor Leon. The Brooklyn Law School Community mourns the loss of a beloved colleague and esteemed teacher. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Chaya, his children, Baruch, Simcha, Chaim and Rifka, and his entire family. Paul Windels, Jr., Chairman of the Bd. of Trustees Joan G. Wexler, Dean
