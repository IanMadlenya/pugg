
        ARONS-Eleanor On December 27, 1998, at the age of 83 at Morningside Nursing Home, Bronx, NY. She is survived by her three children, Ludwig, Raymond and Lynne; three grandchildren, Jonathan, Nicholas and Anthony Arons. She was a gifted painter, studying with Samuel Breecher, and created master works and private collections throughout the country. She was married to Hy Arons, a popular pianist of the 1940s and 1950s who died in 1991. She will be remembered for her talent, zest for life and charismatic personality.
