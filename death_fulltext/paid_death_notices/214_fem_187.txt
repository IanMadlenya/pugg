
        BEHAR-BROWN--Pearl. Of Long Beach, NY, passed away on November 25, 2004. For many years Pearl had been the Court Clerk of the City of Long Beach. She was beloved by all who knew her for her heart of gold, of which she gave unselfishly. She will be sorely missed by family and friends who will cherish her memory. Pearl is survived by her husband Bernard (Chuck) Brown, sister Bette Jaffe, brother Louis Beraha, nieces and nephews, grandchildren Kim and Michael, and great-grandchildren Christopher and Dominique. Services at Gutterman's, 175 North Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY, at 10 am, Sunday, November 28, 2004.
