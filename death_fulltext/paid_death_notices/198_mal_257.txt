
        BENNETT -- Harold. The American Bible Society mourns the passing of the Rev. Dr. Harold C. Bennett, Vice Chairman of the American Bible Society Board of Trustees. He served on the Board since 1980. Dr. Bennett had a long and illustrious ministry with the Southern Baptist Convention including service as one of the Vice Presidents of the Baptist World Alliance. The Society extends its condolences to Dr. Bennett's wife, Phyllis, of Brentwood, TN, and sons, Jeffrey Clark Bennett and John Scott Bennett and daughter, Cynthia Ann (Bennett) Howard. His love and enthusiasm for the Bible cause was inspirational. He will be missed. >>AD#
