
        BENNETT-William August, 80, died October 16, 1997, in Houston, Texas. Mr. Bennett was born on April 17, 1917, in Brooklyn, New York, attended New York University, where he was a member of Beta Gamma Sigma and graduated in 1939. He married Doreen May Snoddy in 1943, and they were together for over fifty-two years. Mr. Bennett was Senior Vice President of Frank B. Hall & Company of New York. He retired in 1982, to Fripp Island, South Carolina. He was a member and Elder in the Presbyterian Church. Mr. Bennett was preceded in death by his wife, Doreen May Bennett and is survived by his daughter, Barbara Bennett Collier and son-in-law, Timothy J. Collier of Houston, Texas; two granddaughters, Heather Carlisle Collier and Erin Leigh Collier of New York City, New York. Memorial services will be held in Beaufort, South Carolina. For those desiring, memorial contributions may be given to The Hospice at the Texas Medical Center, 1905 Holcombe Boulevard, Houston, Texas 77030 or the Friends of Caroline Hospice, Beaufort, South Carolina.
