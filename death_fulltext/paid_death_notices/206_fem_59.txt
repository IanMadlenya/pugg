
        AUGENTI--I. Edith, 88 of NY, NY passed away February 28th. Born in Brooklyn, NY June 28th, 1915 the daughter of Dominick and Clara (Cassino) DiRocco. She was predeceased by her beloved husband Bruno J. Augenti and a sister, Sally Kalli. She is survived by her son Jory B. Augenti and wife Brenda, her daughter Marta A. Ettline and husband David, grandchildren, nieces and nephews. She will be remembered with love and admiration by her family and many devoted friends. Memorial donations may be made to: Memorial and Honor Program, St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis, TN 38105.
