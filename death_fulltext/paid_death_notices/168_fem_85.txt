
        DAY-Marsha, 52. On January 7, 2001 of brain cancer. A social worker, Marsha was a national leader for runaway and homeless youth, gay youth and an activist for HIV/AIDS and women's rights. Marsha is survived by her partner, Gillian Francis, mother & brother. Funeral is in Abilene, Texas on January 10th. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Astraea Foundation-Marsha Day Memorial Fund, 116 E. 16th St., NYC 10003.
