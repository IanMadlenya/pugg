
        TAUBER-Laszlo M.D. Died July 28, 2002 of congestive heart failure at his home in Potomac MD. He was 87 years old. He is survived by his wife Diane, his son Dr. Alfred Tauber, his daughter Dr. Ingrid Tauber, his stepdaughter Rachael Tauber and four grandchildren; Joel, Dylan, Benjamin and Hana. Services will be held July 30 at 12:00 PM, at the Washington Hebrew Congregation. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Tauber Institute for the Study of European Jewry at Brandeis University, C/0 Sylvia Fuks Fried, Mailstop 075, Waltham, MA 02254.
        TAUBER-Dr. Laszlo. The Trustees and Staff of the Museum of Jewish HeritageA Living Memorial to the Holocaust, express profound sadness on the passing of Dr. Laszlo Tauber, a Holocaust survivor, Museum founder, Trustee, and devoted and staunch supporter of education and Holocaust-related causes. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family. Robert M. Morgenthau Chairman David G. Marwell Director
