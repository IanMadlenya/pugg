
        BENDER-William. Born September 3, 1917, died at home February 7, 2003. Survived by his daughters Debbie and Amanda; his niece Susan Rotgard and his dog Freyda. A lifelong leftist and union organizer, born and lived in NYC until retiring to his home in Southampton. Services Friday, February 14, 2003 at 2pm at Brockett Funeral Home, 203 Hampton Road, Southampton. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be sent to causes he cared deeply about, ARF, Southampton Hospital or ACLU.
