
        PITMAN-David B., M.D. Age 89, on February 13. Beloved husband of Velma ''Mimi'' (Smale), devoted father of Roger, Douglas, Jeffrey and Peter, cherished brother of Laura Kaplan. Born in Manhattan, he received an M.D. degree in 1933 and practiced in Port Washington until 1972. He was the first physician from Nassau County called for service in the US Army during WW II. In 1962 he received the University of Vermont's Distinguished Alumni Service Award. Donations may be made to the University of Vermont College of Medicine, Century Club, which he founded.
