
        HARRIS-Jeanette. The Grand Street Boys Association and The Grand Street Boys Foundation announced with extreme sorrow the passing of our beloved Executive Secretary, Jeanette Harris. She served our organizations and our charitable endeavors for fifty years. Her charm and wonderful spirit will always be with us. Funeral services at St. Andrew's Episcopal Church, Castle Hill & Lafayette Ave, The Bronx, Friday, November 17 at 11 AM.
