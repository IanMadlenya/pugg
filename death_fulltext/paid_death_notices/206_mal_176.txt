
        BASCH--Peter. On March 15, 2004. Died quietly in his sleep at 2:22AM after enjoying the success of the recent publication in Germany of ''Stars'', a collection of his photographs from the '50's and '60's. He is survived by his loving wife, Jacqueline Bertrand, his children, Michele and Peter Michael, and his grandchildren, Matteo, Davide, and Natascia Blanc. Services Wednesday 2:30PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th St and Amsterdam Ave.
