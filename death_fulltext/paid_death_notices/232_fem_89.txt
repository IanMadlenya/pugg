
        CANE--Anne. On May 12, 2006, age 86. While her passing is mourned by her daugther Jane, son Robert and his wife Alison, her sister and brother-in-law Shirley and Jon Brodsky, her nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews of the Brodsky and Amr families, the Nowak family, and her many friends, they all take solace in her courage, ethical sense, wit and wordsmanship. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Ave, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
