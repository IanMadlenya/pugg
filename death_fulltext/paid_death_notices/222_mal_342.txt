
        LIEBERMAN--William S. The Metropolitan Museum of Art will hold a memorial service for William S. Lieberman on Monday, September 12, at 5:30PM. He was the Museum's Jacques and Natasha Gelman Special Consultant for Modern Art at the time of his death on June 1, and for the previous 24 years served as chairman of the department of modern art at the Metropolitan. Inquiries may be addressed to the Development Office, The Metropolitan Museum of Art, 1000 Fifth Ave., NY, NY 10028.
