
        DEUTSCH--Freda Smukler, born April 5, 1915, in Bialystok, Poland, passed away peacefully in Los Angeles on September 20. Dedicated wife to radiologist Irvin (died 1997), and loving mother and grandmother, she is survived by her children, Diane Thome, Nina Deutsch, Laurence Deutsch, and Mona Deutsch Miller (and husband, Steve), grandchildren, Thais Miller and David, Derek, and Lara Deutsch, and brothers, Irving (and wife, Ruth) and Leon (and wife, Bea) Claremon. She earned a B.A. from Hunter College in 1935 and an M.A. Psychology from Columbia in 1937. Funeral services at Riverside Memorial Chapel on Thursday, 11:30am.
