
        BRADLEY-Lucy Glazebrook, 79, beloved wife and mother, died on May 9th, in Sagaponack, New York. Educated at Dobbs and Bennington. Married Vivian Mercier and later George Crawford Bradley. Predeceased by her brother Otis Allan Glazebrook III, she is survived by her sister Anne Kehoe, as well as by her husband George, four children (Ciaran Mercier, Christine Wooding, George Bradley, Jr., Anne Bradley), two grandchildren (Martha Mercier, Beatrice Bradley) and numerous nieces and nephews. Memorial service 2 PM, Saturday, May 17th, Saint Ann's Church, Bridgehampton, NY. Brockett Funeral Home, Southampton.
