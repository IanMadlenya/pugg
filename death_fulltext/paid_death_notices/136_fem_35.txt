
        HEDGES-Camille F. (nee Fenn). 83, of Sag Harbor, New York, died May 4, 1998 at the Good Samaritan Nursing Home in Socorro, New Mexico. She was predeceased by husband, Thomas B. Hedges. She is survived by daughter, Kathleen, and her husband, Walter Jon Williams of Belen, NM; daughter, Alice H. Emigh, of Albany, New York, sister, Dolores Fenn of NYC and Sag Harbor, and brother Gene Fenn of Paris, France.
