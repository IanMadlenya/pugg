
        FELDMAN-Michael, 82, in Key Biscayne, May 3. Adored husband of 56 years of Grace. Revered father of Judith, Daniel and Richard. Loving father-in-law of Michael, Laurie and Barbara. Beloved grandfather of Nicholas, Jesse, Jaron, David, Jack, Ethan, Eric and Jill. Never one to settle for second best, he led a rich, full live and pushed everyone to their limit. Funeral Wednedsay, May 7, 10:45 AM, Riverside Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, 362-6600. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Miami Lighthouse for the Blind, 601 SW 8th Ave., Miami.
