
        MISTRETTA-Frances V. Formerly of Murray Hill, died at 2:45PM on April 2, 1999 of complications brought about by a stroke at Good Samaritan Nursing Home's Alzheimer Unit in Jasper, IN, where she had resided since 1996. She was born in Brooklyn on August 10, 1911 to Francesco and Christina (Angelo) Mistretta. Surviving are a sister, Lillian Rubino, of Lakehurst, NJ and several nieces and nephews. She had been a travel agent for over 30 years and had previously worked for the City of New York as an Inspector for the Department of Marine and Aviation. She was a graduate of Adelphi College. She was buried at the family plot in Holy Cross Cemetery in Queens.
