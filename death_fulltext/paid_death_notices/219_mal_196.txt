
        HAUPTMAN--Philip J., M.D. The staff of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital acknowledges with sadness the passing of our esteemed colleague and friend Philip J. Hauptman, M.D., associate attending physician in the department of psychiatry. Dr. Hauptman was particularly active in the hospital's geriatric psychiatry practice. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his wife Natalie and all of those who loved him. Richard F. Daines, MD President and CEO Carl W. Braun, MD President, Medical Board Richard Rosenthal, MD Chairman Department of Psychiatry
