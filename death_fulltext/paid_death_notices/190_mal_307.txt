
        FLINGOU-Marc R. Of White Plains, New York. Died on Saturday, November 23, 2002 of cancer at the age of 54. He was born in Brooklyn, NY on February 4, 1948 to Marjorie (Sowerby) and Jean Flingou. He is survived by his wife Judith A. Ranta of White Plains. He is predeceased by his parents and his brother Paul Flingou. Marc received his Bachelor's from Brooklyn College and his M.S. from Pace University. Marc was previously employed by I.B.M. and then worked and retired from AT&T.
