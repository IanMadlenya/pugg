
        WEINSAFT-Harry. After a long illness on March 16 at age 76 in Detroit. Served in WW II, US Army 10th Mountain Division. Member of Crew of Exodus 1947. Survived by his wife Madeline and family in Detroit, Israel, New York, Alaska, Kibbuz Afikim. Next to his parents and sister Noemi who passed away six weeks ago. He will be greatly missed. Sofie and Nathan Holz and Family
