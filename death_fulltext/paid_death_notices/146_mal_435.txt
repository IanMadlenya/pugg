
        PRITCHARD-Wilbur L. The Directors and members of the Townsend Harris Alumni Association, Inc., wish to express their sympathy to the family of Wilbur L. Pritchard '39 on his passing. In 1991, Mr. Pritchard was inducted into the Townsend Harris Hall of Fame in recognition of his achievements as an aerospace engineering executive. His gentle smile and warm manner will be missed. Saul Grossman, Ph.D.-Chairman Lester Friesner-President Charles Puglisi-Secretary
