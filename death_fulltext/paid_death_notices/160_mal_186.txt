
        MELMAN-Morris. Beloved husband of the late Dina, died on May 13, five days short of his 92nd birthday. His vital spirit and extraordinary love of life will be cherished by all who knew him. Survived by loving daughters, Gisele Melnick & Faith Kanner, son-inlaw, Michael Melnick, grandchildren, Amy, Alyssa, Gary & Adam, great grandchild, Quentin, & companion Shifra Blinova, all will miss him dearly. Funeral services on Wednesday, May 17, at 11:15 A.M., Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76 Street, New York City. Shiva will be observed at the Melnick residence Wednesday evening through Friday at 4:00 P.M.
