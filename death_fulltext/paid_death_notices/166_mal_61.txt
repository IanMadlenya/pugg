
        ALPER-Jerome Milton. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Jerome Milton Alper, beloved father of Andrew M. Alper, a long-time contributor and advocate of the work of UJA-Federation. To Andrew and his brother, Alan Alper; to his stepchildren, Robin Howe and Jeffrey Teitel; to his six grandchildren; and to the entire family, we extend our sincerest condolences. James S.
