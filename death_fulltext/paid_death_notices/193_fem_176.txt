
        BOROWIK-Angela. On February 20, 2003, age 100. Devoted wife of the late Lucian. Loving mother of Joan Kolobielski and Thomas Borowik. Grandmother of six. Greatgrandmother of four. Born in Poland, daughter of Abraham Gepner, a well-known elder of the Warsaw Ghetto. She escaped Nazi tyranny and shepherded her family to the New World. In New York she created a safe haven for family and fellow refugees. We will remember her tenacity, courage, intelligence, sense of humor, esthetic zeal and family loyalty. She has not really left us, for she is alive in our hearts.
