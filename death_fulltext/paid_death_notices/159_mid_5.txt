
        HART-Dorothy, 94. Beloved wife of the late Theodore Hart. Devoted mother of Larry (Jennifer) Hart. Author of ''Thou Swell, Thou Witty: the Life and Lyrics of Lorenz Hart'' and Co-editor of ''The Complete Lyrics of Lorenz Hart.'' She devoted her life to keeping his flame alive. A memorial service will be held in Palm Springs, which will be announced at a later date. Hillside Mortuary 800-576-1994
