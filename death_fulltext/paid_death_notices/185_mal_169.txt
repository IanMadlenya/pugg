
        SMITH-Martin Post. On June 11, 2002, of Bolton Landing, NY, formerly of Garden City, NY. Founding member of both The Lake George Antique Boat Museum and The Saratoga Automobile Museum. Martin was an educator for 32 years, and was a member and past president of the Freeport Rotary Club. He was also an Lt. JG in the U.S. Navy during the Korean occupation. Beloved husband of Doris W.B. Smith. Loving father of Deborah A.P. Snyder, Queensbury, NY; Martin P. Smith Jr. and Henry Heller Smith, both of Bolton Landing, NY. Cherished grandfather of Alyssa Mae Post Snyder. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave at 12th St, Garden City, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Friday. Religious service at The Cathedral of the Incarnation, Garden City, Saturday 11 AM. Interment to follow at Greenfield Cemetery.
