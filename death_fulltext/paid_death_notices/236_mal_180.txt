
        DIAMOND--Milton. 88 of Lauderhill, FL, formerly of Long Island, NY, passed away September 15, 2006. Beloved father of Marsha Chenoff and Susan Joy Diamond. Grandfather of Jodi & Tracy. Brother of Helen Forrest & Rose Epstein. Father-in-law of Robert Chenoff. Beloved husband of the late Beatrice Diamond. He served in WWII and Korea and was a Lt. in US Army and earned the Purple Heart and Bronze Star. Funeral Service Sunday, September 17, 11:15 AM at Star of David Memorial Gardens, N. Lauderdale, FL 800-456-5252.
