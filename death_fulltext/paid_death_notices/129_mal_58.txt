
        NACHBAR-Martin. On October 4, 1997. Beloved husband of Barbara. Devoted father of Paul; loving brother of William and Edwin. He was a Board member of the Mental Health Assocation of Nassau County and a Board member of Central Nassau Guidance and Counseling Services, Inc. He was retired from BDO Seidman, CPAs, as managing partner of the Long Island office. Services Monday, 12 noon, at ''Gutterman's, Inc,'' 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, Long Island.
