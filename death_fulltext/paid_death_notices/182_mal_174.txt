
        CROSBY-Ralph H. On March 10, 2002. Passed away peacefully at his home. He was 96. Beloved husband of Joan. Mr. Crosby graduated from MIT in 1929. Family will receive friends at the Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Avenue, Scarsdale, NY today 2-4 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial will be Wednesday, 10 AM at Our Lady of Fatima Church. Interment to follow at the Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
