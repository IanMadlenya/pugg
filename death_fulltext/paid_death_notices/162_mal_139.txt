
        DALTON-Jack. On July 7, 2000. Dean Emeritus, School of Library Service, Columbia University. Former Librarian, University of Virginia. Survived by his wife Margaret Stieg Dalton, son John Preston Dalton, brother Robert Dalton, sister Antoinette Nelson, nieces and nephews. In loving memory of a wonderful man. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Alderman Library, University of Virginia, Director of Development, Charlottesville, VA. 22903.
