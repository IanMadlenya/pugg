
        LOBELL-Mimi. Born Miriam Comings, in 1942. Died on April 6, 2001, after a brief illness with cancer. Mimi attended Middlebury College and the University of Pennsylvania. She received a masters degree in architecture from Penn, was a registered architect, worked for distinguished architectural firms, and was a professor of architecture at Pratt Institute since 1972. She was a pioneer in Goddess studies, and approached architecture from a symbolic and mythological point of view. She is survived by her loving husband of 35 years, John Lobell, and her two brothers, Gordon Comings and Dr. David Comings. She will be missed by her family, friends, colleagues, and students. Private memorials will be held.
