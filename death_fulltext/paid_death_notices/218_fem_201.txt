
        DANIELS--Emma A. Lutheran Medical Center mourns the passing of Emma A. Daniels, a friend and, through her stewardship of the May Ellen and Gerald Ritter Foundation, a supporter of Lutheran Medical Center. Ms. Daniels distinguished herself as a voice of eloquence, grace and compassion as president of the May Ellen and Gerald Ritter Foundation. The Board of Trustees, physicians and staff of Lutheran Medical Center express their deepest sorrow and heartfelt sympathy to her family and friends. Kathryn S. Wylde, Chair Board of Trustees Wendy Z. Goldstein, President and CEO Frank Siguenza, D.O. Pres Medical and Dental Staff Lutheran Medical Center
