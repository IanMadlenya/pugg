
        BUCHANAN -- Mary Jane. Passed away on August 10, 2003, after a long illness. She was 71. She lived in White Plains, NY, and Palm Beach, FL. Mary Jane was the greatest mom ever to daughter Suzanne Faupel and son-in-law Scott. She was adored by her granddaughter Bridgette, ''the love of her life.'' Her husband George Buchanan passed away in 1991. Mass will be held August 12 at 11 a.m. at Our Lady of Sorrows Church, White Plains. Interment immediately following the service at Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. For more information: Ballard Durand Funeral Home, 914-949-0566.
