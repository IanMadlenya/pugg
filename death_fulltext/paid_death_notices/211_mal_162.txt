
        WASHER--Judah I., Rabbi. The Officers, Trustees and Membership of The Jewish Center of Teaneck, mourn the passing of its distinguished Rabbi Emeritus, Judah I. Washer. His accomplishments and influence are far reaching, and he will be remembered reverently and fondly. We extend our sympathy to his daughter, Dassah (and Dr. Howard Wang), son, Sid Washer, and grandchildren. Rabbi Stanley Bramnick, David Feldman, Rabbi David Bilow, President
