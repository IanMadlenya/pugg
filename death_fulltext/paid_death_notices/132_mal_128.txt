
        MICHELSON-Leonard R. Beloved husband for 57 years of Josephine. Devoted father and best friend of son Leslie and daughter Nancy Ben-Dror. Beloved grandfather of Emily, David and Daniel. His character and indomitable spirit were a source of great joy and inspiration to all. May this very good man rest in eternal peace. Service at 12 noon Sunday, January 11, 1998, at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, New York.
