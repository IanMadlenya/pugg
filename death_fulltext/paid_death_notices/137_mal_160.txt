
        MORRIS-Larry. On June 14, 1998 at the age of 87. Beloved husband of the late Libby. Adored father of Stephen and Marjorie. Dear father-in-law of Marlene. Loving grandfather of David, Gary, Allison and Eric. A pioneer in motion picture theatre exhibition. He will be deeply missed. Service 1:30PM Wednesday, June 17, 1998 at Boulevard-Riverside Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York.
