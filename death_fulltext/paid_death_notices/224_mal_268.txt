
        CORCORAN--John W. Of NYC, Rye and Windham, NY. On September 25. Beloved husband of Susan. Loving father of Elise Dobi, Laura, Sanford and Pamela Corcoran. Cherished grandfather of five loving grandchildren. Former Vice-Chairman of Donaldson Lufkin and Jenrette. A graduate of Dartmouth College and Harvard Business School. Family will receive friends Wednesday 79 PM and Thursday 2-5 PM and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st St. Mass of Christian Burial Friday, 10 AM at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 65 East 89th St. Interment to follow at the N. Hardyston Cemetery, Hardyston, NJ. Memorial donations may be made to Spence Chapin Services, Attn: Linda J. Wright, 6 E. 94th St., NY, NY 10128.
        $(6$)CORCORAN -- John W. Spence-Chapin Services to Families & Children acknowledges with great sadness the sudden passing of John W. Corcoran. Mr. Corcoran served on the Board of Directors for 20 years from 1978 to 1998 and gave generously of his time, energy and financial expertise to benefit the agency's work on behalf of children in need of permanent loving families. We extend sincere sympathy to his wife Susan and his family. Spence-Chapin
