
        WEICHSELBAUM-Walter. On October 2, 1997. Age 78. Cherished husband of Natalie (Gallin). Beloved father of Wilma and Paul (Elyse Meltz). Loving grandfather of Noah and Emma. Dear uncle, cousin, friend. Cared and gave purpose to David Gallin's life. Philatelic dealer. Loved his stamps and his home. In remembrance forever beyond his forgetting... due to Alzheimer's.
