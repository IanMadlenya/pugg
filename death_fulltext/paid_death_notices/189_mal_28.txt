
        EGENOLF - Herbert. The Board of Directors and members of the International Fine Print Dealers Association note with sadness the passing of our colleague, Herbert Egenolf on August 31, 2002, in Dusseldorf, Germany. He was a world-renowned dealer, an expert in Japanese prints of the ukiyo-e world, respected for his integrity, connoisseurship, honesty and kindness. We extend our condolences to his business partner and friend Veronica Miller, who will carry on the branch of his gallery in Venice, California. President, Robert Newman
