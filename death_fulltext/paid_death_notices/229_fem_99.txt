
        MILLER - Edna. Passed peacefully on February 6 after 100 full and happy years. Loving wife of the late Dr. Bernard Sager and the late Dr. Milton Miller. Beloved mother of Robert & Jill Sager, cherished grandmother of Kim and William Sager, proud great-grandmother of Samuel and Hayley Sager. Her spirit and love of life, her sense of humor and kindness will be missed by all whose lives she touched. She will live forever within our hearts and memories. Rest in peace. Services were held.
