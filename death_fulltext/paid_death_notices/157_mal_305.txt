
        ROSENBERG-Albert J. The Board of Directors of the Henry Nias Foundation, Inc. records with profound sorrow the loss of our esteemed president emeritus. He will be remembered for his wisdom, compassion and sense of humor. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Edith and his sons, Dr. Lawrence and William. We mourn with them and share their loss. Stanley Edelman, M.D. Chairman, Board of Directors Henry Nias Foundation, Inc.
