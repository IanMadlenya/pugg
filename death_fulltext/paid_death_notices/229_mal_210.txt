
        FELLER--Sid. We mourn the passing of a fine musical talent and dear friend. His arrangements for singers Jane Froman, Ray Charles and Mel Torme were brilliant and enduring. His humor was irrepressible, his love of family and friends, limitless. Our deepest sympathy goes to his wife Gertrude, his children Lois, Debbie, Bill and Jane and his grandchildren. Joyce Liskin, Babs, Bobbie, and Win
