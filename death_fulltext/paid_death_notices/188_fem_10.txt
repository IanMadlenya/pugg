
        SWIERSKI-Lee. 64, died peacefully at home after a valiant battle with cancer. She joins in heaven her father, Raymond F. Riley, and her sister, Ellen Riley. The family members she leaves behind to celebrate her beautiful life are her husband, Ron, her mother, Leonore Riley, her daughter Lyn, and her partner Kate, her son Chris, and his wife Karen, and her son Tom. Lee retired last year after a fulfilling 20 year career as senior legal assistant at Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison. Reposing at the Beney Funeral Home, Inc., of Syosset, 79 Berry Hill Rd., Sunday and Monday, 7 to 9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 9:30 AM, at St. Dominic's R.C. Church. Interment Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, NY 11590 (516-832-7100) would be appreciated.
