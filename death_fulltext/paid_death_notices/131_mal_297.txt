
        WEINER-Milton A. On December 19, shortly after his 70th birthday. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of South Orange and Livingston, NJ. Beloved husband of Eleanor (nee Sheitelman), devoted father of Michelle Bettman, Bruce and Marc Weiner, dear brother of Lenore Salant and Bernice Freund, loving grandfather of Lauren, Jordan and Brittany Bettman, Julie and Adam Weiner. Funeral services were held at Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vaux Hall Road, Union, NJ, on Sunday, December 21. Shiva through Tuesday at the home of Michelle Bettman. Donations in his memory may be made to the American Heart Association or the American Cancer Society.
