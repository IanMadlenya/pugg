
        BLUM - Maxwell V. The membership and Board of Governors of the Boca Rio Golf Club, Boca Raton, Florida, announce with deep regret and sorrow the passing of our dear friend and esteemed member, Maxwell Blum, and extend our sincerest condolences to his wife Eleanor and his daughter, our esteemed member, Marjorie, and their family. Funeral to be held 10:30 AM, Thursday, March 13, Temple Emanuel in Newton, Massachusetts, 617-332-5770. Robert P. Adler, President Boca Rio Golf Club, Inc.
