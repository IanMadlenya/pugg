
        SCHUR-Fae. On January 18th, 1999, age 91. Wife of the late Aaron Schur. Mother of sons Larry, Joel, Paul & late daughter Susan. Adored grandmother of eleven grandchildren and great grandmother to thirteen great grandchildren. Graveside services will be held Wednesday, 11 AM, Sharon Gardens Cemetery. A proud and courageous lady and mother. One of the last of her kind.
        SCHUR-Faye. The Solomon Schechter School community notes with profound sorrow the death of Faye Schur, mother of our longtime trustee Larry Schur and grandmother of our own Jeffrey Tieman. We pray that her memory will be a blessing to all who knew and loved her. The Solomon Schecter School of Westchester, M. MacSchwebel, Chairman, Board of Trustees James M. Dubin, Vice Chairman, Board of Trustees Dr. Elliot Spiegel, Headmaster
