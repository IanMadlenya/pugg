
        LESTER--Yetta G. Died in NYC, on October 9, 2004, in her 94th year. Loving wife of the late Abraham (A.J.) Lester, beloved mother of Ann Stone and the late Elaine Isenberg, mother-in-law of Leon Stone, grandmother of Julie, Hal, Susan, Tom, Stephanie, Marc, Paul and Robin. Greatgrandmother to Chelsea, Samantha, Ethan and Sophie. Revered by friends and family, Yetta's loving kindness will be remembered by all who knew her. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Lester Scholarship Fund of Riverdale Temple, 4545 Independence Ave, Riverdale, NY 10471; or a charity of your choice. Graveside services Monday, October 11, 12 noon, Temple Israel Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY.
