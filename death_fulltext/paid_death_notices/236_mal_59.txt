
        DONADONI--Charles, age 86, died September 5th, 2006. In loving memory of a man who flew kites at the beach and rode his bike in Central Park. He will be dearly missed by his family; Charles and Diana Donadoni, Susan and Arthur Zanko, and his four grandchildren Christopher and Danielle Donadoni, and Jennifer and Laura Zanko. Arrangements: OB Davis, Miller Place, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions to Sloan Kettering Memorial Hospital.
