
        HACZYNSKI--Leo J., Ph.D., 81, died March 4th at Briar Cliff Manor in Johnston, Rhode Island. A native of Woonsocket, Rhode Island, he receive a B.S. from the University of Rhode Island and an M.A. and Ph.D. from Fordham University. He was a popular and inspiring teacher at Power Memorial Academy, St. Francis College, Brooklyn and Assumption College, Worcester, MA, but principally at Iona College in New Rochelle (10 years) and the University of Prince Edward Island (16 years). He is survived by a sister, Helena Kupla, of Phoenix, Arizona; two nieces, Priscilla Boyd of Milford, Connecticut, and Christine Dickins of Phoenix; and several grand nieces and nephews. A mass of Christian burial will be held Saturday at 10 am in Our Lady Queen of Martyrs Church, Woonsocket. Burial will be private. Donations in his memory to the UPEI Scholarship Fund, 550 University Avenue, Charlottetown, PEI C1A4P3 Canada would be appreciated.
