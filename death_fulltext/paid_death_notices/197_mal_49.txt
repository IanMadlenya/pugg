
        RIVLIN--Michael Adrian. 52, beloved son of Norma and Archie Rivlin and brother of the late Robert Rivlin. Died on June 1 at Lenox Hill Hospital, New York City after a long illness. He was an accomplished writer and activist who devoted his short life to environmental concerns. Services private. His remains will be scattered over the Arizona desert which he loved so dearly.
