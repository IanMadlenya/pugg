
        TAPPER-Albert, M.D. Died on March 5, 1997, at the age of 89. Adoring husband of Jean L. Tapper, loving father of Helaine T. Gold and Michael Tapper, devoted grandfather of Roger Gold. A devoted physician for more than 40 years, he was loved and cherished by his many patients & esteemed by his colleagues. Services will be held Friday, March 7, at 10:45 AM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, at Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be sent to the Visiting Nurse Service of NYC or the Dorot Services for the Jewish Elderly, 171 West 85th Street, NYC 10024.
