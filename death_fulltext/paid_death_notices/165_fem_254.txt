
        STONE-Edith Wood Pershouse, October 6, 2000 at Goodwin House West, Falls Church, VA. Born NYC March 10, 1905. Daughter of Dr. Francis Carter Wood and Edith Sterling Wood. Wife of the late Aubrey Pershouse and the late Bradford W. Stone. She is survived by a brother, a sister, two children; three stepdaughters; seven grandchildren and eleven great grandchildren. A service and interment will be held in Englewood, NJ.
