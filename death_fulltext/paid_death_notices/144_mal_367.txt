
        GRUEN-Kurt H. The family and friends mourn his passing on Thursday, Jan. 21. Born in Vienna, Austria, a U.S. Army veteran of World War II, he became a Certified Public Accountant servicing many clients and friends. Survived by his companion, Hanna Broz, nieces Yvonne Zaros, Susan Decker, Judy Teilhaber, and Ethel Auerbach, and his grand nieces and grand nephews. Services at Riverside, 180 West 76 Street, (362-6600), Sunday, 12:15 PM.
