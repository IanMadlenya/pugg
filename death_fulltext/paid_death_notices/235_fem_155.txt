
        HAMERSCHLAG -- Barbara B. Of Garden City. On August 21, 2006 in her 75th year. Beloved wife of the late James Hamerschlag. Loving mother of James & beloved motherin-law of Elizabeth. Cherished grandmother of Grace and dearest sister of Msgr. Austin P. Bennett & the late Gerard E. Bennett. Mrs Hamerschlag bravely fought cancer with high spirits and tremendous faith in God.
