
        FLEMING-Gus. The Philharmonic-Symphony Society of New York, Inc., notes with sadness the death of Gus Fleming, Director of Concert Halls at Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts since 1988 and a longstanding friend and associate of the New York Philharmonic. Responsible for managing Avery Fisher Hall, the Philharmonic's home, Mr. Fleming played an essential role in the Orchestra's many successes during the past 12 years. His contributions and dedication will long be cherished by the entire Philharmonic family. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife, children, and entire family. Kurt Masur, Music Director Members of the Orchestra Paul B. Guenther, Chairman William Thomas, Acting Executive Director
        FLEMING-Gus. The Board of Directors, artists and staff of the Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center mourn the loss of our dear colleague and friend, Gus Fleming, Director of Concert Halls at Lincoln Center. Gus's untiring devotion to Lincoln Center was an inspiration to all of us. He will be very sorely missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to his family, friends and colleagues. Donaldson C. Pillsbury, Chairman Peter Frelinghuysen, President David Shifrin, Artistic Director Jacqueline Taylor, Exec. Director
