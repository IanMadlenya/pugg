
        BADAMI-Nick F. Of New City, New York. Died on March 23, 1999 at Westchester Medical Center. Mr. Badami was born in the Bronx June 27, 1930 to Bernard & Alfina Tornatore Badami. He was a Rockland resident for thirty-eight years, residing in West Nyack and New City. Mr. Badami was the President and CEO of Ritangela Construction Corp., Bardonia, NY for thirty-nine years. He was also president of the Association of General Contractors for New York State, Director of the Construction Industry Council, Past President of the N.Y.S. Asphalt Association and Paving Association, Director of the National Association of General Contractors, President and CEO of Maybrook Materials, Maybrook, NY, and was a member of the Board of Directors of Dominican College. He was the recipient of the Governors Award and also a recipient of the Bronze Star during the Korean War. He was a member of St. Anthony's Church, Nanuet, NY. Nick married Rita Cincotti at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church, New York City, in 1954. He is survived by his wife Rita; daughters Angela Mancino, Carla Whittle and Patrice Flannery; brothers Richard and Ben; grandchildren Julia, Charlie, Gabriella and Nicholas. He was predeceased by his son Nicholas in 1991. Mass of Christian burial will be held on Friday at 10 AM at St. Anthony's Church, Nanuet, NY. Burial to follow in St. Anthony's Cemetery. Visiting at Higgins Funeral Home, New City, on Wednesday from 7-9 PM and Thursday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Donations to American Cancer Society, Rockland County Unit.
