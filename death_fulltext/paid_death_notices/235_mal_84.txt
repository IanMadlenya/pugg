
        KRINSKY--Josephine B. The Board of Trustees and University Leadership Council of Antioch University extend their deepest condolences to Robert Krinsky and his wife Carol on the loss of his mother, Josephine Bachrach Krinsky. Bob is a member of the Antioch College class of 1951. In lieu of flowers please send contributions to Antioch University in honor of her devoted son Bob Krinsky.
