        STEIN-Toby Robin, 13. Died on June 25, 1999, following a fourmonth battle with cancer. Throughout his illness, he demonstrated the grace and courage that were the hallmark of his short life. Son of Stanley & Carolyn, brother of Myra, Rusty and Cory, he was devoted to his family and to his friends at The Browning School. A memorial service will be held at noon Wednesday at the Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, 30 West 68 Street, New York City. Friends will be received at the Stein residence after services. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Toby Stein Memorial Fund at The Browning School, 52 East 62 Street, New York, New York 10021, 212-838-6280.
