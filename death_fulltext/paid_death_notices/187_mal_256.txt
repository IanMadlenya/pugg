
        SELCH-Frederick Richard ''Eric''. At age 72. On August 22, 2002, at home. He is survied by his wife of forty-nine years, Patricia Bakwin Selch, three sons, Nicholas, of Lancaster, PA., Jason of Chicago, IL. and Gregory of NY. NY., a daughter Andrea Selch, Ph.D. of Hillsborough, N.C., eight grandchildren, two brothers, John & Grant and two sisters The Rev. Cecily Whiteford & Adrianna Coe. A memorial service will be held Sunday, September 8th, at 1:30 P.M., at the Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory may be made to Hamilton College, Clinton, New York 13323.
