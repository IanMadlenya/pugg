
        RAICHLE-Elaine Lucas, 86. Died Wednesday, March 13, 2002 in East Windsor, New Jersey. Wife of the late Donald R. and mother of the late Alan. She is survived by two sons Douglas R. of Lawrenceville, N.J. and Donald R. of Irvington, N.J., one daughter, Lynne N. Potts of Pepperell, MA. and one sister, Nadine Charles of Sterling, Va. Memorial service will be held Sunday, April 7, 2002 at 2PM at the Meadow Lakes Meeting Room in Hightstown, N.J. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Lancelot Owens Scholarship Fund, 155 Morgan Street, Jersey City, New Jersey 07302.
