
        MOSSE-George L. The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum mourns the death of George L. Mosse, the Museum's first J.B. and Maurice C. Shapiro Senior Scholar in Residence. A pioneering Holocaust scholar and remarkable educator, his generosity as a colleague, teacher, and mentor will be deeply missed. Miles Lerman Chairperson, United States Holocaust Memorial Council Ruth B. Mandel, Vice Chairperson Sara J. Bloomfield Acting Director, United States Holocaust Memoral Museum
