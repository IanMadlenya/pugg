
        KARLINSKY-Raymond E. Age 74, on November 14, 1998. Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of NYC. Beloved husband of Bette, loving father of Martin, Debi and Joanna. A lover of life, full of generosity, wit and vitality. We will miss his love and guidance greatly. Contributions in his memeory may be made to the Raymond Karlinsky Scholarship Fund-Little Village School-Jack Joel Center for Special Children, Bellmore, NY.
