
        ADLER--Julius Ochs, 78, died on August 18, 2003 after a long illness. Mr. Adler is survived by his wife, Deirdre Spencer Adler, a son, Douglas O. Adler, two daughters, Anne A. Tarbell and Mary B. Malhotra, as well as an aunt, two sisters, three step children, 13 grandchildren, and two great grandchildren. Mr. Adler was the son of Barbara Stettheimer Adler and Major General Julius Ochs Adler, Vice President, General Manager and Treasurer of The New York Times from 1914 to 1955. From 1977 until 1992, Mr. Adler and his wife owned and operated the Canterbury Bookstore in Manhattan. Earlier in his professional career, Mr. Adler was affiliated with Interpublic, the New York Mets and The New York Times. Mr. Adler served in the United States Air Corps during WWII. He attended Princeton University. Mr. Adler's funeral will be private. Donations in his memory can be made to Lenox Hill Hospital, 100 East 77 Street, New York, New York 10021.
