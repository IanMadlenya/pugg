
        FINKELSTEIN--Paul. 85, died on February 25, 2005, after a short courageous battle against cancer. He was a selfmade entrepreneur of several businesses. He was active in NYC and Florida communities and was a member and Past President of B'nai B'rith Queens Council and was currently President of Hollywood Civic Association in Hollywood, Florida. Paul was a teacher and scholar to all who knew him. He is survived by his son Larry, daughter-inlaw Carol, daughter Ellen Simon, son-in-law Joel Simon and grandchildren David and Mallory. Funeral service will be held at ''Schwartz Bros.Jeffer'' Queens Blvd. and 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY, Sunday, February 27, 2005 at 1 PM.
