
        CARROLL-Vincent J. Of Williston Park, NY died peacefully at his home. Past president of St. Aidan's Parish Council, member of St. Aidan's Holy Name Society. Retired Air Corpsman WWII the European and Pacific Theatre. He was a leading authority of the use of antibiotics to treat agricultural diseases. Devoted husband of the late Elizabeth (nee Willett), loving father of Vincent, Margaret Foehr, Ann, Mary, A. Patricia Roszkowski, Elizabeth Cunningham, Martha Mixa, James, and Marion O'Sullivan. Proud grandpa of 26 grandchildren. Caring brother of Kathryn Healy, Agnes Leahy, Marion DeHaven and the late James Carroll and the late Margaret Bracken. Dear brother-in-law of John Leahy, Cecelia Carroll and William DeHaven. Reposing at the Weigand Bros. Funeral Home, 49 Hillside Avenue, Williston Park, Monday and Tuesday, 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10 AM, St. Aidan's Church. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations in his name to his Alma Mater, Class of '41, Xavier High School, 30 West 16 Street, New York, would be appreciated.
