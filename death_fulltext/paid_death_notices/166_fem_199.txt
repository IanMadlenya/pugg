
        AMES-Ann F. Of New York, NY. Died on November 23, 2000 at the age of 96. Mrs. Ames was the wife of the late Irving M. Ames. She is survived by her daughter Susan Ames. Mrs. Ames was awarded a Jurist Doctor degree from Cleveland-Marshall College of Law at Cleveland State University, a member of the Class of 1928. Funeral services will be private.
