
        GUTERMAN--Frederick H., 83, of NYC, June 28. Quietly passed away after a long struggle with Parkinson's disease. Mr. Guterman served as a VP for Allen B. Dumont Labs, President of ITT, Vice President of Arma and President of Horn & Hardart. He is preceded in death by his parents, Ruth ''Chickie'' Guterman and Herbert C. Guterman. He is survived by his son, James Ludewig Guterman of NYC. A memorial will be held on July 8th, at 926 5th Ave., 5-9 PM.
