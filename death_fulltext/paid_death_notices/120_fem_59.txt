
        URSTADT - Susan Powers. On January 5, 1997 after a heroic fight with leukemia, at home in New Canaan, CT. attended by husband Jeffrey and children Elizabeth, Bryant, and Jordan. She was founder of the Susan P. Urstadt Literary Agency. Service at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church at 11 AM. Friday, January 10. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Joshua B. Powers Endowment for Education in Turtle Conservation of the Carribbean Conservation Corp., PO Box 2866, Gainesville, Fl. 32602 and the New Canaan Public Library.
