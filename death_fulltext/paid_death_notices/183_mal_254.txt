
        RAPHAEL-Dr. Jerry. Of NY, NY died March 22, 2002 of a cerebral aneurysm at the age of sixty-one. Beloved father of Marisa Raphael and brother of Madeleine Klein. Dr. Raphael received his Ph.D. in Clinical Psychology from Yeshiva University and had an active private practice since 1980. He graduated from the Object Relations Institute for Psychotherapy and Psychoanalysis in 1995 and was a highly valued member of the Institute from 1996 thereafter. He served as a member of the faculty and training committee as well as a supervisor. Dr. Raphael was a lover and supporter of the arts. He will be sorely missed by his family, friends, and colleagues. Memorial service to be held at Kritil Funeral Home at 1297 First Avenue on Sunday, April 28 at 1 P.M.
