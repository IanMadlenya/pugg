
        REESE-William W.. The Officers, Directors and Members of the New England Society in the City of New York greatly mourn the sudden and untimely passing of our devoted president-elect, William W. Reese. As a member and director for many years, he gave caringly and unselfishly to the Society. Our deep condolences to his beloved wife, Sonia, and the entire family. Cornelia H. Greenspan, President New England Society in the City of New York
        REESE-William Willis, II. The Order of Colonial Lords of Manors in America expresses shock and deep sorrow at the death of our dedicated treasurer and dear friend, William Willis Reese, II, and sends its deepest sympathy to his widow, Sonia, his mother, and the rest of his family. Timothy Field Beard, President Joseph Kerr Pistell, Secretary
        REESE-William W. The Society of Colonial Wars in the State of New York deeply mourns the loss of our newly elected Governor. A devoted member and benefactor since 1975. The Society will sorely miss our dear friend. M. David Sherrill, Deputy Governor James B. Gardiner II, Secretary
        REESE-William W. The Huguenot Society of America records with deep sorrow the death of William W. Reese, a member since 1979, a Life Member since 1997, and a former President of the Society. Courtney A. Haff, President
