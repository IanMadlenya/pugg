
        WEBER-William D. An independant art consultant and dealer, died in Catania, Sicily, on March 25, 2002, from a cerebral hemorrhage resulting from an accidental fall. He will be missed by his many friends and associates and by his loving sister, Linda Van Ausdale, her husband David, his nieces Debbie and Susan, great-nephew and niece, Dean and Alissa and most of all, by his companion of 28 years, Albert Meyer.
