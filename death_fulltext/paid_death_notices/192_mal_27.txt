
        BAUMWELL - Maxwell J. Age 87 of Marlboro, NJ. Died January 2, 2003 at his home in Marlboro, NJ. Born in Brooklyn, NY, he lived in Queens for 45 years, before moving to Marlboro 15 years ago. Mr. Baumwell was self employed as a typographer in New York, New York for 52 years, retiring in 1992. He was the President of the Typographers Association of New York, and the President of the International Typographers Association. He was inducted into the New York City School of Arts and Science Hall of Fame in 1987. He was an award winning pioneer in digital photo typography, and an avid painter. Mr Baumwell was predeceased by his beloved wife Julia in 1997. He is survived by his son Clyde, and daughter-in-law Robin Grey of Madison, NJ; his daughter Stephanie Gerstein of West Orange, NJ; his brother Earl of Great Neck, NY; and his three grandchildren, Kate, Blair and Alex. A graveside service will be held at 2PM on Friday, January 3, at Temple Beth El Cemetery in Neptune, NJ. For information Bloomfield Cooper Manalapan.
