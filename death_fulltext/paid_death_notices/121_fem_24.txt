
        BOORSTEIN-Hazel (nee Shapiro), age 98, of Vineland, NJ, formerly of Springfield, NJ, died February 2, 1997. She was the beloved wife of the late Isidore Boorstein; cherished mother of Louise B. Schwartz of Vineland, NJ; loving nana of Randy Jill Schwartz, Jay Finestone, Harriet Schwartz Crew, and Geoffrey Crew; adoring greatgrandmother of Adam, Laura, Alexander and Hilary.
