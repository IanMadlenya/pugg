
        COMSUDI - Elizabeth. Passed away Monday, October 15th in Austin, Texas. She was born in New Brunswick, New Jersey, April 15, 1914 and spent much of her early youth in Lemnos, Greece returning to the U.S. in 1925 to live in New York. Upon retiring from the New York County Medical Society in 1980, she moved to Dallas, Texas and in September 1998 moved to Austin, Texas. She was preceded in death by her husband Constantine Comsudi. She is survived by her three children, Elaine Tate and husband Lawrence of Dallas, Nicholas Comsudi and wife Sandra of Austin, Theodore Comsudi and his wife Sharon and five grandchildren. Services will be at 10:00 A.M. Saturday, October 20, 2001 at Restland Abbey Chapel. Interment to follow at Restland Memorial Park. There will be an open visitation on Friday, October 19, 2001 at Restland Funeral Home.
