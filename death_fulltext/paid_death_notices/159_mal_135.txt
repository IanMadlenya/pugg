
        CUMMINGS-Robert Lincoln, age 87 of Glen Head, New York on April 11, after a long illness. Survived by his beloved wife Marie Powers Maxwell Cummings; by 3 children, Dorcas O. Cochran, Lila Cummings Stoddard, and Robert L. Cummings III, and 7 grandchildren; and by 3 stepchildren. Mr. Cummings was pre-deceased by his first wife Dorcas Oakley Ferris Cummings. A graduate of Milton Academy and Harvard University, Mr. Cummings was a long-time former Senior Executive of Pan American World Airways and a founder and President of New York Airways. He also served as Treasurer of the Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory. A funeral service will be held Friday, April 14, at 11 a.m. at St. John's Church, Cold Spring Harbor, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory, P.O. Box 100, Cold Spring Harbor, New York 11724 in memory of Dorcas O. and Robert L. Cummings.
