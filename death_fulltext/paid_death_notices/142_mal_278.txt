
        TENENBERG-Harry. On November 16 in NYC. Teacher, union organizer, civil rights activist, founding member of Encounter. Funny, good, generous friend. He is survived by his loving family: daughter, Nancy Ferleger, sister, Pearl Boxer, brothers, David and Daniel Tenenberg, son-in-law, Len Ferleger, grandson, Ben Ferleger, nephews, Alan and Joseph Boxer, Jerry and David Tenenberg, and niece, Betsy. A memorial service will be held Friday, December 11 at 6 PM at the Greenwich House Music School, 46 Barrow St., NYC.
