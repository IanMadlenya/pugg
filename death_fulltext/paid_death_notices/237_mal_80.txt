
        DROHAN--Gerard of Scarsdale, NY passed away suddenly on October 7, 2006. He was born on February 10, 1932 in Yonkers, NY to John Patrick and Frances Treacy Drohan. Gerard was a veteran of the Korean War and was a member of the NYC Plumbers Local No. 1 for over 50 years. He was a graduate of Mechanics Institute and the recipient of the George E. Hoe prize for plumbing. Gerard was a member and Past President of the General Society of Mechanics and Tradesmen of the City of New York. He was a member of the Holy Name Society of Immaculate Heart of Mary Church. Gerard is survived by his wife Margaret (nee Boyle), his children Margaret Mary, Patricia Stanton (Lee), Beth Ann and Gerard and his grandchildren Meg, Lee, Christina and Laura Stanton and Katya Drohan. Also surviving are his brothers John, Joseph, Edward, David and his sister Carol Curran. Gerard was predeceased by his brother Donald. Gerard was deeply loved by his family and friends and will be sorely missed. Friends may visit the Edwin L. Bennett Funeral Home in Scarsdale on Wednesday and Thursday from 24 & 7-9P M. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated on Friday at 10A.M. at the Immaculate Heart of Mary Church in Scarsdale, NY. Interment will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery in Yonkers, NY.
