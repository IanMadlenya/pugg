
        FRENCH--Lauretta. Of New York City. Died December 3, 2005. Beloved mother of Lauretta Hicks Jones of Tallahassee, FL and Frenchie Longino of Washington, DC. She was the wife of the late Theron Hicks and Melvin Longino. She will be missed by her grandchildren, great grandchildren, extended family and all who knew and loved her. Lauretta French, a nurse, worked for the Greater New York Red Cross Blood Center, Bellevue Hospital and numerous medical institutions in Louisville, KY. Memorial services are to be arranged in Tallahassee, FL. at a later date.
