
        SAJI-Keizo. The Rockefeller University community deeply mourns the passing of our beloved friend, Keizo Saji, a cherished member of the Rockefeller University Council for over fifteen years. His commitment to the University's mission of biomedical research and education will be greatly missed. Our deepest condolences are sent to his wife, Keiko, and the entire Saji family. David Rockefeller, Honorary Chairman Richard B. Fisher, Chairman Arnold J. Levine, President Torsten N. Wiesel, President Emeritus Richard M. Furlaud, Chairman, Rockefeller University Council
