
        LANE-Miriam (Mitzi). President and Founding Board Member. Tirelessly devoted to the Sirovich Senior Center for 65 years. As a young woman, she personally gave her time to help poor immigrants on the Lower East Side with food and money through the Rose Sirovich Relief Society. A leader and a friend. We will all miss her. William I. Sirovich Memorial Association
