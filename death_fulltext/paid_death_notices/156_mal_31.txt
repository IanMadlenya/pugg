
        BALDWIN-John Low, Dr. On December 31, 1999. Esteemed and beloved conductor, dear friend, revered teacher. For 38 years his dedication and devotion gave great joy and inspiration to hundreds of our members. He generously shared his unique personality, humor, wisdom, and musical gifts touching countless lives. John was beloved by all who knew him. Brothers Sing On. The University Glee Club of NYC Edward J. Greenberg, President
