
        JAMES--Helen Chenoweth. May 8, 1909--August 28, 2005. Loving wife of the late Parker Kent James. Cherished mother of Shirley (Pete) Longshore and Patricia James. Beloved grandmother of Patricia (Chad), James, Irene and Kathryn. An inspiration to her family and many friends, she lives on in our hearts forever. Memorial service September 10, 1 PM, Applewood Estates, Freehold, NJ (732-780-7370). Donations to Helen Chenoweth James Research and Education Fund appreciated (send C/O St. John's Church, 11 Wilmot Rd., New Rochelle, NY 10804).
