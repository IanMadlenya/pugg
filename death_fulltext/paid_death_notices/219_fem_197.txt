
        BARASCH--Gloria Phyllis. On April 27, 2005 at the age of 81. Most beloved wife of Norman. Treasured mother of Marc and Douglas. Adored grandmother of Leah, Katie and Nicholas, and much loved great-grandmother of Antonio and Benecio. Unforgettable to her friends (and to their friends). Endlessly loved, heedlessly adored, and bottomlessly missed by all who ever knew her.
