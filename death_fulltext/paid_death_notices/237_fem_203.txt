
        OSTROVE--Cecil. Devoted wife of the late Samuel. Loving, beloved mother of Arthur (Carol), Mitchell (Carole), Alice Miller (late Norman) (Fran Levey); grandmother of Joan (Joi Lewis), Marjorie, Michael (Ariela Katz), James (Erika); great-grandmother of Samuel and Shulamit. Survived by dear sister Nettie Ducas, loving nieces and nephews. Cecil died peacefully in Miami on Oct. 26 at 91. A tireless worker for social justice who was loved by all who knew her. Funeral to be held at Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd., Sun. Oct. 29 at 1:30PM. Donations may be made to TINR or the charity of your choice.
