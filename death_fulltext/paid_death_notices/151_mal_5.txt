
        LOPATIN-Harry. The family of Harry Lopatin grieves at his passing on Friday, July 30, 1999, after a long illness. He will be remembered by his beloved family and his friends in The International Ladies Garment Workers Union, The Workmen's Circle, The Jewish Daily Forward and the echelons of the labor movement to which he gave of himself all his life. He is mourned by his wife Adele, his children Anne, Allan, Dawn, Leonard, Karen and Eileen; and grandchildren Lisa, David, Priscilla and Amy. Funeral Sunday, 11:30 AM, at Parkside, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn.
