
        GERRITY--James Francis II, of Chestnut Hill, MA and York Harbor, ME, Chairman of Gerrity Lumber Co, died March 24 in Chestnut Hill. A funeral will be held Saturday, April 3, at 12 PM in the First Parish Congregational Church, 180 York St., York, ME. Survived by his wife, Ruth (Mathes) Gerrity of Brookline, MA, three daughters, Margot Finley of Brookline, MA, Ruth Timme of Avon, CT and Betsey Lamson of Hilton Head, SC, two sons, James of Weston, MA and Peter of Cumberland, ME, 14 grandchildren, and one great-grandchild. Memorial donations may be made either to the Mass. Eye and Ear Infirmary, 243 Charles St., Boston, MA 02114 or the Jackson Laboratory, Bar Harbor, ME 04609 or to the Health Care Dimensions Hospice, 764 Main St., Waltham, MA 02451. Lucas and Eaton Funeral Home, York, ME.
