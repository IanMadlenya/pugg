
        O'MALLEY-Thomas, Jr. age 60 died December 19th, 1998 in Indianapolis. He was an attorney and a member of the New York Bar Association. He worked for the Federal Government until his death for The Dept. of Housing and Urban Development. He is survived by his wife, Rosemary O'Malley, & a daughter, Margaret O'Malley. He was a graduate of Le Moyne Law School in NY City. Services will be held on Wednesday, December 23rd at 11:00 am in the St. Thomas Aquinas Catholic Church. Burial is in Oaklawn Memorial Gardens, Indianapolis.
