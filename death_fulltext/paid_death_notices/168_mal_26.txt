
        KAUFMAN-Joe. Died at the Jewish Home and Hospital in Manhattan, January 1, 2001, at age 89, of complications from Alzheimer's Disease. An award-winning illustrator and author of Golden Books for children, Joe leaves his wife Evalyn of Manhattan; a sister, Mollie Kozlowski of Florida; a first wife, Mildred Kaufman of Manhattan; two sons of that previous marriage-Marshall Kaufman of Manhattan, and Dr. Arthur Kaufman of Albuquerque, N.M.; and two grandchildren-Dr. Holly Kaufman of San Antonio, Texas, and Will Kaufman, a senior at The University of Chicago. A memorial service will be planned later.
