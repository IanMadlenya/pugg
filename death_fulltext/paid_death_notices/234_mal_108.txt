
        CANADE--Vincent, born July 9, 1916, to the late artist Vincent Canade and Josephine Canade, died January 26 of this year. Born in New York, Canade proudly served in the US Air Force during World War II in the 22nd Bomb Squadron and the 341st Bomb Group. A printer by trade, Canade spent most of his life in Brooklyn. Canade was predeceased by his parents, brothers, George and Eugene, and sisters, Laura, Celia and Florence. He passed away surrounded by his loving nieces, Bianca Bakalar, Melanie Micale, Francesca Sautman, Georgie Taylor, their spouses, and godson, Robert Arcaro.
