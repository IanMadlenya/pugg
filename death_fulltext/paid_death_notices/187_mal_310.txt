
        SELCH-Frederick. The Allen-Stevenson School mourns the passing of former trustee member, Frederick Selch, who served on the board from 1975-1988. We express our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife, Patricia; children, Gregory '81, Jason '75, and Nicholas '72, Andrea and the entire Selch family. An enthusiastic supporter of the School, Eric will be missed by the entire AllenStevenson community. David R. Trower Headmaster Ronald S. Rolfe '60 President of the Board
