
        SIMON--Rose, vital 97 years old died on February 23, 2007. Beloved wife of the late Martin. Devoted mother of Jonathan Simon and Hope and Seymour Grayson. Cherished grandmother of Jeffrey and Peter Grayson. Dear sister of Herman Eisen and the late Charles Eisen and Gladys Leipzig and sister-in-law of Natalie and the late Sylvia. She was a dress designer for Martin Simon, Inc. in NYC. She was a professor at the Fashion Institute of Technology and was a consultant at the Costume Institute of the Metropolitan Museum of Art. Graveside services Sunday 11 am at Mount Lebanon Cemetery, Glendale, NY.
