
        MAJZLIN--Freda. Freda (age 92) died August 27, 2006, of natural causes attendant Alzheimer's disease. Beloved wife of the late Gregory, devoted mother of Leonard and Jerome. Cherished grandmother of Lesley Anne and David. Loving sister of Hy Blackbeer and the late Esther Fox. Graveside service and interment will be private. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, donations be made in her memory to: George School, Bucks County, PA or the Public Theater, NYC or The Alzheimer's Foundation of America, to continue her passionate commitment to education, the theater and the field of geriatrics.
