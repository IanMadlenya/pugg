
        LESLIE--Georgie Burke of Rancho Bernardo, San Diego, CA on August 18, after a mercifully short battle with brain cancer. Wife of the late William Leslie, she is survived by her children Meredith Leslie Welch of Riverside, CT, Karen Leslie McKay of Reno, NV, William Leslie III of Lake Bluff, IL, a brother James Franklin Burke of Cincinnati, OH, eight grandchildren and five great-grandchildren. Her son David Burke Leslie of Bend, OR died in 2003. She is also survived by her loving companion Robert S. Albritton. Throughout her life she created beautiful handwork as gifts to friends, family and to organizations in the communities where she lived including Bronxville, NY and Rumson, NJ. In lieu of flowers memorial donations to The Old Globe Theatre Costume Dept, PO Box 122171, San Diego, CA. 92112.
