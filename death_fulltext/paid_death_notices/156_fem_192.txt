
        BOROCK-Sylvia (nee Goldberg). Died suddenly. Beloved wife of the late Nathan. Adored mother of Ella and Albert Platnick, and Jaclyn Borock. Cherished grandmother of Dayna and Andrew Kamiel and Joshua Platnick. Loving G.G. of Casey Nathan Kamiel. Dearest friend and sister to Frances and Guster Varet. Sylvia leaves many broken hearts. Services today, 11:30 AM, at ''Boulevard-Riverside,'' 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, LI.
