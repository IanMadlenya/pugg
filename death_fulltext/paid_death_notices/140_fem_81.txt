
        SOREFF-Helen. 72, died peacefully in her sleep at her home in New York on September 10, 1998. She was a wonderful artist, teacher, mother and friend to many. Helen was a provacative, lively and imaginative person. She was a devoted wife and mother to her husband Stephen, and her children, Alexander and Zachary. She will be missed by them, her students, and the art-world. A memorial service will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, at 1:15 PM, today.
