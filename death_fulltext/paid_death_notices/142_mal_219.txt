
        BARON-Raymond S. The Officers and Directors of the Helen Matchette DeMario Foundation mourn the loss of our co-director, friend and colleague Raymond S. Baron. His dedication and decency will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy and condolences to his dear friend and companion, Midge Grant, his children, Ronnie and Fred Hirsh, and Margaret and Richard Baron, his grandchildren, and all his family.
