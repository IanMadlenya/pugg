
        GOLDEN-Emma Jane. Age 77, of Ridgewood, died suddenly Sunday September 17, 2000. Em received her B.A. degree from Wellesley College and her Masters in Education from Columbia Teachers College. She taught at Brearley School in NYC and the Shady Hill School in Cambridge. She was a member and past President of the Wellesley Club of Ridgewood, a member of the College Club of Ridgewood and The Society of Valley Hospital. Em is survived and dearly missed by her husband Bal, sons Peter and Bob, daughter Betsy and grandchildren Casey, Nikki and Mandy. Sister and sisterin-law of Kenneth and Jane Krakauer. The family will receive visitors at home from 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM Wednesday (9/20). There will be a memorial service at the Unitarian Society of Ridgewood (Cottage Place) on Thursday (9/21) at 11:00 AM.
