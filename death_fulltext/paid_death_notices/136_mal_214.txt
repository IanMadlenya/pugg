
        ROSS-Louis C., 93. Passed away on Sunday, May 17, 1998 in San Diego, CA. A 34 year resident of Eastchester, NY, he lived in San Diego for the past 12 years. He was a C.P.A. and had an active practice well into his 70's. A good man with a gentle nature, he will always be remembered by his wife Blanche, son David, daughter Jane Burdick, his grandchildren Gary & Stacey Ross and Jennifer and Janna Stammer as well as all who knew him. Services are today, 1:00 PM, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, N.J. Donations may be made to the Alzheimer's Assoc.
