
        JANOWITZ-Abe. Peacefully passed away in his 95th year in K.C., MO., on July 12. The long-time NYC resident was a revered educator, accomplished lyricist and respected poet. He was a loving husband, father and grandfather. He is survived by his sons Bob Janowitz and Ken Jordan (nee Janowitz); his grandchildren Regina Janowitz, and Kimberly, Lindsay and Bradford Jordan; and his brother, Jules Janowitz. The family is honoring Abe's wish that there be no formal funeral or service. He will be forever remembered in our hearts.
