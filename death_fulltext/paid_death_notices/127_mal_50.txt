
        BRANDT-Joseph. Joseph died July 19 at age 88. Survived by wife, Sylvia, daughters, Elaine of Los Angeles, and Joan (Trubenstein) of Staten Island, grandchildren, Joseph and Carrie Ann. He served in two wars, the Abraham Lincoln Brigade of the Spanish Civil War and World War II. He dedicated his life to making this a better world for all, especially the working class.
