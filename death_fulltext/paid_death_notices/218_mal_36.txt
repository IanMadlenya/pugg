
        SALGO -- Nicolas M. The Board and Officers of the American Hungarian Foundation deeply mourn the death of Ambassador Nicolas M. Salgo, who served with distinction as a member of our Board and was a Laureate of the Foundation's George Washington Award. Our sincere condolences are extended to his wife, Josseline, son, Miklos, daughter, Christina and grandchildren. Dr. Zsolt Harsanyi, Chairman Professor August J. Molnar, President
