
        WINOKUR-Samuel, 91, a business executive and community leader. He raduated from The Hotchkiss School, Yale College and Yale Law School, where he was Editorin-Chi ef of the Yale Law Journal. Selected as clerk to U.S. Supreme Court Chief Justice William Howard Taft. He was an attorney at Sullivan and Cromwell, an executive at Grand Union Company and Seeman Brothers, Director of The Air Wick Company and Circuit City Stores, and until his retirement at age 86, developed several Culligan water treatment franchises. Active as well in community activities, his love and advice will be missed by all. He is survived by his wife, Catherine, four sons, Dr. Jay, Richard, Dr. Andrew and Matthew, a stepdaughter Frances Ellen McSherry, ten grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on June 8th, time and place to be announced.
