
        ADELSON--Howard L. The Officers, Trustees, Advisors and Members of the National Committee on American Foreign Policy note with deep sorrow the death of professor Howard L. Adelson, a beloved officer of the National Committee. A noted scholar, a wise, warm and gentle human being, he will be greatly missed. Our heartfelt condolences to the Adelson family. William J. Flynn Chairman of the Board George D. Schwab President
