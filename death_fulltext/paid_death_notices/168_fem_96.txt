
        DUSENBERY-Elsbeth B. Of Montclair, NJ, on December 24, 2000. Beloved wife of 59 years of John F. Dusenbery , sister of Janet B. Hodgman, formerly of Glen Ridge, NJ, also survived by several nieces, nephews, grandnieces & grandnephews. Daughter of the late Clement S. Brainin and the late Helen (nee Stall) Brainin. Memorial services will be held on January 13, 2001 at 2 PM at the Montclair Art Museum, 3 South Mountain Ave., Montclair, NJ. The family asks that in lieu of flowers, donations be made in her memory to the Montclair Human Needs Ministry or any local food bank. For information Van Tassel Chickene Funeral Home, Bloomfield, New Jersey.
