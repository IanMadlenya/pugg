
        OSHRIN--Honorable Alan David, of Miller Place, NY on August 17, 2003 in his 64th year. Beloved husband of Judith. Devoted father of Benjamin Oshrin and Gayle Oshrin. Loving brother of Brian and Donna and loving uncle of Adam and Scott. Dear son-inlaw of Adele Recht. Judge Oshrin was Justice of Supreme Court, State of New York and was currently sitting as District Administrative Judge of Suffolk County. Interment was at Washington Memorial Park, Coram, on Tuesday. A future memorial service will be held at a later date. For more info., Moloney's Lake Funeral Home, Lake Ronkonkoma, NY.
