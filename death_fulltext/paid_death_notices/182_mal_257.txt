
        MA-Siki. On March 11, 2002. The Ma family mourns the passing of their dear father, husband, brother and son, Siki Ma. A dedicated owner of an optical enterprise, Siki Ma led by example and did much to expand his business. We are grateful and fortunate to have known a man who was as compassionate, kind, understanding, and caring as he was. Survived by his wife Eleanor, daughters Kaleena, Kamie, and son, Kenneth. Funeral services held on Friday, March 15th. A memorial service to commemorate his life is to be held on Tuesday, March 19th, 2:00 PM at the Mahayana Buddhist Temple Association, 133 Canal St., NYC. Please make donations to the International Myeloma Foundation or The American Cancer Society.
