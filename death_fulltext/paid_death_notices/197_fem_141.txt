
        MARYMONT--Marcia (nee Stettin) of Voorhees, NJ, formerly of Teaneck, NJ. Loving wife for close to 50 years of Joseph Marymont. Sister of Esther Beck and Ireene Mickenberg. Also survived by nieces and nephews and many dear friends. She dedicated her life for 55 years to the service of handicapped children at the New York Philanthropic League. Services Monday, 1 PM, Platt Memorial Chapels, Inc., Cherry Hill, NJ. Contributions in lieu of flowers can be made to a charity of the donor's choice. >>AD#
