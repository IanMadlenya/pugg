
        WERBEL--Mildred. 96, died in Honolulu. Widow of Bernard Werbel. Worked in NYC schools and at Werbel publishing. Moved to Hawaii 1983. Bridge Life Master and world traveller. Her love of family, generosity, intelligence, sense of humor, impressed all who knew her. She will be remembered and greatly missed by family and friends. Survived by son Stephen, brother Seymour, granddaughters Nina, Julie, Lea, and great grandsons Ethan and Zeke. >>AD#
