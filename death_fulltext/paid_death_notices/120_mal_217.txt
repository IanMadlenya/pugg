
        BALLANCE-Webster E., 86, died Jan. 17, 1997 in Irvington, VA. Mr. Ballance was a retired Foreign Service Officer & member of the Diplomatic Corps. He is survived by 2 daughters, 3 grandchildren, & 5 great-grandchildren. Graveside services will be held in Berryville, VA., on Wednesday, at 1 P.M. For information call Enders Funeral Home in Berryville, 540-955-1062.
