
        ROGERS - Anthony Nicholas, passed away August 16, 1997 in Los Angeles, California. He was born March 24, 1953 in Pasadena, CA., and owned a food importing business in Los Angeles and an art gallery in Santa Fe, New Mexico. He is survived by his sister, Maria A. Rogers, his brother, George M. Rogers, and his loving family and friends. He was the eldest child of Mary and George C. Rogers who preceded him in death. A vigil service will be held Tuesday, August 19th at 7:00 PM at Cabot & Sons Chapel, Pasadena and a Funeral Mass will be held on Wednesday, August 20th, at 10:00 A.M., at St. Andrew's Church, Pasadena.
