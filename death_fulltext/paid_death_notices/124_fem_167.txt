
        KOUZAN-Athena. Age 67 of Brooklyn, NY. Died May 14, 1997 following a long and courageous battle with cancer. Born in the Bronx, she lived most of her life in Brooklyn. Member of the NY State Nurses Association, she worked for Kingsbrook Jewish Medical Center for most of her career retiring in 1994. Athena will always be remembered for her love and kindness. Survivors include her brother William of Plymouth, NY; six nieces and nephews, several cousins and a dear friend Crete Manolatos. Friends and relatives are invited to attend a memorial service to be held on Sunday, June 1, at 1 PM, South Presbyterian Church, 343 Broadway, Dobbs Ferry, NY.
