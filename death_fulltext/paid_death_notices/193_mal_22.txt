
        MARTIN-Stan. The Board of Directors and staff of The ALS Association Greater New York Chapter are deeply saddened by the passing of our our Board member, Stan Martin. With the passing of his father to ALS (Lou Gehrig's disease) many years ago, Stan was committed to advancing ALS research and patient care. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Alan R. Griffith, Chairman John Ernst, Chair. Emeritus Dorine Gordon, President Wendy J. Schriber, Esq., VP Jacqueline Reinhard, E.D.
