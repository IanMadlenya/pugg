
        SCHMIDT-Benno C. The Board of Trustees and staff at The Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our dear friend & former Trustee, Benno C. Schmidt. We extend our most heartfelt condolences to his children, Benno Jr., Ralph, John, and William; his stepchildren, Ruth, Stephen and James; and to his entire family. Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Joel S. Ehrenkranz, President Maxwell L. Anderson, Director
