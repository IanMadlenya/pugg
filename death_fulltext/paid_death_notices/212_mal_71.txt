
        NAVON--David H. Age 79. Of Amherst, MA., after a brief illness, on September 8, 2004. A world-wide, renowned scientist, music was also the great passion of his life. He gave unconditional love to his wife, Bobbe (Amsel) Navon, son Marc, daughter-in-law Michelle, daughter Beth, son-inlaw Jonathan and grandchildren Max, Elana, Evan & Julia. Beth and Jonathan will be receiving friends at their home Monday, Sept. 13, 4-8PM.
