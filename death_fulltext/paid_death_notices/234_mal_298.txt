
        McCAFFREY--Raymond, M.D. A leader in gynecologic education and car passed away on July 24. Dr. McCaffrey held many positions at New York Presbyterian Hospital during the past four decades and played an integral role in moving forward the mission of the hospital's Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology. Throughout his career, he maintained a practice at Columbia University medical Center, and served as a professor at the Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons. Despite his distinguished academic career, Dr. McCaffrey was primarily known as a skilled surgeon and caring physician who was recognized as one of the top doctors in New York. He is survved by his wife, Janet; a son, Raymond, of Annapolis, MD; four daughters, Patricia McCaffrey, of Chicago, IL; Mary Beth Duncan of New York City and Greenwich, CT; Karen McCaffrey of New York City; and Meg Margolis of Fairfield, CT. He was the brother of Eileen Fox, of Wilmette, IL. He is also survived by his grandchildren, Christopher, Cecilia and Patrick Duncan. He was pre-deceased by his wife of 36 years, Mary, and his brother, the Rev. Joseph McCaffrey. A wake will be held on Friday from 4 p.m. to 8 p.m. at Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, N.J. Services will be held at St. Gabriel the Archangel Roman Catholic Church, Saddle River, N.J. on Saturday at 10 a.m. Donations may be made to Misercordia Heart of Mercy, 6300 North Ridge Avenue, Chicago, IL, and the Raymond M. McCaffrey, M.D. Education Fund, Department of OB/GYN, c/o Abigail Holcombe, 622 West 168th Street, PH 16-28, New York, NY 10032.
