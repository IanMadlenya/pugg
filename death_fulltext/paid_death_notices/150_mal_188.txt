
        FEIBEL-Arie, M.D. The Medical and Dental Staff of Coney Island Hospital mourns the loss of our dear friend and long time colleague, Arie Feibel. Dr. Feibel, Chairman, Department of Rehabilitation Medicine, a beloved and respected leader for many years. In addition to a comprehensive understanding of medicine, Dr. Feibel was unusually knowledgeable about every aspect of culture, politics and life. We will remember his dedication, energy, and commitment as we continue his work. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, Ruth, and son Theodor and all who loved him. His brilliant intellect and engaging wit will be missed by us all.
