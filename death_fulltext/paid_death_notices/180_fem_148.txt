
        BARSKY-STEINER-Fay. Age 83. Longtime resident of Delray Beach, FL. Passed away peacefully on January 13, 2002. Predeceased by Charles Steiner, loving first husband for 35 years, and by second husband Louis Barsky. Cherished mother of Barry and Richard Steiner and daughters-inlaw Marilyn and Diane. Loving grandmother of Chad and Alex Steiner. Devoted sister and sisterin-law to Sig Posner, William and Mildred Posner, and George Karl, who predeceased her, & to Yetta and Herbert Berman, Doris and Isaac Russo, Martha Karl & Helen Posner. She adored her many nieces and nephews and their children. She touched the lives of all who knew her. Service Thursay, January 17th, 12:30PM at ''Boulevard-Riverside'' Chapels, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, N.Y. Donations in her name may be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
