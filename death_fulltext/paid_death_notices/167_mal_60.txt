
        KURMAN-Martin, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joines with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Martin Kurman, M.D., a distinguished member of the medical staff in the Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for over 40 years. He forever will be remembered for the high quality, compassionate care that he provided to our patients, and for his dedication and commitment to the education of physicians in training. To his family, friends and loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Robert G. Newman, M.D. President & CEO, Continuum Matthew E. Fink, M.D., President and CEO., Beth Israel Henry Singman, M.D. President, Medical Board Arnold Friedman, M.D., Chairman, Obstetrics and Gynecology Robert L.
