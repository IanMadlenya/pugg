
        PIVIROTTO--Richard R. The Directors, officers and staff of General American Investors Company, Inc. are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed colleague, Richard R. Pivirotto, who served the Company with great dedication for more than 35 years as a Director. His wisdom, judgment and counsel were highly regarded by the Board. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Mimi, his children, and to other members of his family. Lawrence B. Buttenwieser Chairman, Board of Directors
