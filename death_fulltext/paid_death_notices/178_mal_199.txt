
        LARKIN-Edward Joseph. Age 67. Died suddenly November 11, 2001. Devoted husband to Patricia (nee Tarpey). Loving father to his nine children, Eileen, Kathleen, Kevin, Patricia, Mary, Thomas, Maureen, Elizabeth and Colleen. Second father to John, Jill, Billy, Joey, Lisa and John. ''G'' to Edward, Patricia, Matthew, Cari, Andrew, Victoria, Ryan, Morgan, Thomas, Julia and Jack. Beloved son of the late Thomas A. & Margaret M. Larkin. Dearest brother of Maureen L. Watson. The joy of his life was his family, although his love of Wall Street and the markets was great. Voted youngest partner of Goodbody and Company, former member New York and American Stock Exchanges, were among highlights of a long and illustrious career. Visiting on Tuesday and Wednesday, 2-5 and 7-9PM, McLaughlin Funeral Home, Third Avenue at 97 Street, Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial Thursday, 10 AM, Saint Anselm Church.
