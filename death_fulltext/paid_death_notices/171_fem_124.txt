
        FLANDERS-Jane. Of Pelham Manor died Thursday afternoon, April 12, 2001. She was 61. Beloved wife of Steven Flanders, she was the cherished mother of Kathryn Flanders Ryan of Providence, R.I., Benjamin Flanders of Cincinnati, Elinor Flanders of Chicago, and grandmother of Jack Ryan of Providence. A memorial service will be held at Huguenot Memorial Church, 901 Pelhamdale Ave, Pelham Manor, N.Y. 3PM Saturday, April 21st. The family will receive visitors at Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave, Pelham April 16 and April 17, 2-4 and 7-9PM.
