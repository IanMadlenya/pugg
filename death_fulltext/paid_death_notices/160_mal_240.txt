
        DWYER-Honorable Thomas W. On May 19, 2000, at age 66. Nassau County District Court Judge for 14 years. Lifelong resident of Nassau County, graduate of Chaminade High, St. John's University and St. John's Law School. Beloved and devoted husband to Mary Ita (nee Feeney). Proud and loving father to Tracy, Kathleen, Mary, Thomas, Sean, Megan and father-inlaw to John Aguilar, James Ruscick, Harold Tobin and Melissa. Doting grandfather to Juliana, Kira, Leah, Mackenzie, Molly and Logan. Cherished son of Margaret and the late Honorable Thomas A. Loving and admired brother of Jack, Kevin, Michael and Jane. Cherished friend to many. Our world is richer because he was part of it. Wake Monday and Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Weigand Bros. Funeral Home, 49 Hillside Ave., Williston Park, NY. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10 AM, at Saint Aidan's RC Church. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to Big BrothersBig Sisters of Long Island, 70 Acorn Lane, Levittown, NY 11756.
