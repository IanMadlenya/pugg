Survived by his wife Charlette (nee Payne), two daughters Ashley & Chelsea; his mother Lula Thompson of Lumberton, NC, one sister Lisa Fenner of Charlotte, NC, and one brother Mike Thompson of Burlington, NJ. Memorial service will be held on Monday, November 12, 2001 at 2PM at the Cathedral at 277 Madison Ave., Perth Amboy, NJ.
