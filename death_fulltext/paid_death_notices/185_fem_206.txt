
        KESSLEROHEN - Estelle/d. Born in 1909, died in New York City on June 24, 2002. She married Louis M. Cohen, a lawyer, and textile manufacturer and founding member of the United Jewish Appeal. They had two daughters Hannah and Sandra. She later married Henry H. Kessler, founder of the Kessler Institute for Rehabilitation in New Jersey. She leaves five grandchildren: David, Brian, Kenneth, Nikolas and Alexandr. She also stayed close to her son-in-law Herbert Halberg and David's wife Frances. Estelle supported many foundations during her life and the family would greatly appreciate contributions in her name to the Kessler Institute of Rehabilitation International, and The American Jewish Committee, UJA and Cerebral Palsy. Services Friday, June 28, 2002, 12 noon at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave at 65th St. It will be followed by a burial service at Mt. Carmel Cemetery.
        KESSLER-Estelle. The Women's Campaign Board of the American Jewish Committee is deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed Board member, Estelle Kessler, a woman who dedicated herself to the goals and mission of the AJC for nearly 40 years. She will be missed. We send our heartfelt condolences to her family. Elaine Petschek, Hon. Chair Karen Levy, Chair
