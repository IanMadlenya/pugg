
        FRIEDMAN-Florence Zirinsky. Age 94. Died peacefully September 11, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Friedman. Wonderful mother to Bill & Lynne & the late Joan Laura. Devoted grandmother of Katie, Margo & Tom. Loving great-grandmother & aunt to many nieces & nephews. Loyal & special sister of Evelyn Kossoy & the late Doris, Sylvia & Lawrence. She was one of the founders of the AAUN Womens Division, president of Sisterhood of Union Temple & active Red Cross volunteer. Her sharpness and candor will be sorely missed. A Private funeral was held.
