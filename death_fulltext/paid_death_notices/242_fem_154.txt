
        SCHNEIDER--Estelle C., age 80, much loved wife of Dr. Philip Schneider, passed away on March 19th. She is survived by her devoted husband, adoring children (Marcie Schneider and her husband Michael Ader, Cindy Bogorad and her husband Len Bogorad), five grandchildren, her sister and brother-in-law (Judy and Howard Seed), and nieces and nephews. She will be missed by many.
