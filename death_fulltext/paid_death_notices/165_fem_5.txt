
        GUINIER-Doris I. Suddenly passed on September 28, 2000. She was a retired guidance counselor of the NYC Board of Education and a devoted, active participant in many community organizations. Beloved mother of Clotilde Y. Stenson, beloved grandmother of Lisa M. Stenson, Esq., and beloved matriarch of her family. Her wisdom, strength of character and energetic spirit will be deeply missed by family and friends, and she will live on in our hearts forever. Visitation will be held on Thursday, October 5, 4-8 PM, at J. Foster Phillips Funeral Home, Inc, 179-24 Linden Blvd, Jamaica, NY 718526-5656. Funeral service will be held on Friday, October 6, at 10:30 AM, at St. Albans Congregational Church, 172-17 Linden Blvd, St. Albans, NY. Interment will be held on Saturday, October 7, at 11 AM, at Oakland Cemetery, Sag Harbor, NY.
