
        FALKOW--Michele G., 48, the beloved wife of Lewis R. Steinberg, died at home on March 24, 2006 of esophageal cancer. She was the devoted daughter of Seymour and Florence Falkow, the loving sister of Stephen Falkow, the aunt of Zachary Falkow, as well as the daughter-in-law of Shirley Sobel and the sisterin-law of Carol Strasser and Eric and Adele Steinberg. A librarian, lawyer, and law professor, she was also an avid recreational runner and a four-time finisher of the New York City Marathon. After graduating from Radcliffe College (Harvard University), where she was Arts Editor of the Independent, she received a J.D. from the New York University School of Law and an MLS from the Columbia University School of Library Service. She held a variety of professional positions, including an Associate Librarian and Adjunct Associate Professor at Fordham Law School, as Assistant Legal Writing Professor at St. John's University School of Law, as an Adjunct Professor at Rutgers University School of Communication, Information and Library Studies, and as Associate Director of the Library at Paul, Weiss, Rifkind, Wharton & Garrison. She was also a past President of the Law Library Association of Greater New York, a Board Member of the Lar Lubovitch Dance Company and a Member of the Schlesinger Library Council at the Radcliffe Institute for Advanced Study at Harvard. Her funeral will be held at The Riverside Funeral Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, at 1:15PM on Sunday March 26, 2006. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to the Michele G. Falkow '79 Fund for the Schlesinger Library, Radcliffe Institute for Advanced Study, 10 Garden St., Cambridge, MA 02138.
