
        GOODMAN--Saul. Born April 20, 1919, died September 8, 2003. Beloved uncle of Sandra G. Ortner, I. Michael Goodman and Michael Steig. Dance author and lecturer. Feature writer for ''Dance Magazine'' where he published 232 dancer biographies and 13 featured articles. Publications: ''Dancers You Should Know, Baryshnikov: A Most Spectacular Dancer''.
