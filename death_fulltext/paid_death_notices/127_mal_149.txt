
        SULLIVAN-Roger W. On August 12th, beloved husband of Susan; devoted father of Farley and stepfather of Mark and Warren Dabney; loving brother of Barry. Outstanding educator and principal of Smithtown High School. In lieu of flowers and gifts, donations appreciated in his name to the American Heart Association. Memorial services Friday, August 15th, 6 PM, at Temple Beth Sholom, Edgewood Avenue, Smithtown.
