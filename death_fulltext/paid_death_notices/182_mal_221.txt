
        JEFFCOAT-Albert E. (Jeff). Formerly of Larchmont, NY died March 10 at his home on Bainbridge Island, WA, after a valiant battle with cancer. His courage, spirit, and optimism throughout his life will be remembered through his accomplishments as foreign correspondent of the Wall Street Journal, founder of the Manhattan Theatre Club, and author of the critically acclaimed non-fiction ''Spirited Americans''. At the time of his death, he was working on a second book, ''Soul, Searching''. As a loving husband to his wife Jan, and devoted father to sons Thomas, Peter, John and Ross; daughters Lindsey and Catherine; and four grandchildren, he will never be forgotten. Memorial services will be held at St. John's Episcopal Church in Larchmont, 11AM, Thursday April 11. In lieu of flowers, remembrances may be made in his name to the Manhattan Theatre Club, NY or the Grace Episcopal Church Library Fund, Bainbridge Island, WA. 98110.
