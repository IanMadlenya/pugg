
        BOBROW--Elias Naum, M.D. July 18, 2004, at home in New York City. Beloved husband of Felicitas. Born in Buenes Aires, he was a resident of Jacksonville, FL, and NYC. He will be fondly remembered for his warmth, wry sense of humor and his industrious work habits. Funeral services at Crestwood Memorial Chapel, 33 Spring St, Manhattan, on Tuesday, July 20, 9:30 am, Cantor Daniel Pincus Officiating. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery.
