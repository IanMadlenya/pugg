
        GOSSETT-William T. The President and Trustees of Columbia University express deep sorrow at the passing of our Trustee Emeritus William T. Gossett, Esq. He generously contributed his wisdom and talent to Columbia and higher education for the advancement of human welfare and equality in America. Columbia University extends its profound sympathy to Mrs. Kathryn Gossett, and their family. George Rupp, President of the University Stephen Friedman, Chair of the Trustees
