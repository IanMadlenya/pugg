
        SKOLNIK--David. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of David Skolnik, beloved father of our cherished friend, Rabbi Gerald C. Skolnik. A dedicated spiritual leader in the Queens community, Rabbi Skolnik is a member of the Board of Directors and the Commission on Jewish Identity and Renewal at UJA-Federation. May the members of the Skolnik family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
        SKOLNIK--David. The Officers, Trustees and Members of The Forest Hills Jewish Center mourn the loss of David Skolnik (in Israel), beloved father of our Rabbi, Gerald C. Skolnik. Devoted to his family, David was a man of grace, warmth and good humor. We pray that the Rabbi, Robin and their children, his mother Lillian, and his sister Bea Krieger and her family find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Cantor Adam Frei Matthew Beizer, President Shira Shapiro, Exec. Dir.
