
        TANNER-Frederick C., Jr. Of Greenwich, CT. On February 23. Survived by his wife Dorelle Moulton Tanner, daughters Alexandra Tanner and Fredericka Tanner, stepson Vincent Andreus, 3 grandsons and three step-grandchildren. Brother of Ogden Tanner and Jane Trimingham. Graduate of Hotchkiss and Yale '39. Attended Harvard Law School. Service at 4:30 PM on Thursday, February 26, at Round Hill Community Church, 395 Round Hill Rd., Greenwich, CT. Contributions may be made to the Yale Alumni Fund-Memorials, PO Box 1890, New Haven, CT. 06508.
