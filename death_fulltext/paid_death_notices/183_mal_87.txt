
        SELIGSON-King Cecil. Died at home in NYC on March 28, 2002, at age 85. Known in business as Cecil King, he was an interior designer and manufacturer to the trade for 64 years. He is survived by his wife, Olga; daughters, Carol King and Bonnie Joy, and grandson, Nicholas Seligson-Ross. A memorial service will be held on Monday, April 15, at 6:30 PM.
