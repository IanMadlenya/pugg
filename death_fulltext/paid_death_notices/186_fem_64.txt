
        BEAL-Rose Sedlis, 107 years old. Of Coconut Creek, FL., formerly of Brookline, MA. died July 11, 2002. Vital until her last moments, she was a devoted wife, mother, grandmother and great grandmother. Rose was an extraordinary woman who deeply inspired us all. Her impact on our lives has been profound and we feel truly blessed. Her adoring grandchildren, Dannie and Bill Sedlis, Matthew & Maggi Goldstein Great-granddaughter Emily
