
        BEINBRINK-Jeffrey R., Captain USN retired, age 51, son of Shirley and Robert Beinbrink of Manhasset, NY passed away on March 27, 1997. A graduate of Annapolis, he was Commanding Officer of the fleet Training Center in San Diego. He is survived by his wife Barbara, sons Will and Andrew of San Diego, a sister Nancy Gilman of Manhasset, NY and a brother Glenn from Charleston, SC. Contributions to the American Cancer Society would be appreciated.
