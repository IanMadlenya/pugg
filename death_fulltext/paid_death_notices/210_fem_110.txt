
        SANDORF--Florence Kurtz. On July 20, 2004 at age 68. Beloved wife of Irving, loving mother of Julie, Lisa and Caralynn, adoring mother-inlaw of Michael, cherished grandmother of Sarah and Aidan. Entrepreneur, artist, great talker, and lover of animals, she led a rich and fulfilled life. She was universally loved by family and scores of friends alike. Funeral services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76 St on July 23 at 10 AM. Contributions in her honor and memory can be made in her name to Horse of Connecticut Inc, 43 Wilbur Rd, Washington, CT 06777, or The Fund for Public Schools, Inc, 52 Chambers St, Room 305, New York City 10007.
