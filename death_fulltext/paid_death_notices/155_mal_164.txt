
        RUBIN-Israel. December 4, 1999. Beloved husband of Anna Halberstam Rubin. Beloved father of Edward Rubin, Haya Rubin and Miriam Kipper and beloved zayda of nine. A Holocaust survivor, he was Emeritus Professor of Sociology at Cleveland State University. Despite a deep and broad knowledge of Torah, Talmud, Jewish history and culture he was a humble and faithful man. We will miss him all our lives. Donations to Bikur Cholim, 3789 Shannon Road, Cleveland Heights, Ohio, 44118.
