
        FRICK--Helmut. Mutual of America, its Board of Directors, together with all of its officers and employees, mourn the passing of our former Senior Field Vice President and respected colleague, Helmut Frick. During his 35 years with the Company, Helmut provided clients with faithful and dedicated service, and was a wise mentor to many who followed. His professional contributions helped to build the structure of our Company. We cherish his memory, and extend our sincere condolences to his wife Helen, his son Erik, and daughter Lisa. Thomas J. Moran Chairman, President & CEO Mutual of America Life Insurance Company
