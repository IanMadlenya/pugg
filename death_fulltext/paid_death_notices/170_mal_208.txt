
        STICKNEY-Richard Warren. 81 years, of Somers, NY, formerly of New Canaan, CT. Husband of Margaret. Brother of John T. Stickney of Honolulu, HI, and Patricia Stickney of NYC. A decorated captain of the US Army during World War II. He was wounded at the Remagen Bridge after being one of the first soldiers to cross it. He received the Bronze Star, the Purple Heart and the Air Medal. Funeral services at St. Matthew's Church, Bedford, NY, Monday, 11 AM. Interment Arlington National Cemetery.
