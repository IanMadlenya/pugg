
        KLOOGMAN-Raoul. September 21, 2001 of Great Neck, NY. Born in Vienna, Austria, May 25, 1914. Beloved husband of the late Ruth (Weisman) Kloogman. Loving father of Joan Kloogman Borkow, Judith and Jerrald Weinstein, and Helen Kloogman and Michael Gold. Loving grandfather of Dana & Chad, Matthew, Jacob, Rachael, Ari, Dov, Ruthie & Aaron. Loving great-grandfather of Casey, Tanner and Juliette. Loving brother of Edith Green and Nina Ottolenghi. A survivor of the Holocaust, he was a dedicated and respected physician in the Great Neck community for more than 40 years. He died peacefully at North Shore University Hospital, which he helped found. Services will be held Sunday, September 23, 2001 at 12:45 PM at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, New York.
        KLOOGMAN-Raoul. Great Neck Synagogue notes with sorrow the passing of its longtime esteemed member. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire bereaved family. Dale Polakoff, Rabbi Dr. Ephraim Wolf, Rabbi Emeritus Howard Wolf, President
        KLOOGMAN-Raoul. We mourn the passing of the father of our esteemed member, Judy Weinstein, and pray that the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Young Israel of Great Neck Yaacov Lerner, Rabbi Michael Frank, President
