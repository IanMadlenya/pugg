
        de ROTHSCHILD-Edmond. The Board of Directors and staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and of the Israel Museum, Jerusalem mourn the death of Baron Edmond de Rothschild, Founding Chairman of the Israel Museum's International Council. In addition to his enormous contributions to the Israel Museum, the Baron served for fifteen years as International Chairman, calling upon friends and associates to provide the funds to build a major museum for art and archeology in Jerusalem. We offer heartfelt condolences to his wife Baroness Nadine de Rothschild who succeeded him as Chairman and to their son Benjamin who continues the family tradition of leadership in business and in charity. Teddy Kollek, President, I.M. Dov Gottesman, Chairman, I.M. James Snyder, Anne and Jerome Fisher Dir, I.M. Maureen Cogan, Chairman, A.F.I.M. Judy Steinhardt President, A.F.I.M. Michele Cohn Tocci, Executive Director, A.F.I.M.
