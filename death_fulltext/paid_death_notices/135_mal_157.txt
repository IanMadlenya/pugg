
        CHOQUET-Claude, 73. Died April 6, 1998 in New York Hospital after a long illness. An outstanding graduate of The Ecole Normale Superieure in Paris, he first came to the U.S. in 1954, and taught French for a year at Yale before joining the faculty of the Lycee Francais de New York as a teacher of philosophy where he remained until his retirement in 1986. Burial on April 17 in St. Martin le Beau, France. A memorial mass will be held at St. Thomas More, 65 East 89 Street, NYC, Wednesday, May 6, 6:30 P.M.
