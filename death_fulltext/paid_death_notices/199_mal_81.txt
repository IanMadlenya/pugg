
        SEELER--Richard W. It is with great sadness that the Real Estate Board of New York mourns the loss of its beloved member and esteemed former chair Richard W. Seeler. His contribution to the real estate industry spans five decades, and he will be sorely missed by his many friends and colleagues in the industry. Our thoughts and prayers go out to his family, friends and colleagues. Officers and Members of the Real Estate Board of NY Burton P. Resnick, Chair Steven Spinola, President
