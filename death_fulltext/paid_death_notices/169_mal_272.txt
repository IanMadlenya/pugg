
        BUNIN-Alan I. At 49, of a heart attack on February 17. A generous and humorous man, he was the mainstay of public radio in Hawaii and was highly regarded in musical circles. He is survived by his wife, Helen, a daughter, Natasha, his mother, Estelle and his sister, Karen Huss. He will be missed by the family who loved him and his thousands of radio listeners. Sheldon and Harriet Bunin
