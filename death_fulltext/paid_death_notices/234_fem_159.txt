
        BIRRITTELLA--Emily M., (nee Domchek) 94, of Plantation, Florida, initially of Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, passed away peacefully at home on July 26, after a wonderful life. Loving mother of Roseann (Buffy) and James, motherin-law of Brenda, cherished grandmother of Jessica. Predeceased by her beloved husband James, parents Anna and Anton Domchek and sister Rose Yuscak. She is survived by her sisters Betty Sansone and Josephine Frankevich, brother Frank Domchek, in-laws Marian Terlizzi, Harriet Birrittella, Connie and Otto Revellese, Rosetta and Ralph Damiano and 23 nieces and nephews. The family will receive friends and family at Hastings Funeral Home, 15 Spring St., Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, from 4-9:00 p.m. Sunday, July 30. Funeral mass will be held at 10:00 a.m., July 31st, at St. Matthew's Roman Catholic Church, 616 Warburton Ave., Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. Burial will follow at St. Joseph's Cemetery, 209 Truman Ave., Yonkers, NY. Family suggests donations be made to The American Lung Association, 61 Broadway, New York, NY 10006.
