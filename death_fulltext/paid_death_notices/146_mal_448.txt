
        PRITCHARD-Dr. Wilbur L. The City College of New York mourns the passing of Dr. Wilbur L. Pritchard, a distinguished alumnus of the School of Engineering, Class of 1943. Dr. Pritchard, a pioneer in satellite engineering and an early advocate of satellite broadcasting, was the president of W.L. Pritchard & Co., Inc., a consulting engineering firm doing technical and economic studies in telecommunications for private firms and governments. Dr. Pritchard was awarded an honorary Doctor of Science degree from CCNY in 1993. He was a firm friend of his Alma Mater and a strong supporter of public higher education. On behalf of the City College community I wish to extend my deepest sympathy to his family. Yolanda T. Moses, President The City College of New York/ CUNY
