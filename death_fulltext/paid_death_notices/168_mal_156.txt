
        FOX-John L., MD, 83. Died on Saturday, January 6, 2001 at Carolinas Medical Center in Charlotte, NC. The cause of death was due to complications from cardiopulmonary disease. Dr. Fox was born and raised in Brooklyn, NY. He attended Xavier High School in New York City and graduated from St. John's University and New York Medical College. He served as a Captain in the U.S. Army during WW II in the European Theater. During his tour he was attached to the 236-305 General Medical Corps. He was Director of Anesthesia at the Hospital for Special Surgery in New York City for many years until his retirement in 1987 and remained on the Honorary Staff. Dr. Fox resided in Garden City, NY for 32 years. Following his retirement he moved to Sag Harbor, NY and recently made his home in Charlotte, NC. He is survived by his wife of 45 years, Dolores (nee Klein), and children; John of Garden City, NY, Kevin of Fort Wayne, IN, Michael of Midlothian, VA, Mary Giedraitis of Charlotte, NC, Catherine Bell of West Islip, NY, and Elizabeth Stewart of Cumming, GA, and twelve grandchildren. He was predeceased by his brother Vincent Fox. A Mass of Christian Burial was held at St. Gabriel RC Church in Charlotte, NC on Monday, January 8. Interment followed in Forest Lawn East Cemetery, in Matthews, NC. Services were in the care of Hankins & Whittington Matthews Chapel, 3700 Forest Lawn Drive.
