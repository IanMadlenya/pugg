
        BRENNER -- Ilse K. (nee Schaeffer). Of Fort Lauderdale, FL, and Forest Hills, NY, passed away peacefully on September 14, 2003. Widow of Max Brenner, married for 58 years. She was a devoted and caring daughter, sister, wife, mother, sister-in-law, grandmother, and great grandmother. We were blessed to have her with us for more than 88 years and cherished all of our wonderful memories. She will be sorely missed by her daughter, Joan S. Toder, and her son-in-law, Richard S. Toder, of Scarsdale, NY. Her passing will also be mourned by her grandchildren, Elizabeth Toder, of NY, and David Toder and his wife Colleen, of Highland, NY. Her thoughtfulness will be remembered by her great grandchildren, Sol, Max, and Kiernan. Contributions in her name may be made to: Westchester Alzheimer's Association, 785 Mamaroneck Avenue, White Plains, NY 10605-2523.
