
        LANGER-Ruth. At the age of 74. Beloved wife of the late Isaac. Devoted mother of Marcelle Langer Sheehan, the late Gabe and the late Esther. Cherished grandmother of Spencer and Melinda Sheehan and Elaine Langer. She was a Holocaust survivor from the Lodz ghetto and her strength and memory will live forever in our hearts. Services Monday, 11:30 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
