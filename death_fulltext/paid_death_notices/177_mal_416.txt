
        COHALAN-Florence D., Rev. Msgr. 93, of New York City. Ordained to the priesthood June 11, 1938. Msgr. Cohalan served the Archdiocese of New York at the Church of St. Peter, Staten Island, NY, as Professor of History at Cathedral College in NYC and as Pastor of the Church of St. Paul, Staten Island, NY. In retirement he served the Church of the Annunciation at Crestwood, NY and the Church of St. Agnes, NY, NY. Survivors include three nieces: Maureen Cohalan Curry, Derval Brown, Tyrell Connor; one nephew, Daniel F. Cohalan; many grand nieces and nephews and great grand nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell-The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st Street on Wednesday & Thursday 2-5, 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian burial Friday 10:30 AM at the Cathedral of St. Patrick, 5th Ave. & 51st Street. Interment Calvary Cemetery.
