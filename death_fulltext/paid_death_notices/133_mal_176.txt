
        PANZARELLA-Dr. Frank P. On Feb. 10, 1998. Devoted father of Frank Jr., Nancy and the late Paul. Grandfather of Christopher and Allison. Son of the late Amalia and Angelo. Father-in-law to Meredith. Beloved brother of Angela, Eva, Theresa, John, Joseph and the late Edward, Stanley and Arthur. Brother-in-law of Laura and Tommi. Dr. Panzarella, Ph.D. in Biochemistry, was CEO and President of Doak Pharmaceutical Company. He purchased Doak in the early 1960's and originated the skin care dermatologic concept which resulted in his discovery of Formula 405 and many other dermatological products. He graduated from Bates College in 1930, was sent to England during W.W. II and completed his Ph.D. in biochemistry a few years later. He then became a professor at Fordham University. He was honored as Man of the Year Boys Town of Italy and owned Horti Laboratories. Dr. Panzarella who was 89 on January 17th had end stage kidney disease. He admired Dr. Speck, CEO of Columbia Presbyterian Hospital in Manhattan, for presenting to him the finest group of doctors who helped him to survive because of his determination and will never to give up. ''Every day,'' he would say, ''is beautiful because I am alive.'' He inculcated so many around him with a profound respect for life for the elders in our society. Reposing at Edward F. Lieber Funeral Home, 266 North Central Avenue, Valley Stream. Visitation Thursday and Friday, 2 to 4 and 7 to 9. Funeral mass on Saturday, 9 AM, at Blessed Sacrament RC Church. Interment St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. In lieu of flowers please send a donation to benefit the Dialysis Department at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, 622 W. 168 St, NYC toward the purchase of a new EKG machine and stateof-the-art dialysis machine. May God bless all of the people who became a part of our father's life.
