
        LIEBOWITZ - Louis. 91. Died peacefully March 18. Father of Bob, Rick, Laura and Betty, with his dear deceased wife Freda. He leaves behind his wife Betty, grandchildren Bob, Larry, David Emma, Alex, Roni, Jon, Jeremy, Ann, Jesse and Lori. Beautiful great-grandchildren Cathy, Sarah, Lily, Zeke, Nate, and Claire. Devoted in-laws Neal, Priscilla and Lorraine and his mejores amigos. Orphaned at a young age, he became self-educated and lived his life with strength, acceptance and humor.
