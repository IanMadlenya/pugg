He is survived by his wife, Betty Beatus, daughters Allison Asaro, Carolyn Lockett, and grandchildren, Christopher and Kelly Lockett. We will mourn his death and celebrate his life forever. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Monday, October 15th, 9-11 AM, with services 11:30AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Doctors Without Borders, 6 East 39 Street, 8th Floor, New York, NY 10016.
        BEATUS-Howard Z. We mourn the death of our friend and patient, Howard Beatus. We will miss his friendliness, generosity and elegance. An astute businessman, gourmand and Giants fan, he faced several major illnesses with courage and intelligence, protecting his beloved wife Betty, children Allison and Carolyn, grandchildren, friends, and us from his suffering. Rest well, dear Howard. Peter H. Rubin, M.D. and Staff: Judy, Felicia, Patty, and Jane
