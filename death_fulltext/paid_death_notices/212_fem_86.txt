
        SVIRIDOFF--Doris. 85, beloved wife of the late Mitchell Sviridoff, died peacefully on September 8, 2004. In recent years, she divided her time among New York City, Martha's Vineyard and Los Angeles. She is survived by her daughters Karen Segal of Los Angeles and Michele Sviridoff of New York City, her grandson Christopher Nichelson and her great grandchildren Kiyoshi and Sanae.
