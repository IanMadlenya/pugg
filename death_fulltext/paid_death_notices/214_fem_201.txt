
        MARCUS--Anne. November 28, 2004, in her 94th year. A proud graduate of Hunter College, she taught mathematics for many years at Ellenville (NY) High School. Beloved wife of the late Bernard Marcus and mother of Kuni Yavneh and David Marcus. Grandmother of Tenli and Naomi Yavneh, and Gregory and Eugene Marcus. Greatgrandmother of eight. Sister to Ruth Bendiner, Shirley Marcus and the late Carl Marcus. Services private.
