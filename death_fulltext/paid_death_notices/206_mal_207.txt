
        KOSSOW--Daniel H. Of Armonk, NY on March 15 at age 73. Much loved husband and friend of Kari and father of daughters, Karen, of Arlington, VA, Ellen of San Francisco, CA and son Jon and his fiancee, Molly, of Menlo Park, CA. A graduate of NYU and Harvard Law School ('55) Dan loved film and theatre and was a much respected entertainment lawyer in NYC for many years. Arrangements private. In lieu of flowers donations in Dan's memory may be made to the Armonk Volunteer Fire/Ambulance Company, 40 Maple Ave, Armonk, NY 10504 or the North Castle Public Library, 19 Whippoorwill Road E., Armonk, NY 10504.
