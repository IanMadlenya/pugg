
        WEINSTEIN-Constance G. Beloved wife of the late Arnold. Cherished mother of Susan Schuyler and Tom Mullen and Steven and Ellen Weinstein. Doting grandmother of Jed, Josh and Lauren. Dear sister of Lucille Porter, David Gottlieb & the late Solon Gottlieb. Her proud & independent spirit, integrity and wise counsel will be missed by her family and many friends. Contributions may be made to the Arnold A. Weinstein Fund at the Henry George School of Social Science, 121 East 30th Street, New York, N.Y. 10016. Service Wednesday, 11:15AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        WEINSTEIN-Constance G. The trustees, staff and faculty of the Henry George School of Social Science mourn the passing of the School's Secretary and Treasurer. Connie was tireless in her devotion to the School's mission of promoting social justice. We extend our sympathy to her daughter, Susan Schuyler, and her son, Dr. Steven Weinstein, and the family.
