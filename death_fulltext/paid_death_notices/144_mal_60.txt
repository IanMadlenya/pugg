
        EVERETT-George Todd. Died peacefully at home in Chapel Hill, N.C. On January 1, 1999. Husband of Helen Burton Everett, father of Todd Everett, Davis Everett, and Susan Rawes and grandfather of Caleb and Silas Everett, Rachel and Samuel Rawes, and Zoe Everett. Retired in 1988 as General Manager for Latin American Operations after 30 years with General Mills. His positive energy, generosity, and caring for family and friends will be deeply missed. Donations may be made to The Lake Champlain Committee (14 S. Williams St., Burlington, Vt. 05401).
