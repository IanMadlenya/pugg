
        MCCOMBS-A. Parks, M.D. Age 96 of Sarasota, FL. Passed away December 13, 1998. She was born March 2, 1902, in Charlotte, NC, and moved to the Sarasota area 23 years ago from NYC. She interned at Bellevue Hospital, then joined her Aunt, Dr. Connie Guion, in private practice in NYC. She was an Assistant Professor of Medicine at Cornell Medical College 1955-68. Director of the Medical Department of the N.Y. Infirmary from 1955-60, and Consultant in Medicine to the N.Y. Infirmary from 1960-76. She received her Bachelor of Science degree from Connecticut College in 1925, and her M.D. from Cornell University Medical College in 1929. She was a member of Phi Beta Kappa. Listed in ''Who's Who of American Women'' and active in many medical associations during her career. She is survived by several cousins, including Ann W. Haskell of Charlotte, North Carolina. Services and burial will take place in Charlotte, NC at a later date. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be sent to a charity of the donor's choice.
