
        PITT-Jane Ries. Died peacefully on April 16, 2003, at age 64. Predeceased by her husband Martin Pitt, she is survived by her loving children Jennifer and Eric. She committed her life to her family and friends, and the medical treatment of children, and had a passion for the arts, music, and good food. Burial Service at Westchester HillsStephen Wise Cemetery, Route 9A, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, Friday, April 18 at 11AM, followed by a Memorial Service at Temple Beth Shalom, 740 North Broadway, Hastings-on-Hudson, NY at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Temple Beth Shalom-Jane Pitt Memorial Fund, to be distributed to charities important to Jane including those promoting children's health.
        PITT-Jane, M.D. The Division of Pediatric Infectious Diseases and the Department of Pediatrics at Columbia University College of Physicians & Surgeons deeply mourn the loss of Jane Pitt, MD, one of our treasured long-time colleagues. We will miss her brilliance and desire to help others, particularly children afflicted with HIV infection. She made numerous important contributions to the medical understanding of this terrible virus, and the world is a better place because of her efforts. Her great respect for human ethical values and efforts to heal the world were an example to all of us. We will miss her, and extend our deepest sympathy to her family. Anne Gershon, M.D., Director Div. of Infectious Diseases John M. Driscoll, Jr., M.D. Reuben S. Carpentier Prof. Chairman, Dept. of Pediatrics
