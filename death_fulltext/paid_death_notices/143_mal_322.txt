
        LANGER-Michael Adam, M.D., 45, of Los Angeles died Dec. 22, 1998. Dr. Langer was a dedicated physician, loyal friend and a loving son, brother and uncle. A kind and courageous man he will be missed by family and many friends. He is survived by his parents, Martin and Estelle Langer, brother and sister-in-law, Robert and Jennifer Langer and nieces Jessica and Stephanie Langer. Services were held, Thursday, December 24, 1998 at Pacific View Mortuary Chapel, 3500 Pacific View Dr., Newport Beach, CA. Donations may be made in Dr. Langer's name to the charity of your choice.
