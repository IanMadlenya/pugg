
        GRENADER-Max. Devoted son of the late Esadore and Ida. On June 21 at the age of 77. Max was a native of Houston, Texas. He moved to Manhattan 54 years ago. He worked as an office manager and was well travelled and thoroughly loved the fine arts. He is survived by Harriet and Joseph, Joan and Harry and Pearl and Sig Altman. He leaves eight nieces and nephews. Services today, 11AM, Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, N.Y. Call Sinai Chapels for information: 718-445-0300.
