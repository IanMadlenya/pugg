
        BALDACCINI-Renzo. Paintings conservator, art historian, friend to art of all centuries. Born Florence, 1912. New York Journal American, 1963, said of his own paintings, ''a striving to extract from life's incongruities a sense of order and beauty.'' Loyal husband to Louisa. Beloved godfather of Christopher Berry. Dear mentor and friend to Gloria and Eliot. Service St. Ignatius Loyola, 84th St. and Park Ave., 9 AM, Tuesday.
