
        KRANTZ-Jane Iris. Of West Nyack, died on July 27, 2001, at age 74. Born in NYC, on July 15, 1927, to Sigmund and Sadie (nee Theise) Steinheimer. On December 22, 1946, she married Rabbi Abraham Krantz. In addition to her husband, she is survived by her children Jeremy Krantz (wife Robin) of Cortlandt Manor, NY, Melissa Krantz (husband David Fleisher) of Harrison, NY, and Deborah Feldman (husband Marc) of Woodland Hills, CA. Also survived by seven grandchildren and predeceased by a sister Beverly Wegbreit. For information, Zion Memorial Chapel, 914-381-1809. www.zionmemorialchapel.com
