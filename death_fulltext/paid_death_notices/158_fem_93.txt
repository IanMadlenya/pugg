
        ALTSTADTER-Gertrude (Trudy) nee Waldbaum. On March 4, 2000, age 70. Of Sunrise, FL, and formerly of Whitestone, NY. Loving wife of 44 years to Seymour, beloved mother to Eric and Meryl Altstadter, and Robin and Jeffrey Roude, devoted and cherished grandmother to Jonathan, Emily, Amanda, and Brett. Dear sister to Ida and Melvin Goldstein, Florence Frieze, Bill and Yetta Waldbaum, Betty and Sol Malkin, and Ruth Altstadter. She was a brave and loving person and a friend to many. Throughout her illness she showed us her strength and courage. She left us too earlyshe will be greatly missed.
