
        RINDNER--Sandra. Of New York, NY died July 3, 2006, after a brave battle with cancer. Sandy was an extraordinary woman who dedicated much of her life to the well-being of animals. As a Rescuer of small breed dogs, she was devoted to the welfare and placement of abandoned pets, working tirelessly to save each little orphan. Sandy is survived by many friends who deeply miss her sharp wit, boundless energy and creative brilliance. She leaves a legacy of caring and compassion. Memorial Service at the Hartsdale Pet Cemetery on Saturday, July 22, 12:30. Donations to Miss Rumples' for Small Dogs, Inc., PO Box 1776, NYC 10159.
