
        TILLINGHAST-Charles Carpenter, Jr. The Horace Mann School Community notes with profound sorrow the passing of their dear friend and esteemed alumnus, Charles Carpenter Tillinghast, Jr., HM '28. His distinguished family has been a prominent part of Horace Mann's tradition, history and growth since 1920. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Lisette, to his son, Charles, HM '54, and to his entire family. Michael D. Hess, '58 Chair, Board of Trustees Dr. Eileen Mullady, Head of School
        TILLINGHAST-Charles Carpenter, Jr. The Directors of the Henry Luce Foundation mourn the passing of their distinguished colleague and friend who served with great loyalty on the Foundation's Board from 1974-1996. He will be remembered for his wise counsel, unerring common sense and often uncommon insight. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Lisette and to all of his family. Henry Luce, III, Chairman and John W. Cook, President
