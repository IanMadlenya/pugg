
        SHULMAN-Helen D., 79. Of Great Neck on December 23. A light has been extinguished in the lives of her husband, Dr. Bernard Shulman; sons Donald (Paula) and Matthew (Aline); & grandchildren Zara and Jean-Philippe. Funeral at Riverside-Nassau North (Great Neck) at noon, Friday, December 26. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Tampa Bay Holocaust Museum, 5001 113th St, Madeira Beach, Florida 33707.
