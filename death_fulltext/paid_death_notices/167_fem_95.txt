
        WOLFSON-Doris. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Doris Wolfson. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        WOLFSON-Doris. December 8, 2000. Beloved wife of Monte Wolfson. Loving mother of Robert, Suzanne and Jon. Cherished grandmother of Jayne and Thomas. Devoted sister of Roy Hertz, Ruth Kahn and Nancy Nadler. Service Tuesday, December 12, 3:30 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
