
        BORENSTEIN-William. Age 59. Passed away on October 15, 2002 after a brief illness. Devoted husband of Laura Nowak, loving father of Lea and Lou, cherished brother of Reva Metzger, Elie Rosenberg and Ruth Maass. Contributions in his name may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice, Beth Israel Hospital.
        BORENSTEIN - William. Treasurer of the W. 13th St 100 Block Association, will be missed for his dedication to the community, his caring nature and his warm smile. Bill touched the lives of many and brightened each one. W. 13th St. 100 Block Assn.
