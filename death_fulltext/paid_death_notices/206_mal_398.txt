
        LEVY--Edward. The Officers, Board of Governors and Members of Metropolis Country Club mourn the passing of our esteemed Honorary Life Member who died at the age of 96 and was a member of Metropolis Country Club for 53 years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Helen, his son James, his daughter-in-law Leslie, his grandchildren Caren and Jonathan Greenhouse, Laurie Levy and his great grandchildren Sophie and Caroline. Ronald M. Stuart, President Joyce Baumgarten, Sec'y
