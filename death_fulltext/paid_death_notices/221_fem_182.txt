
        BREWER -- Diana Burkett, previously of Wilton, CT, died at her home in Ocala, FL on June 19. She was 75. Mrs. Brewer, a graduate of Wellesley College, was formerly a reporter for the Miami Herald. An avid golfer at age 19, she was women's champion at the Westchester Country Club in Harrison, NY. She is survived by her husband of 49 years, Hugh Cleland Brewer, Jr.; five children, Hugh C. Brewer III, Daryln Brewer Hoffstot, Jeffrey Brewer, Jennifer Giem and David Brewer and 12 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
