
        SCHWAGER-Robert G. A longtime resident of New York City and CEO of International Cultured Pearl passed away February 22, 2000. Born in Vienna, Austria on August 15, 1917. He is survived by his wife Elizabet, his children Susan Wulkan & Ronny Schwager, grandchildren Wendy, Lenny and Joey, and sister Herta Selzer. A Memorial service will be held March 5 from 11 A.M.-1 P.M., at Reddens Funeral Home, 325 West 14th Street, New York. The family requests that in lieu of flowers, a contribution be made to your favorite charity in memory of Robert Schwager.
