
        GOODMAN--Dr. Edmund N. The Columbia University Department of Surgery mourns the loss of our esteemed colleague, teacher and friend, Dr. Edmund N. Goodman. Dr. Goodman earned his MD and DSc degrees from Columbia University and spent his entire professional career in our department. He was a skilled technical surgeon of urbane manner and wit, who also trained several generations of young surgeons. We extend sincere condolences to his wife, Marian, and his children. The Department of Surgery Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons Eric A. Rose, M.D., Chairman and Surgeon-in-Chief
