
        COHEN-Dorothy ''Dolly'' Suser, 90 years old, died peacefully in her home in New York City on Sunday, September 23, 2001. Beloved wife of the late Arthur Cohen. Adored mother of Morey, Larry, Kenneth and Susan. Devoted grandmother and great-grandmother. Loving sister of Sally Harvey of Jerusalem, Israel. Loving aunt to eight nieces and nephews. Your spirit will never leave us. You will be missed by all who knew you. Services will be held on Tuesday, September 25, 2001 at 1PM at Riverside Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in her memory can be made to your charity of cohoice.
