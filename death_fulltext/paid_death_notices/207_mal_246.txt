
        RAPHAEL--Alan H. The Board and staff of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged (JASA) express their profound sorrow at the passing of Alan H. Raphael. Mr. Raphael has been a supporter of JASA for many years and his children have followed in his footsteps. We extend our condolences to his daughter, Cathy Raphael, and her brother, John Raphael-who has provided key support to assist our most vulnerable clients for many years. David M. Warren, President Aileen Gitelson, CEO
