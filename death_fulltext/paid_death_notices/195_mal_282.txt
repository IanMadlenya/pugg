
        PHILLIPS-Nathan. 90, died on April 22, 2003, at his home in Westport, CT. Beloved husband of Sylvia, cherished father of Joan Phillips and Shelly Metz and Laurie and Roy Witkin. Loving grandfather of Mark and Deborah Phillips, Neil Phillips, Nicole and Brian Keldsen, Scott Witkin, Josh and Nicole Witkin, and Jennifer Witkin. Adored great-grandfather of Emily, Samantha and Jake Phillips. His love of family, zest for life, strength and indomitable spirit will always be remembered. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Shiva on Sunday, April 27, at the Witkin home in Westport, CT, 1-7 PM.
