
        CLIFFORD--Jonathan. Died in his home on Tuesday, July 29, 2003 in New York City after a courageous battle with lung cancer. He was 68. Jonathan was known for his fast and acerbic wit and his keen intelligence. He attended Gov Dummer Academy and Harvard University. He is survived by Vincent Hart, his partner for 35 years and his sister Mary Lewis and brother-in-law Arthur Lewis of Boston, and nieces Meghan, Gillian, Heather Lewis and husband Thomas Moylan and nephew Gregory Clifford Lewis. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street on Thursday 6-9PM. Funeral Mass Friday 11AM at St. Ignatius Loyola R.C. Church, 84th St and Park Ave. >>AD#
