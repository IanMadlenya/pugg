
        SKOBLE--Herbert, of New Rochelle, NY, passed away at home June 10th, 2003. He was a television commercial film director who spent his career in NYC and in Europe. He was known for his love of travel, tennis, friends, food and cooking. He is survived by his three daughters, Luisa of Westerly, RI, Jenny of Los Angeles, CA and Emma of Paris, France and four grandchildren. Memorial service will be held at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Rd., Mamaroneck, NY, on Saturday, June 14th, at 10:30 AM, with a meal to follow, of course. Donations in lieu of flowers can be made to the American Civil Liberties Union. >>AD#
