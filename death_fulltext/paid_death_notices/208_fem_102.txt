
        RUNSDORF -- Elizabeth Benge, formerly late of Brooklyn, NY, 84, died Sunday May 9 in Rockville, Md. Beloved wife of the late Dr. H. Norman Runsdorf, devoted mother of Blithe, Merry (George) Mendelson, Lynn (Victor) Adams, Helen (Robert) Greco, and Joseph (Marla). Loving grandmother of Jessica Mendelson Cowan, Michael Mendelson, Rebecca Appelbaum, Bryan Adams and Hadleigh Runsdorf. Donations to the Brooklyn Botanic Garden or the Salvation Army Foster Home and Adoption Services will honor her work with these organizations.
