
        ABELSON-Jacob, DDS. Of NYC died on February 9, in Ft. Lauderdale, FL. He is survived by his wife of 57 years Charlotte, his two children David and his wife Michelle, Lynn and her husband Ken, and his five grandchildren Matthew, Daniel, Jonathan, Lauren and Jordan. A Phi Beta Kappa graduate of CCNY and then Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery, he practiced dentistry in NYC for 55 years. He was Clinical Profesor of Dentistry at Columbia University and a fellow of the Greater NY Academy of Prosthodontics. He was loved and revered by everyone whose life he touched. A memorial service will be held in NYC in the near future.
