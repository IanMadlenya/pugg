
        NOBLE-Anna (Loughlin), age 88, on Sunday, Sept. 14, 1997, at home. She was a Lease Custodian with Charles F. Noyes Co., New York, for over 50 years, retiring in 1974. Survivors include her brother, William Loughlin, a sister, Rita Walsh, and many nieces and nephews. Moore's Home for Funerals, 1591 Alps Rd., Wayne, NJ, 973-694-0072.
