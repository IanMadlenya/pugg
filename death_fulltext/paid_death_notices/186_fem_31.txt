
        ROSEN-Dorothy. On July 4, 2002, at age 95. Devoted, patient and loving wife of Murray Rosen. Dedicated and wise mother of Norma Revman. Loving and proud grandmother of Caren Feingold, Robin and Rick Schottenfeld. Adoring and playful great-grandmother of Michael and Jason Schottenfeld. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the AntiDefamation League or the Visiting Nurse Service of NY Hospice Program.
