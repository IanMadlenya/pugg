
        JACOBS--Abner. Abner Jacobs, beloved husband to Peggy Jacobs, father to Joseph and Susan Jacobs, father-in-law to Michelle Jacobs, grandfather to David and Stephanie Jacobs, stepfather to Pamela Kelly, and step-grandfather to Jamie and John Kelly, passed peacefully at the age of 95 on April 24, 2006. Abner was a kind, gentle, and generous soul who will be greatly missed by all of those who had the privilege of knowing him. The memorial service will be private.
