
        HISKEY-Clarence F., PhD. 86. Of Guilford, CT. Chemist and Professor of Chemistry at University of Tennessee, Columbia University, Brooklyn Polytechnic Institute. Worked for Tennessee Valley Authority and Manhattan Project. Director of Analytical Research at Endo Laboratories. Survived by children Iris Hiskey Arno and Nicholas Sand, grandchildren Max, Zack and Sorrel, and long time companion Betty H. Freedman. His unfailing scientific curiosity, intelligence, humor, love, and unique personality will be missed.
