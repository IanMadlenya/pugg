
        POOR-Nancy (nee Anna Easton Poor). Age 74, died on June 30, 2002 in New York. She is survived by her son, John Marc Parrott and his two children, David and Julia, of Philadelphia and her two brothers Charles Lane Poor III of Washington, DC and J. Sheppard Poor of Rumson, NJ. Although Nancy had many occupations including model and speech pathologist, she adored music. A celebration of her life and her passion for music will be held on Sunday, September 22 at 4PM at the Church of the Transfiguration at 1 E. 29 St, NY, NY. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, September 28 at 1PM at the SixMile Run Reform Church on Rte 27 in Franklin Park, NJ. Donations may be made to Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center at 1275 York Ave, NY, NY 10021 or to the musical group of your choice.
