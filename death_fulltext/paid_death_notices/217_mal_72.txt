
        SHAW--Leon. Died on January 27th, 2005 in Delray Beach, Florida at the age of 77. He is survived by his wife Beatrice Albrecht-Shaw and his two daughters Dodie Dorf and Debbie Kase, by his deceased wife Mickey Ehrlich Shaw. He is also survived by his sons-in-law Douglas Kase and Barry Dorf, and his grandchildren Marni and Jordie Kase. He was interned at Wellwood Cemetery on Long Island on January 31st, 2005. Donations in his honor can be made to United Cerebral Palsy or the American Cancer Society.
