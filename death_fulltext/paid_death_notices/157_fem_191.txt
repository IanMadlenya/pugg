
        JOSEPH-Jean L. Died February 20, 2000 at age 82. Loving wife of the late Maurice. Loving mother of Susan and Richard. Also survived by her sister-in-law Anabel Meister, grandson John and daughter-in-law Clare. Services were held February 21st.
        JOSEPH-Jean. National Council of Jewish Women, Peninsula Section, mourns the passing of their valued Board Member and extends condolences to the family. Adrienne Lurie, President
