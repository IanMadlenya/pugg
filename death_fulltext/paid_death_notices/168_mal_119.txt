
        STONE-Saul. The officers, Board and staff of the Jewish Community Relations Council of New York mourn the passing of Saul Stone, beloved father of our esteemed board member, Professor Richard Stone. May he and his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Gedale B. Horowitz, President Ezra G. Levin, President Elect Michael S. Miller, Executive V.P.
