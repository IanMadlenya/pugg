
        STACKPOLE -- William, of Lawrenceville, NJ, died on July 11, 2004, at Princeton Medical Center. He was 78. Educated at Harvard University and Columbia Law School, he practiced law in Manhattan and Princeton. In 1988, after earning a Master's Degree in Psychology, he became a clinical counselor in Princeton. He is survived by his wife, Willa; his son, William Jr. of New York; daughters, Amy Brigham of Bronxville, NY, Abigail McCall of Lawrenceville, and stepdaughters, Leslie Gregg of Charlottesville, VA, and Cameron Gregg of Santa Fe, NM. Funeral service will be private.
