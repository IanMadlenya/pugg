
        HARRIS-Irma R. (Feder). On July 14, 1997. Age 91. Widow of James L. Feder, died 1964 and Nelson M. Harris, died 1980. Survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Jane and James Gollin, her grandsons, Timothy J. Gollin, and his wife, Mary; and Douglas Gollin, and his wife Cheryl Doss; and her great grandchildren Andrew, Elizabeth and Eleanor Gollin and James Doss-Gollin. At her request, there will be no services.
