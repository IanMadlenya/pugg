
        BOYD-William F., DDS. The Board of Directors and Staff of The Nassau County Dental Society mourn the death of Dr. William F. Boyd, on August, 27, 2000. Dr. Boyd was a past president of the Society (1976) and served as its Executive Director from 1990 to 1998. His good humor, gentle manner and wisdom will be missed by not only his family and friends, but also by his fellow dentists and the countless people that he helped throughout his professional life. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Arline, daughter Helen and her husband Daniel Popeo, grandchildren John and Margaret Popeo of New Hartford, New York, daughter Virginia and her husband Timothy Brosnan of Garden City, New York. David J. Miller, DDS President Gabrielle K. Wiener, MPA, Executive Director
