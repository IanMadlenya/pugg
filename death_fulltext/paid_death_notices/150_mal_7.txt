
        KAPLAN-Harry A. On June 30th in White Plains at age 97. Beloved husband of Esther, devoted father of Edward and Ruth, father-in-law of Kenneth Canaver, and grandfather of Alexander. Services Thursday, July 1st, 12 noon at Riverside Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, NY. Contributions in his name may be made to the charity of your choice.
        KAPLAN-Harry A. Executive Vice President of Joseph A. Kaplan & Sons Inc. from 1924 to 1987. He helped found our shower curtain company with his late brother, Joseph A. Kaplan. Beloved uncle and dearest friend; he will be deeply missed. Harold M. Kaplan
