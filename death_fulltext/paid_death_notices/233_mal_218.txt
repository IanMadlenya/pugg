
        MALLONN--Hubert R. ''Bob'', 75, of Morehead City, North Carolina died on Friday, June 2, 2006 at Pitt Memorial Hospital in Greenville, NC. Mr. Mallonn was a veteran and served in the U.S. Army Infantry. He was the Night Shift Executive Foreman of the Composing Room here at the New York Times. He was with the New York Times for 36 years. He is survived by his wife, Evelyn Mallonn, children: Patricia Gajda of Waldorf, MD, Robert Mallonn of Long Island, NY, Brian Mallonn of Florida, Marilyn Feirer of Alpharetta, Georgia and a sister Dita Ansorge of Germany. Also surviving are six grandchildren: Jennifer, Kristin, Janine, Kyler, Michael and Brenna. $(6$)MALLONN -- Hubert. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Hubert Mallonnn, associated with The Times since April, 1958 until retiring in June 1994.
