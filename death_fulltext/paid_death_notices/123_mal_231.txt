
        O'CONNOR-Dr. Donald J. In his 74th year, of Glen Rock, NJ. Died April 18, 1997. He was a member of the Manhattan College Engineering faculty since 1952. Beloved husband of Anita (nee Lordi). Loving father of Dennis, Arlene O'Connor-Bell, and Jeanette O'Connor. Devoted brother of Fr. Samuel David O'Connor, and adoring grandfather of Christopher Connor Bell and Kristin O'Connor. Funeral Mass will be Tuesday 10 AM, at St. Catharine RC Church, Glen Rock, NJ, with interment to follow in Ascension Cemetery, Monsey, NY. Visiting Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ, 201-444-7650. Instead of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society, 20 Mercer Street, Hackensack, NJ 07601 in Dr. O'Connor's name would be appreciated.
