
        RAYMOND--Harriet Stemmler. Age 103, born in Brooklyn, NY. She was advertising manager for Celanese Corp. of America. She received the Advertising Woman of the Year by the Advertising Federation of America in 1959. Lived in Sarasota, FL for 20 years. Services will be October 16, at 11 A.M., Sea View Cemetery, Mt. Sinai, NY.
