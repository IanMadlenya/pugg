
        GOODSELL-Percy Hamilton, Jr., 89, of 77 Tress Road, Cheshire, CT, died March 24, 2000. Born in Hartford, CT., September 24, 1910 and the only child of Percy and Katharine (Seymour) (Day) (Jackson) Goodsell. He worked as a genealogist with The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society, retiring in 1961; consulting until the mid-1980s. He was a descendant of Oliver Wolcott, former governor of Connecticut and Signer of the Declaration of Independence, historian and lecturer of the American Colonial period. Distinguished member of many genealogical societies, including the Society of the Cincinnati, the Society of the War of 1812, and President-General of Descendants of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence, Inc. He is survived by his double first cousin, Mr. Joseph Hamilton Jackson, of Westhampton Beach, Long Island, New York, as well as other second cousins. Funeral services, Saturday, April 1, 2000 at 2:00PM in Grace Church, 45 New Park Ave., Hartford, CT. Burial following in Cedar Hill Cemetery, Hartford. Contributions to: Scholarship Fund-Descendants of the Signers of the Declaration of Independence, C/O Mrs. Frederick Leadbetter, 101 Holly Avenue, Chestertown, MD 21620.
