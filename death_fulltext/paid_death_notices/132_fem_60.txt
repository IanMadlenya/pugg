
        RICKLIN-Fay. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Fay Ricklin, beloved mother of Gwen M. Greene, a member of the Wall Street Division Steering Committee, co-chair of the Wall Street Women's Forum and an exemplary leader in our organization. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
        RICKLIN-Fay. The Board of Trustees of the University of Rochester and the Trustees' Council of the College acknowledge with sorrow the passing of Fay Ricklin, a supporter of the University, and the devoted mother of Gwen Greene, President-elect of the Trustees' Council. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Gwen and her husband, John. Robert B. Goergen, Chairman Thomas H. Jackson, President
        RICKLIN-Fay. Of New Rochelle, January 4. Beloved wife of the late Morris Meltzer and the late Sol Ricklin. Loving mother of Gwen Greene and Deborah Meltzer. Cherished grandmother of Amelia and Julia Wallace. Devoted sister of Muriel Quittell and Libby Eisen. Loved by John Greene, Randy Wallace and Janet Ricklin. Donations to UJA/Federation.
        RICKLIN-Fay G. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its cherished member, and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. Amiel Wohl, Rabbi
