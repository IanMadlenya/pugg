
        KECKEISSEN--Rita G., on Monday, December 15, 2003, after a short illness. She graduated from the College of Mount Saint Vincent in 1939, and received a Master's Degree from Columbia University in 1959. She retired in 1983 as Reference Librarian of Columbia University, but continued to work as a researcher for the Oxford English Dictionary. She is survived by her loving sister, Gertrude K. Farrell, and nieces and nephews and their children. She was predeceased by Ann K. Lombardo, and Sr. Mary Gertrude Keckeissen, S.C. Friends may call Wednesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM at Bennett Funeral Home, 824 Scarsdale Ave., Scarsdale, NY. Mass of Christian Burial is at 9:45AM at Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, 8 Carman Road, Scarsdale, NY.
