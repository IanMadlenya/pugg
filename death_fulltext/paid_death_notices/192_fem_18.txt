
        BLY-Estelle S. Of New York (formerly of Great Neck) age 91. Loving widow of Saul Bly died peacefully on January 3rd, after a brief illness. Beloved mother of Linda Boxenbaum (Lewis) and Susan Obel (David). Adored grandmother of Nancy Rotenier (Robin), David Boxenbaum and Karen and Kenneth Obel (Jennifer). Loving great grandmother of Samuel and Sophie Rotenier and loving sister of Beverly Apple (Jerry). Retired for 26 years, she was the epitome of a kindergarten teacher, bringing love and good humor, sensitivity and caring to all whom she touched, regardless of age. Funeral services on Sunday, January 5 at 11:45 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street & Amsterdam Ave.
