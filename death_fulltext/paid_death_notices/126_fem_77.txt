
        LUBETKIN-Alice Green. Age 91 on July 7, 1997, following a long illness. Beloved wife of the late Jacques, loving mother of John, Elizabeth and James Lubetkin, and mother-in-law of Linda Lubetkin, Barry Lipton and Roberta Lubetkin. Cherished grandmother of Jordan, Dylan, William and Alexander. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday, July 9th 6-9 PM. Services private. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Wellesley College, in care of the Office of Resources, Wellesley, Massachusetts 02181.
