
        BATES-Harry Cole III. Died March 4 in his 70th year at his home in Westbrook, CT. A graduate of the Hackley School and of Hamilton College, he earned an M.A. from Columbia in American History, and had taught that subject in independent and public schools in NY and CT. A memorial service was held March 6 in the Church of the Holy Advent, Clinton, CT. He is survived by brothers, Robert P. Turner of Weston, CT and Charles T. Bates of Irvington, NY, and by many nieces and nephews.
