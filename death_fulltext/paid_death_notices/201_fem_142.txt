
        CAREY -- Diane D. (nee Downes). Age 74, of Manhattan, NY, passed away on October 21, 2003, at Lenox Hill Hospital. Born to Charles H. and Vera C. Downes, ''The lovely little person.'' Devoted wife of the late Michael J. Carey. Beloved sister of Patrick H. Downes. Beloved sister-inlaw of Deborah J. Downes. Before the time of mentors and networks, she forged an outstanding career in nursing and administration at Lenox Hill Hospital...A career of special relationships. It was these relationships and her lifelong friendships, which defined her life.
