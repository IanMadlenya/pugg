
        ALEXANDER-Charles W. Suddenly on November 27, 1999. Loving husband and best friend of Rhoda Landau Alexander. Beloved father of Michael, Jimmy, JoAnn and Richard. Cherished brother of Ira. Devoted grandfather to Jessica, Jacob, Jack, Max, Luke and Noah. Admired fatherin-law of Jane, Kim, Cindy and Patrick (Rodgers). Charlie devoted his talent and time in retirement as a volunteer arbitrator to the Education and Assistance Corporation. Dad, your love, humor, charm, loyalty, guidance and strength will live within us every day and always. Memorial services were held on November 28, 1999, at Gutterman's Inc, Rockville Centre, NY.
