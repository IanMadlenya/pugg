
        BUCKINGHAM-Virginia K. Age 88, of East Moriches, died on January 20, 2000, at her home. Beloved wife of the late Leroy. Virginia is survived by step-grandsons Mark Patronsky of Wisconsin, Ross Patronsky of Illinois and Neal Patronsky of Arizona, nephews William Kirk of Maryland, Howard Kirk of Florida and Peter Kirk of Maryland, her sister-in-law Jessie Kirk, also of Maryland. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, April 9, 2000, at the First Presbyterian Church of East Moriches. Contributions may be made to the Memorial Fund of the First Presbyterian Church, Box 565, East Moriches, NY 11940. For information, Sinnickson's Moriches Funeral Home, Center Moriches, NY.
