
        MOINESTER-Isaac E. Age 86, died January 23, 1999, Sunrise, FL. A principled philosopher, writer and retired printer for The New York Times. Passionate music lover and reader. A quiet and introspective intellectual. Joins his wife Rose with God. Devoted father of Deanna, Cindy, Murray and Alice. Grandfather of eight, great-grandfather of nine. ''Go peacefully as you have lived, drive carefully.'' Graveside service Sunday, January 24, 2:30 PM, Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, LI.
