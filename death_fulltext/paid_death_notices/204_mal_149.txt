
        MORAN--John Patrick. 76, died Wednesday January 7 at a local hospital in Charleston, SC. He was a member of the US Navy serving during WWII. He was a graduate of Long Island University and served honorably, loyally and efficiently as a member of the New York Police Department for 38 years rising from Patrolman to Assistant Chief. Survived by his wife of 52 years, Sally Connolly Moran; one son, Gerard; two daughters, Celia and Mary; one brother, James Moran; and two grandchildren, Matthew Sean and Kristin Mary Blumm. Contributions may be made to Charleston Catholic School Scholarship Fund, 888 King St., Charleston, SC 29403. Condolences may be sent to the family at www.mcalister-smith.com
