
        LEVINE-Shirley. Beloved wife of Jules. Devoted mother of Dr. Robert and Dr. Johanan. Dear sister of Rose Fischner. Dearest aunt of Aaron and Ronald Neretin and their families. Fond grandmother of Didi. Devoted teacher of generations of New York City kindergartners. Loved by all who knew her. She was a source of warmth, dignity and inspiration; perpetually joyful and an accomplished Hebrew scholar. Service Tuesday, 11 AM, at the West Side Institutional Synagogue, 122 W. 76th St.
