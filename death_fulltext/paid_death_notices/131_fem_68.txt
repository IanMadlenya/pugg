
        THOMAS-Doris A. Formerly of Lewisboro, NY died peacefully at home in Norwich, VT., December 2, 1997. Beloved wife of Gordon A. Thomas, devoted mother to Krista Lynn Thomas and Alix Elizabeth Jenkins. Her love of life, generosity, and compassion enriched a wide circle of family and friends. She will always be cherished by the Dartmouth class of 1949 for her many contributions to its activities. In lieu of flowers a memorial gift may be made to Friends of Hopkins/Hurd at 6041 Wilson Hall, Hanover, New Hampshire, 03755 Attn: Chris Larabee or Hospice Vnh-20 South Main Street, White River Junction, Vermont 05001.
