
        SKOPP-Roberta, 51. Of cardiac arrest. Survived by her husband Stephen, mother Sara, brother Jeff, niece Aiyla and nephew Avram. A native New Yorker, she lived in Los Angeles where she worked as publicist for Harman International. Her career spanned the music, financial and consumer electronics industries. Private service to be held on Wednesday. Memorial service to follow in fall. The family requests that memorial donations be sent to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation. Roberta's courage, humor and loving spirit will remain with all of us whose lives were enriched by her friendship.
