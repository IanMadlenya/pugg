
        HICKEY-Roger Andrew, Jr. Of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, died June 6. Ladenburg Thalmann employed him from 1965 until his retirement in 1990. He is survived by his wife, Nancy Lloyd, whom he married in 1952; son, Mark and wife, AnnMarie with children Christina and Laura; son Laurence and wife, Honorable Nancy Smith and children, Conor and Margaret; brother, John F. Hickey of CO.; sister, Ann Sollas of Bay Head, N.J., and numerous nieces and nephews.
