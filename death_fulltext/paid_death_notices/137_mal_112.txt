
        MALICSI-Alberto C., Jr. Died in the grace of our Lord at age 62 on June 11, 1998 in Zamboanga City, Philippines. He is survived by his sons, John Robert, Jean Paul, Sean Basil, a brother, sisters, nieces, nephews, brother-in-laws and sister-in-law. Interment will be held on Saturday, June 13, 1998 at 8:00 AM, at Baliwasan Cemetery, Zamboanga City, Philippines. Masses & prayers will be appreciated and in lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the Alberto C. Malicsi, Jr. Scholarship Foundation, Scout Limbaga, Pasonanca, Zamboanga City 7801, Philippines.
