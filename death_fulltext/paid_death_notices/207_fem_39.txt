
        ISAACS--Rose B. Longtime Baltimore resident, recently of New York City, died peacefully in NYC on April 5, 2004. She was the wife of the late Rufus, and loving mother of Fran Gilmore (John), and Dr. Ellen Isaacs, and grandmother of Aram. A memorial service will be held Thursday, April 8th, at 1:30 PM at the Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Donations in her memory can be made to: The Maryland Institute College of Art, 1300 Mount Royal Avenue, Baltimore, MD 21217.
