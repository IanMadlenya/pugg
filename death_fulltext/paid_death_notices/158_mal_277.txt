
        ROUNICK-Jack A. Born November 14, 1926. Of New York, Easthampton and Miami, passed on Monday, March 20, 2000 in Miami, Fla. after a long illness. Former President of Ma-Ro Hosiery and Rex Knitting Mills. Son of Max and Bessie Rounick and brother of the late Herbert Rounick. He is survived by sisters, Marjorie Blum and Eleanor Weiss and beloved sons, Scott, Christopher & Gustaf Rounick. He will be greatly missed. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to your favorite charity. Funeral service will be held at the Independent Jewish cemetery in Sag Harbor, NY, Friday, March 24, 2000 at 1 P.M. Any inquiries can be made at Frank E. Campbell, 212-288-3500.
