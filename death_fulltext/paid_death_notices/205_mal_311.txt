
        MORGAN--Frederick. On February 20, 2004, after a long illness. Founding editor of The Hudson Review and poet. ''We have lost Fred, but we keep so much of him with us.'' Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, February 24, at 1:30 PM at Saint Thomas Church, Fifth Avenue at Fifty-third Street. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center or The Doe Fund.
