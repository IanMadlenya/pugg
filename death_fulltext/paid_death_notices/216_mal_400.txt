
        QUINN--James Joseph, of Bay Shore, LI, formerly of the Bronx, and citizen of the Republic of Ireland, on January 28, 2005, after a most courageous year with cancer. Survived by his cherished little flower and wife of 46 years, Therese; his six children, James, John, Janice, Joseph, Jill and Jeffrey, who were his life; his children-inlaw, Patricia, Michael, Michele, Michael and Tracy; his beloved grandchildren, Connor James, Austin Patrick, Trevor Michael, Anna Therese and Madison Grace Quinn; his little buddies, Madison, Shamus, Sydney, Alex, Riley and Camden; his siblings, Agnes Daly (Virginia), Mary Labuda (Pennsylvania), Kathleen Scheule (California) and Francis Quinn (Rockville Centre); his cousin, Sr. Mary Quinn Shalley, OSJ; numerous nieces and nephews, and several relatives abroad. Son of the late Angela (Ryan) Quinn of Newfoundland and the Bronx, and the late James J. Quinn of Laughtadurkan, County Mayo, Ireland. Reposing at the Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Causeway). Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial Monday, 11 AM, at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, LI. Interment St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale, LI. Visiting Saturday 7-9:30 and Sunday 24:30 and 7-9:30 PM.
