
        PRESTON - Barbara Grassi Thorne. April 2, 1999, of Armonk, NY. Wife of the late John N. Thorne, Jr., and Seymour Preston. Mother of Angela Sturgis, Zoe Thorne and Bridget Thorne. Sister of E. H. A. ''Bud'' Grassi. Also survived by five grandsons. Memorial service at St. Matthew's Episcopal Church, Bedford, NY, Wednesday, April 7, 11 AM. Interment private. Contributions in her memory may be to the American Cancer Society.
