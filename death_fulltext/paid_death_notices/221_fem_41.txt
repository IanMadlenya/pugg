
        LURIE--Sanna Klaveness. Died Monday, May 30, 2005, after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Dear daughter of Clara Normann Klaveness. Sister of Harda K. Fellows. Devoted aunt and cousin. Beloved wife of the late Iles Brody. She enjoyed her interesting and independent life as a fashion director for B. Altman & Co, and as a fashion editor for Harpers Bazaar. A caring and devoted friend who treasured her friendships. She will be missed and remembered always. Skaal, Sanna!
