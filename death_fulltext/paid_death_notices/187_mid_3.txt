
        PATTON-Theodore R., Jr. On August 16, 2002 of New Hyde Park and Hampton Bays, NY. Beloved husband of Diane for 39 years. Loving father of Claudia Dudek and her husband Stanley, Claire Patton and Heather Patton. Daer grandfather of Teddy and Edna Dudek. Mr. Patton was a graduate of the Admiral Farragut Academy and Lycoming College. He was a proud member of the U.S. Marine Corps, Tenth Special Basic Class and retired as a Major. Mr. Patton was a long time stockbroker with Prudential Bache and Fahnestock and Company. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave., at 12th Street, Garden City, NY, Saturday and Sunday, 2-4 and 7-9. Religious service 10 AM Monday at the Cathedral of the Incarnation in Garden City. In lieu of flowers donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, NY, NY, appreciated.
