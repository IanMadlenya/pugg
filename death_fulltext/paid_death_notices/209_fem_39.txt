
        FEILER--Lia (Lily). Died on June 4, 2004, in Durham, North Carolina, at the age of 88. Translator, editor, and writer, she was a committed intellectual and a vibrant presence who will be greatly missed. Devoted to her family, particularly her late husband of 68 years, Alfred Feiler, Lily is survived by her two daughters, Ylana (Miller) and Mirriam (Rosen), their husbands, Martin and Howard, and her grandchildren Joshua Miller, Zinaida Miller, Benjamin Rosen, and Gabriel Rosen. The funeral will take place on Tuesday, June 8, at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st and Amsterdam Avenue at 10 am. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Human Rights Watch or a charity of one's choice.
