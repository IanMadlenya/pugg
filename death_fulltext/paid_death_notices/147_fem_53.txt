
        HALLER-Evelyn M. Of Eastchester, N.Y., formerly of Bronxville, N.Y. On March 27, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Peter H. Haller. Survived by her brother John Magnus, her niece Erica Magnus and many friends. She was a noted animal sculptor and member of the Society of Animal Artists. Memorial donations may be made to the American Cancer Society or the Humane Society.
