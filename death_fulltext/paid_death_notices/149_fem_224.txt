
        PARKER-Gertrude, of Verona, NJ, died June 18, 1999 at the age of 79. Beloved mother of Patricia and Alan B. Abramson, Barbara Parker, and Bruce and Charlene Parker. Adored grandmother of Adam and Joshua Abramson and the late Jonathan Parker Abramson. Cherished wife of the late Abel Parker and the late Ben Spindel. Our world is greatly diminished and we will miss her dearly. Graveside services were held on Sunday, June 20.
