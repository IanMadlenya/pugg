
        WOJACK-Randy. The Board of Directors, Staff and Volunteers of Gay Men's Health Crisis mourn the passing of our esteemed former Board member and volunteer Randy Wojack. A memorial to honor his life and spirit will be held at GMHC on November 19 at 6 P.M. Donations in Randy's name may be made to GMHC, The Tisch Bldg, 119 West 24th St, NYC 10011. Bill McCarthy, Co-Chair Ana Oliveira, Executive Director
