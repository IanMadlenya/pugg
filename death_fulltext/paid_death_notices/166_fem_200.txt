
        BERKE-Helen Kirschner (nee Strauss). On November 8, 2000 in Belmont, MA. Born on September 11, 1914 in Brooklyn, New York. Beloved mother of Suzanne Kirschner Evenchik, grandmother of Alexander Laurence, and motherin-law of Leonard. Wife of the late Rudolph Berke, M.D. She was a warm and compassionate human being and a gifted opera singer, pianist, poet, counselor and teacher. Her life will continue to inspire us. A memorial service will be held in the spring at the New York Society for Ethical Culture. For information, please call (617) 864-4223
