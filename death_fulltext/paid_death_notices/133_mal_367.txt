        GARSON-Arthur. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Arthur Garson, a generous supporter of our organization through our Intimate Apparel Division, and the patriarch of a family whose relationship with us we greatly cherish. In addition to Mr. Garson's involvement with UJA-Federation, his son, Thomas A. Garson, is past chairman of the Intimate Apparel Division and currently serves as honorary chair. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the senior Mr. Garson's wife, Patricia; their four children; their seven grandchildren; & their great-grandchild. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P. Jeffrey S. Rosenzweig and Marty Shapiro, Chairs, Intimate Apparel Division
        GARSON-Arthur. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Community Service Society of New York note with sorrow the passing of Arthur Garson, a trustee from 1962 to 1979, a board officer and cofounder of CSS's Retired & Senior Volunteer Program. Our heartfelt condolences go to his family. David R. Jones, Esq., President Stephen R. Aiello, Ph.D., Chair
