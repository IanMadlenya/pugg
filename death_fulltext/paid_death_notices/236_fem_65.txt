
        RAMEY--Dr. Estelle R., age 89. Retired professor of Physiology and Biophysics at Georgetown University Medical School died at her home in Bethesda, MD on September 8th, 2006. She is survived by her beloved husband of 66 years, James T. Ramey, former commissioner of the Atomic Energy Commission during the Kennedy and Johnson administrations; her children James N. Ramey M.D. (Margaret E.) of Bethesda and Drucilla Stender Ramey (Marvin Stender) of New York City; her grandchildren James T. Ramey (Daniela Michel), Sarah M. Ramey, Jessica S.R. Stender, Margaret M. Ramey, and Caitlin E. Vaughn; her brother Jack Rubin. Interment. Private. A memorial service will be held at Georgetown University in the near future. Memorial contributions may be made to The Association for Women in Science, 1200 New York Avenue N.W., Suite 650, Washington, D.C. 20005 or to Sanctuary For Families, P.O.
