
        SMITH--Robert G. 93, of Arnold, Maryland, Pfeiffer professor of political science emeritus at Drew University, died Wednesday, December 13, in Arnold. He retired in 1977 after 40 years at Drew, where he founded the political science department and several off-campus programs. A Bronze Star recipient for his Army service in World War II, he is survived by his wife, Lois Squier Smith, two sons, two grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren. Memorial contributions may be made to the Robert G. Smith Scholarship, care of Development Office, Drew University, 36 Madison Avenue, Madison, NJ 07940.
