
        WALLEY-Miron M. Age 88, an attorney. Died of congested heart failure on February 12, 1997 in Philadelphia, PA. Formerly of Plainfield, NJ and Palm Beach, FL. Beloved husband of the late Silvia S. Walley and devoted father of William A. Walley, Barbara W. Schaff, and David G. Walley. Loving grandfather of eight grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. The family kindly asks that contiributions in his memory be made to The Rutgers Law School Alumni Fund, College Avenue, New Brunswick, NJ. Memorial service will be announced at a later date in Palm Beach, FL.
