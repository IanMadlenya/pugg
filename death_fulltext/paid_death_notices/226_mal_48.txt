
        WILDER--Steven. The Board of Directors, staff and clients of We Can are deeply saddened by the passing of Board member and Chair, Steven Wilder. Mr. Wilder has left a legacy of great compassion and generosity to New York's poor and homeless population and his talents, kindness and wonderful spirit will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family and many friends. Robert J. Sann, Executive Director
