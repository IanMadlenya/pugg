
        SPIEGEL-Evelyn. 88, of Scarsdale, NY, died peacefully on January 3, 2003, at the Westchester Medical Center in Valhalla, NY. Beloved wife of the late Leo Spiegel, devoted mother to her sons Robert Spiegel of Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, Dr. Edward Spiegel of Scarsdale, NY, Dr. Allan Spiegel of Palm Harbor, FL; loving grandmother to her seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren, and survived by three sisters who deeply mourn her passing. Funeral services on January 7, 12 noon, at Weinstein Memorial Chapel, 1652 Central Park Ave, Yonkers, NY.
