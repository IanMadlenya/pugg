
        LIEBERMAN-Hillel. The Yeshivah of Flatbush mourns the tragic passing of Hillel Lieberman, ''al Kiddush Hashem'' at the Tomb of Joseph. We offer heartfelt condolences to his parents, our esteemed former H.S. Principal, Honorary Member of our Board of Education and Rabbi of Cong. Beit Torah, Rabbi Sidney Z. and Bracha Lieberman, wife Yael Lieberman, seven children, sisters Tehila Lieberman and Elyorah Ellison, and family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion as we all pray for peace. Paul Kopyt, President Dennis M. Eisenberg, Exec. V.P. Bebe Levitt, Alumni Director
