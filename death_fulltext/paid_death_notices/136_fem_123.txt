
        SEIDEL-Regina. On May 18, 1998 in Williamsburg, VA., formerly of NYC. Beloved wife for 65 years of the late Dr. Harry Seidel. She is survived by and will be remembered as the loving mother of Suzanne Schoenbaum. Cherished grandmother of Carol Wolf and Robert Schoenbaum. Adored great-grandmother of Michelle and Rachel Wolf. Dear sister of Joan Scheichet, Dorothy Tave and Sophie Lawrence.
