
        WENG-Sin-Tzu. Passed away on June 26, 1997, in his home town, Ning Bo, China. He was President and CEO of Multi Resources International Corporation and President and CEO of Benilite Corporation of America. He was born on February 26, 1921, and is surivived by his wife Rosalta, son Steven, daughter Donna Lee, son-in-law Craig, and granddaughter Michelle. Memorial services will be held at a later date.
