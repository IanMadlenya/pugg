
        POGASH-Sylvia Namm. Died last Saturday, June 12, at the age of 89. Born in New York City, Mrs. Pogash was a longtime resident of northern New Jersey, before moving to Palm Springs, CA. She leaves her husband, Israel M. Pogash, her daughters, Susan Pogash of Chicago and Carol Pogash of Orinda, CA, and two grandchildren, Jacob and Rachel Wood.
