
        WEISS - Dr. Samuel M. passed away July 2, 2002, at Memorial Sloan - Kettering Hospital. He was a graduate of NYU Dental School and practiced for 47 years. He is survived by his wife Barbara Weiss, children Drs. David and Jewel Weiss, Barry and Susan Weiss and Gail and Chehab Chehab; a brother Dr. Jonas and Susan Weiss, and five grandchildren. He will be sorely missed.
