
        BURKO-David. Passed away March 5, 1997 in Miami Beach, FL. Beloved husband of Doris Burko. Cherished father of Diane Burko and adored grandfather of Jessica Burko. He was an active member of the real estate community of New York and Brooklyn for over forty years. He was deeply respected and loved by all those who knew him. Family suggests contributions to ''The Zimmerman Home for the Aged Burko Wing'', mail to Burko, 275 South 19 Street, Philadelphia, PA. 19103.
