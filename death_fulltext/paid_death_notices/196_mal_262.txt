
        ZAMBELLI--Alfred F., B.A., M.A. (Yale), World War II veteran, Fulbright Scholar, longtime New Yorker and well-known antiquarian book dealer, died peacefully on May 24, 2003, age 89. He is survived by his daughter, Pia, his son-in-law, Lorne Silcoff and grandchildren, Michael and Jonathan. >>AD#
