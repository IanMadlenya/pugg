
        RULE--Daniel Rhodes, age 63, on April 3, 2004 in NYC. Born on August 25, 1940, and raised in Los Angeles, CA. Graduate of Occidental College, followed by fellowships at Bayreuth and the Komische Opera in Berlin. As a Ford Foundation intern, he began a nearly 20 year career in management with New York City Opera, culminating in his last four years there as Managing Director. He was then General Manager of Central City Opera in Colorado for 10 years. He brought exceptional professionalism and skill to his vocation, along with unique supportiveness for the singers, stage directors, and other artists with whom he worked. He leaves behind cousins in Cleveland, San Diego, and NY, plus countless friends and colleagues who respected and loved him very much. A memorial service is scheduled for 11AM on Friday, April 30, 2004, at NY Society for Ethical Culture, 2 W. 64th St, NYC.
