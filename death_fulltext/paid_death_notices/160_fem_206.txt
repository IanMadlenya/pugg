
        ROTHSTEIN-Sandra Zuckerman. Of North Hills, NY and Boca Raton FL. On May 24 after a valiant battle with breast cancer. Adored wife of Edward. Beloved mother & mother-in-law of Edward & Susan Zuckerman, Ellen and Kenneth Slater and Linda and Glenn Gruen. Loving grandmother of Sam and Jackie Slater. Widow of the late Philip Zuckerman. A unique human being who distinguished herself as a Board Member of the American Cancer Society of L.I. She was the Chairman of The Babe Zaharias Golf Tournament since its inception. Her life was devoted to helping others help themselves. Services Friday, May 26, 11:30AM at ''Gutterman's''. 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, LI.
