
        COHEN-Roberta Broner. On Sunday, July 11, 1999, of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Washington, DC. Beloved wife of Norman G. Cohen. Loving mother of Jeffrey N. Cohen (Fran G. Cohen) of Washington, DC, Dr. John B. Cohen (Dr. Leslie Rodnan) of Washington, DC, and Nancy Cohen Roberts (Marc R. Roberts) of NYC. Grandmother of Craig, Jodi, Gabriel, Julia and Eli Cohen, Michael and Elisabeth Roberts. Sister of Herbert J. Broner and the late Sydna Broner. Services will be held at Adas Israel Congregation, 2850 Quebec St., NW, Washington, DC, on Thursday, July 15th, at 11 AM. Interment Adas Israel Cemetery. Contributions may be made to The Norman G. and Roberta B. Cohen Children's Fund at the Jewish Social Service Agency, 6123 Montrose Road, Rockville, MD. or The Jewish Foundation for Group Homes. Ives Pearson Takoma Funeral Home.
