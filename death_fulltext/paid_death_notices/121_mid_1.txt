
        CORTELYOU-Irwin Fearn, on January 29, of Rumson, NJ. Born Mobile, AL Sept. 23, 1896, daughter of George Fearn, Jr. and Aline Kennerly Irwin. Married April 18, 1922 to George Bruce Cortelyou, Jr. who died in 1973. He was a former Treasurer of New York Life. They had no children. She was a real estate broker in Monmouth County and art historian specializing in early American art. Services at 2:00 P.M., Tuesday, February 4, at All Saints Episcopal Church, Navesink. Interment private.
