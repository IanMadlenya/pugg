
        GREENBERG-Renee. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with profound sorrow the death of Renee Greenberg, a very special lady whose life was filled with achievement. We extend our sincere sympathy to her devoted and loving husband, Irving, valued and respected member of the Home's extended family, her children and grandchildren. We hope that your loving memories of Renee will help to ease your sorrow during this time of sadness. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec. V.P.
