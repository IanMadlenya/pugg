
        AMBROGI-Ron. The Board of Directors of the Goldman Memorial Band, Captain Ken Force, President; Dr. Christian Wilhjelm, Music Director and the Members of the band are saddened to learn of the death of our devoted and dedicated band member, Ron Ambrogi. Mr. Ambrogi's wonderful musicianship as a longtime member of the trumbone section, coupled with his positive outlook which made us all richer by his presence, will surely be missed. The Goldman Band family extends our sincerest condolences to his family.
