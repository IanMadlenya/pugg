
        STANTON--Sherry F., M.D., on December 29, 2004 at home in Sewell, NJ, age 54. Dr. Stanton was a Dermatologist practicing in Staten Island, NY. Survived by her mother, Irene Stanton, three sisters, Holly Stanton, Melinda Stanton and Sally Devires. Sherry is also survived by Special Friend Debbie Roney. A Memorial Service was held on January 5, 2005 at the Budd Funeral Home, Woodbury, NJ. Contributions may be made to Sherry F. Stanton, M.D. Education Fund, 47 Aldridge Way, Sewell, NJ 08080.
