
        SCHREIBER--Sidney, 95, of Chicago, formerly of Cedarhurst, NY, passed away on Saturday, May 15, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Hazel Sukoff Schreiber, loving father of Betsy Louise Schreiber Katz (Paul) and Eliot Bruce Schreiber (Jayne), devoted grandfather of Amy and David Katz, Abigail and Henry Schreiber. A veteran of WWII, an art teacher in the NY Public School System, and a travel writer for the South Shore Record and the Nassau Herald newspapers. Sidney was known for his warm heart, keen sense of humor and brave spirit. Private services will be held at Beth Moses Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY, on Thursday, May 20, at 11AM. Philanthropic gifts to Northwestern Memorial Hospital's Division of Vascular Surgery in Sidney Peter Schreiber's honor will support research and education of peripheral arterial disease. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to The Division of Vascular Surgery, attn: Liten Swanson, at Northwestern Memorial Foundation, 676 North St. Clair, Suite 2050, Chicago IL 60611.
