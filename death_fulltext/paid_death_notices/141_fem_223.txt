
        MITTENTHAL-Minnie. Lived with grace and dignity for 95 years. Devoted wife of the late Phillip Mittenthal and the late Henry Brand. Treasured by daughter and son-inlaw Phyllis and Larry Bangser and their children and grandchildren, and by Larry and Estelle Mittenthal and Richard and Joyce Mittenthal and all of their children and grandchildren. Memorial gifts may be given to the charity of your choice. Services private.
        MITTENTHAL - Minnie. Sister hood of Park Avenue Synagogue mourns the passing of a longtime, devoted and very generous member. She will be greatly missed. Matilda G. Goldberg, President Sisterhood
