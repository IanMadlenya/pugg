
        WISNIEWSKI-Harriet M., MD. The Sisters of Charity, Board of Trustees, Auxiliary and Staff of Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center note with sadness the death of our long-time friend and colleague, Dr. Harriet Wisniewski. Dr. Wisniewski was an ardent supporter of the values and mission of the hospital as a dedicated member of the Department of Radiology, President of the Medical Staff and long-time members of the Medical Staff Executive Committee. We will miss her wisdom, compassion, carism, and professionalism. We extend our condolences to her entire family. Thomas A. Coleman, Chairman Karl P. Adler, MD, Pres. and CEO
