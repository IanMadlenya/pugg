
        TUFT--Leonard W., 83, retired Executive Vice President of RCA, died Friday, November 11, of heart failure. Beloved husband of Irene Yuss and father of Sarah and David. At RCA, he negotiated commerical satellite agreements, retiring after 37 years to practice law. Born in Philadelphia, PA, he graduated first in his class from Temple University and received his juris doctorate from Harvard Law School. As a navigator in WWII he earned two Purple Hearts and the Distinguished Flying Cross. He was an ardent Democrat and a delegate to the 1960 Democratic National Convention. Funeral services will be held at 1 pm, Monday, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, NYC.
