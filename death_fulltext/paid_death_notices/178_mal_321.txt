
        McCOOEY-Robert H. The Behavioral Health Services Board, Administration and Medical Staff of Saint Vincent Catholic Medical Centers expresses its deep regret upon the passing of Robert H. McCooey. We mourn the loss of a dedicated leader who for 28 years, as member and chairman of our St. Vincent's Westchester Advisory Board, friend and supporter, demonstrated an untiring commitment to our mission that inspired all of us. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his devoted family. Saint Vincent Catholic Medical Centers Edward V. Lahey, Jr., Chairman Behavioral Health Services Board Brian L. Fitzsimmons, Ph.D. Senior Vice President-SVCMC, Pres.-Behavioral Health Services
