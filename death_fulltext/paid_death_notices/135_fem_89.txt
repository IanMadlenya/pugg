
        JAMESON-Dorothea. On April 12 in her 77th year. Beloved wife of Leo M. Hurvich. University Professor emeritus of Psychology and Visual Science, Department of Psychology and Institute of Neurological Sciences, University of Pennsylvania. Elected to the National Academy Sciences and The American Academy of Arts and Sciences. Arrangements private. Dottie would be pleased by contributions in her memory to The Center for Constitutional Rights, 656-66 Broadway, NY, NY 10012.
