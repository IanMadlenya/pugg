
        DOFT-Emanuel, 96. Of Lawrence, NY. Husband of the late Pauline Passman, father of Alan (Elisabeth), the late Miriam Escott (Barry), Avrom (Arlene), and Barry (Shirlee). A loving grandfather of 14 & great grandfather of 18. Ardent lifetime Zionist and supporter of Israel prior to and since its creation, and advocate of Jewish education. Funeral on Wednesday, 9:30 AM, Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York, 516-295-3100. Contributions may be made to The Zionist Organization of America, 4 East 34th Street, NY, NY 11016, or The Board of Jewish Education, 426 West 58 Street, NY, NY 10019.
        DOFT-Emanuel. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Emanuel Doft, beloved father of Alan, Avrom, and Barry Doft, dear friends of UJA-Federation. The Doft family has expressed their devotion to the Jewish community in countless ways, especially through their exceptional philanthropy and selfless leadership. Alan and his wife, Elisabeth, and Avrom and his wife, Arlene, are all generous, committed supporters of UJA-Federation's mission. We extend our deepest sympathy to the Doft family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Executive VP and CEO
        DOFT-Emanuel. The Directors and staff of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York extend deepest condolences to Avrom I. Doft, Chairman of the BJE Board, on the passing of his father, Emanuel. May the family find solace in their memories of his good works, and be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Philip Schatten, President Chaim Lauer, Exec V.P.
