
        FRIEDMAN-Rivka. The National Officers of EMUNAH are deeply saddened by the passing of Rivka Friedman, beloved wife of our colleague Israel Friedman, Executive Vice President of the R.Z.A. Rivka was a Life Member and together with her esteemed husband, devoted her life to EMUNAH and Klal Yisroel. May her family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Marcia Genuth, National President
