
        ZALK-Sue Rosenberg, age 56, of NYC. Passed away July 11th while visiting in Florida. Sue was Vice President of Student Services, Graduate School, CUNY. She is survived by her father, Dr. Samuel Rosenberg; husband, Richard Zalk; partner, Joel Lyons; sister, Frances (Richard) Tesser; brother, Dr. George (Jessica) Rosenberg. A memorial service will be held in NYC at a later time. For more information, please call Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapel, West Palm Beach, Florida.
