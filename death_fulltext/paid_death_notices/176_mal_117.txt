
        PENTLAND-Dale Murray, 65, of Ashley Falls, MA, died on September 2, 2001 of Pancreatic Cancer. In addition to his partner, and companion of forty years, Russell Lyons, he is also survived by a sister, Joann Demurs, of Houston, TX. A memorial service will be held at 11 a.m., on Saturday, September 22, 2001 at the Methodist Church in Ashley Falls, MA. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to HospiceCare in the Berkshires 369 South St, Pittsfield, MA 01201.
