
        SASS-Sherman G. On February 14, 2001, in Boston, MA. He was a beloved colleague from 1955 to his retirement in 1991, and then as a consultant to our company. Sherman served as mentor to many of our staff members during his long tenure as head of our Boston office and regional manager for New England. His deep knowledge of our field was publically recognized when he received the Cushing-Gavin Award for his contributions to the field of labor relations. We were most fortunate to have been associated with him and send our deepest sympathy to his family. Funeral services will be held at 10 AM Friday, February 16, at the Stanetsky Memorial Chapel, Brookline, MA, 617-232-9300. Martin E. Segal, Founder and Chairman Emeritus Robert D. Krinsky, Chairman Howard Fluhr, President The Segal Company
