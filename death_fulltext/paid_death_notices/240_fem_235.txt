
        TOMBERG--Constance, suddenly on January 19, in Paris, her adopted city. Beloved friend of Danielle Maurice, cherished sister of Ruby T. Senie and Sally T. Salzer; loving aunt of David, Daniel, Peggy and Michael; adored grandaunt of Cordelia, Charlie and Jessie. On both sides of the Atlantic her friends and family will sadly miss her warmth, friendship and creativity. A memorial service was held in Paris.
