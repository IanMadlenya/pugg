
        RISCO NELSON-Ginger. Died September 11, 2001 in the tragic event at the World Trade Center. Ginger, beloved wife and companion to William Nelson, was a Senior Vice President at Fred F. Alger Management, Inc. Ginger leaves her devoted mother Mary Caggiano-Malfi and three brothers, John, Joseph and Rick Caggiano, many family members and friends. A memorial service will be held at St. Paul's Chapel on the campus of Columbia University on October 12 at 3:30PM to celebrate her remarkable life. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be given to the: Ginger Risco Memorial Scholarship Fund, The School of General Studies, 408 Lewisohn Hall, Columbia University, New York, N.Y. 10027.
