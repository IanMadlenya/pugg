
        YOUNG-Alexander W. The New York Dermatological Society notes with deep regret the passing of our esteemed and beloved colleague, Alexander W. Young, M.D. on May 10, 1997. Dr. Young was the first Director of the Dermatology Service at St. Luke's Hospital until he resigned in 1995. He also was the Director of the Cutaneous Vulvar Service in New York. Dr. Young was Clinical Professor of Dermatology at the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University.
