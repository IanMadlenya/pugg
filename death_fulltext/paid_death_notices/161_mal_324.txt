
        DIVINEY-Msgr. Charles E. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of Pastor Emeritus of Saint Charles Borromeo Church, 21 Sidney Place, Brooklyn, on June 28. He also served as the Vicar General of the Diocese of Brooklyn from 1965 to 1978. Reposing at Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221 St, Queens Village, NY on Thursday, June 29, from 7-9pm and Friday, June 30, from 2-4:30pm. Mass of Transferral on Friday, June 30, at 7:30pm: Funeral Mass on Saturday, July 1, at 10:30am. Both Masses at Saint Charles Borromeo Church. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Regis High School, 55 E. 84 St, NY, NY 10028 or Little Sisters of the Poor, Queen of Peace Residence, 110-30 221 St, Queens Village, NY 11429. All Priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of The Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily, Bishop of Brooklyn
