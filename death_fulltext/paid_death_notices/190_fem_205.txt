
        LUBIN-Anna. (Milberg). Beloved wife of the late Morris Lubin, died peacefully in her 99th year on November 25th. She is survived by her sons, Mel, Larry and Edward, her daughters-in-law, Sarah, Ellie, and Harriet, eight grandchildren, 19 great-grandchildren and her sisters, Gertrude Kleinman and Dorothy Price. She will be remembered for her vigor, independence and devotion to the causes of her Borough Park Community for over 50 years. Graveside funeral, Knollwood Cemetery, Ridgewood, NY, Tuesday, 2:15PM. Shiva at Mel Lubin, Englewood, NJ.
