
        KELLY-Michael. The Board of Directors and staff of the American Society of Magazine Editors are deeply saddened by the untimely and tragic death of editor and columnist, Michael Kelly. He was one of journalism's greatest, and his friends and colleagues will remember him fondly. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Madelyn, his sons, Tom and Jack, and the entire family. Marlene Kahan, Exec. Dir.
