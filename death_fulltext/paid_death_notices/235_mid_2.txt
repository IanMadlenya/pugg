
        CONWAY -- Eugene Robert on August 3, 2006. Age 78. Beloved husband of the late Joanne. Loving father of Kimberly Biehle and her husband Stephen. Cherished grandfather of Alexandra and Noelle. Devoted brother of Ruth McMonagle and the late Dorothy Wallace. A World War II veteran and lifetime New Yorker who graduated Fordham University and received an MBA from New York University. He retired as a Vice President of Manufacturers Hanover Corp. Holding Co. in New York after 30 years of service. Friends may call Sunday August 6th, 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81st St. Mass of Christian Burial Monday August 7th at 10 AM, Epiphany Church, 22nd St at 2nd Ave, NYC.
