
        SALISBURY--Nancy, Sr., rscj, Mentor to Generations. Beloved and dedicated former teacher and headmistress of Convent of the Sacred Heart, Greenwich, CT, past trustee and mentor to generations of alumnae and staff passed away on Monday, September 27th. She was a remarkable educator, sympathetic to all her touched her life, genuine in character, strong in her faith, loving in service to those she served at our school and in many other Network of Sacred Heart schools. Our students, faculty, alumnae, Religious of the Sacred Heart and Board of Trustees extend heartfelt condolences to her family and all who knew and loved her. Sister Joan Magnetti, rscj Headmistress Mr. John N. Irwin III Chair of the Board of Trustees Convent of the Sacred Heart, Greenwich, CT
