
        BUSLIK--Rita Jayne (nee Doneger) of Boca Raton, FL. Beloved wife of the late Jerome. Loving mother of James and Debra Buslik, Audrey Schneiderman - Gold berg and Maynard Goldberg. Dear sister of the late Morton and Henry Doneger. Cherished grandmother of Jeffrey, Bradley and Daniel Bus lik and Andrew Schneiderman. She will be loved and missed by all who knew her. Graveside services were held Friday, December 17.
        BUSLIK--Rita. The officers, Board of Directors, & staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn with profound sadness the loss of Rita Buslik, beloved mother of James Buslik, a cherished leader whose involvement as President of the Sid Jacobson Jewish Community Center strengthens our efforts to enrich Jewish life in New York. We extend our deepest sympathies to James and his wife Debra; & to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
