
        RADDOCK-J. Joseph. Boys Town Jerusalem mourns with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed Board Member, Chai Founder and cherished member of our extended family, Jay Raddock. Together with his wife, Sharon, the Raddocks' generous support enabled Boys Town to fulfill its mission of turning young boys from limited backgrounds into young men with limitless futures. We extend heartfelt condolences to the entire family. Rabbi Moshe Linchner, Dean Michael J. Scharf, President Raphael Benaroya, Chairman Josh S. Weston Honorary Chairman Rabbi Ronald Gray, Exec. V.P.
