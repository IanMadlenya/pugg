
        GOMBERG--Edith S. Lisansky, 85, (Ph.D. Yale '49) of Brooklyn, NY, has died peacefully at home in Ann Arbor, MI. She was a questioning and iconoclastic achiever and a role model to all who knew her. A pioneer in the study of alcoholism, she served on the faculties of Yale, Rutgers and the Universities of Puerto Rico and Michigan. She married twice and leaves five children (see www.edithgomberg.com).
