
        ROLAND--Albert ''Budd.'' On December 7. Graduate of CCNY, member of NYU Dental Class of 1946, past president of Richmond County Dental Society. Loved dentistry, travel, music, theater and swimming 50 laps every morning. Survived by his wife Jean, sister Vera Porcino, brother Jack, his sisters-inlaw Natalie Roland and Elizabeth McKeever and five nieces and nephews. Harmon Funeral Home, Staten Island, visitation, Tuesday, 2-4, 7-9.
