
        KEIFFER-Edwin Gene. Former Chairman of the Board and Chief Executive Officer of E-Systems (acquired by Raytheon in 1996). E. Gene Keiffer, 68, died on June 15, 1998 while hospitalized at the Little Rock, AK Cancer Institute. He valiantly fought multiple myeloma for more than five years. He is survived by his wife, Carole, children Edwin Paul Keiffer, Cheri Ann Jones, Judith Susan Keiffer, Amy Lynn Womack, 4 grandchildren, & brother Emmit F. Keiffer. Private graveside services will be held for family and friends Friday, June 19, 1998, followed by a memorial service at 2PM, Sparkman/Hillcrest Northwest Highway Chapel.
