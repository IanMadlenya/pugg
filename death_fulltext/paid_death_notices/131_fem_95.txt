
        RINGSTED-Margaret Jo. The managers and staff of Novo Nordisk of North American, Inc. mourn the passing of our former colleague, Margaret Jo Ringsted, in Paris, France, on December 4, after a long illness. Margaret Ringsted was Director of Corporate Communications for Novo Nordisk A/S and worked for the company over twenty years. From 1991-96 Margaret lived in New York City heading up Novo Nordisk's communications activities in North America. Her enthusiasm, commitment and sense of style will be deeply remembered. Our heartfelt condolences to Margaret's family and many close friends. A service will take place December 19, Hellerup Kirke, Hellerupvej 5750, Ringe, Denmark.
