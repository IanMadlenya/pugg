
        NOLL-Marie Underhill. President Hunter R. Rawlings III, members of the Board of Trustees, and staff of Cornell University mourn the passing of Marie Underhill Noll, Class of 1926. Mrs. Noll was a Presidential Counselor and with her late husband, A. Robert Noll, was recognized as a foremost Benefactor of Cornell. Mrs. Noll's many Cornell friends extend heartfelt condolences to her sister-in-law Claribel Underhill; her cousins, Virginia Miller and Howard Miller; her nephew and niece, George Underhill and Georgiana Underhill Gehret; and the rest of her family during this time of great sadness.
