
        SOMSBY--Ada N. October 12, at age 101. Widow of Saul. Beloved mother of Joyce and Myron Friedman, and Marjorie and Stanley Rose. Loving grandchildren Amy Carusone, Alyssa Owens, John Friedman, Henry Rose, Jane Berlant. Adored great-grandchildren Rebecca and Holly Owens, Benjamin and Adam Rose, Amie and Marissa Berlant. She was as Assistant Director of Camp Wenonah for Girls, Naples, ME. for many years. Graveside services.
