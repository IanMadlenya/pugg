
        HABER--David Mark. Died January 10, 2005, after a long battle with cancer at age 59. Beloved husband of the late Linda Russell Haber and brother of Keith and Alan. Dear brother-in-law of Anita. Loving uncle of Henry, Clark, Kate and Briana Haber. He was the sales manager for the last 15 years for Climax Mfg, a gift box company. He had a great love of adventure. He loved to sail, golf and fish. He was someone who would always lend a helping hand and was a loyal friend to all who crossed his path. A Memorial Service will be held for him on Saturday, January 22, at 2 pm, at the 15th Street Meeting House, 15 Rutherford Place, NYC.
