
        GRUBERG -- Lawrence, of Boca Raton, Florida (formerly of Eastchester, New York) passed away on Friday, January, 21, 2005. Founder of Detergent Labs Inc., and a former member of Wood Ridge Country Club and Beth El Temple in New Rochelle, NY. A remarkable man whose kindness and compassion touched all he knew. Devoted husband of Rita. Adored father to Laurie (Rick), Amy (Wayne) and Bonnie (and the late Joel). Cherished ''Papa'' to his seven grandchildren: Lisa, David, Rachel, Jill, Jessica, Jenna and Melissa. Services were held Monday, January 24, 2005, in Boca Raton, Florida.
