
        STAMM--Eugene B. Died on January 19 in New Orleans after a long illness. He was 87. He worked as an industrial caterer and was also a photographer, gardener and sailor. He will be deeply missed by his daughters Lindsay (David) and Elisabeth (Mario), his grandsons Daniel and Andrew, and his wife Virginia Darmstadter. He was predeceased by his first wife of almost 60 years, Eleanor Stamm. A memorial service will be announced.
