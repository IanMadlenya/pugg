
        MORSE--Joann Ryan. Died Sunday May 9th. A graduate of Vassar College and Yale University Graduate School of Arts and Sciences, she retired from the Barnard College faculty in 1995 as a Professor of English after almost 40 years of teaching. She is survived by her husband, Dean Weber Morse, of New York City, her sister Maureen Ryan Willson and numerous nieces and nephews. Funeral Mass Wednesday, May 12, 10AM at Corpus Christi Church, 529 W. 121 St, NYC. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Friends of Liturgical Music at Corpus Christi Church.
