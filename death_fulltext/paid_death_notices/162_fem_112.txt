
        BASHAM-Christie Rinehart. 68, of Washington, D.C. and Friendship, Maine, on Sunday morning, July 16 in her Washington home. Greatly beloved sister of Jonathan Rinehart, aunt of Annabelle and Clem and loved by many devoted friends, she was a distinguished and highly respected contributor to the development of network television news and a pioneer of opportunity for women in the field. Services will be held on Wednesday, at 2:00 PM at the Navy Chapel on Nebraska Ave. in Washington. Memorial contributions may be made to the Wildlife Conservation Society at the Bronx Zoo in NYC.
