
        SCHEINES--Morton, 72, died of pneumonia, June 8, in New Milford, CT. This dedicated cineaste and soft-hearted curmudgeon is survived by his wife Tricia, his son Prof. Richard of Carnegie Mellon Univ., his daughter Elizabeth, a film archivist, & brother Joseph, as well as by 5 loving but irreverent grandchildrenEmily, Robin, Katie, Max and Lea. Memorial service-Ethical Culture Soc., 2 W. 64 St, NYC, 3:30 Wednes. June 11. >>AD#
