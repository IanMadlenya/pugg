
        WESTERKAMP--Paul Richard, 94 years old of Bayside, Queens, NY, died Wednesday, August 20, 2003 in Victoria House Nursing Home in Matawan. He attended Fordham University where he received his BA in accounting. He continued his education at Columbia University and received a Master's Degree in gas engineering. He worked as an HMO Administrator for Consolidated Edison in NY.. an Active fund raiser and volunteer for the Boy Scouts of America, and for the Association for the Help of Retarded Children. He was also a founder of the John Golden Pack, the first Cub Scout pack in the US for retarded children. Former First Lady, Eleanor Roosevelt, was the Pack's first Den Mother. He was a two time recipient of the Good Scout Award, and winner of the Silver Beaver Award from the Boy Scouts of America for distinguished volunteer activities. He was preceded in death by his first born son Dennis, in 1986, and his wife Kathleen of 62 years, in 2001. Surviving are his son and daughter-in-law, Patrick and Orr Westerkamp of Matawan, NJ, two grandchildren Richard and John, and two nephews Jack and Edward Morgan. Visitation will be held 2-4 & 7-9 PM, Friday, August 22, at the Waitt-Nelms Funeral Home, Morganville, NJ. Services will be held at St. Robert's Bellamine, Queens, NY, Saturday, August 23, 2003 at 9:45AM. Interment will follow in the Evergreen Cemetery, Brooklyn, New York.
