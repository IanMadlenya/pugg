
        FORMAN-Marsha. Congregation Talmud Torah of Flatbush mourns the passing of Marsha Forman. Marsha possessed keen intellect, ready wit and a sweet character. She will be lovingly remembered. Our condolences to her devoted husband Sol and daughters Marilyn, Amy and Ellie. Dr. Leo Landman, Rabbi Rabbi Isaac Tuchman, Pres.
        FORMAN-Marsha. The Jewish Community Relations Council of New York extends heartfelt condolences to our esteemed Board member Amy Rubenstein on the passing of her beloved mother. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Gedale B. Horowitz, Pres. Michael S. Miller, Exec. V.P.
        FORMAN-Marsha. The Trustees and staff of The Jewish Museum note with sorrow the death of Marsha Forman, mother of Museum Trustee Amy Rubenstein, and extend our deepest sympathy to the entire family. Robert J. Hurst, Chairman Philip A. Laskawy, President Joan Rosenbaum, Director
