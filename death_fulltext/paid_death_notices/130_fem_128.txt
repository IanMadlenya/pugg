
        O'NEIL-Beatrice L. On November 17, in California. Beloved wife of the late George M. O'Neil, Jr. Adored mother of Susan, Michael, John, Jane and Ann. Grandmother to nine. Sister of Lawrence, Mary Agnes, and John. Aunt to many nieces and nephews. She shall be missed dearly. Lawrence Funeral Home, Darien, Connecticut, on Thursday 3-5 and 7-9. Mass of Christian Burial, 10 A.M., Friday, St. John's, Darien CT. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
