
        HARDART-Frank, Jr., M.D. The Sponsors, Executive Staff, Medical Staff and Auxiliary of Saint Vincents Catholic Medical Centers of New York mourn with deep sadness the passing of our esteemed colleague, Frank Hardart. Dr. Hardart served with distinction on the medical staff of Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center of New York for more than 50 years. He will long be remembered for the compassion and skill with which he provided care to his patients and their families as well as for the example he set daily in living out the mission of his profession and our hospital. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Fran, who served as the President of the Auxiliary of Saint Vincents and his entire family. Elizabeth Vermaelen, S.C. Most Rev. Joseph Sullivan Co-Chairs David J. Campbell, Pres & CEO
