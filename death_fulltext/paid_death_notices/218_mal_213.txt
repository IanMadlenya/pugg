
        SCHILDKROUT -- Herman, M.D. In his 95th year on March 17, 2005. Beloved husband of Mollie for 72 years, devoted father of Enid and Elliot, adoring grandfather of Alicia, Benjamin, Aaron, and Emily, father-in-law of Barbara Schildkrout and John Van Couvering. Caring and dedicated physician. We treasure memories of his wit and energy, his liberal and intelligent vision of the world, his love of family and friends, and the joyous sound of his music. Services at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Sunday, March 20 at 11:15AM
