
        HEFLICH--Harry P., 91, husband of the late Estelle Heflich, passed away December 29, 2003. He leaves two children, Louise Wallace, and husband John, of Key Biscayne, FL, and Herbert Heflich, and wife Marilyn, of Martinsville, NJ, and two grandchildren, Marc and wife Laura, and Bradley. He was a graduate of Townsend Harris High School, 1928, and NYU. He travelled extensively and loved to share his experiences. He was a dedicated and devoted husband and father. Contributions in his name may be made to Alzheimers Association, Greater New Jersey Chapter, 400 Morris Avenue, Suite 251, Denville, New Jersey 07834.
