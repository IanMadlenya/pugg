
        ROSENWALD-Dorothy B. Died January 24, 1998, after a long illness in her Kings Bay, Tobago, home at the age of 92. Beloved wife of the late James B. Rosenwald, she is survived by her daughter Patricia R. Heller and her husband Ben of New York City and Sharon, CT, her son James B. Rosenwald, Jr., and his wife Mimi of Ossining, NY, her sister Eleanor B. Handwerger and niece Blair Walker of Longmeadow, MA. She was a caring, loving grandmother of ten and great-grandmother of ten. A service in her memory is to be scheduled at a later date. Memorial contributions can be made to the Ackerman Institute for the Family or the Irvington Institute for Immunological Research, both of New York City.
