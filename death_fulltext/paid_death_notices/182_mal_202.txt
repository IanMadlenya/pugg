
        AARON-Joseph H. On March 12, 2002 at age 92. Husband of the late Claire B. Aaron, father of Judith Aaron Auerbach, and grandfather of Jeffrey Aaron Auerbach and Pamela Jane Auerbach. He is also survived by three cherished greatgrandchildren, Cole, Samuel Aron son, Jonah Morse Aronson, and Dalia Neiman Auerbach. Teacher and friend to many, he enriched the lives of all around him. Services at Jeffer Chapels, 4620 Fort Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn at 2:30 PM, March 14, 2002.
