
        COLISH-Harry J. A resident of New York City died on October 7th. Mr. Colish wishes to thank his friends for the kindness and affection they have shown to him over the past few years, with special thanks to those friends with whom he was closest in his later life. Funeral services Wednesday, 11 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
        COLISH-Harry J., 93. Born in Vienna, he was a successful businessman, an avid traveller with an astounding wealth of knowledge of European and Asian art and history. A generous friend, and benefactor of public institutions in New York. He will be greatly missed by his many friends. Marianne Bergman Peter Last
