
        HARRISON-Josephine Elizabeth. Of Palm Beach, Florida, died on Sunday, September 5, 1999 at Good Samaritan Hospital after an extended illness. She is survived by her husband, Ridgely Harrison; her two daughters, Christine Berry and Cynthia Shafer; and her two sons, Michael and Bryan Cozad. Additional survivors include her seven grandchildren and eight step-grandchildren who will miss her greatly. A private ceremony will be held at home. Interment will follow in Harbor Springs, Michigan at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to New York Botanical Gardens, 200th St. and Southern Blvd., Bronx, NY 10458.
