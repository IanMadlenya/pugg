
        JUNGBLUTH-Henry, 86. Passed away March 17, 1999. Of Franklin Square, Long Island. Henry Jungbluth was a World War II Veteran. He was employed by The New York Times in 1936 and retired 1974. He was member of The International Typographical Union, AFL-CIO for at least forty years. Survivors are his wife Evelyn of 63 years, his daughter Laura Denton and two grandchildren.
        JUNGBLUTH-Henry. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Henry Jungbluth, associated with The Times from 1941 until retiring in 1974.
