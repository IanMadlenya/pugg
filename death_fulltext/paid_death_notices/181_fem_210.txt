
        derSTEPANIAN-Alice. Born Keshishian, a descendant of the Cilician Armenian royal families. Beloved mother, grandmother and great grandmother to the derStepanian family. Passed away Monday, February 18, 2002 in Englewood, N.J. She was the wife of the late statesman and writer Mihran derStepanian. A graduate of the American Academy majoring in Music and Education. She served in educational, religious, cultural and political organizations. She is survived by her daughters Irma and Sylva derStepanian, grandchildren, great grandchildren and relatives in America, Canada, Europe, Armenia, Australia, Asia and Middle East. Funeral services will be private.
