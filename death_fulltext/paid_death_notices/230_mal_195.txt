
        MCGUCKIN--Robert H. III. Died on Sunday, March 12, 2006 at age 63 at his home in Alexandria, VA. after a valiant nine month battle with neuroendocrine cancer. Beloved husband of Barbara J. Katz. Devoted father of son Robert Lawrence and his wife Dana Elizabeth and daughter Catherine Eli zabeth. Loving grandfather of Caitlin Elizabeth, Mary Kathleen and Robert Michael. Director of Economic Research at the Conference Board in New York City and former Chief of Center for Economic Studies at the U.S. Census Bureau. Friends may call at Jefferson Funeral Chapel, 5755 Castlewellan Drive, Alexandria, VA on Wednesday, March 15 from 7-9 PM where funeral service will be held on Thursday at 12:30 PM. Private family burial will follow. The family will observe Shiva from Friday March 17 until Thursday March 23. In lieu of flowers contributions in his name may be made to the American Economic Association Pipeline Project, (supporting underrepresented minorities pursuing graduate studies in economics), Vanderbilt University, 2014 Broadway, Ste 305, Nashville, TN 37203 or the Health Care Reform Research Program of the Center for American Progress, 1333 H.
