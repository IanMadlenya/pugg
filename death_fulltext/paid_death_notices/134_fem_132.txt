
        FRIEDMAN-Lorraine. A void has been created in our hearts with the untimely loss of our past President and dear friend. Lorraine shared with us her unparalleled zest for life, unflagging optimism, radiant charm, and warm sense of humor. Her kindness and generosity towards others was boundless and she was a model of virtue for all to follow. Her spirit will endure within us. Our heartfelt condolences to Sidney, Cara, and Glen and the entire Blumenthal family. Emek Hanahar, Riverdale Chapter of Amit Women
        FRIEDMAN - Lorraine (nee Blumenthal). We are profoundly saddened by the passing of Lorraine, beloved wife of Sidney, mother of Cara and Glenn, daughter of Rose and the late George, step-daughter of Morris, sister of Dean (Gail), Jack (Lynn) and Joseph (Sarah), aunt, cousin, niece and friend of many who admired her exemplary courage, love and goodness. The Blumenthal Family
        FRIEDMAN-Lorraine. The Riverdale Jewish Center mourns the loss of our member, beloved wife of Sidney and mother of Cara and Glen. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
