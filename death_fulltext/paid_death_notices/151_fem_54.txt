
        SOREL-Claudette Marguerite. Renowned concert pianist, teacher and author died at her home in Hampton Bays, NY Friday, August 6th, 1999 at age 66. No immediate family members survive. It was her request that no funeral service be held. Memorial contributions may be made to the Elizabeth and Michel Sorel Charitable Organization which supports opportunities for women in music. Their address is: 28 West 44th Street, Suite 711, New York, New York 10036.
