
        SMITH--Alexander B. The John Jay College of Criminal Justice mourns the loss of Professor Emeritus Alexander B. Smith, J.D., Ph.D. A member of the original faculty, first Dean of Studies and first Chair of the Division of Social Sciences, he advised Presidential and Congressional Commissions on troubled youth and drug abuse. Published widely in the criminal justice field, he continued to teach, do research and write until his recent death at age 95. On behalf of the College Community, President Jeremy Travis extends condolences to his wife, Jennie, daughter Lynn Smith-Ary and son-in-law Zander Ary.
