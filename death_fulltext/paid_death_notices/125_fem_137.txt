
        MOSS-Sarah Lynn, 36, died on Monday after a short illness. She is survived by her parents, four sisters, a nephew, two nieces. Moss, a graduate of L.S.U. and The Natural Gourmet Cooking School was chef at The Botany Restaurant. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, June 18 at the John Krtil Funeral Home at 1297 First Avenue. Visitation 5:00 P.M., Service 5:30 P.M. Contributions may be made to Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, the American Lung Association or the Fresh Air Fund.
