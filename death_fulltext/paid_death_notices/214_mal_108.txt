
        SMITH--William J. of Brewster, NY passed away Sunday, November 7, 2004 at the age of 60. Husband and best friend to Ann Marie, father to Stephen and Greg, and brother to Trudy. Bill loved his family above all. In nearly 40 years as an educator, most recently as principal of Edgemont Jr.-Sr. High School in Scarsdale for 12 years, Bill's life and words moved thousands to reach for the stars. Bill graduated from Mount St. Michael Academy High School in the Bronx and ear ned Bachelor's and Master's degrees from Iona College in New Rochelle. In lieu of flowers, contributions in memory of Bill may be made to the American Cancer Society, American Heart Association, or the Friends of Karen.
