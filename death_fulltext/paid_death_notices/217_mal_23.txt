
        SAIDEMAN--Sanford, passed away on January 31, 2005 after a long illness. He was 74 years old. Sanford was a successful attorney and also an administrator of the S. Klein Estate. He is survived by his wife Rochelle, his children Mara, Lewis, Seth and his brother, Reuben. Funeral services will be held Wednesday, February 2, at 9:30 AM at ''The Riverside,'' West 76 Street & Amsterdam Avenue. Donations can be made in his memory to the United Jewish Appeal. He will be remembered with great love always.
        SAIDEMAN -- Sanford. The Officers, Trustees, Clergy and members of Park Avenue Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Rochelle & the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Sr. Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman, Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft, President
