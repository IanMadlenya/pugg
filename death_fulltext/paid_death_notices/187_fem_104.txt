
        LEVINE-Ann Packer. On August 14, 2002. Devoted wife of Irving Levine. Beloved wife of the late Mac Packer. Loving mother of Jack and Ilene Packer, and Arlene and Dr. Scott Baden; and her precious jewels, her grandchildren Steven and Allison Packer, and Shaun and Jamie Baden. She was State Committee Woman and Co-Leader of the High-Way Democratic Club. This year's recipient of the Lifetime Achievement Award and Woman of the Year. Services Friday 12 Noon at Sherman's Flatbush Memorial Chapel, 1283 Coney Island Ave. (Ave. J), Bklyn.
