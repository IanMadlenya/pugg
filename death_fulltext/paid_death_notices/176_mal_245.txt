
        AUERBACH-Louis. Age 75, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Bergenfield, NJ and Marlboro, NJ, died Monday, Sept. 17, 2001 at his home. Born in Poland he was a Holocaust survivor, coming to the US in 1947. In America, the country he loved, his life was testament to what strength, determination and a strong work ethic can achieve. Before retiring in 1991, Mr. Auerbach owned various restaurants in NYC including the world famous Stage Door Deli in Manhattan. Louis Auerbach is survived by his beloved wife of 53 years, Helen Auerbach of Boynton Beach, FL, his daughter Pamela Auerbach of Edgewater, NJ, his son and daughter-in-law, Steven and April Auerbach of Franklin Lakes, NJ, his brother Baruch Auerbach of North Miami, FL and his two grandchildren Nicole and Lana Auerbach. An extraordinary man whose memory will live on, he will be greatly missed by his family, friends and the lives he touched over the years. Services will be held on Thurs at 1 PM at the BloomfieldCooper Jewish Chapel of Manalapan, NJ. Donations may be made to the U.S. Holocaust Memorial Museum in Washington, DC.
