
        PATTERSON--Ellmore C. November 29, 1913-November 5, 2004. Former Chairman and Chief Executive Officer of J.P. Morgan & Co. Survived by his wife, Anne, his five sons, Michael, Arthur, Robert, David and Thomas, 14 grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. Memorial service at 11:00AM, November 23, at St. Thomas Church Fifth Avenue (at 53rd St). Contributions in his name may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Ctr.
