
        ROSEN--Ruth (nee Jacobson), died peacefully at home in Tamarac, Florida. on Wednesday, September 7, 2005 at the age 95. The beloved wife of the late Harry Rosen, founder of Junior's Restaurant in Bklyn, NY. Devoted and loving mother of Marvin (Bonny) Rosen and Marcia (Harold) Cohen; cherished grandmother of Ina Friend, Barry (Jamie) Cohen, Andrew (Michelle) Cohen, Sheri Rosen, Beth (William) Bauer, and Jeffrey (Liza) Rosen; adored great grandmother of Matthew & Scott Friend, Reese & Harley Cohen, Ross & Harris Cohen, Casey, Jas min, Avery, & Colin Rosen & Landon Bauer. We want to thank Doreen Taylor for all the love & devotion she gave to our mother & father. Mom, we will all miss you very much. Services Sunday 10:30 am on September 11, at Star of Daivd Memorial Chapel 7701 Bailey Rd., No. Lauderdale, FL., 1-800-456-5252. Donations may be made to Hospice By The Sea, 1531 Palmetto Park Rd, Boca Raton, Fl. 33486.
