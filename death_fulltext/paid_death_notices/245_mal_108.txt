
        PRESBY--Philip Alan. After a more than two-year bout with lymphoma and pneumonia, Phil Presby finally succumbed to his illness on Thursday, June 7. He was 43. He was born in Dayton, Ohio on August 8, 1963 to J. Thomas Presby and Elaine Smith Presby and grew up in West Orange and Essex Fells, NJ. After graduating from Newark Academy and Franklin and Marshall College, he attended Brooklyn Law School. He practiced corporate law at Shea and Gould for two years and then studied American history at New York University. Most recently, he was working on a novel and writing for the East Hampton Star newspaper. He is survived by his parents, his wife Jennifer, and his sisters Terry Presby and Mona Presby Bent. After a private family service on Monday, a memorial gathering will take place at the Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel on Thursday, June 14 at 6:30pm. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Leukemia and Lymphoma Society and the Animal Rescue Fund of the Hamptons.
