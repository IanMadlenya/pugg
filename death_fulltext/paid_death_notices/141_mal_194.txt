
        LEWIS-Harold S. October 18, 1998, in his 82nd year. Beloved husband of Marianne, father of Harold and Dale, Leslie and Debra, grandfather of Katherine and Ethan. A distinguished engineer, he was a partner in the New York firm of Jaros, Baum & Bolles. His love of family, home and nature, and his wisdom, illuminated the lives of an adoring family and devoted friends. Private service. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Alzheimer's Support Network, 660 Ninth St. N., Naples, FL 34102.
