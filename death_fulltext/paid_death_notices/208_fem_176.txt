
        MORRIS--Persis Warren. Age 88, on May 21, 2004, at her residence in Falls Church, VA. Beloved wife of the late Stephen V.C. Morris. Surviving are her children, Helen Morris Scorsese, Christopher W. Morris and James M. Morris, four grandchildren and one sister. A funeral service will be held on Tuesday, May 25, at 11 am, at the Christ Church, 3116 O Street, NW, Washington, DC 20007. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in her name to the Boston Symphony Orchestra, 301 Mass Ave, Boston, MA 02115.
