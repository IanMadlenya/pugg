
        McDOUGALD--Mildred Holloway, died on March 16, 2007, in Somers, NY, at the age of 97. Born in Charleston, SC, she was a long-time New Yorker, teaching in the New York City Public School system from which she retired as an assistant principal. She will be missed by many. Widow of Cornelius McDougald, a New York City attorney, loving mother of Connie, Cornelius Travis, and of Mary Kohn who predeceased her, adoring grandmother of Sarah and Jeremy Kohn. A Memorial service in her honor will be held at 3 o'clock on Saturday, April 28th, at the Community Church of New York, 40 E. 35th St., NY, NY.
