
        LEMONNIER-Jeanne. Mother of Joe and John Paul, wife of the late Ernest LeMonnier, died peacefully in her home in Manhattan after a long illness. Services scheduled for Wednesday, May 14, 1:30 P.M., at Riverside Chapel, Amsterdam Avenue between 75th and 76th Streets, in Manhattan. Shiva to follow at the home of Joe and Janet LeMonnier in Hoboken, N.J.
