
        HATA -- Yoshie Sakamoto. Born March 16, 1914 in Washiwa Oahu, Hawaii passed on in peace February 5, 2004 at St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital in New York City at the age of 89. Yoshie Hata was the daughter of Shinaemon Sakamoto and Toshi Matsunaga. Her husband, Shizuo, predeceased her in 1995. Yoshie was one of five siblings: Jane, Mae, Raymond and Susan who reside in Hawaii and California with their families. The memorial service was held Sunday, March 28th at Japanese American United Church in New York City.
