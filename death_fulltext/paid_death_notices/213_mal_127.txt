
        BATEN--Charles. Born June 30, 1901, died at his home in West Palm Beach on October 8, 2004. Husband for more than 64 years to the late Rita (''Kitty''), Dad to Mickey (Jill and Bobbie Geza) von Molnar, Papa to Amanda, Anina, Blythe (Joe), Karen, Melissa (Lee), great grandfather to Cas, Lainie, Zachary and Isobel. From your family & all who adored you, we thank you for being kind, generous, patient, modest, funny, loving and a dapper gentle man.
