
        DeBLASIO-Guido Alfred, M.D. Of Pelham, N.Y. on April 18, 2000. Practicing surgeon at Mount Vernon Hospital for forty years. Beloved husband of Patricia. Dear father of John, Claire Foss and Robert. Loving grandfather of Jennifer, Matthew, Rebecca, Amanda, Michelle and Nicholas Guido. Graduate of Cornell University and Yale Medical School, he served as a Major in the Army Medical Corp. in WWII. Calling hours at the Fred H. McGrath & Son Funeral Home, Bronxville between the hours of 2-4 and 7-9PM Thursday. Service at the funeral home on Friday, April 21st, 10AM. Interment Woodlawn Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
