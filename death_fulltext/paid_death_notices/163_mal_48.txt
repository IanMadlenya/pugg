
        MILKIN-Howard. NYC filmmaker, editor, television producer. Died in Paris July 18. Born Jan. 16, 1934. Films include Academy awardwinning ''Interview with Five Mylai Veterans,'' Flaherty award-winning ''Sunday,'' and Detroit Institute of Art film on Tony Smith's sculpture. Television work includes WNET ''Music and Art of the Renaissance'' and ''Lay My Burden Down.'' Survived by his loving wife of 44 years, Alice, his loving son, Marc, and granddaughter, Emma. Memorial service Sunday, August 13, 2 PM, Walter B. Cooke Funeral Home, 117 W. 72 St.
