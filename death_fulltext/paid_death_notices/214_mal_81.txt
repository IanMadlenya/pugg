
        HACHENBURG -- Robert. Died on November 7, 2004, at age 86 at his home in Philadelphia, PA. Professor of Law at Temple University School of Law following a twenty year career at A.M. Greenfield & Co., where he was Vice President & General Counsel. Served as a naval intelligence officer from 194346 with service in Japan during occupation by U.S. forces. AB (Phi Beta Kappa) and LLB from Univ. of Pennsylvania. Survived by his wife, Pauline Cooper Hachenburg; his three daughters, Ruth Adelman of Englewood Cliffs, NJ, Susan Reyniers of Montpellier, France, and Lisa Taylor of Seal Rock, OR; seven grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Predeceased by his wife of 54 years, Selma Vigderman Hachenburg.
