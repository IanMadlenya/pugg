
        ARPS-Ruth Collicott. Formerly of New York City, died on July 29, 2002, at Seabury Retirement Community, Bloomfield, Conn., at age 95. Wife of the late Leslie H. Arps, a founding partner of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom, she is survived by four nephews. Mrs. Arps was born in Evansville, Indiana, and graduated from Ohio State University in 1929. She was a past President of the Women's Division of the Legal Aid Society of New York, and an active fund raiser for the New York City YWCA. Mrs. Arps also established scholarships at Indiana State University in memory of her parents, Jacob and Lydia Gemmer Collicott and at Ohio State University, College of Education, for graduates of Columbus, Ohio, public schools and at Oberlin College in memory of her mother and her aunt, Esther Gemmer Wattles. A memorial service will be held at Seabury Retirement Community at 2:00 PM, Thursday, August 1, 2002.
        ARPS-Ruth. The partners, associates and support staff of the law firm of Skadden, Arps, Slate, Meagher & Flom LLP and Affiliates mourn the loss of our beloved friend, Ruth Arps. Mrs. Arps, the widow of one of the firm's founding partners, Leslie H. Arps, will be fondly remembered for her caring nature, unwavering good cheer and invaluable contributions to the culture and success of the firm and for her tireless work on behalf of the Legal Aid Society and other charities. She will be deeply missed. Our thoughts and prayers are with her family. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, August 1, 2002, from 2 to 4pm in Heritage Hall at Seabury Retirement Community, 200 Seabury Drive in Bloomfield, CT.
