
        HUGHES-Mark F. The Board of Directors of the New York County Lawyers' Association mourns the passing of Mark F. Hughes, former president of the Association (1965-1967), who died Wednesday, December 10 at the age of ninetytwo. During his term, NYCLA established the William Nelson Cromwell Award which is presented at the Association's Annual Dinner. His commitment to NYCLA and the law will be missed.
