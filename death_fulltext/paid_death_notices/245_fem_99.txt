
        GALLUP--Prudence Anne. On June 10. Not quite 65, much too young, an intellectual,highly literate, deeply curious, strongly opinionated, world traveller, cosmopolitan with a wonderful sense of humor. Mourned and missed by her brothers Allyn and Christopher and their wives Sheila and Chrystal, her nieces Marcy and Laurie, her nephew Christopher and their families, her partner Leeanne Lipston, and her many friends
