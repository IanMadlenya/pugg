
        SHAPIRA--Karen A. The Officers, Board of Directors and staff of The American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) mourn the untimely passing of our esteemed Board member, Mrs. Karen A. Shapira. A founding member and leader of UJC and a past chair of the United Jewish Federation of Pittsburgh, Karen's heartfelt devotion to Israel and her concern for the welfare of the global Jewish community, led to a lifelong involvement, with her beloved husband, David, in advocacy and acts of hesed on behalf of klal yisrael. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, her children, and the entire Shapira family. May her memory be for a blessing. Judge Ellen M. Heller, President Eugene J. Ribakoff, Chairman of the Board Steven Schwager, Executive Vice President
        SHAPIRA -- Karen. The Pardes family mourns our precious friend and board member Karen Shapira. We remember her brilliance, love of Torah study, optimism regarding the future of Israel and Klal Yisroel in its fullness and entirety, her vitality and wonderful joy in life. We extend our compassion and shared grief to her beloved family, husband David and Deborah, Laura and Jeremy. Rabbi Daniel Landes, Dir. The Boards of the American Pardes Fdtn and the Pardes Institute of Jewish Studies
