
        CAMMISA-Marian A. A resident of Croton-on-Hudson. Died July 22, 2000 at Harlem Valley Hospital Center, she was 83. Miss Cammisa was born in Dobbs Ferry, NY on January 21, 1917 and was the daughter of Frank and Maria Rago Cammisa. Miss Cammisa was a former legal secretary and sang opera at Carnegie Hall and in May 2000 made a television commercial for ''Adopt-a-Pet'' month. Miss Cammisa is survived by one brother Sam Cammisa, two sisters Yolanda Denman and Angela Reilly and several nieces and nephews. Donations may be sent to Pompei Church, Palisade Avenue, Dobbs Ferry, New York 10522.
