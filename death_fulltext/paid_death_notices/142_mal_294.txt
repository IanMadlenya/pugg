
        TIM-Stephen K-M. On November 21, 1998. Beloved husband of Nancy (nee Shopis). Brother of Phonyin Tim of Victoria, BC, and Peter Tim of Johannesburg, South Africa. Also survived by three nephews. Reposing Tuesday 2-5 and 79 PM, at Henry McCaddin & Son Funeral Home, 24 7th Ave, Brooklyn. Mass of Christian Burial Church of St. Augustine, Wednesday, 9:45 AM. Interment Middlebury, CT. In lieu of flowers donations to St. Augustine's Food Pantry, 116 6th Ave, Brooklyn, NY, 11217, appreciated.
        TIM-Stephen. The Brooklyn Botanic Garden has lost a great friend, Vice President for Science, Library and Publications, Dr. Stephen K-M Tim. For 27 years Dr. Tim devoted his talents as a botanist, writer, editor and artist to the work of the Garden. His creativity, fairness and unfailing good humor will be deeply missed. Our hearts go out to his wife Nancy and his family. Earl D. Weiner, Board Chairman Judith D. Zuk, President Consuelo Seidel, Auxilary Pres.
