
        TRIBUNO - Mario P. Of Princeton, New Jersey, formerly of Sands Point, New York, died Monday, September 23, in Cedar Rapids, Iowa. Mario retired in 1980 as CEO of GB Raffetto Inc. He is survived by his wife, Marie Louise, son Robert of Cedar Rapids, daughter Diane of Auckland, New Zealand and three grandchildren. Services 11 AM Friday at St. Matthews Catholic Church in Cedar Rapids. Contributions to Hospice of Mercy, 701 10th St., SE, Cedar Rapids, IA. 52403-1292.
