
        FOOSE-Thomas Trentman. Died in Cobble Hill Nursing Home August 16. A longtime resident of Brooklyn Heights, and before that Manhattan. Born and raised in Dayton, OH, he would have been 84 on September 10. He was an only child. His sole surviving relative is Martha June Baker, who resides in Dayton. He served four years in the Armed Forces in the South Pacific in W.W. II. He came to New York where he practiced his love for the theatre. For quite some years he was a Theatre Historian for Otis Guernsey, founding editor of ''The Best Plays of the Year.'' He was a drama critic for the brief revival of The Brooklyn Eagle. He also worked on theatre projects with Terese Hayden for 50 years.
