        BLUM-Phyllis. The Board of Directors and Staff of Family Focus Adoption Services mourn the loss of our Board Member and friend, and one of the founders of our agency, Phyllis Blum, who died yesterday. Phyllis was an outspoken and creative woman who was always thinking of ways to help Family Focus. All of us will miss her caring and her commitment. Our deepest sympathy go to her husband Harry, and children, Lilly and Marty. Kathleen Wallace, Board President Maris Blechner, Executive Director
