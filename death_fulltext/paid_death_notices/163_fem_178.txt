
        LAVIN-Jean. Of Orangeburg, NY. She had a life of spirit, love, courage and artistic talent. It was a privilege and a pleasure to be her child. Mrs. Lavin was the widow of Jack Lavin; beloved mother of Stanley Lavin of Great Neck and Eileen Rogers of Orangeburg, NY; grandmother of Evan and Michael Rogers and Andrew, Elizabeth and Nancy Lavin; great-grandmother of Alana, Stacy, Corey, Mark, Amber & Matthew Rogers & Madeline Sarah Lavin. Funeral Friday, August 25 at 12 PM at Hellman Memorial Chapels, 15 State Street, Spring Valley, NY.
