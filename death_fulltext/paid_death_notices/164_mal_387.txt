
        BURACK-Abraham W. Of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Bronxville, NY, passed away in New York City on September 22, 2000. Cherished and beloved husband of Harriet. Devoted father to Herb and Donna. Father-in-law of Jodi. Future father-in-law of Sandy. Beloved grandfather to Ross and Jeffrey. Brother of Dorothy Jaffe. Loving friend to all. He will be missed for his guidance, wisdom, strength of character and diplomacy. Services on Sunday, 12 noon, at ''The Riverside,'' Broad Street, Mount Vernon, NY.
