
        STEINMAN--Joan. Congregation Rodeph Sholom and its Board of Trustees mourn the passing of our beloved and longtime member, Joan Steinman, the widow of our esteemed past President and Chairman of the Board, Lewis Steinman. Joan was integral to every aspect of Temple life. The Steinman Religious Education Center is a vital part of her family's permanent legacy. We will miss her elegance, intellect and devotion. We send our profound condolences to her children, Jeffrey and Jody, Peter and Todd, Robert and Hilary, her mother Gracie Weisbrot, grandchildren, Carlo, Lucy and Paulina, and all their dear ones. Robert N. Levine, D.D., Senior Rabbi Roger Meltzer, President
