
        JACOBS-Lenard S. Of Morristown, on Tuesday, November 20, 2001 formerly of Manhattan and Rye, New York. Father of Michelle. Grandfather of Jagger and Stryker. Brother of Barbara, Mildred and Edward. He was a pioneer in the entertainment licensing business at Warner Brothers and in his own business before retiring. He served in the Navy in World War II. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Valerie Fund, 2101 Milburn Avenue, Maplewood, NJ 07040.
