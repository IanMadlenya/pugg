
        MEIKLEJOHN-Robert Pickens, of Monroe Twp., N.J. on December 26, 1997. Formerly of Greenwich, CT. Ten years service with W. Averell Harriman in his successive capacities including U.S. Minister in charge of Lend Lease supplies for Great Britain, U.S. Ambassador to the Soviet Union and Secretary of Commerce of the United States. He was a participant in the Teheran, Yalta and Potsdam conferences and responsible for the official White House record of Teheran and Yalta. After years of diplomatic service he was a Vice President of Allied Chemical and Dye Corp., a division of General Dynamics. He is survived by his wife Madelaine Coletti Meiklejohn, a son Robert H. Meiklejohn of Fairfield, CT, a daughter Carol Ann Westervelt of Chester, N.J. He is predeceased by his daughter Blanche Alexander. He is also survived by five grandchildren and 3 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be held 2 PM Saturday at the Lester Memorial Home, 16 West Church St., Jamesburg, N.J. Memorials may be sent to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, Columbia University, 710 West 168 St., New York, New York, 10032.
