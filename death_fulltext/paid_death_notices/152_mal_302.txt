
        ROSNER-Miguel (Michael). A resident of NYC and formerly of San Francisco died Saturday, September 25 after an extended illness. Born December 4, 1940 in Lisbon, Portugal. He was the son of Isidor and Ana Rosner. He is survived by his sisters Mimi Rosner Cohen of Stamford CT. and Bella Rosner of Newtonville, Mass. His aunts, Dora Gopnick and Lotchen Fligler, nephew Joshua Schapiro and nieces Amy Kruglak, Rena Kopelman and Judith Schapiro. Contributions in his honor can be made to the Amnesty International.
