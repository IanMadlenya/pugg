
        CHERNOFSKY-Ruth. The Leadership of EMUNAH of America mourns the untimely loss of its Honorary National President, Ruth Chernofsky. Her devotion and philanthropic endeavors enabled our organization to expand its network of educational facilities which enriched the lives of thousands of children in Israel. She will be greatly missed by the entire EMUNAH family. We express our sincerest condolences to her husband, Morris and her children, Philip and Toni, Neil and Ilona, Benett and Nancy, her grandchildren, and her nieces and nephews who she embraced so warmly. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Dr. Sylvia Schonfeld National President Shirley Singer,Exec Vice President Melanie Oelbaum, Honorary National President
