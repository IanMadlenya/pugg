
        EIDELSBERG-Robert. The Board of Governors and members of Bocaire Country Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Tuesday, February 24, 1998. He will be missed. Services will be held on Friday, February 27, at 11 A.M., at Temple Beth El, 333 S.W. 4th Ave., Boca Raton, Florida. Interment will be at Eternal Light. Barry Berger, President
