
        ROBINSON-Horace B.B. The Society of Mayflower Decendents in the State of New York mourns the death of Horace B.B. Robinson, our passed Governor and present Elder. His perserverance, hardwork and devotion to duty were an inspiration to us all. We extend our sympathy to his wife and three sons. Walter R. Brewster Jr, Governor Catherine A. Brawer, Secretery
