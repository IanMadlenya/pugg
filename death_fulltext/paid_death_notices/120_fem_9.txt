
        CROSS - Jeanette W. 83, of Delray Beach, FL. died on Wednesday, January 1, 1997, at her home following an extended illness. Mrs. Cross had been a resident fo the Palm Beach area since 1984, coming here from Greenwich, CT. She was a member of the American Kennel Club and served as Judge in dog shows for over 50 years, including the Westminister Dog Show in New York City. There are no immediate members of her family surviving. Funeral Services and interment will take place in Greenport, Long Island, NY. For information, contact Quattlebaum-Holleman-Burse Funderal Home, 1201 South Olive Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33401.
