
        MILLER-Sidney M. Of Pleasantville, NY, and Orleans, MA. Died February 26 at home at age 78. He is survived by his loving wife of 56 years, Barbara; his sister, Dorothy Bogdanoff; his brother, Hyman; his four sons, Paul, Bruce, Eric and Adam; four devoted daughters-inlaw; as well as four grandchildren, Luke, Max, Hannah and Alex. Sidney was Chairman of the Board of L. V. Myles, Inc., a manufacturer and importer of women's and children's sleepwear, robes and loungewear. In 1947, he joined a small business representing sewing contractors and over 50 years built the firm into one of the most respected companies in the field. In addition to his business career, Sidney was very active in community affairs, serving for many years as President of Usonia Homes, Inc., a cooperative residential community dedicated to the ideas and designs of Frank Lloyd Wright. In addition, he served for 12 years on the school boards of Bear Ridge and the Inaugural Board of Byram Hills. A memorial service will be held Sunday, March 1, at 10 AM, at Congregation B'nai Y'Israel. Contributions in Sidney's name may be made to Columbia College, Columbia University, Suite 917, 475 Riverside Dr., NY, NY 10115.
        MILLER-Sidney M. The employee of L.V. Myles Inc. express their sincere sorrow at the passing of our beloved chairman. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Barbara, his sons, Paul, Bruce, Eric and Adam, his daughter-in-laws, Debby, Sharon, Cheryl, and June, and his grandchildren, Luke, Max, Hannah and A.J.
