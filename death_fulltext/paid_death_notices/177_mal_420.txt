
        NAGAMATSU-George R., M.D. Emeritus Professor at New York Medical College, passed away on October 24 of natural causes. He is survived by his wife, Kazuko. A memorial service will be held on Friday, November 16, at 4 PM, at All Souls Church, Lexington Av. & 80 St. In lieu of flowers, donations can be sent to The Society for Urology and Engineering, 550 Madison Ave., Suite 3432, NY, NY 10022.
