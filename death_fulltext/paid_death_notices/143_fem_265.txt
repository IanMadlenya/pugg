
        RAFFEL-Jeannette. Born Oct. 14, 1909 to Louis and Fannie Raffel of Ansonia, Connecticut, died on Dec. 23, 1998. She was a graduate of Hunter College with a Teaching Masters from Columbia University. Jeannette taught Elementary School in Long Beach Long Island for over thirty years until her retirement when she moved along with her sister Dorothy Raffel to Coral Springs, Florida. Survived by her sisters Phyllis Weisberg of Norfolk, VA., and Esther Glass of Jackson Heights, New York; Aunt Jay is also survived by seven adoring nieces and nephews along with seventeen grandnieces and nephews with each of whom she maintained a close and active relationship.
