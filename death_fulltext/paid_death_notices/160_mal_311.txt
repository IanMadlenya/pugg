
        FINK-Dr. Robert Alan. Age 50. Died May 27. He was born in Harrisburg, PA. Beloved son of Shirley Schwartz and the late Lloyd Fink. Loving father of Sunny and Loni. Devoted brother of Jay. Drowned in the waters of the Saint Lucie River. He was an emergency room physician for 22 years, last practicing at the Saint Lucie Medical Center, where he was Medical Director. Memorial service at Aycock Funeral Home, Stuart, Tuesday, 5 PM.
