
        SCHAPIRO--Edith R. Peacefully, on February 17, 2005, in her 98th year. Beloved wife of the late Jacques. Dear mother of Inez Reiser and grandmother of Dr. Janet Reiser (Dr. Paul Wax) and Peter (Robin) Reiser. Greatgrandmother of Rebecca and Sarah.
