
        DELMAN--Perry. Of Boca Raton, FL. Devoted and beloved husband for 50 years of Geraldine. Loving father of Robert and Scott. Adoring grandfather of Edward, Jeffrey, Jonathan and Rebecca. Dear brother of Alan Nudelman. Former Chief Executive Officer of Delco International Ltd., a leading importer and manufacturer of tabletop items for the food service/ aviation industries. Member of Boca West Country Club (Boca Raton, FL) and Engineers Country Club (Roslyn Harbor, NY). A funeral service will be held at 10:00am on March 5 at Sunset Chapel, 1285 Northern Boulevard, Manhasset, NY. For those desiring, donations to Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, New York 11590, in his memory would be appreciated.
