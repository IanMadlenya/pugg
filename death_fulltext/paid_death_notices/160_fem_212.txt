
        BROAS-Ethel S. Of Poughkeepsie, NY, died on Thursday, May 25, 2000 in her 87th year. Beloved wife of the late S. Wilton Broas; devoted mother of Donald S. Broas, New York, NY; dear grandmother of Nancy Beth Gray, Donald S. Broas, Jr., Kimberley Anne Broas and Gregory Michael Broas, and great grandmother of Hayley Gray. Visitation will be held at Parmele Funeral Home, 110 Fulton Ave., Poughkeepsie, NY on Friday and Saturday from 6-8 PM. Funeral Services Sunday 4 PM from the funeral home. Memorial contributions to American Heart Association, 22 IBM Road, Poughkeepsie, New York 12601.
