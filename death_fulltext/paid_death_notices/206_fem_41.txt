
        SOLOMON--Ruth K. Beloved mother of Susan Solomon, mother-in-law of Paul Goldberger, grandmother of Adam Hirsh and Ben and Alex Goldberger, great grandmother of Thibeaux Hirsh, died on March 2nd. She cherished all of life's gifts, from Mozart to Scrabble, and she inspired us as she met every challenge with courage and love. Services 10AM, Thursday, March 4, at Riverside Funeral Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. at 76 St., with burial to follow at Shaarey Pardes Cemetery, East Hampton. In lieu of flowers her family would be grateful for contributions in her memory to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation.
        SOLOMON--Ruth. The New York Chapter of the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation International records with deep sorrow the passing of Ruth Solomon, beloved mother of our esteemed longtime Board Member Susan Solomon. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the bereaved family. Wendy C. Schmidt, President Alexandra Ziogas, Exec Dir New York Chapter Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation International
        SOLOMON--Ruth. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons mourns the passing of Ruth Solomon, beloved mother of Susan Solomon, and extend deepest condolences to the family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
