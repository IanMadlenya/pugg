
        SMITH-Mary Allen Gregg on April 30. Beloved wife of the Rev. John C. Smith and mother of John Clark Smith and Alexandra Gregg Smith. A service will be held at 11:30 on May 2 at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71 Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Blue Ridge School in Dyke, VA. 22935.
        SMITH-Mary Allen Gregg. The Members of The Thanksgiving Eve Ball Committee of the Junior League of the City of New York deeply mourn the loss of a longtime committee member and friend Allen Smith. Her warmth, knowledge and presence will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to her husband Jack, and children, Alexandra and Clark.
        SMITH-Mary Allen Gregg. The New York Auxiliary of the Blue Ridge School greatly mourns the passing of our devoted member and friend. The leadership, life and spirit she gave to the auxiliary will serve as an inspiration for the future. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family.
        SMITH-Mary Allen Gregg. The National Society of Colonial Dames in the State of New York, records with deep regret the death of its distinguished former Board Member. Elizabeth Fuchs, President
