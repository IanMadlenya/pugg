
        POLSKY--Dr. Zita Norwalk, 85, died in Atlanta, GA on May 6, 2007 after a lengthy illness. She touched the lives of countless clients in her psychotherapy practice. She was a devoted mother, grandmother and great-grandmother. She is survived by daughter Aviva Stern, son Joshua Polsky, sister Carol Bobbe, four grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
