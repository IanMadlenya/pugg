
        LEONARD--Paula, 96, October 17, 2006. Mrs. Leonard was a graduate of The Dalton School, Randolph-Macon College, and the University of California. She fashioned designs that were featured on the cover of Vogue from her New York Studio. Member of the 3rd Order of St. Benedict and Professor at Pratt Institute.
