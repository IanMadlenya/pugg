
        FEINBERG-Seymour D. Dr., 85. Beloved husband of the late Dr. Constance Buxer Feinberg. Loving father of Andrew and Kenneth. Devoted grandfather of Caroline, Lance and Dylan. Loving brother of Bernard and Beatrice. Cherished father-in-law of Rikki and Adele. He enjoyed a distinguished career as a US Army Officer in WWII and as a Dentist for more than 40 years. He was a generous supporter of many causes, especially civil rights, medical research and the State of Israel. An avid tennis player and fan, he met his wife on a tennis court, where part of his heart always was. He will be missed. A memorial service will be held in September.
