
        NEWCOMB--Louise Blaser. Louise Blaser Newcomb died on Saturday, October 11, 2003, in her home in Salisbury, Connecticut at the age of 96. She was the widow of Russell Ladd Newcomb. Mrs. Newcomb was born in Somerville, Massachusetts in 1907. She attended local schools and graduated from Thayer Academy in Braintree, Massachusetts. She worked in the Boston area following graduation at a number of companies including AT&T, and married Russell on Thanksgiving Day, 1937. Together they lived in New York City, Tarrytown, New York and Chappaqua, New York, where she founded the Chappaqua Dancing School. In 1955, the Newcombs moved to Norwich, Vermont, where they created Norwich Enterprises, which had real estate and retail holdings. For many years, Mrs. Newcomb managed the North Country Store in Norwich, VT. Following Russell's death in 1980, Louise moved to Kent, Connecticut and subsequently Salisbury, Connecticut. She was passionately interested in helping provide community-wide access to books of all types and was a trustee of the Norwich, VT public library, the Kent, CT public library, and more recenty, was active in expanding the library at Noble Horizons in Salisbury, CT. She is survived by her two sons, Danforth and Jonathan, her sister Harriet Buckley, and four grandchildren. A reception for family and friends will be held at her home in Noble Horizons in Salisbury on Sunday, October 19. Memorial contributions may be made to the Louise B. Newcomb Book Fund, Noble Horizons, Salisbury, CT. 06039. For more information contact The Kenny Funeral Home, 41 Main Street, Sharon, CT.
