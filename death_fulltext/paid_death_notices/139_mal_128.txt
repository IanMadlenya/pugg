
        KESSLER-Len. 71. Died on Sunday, August 9, 1998, of natural causes at his home in West Palm Beach, FL. Retired, Len was a NY public relations man par excellence. He is survived by his brother Ben, his daughter Lise, and his son Andreas. A memorial service will be held at 4 P.M., today at Campbell's, 1076 Madison Avenue. He was dearly loved and will be greatly missed.
