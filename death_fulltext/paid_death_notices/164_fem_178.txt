
        KNAPP-Emily (Litchfield), 90. Of Miami, died September 17th. Daughter of Norman and Cora Snowden Litchfield, she was a librarian in Fairfield, CT, a children's librarian for the New York Public Library System and at the Fifth Avenue School, East Northport, NY. She lived for many years in Cold Spring Harbor, NY. Gifts in lieu of flowers, may be sent to St. Thomas Episcopal Church, Coral Gables, Florida.
