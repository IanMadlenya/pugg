
        ROCHETEAU-Ralph C. Jr. April 1, 1924-November 1, 2001. Died at home in Hyannis, Massachusetts. Survived by his wife of 54 years, June, and children, Ralph, Joseph, Diane, Michael, and Joanne, and by brother, Albert. Served in the U.S. 3rd Army during W.W. II. Awarded Silver Star and Bronze Star for bravery. Grandson of Provincetown (MA) pioneer, Capt. Joseph W. Rocheteau (d. 1931). Son of Ralph C. Rocheteau, Sr. (d. 1955), and Clara Rocheteau (d. 1952) of Provincetown.
