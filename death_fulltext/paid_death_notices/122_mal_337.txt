
        RISKIN-Harry. Lincoln Square Synagogue records with deep sorrow the passing of its former member, Mr. Harry Riskin, beloved father of our founding Rabbi, Shlomo Riskin, and of Judi Stern and of Myra Abraham. He will be remembered for his love of life, of people, of the land of Israel and especially for his love and pride in his whole family. Adam Mintz, Rabbi Richard Kestenbaum, President Herschel Cohen, Assoc. Rabbi Sherwood Goffin, Cantor
