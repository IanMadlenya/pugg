
        GUNST-Lee Sanders. Died at age 70, on April 27, 2001, after a courageous struggle against cancer. He loved his family, he loved his friends, he loved tennis and he loved life. He is survived by his loving companion Maryln Kimmel and his sister and brother-in-law Pat and Fred Hofmann. He was a loving and proud father, grandfather and father-in-law. He is survied by three children, their partners and six grandchildren; Kathy Gunst and John Rudolph of South Berwick, ME, and their daughters Maya and Emma Rudolph, Michael Gunst and Donna Eberle of Boulder, CO, and their children Shay and Mayze Gunst, Lee A. and Andrea Gunst of Exeter, NH, and their sons Matthew and Eric Gunst. He was the beloved husband of Nancy L. Gunst who died in 1991. He was a respected leader. He founded and lead the Westchester Senior Tennis League. He was a member of the Board and longtime member of Beach Point Club. He was president of several companies; Riverdale, CohamaRiverdale, Wellman Decorative Fabrics and Licensing By Design. He attended Lafayette College. A celebration of his life will be held on Friday, May 4, 11:30 AM, at Beach Point Club, 900 Rushmore Ave, Mamaroneck, NY. If friends so desire, contributions may be made to Gilda's Club of Westchester, 80 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY 10601-5101. Pop-Pop, we miss you.
