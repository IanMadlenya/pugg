
        LASSER-Tessie, on February 2, 1997, age 96 years. Wife of the late Milton Lasser, mother, by birth and marriage, of Arnold, Ruth, Joseph, Ruth, Howard and Barbara Lasser, and Judith and Mark Litt. Grandmother by birth and marriage of 18 grandchildren and 19 great-grandchildren. Her focus was her family. Funeral service Monday 11:30 AM, Temple Israel of New Rochelle, 1000 Pinebrook Blvd, New Rochelle. Memorial contributions to Temple Israel of New Rochelle Religious School or Nursery School would be her wish. For further information contact The George T. Davis Funeral Home, New Rochelle.
