
        SHERMAN--Winnie, age 101, died May 16, 2004 at her home in Jamesburg, NJ. Wife of the late Lee D. Sherman. Mother of the late Dr. Robert Lee Sherman. Survived by two daughters, Marjorie Cahn of MI and Dr. Lucy Goodenday of OH, a son, Paul Sherman of Morganville, NJ, seven grandchildren, and nine great grandchildren. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, May 18, 1:45 PM at Woodbridge Memorial Gardens, Woodbridge, NJ. Memorial contributions to Catherine Lorillard Wolfe Art Club, Grace Church Parish House, 802 Broadway, NY NY 10003.
