
        THOMAS-Robert. The Police Surgeons of the New York Police Department record with profound sorrow the loss of our Chief Police Surgeon. Chief Thomas died suddenly on May 19th, 2003. He will be remembered for his wisdom, his leadership, compassion and his good sense of humor. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife and family. We mourn with them and share their loss. The Medical Division & the Police Surgeons of the New York Police Dept
        THOMAS-Robert E., M.D. The Society of Honorary Police Surgeons of the City of New York mourns the loss of our friend and advisor, Chief Robert E. Thomas, M.D. He is remembered as an astute physician and gentleman. Ira D. Rothfeld, M.D., Pres. Nicholas Camarinos, D.P.M.
