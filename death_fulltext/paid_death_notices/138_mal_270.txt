
        ZUCKER-Henry L. The American Friends of The Hebrew University of Jerusalem mourn with great sorrow the passing of Henry L. Zucker of Cleveland, Ohio, an Honorary Governor of The Hebrew University and member of AFHU's Council of Trustees. In 1989, he was the recipient of the Scopus Award, the highest honor given by the American Friends, when the Henry L. Zucker Fellowship Program for Graduate Study in Social Work was established at The Hebrew University. To his wife, Ellen, and the entire family we extend our deepest condolences. The Hebrew University of Jerusalem Professor Menachem Magidor, President The American Friends of The Hebrew University Keith L. Sachs, President Fred S. Lafer, Chairman of the Board Adam B. Kahan, Executive Vice President
