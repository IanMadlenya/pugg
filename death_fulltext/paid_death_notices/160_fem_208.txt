
        SANTERAMO-Angela (nee Castellaneta). Died on May 23, just short of her 93rd birthday in Tamarac, FL. Beloved wife for 46 years of Michael who died on May 17, 1975, & sister of Leonardo Castellaneta. Cherished mother of Nicholas, Cesare, Rose Grove and Mary Devins. She is survived by her six grandchildren and nine greatgrandchildren. Her ashes will be buried beside her husband's in Fairmount Cemetery, Newark, NJ.
