
        CLARK--Donald G.C., M.D. The Members of the Society of Memorial Gynecologic Oncologists and the Division of Gynecology at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center record with profound regret the recent death of their colleague and friend Donald G.C. Clark, M.D. Dr. Clark was a member of the Gynecology Service at Memorial SloanKettering Cancer Center from July 1, 1956 until July 1, 1987. He was appointed to the Emeritus Staff in 1987. He was regarded as an excellent surgeon and teacher who devoted his career to caring for women with cancer. He will be missed by all.
