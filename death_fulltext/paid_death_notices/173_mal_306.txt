
        FELDMAN-Saul M., M.D. of Fairfield, CT, prominent Bridgeport doctor, died June 29 in Bridgeport. Dr. Feldman is survived by his wife, Nancy (Newman) Feldman, his children, Bruce Feldman and Terri Silvers and Marci Sapers, step daughters Deborah Messulam and Allison Peyton, and eight grandchildren, a brother Dr. Morton Feldman, a sister, Bonnie Reese. Services will take place Sunday, July 1 at 11:00 at Congregation B'nai Israel, 2710 Park Ave, Bridgeport, with interment following at Beth El Cemetery, Westwood, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Vincent Medical Center Foundation, Saul M. Feldman M.D. Memorial Fund, 2800 Main St, Bridgeport, CT 06606.
