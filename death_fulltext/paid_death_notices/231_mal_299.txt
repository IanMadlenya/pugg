
        RICHMAN--Leonard R. Died Thursday, April 27, 2006, in Boca Raton, FL. He was 80 years old. He served the nation with distinction as a Marine in the South Pacific and China during and after World War II. He lived for more than 48 years in Long Beach, NY. A Commanding Officer in the Auxiliary Police, he was also an avid fisherman and collector of police uniform patches. Richman married the former Jacqueline Corman in 1949, a marriage of nearly 57 years. Adored father of Michael and Laurence, cherished grandfather of Joseph and Peter, beloved brother of Mildred. Contributions in his name may be made to God's Love We Deliver, 166 Ave of the Americas, NY, NY 10013. Services Sunday, April 30, 2006, 11 AM, at Beth Israel Cemetery, Woodbridge, NJ.
