
        RAI-Vishva Roop. Of Parsippany, N.J. Beloved husband of Roopa, father of Ajay, brother of Kanti, Savitri, Shanta and Veena. Devoted uncle and friend. Died suddenly November 19, 2002 while on a business trip in Singapore. Funeral was held in Jaipur, India on November 23rd. Memorial service 11AM Saturday, January 18, 2003, in the United Nations Chapel, 777 U.N. Plaza (44 St between 1-2nd Avenues), N.Y., N.Y. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Center for Independence of the Disabled in New York, 841 Broadway, N.Y., N.Y. 10003, or Disha Foundation (for the Disha School) in Jaipur, C/O Ajay Rai, 2166 Broadway, New York, New York 10024.
