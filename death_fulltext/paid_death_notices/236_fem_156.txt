
        SERKEY--Hanne, on September 28, 2006 at age 92, after a life of loving care for family, friends and those in need. Devoted daughter of Siegfried and Fanny Mann, she is survived by her beloved husband, Rene, sons Robert and Richard, daughters-in-law Louise and Pauline, grandchildren Ethan, Rebecca and Jakub, and nephews and nieces Peter, Deirdre, Tony and Angela.
