
        WINSTON - Thomas Lee, 1941-2001, died suddenly on Saturday, March 17. He was born and raised in Dallas, Texas. An actor and operatic tenor, Mr. Winston was a forty-year veteran of Broadway, Off-Broadway and regional theatre. He also performed extensively with opera companies here and in Europe. With his companion, actress Pamela Nelson, he founded The Heliotrope Group, to develop new musicals. Mr. Winston was organist at St. Clement's Church. Funeral services are today, Thursday, at 11 AM, at St. Clement's. Donations St. Clement's, 423 West 46th Street, NYC.
