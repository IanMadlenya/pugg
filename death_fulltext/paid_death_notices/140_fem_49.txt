
        NELSON-Tara. The Milstein family and the Brokers and Staff of Douglas Elliman, offer their profound condolences to the family of Tara Nelson, who died in the crash of Swissair flight 111. Tara's many friends who knew her when she worked at Douglas Elliman remember her as an extremely professional, charming, warm and caring member of the group, cooperative and helpful to brokers, customers and staff alike. A loss so hard to comprehend. Our thoughts and prayers are with Tara's parents, our colleagues Bob and Sheila Nelson, in this time of incredible tragedy. Howard P. Milstein, Chairman The Brokers and Staff of Douglas Elliman
