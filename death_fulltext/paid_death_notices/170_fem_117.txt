
        HAWKINS-Evelyn Byrd. Died on March 13, 2001 in her 95th year. Wife of the late Dexter C. Hawkins. Mother of Evelyn B. Patterson, Eliot D. Hawkins and the late Alice C. VanTuyl. Grandmother of eight and great-grandmother of nine. Funeral service Monday, at 11 AM, at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71 Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60 Street, New York City 10022, ''in memory of Evelyn Hawkins''.
