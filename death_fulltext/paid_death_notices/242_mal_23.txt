
        FEIN--Lester. The Board of Directors, staff and athletes of Maccabi USA/Sports for Israel mourn the loss of our dear friend and leader, Lester Fein. The impact of his caring and dedication will be felt for many years to come. Lester was a gentle soul with a brilliant mind and a kind heart who inspired many, a Mensch of a man. Our most sincere condolences to his wife Cecile and their family. May his memory be for a blessing. The Maccabi USA Sports for Israel Family
