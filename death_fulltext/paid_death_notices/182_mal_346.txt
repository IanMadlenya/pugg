
        SCHWARTZ-Lawrence N., 72, of Long Island & Siesta Key, Florida. Died March 12, 2002 at the Mayo Clinic in Jacksonville following a January 4, 2002 kidney transplant. He is survived by his devoted wife Helen, daughter Terry Merrill, son Michael, and four grandchildren. He retired in sales from Arkin-Medoin 1998, and was a longtime employee of Queens Litho. He was a five-year veteran of the Marine Corps and served as sergeant in Korea. Memorial service at 12:30PM on Tuesday, March 26th at Temple Beth El of Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the National Kidney Foundation.
