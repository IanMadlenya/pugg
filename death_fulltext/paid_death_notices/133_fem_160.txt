
        WOLF-Dorothy. Of Hollywood Fla. formerly of Riverdale, NY. On February 16. Widow of the late David Wolf. Beloved mother of Daniel and daughter-in-law Cheryl. Proud and loving grandmother of Eric, Jordan and David. Devoted and loving sister to Banjamin Loeb, sister-in-law Jean and niece Ellen. She will forever be remembered in our hearts and the hearts of so many whose lives she touched with her time and talent, loving dedication and sense of humor. She was a truly unique and wonderful human being and will be sorely missed. Donations in her memory may be made to the Hollywood Art and Culture Center/ Dorothy Wolf Memorial Fund, 1650 Harrison Street, Hollywood, Fla. 33020. A ceremony will be held in her honor at the center on Monday, March 9 at 11 A.M.
