
        WILMER--Rev. Richard H. Jr., 86, an Episcopal priest and scholar, died in Pittsburgh on March 3, 2005. He was Episcopal chaplain at Yale University and later Dean of Berkeley Divinity School. He is survived by his wife, Sarah King Wilmer of Pittsburgh; two sons, Richard IV of Arlington, VA and Stephen E. of Dublin, Ireland; three daughters, Margaret Bartlett of New Vernon, NJ, Natalie Blenk of London, England, and Christine Barkus of New York City; and 10 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on April 9 at 2 PM in Calvary Episcopal Church, Shadyside, Pitt sburgh. The family suggests memorial contributions to the National Cathedral, 3101 Wisconsin Ave., NW, Washington, DC 20016-5098.
