
        BRUEN-Francis William. On June 14, 2001. Retired member of the N.Y.C.P.D. Born in Manhattan, NYC, January 13, 1918, and a resident of Vinegar Hill, Manhattan, for forty-two years, prior to moving to Stuyvesant Town, NYC. Father of none, and godfather of many. Predeceased by his mother, Elizabeth (nee Reynolds) and father, Bartholomew, both of Mohill, County Leitrim, Ireland, and by brother, Gerard and sister, Rita. Visitation at Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Ave, New York, Saturday & Sunday 2-5, 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial at St. Emeric's Church, 740 East 13th Street, NY, NY between Avenues C & D Monday 9:30 AM. Interment to follow in family plot at St. John's Cemetery, Middle Village, Queens, New York. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
