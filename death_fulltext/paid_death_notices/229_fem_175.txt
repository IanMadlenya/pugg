
        TSCHACBASOV -- Irene Ze von. Artist, muse, entrepreneur and philanthropist, died peacefully in her sleep Saturday, February 18. Beloved mother of Leonard Barton, devoted wife of the late Nahum Tschacbasov, sister of Fred Zivitofsky, and the late Martin Zivotovosky, sister-inlaw of Anne and Beverly, loving aunt and great-aunt, grandmother of Cookie Cohen and Robin Murphy, dear friend of Minnie Itkowitz and her gracious family. Irene leaves a legacy of good humor, style, extraordinary beauty, charm, tenderness and joy of life. She will be missed by family, friends and a host of neighbors at the Hotel Chelsea, her home for the past 50 years. Funeral services will take place Wednesday, February 22, 11:30 am, at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave (at 91 St). The family requests that in lieu of flowers, contributions be made to Lighthouse International.
