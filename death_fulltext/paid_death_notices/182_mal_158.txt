
        STEVENS-Dr. Peter H. Distinguished psychologist, adjunct professor at New York University and CUNY, career consultant at Stevens Institute of Technology, and author of the book ''Crisis Prevention,'' passed away peacefully on January 15, 2002 at 88 years. Greatly beloved for his skillful warmth, he will be sorely missed, particularly by his devoted niece, Sybil Taylor. Memorial, April 13 call 212-242-7410 for details.
