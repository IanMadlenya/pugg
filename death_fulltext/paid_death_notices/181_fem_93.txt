
        RAMSEY-Susan Laura. July 16, 1950-February 5, 2002. On February 5, 2002, Susan lost her three year battle with cancer. Throughout her illness she displayed unwavering strength and the bravery of a fearless warrior. She was an accomplished writer and martial artist, a role model to many, and lived life always exhibiting her steadfast commitment to equality, family and peace. She was a devoted mother and wife, a loving daughter and sister, and a caring friend. Mourning her loss is her husband, her two daughters Megan and Lauren, her parents, her two sisters, and everyone who knew her. A memorial service was held on Saturday, February 9, at the First Unitarian Church in Brooklyn Heights, NY.
