
        TUCK-David Hallam. 70, died Palm Beach, January 15, after a brief illness. Survived by a brother, James M., of Guadalajara, two sisters, Clare Fahnestock Moorhead and Lee Fahnestock Leggett, both of NYC. Graduated from St. Paul's School, Hanover, NH. Attended University of Virginia and George Washington University. Served in US Army, 1954-1957, stationed in Germany. As stockbroker he worked at Auchincloss Parker & Redpath in Washington, DC, at Dominick & Dominick in Montreal and NYC. Recently lived in Christiansted, St. Croix. His sense of humor never failed him, nor his interests in good reading, professional football and the stock market. Funeral at Woolworth Chapel, Woodlawn Cemetery, Bronx, January 23, 2 PM. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
