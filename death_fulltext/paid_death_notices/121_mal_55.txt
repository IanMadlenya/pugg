
        MORRIS-Gerry. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Friar Gerry Morris on February 2, 1997. He was a dedicated contributor to the Friars Epistle. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Funeral services will be private. A memorial service will be at a later date. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
