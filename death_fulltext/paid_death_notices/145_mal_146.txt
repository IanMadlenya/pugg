
        TANG-Zugu. On February 8, 1999 at age 80. Beloved husband of Jingwen. Devoted father of Deliang Tang and Yen Nan Wong, and father-in-law of Frances Tang and Thomas Wong. Loving grandfather of Edward and Christina Tang, Roger and Linda Wong. A loyal and patriotic Chinese immigrant. He served his country with honor in WWII and treated all he met with gentle humor, warmth and kindness. Memorial service February 13, 2PM at First Presbyterian Church, Englewood, NJ.
