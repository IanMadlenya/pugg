
        FRANKLIN--Suzanne Considine. 73, died Friday, April 8, 2005. She was employed for 35 years in the art department of Fortune Magazine and then as an assistant picture editor for Business Week, Time and Money magazines. Survived by husband Bill Franklin, son Jim, daughters Anne and Margo Considine, brother John Butruille, sisters Mary Louise Blake, Anne Keck, and Odette Rossi. A Mass was offered on Monday at Our Lady of the Mount Church in Warren, NJ. For information, Higgins Funeral Home. Visit: www.higginsfuneralhome.com.
