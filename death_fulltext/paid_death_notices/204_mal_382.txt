
        STEELE--William H., aged 76 years, on Sunday, January 25, 2004. He was the son of the late Sally and Paul Vining of West Springfield, MA and the brother of the late Pauline Van Wert of Spruce Head, ME. He attended the Choate School in CT and Yale University. He is survived by and lovingly remembered by his four nieces and nephews: George Van Wert of Richmond, KY, Susan Gonzalez of Fairfield, CT, Sally Glassbrook of Chapel Hill, NC, and Alan S. Van Wert of Portsmouth, ME. He is also survived by many grand-nieces and grand-nephews. Visitation Friday, January 30 from 3-7 PM at John Krtil Funeral Home, 1297 First Avenue (70th Street), NYC. Interment services are private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory preferred to the Actors' Fund of America, 729 7th Avenue, 10th Floor, New York, New York 10019.
