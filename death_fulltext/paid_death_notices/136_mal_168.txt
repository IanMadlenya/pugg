
        APPEL-Saul Baer, MD, in El Paso, Texas, on April 17, 1998. Beloved husband of Cecile, cherished father of Deborah & Richard Spindel, Carol and Ira Cohen, Anne, Allen, Gary, grandfather, uncle, brother of Ethelyn and Mark Bruck, Sybil Silberman, brother-in-law of Elaine and David Gill. He served his country as a major in the U.S. Army Medical Corps and was President of the El Paso and Texas Heart Associations. He was honored by the El Paso Medical Society for 50 years of devotion and service as a cardiologist to the community and medical profession. Graduate of NYU-Bellevue, 1947. He is greatly missed by all those whose lives he touched.
