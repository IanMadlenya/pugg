
        CUCCI-Vincent E., Esq. Formerly of Manhattan, on May 20th, 1999. He was 89 years old. Vincent was born July 26, 1909 in Manhattan. He earned a Bachelor and Law degree from St. John's University in 1933. He practiced law in the state of New York for over 60 years until his retirement in 1993. Mr. Cucci provided advice and counsel on numerous investigations for the Manhattan District Attorney's office. He was a respected member of the New York State Bar. Beloved husband of the late Phyllis Barba Cucci. Loving father of Dorothy A. Siclare, Vincent, John and Richard Cucci. Dear brother of Flora Green and Rose Barba. Adored grandfather of Vincent III, Nicole, Grace, John, Michael, Phyllis, Christen, Joseph and Dorothy Esther. Funeral from the Casey Funeral Home, 350 Slosson Avenue, Staten Island, New York on Monday. Funeral Mass, St. Teresa's Church, Staten Island, NY, 9:30 AM. Interment Calvary Cemetery. Friends will be received Sunday 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM.
