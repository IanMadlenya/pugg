
        MERAGE-Andre. Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., expresses its sorrow at the passing of Andre Merage, cherished husband of Katherine Merage. Andre was the beloved father of Paul and Elisabeth, Louise, Camron & Carrol, David and Laura and Lin; beloved grandfather of Richard and Giulia, Michelle and Paul, Lauren, Greg, Jeff & Alissa, Darian, Jonathan, Sabrina, Courtney and Spencer; beloved great grandfather of Matteo, Grant, & Isabel. As a Guardian of the Dream, Andre was a generous supporter of the Hadassah Medical Organization and the Hadassah College of Technology. He was devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. His legacy will live on through his good name. He will be missed by all who knew him. Bonnie Lipton, National President
