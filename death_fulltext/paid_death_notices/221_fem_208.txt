
        LEVISON--Isabelle C. A resident of Florida, died June 25, 2005. A native of Norfolk, Virginia, she also resided in New York City and Detroit, Michigan. She was a founder at Mount Sinai Medical Center and Miami Jewish Home for the Aged, and a patron of the arts. Survived by her children Sally A. Edwards & Edward J. Minskoff, sister Rena Kriegel, four grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
