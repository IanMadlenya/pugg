
        TOPCHIK-Wolf B., 102, of Bethesda, MD, formerly of Passaic, NJ, on May 2, 1999. Beloved husband of the late Libby nee Pruzansky, loving father of Robert J., Dr. Abraham, and Sylvia Levine. Dear brother of Martin, Alfred, and George. Dear grandfather of 10 grandchildren and nine greatgrandchildren. Service will be Tuesday, May 4, at 1 P.M. from Jewish Memorial Chapel, 841 Allwood Road, Clifton, New Jersey.
        TOPCHIK-Wolf B. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University mourn the passing of Wolf Topchik, the beloved father of our President, Robert J. Topchik. A kind, gentle and considerate man, Wolf was devoted to his family. Along with his late wife, Libby, he was passionately committed to the establishment of The State of Israel. Our heartfelt condolences to his sons, Dr. Abraham Topchik, and Robert Topchik, daughter, Sylvia Levine, their families, grandchildren, and great grandchildren. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Alan L. Aufzien, Chairman, Board of Directors Melvin S. Taub, Chairman Emeritus Stephen Lecker, Exec. V.P.
