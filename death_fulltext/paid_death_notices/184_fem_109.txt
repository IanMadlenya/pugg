
        AIBEL-Bertha G. (nee Glasser). On May 14, 2002 at the age of 98. Beloved wife of the late Irving J. Aibel. Devoted mother of Judy and Lonny Brickman & Benjamin and Nancy Aibel and the late Herbert M. Aibel. Loving grandmother of eight and great grandmother of eleven. Interment was private. Donation in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        AIBEL-Bertha G. Congregation Emanu-EL of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Bertha G. Aibel. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        AIBEL-Bertha. With deep sorrow we mourn the passing of our beloved aunt and friend. Emily and Larry Aibel and Family
