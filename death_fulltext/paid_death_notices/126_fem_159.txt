
        RUBEL-Fannie W. Died suddenly on May 30. Wife of the late Adolph A. Rubel. Respected grandmother, friend, photographer and nature lover. Her wry sense of humor and individualism will be missed by all who knew her. Memorial tribute will be held on August 2 at 2PM at the Epiphany Branch Library, 228 E. 23 St. Contributions may be made in her memory to charitable animal funds.
