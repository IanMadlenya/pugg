
        BARREL-Sadye (Syd). The officers, board, and staff of the Smithsonian Institution National Museum of the American Indian express their condolences on the passing of Syd Barrel in Palm Beach, Fla. She was the beloved mother of Iris Barrel Apfel, a keen supporter of the National Museum of the American Indian, and sonin-law of Carl Apfel. Mrs. Barrel will be remembered and appreciated for her business acumen and generous support of many philanthropies. W. Richard West, Director John L. Colonghi, National Campaign Director National Museum of the American Indian
