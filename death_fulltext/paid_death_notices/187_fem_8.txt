
        CASSON-Ann Bard. Died on July 25th at the age of 74. She lived and worked for 25 years in Richmond, VA, as a social worker and licensing specialist with the State Dept. of Social Service. Ann grew up in Croton-On-Hudson, matriculated at Smith College and received her M.S.W. at Bryn Mawr. She was an avid environmentalist and birder. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Mort Casson of Richmond, & her son, Gerald Casson of Seattle. Interment at a later date will take place in upstate New York.
