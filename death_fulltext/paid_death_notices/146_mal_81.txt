
        COHN-Isidore ''Tibby''. The Riverdale Jewish Center mourns the loss of our member Isidore Tibby Cohn. We extend profound condolences to his wife Florence, his daughter Abbe Grosser, his son Dr. Rick Cohn, his grandchildren and the entire family. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
