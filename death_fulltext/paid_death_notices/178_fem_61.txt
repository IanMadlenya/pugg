
        EATON-Elizabeth Witsell, 75, of Manhattan, on November 4, 2001. Devoted wife of the late William Mellon Eaton. Loving mother of Carolyn, Alexander, Sarah and Lisa. Treasured grandmother of Eliza, Lily, Jay, Nina and William. Sister of Frederick C. Witsell, Jr. Avid tennis player and golfer, lifelong traveler and cherished friend to many. Service Friday at 11AM at the Church of Heavenly Rest, 90th & 5th Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory and in honor of Manhattan and New Hampshire may be made to the Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60 Street, New York, NY 10022.
