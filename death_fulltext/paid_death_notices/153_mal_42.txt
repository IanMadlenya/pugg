
        SMOLEN-Arthur E. Friends and family will greatly miss the special love and devotion of Arthur E. Smolen, born in Brooklyn, April 28, 1918. He was a devoted husband of 56 years to Selma, loving father to Ellen, devoted brother to Florence, caring uncle and great uncle to Carol, Andy, Michael, Jodie, Marcia and Amanda. He was a loyal and dependable friend to many. For more than 35 years, Arthur was a dedicated employee of Loews Corporation, where he was a Vice President. He was a hard working man who took great pride in his involvment in the success of this Fortune 500 company. Arthur proudly served in the U.S. Armed Forces during World War II for which he received the Purple Heart. He died October 2, 1999, of natural causes. The family will receive visitors at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, Monday evening, October 4, from 7-9 PM. Funeral services will be held 9:30 AM, October 5, at Temple Shaaray Tefila, located at 250 East 79th Street.
