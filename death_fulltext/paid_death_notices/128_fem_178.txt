
        MOSER-Louise Klauber. With love and sadness we record her peaceful death at home in Larchmont, NY on September 20, 1997. She is survived by sons David and Ronald and grandsons Gregg and Adam. The family welcome those who knew her to her home from 3pm to 5pm on September 23, 1997 to share an appreciation of her life. In lieu of flowers, donations to United Hospital Hospice, 406 Boston Post Road, Portchester, NY 10573.
        MOSER-Louise K. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Louise K. Moser. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
