
        BRANDMAN-Natalie Damask, 82, a former Manhattan resident died February 18, 2001 in Reno, Nevada. She was the wife of Arthur Brandman who died in 1997. She is survived by two sons, Richard Damask and Kenneth Damask, daughter-in-law Susan Damask, and two sisters, Lillian Baskin and Shirley Speiller. Her husband Harold Damask M.D. preceeded her in death in 1964. A graveside service is scheduled at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Flushing, New York at 11 AM, Sunday, February 25, 2001.
