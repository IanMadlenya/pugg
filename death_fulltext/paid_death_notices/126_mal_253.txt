
        SLOANE-Charles B. The Board of Directors and staff of the American Associates, Ben Gurion University of the Negev, deeply mourn the loss of Charles B. Sloane, a generous benefactor and friend of Ben Gurion University. Charles Sloane established a graduate fellowship award at Ben Gurion University in memory of his late brother, Stanley Laurie Sloane, a generous supporter along with his wife Harriet Sloane and was a former member of the University's Board of Governors and the National Board of Directors of the American Associates. We extend heartfelt condolences to his nephew, Robert Laurie Sloane of New York City, his niece, Ann Goodman Sloane of Rockville, MD., and sister-in-law Harriet Sloane of New York City. Kenneth L. Tucker, President Kenneth M. Farber, Exec V.P. Lis Gaines N.Y. Regional Chairperson
