
        KARMASON -- Marilyn G., M.D. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Dr. Marilyn G. Karmason, a psychiatrist at NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital/Weill Cornell Medical Center and Weill Medical College of Cornell University, where she spent her entire medical career and made many important contributions. An exemplary and compassionate clinician, skilled teacher, and devoted member of her department, Dr. Karmason helped strengthen ties between Weill Cornell and The Rockefeller University Hospital. She was much beloved by all. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to the Karmason and Spritz family. Dr. Herbert Pardes President & CEO, NY-Presbyterian Hospital Dr. Antonio M. Gotto, Dean Weill Cornell Medical College Dr. Jack D. Barchas Chair of Psychiatry Dr. Philip J. Wilner Executive Vice Chair of Psychiatry Weill Cornell Medical College & NY-Presbyterian Hospital
