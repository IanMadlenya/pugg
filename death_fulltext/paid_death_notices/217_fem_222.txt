
        MARMOR--Louise Tulchin, 86, of Jackson Heights, NY, died on February 23rd. Beloved wife of Benjamin Marmor, mother of Neil and Joan Marmor, Susan and David Smolin, Michael and Gloria Marmor, Bonnie and Steve Goldstein. Cherished grandmother to Craig and Peter Smolin, Nicholas and Daniel Marmor. Contributions in her memory may be made to the NY Chapter of the Alzheimer's Association. Services at ''Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer,'' Queens Blvd. and 76th Rd., Forest Hills, at 12 noon, Sunday, February 27th.
