
        BRILL-Florence M., on February 4, 2000, devoted wife for 56 years of the late Jerome; loving, proud and generous mother of Judy and Jerry, Arthur and Carol, and Connie and Jerry; doting grandmother of Irene, Robert, Jonathan, Paul, and Matthew; delighted greatgrandmother of Sophie; caring and affectionate aunt, friend and confidant. She loved and was loved by so many for nearly nine decades. An attorney, she lived a wonderful and bountiful life, brimmed full of education, artistic talent and, above all, family. Services will be private.
