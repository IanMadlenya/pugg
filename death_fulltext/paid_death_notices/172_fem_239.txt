
        HUBBELL-Ruth. Died peacefully after a brief illness on May 25. She was the beloved wife of the late Jack Hubbell; mother of John Hubbell, Jean Asher, Susan Jaeckel and Pat Becker; grandmother of five and great grandmother of three. She was an extraordinary woman who will be missed by all who knew her, most especially her family. A memorial service will be held on Friday, June 1, at the Rye Presbyterian Church at 1 PM.
