
        KOURTIDES--Asimenia. On January 9, 2005. Beloved wife of the late James Kourtides and loving mother of Ellen Kourtides - Vassilakis and Reno Kourtides. Dearest mother-in-law of Dianne Kourtides and Hon. Theodore Vassilakis. Grandmother of Christine Rachler and Nico Kourtides. Born in Constantinople and raised in Roumania, and after a brief stay in Greece, Asimenia arrived in this country in 1955 and was happy to make New York her permanent home. She leaves behind her many relatives and friends by whom she will be deeply missed. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell at 1076 Madison Ave. on Wednesday, January 12, 2-4 and 6-9 PM. The funeral service will be held on Thursday at 9:30 AM at the Holy Trinity Greek Cathedral at 319 East 74th St., NYC. In lieu of flowers donations in Asimenia Kourtides' name may be made to the Disabled American Veterans for Iraqi Troops P.O. Box 14301 Cinn, Ohio 45250-0301
