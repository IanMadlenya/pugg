
        GOLDSTEIN-Betty. On March 25, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Isaac. Devoted mother of William and Michael, and mother-in-law of Diana and Jinny. Loving sister of Pearl Rochelson. Cherished grandmother of Andrew, Marc and Eric. Caring and dear friend to many. Services Wed., 9:30AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. Amsterdam Ave. Donations in her memory can be made to the Jewish Guild for the Blind, the Lighthouse, or Guiding Eyes for the Blind.
