
        TIFFANY-Gordon, 86 years old. From Weare, N.H. Died Tuesday at his home. Born in Port Chester, New York. The son of Henry Dyer & Beatrice (Gordon) Tiffany. He attended St. Paul's School in Concord, NH, and was a 1935 graduate of Yale University. He was a graduate of Columbia Law School and later graduated from the Boston Museum of Fine Arts School. He is survived by one son, William F. Tiffany of Weare, NH, one daughter, Jean G. Tiffany of Portsmouth, NH, five grandchildren, two great grandchildren, several nieces and nephews. He was predeceased by his wife, Ellen (Auchincloss) Tiffany.
