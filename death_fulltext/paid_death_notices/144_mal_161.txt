
        ARKIN-Benjamin. 77, of Boca Raton, FL and Dobbs Ferry, NY. Beloved husband of Rita. Loving father of Leslie and Russell; devoted father-in-law of Stephen Horowitz and Elaine B. Arkin; adoring grandfather of Anna Irene Arkin. Born May 6, 1921 in NYC, died January 8, 1999 in Boca Raton, FL of colo-rectal cancer. A loving, kind and gentle man who enriched those whose lives he touched. Interment private. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Rd., Boca Raton, FL 33486.
