
        PORTER-Patrick. Died on May 9, 2002 in New York City. The many friends of Patrick Porter mourn his loss. As a member of the British Army's invading force, he landed on Normandy Beach on DDay. An extraordinarily creative fashion designer and former Professor of Design at the Pratt Institute. He is survived by his companion, Charles B. Selden. Condolences in the form of charitable contributions may be made to Doctors Without Borders, Box 94, Tom's River, N.J. 08754.
