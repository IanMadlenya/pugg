
        GELLERMAN-Dr. Saul William. Former Dean of the Graduate School of Management, University of Dallas. Worldwide management consultant. Died on March 26th, 2003 in Cleveland. He was a graduate of the University of Pennsylvania where he received a Ph.D. in Clinical and Industrial Psychology. He is the author of 10 published books and 29 films on management-related topics. He is survived by his wife, Patricia A. Gellerman, three children, and one grandson, and two step-children and two stepgrandchildren. Memorial service will be held at a later date in Denver, CO.
