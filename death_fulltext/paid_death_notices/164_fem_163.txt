
        RUBENSTEIN-Muriel Rita (nee Krawitz). Of West Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Hartsdale, NY, passed away September 6, 2000. A ''profile in courage'' who fought colon cancer with dignity and bravery for three years. Beloved wife of Bert; most cherished mother of Daniel (Leah) and Kenneth (Linda); adored grandmother of Harry, Deborah, Emily, Eric and Jason. Devoted sister of Selma Friedland, brother Herman Krawitz, and the late Seymour Krawitz; special sister-in-law; most favorite aunt to her nieces and nephews; and irreplacable friend. We have all been blessed to have had Muriel in our lives and she will never be forgotten. Services were held September 10, 2000.
