
        AUERBACH - Rabbanit Recha. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the beloved sister of our member, Manfred Meyer. May her entire family be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President
        AUERBACH-Recha. Bar-Ilan University in Israel and its American Friends extends sincerest condolences to our honorary alumnus, Mr. Manfred H. Meyer on the loss of his beloved sister, Recha Auerbach. May he derive solace from inspiring memories and continuing commitment to the great causes of Israel and the Jewish people. Rabbi Emanuel Rackman, Chancellor Professor Moshe Kaveh, President Jane Stern Lebell, President American Friends General (Res.) Yehuda Halevy, Executive Vice President
