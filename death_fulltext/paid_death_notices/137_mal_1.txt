
        CIZNER-Morris. On May 29, 1998. Beloved father of Allen and Arlene and grandfather of Jennifer and Aaron. Loving brother of Rosalind. A gentle, good and truly remarkable human being. He gave tirelessly of himself to care for his late ill wife and he was the very best father imaginable. A respected and successful lawyer for over 50 years who truly loved the law and shaped it. He will never be forgotten by his family and colleagues. Services Tuesday, June 2, 10 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
