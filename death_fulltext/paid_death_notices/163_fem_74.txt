
        EISENSTADT-Sidonie Judith. Age 94, died on August 8, 2000, at the home in Ft. Lauderdale, Florida, that she shared with her nephew Steven and his family for the past year. A graduate of the Columbia School of Social Work, she served the NYC Department of Social Services at the Lenox Hill Neighborhood House until her retirement in 1971. She then embarked on her volunteer career as a guide at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. She was a beacon in the lives of the many she knew and loved. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Lighthouse for the Blind.
