
        ABRAMS--Dorothy. 93. Devoted wife of the late Benjamin Abrams, loving mother of former New York Attorney General Bob and Diane Abrams, and Marlene and Sandy Kitrosser, devoted grandmother of Rachel and Rabbi Ian Pear, Becky Abrams, and Bari and Jeff Wolf, adoring great-grandmother of Gavriella and Michaella Pear. Died peacefully on Sunday. Funeral service at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. at 91st St., on Monday, August 11, at 10 AM. Donations in her memory may be sent to Shir Hadash, 11 East 86th St., NYC 10028.
        ABRAMS--Dorothy ''Dottie.'' We record with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved mother of our dear friend Robert Abrams. To Bob, his wife Diane and the entire family we extend our heartfelt condolences. Richard and Lucy Halperin The Velocity Foundation
