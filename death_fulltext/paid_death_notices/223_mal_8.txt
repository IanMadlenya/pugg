
        AARONI--Abraham. 98, beloved husband of Celia (Siegel) who predeceased him two weeks ago, dear brother of Yetta, Minnie, Ida, Sam, Tillie and Paul, adored by his many nieces, nephews, great and grand nieces and nephews. Cherished by generations of devoted students and loyal friends. Most of all, he was the consummate teacher, a mentor respected by all he touched with his charm, search for perfection, love of language and lifelong dedication to knowledge, history and culture. He practiced teaching as an art, his life was a masterpiece. Graveside service at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ, August 1, 10 AM.
