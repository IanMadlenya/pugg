
        WILLIAMS--Edith Huntington. Beloved wife of the late David Benton Williams, beloved mother of David, Deborah and Howard, and the beloved grandmother of David, Anne, Benjamin, Elizabeth, Emily, Margaret, Zoe and Jonathan died peacefully at Lenox Hill Hospital on May 18, 2004 which was the birthday of her late husband. She was 81 years old. A memorial service will be held on Thursday June 3, 2004 at Saint James Church located at 71st and Madison Avenue at 1:30 PM. In lieu of flowers the family asks that contributions be sent to The Church at 9 Point O Woods Avenue, Point O Woods, NY 11706.
