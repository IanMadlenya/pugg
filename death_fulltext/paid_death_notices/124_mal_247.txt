
        WALLIS-William J., of Rockville Centre, L.I., on May 20, 1997 at age 80. Beloved husband of Helen M. Brother of Leslye Perry and esteemed uncle of eleven nieces and nephews. He retired as Vice President and Secretary of the Executive Committee of Macy's. An honor graduate in 1938 of Emory and Henry College, Bill was awarded an Honorary Doctor of Laws by his Alma Mater in 1971 recognizing his many services to the college, Charitable and Business Organizations. He was a Past Master and Trustee of LynbrookMassapequa Masonic Lodge, Past President of the Metropolitan Financial Executives Assoc; Director of the Controller's Congress of the National Retail Merchant's Assoc; Ruling Elder, Clerk of the Session and Treasurer of the United Church of Rockville Centre; Secretary, Treasurer and Chairman of the Finance Committee of the Wayside Home school for Girls (Salvation Army); Trustee of the Presbytery of Long Island; and member of several other charitable and business organizations. Friends may call at the Fairchild Sons Funeral Home, 20 Lincoln Ave, Rockville Centre, Thursday, 2-4 & 7-9PM. Masonic and religious services at 8 and 8:30 PM at the funeral home. Interment Friday, 11 AM, Pinelawn Memorial Park.
