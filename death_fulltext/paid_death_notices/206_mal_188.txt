
        KIAER--Dr. Donald C. On behalf of the Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of the North Shore-Long Island Jewish Health System, we express our sorrow on the death of our colleague, Dr. Donald C. Kiaer on March 13. A longstanding staff member of North Shore University Hospital at Glen Cove, his wisdom and cooperation as medical director provided extraordinary assistance in the early joining of his institution with North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset more than 15 years ago. We commemorate his constant professionalism and his outstanding service to the community and his patients. Our sincerest condolences to his wife Kathleen & all his family. Michael Dowling President & CEO Roy Zuckerberg Chairman North ShoreLIJ Health System
