
        HORVITZ-Ann Battie. On Monday, August 12, 2002, of Washington, DC. Beloved wife of Wayne L. Horvitz. Devoted mother of Bill, Lee, Wayne and Philip. Ms. Horvitz, 80, died of a stroke after a long illness. She is survived by her husband and sons, by daughters-in-law Patti Trimble and Robin Holcomb, and by grandchildren Nica, Asa and Lowell Horvitz. There will be a private funeral service and a memorial service will be scheduled for the fall. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to North Capitol Neighborhood Development, 1330 N. Capital St. N.W., Washington, DC 20001.
