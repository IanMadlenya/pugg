A memorial service will be held at St. Thomas Church, 5th Ave., 1 W. 53rd St., Tuesday, October 23, at 3 PM. Doreen, you will forever be in our hearts and memories. Your love, guidance and friendship will stay within us always. We miss you very much and wish you eternal happiness and peace. Love, Susan and Harry B. Freeman, III
