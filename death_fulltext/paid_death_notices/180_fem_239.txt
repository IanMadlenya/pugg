
        AUER-Jean, ''Pancho'', (nee Patchett) of La Quinta, CA and Westhampton Beach, NY passed away on January 22, 2002 after a long battle with emphysema. Born in Preston, MD, Pancho became a nationally known photographic model in the 40's, 50's, and 60's gracing covers of more than 40 magazines including Vogue, Harper's Bazaar, Glamour and Life. She is survived by her husband of fifty years Louis', her daughter Amy of Westhampton Beach, her sister Mrs. J. Fred King and her brother Frank Patchet, Jr. Funeral services private. The family requests contributions in her memory be made to the American Lung Association, 1740 B'way, NY NY 10019 or the charity of your choice.
