
        LEWIS-Julianne Oppenheimer, September 21, at 80. A tireless community leader in Garden City, NY. Past president of the school and library boards. A 1942 Skidmore College graduate and a WAVE in W.W. II. Loving wife of Lawrence (1908-1982), she leaves Lawrence Jr., Robert, Geoffrey and James and grandchildren Robby, Cathy, Evan, Eleanor and Jon. Service: September 25, 10AM, Garden City Community Church. Memorial donations welcome at Friends of The Garden City Public Library.
