
        UNGAR--Manya, of Scotch Plains, NJ, former President of the National PTA. Wife of H.F. ''Skip'' Ungar. Mother of Paul and Michael. A memorial service will be held at Memorial Funeral Home, 155 South Ave, Fanwood, NJ, on Monday, July 14, at 2 PM. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society and the National PTA. To honor her memory, do as she did: volunteer and be active in your local schools, PTA and community. >>AD#
