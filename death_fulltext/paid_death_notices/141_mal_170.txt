
        SAPHIR-Robert. The Executive Board, Board of Governors and Members of The New York State Camp Directors Association mourn the death of their esteemed past President and founding father. Bob served as President NYSCDA for over 20 years and will always be remembered for his efforts and energy on our behalf. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Shirley, children Marc, Marge and Bob and grandchildren Douglas and Kate. David Fleischner, President
