
        WEINER-Leslie E., 83. Died July 10 at Jewish Home & Hospital in the Bronx. Beloved father of Joseph and Elizabeth, brother of Rosalind Brackman, loving grandfather of Sofia & Gemma, widower of Adeline Weiner. Longtime resident of Roosevelt, New Jersey, playwright, insurance agent. Funeral will be Tuesday, July 13th, Roosevelt Cemetery, 12:00 noon. Contributions in his memory may be made to Roosevelt Arts Project, Roosevelt, NJ, 08555.
