
        YOUNG-Raymond Oliphant. On July 24, 2001. Retired V.P. of Citibank and retired manager and agent of Banco Rio de la Plata. Fighter pilot in the R.A.F. during World War II. Predeceased by his loving wife Mary Angela. Devoted father of Patricia and Michael Gallo, Theresa and Serre Murphy and Steven. Cherished grandfather of Jennifer, Gina, Michael, Brendan and Annabeth. Loving brother of Juna Montgomery. Reposing at William E. Law Funeral Home, 1 Jerusalem Avenue, Massapequa, NY. Mass of Christian Burial at Maria Regina RC Church, Seaford, NY at 9:45AM on Saturday. Family will receive friends Friday 2-5 and 7-9:30PM. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the American Lung Association.
