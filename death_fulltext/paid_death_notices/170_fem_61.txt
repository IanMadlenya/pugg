
        BARTOW-Elizabeth Vaux, 85, of Boca Grande, Florida, died Tuesday, March 6, 2001 at Bon Secours Venice Hospital. She was born April 23, 1915 in Philadelphia, PA and came to Boca Grande, FL 25 years ago from Tuxedo Park, NY. She was a member of St. Andrews Episcopal Church in Boca Grande. Survivors include 3 daughters; Josephine Staunton of New Canaan, CT, Beulah Durfee of Bedford, NY, Jeannie Fenton of Chadds Ford, PA and a son; Clarence N. Bartow, Jr. of Garrison, NY, a sister; Emily Large of Philadelphia, PA. Also surviving are 13 grandchildren and 7 great grandchildren. A memorial Service will be held 11:30 A.M. on Saturday, March 10, 2001 at St. Andrews Episcopal Church in Boca Grande. Memorials may be directed to: American Farm School, 1133 Broadway, New York, NY 10010. For information Farley Funeral Home Venice Chapel.
