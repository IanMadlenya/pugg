
        SCHWARTZ--Max. On December 20, 2005 at the age of 96. Beloved husband of the late May Schwartz. Loving father of Daniel, Harriet and Norma. Cherished grandfather of Allison and (David), Lisa and (Frank) David & (Laura) and Stephanie. Grandfather of seven great-grand children. Max graduated from Columbia University School of Pharmacy in 1931 and was active in his profession for 55 years. He was also the former president of 420 Riverside Drive Realty Corp. He was at the center of an extended family who cherished him until the end. Contributions may be made in his memory to the Dan Marino Foundation, PO Box 267640 Weston, FL. 33326.
