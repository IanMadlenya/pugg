
        KIMBALL - Richard A. The Trustees, Fellows, Residents and staff of the American Academy in Rome mourn the death of Richard A. Kimball, esteemed Director, 1959-65. He and his late wife Josephine Dodge Kimball brought warm humanity, wisdom and buoyant spirit to the Academy community and made an invaluable contribution by founding the Friends of the Library. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family. Michael I. Sovern, Chairman, Board of Trustees Andrew Heiskell, Chairman, Executive Committee Adele Chatfield-Taylor, Pres.
