
        COHEN-Harold L. On April 30, 2001, at age 84. Beloved husband of Gertrude. Loving father of Gary, Joseph and Martin, and fatherin-law of Naomi & Leah. Devoted brother of Lilyan Lippan. Cherished grandfather of Jonathan, Jason, Jeremy, Meir, Michelle and Lisa. His strength of character, humility, good humor and loyal friendship marked his noble and beautiful life and will be sorely missed by family & friends. Shiva is being observed at the home of the deceased until Monday.
