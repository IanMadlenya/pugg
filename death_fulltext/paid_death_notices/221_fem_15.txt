
        WARREN -- Lucy Sinclaire, died at home on May 31 at age 93. Wife of the late Dr. Robert F. Warren of Brooklyn. Mother of Fiske Warren of Manhattan & Cicily Hajek of Sharon, CT. Grandmother of Schenley, Matthew, Luke, Chris, Mathilde and Justin and great-grandmother of three. She loved her family and her homes and friends in Brooklyn and in Northville, New York. She will be missed by all of us. Funeral will be in Northville.
