
        SEIDENSTEIN-Donna. The staff of the Kings County District Attorney's office mourns with deep sorrow the untimely passing of Donna Seidenstein. Her boundless energy, zest for life and compassion will be missed by all who came in contact with her, especially those victims of Domestic Violence for whom she worked so tirelessly. Her colleagues and friends send their heartfelt condolences to her husband and best friend Lt. William Seidenstein, their children and her entire family. She will be truly missed. Charles J. Hynes, District Attorney & staff of the Kings County District Attorney's Office
