
        RAYMER-Melvina S., 80, longtime former resident of New York City, died of cancer on December 24, 1998 at Hospice House, Barnstable, Mass. Widow of Paul H. Raymer. Survived by her children, Paul H. Raymer, Falmouth, Mass. and Victoria E. Raymer, MiltonErnest, Bedfordshire, England and three grandchildren. Private family service. Memorial contributions to Hospice of Cape Cod, 923 Route 6A, Yarmouthport, Mass 02675, will be appreciated.
