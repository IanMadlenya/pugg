
        AMOS -- Rev. James G. passed away on Saturday, November 12, 2005 at The Aultman Woodlawn Nursing Home in Canton, OH. He was 82. He was the beloved Pastor of The Gustavus Adolphus Lutheran Church for the last 421/2 years in Gramercy Park, in New York City. He also served as Pastor for The Our Savior Lutheran Church in Toledo, OH, and The Frederick Lutheran Church in St. Thomas. Pastor Amos was born in Massillon, OH on July 23, 1923. He was the son of the late Philip and Lily Graham Amos. In addition to his parents, he was preceded by his wife, the former Joan Demetral and two brothers, Wilson and Maurice Amos. He was the loving godfather of Mindy and Bradford Chin. Surviving are one sister and brother-in-law, Gloria and Richard Buxser, of Canton, OH, two sisters-in-law, Elaine Amos, of Brewster, OH, and Delores Amos, of Mississippi, ten nieces and nephews, and numerous great nieces and nephews. Viewing will be at Gustavus Adolphus Lutheran Church, 155 East 22nd Street, at Third Avenue in Manhattan on Friday, November 18th, 2005 between the hours of 2 to 4 P.M. and 6 to 9 P.M. Viewing on Saturday, November 19, 2005 from 5-7 P.M. with Memorial Services following at 7 P.M. On Tuesday November 22, 2005, there will be a Memorial Service at The First Evangelical Lutheran Church, in Beach City, OH at 11 A.M. Interment will be at South Lawn Cemetery in Beach City, OH following the service. Contributions in his memory may be made to: The Pastor James and Joan Amos Scholarship Fund at Gustavus Adolphus Lutheran Church, 155 East 22nd Street, New York, New York 10010, 212-674-0739.
