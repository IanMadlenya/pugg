
        MCSHERRY-Ursula Mary. Beloved wife of Richard. Loving mother of Jeanne-Marie and Richard. Loving sister of Julie Coletto of Boca Raton, Florida, Evelyn Larkin of Dumfries, Virginia and Thomas Healy of New Hyde Park, NY. Ursula's kindness touched numerous lives. She was a devoted volunteer. Her efforts included work for Hospice Care of Long Island and St. Francis Hospital. A Funeral Mass will be held at 9:45 A.M., at St. Mary's RC Church, Manhasset. Donations in lieu of flowers can be sent to: Hospice Care Network, 900 Merchants Concourse, Westbury, N.Y. 11590.
