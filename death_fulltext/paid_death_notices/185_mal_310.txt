
        KULLMANN-Eugen, 87. Died on June 24th, 2002 in Mount Vernon, Ohio. He was preceded in death by his father Moritz and mother Fanny (Siegel, and sister Anneliese Kaboolian. Beloved teacher, he is deeply mourned by his former students from Kenyon College as well as those who studied with him at Bard College and The New School for Social Research. Burial will take place on June 27 at 11:00 A.M. in Beth El Cemetery, Washington Township, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made to the Knox County Humane Society, 400 Columbus Road, Mount Vernon, Ohio 43050.
