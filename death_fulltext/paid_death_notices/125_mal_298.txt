
        WACHTEL-Arthur, died on June 26, 1997 on the 65th anniversary of his marriage to his beloved wife Elizabeth. Retired civil court and acting supreme court judge. Devoted father of Dr. Daniel Wachtel and daughter-in-law Paula, cherished grandfather of Devorah Vineburg and Dr. Marian Wachtel. Loving great grandfather of Michael, Benjamin and Isaac Vineburg. Services will be officiated by Rabbi Sidney Vineburg on Sunday June 29, 12PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
