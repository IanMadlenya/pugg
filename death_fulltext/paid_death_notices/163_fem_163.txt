
        MURPHY-Catherine M. (Heffernan). 94, formerly of NYC, died on August 17, 2000, at the Jersey Shore Medical Center, Neptune, NJ. Predeceased by her husband Edward F. in 1952, and by her sister Marion T. Healey in 1997. Surviving is her niece Marion K. Healey Reilly and her husband Richard C. Reilly of Sea Girt, NJ. She also leaves great-nieces and nephews and many great-great nieces and nephews. Visiting will be on Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at the O'Brien Funeral Home, Hwy 35 at New Bedford Rd, Wall, NJ. A Mass of Christian Burial will be offered on Tuesday, 9:30 AM, at St. Mark's RC Church, Sea Girt, NJ. Entombment will follow at St. Catharine's Mausoleum, Sea Girt, NJ.
