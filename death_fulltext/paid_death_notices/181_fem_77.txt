
        PUPKO - KRINSKY - Rokhl. The YIVO Institute for Jewish Research mourns the passing of Rokhl Pupko-Krinsky, woman of valor, one of the Vilna Ghetto ''Paper Brigade'' at the YIVO who saved Jewish books and papers. She was a teacher, survivor, exemplar of Yidishkayt. She will be sorely missed. May her memory be for a blessing. Bruce Slovin, Chairman Dr. Carl J. Rheins, Exec. Dir.
