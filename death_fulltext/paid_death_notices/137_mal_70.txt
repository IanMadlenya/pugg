
        JOHNSTON-William O. Of Henderson, KY. Died in New York City on June 4, 1998. A devout Episcopalian and founder and President of the Episcopal Churchpeople for a Free Southern Africa. He devoted his life to the struggle for freedom in Africa and to racial and social justice for all people. He will be sorely missed. A funeral service will be held at Fernwood Cemetery in Henderson on Thursday, June 11. New York memorial service to be announced. Contributions to Greenpeace in lieu of flowers.
