
        CALLAHAN-Helen Mary. Passed away on Wednesday, August 30, 2000. She was born on February 1, 1920 to Matthew and Helen Skelly, and was married to the late Daniel J. Callahan for over 50 years. Mrs. Callahan is survived by her daughters Geraldine Greene and Jacqueline Callahan-Stewart, and her son, Daniel M. Callahan. She leaves behind three loving grandchildren Deirdre Callahan, Sean Greene and Christopher Stewart, and will be deeply missed by her brother Joseph Skelly. A life long New York City resident, Mrs. Callahan was a new York City school teacher for 25 years. In retirement, she has been active in various church and philanthropic causes, and truly enjoyed her extensive volunteer work with The Ronald McDonald House. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, NY, NY on Thursday, August 31 from 2-5 & 79PM. Mass of Christian Burial to be held at St. John the Martyr Church, 250 East 72 Street at 2nd Avenue, Friday 10AM. Interment to follow at Gate of Heaven Cemetery in Valhalla, New York.
