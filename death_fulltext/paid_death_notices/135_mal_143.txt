
        RIGGIO-Christopher Michael. Son of Drs. Maureen A. Relland and Robert R. Riggio, died at the age of 27 on April 12th, 1998, from complications related to the therapy of Non-Hodgkin's Lymphoma. He was an alumnus of the Fleming School, Horace Mann H.S. and Skidmore College. A private funeral service has been arranged. In lieu of flowers, a contribution to The Rogosin Institute, 505 E. 70 St, NYC 10021, would be appreciated.
