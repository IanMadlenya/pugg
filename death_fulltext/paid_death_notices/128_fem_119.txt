
        PITMAN-Marian Coggeshall, July 12, 1905-August 25, 1997. Survived by husband, Lorraine Farquhar Pitman of Bloomfield, Conn.; daughter, Sandra Janie Pitman of El Cajon, CA; brother, Dr. Bayard Coggeshall and wife Mary Achilles of Basking Ridge, NJ. Amongst many volunteer activities she was Connecticut Co-Chairman for fund raising to build Lincoln Center. A private burial took place on August 28, 1997 in Simsbury, CT.
