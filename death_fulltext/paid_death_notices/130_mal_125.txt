
        EDER-Barbara. The Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University sadly notes the passing of Barbara Eder, wife of Dr. Howard A. Eder, professor emeritus of medicine who has been one of our medical school's leading lights for over 40 years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to him, to his daughter and son-in-law Drs. Susan Eder and Jonathan Flescher, both Einstein alumni, class of '83, and to the entire family. Dominick P. Purpura, M.D. Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
