
        HALL-Sr. Thelma M. of the Sisters of the Cenacle, suddenly on October 21, 2002 in Southampton, NY. Sr. was a member of the Sisters of the Cenacle for 53 years. She is survived by her niece Jeanne Flight of Annapolis, MD, nephews Keith R. Hall of Virginia and David Hall of New York. Memorial Visitation Thursday 2-9 PM at the Cenacle in Ronkonkoma, NY. Memorial Mass Friday 11 AM at the Cenacle Convent, Ronkonkoma, NY. Burial of ashes to follow in the Cenacle Cemetery.
