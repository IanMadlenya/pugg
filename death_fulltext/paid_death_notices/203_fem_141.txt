
        SWEET--Nancy Lees, 86, formerly of Pelham, died Wednesday, November 19, 2003. Devoted wife of the late Dr. A.Y. Sweet. She is survived by her daughter, the Honorable Deborah Sweet Eyler of Annapolis, MD.; her daughter Elizabeth Sweet Czernek of Briarcliff Manor, NY; her grandsons Joshua and Jason Czernek and Andrew Quinn. A memorial service will be held December 27 at 12:30 in the chapel at the Osborn in Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Red Cross, 431 18th St. N.W., Washington, D.C. 20006, would be preferred.
