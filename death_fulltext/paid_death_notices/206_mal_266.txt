
        PISTACCHIO -- Clement G., J.D. On March 19. Beloved husband of Joan Garvin. Loving brother-in-law of Steven and Mary Taube, Richard and Ann Monday, and Eileen Garvin. Wonderful uncle to Fred and Tracy Stout and Andrew, Matthew and James Taube; great-uncle of Anna Kay Stout. Clem was an attorney in general practice. Born in Brooklyn in 1950, he was a lifelong New Yorker. He graduated from St. John's University and Fordham Law School. He was a member of the New York State Bar Association and was actively involved in Metropolitan Maltese Rescue, for which he also served as legal counsel. Clem was known for exceptional friendship and generosity. Family will receive friends on Tuesday, March 23 from 3-5 PM and 7-9 PM at Horne - Dannecker - O'Connor Funeral Home, 445 W. 43 St., NYC. Funeral Mass Wednesday, March 24, 10 AM at Guardian Angel Church, 10th Ave. at 21 St., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Jacob Perlow Hospice, Metropolitan Maltese Rescue or Guardian Angel Church.
