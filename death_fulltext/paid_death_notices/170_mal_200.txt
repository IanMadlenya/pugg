
        McCUTCHEN-Richard J. ''Dick'' of Clinton, CT. At age 76, of 24 Partridge Lane died March 3, 2001. He was the husband of Gerda Mahncke McCutchen. Known professionally as ''Dick McCutchen'' he was a writer/producer of television news and award winning documentaries, including an Emmy nomination in 1967. He worked at all major television networks and also produced films for the March of Dimes and the Save the Children Foundation. He retired in 1991, moved to Clinton, Connecticut with his wife of 50 years. He was born in Tarrytown, New York, March 1, 1925, a son of the late Clarence and Lillian E. Johnson McCutchen. He leaves in addition to his wife a daughter Mrs. Matthew J. (Kim C.) Haudenschield of New York, a brother John McCutchen of Milford, CT. and a granddaughter Julia T. Haudenschield. He was predeceased by a daughter Susan Amy McCutchen. A memorial service will be held Saturday, March 31, 2001, 11 AM at the United Methodist Church in Mamaroneck, New York. For information contact Oak Grove Cremation Service, West Haven, CT.
