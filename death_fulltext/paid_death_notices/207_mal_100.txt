
        SISK--Thomas Robert, of Avon By the Sea, NJ and Naples, FL died April 5, 2004 in Naples, FL. He was the husband of Tarsilla Musella Sisk. Mr. Sisk was born in New Haven, CT, son of the late Edward Joseph and Alice Carroll Sisk. A veteran of the Korean War, serving with the U.S. Army, he retired from the financial printing business in Manhattan, NY. In addition to his wife of 42 years, he is survived by two sons, Dr. Alfred Mitchell of New Milford, CT and Edward J. Sisk of New York City; a daughter, Alicia Matossian of New York City; three brothers; three sisters; and eight grandchildren. A Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated on Tuesday at 11AM at St. Francis Xavier Church, New Milford, CT. Burial at St. Francis Cemetery, New Milford, CT. Friends may call at the Lillis Funeral Home, 58 Bridge Street, New Milford on Monday from 1-3 P.M. and 5-8P.M.
