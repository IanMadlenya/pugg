
        EBERSMAN-Selma. Of New York and Bristol, RI, died October 29 in Baltimore at the age of 90. She was the daughter of the late Edward and Frances Rosenthal of New York. A retired librarian at the Rhode Island School of Design, she is survived by her son, Neil Hertz, her grandchildren, Ellen and Tom Hertz, and her greatgrandchildren, Anouk Werro, Julien Werro and Benjamin Browning. A memorial service will be held in Baltimore on Sunday, November 19.
