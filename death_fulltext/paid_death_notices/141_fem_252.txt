
        LAWRENCE - Barbara Childs. Died peacefully at her home, South Pole Farm in Norfolk, Connecticut on October 23, 1998 at the age of 86. She was the widow of James F. Lawrence and was predeceased by two children, Peter F. Lawrence & Jane Lawrence Mali. She is survived by four children: Barbara L. Garside, J. Vinton Lawrence, Suzannah L. Wood all of Norfolk; and Starling R. Lawrence of New York City and 13 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at The Church of Christ Congregational in Norfolk at noon Saturday, November 21. Please omit flowers., Contributions in her memory may be made to the Norfolk library.
