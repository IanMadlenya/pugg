
        SOKOLOW-Anna. Died at home in New York City on March 29th. at the age of 90. A service will be held for this choreographer, dancer, and teacher, at Redden's Funeral Home, 325 W. 14 St. NYC, Sunday, April 2, at 11 AM. A memorial service will be arranged in the future. She is survived by a nephew and niece. Contributions to her memory and the perpetuation of her work can be given to Players Project Ltd., 543 Broadway, 9th floor, N.Y., N.Y. 10012.
