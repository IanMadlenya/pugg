
        DENARO-R. John. May 11, 2001, of heart failure. Age 56. Beloved son of Mary Denaro and partner of David Cawley of Brooklyn. Organist and Choir Director, St. Saviour's RC Church, Brooklyn. Director Park Slope Singers and member Stonewall Chorale. He is greatly missed by friends, family and the many Brooklyn communities he has served.
