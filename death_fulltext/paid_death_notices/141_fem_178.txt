
        BRAND-Edith. Born in Monroe Michigan in 1892 and lived in New York City most of her life, passed away on October 20, 1998. She was the great great niece of Judah P. Benjamin, Secretary of State of the Confederacy. She is survived by daughter Barbara Brand, granddaughter Jill Gandolfi, and three great granddaughters Anandi, Liana and Jessi. Services will be private.
