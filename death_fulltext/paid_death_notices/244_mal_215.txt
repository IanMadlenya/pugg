
        MERRITT--John C., of Bedford, NY, on May 22nd, 2007, at age 91. Real Estate developer. He is survived by his wife, Natalie Hussar Merritt and his two children, John Jr. and Susan Coates, his four grandchildren, Kevin, Cameron, Allyson, and Chelsea, his sister Marjorie, and numerous nieces and nephews. His extended family includes his wife's children, Jane, Andree and John, grandchildren, James, Margaret, Patrick, Sally, Peri, Andrew, Reed, and Caroline. He was predeceased by his first wife Dorrit and Natalie's children Susan and Nancy. John Merritt was a true gentlemen, generous and gracious and will be greatly missed by his family, friends and all those who dealt with him in his long business life. Viewing at 7-9pm Thursday, May 24th, at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY. Funeral at 3pm Friday, May 25th at the North Greenwich Congregational Church (Church of the Advent), Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Church of the Advent, 4 Connecticut Ave, Greenwich, CT 07830, or to the National Parkinson's Foundation Inc., 1509 North West 9th Avenue/Bob Hope Road, Miami, FL 33136-1494.
