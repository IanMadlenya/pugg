
        SLATTERY-Louis R., M.D. October 16, 1908-March 19, 2001. Loving father and devoted husband, in his 93rd year, at home in Manhattan, after a short illness. Survived by his children and grandchildren. Flushing High School '25, Columbia College '29, Columbia P&S '33. Veteran of the South Pacific campaigns of WWII, where he met and married Lela Hendry, his wife of 53 years, who died in 1997. Professor of Clinical Surgery at NYU School of Medicine, gifted surgeon, proudly affiliated with NYU for 60 years. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the S. Arthur Localio Surgical Research Laboratory, Department of Surgery, NYU School of Medicine, 550 First Avenue, NY, NY 10016. A memorial service will be held in Farkas Auditorium, NYU School of Medicine, Tuesday, April 17th, 4PM. His friends, former patients and students, and colleagues may contact his son John at 12 East 97th Street, NY, NY 10029.
