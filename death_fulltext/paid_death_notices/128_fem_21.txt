
        HACKER-Lois Wetherell, on September 2, at the age of 67, in her home in Manhattan, of a brain tumor and associated complications. At the time of her death, she had been married to Andrew Hacker for 42 years, and is also survived by her daughter, Ann Gower of Harwich, Ma. At her express request, there will be no funeral or memorial service. Friends or relatives wishing to make a tangible tribute in her memory may send a contribution to the Mina Rees Library Matching Fund, c/o Susan Newman, CUNY Graduate School, 33 W. 42 St, New York, NY 10036.
