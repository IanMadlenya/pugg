
        DEANE--Thomas J., born July 23, 1924 in Southampton, NY, died at home in Essex, CT on February 23, 2004. He was a son of the late Mary McDonnell and Francis J. Deane. He served in the Navy in the Pacific during WWII and was a graduate of New York University. He spent his career in contract negotiations, retiring from Avis Renta-Car in 1992. He is survived by his children, Michael, Susan, Eric, Liz, Sarah and Marcy; daughters-in-law, Monica and Andrea; and son-in-law, Manuel Jimenez, and five grandchildren and his beloved companion, Barbara S. Crawford. He was pre-deceased by his wife of 43 years, Barbara Taylor Deane. A memorial service will be held at Essex Meadows in Essex, CT on Saturday, March 6, 2004 at 2PM. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Connecticut Hospice, 100 Double Beach Road, Branford, CT. 06405, are requested.
