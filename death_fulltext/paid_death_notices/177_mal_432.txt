
        EMMET-Winthrop S., 91. October 26, 2001 in West Stockbridge, MA. He was a graduate of Groton, Harvard College (1934), and Harvard Law School. He was a World War II Navy officer awarded the French Croix de Guerre and helped try Nazi war criminals at Nuremberg in 1945. He was later a New York lawyer and trust officer for National City Bank. He is survived by his wife of 25 years, Mary-Jane Emmet; a daughter, Daphne Emmet Hallowell; three stepchildren, Francis Criss Laidlaw, William Laidlaw, and Christian (Annie) Selke; a grandson Christopher Emmet Hallowell; and four step-grandchildren. Memorial service 11 A.M. November 2, 2001, Trinity Church, Lenox, MA. Burial private. In lieu of flowers, send contributions to Lenox Library, Lenox, MA. 01240, or Hospice Care In The Berkshires, 369 S. Street, Pittsfield, MA. 01201.
