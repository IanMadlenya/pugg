
        WELDEN-Richard C. Died on June 29, 1998. Husband of Mary T. Welden. Father of Thorn, Wickford and Trafford. Brother of Robert E. Welden. Born May 6, 1924 in Hartford, CT. Graduate of Loomis, 1942, Yale University, 1948 and Harvard Law School, 1951. U.S. Army veteran of World War II serving in India and China. Partner with law firm of Decker, Hubbard, Welden and Sweeney, NYC. A gentle man, he was an avid bird watcher & nature lover. Modest and loyal, he was always ready to assist friends and family. He loved hiking, tennis, Central Park and the beauty of the English language. Always on the go, full of energy and determination, he was a man of great integrity. He was always cheerful. We will miss him greatly. Memorial service on Tuesday, July 14, 1998, 11 A.M. at the Brick Presbyterian Church, Park Ave. at 91 Street. Donations in his memory may be made to the National Audubon Society or the Central Park Conservancy.
