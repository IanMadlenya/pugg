
        VERN-Harriet, 81. Died peacefully in Orlando, FL., after a long illness on February 26, 1999. Predeceased by her husband, magazine photographer, Ike Vern, on February 23rd, 1987, and her son, Andrew Vern, December 17, 1977. She was a passionate feminist, fighter for human rights, innovator and teacher in the field of psychiatric social work. She is survived by her loving daughter, Jane Vern, Box 602, Kent, CT. 06757, and her halfbrother Bruce Weber of Silver Springs, Maryland.
