
        PIERREPONT--Nancy Weller. Died peacefully at home on August 13 after a brief illness. She leaves three children, Nancy Hoppin of NYC, Peggy Pierrepont of Natchez and Jay Pierrepont of San Francisco, three grandchildren and many devoted friends. She was a talented interior decorator who created rooms of distinction, beauty and warmth. She touched many lives and will be deeply missed. A memorial service will be held at St. James Episcopal Church in NYC in early October. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Gregory Long, NY Botanical Garden, 200th St. and Southern Blvd., Bronx, NY 10458 or to The Neighborhood House, Northeast Harbor, ME 04662.
