
        SHAW - KENNEDY -- Bart J. Kane Lodge No. 454 F.& A.M. mourns the loss with great sorrow of our esteemed brother on December 6 at his home in Charlottesville, VA. He was a Life Member for 55 years who loved to return to NYC and Kane Lodge frequently. He shall be missed and remembered. A memorial service is scheduled at 1:30PM on Wednesday, December 21, 2005, at Grace Episcopal Church in Keswick, VA. Contributions may be made to the SPCA of Charlottesville, PO Box 7047, Charlottesville, VA 22906. C. Christian Winslow, Master Paul D. Reitz, Secretary
