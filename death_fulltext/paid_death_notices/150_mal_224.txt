
        CASPERS-Paul Eric. The Officers, Board of Governors and the entire membership of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of their dear friend and devoted General Manager of the last quarter of this century, Eric Caspers. Eric's style, elegance, grace and sensitivity will be forever remembered by all who had the rewarding experience of knowing him. We send our heartfelt condolences to his cherished wife, Alethea, his devoted daughter, Nicole, and adored granddaughter, Caroline. We hope that they will always remember the love and affection that all of Beach Point had for him. Paul N. Gardner President, Beach Point Club
