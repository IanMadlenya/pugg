
        BRAUER-Paul H., M.D. On December 19, 1999, at the age of 87, from complications following heart surgery. He leaves his wife Arlette, his son Alan, his daughter Janet Weinberger, his grandchildren Eric and Mark Weinberger, his daughter-in-law Donna, and his son-in-law Steven Weinberger. He will be sorely missed by his family, his friends and his patients. A memorial gathering will be held on January 16 at 3PM at the Cosmopolitan Club, 122 E. 66 St., NYC.
