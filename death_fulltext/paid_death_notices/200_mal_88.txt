
        ABELES--Dr. Hans. Passed away September 6, 2003, at the age of 90. He was the devoted husband of Ilse (nee Echter) and loving father of Naomi Kraft and Terry Abeles. Beloved grandfather of Marissa and Monika Kraft. He is also survived by his brother Alfred Abeles. Dr. Abeles was a pulmonary physician with the Albert Einstein Medical Center for many years. He was the past Commissioner of Health for the Department of Corrections with the New York City Health Department. A memorial service was held on Sunday. For information contact George T. Davis Funeral Home, 914-632-0324.
