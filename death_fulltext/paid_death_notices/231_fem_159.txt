
        KORMOS--Danielle Tabone, of Berkeley, CA, on April 10th, 2006. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Harry R. Kormos, her sons, Remy and Cyril Kormos, four granddaughters, and her mother, brothers, sisters in France. As a mother, a friend, and a professor at the University of California at Berkeley, she dedicated her life to helping and guiding others. Her generosity, insightful thinking, and incredible strength were an inspiration to many around the world, as was the enormous courage and dignity with which she fought cancer. She will be dearly missed.
