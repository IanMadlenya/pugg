
        PERLICH-Toby G. On May 30, 2002, at the age of 91. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Myron Perlich. Superlative mother of daughter Kay Brover and husband Arthur Bennett of NYC, and son Martin Perlich of Los Angeles. Baby sister of Rabbi Albert S. Goldstein of Brookline, humorous and elegant grandma of Alice Andrews, Max Perlich, Miles Perlich and Neil Porter, and loving great grandma of Sophie, Lliana and Maya. Toby was our mother goddess, our soulful word crafter, and our funny bone. Service Friday June 14, 2002, 1 PM at ''the Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory to Hadassah.
