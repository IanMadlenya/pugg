
        DOBSEVAGE--Alvin Philip, 82, on February 26th, 2005. A WWII 10th Mountain Division veteran, a linguist, Latin scholar and educator, he is mourned by Ruth, Michael and Sarah Dobsevage; Gertrude, Irving and Robert Diskin; Beth Dobsevage; Dr. Tina Dobsevage, Dr. Jonathan House, and Antonia and Gregory House. A memorial gathering will be held at the family home in Bethel, CT, on Sunday, March 13th, 1-5PM.
