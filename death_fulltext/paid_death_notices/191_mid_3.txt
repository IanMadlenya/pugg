
        BORDEN-Elizabeth (McGinley). Of Cambridge formerly of Milton and Concord, November 19, 2002. Beloved mother of Jane Chermayeff of NM, Elizabeth ''Betsy'' Carlson and her husband Robert of CA, and Dr. Spencer Borden IV and his wife Elizabeth Clemens of Concord. Grandmother of Jack Batchelder, Ben Batchelder, Nicholas Chermayeff, Christopher Badger, Lisa Badger, Jennifer Mikell, Richard Borden III, Rebecca Borden, Sarah Borden, and Andrew Borden. Also survived by eight great grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. A graveside service will be held on Saturday December 14, 2002 at 11 AM in Mt. Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge. Relatives and friends are respectfully invited to attend. Late member of the board of trustees for Milton Academy, President of the Alumnae Association, Member of the Finance Committee, and a volunteer for the VNA. Memorial donations in Mrs. Borden's memory may be made to Radcliffe College, 10 Garden St., Cambridge, MA 02138. For information J.S.
