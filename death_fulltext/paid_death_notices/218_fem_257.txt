
        WASSER--Sylvia. On March 30, 2005 at age 81. Beloved wife for 56 years of Adolph. Loving mother of Steven (Stephanie) and Daniel (Marcia). Cherished grandmother of Jacqueline, Merrill, Maddie, Caryn, and Melissa. A spirited woman guided by Jewish ethics with a love of learning and intellectual curiosity, her determination and remarkable ability to rise above adversity were an inspiration to all who knew her. Services Thursday, March 31, 12:45PM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
