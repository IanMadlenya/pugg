
        SAMET-Gertrude L. On February 27, 2000. Age 82. Loving wife of the late L. Reyner Samet. Beloved mother of Howard and Stephen Conn. Dear sister of Doris Sostmann. Services Tuesday, Febrauary 29, 11am at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Avenue at 65 Street.
        SAMET-Gertrude L. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Gertrude L. Samet, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
