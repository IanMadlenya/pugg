
        DUBNER--Anita, died suddenly on February 3, 2004, at age 72. Wife of the late Sidney Dubner, mother of Paul and Margie (Mermelstein) and Sandy and Leslie (Scharoff). Grandmother of Seth, Mimi, Stephanie and Adam. Sister of Liela Fleischman. Her memory will live in the hearts of all who knew her. Funeral 10:00 A.M. on Thursday, February 5, at Westchester Reformed Temple, Scarsdale, New York.
