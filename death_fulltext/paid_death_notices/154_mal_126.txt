
        WASSERSTEIN-Ian Jacob. On November 9, 1999 died at home. Lived life as fully as possible with optimism and enthusiasm. Died of cancer, a patient at Memorial Sloan Kettering-Cancer Center, NYC. which was part of his extened family. Son of Sara Frischer and Stephen Wasserstein. Brother of Robby. He opened our eyes to the things before us which we would not have noticed if he weren't there. In lieu of flowers, donations should be made to The Aubrey Fund for Pediatric Cancer Research, MSKCC, 633 3rd Ave, NY, NY 10017. The family will be sitting shiva at home for the week.
