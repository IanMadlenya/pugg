
        LEBOW-Ruth. The National Board of Directors and Membership of ARMDI-American Red Magen David for Israel record with profound sorrow the passing of Ruth Lebow, beloved wife of the late Sylvan Lebow and mother of Mark Lebow, ARMDI National President and Joy Cohen of Los Angeles. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Mark and his wife Patricia E. Harris and all the members of the bereaved family. May her memory be a blessing. Robert L. Sadoff, M.D. National Chairman Benjamin Saxe Executive Vice President
