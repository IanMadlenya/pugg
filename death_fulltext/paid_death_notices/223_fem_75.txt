
        GETZ GRINNELL -- Ileen, adored and adoring wife to Mark Grinnell, mother to Alice and Max, daughter of the late Lawrence and Esta Getz, sister to Jerry Getz and beloved friend to many, passed away from cancer on Thursday, August 4, 2005 at St. Vincent's Medical Center in NYC. Originally from Levittown, PA, Ileen was an actress and will be remembered for her many roles in television, stage and film. Ileen's greatest joys were her family, friends and her work. Funeral services were held on August 7, 2005, her 44th birthday. Memorial services to be held in NYC and Los Angeles will be announced at a later date.
