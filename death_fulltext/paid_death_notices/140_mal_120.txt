
        MORTIMER-John. The Community Service Society of New York mourns the untimely death of its former Trustee who served with distinction from 1983-88. Mr. Mortimer brought his expertise in labor relations to his work as Chair of the Personnel Committee and as a member of numerous committees including the Search Committee to select a CEO. His generosity of spirit and contributions will long be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. David R. Jones, Esq, Pres/CEO Stephen R. Aiello, Ph.D., Chair Community Service Society of NY
