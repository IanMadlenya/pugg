
        PERALTA-RAMOS--Inga Lucia. Passed away April 2, 2006. Married to Standard Oil heir Paul Peralta-Ramos in 1959. She was active as a Board member of the Children's East Harlem Tutorial Program, and the Millicent Rogers Museum in Taos, New Mexico. She also volunteered her time to the children's cancer ward of the MemorialSloan Kettering Hospital. A fashion model and a notable on New York's best dressed list in the 1970's. Mrs. PeraltaRamos died peacefully in her home in Huntington Beach, California. She is survived by her children Christina Lucia Luera, Philip Jaime PeraltaRamos, Isabella Millicent Kelsay, and her grandchildren Ethan and Sydney WatkinsKelsay.
