
        FRIEDMAN--Stanley, M.D. Passed away May 19, 2004, his 79th birthday. He leaves three loving children, Jennifer, Steven and William, two adoring grandsons, Silas and Samuel, devoted sister, Sybil and three loving children-inlaw, Jonathan, Val and Brooke. He was a WW II U.S. Maritime Service officer (1943-45). He was a Mt. Sinai Hospital faculty member for 38 years as a devoted physician, scientist and teacher. He was a tremendously caring and loyal friend whose enormous vitality, intelligence and wit will be missed. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society. Memorial service at the UN Tillman Chapel, First Ave & 44th St, on Wednesday, June 16, 3:30PM. Reception follows.
