
        BIRNBAUM-Henry. It is with sadness that Pace University mourns the loss of a friend and dedicated librarian, Mr. Henry Birnbaum. Serving as University Librarian from 1961 until his retirement in 1994, Mr. Birnbaum died on Tuesday, July 20th. He will be greatly missed. A memorial service for Mr. Birnbaum will be held on Monday, July 26, 1999 at 12:15 P.M., at the Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, (212) 362-6600. Dr. Patricia O'Donnell Ewers President, Pace University
