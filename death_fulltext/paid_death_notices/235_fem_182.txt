
        BORN--Eleanor L. 87, formerly of New York City and the beloved wife of the late Dr. John H. Born, passed away on August 23 in Delray Beach, FL. She is survived by her sons, John R. Born, Sr. of Cambridge, MA and Dr. Christopher T. Born of Providence, RI, seven grandchildren and five greatgrandchildren. In her memory, contributions can be made to the Hospice of Palm Beach County, Bethesda Inpatient Unit, 2815 South Seacrest Boulevard, Boynton Beach, FL, 33435, Attn: Mr. John Panico. For more information: Lorne & Sons Funeral Home, Delray Beach.
