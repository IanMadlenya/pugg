
        FRITZHAND -- Lillian. Died May 17, 2004 at the young age of 961/2. Devoted mother of the late Nicholas, she was also the much loved and loving mother of Martin and James; the adoring grandmother of Nicholas, Zachary and Jeremy; the favorite aunt of Amy, Lawrence and Yohko; great aunt of Ali and Gary, Noah and Adam; dear cousin of Judy and Leah, and devoted friend of Anne and Aileen. You were a bright light in our lives, and we will always miss your warmth and sense of humor, your keen mind and all-embracing heart.
