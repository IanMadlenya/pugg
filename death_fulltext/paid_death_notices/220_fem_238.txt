
        MICHAELS--Phoebe. After a long illness, Phoebe passed away quickly and peacefully on May 27, 2005, after joking and chatting with her beloved niece Carrie. Her warmth and humor stayed with her until the end. She touched the lives of all who knew her. She is survived by her sister Marilyn Beckmann, her niece Carrie Beckmann Brittenham, nephew-in-law Edward, and great-nephews Conrad and Michael Brittenham.
