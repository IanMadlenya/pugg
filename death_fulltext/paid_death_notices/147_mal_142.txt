
        GERSTENFELD-Saul. The National Jewish Outreach Program mourns the untimely passing on March 31st, 1999 of its special friend and generous supporter, Saul Wayne Gerstenfeld. A devoted Jew and loyal American, Saul dedicated his life to helping others and we will miss him greatly. A memorial service will take place at Cong. Machane Chodosh, 67-29 108th St., Forest Hills, Sunday, May 2, at 10 AM. Yehi Zichro Baruch. Rabbi Ephraim Z. Buchwald, Dir.
