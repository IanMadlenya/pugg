        GOLDMAN--Robert. The directors and shareholders of Biosytes USA mourn the passing of our esteemed president and founder. His life's work in pharmaceuticals resulted in innovative technology in controlled release medications. The 12hour cold capsule we now take for granted, we owe to Bob's inventive mind. Although few people around the world know his name, millions have benefited from his technology. We honor his scientific contributions and will never forget his loving kindness and generosity.
