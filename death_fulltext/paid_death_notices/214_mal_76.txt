
        RESNICK--Robert Lewis. On November 6. Last survivor of the flag raising at Iwo Jima. Man of courage, great intelligence, phenomenal wit. He is survived by his children Laura Resnick-Ostrer, Dana and Bryan Resnick; grandchildren Evan, Josh, Courtney and Zach, and new and childhood friends. Services Monday, 11:30 am, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY.
