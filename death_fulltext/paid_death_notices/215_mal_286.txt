
        WEINER -- Seymour (Sy). Born February 22, 1924 in the Bronx, N.Y. died Thursday, December 23, 2004 in Boca Raton, FL where he lived. He was married for 55 years to his loving wife Sara Sue Weiner. He is survived by Sara as well as his dear sister-in-law and brother-in-law and his nieces and nephews. He proudly served as Master of his Masonic Lodge in NY. He will be missed by his loving family and many friends.
