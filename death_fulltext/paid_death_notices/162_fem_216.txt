
        NIERENBERG-Joan Werblin. Age 80. On July 16, 2000 after a valiant battle with Alzheimer's. Former Life Magazine copy chief and SUNY Purchase Trustee. Born in Somerville, N.J., graduate of Mount Holyoke College, and longtime resident of Chappaqua, N.Y. Survived by her husband, David E. Nierenberg of Briarcliff Manor, N.Y., and sister Elinor Glass of Bedminster, N.J. She also leaves behind her loving children Wendy Gibney of Berkeley, Ca., David Nierenberg of Lebanon, N.H., Lorie Nierenberg of McLean, Va., and six grandchildren, of whom she was so proud. Memorial contributions may be made to the Alzheimer's Association-New York City Chapter, 360 Lexington Ave, Fifth Floor, New York, NY 10017.
