
        CAVANAGH-James M., Msgr. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of retired Pastor Emeritus of Our Lady of Angels Church, 7320 Fourth Ave., Brooklyn, on June 21. Reposing in McLoughlin Funeral Home, 9620 3rd Ave., Brooklyn, on Thursday, June 22, from 2 to 5 and 7 to 9 p.m., and Friday, June 23, from 2 to 5 p.m. Mass of Transferral on Friday, June 23, at 7:30 p.m. Funeral Mass on Saturday, June 24, at 10:30 a.m. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
