
        SAMII-Mohammad Reza, 42, architect. Beloved husband of Negar, and loving father of Guive, died in NY Hospital, August 26, 1998. Member of the American Institute of Architecture, his work in all three NY airports has been much admired. He was a loyal, sincere and extremely kind husband. He had the most beautiful relationship with his son, Guive, for he was the most caring and loving father. We thank all our friends and family whose help and support enabled us to pull through this extremely difficult time.
