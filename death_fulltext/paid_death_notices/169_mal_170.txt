
        LE BOUTILLIER-Martin, died Tuesday, February 13, 2001. He graduated Hotchkiss class of '40, and Yale '44. He then served in the South Pacific as a PT boat captain during W.W. II. Martin returned to NY and in a career in investment banking, ultimately serving as President and Chief Operating Officer of Paine Webber. Martin was an avid tennis player, fly fisherman, hunter and yachtsman. Throughout his life, Martin faced adversity with bravery and celebrated the joys of life with gusto. Survived by wife Janet, sister Cornelia, brother Charles, daughter Clelia, sons Martin and John, stepchildren Duncan and Sarah and nine grandchildren. Memorial service will be held at 11 o'clock AM, Saturday, February 17 at Grace Episcopal Church in Old Saybrook, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hotchkiss Scholarship Fund, POB 800, Lakeville, CT. 06039, or Memorial Fund, Grace Episcopal Church, 336 Main Street, Old Saybrook, CT 06475.
