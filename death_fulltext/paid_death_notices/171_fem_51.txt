
        COLWELL - Pamela Meredyth. Died peacefully on April 4, 2001 in Manchester, Massachusetts at the age of 94. She was predeceased by her husbands Lt. Gordon Calthrop Thorne, DSO of England and Kent Galbraith Colwell of Morristown, New Jersey. She is survived by a daughter, Jennifer C. Hayden and her Husband Malcolm of Harpswell, Maine and a son Frederick G.P. Thorne and his wife Susan of Manchester, Massachusetts. She is also survived by a stepdaughter Ann Catherine Menniger of Franconia, New Hampshire and a stepson David Galbraith Colwell and his wife Edna of Fallsbrook, Calfornia. She is also survived by her former daughter-in-law Phoebe T. Colwell of Nyack, New York. She leaves 14 grandchildren and 17 great-grandchildren. Pamela Colwell was born in London, England, the daughter of Grace Potter Grant and Captain Stuart Colquhoun Grant. Her first husband, Lt. Col. Gordon C. Thorne, was killed during World War II. In 19 she married Kent G. Colwell of Morristown, New Jersey. She was in active numerous community affairs including the Morristown Memorial Hospital, The Mount Kemble Home, The Episcopal Diocese of Newark, The Victoria Home and the Daughters of the British Empire. She was an honorary member of the Princeton University Class of 1917. She was a woman of conviction, courage and wit. A marvelous communicator, she skillfully held a large family together with love, warmth, and understanding. She was a woman deeply loved by her family and friends and will be missed. A memorial service will be held on April 17, 2001 at 11:00AM at Emmanuel Church, Manchester, Massachusetts. Interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be sent to the Victoria Home for Retired Men & Women, North Malcolm Street, Ossining, new York. 10562; Morristown Memorial Hospital, Madison Avenue, Morristown, New Jersey 07960; or the Quebec Labrador Foundation, Main Street, Ipswich, Massachusetts 01938.
