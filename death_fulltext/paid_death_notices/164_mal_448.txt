
        SHELLER-Robert G., Esq. Died peacefully on September 26, 2000 at the age of 93. Husband of the late Helen Reiser Sheller. Father of Elly Sheller Rufer and the late Ed Sheller. Grandfather of Alexandra and Christopher Sheller, Daniel and Kate Rufer. Brother of the late Frances Snyder and uncle of Joan Steiner and Martin Snyder. Father-in-law to John Rufer. Brother-in-law to the late Florence Reiser Shultz. Litigator extraordinaire! He was a force in the courtroom for over 60 years. Avid card player: from casino with his grandchildren, to weekly bridge games with the ladies. We will always miss his amazing intellect, his incredible wit and his pragmatic approach to life. As requested, no services will be held. Donations may be made in his memory to the Summit School, 187-30 Grand Central Parkway, Jamaica Estates, New York 11432.
