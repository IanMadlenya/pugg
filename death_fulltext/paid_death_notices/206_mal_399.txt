
        MIRON--Julie. Of New York, Boston and Palm Beach, at Miami Beach on March 28, 2004 after a brief illness. Founder and Chairman of Miron Building Products and its affiliated companies in New York and Florida. He was a visionary in pioneering a full service source from the foundation to the roof for residential contractors and, later, home owners. He changed the lives of thousands who worked with him and many more who lived in the homes he supplied and in the communities he helped build. Born in Brooklyn, NY, son of Abraham and Pauline Miron. He is survived by Ryna Greenbaum, his devoted and beloved companion of 26 years. Admired and loving father of Stephen and Stephanie of Greenwich, CT and Kenneth and Andrea of Poughkeepsie, NY. Grandfather of Alexis, and Anastasia, Alexandra, Tatyana and Nicholas; Russel and Randy, Monique and Jonathan Cayle, Gregg. Great-grandfather of Andrew Coleman and Leigh Anne Miron. Dear brother of the late Mae Miron Held; Michael and the late Ruth Miron; Herbert and Marcy. Brother-in-law of the late Marvin and Bess Tannenbaum; Harold and the late Frances Jacobson; Jess and Eugenia Frankel. Predeceased by devoted wife and mother, Ann Eagle, who forever lives in the hearts of her children, grandchildren, sister, nieces and nephews. Funeral services to be held Wednesday, March 31 at 11 AM at Temple Beth El, 2815 N. Flagler Drive, West Palm Beach, FL. Interment and graveside services to be held Friday, April 2, 1:00 PM at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, 13004 Horace Harding Expressway, Flushing, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made in Julie Miron's memory to the American Jewish Committee; Dartmouth College; Benedectine Hospital (Kingston, NY; Brigham & Womens Hospital, Boston), or a charity of your choice.
