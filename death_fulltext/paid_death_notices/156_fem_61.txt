
        BREINDEL-Blanche. On January 5, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Samuel Breindel. Died after a brief illness in her 93rd year. We remember her as a cherished, devoted, noble, gentle woman. She was an active Life Member of Amit Women-American Mizrachi. She was devoted to her children: Arthur (Elayne), Annette (the late Benjamin), Phyllis (Jack), Geraldine (Bob) and her grandchildren; Tina, Daniel, Remy, Andrew and Cybele and great-grandchild Jed. She was an Acheis Chail. Services today, January 7, 1:15 PM at Parkwest Riverside Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
