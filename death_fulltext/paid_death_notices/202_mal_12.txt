
        HALL--Bruce Edward. October 31 in Manhattan. Actor, writer, raconteur, Episcopalian. Beloved son of Jane Ann and Herbert of Vero Beach, FL, brother of Amy of Ossining, NY, Judy of Gloucester, MA, and Stephen of Voorhees, NJ. Leaves his dear Andy and countless friends. Service at St. Bartholomew's, Thursday, 4:30 PM. Donations to the Actors Fund, ''Aurora AIDS Initiative,'' 729 7th Ave, NYC, 10019.
