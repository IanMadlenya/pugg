
        SCOTT-John Francis (Jack). Born in NYC, September 4, 1919. Long time resident of Centerport, NY. Piloted thirty-five missions over Europe in WWII. Captain for Pan American for thirty-four years. Preceded in death by Susan Thomson Ferrier, his wife, of fiftyfive years and their daughter, Susan Helen Ivener. Survived by daughter, Barbara Scott Markay of Santa Monica, CA and son, Craig R. Scott of Trophy Club, TX. In Dallas, TX on January 22, 2001.
