
        JOHNSON--Sue Storer, 69, died on December 26, after an extended battle with Parkinson's disease. In her youth, Sue was a member of the U.S. swim team. An engineering graduate of Purdue, she served on the Knapp Commission and was an administrator for the New York State court system. She is survived by three daughters and six grandchildren. Donations to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation are encouraged.
