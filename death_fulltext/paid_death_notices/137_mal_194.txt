Kind and caring, a true gentleman, graced with rare integrity, quiet dignity, and a wonderful dry, quick wit. We will love him forever. Services ''Boulevard-Riverside'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, Long Island, at 11 A.M., on Friday.
