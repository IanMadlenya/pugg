
        RUBEN-Richards. A great artist, teacher, friend, raconteur, and lover of life, died in Venice, Italy, Tuesday, July 21. The artists, collectors, friends and employees of the Anita Shapolsky Gallery feel a great loss of a special person. His humanity touched everyone who came into contact with him. Our condolences to his wife Gail, his daughter and son-in-law Catlin and Richard Farrow, his son John Ruben, and all of the grandchildren. ''Goodbye, Oh Silent Smiler''. Anita Shapolsky
