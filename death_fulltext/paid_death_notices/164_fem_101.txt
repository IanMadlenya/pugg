
        KAY-Frances. Died Frida y, September 8, 2000, in Houston, Texas. She was born in Baltimore, Maryland. She was very active in New York civic affairs and worked many years with Henry M. Stevens in New York City. Mrs. Kay is survived by her daughter, Lani Laurents, four grandchildren, ten great-grandchildren, one greatgreat-grandchild, one niece and one grandniece. A private service will be held in Houston, Texas.
