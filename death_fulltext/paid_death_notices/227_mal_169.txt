
        LEVIDOW--Richard D., Esq. His 74-year path ran from Queens, NY, via Cornell to Yale Law, 1955. Widowed in 1988 after retiring from 30 years at his Manhattan firm, Richard followed his dear son, Bjorn, to Seattle, where he found his true calling as a public defender. He loved the Knicks, tennis, poker, Bjorling and life with his second wife, Mamie Rockafellar. Remembrances can be made to Sojourner Place, 5071 8th Av NE, Seattle 98105, or the ACLU.
