
        KANTOR-Sidney, on August 26, 1997. CEO of Sheridan Creations Inc., Simarka Productions Inc. and Silver Bullet Messenger Service. Beloved husband of Mary Yung. Loving father of Iris, Robert, Beth, Debbie and Howard. Adoring grandfather of Trpti, Bobby, Sydney, Nora, Ross, Perry and Charlotte. Dear brother of Helen and Rita. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Thursday 2-5 and 79PM with service Friday, 11:30AM. Interment to follow at Ferncliff, Ardsley, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation or the Brooklyn Academy of Music.
        KANTOR-Sidney. We are deeply saddened by the sudden loss of our Founding Director, a man gifted with uncommon creativity, wit and generosity. Together with his wife, Mary, he encouraged so many to support our research program at its onset and guided us to a successful collaboration in China. Our heartfelt condolences to Mary and the family. The Officers and Board of Directors of the Samuel Waxman Cancer Research Foundation Alan P. Safir, Chairman Samuel Waxman, M.D., Scientific Director
        KANTOR-Sidney. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Brooklyn Academy of Music mourn the sudden death of our esteemed longterm Board member and colleague, Sidney Kantor. Sidney was a bulwark of strength and support to BAM over many years. We will miss him greatly and are truly grateful for his friendship and his extraordinary spirit. Brooklyn Academy of Music Bruce Ratner, Chairman Harvey Lichtenstein, President
