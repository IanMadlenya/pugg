
        SAVITT-Cantor Marvin. The Old Westbury Hebrew Congregation notes with great sadness the passing of our beloved Cantor Emeritus Marvin Savitt, who served our congregation with distinction for 34 years. We extend condolences to his wife Harriet, daughters Ellen Friedman and Julie Savitt, son Joseph and their respective families. For Shiva information please call us at 516-333-7977. Rabbi Seth J. Sternstein Cantor Jacob Merlis Rabbi Emeritus Melvin Kieffer Roger Fisher, President
