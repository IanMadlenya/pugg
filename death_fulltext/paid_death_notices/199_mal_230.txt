
        SPRINGER--Nathan, 74, on August 12th, 2003. Born August 8th, 1929 in Brooklyn to Morris and Yetta. Served as cadre in the US Army at Chaffee, AK. A dedicated truant officer, he counseled and guided the troubled children of NYC. Nathan is survived by his sister, Estelle Warren, his children, Mark, Gary, Mathew, granddaughter, Jamie Nadine, and exwife, Sharon. Family requests donations to be made to the Alzheimer's Association.
