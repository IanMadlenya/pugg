
        HIRSCHHORN--Ronald David. Died on September 3, 2005 in Durham, NC at the age of 63. Survived by daughter Jody Stambouli of Los Angeles, CA. and son Jason Hirschhorn of NYC. Ronnie was an unforgettable person. They threw out the mold when they made him. He will be missed. Funeral services 11:15AM, Thursday, September 8 at Riverside Memorial Chapels, 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. Memorial donations may be made to B'nai B'rith Disaster Relief Fund, www.bnaibrith.org
