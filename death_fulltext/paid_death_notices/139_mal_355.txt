
        ROSENBERG-William H. The A.A.S.R. of Freemasonry Valley of New York City sorrowfully announces the passing of our illustrious Brother on August 28. His love of humanity illuminated the Lodge and all with whom he came in contact. The myriad members he sponsored over the years are a memorial to his memory and love of man. Services were held Sunday, August 30. Francis Dumaurier, M.S.A. and Commander-in-Chief
