
        WOO--Chin-Fu. Beloved husband of Yu Tsung; devoted father of Lily and Rose; sonsin-law, Michael and John; cherished grandfather of Natalie, died on Sunday, September 14, at Fairview Nursing Care Center. He was 93. Born in China in 1910 and became a U.S. citizen in 1970. Earned his B.S. degree from Nankai University in Tientsin and his M.B.A. degree from the University of Washington in Seattle. Founder, publisher and editor of the United Journal since 1952. Author of The Principles of Desirable Society. You will always be with us. We love you.
