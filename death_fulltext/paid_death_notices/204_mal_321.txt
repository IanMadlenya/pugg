
        KASS--Benjamin, in his 88th year, died on January 22, 2004. He was the light of our lives and the adored husband of Marilyn, father of Jane, Susan and Michael, Mitchell, Richard and Susan, Adam and Barbara and grandfather of Jennifer, Philip, David, Sarah, Matthew, Aaron, Kimberly, Brittany, Brian and Kenzy. An avid gardener, craftsman, marathon runner, past-president of the Ranachqua Foundation, Bronx, New York, and businessman. He had a love for the fine arts and classical music and was a beloved friend and leader to all who knew him. His spirit lives on in all of us. We love you Dad and Grandpa. Sleep on gentle love.
