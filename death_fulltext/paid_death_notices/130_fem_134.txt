
        EBIN-Muriel Rosenthal. Age 88, passed away on Thursday, November 20, 1997 in San Antonio, TX. She was born on August 14, 1909 in Brooklyn, New York. She was a graduate of Hunter College in New York City and was a longtime high school art teacher. Mrs. Ebin previously resided in Brooklyn, New York and Royal Palm Beach. She was preceded in death by her first husband: Benjamin H. Rosenthal. Survivors include her husband: Herbert Ebin; sons: Saul H. Rosenthal and wife, Cindy and Daniel M. Rosenthal and wife, Deanne; and grandchildren: Jesse, Sadie, and Amy Rosenthal. Graveside Services will take place at Knollwood Park Cemetery in Ridgewood, New York.
