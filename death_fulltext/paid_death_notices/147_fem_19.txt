
        BERMAN-Gertrude. The Univeristy of Haifa family mourns the passing of longtime friend and supporter Gertrude Berman, beloved wife of Bennett and loving mother of Helaine and Jeffrey. Gertrude and Bennett helped to establish the Berman Center for Psychological Counseling and its faculty will miss her support and advocacy. May the entire Berman family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and of Jerusalem. Yehuda Hayuth, President Yael Metser, Vice President Paul Jeser, American Society
