
        VILLA-Anthony L. 80, of Peapack, NJ, died on Friday, January 12, 2001. Devoted husband of the late Diana Gambrill Villa and beloved father of Anthony G., Peter L., L. Blair, Nicholas G., Diana Villa Noble and Wendy Villa Robins, and the loving grandfather of nine. Born in NYC, March 3, 1920, the son of Count and Countess Alfonso P. Villa. He was a graduate of Groton School and Harvard University. He served during the Second World War with several government agencies and was administrative assistant to the Vice President of the Allied Commission in Italy. Post-war he became the Senior Partner and subsequently Chairman of the brokerage firm Jesup and Lamont & Co; he was then associated with Tucker Anthony & Co. until retirement in 1987. He was at various times a member of the New York and American Stock Exchanges and director of several listed companies. He was a member of the Somerset Hills Country Club, The Raquet and Tennis Club, and a longstanding Governor of The Brook. A memorial service will be held at St. Brigid's Church, Peapack, NJ, on Thursday, January 25, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the Peapack-Gladstone First Aid Squad, 1 St. Luke's St., Gladstone, NJ 07934.
