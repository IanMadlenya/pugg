
        SHAMES-Leonard. October 25, 1997. Husband of Helene (nee Weisman) of NY, brother of Gertrude Schiffer and Isadore Shames of Monticello. Funeral services were held Monday. Interment was in Montefiore Cemetery, Philadelphia, PA. Family will be at the home of Norman Pessin. Contributions in his memory may be made to Volunteer Services for Children.
        SHAMES-Leonard. Volunteer Services for Children mourns the passing on October 25, 1997 of Helene's beloved husband. With wit and integrity he was a true friend to the disadvantaged children of New York and to all of us at VSC.
