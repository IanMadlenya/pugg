
        DEMETRIOU - Kyriacos (''Mr. Kay''). May 14, 1999. Beloved husband of Niki. Loving father of Gloria and father-in-law of Paul Galietti. His noted talents as a barber were well known throughout the world. The contents of his Broadway Barbershop were donated to the Museum of the City of New York.
