
        CIMINE-Philip Joseph on Sunday, March 8, in his sleep of natural causes. Devoted husband of Isabel. Beloved father of Mary Ellen of Tampa and Diane of Irvington, N.Y. Loving grandfather of Cara, Ian and Dana. Recipient of the Bronze Star Medal for service to his country during WWII, 1941-1945. Retired from Manufacturers Hanover Trust Company in 1975 after 25 years of employment. A wake is being held at La Bella Funeral Chapel, 2625 Harway Avenue, Bklyn, N.Y., Monday and Tuesday, 2-5PM and 7-9:30PM. Burial will take place March 11th at Veterans Memorial Cemetery in Calverton following a 9:30 mass at Most Precious Blood Church, Bklyn. NY.
