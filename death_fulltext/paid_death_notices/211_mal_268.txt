
        BIANCA--Daniel M. The partners and staff of Mendes & Mount mourn the loss of our dear friend and retired partner Daniel Bianca, who for more than 40 years was our trusted colleague, valued partner and loyal friend. He served many years as a member of the firm's Management Committee, including five years as its Chairman. His guidance, wise counsel and friendship will be missed by us all and we extend our condolences to his widow, Marie, his children, Daniel, Robert, Donna, Karen, Lynn, his grandchildren and other relatives. A Funeral Mass will be held Thursday, September 2nd, 10 AM, at St. Gregory the Great Church, 215 Halstead Ave., Harrison, New York.
