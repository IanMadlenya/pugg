
        BANKS-Richard D. Age 45. Died August 30th of AIDS, surrounded by his family. He is survived by his mother Helen Bangs; sister Claudia; brothers David and Gregg; sisters-in-law Cary and Kristy; and six nephews and nieces. Will now join his late companion Joseph Tonti. Richard showed great courage and leadership in his battle against AIDS; growing stronger emotionally and mentally even as he weakened physically. He is an inspiration. A memorial service will be held Saturday, September 6 at 3 P.M. at St. Michael's, 225 West 99th St, NY, NY 10025. Donations can be made to either St. Michael's or The Gay Men's Health Crisis, 119 W. 24th St. (Attn: Development), New York, NY 10011.
