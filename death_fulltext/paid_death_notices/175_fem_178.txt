
        BERENSON-Birdie. 97. Died on August 21, 2001. Compatriot of Eleanor Roosevelt. U.N. & human rights activist. Past President of B'nai Brith. A woman of valor. Loving mother of David and the late Robert. Cherished mother-inlaw of Joan and Marion. Grandmother of 5. A graveside service will be held today at 11AM at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Jewish National Fund for the State of Israel, 42 East 69th Street, NY, NY 10021.
