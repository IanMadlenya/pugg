
        MacNAIR-Margaret Prichitt, 87. Of Devon, PA, on November 26. Raised in NYC, she attended Brearley and Parsons, became an interior designer, artist and antique dealer. Married to the late Pierce MacNair, she leaves four children-Victoria Todd, Andrew MacNair, Dora Smith and Caroline MacNair, and 4 grandchildren-J. Harrison Todd, Sarah Todd, Swanna Smith and Nicholas Smith. Anyone who knew her will never forget her smile and great courage.
