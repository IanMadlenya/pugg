
        BARRETT-Betty. Of NYC. A sculptor who received training at the New School for Social Research and whose works were displayed at the Library for Performing Arts, Lincoln Center, and the White House. In addition, her sculptures are represented in private collections in both the U.S. and England. Betty is survived by her husband Herbert, President and founder of Herbert Barrett Management. She is also survived by daughters Nancy & Katherine and a son-in-law Richard. Grandchildren include Wendy, Andrew, Sarah, Benjamin, and Sandy. Services are private.
