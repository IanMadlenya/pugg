
        CITRIN-Ceil. 90, on October 26, 2001, of Ft. Lauderdale, FL, formerly of NYC. Devoted wife of the late Max Citrin, adored mother of Beverly Sunshine, Stuart Citrin and Marlene Borey. Dear mother-inlaw of Dr. Ian Sunshine and George S. Borey, Esq. Loving grandmother of Toby (Ken Savell), Jonathan Sunshine and Douglas (Rachel) Sunshine. Adored great-grandmother of Morgan and Brooke Savell. Services were held in New York at Riverside Chapel, 76th St and Amsterdam Ave, NYC. Her brave spirit celebrated her joy and wonder of life around her and her deep love of family. We all rejoiced in her loving spirit and warm, radiant personality and shall cherish her life and memory forever.
