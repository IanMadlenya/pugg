
        BJORGE--James R. Died January 30. Survived by his sister Margaret B. Adams, Santa Barbara, CA. Graduate of US Naval Academy 1948-A; Yale Law School 1953. Director of International Choral Festivals, Lincoln Center. Memorial Service March 26th, 5:30 PM, the Yale Club. Funeral service April 19, 11 AM, USNA. Contributions in his memory to The Yale Alumni Chorus Foundation, PO Box 209036, New Haven, CT 06520-9036, or The 1948 Class Fund, USNA Foundation, Annapolis, MD 21402-5068, or the choral organization of your choice.
