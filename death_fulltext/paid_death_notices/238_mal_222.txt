
        DEAN--Andy Chen, M.D., Ph.D. We are deeply saddened by the passing of our dear friend and colleague Dr. Andy Chen Dean. Dr. Dean was director of the Epilepsy Monitoring Unit at NewYorkPresbyterian Hospital and assistant professor of neurology and neuroscience at Weill Cornell Medical College. He was also Physician Compliance Leader for the Department of Neurology and Neuroscience and an examiner for the American Board of Clinical Neurophysiology. An exceptional physicianscientist with special interest in transcranial magnetic stimulation, and a beloved clinician and teacher, Dr. Dean will be greatly Missed. Our heartfelt conDolences are extended to the Dean family. Dr. Herbert Pardes, President and CEO, NY-Presbyterian Hospital Dr.Antonio M. Gotto Jr., Dean, Weill Cornell Medical College Dr. M.
