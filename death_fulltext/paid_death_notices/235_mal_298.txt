
        TREVOR--John Jr. The New York Genealogical and Biographical Society notes with sadness the death of John Bond Trevor, Jr., F.G.B.S. on Sunday, August 27 at Windover, his house in the Adirondacks. Mr. Trevor was a longtime Trustee and President of the Society from 1986--1989. The Board and staff of the Society extend their condolences to his family. Henry CB Lindh Chairman William P. Johns President
