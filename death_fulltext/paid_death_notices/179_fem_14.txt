
        SCHWARZBART-Edna. 92, of Anaheim Hills, CA, formerly of New York City. Beloved wife of the late Elias, loving mother of Ellen McKoy, passed away December 1, 2001. She is survived by her adoring family, including her son-in-law Keith, grandsons Chris and Keith Jr., and great-grandson Conner. Private memorial service. Donations may be made to California Hospice Foundation, 1510 J Street, No. 135, Sacramento, CA 95814.
