
        CELNIK-Saul. October 8, 1998. Survivor of the Warsaw Ghetto. Beloved husband of Ann (nee Kalina), father of Olga and Steve Weitzman & Joyce & Paul Levine, grandfather of Nili Richards, Aron Weitzman, Caralyn and Marissa Levine, great-grandfather of Jessica & Matthew Richards. Services Sunday, October 11, at 1 PM at Parkside Memorial Chapels, 98-60 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills.
        CELNIK-Saul. The International Network of Children of Jewish Holocaust Survivors mourns the death of the beloved father of Joyce Celnik Levine and Olga Weitzman. May his devotion to his family, Holocaust remembrance and the Jewish people be an inspiration and blessing.
