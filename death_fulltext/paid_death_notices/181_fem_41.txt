
        FARRELL-Marie O'Neill. Of Danbury, CT, formerly of Eastchester, NY, died February 2, 2002. She was 87. Born July 2, 1914, in New York, NY, to William and Julia O'Neill. Loving mother of Vincent Farrell, Jr. of Katonah and Patricia Farrell Savage of Washington, CT. Beloved grandmother of five. She was predeceased by her husband Vincent Sr. in 1975. Calling hours Monday, 2-4 and 7-9, at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, with the Funeral Mass at St. Patrick's Church in Bedford, NY, on Tuesday.
