
        MARQUSEE-John Edward. A compassionate businessman, with a deep concern for, and commitment to, social and racial justice died September 6th, after a long illness. He leaves Janet, his wife of 47 years; his children Mike and Liz, Jeff, Joanne and Larry, Susan and Alan, and Ellen and Barry, each of which he respected and revered. And of special joy were his grandchildren Alex & Hannah, Rebecca and Sarah, Alison and Lindsay, & Rachel and Isabel. A memorial service will be held at 2:00 PM Tuesday, September 14 at the Ethical Culture Society at 2 W. 64th Street, NY, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Human Rights Watch, 350 5th Ave, New York, NY 10118 would be appreciated.
        MARQUSEE-John. The Advisory Council of the Herbert F. Johnson Museum of Art, Cornell University, mourns the passing of its beloved member, John Marqusee, Cornell Class of 1951. He was a man who loved life and loved people, a practical idealist, and a great collector. He will be missed by all of us.
