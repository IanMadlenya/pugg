
        MILLS--John Francis, Esq. 63, of Lido Beach, New York. On August 15, 2003. John is surived by his beloved wife Donna and their children Jacqueline and Kevin, his son John Jr., his daughter Jennifer Mills Berlingieri, her husband Michael Berlingieri, and John's grandchildren, Julian and Alexandra. John was precedeased by his parents Madeline and Raymond Mills and his nine brothers and sisters. John will always be loved and remembered for his loyalty, dedication and generosity to his family and friends. John was proud of his 15 years of service as a New York City Firefighter with Engine Company 210. He had a lifelong love and affection for all firefighters. He served our country as a member of the United States Army. John was a partner of the law firm Colleran, O'Hara and Mills. He was a distinguished member of the legal community, and was a long term advocate for organized labor. John served as counsel to the Firefighters Association of Greater New York and the New York City Ironworkers. Reposing after 7 PM, Monday, at the Charles J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 603 Wantagh Avenue (Exit 28N SS Pkwy), Wantagh. Funeral Mass Wednesday, 10:30 AM, Our Lady of the Miraculous Medal RC Church, Point Lookout, NY. Interment to follow Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, NY. Family will receive friends, Monday, 7-9 PM, and Tuesday 2-5 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to the John F. Mills Scholarship Fund at Lawrence Woodmere Academy, c/o Lawrence Woodmere Academy, 336 Woodmere Blvd, Woodmere, NY 11598.
