
        ENTINE--Marjorie. 81. Passed away Monday, March 8. She is survived by her two sons Michael and Eric and daughter-in-law Terry as well as her three devoted grandchildren Michelle, Christopher and Nina. She lived her life with integrity and dignity. Her caring and concern for the ones she loved was unsurpassed. She will always be an inspiration for all those whose lives she touched. Service will be held Thursday, March 11, 11:30 AM, at the ''Riverside'', 76th St., and Amsterdam Ave.
        ENTINE--Marjorie. Our sincere sympathy to the Entine family. Midge will always be in our hearts and minds. Charlotte and Joe Brodie and Shirley Lavender
