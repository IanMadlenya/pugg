
        ZUCKERMAN-Morris. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute mourns the passing of Morris Zuckerman, beloved father of Joseph D. Zuckerman, Chairman of the Departments of Orthopaedic Surgery of the Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute and NYU Medical Center. Morris Zuckerman will always be remembered as a committed friend of HJD and generous benefactor of our major initiatives. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Lee, sons Nathan, Howard and Joseph, grandchildren and to the entire family. William A. Perlmuth, Chairman of the Board Victor H. Frankel, M.D., President John N. Kastanis, FACHE, Chief Executive Officer
