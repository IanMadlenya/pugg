
        POSNER-Hamilton. On January 19, 2001, in his 93rd year. Beloved husband of the late Rose Posner, devoted father of Maida and Harry, loving grandfather of Philip, Karen, Elisa. Dear brother of Jane Lane and Arthur Posner. Former President and owner of Posner Hair Products and Cosmetics. We will all miss him. Services Sunday, January 21, 11:15 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
