
        RAPPEPORT--Stanley I., age 91, devoted longtime companion of Rita Cooper, brother of Jonas Rappeport of Baltimore, MD, and Evelyn Berman of Boca Raton, FL, passed away on February 22, 2007. He graduated from Johns Hopkins University, worked as a medical writer, and was a dedicated pacifist and vegetarian. Services will be held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91st Street, (212) 769-4400, Sunday, February 25, 12:30 pm. Donations in his memory may be made to the New Israel Fund (www.nif.org).
