
        REISS-Ruth Margaret, 37, of Boston, MA., died on April 22, 1997. Cause was complications from a bone marrow transplant for leukemia. Education included Miss Porter's School, Farmington, CT, Amherst College, Amherst, MA, '82, Masters in Education and English, Harvard University. Upon graduation from Amherst College, Ruth joined Morgan Guarantee Bank, NYC, where she was elected Vice President. In 1989, Ruth changed careers and after obtaining the Master's Degrees from Harvard she joined The Boston Foundation and became Director of Development. Ruth is survived by her parents Beverly B. and Theodore J. Reiss of Rumson, NJ, a sister Heidi M. Reiss, also of Rumson, a brother Carl A. Reiss, NYC and her maternal grandmother Lucille A. Blanchet, NYC. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Miss Porter's School, Ruth M. Reiss Fund, 60 Main Street, Farmington, CT. 06032. Friends may call at Frank E.
