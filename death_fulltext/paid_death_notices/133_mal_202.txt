
        TRISTMAN-Richard G. Scholar, author and revered professor of literature, died February 12, 1998. He exerted a deep and lasting influence on generations of students, colleagues and friends. He was a gentleman and a visionary. He is mourned by his mother, Jeanette of Miami Beach, his two sons, David and Jonah, his longtime friend, Gina Pollara, and by countless friends. Contributions may be made in his name to the Department of Medical Oncology, Montefiore Hospital, 111 E. 210th St, Bronx, NY 10467.
