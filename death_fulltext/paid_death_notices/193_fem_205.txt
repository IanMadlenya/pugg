
        REILLY - Mary P. (nee Walsh) of Manhattan and Sag Harbor. On February 23, 2003. From the age of 13 until her retirement she was a church organist in NYC. Beloved wife for 54 years of Gerard. Loving mother of Pat, Terry, Michael, Margaret and Chrissy. Devoted grandmother of Ellen, Kate, Giulia, Annie, Peter and Timothy. Also survived by her brother Kevin Walsh and her sister Kathleen Poole. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 2nd Avenue (at 20th St), NYC Wednesday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday 11 AM at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment Green-Wood Cemetery.
