
        BORENSTEIN-Samuel. The Yeshiva University family is deeply saddened by the passing of our cherished longtime friend, who, with his first wife, Blume, was a Founder of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine and with his second wife, Lillian Brown Borenstein, was a YU Guardian. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his sister, Mrs. Celia Wendroff, to his stepchildren, Benjamin (and Francine) Brown and Warren (and Marilyn) Brown, and to all members of the bereaved family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman Yeshiva University Board of Trustees Robert A. Belfer, Chairperson Albert Einstein College of Medicine Board of Overseers
