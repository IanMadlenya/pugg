
        PETERMAN-Solomon. Of Toms River, N.J. (formerly of NY, NY), father of Roberta Peters Fields, Metropolitan Opera soprano, on Monday, June 11. Age 93. A dear sweet person who loved his family dearly and had a great sense of humor. He will be missed tremendously but will forever be in the hearts of his family and friends. Beloved husband of the late Ruth (nee Hirsch) and Sarah (nee Wallach) Gordon Peterman. Loving father of Roberta Peters Fields and stepdaughters Judith Gordon Cook and Linda J. Sclarow. Cherished grandfather of Bruce and Paul Fields and step-grandchildren Dr. Gabriel Cook, Benay Taub, Jonathan Cook, Andrea Sclarow and Benjamin Sclarow. Adored great grandfather of Julie Robin, Jessica and twins Remy and Noah Fields & step great-grandchildren Matthew, Eliana, Joshua, Kian, Samarah and Darian. Funeral service Wednesday, June 13, 10:A.M. at Congregation B'nai Israel, 1448 Old Freehold Road, Toms River, NJ. Interment Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Memorial donations may be made to Congregation B'nai Israel or to the American Cancer Society. For information, Belkoff-Goldstein Funeral Chapel, Lakewood, New Jersey.
