
        TANCER--Michael. Dearest husband of Francine Tancer for 55 years, passed away on March 24 after a long and beautiful life. Loving and greatly loved in return by his devoted daughters Joan and Susan and son-in-law Les Tolchin, granddaughter Jennifer and grandson Eric and sister Frances Decker. He loved and lived life fully with great courage fighting illness and disabilities with amazing determination. He served in the US Army Air Force Intelligence Unit during WW II in the Pacific. He graduated from NYU and the NYU Law School, however, his career was in business. He founded and was President of Tancer & Two a costume jewelry company. He was President of Coro, Inc. He was an avid reader, golfer, friend to many, elegant and handsome, generous & compassionate. Our mighty oak has fallen. We will miss him forever. Funeral services at Temple Kol Ami, White Plains, Monday March 29 at 10:30AM. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to The American Cancer Society, or the Arthritis Foundation.
        TANCER--Mike. The Board of Governors and members of Fairview Country Club note with profound sorrow the passing of our beloved longtime member, Mike Tancer. On behalf of all our members, we express our deepest sympathy to his wife, Fran, and their entire family. Jacqueline Bellsey, President Dale Winston, Secretary
