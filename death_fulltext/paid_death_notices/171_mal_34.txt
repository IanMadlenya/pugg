
        CRAVEN-Richard. Of NYC, died on Wednesday, March 28, 2001, at the age of 72, at his home on Martha's Vineyard, after a long and heroic battle with ALS (Lou Gehrig's Disease). He is survived by his adored and loving wife Carol; his three children Richard T. Craven, Fayetteville, NC, Priscilla Craven, Boulder, CO, and Michael Craven, Beverly Hills, CA; his stepson Michael Boardman and his wife Kate Tabner, of NYC; and five grandchildren. Contributions in his memory may be made to Hospice of Martha's Vineyard, PO Box 2549, Oak Bluffs, MA 02557; or Deerfield Academy, Deerfield, MA 01342. A memorial service is planned for June 23 on Martha's Vineyard.
