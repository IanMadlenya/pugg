
        TANNOR--Solomon. Hadassah, The Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc. mourns the passing of Solomon Tannor, beloved husband of Norma, of blessed memory, cherished father of Merrill, Robert and Erica, Evan and Ilene and loving grandfather of Amanda, Samantha, Bradley, Jaymie and Cole. As a Guardian of Life, Sol was a generous benefactor of Hadassah, devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. His legacy will live on through the programs he supported at the Hadassah Medical Organization. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. June Walker, National President
