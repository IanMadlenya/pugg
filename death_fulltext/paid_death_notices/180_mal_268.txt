
        WHITEHOUSE - Eugene M., (1958-2002), Chief Operating Officer at M.H. Meyerson & Co., Inc. passed away Thursday, January 10, 2002. Gene was part of our family at M.H. Meyerson & Co., Inc. for 20 years. His untimely passing touched us all very deeply. He was well loved and respected by his co-workers. The staff deeply mourns his death and he will be sorely missed. Our love and condolences to his wife, Elva, daughter, Tamara, son, Russell, parents, Sandra & Don Whitehouse and family. Lovingly, M.H. Meyerson & Co., Inc.
