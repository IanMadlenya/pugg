
        FRIEDMAN-Israel S. ''Sol'' died peacefully at home January 25, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Blanche Friedman for 62 years. A WWII veteran and retired dentist, he worked 30 years for the Veteran's Administration. He is survived by his daughter Lois, son Matthew and his wife Mary and beloved grandchildren Jessica, Carly and Daniel. A wonderful husband, father and grandfather, he will be terribly missed.
