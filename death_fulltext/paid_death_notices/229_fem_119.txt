
        PERKINS--Joan T. Joan Titcomb Perkins, beloved wife of Roswell B. Perkins, mother of Roswell, Jr., of Little Compton, RI, Laura, of Cambridge, MA, and Nancy, of Washington, D.C., grandmother of Renny and Samantha Waldron, James and Christine Perkins, and sister of Elizabeth Blow, of Rhinebeck, NY, died of cancer in her home in Manhattan on February 13, 2006. In addition to her immediate family, Joan leaves nieces, nephews, and a host of friends and godchildren who will miss her enthusiastic, generous spirit and wonderful sense of humor. A memorial service will be held Friday, February 24 at 11 A.M., at All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Ave. (between 79th and 80th Streets). In lieu of flowers, contribution may be sent to either: Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 1250 Broadway, NY, NY 10001-3701, or to Little Compton Community Center, PO Box 926, Little Compton, Rhode Island 02837.
