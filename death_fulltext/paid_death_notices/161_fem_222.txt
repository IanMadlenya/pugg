
        NEWMAN-Dr. Jane, 88, originally of St. Helens, Oregon, passed away June 24th in Rockville Centre, N.Y. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Herbert Newman, adored mother of Mike and Ellen Ferguson. Cherished grandmother of Matthew, Dylan, Max, Tzahuiztil, Conor and Kara. Known professionally as Dr. Northup and affectionately as Dr. Jane, she was a practicing physician at Manhattan General Hospital, New York Infirmary, Booth Memorial Hospital and ILGWU Health Center. Memorial service June 30th, 11am1pm at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel7, 20 Lincoln Ave, Rockville Centre, NY
