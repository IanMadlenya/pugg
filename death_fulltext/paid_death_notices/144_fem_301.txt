
        KIRSCHENBAUM - Zelda Celia. (nee Rosenblum) November 4, 1899-January 26, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Gabriel Kirschenbaum. Devoted mother of Deby Salama and Dr. J. Michael Kirschenbaum (Paulenne). Cherished grandmother of Michael Foster Shapiro (Stacey) and Sasha Aelise Salama, Lisa, Nina and Jill Kirschenbaum. Died peacefully at her home in Pembroke Pines, Fla. Affectionately known as Cele, she was a Hunter college graduate and at one time danced with Martha Graham's troupe. Taught English and Physical Education at Seward Park High School in NYC.
