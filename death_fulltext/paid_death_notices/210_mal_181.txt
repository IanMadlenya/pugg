
        SHANLEY--Robert E. Died on June 3, 2004 in Fort Lauderdale, Fla. He was the husband of the late Katherine Teaman McGuire and the late Aline Cyr. He is survived by one brother, Francis Shanley of Brooklyn and eight nieces and nephews. Mr. Shanley was born in Brooklyn, N.Y. on December 20, 1918. He retired from the New York City Police Department, Detective Division in 1973. During World War II he was a volunteer serviing in the U.S. Navy Reserve. He was with the 70th C.B. in North Africa. In 1944, he returned to the United States and served in submarines. His last assignment was on the U.S.S. Cobbler. Burial was in the National Cemetery in Santa Fe, N.M.
