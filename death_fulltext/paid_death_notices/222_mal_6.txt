
        PRESENT--Ignatz, July 30, 2005, age 93. Beloved husband for 60 years to Rose; devoted father to David (Karen) and Larry (Suzy); cherished grandfather of Alex, Emily, Michael, Adie and Elie. We will always remember his strength of character, love for his family, devotion to Jewish life and learning, and the integrity and sincerity with which he led his life. He will be deeply missed by all of us who admired & loved him.
