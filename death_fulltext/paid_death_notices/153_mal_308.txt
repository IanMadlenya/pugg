
        ARTHUR-John W. Jr. On October 23, 1999. Husband of the late Doris Sawyer Arthur. Father of Meredith Quarrier, Eleanor Raybold and David Arthur. Also survived by seven grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Mr. Arthur began his banking career in Manhattan rising through the management ranks. He was elected President of the American Institute of Banking in 1950. In 1965 he was appointed Vice President of the Trust Department of the Bank of New York where he remained until his retirement in 1968. Memorial service was at St. David's Episcopal Church, Southfield, Michigan.
