
        VUILLET--Sarah, 71. A mainstay of Locust Valley Friends Academy for more than 30 years as a Spanish teacher, Upper School Principal, Director of College Guidance and Associate Head and even golf coach, died April 20 of cancer at a hospital near her home in Pine Plains, NY. She was a member of the Women's Long Island Golf Association and a club champion at Nassau Country Club. Most recently she played at Wyantenuck Country Club and the Millbrook Golf and Tennis Club. Robert Vuillet, her husband of 49 years and daughters Heather Lende, Kathleen Augustine, Suzanne VuilletSmith survive her as do eight grandchildren and a sister. Funeral Monday, April 24 at 11:30, Trinity Church, Roslyn.
