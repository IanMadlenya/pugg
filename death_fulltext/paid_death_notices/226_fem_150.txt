
        GROLLMAN--Elaine. Of Bayside, N.Y. On November 17, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Saul. Loving mother of Jean. Talented actress. Member of SAG and AEA. Survived by brothers Alvin Feinstein and Martin Forrest. Her sense of humor, generosity and zest for life will be missed by many. Services Sunday, 1:15PM, Sinai Chapel, 162-05 Horace Harding Epressway, Fresh Meadows, N.Y. Donations may be made to the Hospice Care Network, 99 Sunnyside Blvd, Woodbury, N.Y. 11797.
