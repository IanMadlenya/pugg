
        ARISON-Ted. The American Committee for the Tel Aviv Foundation expresses profound sorrow at the passing of our Founder, original Chairman, leader and most generous donor, Ted Arison. Ted was a giant. Through his example, he brought many other supporters and donors as well. He did more for the Tel Aviv Foundation and the City of Tel Aviv-Jaffa than any other. Our heartfelt condolences go out to his beloved wife, Lin, and his son and American Committee Board Member, Micky Arison and his family, his daughter and generous supporter, Shari Arison Dorsman and her family, his sister Aviva Tamir and his niece, our dear friend Talia Glantz. American Committee for the Tel Aviv Foundation Uzi Zucker, Chairman Ira Riklis, President Shimon Topor, Sec./Treasurer Jeannie Gerzon, Exec. Director Ron Huldai, International Chairman and Mayor of Tel Aviv-Jaffa David Harnik, General Manager, Tel Aviv Foundation
        ARISON-Ted. It is with profound sorrow that the American Friends of Tel Aviv University mourns the passing of its friend and supporter, Ted Arison. His deep and abiding commitment to education, youth and Israel establishes him as a role model for others to strive to follow. We owe him an immeasurable debt of gratitude for his vision and generosity in making Tel Aviv University the institution of higher learning in Israel that is setting the pace in terms of excellence, diversity and creativity. He will be sorely missed. Our heartfelt sympathy is expressed to his wife Lin, daughter Shari, son Mickey and the entire Arison family. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University Alan L.
