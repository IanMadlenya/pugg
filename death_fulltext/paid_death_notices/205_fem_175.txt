
        BARMANN--Dorothy E. on Sunday, February 22, 2004. Born on October 24, 1911 in NYC. Daughter of the late Tobias and Julia (nee Lachmann) Phillips. Widow of Bernard, who predeceased her on February 6, 2001. A 1932 graduate of Hunter College, she taught in both the NYC School System and in Bayonne, NJ after moving there with her family in 1955. Survived by sons Neil and Joel and grandson Todd. Graveside services at Beth Israel Cemetery and Woodbridge, NJ will be private.
