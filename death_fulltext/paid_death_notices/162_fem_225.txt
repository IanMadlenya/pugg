
        McKEON-Sr. Kathryn. Beloved member of the Sisters of Charity, Convent of Mary the Queen, died Friday, July 28, 2000. Survived by her sister, Agnes Beebe, and by her nephews. Mass of Christian Burial Monday, 9:30 AM, at the Convent of Mary the Queen, 35 Vark St., Yonkers. Interment at St. Joseph's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Sisters of Charity Development Fund, 6301 Riverdale Ave, Bronx, NY 10471.
