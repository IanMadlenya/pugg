
        LATOY--Helen Dodge. Mother of the late Marilyn Sewell McGuire, died on March 18, 2004. She was born in Douglaston, Long Island, NY, and also lived in New York City for many years. Mrs. Latoy spent seven years in Honolulu and was witness to the Pearl Harbor attack on December 7, 1941. She attended Abbot Academy (Phillips Andover) and graduated from the Misses Stones' School in Washington, DC. Upon graduating from high school, Mrs. Latoy attended the College of William and Mary, as well as Webber College in Babson Park, FL. She was a member of the Panhellenic Society of Ft. Lauderdale, as well as the local chapter of Chi Omega sorority. She is survived by her grandchildren R. Andrew McGuire and Carrie L. McGuire Lee, their respective spouses, Mariana McGuire and Jason Lee, and son-in-law, Robert L. McGuire and his wife Nell Lewis McGuire.
