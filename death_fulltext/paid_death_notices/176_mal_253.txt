
        GLICK-Steven Lawrence, 42. Beloved husband and best friend of Mari; loving father of Colin and Courtney; devoted son of Ester and the late Herbert, caring brother of Ellen, Stefanie, Gordon and Robert; joyful son-in-law of Libby Adler Mages and brother-in-law of Wendy. Died while attending the Waters Financial Technology Congress, on the 106th floor of the World Trade Center, Tower One, Tuesday, September 11, 2001, when the building was attacked. A graduate of Northwestern University, Class of 1982 and the Harvard Business School, Class of 1989. Employed with Credit Suisse First Boston as a Managing Director and previously a Partner at Greenwich Associates. Memorial Service will be held at the Greenwich Reform Synagogue at 257 Stanwich Road, Greenwich, CT at 11:00 A.M. on Thursday, September 20th. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to: The Greenwich Chapter of the American Red Cross, 231 East Putnam Avenue, Greenwich, CT 06830
        GLICK-Steve. We are deeply saddened by the loss of our friend and dinner group member. We will miss his good-natured kindness, his cheerful view of life and his loyal friendship. We share our sadness with his family and will remain united in our support for Mari, Colin and Courtney.
