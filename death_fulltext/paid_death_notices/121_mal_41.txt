
        CHASIN-Philip. The Board of Directors and the staff of the City College Fund record with deep regret the passing of Philip Chasin. A member of the Class of 1931, Mr. Chasin was an honorary member of The Board of Directors and one of the founders of the City College Fund. An authority in his profession, he will be missed by all who knew him. Stanley H. Kaplan '39 Chairman Seymour L. Gartenberg '52 President Gerald C. Crane '59 Executive Director
