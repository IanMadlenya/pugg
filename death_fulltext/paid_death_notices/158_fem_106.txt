
        SULLIVAN-Lea E., 83, of Sparkill, NY, formerly of Washington, DC. Died March 10, 2000, at New York Presbyterian Medical Center. Beloved wife of the late C. Meryl Sullivan and cherished mother of Maria A. Sullivan. She will be dearly missed for her courage, strength, and inimitable dry humor by those whose lives she touched. Family will receive friends at the Moritz Funeral Home, 98 Route 303 South, Tappan, NY, on Sunday, March 12, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass, Sacred Heart Chapel in Sparkill, Monday, March 13, 11:30.
