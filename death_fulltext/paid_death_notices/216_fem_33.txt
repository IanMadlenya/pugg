
        CRIMI--Patricia Wheelan, of Locust Valley, NY and Vero Beach, FL on January 3, 2005 surrounded by her loving family. Beloved wife of Michael J. Mother of Robert, Gary, Pamela, Michael L. and Carolyn. Grandmother of seven. Daughter of the late Robert B. Wheelan, one of the founders of North Shore University Hospital, where her husband served as president from 1978-1979. Member of the Locust Valley Players. Visitation at the Funeral Home of Dodge-Thomas, Glen Cove, LI, Friday 3-5 & 7-9PM. Service at St. John's of Lattingtown, Locust Valley, NY, Saturday January 8, 11AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to L.I.J.-North Shore University Hospital, 300 Community Drive, Manhasset, NY 11030 or Schepens Eye Research Institute, Boston, MA.
