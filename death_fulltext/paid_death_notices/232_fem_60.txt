
        BOTUCK--Vivian. Of Huntington Station, LI, died Friday, May 5, after a brief illness. She was born March 31, 1905 in New York City, the daughter of Sophia Behrman and Max Raymond. Just a month ago she had celebrated her 101th Birthday. Her husband Eli Botuck predeceased her, as did her parents and two brothers, Bernard and Walter Raymond. She is survived by her daughter, Anne Botuck, of Huntington Station, and numerous nieces and nephews. She earned Phi Beta Kappa membership at Radcliffe and studied at the Sorbonne in Paris. She taught Chemistry and Physics at Julia Richman H.S. for 36 years. She retired in 1964. In 1941 she married Eli Botuck. A memorial celebration will be held at a later date. In lieu of flowers donations should be sent to the Anne Botuck Supplemental Needs Trust Fund at 38 Whitson Rd., Huntington Station, NY 11746.
