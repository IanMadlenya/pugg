
        CAMERON--Donald J., M.D. An outstanding physician and educator at Weill Cornell Medical Center, died on April 23, 2004. After obtaining his M.D. from Cornell University Medical College in 1955, he continued to serve at Cornell for many years becoming a Senior Attending Physician with expertise in internal medicine, cardiovascular and renal disease, and geriatrics. He was a superb teacher and enthusiastic supporter of the Department of Medicine. Beloved by patients, medical students and residents, his passing is acknowledged with deepest sadness. Ralph L. Nachman, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Medicine Weill Medical College of Cornell University Physician-in-Chief New York-Presbyterian Hospital
