
        WATSON-Peggy Bernier. Died March 5 in Rhinebeck, NY. The singing dancing comedienne got her first show at age 15 with Al Jolson in ''Big Boy'' on Broadway. Became toast of town in Chicago with Paul Ash at the Oriental where she met husband baritone Milton Watson. She starred in ''Good News'' performing and recording title song and ''Varsity Drag'' which became nationwide hits. Her stock in trade-stealing the show! Survived by her daughter Peggy Anne Lombard of Red Hook, NY, granddaughter Jennifer Lombard of NYC and grandson Kirk Lombard of San Francisco.
