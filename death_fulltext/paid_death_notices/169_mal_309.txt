
        RADITSA-Leo Ferrero. Died unexpectedly on February 22nd. at the age of sixty-four, in Annapolis, Maryland. A devoted teacher at St. John's College, he was an ancient historian, and an essayist on contemporary politics, psychiatry, literature and art. He was an accomplished painter and poet, and short story writer. He will be deeply missed by his family and friends, and beloved students. He is survived by his son, Sebastian, and his sister, Bosiljka Raditsa. A Memorial Service will be held in his honor at St. John's College in Annapolis on Saturday, March 24th. at 2 P.M. A Second Memorial in New York City is being planned. In lieu of flowers, send contributions in the memory of Leo Raditsa to The St. John's Library, Attention: Jeff Bishop.
