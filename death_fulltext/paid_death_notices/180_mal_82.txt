
        BEAUREGARD-Gilles. Sadly, Office & Professional Employees International Union AFL-CIO, CLC mourns the passing of its Secretary-Treasurer on January 3, 2002. Gilles dedicated over 40 years of his life to the members of OPEIU working to gain the benefits of collective bargaining for working people. For nearly 16 years Gilles had been Secretary-Treasurer of the OPEIU representing 145,000 workers throughout the United States and Canada. Gilles Beauregard's devotion to OPEIU and the labor movement began in 1959 when he became President of OPEIU Local 57 in Montreal, Canada. He became Business Representative of OPEIU Local 57 in 1962, Regional Representative of the Eastern Canada Council in 1965, Regional Director of Ontario in 1976, International Vice President OPEIU 1980, Secretary-Treasurer of the OPEIU 1986. His leadership, dedication and strength of character will be missed. We send our condolences to his family and friends. International Executive Board Office & Professional Employees International Union, AFL-CIO, CLC
        BEAUREGARD-Gilles. On Jan. 3, 2002, of Greenbelt, Md. Secretary Treasurer of the Office and Professional Employees International Union AFL-CIO, CLC (OPEIU) after a career spanning nearly 40 years of commitment and dedication to the Canadian and American labor movements, Gilles has passed. His career began as President of his local union OPEIU 57 in 1959. In 1962 he became Local Union Business Representative. From there, he ascended to position of Regional Representative of the Eastern Canada Council in 1965, OPEIU International Representative in 1974, Regional Director for Ontario in 1976, Vice President of OPEIU 1980. The OPEIU elected him its Secretary-Treasurer of the International in 1986, the office he served in until his untimely death. The loss of his leadership will be felt for many years. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family. Office and Professional Employees International Union, AFL-CIO, CLC
        BEAUREGARD-Gilles. With deep sorrow the Office & Professional Employees International Union, Local 153, AFL-CIO acknowledges the death of its International Union Secretary-Treasurer, Gilles Beauregard on January 3, 2002. For 16 years Gilles Beauregard served the members of OPEIU in the United States and Canada as its Secretary-Treasurer with commitment, devotion, and the utmost integrity. His passing leaves us devastated. We mourn the loss of our Secretary-Treasurer, leader, teacher and friend who worked tirelessly for the betterment of the OPEIU membership. His example inspired us to try to work up to his standards. We send our condolences to his family. Local 153 Executive Board Office & Professional Employees International Union, AFL-CIO, CLC
