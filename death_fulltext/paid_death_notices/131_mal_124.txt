
        ELICOFON-Edward I. Attorney and art collector. In his 94th year, on December 8, 1997. Loving husband of the late Diana Preiser Elicofon, devoted proud father of Alan and Harriet Elisofon, Carol and Joel Matcovsky, Barry Elisofon, Francine and Robert Follick, and great friend of many. He is also survived by his grandchildren, David, Michael, Danielle, Peter, Diana, Laura and Benjamin, and great-granddaughter Margaret. Son of the late Sam and Sarah, he is predeceased by his brothers and sister Eliot Elisofon, the noted Life Magazine photographer, and George and Belle Elicofon. Funeral services will be held Tuesday, December 9, at 12:45 P.M., at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue in Manhattan. Burial at Beth David Cemetery in Elmont. Shiva will be observed at the home of Alan Elisofon at 2 Sunset Lane, Harrison, New York.
        ELICOFON-Edward. The Gesher Foundation records with deep sorrow the passing of the beloved father of our dear friend, Alan Elisofon. Our sincere condolences to the entire bereaved family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Daniel Tropper, President Marcel Lindenbaum, Chairman Hillel Wiener, Exec. V.P.
        ELICOFON-Edward I. To our dear Mr. E. with much love and appreciation for your warmth and kindness. You are like a father to me. We will never forget you. Patricia, Alison, Edward Marcus and Okemo
