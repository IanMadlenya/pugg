
        DeBARBIERI-Marie T. Of Pearl River, N.Y. and Mt. Chase, Maine, died on February 2, 2002. A member of the mathematics Department at the Pearl River High School for nearly four decades. Her love and care is a legacy that will remain with young people, faculty, staff, and all who knew her. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated Fri. 10 AM at St. Aedan's Church, Pearl River, NY. Burial will be private. Visiting Wed. and Thur. 24 & 7-9 PM. at Assumma-Shankey Funeral Home. Contributions may be made in her memory to the Maine Chapter of the Nature Conservancy, P.O. Box 338 Topshan, Maine 04086. In wildness is the preservation of the world.
