
        HOFFMAN-Arnold August. Born December 2, 1912, passed away January 13, 2001. Arnold August Hoffman was the man of the hour, the man of the year, the man of the millenium-a man for all times and all seasons, a true Rennaissance man, and a true friend to one and all. He was an avid horseman, jeweler and fine arts antique dealer, beloved husband, and adored father, who will be truly missed for eternity. May your soul rest in peace. Your loving wife and children, Julie Ann, Eric Arnold, and Marni Ann. Memorial service will be held at Forest Lawn Memorial Park on Forest Lawn Dr, Hollywood Hills, Ca. at 10:30 AM in the Old North Church, Saturday, January 27, 2001. 800204-3131.
