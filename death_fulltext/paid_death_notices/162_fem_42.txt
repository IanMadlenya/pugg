
        HOFFMAN-Ruth. July 6, peacefully after a long, happy life. Devoted wife of the late Joseph. She leaves Richard, Perla and granddaughter Alegre Hoffman, Frances and William Swirsky, grandsons Peter Genzer, wife Sheri and great granddaughters Rachel and Rebecca, and Douglas Genzer and wife Katrina Seville. A Life Member of Hadassah and former Treasurer of the Great Neck Chapter of Hadassah and Scopus Group. Remembered with love by family, and friends, as a loving, devoted and caring person with a warm smile and outstretched hand. Donations to Great Neck Chapter of Hadassah. Funeral Friday, 1PM at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY.
        HOFFMAN-Ruth. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck George N. Abrahams, President
