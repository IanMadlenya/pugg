
        GRACIE-Charles Douglas. Died on December 4, 2001, at age 90. Mr. Gracie was an owner of Charles R. Gracie & Sons, dealers in hand-painted Chinese wallpapers and oriental art and antiques. He is survived by a daughter, Carol, a son Brian, grandchildren Jonathan and Geoff Gottlieb, Michael and Jennifer Gracie and five great-grandchildren. Donations in his memory will be appreciated by Lewisboro Senior Outreach, 65 Old Bedford Road, Goldens Bridge, NY 10526.
