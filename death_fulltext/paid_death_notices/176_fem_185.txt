
        WEIL-Joanne. Missing in the World Trade Center tragedy. The Board of Governors and membership of Fairview Country Club mourn the sudden loss of our beloved member, Joanne Weil. Joanne grew up at Fairview and will be deeply missed by her many friends and family. On behalf of all our members, we express our deepest condolences to Joanne's parents, Judith and Floyd, and her entire family. Bernard Steinweg, President Leonard Golub, Secretary
