
        BECHTOLD-Paul E. Age 86. Of Port Washington, Long Island. Died May 18, 1998. Surviving are his wife of 57 years, Mary Louise, his sons Stephen of Northport and Alec of Port Washington, four grandsons, Reuben, Mark, Ned and Gus and one granddaughter, Sandy. Memorial gathering at 28 Kaywood Road, Port Washington Saturday, June 6. The family has suggested donations to the Port Washington Public Library.
