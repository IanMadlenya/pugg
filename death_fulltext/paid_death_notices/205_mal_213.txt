
        KAMM--Larry. Television Director/Producer for the ABC Television Network for 36 years and a devoted husband and father, died February 13, 2004. He was a vital member of ABC Sports and one of the most versatile directors in television. From the Olympics to the Super Bowl he worked virtually every major sports event all over the world. From Moscow to Mexico City he was an insatiable passionate broadcaster. Among his many other assignments were: Monday Night Football, the NBA, Major League Baseball, the World Cup, College Football and the Indianapolis 500. He was the devoted son of Phyllis, husband of Claire, father of Lauren and Kristin and brother of Robert and Lewis. Information on donations for a scholarship in his name and a memorial service will be forthcoming.
