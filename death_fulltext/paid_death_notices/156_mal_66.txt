
        EPSTEIN-Nathan B.. The Board of Directors and staff of FEGS are deeply saddened by the passing of Nat Epstein, esteemed treasurer, boardmember and friend. Nat brought to FEGS the wisdom and experience gained over decades of business management and civic leadership. Nat was a gentle, kind man who strongly believed in FEGS' mission of helping each and every individual achieve the highest level each is capable of achieving. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his children, grandchildren, and the entire Epstein family. Joseph Stein, Jr., President/Chair, FEGS Alfred P. Miller Executive VP/CEO, FEGS
