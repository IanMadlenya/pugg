
        McALARY-James J. Jr., 42. Of Spring Lake Heights, NJ who was lost in the World Trade Center Tragedy on September 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Jeanne (nee McCutcheon) McAlary, devoted father of Jillian, Joseph, and James, loving son of James J. Sr. and Ann (nee O'Rourke) McAlary, dear brother of John and Bryan McAlary, Joanne Sullivan and Karen Higdon, son-in-law of William and Virginia McCutcheon, brotherin-law of William, Thomas, Carolyn, Mark, Jim, Maureen, and Lori. He is also survived by many nieces, nephews, and cousins. James was a wonderful husband, father, son and a true and loyal friend. He will be enormously missed by many. Relatives and friends are invited to call at the O'Brien Funeral Home, Hwy 35 at New Bedford Road, Wall, NJ on Thurs. September 27, 2001 from 2-4 & 7-9PM. A Memorial Mass will be offered on Fri September 28, 2001 at 11 AM at St. Margaret's RC Church, 3rd and Ludlow Avenue, Spring Lake, NJ 07762. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to the James J. McAlary Jr. Children's Memorial Fund, P.O. Box 38123, Albany, NY 12203.
