
        YOUNG-Robert F. Former president and publisher of Family Circle magazine, he was a longtime resident of Hastings-on-Hudson and Carmel, NY, recently moved to Greensboro, NC. Died May 27, 1998. Cherished husband of Judith, devoted father of Melinda Rosengarten, Julie O'Connor, Steven and Christopher. Beloved brother of Lenore Cerrone and proud grandfather of Caitlin, and Justin; Robbie & Rennie; Kristen, Brianne and Kenneth. Memorial contributions to the Louis August Jonas Foundation, Rhinebeck, NY. A memorial service will be held in June in Manhattan.
