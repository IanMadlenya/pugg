
        HILLER-Arthur. The National Board and Membership of Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, mourns the loss of Arthur Hiller, 90, of Coconut Creek, FL, formerly of Chicago, father of our esteemed National President, Bonnie Lipton. Mr. Hiller is survived by his loving wife, Nellie, of 65 years, two devoted daughters, and sons-in-law, Bonnie and Alan, Claudia & Dick Nanberg, adoring grandchildren, Andrea, Susan, Ronnie, Steven, and Michael, & eight great grandchildren. Mr. Hiller took great pride in Hadassah's achievements, & was a generous supporter of our programs. Hadassah has established the Arthur Hiller Memorial Fund. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. June Walker, National Treasurer Ellen Marson, National Executive Director
        HILLER-Arthur. The Conference of Presidents of Major American Jewish Organizations mourns the passing of the beloved father of our colleague and friend Bonnie Lipton, President of Hadassah, the Women's Zionist Organization of America. We pray that she and her family will be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Mortimer B. Zuckerman, Chairman Malcolm Hoenlein, Executive Vice Chairman
