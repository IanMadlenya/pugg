
        GERSTMAN-Berenice S., 92. Formerly of Brooklyn, NY. on August 17 in Somerset, NJ. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Mrs. Gerstman was an elementary school teacher in the New York City school system for 35 years. She also taught gym, reading and served as acting principal. She was a trustee of Union Temple, Brooklyn. Mrs. Gerstman was a champion athlete, especially excelling in swimming competitions. She is survived by her sister Ruth Feuerstein, a niece Frances (Daniel) Gold, a grand niece Suzanne Gold, grand newphew Nicholas (Marcy) Gold and two great grand nephews.
