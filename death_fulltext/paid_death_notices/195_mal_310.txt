
        NETTER-Kurt Fred. The officers, Board of Directors, & staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Fred Netter, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. A former UJA - Federation Board member, Mr. Netter advocated tirelessly on behalf of Holocaust survivors, & provided outstanding leadership to Selfhelp Community Services, a beneficiary agency. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Alice, & to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        NETTER-Kurt Fred. Congregation Kol Ami records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member and conveys sincere condolences to his bereaved family. Rabbi Shira Milgrom Rabbi Tom Weiner Ken Simon, President
        NETTER-Kurt F. The Board of Governors and members of Beach Point Club note with deep sorrow the passing of our fellow member, Kurt F. Netter, and extend sincere condolences to his family. Alex C. Solowey, President
        NETTER-Kurt F. We mourn the passing of this extraordinary man and beloved father and father-in-law of our dear friends, Nadine and Peter Levy. Abbi and Paul Neuthaler Nancy and Dan Neff
