
        BOSSE-Janet Cowan, 70. Died at her home in New York City, on Sunday, July 23, 2000. Born in Detroit to Grace Avery and Glen P. Cowan. She grew up in Cleveland. After dancing professionally, she matriculated to Sarah Lawrence College, where she began a lifelong devotion to fine arts. Between 1965 and 2000, her art work was displayed at more than 70 exhibitions in the United States. She was a founding member and past president of Noho Gallery in NYC. She is survived by her brother, David A. Cowan of Ohio; and nieces and nephews. A memorial celebration will be held at Noho Gallery on August 27. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Healthcare Chaplaincy.
