
        CARVER--R. David, 70, of Riverdale, NY, died August 3, 2004. Survived by his loving wife Rosemarie, his sons Alexander and Andrew, his daughter Christina Cahill, his son-in-law Brendan Cahill, and his four grandchildren. Also surviving are his beloved step-father, Cornelius Hurlbut, his sister Gay, his brother Douglass, nephews and nieces. Funeral 10AM, August 6 at Christ Church Riverdale, 5030 Henry Hudson Pkwy and W 252 St. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to The Lawrenceville School, Lawrenceville, NJ.
