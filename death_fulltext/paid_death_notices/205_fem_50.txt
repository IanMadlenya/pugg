
        JOHNSON - JONES -- Thelma. Family, friends, and students mourn the passing of Thelma Johnson-Jones, 39, on Jan. 21, 2004. Ms. Johnson-Jones had worked for more than 13 years as math instructor for the STEP Program at the Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery. She was a math teacher extraordinaire who will be remembered for her love of teaching children.
