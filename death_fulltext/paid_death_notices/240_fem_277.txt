
        GOODSIDE--Dr. Samuel (Hacohen), Shulamith School for Girls extends its condolences to its esteemed H.S. Principal, Dr. Susan R. Katz on the loss of her dear father. A distinguished educator, former Principal of Hili, Ramaz, a gentlemen who was revered and loved by all. The Goodsides were also the Pillars of the Bell Harbor Community for years. Shiva will be observed until Friday afternoon, February 2nd at 2209 Ave I. Burial was in Israel. Sheldon Fliegelman, Chairman of the Board, Sam Gross, President Rabbi Moshe Zwick, Executive Director
