
        KRAMER-Evelyn Sandea. Of Adventura, FL. Born Chicago, IL 7-1212 to 9-7-2000. Designer extraordinary of Millinery for Lili Dache and Creative Manager of the late Goodfriends Stores of Chicago. She is survived by her daughters, Sandra J. Cole of Malibu, CA., and East Hampton, NY and Dr. Estyne Del Rio Diaz of New York. Died September 8th in her daughter's home in East Hampton in her sleep. She was a great lover of Arts and Poetry, and wrote her own poems throughout her life. She loved Dancing and won many dance contests throughout her life even as a senior citizen in Florida.
