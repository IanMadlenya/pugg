
        CRUIKSHANK-Harold T. 85, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of London, England, passed away on May 30, 2002. Mr. Cruikshank was born and attended high school in Glen Ridge, NJ. He then joined the US Navy and served in the South Pacific Theater from 1941 to 1945. Mr. Cruikshank retired from Esso in 1973, serving as Senior VP of Esso Europe London and Director of Esso Petroleum Company, London. Prior to that he was Deputy Marketing Coordinator of the parent organization in New York. After his retirement he served as Senior Management Consultant for Arthur D. Little in London. He was Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the American School in London, Chairman of the Board of Trustees of the American School in London, Chairman of the American Society in London and Chairman of the Board of Directors of the American School in London Foundation, Princeton. He is survived by his wife, Janice T. Cruikshank of Boynton Beach, FL; daughter, Bonnie J. (William) Hyde of Glen Ridge, NJ; grandson, David C. Hyde and granddaughter, Jennifer A. Hyde. He is predeceased by his wife, Bessie Hanna in 1973. Funeral mass and entombment will be held in Florida. For further information contact Lorne and Sons Funeral Home, Delray Beach, FL.
