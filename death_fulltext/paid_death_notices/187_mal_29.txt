
        DENEFF-Carl. 92 died August 1, 2002. Retired physician and doctor on duty during the Japanese Attack on Pearl Harbor, December 7, 1941. The bravery and strength in which you used to save your fellow americans is the model you used for the rest of your life. Loving husband to the late Gertrude. Beloved father to Marla, Lewis and Steven. Grandfather to Charles, Jack and Michael. Dedicated physician for over 50 years in Brooklyn, NY. Love by all who had the pleasure of knowing him. You will be greatly missed by your family.
