
        SAMTON--Henry A., 97 years, nee Hans Szamatolski in Berlin, Germany died June 19th in Rye, New York. Husband for 67 years of late Margot Samton, father of Annette Marcus, Claude and Peter Samton; Founder Miltenberg & Samton Import-Export; Member 2 US Trade Missions Africa; longtime resident of Scarsdale; Extraordinary Father, Grandfather, GreatGrandfather, Father-in-law, Grandfather - in - law, Uncle, Grand Uncle, Dear Friend; A Jurist in Germany, he fled persecution, became a respected, productive and dedicated American...He will be sorely missed!
        SAMTON--Henry. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn the loss of Henry Samton, beloved father of Peter Samton, our esteemed colleague and friend. Henry embodied the graciousness of good aging and was a true gentleman. We extend our condolences to Peter, Emily and the entire Samton and Marcus families on the loss of a beloved father, grandfather and great-grandfather. Milton Gilbert, President Daniel Reingold, Exec V.P.
        SAMTON--Henry. The partners and staff of Gruzen Samton, Architects, Planners & Interior Designers wish to express our sincere sorrow to the entire Samton family on the passing of Henry Samton, father of our partner, Peter Samton. Henry Samton will be missed for his vitality, wit and dignity.
