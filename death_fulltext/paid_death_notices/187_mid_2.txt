
        HEILMAN-Lucia. Beloved wife of the late Henry Heilman, mother of Samuel Heilman and his wife Ellin, grandmother of Adam, Uriel, Avram and Jonah. A righteous and valorous woman, she died at home peacefully having survived the horrors of Shoah and many years of a dignified life in Brookline, MA. Service today 8:30 AM at Riverside, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment in West Roxbury, MA at the Young Israel Cemetery on Grove Street, 1:00 PM.
