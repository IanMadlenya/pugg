
        EISENBERG-Julius. On August 18, age 90, in New Haven. Husband of Rose, father of Alice Mattison and Savita Eisenberg, grandfather of Jacob, Ben and Andrew Mattison, brother of Florence Reback and the late David Eisenberg, Ina Levy and Evelyn Millard. A retired court reporter in New York's Civil Court, he was President of the New York State Shorthand Reporters Association and taught court reporting at Hunter College.
