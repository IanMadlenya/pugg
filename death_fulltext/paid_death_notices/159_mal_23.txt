
        URIBE-Charles. On March 31, 2000 at age 63 peacefully after a long and courageous battle. Cherished father of Lauren and Ali. Beloved companion to Myriam and husband to Eileen. He will be missed by his family, friends, associates and all whose lives he so profoundly touched. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St, Saturday and Sunday, 1-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian burial at Saint Patricks Cathedral, 5th Avenue at 51 St, Monday 10AM. Interment Kensico Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Charles Uribe Memorial Fund, c/o Dr. Joseph Hayes, New York Presbyterian Hospital, Cornell Campus, 525 E. 68 Street, NY, NY 10021.
