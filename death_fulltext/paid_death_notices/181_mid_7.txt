
        WASHNITZER-Jeanne. (nee Noble). Age 104. On January 31, 2002 in Toledo, OH. Wife of the late George Washnitzer. She is survived by sons Robert of Cleveland, OH and Gerard and his wife Lily of Princeton, New Jersey. Also surviving are grandsons and their spouses George and Connie of Lexington, MA, Bernard and Lisa of NYC and James and Janet of Milburn, NJ. Also survived by five great grandchildren.
