
        BAUM--Richard T. Of Chappaqua, New York. On January 30, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Jean Knapp Baum, devoted father of Kathryn Russell and Judith Baum, loving and adored grandfather of Jean Russell, Katherine Baum & Christine Baum. A graduate of Columbia University and Columbia School of Engineering, he spent his professional career as a partner in the engineering firm, Jaros, Baum and Bolles. He was the recipient of many professional honors including the Egleston Medal from Columbia School of Engineering and election to to the National Academy of Engineering. Friends may call at the Oelker and Cox Funeral Home, 262 Main St, Mt. Kisco, N.Y. Wednesday from 6-8PM. Funeral service at the First Congregational Church, Chappaqua, Thursday at 10AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Columbia University School of Engineering, or Mt. Sinai Hospital.
        BAUM -- Richard T. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourn the passing of Richard T. Baum who served as a Trustee and Life Trustee for the past 34 years. A partner with his family's prestigious engineering firm, Jaros, Baum & Bolles, Richard Baum also represented the second generation of his family to serve on the Mount Sinai Board. He provided wise counsel for the building of facilities to house the new Medical School and other essential construction projects, and played an important role on the Board's Academic Affairs Committee. We extend our condolences to his daughters, Kathryn and Judith, and to the entire Baum family. Peter W. May, Chairman Kenneth L. Davis, M.D. President and CEO
