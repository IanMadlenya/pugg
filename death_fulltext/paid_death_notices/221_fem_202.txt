
        RYAN--Dorothy Devereaux. Devoted wife of the late William Henry Ryan. Mother of the late George Devereaux Ryan. Survived by her loving daughter, Betsy Ryan, of 150 East 72nd Street, and her beloved friend Betty Seeler, of Forest Hills. Also survived by her dear sister-in-law, Mrs. Joseph C. Devereaux, of Manhasset. Mass of the Resurrection to be celebrated at the Church of St. Ignatius Loyola, at 10 am, on Tuesday, June 28th. Interment private. A memorial service in her honor to be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Isabel O'Neill Foundation.
