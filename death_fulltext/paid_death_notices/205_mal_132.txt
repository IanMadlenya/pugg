
        RADIN--Arthur. Died on February 8 at North Ridge Medical Center in Fort Lauderdale, FL. at age 89. He was past President of Victory Container Corp. Beloved husband for almost sixty years of Caroline. Devoted father of Howard, Michael and Alice Radin and loving grandfather of Cynthia, Alan and Daniel Landy, Sara Katz and Mark Radin. Burial Tuesday 1PM at New Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, N.J.
