
        JOHNSON--Paul C. Novelist, carpenter, peace activist and homesteader, formerly of New Mexico and Brooklyn, passed away peacefully at home, with family, in Las Vegas, New Mexico, on November 20, 2006. A memorial celebration will be held for him at the Judson Memorial Church, 55 Washington Square South, NYC at 1:00pm, on Saturday May 26, 2007. All friends welcome. 505-799-6972.
