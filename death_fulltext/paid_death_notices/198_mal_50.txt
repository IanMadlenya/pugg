
        FARREL-Franklin ''Tot'' III. Age 95, of North Branford, CT, died suddenly July 2, 2003. He is survived by his wife, Sallie Gibson Farrel of North Branford, his children, Sallie F. Brown, Franklin ''Toby'' Farrel IV, Lisa F. Totman, 10 grandchildren and 15 great grandchildren. There will be a memorial service in the Fall at a time to be announced. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Peabody Museum of Natural History, 170 Whitney Ave., New Haven, CT., 06511 or to the Yale Hockey Association, care of the Dept. of Athletics, PO Box 208216, New Haven, CT 06520-8216. For information: The Jenkins-King Funeral Home, 12 Franklin Street, Ansonia, CT. 06401.
