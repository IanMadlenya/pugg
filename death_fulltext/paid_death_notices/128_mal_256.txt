
        PAKULA-Peter F. 5/20/39-9/15/97. Of Amsterdam, The Netherlands, formerly of New York City. Adoring and adored husband of Marlies Tander, cherished and devoted son of the late Ruth and Leonard, loving and beloved brother of Judy. Cousin, nephew and friend to many. Died suddenly and peacefulliy in his sleep. An inspiration and a joy forever in our memories. Gone too soon, home at last. Memorial service New York City, Sunday, September 28, details to be announced.
        PAKULA-Peter F. Darling brother and protector-death separates and will reunite us. Nothing can take you from me. XXX OOO. J.
