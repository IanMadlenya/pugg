
        SHIMON--Lucille (Libby) died Friday, March 16, 2007, with her beloved daughter and granddaughter at her side. Libby possessed a fierce intelligence and creative abilities in poetry and needle work. Her loyalty and devotion to those she loved were endless. She is survived by her loving husband Paul, children Judith and Benjamin, granddaughter Hilary, sisters Estelle, Trudy and brother Myron. She lives in our hearts.
