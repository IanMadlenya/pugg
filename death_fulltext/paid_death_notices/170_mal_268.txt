
        ROSENTHAL-Harry. Age 79, of Valley Stream, NY. March 20, 2001. Beloved husband of Ruth . Loving father of Richard. Loving Opa of Max. Loving father-in-law of Stacy Fischer Rosenthal. Born in Germany on December 18, 1921, Harry was a former president of Temple Gates of Zion in Valley Stream. He donated his time to Meals on Wheels and Winthrop Hospital. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to Camp Ramapo Anchorage, Call 845-876-8403
