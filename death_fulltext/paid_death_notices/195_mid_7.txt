
        GODSICK-Marion Adlerman. On April 16, 2003 at age 94. Beloved wife of the late William Howard Godsick, M.D., loving mother of Steven and his wife Jane and Peter and his wife Aileen, cherished grandmother of Jeffrey, Christopher, Anthony and Victoria and great-grandmother of Griffin, Samantha, Trevor, Emily, Eliza, Charlotte and Isabella. Loving sister of Leona and Elaine. She was the daughter of Sadie and Judge Jerome Adlerman. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Funeral private.
        GODSICK-Marion Alderman. The trustees and staff of the NYC Police Foundation express their most sincere sympathy to Steven and Peter Godsick on the passing of their mother. Our deepest condolences are extended to their wives, Jane and Eileen, and the entire family. George D. Benjamin, Chairman Pamela D. Delaney, President NYC Police Foundation, Inc
