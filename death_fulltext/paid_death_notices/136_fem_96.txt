
        BOZYAN-Margaret Bliss. Of Hamden, CT, and Middletown, RI. Died at home on May 13 at age 97. The widow of H. Frank Bozyan, Yale University Organist Emeritus, she is survived by four children, Elinor B. Warburg, Gladys B. Lavine, Margaret B. Jefferys and Frank Arakel Bozyan; eleven grandchildren; and seven greatgrandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, May 20, at 11 AM, in Dwight Memorial Chapel at Yale University. For information, contact Beecher & Bennett Funeral Home, 203288-0800.
