
        CUNNINGHAM - RUNDLES - Mary Alice. New York City, September 28th, 1997, age 84. Beloved mother of Ward, Susanna, Charlotte, daughter-in-law Marietta, son-inlaw Jim, grandmother of Kiki and Alison, sister of Jane. Teacher of 19 years of classes of medical students at New York University Medical Center. A burial service was held October 1 for the immediate family at the Cathedral of St. John The Divine. Contributions in her memory may be sent to The Children's Blood Foundation, 333 East 38th Street, New York, New York 10016-2745.
