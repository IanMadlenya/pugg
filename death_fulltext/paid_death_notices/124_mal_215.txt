
        FINKELSTEIN-Irving C., 98, of Bal Harbour, FL., passed away May 17, 1997. He is survived by daughter Mrs. Erna Finkelstein Kislak, Grandchildren; Todd (Nancy) Kislak, Marc (Lisa) Kislak and Michael (Linda) Fields, Great Grandchildren; Paul Fields, Michelle, Sarah and Sasha Kislak. Owner of Finkelstein Sewing Machine Co. The largest second hand sewing machine dealer in the world. Inventor of a Spreading Machine called C.R.A. (Cutting Room Appliances). Services Wednesday May 21, 1997 in Miami.
