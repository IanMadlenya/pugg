
        WYCKOFF -- Dr. DeWitte Campbell. The Wyckoff House & Association mourns the passing of Cam, Thomas W. Synott Prof. at Princeton Theological Seminary from 1954-83 and a great friend and pillar of the Association. Cam attended the first meeting in 1937 and served as Vice President and Editor. He leaves a legacy in his generous support for the Pieter Claesen Wyckoff House. E. Lisk Wyckoff, Jr., Chair, and the Board of Director
