
        GRODY -- Jeannette (nee Kessler), of Pearl River, NY on September 7, 2004. She lived to be 100. She was a devoted mother, loving grandmother and great grandmother. Mrs. Grody was a former teacher for the New York City Public School System. She is survived by Ruth Hamer of Pearl River and Donald Grody of NYC, NY. Predeceased by her husband Charles. Funeral service is 10:30 AM Thursday, September 9, 2004 at Wyman-Fisher Funeral Home, Inc., 100 Franklin Ave., Pearl River. Burial will follow at Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, NJ.
