
        SHAPIRO-Alexander. Died Aug. 29, 1997, at his home in his 94th year. Beloved husband to the late Rose for over 65 years, father of Irwin (Ruth) Shapiro and Barbara (Manfred) Joseph, grandfather of Paula and Howard Friedman, Deborah & Warren Hammerschlag, Michael & Faiga Joseph, Samuel & Monica Shapiro, David Joseph, Jonathan Shapiro & bride-to-be Hannah, and fifteen great grandchildren. He was Founder and Chairman of ALRO. Throughout his life he insisted on real values and taught this lesson to his family. He will surely be missed.
