
        GILMORE-Samuel. Passed away April 17, 1997, Palm Beach, Fla. Loving, devoted husband to the late Libbie Ritter Gilmore. Steadfast, caring friend to Tony, Ursula and devotees: Jane, Nancy, Jana, Fara, Ann Marie, Marian and Barbara. Former member of the City Athletic Club & Old Oaks Country Club in New York. He was a fine gentleman, kind & compassionate and will be missed. Interment Maimonides Cemetery, Elmont, NY, Friday, April 25, 1997, 11 A.M.
