
        CONGDON--James B. died peacefully on January 14, 2007, at Cathedral Village, Philadelphia, PA. Born in Cape Town in 1924, and raised in Cedarhurst, New York, he attended St. George's School and graduated from Yale University. He served in the Pacific as an officer in the Navy during World War II. With his late wife, Eleanor Lee Congdon, he raised five children in Bedford, New York. He moved to Philadelphia and was remarried to Annette Ballard Congdon in 1994. He is survived by his wife; by four children, Hope Callen, Celia Congdon, Nina Thayer, and Charles Congdon; by two step-daughters, Nancy Ballard and Caroline Ballard; and by 13 grandchildren, two step-grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. His son, James Congdon, Jr., predeceased him. Service at St. Mary's Church, Cathedral Village, 600 E. Cathedral Rd., Philadelphia, PA, Friday, January 19, at noon, with reception to follow at Cathedral Village. Gifts may be made to American Red Cross -SEPA Chapter, 23rd and Chestnut Streets, Room 329, Philadelphia, PA 19103.
