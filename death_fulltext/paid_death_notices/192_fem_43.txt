
        TOFFEL-Hattie. 90. Of Lawrence, New York, and Palm Beach, Florida, passed away on December 30th, 2002. She is survived by her loving husband Harry, daughter Sandra Kaiser, brother Harold Friedman, sister-in-law Yetta Epstein, grandchildren Kenneth, Andrew and Stacy Kaiser and great-grandchildren Adam, Steven, Margot, Jordan and Katie Kaiser. Services were held on January 2, 2003 at Menorah Gardens of Palm Beach.
