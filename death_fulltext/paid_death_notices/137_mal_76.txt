
        HUMPHREYS-Ronald R. on Monday, June 8, 1998 of Manhattan, NY. Father of Olivia K. & Colin A. both of Bloomfield. Son of Richard and Ellen Shaddock Humphreys of Suffern, NY. Brother of Kathleen Adam of Clifton Park, NY, Nancy Zelen of Suffern, NY and Brian Humphreys of Manhattan, NY. Former husband of Carol Meyers Humphreys of Bloomfield. Relatives and friends are invited to attend the funeral from the O'Boyle Funeral Home, 309 Broad Street, Bloomfield, Thursday at 9:30 AM. The funeral mass will be offered in Immaculate Conception Church, Montclair at 10:30 AM. Friends may call Tuesday 7-9 PM and Wednesday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. For those who wish, in lieu of flowers, the family requests memorial donations be made to the Children's Brain Tumor Foundation, 274 Madison Avenue, Suite 1301, NY, NY 10016 or the State University of New York College at Oswego Athletic Association in his memory would be appreciated.
