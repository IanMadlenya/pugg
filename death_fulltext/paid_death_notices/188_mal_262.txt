
        GINSBURG-Harriet & Jeffrey. The Board of Directors, Officers and Employees of Seeds of Peace extend their condolences to our esteemed Board Member, Alan Ginsburg, and his family on the loss of his wife, Harriet, and his son, Jeffrey. Harriet's strength and love kept Alan and the family together and allowed them all to make meaningful contributions to the world. We extend our deepest love and condolences to Alan, his children, grandchildren and Diane. Words cannot express how sad we are. Seeds of Peace
