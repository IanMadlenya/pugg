
        KUHN-Jack W. Jr. The Officers and Board of Trustees of the Union of American Hebrew Congregations profoundly mourn the death of a cherished friend and colleague. A long time Trustee, Jack served our sacred cause with caring concern and dedication to the great ideals of our faith. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his loving wife, Heloise, his children and all his loved ones. Jerome H. Somers, Chrmn Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, Pres Rabbi Alexander M. Schindler President Emeritus
