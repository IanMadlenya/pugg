
        NAPOLITANO--Joseph. May 6, 1918 to January 14, 2004. Beloved husband of the late Jennie and brother of the late Michael, Min and Jennie. He is survived by his daughter Catherine, son-in-law Charles Cicchetti, son Anthony and many relatives and friends. Donations in his memory to Avesboro Elementary PTA, 1605 Avesboro Road, Garner, NC 27529 or Hospice of Wake County, 1300 St Mary's Street, Raleigh, North Carolina 27605 would be appreciated. He was a kind and gentle man. May he rest in peace.
