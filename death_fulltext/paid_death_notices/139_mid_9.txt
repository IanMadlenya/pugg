
        GORDON-Yaffa. AMIT is deeply grieved at the passing of its beloved Honorary National Treasurer. A woman of noble character and spirit, Yaffa lived a life dedicated to Torah values and contributed much to the advancement of AMIT and the thousands of children at risk in Israel. She was a devoted supporter of the State of Israel and the cause of religious Zionism. Heartfelt condolences to her beloved husband Isadore, devoted sons David, Nachum and Daniel, and the entire family. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Director
        GORDON-Yaffa. We mourn the passing of Yaffa Gordon, beloved mother of our esteemed partner Nahum L. Gordon and our esteemed retired partner David Z. Gordon. We extend to her devoted husband Isidore, to Nahum and Barbara, David and Karen, Daniel and Beatrice and her grandchildren and great-grandchildren the prayer that Yaffa Gordon's memory be a blessing to them and that they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Sherman & Gordon PC
        GORDON-Yaffa. Riverdale Jewish Center extends profound condolences to Isidore Gordon on the loss of his wife and David Gordon on the loss of his mother. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President
