
        BLECHNER - Hannah. Ohr Torah Stone Institutions of Israel records with sorrow the passing of Hannah Blechner, beloved wife of Norbert Blechner, of blessed memory. The Blechner College of Judaic Studies proudly bears their name dispatching hundreds of ambassadors of Jewish knowledge and secular learning to communities throughout the world. May the Almighty comfort her children Barbara, Jane and Mark and her grandchildren and great grandchildren. Shlomo Riskin Chancellor $(6$)BLECHNER--Hannah. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the sister of our member, Margaret Kahn. May her entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Joseph S.
