
        REIS-Donald J., MD of New York City and Stonington, CT, beloved husband of Cornelia Langer Noland and stepfather of Lyn, Cady and William Noland, died at age 69 on November 1, 2000. He was the George C. Cotzias Distinguished Professor of Neurology and Neuroscience at Cornell Medical Center and a renowned international neuroscience pioneer. Doctor Reis trained more than one hundred scientists around the world and received numerous awards for his work throughout a long and illustrious career. A memorial service is scheduled at the Century Association, 7 West 43rd Street on Friday, November 17th at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the Department of Neurology and Neuroscience, WeillCornell Medical Center, 1300 York Avenue (Room F613), NY, NY 10021 or to the Citizens Commit tee for New York City, 305 Seventh Avenue, New York, NY 10001.
