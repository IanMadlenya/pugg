
        BLOCK--Ruth. May 18, 1921-June 21, 2005. Hunter College graduate and former longtime photo editor of the New York Times Sunday Magazine. Predeceased by her parents, Robert and Bertha (Ente) Block. Survived by brother Lawrence W. Block, nephews Peter H. (Pat) and Thomas S. Block, (Ellen) niece Elizabeth H. Block, grand-niece and nephew Marissa and Andrew Block, and dear friends Ursula Mahoney, Rebecca Abramson, and June Schwartz. Memorial service at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave., Friday, June 24th, at 11AM. Burial Friday, 1PM, at New Montefiore Cemetery, Farmingdale, NY. Donations can be made to the Scholarship and Welfare Fund at Hunter College.
