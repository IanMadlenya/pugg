
        PAPARO--Sophie, 101. A resident of the Sephardic home, passed away January 17, 2006. She was a devoted wife to Meyer, who is deceased. Sophie lived for all the joys and wonders of her family. Two of her children, Irving & Mildred, are deceased. She is survived by her daughter Rita Elias, 10 grandchildren, 21 great-grandchildren and 5 great - great - grandchildren. Funeral will be held 11 AM Mount Hebron Cemetery on Thursday, January 19. Donations may be made in her memory to the Sephardic Home, 2266 Cropsey Ave, Brooklyn, NY 11214.
