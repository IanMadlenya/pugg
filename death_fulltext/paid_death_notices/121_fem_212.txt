
        ALTER-Margaret L. (Marge), of Walnut Creek, CA., formerly of Scarsdale, NY, passed away January 19, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Alfred, loving mother of Caryl Dreiblatt and Donna LaCroix, cherished grandmother of Nicole and Allison LaCroix and Stefanie Dreiblatt, dear mother-in-law of David Dreiblatt, devoted aunt of Diane Paul, and loving friend of John Tworek. Memorial service was held. Remembrances may be made to the American Heart Association or a charity of your choice. She will be missed by all.
