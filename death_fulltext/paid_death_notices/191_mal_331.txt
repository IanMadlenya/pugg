
        SILVERBUSH-Seymour Herbert, on December 29, 2002. Born August 12, 1916, in NYC. Retired independent businessman, union organizer, and veteran of WWII. Beloved husband of Elsie Wagner Silverbush for 62 years. Devoted and loving father of Jeffrey and Michael, and father-in-law of Andrea, Lenore and Aliza. Cherished grandfather of Rhona, Lori, Robert, Mark, Benjamin and Adina, and grandfather-inlaw of George and Tom. Loved and respected by all who knew him, for his generous spirit, egalitarian values, warmth, wisdom and humor. He will be deeply missed and always remembered.
