
        McGOVERN-John H., M.D. The Boards, Faculty, and Staff of Weill Cornell Medical College and NewYork - Presbyterian Hospital mourn the passing of our esteemed colleague, Dr. John H. McGovern, who was a valued member of our Department of Urology for nearly half a century. Highly regarded by his peers throughout the U.S. and the world, Dr. McGovern served as president of the Society of Pediatric Urology (198081), the American Association of Clinical Urologists (1988-89), and the American Urological Association (1989-90). A graduate of Columbia University and Downstate Medical College, Dr. McGovern joined The New York HospitalCornell Medical Center in 1957 as a resident in urology and was clinical professor of urology when he retired in 1999. We extend our condolences to his wife and family.
