
        O'ROURKE-Patricia F. Died on May 8, 1999 at her home in Yonkers, N.Y. Formerly of the Bronx, she was the only child of the late James and Frances O'Rourke. A dedicated teacher, she was the retired Principal of Intermediate School 180 in the Bronx. Friends may call at the Hastings Funeral Home, Inc., 15 Spring St, HastingsOn-Hudson on Wednesday and Thursday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass at Christ the King Church on Friday at 11 AM. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Patricia O'Rourke Scholarship Fund, Aquinas High School, 685 East 182nd Street, Bronx, N.Y. 10457-1801 preferred.
