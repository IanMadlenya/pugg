
        KELLNER-Warren, age 84, of NYC, on Thursday, December 23, 1999 in Norwalk, CT. A member of Temple Emanu-El of the City of New York, the former President and owner of the Kitty Kelly Shoe Corporation, and in later years was Vice-President and General Manager of Michele Perkins and Associates. Dearly beloved husband and best friend of Natalie Steinhardt Kellner. Cherished father of Michele, John, Herbert (Skip) and Mary. Adored and adoring grandfather of Joanna, Catherine and Jennifer. Memorial contributions to American Heart Association or American Cancer Society. Services will be private.
        KELLNER-Warren. I'll always love you and you will always be my best friend, most wonderful Pop-Pops. Joanna (Lolly-Lora)
        KELLNER-Warren. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Warren Kellner, our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
