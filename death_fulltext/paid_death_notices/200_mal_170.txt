
        DUBLER--Philip, age 83, was a very remarkable person who, in life, gave freely and enjoyed every moment he shared with his devoted wife Shirley. He was an adoring father and special friend to his children Karen and Steven. He was a loving grandfather to Justin and Alex and shared a very special loving relationship with his grandson Scott. He leaves behind his loving sister Gertrude and brother-in-law Jack, daughter-in-law Pam, his adoring sister-in-law Lois and brother-in-law Wade, his cousin Lester and a multitude of cousins and devoted friends.
