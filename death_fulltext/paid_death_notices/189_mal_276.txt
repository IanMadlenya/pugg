
        COSTIKYAN-Kent R., Jr. Of South Norwalk, CT, on October 21, 2002, aboard his sailboat in Norfolk, VA. Beloved father to Kent R. III, Timothy, and Warren, stepfather to Elisabeth Worthington Ruyter, Andrew Burris Jenkins, Andrea Georgia Jenkins, Susan Delatour Cary. Husband to the late Elisabeth Delatour Costikyan, and brother to Thomas Wilson Costikyan. Kent was a graduate of The Hotchkiss School and Yale University, Class of 1948. He was an avid racing and cruising sailor. He passed following a final run south down The Chesapeake Bay. A celebration of his life will be held at 1 P.M. Sunday October 27, 2002 at Saint Luke's Episcopal Church in Darien, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to The Quebec-Labrador Foundation/Atlantic Center For The Environment, 55 South Main Street, Ipswich, MA 01938.
