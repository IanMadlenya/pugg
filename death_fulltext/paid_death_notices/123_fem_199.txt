
        FINS-Ruth (Lovett) The family of Ruth Fins mourns the death of their beloved wife and mother on April 27, 1997. Devoted wife, loving mother, and loyal friend to many. She is survived by her husband of 40 years Hank, daughter Robin L. Fins, son Dr. Joseph J. Fins and his wife Rabbi Amy B. Ehrlich. Funeral Services will be held at Central Synagogue of Nassau County, 430 DeMott Av, Rockville Centre, NY on Wed, Apr 30, 1997 at 11:30am. Contributions in her memory may be sent to Central Synagogue of Nassau County.
