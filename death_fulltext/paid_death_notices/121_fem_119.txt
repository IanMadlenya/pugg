
        MILLER-Helen Pearl, 97, beloved wife of Paul, mother of Fanchon Scheier, mother-in-law of Saul, grandmother of Joshua and Joanna, great grandmother of Eliza, former soprano soloist of the YMHA, and Temple of the Covenant. She had a great appetite and zest for life.
        MILLER-Helen. Temple Shaaray Tefila records her death with great sorrow. We offer sympathy to Paul, Fanchon and all her family. After a long life, our sanctuary will embrace her finally on Wednesday, February 19, at 1:30 P.M. Harvey M. Tattelbaum, Rabbi Dr. Ellen Saxl, President
