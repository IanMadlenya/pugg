
        WEAVER-William B. Age 49. On March 21, 2000. Beloved husband of Beth, loving father of Courtney and Charlotte, devoted son of Leonard and Dorothy Weaver, loving brother of Steven and David. Bill graduated from Duke University and received his MBA and J.D. degrees from Northwestern University. He was a prominent investment banker with First Boston and Lehman Brothers and was most recenlty President of Weaver Capital. Bill also served on the Board of the Duke University Library. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him. Memorial service St. Ignacious Loyola Church, Park Ave at 84 St, on Tuesday April 4th, 10am. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The Church of St. Ignatius Loyola, 980 Park Ave, NY, NY 10028 or Duke University Library, Box 90197, Durham, NC 27708-0197
