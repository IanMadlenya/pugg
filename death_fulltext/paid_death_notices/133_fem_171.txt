
        ROSS-Phyllis Shartsis. Of Beverly Hills, CA. Died February 15, 1998, after a brief illness. Formerly of New York, she had been an executive with ABC Radio Network. Ms. Ross is survived by her sisters Elaine Max of Charleston, W.Va. Diane Wax of Washington, D.C. and her aunt and uncle, Florence and Herbert Duchan of Plantation, FL. She leaves many wonderful, caring friends in New York and California.
