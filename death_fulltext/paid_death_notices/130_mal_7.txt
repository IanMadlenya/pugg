
        AUERSWALD-Dr. E.H. (Dick), October 23, 1997, San Francisco, at age 71. Dr. Auerswald was a founder of the family therapy movement and the originator of the ecosystemic therapeutic paradigm. In his work, he developed family-centered, community-based, health delivery systems in New York and Hawaii. He served on the Board of Directors of the American Family Therapy Association and the journal ''Family Process''. He is survived by his companion Alma Zito Menn, his children Sarah and David and their families, and his niece Colette and nephew Philip. Donations may be made to the ACLU.
