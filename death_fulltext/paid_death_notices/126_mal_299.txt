
        WANNING-Andrews of Saugerties, New York, and Blue Hill, Maine, age 84. Professor Emeritus of Bard College, 17th century scholar and lover of literature, music and sailing. Graduate of Choate, Yale and Cambridge University, Professor Wanning also taught at Yale and Harvard. Stricken while traveling in England, he died in Kingston, New York, on July 25. He leaves his former wife, Patricia Emerson Watson, his brother Thomas Edwards Wanning, his sons Rufus and Thomas Wanning and daughter Esther Wanning, and two grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at the Bard College Chapel at noon Friday, Aug 1. Donations may be made to Bard or Magdalene College, Cambridge, England.
        WANNING-Andrews. The administration, trustees, faculty, alumni/ae of Bard College mourn the passing of Andrews Wanning, Professor Emeritus of English at Bard and a longtime friend and supporter of the College. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Leon Botstein, President David E. Schwab II, Chairman Board of Trustees
