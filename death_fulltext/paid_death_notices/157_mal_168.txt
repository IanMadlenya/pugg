
        HOLMES-Walter J., 93, formerly of the Bronx died Friday in Albany, NY. He retired as Director of Public Relations for NYS Comptroller Arthur Levitt. He was a 60 year member of the Bronx and Albany Lions Clubs. He was a past district governor of Lions district 20-R2, past International Counselor and receipient of the Lions Melvin Jones Award. He was the husband of Grace Pritchard Holmes; father of Ellen Misita of North Merrick, NY; Grandfather of Andrew Misita. Contibutions to Albany Lions Club or Bronx Lions Club.
