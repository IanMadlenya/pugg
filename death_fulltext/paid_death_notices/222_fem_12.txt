
        TREDWELL--Constance C. Of Essex, CT. Died June 28, in Middletown, CT, age 75. Performed with The Noteworthies on numerous TV variety shows and many NYC nightclubs. Practiced law in Hartford, CT. An active member of the Alumni Association of Rider College and President of the Board of Ivoryton Playhouse Foundation. Survived by her brother Richard.
