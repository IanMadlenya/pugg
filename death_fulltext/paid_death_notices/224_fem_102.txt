
        ZWEIG--Harriet. On September 11, heart and soul of husband Sol, married for 59 years. Beloved mother to Peter and his wife Linda (who became a daughter). She was exceptionally proud of her lovely accomplished granddaughters Julie and Lea. As a teacher in New York for 35 years she was unbelievably loved by both pupils and parents. Memorial service in the Pines of Boca Lago Country Club, Florida on Friday, September 16 at 11 AM.
