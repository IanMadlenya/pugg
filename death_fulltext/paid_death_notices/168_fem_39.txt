
        WINEAPPLE-Maxine B. Died peacefully in her sleep early Wednesday morning, January 3, 2001 after a long and courageous ordeal with Alzheimer's disease. Born in Boston, Massachusetts, she lived in Scarsdale, NY then Manhattan. and most recently in DelRay Beach, FL. Maxine is survived by her son John, daughter Patty, brother Arthur, grandsons Andrew and James. Maxine graduated from Wheelock College in Boston and received her M.A. from Columbia University. Maxine will long be remembered for her style and elegance coupled with her beauty and brains. Maxine's life will be celebrated with a private memorial service with her immediate family.
