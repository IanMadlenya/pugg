
        REID-Mrs. Fergus. The Officers and Directors of The Garden Club of America express deep sorrow on the death of their former President, Mrs. Fergus Reid, Jr., and extend deepest sympathy to her family and friends. Etheldreda Seabury Reid served the Garden Club with great distinction and extraordinary ability as its 13th President (1953-1956), and in many other capacities. Beginning in 1944, she served as Recording Secretary, Treasurer, Vice President, Director and Chairman of the Nominating and Policy Committees. Mrs. Reid, a member of the Piscataqua Garden Club of York Harbor, Maine since 1933, was the recipient of many awards from The Garden Club of America during her long and distinguished career, including the Medal of Merit and the Zone Creative Leadership Award. Mrs. Frederick C. Hansen, Jr., President
