
        SIERRA-Marcelino F., PhD. The administration, faculty, staff and students of the State University of New York Health Science Center at Brooklyn mourn the untimely passing of our colleague, Marcelino F. Sierra, PhD, Associate Professor of Pathology and Director of Clinical Microbiology at University Hospital of Brooklyn and Kings County Hospital Center. His intellect, professionalism and warmth will long be missed. Our condolences go to his wife, Dr. Lorraine Clarke, and all family members and friends. Eugene B. Feigelson, MD Interim President
