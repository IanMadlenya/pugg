
        WENCKHEIM--Countess Dorothea, on April 4, 2007. Beloved wife of Count Nicholas Wenckheim. Cherished by family and friends. Died peacefully at home. An accomplished painter, her works are exhibited in museums and private collections around the world. A brilliant woman who lived life to the fullest. Services on Tuesday, April 10th, at 6pm, Frank E. Campbell Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave. on 81st St. In lieu of flowers donations to Doctors Without Borders.
