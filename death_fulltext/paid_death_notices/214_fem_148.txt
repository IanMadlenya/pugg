
        SINGER--Ruth (Shapiro). On November 18, 2004. Widow of the late Max Singer and longtime companion of the late Louis Cantor, both attorneys. Much beloved mother of Stephanie and Lise, grandmother of Gillian Wander, mother-in-law of John DeGroat and sister of Beatrice Fischer, Jack Shapiro and Francine Ellmann. Ruth was an accomplished sculptor, the President of the New York Browning Society, a lover of language, wit, conversation, art, poetry and song. Treasured aunt, great-aunt and friend. She will be sadly missed by all who knew and loved her. Service Sunday, 11:15 a.m. at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
