
        CIRILLO--Nicholas W., 71, of Bedford Village, New York died in Boca Raton, Florida on Thursday, January 29, 2004. He will be deeply missed by his loving wife, Joan. He is also survived by his children: Donna (Ken) Wright, Gregory (Lisa) Cirillo, Nicholas (Marianne) Cirillo, Jr., Paul (Laura) Cirillo and nine beloved grandchildren. Also survived by his brother William Cirillo and sister Monica Rippa. A memorial service in celebration of his life will be held on Saturday, January 31, 2004, from 3-4 P.M. at Babione Funeral Home (561-395-8787) in Boca Raton.
