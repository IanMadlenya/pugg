
        MAYERS-Harry. On October 17, 2002 at the age of 93. Devoted husband of Helen Mayers. Beloved father of Melvin Mayers and Phyllis Mayers. Dear father-in-law of Elaine Mayers. Adored grandfather of Candace Shaw, Brian Krinsky and Jennifer Mayers. Proud great grandfather of Harleigh, Zachary and Harrison Shaw. Brother of Florence Goldberg. He will always be loved and remembered. Services took place on Friday, October 18, 2002.
