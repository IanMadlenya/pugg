
        MORRIS--DuBois S. Jr., died January 12, 2005, in Ferrisburg, VT. Born in Kuling, China, July 3, 1911, to Alice Buell & DuBois Morris. Survived by his beloved wife, Mary Derby Morris, five devoted children, 10 grandchildren & five greatgrandchildren. Alumnus of Hotchkiss & Princeton. Memorial service at the Emmanuel Church in Weston, CT 11:00 AM, Saturday, January 22. Contributions in his name to Addison County Home Health of Middlebury, VT.
