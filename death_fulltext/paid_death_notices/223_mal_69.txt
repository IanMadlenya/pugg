
        SILVER--Louis of Boca Raton, FL. died on August 3 in his 101st year. Adored father, grandfather and great-grandfather, he leaves his children Robert (Judi) of Boca Raton, Don (Bobby) of Hartland, VT. and Rona Rutchik (Martin) of Norwich, CT., seven grandchildren and eight greatgrandchildren with whom he shared a wonderful life.
