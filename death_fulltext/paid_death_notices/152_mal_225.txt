
        CHERRY-James R. The NYU Community mourns the passing of its long-time member, James Cherry. He served this institution with distinction for more than thirty years as a member of the Medical Center Board of Trustees, the University's Board of Trustees, the NYU Institute of Fine Arts' Board of Trustees, and a past chairman of the Albert Gallatin Associates. He was a kind and generous man, and he will be greatly missed. L. Jay Oliva, President Martin Lipton, Chairman Board of Trustees
        CHERRY-James R. Remembered with affection for his kindness and dedication to the arts. In 1992 he received the Town Hall Friend of the Arts Award. He will be missed by us all. Marvin Leffler, President The Town Hall Foundation
