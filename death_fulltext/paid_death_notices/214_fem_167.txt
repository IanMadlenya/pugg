
        CONSOR--Mollie (nee Wiener) 92, loving wife of Robert (deceased). Passed away peacefully on November 22, 2004. She is survived by her children Harvey and Yvette Consor and Helaine (Arthur, deceased) Friedman and grandchildren Glenn and Gwen Consor, Jill Consor, Lisa Consor and Jarrett Behar and David Friedman. Mollie was a devoted daughter and wife, a deeply loved mother and dearest friend to her grandchildren. She resided in Long Beach for over 60 years and was a tireless worker for Temple Israel, Hadassah, B'nai B'rith and Long Beach Hospital. Mollie was held in high respect by all who came to know her. May she rest in peace with her beloved Bob. Services Wednesday 10:30am at I.J. Morris Inc., 46 Greenwich St., Hempstead, N.Y. 516-486-2500. Interment Beth Moses Cemetery. Donations may be made to Hadassah in her memory.
