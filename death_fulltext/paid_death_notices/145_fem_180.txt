
        SCHWABE-Gretl (formerly Gretl Spanier). Died February 20, at the age of 85, at her home in New York City. Born in Krumbach, Germany, she immigrated to the United States in 1936. She married her late husband, Herman Schwabe in 1940. Beloved mother of Jerrold and the late Ronald, and cherished grandmother of Scott and Craig. Services will be held at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amersterdam Avenue, on February 22, at 12 noon.
