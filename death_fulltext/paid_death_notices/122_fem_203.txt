
        SABO-Mildred Catherine, passed away in California on March 23, 1997. Daughter of the late William Ronald and the late Susan Adelia Ronald. She was born in Brooklyn and lived in Lakewood, N.J. Employed at the Dime Savings Bank in Brooklyn until 1974. Wife of Eugene G. Sabo, predeceased by previous husband, Alfred E. Kayser. Survived by son William Ronald Jones and daughter in law, Beverly Ann Jones of San Francisco, sister Dorothy Hilziner and 3 grandchildren. Services Saturday 10:00 A.M. at D'Elia Funeral Home, Lakewoood, New Jersey.
