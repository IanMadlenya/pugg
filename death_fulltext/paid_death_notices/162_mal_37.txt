
        GOLDSTONE-Abraham M. Of Forest Hills, NY and Bethlehem, NH, and formerly of Brooklyn, NY, passed away on July 4, 2000 at the age of 91. He was a graduate of Brown University and received his Master's from Columbia's Teachers College. After 37 years of service for the City of New York, he retired as Special Assistant to New York City's Director of Personnel, Harry I. Bronstein in 1974. Beloved son of Sarah and Jacob, he was the youngest brother of Rebecca, Al, Lou, Irving and Pearl. Adored by his wife Harriet, and his children, the late Susan and David, Abe was devoted to his family including his wife's mother, Anna Bloch, her twin sister Charlotte Schecter, his three wonderful nieces, Gail, Diane and Debbie, his daughter-inlaw Dorothy, and his beloved grandchildren, Joey, Daniel and Jenny. There was no limit to his generosity, love of learning, loyalty, friendship, and devotion to Judaism. He will be sorely missed by everyone whose life he touched from the Bethlehem Hebrew Congregation to the Jewish Communal Center in Brooklyn to Temple Gates of Prayer in Flushing. He is survived by his loving son, David, and his three grandchildren. Services Thursday, July 6, at 9:30 AM at Sinai Chapels, 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Flushing, New York. Shiva will be observed at David's home. Information: 718445-0300 or 718-261-7161.
