
        LEVINSON--Adeline, beloved wife of the late Bernard, the last of the Bahn sisters Shirley, Annie, Rosie, Betty, Sylvia and brother Marty, died Sept. 3, 2003 two months before her 83rd birthday. Cherished by daughter, Bonnie, son Mark, granddaughters Liza & Emily and son-inlaw Dr. Donald Kay. Known to her family as ''Aunt Edie'' and friends as Addie, she was a real New Yorker, former ''Miss Brooklyn'', who loved movies, theater, museums, and a good veal parmesan. She will be missed. Graveside service Wellwood Cemetery Friday, Sept. 5 at 2 PM.
