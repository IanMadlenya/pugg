
        MANNARA-Lydia P. On June 23, 2001 (in New York City). Beloved sister of Anthony and his wife Marina, devoted aunt of Alexander and Mark, loving daughter of the late Dr. Paul and Luciana Mannara, and caring friend of many. A Mass of Christian Burial wil be celebrated at St. Ignatius Loyola Church, Park Ave. and 84 St., Tuesday, June 26, at 9:30 AM. Visiting hours Monday, June 25, from 5 to 7 PM, at Frank E.
