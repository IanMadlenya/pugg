
        PAINE-Stephen D. The Board of Trustees and staff at the Whitney Museum of American Art note with great sadness the passing of our beloved patron and founding member of the Museum's Drawing Acquisition Committee, Stephen D. Paine. He will be warmly remembered for his steadfast support of American artists and his dedication to furthering the study and appreciation of American Art. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Susan. Flora M. Biddle, Hon. Chairman Leonard A. Lauder, Chairman Gilbert C. Maurer, President David A. Ross, Director
