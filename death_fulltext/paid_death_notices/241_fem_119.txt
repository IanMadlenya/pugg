
        NORMAN--Regina. One who loved life in New York, especially the world of theater and opera, her family and many friends -along with Sam, a prince among terriers -died on February 13, 2007. She is survived by her wonderful children Valerie Hovasapian and Frederick Hovasapian Jr.; her grandchildren Nadia, Claire and Frederick Hovasapian III, and her brothers Bruce Norman and Eric Norman. A memorial celebration of her life will be held in the near future.
