
        FEIT-Dudley. The Israel and Fennie Feit Foundation mourn the irreplaceable loss of our President, devoted husband of Hattie, beloved brother of Herbert. Loving father of Elliot and Nikki, Joseph and Surella, adored grandfather of Laurie and Zvi Adler, Alison and Jordan Mann, Neal, Jeremy, Rebecca and Jeffrey. Great-grandfather of Tuvia and Elisheva Adler. May his memory be a blessing to all Klal Yisroel.
        FEIT-Dudley. AMIT is deeply grieved by the passing of the beloved husband of our devoted Honorary National Secretary, Hattie Feit. A warm, compassionate, engaging personality, Dudley Feit epitomized the highest ideals of Torah, religious Zionism, and community activism. He touched the lives of thousands. Our hearts reach out to comfort his dear wife Hattie, devoted brother Herbert, beloved sons Elliot and Joseph, and the entire family. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Evelyn Blachor, National Pres. Marvin Leff, Executive Director
