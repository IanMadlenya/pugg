
        LAZERUS -- Gilbert (Gil), passed away on May 24, 2004. Born June 24, 1912, in New York City. Son of Jacob and Bessie Lazerus. Married in 1940 to his late beloved wife, Judith Kaplan. Devoted father of Bruce, June and daughter-in-law Allison. Doting grandfather of Jackie and Amanda. Dear friend since 1981 of the Hon. Shirley Kram. Yale 1931. Columbia Law 1934. Joined Stroock, Stroock & Lavan in 1945; partner beginning 1955; later, of counsel. Services private. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the charity of your choice.
