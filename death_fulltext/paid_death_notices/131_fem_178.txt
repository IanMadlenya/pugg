
        PASHMAN-Ruth Margulies. At age 86. Widow of the late Henry Margulies and Julius Pashman. Devoted mother of Leah Margulies and the late Ann Dee Goldschmidt. Beloved grandmother of Henry and Carl Goldschmidt and Ryan Margulies. Loving and good friend to stepdaughter, Mona Gross and her son, Ronald Cohen. Will be missed by her friends at Three Arrows and all who knew her. Services Sunday, 12 Noon, Hirsch & Sons, 1219 Jerome Avenue at 167 Street, Bronx.
