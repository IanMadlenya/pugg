
        FELLER--Sheila. Beloved mother of Marcy and Andrea, adored and adoring grandmother of Julian and Gabriel, cherished mother-inlaw of Gabrielle and David, died December 6th, 2006, after a brief illness. Writer and community activist, former child actress, she embraced life, and all those she loved, with passion. She is deeply missed by many. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th Street, Friday December 8th at 11:30AM.
