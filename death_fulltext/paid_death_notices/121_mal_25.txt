
        NOSHPITZ-Joseph D., M.D. On Tuesday, January 28, 1997. Dr. Joseph D. Noshpitz of Washington, D.C. Beloved husband of Charlotte Noshpitz. Devoted father of Claude Noshpitz. Loving brother of Ruth Kellman and Rose Bennett. Adoring grandfather of Jonathan Cummings. Services at the Danzansky-Goldberg Memorial Chapel, Inc., 1170 Rockville Pike, Rockville, MD., Sunday, February 2, 1997 at 11 A.M. Interment Mt. Lebanon Cemetery. The family will be in mourning at the late residence. Expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to The Joseph Dov Noshpitz Memorial Fund, The American Academy of Child and Adolescent Psychiatry, 3615 Wisconsin Avenue, N.W., Washington, D.C. 20016.
