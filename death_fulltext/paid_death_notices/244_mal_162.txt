
        SCHLAEGER--Ralph, M.D., died March 13, 2007. He was a Radiologist at Presbyterian Hospital and Professor at Columbia University, College of Physicians and Surgeons for 51 years. In recent years he served as a consultant at Presbyterian Hospital and an esteemed Emeritus Professor of Clinical Radiology at Columbia University. Born in Milwaukee, Wisconsin, he received B.S. and M.D. degrees from the University of Wisconsin. After serving as a Captain in the U.S. Army Medical Corps he did post doctoral studies at the University of Pennsylvania Graduate School of Medicine. A life long bachelor, he devoted his life to the practice and teaching of Radiology. He lived modestly, invested wisely and at his death bequeathed the bulk of his estate to The Ralph Schlaeger Charitable Foundation. His life will be celebrated at a Memorial Service to be held at 4:00 p.m., Friday, June 15, 2007 at the Pauline A. Hartford Memorial Chapel of the Columbia University Medical Center, 622 West 168th Street, in Manhattan.
