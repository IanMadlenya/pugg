
        CHESKY-Kenneth, M.D. Prominent NY cardiologist on Monday, June 5, age 78. After graduating from Emory Medical School, Dr. Chesky served in the Navy for 3 years. When he returned from service he spent 2 years interning at the prestigious Boston City Hospital. Dr. Chesky moved to NY where he began his private practive through which he served and was affiliated with the staff and faculty of Mount Sinai Hospital for almost 40 years up to his retirement in 1986. Dr. Chesky is survived by his brother, Julius Chesky, nephews, Jeffrey, Norman and David Chesky, niece, Doralee Chesky and great nephew, Barry Chesky.
