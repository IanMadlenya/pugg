
        BEDELL--Robert ''Bobby.'' Of St. Augustine, FL, and formerly of Long Beach, NY, November 27, 2004. Devoted loving son of Hannah. Beloved brother and brother-inlaw of Jack and Susan Bedell. Cherished uncle of Elissa and Andrew Silverman and Joanna Bedell. Brother of Gary. Joining his sweet late brother Chester. Family and friends will remember and miss the devoted, kind and most charismatic caring person. Vietnam Veteran--hero to all. He was 56.
