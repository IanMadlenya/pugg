
        BUZZEO-Louis. Of Pound Ridge, NY. (husband of Cornelia, deceased). Our loving father passed away peacefully on the morning of May 13, 1999. A WWII veteran, entrepreneur and creative spirit. He will be dearly missed by his brother Mario, his children Paula, Martin, Bernadette, Elizabeth, Marie and Anthony and sons-in-law Michael and Richard. Reposing at Craft Memorial Home, 40 Leicester Street, Port Chester, N.Y.
