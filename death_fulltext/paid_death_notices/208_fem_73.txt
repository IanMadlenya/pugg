
        PAULSON--Silvia B. Age 92, on May 8, 2004, died peacefully at home with her family. Beloved wife of the late Martin Friedlander and Philip Paulson. Cherished mother and mother-in-law of Judith Friedlander & Erwin Fleissner, Sara Adler Gottlieb & Paul Gottlieb. Adored stepmother of Howard & Elinor Yahm and Robert Paulson. Loving grandmother of Lisa Adler and Rebecca & Sarah Yahm. Scholar, teacher, lover of life. Service Monday, 11:15 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. at Amsterdam Ave. Donations in her memory may be made to Hunter College Foundation and John Kerry for President Campaign.
