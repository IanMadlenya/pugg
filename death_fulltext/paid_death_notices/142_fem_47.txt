
        WEITZENBERG-Ann, on November 4, 1998, died peacefully at her home in Scarsdale after a brief illness. Predeceased by her husband, Nat. Survived by her children, Sheila, Irene, and Stanley, and her grandchildren, Randi and Laurie. A most dignified, gracious and courageous woman. May she forever be bound up in the arms of God with her husband.
