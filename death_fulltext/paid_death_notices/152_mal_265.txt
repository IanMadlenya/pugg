
        CARLITZ - Leonard. Former James B. Duke Professor of Mathematics at Duke University (1932-1977), passed away Friday morning, September 17, in Pittsburgh, PA. Born in Philadelphia in 1907, Carlitz was one of the most prolific and insightful mathematics researchers of all time. His 770 publications on number theory and related topics appeared in 110 different professional journals. He is survived by two sons, Michael and Robert, and two granddaughters, Natasha and Ruth. Clara, his wife of 59 years, died in 1990.
