
        LOVEJOY-Allen F. The Council and membership of The American Numismatic Society, New York, mourn the loss of their esteemed Councillor and Patron, Allen F. Lovejoy, who died May 8, 1997. A member of the ANS governing board since 1988, he served as First Vice-President and member of the Executive Committee. Dedicated to the financial well being of the Society, he headed the ANS Development Committee. To his wife, Betty, his children and his grandchildren, the Society extends its deepest sympathy.
