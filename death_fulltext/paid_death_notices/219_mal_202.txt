
        ABRAMSON--David A., Ph.D. 90, on April 18, 2005. Beloved husband of 63 years of Lillian (Salwen) Abramson. Father of Joel (Blima) and Rebecca. Grandfather of Racheli (Abramson) and David Harris, Gabriel Abramson Rhodes and Shayna Abramson. Great grandfather of Hadas and Ariel Harris. Ph.D. in Research Design and Statistics. Innovative educator and psychologist. Past Director of Curriculum and Development and Educational Research, New York City Board of Education. WWII Veteran. We loved him dearly and will miss him and his distinct sense of humor.
