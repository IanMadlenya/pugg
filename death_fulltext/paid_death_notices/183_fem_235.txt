
        STEG-Dr. Doreen. Of Villanova, PA., loving wife, mother and grandmother died in her sleep on April 21, 2002. She was 74 years old and had been married for over 54 years to Dr. Leo Steg. After escaping from the German occupation of her birthplace in Belgium, she came to the U.S. and earned a degree from NYU and a PhD in Philosophy at the University of Pennsylvania. She was a professor at Drexel University and the founding chairman of the Department of Human Behavior and Development. Doreen was a world renowned expert in early childhood education and received the Gold Medal from the American Cybernetics Society and was a member of the Board of Directors of the International Society of Cybernetics. She loved the beach and her summer home in Loveladies, N.J. Doreen's great intellect, passion for life and love for her family and the State of Israel will be treasured and missed by all who knew her. Doreen is survived by her husband, three daughters, four grandsons, two siblings, and numerous nieces and nephews. All family and friends are invited today, April 24 at 1:00 PM to Robert Schoem Funeral Chapel, 150 West State Route 4, Paramus, N.J., 201843-9090. To honor Doreen's memory, please contribute to the Jewish Federation in your area.
