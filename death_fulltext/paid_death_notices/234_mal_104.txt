
        BARRETT--Arthur L. 88, on July 7, 2006, in Goshen, NY. Devoted husband of Mary B. Barrett, loving father of David and Didi, and proud grandfather of Alec and Annabel Barrett. Raised in Shanghai, China, educated at Grinnell College and Harvard, he was a dedicated teacher and retired chairman of the Middletown (NY) High School English Department.
