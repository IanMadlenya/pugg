
        BLOTNER--Norman David. Born December 16, 1918, in Beverly, MA. Resident of New Rochelle for 45 years. Died June 23, 2006. Harvard University, Class of 1940. Served in US Navy for five years during WWII, entering as ensign and achieved rank of lieutenant commander. Received a law degree from Harvard Law School, where he coached the Harvard lacrosse team. An All-American lacrosse player and avid athlete his entire life, he played tennis through 2005. After practicing law for six years, joined Lane Bryant as VP Real Estate, Legal Matters, and stayed until retirement. Predeceased by Helen Whitman and Ethel Schanzer. Survived by his loving companion Vera Rosen, of New Rochelle, and her children, and Lucy.
