
        HOCKSTADER-Leonard A. II, age 66, died suddenly on May 8 of acute asthma. He is survived by his wife, Diane Deschamps Hockstader, a son, Lee Hockstader, two daughters, Kalen Hockstader Holliday and Avery Diane Hockstader, a granddaughter, Pierson Holliday and a brother Alan Hockstader. A memorial service will be held at 3:30 PM, Monday, May 18, at Frank E. Campbell, 81st St. and Madison Ave. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to the American Lung Association, 1740 Broadway, NY 10023.
