
        LONGOBARDI-Violet. Widow of Vincent, mother of Alice Givan, mother-in-law of Curtis, grandmother of Ben and Becky, died on July 25 at the age of 91. She was a longtime and committed member of Brooklyn Friends Meeting and worked devotedly for the Mary McDowell Center for Learning. She had a humane and loving spirit. Her family and friends will miss her. Memorial meeting to be held in early September. Those who wish may make contributions in her memory to the Mary McDowell Center for Learning, 20 Bergen St., Brooklyn, NY 11201.
        LONGOBARDI-Violet. Beloved longtime member of Brooklyn Monthly Meeting, Religious Society of Friends. Founding member of Mary McDowell Center for Learning. Treasured by all who knew her for her wisdom and generosity.
