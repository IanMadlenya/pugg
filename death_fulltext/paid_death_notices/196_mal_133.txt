
        YANKAUER - Theodore III (Tim), 71. Died on May 11, 2003 after a short battle with brain cancer. Devoted father of Jeff and Stephen. Loving brother of Ellen and adoring grandfather of Sarah. He will be deeply missed by his family and friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to New York-Presbyterian Hospital, Ofc. of Dev., 654 West 170th Street, NY, NY 10032.
