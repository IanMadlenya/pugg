
        ERDREICH - Seymour. On May 7, 2003, in Vero Beach, FL. Born 1914, in Selma, AL. Graduated from Maritime College 1936. Served in the Merchant Marines and as a Lt. Commander in the Navy in WWII. Predeceased by his sister Marie. Survived by his wife Shirley, daughter Marjorie and grandson Maxwell.
