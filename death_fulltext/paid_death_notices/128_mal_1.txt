
        FISHELMAN-Herbert. On August 26, 1997. Businessman, proprietor of Plaza Furniture Company of Jackson Heights and NYC, friend of organized labor, accomplished violinist, selflessly shared his love, kindness, humor, wisdom and optimism with friends and family for 76 years. He is mourned by Harriet, devoted wife of 53 years; children Norman Fishelman, Linda Eiges, Carol Schulman and Robin Burnett; grandchildren Valeria, Denise, Jackie, Katie, Scott, Emmanuel, Monica, Marielle and Daniel; his sister Sylvia Fial, brother Alvin Fallis and extended family. Contributions in his name may be made to Barge Music, c/o Linda Eiges, 629 11th St, Brklyn NY 11215.
