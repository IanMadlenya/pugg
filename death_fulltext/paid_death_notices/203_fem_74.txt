
        FRIEDMAN--Abby Noselson, beloved wife of B.H., mother of Daisy and Jackson, mother-in-law of Petra BertilssonFriedman, sister of Kalman Noselson, sister-in-law of Sanford Friedman, died peacefully December 10, 2003 at 77 after a year's courageous fight against cancer. She was a champion of Choice and other Women's Rights. In lieu of flowers, please consider a contribution to Planned Parenthood, Hudson-Peconic, Smithtown, NY; Meals-onWheels of East Hampton, NY; East End Hospice, Westhampton Beach, NY; or Cabrini Hospice, NYC. A memorial gathering will be held Wednesday, December 17, 69 PM at The Century Association, 7 West 43 Street.
