
        DEAL-Charles M. Age 93. Died November 13, 1999, Pennswood Village, Newtown, PA. Graduated St. Lawrence University 1926, L.L.B., J.S.D. Brooklyn Law School. Resided Brooklyn Heights until retirement from A.T.&T. serving as a corporate lawyer specializing in stock transfer matters. Retired to Bronxville, New York and moved to Pennswood Village in 1980. Wife Rosiland Leale predeceased him. Survived by niece Virginia Brookins, Wayne, PA, grandnephews Christopher Brookins, Cambridge, MA, Geoffrey Brookins, New York City, grandniece Stephanie Manzella, London, England and nephew Robert Cerf of New York City.
