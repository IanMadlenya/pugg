
        McDOUGALL--James Kinloch, died September 13, 2003 of cancer. A Member of the Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center, Jim was internationally known for his research on the role of DNA viruses in cancer. All who knew him will miss his kindness, generosity, intelligence, creativity, support of his family, love of soccer, his beautiful garden and observance of Scottish traditions. He is survived by his wife, Denise Galloway, their two daughters, Jean and Katherine McDougall, of Mercer Island, WA and by four children from his first marriage, Elizabeth, Barbara, Joanne and Hamish, his brother John and their families in England. Contributions may be sent to the FHCRC, 1100 Fairview Ave. N., Seattle, WA 98109.
