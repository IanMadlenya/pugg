
        SNYMAN-Marta. Marta Snyman, a retired stockbroker and teacher, died on July 7th, 1999 of a brain hemorrhage at Tulane University Medical Center in New Orleans. She was 57. Ms. Snyman was born in New York City and lived in New York City and New Orleans. She was a graduate of Clearwater High School in Clearwater, Florida and the University of Florida at Gainesville. She was an actress and art patron. Survivors include her companion, Tom Lawton. Memorial services will be held privately in New York City and New Orleans.
