
        SCHLESINGER--Paul. Died April 23, 2004, attained 96 years of age. Born September 6, 1908. Beloved and dedicated husband of seventy years to Helen (deceased), his partner in life. Father and friend to his children Stanley and Helen and Stuart and Linda. Proud and loving grandfather to Kenneth and Alison, Karen and Gary Yablon, Adam and Natasha, Michael and Bari and Lauren. He was privileged to know and enjoy his eleven great-grandchildren. His family was blessed by his presence and he shall always be remembered. Funeral services Monday, April 26, 12:45 pm, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave. The family will observe shiva at 30 East 9th Street through Wednesday.
        SCHLESINGER--Paul. Julien & Schlesinger, PC, extends its deepest sympathy and condolences to our senior partner, Stuart A. Schlesinger and his wife Linda, and to their children Adam and Natasha, Michael and Bari, and Lauren, upon the loss of their father and grandfather. Adam and Michael are associates of the firm. Our deepest sympathies to Stanley and Helen Schlesinger, and their children Kenneth and Allison, Karen and Gary. He is also survived by 11 greatgrandchildren.
        SCHLESINGER--Paul. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Paul Schlesinger, beloved father of our devoted member Stanley (Helen) Schlesinger, grandfather of Kenneth (Alison) Schlesinger and Karen (Gary) Yablon. To them and the entire family our deepsest sympathy. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
