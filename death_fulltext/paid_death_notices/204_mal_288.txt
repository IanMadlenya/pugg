
        TODEL--Mortimer. The Mental Health Association of New York City, deeply mourns the passing of our distinguished friend and colleague. His involvement with our organization goes back more than forty years. As a founder and past president of the Association's Board, we revere him for his generosity and advocacy on behalf of those struggling with mental illness. He remained active with the Association until his death. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Lawrence M. Chong, President Leonard M. Polisar, Co-Chair Howard F. Sharfstein, Co-Chair Cynthia Zirinsky, Vice President Giselle Stolper, Executive Director
