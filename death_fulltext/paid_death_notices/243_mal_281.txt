
        WAGGER--Harold Jerome, passed away in Durham, NC on April 22, 2007 surrounded by his family. He was a WWII hero with a Silver Star and President of Kramer Office Furniture New York. He is survived by his loving wife Renee, children Michael, Lane and Rosanne, daughterin-law Vicki, granddaughter Sara, sister Diane and brother-in-law Marvin Wender. Funeral service Tuesday April 24, in North Carolina
