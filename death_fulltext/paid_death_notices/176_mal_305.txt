
        SABER-Scott H. Age 36, perished in the inferno at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. Cherished son of Elaine Saber and the late Richard Saber, loving brother of Bruce (Lisa) and Brian, adoring uncle to Rebecca, Arielle and Richard; a special friend to alla man with the warmest heart. A Memorial Service will be held at 11am on Thursday, September 20th at Temple Shaaray Tefila, 250 E. 79th St.(at 2nd Av) N.Y. To honor Scott's love of children and sports, the family is establishing the Scott H. Saber Memorial Fund at the Central Park Conservancy to maintain in perpetuity the East 76th Street Children's Playground. Donations in lieu of flowers may be sent to the Scott H. Saber Memorial Fund, Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60th Street, 8th Floor, New York, NY 10022.
        SABER-Scott H. The Jewish Center of the Hamptons mourns the passing of Scott Saber, beloved son, brother and uncle, 9/11/01. We extend our deepest condolences to his family, Elaine Saber, Bruce Saber and Lisa Sotto, Brian Saber, Rebecca, Ariel and Richard. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Rabbi Alon Levkovitz Cantor Debra Stein Donald Zucker, President
