
        SULLIVAN-Pearl Steinhaus. Died on Jan. 5, 2000, at age 87. Survived by her brother Henry Steinhaus of Sarasota, Florida, and Leawood, KS, nephews Richard Z. Steinhaus Jr., of New York, Peter M. Steinhaus of Orlando, FL, Stephen Steinhaus of Chicago, IL, and niece Dana Lipsig of New York. Predeceased by her husband Paul Wilson Sullivan and siblings Dr. Florence Lipsig, Stanley Steinhaus and Richard Z. Steinhaus. A graduate of the College of the City of New York, she was production manager of the ''Saturday Review of Literature'' (under Norman Cousins) for many years. She was well respected in the periodical and magazine field. In accordance with her wishes there will be no funeral.
