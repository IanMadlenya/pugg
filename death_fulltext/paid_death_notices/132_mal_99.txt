
        BRAUNSCHWEIGER-Leo, on December 31, 1997. Beloved husband of Flora for sixty years. Loving father of Leonard and Mark. Dear brother of Celia, Claire and Hannah. Father-in-law of Leslie. Uncle of many nieces and nephews. He, along with his late brother Max, were the owners of the Hudson View Market for forty years. He was a life member of Isachar Widows and Orphans Benevolent Society, founded in 1857. Services were held Sunday, January 4, 1998.
