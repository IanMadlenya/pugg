
        HAMILTON--Ruth Taft. Of Needham, MA., formerly of Tenafly, N.J. Died peacefully September 9, 2003. She is survived by her daughter Betsey Taft Colby of Wayland, MA., her son Ned Taft of Wellesley, MA., five grandchildren, two stepchildren and five step grandchildren all of whom loved her dearly. A family celebration of her life will be held later. Donations may be sent to North Hill Employee Recognition Fund, 865 Central Ave, Needham, MA. 02492.
