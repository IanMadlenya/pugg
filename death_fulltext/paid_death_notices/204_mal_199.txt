
        YELLIN--Herbert. Beloved friend, and inspired founder and Chairman Emeritus of the Old Stone House of Brooklyn. The members of the board of directors will never forget him. Died Monday, January 12, 2004. Funeral 11:30AM today, Clark Funeral Home, Yorktown Hts., N.Y. Interment immediately following at Mount Judah Ce metery, Brooklyn. The family requests, in lieu of flowers, donations to Gilda's Club, Westchester, N.Y. and/or Old Stone House Historic Interpretive Center, Brooklyn.
