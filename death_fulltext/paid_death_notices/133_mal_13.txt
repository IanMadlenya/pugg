
        KRAMER-Philip. January 30, 1998 in Boca Raton, FL. Beloved husband of Sarah. Loving father of Noelle and Robert, Marilyn and Howard, Glenn and Lynn. Devoted grandfather of Seth, Marc, Allison, Jeffrey, Randee, Douglas, Alexander, Robyn and Kira. Loving great-grandfather of Ross, Brett and Adam. Mr. Kramer will be dearly remembered by his loving family and many devoted friends and business associates. He died after a long and valiant battle with Alzheimer's. Mr. Kramer received his MBA from the Harvard Graduate School of Business. He served as an officer with the Navy in World War II. He was President and COO of Amerada Hess. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 181 W. 76th St., New York City, Sunday, February 1, at 1:45 PM. We will all miss him.
        KRAMER-Philip. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Sidney Freedman, President
