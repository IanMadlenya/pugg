
        TRAVIS--Sedel. Garfunkel, Wild & Travis, P.C. mourns the passing of our friend, Sedel Travis, mother of our founding partner, Norton L. Travis. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to her family. May their cherished memories help to lighten their hearts at this sad time. Donations may be made to Union For Reform Judaism, Tsunami Relief Fund, with notation ''In Memory of Sedel Travis,'' 633 Third Ave, NY NY 10017.
