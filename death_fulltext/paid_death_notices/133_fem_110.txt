
        JOHNSTON-Becky. Age 60, of cancer, February 11, 1998, in New York Hospital. She was a practicing psychotherapist in NYC. After graduation from Stamford Univ., she served with the Peace Corps in the Phillipines. Prior to establishing her own practice, she was a supervisor with Jewish Family Service where she developed many programs including a crisis intervention unit for the Borough of Brooklyn. A lover of animals, a superb gardener, and wonderful friend. She is survived by her mother, Beatrice, and two brothers, Alex and Spencer of Northern California, and a companion, Sandra Sinclair. Her cmpassion and gentleness will be missed by many. Donations in her name can be made to the Sierra Club. Memorial service will be private.
