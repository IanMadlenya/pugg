
        COURTNEY - Christina (nee O'Neill). Fortified with the Sacraments of Holy Mother Church. Born in Tullamore, County Offaly, Ireland on January 1, 1904. She passed away peacefully November 20, 1999 at her daughter's home in Chesterfield, Missouri. Beloved wife of the late William Joseph Courtney. Cherished mother of William (Marie) Courtney, John Courtney, Mary (O.G.) Reichardt and Bernadette Courtney. Dearest grandmother of Christine, William (Joann) & Peter Courtney. Great grandmother of Kathryn, and friend of many. Funeral mass will be held on Wednesday at 10AM at Queen of Peace Catholic Church, 141-36 77th Ave, Flushing, New York. Interment immediately following at Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery 172-00 Booth Memorial Avenue Flushing, New York. Visitation Tuesday 4-8 PM at Quinn-Fogarty Funeral Home, 162-14 Sanford Avenue, Flushing, New York. Masses or contributions to charity of one's choice.
