
        HARRIS--Jessie. Reporter for PM in the 1930's & 1940's and who worked as an analyst for TASS in New York for 60 years, died three days after her 94th birthday at her home in Rego Park, N.Y. She maintained an unshakeable belief in social justice and respect for all life. Ms. Harris is survived by numerous nieces and nephews. She was beloved by all of them.
