
        SMITH-Elena Esther Levine, 39. Wednesday, December 26, at her home in Dennis, Massachusetts, of cancer. Cherished wife of Paul Smith. Beloved daughter of Emily and Larry Levine. Adored sister of Anne Levine and Joseph Brillinger and Jeffrey Brillinger. Elena was a graphic artist, jeweler and coat designer for her father, Larry Levine. There will be a memorial service Saturday evening at Dennis Union Church, Dennis, Massachusetts. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Hospice and Palliative Care of Cape Cod.
