
        BERGREEN-Morris H. The President, Board of Governors, Fellows and staff of the Oxford Centre for Hebrew and Jewish Studies, Oxford, England, as well as the American Friends of the Centre, are profoundly saddened by the death of their colleague, supporter and friend, Morris H. Bergreen. As President of the Skirball Foundation he made an enormous contribution to the academic development and international reach of the Oxford Centre, strengthening in particular its graduate student and research links with the United States. His wise counsel and unwavering commitment to scholarly excellence will be immeasurably missed. We send our deep sympathy to his wife Adele and to all his family.
        BERGREEN-Morris. The Board and Staff of the American Friends of the Israel Museum and the Israel Museum, Jerusalem are deeply saddened by the death of our National Committee Member and longtime Patron Morris Bergreen. As President of the Skirball Foundation, Morris helped cultivate the growth of the Israel Museum. Our condolences to his wife Adele & to the entire family. Judy Steinhardt Jack Nash Co-Chairs, AFIM James S. Snyder Anne & Jerome Fisher Dir., IM Carolyn Cohen Exec. Dir., AFIM
