
        SCHULMAN-Sol. Of Tamarac, passed away May 9, 2003. Beloved husband of Lenore Diamond Schulman. Cherished father of Larry (Joy) Solomon & Michael Diamond. Grandfather of John & Eric (Risa) Diamond, Lindsay & Michael Solomon. Greatgrandfather of Melissa & Jeffrey Diamond. Sol was an ardent supporter and major contributor of the Jewish Federation and helped build the David Posnak Jewish Day School, and the preschool at Temple Beth Torah in Tamarac and the Riverdale Jewish Day School in New York. He also built the Park East Synagogue Day School in New York. He was a giant among men and will be missed by all. Temple service Sunday, May 11th, 1:30 PM, at Temple Beth Torah, 9105-15 NW 57th Street, Tamarac, FL. For information, Star of David; 954722-9000.
        SCHULMAN-Sol. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our dedicated Honorary President and benefactor Sol Schulman, beloved husband of Sisterhood Honorary President and Chairman of the Women's Division Sponsor, Lenore. Sol and Lenore's leadership provided a foundation for the Minskoff Cultural Center Rabbi Arthur Schneier Park East Day School. Deepest sympathy to the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President
