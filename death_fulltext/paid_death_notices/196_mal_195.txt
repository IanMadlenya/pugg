
        TOBIAS--Melvin, died peacefully May 19, 2003. Devoted husband to Beatrice for fiftysix years. Loving father of Nancy and Jeffrey. Brother of Norma Fund and Audrey Holder. WW II Navy veteran who proudly served his country, he was President of Consolidated Electric Meter Co., Inc. for over thirty years. His courage, optimism, humor and intelligence enriched everyone who knew him. He will be deeply missed. >>AD#
