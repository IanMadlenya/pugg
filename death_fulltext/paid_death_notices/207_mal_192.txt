
        SARSHAD--Hossein, 97, of New York, NY, born March 20, 1907 in Tehran Iran, died Sunday March 28, 2004 at Amsterdam Nursing Home Corp., New York, NY. Mr. Sarshad was a Colonel in the Iranian military and was a music director in the Iranian Military College under the reign of the former Shah of Iran and was a music composer and a music teacher in New York. Surviving are his wife Dorothy (nee Barkau) Sarshad, a son, Eric Sarshad and many nieces and nephews.
