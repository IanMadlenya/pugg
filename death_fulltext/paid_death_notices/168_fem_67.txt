
        HARRIS-Marjorie F. Age 79 of East Quogue, NY and Sarasota, FL died January 2, 2001. She was born on August 15, 1921 in The Bronx. She was a homemaker and educator, attended Hunter College and was a member of the United Federation of Teachers. She was a Catholic. Survivors include her husband of 52 years, Melville S., a daughter, Barbara J. of Sarasota; a sister, Kathleen Brainard of Albuquerque, NM; a brother, John Gardiner, Jr. and one granddaughter. A memorial liturgy was held in Sarasota, FL. at the Church of the Incarnation. A graveside service will be held in the spring at South Hampton Cemetery. Donations may be made to God's Love We Deliver, 166 Sixth Ave, New York, New York 10013-1207.
