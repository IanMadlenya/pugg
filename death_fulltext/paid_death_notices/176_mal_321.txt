
        GORMAN-James Edward. On Wednesday, September 19, 2001, of Ijamsville, Maryland, devoted husband of Mildred F. Gorman and father of Mary Pat Gorman (Les Tribble), of Summit, New Jersey and Joseph P. Gorman of Washington, D.C. Grandfather of Liz and Matt Tribble and brother of Patricia Supple. Friends may call 5-9 P.M., Friday, September 21, at the Olin L. Molesworth P.A., Funeral Home, 26401 Ridge Road, Damascus, Maryland. A Memorial Mass to celebrate his life will be held 2:00 P.M., Sunday, September 23, at St. Patrick's Catholic Church, Pearsall Avenue, Glen Cove, New York. Interment will be private. The family requests that in lieu of flowers expressions of sympathy be in the form of donations to the charity of your choice to benefit the victims of the September 11th tragedies.
