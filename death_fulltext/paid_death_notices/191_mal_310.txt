
        MORTON-Herbert C. 81, died on December 21, 2002, at his home in Bethesda, MD. He was an editor and publisher and author of books on economics, the work place, scholarly communication, and lexicography. He is survived by his wife, Barbara Orlans Morton, and two daughters by his first wife Doris B. Liebenberg of Minneapolis-Janet Morton of Cary, NC, Martha Fusaro of White Plains, NY, and a granddaughter. He was married to Kathryn (Peg) Aring, who died in 1980. A memorial gathering will be held January 19, 2003, at 2:30 PM, at River Road Unitarian Church, 6301 River Road, Bethesda, MD. Contributions may be sent to Montgomery Hospice, Rockville, MD.
