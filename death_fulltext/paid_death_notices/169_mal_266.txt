
        NICHOLSON-Erwin ''Nick''. On February 21, 2001. He was the Executive Producer of the daytime drama The Edge of Night for 20 years. He won an Emmy for the show in 1972, the second Emmy every given for a daytime drama. Before joining The Edge of Night, Mr. Nicholson directed the daytime dramas Brighter Day and The Secret Storm. In 1997, Mr. Nicholson brought his extensive experience in the television industry to the National Academy of Television Arts & Sciences. In 1994 The New York Chapter of NATAS presented him with the Silver Circle, an award for distinguished television professionals. Donations in his memory can be made to the Actors Fund in Englewood, N. J.
