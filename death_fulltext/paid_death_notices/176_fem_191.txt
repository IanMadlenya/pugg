
        SEYMOUR-Karen Lynn. Wife of William Dietrich and mother of Sarah and William Dietrich, was among those lost in the tragedy at the World Trade Center on September 11, 2001. She is also survived by her father, David Seymour of Littlestown, PA., her mother Catherine Seymour and sister Debra Anderson, both of Sykesville, MD, and her brother Glen Seymour of Abingdon, MD. A memorial service will be held at Saint Vincent de Paul R.C. Church, 250 Bebout Avenue, Stirling, N.J. on Thursday, September 27th at 4PM. A reception will follow at the Primavera Regency, 1080 Valley Road, Stirling, N.J.
