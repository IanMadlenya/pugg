
        NOVEMBER-Barbara. The Board of Directors and staff of COPAY, Inc. in Great Neck, NY extend their condolences to the family of Barbara November who died August 31, 2000. For the past 20 years Barbara was a member of our Board and participated in many activities which helped to improve the lives of others. She served her community well and will be missed by all who knew her. Diana Vargas-Singh, President COPAY, Inc.
