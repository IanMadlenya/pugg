
        LOWELL--Stanley. The Officers, Board and Staff of the Jewish Community Relations Council mourn the loss of Stanley Lowell. He served with distinction as our Vice President and Trustee and as one of the founding Chairs of the Greater New York Conference for Soviet Jewry. May our colleague, Lee, and the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Matthew J. Maryles, Pres. Michael S. Miller, Executive Vice Pres. & CEO
