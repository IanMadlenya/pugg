
        REISS-Julius. Died June 14 at age 93. He was a union organizer and man of great intellect and heart. He is survived by his wife Gertrude, his children Helen, David and Andrew, and his grandchildren Emily, Peter and Joshua. A memorial service will be held Sunday, June 24, at 2 PM, at Park Slope Jewish Center, 14th Street and 8th Avenue, Brooklyn.
