
        BURT--Jane Duffy, of Sea Ranch Lakes, FL, formerly of Larchmont, NY, died at home on February 18, 2007. Mrs. Burt was the wife of the late Wilbur F. Burt, Jr., and daughter of the late Florence and Stephen V. Duffy. She is survived by five children, Wilbur F. Burt, III of Plainville, MA, Joseph D. Burt of Boca Raton, FL, Mary Anne B Callahan of Lauderdale, FL, Patricia B. McMullen of Orlando, FL, Peter A. Burt of Fort Lauderdale, FL, 12 grandchildren and a sister, Constance D. Corroon of Rye, NY. She was the sister of the late Joseph Duffy, Miriam Scavullo, Rev. Stephen Duffy, S.J., Sr. Helen Marie, Rev. Edwin Duffy and Florence Doherty. Mrs. Burt graduated from the Convent of the Sacred Heart in New York City and Manhattanville College. Friends may call the Baird Case Funeral home in Fort Lauderdale on Wednesday, February 21st from 3:30 to 7:00 pm. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Thursday, February 22nd at 10:00 am at Assumption Catholic Church in Pompano Beach. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated on Saturday, April 14th at 11:00 am at the Church of the Resurrection in Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society, South Florida Division, 3407 NW Ninth Avenue, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33308 would be appreciated.
