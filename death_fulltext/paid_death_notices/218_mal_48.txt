
        SELTZER--Martin, 74, on March 3, 2005. Of Marco Island, FL, and Long Branch, NJ. Martin was a leading Public Adjuster for over 40 years and Past President of the Jewish Congregation of Marco Island. Beloved husband, father, brother and grandfather. Service Sunday, March 6, 11:30AM, Hellman Garlick Funeral Home, 1300 Pleasantville Road, Briarcliff NY. He will be missed.
