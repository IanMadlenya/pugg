
        RAVEN--Claus (born Rawitscher) of Clifton, NJ. Holocaust survivor born in Berlin, Germany. Age 81, on June 7, 2003. Beloved husband of 60 years to the former Rahel Karlsberg. Devoted father to David Raven and Diane Raven Reilly. Beloved father-inlaw of Michele and Patrick. Special ''Pop'' to Leah, Dan, Ben, and Sonni. He beautified New Jersey with his landscape designs. He nourished us with his wit, warmth and cuisine. Founder and leader of the Raven Tennis Group. Services on Wednesday at 1:00PM at the Jewish Memorial Chapel, 841 Allwood Road, Clifton, NJ (973-779-3048). Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society. >>AD#
