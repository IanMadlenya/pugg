
        GELLER--Murray. 79, of Cliffside Park, NJ (formerly of Brooklyn, NY), on February 18, 2004. Founder and President of Great Eastern Pet Supply Inc. from 1956 to 2002. Survived by Adele, his wife of 54 years and business partner for 46 years; daughters Lois Gross of Columbia Falls, MT and Doreen Leopold of St. Paul, MN; brothers Kenneth and Milton Geller and seven grandchildren. Funeral services Sunday, February 22, at 11 AM at Wellwood Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY.
