
        STEVENS-Carleton H. III, 53, of Hamden, CT., died May 15, 2000. The cause of death was amyotrophic lateral sclerosis or Lou Gehrig's disease which he had for over four years. Husband of Susan Swords Stevens, father of Virginia Stevens and Peter Swords Stevens. Also survived by his parents, Elinor and Carleton H. Stevens Jr. of New London, NH, a sister and three brothers. A memorial service will be held at Trinity Church on the Green, New Haven, Friday at 10:30AM. In lieu of flowers please make donations to Gaylord Rehabilitation Research Institute, P.O. Box 400, Wallingford, Connecticut 06492.
