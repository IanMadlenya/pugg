
        MICHELSON -- Naomi (nee Katz). On Thursday, December 11, 2003, at her home in Fort Lauderdale, FL. Born on September 25, 1906, longtime resident of NYC, Westport Island, ME, and Ft. Lauderdale, FL. Beloved aunt of Robert and Carolyn Washor, Lenore and David Krieger, Diane Katz Feinman, Laurie Katz Bell and the late Albert Katz. Devoted sister of her late siblings Nathan Katz, Beatrice Katz Washor and Barbara Katz Krieger. Grateful friend of Queen Ester Clark. She will be mourned by many more family members and friends.
