
        GAWRON-Michael A. Age 57, of Brooklyn. Died on October 12. Burial was in Chicopee, MA. He was an Associate at William B. Tabler Architect and was most recently Project Architect on Brooklyn Renaissance Plaza which includes the Marriott Hotel and Office Tower. A memorial celebration will be held at the Cathedral Church of St. John the Divine, Manhattan on December 2, at 4:30 PM.
