
        KING--Ronald, 78, passed away on November 4, 2006. Born and raised in New York City, he graduated from LIU and earned his Masters Degree at Teachers College, Columbia University. He was the first Principal of Martin Luther King H.S. He retired as Executive Director of the PSAL for the Board of Education of NYC. He is survived by his wife Sondra and his children Terry, Roni, and Rodney and three grandchildren, Chloe, Major and Jet.
