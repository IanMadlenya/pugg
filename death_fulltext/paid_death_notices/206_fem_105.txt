
        COHEN--Sara. Age 93. Beloved wife of the late Emanuel. Cherished mother of Dr. Charles I. Cohen and Ellen Dede. Adored grandmother of Dr. Deena and Rabbi Samuel Zimmerman, Drs Matthew and Sharon Cohen, Dr. Jessica and Richard Langer and ten great grandchildren. Aunt Sara to generations of the Cohen, Rubin, Novick and Posner clans and Bubby to one and all. A lifelong Zionist and Eternal Optimist. Longterm president of Borough Park Hadassah. She lived a long, full, productive life with great joy in every minute. Funeral Thursday March 11 at Jeffers Funeral Home, 4620 Ft. Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn. Shiva will be at the home of Charles Cohen. Donations in her memory to Hadassah.
