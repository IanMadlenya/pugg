
        OWENS-Grady Jr. On September 12. Of Orange County, NY. Businessman and community leader. Son of the late Grady and Bridget Healy Owens. Born July 20, 1938. Survived by his wife of 29 years, two daughters, Diane Fulston, Erin Owens one son, Grady III and one grandson Bryce Fulston. A brother Franklin Owens and three sisters, Catherine Owens, Maureen Dubison, Alice Gray. We mourn the passing of our beloved brother. His cherished memory will live forever in our hearts.
