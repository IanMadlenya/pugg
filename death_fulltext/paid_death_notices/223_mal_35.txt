
        GERSON--Joe. The Trustees and staff of Jewish Child Care Association are terribly saddened by the death of Joe Gerson. Joe was a good friend, energetic and generous supporter and proud allumnus of JCCA; We will miss him. Our hearts go out to his wide circle of friends and admirers and especially to Joe's family, his wife Audrey & their children Robin, Rhonda and Daniel. We will remember Joe with affection and gratitude. Leonard Elman, President Barbara B. Mann, Chair Richard Altman, CEO
