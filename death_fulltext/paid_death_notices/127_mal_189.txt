
        KAUFTHAL-Leo. Beloved husband of Edith Kaufthal, passed away in his 86th year. He was a gentle and righteous person, and a role model for his children and grandchildren. Survived in deep mourning by his children, Judy and Uri Kaufthal, Linda and Ilan Kaufthal, his grandchildren, Jonathan, Jeremy, Joshie, Daniel, David, and Josh Kaufthal. We will sorely miss him. Shiva at 143 East Linden Avenue, Englewood, N.J.
