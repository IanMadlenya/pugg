
        RIDGWAY--Thomas Jr. 91, beloved husband of Jane Pope (Akers) Ridgway, who died in his summer home in Newport, Rhode Island, on September 5, 2005. He was born in Philadelphia, PA, on May 9, 1914. He was the son of the late Thomas and Edith Ridgway. A memorial service will be held in St. Columba Berkeley Memorial Chapel, Vaucluse Avenue in Middletown, Rhode Island, on Wednesday, September 28, 2005, at 12 noon. Relatives and friends are invited to attend. Call Memorial Funeral Home, 375 Broadway, for information. Newport. www. Memorialfuneralhome.com
