
        VALLEY-Astrid E. Age 92, on Sept. 12, 2000 in Oakmont, PA. Daughter of the late Olaf and Hannah Westlund Valley. She was a teacher for 40 years in the New York City Public Schools. She authored the book ''Marching Bonnet'' that was published by MacMillan in 1948. She is predeceased by sister Alva Swenson. Surviving is nephew Rev. Bruce H. Swenson and his family in Pittsburgh, PA
