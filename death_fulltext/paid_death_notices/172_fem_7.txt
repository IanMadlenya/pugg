
        BROWNE-Hilda L. Age 76. Beloved wife of the late Walter F. Browne. Devoted mother of Walter Shawn, Stephen, Susan and Roger. Proud grandmother of Amber, Dennis and Allison Browne, Walter Promnitz. Dear sister of John, cousin of Bonnie, Neville, Phoebe and Ruth, Australia. Passed away on February 21 of cancer at NYU with children by her side. New York State Registered Representative. Member of the American Australian Association and Australian-New Zealand Society. Active member and recent Regent of the Niagara Chapter of the Daughters of the British Empire. A Memorial service on Saturday, May 5, noon at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave at 51st Street, gathering to follow.
