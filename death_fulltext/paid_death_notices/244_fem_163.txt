
        ZULLER--Harriet. Died on May 21, 2007, at age 75, after bravely battling cancer. A dedicated teacher. An adoring mother to Michael and Dana, Randi, Eliane and Adrian. A treasured grandmother to Devon, Jillian, Geoffrey, Chelsea, Daniel, Alexander and Chloe, and above all a devoted wife of 55 years to Norman, who passed away only three months ago. She will forever be remembered for her strength, beauty and grace.
