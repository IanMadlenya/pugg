
        GINSBURG--Arnold. On August 27th, 2003. Age 85. Most beloved husband and life partner of Esther. Loving and devoted father of Howard (Sherry), Benjamin, Stephen (Jeri) and Kenneth (Ruth). Cherished grandfather of Scott, David, Bradley, Chad, Courtney, Kimberly. Dearest brother of Irma, Everett, Lee and the late Jesse. Founder, Ginsburg Manufacturing Co. A giant to those privileged to know him. Arnold defined dignity, integrity, kindness and grace--always giving and walking the extra mile. Forever in our hearts. Services Friday, 10AM at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', Great Neck, N.Y.
        GINSBURG -- Arnold. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck. Myron Fenster, Int. Sr. Rabbi Sidney Freedman, President
