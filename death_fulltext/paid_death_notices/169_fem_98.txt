
        KANNER-Selma. Born October 5, 1910 passed away on the evening of February 9, 2001 in NYC. Dearest Selma, wife of the late Louis Kanner, brought joy and kindness to all of her friends and family. She will be lovingly remembered by her brothers Harold and Arthur; daughter Rita Diane Skyler; son-in-law Martin Skyler; grandchildren Edward, Tristine, Lisanne and her husband Kevin Black.
