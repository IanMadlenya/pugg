
        FENNIMORE-Tony. On July 27, 2000. Beloved husband of Sharon Fitzpatrick. Devoted father of Adam, Christopher and Kamla Fennimore. Dear brother of Linda and Don Fennimore. Also survived by many dear friends. Services Monday, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Visiting hours Sunday, 2-4 and 6-8 PM.
        FENNIMORE-Tony. 55, of New York City, died on July 27, 2000. He was a Partner in the New York City foundation engineering firm of Mueser Rutledge Consulting Engineers. The Partners & staff of the firm deeply mourn his passing and are grateful for his 30 years of service. He was a valued Partner, Friend, and Mentor whose dedication and guidance will be greatly missed. Tony was a highly regarded structural engineer with extensive experience in the analysis and design of waterfront structures. Among his noteable projects are the redevelopment of the Queens West Waterfront in Queens, NY, Port Albany, NY and Port Elizabeth, NJ. He was a member of the American Society of Civil Engineers, the Tau Beta Pi Honorary Engineering Society, the Construction Specifications Institute, The Moles, and the Structural Engineers Association of New York. To his wife Sharon, children Adam, Christopher and Kamla, brother Don, and sister Linda, we offer our heartfelt sympathies.
