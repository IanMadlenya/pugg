
        ECKMAN--Sidney, 86, of Greenwich, CT on September 21, 2004. Veteran WW II. Worked 48 years for MGM Pictures as Division Manager, Assistant to Senior V.P., Producer's Representative. Member of Will Rogers Motion Picture Pioneers, Variety Club, Motion Picture Association and Masons. We'll miss his infectious smile and engaging personality. Survived by wife of 52 years, Adeline, son Dr. Mark Eckman, daughter Heidi Silverstein, brother Harold of MA, and four grandchildren.
