
        GLUCKSMAN-David N. Of Forest Hills, NY, died peacefully on November 24, 2002 after a brief illness. Beloved and devoted husband of Sondra (Sunny). Loving father of Diane, James, and the late Mark. Grandfather of Deborah. Brother and friend. Proudly served in World War II. Awarded the Bronze Star. Respected businessman. Services will be held at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Forest Hills, NY, Tuesday, November 26, 2002 at 10 AM. Shiva will be observed at home. Contributions in his memory may be made to St. Francis Heart Center, 100 Port Washington Blvd., Roslyn, NY or the Cystic Fibrosis Research Foundation, c/o Long Island Jewish Hospital, New Hyde Park, NY.
