
        LEWIS-David J., 85, on March 15, 2000, after a long illness died peacefully at home with his loved ones beside him. Wife of 59 years, Gloria (nee Lehrer); children, Patricia and Gerald Borakove and Alisa and Richard Altabef; grandchildren, Jason, Nomi, Morry, Jacob and David. Services private. Memorial service at Little Neck Jewish Center, Sunday, March 26, 2000, at 3:30 PM.
