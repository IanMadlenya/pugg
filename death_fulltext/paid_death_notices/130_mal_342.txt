
        SAROT-Dr. Eden E. Professor of Modern Languages and Linguistics at Seton Hall University from the 1930's until his retirement in 1976, died Tuesday evening, November 25, 1997, in Sunrise, FL. He was preceded in death by his parents, Nathan and Sadie Sarot, his brother, Irving Sarot, MD, and his sister Gloria Gold. He is survived by his loving and devoted sister and brother-in-law, Muriel and William B. Sherman and many nieces and nephews. Interment was private on November 28, 1997, in New York City.
