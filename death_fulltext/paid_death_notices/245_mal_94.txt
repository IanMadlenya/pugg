
        DEBLINGER--David B., on June 7th, 2007. A WWII veteran, he worked as an accountant for the City of New York. Patron of numerous organizations devoted to charity, social justice, the arts and conservation. Lifelong student of spiritual concepts. Husband of the late Cecilia. Father of Larry Deblinger, Ann Deblinger Reiner, grandfather of Jessie and Susie Reiner and twin-brother of Sylvia Goldstein. Funeral service Sunday, June 10th at New Mt. Zion Cemetery. For more information, Zion Memorial Chapel 914-381-1809.
