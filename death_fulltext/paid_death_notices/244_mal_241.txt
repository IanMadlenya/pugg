
        IHLE--William Arthur, Dr., of Delray Beach, Florida died on May 24, 2007 after a noble life of 92 years. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Elizabeth Tracy Ihle. Dr. Ihle will be sorely missed by his family: John Ihle and George Thulin of Hypoluxo, Florida, Kathy and Joseph Sweeney of Summit, New Jersey and Robert and Mary Ihle of Kew Gardens, New York. He was the son of Katherine Fitzgibbons and Henry John Ihle. Dr. Ihle was a graduate of Fordham University and Georgetown University School of Dentistry. He was a former resident of Port Chester, New York where he was in private practice and was a member of Dental Staff at United Hospital. Dr. Ihle was a retired Captain in the US Naval Reserve and a former member of the New York Academy of Dentistry and the Navy League of Boca Raton, Florida. Relatives and friends are invited to attend Arthur's Life Celebration service at Kraeer Funeral Home at 1353 North Federal Highway, Boca Raton, Florida on Tuesday, May 29, 2007 from 2:00pm to 4:00pm. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held on Wednesday, May 30, 2007 at Ascension Catholic Church in Boca Raton, FL at 10:30am. Interment will be held at a later date with full military honors at Arlington National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Avenue, West Palm Beach, FL 33407.
