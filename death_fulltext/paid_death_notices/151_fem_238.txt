
        SCHWAB-Ruth L. On August 27, 1999, in New York City. Beloved wife of the late Joseph H. Schwab; adored mother of Fredrica S. Friedman and James L. Schwab; and mother in-law of Stephen J. Friedman and Amy A. Schwab; cherished grandmother of Vanessa and Alexander Friedman and Kate and Joanna Schwab; devoted sister, sister in-law, aunt and friend. Her great goodness, wisdom and generosity of spirit will continue to be treasured by all who knew and loved her. ''They that love beyond the world cannot be separated by it.'' Contributions in her memory may be made to The Hospital for Special Surgery.
