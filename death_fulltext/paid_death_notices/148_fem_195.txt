
        JAFFE-Marlene Mae. March 29, 58 years old. Born in San Francisco. Daughter of the late Burton and Zella Jaffe. Very loving sister of Dr. Reuven Ralph and Ruth Jaffe. Beloved aunt to Naomi Devorah Jaffe and Adina and Rabbi Leslie Lipson and daughter Rebecca. A loving niece and cousin. Marlene had a wonderful sense of humor and much compassion. After her formal education, she worked for ABC-Paramount and for almost two decades was the ''heart'' of Time, Inc., where she also hosted many events for underprivileged children. Marlene was a cherished friend to many and supported Jewish causes, especially her synagogue. Memorial service on Sunday, June 6, at 12 PM, at Ansche Chesed, 251 West 100th St, NYC. Contributions may be made to Cancer Care, 1180 Aveune of the Americas, 2nd Floor, New York, NY 10036, or the cause of your choice.
