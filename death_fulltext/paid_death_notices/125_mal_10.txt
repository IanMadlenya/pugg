
        PRIETO-Michael, Esq. On May 22, 1997, of New York City. Beloved son of Jose and Teresa Prieto, dear brother of Raquel T. Prieto and Jose Prieto, Jr. He was an attorney for civil and criminal cases. He earned his BA degree at George Washington College in 1984 and received Jurisprudence in 1987 at Temple University. Funeral services were private.
