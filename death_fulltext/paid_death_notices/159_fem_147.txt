
        LIEBGOTT-Pearl. On April 18, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Murray. Devoted mother of Frances, Marcelle and Marty. Dear sister of Blanche, Eva, Gerty, Anna and Rose. Adoring grandmother of Karen, Elizabeth, Dana, Jonathan, David, Blythe, Mara and great grandchildren. Supporter of Jewish ideals and friend to Israel.
