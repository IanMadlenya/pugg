
        PETTINATO-Lawrence G. Jr. Passed away peacefully Sunday, March 3rd at home with his loving wife and children at his bedside. He is survived by his wife Elizabeth N. (nee Brown) whom he cherished, his sons Lawrence III and Michael whom he adored, his loving parents Edith and Lawrence, Sr. and his devoted brothers John and Thomas. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Ave. (at 20th St,) NYC, Tuesday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday 10 A.M. at Epiphany R.C. Church. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Morristown, NJ. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Pettinato Boys Education Fund will be appreciated.
