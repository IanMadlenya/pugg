
        STERN-Philipp K. On April 19, 1999 at his home in Lakehurst, NJ. Beloved husband of Sylvia, loving father of Erica Stern of Lakewood, NJ, Leda Walker of Morris, New York, and Sheera Stern of Metuchen, NJ, adoring grandfather of Jeffrey vom Saal, Emily Marvel, Daniel vom Saal and Laura Milanette, Associate Emeritus of The Casualty Actuarial Society.
