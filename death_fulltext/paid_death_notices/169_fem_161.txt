
        GEIGER-Ella. Died suddenly in her home in New York, on February 16th, 2001. Beloved wife of the late George. Loving mother of Peggy Ellis and mother-in-law of Mark Ellis. Adoring grandmother of Elizabeth. Dear sister of Helena Spelman and Gisella Eisenstark. Cherished aunt of Agnes Bruce, Joelle Kent, Jay and Blanche Eisenstark and their children. Services Tuesday, February 20th, 10 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to the Brearley School, 610 East 83rd Street, New York, New York 10028, attention: Development Office. May she rest in peace.
