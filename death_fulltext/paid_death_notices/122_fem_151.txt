
        LYONS-Evelyn. Died peacefully on March 18, 1997 at the age of 85. Loving mother of Ronnie (Debbie), Lloyd (Gerri) and Steve, sister-in-law Soni, cherished grandmother of Debra (Randy), Lori (Jeffrey), Flossie (Jimmy) and Sterling. Adored greatgrandmother of Ara, Elyse, Kylie, Skylar, Gabriel, and Shelby. She was the Executive Secretary to the National Fashion Association in NYC until her retirement. She will always be remembered for her compassion and sense of humor. She will forever be in our hearts. Services Thursday March 20, 1997 in Hollywood, FL.
