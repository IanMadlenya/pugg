
        TROY-Florence W. On September 22, 1999. Devoted wife of the late William A. Troy. Loving mother of Aileen and Alan Janover. Proud Ranny of Tom and Sarah, Jim and Marcy. Adored great Ranny of William, Kate and Leah. Friends may call on Thursday 3-5 and 7-9 at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Services Friday, September 24, 12 noon at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
        TROY-Florence W. Congregation Emanu-El of the city of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Florence W. Troy our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories wil linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
