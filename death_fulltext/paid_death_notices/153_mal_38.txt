
        LENSE-Robert. Passed away on October 2, 1999. Beloved and dedicated husband to Rose Marie, married for 51 years. Loving father to Richard, JoAnn and Ronald. Devoted brother to Jack and Daniel and loyal friend to countless others. Robert was a proud graduate of the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy, Class of 1944, who held a Masters Seaman's License. Services today at 12 noon, at the U.S. Merchant Marine Academy in Kings Point.
        LENSE-Robert. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
