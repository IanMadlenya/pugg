
        LEWIN--Jacob, beloved husband of Bernice, loving father of Elizabeth Lewin-Arnoul and Judith Lewin; cherished grandfather of Lillian Arnoul and father-in-law of Keith Arnoul. He was a childhood Holocaust survivor and U.S. Army veteran. Services were Monday, March 26th at Bloomfield - Cooper Jewish Chapels, Ocean, NJ. Donations may be made to the Shoah Foundation.
