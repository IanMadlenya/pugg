
        CARAPETYAN - Dr. Haig, died on December 15, 1996. He was born in Isphahan, Iran on November 16, 1904. He arrived in the USA in 1932. He received his medical degree in 1934 at the Univ. of Michigan, in Ann Arbor, MI and received his OB/GYN Degree in 1937 from Massachusetts Memorial Hospital in Boston, MA. He established an infertility Clinic at St. Luke's Hospital in New York City. In 1988, he received a citation of merit for his distinguished service to St. Luke's Hospital. He was an honary member of the Metropolitan Opera Club and he belonged to the West Side Clinic Society. He is survived by his wife of fifty-five years, a brother, a son and four grandchildren.
