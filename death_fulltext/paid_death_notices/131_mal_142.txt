
        BERNSTEIN-Jeffrey M. On December 7, 1997. Devoted son of Paula and the late Robert. Cherished brother of Barbi and Dan Radd and the late Roni Sunshine. Loving uncle of Amber Sunshine. In respectful observance, Paula Berns, Inc., of which Jeffrey was president, will remain closed on Wednesday.
