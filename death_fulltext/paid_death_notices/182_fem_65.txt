
        CAREY-Constance A. On March 3rd at home in Califon, NJ in her 74th year. Daughter of the late John P. Carey and Kathryn Ward. Sister of Dee-Dee Gallagher, W. Ward Carey and the late John P. Carey, Jr. Also survived by sixteen nieces and nephews. Teacher, writer, author of ''The Chekhov Proposal'' and psychotherapist. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, March 6 at 12:00 noon, Saint John Neuman Church, Califon, NJ. A memorial service will be held at 1 E. 91st Street, NY, NY at a date to be announced. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Convent of the Sacred Heart (Kenwood), Albany, New York.
