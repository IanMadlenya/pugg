
        ROGOFF-Shirley. In her 95th year, died April 12, 1997 at her residence in Virginia Beach, VA. A native of NY, she was the widow of Martin Rogoff. Devoted mother of twins, Jerry and wife Rena of Virginia Beach, VA, and Marvin and wife June, of Boca Raton, FL, and Roslyn Heights, NY. Cherished grandmother of Robin and husband Michael Star of Rockville, MD, Maury and husband David Gostfrand of NY, NY. Martin and wife Ellen of Merion Station, PA, and Bruce and wife Janice of Wellesley, MA. Loving great grandmother of Matthew, Drew, Harrison, Tess, Andrea, Corey, Kyle, Jenna, and Leah. Services at Mount Zion Cemetery, Maurice Avenue, Maspeth, Queens, NY today at 1:30PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Multiple Sclerosis Society.
