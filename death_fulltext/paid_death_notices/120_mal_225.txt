
        MAUTNER-John Samuel. Jan. 15, 1997. Son of the late Jesse and Hilda, brother of J. Jay. A graduate of Phillips Exeter Academy and Harvard College, and professor of German language and literature at Columbia University until his illness caused him to retire. Since then, he devoted his life to writing poetry and to administering to the needs of his fellow dialysis patients at the Rogosin Institute of New York Hospital. Friends will be received at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, on Wednesday evening, 7-9 P.M. with a service on Thursday, 1:30 P.M. Entombment Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Valhalla, N.Y.
