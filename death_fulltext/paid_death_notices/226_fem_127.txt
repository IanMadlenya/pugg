
        MORGENTHAU -- Jane, Wertheimer. Age 94 died November 14, 2005. Wife of Charles S. (deceased) and mother Kate (deceased). Jane, a graduate of Connecticut College was a lifetime avid reader of books, though born with greatly impaired eyesight. She worked at The Book Store in her hometown of Saint Louis, MO and in New York City at the Cokesbury Bookstore and the McBurney School Library. Friend, confidant and frequent hostess to her many cousins and dear friends, Jane always shared her interest and love in the lives of these individuals. A memorial gathering in her honor will be held on December 4, 2005 at 2PM at her home, 40 East 88th Street. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Kate Morgenthau Library Fund of Connecticut College.
