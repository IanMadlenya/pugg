
        DUNNINGTON-Walter G., Jr. The members of the Board of the Women's Committee of the Central Park Conservancy mourn the loss of a dear friend and faithful supporter, Walter G. Dunnington, Jr. We are saddened by his passing and extend our deepest sympathy to his bereaved wife, Pat, our devoted board member and cherished friend, and our sincere condolences to their family. Toni Peebler, President
