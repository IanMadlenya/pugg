
        LEWIS-Jesse, age 88, on September 11. Beloved husband of Florence, devoted father of Leon, Sarah, and Martin. Beloved brother-in-law of George and Abigail and family, and father-in-law of Dixie, Joel and Beverley. Devoted grandfather of Callie, Matthew and Naomi, Zoey and husband Reuben, Jenn, and Toby. Great grandfather of Alex, loving uncle of Paula and children Andrew and Jeffrey. We have lost an exceptional human being. His generosity, gracious demeanor, wit and charm, have left an enduring impact on all who knew him.
