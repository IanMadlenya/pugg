
        SILBERMAN-Aron. Age 99, died September 10, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Anna Silberman; dear father of Paul and Maurice; father-in-law of Marion and Edna; loving grandfather of Joseph, Barbara and Steven, Nina, Jeremy and Alisa, Mark and Pamela; adored great grandfather of Eli, Anna, Noah and Nathaniel. He will be deeply missed. Funeral service Sunday, Sept 13, 11:30, Boulevard Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett.
