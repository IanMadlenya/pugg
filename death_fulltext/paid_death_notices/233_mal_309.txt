
        MILIKOWSKY--Jonathan. In Pittsburgh, PA, Jonathan Milikowsky, 23, of 3 Edgehill Road, New Haven, died at Children's Hospital on Sunday, June 25. Born in New Haven, CT, June 14, 1983, he was a devoted son of Daniel and Sharon (Burger) Milikowsky. Beloved brother of Matthew Milikowsky and Jennifer Milikowsky, both of New Haven. Cherished grandson of Bob and Mary Burger of North Carolina, and the late Matthew Milikowsky and Esther Milikowsky Copelon. Also survived by many adoring aunts, uncles and cousins. Funeral services at Congregation Mishkan Israel, 785 Ridge Road, Hamden, CT, Wednesday morning at 11:00 o'clock. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Jonathan Milikowsky Memorial Fund at Carnegie Mellon Crew Team, 5017 Forbes Avenue, Pittsburgh, PA 15213 or to the Foote School, 50 Loomis Place, New Haven, CT 06511. A period of mourning will be observed at the family home, 3 Edgehill Road, New Haven, CT Wednesday following the funeral until 7 P.M.; Thursday 2-4 P.M., 6:309:30 P.M.; and Friday 2-4 P.M.
