
        HERSH-Lawrence. Died August 5, 1999, peacefully at home after a long illness. Beloved husband of Carol, cherished father of Amy and Edward, loving father-in-law of Hilary, devoted grandfather of Aaron. Founder of Forrest Engraving Co. in New Rochelle. He was proud of his army service in WWII and devoted to community service to The Yorktown Jewish Center, The Yorktown Volunteer Ambulance Corps, and Lions Club.
