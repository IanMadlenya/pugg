
        WOLF--Gerald Liebers. Beloved husband of Audrey Laibson Wolf, father of Marc, Ralph, Amy, father-in-law of Kevin, grandfather of Molly, brother of Ellis, dear brotherin-law and uncle. We will miss his warmth, love, humor, curiosity, rich appetite for new adventures and his music. He was an outstanding anesthesiologist, internationally known for his research in operating room fires, superb teacher and mentor. He was Emeritus Professor of Anesthesiology at the State University of New York/Brooklyn and Visiting Research Professor at Cooper Union. A graduate of Vanderbilt Medical School. Services Sunday, 11:30 AM, at Temple Sinai, 1 Engle St., Tenafly, NJ. Donations may be made to the North Jersey Symphony Orchestra or Temple Sinai Music Fund. For information, ''Wien & Wien'' 800-322-0533.
