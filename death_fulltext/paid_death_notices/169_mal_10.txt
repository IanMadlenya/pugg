
        KNIPPENBERG-Jack J. Age 59 years of Hawthorne, NJ. On Thursday, February 1, 2001. Beloved husband of Karin (nee Hoock). Loving father of Susan Knippenberg of Hoboken, NJ and Christine MacDougall of Langhorn, PA. Dear grandfather of Devon and Casey MacDougall. Brother of Sigi Sauthoff in Germany. Jack was a graduate of Pace University, valedictorian of his class. He was also an instructor at Pace. Jack began his career in banking at Chase Manhattan Bank in New York City, working his way from Junior positions to the Management Team. He served as the Senior Chase Officer in Istanbul, Turkey. He retired in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia as CEO of the Saudi Investment Bank. Visting hours Saturday, February 3, 2001 from 12 noon to 1:30 PM with a funeral service to follow at 1:30 PM at the Browning-Forshay Funeral Home, 557 Lafayette Avenue, Hawthorn, New Jersey. The family requests flowers be omitted and memorial donations be made to the North Shore Animal League, 25 Davis Avenue, Port Washington, New York 11050 in his memory.
