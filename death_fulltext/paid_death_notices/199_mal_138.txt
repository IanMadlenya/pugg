
        DEVENS--Charles. Of Milton, MA died on August 13, 2003, at age 93, in Prouts Neck, ME. He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth Chace Emmet, three children, Charles Devens, Jr. of Essex, MA, Edith D. Iler of Beverly, MA and Robert S. Devens of New York, eight grandchildren and one great grandchild. His wife of 64 years, Edith Wolcott Devens, died in 1998 and a son, William L. Devens, died in 1968. A private service will be held.
