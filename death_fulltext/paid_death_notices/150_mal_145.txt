
        BERSON-Irving B. On July 7, 1999. Loving husband of the late Mildred. Devoted father of Ilene Tanenbaum, Joan Winokur, Susan Turchin and Vicki Stein. Cherished grandfather. Mr. Berson was the President and Founder of Berson & Wishna and Wright & Lato. He was an active member of the East Midwood Jewish Center in Brooklyn and in later years a member of the Fort Lee Jewish Center. A funeral service is scheduled for 12 noon today at Temple Beth El, 415 Viola Road, Spring Valley, NY. For information contact Hellman Memorial Chapels, 914-356-8600.
