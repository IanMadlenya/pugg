
        GAMBLE-Adeline Martocci Devine, 92, died on Wednesday at Morris Hall, Lawrenceville. Wife of the late Eugene J. Devine and the late Robert I. Gamble, she is survived by a daughter, Alice Ann D. Connolly of Pennington, NJ and a son, Eugene J. Devine of Arlington, VA, eight grandchildren, six great-grandchildren. She retired after teaching 39 years at P.S. 71, Pelham Bay, the Bronx. Memorial contributions may be made to Morris Hall P.O. Box 6498, Lawrenceville, New Jersey 08648.
