
        FRAGA-Rafael F.., 75. World War II veteran, recipient of Bronze Star, and native New Yorker, died peacefully at home in California, on February 13th. He was a civil engineer licensed to practice in seven states. Beloved father of Jennifer, William, and Bruce. Grandfather of John and Cassie. Former husband of Patricia Hurley. Sadly missed for his sense of humor by relatives and friends.
