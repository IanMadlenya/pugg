
        RONK--Glenn Emery. Of Wilton, CT, on January 15, 2005, at age 79. Beloved husband of Barbara Lee, devoted father of four children, Connie, Edward, Martin and William, as well as grandfather of nine and great grandfather of two. A veteran of World War II, graduate of the University of Southern California, and long time senior executive in the electronics industry, retiring as an EVP of General Signal of Stamford, CT. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made in his memory to The American Cancer Society, 372 Danbury Rd, Wilton, CT 06897.
