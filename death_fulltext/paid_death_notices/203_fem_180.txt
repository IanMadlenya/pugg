
        KONNER--Catherine. On Saturday, December 27, 2003, Catherine Konner died peacefully at her Chevy Chase, MD, home. Wife of Stuart Garfinkle and mother of Harper Garfinkle. She is also survived by her mother Joan Weiner Konner of NYC, father Jack Konner of Ridgefield, CT, and sister Rosemary Steinbaum, of Montclair, NJ. Memorial service will be held at Temple Sinai, 3100 Military Rd. NW, Washington, DC, Monday, December 29, at 11 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Ratner Museum, 10001 Old Georetown Road, Bethsda, MD 20814; or the Norwood School, 8821 River Road, Bethesda, MD 20817.
