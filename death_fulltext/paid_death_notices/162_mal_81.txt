
        LUCIA-Ralph J. On July 6, 2000. Beloved husband of Constance (nee Bochino). Devoted father of Thomas and father-in-law of Vivian. Loving grandfather of Danielle and Carolyn. Dear brother of Frank and Viola (Milillo). Also survived by three nephews, two neices and several grand neices and grand nephews. Ralph had been the President of Lucia Brothers Funeral Home Inc. He was a US Army veteran of World War II, serving with 779th Tank Batalion in Manila. He was an active member of the Claremont Council of the Royal Arcanum, Belmont Community Center, Catholic War Veterans and Holy Name Society. Reposing Sunday and Monday 2-5 and 7-9PM at Lucia Brothers Funeral Home., 569 East 184 Street, Bronx. Mass at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church, 10:15 AM,Tuesday. In lieu of flowers donations to The American Cancer Society, 1 Executive Blvd, Suffern, NY 10901.
