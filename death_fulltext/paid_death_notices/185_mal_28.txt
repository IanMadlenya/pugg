
        LLOYD-Rev. Dr. Nathaniel Tyler. The Reverend Dr. Nathaniel Tyler Lloyd, Pastor of Trinity Baptist Church in the Williamsbridge section of the Bronx, New York, passed away on Wednesday, May 29, 2002. He was 71 years old. Pastor Lloyd came to Trinity Baptist Church in 1960. During his 42 year tenure, the church experienced tremendous growth under his leadership-a new edifice, increased membership, and a transformative impact in the lives of individuals and institutions in its surrounding communities. National Baptist Convention U.S.A., Inc., and other local and national ministries, Pastor Lloyd was a past president of Lott Carey Baptist Foreign Mission Convention, and at the time of his death, he was a vice president of Baptist World Alliance. Pastor Lloyd was a prolific preacher, renowned for his love of worship and his giftedness as a liturgist. Pastor Lloyd is survived by his wife, Mrs. Portia K. Tyler Lloyd, his son, Dr. Mark David Tyler-Lloyd, and his daughter, Rev. Naomi Elaine Tyler-Lloyd. His body will lie in state on Thursday, June 6, 2002, from 9 AM to 9 PM at Trinity Baptist Church, 808 East 224th Street, Bronx, New York. Final viewing will be held at the church on Friday, June 7, from 8 AM to 8:45 AM. A farewell service of worship and celebration will begin at 9 AM. For more information contact McCall's Bronxwood Funeral Home, 718-231-7647.
