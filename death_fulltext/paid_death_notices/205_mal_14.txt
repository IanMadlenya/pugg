
        MUNNELL--Dr. Equinn William, noted obstetrician and gynecological surgeon, pioneer in ovarian cancer, skilled teacher, and beloved father and husband to his family, died on January 21, 2004. Born 1913, he was Emeritus Professor of Clinical Obstetrics and Gynecology at the Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. A graduate of Amherst College and Cornell University Medical School, he was a past President of the New York Obstetrical Society, Sloane Alumni Society and New York Medical Society. Equinn Munnell was a long time resident of Gramercy Park and Southport, CT, and a former member of the Pequot Yacht Club. The cherished husband for 65 years of Frances Clark Munnell, deceased, he is survived by his children, Thomas Clark Munnell of Boston and Marion, MA, Catherine Munnell Smith of Washington DC, and Elizabeth Hamilton Munnell of Cambridge, MA, seven grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. Memorial service will be held on Saturday, February 7 at 2 PM in Calvary Episcopal Church at 21st St and Park Avenue South, NYC. Memorial gifts may be sent to Sloane Hospital for Women at New York Presbyterian Hospital, 654 West 170th St, NYC 10032.
