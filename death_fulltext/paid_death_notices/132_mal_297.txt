
        SEIGEL-Samuel E. Attorney and Counselor at Law, on January 21, 1998; only twelve days after the passing of his beloved wife and best friend, Pauline M. Seigel. Born March 29, 1912. He was a community leader, innovative President of the Kiwanis Club of Elmont, NY and founding chairman of the Aircraft Noise Control Council of Nassau County, Inc., credited with creating the impetus to compel the aircraft industry to add noise suppressors to jet planes. Father of Florence (Ralph) Belkind and Elaine (Martin) Schwartz. Grandfather of Dennis, Deborah and Alison Schwartz. Service at Boulevard-Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, New York, Sunday, January 25, 1998 at 11:30 AM. Interment at the J&B Moroch Family Circle plot at Wellwood Cemetery, Pine Lawn, NY.
