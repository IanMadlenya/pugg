
        KIRCHHOFER-Robert J, Age eighty-nine of Sun City Center, FL, died on July 11. Formerly a resident of Pelham, NY, he worked for Caltex Oil for forty-nine years. Mr Kirchhofer lived in Jamaica, India, Holland & New York. Preceded in death by his beloved wife Vera & son Kenny. Devoted husband of Dorothy, beloved father of Carol Brown, Valerie Schechter & Leslie Bagon. Cherished grandfather of Courtney, Eric, Nathaniel, Dana & Sarah. Proud greatgrandfather of Jonas & Maia.
