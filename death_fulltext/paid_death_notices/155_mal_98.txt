
        DiCHIERA-Edward F. On December 2, 1999 in New York City. Devoted brother of Alfred DiChiera and his wife, Elisa Irma Orellano of Buenos Aires, Argentina. Dear uncle of many nieces and nephews, and cousin to many. Retired engineer for the City of N.Y. Board of Education. Mass of Christian Burial, Thursday, December 9, 1999, 10 am at St. Gabriel's Church, 3250 Arlington Avenue, Riverdale. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to Montclair State University Alumni Association, 1 Normal Avenue, Upper Montclair, New Jersey 07043 or the charity of your choice.
