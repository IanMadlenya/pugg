
        YABLOKOFF-Jack. Most beloved husband and best friend of Helen Schraub Yablokoff. Cherished and loving father and fatherin-law of Randy, Susan and Michael Minoff, Robin and Larry Kaufman. Adored grandfather of Matthew, Meredith, Erica, Amanda and Jared. Founder of Yablokoff's Kingsway Memorial Chapel, Juilliard educated cellist and proud member of the Brooklyn Trio, in addition to many other chamber music groups. He was well loved, well respected, and the legacy he leaves will inspire us all. Services Sunday, 12 noon, at the Yablokoff Kingsway Memorial Chapel, 1978 Coney Island Ave., Brooklyn.
