
        CORY--David. 76, of New Rochelle, NY. Passed away peacefully June 30, 2004. Dear husband and lover to Sally A. Cory, father to Leslie and Robert Cory, Lucy Landesman Halperin, Paul and Peter Landesman, brother to Ellen Epstein Cory. Beloved fatherin-law, grandfather and uncle. President and Founder of Tee Pee Olives Inc., Italica Imports and the H.B. Hunter Company. A world renowned expert on Spanish olives and Mediterranean olive oil. A benefactor to numerous charities and a generous and caring mentor to his employees. ''Un fuerte abrazo'' from his family and friends.
