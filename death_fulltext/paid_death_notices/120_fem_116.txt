
        McCAIN-Jane Murtagh, died November 26, 1996 in Pinehurst, NC. Mrs. McCain was born on January 22, 1910 in West Chester, PA. She graduated from the Shipley School, Bryn Mawr, PA., in 1927 and from La Lierre, in Paris, in 1928. She was married to Samuel Adams McCain, general counsel of Corn Products Company who died in 1964. She is survived by her daughter Sarah (Mrs. John) Currie of Southern Pines, NC, and two nephews, and was pre-deceased by her son, Samuel Adams McCain, Jr. Mrs. McCain was an active member in the Riverdale, New York community from 1940 to 1991. A memorial service will be held at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be made to Christ Church, 5030 Henry Hudson Parkway, Riverdale, New York, 10471, or a charity of your choice.
