
        MORGAN-Rev. Edward C., of Fairfield, CT. Born in 1909, in Warner, NH. Attended Mt. Herman Prepartory School in Northfield, MA, and graduated from Harvard College in 1932. He studied at Harvard Divinity and graduated from The Episcopal Theological Seminary, Cambridge, MA, in 1935. He was ordained to the deaconate in June 1935, by the Right Reverend Henry Knox Sherrill, presiding Bishop of the Episcopal Church of America. He was assigned to St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Bridgeport, CT, in 1942, and held that position for over 30 years. Rev. Morgan retired but remained active within the ministry of St. John's Episcopal Church until 1991. He was a member of Masonic Temple of America, St. John's Lodge No. 8, A.F. and A.M., Stratford, CT, and was past Grand Chaplain of the Masonic Order of CT. Rev. Morgan was a direct descendant of Miles Morgan, founder of Springfield, MA, and also of William Janes, a found of New Haven, CT. He is survived by his wife Marcia Childs Morgan, his children Marcia M. Fish, Edward C. Morgan, Kingsley Morgan, Sarah M. Orefice, Margaret M. Blanda, a sister Olive M. Turner and 13 grandchildren and 12 great-grandchildren. The body of Rev. Morgan will be received into St. John's Episcopal Church, Bridgeport, and lie in state. Public visitation will be on Tuesday, September 2, from 48 PM. Burial is private.
