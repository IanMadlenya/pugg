
        LEE-Jack, 91. Of North Miami Beach went to heaven to meet his wife, Edith & daughter Judy Ann. Jack ''The Great Lee'' ''The Professor'' is survived by his daughter, Nancy Ericksen and his grandson, Lars Ericksen. Jack arranged Lionel Hampton's Band and managed Louis Armstrong & Eddie Haywood. He was an avid golfer with Perry Como, Dean Martin & Eddie Fisher. He was ''The Music Man.'' We love you.
