
        SZOKE-Elizabeth, age 91, died March 15th, 2000 after a short illness. Mrs. Szoke was a retired language teacher at Riverdale Country School in the Bronx. Born in Hungary, she moved to the U.S. in 1939 along w/her husband Stephen and daughter MaryAnn. She is survived by a grandson, Daniel Porcher, of Hopewell, NJ and two great-grandchildren, Rachel and Adam. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, March 25th, 2 PM, at Friends Meeting House on E. 15th St., NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Friends Service Committee.
