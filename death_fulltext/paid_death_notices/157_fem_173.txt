
        DALSIMER-Adele M. Of Brookline, MA, on February 13th, 2000. Co-founder and Co-director of the Irish Studies Program at Boston College. Distinguished author, scholar, and teacher. Cherished wife of Dr. James S. Dalsimer. Beloved mother of Jennifer Dalsimer Archer and Joshua Dalsimer and proud mother-in-law of Glenn Archer. Doting grandmother of Nate and Samantha Archer. Devoted daughter of Ida and Buddy Mintz and dear sister of Sandy Mintz. Adored friend, cousin, aunt, and colleague to so many. We mourn the passing of this extraordinary woman of passion and accomplishment. She will be deeply missed.
