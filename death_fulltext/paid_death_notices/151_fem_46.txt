
        WECHSLER-Jeanne L. Age 95, on August 5, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Arnold; loving and devoted mother of Robert, Lawrence and Stephen; dear sister of Irving, Mae, Doris, Frances, Abraham, and the late Morris, Harry and Mal; adoring grandmother of Sharon, Alisa, Jonathan, Michael, Amy and Jodi; and proud greatgrandmother of Emma, Cassidy and Zoe. Lovingly remembered by family and friends. Services will be held at Gutterman's, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, 9:45 AM, Friday, August 6. Contributions may be made in her name to the American Cancer Society.
