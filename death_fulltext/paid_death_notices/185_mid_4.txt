
        GREENBERG-Arthur. The Board of Directors, staff, participants and friends of the Association for the Help of Retarded Children wish to convey our heartfelt condolences to Ann Greenberg on the loss of Arthur, her beloved husband. Ann, the founder of our Association, was supported in all her efforts by Arthur. Arthur Greenberg, who served in the United States Army for many years, went on to a 32 year career in the U.S. Postal Service. Arthur is survived by his wife Ann, daughter Bonnie, and three grandchildren, Matthew, Sarah, and Devin. The funeral will be held at Schwartz Brothers Funeral Home, on Queens Blvd, in Forest Hills on June 27 at noon.
