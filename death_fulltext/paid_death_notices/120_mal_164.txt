
        SUNA-Conrad, an Executive at Dean Witter Discover, died Wednesday Morning, Jan. 15 after a long battle with cancer. He was 62. He is survived by his wife, Selma, of New Milford, NJ, his only child, David, whom Mr. Suna was able to see graduate law school and start his career as a trial attorney for the US Dept. of Justice in Miami, FL. and his brother, Robert, of Hastings, N.Y., who supported him unselfishly throughout his illness. Services ''Gutterman-Musicant, corner Passaic and Park Streets, Hackensack, NJ today at noon.
