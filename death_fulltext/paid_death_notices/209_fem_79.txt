
        HAASE--Mary Louise Mallery, Ph.D. W. Winfield, NY, May 22. Educator, actress, mother. She had held memberships in SAG, AFTRA and Actors' Equity during early career off-Broadway and in the northeast. She held a lifelong passion for learning, teaching at SUNY Rockland, Adelphi University, Syracuse University. Survivors: daughter, Rogina L. Jeffries; grandchildren, Julia P. Faulkner Jeffries and Philip J. Roosevelt Jeffries of Boston; brother Roger H. Mallery, sister Sylvia E. Mallery. Memorial service at W. Winfield Federated Church June 19, 10:30.
