
        FAYARD-Annette Colgate. Died August 5, in Pt. Grimaud, France. Beloved wife of Michel; mother of 5 devoted children, and grandmother to 12 adoring grandchildren. Sister of Mimi Kirk of Greenwich, CT., and John K. Colgate Jr., of Mill Neck, NY. Annette graduated from Miss Porter's School and Bennett College, and lived her 45 years of married life in Paris. She will be missed on both continents.
