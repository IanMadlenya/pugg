
        CAMPBELL-Lady Shelagh Jocelyn Morrison-Bell Moore, age 87, formerly of New York, died April 21, 2001 in England. Journalist and author of Resident Alien. Survived by her son David A. C. Moore, daughter-in-law Christiane Citron & granddaughter Cornelia, of Denver, CO. The Memorial service England August 3. Contact David Moore tele. 303.777.2242.
