
        KING-WOOD--O. David. Who taught English and French at St. Bernard's School for fortyones years, died on September 3, 2003, nine days before his ninetieth birthday. Two generations of students and colleagues mourn his loss, but will cherish his memory. Instead of flowers please send contributions to the Central Park Conservancy, David King-Wood Tree Fund, 14 East 60th Street, NY, NY 10022. A memorial program will take place at the school, 4 East 98th Street, Thursday, October 9, at 5:30 P.M.
