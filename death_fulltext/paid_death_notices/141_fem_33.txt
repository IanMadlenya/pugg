
        WARNER-Nancy Hill. The Alumnae Board, Trustees, faculty, staff and alumnae of The NightingaleBamford School mourn the loss of our beloved friend. A member of the Class of 1932, Mrs. Warner served as President of the Alumnae Board and as an Alumnae Fund Class Agent. In 1993, she received the Frances Nightingale Service Award for her extraordinary commitment to our School. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. Andrea Demirjian '81 Alumnae Board President Dorothy A. Hutcheson, Head of School
