
        CROW-Lt. Gen. Duward Lowery. USAF (Ret), age 78, of San Antonio, TX, died Tuedsay, October 28, 1997. Known to friends and family as Pete, he was born in Ft. Payne, Alabama, on June 26, 1919. He graduated from West Point in 1941 and received his MBA from Harvard Business School in 1948. Twice recipient of the Distinguished Service Medal, he served as Director of Budget, Comptroller, and Assistant Vice Chief of Staff of the USAF. From 1974-1978 he worked at NASA headquarters in Washington, DC as Assistant Deputy Administrator. In 1978 he retired to San Antonio, TX where he remained active in finance and real estate. He was preceded in death by his wife, Eleanor Stribling Dance, of San Antonio, in 1994. He is survived by his three daughters, Eleanor Dance Crow, Cheney Gaylord Crow, PhD, and Elizabeth Crow Perkins; three granddaughters, Florine Eleanor Canelle, Rainey Dupre Perkins, and Catherine Arianna Perkins; his brother, Paul Crow of Fort Payne, AL; and many nieces and nephews. Graveside service will be on Friday, October 31, 1997 at City Cemetery #
