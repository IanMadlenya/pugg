
        HIRSCH--George. Beloved brother, uncle, great-uncle and friend, passed away November 2nd in New York City at age 76. He was born in Vienna, Austria and came to the United States at age 8. He graduated from Queens College and Columbia Law School, and practiced tax law. He had a great sense of humor and a kind and generous spirit. He is survived by sister Eva Shultis, niece Amy Frey, nephews Charles Shultis and David Shultis, and a greatniece and nephews. He will be greatly missed.
