
        EBLEN-William R. The Trustees of the Rene Dubos Center for Human Environments mourn the passing of its founder and President, December 16 extending their deepest sympathies to his wife Ruth and family. Dr. Eblen's collaboration with Rene Dubos has influenced environmental decisionmaking worldwide through forums and related U.N. activities that epitomized the Debos maxim ''Think Globally, Act Locally''. Dr. Noel J. Brown, Chairman
