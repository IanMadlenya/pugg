
        FIGATNER--Antoinette (Tony), died peacefully at her home on March 23, 2005, at age 101. Her mental sharpness and vibrancy were a constant to the end. Beloved wife of the late Joseph, devoted mother of Erny F. Libros and Lucy F. Ullmann, cherished grandmother of John, Steven, Laurie, David and Nicole. An elegant woman who had a curiosity and zest for life was an inspiration to all. Services Friday, 1PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue.
