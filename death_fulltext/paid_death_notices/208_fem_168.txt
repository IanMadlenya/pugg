
        KELLER--Carole. Douglas Elliman mourns the loss of our friend and colleague, Carole Keller. Carole was a valued and successful sales agent who was recognized as a highly ethical and professional person. She will be remembered in our hearts as a true example of strong determination and a willingness to go the extra mile, always with a smile and graciousness. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family and friends.
