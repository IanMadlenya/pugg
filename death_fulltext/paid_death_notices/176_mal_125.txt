
        LAPORTE-William Frederick, 88, of NYC and Buck Hill Falls PA on September 10, 2001. Cherished husband for 53 years of the late Ruth Hillard Laporte, beloved father of Suzanne L. Westcott, Lynn A. Ducommun and William F. Laporte, III and devoted grandfather of nine grandchildren. Funeral service Thursday, September 13 at 2PM at Madison Avenue Presbyterian Church, 921 Madison Avenue, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to New York-Presbyterian Hospital, Development Office, 525 East 68 St, Box 123, NY NY 10021.
        LAPORTE-William F. The Board and residents of the River House mourn the passing of our longserving friend and Director whose tireless dedication to our building has benefited us all. Our heartfelt condolences are extended to his family.
