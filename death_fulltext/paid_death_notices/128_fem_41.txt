
        FARGO-Margaret Morse. Of Edison, NJ. Died on September 3, 1997 on Nantucket Island. She was the wife of the late Alvin W. Fargo, Jr. and is survived by her sister, Louise M. Tweedy, her three children, Lorine, Jane and Mike Fargo, three grandsons and three nieces. A Memorial Service will be held at Grace Episcopal Church, 600 Cleveland Ave, Plainfield, NJ on Saturday, September 27, 1997 at 3pm. In lieu of flowers memorial donations may be made in Mrs. Fargo's memory to the WardlawHartridge School, Edison, NJ.
