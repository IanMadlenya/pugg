
        CARLSON--Robert O., Ph.D. 83, died January 31 in Scottsdale, AZ. He directed opinion research for Standard Oil Company (NJ) and was president of the American Association for Public Opinion Research and of the Public Relations Society of America and dean of the school of business administration and banking of Adelphi University. He belonged to the Metropolitan Club. He is survived by Eileen Evers Carlson, his wife for 38 years, and by the loving memories of countless friends and students stimulated by his intelligence and warmed by his good humor. Mass at Our Lady of Joy, Carefree, at 2 P.M. Friday, February 4.
