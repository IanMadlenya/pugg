
        HAMPTON-Renee Smith. 89, of Greenville, De, formerly of Manhasset, LI, NY, on January 2, 2001. Mrs. Hampton is survived by her husband, Willard G. In lieu of flowers, donations to Christ Church, Manhasset, NY 11030 or Wilson College, Chambersburg, PA 17201.
