
        SARCH-David Joseph Anthony, October 27, 1940 - May 9, 1998: On May 9 suddenly, at home, of a heart attack. Loved immensely by Ros, Louise and Terri, his family and all his many friends throughout the world. A highly respected lawyer and advisor-a great loss to his profession. Mr. Sarch will be profoundly missed. Funeral will be held at The Priory Church of St. Gregory the Great, 10 St. James Square, Cheltenham, Gloucestershire, on Saturday May 16, 1998 at 11:30am. Flowers very welcome to Selim Smith & Co, 74 Prestbury Rd, Cheltenham, Gloucestershire GL52 2DJ: Tel (01242) 525383.
