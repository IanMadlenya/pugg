
        KELLEY-Florence Maher, R.N. Mother of Paul Kelley, O.D. of Branford, CT., sister of Eileen Maher of NYC, sister of the late Helen Porter Maher-McKernon, Sister Mary Gratia Maher, R.S.M., Arthur C., Stuart E., Robert D. and Rev. Gordon H. Maher. She was an alumna of Lenox Hill Hospital School of Nursing, Class of 1927.
