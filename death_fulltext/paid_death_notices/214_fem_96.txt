
        KUHNS--Margaret E., 84, of New York, NY, died Thursday, November 11, 2004 at Avon Health Center, Avon, CT. She was born November 16, 1919 in Wilkinsburg, PA daughter of the late John H. and Margaret (Hood) Kuhns and had lived in New York City, NY prior to moving to Avon three years ago. A memorial funeral service will be celebrated Monday, November 15, 3 PM at Vincent Funeral Home, 880 Hopmeadow St., Simsbury, CT with Rev. Mel Kawakami officiating. Private burial will be in Simsbury Cemetery. Memorial donations may be made to the Salvation Army, 855 Asylum Ave., Hartford, CT 06105.
