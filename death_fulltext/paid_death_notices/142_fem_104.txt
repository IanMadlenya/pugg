
        WEBER-Frieda. Beloved wife of the late Harry Weber, died in Brooklyn at age 83 on November 10th, 1998, three months after suffering a major stroke. Loving mother of Cynthia, Nathan, Robert and Sheila; loving mother-in-law of Wendy, Lynn and Jeff; adored grandmother of Lisa, Dana, Rebecca, Vanessa and Seth; loving sister of Rose, Rae, and Gurty. We mourn her passing. Rest now, Mom, with Dad, in peace.
