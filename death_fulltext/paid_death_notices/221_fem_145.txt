
        ROTHSTEIN--Ruth S. Ruth, previously of Scarsdale, New York, died on June 6 at her home in Palm Beach, FL. Ruth, one of the first female officers in the U.S. Air Force, was best known in her later years for her contribution as a volunteer for the American Cancer Society. Predeceased by her husband Alex, Ruth is survived by her daughters Joan and Betsy. A private burial took place in New York. Anyone wishing to make a contribution in Ruth's name, kindly direct it to the American Cancer Society, Westchester Division, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, NY 10601.
