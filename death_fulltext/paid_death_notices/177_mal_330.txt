
        EDELSON-Robert. 89, formerly of Brooklyn, passed away peacefully on October 23, 2001. Caring, devoted and loving husband of 65 years to Beatrice. Proud father of Drs. Kenneth and Stephen. Loving father-in-law of Carol. Doting grandfather to Dana, Richie, Alex, Bradley and Joshua, and devoted friend to many. He will be missed. Funeral held in Atlanta.
