
        PENCER-Eileen. The members of the Board of Trustees of the Lower Eastside Service Center along with all staff members are profoundly saddened by the untimely death of our beloved President and CEO, Eileen Pencer. She was a visionary whose remarkably good judgement and steady leadership helped guide both the expansion of LESC and the success of Angel Street Thrift Shop. We cannot say enough about her tremendous courage, spirit and will to live. She has been an inspiration to us all. We will always love her and cherish her memory. We remember her with heartfelt gratitude and extend our condolences to her family. Contributions may be made to the Eileen Pencer Memorial Fund made payable to Friends of LESC, attn: Lee Pavis, 157 Chambers St., New York, NY 10007. Peter Santoro, Board Chairman Elaine Katz, First Vice President
