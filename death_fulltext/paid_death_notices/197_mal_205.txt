
        BENEDETTO--James (''Jimmy Red''). Age 64, on June 19, 2003. Well-loved NYC restaurateur. Owned Village landmark ''Googies'' 1962-2003. He opened ''Les Routiers Bistro'' on the Upper West Side in 1990. He is mourned by his wife Susan, son Michael, twin daughters Emily and Katy, mother Mary, and sisters Michele and Phyllis. Visitation Saturday, June 21st, 27PM at Perazzo Funeral Home, 199 Bleecker St., NYC. >>AD#
