
        HURLEY-Miriam Greene. Age 90, of Boston and Chilmark, Martha's Vineyard (formerly of Weston), on Thursday, February 1, 2001. Wife of the late Donald J. Hurley. Mother of Cornelia Hurley McPeek of Chestnut Hill, Donald Hurley, Jr. of Lloyd Harbor, NY, Stephen Nash Hurley of Boston and Rosamond Page Hurley Shugrue of Boston. Gandmother of several grandchildren. There will be a private family Mass. Relatives and friends are invited to attend a memorial service on Friday, March 9, 2001, at 3 PM, at the Memorial Church at Harvard University, Harvard Yard, Cambridge. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Miriam Greene Hurley Memorial Fund at the Martha's Vineyard Garden Club, RFD 319X, Vineyard Haven, MA 02568.
