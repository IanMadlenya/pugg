
        KATZENSTEIN--Sylvia. Sutton Place Synagogue records with deep sorrow the passing of our founding member and benefactor. A cultured and beautiful woman whose elegant presence will be missed. Condolences to her daughter Barbara Jimenez and grandson Luis. Allan Schranz, Rabbi Mark Wiener, President
