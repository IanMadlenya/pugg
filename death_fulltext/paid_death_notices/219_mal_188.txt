
        PETTERSON--James B., age 80, died on Monday, March 28, 2005. Born October 9, 1924 in Worcester, MA, he was the son of B. Elmer and Ellen M. Petterson. Mr. Petterson graduated cum laude from Harvard in 1945. He then went on to serve in the U.S. Navy and to graduate from Harvard Law School. Mr. Petterson was a longtime Sr. Vice President of Bankers Trust. He was a member of The Harvard Club. He leaves a nephew and nieces. Funeral services will be private in Worcester.
