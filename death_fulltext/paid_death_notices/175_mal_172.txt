
        TILLIM-Sidney. Painter and critic. On August 16th in his 76th year. Dearly beloved husband of Diane Radycki-Tillim and the late Muriel Sharon. He is survived by his cherished niece Dena Dekel, the loving and supportive Tillim-Karlin family, and 38 years of devoted Bennington College students, no less than the worlds of art and bookcollecting that he engaged passionately. He will be greatly missed. Viewing Saturday 2-5PM and 7-9 PM and service Sunday 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Interment Spring Valley, NY. A memorial service is planned for October. Contributions in his memory may be made to the New York Public Library for the express purpose of establishing a fund for the maintenance and preservation of Sid's papers.
