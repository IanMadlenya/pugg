
        COLENDA--Frank G., MD. Of NYC died unexpectedly on February 10, 2004. Dr. Colenda was a well-respected internist who practiced in Manhattan for more than 30 years. He was a senior attending physician at Lenox Hill Hospital. Funeral services are tentatively being planned for Friday and/or Saturday at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Please call (212) 288-3500 for more details. Donations in his name may be made to the American Heart Association.
