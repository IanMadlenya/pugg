
        MANDEL -- Eva Levine. Peacefully, on March 6, 2004, age 99, of Brooklyn Heights, NY. Beloved mother and mother-in-law of Ira and Barbara Levine, and Edward and Judy Levine. Adored grandmother of Judy and Alan Garet, Ronald and Cheryl Levine, Karen and Joseph Carelli, and Daniel and Lauren Levine. Loving greatgrandmother of eight. A graveside service will be held Monday, 1:30 pm, at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY. She will always be the ''belle of the ball'' and she touched the hearts of anyone who was fortunate to have known her. For information contact Oyster Bay Funeral Home.
