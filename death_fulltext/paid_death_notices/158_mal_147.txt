
        MIDDENDORF-Henry S. March 9, 2000. Beloved brother of J. William Middendorf, former Secretary of the Navy and Ambassador to the Netherlands, Sarah Lofving of Portland, Me., and the late William Kennedy Boone Middendorf, a banker. He had been president of the Sons of the Revolution, the New York Genealogical and Biographical Society, and the Youth Foundation. A veteran of World War II. He practiced law in the metropolitan area for many years until his death. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Youth Foundation, 36 West 44 Street, New York, New York 10036, Room 716.
