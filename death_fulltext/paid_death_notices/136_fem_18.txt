
        GRUSHACK-Cynthia. The Board, Staff and members of United Cerebral Palsy of New York City, mourn the passing of Cynthia Gruschack, who served over the years as assistant to our founder, Jack Hausman. Both at Belding Heminway and since 1994 at UCP, she helped to assure support for our vital services. We extend our deepest condolences to her husband Louie, her son Ira and daughter-in-law Jo, her daughter Allison and son-in-law Leonard Isaacs, and to her grandchildren Sidney, Jesse, Sammy, Adam and Justin. Leo Hausman, President Edward R. Matthews Executive Director UCP of NYC, Inc.
        GRUSHACK - Cynthia. Cynthia was my right arm for many years and a good friend to people with cerebral palsy. I'll miss her humor and support and will remember her courage. Louie and the family have my profound sympathy. Jack Hausman
