
        SIEGEL--Ann L. The Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center community mourns the death of Ann L. Siegel, a committed and caring friend and a thoughtful supporter of this institution for more than a quarter century. An inspiration to all who knew her, she touched countless lives with her enthusiasm and energy. We extend heartfelt condolences to her husband, Herb, and to the entire family at this time of loss. Harold Varmus, President Douglas A. Warner III Chairman, Boards of Overseers and Managers Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center
