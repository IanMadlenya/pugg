
        WALKER--Donald A. Distinguished Professor of Physics at SUNY New Paltz. Died on September 15, 2005. A nuclear physicist and computer specialist, he graduated from Queens College and Oregon State University where he received his Ph.D. in 1968. An Air Force veteran of the Korean War, he had many interests in addition to science. As a young man, he studied trumpet with William Vacchiano. Survived by his wife Maureen, children Daniel and Katharine and his brother Henry J., he was pre-deceased by his twin brother David S. Services 2PM Saturday, September 24, at the Reformed Church in New Paltz.
