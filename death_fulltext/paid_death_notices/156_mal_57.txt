
        WEINBERG-Jeshajahu (Shaike) The United States Holocaust Memorial Museum deeply mourns the death of its visionary Founding Director, Jeshajahu (Shaike) Weinberg, on January 1, 2000 in Tel Aviv, and extends its condolences to his family and friends. The Museum will announce a memorial program at a later date, and a memorial fund is being established in his honor. Miles Lerman, Chairperson Ruth B. Mandel, Vice Chairperson Sara J. Bloomfield, Director
