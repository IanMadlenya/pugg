
        TAUB-Martin G. The members, Board of Directors and the officers of Metropolitan Package Store Association mourn the passing of Martin Taub.         TAYLOR-Marion. Graham-Windham Services to Families and Children is deeply saddened by the loss of our Board member, Marion Taylor, and extends heartfelt condolences to her family. James Gorman, Chairman
