
        CLAPP-STEEL-Martica (Hartley) Maiden name: Gloria Martha Alberni Argilagos De Mola. Born Cuba July 4th 1927. Died February 2nd 2002. She was a small but powerful woman; she often referred to her self as a good thing in a small package. Her laurels are too many to mention. She will be missed around the world from USA to China. Family in the USA, Bermuda, Bahamas, Cuba and South America survives Martica. The family is asking for those who wish, to light a candle on the evening of Sunday, February 24, and have a 3-minute silence. Thank you all, that helped her, on the journey of life. Christopher and family
