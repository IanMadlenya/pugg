
        DENHOLTZ -- Dr. Myron (Mickey). On August 8, 2003. Age 77, of West Palm Beach, formerly of Maplewood, NJ. A psychiatrist, he was a pioneer in behavior modification. He graduated from NYU Medical School. He trained at Metropolitan Hospital. He was an Associate Professor of Psychiatry at NJ Medical School, Director of Psychiatry at Elizabeth General Hospital and Chief of Outpatient Clinic of Psychiatry at NY Beth Israel Hospital. Survived by wife Marcia, daughters Ruth and Nancy, three grandchildren and brother Edward.
