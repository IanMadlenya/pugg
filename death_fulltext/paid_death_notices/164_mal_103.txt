
        SCHWARTZ-Sy. The entire staff of the Miami Project to Cure Paralysis and the Buoniconti Fund join in mourning the passing of our dear friend and colleague, our Public Relations Legend, Sy Schwartz. Our sincere gratitude for his passion and tireless efforts in the advancement of our message and our cause. He was the consummate professional and will be missed. Our deepest sympathies go to his wife Sheila, his sons, Laurence and Matthew, to all his friends and family, and to his extended family of associates at Howard J. Rubenstein.
