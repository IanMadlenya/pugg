
        WYNER--Jay S., 83, of Sands Point, NY died on September 20, 2004. Adored husband of Ethel; beloved father of Shari and Richard Narva and Janis and Paul Sheinkopf; dear grandfather of Matthew, Laura, Arielle, Zachary, Josh, Jamie, Sarah and Shoshi; and loving brother of Ruth Schwartzberg, Dorothy Goldfarb, Sally Seidler and the late Maxine Diamond. Services will be held at noon on Thursday, September 23 at The Community Synagogue, Port Washington, New York. He was loved and admired by all who knew him and he will be in our hearts and thoughts forever.
