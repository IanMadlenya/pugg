
        FAVA-Frank. Gone Fishin' Beloved son, brother and friend, passed away on the evening of April 24, 2003. Cause of death was cardiac arrest. He is survived by his mother, Beverly Morse, his sister, Darice Lang, his stepfather John H. Morse Jr., his brother-in-law Joseph Lang, his god-daughter Abbi K. Davis and a wealth of friends dear to his heart, to numerous to mention. Memorial service at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St., Sunday, April 27th 6PM. Contributions to Frank's memory may be made to New York City Relief for the Homeless at 1181 East Broad Street, Elizabeth, NJ 07201.
