
        STEINER-Arlyne L. Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of the beloved wife of Leonard Steiner and the adored mother of Robin (Kathy) Steiner, Ellyn (Michael) Greenspan, and Beth (Timothy) Sinatro. With her husband and with Robin and Kathy, Mrs. Steiner was a long-time friend of our medical school and a respected member of our Society of Founders. Robin serves as secretary of our Young Men's Division; Leonard is a Guardian of our University. To them and to the entire family, we offer our deepest sympathy. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura, Dean Stephen R. Karafiol, Chairman Young Men's Division
