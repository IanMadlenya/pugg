Devoted and loving father of Katy, Daniel, Sarra and Alison. Loving son of Edmund and the late Sara Tischler. Loving and cherished twin brother of Susan. David remains in the hearts of the many who love him as they remain in his. Services are on Wednesday, 12 noon at The ''Boulevard-Riverside'' Chapels, 312 Coney Island Avenue at Prospect Park, Brooklyn.
        TISCHLER-David Charles. Your love, selflessness, brilliance, romance of life and mostly your devoted love of your family and friends will always be remembered. Words cannot express our sorrow for your untimely demise and for your family who love you deeply. To Leslie, Katie, Danny, Sarra, Ali, Ed and Susan and to David, who is now resting in peace, our heartfelt love. Diane, Joseph and Jeffrey
