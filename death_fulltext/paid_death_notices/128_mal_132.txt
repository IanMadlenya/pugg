
        SCHAFFER-Abraham I. The Cardiology Section of the Department of Medicine, Lenox Hill Hospital records with sadness the loss of a respected senior staff member. Even after retiring, ''Abe'' continued to participate in conferences encouraging younger cardiologists to emulate his dedicated approach to patient care. His presence, humor and intellect will be missed. Nicholas DePasquale, M.D. Neil Coplan, M.D. Richard Kutnick, M.D.
