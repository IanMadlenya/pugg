
        HALPERN-Stephen J., 60. Of Lincroft, New Jersey, died suddenly September 20, 1997. Doctor of Law, he was a member of the New York and New Jersey Bar, Cornell Law School Association, Phi Alpha Delta Law Fraternity and Horace Mann Alumni Association. Adored & loving husband of Edith. Devoted father of Nicole of Chicago and Lincroft and Jeff. Beloved brother of Enid Ofshlag of New Jersey and Boca Raton, Florida and Willa Rose Abramson, Miami, Florida. Cherished and loved by his many friends, he will be sorely missed. Memorials to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center of New York.
