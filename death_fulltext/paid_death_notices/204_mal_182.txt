
        BROWN--Fr. Arnold, O.F.M., beloved Chaplain of the Franciscan Family Circle Pilgrimages for 47 years. Died unexpectedly December 29 at St. Anthony Friary, St. Petersburg, FL. Funeral Mass celebrated on what would have been his 81st birthday, Friday January 2, 2004. Interment in Calvary Cemetery, Clearwater, FL. Special Memorial Mass for friends in metropolitan area, 2 P.M., Sunday, January 18, 2004 in Upper Church of St. Francis of Assisi, 135 West 31st St., New York City.
