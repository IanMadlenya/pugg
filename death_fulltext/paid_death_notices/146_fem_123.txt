
        SUMNER - ROTHENBERG - Pamela. 42. Beloved and cherished wife of Richard Rothenberg, died suddenly on Saturday, March 6, 1999. She is deeply mourned by her parents, Martha and Nolan Sumner of Dallas, Texas; by her inlaws, Marion and Jack Rothenberg of New York; by her brother, Bill and his wife, Sabrina; and by her sister-in-law Ellen Klein, her husband, Michael and their son, Andrew. Pam, a gifted pianist who held a Master's Deg. in Piano Performance from North Texas State University, played in many Broadway musicals. Her talents and skills were internationally recognized and praised. She will be affectionately remembered and greatly missed by all. Memorial Service will be held on Thursday, March 11th, at 1:00 P.M., at the New York Society for Ethical Culture, 2 West 64 Street (at Central Park West).
