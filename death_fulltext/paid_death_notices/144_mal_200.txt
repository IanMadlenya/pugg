
        LESCH-Samuel. Died peacefully in his sleep in Santa Rosa, Ca. on January 7, 1999. Loving husband for 60 years to Annie Lesch of Rohnert Park. Loving father of Harry Lesche of Eureka and Barbara Lesche McCaffry of Rohnert Park. Beloved grandfather of Benjamin Joseph Lesche and Jacob Paul Lesche. A native of New York, age 90. A member of the Forton Lodge of New York and Congregation Nershalom of Cotati. Funeral services were held on Sunday, January 10, 1999 at the Eggen & Lance Mortuary. Rabbi Elisheva Sachs officiated. Interment Shomrei Torah Cemetery at Santa Rosa Memorial Park. Those desiring may make memorial contributions to their favorite charity.
