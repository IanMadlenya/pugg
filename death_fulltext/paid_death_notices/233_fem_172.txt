
        SWETZ--Elizabeth (Betty), died June 19th, 2006. Born September 11th, 1923 in Byesville, OH, she received her RN from the Cadet Nursing Corps and her BSN from Capital University, OH. In 1960 she moved to NYC, began working at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center until retirement in 1999. A longtime member of the Association of Operating Room Nurses (AORN), she served as President, Vice-President and member of the Board of Directors of AORN of NYC Chapter 3004, served on many committees, praised for her fundraising activities for the Chapter. She was a longtime member of Marble Collegiate Church, NYC. Memorial service at Marble Collegiate Church, 29th St. at 5th Ave., NYC (date to be announced).
