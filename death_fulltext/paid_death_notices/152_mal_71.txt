
        GOLDMAN-Hon. Leonard. The Grand Lodge Knights of Pythias, Domain of New York mourns the passing of one of their most distinguished members, Grand Secretary Hon. Leonard Goldman on September 2, 1999. To his wife Sheryl, son Robert, mother and sisters, we extend our heartfelt condolences in their hour of bereavement. The Pythian Order and all those whose lives he touched will truly miss him. Please make all donations to the Leonard Goldman Scholarship Fund.
