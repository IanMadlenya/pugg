
        BAKST--Marion. Age 89, died peacefully January 22, 2005, at The Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale, NY. Beloved wife of the late Milton Bakst. Mother of Lois Montgomery and Robert Ira Bakst. Devoted grandmother of Lisa and Eric. Also survived by her loving brother Bill and her devoted son-in-law Franklin. Lifelong resident of New York City. Her great humor and generous spirit will be missed by all. Services Tuesday, January 25, 12 noon at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Hebrew Home for the Aged in Riverdale, 5901 Palisade Avenue, Bronx, NY.
