
        CHAN-Stephen. The President, Boards of Governors and Overseers, faculty, administration and students of Hebrew Union CollegeJewish Institute of Religion record with deep sorrow the death of Stephen Chan, Chairman and Trustee Emeritus of the Hagop Kevorkian Fund. We extend our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his beloved family. He has left our world with a rich legacy. May his family be comforted together with all those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Sheldon Zimmerman, Pres. Burton Lehman, Chair, Board of Governors Dr. Paul M. Steinberg, Special Assistant to the Pres.
        CHAN-Stephen. New York University mourns the death of its friend and benefactor, Stephen Chan. A 1931 alumnus of the School of Commerce and a trustee of the University's Institute of Fine Arts, Mr. Chan also generously supported the study of the Near East at NYU through his positions as chairman and executive vice president of the Hagop Kevorkian Fund. All his dealings with the University were characterized by his commitment to excellence in scholarship. His passing is a loss to the NYU community. L. Jay Oliva President Martin Lipton Chairman
