
        FINK--Paul, 91, devoted husband of the late Enid Baskin Fink, father of Robert and Matthew Fink and the late Jonathan Baskin, on May 25, 2005, in Hamden, CT. Memorial service to be held at 1 PM June 8 at Temple Israel, 14 Coleytown Road, Westport, CT. Please call 203 227-1293 for directions. Paul Fink is survived by his sons, Robert and Matthew; sister, Geraldine Wasserman; and grandchildren, Evan, Lucas, and Abigail Verney-Fink. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Paul Fink's name to Amnesty International USA, 5 Penn Plaza, 14th floor, New York, NY 10001, or to the Gay Men's Health Crisis (212 367-1585) in memory of Jonathan Baskin.
