
        WOHLHORN RYDER-Janet. On July 22, 1999 in West Palm Beach, previously of New York NY. survived by her husband, Theodore Ryder of Atlantis FL, 2 daughters, Linda Simard and June Wohlhorn and brother, Warren Andrews. In Florida, Janet was former President of Snelling Employment Services of West Palm Beach. Former Chairman of both the Better Business Bureau of Palm Beach, Martin and Broward Counties and the Executive Committee of the Crossroads Program. She was also a member of the Executive Women, Executive Club of the Palm Beaches and Rotary and Toastmasters Clubs. In NY, she was the former Comptroller of Sawyer Ferguson Walker, and on the executive board of Advertising Women of New York. A memorial service will be held at the United Nations Chapel on August 21 at 12:30PM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Janet Wohlhorn Memorial Fund, C/O Executive Women of the Palm Beaches Scholarship Fund, P.O. Box 7476, West Palm Beach, FL. 33405 or to the Crossroads Program, C/O Palm Beach Community College Foundation, 812 Fern St, West Palm Beach, FL. 33401.
