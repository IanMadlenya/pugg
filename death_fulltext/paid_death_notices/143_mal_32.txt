
        PHILIPP-O. George. Of NY and Skytop, PA. Died in NYC on November 28, 1998 at the age of 83. Beloved husband of 61 years of Frances Clarkson. Father of John Clarkson Philipp of Sonoma, CA, and Laurie Coughlin of Washington, DC, father-of-law of Timothy Coughlin. He adored his seven grandchildren: Susan Blair Reese, Christine Ann Philipp, George Clarkson Philipp, Elisabeth Ann Coughlin, Timothy Crathorne Coughlin, Jr., Mary Blair Coughlin, John Clarkson Coughlin, and one great-greatchild, Paul Clarkson Philipp Reese. Born in Beaver Falls, PA, he attended Northwestern Univ. and was a member of Phi Gamma Delta fraternity. He was chairman of O. Philipp and Co., a foreign trade company based in NY with offices abroad in South America and Mexico. He was co-founder of the Forum School for Autistic Children in Waldwick, NJ, and was active on that Board for many years. He was a member of the River Club of NY and the Arcola Country Club of Paramus, N.J. A private family service will be held on Thursday, December 3rd, at the Old Stone Church, Upper Saddle River, NJ. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to NY Presbyterian Hospital, Attn: Box 123, 525 East 68 Street, New York, N.Y. 10021.
