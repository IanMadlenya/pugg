
        KANTROWITZ--Rosalind. On March 19, 2005. Former librarian at Robbins Library, Arlington, MA, until her retirement in 2003. Beloved mother of Barbara, Lore and Andrea. Loving mother-in-law of Daniel Hertzberg, Charles Ashdown, and Scott Benjamin. Cherished grandmother of Caitlin, Michael, Benjamin, Mark, Anya and Sophia. In lieu of flowers, please send donations in her memory to Hunter College in New York or the Eastern Massachusetts Literary Council.
