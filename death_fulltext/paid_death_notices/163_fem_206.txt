
        BENIOFF-Florence. The Cornell University community mourns the passing of Florence Benioff, mother of devoted Cornell alumni Barbara Friedman and Richard Benioff. We express our sincere condolences to Barbara and Stephen Friedman and Richard and Mary Benioff. We also send condolences to Mrs. Benioff's grandchildren and great-grandchildren. Hunter R. Rawlings III, President, Cornell University Harold Tanner, Chairman of the Board of Trustees, Cornell University Antonio M. Gotto, Jr., M.D., Dean, Weill Medical College of Cornell University Sanford I. Weill, Chairman of the Board of Overseers, Weill Medical College of Cornell University
        BENIOFF-Florence. The Board of Directors, faculty and staff of CLAL-The National Jewish Center for Learning and Leadership, extend deepest condolences to Barbara Friedman, our esteemed Board Chairman, her husband, Stephen, and the entire family on the passing of Barbara's mother, Florence Benioff. Her zest for life, wisdom and enthusiasm were an inspiration to everyone she touched in her remarkable life. May her memory be a blessing to all those who loved her and may they find a place of comfort at this time. Rabbi Irwin Kula, President Donna M. Rosenthal, Executive Vice Chairman
