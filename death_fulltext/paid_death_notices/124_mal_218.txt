
        HOUCK-James D., 42, Managing Director and Chief Administrative Officer of Lehman Brothers Japan, died April 26 at Wilcox Memorial Hospital on the island of Kauai, HI. The cause of death was cancer of the esophagus. James was born in Hartford, CT., and graduated from Colby College and from the Wharton School of Business in 1984. James is survived by his wife, Marilyn, of Hawaii; his father, Dr. John H. Houck of Massachusetts; a brother, John W. Houck of New York; a sister, Katherine Houck Fredrickson of Illinois; and eight nieces and nephews. He will be missed by all who knew and loved him.
