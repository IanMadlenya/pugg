
        REEMTSMA-Keith. The Department of Transplantation at the University of Rome ''La Sapienza'' mourns the loss of Professor Keith Reemtsma, a great friend and colleague. Professor Keith Reemtsma has revolutionized the field of transplantation by successfully performing the first organ transplant from a chimpanzee into a man. This generated a new field of research on the biology of xenotransplantation. Educator, scientist and organizer, Professor Keith Reemtsma will always be remembered for his integrity, wisdom, kindness and guidance. Our deepest sympathy goes to his wife, Judy, and his sons. Professor Raffaello Cortesini Director of the Interuniversitary Consortium of Transplantation
        REEMTSMA-Keith. The staff of the Division of Immunogenetics of the Department of Pathology, Columbia University deeply mourns the loss of Dr. Keith Reemtsma, a great leader, collaborator and friend. Dr. Reemtsma was a pioneer in transplantation and transplantation immunology. His work has had a great impact on the development of research on xenotransplantation. His wisdom, passion and skills will continue to inspire his students and colleagues. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Judy, and to the entire Reemtsma family. Dr. Nicole Suciu-Foca Director, Immunogenetics Div. Columbia University
