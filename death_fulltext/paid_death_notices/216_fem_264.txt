
        KURZON -- Mindy. Beloved wife of the late Lawrence Kurzon, died peacefully at home in Tamarac, FL on January 22, 2005. She is survived by her loving children, Susan Goldin and Robert Kurzon and their spouses Archie and Jane, her beloved brother Philip Needelman and her cherished granddaughters Wendy Kurzon Cherwin, her husband Matt and Carrie Kurzon. Mindy's goodness, love, courage and sparkle will be remembered by all those whose lives she touched. A private service will be held.
