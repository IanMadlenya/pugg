
        BENNETT -- Marcia Marx. Age 74, internationally renowned artist, passed away on May 3 in her beloved New York City. She was born in Newark, NJ and attended Yale University, School of Fine Arts. Most recently, she was given a ''one woman show'' at the Houston Holocaust Museum. She will be missed for her humor, talent and friendship to those she touched in her life. She is survived by her sons, Gregory and Shawn, and her brother Kelly Marx. A memorial service will be held Friday, 4PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave, NYC.
