
        HOPKINS-John J., Sr., the Honorable on Saturday October 16, 1999 of Upper Montclair, NJ. Husband of 49 years of Lois Ginley Hopkins. Father of Gail H. Shepard and her husband Thomas of Verona, Patrice H. Musicaro and her husband Michael of Reno, Nevada, Dr. Diane H. Williams & her husband Alan of Beaverton, Oregon, John J. III (Jay) & his wife Barbara of West Long Branch & Francis Ginley & his wife Patricia of Bloomfield. Brother of Mary Wedlock of Scranton, PA, Frank Hopkins & Ann Lerche both of Silver Springs, Maryland & the late Paul Hopkins. Also survived by 13 grandchildren. Relatives and friends are invited to attend the funeral from the O'Boyle Funeral Home, 309 Broad St., Bloomfield, on Thursday at 10:00 am. The funeral service will be held at First Congregational Church of Montclair, 40 South Fullerton Ave. Montclair, at 11:00 am. Interment private. In lieu of flowers the family requests memorial donations be made to the Boston College Law School in memory of the Honorable John J. Hopkins Sr. C/O Louise Parent, Associate Director-Law School Fund, Boston College Law School, 855 Centre St., Newton, Mass. 02459, or the University of Scranton, c/o the Office of Institutional Advancement Scranton, PA 18510-4615.
