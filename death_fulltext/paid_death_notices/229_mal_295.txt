
        VAN DYKE--William D, 85, died February 23. He is survived by his devoted wife, Lee Van Dyke. Bill was a Captain US Army, WWII awarded the Purple Heart and Silver Star. He will be deeply missed and forever loved. He leaves a brother, Robert Van Dyke and sister, Camilla Thomas. Memorial service will be held Monday February 27, 2PM, at St. Peter's Church, 619 Lexington Ave., at 54th St. In lieu of flowers contributions to Andrew Heiskell Library for the Blind, 40 W. 20th St., NYC 10011.
