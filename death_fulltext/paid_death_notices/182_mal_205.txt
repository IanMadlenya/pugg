
        GREENE-John Sullivan. Age 80. Dedicated educator, lover of music, loving and devoted husband of Linda, father of Joshua, Benjamin and Lynda, grandfather of Bryan and great grandfather of Mia. Passed on March 13th, 2002. His wisdom, love and wonderful sense of humor will be missed by all. Funeral services will be held on Friday, March 15 at Frank Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, NYC at 11:30AM. Viewing will be held on Thursday, March 14, 7-9PM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering.
