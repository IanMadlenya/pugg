
        DUNN--Norman S. of Pound Ridge, NY and Boston, MA on May 31, 2006. He is survived by his beloved wife Mildred, his son Jeffrey, his brother Arnold, several nieces and nephews, and his muchadored grandchildren, Lucas and Noah. Born in Woonsocket, RI on September 17, 1921, he graduated cum laude from Providence College and became a highly successful businessman in the textiles industry. He was Executive Vice-President and Treasurer of Chelsea Industries for fifty years, and involved in numerous philanthropic organizations, including serving as an overseer of the Beth Israel/ Deaconess Hospital, Boston and honorary trustee of The Rehabilitation Center for the Aged in Boston. Norman was a kind, gentle, and generous man who was admired, loved, and adored by all who knew him. Funeral services will be held Sunday, June 4 at 1pm at the Leo P. Gallagher Funeral Home, 2900 Summer St, Stamford, CT with interment at Pound Ridge Cemetery, Westchester Ave, Pound Ridge, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Parkinsons Disease Foundation, 1359 Broadway, NY NY 10018.
