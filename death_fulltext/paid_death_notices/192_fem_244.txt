
        RYAN-Julia Regina (nee Healey). Age 89, died peacefully on Friday, January 24, 2003 at her home in Wyckoff, NJ. Julia was born in Brooklyn, NY on September 11, 1913. She was a resident of Brooklyn for many years before moving to Wyckoff 25 years ago. Julia taught in the elementary grades for the Diocese of Brooklyn for 20 years before her retirement in 1976. She was a parishioner of St. Elizabeth's R.C. Church in Wyckoff, NJ. Surviving are her devoted children, Edward G. Ryan of Charlotte, NC, Virginia Leskauskas of Wyckoff, NJ, Marcella Sahni of Farmingdale, NY, and Christine Amundsen of Ridgewood, NJ, ten grandchildren, 11 great grandchildren, and her sister Marcella Butterly of Herbertsville, NJ. Julia was pre-deceased by her husband Edward H. Ryan in 1966. Funeral services will be held Monday 10:30 AM from the Vander Plaat Funeral Home, 257 Godwin Avenue, Wyckoff, NJ. Funeral Mass to follow at 11 AM at St. Elizabeth's R.C. Church, 700 Wyckoff Avenue, Wyckoff, NJ. Interment will be in the Holy Cross Cemetery in Brooklyn, NY. Visiting is Saturday 7-9 PM and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Memorial contributions may be made to the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, Ovarian Cancer Research, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021.
