
        SMETANA--John A. Jr. of Wainscott, NY and Palm Beach, FL. Born Derry, PA February 5, 1928. Cherished husband of the late Virginia and his beloved late Julianne. Died of heart condition on October 31, 2005 at the residence of his close friends. A Veteran in the U.S. Navy, Submariner Service, attended St. Vincent College and St. John University Law School. Served as Kings County Assistant District Attorney and had years of notable service to the City of New York. John's sharp wit, blunt honesty and sincere friendship will be missed by all. Reposing at the Beney Funeral Home, Inc. of Syosset, 79 Berry Hill Rd. Visiting Thursday 6-9 P.M. Religious service 8:00 P.M.
