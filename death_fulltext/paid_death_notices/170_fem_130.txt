
        HAWKINS-Evelyn Byrd. Died on March 13, 2001 in her 95th year. Wife of the late Dexter C. Hawkins. Mother of Evelyn B. Patterson, Eliot D. Hawkins and Alice C. VanTuyl (correction). Grandmother of eight and great-grandmother of nine. Funeral service Monday, at 11 AM, at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71st Street, NYC. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60th Street, New York City 10022, ''in memory of Evelyn Hawkins''.
