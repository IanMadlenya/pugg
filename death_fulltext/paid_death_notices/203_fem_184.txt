
        SCHWARTZ--Paula Reibel, 78, of ovarian cancer on December 21. Playwright, author of 36 Elizabeth Mansfield Regencies and three historicals by Paula Reibel, Paula Jonas and Paula Reid. She wrote as she lived, with intelligence, wit and style. Condolences to her family. Devoted readers and book business friends mourn her passing. Victoria Pryor, Arcadia
