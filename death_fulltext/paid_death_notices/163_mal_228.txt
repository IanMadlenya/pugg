
        SHLEFFAR-Irvin Ezekiel (Zeke), DDS. Died August 15 at 89, after a long illness characterized by the same good cheer, courage, and grace with which he lived. Beloved husband of the late Ida, survived by adoring daughters Susan (Gary) Palestrant of Ossining & Jo Ellen (Robert) Winters of Philadelphia; cherished grandchildren Michael, Gregory, and Rachel; loving relatives and treasured friends. His unquenchable wit and love of life endeared him to all. Services Friday, 12:30 PM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mount Vernon (914-664-6800). Memorial donations American Diabetes Assoc.
