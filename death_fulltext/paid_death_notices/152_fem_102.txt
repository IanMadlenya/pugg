
        MATHEWS-Margaret S. On September 10, 1999, peacefully, at her home in Ponte Vedra Beach, FL, in her 88th year. She was the loving wife for 59 years of the late William H. Mathews, M.D. Formerly of Montreal and Atlanta, she was a valiant, elegant and remarkable woman who will be sorely missed and dearly remembered by her son Richard S. M. Mathews of NYC and Bridgehampton, NY, her daughter Ann of Waterloo, Ontario, her grandchildren Alexander Mathews, Leighann and Michael Hobson, and a host of loving friends. Her memorial will be held at noon, Monday, September 13, at Christ Episcopal Church, Ponte Vedra. In lieu of flowers, contributions to charity would be gratefully appreciated.
