
        HURST-Milton Albert. On May 20, 2000, 81 years old. Son of the late Clarence and Sadye Jonas, husband of Ruth, father of Marc and Molly, and Mitchell, grandfather of Adam and Jonah, brother of Mildred Siegel. A Veteran of WW II, bearer of the Purple Heart. Longtime resident of Woodmere, Long Island, and recently of Goshen, New York. He fought hard in Europe and was wounded twice. He fought hard now and went at last to his rest. We will miss him. Funeral Monday at Hellman Memorial Chapel in Spring Valley, New York, 914-356-8600. Burial at Machpelah Cemetery. Shiva at Karp-Hurst home, call 914-639-3839 for Shiva location. Donations in his memory to AHRC at 189 Wheatly Road, Brookville, New York 11545 will be appreciated.
