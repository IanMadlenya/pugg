
        BORRI-Catarina, of NYC, passed away peacefully on January 24th, 1999 at JFK Hospital in Edison, NJ. Born in Biella, Pr. Vercelli, Italy in 1903, is survived by her only son, Tullio, of Metuchen, NJ, 3 grandchildren, Gregg Borri and Claire Hallock of NYC, Catherine Lyness of Summit, NJ, and 6 great-grandchildren. She was a strong, loving and caring mother. A private memorial service will be held at a later date.
