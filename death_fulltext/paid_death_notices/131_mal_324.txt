
        SCHECHNER-Kenneth S., Esquire. Died on December 23, 1997. Cherished son and brother of Alan, Diane, and Karen after a heroic battle with brain cancer. His courage, dignity and sense of humor will always be remembered by all who knew him. Services will be held at Star of David Memorial Chapel, Bailey Road, North Lauderdale, Florida, Friday, December 26, 11:15 A.M. Contributions in his memory may be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, C/O Research Fund, 1275 York Avenue, New York, New York 10021. A Memorial Service will be held in January in New York.
