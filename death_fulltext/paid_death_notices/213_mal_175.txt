
        HABBERTON -- John Law rence, 92, died September 24, having suffered a fall at his assisted living home in Falls Church, VA. Mr. Habberton helped form the Business Council for International Understanding and led it until his retirement in 1983. The Council had been initiated by President Eisenhower in 1955. It maintained programs that bring the private sector into working relationships with government agencies, particularly the State Department. Inurnment will be at Arlington National Cemetery on Tuesday, October 19, at 2 PM.
