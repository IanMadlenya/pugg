
        HAYMAN-Seymour ''Sy,'' age 83. Beloved husband of Dorothy. Devoted father of Jonathan and Valerie. Son of the late Louie and Ettie Hayman. Dear uncle of Ellen, Robert, Nicholas, Laura and Karen, & Joanne Larzik. Services on Wednesday, September 9th, at 12 PM, at Westchester Reform Temple, Scarsdale, New York.
        HAYMAN-Seymour. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Seymour Hayman. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Dorothy and to the entire family. William Ettelson, President Michael Roth, Secretary
