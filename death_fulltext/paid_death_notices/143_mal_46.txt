
        LOWE-William Hyslop. Tuesday, December 2, 1998 of Hackensack NJ. ''With grace and courage, he battled Alzheimers Disease'' for sixteen years. He is survived by his beloved wife, Barbara Conway Lowe, and his devoted children, Barbara Manternach of Brooklyn, NY, William of Ketchum, Idaho, & Peter of Greenwich, CT. Loving grandfather of five grandchildren. Funeral Friday, Dec. 4, 9:30 AM, The G. Thomas Gentile Funeral Home, 397 Union Street, Hackensack. The Mass of Requiem at St. Anthony of Padua Episcopal Church at 10:30 AM. Interment Mt. Carmel Cemetery. Family and friends may call on Friday from 8:30 to 10:30 AM.
