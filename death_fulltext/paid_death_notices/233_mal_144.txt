
        BASTIAN--Bryce. Of Bronxville New York, died peacefully on June 9, 2006 after a noble struggle with a terrible cancer. He is survived by his wife, Sue; children Chris, James and Maribel; grandchildren Alastair, Isabelle and Miles Bryce; sisters Cheryl and Lynn; brother Ron; and numerous nieces and nephews. He will be missed by many. Memorial service at the First Unitarian Church in Brooklyn, New York, July 22 at 2:00 pm.
