
        SCHOEN-Donald R. The Governors and staff of Lawrence Hospital are deeply saddened by the death of our long time Governor, Don Schoen. He was a respected business and community leader and loved as a dear friend of the Hospital. His commitment to quality patient care and his contributions to strategic planning were deeply valued and he will be greatly missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his entire family. George E. Austin, Chairman of the Board Roger G. Dvorak, President
