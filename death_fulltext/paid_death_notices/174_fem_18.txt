
        FALTERMEIER-Gloria J. On July 1, 2001 after a long and courageous battle with cancer at Cabrini Hospice. Beloved daughter of John and Gloria and sister of John Jr. and Brad. She is survived by many loving friends. A native of Rockville Centre and a 1982 graduate of University of California at Santa Barbara and 1988 recipient of a Master's degree from Columbia University School of Social Work, she was a psychotherapist practicing in NYC. Services will take place on Thursday, July 5 at Dimiceli & Sons Funeral Home, 182 Lexington Avenue, NYC, interment following at Greenwood Cemetery, 500 Twenty Fifth Street, Brooklyn, and further services following at Gilda's Club, 195 West Houston Street, New York City.
