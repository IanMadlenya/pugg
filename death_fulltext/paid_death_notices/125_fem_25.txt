        SCHULHOF-Peggy Hirsch. The leadership and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Peggy Hirsch Schulhof, beloved sister of David Hirsch, a dedicated friend and supporter of UJA-Federation. We extend our deepest sympathies to her brother, David; her children, Mark, Amy, and Dean; her longtime companion, Jeffrey Stein; her sisters, Nancy Rubin and Patrice Feinstein; and the entire family. May you be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
