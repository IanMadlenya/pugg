
        FURMAN-William. Age 91, on August 18, 2000. Loving husband of Mathilda and father of Roger. The kindest of persons with a sparkle in his eye, he had courage and principle, fighting for a decade in the anti-Nazi resistance movement within Germany. Family will receive friends on Tuesday, August 22, at 11:30 AM, and services will begin at 1:00 PM, at Williams Funeral Home, 5628 Broadway, at 232nd St, Riverdale, NY. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to either the International Rescue Committee or the Ethical Culture Society.
