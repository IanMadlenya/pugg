
        ROSKIN-Samuel. Age 89 years, on April 19, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Gussie, loving father of Carol and Bill, and father-in-law of Henry. Adoring grandfather of Lila, Jonathan and Robert, devoted brother of Al, Sol and Ruby and brother-in-law of Shirley, Carolyn, Millie, Gerty and Louise. Revered uncle to numerous nieces and nephews and a special friend. Past president of the Workmen's Circle Home for the Aged, and past president of the Lithographers Guild. In his memory donations may be made to the Workmen's Circle Multicare Center, 3155 Grace Ave, Bronx NY 10469 or the Karen Horney Clinic, 329 E. 62 St, NYC 10021.
