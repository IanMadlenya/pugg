
        CROSS--Lucille V., died in Auburn, CA, on March 21, 2007, in her 98th year. She formally lived in Pompano Beach, FL, and was the wife of the late Edwin J. Cross, founder of the Wall Street investment firm Roosevelt & Cross. She is survived by her daughter Nancy, of Lake Orion, MI, and her son Edwin J. Cross, Jr., of Meadow Vista, CA, two grandchildren and six greatgrandchildren. Funeral services will be held on May 5, in Westwood, NJ.
