
        FRIEDMAN-Edward A., Jr., M.D. Of Mt. Kisco, NY. Died peacefully June 23, 2001, after a long illness. Son of the late Edward and Lucille Friedman. Beloved husband of Sue (Richman). Loving father of Doug and Diana (Frost), James and Kendra (Boeckman), Edward III, Laurence and Judi (Landzberg). Devoted grandfather of Max and Kyle, Justin and Evan, and Spencer and Oliver. Loving brother of Enid Randall (Friedman) and wonderful son-in-law of Lucile Richman. A graduate of New York Medical College, he practiced as pediatrician and allergist in Westchester County for 40 years. On staff at Northern Westchester Hospital and Westchester County Medical Center. Proud veteran of W.W. II and the Korean War. Ed was a world traveler, an accomplished photographer, enthusiastic amateur actor, world class collector of a variety of arts and antiques including museumdisplayed 1939 World's Fair memorabilia, lifelong jazz enthusiast, witty raconteur and warm companion. A memorial service will be held on Wednesday, June 27, at 1:30 PM at Cassidy-Flynn Funeral Home, 288 Main Street, Mt. Kisco, NY, 914-666-4840. Family will be receiving visitors Tuesday, June 26, 6-9 PM at Cassidy-Flynn. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to American Lung Association or Doctors without Borders.
