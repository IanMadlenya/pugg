
        SULLIVAN-Daniel J. Of New Canaan, CT, on June 10, 2002. Born on November 9, 1932, in Brooklyn, N.Y., to Daniel J. and Mary M. Sullivan and formerly of Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y. and Katonah, N.Y. Beloved father of Kathryn E. Sullivan of Mt. Vernon, N.Y., Margaret A. Egan of Pawling, N.Y., Daniel J. Sullivan of Lexington, MA, Kieran J. Sullivan of Kingston, N.H., and Maura S. Gaughan of Natick, MA. Brother of Francis P. Sullivan of Savannah, GA; grandfather of eight; uncle of numerous nieces and nephews and fiance of Claire O'Gara Grimes of Pound Ridge, N.Y. Predeceased by his parents, by his brother John L. Sullivan, and, in 1982, by his beloved wife of 26 years, Joan M. Sullivan. A 1958 magna cum laude graduate of St. John's University, he was a certified public accountant and devoted his entire career to Price Waterhouse, from which he retired in 1992 after 22 years as a partner. He served for two years in the United States Army, including one year's tour in Japan. He was a member of the Country Club of Darien, The University Club, The New York Athletic Club, The Waccabuc Country Club, and the Senior Men's Club of New Canaan. Calling hours will be Friday, June 14, from 2PM until 5PM and from 7PM until 9PM at Hoyt Funeral Home, 199 Main Street, New Canaan. Funeral services will be held on Saturday, June 15, at 1 PM, at St. Thomas More Church, 374 Middlesex Road, Darien, CT. Interment will be at Lakeview Cemetery in New Canaan directly from church. A reception will follow at the Country Club of Darien. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to Nativity Preparatory School, 30 Lamartine Street, Jamaica Plain, MA. 02130.
