
        La PORTE-Rev. George H., March 19, suddenly. Beloved husband of Marilyn, dear brother of Howard (Mary Lou), uncle of Jennifer. Adlerian psychotherapist, respected member of Adler Institute & Clinic; Unitarian minister w/graduate degrees from NYU and Union Theological Seminary. Funeral at Community Church of New York, 40 East 35 St., Friday, March 21, 11 AM. Donations in his name may be made to Greenhouse, Mohonk Mountain House, New Paltz, NY 12561 or community outreach programs, Community Church of New York, NYC.
        La PORTE-George. Devoted psychotherapist. The Alfred Adler Institute and the Adler Center for Mental Health mourn the sudden loss of our dear friend and colleague. Thank you for your years of service and your warmth and humanity. Barbara Berger, The Adler Center, Robert Ellenbogen, Alfred Adler Institute
