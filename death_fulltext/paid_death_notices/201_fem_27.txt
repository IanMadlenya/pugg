
        FITZGIBBON--Dorothy Rogers. 91. A resident of Ridgewood, NJ, died peacefully at home on October 3, 2003. Beloved wife of the late Harold J. Fitzgibbon. Loving mother of Mary Ann and Joan Fitzgibbon, both of New York City, and of Margaret Fitzgibbon Watson and her husband Drew Watson, also of Ridgewood. Devoted grandmother of Andrew Watson. Born in New York City, she was a model for fashion designer Mollie Parnis from 1929 to 1944. Visitation Tuesday, October 7, from 2-4 and 7-9 PM, at Feeney Funeral Home, Ridgewood, NJ. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, October 8, at 10:45 AM, at Our Lady of Mount Carmel Church in Ridgewood.
