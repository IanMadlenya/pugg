
        KENNEDY--Mary Elizabeth, 49, of Fairfield, CT, died Monday after a valiant struggle with cancer. Ms. Kennedy was a Sales Executive at Mottahedeh Inc. prior to becoming a Social Worker. Survivors include her beloved parents, John M. and Yvonne Kennedy of Fairfield; 7 loving siblings and many adoring nieces and nephews. Memorial Mass November 13th, 11:00 A.M. at St. Augustine's Cathedral, Bridgeport. Donations to Special Olympics of CT, 999 Oronoque Lane, Stratford, CT 06614
