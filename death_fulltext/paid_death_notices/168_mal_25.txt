
        JORDAN-Mark G. Died January 1, 2001. Loving son of David and Gladys. Brother of Robert, Alan and Daniel of blessed memory.
        JORDAN-Mark. The Yeshiva University family mourns the passing of a respected member of the Jewish community. He was the beloved son of Gladys L. and the late Dr. David M. Jordan, YU Guardians, Founders of our Sy Syms School of Business, and Fellows (Bonim) of our affiliated YU High Schools, where they established a scholarship in their name. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Gladys Jordan as well as to his brothers, Alan (and Iris) and Robert (and Karen), and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University Robert M. Beren, Chairman Board of Trustees
