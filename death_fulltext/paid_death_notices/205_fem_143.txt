
        ZELLER--Helen L. Late Tuesday, February 17, 2004. Loving wife of the late Dr. Joseph B. Zeller. Mother of Stuart and the late Dr. Malcolm M. Zeller, Marion and Carole. She will be missed by her grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. Her career in the New York City school system made her an inspiration to countless students. Her skills in crafts and arts were and are an enjoyment to generations. Services are at Riverside Chapel, 76th Street at Amsterdam Avenue, New York City. 9:30 A.M. Friday, February 20, 2004.
