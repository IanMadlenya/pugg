
        LARSEN-Patricia Pike, 79, of Cambridge, MA on May 29, 2000. The widow of H. Irgens Larsen and the sister of the late Nancy Pike Talbert, she is survived by five children, Siri Mortimer and Rikk, Peik, Per and Leif Larsen, her sister Daphne Pike Gratiot, sixteen grandchildren and two great-grandchildren. She devoted her life to gardening and the arts. Her forthcoming book, ''Letters from Lost Thyme,'' has been published by Books & Co. She was a Fogg Fellow and a member of the International Council of the Museum of Modern Art. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory can be made to Planned Parenthood, 1055 Commonwealth Ave., Boston, MA 02215. A memorial service will be held on October 8th in Cambridge, MA.
