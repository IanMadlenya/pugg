
        KAHN - Leonard. One hundred and a half years. Died March 8. Husband of departed Stella. Father of Marjorie Greene and Richard Kahn. Grandfather and great grandfather. An optimist who loved all people and his family. Services at Mt. Ararat, Farmingdale, L.I., Wednesday, March 12, at 11:00AM. Shiva will be observed Wednesday and Thursday at the home of Richard Kahn. In lieu of flowers, donations to a charity will be appreciated.
