
        ALEXANDER-Donald Louis, 64, of Midlothian, VA. died October 6, 1999. Born in Queens, beloved father of Christine, Donald, Jennifer, John and husband of Sandra. He was the General Manager of Packaging Corporation of America. He attended St. Paul's Episcopal School in Garden city and was a graduate of Dartmouth College, 1956. Contributions may be made to The Dartmouth College Scholarship Fund.
