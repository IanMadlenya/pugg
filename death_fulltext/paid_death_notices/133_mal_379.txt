
        NEALON-Thomas F., M.D. The Board of Trustees, Sisters of Charity, Auxiliary, Medical Staff & Administration of Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center note with sadness the passing of our esteemed friend and colleague. A member of the Saint Vincent's staff since 1968, Dr. Nealon formerly served as Chairman of the Department of Surgery at the Medical Center for more than twenty years. He held several academic appointments including Professor of Surgery at New York University School of Medicine and New York Medical College. Affiliated with many professional organizations, Dr. Nealon served on the Board of Governors of the American College of Surgeons, was a former President of the New York Cancer Society, former president of the New York Surgical Society, as well as an active member of the very prestigious American Surgical Society. Dr. Nealon took great pride in his close affiliation with the Sisters of Charity and Saint Vincents Hospital, as well as his tremendous dedication to surgical residency training. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Kay, and their children. Thomas A. Coleman Chairman of the Board Karl P. Adler, M.D. President and CEO Saint Vincents Hospital and Medical Center
