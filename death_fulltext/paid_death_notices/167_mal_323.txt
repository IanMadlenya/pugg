
        POMERANTZ-Howard E. Husband of Ruth Brooks, father of Alan and Jan, father-in-law of Everett Wilcox, stepfather of Rachel, Eric and David, loving grandfather of Sarah and Rebecca, step-grandfather of Merrill, Alex, Daniel, Taela, Gabriela and Tomasz, and former husband of Ethel, passed on December 24, 2000. He was a retired cosmetic manufacturer, gifted amateur photographer, rug maker and longtime volunteer at SCORE. He will be missed.
