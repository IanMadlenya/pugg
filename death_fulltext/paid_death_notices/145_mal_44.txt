
        LESSEN-Dr. Martin. Rochester, NY, died January 24, 1999 at age 78. He is survived by his wife Elizabeth of Rochester, daughters Margot Lessen Brody of Wayland, MA, Deborah Lessen Calomino of NYC, son Dr. David Lessen of NYC; sisters Anna Needleman and Sarah Nichols; grandchildren, other relatives and friends. Memorial to be held in Rochester.
