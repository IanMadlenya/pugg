
        DUNHAM-William P., 82, died at his winter residence in Naples, Florida on Tuesday, May 16, 2000. He was also a resident of St. Huberts, New York and East Orleans, Massachusetts. He will be missed and loved by his wife Edie, his children & their spouses Sally Taylor, Bill Jr. and Nelie Dunham, Ann and Mike Hartson and his grandchildren, Kristen and Kevin Taylor, Henry and Melanie Dunham, Hilary and Brooke Hartson. His daughter Susan Hope died in 1988. Services will be held at the Keene Valley Congregational Church on Saturday, May 27, 2000 at 1:00. Burial will be at Norton Cemetery, Keene, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Keene Valley Neighborhood House or the Adirondack Trail Improvement Society, both of Keene Valley, NY 12943, would be appreciated.
