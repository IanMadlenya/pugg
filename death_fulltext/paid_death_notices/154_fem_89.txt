
        WEINSIER-Joan (nee Tuby). On November 10, 1999. Loving daughter of Evelyn Tuby and the late Dr. Robert Tuby. Beloved wife of Philip. Adored and treasured mother of Michael and Wendy and mother-in-law of Marcy. Cherished grandmother of Matthew. Devoted sister of Suellen Globus and loving aunt to three nieces. Her extraordinary compassion, kindness, honesty, warmth, beauty, style and elegance are her legacy to us all. She will be deeply missed by all whose lives she touched. She graduated from Finch College, enjoyed a successful modelling career and studied drama with Lee Strasberg and Claudia Frank. In later years, she enjoyed a successful career in real estate sales and most recently, art sales. Services will be held at Temple Beth-El, 5 Old Mill Road, Great Neck, New York, Friday, November 12, 1999 at 11 AM. For further information call Schwartz Brothers, 718-263-7600.
