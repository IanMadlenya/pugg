
        ERDMAN--Barbara Claudia Mason. Of Greenwich, CT, passed away Friday, June 24, at New York Presbyterian Hospital, at age 57. She was born in Hammond, IN, and graduated from Purdue University with a B.A. in English literature. She flew for TWA from 1972 to 1986, and was a realtor with Sotheby's at the time of her death. She was predeceased by her husband, William Erdman, and leaves a daughter, Alexandra Marie, 18. Mrs. Erdman was active in the community and enjoyed travel and golf. Besides her daughter, she is survived by a brother, Wayne Winarski of Pennsylvania. A service will be held at Round Hill Community Church, at 395 Round Hill Road, on Tuesday, June 28, at noon. Donations in her memory may be made to the Robert L. Fine Cancer Laboratory Research Foundation, Inc., 650 W. 168th St, Room 20-05, NYC 10032.
