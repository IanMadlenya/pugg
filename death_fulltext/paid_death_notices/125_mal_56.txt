
        KARP-Richard. On June 5, 1997. Beloved husband, father, grandfather, uncle, friend. Educator, supporter of Jewish causes, lover of life. Loving, generous and kind. Mourned by wife Lita, children Miriam and Steven, Judi and Sidney, Molly and Marc, Charles, and grandchildren Sam, Anna, Seth, Adam, Elana and Jonah, nieces Susan and Rachel, nephew Gary. Service today, 12:30 PM, ''The Riverside,'' 76th St and Amsterdam Avenue. Shiva observed until Tuesday afternoon. Donations to Educational Alliance, Bialystoker Synagogue, or UJA-Federation.
        KARP-Richard. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Educational Alliance mourn the untimely death of Richard Karp. A life-long, lower East-sider and Trustee since 1978, Richard served as Co-Chair ofthe Camps Committee and was Assistant Secretary of the Board for a number of years. We extend our profound sympathy to his wife Lita, his children and their spouses and his grandchildren.
