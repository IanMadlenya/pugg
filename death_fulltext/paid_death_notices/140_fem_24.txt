
        ROTH-Genevieve Kolczynski. 91, of Rockville Centre, New York passed away peacefully September 4, 1998 at her home of 63 years. Beloved wife of the late Arthur T. Roth, and loving mother of Donald Roth and Carol Madigan, she also leaves their spouses Trish Roth and Jay Madigan, five grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Friends may call at Macken Mortuary, 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Centre from 2-4 and 7-9 PM on Sunday and Monday. Entombment will be at Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury, following a Mass of the Resurrection at St. Agnes Cathedral, Rockville Centre on Tuesday at 11 AM. The memory of her unfailing kindness, generosity and strength will be cherished by all who knew her.
