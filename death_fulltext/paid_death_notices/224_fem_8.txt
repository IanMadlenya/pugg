
        ASHER--Norma Hayim. 81, of NYC and Boca Raton, FL. Born Norma Keats and married 48 years to the late Alvin Hayim. Graduated University of Pennsylvania. Survived by her husband of eight years, Leonard Asher of Short Hills, NJ, her brother, Ira Keats of Yardley, PA, three children Stuart Hayim of Great Neck, Scott Hayim of St. Louis and Nancy Sagon of Amherst, NH, and 10 grandchildren. Norma had life time involvement in various charitable causes and was a patron and past board member of the Boca Raton Museum of Art.
