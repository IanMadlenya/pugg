
        O'NEILL--Terence P. On December 7, 2005 of Rye, NY. Born in Cork City, Ireland. At age 26 he opened his first restaurant, The Green Derby, and went on to own many successful restaurants. He was also involved in the development of other business enterprises, including ventures in real estate, travel, golf, and airline industries. He was a founding partner in Waterville Golf Links and the creator of The Father Son International Golf Tournament. Beloved husband of Barbara Mary, devoted father of Terence Kane. Dear brother of Nellie Curzon, Nora Morey, Breda Dewis and the late Gerry, Kathleen and May. Family will receive friends from 3-7PM on Friday, December 9, 2005 at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, NY. Mass of Christian Burial will be 10AM on Saturday, December 10, 2005 at the Church of the Resurrection, Rye, NY. Interment will be at Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York. Memorial contributions may be made to Catholic Charities of New York, 1011 First Avenue, NY, New York 10022.
