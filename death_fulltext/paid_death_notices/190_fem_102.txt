
        FELDMAN-Esther (Zuckerman), 93, on November 13. Beloved wife of the late Joseph P. Feldman, loving mother of Kenneth, Carole (Bisker) and David, cherished grandmother of Ellia and Joshua, sister of Lucille Zinman. NYU class of '29, retired social worker at the Jewish Home and Hospital. She was a compassionate, creative individual who loved people, and will be deeply missed by all who knew her. Services are today, 12:45 P.M., at Riverside Memorial Chapel.
