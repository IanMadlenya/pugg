
        MASLOW--Will. With sad hearts, the Maslow, Horton, and Armand families bid farewell to their beloved husband, father, grandfather, and great-grandfather who died peacefully at home in his one hundredth year. He leaves behind his wife of 73 years, Beatrice, his daughters Laura and Catha, his grandchildren Aaron, Cecile, Philippe and Noah, and his four great-grandchildren.
