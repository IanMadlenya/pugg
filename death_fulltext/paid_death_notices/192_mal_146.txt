
        SAPIRO-Burton Maurice. 76, of Jupiter, FL, and Lenox, MA, died peacefully on January, 10, 2003, in West Palm Beach. Beloved husband of Gloria. Loving father of Miriam and Elaine. Adoring grandfather of Max and Ellie. Devoted brother of David. Proud father-in-law of Stephen Labaton and Dan Vassilovski. His family and friends were blessed by his love for them, his unpretentious good grace, and his uncompromising integrity.
