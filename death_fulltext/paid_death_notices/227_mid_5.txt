
        FRIEDMAN--Margaret ''Peggy'' of Harrison, NY. Died December 14, 2005 at age 85. Born April 11, 1920 in NYC to Arthur & Irene (Feigenbaum) Solomon. Peggy was an Elementary School Teacher and a member of Old Oaks Country Club. Survived by her husband Harry of 63 years, children Richard Friedman (Christine), Carol Moore and Susan Darnell (Ron) & grandchildren Colin, Courtney, Kaela and Elizabeth. Funeral Service Friday 12 noon at Zion Memorial Chapel in Mamaroneck, 914381-1809 followed by an interment at Mt. Judah Cemetery.
        $(6$)FRIEDMAN--Peggy. The Officers and Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of Peggy Friedman. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to fellow member Harry Friedman and to all the members of his family. Allen J. Ross, President
