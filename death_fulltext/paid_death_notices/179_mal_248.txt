
        LEVIN-Irving T. Loving husband of Martha and devoted father of Seth. Father-in-law of Amy Feinstein. After demobilization from U.S. Army he was named director of the United Seamen's Service Clubs for Merchant Marine in New York, Honolulu and Tokyo. Later he was president of Gessler Publishing Company. Services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue, Friday, December 21 at 11:30 AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to East End Temple, 403 First Avenue, NY, NY 10010.
