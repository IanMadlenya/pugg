
        CORSI--Francesca. New York City Opera mourns the passing of Francesca Corsi, Principal Harpist of our orchestra for thirty-five years, who passed away in August. She was a valued member of our company, a gifted musician and a good friend who will be remembered with great fondness. Her dedication to our company was unswerving. She will be missed. Paul Kellogg General Director George Manahan Music Director
