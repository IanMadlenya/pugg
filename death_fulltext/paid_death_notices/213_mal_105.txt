
        SHAPIRO--Harris. The Officers, Directors, Trustees, Clergy and Congregation of Temple Emanu-El of Closter mourn the passing of our beloved friend and guiding light, Harris Shapiro. A man of action and compassion, Harris' tremendous support, deep insight and acts of loving kindness will inspire us from generation to generation. Harris will always be a part of Temple Emanu-El, just as he will always remain in our hearts. We extend our heartfelt condolences to our dear Shirley and the entire Shapiro family. May the memory of our good and righteous friend be a blessing for all. Rabbi Geoffrey Haber Mark Hirschberg, President
