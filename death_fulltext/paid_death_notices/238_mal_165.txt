We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Howard F. Sharfstein, President $(6$)GLASS--Daniel, Attorney at Law, of Manhattan, New York City, died unexpectedly on Sunday morning, November 12. He was 88. He is survived by his loving wife of 45 years, Madeleine and his son, Kenneth. Daniel was a man of great intelligence and integrity with a wonderful sense of humor. He was well-known in the entertainment industry, and a longtime member of the Friars Club. He will be missed by all who knew him. Funeral services will be held at noon on November 15, 2006 at Central Synagogue, 652 Lexington Ave., NYC. Memorial donations may be made to The Central Synagogue Caring Committee for the Homeless, 652 Lexington Ave., NY, NY 10022.
