
        REED--Barbara Ann, devoted mother and loving grandmother, died on March 4, 2007, after a courageous battle with cancer. She leaves behind family, friends and colleagues who will always remember her love of life, love of family and her beauty and elegance. She is survived by her daughters, Kim Itri and Daun Dees, and her six grandchildren. A memorial service will be held on Friday, March 16, from 5:00-8:00 pm at the University Club in New York City.
