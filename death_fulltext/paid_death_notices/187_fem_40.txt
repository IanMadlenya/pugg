
        HUGHES-Hilda. Of Franklin Square, NY. Born in Brooklyn on Sept. 29, 1916. Deceased in Westchester County, August 1, 2002. Former proprietor of ''The Tavern'' in South Ozone Park, NY. Devoted mother of Douglas Hughes of Cooper City, FL and Dennis Hughes of Manhattan. Also survived by her sister Helen Bass and her beloved grandchildren Christine Sullivan and Scott, Brian, Vanessa, Lauren and Jason Hughes. A Mass of Christian burial will be celebrated on Wednesday, August 7 at noon at Our Lady of Hope RC Church, Eliot Avenue at 71st Street, Middle Village, NY. Interment will follow at St. John's Cemetery in Middle Village.
