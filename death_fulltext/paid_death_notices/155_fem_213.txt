
        FISHER-Jeanne. Beloved wife of Mead Hunt and adoring mother to Laura. Died with bravery and dignity at her home after a long illness. Jeanne attened Elmira College and New York School for Visual Arts & had a distinguished career as a commercial illustrator. Viewing will be Thursday, Dec. 23, 1999, from 7-9PM at the Stevenson Funeral Home, Piermont, N.Y. Services will be Friday, December 24th at Nyack Reform Church, 18 South Broadway, Nyack. Burial will follow at Rockland Cemetery in Sparkkill, New York. Memorial donations requested to Doctors Without Borders, POB 110, New York, New York 10277.
