
        SCHREIBER-Robert J. The Jewish Community Endowment Foundation expresses its sincerest condolences to Naomi Schreiber and the family of Robert Schreiber. Bob helped to create our Foundation, served as a member of our Board, the Chair of our Publicity Committee and the Co-Chair of our Legacy Committee. He was a believer in Community, a leader and a role model for us all. Through his good name and deeds, Bob's memory will live in our Jewish Community. Benson Zinbarg, President Joel Feerst, Exec. Director
