
        ZABLOW--Rhoda. Formerly of Freeport, NY, passed away at 95, at her home in Williamstown, MA on April 11, 2006. Widow of Nathan P. Zablow, a prominent attorney and former Administrator of the Assigned Counsel Defender Plan for the Nassau County Bar Association. Loved by her children Suellen Solomon & Dr. Bruce Zablow and their spouses, Dr. Paul R. Solomon and Dr. Robin J. Zablow and will be missed by her grandchildren Todd M. Solomon and Jessica A. Solomon.
