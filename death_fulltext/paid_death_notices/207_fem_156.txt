
        BRADEN--Adelaide Weld of Lloyd Harbor, NY died April 20, 2004 in her 85th year. Beloved wife of William Braden, and the late Robert Bacon Whitney and James Knott. She is survived by her daughter, Hope Whitney Lapsley and her son, Robert Bacon Whitney Jr. and seven stepchildren, Lilias K. Bostwick, David H. Knott, Catherine K. Welch, Susan K. Childs, Nora K. Cushing, William Braden Jr. and Maria Clark and daughter-in-law, Susan Jardine-Paterson. She is also survived by her two sisters, Rose Weld Baldwin and Katharine Weld Bacon. She was predeceased by her sons, Michael Whitney, Stephen Weld Whitney and James Knott Jr. She was a devoted grandmother of 26 grandchildren and great-grandmother of 33 great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Saturday April 24 at 12pm at the Church of the Advent, Westbury, LI. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Family and Children's Association, 100 East Old Country Rd, Mineola, NY 11501 or Planned Parenthood of Nassau County, 540 Fulton Ave, Hempstead, NY 11550.
