
        KORN - Peggy. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York mourns the passing of Peggy Korn, the beloved wife of our late revered member of the Board, Richard K. Korn. A woman of great strength, she lived her life with extraordinary dignity. Her concerns for the betterment of humanity were translated into concrete acts of righteousness. Along with her family, it is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
