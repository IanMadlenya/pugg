
        BERELSON-Thelma D. On July 22, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Irving. Loving mother of Ellen and her husband Lawrence Franks and the late Philip. Funeral services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Tuesday 11:30 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the League for the Hard of Hearing, 71 West 23rd St., NY, NY 10010.
