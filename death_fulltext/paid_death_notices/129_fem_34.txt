
        PALIN-Anna. The Board of Directors, residents and staff of the Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the death of Anna Palin, wife of the late Louis Palin. We extend sincere sympathies to her children, our respected friend and Director of the Hebrew Home Board of Directors, Michael (Caryl) Palin and Carol (Mort) Sehres; grandchildren Jill, Stacey, Dorothy, Andrea, and Dean and her ten greatgrandchildren. Our thoughts are with you at this unhappy time. Milton A Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Jacob Reingold, Vice Chairman Daniel A. Reingold, Exec VP
