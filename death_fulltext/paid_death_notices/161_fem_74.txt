
        KRAMER-Katherine (nee Kolodny), 76, died on June 9, after a brief illness. Born in New York, and long residing in Oakdale, she was a dedicated and skilled social worker. Beloved wife of the late poet Aaron Kramer, mother of Carol of Tucson and Laura of Montclair, and grandmother of Nora and Joanna Gordon, she was a woman of enormous integrity, and will be greatly missed.
