
        OWEN-Vera Dammann. Born January 14, 1904, Yorkshire, England. Died April 5, 2001, Somers, NY. Predeceased by her first husband, Pierre Dammann, her second husband, Joseph W. Owen, and her son, Jean-Pierre Dammann. Survived by her daughter, Jacqueline Dammann Everett, of Mt. Kisco, NY, six grandchildren, seven great-grandchildren, and two sisters. Graduate of Cambridge University and the Sorbonne. Formerly of Riverdale-onHudson, NY, she developed a bilingual program for the NYC Board of Higher Education. Previously, she headed the family's importing company, translated psychoanalytic texts, and worked on the Normal Child Development Study at Columbia University. Memorial service 11 AM, Saturday, May 12, at the Church of St. Mary the Virgin, 191 South Greeley Ave., Chappaqua, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Nature Conservancy, 4245 North Fairfax Drive, Suite 100, Arlington, VA 22203.
