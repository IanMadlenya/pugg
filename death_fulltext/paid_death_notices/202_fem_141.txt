
        GINSBURG--Freda Gordon. Born 1903, lived in the Bronx and Brooklyn, NY. Mother of the late Helene Ellinger, grandmother of Diane Rosmarin, and Mark Ellinger, great-grandmother of Adam, Seth, and Jodi, and great great-grandmother of Alexis. Worked as a bookkeeper in the floral industry in Manhattan for many years. A thoughtful and gracious woman interested throughout her entire life in world affairs, and NYC. Voracious reader and avid NYC sports fan. Loved NYC and all the cultural opportunities it offered including the theatre and symphony. Graveside services Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, NJ, today at 2 P.M.
