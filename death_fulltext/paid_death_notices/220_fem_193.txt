
        HEIDE--Sister Muriel, RSCJ. Age 88, died May 22. Daughter of the late Gertrude and Herman L. Heide of NYC. She entered the Society of the Sacred Heart in 1935. She is survived by three sisters, Mrs. Edmund Sullivan, Mrs. James Kane and Mrs. Daniel Quigley and a brother, Padre Pablo Maria, OCSO. Predeceased by siblings Mrs. Edward McCrossin, Rt. Rev. Msgr. Herman Heide & Mrs. Dorothy Hynes. Services at Kenwood Convent include a Vigil at 4:30 on Wednesday May 25 and a Mass of Christian Burial at 11 A.M. on Thursday, May 26, 799 South Pearl Street, Albany, NY.
