
        TOWNSEND -- Jo - Ann, died on October 12th of complications during a procedure for a cerebral aneurysm. She was a practicing clinical psychologist and psychoanalyst for 31 years, a colleague and friend who inspired others with her remarkable honesty, fairness, intellect and passionate commitment to her work. She touched the lives of many with her incredible smile and sense of humor. A profoundly connected and loving friend, wife, mother and sister, she is survived by her husband Michael Devorkin, her daughter Sarah, her brother Joe and sister Ruth. A memorial service will be at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 91st & Amsterdam Ave., NYC, on Oct. 18 at 1:30 pm. Contributions may be made in her memory to Bank Street College School for Children, 610 W. 112 St., NYC. She was greatly loved and will be deeply missed.
