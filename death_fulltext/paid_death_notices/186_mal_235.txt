
        KLEIN - Fred. 1913 - 2002. Adored husband of Marilynn for 35 years, caring uncle of Michael, Patti and Sally, and sister-in-law Dorothy Klein. Born in Hungary, Fred had a stellar 57 year career as a French horn player with CBS Symphony, Rochester Philharmonic, Mexico Symphony, Goldman Band, New York Philharmonic, Broadway shows and TV. He played the first performance in the US of Britten's Serenade for Horn, Tenor and Strings. Fred and Marilynn shared an avid interest in transatlantic ship travel and a passion for New York City. He was unforgettable to all who knew him. Fred was proud to donate his body for research to Columbia University. There will be a celebration of Fred's life in September.
