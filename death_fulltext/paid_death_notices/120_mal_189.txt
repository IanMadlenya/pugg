
        PFLUEGER-Edward M. Age 91, January 15, 1997. Beloved husband of Kathleen Powers Pflueger. Survived by several nieces and nephews. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Saturday, January 18 and Sunday, January 19, 3-5PM. Services and interment private. Memorial service at St. James Episcopal Church to be announced at a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        PFLUEGER-Edward M. The Board of Managers and staff of East Side House Settlement express their sorrow at the passing of Mr. Edward M. Pflueger, husband of our good friend and long time Board member, Mrs. Kathleen Pflueger. We extend our heartfelt sympathy. John A. Sanchez, Exec. Director Jack McAlinden, President
