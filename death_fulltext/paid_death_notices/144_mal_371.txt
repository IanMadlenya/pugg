
        SCHWARTZ-Charles. In his 80th year. After a courageous but brief battle with cancer. Beloved husband of Henrietta. Loving father of Carol and Ira Wallis and Steven and Claudia Schwartz. Loving step-father of Amy Tarcher and Burt and Ann Tarcher. Adored grandfather of Ben and Jason Wallis, Julian and Eli Schwartz and Christopher and Alessandra Tarcher. Also survived by his sisters Miriam Siegel and Edith Capa. Charles will be dearly remembered. Funeral services Sunday, 11:30 AM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, LI.
