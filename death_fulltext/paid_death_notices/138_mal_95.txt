
        DONNELLY-John A. Died on July 7th, 1998, at age 78 at home in Vero Beach, Florida. Due to complications from Cancer. Born in Brooklyn, NY, May 29, 1920. Was a WWII Veteran and had a long and celebrated career as a Television Producer in Advertising. Survived by his loving wife of 48 years, Joan T. Donnelly and his children Jack Donnelly of Pound Ridge, NY, Brian Donnelly of Overland Park, Kansas, Maryann Kreischer of East Meadow, NY. Donations in his memory should be sent to: Hospice of The Treasure Coast, POB 1748, Fort Pierce, FL 34954. Funeral services were held on July 9th, 1998 at Holy Cross RC, Vero Beach, FL.
