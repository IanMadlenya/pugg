
        GUTTAG-Alfred. On Jan. 29, 2002. Loving son of Bianca. Beloved husband of Mina. Caring father of Arlene and Barton. Grandfather of Alexa, Matthew, Barbara and Beverly. Great-grandfather of Cedar. Former Eagle Scout, proud member of the 754th Tank Battalion & Guadalcanal veteran. Founding member and chairman of the Charlotte Harbor Marine Advisory Committee with twenty-five years of dedicated service to the Coast Guard Auxiliary. An avid fisherman and boatman and the nemesis of cobia and triple tail. He always put one foot in front of the other until the end.
