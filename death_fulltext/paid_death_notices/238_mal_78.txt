
        CEPERO--Enrique, PhD. The trustees, faculty and staff of Cold Spring Harbor Laboratory are deeply saddened by the loss of Enrique Cepero, a friend and promising young researcher who passed away November 4, 2006 after a three-year battle with cancer. Henry was a beloved husband of Jennifer, devoted father of Katerina Mari and Ana Laura, adored son of Serapio and Ana, and cherished brother of Ernesto and Eduardo. His friendship and dedication to cancer research will be missed by his colleagues. At the request of the family, memorial contributions may be made to the Damon Runyon Cancer Research Foundation. Eduardo Mestre, Chairman Bruce Stillman, President James D. Watson, Chancellor W. Dillaway Ayres, Chief Operating Officer
