
        MOREHOUSE-Edward J. Of Chappaqua, died Thursday, September 28, 2000, at Northern Westchester Hospital. He was born on December 8, 1920, in San Francisco, CA, to the late Edward C. and Katherine (Kerwin) Morehouse. He was 79. Mr. Morehouse was a graduate of UC-Berkeley. He worked for 25 years as an executive vice president of HarrimanRippley and retired as a vice president for Merrill Lynch in 1985. He was a former member of the Campfire Club and a devoted fisherman as well as as an avid turkey hunter. During WWII, Mr. Morehouse served in the US Navy in the Pacific. He held the position of Executive Officer on the submarine Pomfret. He married Martha Jean Bernstein on October 26, 1946, in Kingston, NY. She died in May of 2000. He is survived by three sons, John, Peter, and Stephen; a daughter, Margaret; one brother, Vincent; and his sister, Jean Ritchie. He was grandfather of six. His son Jeffrey died earlier. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, October 7, at 11 AM, at Bedford Community Church, 160 Bedford Center Road, Bedford Hills, NY. Interment was private. In lieu of flowers, donations in his memory to the American Cancer Society, Westchester Division, Inc., 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, NY 10601 would be greatly appreciated.
