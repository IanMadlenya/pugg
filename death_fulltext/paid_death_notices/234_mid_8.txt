
        HOLLANDER--Edith Orrin-ger, on June 26, 2006 in San Rafael, CA. Oldest of five children of the late Albert and Jennie Orringer. Beloved wife of the late Bertram Hol lander. Survived and loved by her son Michael and his wife Clare, and by her sister Mildred Orringer, and many nieces, nephews and cousins. Edith was a Life Member and Manhattan's ''Woman of the Year'' (1970) for Hadassah.
