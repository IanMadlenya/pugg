
        SCOTT-Orland M. A long-time resident of Bronxville, New York, died on Thursday, July 8, at his home in Ocean Ridge, Florida. He was 82. Born in Ash Grove, MO. He graduated from the University of Missouri in 1938, and joined the IBM Corporation. He was an officer in the U.S. Navy during World War II, as an IBM specialist. Returning to IBM in 1946, he later became a Corporate Vice President and retired in that capacity in 1976. An avid golfer, prominent civic leader & an elder of the Reformed Church in Bronxville. He served his community in countless ways. He is survived by his wife of 61 years, Lorrayne (Joni), son Roger of San Jose, California, daughter Sherry Ann of Reno, Nevada, as well as five grandchildren, and 4 great-grandchildren. Burial will be private. Memorial service in Delray Beach, FL. in the fall. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to the National Parkinson's Foundation, Inc., Miami, FL or Hospice by the Sea, Boca Raton, FL.
