
        HIMOFF-Dr. Ick. Of White Plains, NY on May 15, age 94. Husband of Helen, father of Eve-Ellen Starkman and Gregory Himoff. Brother of Dudley and Robert Himoff. Grandfather of Amy, Wendy, Arielle and Dana. Predeceased by his sister, Mildred Lewis. Funeral service Thursday 10:30AM at Congregation Kol Ami Soundview Avenue, White Plains, NY. Interment Washington Cemetery, Brooklyn, New York. Memorial contributions in his memory may be sent to Congregation Kol Ami.
        HIMOFF-Dr. Ick. Congregation Kol Ami records with sorrow the passing of our cherished member and conveys sincere condolences to his bereaved family. Rabbi Shira Milgrom Rabbi Thomas K. Weiner Cantor Annie Bornstein Ken Simon, President
