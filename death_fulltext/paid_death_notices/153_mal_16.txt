
        NEUSTADTER-Helene, nee Lerner. Congregation Shearith Israel, the Spanish & Portuguese Synagogue, mourns the loss of the mother of its Segan and esteemed Trustee, Peter Neustadter. We extend our deepest sympathy to Mr. Neustadter, his wife Naomi, and his family. The burial and Shibah are being observed in Israel. Alvin Deutsch, Parnas
