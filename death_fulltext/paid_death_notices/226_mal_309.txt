
        WALDENBERG--Philip. Beloved husband of the late Gertrude. Devoted father of Carol Testa (Michael), Stefani Weiss (Steven) and Adair Waldenberg (Jon). Cherished grandfather of Matthew Testa, Marlisse Testa-Shrode (Jason), Jonathon Weiss, Jeremy Weiss (Amy), Pearce Peck-Walden (May), and Shannon Peck-Walden, and great-grandfather of Lexi Shrode. Loving brother of Harold Waldenberg. He was the first treasurer of Great Neck Synagogue and past president of Anshei Emuna Congregation, Delray Beach, FL. Services Sunday 12 Noon at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY. Interment at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, NY.
