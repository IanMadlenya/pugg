
        KRAMAN-Eli H. Age 81, on December 4, 1998. He will be remembered for his talent as a painter. He bore his final illness with great dignity and courage. Beloved husband of Esther. Loving father of Nadine, Cynthia and father-in-law of Abbot Genser. Dear brother of Al. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., at 81st Street, Sunday December 6, 11 AM. Interment to follow at New Paltz Rural Cemetery. Contributions in his memory may be made to Jacob Perlow Hospice, c/o Beth Israel Hospital.
