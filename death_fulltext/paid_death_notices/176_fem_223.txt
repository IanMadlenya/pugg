
        WIGHT-Elma Needles. Of Maplewood, NJ formerly of Skidaway Island, GA and Madison, NJ on September 24, 2001. Wife of 61 years to John Welles Wight. Mother of Susan Wight Poinier of Duxbury, MA; John Welles Wight, Jr. of Madison, NJ and William Needles Wight of Short Hills, NJ. Sister of Margaret Williams, Mary McJunkin Gray, Carolyn Homer, Sally Toffey and the late Thomas Needles. She is also survived by ten grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. She was the daughter of the late Enoch R. Needles and Ethel Schuman Needles. A memorial service will be held at the Presbyterian Church of New Vernon, New Vernon, NJ on Friday September 28, 2001 at 10:00 am. Those who wish may contribute in her memory to either Wellesley College, 106 Central St., Wellesley, MA, 02481-8203, Attn: Development Services, in Memory of Elma N. Wight or the American Red Cross Disaster Relief Fund, 1 Madison Avenue, Madison, NJ, 07940, to aid those affected by the World Trade Center Disaster. For information, Wm. A. Bradley & Son, Funeral Directors, Chatham, NJ (973) 635-2428.
