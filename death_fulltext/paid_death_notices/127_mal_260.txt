
        HASSETT-John A. The President, the Board of Trustees, the Manhattan College Alumni Society and the entire Manhattan College community mourn the death of John (Buddy) Hassett, class of 1933. Athletically inclined, Buddy played both basketball & baseball for Manhattan College. He was president of the Athletic Association and Captain of the Basketball Team in his senior year. A loyal alumnus, Buddy was inducted into the Manhattan College Alumni Society Athletic Hall of Fame in 1981. To the Hassett family we extend our prayerful condolences. Brother Thomas J. Scanlan, Pres.
