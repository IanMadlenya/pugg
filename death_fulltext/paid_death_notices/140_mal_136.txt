
        GORELICK-Ruth. Of Coconut Creek, Florida, formerly Tamarac, Florida, Ellenville and Brooklyn, New York. Died on August 29th. Past President of Nachamah Group of Hadassah. Beloved wife of Murray. Loving mother of Norrie and Evan, and mother-in-law of Alis & the late Howard. Adoring grandmother of Jeremy and his wife Melissa, Michael & Stephen; and great grandmother of Carly. Dear friend, forever remembered and loved.
