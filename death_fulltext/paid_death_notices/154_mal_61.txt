
        ROWE-Colin F. 1920-1999. Renowned architectural theorist, urban designer, architect, author and Professor Emeritus at Cornell University. He had an indelible influence on architectural education and practice in the U.S. and Europe over the last 40 years. He will be dearly missed by all those who learned from, taught with, and loved him. With deepfelt condolences to his family and friends. Blake Middleton Martha Eddy
