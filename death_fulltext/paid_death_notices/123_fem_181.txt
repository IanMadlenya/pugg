
        STEINIGER-Joan B., 84. On Wednesday, April 23, 1997 at Lenox Hill Hospital, NY City. Beloved wife of the late Edward L. Steiniger and dear mother of the late Dr. Gerald E. Steiniger. A past-Vice President of the Ladies of Charity, NYC and Morality in Media, NYC. Mrs. Steiniger was also a Lady of the Order of the Holy Sepulchre. She is survived by her daughter, Pamela J. Saelzler of Fredericksburg, VA, her sister Kathleen Johnson of Denver and her brothers, Michael and John Bergin of Maspeth, Queens. Viewing will be at Gleason's Funeral Home, 149-20 Northern Blvd., Flushing, NY, (718-359-6300) on Sunday, April 27, 1:00-5:00PM with Rosary at 3:00PM. Funeral mass 10:00AM on Monday, April 28 at St. Jean Baptiste Church, 184 East 76 St., NYC. Interment Calvary Cemetery, Queens, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Ladies of Charity, 1011 First Ave., NYC 10022 or St. Jean Baptiste Restoration/Window Fund, 184 E. 76 St., NYC 10021.
