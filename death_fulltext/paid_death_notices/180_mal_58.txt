
        MICHALSON-Howard Alan. On January 3, 2002, at age 72. Respected securities broker with Lehman Brothers for 41 years and most recently with UBS PaineWebber and served as a U.S. Naval officer. Beloved husband of Lynn (Morris), loving father of Douglas Michalson and Beth Fiore. Dear brother of Robert and brother-inlaw of Nancy. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St., Saturday, January 5th, 7-9PM with service Sunday, 11:30 AM. Interment to follow at Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the American Cancer Society or the Central Park Conservancy.
