
        GLOTZER--George. Age 93. Died July 12, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Diana (Sorkin). Loving father of Sandy Wang (Michael) and Alan (Susan Miller); proud grandfather of Andy Wang (Karen), Kathy Seward (Duane), Emily, and Owen; adoring great grandfather of Lydia and Sophie Wang, and Zachary and Macy Seward; cherished uncle, cousin and friend. Former Executive Vice President of Local 1199 and Head of the Drug Division. Humanist, traveler, painter, story teller. Committed to social justice and activism. Loving, articulate and witty throughout his life. A private service was held on Cape Cod.
