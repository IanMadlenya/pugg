
        KIRCHMAN--Milton F. Architect on May 22 at age 95. Dear husband and companion of Rose. Father of Ernest, Laura and Ann. Grandfather of Manoli and Laertes Manuelidis, Dana and Ronit Kirchman, great-grandchildren Thalia, Melina and Chloe Manuelidis. He served on the Board of NYC Health and Hospitals Corporation and was an avid scholar. Funeral services Private.
