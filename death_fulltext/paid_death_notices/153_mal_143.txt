
        FEINSTEIN-Marnin, Dr. On October 10, 1999. Of Raanana Israel formerly of Paramus, NJ. Professor of Jewish History and Hebrew Literature at City College of New York and Columbia University. Dean of Herzliah Hebrew Teachers Institute of New York. Accomplished author, artist, musician and orator. Survived by his sister Alitta Irwin & his children, Karma Cohen and Rami Feinstein.
