
        PASTERNACK-Lillian Illions. Born 12 December, 1911. Second eldest of five siblings. Died Lenox Hill Hospital on 6 January, 2003. Teacher of many who will never forget her dedication to their development. Wife of Jack who never forgot her deep devotion to his potential during 58 years of marriage. Survived by son Fred who will never forget her determination to help him be all he could be and by grandsons, Alexander and Gregory who experienced her unconditional love. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Lenox Hill Hospital would be appreciated.
