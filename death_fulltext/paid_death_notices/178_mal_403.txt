
        VANDOW-Benjamin. Age 85, on November 24, 2001 after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Blanche, cherished father of daughters, Marjorie Vandow and Ellen Klein, and step-sons, Alan Chodosh and the late Daniel Chodosh. Adored grandfather of Gabriel and Rebecca Fields, Michelle and Amy Chodosh, Jonathan, Ethan and Yael Chodosh, and Lonnie and Brielle Klein. Dear brother of Jeannette Silverstein. Devoted husband for 34 years to the late Lillian Vandow. Ben was a manufacturers representative in the gift industry for over 50 years as owner and president of Van Dow-Fenton, Inc., a leader in its industry. He was loved by all who knew him. His memory will be an inspiration to all who had the privilege of knowing this dear gentle man. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Whitestone Hebrew Centre, 12-45 Clintonville Street, Whitestone, New York 11357.
