
        LEVITT--Esther, 91. On October 19, 2005 at her home in Greenwich Village. She was the dynamic VP of the Security Plan of District 65. She was a world traveller, a lover of the arts and always spoke the truth. Barbara and I will miss her. She was my mentor, friend and ''aunt''. Graveside Services Sunday, October 23, 1 P.M. at New Montefiore Cemetery, NY. Richard Marks
