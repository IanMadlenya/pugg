
        ATWATER-John Jacob, Jr., 82, husband of Julia Papai Atwater and a resident of Onancock, VA, formerly of Chester, NJ, passed away on Friday, September 7th, 2001. Born in NYC, Mr. Atwater was the son of the late John Jacob Atwater and Marjory Wilcox Atwater. He served in the U.S. Coast Guard during WWII. He is also survived by four children, three grandchildren, and a sister, Janet Wall of Union Island, BWI.
