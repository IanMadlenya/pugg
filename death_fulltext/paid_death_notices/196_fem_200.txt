
        SELIGMAN--Janice Lerner. Died peacefully at home on Thursday, May 22, after a long illness. She is survived by her beloved daughters Ellen and Maggie and her grandchildren Alex and David. She had for many years been an active fundraiser for The New Lincoln School. She was well-known by her friends not only as vivacious, witty and generous, but as someone who remained a strong and outspoken supporter for many social and other causes. All who knew her will miss this courageous and great lady. In lieu of flowers donations may be made in her name to Gay Men's Health Crisis and The Southern Poverty Law Center. >>AD#
