
        BISS-Egon J., 86, Doctor of Law from the University of Vienna, 1937. Son of the late Anna Deutsch Biss and Adolph Biss who perished in Auschwitz. Adored and adoring husband for fifty-seven years of Elaine. Beloved father of Vivien Biss Goldbaum Feder, Esq. (Norman) and Micky Biss; cherished grandfather of Robert Daniel Goldbaum, Esq. (Lisa) and Susan Melissa Goldbaum, Esq. Brother of the late Robert Biss and Lily Biss Popper. His wit, humor, humanity and intelligence will forever be missed by his heartbroken family. Memorial contributions in his memory may be made to the U.S. Holocaust Museum in Washington, DC, Mogen David Adom or Hadassah. Services in Hollywood, FL at Levitt Weinstein Chapel on September 21, 1999 at 10 AM; interment at Riverside Cemetery in Lodi, NJ on Wednesday, September 22, 1999 at 11 AM.
