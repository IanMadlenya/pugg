
        SILVERSTEIN--Frank. The partners, associates and staff of Bender, Jenson & Silverstein, Esqs. of White Plains, NY, mourn the loss of their friend and colleague Frank. The firm and the profession will miss him. Frank was on the Board of Managers of the New York Chapter of the American Academy of Matrimonial Lawyers and was involved in many other organizations. His passing came too soon, there was much left of his life. >>AD#
