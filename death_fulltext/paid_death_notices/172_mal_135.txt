
        BORTNER-Allen, 83. Of Boca Raton, FL, passed away on May 6th, 2001. Formerly of Searingtown, LI, NY. Husband of the late Julia Bortner. He is survived by his loving children, Kenneth, Eileen and Steven, 7 cherished grandchildren and 2 devoted sisters. Mr. Bortner was President of the Charles Offset Co. in NYC from 1969. The company moved to New Haven, CT. in 1988 as Northeast Graphics. Also Founder and President of Marketing Showcase Co. Funeral services were held in NY on Tuesday, May 8th. Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 561-496-7550.
