
        KUHN--Mary A., 96 years old, passed away on April 22, 2007. She was a lifelong resident of the Bronx and Mary Manning Walsh Nursing Home. She is survived by her three children; Dolores M. Ingles; Rev. George J. Kuhn (Pastor of St. Joseph; church in Yonkers) and Mary T. Kuhn. Also survived by six grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by her husband George F. Kuhn (FDNY). In accord with her wishes, her body was donated to NY Medical College for Medical Research. A memorial mass will be celebrated on Saturday May 5th at St. Joseph's Church, Yonkers at 12 noon. Donations may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1470 Eastchester Rd., Bronx, NY 10461. Whalen & Ball Funeral Home, 168 Park Ave., Yonkers, 914-965-5488.
