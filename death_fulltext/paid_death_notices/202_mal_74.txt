
        MOREAU -- Pierre. Passed away in Limoges, France, on Monday, November 4, 2003, at the age of 75. He lived a rich, full life, and was respected and loved by everyone who knew him. He left behind his loving wife Janine and his son Jean-Paul. He was the most courageous and selfless man we ever knew, and we will miss him forever. Janine and Jean-Paul
