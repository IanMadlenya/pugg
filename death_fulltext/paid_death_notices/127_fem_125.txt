
        BISHOP-Margaret M. The Board of Trustees of Reading Reform Foundation of NY records with sorrow the death of its founding President, Margaret M. Bishop, of Sunnyvale, CA. She was a compassionate person who cared deeply about the teaching of reading to all people. She devised a brilliant reading program which she used in her work at Fortune Society. We extend our condolences to her husband, Edwin, and her children, Molly and Peter. Sidney S. Korzenik, Chairman Emily R. Goldberg, President Sandra Priest Rose, Treasurer
