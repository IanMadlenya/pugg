
        DONOHUE - Margaret. On December 14, 2002. Beloved mother of Richard and his wife Helen. Adoring grandmother of Evan and Jordan. A longtime member of Jewish Women International and a generous contributor to the Residential Treatment Center for Boys and Girls in Jerusalem. A charitable and special friend, she will be deeply missed. Service Sunday, 12:45 PM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Avenue.
