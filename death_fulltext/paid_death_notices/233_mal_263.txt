
        GOLDBERG--Sheldon. Born August 28, 1930, died June 19, 2006. Native New Yorker, Sheldon died at his home in Southampton, NY after a long battle with cancer. A graduate of Brooklyn Law School, he was an expert advisor of tax law and estates planning. Retired in 1998 from Edward Isaacs & Co., where he was managing tax partner. He is survived by his wife, Miriam, his sons Seth and Hal Goldberg. Memorial contributions may be made to Memorial Sloane-Kettering and Southampton Rose Society.
