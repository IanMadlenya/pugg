
        VACEK--Michael E. The governors, members and staff of the Real Estate Board of New York mourn the passing ofMichael Vacek. Michael represented REBNY with distinction before the New York State Legislature for nearly two decades. His work always met the highest professional standards. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his children, Michael, Jr., Thomas, Timothy and the entire Vacek family. John E. Zuccotti, Chairperson Steven Spinola, President
