
        JAMES--Daniel Willis. Died peacefully on Sunday, April 4, 2004, N.Y., N.Y., age 83. Devoted father of Mimi James, Mary James-Johnstone and Rosalie Stevens and beloved brother of Philip James and Philip's wife Mai. He is survived by six grandchildren. After serving in WWII, in which he was awarded the Distinguished Flying Cross, he worked at Merrill Lynch. Funeral services on April 20th at 10AM at Corpus Christi Monastery in the Bronx. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Veterans of Foreign Wars Foundation would be appreciated.
