
        NEUSTADT-Etta. 97, of Mamaroneck, N.Y., on October 2, 2000. Beloved wife of the late Samuel. Loving mother of Jack and his wife, Rhena, and Paul and his wife, Zelda. Cherished grandmother of Beth Figman and husband Scott, Mark Neustadt & his wife, Rachel Friedman. Devoted great grandmother of Jordan & Ted Figman, Chelsea Sage Figman, Noah Daniel Figman and Isaac Nathaniel Neustadt. Last surviving sibling of Al Gottlieb, Max Gottlieb, Rose Gottlieb Christiano, Mollie Gottlieb Blau, Louis Gottlieb, Moe Gottlieb and Leon Gottlieb. Truly a woman of valor, loved by her children, grandchildren, great grandchildren, and many nieces and nephews. Funeral services will be today, October 3, 1:00 PM, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mt. Vernon, NY. Interment at Cedar Park Cemetery, Emerson, NJ. Donations made to Sarah Neuman Nursing Home, Mamaroneck, New York, Greenberg Hebrew Center, Dobbs Ferry, NY, and Fort Lee Jewish Center, Fort Lee, New Jersey.
