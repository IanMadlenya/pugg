
        PAPALEO--Joseph, passed away in his home in Tampa, FL on August 4, 2004. An award-winning author and Professor of Fiction Literature at Sarah Lawrence College for 35 years, Joseph was a decorated veteran of World War II. He leaves behind his loving wife of 21 years, Tonie, children, Joseph, William, Robert and Anthony and grandchildren, Liana, Matteo, Nicholas, Taylor, Ryan and Aaron. Visitation will be Friday from 2-4 PM and 7-9 PM at John J. Fox Funeral Home. Mass of Christian Burial will be Saturday, 10:30 AM, at Immaculate Conception Church, Tuckahoe. Interment private.
