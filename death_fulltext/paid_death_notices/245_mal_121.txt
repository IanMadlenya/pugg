
        FEDER--Walter, M.D. The medical staff of Maimonides Medical Center is deeply saddened by the death of Dr. Walter Feder. Dr. Feder was associated with Maimonides Medical Center for more than 40 years. He served as chief of the cardiology division from 1967-1976. He was an excellent clinician who treated his patients with great concern and caring. He will be sorely missed by all his colleagues, friends and patients. Sincere condolences are extended to his entire family. I. Mayer, M.D., President W. Wexelman, M.D., Pres Elect S. Farber, M.D., Secy/Treas
