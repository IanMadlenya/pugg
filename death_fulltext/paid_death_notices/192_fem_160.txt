
        MILLER-Eileen (nee Eskow). After her courageous battle against brain cancer, died peacefully on January 14, 2003, at age 47. Precious and beloved sister of Valerie and husband, Jim O'Halpin, sister, Karen Seader and sister, Laurie, and husband, Michael Getlan. Her good humor and passion for life affected everyone who knew her. Services Friday, January 17, 11 AM, at Temple Beth-El of Great Neck, 5 Old Mill Rd.
