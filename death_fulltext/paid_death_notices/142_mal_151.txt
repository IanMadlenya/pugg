
        STATELMAN-Richard T. On November 10, 1998. Following his 15 year career in the banking industry, he dedicated his life to the struggle against AIDS. He headed first the Brooklyn then the Bronx AIDS network. He is the beloved son of Edward and Genevieve Statelman. Richard touched the lives of all who knew him. Donations may be made to St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center, 130 W. 12 St., Suite 6B, NYC 10001.
