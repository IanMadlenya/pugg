
        WALTERS-Madeline R. Of Bergenfield, New Jersey. Formerly of Tenafly, NJ and Astoria, NY. Died February 18, 2002. A graduate of the City University of New York and a former member of Precious Blood Church in Astoria. She was for many years secretary and executive assistant to the president of the General Cable Corp in NY. Surviving are her sister Dorothy Armbruster of Bergenfield, NJ, her sister-in-law Margaret Walters of PA, 6 nephews and a niece, 10 grandnieces and 3 grandnephews & 2 great-grandnieces. For more information Riewerts Memorial Home, 187 South Washington Ave, Bergenfield, NJ. Contributions may be made to the Interreligious Fellowship of the Homeless of Bergen County, 479 Maitland Ave, Teaneck, New Jersey 07666.
