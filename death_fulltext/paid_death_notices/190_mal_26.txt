
        HERFORT-Stephen Arthur, 51. Died November 1, 2002 Newark, NJ. Beloved son of Judith Handler Pearce and the late Dr. Robert A. Herfort. Stepson of Eli Pearce, the late Myron Ruby and Jane Herfort. Adored and loving brother of John Herfort and the late David Herfort, Michael Ruby, Elizabeth Lyden, Deborah H. Tiede and Robert Herfort. He was a gentle soul of great integrity. May he go in peace. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Mount Carmel Guild, 1160 Raymond Boulevard, Newark, NJ 07102. Graveside service, November 3, 11:00 AM, Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York.
