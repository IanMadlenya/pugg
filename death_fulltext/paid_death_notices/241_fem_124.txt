She is survived by loving sons David and Michael, devoted daughters-in-law Carol and Ann, cherished grandchildren Jenny, Valerie, Daniel, Joel and Alisha, her great-grandchildren Claire, Sarah, Helaina, and Peter, and her niece Sharon and nephew Morton.
