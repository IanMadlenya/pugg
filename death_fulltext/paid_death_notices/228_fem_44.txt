
        JAFFE - Rona. Family, friends and colleagues deeply mourn the passing of celebrated novelist and philanthropist Rona Jaffe, of New York City, in London on December 30, 2005, at age 74. She graduated from Radcliffe in 1951 at age 19. In 1958, at age 26, Rona's debut novel ''The Best of Everything'' stayed on The New York Times Best Seller List for 5 months. Rona went on to write 15 more books, including ''Class Reunion,'' ''Family Secrets'' and ''Mr. Right is Dead.'' She supported the Rona Jaffe Prize for Writing at Radcliffe. Her Rona Jaffe Foundation has supported women writers of exceptional promise. Funeral services Friday 9:30 A.M. at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
