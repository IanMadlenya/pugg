
        HAUPTMAN - Isabel ''Trotie'' (Abelson). New York resident for over 70 years died in her sleep November 3, 2000 at the age of 101. As a graduate of Wellesley College Isabel's loyalty to the school was number 1 on her list. She leaves two daughters, Joannee Marks of New York City and Kit Kuperman of Denver, Colorado, five grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. All of her long time and beloved friends will miss her greatly. Services private.
