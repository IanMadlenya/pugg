
        SALOMON-Mildred Peel, 91 years old, of Palm Beach, Florida, formerly of New York, New York, died Saturday, December 26, 1998, at her residence in Palm Beach. Mrs. Salomon was preceded in death by her husband, Ellsworth G. Salomon. She is survived by her brother, Clifford W. Peel (wife, Mary) of El Cajon, CA. Funeral Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in her memory to the Deafness Research Foundation, 575 Fifth Avenue, Eleventh Floor, New York, New York 10017.
