
        HOLZINGER-John F. Dobbs Ferry, NY, died July 16, 2001. Born in Manhattan January 8, 1911, grew up in Riverdale and graduated from Columbia University in 1931. He served as Lt. Commander in the US Navy, directing landing craft during the WWII invasion at Omaha Beach. John established the real estate firm, Barhite & Holzinger, Inc. in 1935, in Manhattan, moving its headquarters to Bronxville, in 1969. He retired in 1995. On March 4, 1938, John married Claire Heffernan. She survives as does his son John F. Holzinger, his daughter Dana Dyas and four grandchildren. Services will be private. Contributions to Dobbs Ferry Public Library, 153 Main St. Dobbs Ferry, NY 10522 or American Cancer Society, 2 Lyon Place, White Plains, New York 10601.
