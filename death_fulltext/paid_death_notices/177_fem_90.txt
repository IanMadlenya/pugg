
        NIZZARDINI-Adelaide. Of Bronx, on Wednesday, October 10, 2001. Beloved wife of Anthony. Survived by son Robert. Cherished sister of Howard Hurwitz. Adored aunt of Esther Wertlieb, Miriam Shapiro, Amy Thau and their families. Grandmother of four, great grandmother of four. Loving sister-inlaw and aunt. Adelaide worked side by side with her husband Tony at their business, Masonic Supply Co., where they have been tenants of the Flatiron Building in Manhattan for over 50 years. Adelaide was an exceptional lady and will be greatly missed by all whose lives she touched. Services 12 noon Friday at Louis Suburban Chapel, 13-01 Broadway (Route 4 West), Fair Lawn, NJ. The family will receive their relatives and friends at the funeral chapel from 11 AM until 12 noon. For directions to the chapel please call 1-800525-3834. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to Shriners Hospitals, care of Mecca Temple, 71 West 23 Street, NY NY 10010.
