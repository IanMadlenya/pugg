
        SACHS-Beatrice Phillips, 93, of New York City, formerly of Boston, MA, died October 19 in NYC. Loving aunt of Maynard and Suzanne Goldman of Boston and great-aunt to Derek and Emily Goldman of Chapel Hill, NC. Beloved wife of the late Bernard Phillips of Boston and the late Henry Sachs of NYC; devoted sister of the late Harriette Feingold Goldman. There will be no funeral service, but a memorial service will be held in November. Contributions in her memory may be made to Street Signs, 85 Amble Wood Run, Chapel Hill, NC 27516.
