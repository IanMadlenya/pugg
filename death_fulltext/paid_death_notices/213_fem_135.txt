
        OHRBACH--Jean, age 82. Beloved wife of the late Milton Ohrbach. She is survived by her grandson Daniel Viola Johnson. A graduate of Juilliard, she taught piano for many years, sharing her love of music with students both young and old. She is remembered with love and gratitude by her wide circle of family, friends and students. Memorial Service on Friday, October 22, 12pm, 70 Lincoln Center Plaza, Rose Studio, 10th Flr, Rose Building, (67th & Amsterdam Plaza Level).
