
        VIRSHUP-Dr. Bernard, Cardiologist; for the past 25 years a pioneer in Humanistic Medicine; Author of ''Coping in Medical School'' and many other books and articles. He was Clinical Professor of Psychiatry and Behavioral Sciences at UCLA School of Medicine, Associate Clinical Professor at USC School of Medicine, and taught at Art Center College of Design. Survived by his beloved wife Evelyn; three loving sons and 6 grandsons. Memorial services in Los Angeles, CA., at Mount Sinai Mortuary.
