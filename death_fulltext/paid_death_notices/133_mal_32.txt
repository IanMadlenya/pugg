
        GRAD-John D. Died February 2, 1998 at the age of fifty, after a brave battle with brain cancer. He was the son of the late Thelma Grad and James Grad, husband of Rae Krohn Grad, father of Arlen, Rebecca and Aaron, brother of Adrian Grad Adolphe and Maxine Grad, uncle of Julia Adolphe, Sophia and Noa Shems. He was devoted to civil rights law and argued several cases in front of the Supreme Court.
