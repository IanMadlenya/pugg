
        GOLDEN-David M. Born in Providence, Rhode Island December 18, 1946. Having only been diagnosed on November 6, 1998, died of colon cancer on December 28, 1998 in his home in Chappaqua, N.Y. He is deeply mourned by his beloved wife Lee Golden and his sons, Sam & Matt of Chappaqua. David is also mourned by his loving mother Lillian Golden and his sister Carol Golden Einhorn, both of Providence, Rhode Island. David's career in magazine advertising spanned twenty years, where he served in Senior Sales and management positions at ''GQ'', ''Vanity Fair'', ''Departures'' and ''The New Yorker'' magazines. Most recently, he was the Director of American fashion and retail advertising at ''The New Yorker''. He graduated from the University of Pennsylvania in 1968 with a B.A. in English and served as an English teacher in the Peace Corps in Ethiopia. He also worked in book publishing and retail fashion before moving into magazine advertising. David's memory will be a blessing to all who knew him. Services will be held at 9:30 A.M., on Tuesday, December 29 at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, New York. Interment will follow at 2PM at Temple Beth El Cemetery, Reservoir Ave in Providence, Rhode Island. The family will sit shiva in Providence on Wednesday and Thursday, December 30 and 31 from 2 to 4PM and 7 to 9PM and in Chappaqua on Friday, January 1,2-4PM, Saturday, January 2, 69PM and Sunday, January 3, 1 to 4PM and 6 to 8PM. In lieu of flowers, the Golden family would appreciate contributions to Hospice Care in Westchester and Putnam, Inc. located at 100 South Bedford Road, Mount Kisco, New York 10549 or to a charity of your choice.
