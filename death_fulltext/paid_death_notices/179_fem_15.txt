
        STAUFFER-Geraldine Kirk. Our most cherished mother, Geraldine Kirk Stauffer, much beloved wife of the late George Michael Stauffer, passed away on Friday, November 30th at home in New Jersey in the 79th year. Her loss is grievous, but her children Michael Kirk Stauffer, Janet Stauffer Lesko and her treasured grandson, Thomas Lesko are sustained by the imperishable memory of her loving kindness, her gracefulness and her passions for justice and learning. Funeral private.
