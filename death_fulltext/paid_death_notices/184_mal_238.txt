
        HEFFERNAN-Maurice D. On May 17, 2002, of Franklin Lakes. Beloved husband of Betty (nee Gaffney). Devoted father of Maurice D. Heffernan Jr. of Santa Barbara, CA, Kevin Heffernan of South Burlington, VT, Paul Heffernan of New Hope, PA, Patrice Cahill of Franklin Lakes, John Heffernan of Franklin Lakes, and James Heffernan of Canton, GA.
