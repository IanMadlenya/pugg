
        BUSCH-Benjamin. 81. Died on Monday, May 20, 2002, in Lake Worth, FL, after a long illness. For many years, the owner of Symphony Music Shop in Yonkers and an employee of Walter Kane Music Publishing, he was also an accomplished singer of opera and musical comedy. He is survived by his wife Irene, his children Elizabeth Busch-Le Ne, Margaret Busch and the playwright, Charles Busch, and his grandson James Stull. Interment will be at Ferncliff Cemetery, Hartsdale, N.Y. He will be missed by all who experienced his wit, affection and joyous spirit.
