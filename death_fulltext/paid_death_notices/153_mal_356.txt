
        MARTINEZ-Julio. The Project Return Foundation family mourns the untimely passing of our founder, who went on the become New York State Commissioner of Substance Abuse Services. His career was devoted to helping substance abusers find a better life through recovery. We honor his commitment and achievements and send our sincere condolences to all his family. He will be missed by all. Project Return Foundation Jane Velez, President Dr. Judith D. Wallach, Chairperson
