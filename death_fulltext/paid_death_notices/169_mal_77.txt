
        SWIGER-Reverend Msgr. Charles W. Pastor of St. Kilian R.C. Church, Farmingdale, on February 4, 2001. Beloved son of Elizabeth C. Swiger and the late Charles W. Swiger, Sr. Dear brother of Susan Swiger Munch and Terrence C. Swiger and brother-in-law of Roger Munch and Mary Swiger. Loving nephew of Miriam Burrowes and cherished uncle of Terrence, Erich, Siobhan and Claire Swiger. Monsignor Swiger will be reposed Tuesday, from 5-10 PM, and Wednesday, 1-5 PM, in the St. Benedict Chapel of St. Kilian's Rectory, 485 Conklin Street, Farmingdale. Mass of Transferral will be celebrated at 8 PM, Wednesday, in St. Kilian R.C. Church. Celebration of Mass of Christian Burial will be on Thursday at 11 AM. Interment will follow in Calvary Cemetery, Woodside, Queens. In lieu of flowers contributions in his memory to St. Kilian's Centenary Capital Campaign, appreciated.
