
        BUTTON-William H. On the morning of September 24, 1998, William H. Button passed away at his home in Sarasota, FL after a five month battle with cancer. He is survived by his wife, Katharine B. Button, stepmother, Mrs. William H. Button 2nd, sister, Judy B. Keyes, three children, and five grandchildren. Mr. Button will be remembered as a loving, generous and gentle soul. He possessed a wonderful sense of humor and a great, active intellect. The family requests that donations in his memory be sent to: Century Foundation Nursing Education Fund, 1838 Waldemere St., Sarasota, FL 34239
