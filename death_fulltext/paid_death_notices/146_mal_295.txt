
        ROSS-Chester M. March 29, 1917March 11, 1999. Died peacefully in Miami after a valiant struggle with cancer and pneumonia. Services Frank Campbell, 81st and Madison Avenue, Monday, March 15, 11:30AM. Beloved hushand of Blanche, loving and loved father of Dorien and the late Arthur. Devoted brother to Joan Hersh and brothers-in-law Michael Hersh, B.B. Goldstein, Charles Goldstein, adored uncle to Tom, Jane, Buck, John, Patrick and Amy. A lover of life. All who knew him were touched by his warmth and sparkle. A light is out. We will not see another like him.
