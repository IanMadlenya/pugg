
        RUBIN--Martha D. 95, of Clifton, NJ. Died peacefully on October 22, 2006. Devoted wife of the late Edward N. Rubin. Beloved mother of Leta (& Moshe) Arad and the late Aviva Tepley. Dear sister of Ruth (& Wally) Cowan and Norman (& Eleanor) Daitzman. Loving grandmother of Aviva & Naomi Arad, Jamina, Phil & Alan Tepley; doting great grandmother of Lina & Lidia. She will be remembered fondly for her kindness, wit and independence.
