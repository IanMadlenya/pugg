
        HUNT--William Morris of Brookline, MA, on June 23, 2003. Harvard Class of 1936. Along with Robert Whitehead produced ''Much Ado About Nothing'' featuring Sir John Gielgud, Margaret Leighton and Jean Marsh; also produced ''Saint Joan'' with Siobhan McKenna, both in the 1950's. Lovingly remembered by his children, Elise Roselli of California, Charlotte (Demi) Hitz Huang of Washington and William Morris Hunt III of Connecticut; his grandsons: John Hitz, Evan Miles Hunt and Richard Morris Hunt. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, June 28, 2003 at one o'clock at Trinity Church, Copley Square, Boston. J.S. Waterman & Sons. 617-536-4110 >>AD#
