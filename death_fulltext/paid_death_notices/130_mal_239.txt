
        DANISH-Roy. Died on November 20, 1997, at the age of 78. He is survived by his daughters Elisabeth Wheeler and Caroline Thies, his brother Keith, and three grandchildren. Born in Manhattan on March 2, 1919, he graduated from Columbia in 1940, earned an MBA from Harvard in 1942, then served as lieutenant in the U.S. Navy from 1942-1946. His career in broadcasting began with nine years at Mutual Broadcasting System, where he rose to the position of Vice President and Assistant to the President. From 1955 to 1959 he worked at McCann-Erickson as account supervisor and Vice President, then became a partner at Smith-Greenland Advertising in 1959. In 1960, Roy began his 27 year career with Television Information Office, where he served as Director from 1962 to 1987. He worked with his multi-talented staff to maintain a bridge of understanding between the television industry and its many publics. Donations in his memory may be made to the Alzheimers Association, 420 Lexington Avenue, NY, NY 10170.
