
        RANKIN-Langdon G., 90. Of Essex, CT. died October 26, 1998. Husband of Frances Jones Rankin. Father of Mrs. Joan Rankin Davis and Mrs. Judith Rankin Codella. Brother of Mrs. George M. Knowles and Mrs. Roy Byrne. Headmaster Emeritus of East Woods School. Memorial Service at St. Johns Church, Essex, Conn. on Friday at 2 P.M. If desired contributions in his memory may be made to East Woods School, Oyster Bay, New York 11771.
