
        DOWLING--John L. 66, on May 4, 2004, died at his home in Boca Grande, Florida. Beloved husband of Hebe S. Dowling. Devoted father of Meaghan, John and Peter Dowling. Cherished father-inlaw of Michael Chorske and Rebecca Dowling. Loving grandfather of Jack Chorske. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Sunday, May 9, 7-9 PM. A funeral mass will be held at St. Patrick's Cathedral, Fifth Avenue at 51st Street on Monday, May 10 at 10:30 AM.
        DOWLING--John L. It is with great sadness that Peter T. Grauer, president of the Board of Trustees of the Inner City Scholarship Fund, notes the passing of a longtime member of our executive council and the president of Dowling and Company. We are so grateful for the extraordinary dedication John consistently showed to the children attending our inner city schools of the Archdiocese of New York. His generous spirit and wise counsel will never be forgotten. Our hearts go out to his entire family including his wife Hebe and their three children in this time of loss.
        DOWLING--John L. The Board of The 120 East End Avenue Corporation is deeply saddened by the loss of their valued fellow Board member, John L. Dowling. His commitment and contribution to the welfare of our shareholders was significant, but most of all, we mourn the loss of a very special friend. Our deepest sympathy goes out to his wife Hebe, and the Dowling family. The Board
