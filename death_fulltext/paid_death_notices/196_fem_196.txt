
        ROSEN--Ansel. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Ansel Rosen, beloved mother of our dear friend, Lorie Brice. Lorie and her husband, Harvey, have expressed a deep commitment to supporting Jewish causes and helping those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Lorie & the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O. >>AD#
