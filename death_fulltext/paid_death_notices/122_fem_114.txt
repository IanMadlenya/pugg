
        LORENZ-Anna Stella, age 85, died March 13, 1997. For 58 years devoted wife of Gunter Lorenz, MD, beloved mother of Linda Marion Gerstman and son-in-law Stephen Gerstman, DDS, cherished grandmother of Elissa Eve Fullerton and husband John Fullerton, III, JD and Sherry Michelle Gerstman. Loving sister of Malka Schumm and husband Werner. She will be remembered by her family and friends for her boundless love and kindness. Service at Temple Beth Abraham Conservative Chapel, 25 Leroy Ave., Tarrytown, NY 10591 Sunday, March 16, 1997, at 1 PM.
