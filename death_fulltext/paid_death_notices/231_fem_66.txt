
        KALB--Alice Rosalind (nee Fuchs), April 8 at 85. Died peacefully in Palm Beach. Formerly of Montclair, NJ, where she lived with her beloved late husband Irving Kalb. Loving mother of Richard L. Kalb and Lisa Kalb Schaffer (Perry). Adored sister, sister-in-law, aunt and friend. Service Tuesday, April 11, 10:30AM at King Solomon Cemetery, Dwas Line Rd and Allwood Rd, Clifton. Friends may visit at the home of Lisa and Perry Schaffer, 17 Marquette Rd, Upper Montclair following services until 8PM and Wednesday, 11-4PM.
