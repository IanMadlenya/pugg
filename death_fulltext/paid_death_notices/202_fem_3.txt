
        SCHNELLER -- Adeline V. (nee Goldhammer), on October 28, 2003, in Bethesda, Md., in her 94th year. Longtime resident of NYC and Boca Raton. Beloved wife of Maxwell for 74 years. Devoted mother to Marlene Harte and Joan Knight and their husbands. Adored grandmother to Roger and John Harte and their wives; to Jennifer Dormarunno and Regina Donovan and their husbands. Cherished great-grandmother to Amalia, Penina, Joshua and Rachel Harte and Reid Donovan. Interment Sunday, November 2, at noon, at Mt. Sharon Cemetery in Philadelphia.
