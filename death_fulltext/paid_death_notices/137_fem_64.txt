
        ABRAMS-Barbara Feldman. On June 2, 1998 after a gallant battle with Acute Myeloid Leukemia. Artist, sculptor, teacher & designer, she taught 20 years at Parsons School of Design. Survived by her daughters, Lauren and Emma and her husband Roy. Her interest in land preservation for all our children is to be conserved with the Barbara Abrams Feldman Childrens' Land Trust.
