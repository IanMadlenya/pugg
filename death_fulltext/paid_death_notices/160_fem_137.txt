
        SARACHAN-Evelyn. Of Rochester, New York. Beautiful, stylish, spirited wife of the late, great, Judge Goodman A. Sarachan; mother of Donna and Martin Lawrence and Richard H. Sarachan of Rochester, and Niki Singer and Michael Sheets of New York City; grandmother of Julie Lawrence and Wlodzimierz Tomczyk and Romaine and Nicholas Sheets. Happy memories of her optimism and cheerfulness will be with us always.
