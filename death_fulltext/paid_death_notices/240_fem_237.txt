
        SHEPHARD--Geraldine L., died peacefully on January 11, 2008. Daughter of the late Vera Shephard and the late Chester Shephard and sister of Patricia Patterson, she was born at the Presidio in San Francisco. A graduate of Principia College, she moved to New York and opened her own public relations firm. She will be remembered by her countless friends for her great warmth, loyal friendship and wonderful sense of humor. She was a talented artist, photographer, tennis player and outstanding sportswoman. In addition to her sister, she leaves behind her niece Wendy Grant and a grandnephew, Bruce Grant. A Memorial Service will be held at a future date. In lieu of flowers, donations made to the Animal Rescue Fund, PO Box 901, Wainscott, New York 11975, would be appreciated by the family.
