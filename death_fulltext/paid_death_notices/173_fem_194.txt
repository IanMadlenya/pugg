
        AMES-Margaret. On June 22, in Elizabethtown, NY. A classics scholar and author who taught at Riverdale Country School and Hunter College, she is survived by husband Russell of Westport, New York, children David Foley of San Francisco and Barbara Foley of East Orange, NJ, and three grandchildren. Memorial contributions to the Ballard Park Foundation, Westport, New York 12993.
