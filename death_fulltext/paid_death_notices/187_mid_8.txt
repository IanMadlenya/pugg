
        XENIA-Rose. On August 22, 2002 at age 67 after a long bout with cancer. She was a psychotherapist and author of ''Widow's Journey, a Return to the Loving Self.'' Beloved wife of Warren Forma and the late concert cellist, Leonard Rose. Loving stepmother of Carol Forma, Thomas Forma and his wife Donna. Dear step grandmother of Jody, Leah, Vanessa and Adam Mecili. Interment private.
