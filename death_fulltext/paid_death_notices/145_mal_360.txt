
        THOMPSON-Gerald R. ''Jerry'' Of Fort Lee, NJ, died February 24, 1999 at his residence at the age of 73. He had spent his entire career with old Hudson's Bay Company having started in his native Montreal and later assuming posts in London and New York. He rose from warehouse manager to President and ultimately Chairman before retiring 8 years ago. He was a graduate of McGuil University, Montreal, Canada class of '46. He is survived by his beloved wife of 47 years Diane (nee Hainault), and his devoted daughter Lise Bernard and her husband David, cherished grandchildren Andre and Christian. Funeral service and interment will be in Montreal, Canada.
