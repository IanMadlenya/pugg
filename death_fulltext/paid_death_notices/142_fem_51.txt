
        FREY-Phyllis Ruth (nee Nayer). 1928-1998. Beloved daughter of the late Abe and Lena. Succumbed to lung cancer after a valiant struggle. Our exceptional and beloved cousin, confidante, dearest friend and most charismatic personage. Her death leaves a profound rent in the lives of the entire Sanders family including Adam, Emmanuel, Susan, Erika, Jessica, Miranda, Michele, Sophie, Carolyn and Martin. Deeply mourned by her friends Diane, Nilda, Elaine, Shirley, Joanne and Laura. Jerusalem was her love.
