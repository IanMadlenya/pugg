
        KUNIN-Jacob, in Boca Raton, FL. March 17, predeceased by Kate, his wife of 72 years. Beloved father of Phyllis Ratner and Harriet Herskowitz, grandfather of 8, and great grandfather of 14. Mr. Kunin immigrated from Russia to Bridgeport, CT., in 1925, and founded Kays Department Stores. Services Friday at 12:00 noon from Cong. B'nai Israel 2710 Park Avenue, Bridgeport, with interment following at B'nai Israel Cemetery, Fairfield, CT. For information contact Abraham L. Green and Son Funeral Home, Bridgeport, CT.
