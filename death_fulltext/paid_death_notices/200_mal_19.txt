
        JASPAN--Norman. The President and Directors of American Friends of Ariel and the Chancellor, Vice-Chancellor, Directors, Faculty, Fellows and students of Ariel United Israel Institutes, express sincerest condolences to our devoted Director and Treasurer, Michael, on the passing of his father, Norman of blessed memory. Norman was a man of deep integrity and profound wisdom, who demonstrated steadfast devotion and commitment to his family. He accepted life's challenges with determination and strength. May his sons Michael and Ronald and their families be comforted by continuing his many good deeds and generosity. Natalio Fridman, President; Rabbi Shear-Yashuv Cohen, Chancellor
        JASPAN -- Norman. We mourn the passing of Norman Jaspan, our long-time Board member and father of Michael D. Jaspan, our Executive Director. He was also the father of Ronald Jaspan, our Board member and was the husband of the late Jean Rafsky, who was a granddaughter of our Founder, Harry Fischel. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Harry and Jane Fischel Foundation
        JASPAN--Norman. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of the brother of our dear member, Mrs. Ruth Hauser. May her entire family be comforted among all who mourn for Zion & Jerusalem. Ari Berman, Rabbi Andrew Mendes, President Rachel Lurie, Sisterhood
