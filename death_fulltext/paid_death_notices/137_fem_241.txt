
        ROSENBERRY-Cicely K. On Friday, June 26 at Norwell Knoll Nursing Home, Norwell, MA. Adored wife of the late Samuel L. Rosenberry, a former senior partner of Milbank, Tweed, Hadley and McCloy; beloved mother and true friend of Nancy R. Hoit and son-in-law Roger O. Hoit; and devoted grandmother of Roger W. Hoit and Sarah H. Whitman; and great-grandmother of Morgan and Jackson Hoit. She lived all her life in New York until the past few months and devoted her civic life to education and nursing. She was a former Chair of the Board of Trustees of Smith College, from which she graduated with honors and held an honorary degree, former trustee of Hampshire College, former President of the Board of the Spence School, former trustee of the Institute for International Education and a past President of the Junior League of New York and an Overseer of the Boston Symphony. Board member of the Visiting Nurse Service of New York for 50 years, Vice President of the Board and Chair of the Executive Committee, also former Chair of the Advisory Council for Nursing for the Department of Hospitals of NYC, the Emergency Division of the Office of Civil Defense, Vice President of the Board of Managers of the Bellevue School of Nursing and on the Advisory Council for the New York Cornell School of Nursing. Her personal life was devoted to her family and her many wonderful and loyal friends. A memorial service will be held on July 24 at 1 PM at the Church of the Holy Spirit in Orleans, MA. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Visiting Nurse Service of New York, 107 East 70 St., NY, NY 10021 and to Smith College, Office of Advancement, Alumnae House, Northampton, MA 01063.
