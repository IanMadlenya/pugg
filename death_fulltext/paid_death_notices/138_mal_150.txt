
        DAVEY-Harry E., 77. Died peacefully Sunday, on his birthday, July 12, at his home in Southbury, CT. He attended Fordham University, & was a Freight Traffic Manager from 1947-1983, for Grace Line, Prudential Lines and Delta Steamship, serving in Peru with Grace Line from 1947-1958. He also worked for Scarsdale National Bank & Bank of New York, retiring in June 1996. He is survived by his wife of 55 years, Julia Norton (Andrews) Davey, a son, Philip Norton Davey, 2 daughters Claudia Davey and Julia Linn Zirolnik, and four grandchildren. Funeral is on Wednesday, July 15, at St. Teresa's Church, Woodbury, CT. at 10 AM.
