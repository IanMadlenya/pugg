
        FREEMAN -- Richard Ed ward, 63, of Bridgehampton and New York City. After struggling with leukemia for two years, Richard departed on October 5, 2004. He leaves wife Lois, sons Elliot and Gregory, sister Lynn Schwat and husband Martin, and brother Sheldon and wife Linda. His role as husband, father, brother and uncle was an inspiration to all. Working in the industrial packing industry for 30 years, his charm and warmth surrounded his professional friends. Donations to be made to Mount Sinai Medical Center Bone Marrow Transplant Program, 19 E. 98 St., Suite 3D, attn: M. Meade, New York City, NY 10029. Memorial Service on October 12th at 5PM, Central Synagogue.
        FREEMAN--Richard. Central Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member Richard Freeman. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred Youngwood, President
