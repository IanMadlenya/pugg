
        McNIVEN-Charles. The members of the Board of Trustees, Women's Committee, staff, alumni, and more than 6,000 members of the Kips Bay Boys & Girls Club wish to extend their deep sympathy and condolences to wife, Mary, daughter, Patty, and other family members at the recent demise of Charles ''Buster'' McNiven. Mr McNiven, after graduating from Springfield College, devoted 40 years of his life to improving the quality of life of America's most disadvantaged young people. During these years, he worked for Boys Clubs in Springfield, MA.; Tonowanda, N.Y. and from 1949 to 1979 as the Executive Director of Kips Bay Boys Club now known as the Kips Bay Boys & Girls Club, Bronx, N.Y. During his tenure as Executive Director at Kips Bay, Mr. McNiven was influential in assisting the Board raise the funds to move the Clubhouse in 1969 from Manhattan to its present location in the South Bronx. From 1979 to 1998 he served as a Trustee of the organization. Thanks to his contributions as both a Professional Worker and Board member, thousands of children have a safe and positive Boys & Girls Club to attend and have been presented with opportunities to develop their life skills and become caring, productive, and responsible citizens. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Kips Bay Boys & Girls Club, 1930 Randall Ave, Bronx, N.Y. 10473.
