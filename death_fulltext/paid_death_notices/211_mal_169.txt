
        LEHMAN--Edwin. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Edwin Lehman, beloved brother of Burton Lehman, a devoted friend of the New York Jewish community. The Lehman family's commitment to Jewish education and tikkun olam has strengthened our ability to serve those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Edwin's beloved wife Helene; to Burton and his wife Brenda; to their children; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
