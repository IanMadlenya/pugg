
        SIMON-Anne, 73. On November 12th, 2000. She was a lifelong resident of Manhattan. She was retired from the YWCA, where she was employed in the Human Resources Department. She is survived by her sisters, Martha Simon and Helen Ben-Kori. A memorial service will be held on December 7, 2000, at 12 PM, at the Hooper Funeral Home, Beverly Hills, Florida.
