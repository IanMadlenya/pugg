
        BEATINI-Paul, 40. Of Park Ridge, NJ died September 11, 2001 in the World Trade Center Disaster. Husband of Susan (nee Fischer) and father of Julia and Daria. He is survived by his parents Michael and Doris, his in-laws Ed and Blanche Fischer, also Michael, Tom, Nan and Mark his brothers and sister. A memorial service will be held at the Church of the Presentation, 271 West Saddle River Road, Upper Saddle River, NJ (201)-327-1313. All are welcome to attend. The service will be held on Saturday September 22, 2001 at 10:00 A.M. In lieu of flowers, a trust fund has been established for his two children. Those wishing to donate should make checks payable to Susan Beatini, trustee and send to Susan Beatini, 240 Vitmar Place, Park Ridge, New Jersey 07656. Our prayers go out to everyone affected by this tragedy. We would also like to express our deepest appreciation to the firemen, police, rescue workers and volunteers who worked so valiantly to save the lives of others.
