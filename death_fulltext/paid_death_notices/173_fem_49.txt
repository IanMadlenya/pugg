
        MOTTOLA-Jo. On June 2, 2001 at age 81. Beloved sister of Rita, John, Jeanne, Phyllis Sartori; devoted aunt of many nieces and nephews. Jo's successful Secretarial Service resulted in her Memoir, published in 1994 by sister, Rita, highlighting Jo's association with 68 celebrities. A woman of great knowledge of history, literature, opera and film. Her large family, friends, opera singers and doctors will miss her brilliant mind, her dry wit, the sparkle in her eyes and her devotion to all who knew her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81 Street, Tuesday, 2-5 and 79PM. Funeral Mass at St. Paul the Apostle Church, 9th Avenue at 60 Street, Wednesday, 11AM. Interment Pinelawn Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cabrini Hospice, c/o Cabrini Medical Center, 227 E. 19 St, New York, N.Y. 10003.
