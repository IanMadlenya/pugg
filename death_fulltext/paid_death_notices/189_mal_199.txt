
        GUY-Edward, 88. Of Somers, NY, October 13, 2002. Beloved husband of Alice; cherished father of Joyce, Christine and Peter; devoted grandfather of Melissa, Kristopher, Kimberly and Adam. Graduate of Hamilton College and Harvard Law School. Thirty-nine year career in the Federal Reserve System, where he was Senior Vice President and General Counsel of the Federal Reserve Bank of New York. Served in the U.S. Navy as an officer of OSS in the Mediterranean during World War II. Known for his intellect, integrity and quiet tenacity. Loved for his generosity, kindness and passion for jazz and gardening. He will be in our hearts forever.
