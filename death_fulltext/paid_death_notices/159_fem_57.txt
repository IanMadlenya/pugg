
        ROMERIL-Winifred Gosman DeBevoise, 102, of Bellerose Village, N.Y. died on April 5, 2000 at her residence. Wife of the late Albert John Romeril, who died in 1989. She is survived by her two sons, Albert John Jr., of Cutchogue, N.Y. and Robert DeBevoise of Bethlehem, PA., three daughters-in-law Violet H. the Rev. Canon Gwendolyn-Jane and Audrey M. fourteen grandchildren and eight great grandchildren. She was predeceased by a son Stanley DeBevoise, who died in 1973 and a grandson Albert J. III who died in 1977. Friends may visit Sunday 4-6 PM at Thomas F. Dalton Funeral Home, 29 Atlantic Ave. (at R.R. Plaza) Floral Park. Funeral service will be held at the funeral home 1 PM Monday, Rev. Judith Stevens officating. Interment Evergreens Cemetery Bklyn, N.Y. Memorials may be made to the United Methodist Church, 35 Verbena Ave., Floral Park, N.Y. 11001.
