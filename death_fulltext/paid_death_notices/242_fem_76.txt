
        SORKIN--Rosalie, 92, of Boca Raton, Florida. Beloved wife of the late Nat, beloved mother of Ira and Joel, beloved mother-in-law of Ellen, beloved grandmother of Roger, his wife Eva and Peter, beloved great-grandmother of Alina Natalia. A wonderful and caring mother and wife whose joy of life was enhanced by her grandchildren and her great-granddaughter. A member of the choir at Community Synagogue in Port Washington, NY, and a founder with her husband, of Temple Judea in Manhasset, NY. A funeral service will be held at Mount Ararat Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY, on Sunday, March 11th at 11am. Shiva will be at the home of Ira Sorkin, 10 Vanad Drive, East Hills, NY, on Sunday, March 11 from 2pm to 9pm and Monday, March 12 from 12pm to 9pm. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Friends of the Hebrew University, 1 Battery Park Plaza, 25th floor, NY, NY 10004.
