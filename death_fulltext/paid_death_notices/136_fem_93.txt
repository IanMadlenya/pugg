
        SILVER-Esther Doner. Beloved wife of the late Milton Liszt Silver, of a heart attack on May 11, 1998. A leading social work professional in New York, and the Midwest, and a winner of the Presidential Medal of Freedom in World War II, she was ninety-one. Survived by her sister, Margaret Buckingham of Napa, CA, her stepchildren Jeanne Silver Frankl of Amagansett, NY, David Anthony Silver of Los Angeles, CA., and son-in-law Kenneth R. Frankl, three granddaughters, a great-grandson, two nieces, five grand nephews and two grand nieces. Memorial on Saturday, 10 AM at Sleepy Hollow Cemetery, Tarrytown, New York.
