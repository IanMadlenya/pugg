
        NACHTIGALL - Walter. The Friends of Bezalel Academy of Arts and Design deeply mourn the passing of Walter Nachtigall, beloved brother of our esteemed Board Member and colleague, Gertrud Nachtigall Kissiloff. We extend our deepest condolences to her, her husband Bill, their son Ari and the entire family. Romie Shapiro, Chairman of the Board Harry Bauer, President Eillene Leistner, Executive V.P.
