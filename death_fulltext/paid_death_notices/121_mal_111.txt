
        KOLB-Philip A., 61. Of Oceanside, L.I. Passed away peacefully at Calvary Hospital on February 7, 1997. He is survived by his devoted wife of 36 years, Barbara, loving daughter Jane Gamble and son-inlaw Edward, of Carol Stream, Ill., son Thomas of Manhattan and granddaughter, Breana. Phil will be remembered for his loving ways and for the way he and his wife, Barbara, met life together. Services 12:30 PM, Sunday, Feb. 9 at ''Boulevard-Riverside Chapels'', 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, L.I.
