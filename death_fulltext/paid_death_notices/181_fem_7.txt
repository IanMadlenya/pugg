
        STOLLER-Jeanne Hart. Passed away in her sleep January 31, at age 95. Noted fashion director of Altman, Stoller, Weiss Advertising. Beloved wife of the late Max Stoller. Loved dearly by her daughter Mara Kurtz, granddaughter Sasha, son-in-law John, and niece Dolores. Her exceptional style and passionate spirit will be greatly missed. Funeral Sunday, 10:00 AM, ''The Riverside'', Amsterdam Avenue at 76th Street.
