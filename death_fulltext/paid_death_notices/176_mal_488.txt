
        NOLAND - Aaron, 81. Distin guished Professor Emeritus of History at CUNY. Charismatic teacher and author of The Founding of the French Socialist Party and many articles. He is survived by his cherished daughter, Carrie, his beloved grandchildren, Julian and Francesca, his devoted nephew Jonathan and great niece Jenna, and his sisters, Sarah, Sylvia and Hilda.
