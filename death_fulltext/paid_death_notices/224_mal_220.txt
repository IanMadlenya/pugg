
        WIESENTHAL--Simon. The Anti - Defamation League mourns the passing of Simon Wiesenthal, a hero in the fight to bring justice to the millions of Holocaust victims and an impassioned voice of conscience against anti-Semitism and all forms of prejudice. A 1988 recipient of ADL's Jabotinsky Award for ''courageous leadership,'' his life, which epitomized the Biblical injunction, ''Remember, do not forget,'' is his legacy to all of us. Barbara B. Balser National Chair Abraham H. Foxman National Director
        $(6$)WIESENTHAL--Simon. From the end of World War II inMay, 1945 to today you have spent every waking moment to the pursuit of justice for the six million Jews and countless millions of others who were murdered by the Nazis. For decades you, and you alone, pursued this search and kept it alive for the world to know. You can rest in peace ''dear brother''. Your work was well done. Sincerest condolences to Paulinka, Gerald and the entire family. Martin and Joan Rosen and family
