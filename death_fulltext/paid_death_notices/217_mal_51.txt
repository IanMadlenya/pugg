
        LOSEY--Robert Lee, 59, of Manhattan passed away February 2, 2005 after a lengthy battle with Alzheimer's. After graduating from Princeton University (1967) and the University of Virginia Law School (1970), he began his professional career at Saterlee, Stephens, Burke & Burke. He then moved on to work in the legal departments of NL Industries and Revlon, finishing his career as General Counsel of Marvel Entertainment. He is survived by his daughters Annie Losey Teillon and Katie Losey, his friend and former wife Carol, his son-in-law Geoffrey and sister Betty Weik. He was an avid birdwatcher, tennis player, traveler, photographer and outdoorsman. The family wishes to thank Ethel and Patricia for their love, care and support. A memorial Service will be held on Saturday February 5 at 3pm at Quaker Friends Meeting House, Chappaqua, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in our father's honor to the Alzheimer's Association.
