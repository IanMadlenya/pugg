
        ABRAHAMSON-William Fraser, of Cortlandt Manor, NY. Bill was a victim of the WTC senseless tragedy. Beloved husband of Ann. Cherished father of Erik. All who knew him have lost a friend with a great enthusiasm for life, and an unbridled love for all people. A memorial service will be at the Holy Name of Mary RC Church, 110 Grand St., Croton-on-Hudson, NY, at 10AM Saturday, Oct. 13. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the William F. Abrahamson Memorial Fund, C/O McCarthy, Fingar et al., 11 Martine Avenue, White Plains, NY 10606.
