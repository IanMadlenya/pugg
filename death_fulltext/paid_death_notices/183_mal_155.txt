
        CALLEA-Joseph. Died on April 11th, 2002 after a short illness. He is survived by his loving wife, Janet, and his 3 beloved children, Kimberly and twins Joseph and Anthony, as well as his parents and four brothers and sisters. Mr. Callea was a partner of the accounting firm of Joel Popkin and Company in NYC. In lieu of flowers, a fund is being established for the education of his three children. Contributions can be made to the Callea Children's Trust and mailed to 1-34 29th Street, Fair Lawn, New Jersey 07410.
