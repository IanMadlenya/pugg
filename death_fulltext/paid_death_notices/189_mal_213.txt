
        ZELLER-Fred. Died with dignity on October 16th, 2002. Beloved husband of Bertha. Devoted father of Jane, Alice and Rita and father-in-law of Kenneth and Michael. Adoring and proud grandfather of Michael, Rachel, Aaron and Jason. He served as Vice President of J.B. Lippincott and President of Churchill Livingston, Inc. After his retirement, he began a long tenure as President of the Colostomy Society of NY. His memory will be carried on by all of us-the friends and family he left behind. Services will be held on Friday, October 18th, at 11:30 AM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76th St., NYC. Donation in Fred's honor can be made to Colostomy Society of NY, Box 517, G.P.O., NYC 10116 and the Rogosin Institute of New York.
