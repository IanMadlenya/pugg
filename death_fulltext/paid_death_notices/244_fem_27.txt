
        HOLLAND--J. William. The Jewish Theological Seminary expresses its heartfelt condolences to Louise Holland and her family on the loss of her beloved husband, J. William Holland. Generous benefactors to both the Jewish and general communities, Bill and Louise represent a second generation of the Abbell family to support JTS for more than half a century. May God comfort them in their grief. Arnold Eisen, Chancellor-elect Gershon Kekst, Board Chairman
