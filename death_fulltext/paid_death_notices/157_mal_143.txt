
        YAMAGUCHI-William E. Sr, died at home in Larchmont, NY on February 10, 2000 at the age of 75. Following a long career with Time-Life, he set up a consulting practice, bridging the Japanese and American business cultures. Survived by his wife Kim; his children Ellen, Chip and Susan; and three grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 2 PM, Sunday, February 13th at the Larchmont Avenue Church.
