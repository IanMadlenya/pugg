
        NORTON-William S. The AllenStevenson School records with great sadness the death of Dr. William S. Norton, an alumnus from the Class of 1926 and father of Thomas Norton, Class of 1958. Dr. Norton served actively as School Physician and on the Board of Allen-Stevenson from 1947-1972, after which time he was elected Trustee Emeritus. To his wife, Priscilla Waters Norton, and his entire family, we offer our deepest sympathy. Ronald S. Rolfe '60, President of the Board David R. Trower, Headmaster
