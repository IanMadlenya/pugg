
        ZIMRING--Neil Howard. 68, son of Charles Zimring and Barbara Loring was born on August 31, 1936, in New York. He received his bachelor's degree from Georgetown University in 1958 and served as a sergeant with the Military Police in the U.S. Army. Mr. Zimring was a managing director and partner with Dietz International Public Adjusters, Inc., from 1972 to 1987. Subsequently, he was a founding partner of Horizon Public Adjusters Group, Inc. Mr. Zimring is survived by his beloved wife, Sylvia; his son Blake; his son Joshua; his daughter Stacy; his sister Lynn Loring of Beverly Hills; and his Boston Terrier, Sonic.
