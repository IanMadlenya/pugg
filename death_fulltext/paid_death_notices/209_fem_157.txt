
        LAT--Charlene Everette Garcia, 24, of New York City and Saddle River, NJ, entered into eternal rest on Friday, June 18, 2004. Charlene was a 1997 graduate of Phillips Exeter Academy, where she was an early inductee to the Cum Laude Society, and a 2001 graduate of Brown University, where she received her A.B. in Slavic Studies and was the editor-in-chief of the prestigious Brown Journal of World Affairs. Friends and family may pay their respects on both Thursday and Friday from 4:00-9:00 PM at the C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, 306 E. Ridgewood Ave., Ridgewood, NJ 07450. A funeral mass celebrating Charlene's life will be held on Saturday, June 26th at 10:00 AM at the St. Gabriel's RC Church, 88 East Saddle River Rd. in Saddle River, NJ. Interment to follow at the Ascension Cemetery in Monsey, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations in Charlene's memory may be made to the Juvenile Bipolar Research Foundation, 788 Morris Turnpike, Short Hills, NJ 07078 or the UPMASA-PEF. For information C.C. Van Emburgh Funeral Home, Ridgewood. www.vanemburgh.com
