
        STEINHOLTZ-Lester, Dr. Died at his home in DeWitt, NY. He was born July 31, 1922, in New York City where he graduated from City College and NYU's College of Dentistry. His contributions over the years to the professions of oral surgery and dentistry as clinician, professor, mentor of young dentists, thinker and writer were numerous. He became Chief of the Division of Dentistry, Department of Surgery at SUNY in Syracuse and later Professor Emeritus in 1996 when he retired. He will be greatly missed by Eleanor, his wife of 53 years; as well as his daughter Ruth.
