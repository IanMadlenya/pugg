
        STEVENS--William Thomas of Lido Beach, NY on January 18, 2005 at 84. Born September 3, 1920 in New York City to Irving T. Stevens and Mary Coughlin. A 1936 graduate of Xavier High School in Manhattan and a 1940 graduate of the College of the Holy Cross in Worcester, MA. Graduated Columbia University School of Law in 1943. WWII veteran, served in the United States Army from 1942-1946. A wellrespected attorney in private practice and a member of the New York Bar Association for 62 years. Husband of Louise McDonnell Stevens and extraordinary father of seven children, William T. Stevens, Jr., Nancy Coffey, Mary Rafferty, Jean Brennan, Elizabeth Murdy, Thomas Stevens and Kathryn McCormack. Devoted grandfather of 15 grandchildren and one great-grandchild. He will be remembered by many for his wit, charm and intellectual curiosity, but above all for his genuine interest in 'all creatures great and small'. Visitation Thursday at Macken Mortuary, 52 Clinton Avenue, Rockville Center. Funeral Mass Friday, 11 A.M., Our Lady of Miraculous Medal, Point Lookout. Interment Calvary Cemetery, Queens.
