
        SHAPIRO--Lillian Watman, After a long illness, Lillian Watman Shapiro passed away on the evening of November 20, 2006. Mrs. Shapiro was born on February 6, 1915 in Brooklyn, New York. She was the daughter of Anna Salzman Watman and Harry Watman. Her husband George Shapiro and her brother Irving Watman predeceased her. Lillian attended Brooklyn College and graduated in 1936 with a teaching degree. She and her husband resided both in Long Beach and Manhattan and in recent years only Manhattan. She was a benefactor to the Museum of Natural History and New York Presbyterian Hospital. Lillian was completely devoted to her family and friends and is survived by nieces and nephews. Everyone will miss her.
