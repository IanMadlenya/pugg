
        CASSUTO-Abe. The Sephardic Temple of Cedarhurst, NY records with deep regret the passing of Abe Cassuto, a Vice President of the Temple. A Founder of the Congregation, he was a very proud Jew committed to the Sephardic tradition. Condolences to his wife Bella and the entire family. May they be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Arnold B. Marans Hazzan David Abikzer Joseph Camhi, President Isaac J. Russo, Chairman Estelle Ezratty, Sist. Pres.
