
        SIBEN--Eileen (nee Letteau). Beloved wife of Stephen, adored mother of Sharon Morgenstern and Betsy Harris. Dear grandmother of Allison and Jeremy Morgenstern. Loving daughter of the late Jeanne and Maurice Letteau. Daughter-in-law of the late Sidney and Stella Siben. Longtime member of The Bay Shore Jewish Center, supporter of the Sisterhood of The Bay Shore Jewish Center, past president of The Bay Shore Chapter of Hadassah, president of Jem Estates of Brightwaters New York. Services Tuesday 10AM at the Bay Shore Jewish Center. Donations, in her memory, to Bay Shore Jewish Center or Suffolk County Chapter of Hadassah.
