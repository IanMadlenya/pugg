
        SIMONS-Betsy of Franklin Lakes, NJ, passed away peacefully on Sept. 28, 1998 at NYU Medical Center, after a courageous & dignified fight with cancer. Beloved wife of Jeffrey and a devoted and loving mother of Andrew and Jennifer. Mrs. Simons graduated Magna Cum Laude from William Paterson University in 1995. She was a graduate student, a student teacher, & an active founding member of Temple Beth Rishon. Her unwavering compassionate support of her family and friends earn her a special place in all of our hearts. In her own words she said, I have no regrets no woulda's, shoulda's, coulda's, every day take a step toward making your dream a reality, no road blocks, just stepping blocks. Services are on Thursday, 12 Noon at the Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, 150 Route 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to The American Cancer Society.
