
        GLASSMAN-Dr. Ephraim. The Officers, Board of Governors, Members and Staff of The Muttontown Club mourn the passing of our beloved member Dr. Ephraim Glassman, and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Roberta, his sons Lawrence and Edward, daughter Karen and their families. Russell Leavitt, President
        GLASSMAN-Ephraim. The Division of Cardiothoracic Surgery of NYU School of Medicine mourns the passing of Ephraim Glassman, M.D., our longtime teacher, colleague and friend. Dr. Glassman was a former Professor of Medicine and Director of the Cardiac Catheterization Laboratory at NYU School of Medicine. His advice was always sought by many on cardiovascular cases. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Roberta, his children, Larry and Jessica, Edward and Michelle, and Karen, and to his grandchildren. Division of Cardiothoracic Surgery NYU School of Medicine
