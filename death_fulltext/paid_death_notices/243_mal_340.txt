
        RUSH--Burton Carlin, 89, native of Brooklyn and resident of Briar Cliff and Ossining, NY, died March 22. He is survived by his beloved wife, Betsy; four children, Peter, Timothy, Barney and Lucy; and six grandchildren. A rememberance will be held May 13 at The Writers' Center, Sleepy Hollow, NY, from 12-2pm. All friends welcome.
