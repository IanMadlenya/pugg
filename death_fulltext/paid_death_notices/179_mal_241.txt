
        BARRIE-William H. Passed away Dec. 19, 2001, of Boca Raton, FL, formerly of Kew Gardens. Loving husband of Lee to whom he was married 56 years. A pioneer in the beauty industry. Adored father of Ellen Elias and Marsha and David Schwarz. Cherished grandfather of Benjamin and Nina Elias, Nicole and Malcolm Seawell, Elan, Adam & Ana Schwarz. Beloved brother of George and Milton Barrie. Services Friday, December 21, 2001, at the Gutterman-Warheit Memorial Chapel, Boca Raton. For information: 1-800-992-9262.
