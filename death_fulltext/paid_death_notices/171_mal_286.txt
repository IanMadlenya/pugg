
        MANES-Chester. Of Chester, New York, a retired engineer for the New York City Board of Education and a past editor of the political commentary publication ''Contemporary Issues'', died February 23rd. He was a graduate of Townsend Harris H.S. and attended City College until enlisting in W.W. II. After the war, he functioned as a union organizer, then as an engineer for the NY Central Railroad prior to his employment with the Board of Education, where he was a member of the International Union of Operating Engineers. He is survived by his wife Violet and predeceased by a brother, Lt. Col. Eugene Manes.
