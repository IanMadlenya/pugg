
        WAXMAN-Joseph. The Board of Trustees and staff of the New York Chapter of the Crohn's and Colitis Foundation of America record with sorrow the passing of Joseph Waxman on July 27, 1997. He was the father of our esteemed Director of Development Joan Telley. Our sincere condolences to his beloved wife Elsie, his devoted daughters Joan Telley and Susan Tinkler, and to his loving grandchildren Marc and Stacy Tinkler. Dan Crown, Chairman Judy Saphir, President Edith Samers, Executive Director
