
        GERBER--Marion, 82, died on Wednesday, December 10 at her home, in Hanover, NH. She is survived by her daughter, Joanne Gerber, and three grandchildren. In WW II, she was a writer, researcher, and announcer for U.S. government radio programs broadcast to Latin America. After raising three children, she became a bilingual Spanish teacher in New York City. Funeral services were held last Sunday in Hanover.
