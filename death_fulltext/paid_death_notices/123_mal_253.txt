
        SUTTER-Meyer, Monday, April 21st, in his 84th year. Pre-deceased by his parents, Louis and Regina Slutnick, his sisters Tessie Gottlieb and Rose Lissauer, and his beloved wife of 50 years, Beatrice Weinberger Sutter. He leaves a son, Laurence, of Manhattan, neices Harriet Lituchy and Reese Feldman, nephew Robert Gottlieb, and many grand-and-great-grand neices and nephews. Services will be Thursday at 11 A.M. at Nassau North Chapel, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. Contributions may be made to the National Audubon Society and the League of Parker Jewish Geriatric Institute.
