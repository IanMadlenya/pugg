
        FRIEDMAN-Milton, age ninetythree, real estate lawyer, scholar and author, September 16, at home, suddenly. Beloved husband of the late Dorothy Guiterman Friedman, loving father of Alan, father-in-law of Carolyn and devoted grandfather of Wendy, Kathryn and Amy. Interment will be private. A memorial service will be held at a future date.
        FRIEDMAN-Milton R. The Trustees of The Practising Law Institute deeply regret the death of Milton Friedman, author of a major treatise on real estate leases as well as longstanding program Chairman. We extend our deepest sympathy to his family. Barry H. Garfinkel, Chairman Werner L. Polak, President
