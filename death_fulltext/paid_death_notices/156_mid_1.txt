
        HIBBERD-Frederick H. The Officers, Trustees and members of American Yacht Club are deeply saddened by the loss of a distinguished leader and dear friend, Commodore Frederick H. Hibberd, who passed away on January 2, 2000. His leadership during the difficult years of WWII preserved the Club that we enjoy today. We extend our deepest sympathies to Lorna and her family. William P. Ketcham, Commodore, American Yacht Club
