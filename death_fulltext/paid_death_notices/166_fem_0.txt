
        ADELMAN-Renee. Of Lawrence, N.Y. and Boca Raton, FL. Passed away tragically and suddenly on Monday, October 30, 2000. Devoted wife of Seymour, beloved mother of Marcia A. Delman and Debra Buckley. Adored grandmother of Ariel and Ross Buckley, who gave Renee so much joy, pride and pleasure during her life. She is also survived by her brother, Leonard Kleiman & his family. She will always be kept in our hearts, always remembered for her joyful spirit, vitality, kindness, music-making and delightful wit. Her loving memory lives in our dreams and our lives. Forever in our hearts, we will miss her more with each passing day.
