
        RAPFOGEL - Regina. Friends and staff of Metropolitan Council on Jewish Poverty mourn the passing of Regina Rapfogel, mother of our dear friend and leader, CEO William Rapfogel, and Freda Fried. Beloved wife of the late Hyman Rapfogel; mother-in-law of Judy Rapfogel and Matthew Fried; grandmother of Michael, Chaim and Moshe Rapfogel, and Abraham and Kenneth Fried. She served many great causes with dedication and compassion, most recently at United Jewish Council of the East Side, and will be greatly missed. Joseph C. Shenker, President Merryl H. Tisch, Chairman Rabbi David Cohen, Sr. Exec.
