
        RUBINGER-Susan Ellen. On April 9, 2001, after a prolonged and valiant struggle. Beloved daughter of Nancy & Bob. Dear sister of David and Hedy. The world has lost a shining star. Services Thursday, April 12, 11AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
        RUBINGER-Susan Ellen. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses profound sympathy to the family of Susan Ellen Rubinger. Taken from this life much too soon, our hearts reach out with compassion and understanding. May her beloved parents and family find comfort in the sweetness of memories from a life that was beautiful but much too brief. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        RUBINGER-Susan E. NCJW New York Section mourns the loss of Susie Rubinger, daughter of Past President Nancy Rubinger. Her volunteer efforts on behalf of this organization brought smiles and joy to many. Our sincerest sympathy to her loving family. Rita Fishman, President Helen C. Heller, Exec. Dir.
