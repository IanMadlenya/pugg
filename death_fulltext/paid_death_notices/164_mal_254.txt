
        GRONNINGSATER - Arne. The trustees, faculty and administration of Riverdale Country School extend their deepest sympathies to the children of Arne Gronningsater, who passed away on August 30, in Westport, NY. Beloved teacher and friend to generations of students, his spirit remains at Riverdale Country School. Memorial service, Saturday, September 23, 2:00 PM, All Souls Unitarian Church, 1157 Lexington Ave., NYC.
