
        GAW-Archibald W. (Archie). Of Katonah, NY, formerly of the Bronx, NY, died October 19, 2001. He was 90 years old. Archie worked for Mobil Oil Corporation in New york, NY, for over 30 years and was an active member of the Katonah United Methodist Church. He is survived by his brother Donald and many nieces and nephews. Visiting hours will be Monday, 11-1, at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, with a service 1:30 PM, at the Katonah United Methodist Church. Interment Amawalk Hill Cemetery, Amawalk, NY.
