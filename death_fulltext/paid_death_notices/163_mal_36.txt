
        ROEDER-Martin A. On August 3, 2000. Cherished husband of Dorothy (Friedman) for 61 years, adored father of Katherine Roeder, Thomas and Susan Roeder, beloved grandfather of Sarah and Louisa Roeder, Kenneth Hartog and Erik Hartog & Pamela Heller. Son of the late Judge Jehial M. Roeder and the late Evelyn Finegold Roeder. Mr. Roeder was Of Counsel to Herzfeld and Rubin and former managing partner of Guggenheimer and Untermeyer. He had a degree from the Sorbonne in Paris, a BA and LLB from Columbia University and Columbia Law School of New York. He served on the boards of the Emanu-el Brotherbood and the Tax Management Institute of New York University. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Monday 7-9PM with services Tuesday 11:30AM. Interment private. In lieu of flowers contributions to The Jewish Repertory Theater of the 92nd St. YM-YWHA, 1395 Lexington Avenue, New York, NY 10128.
        ROEDER-Martin A. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member Martin A. Roeder. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
