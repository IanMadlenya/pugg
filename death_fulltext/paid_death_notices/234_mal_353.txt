
        EHRLICH - Joel. Died at home in Denver on July 28 after a year long battle with pancreatic cancer. Joel was a principal of Kessler Ehrlich Investments, an independent film producer, Chairman of the Denver ACLU, member of the Boards of the Rose Foundation, Colorado Film Commission and Boulder Jewish Community Foundation. His passion for righteousness and good deeds will be remembered. He is survived by his loving wife, Priscilla Press, devoted sister and brother-in-law, Nancy and Bob Novack and adoring nephews, Jeffrey and Daniel Novack. Funeral services at Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vaux Hall Rd, Union, NJ, on July 31, 2006 at 11:30 a.m.
