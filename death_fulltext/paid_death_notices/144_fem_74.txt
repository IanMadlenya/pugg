
        MARSHALL-Sylvia Hitch. On January 7, 1999 in her 93rd year at Hilton Head Island, South Carolina. Widow of Lawrence Clark Marshall, retired Vice Chairman of the Chase Manhattan Bank. She is survived by her sons, Allerton Delano Marshall of Hilton Head Island and David Lawrence Marshall of Green Valley, Ca; her daughter, Sylvia Marshall Alkema of San Diego, Ca; eight grandchildren and ten great-grandchildren.
