
        FREED -- Sara Ann. The Board of Directors, staff and members of the Caring Community note with much sadness the death of its beloved Board Member Sara Ann Freed. Our hearts go out to her husband, Ira Weissman, other members of her family, and her numerous friends and colleagues. We will miss her wisdom and optimism and her determination to improve the quality of life for the elderly in Greenwich Village. Eleanore Z.
