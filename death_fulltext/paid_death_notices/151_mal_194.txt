
        VAN KIRK-W. Philip, attorney, of Rye, NY and Williamsville, VT, died at home on August 15, age 79. A graduate of Columbia Law, he was managing partner at Burns, Van Kirk, Greene and Kafer in New York and, recently, of counsel to Meighan and Necarsulmer in Mamaroneck. Married to Dorothy Keane for 52 years, he is survived by his wife; three children, Leslie Clark, Philip, Jr. and Geoffrey; and four grandchildren. During WW II, he served as Captain, Army Intelligence, for the 412th Fighter Squadron. A private family service is planned. Gifts in his memory may be made to the Hospice of Westchester, 95 So. Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601-4403.
