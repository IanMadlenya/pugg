
        STAMPLEMAN-Henry. Of New Rochelle, NY on May 22, 2002 at the age of 86 after a short illness. Marketing consultant to Coca-Cola, Pepsi-Cola, General Foods and US Steel, among others. Founding member of Larchmont Temple. Beloved by his wife Doris, sons Jed, Paul and Steven, daughtersin-law Laura and Fern, grandchildren Luc, Lesley, Hannah and James (and his wife Elizabeth) and great-grandchildren Sean and Sophia. Services, Sunday, May 26, 11a.m. at Larchmont Temple, 75 Larchmont Ave., Larchmont, NY.
