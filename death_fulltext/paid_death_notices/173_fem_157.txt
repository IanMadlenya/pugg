
        MOSES-Judith. 61, died at home on June 16. Survived by husband Harry; daughter Anne, son-in-law Ray Buffalo, and granddaughter Violet of San Francisco; and mother Elaine Weil of Philadelphia. Funeral private. There will be a memorial service to be announced later. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Committee to Protect Journalists in New York.
        MOSES-Judith. The Board and staff of the Committee to Protect Journalists mourns the passing of our friend and colleague. We extend our deepest sympathies to her family. As a member of CPJ's Board of Directors, Judith deployed her creative energies and deep knowledge of international affairs to defend journalists around the world. She will be profoundly missed.
