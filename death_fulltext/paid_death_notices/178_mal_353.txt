
        VALLAS-George. On November 23, 2001. Passed at 62 years. Beloved husband of Monica. Cherished father of Elizabeth and Gregory Kreder and Nicholas Vallas. Survived by two grandchildren, Eric and Lauren. Devoted brother of Venus Dimou, Bess and John Papandon. Uncle and dear friend to many. Graduate of NYU School of Engineering with a Master's degree in Industrial Engineering. Founder and President of C.L. International. He built a successful consulting practice deliverying projects around the world. He was deeply loved and will be missed by family, friends and associates. Funeral Tuesday, 10:30 AM from the Litras Funeral Home (Arlington Chapels) 83-15 Parsons Blvd., Jamaica (at Grand Central Parkway). Funeral Liturgy 11:30 AM at St. Demetrios G.O. Church, 84-35 152 St., Jamaica. Interment Flushing Cemetery. Visiting hours Sunday and Monday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to the Salvation Army.
