
        MINSKY - Ruth. The Village Temple mourns the passing of one of our long-time members, Mrs. Ruth Minsky, 95 years old. With her husband, Morton, a Founding Member of the Temple, and one of the famous Minsky Brothers, Ruth was an active and beloved congregant. She will be remembered for her vibrance and elegance. Funeral Monday June 29th at The Village Temple, 33 East 12th Street, NYC, at 12 noon. Rabbi Dennis N. Math Joan Brancaccio, Sheryl Harawitz, Co-Presidents
