
        HORNSTEIN - Lillian Herlands, Professor Emerita of English, Washington Square College, New York University died on April 10, 2000 at St. Lukes-Roosevelt Hospital. Beloved wife of the late George D. Hornstein, Professor of Corporation Law, NYU. She was predeceased by her brothers, Hon. William B. Herlands (Federal Judge, Southern District of New York) and Hon. Herbert S. Herlands (Superior Court, Orange County California). She is survived by her brother, Dr. Robert E. Herlands of Stamford, CT, sisters-inlaw, three generations of adoring nieces and nephews and a legion of appreciative students. Prof. Hornstein was a distinguished scholar, teacher and renowned literary critic of Medieval Literature during a 50 year career at NYU. Her rare talents as educator, researcher, author and administrator were recognized by NYU in conferring their Distinguished Teacher Award and Presidential Citation of Achievement, among other numerous accolades. Funeral service will be at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue, New York, Wednesday, April 12, 2000, at 1P.M.
