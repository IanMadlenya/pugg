        $(6$)GROSSMAN--Beatrice (Bunny). A woman of grace and kindness, celebrated by her many friends and adored by her family, has left us in her 90th year and the world is less for her passing. Beloved wife for over 60 years of the late Sidney Grossman, devoted mother to Gary and Lynn, Judy and Steven and Nancy and Shoshana, adored grandmother of Robert and Amy, Andrew and Alyson, Michael and Meg, and Daniel, and proud great-grandmother of Adam, Julie, Lauren, Sydney, Cole, Maura Kate, Meryl and Greta. Bunny will be missed at the mah jongg table, in the halls of Middle Bay, at the Fountains in Florida, by the pool at 1390 Broadway, and by all those whose life she has touched. Services Tuesday October 17th at 11:30am, The Boulevard - Riverside Chapel, 1450 Broadway, Hewlett, NY.
