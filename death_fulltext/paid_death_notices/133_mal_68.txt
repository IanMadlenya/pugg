
        STEVENS-Roger Lacey. On Monday, February 2nd, 1998, of Washington, D.C. Beloved husband of Christine Stevens; father of Christabel Gough; Brother-inLaw of Nina Stevens; also survived by a nephew, Harlow Stevens, Jr. Services private. A memorial service at the John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts will be announced at a later date. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The Fund for New American Plays, John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Washington, D.C. 20566 or The Animal Welfare Institute, PO Box 3650, Washington, D.C. 20007. For information contact Joseph Gawler's Sons, Inc.
        STEVENS-Roger L. The Board of Directors and staff of the National Book Foundation extend deepest sympathies to the family of Roger L. Stevens. He was one of the Founding Directors of the National Book Awards in 1950. He served with generosity and distinction on the Board of the National Book Foundation for seven years. He was a devoted reader and supporter of literature. His expansive presence and sagacious advice will be deeply missed by all of us. Neil Baldwin, Executive Director
        STEVENS-Roger L. The Members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and friend, Roger L. Stevens, and send condolences to his family.
