
        LITTO-Sara Schein. Passed away at home on September 18, 2002. Adored, respected mother and best friend of Marilyn Sue Goodman. Cherished and devoted daughter of the late Louis and Mary Schein. Treasured sister of the late Samuel Bud Schein, Bernard Schein and Selwyn Schein. Caring sister-in-law of Ruth Schein. Loving aunt of Robert (Isabel), Ilyse and Jessica Schein and the late Eric Schein. Loving almost-sister of Annette Taitt, beloved friend, respected colleague, retired New York City School teacher. Her character, compassion and courage will guide us always. Sorely, sorely missed, she will live forever in our hearts.
