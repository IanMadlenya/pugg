
        GERSTEIN -- Leonard B. Passed away August 25, 2003. Of Boca Raton, formerly of Merrick, Long Island. Loving husband of Esther to whom he was married for 57 years. Adored father of Laurie (Scott) Milim and Lee (Joy Covey) Gerstein. Cherished grandfather of Alicia, Evan, Isaac and Leila. Beloved brother of Marilyn Silverstein. Entombment was in Boca Raton, Florida.
