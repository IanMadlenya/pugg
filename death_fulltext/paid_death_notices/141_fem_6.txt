
        SCHWABER-Bess. Fifth Avenue Synagogue records with profound sorrow the passing of its esteemed member, and extends heartfelt sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Sol Roth, Rabbi, Hermann Merkin, Founding Pres., David Yagoda, Chairman, Dr. William Schwartz, President,
        SCHWABER-Betty. On Tuesday, September 29, of New York. Beloved wife of the late David. Devoted mother of Drs. Jules R. Schwaber and Evelyne Albrecht Schwaber of Brookline, MA and Drs. Paul Schwaber & Rosemary Balsam-Schwaber of Hamden, CT. Cherished grandmother of five and great grandmother of two. Dear sister of the late Benjamin Golding and Frances Leftow. Graveside services at Knollwood Park, Ridgewood, on Thursday, October 1, at 1:00PM. Shiva through Sunday noon at her late residence. Remembrances to Fifth Avenue Synagogue, 5 East 62nd Street, New York 10021. For information call Levine Chapel, Brookline, MA. 617-277-8300.
