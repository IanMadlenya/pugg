
        STEBE-Peter F., of Flushing, NY, on April 21, 1999. Professor of Mathematics at the City College of New York. His wit, humor, love and friendship will be remembered always by his family and life-long friends. Son of John and Mary (nee Byrne); brother of Joan and Nicholas. Husband of Joan (nee Kaletchitz), father of John, Julia, Kathleen and Nicholas, father-in-law to Ivetta, Christian, and Joseph, grandfather of Sara, Christiana, and Alexander. Born in Brooklyn, NY, on November 27, 1934. He received his B.S. from Queens College, his M.S. from NYU and his Ph.D. in Mathematics from the Courant Institute with Wilhelm Magnus, specializing in group theory. He also received a Ph.D. in Economics from CUNY. He taught throughout the CUNY system and enjoyed his bright students immensely. Prior to joining the faculty at CCNY, he served in the Army as a 1st Lieutenant and worked at RCA and the Institute for Defense Analysis. Visitation will be Sunday, April 25, from 2-5 and 7-9 p.m. at Hillebrand Funeral Home, 63-17 Woodhaven Blvd. A funeral mass will be held Monday, April 26, at 9:45 a.m. at Saint Margaret's RC Church, 80 St. & Juniper Valley Rd., Middle Village, followed by interment at Calverton Cemetery. Donations in his memory can be made to the American Mathematical Society.
