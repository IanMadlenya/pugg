
        McDONALD-Kathryn Austin, on May 16, peacefully at Mount Sinai Hospital at age 82. Loving mother of K. Ann McDonald, dear sister of Malcolm Patrick Austin, and adored wife of the late Robert J. McDonald. A former attorney with the Legal Aid Society's Juvenile Rights Division, Family Court Judge and Administrator of the New York City Family Court, Kay McDonald devoted her professional life to helping poor children and their families. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St., on Friday, May 19, 2-5 PM, Saturday, May 20, 2-5 and 7-9 PM, and Monday, May 22, 7-9 PM. Mass of Christian Burial will be held at St. Vincent Ferrer Church, 66th St. and Lexington Ave., on Tuesday, May 23, 10 AM. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Legal Aid Society, Columbia Law School, or New Yorkers for Children.
        McDONALD-Kay. The Board, Staff and Advocacy Council of Citizen's Committee for Children, mourns the death of Kay McDonald. Her relentless advocacy for children and families in trouble has been a model for the work of CCC for four decades. We will continue to honor her life and her committment to realizing the true welfare of children.
