
        HIRSCHFELD-Al. We deeply mourn the loss of Al Hirschfeld, a New York City landmark in his own time, who supported, in wonderfully artistic lines and otherwise, our continuing efforts to preserve the architectural integrity, human scale and quality of life in Carnegie Hill. Our condolences to Louise, who together with her husband, has been a member of our Steering Committee. Citineighbors Coalition of Historic Carnegie Hill Jurate Kazickas Carol McFadden Jane Parshall Co-chairmen
        HIRSCHFELD-Al. We are deeply saddened by the loss of Al Hirschfeld, a great artist, great person, and true believer in art. We send our love and gratitude for all he has done for the Pillow, for dance, and the arts. Ella Baff, Executive Director The Board of Directors Jacob's Pillow Dance Festival
        HIRSCHFELD-Al. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member Friar Al Hirschfeld on January 20, 2003. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Executive Director
        HIRSCHFELD-Al. Spanning four editors and fifty years, his drawings bring vivid life to the pages of The Best Plays. A valued collaborator of great charm and sparkling wit, he will be sorely missed by us all. We mourn his passing and extend deepest sympathy to his family. Jeffrey Eric Jenkins, Editor
        HIRSCHFELD - Al. The Museum of the City of New York mourns the passing of Al Hirschfeld, recipient of the $24 Award and an ''Our Town Treasure.'' He both embodied and captured the spirt of NY. Newton P.S. Merrill, Chairman
