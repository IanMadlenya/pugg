
        ESSERMAN-Pauline. 101 years. Former resident of Forest Hills, died Saturday, June 2. Survived by her niece Judith Feuer and her nephew William Rosenberg, five great-nephews, one great-niece and several cousins. Beloved wife of the late Frank Esserman. Services Monday, 11 AM, at Schwartz Bros-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Boulevard at 76th Road, Forest Hills.
