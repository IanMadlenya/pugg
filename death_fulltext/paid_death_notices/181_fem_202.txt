
        HANSEN-Ruth Catherine Unger. A native of New York, wife of the late Nelson Hansen, died in Cabrini Medical Center, February 18, 2002. Born November 2, 1911, daughter of the late George and Margaret Mulligan Unger. A member of the administrative staff of Bellevue Hospital for more than 40 years. After she retired, she enrolled at the College of New Rochelle and received her Bachelor of Arts degree at age 70. She is survived by her cousin, Kathleen M. Ross of Westin, CT. A mass of Christian burial will be held on Friday, February 22nd at 11 AM in Church of the Epiphany, Manhattan. Interment Calvary Cemetery, Long Island City, New York.
