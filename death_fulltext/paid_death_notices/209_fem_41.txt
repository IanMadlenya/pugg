
        RAPOPORT--Robyrta J. Age 71 of New York City on June 6, 2004. Beloved wife of Bernard and devoted mother of Michael and his dear wife Deborah and their four lovely children Rebecca, Abigail, Serena and Solomon Jonathan. Robbie had a long and outstanding career as Vice President for Fashion of Simplicity Pattern Co. She endured her health problems of the last 12 years with remarkable good cheer and a love of life that allowed her to enjoy her family and long-time friendships with extraordinary vitality, despite her disabling condition. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76 St and Amsterdam Avenue, 1 p.m., Monday.
