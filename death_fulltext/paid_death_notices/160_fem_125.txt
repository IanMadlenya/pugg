
        CHANLER - Randall W. died peacefully at her home in Golden's Bridge, New York on May 13th. She was 89 years old and the daughter of the late Captain Roger Williams & Frances Mcllvaine Williams. She attended Smith college. Her first marriage to John S. Rogers ended in divorce. She was subsequently married for 46 years to the late William C. Chanler. Mrs. Chanler is survived by a sister, Eveline Holt of Virginia Beach, Virginia, & by five daughters: Evelyn Chanler of Rhineback, New York; Shiela Swett of Norwich, Vermont; Elizabeth Janeway of Webster, New Hampshire; Randall Hunt of Washington, DC; & Sarah Walker of Peck, Idaho. Her family included 15 grandchildren & 14 great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. Matthew's Church in Bedford, New York on Friday, June 2 at 11:00 AM. Contributions in her name may be sent to Hospice Care, 100 South Bedford Road, Mt. Kisco, New York 10549.
