
        BITTING-Betty B., 80, of Longboat Key, FL, died April 15, 2003. Born July 20, 1922 in Staten Island, NY. She had been a resident of Longboat Key for 20 years coming from New York City. She was employed with the law firm of Parker, Harrison and Dietz in Sarasota, Florida. She was a veteran of U.S. Marines WW II. Survived by one niece, Martha B. Tart, of West Keansburg, N.J. Memorial donations to Hospice of S/W Florida, 5955 Rand Boulevard, Sarasota, FL. 34238. For more info Toale Brothers Funeral Home, Sarasota, FL.
