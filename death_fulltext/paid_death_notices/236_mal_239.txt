
        LYONS--Dr. Albert S. Physician, surgeon and author, died Sunday, age 94. He wrote ''Medicine, An Illustrated History,'' was often honored for his significant contributions to patients and colleagues, and will be remembered as the gentlest and kindest of men. Survived by his wife Barbara Lyons, daughters Terry and Leni, step-daughter Alix, step-son Fred, grandchildren Michelle and Crispen, and great-granddaughter Sarah Rose. He will be remembered with love by all who knew him. Services at the Campbell Funeral Home, 81st and Madison Avenue, NYC, at 11:30 am, on September 26.
