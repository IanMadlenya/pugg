
        HYAMS-George, M.D. The Board of Trustees and the Board of Directors of the New York County Medical Society mourns the passing of this esteemed colleague. Dr. Hyams' many positions included former chairman of the Board of Trustees and longtime delegate to the Medical Society of the State of New York. He was a compassionate and caring physician who will be greatly missed by all. Milton Haynes, M.D., Secretary
