
        EDELSTEIN -- Victoria, passed away at home on January 9, 2005 at age 81. Born on May 28th, 1923 to Sylvia and Murray Bloom in Brooklyn, NY. She was a graduate of Long Island University and is survived by Murray, a loving and devoted husband of 61 years, their three sons, Lee (Janet), Mitchell, Russell (Natalie), and two loving grandchildren, Seth (Jessica) and Lauren. Vicky had a lifelong passion and commitment to Hadassah. Funeral service at Temple Beth Tikvah, 4550 Jog Rd., Lake Worth, FL, on Tuesday, January 11, at 3 PM. The family requests donations be made to HadassahFountains Chapter, 481 Fountains Drive South, Lake Worth, FL 33467.
