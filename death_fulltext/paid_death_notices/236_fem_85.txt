
        BERKLEY--Alison Beth. Died peacefully on September 14, 2006. Devoted mother to Kate B. Heim and daughter to Robert Berkley and the late Barbara Glasgall Berkley. Graduate of Cornell University, Class of 1965, she also earned a Master's degree from Yale University and a law degree from Benjamin Cardozo School of Law. Diagnosed with cancer some fifteen years ago, she nevertheless had a wonderful, fruitful life in the company of glorious friends and family. She was an inspirational friend to vast numbers of people, all of whom adored her. So many lives have been enhanced by their friendships with Alison. Her wisdom, charm, beauty and loyalty were unbelievable. Above all, her sense of humor was extraordinary. Alison will never be forgotten by all she encountered in all walks of life. In lieu of flowers, contributions should be made to the Anne Moore Breast Cancer Research Fund, 428 East 72nd St., Suite 300, NY, NY 10021. Services Sunday, September 17th at 10AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue, NYC. Interment at Temple B'nai Abraham Memorial Park, Union, New Jersey.
