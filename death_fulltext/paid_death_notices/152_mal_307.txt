
        DAVIS-David Harris. Age 64, of Jackson Heights, NY, formerly of London, England. On September 28, 1999. Beloved husband of Ruth, father of Philip, Andrew and Neil. Graduate of London University. Designed national data systems for large government projects helping thousands of families through Head Start and the Office of Aging. Director of Center for Human Services Management & Accu-Search, Inc. His wit, sensitivity, erudition and culinary gifts will be missed by friends and family members. He was a loving grandpa to Josh and Ashley. Services Thursday, September 30, at noon at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave.
