
        BARASH-Ernest S., M.D. The Association of the Attending Staff of the Mount Sinai Hospital mourns the passing of our long-standing colleague and friend. Dr. Barash began his residency in Orthopedics at Mount Sinai in 1954. He dedicated his life to teaching and to the care of his patients. He will be remembered for his commitment to the elderly in the nursing home population and for his outstanding efforts in Geriatric Orthopedics. We extend our sincere condolences to his entire family. Anthony Squire, M.D., President Vicki Lopachin, M.D., Secretary Amy Weinstein, Administrator
