
        FOTIADES--John. The Department of Medicine at The Mount Sinai Medical Center mourns the passing of John Fotiades, M.D., M.P.H. Dr. Fotiades completed his M.D. at Mount Sinai School of Medicine in 1991. In 1998 he earned his MPH at Columbia University. Dr. Fotiades was employed at the Bronx Veterans Affairs Medical Center as a clinical practitioner in internal medicine. He was an enthusiastic and active VA health services researcher and directed a multi-site study to develop and evaluate patient education materials about bioterrorism. Dr. Fotiades was an assistant professor in general medicine at The Mount Sinai School of Medicine. Funeral services will be held at 10 AM, Friday, January 7, at St. Demetrios Church of Jamaica, 84-35 152 St.
        FOTIADES--John, M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni record with sorrow the tragic and untimely death of John Fotiades, School of Medicine graduate of the class of 1991. An outstanding young physician, he will be missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his family. William Stein, M.D., Pres.
