
        IVES--Helen, a prominent New York City real estate attorney and widow of the late Brewster Ives, former chairman of Douglas Elliman Gibbons and Ives, died peacefully at her home in Manhattan on Sunday, June 8th. She is survived by her nieces, Jane E. Tuohy and Anne T. Scriptunas, and her nephew, John P. Tuohy. A funeral mass will be held on Wednesday, June 11th, at St. John the Evangelist Church, 348 East 55 St., 10:30 AM. Interment private. >>AD#
