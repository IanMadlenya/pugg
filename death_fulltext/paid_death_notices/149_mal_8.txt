
        KAHN-Robert D. On May 25, 1999, died in Bangkok, Thailand, formerly of Hewlett Harbor, NY. The world lost a great man. He was a courageous, charismatic go-getter who never let obstacles stand in his way. His quick wit, creative brilliant mind and profound devotion will never be forgotten by his loving family and friends, near and far. His unique, insightful perspective will forever guide us. Funeral service Tues., 1PM, Wellwood Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
