
        FATT-Arthur C. 94. Of New York City and Delray Beach, Fla. Died Tuesday evening, January 12, 1999 after a long illness. Beloved husband of the late Barbara C., proud father of Marjorie Chester and Barbara Costikyan both of New York City, grandfather of Nancy Cedillo of Madrid, Spain, Priscilla Majcherska of East Hampton, L.I., Jonathan Heine of Chappaqua, N.Y., Christopher Chester of New York City and Katherine Chester of Bristol, R.I. Great-grandfather of four. A recognized pioneer in the field of advertising, he and the late Lawrence Valenstein founded The Grey Advertising Agency in 1921 and named the company for the color of the walls of their third floor walk-up office. Some of his happiest days were spent at his beloved home in Mallorca where he and his wife, Barbara, joyously hosted family and friends. Services and interment private. Memorial service to be announced at a later date.
