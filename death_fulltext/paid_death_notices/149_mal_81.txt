
        KRUPA-Anthony Roland. Died at home in New York City on June 1, 1999. Mr. Krupa was born on December 20, 1929 in Pawtucket, R.I. The son of Michael Krupa and Nancy Bociek Krupa. Known as Tony Krupa, he was a graduate of Parsons School of Design in NYC and worked with Jacques Fath in Paris. Tony Krupa was a noted apparel designer and Vice President and Director of Design for I. Appel Corporation until his retirement in 1993. He was a recipient of the Tommy Award. Mr. Krupa is mourned by friends around the world. A memorial service will be held Wednesday, June 9 at 10AM at St. Francis Assisi Church, 135 West 31 Street, Manhattan, N.Y.
