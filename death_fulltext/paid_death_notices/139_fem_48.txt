
        LITTLE-Dorothy Fay (Bunny). At age 93, on August 5th, in Hanover, New Hampshire. Wife of the late Dwight R. Little, survived by daughters Ann Rubenstein of Santa Fe, New Mexico; Katharine King of Washington, D.C.; and Margaret Warren of Brunswick, Maine; six grandchildren and one great-grandchild. Daughter of Sidney B. Fay, late Professor of History at Harvard University, Mrs. Little graduated from Smith College and taught at the Cambridge School (Weston, Massachusetts), the Bement and Eaglebrook Schools (Deerfield, Massa chusetts), the Pine Cobble School (Williamstown, Massachusetts), and the Northampton (Massachusetts) Day School. In 1933 she co-founded with Ruth Hill McGregor the Greenwood Music Camp (Cummington, Massachusetts) and served as its Director for its first 50 years. In recent years, Mrs. Little was a Suzuki violin teacher in Hanover, New Hampshire. Service arrangements are incomplete. Gifts in memory may be made to the Greenwood Music Camp, Inc., c/o P.O. Box 484, Northampton, MA. 01601.
