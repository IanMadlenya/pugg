
        WING--Nellie Elizabeth, died February 14, 2007. She was born in Kendall, NY. She was a 1940 graduate of Keuka College. From 1944 -1946 she served in the US Coast Guard. She was the administrative assistant to Bill Wilson, the founder of A.A., in NYC from 1947 until 1982. She was the 1st A.A. archivist. She was a Watercolor Artist and a Sculptor as well as the Author of the book: Grateful to Have Been There. She is survived by nephews; William L. Wing of NJ, David Wing of PA, Michael Bowler of NV and two nieces; Kathy Goetch of MD and Maria Oplatka of CA. She was predeceased by her brothers, William F. and Roswell B. Wing and a sister, Mary Bowler. Visiting hours will be Saturday, February 24, 2007 from 1-5 pm at Moore's Home for Funerals, 1591 Alps Road, Wayne, NJ. Memorials may be made to the Alzheimer's Association, 400 Morris Ave., Suite 251, Denville, NJ 07853. For more information visit: www.mooresinfo.com
