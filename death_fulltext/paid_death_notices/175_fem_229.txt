
        McGINN-Dr. Regina M. Of Staten Island on August 26, 2001. Beloved wife of Dr. Joseph T. McGinn. Loving mother of John, Joseph T. Jr., Joanna and Jacqueline. Cherished daughter of Anne and Dr. Arthur Pidoriano. Dear sister of Joanne McKeefrey, Lisa Nolin, Joseph and Dr. Arthur Pidoriano Jr. Funeral from Casey Funeral Home, 350 Slosson Ave., Staten Island, NY, on Wednesday. Mass of Christian Burial St. Ann's Church, 10 AM. Entombment in Moravian Cemetery. Visiting Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. In lieu of flowers donations to the Staten Island University Hospital Conference Center would be appreciated.
        McGINN-Regina. The New York State Chapter of the American College of Physicians-American Society of Internal Medicine mourns the passing of Regina McGinn, MD, FACP. Dr. McGinn was an active and valued member of the Chapter. Her enthusiasm, kind heart and extraordinary spirit were much beloved by all. We extend our deepest sympathy to her entire family. Lawrence Smith, M.D., President
