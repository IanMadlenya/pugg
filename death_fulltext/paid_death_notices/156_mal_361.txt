
        WERNER-Adrian B. Died Friday, January 21st. in Manhattan. We was born in Brooklyn, N.Y. August 10, 1944 and graduated from Brooklyn Tech and City College of New York. He developed numerous real estate projects throughout the United States and, most recently, worked in Coral Gables, Fla. He is survived by three sons, Adam, Seth and Brett and his sister, Frances Lawn.
