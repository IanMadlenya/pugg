
        ROTH--Marc. The Board and Staff of F.E.G.S. Health and Human Services System mourn the passing of Marc Roth, beloved son of Evelyn Roth, F.E.G.S. Vice President and dear friend. Marc's battle was long and courageous. Our sympathies to Evelyn, her children Rebecca and Gregg, grandchildren Matthew and Luke, and the entire Roth family.
