
        EISEN-Charles Jacob. On September 23, 2002, at home in Great Neck, NY. Alumnus of New York University, Certified Public Accountant. Captain in the U.S. Army during W.W. II. Beloved husband for 52 years of Sylvia K. Eisen, father of Amy Eisen Krupsky, Elizabeth Eisen and Claudia Eisen Flack, grandfather of Rachel and Lydia Krupsky, Samuel and Natalie Dworkin, Nathaniel and Caleb Flack. Also survived by his sister Rose E. Simon, brother Dr. Herman N. Eisen, and sons-in-law Kenneth J. Krupsky and Stuart H. Flack. Funeral services will be held 2 PM, Tuesday, September 24 at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza (Opposite LIRR), Great Neck, with interment following at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pine Lawn, NY. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Jewish Theological Seminary, New York, N.Y.
        EISEN-Charles. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Int. Sr. Rabbi-Myron Fenster Steven Markowitz, President
