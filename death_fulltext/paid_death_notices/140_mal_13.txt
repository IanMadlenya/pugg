
        APONTE-John. The officers, members and teams of the Long Island Midweek Baseball League, mourn the untimely passing, on August 31, 1998, of Board Member and friend, John Aponte. His sportsmanship, competitiveness and sense of humor exemplified the ideals of our League and all of Men's Senior Baseball. His wise counsel and dedication will be missed. Heartfelt condolences to his wife Maureen and family.
