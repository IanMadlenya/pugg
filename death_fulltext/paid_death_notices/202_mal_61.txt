
        MONTGOMERY--William W. M.D. Of Chestnut Hill, originally from West Rutland, VT. November 7, 2003. Beloved father of Lynn of Placitas, NM, Scott of Addison, NY, Stuart and his wife Diane of Hopkinton, MA, Leslie of Brattleboro, VT, and Robert of Fairlee, VT. William is also survived by his seven loving grandchildren. A Memorial Service will be held in the Memorial Church, Harvard Yard, Harvard University, on Saturday, November 22, 2003, at 12:00 PM. Relatives and friends are kindly invited. Late Surgeon and Professor, Harvard University Medical School. Decorated Veteran of WWII and Korea, United States Army and Navy. In lieu of flowers memorial gifts may be made to the Massachusetts Eye and Ear Infirmary, 243 Charles St, Boston, MA 02114, Attn: Dr. William Montgomery Fellowship Fund. Bell-O'Dea Funeral Home, 617-277-7652.
