
        WILD-Philip. Died March 12 at the age of 87. The Garfunkel, Wild & Travis, P.C. family mourns the passing of our friend Philip Wild, father of managing partner, Robert Andrew Wild. We remember Phil's warm smile, his kindness and keen sense of humor. We extend condolences to Phil's wife of 64 years, Eve, and to his entire family. A private service is planned. Contributions may be made to Phelps Memorial Hospital, 701 North Broadway, Sleepy Hollow, New York 10591. Garfunkel, Wild & Travis, P.C.
