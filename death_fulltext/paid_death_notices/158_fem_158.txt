
        WOOD-Ruth Cutting Wilmerding, 90, died peacefully at her New York City home on March 12, 2000. She was the daughter of Helen and Lucius Wilmerding and the granddaughter of Mr. and Mrs. Robert Fulton Cutting, also of New York City. Her husband, Dudley P. K. Wood predeceased her. She is survived by brother, Lucius Wilmerding, of Princeton, NJ, and a sister, Mrs. Helen Bastedo of Peterboro, NH. She is also survived by her daughters Grace Montgomery Fowler, of Palm Springs, CA, and Ruth Montgomery Murphy of Bedford Hills, NY, as well as seven grandchildren and 14 great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, at 3:30PM Tuesday, April 18, 2000. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be made to Lighthouse International 111 East 59 Street NYC, NY, 10022 Attention Mark Watson.
