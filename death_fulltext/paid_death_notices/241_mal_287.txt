
        LANDAU--Siegfried. The Brooklyn Philharmonic, Board of Directors, Musicians and Administration mourn the loss of founding Music Director Siegfried Landau and his wife Irene. The Landaus had a deep commitment to Brooklyn, passion for music and an unwavering love for the Brooklyn Philharmonic. Their presence will be missed but their memory will be cherished. We extend our deepest condolences to the Landau family at this time of sorrow. J. Barclay Collins, II, Chairman Michael Christie, Music Director And the Brooklyn Philharmonic Family
