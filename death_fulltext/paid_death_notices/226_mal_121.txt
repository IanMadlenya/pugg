
        FISHER--Lyon H. November 7, 2005. Beloved husband of Margaret (Maggie). Brother of David. In recent years he mentored and taught people of all ages at the Baruch College and will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him, especially those in the fellowship. Memorial service Friday, November 11, 4 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. Contributions in his memory may be made to the North Shore Animal League, Port Washington, New York.
