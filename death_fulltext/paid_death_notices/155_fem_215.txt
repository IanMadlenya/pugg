
        HANDEL-Sandra (nee Wasserman). December 22, 1999. Beloved wife and best friend of Seymour, devoted mother of Rebecca and Matthew, dear mother-in-law of Andrew and Amy, adoring grandmother of Arianna, Molly and Josselyn, beloved sister of the late Phyllis Kaminer. Loyal friend, dedicated librarian, educator, and lover of literature. Her heart of gold touched the lives of many and she will live on in our hearts forever. In place of flowers, donations can be made to the home of Sages of Israel in New York City. Services ''Wien & Wien,'' 402 Park Street, Hacksensack, New Jersey, Thursday at noon.
