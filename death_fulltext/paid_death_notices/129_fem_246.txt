
        KOSHLAND-Dr. Marian (Lafayette, CA). The Weizmann Institute of Science and its American Committee note with deep sorrow the passing of a distinguished researcher in immunology and cell biology, renowned expert in the study of antibodies, former head of the Department of Microbiology and Immunology at the University of California at Berkeley. A lifelong friend and generous supporter of the Weizmann Institute and its vision of progress through science, she was the beloved wife of Dr. Daniel E. Koshland, Jr., a member of the International Board of Governors of the Institute and its Scientific and Academic Advisory Committee, and Vice President of ACWIS. We express our heartfelt sympathy to Dan and the entire family. Gershon Kekst, Chairman Weizmann Institute of Science Haim Harari, President S. Donald Sussman, Chairman American Committee Albert Willner, President Fran Ginsburg, Exec. V.P.
