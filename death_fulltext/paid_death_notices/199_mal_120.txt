
        SAPUTO--Vito. Passed away peacefully on August 11, 2003. Beloved husband of Helen (nee Norman) for 49 years. Devoted and loving father of Douglas, Paul, Teresa, and son-in-law William C. Crerend. Adoring ''Poppie'' to Lucy Anania and William Saputo Crerend. Faithful brother of Andrew, Robert Salvatore and Jack Saputo and the late Vincent Saputo and Mary Saputo Celona. Vito was born in Cinisi, Sicily and moved to NYC with his family at the age of ten. America became his home but Italy was always in his heart. He graduated from Pace College and earned an MBA from Long Island University. A CPA, he served as Treasurer of CIT Service Leasing Corporation where he worked for 30 years. Funeral service on Thursday August 14, Zion Episcopal Church in Douglaston, NY at 10am. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations can be made to Schneider Children's Hospital at North Shore-LIJ Health System Foundation, 125 Community Dr, Great Neck, NY, 11021.
