
        LYON - Eleanor Copeland. Died peacefully at her home on January 20, 2003 in Rye NY after a long and happy life. Born in Brockton, MA. on August 1, 1914. She was the daughter of the late Walter E. and Dr. Lena W. Copeland, beloved wife of the late Frank R. Lyon Jr. She is survived by her children Mary Fuller, Elizabeth Ashton, Frank R. Lyon III and Sarah de Menocal, ten grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held 11AM on Thursday, January 23 at Christ's Episcopal Church, Grace Church Street, Rye, NY. Donations in her memory may be made to Hospice of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, White Plains, NY 10601.
