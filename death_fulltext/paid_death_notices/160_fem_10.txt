
        FLEISIG-Marjorie Hall. On April 26, 2000. Survived by husband Ross Fleisig, daughter Anne Machlin of Binghamton, NY, and Dale Gallis of Shoreham, NY, and two grandchildren, Russell and Thea Gallis. She grew up in Forest Hills, graduated from Adelphi University, and lived for 44 years in Garden City, NY. Marjorie Fleisig was a member of the Garden City Community Church, AAUW, The Community Club and the DAR. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, May 7, at 2 PM, at the Garden City Community Church.
