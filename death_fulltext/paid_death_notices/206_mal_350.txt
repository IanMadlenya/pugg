
        FERGUSON--Percival, PhD died February 29th at the age of 77 after battling Cancer. Percy was a Psychology Professor in upstate NY and a musician in Montreal, upstate NY and NYC. He was an avid recreational ice hockey goalie who played until the age of 69 at Sky Rink in Manhattan. He was preceded in death by his beloved wife Linda Ferguson (August 2, 2003). He is survived by two sons; trumpeter Peter Ferguson of Henderson, NV and Dr. David Ferguson (Dr. JoAnn Sekiguchi) of Ann Arbor, MI and his brother, jazz trumpeter Maynard Ferguson. Percy's charm and sense of humor will be missed dearly by all who knew him.
