
        IVES-Kenneth Appleton, Jr. 64. Died June 21. He was the retired founder and chairman of Appleton Partners, Inc. of Boston. He is survived by his wife of 27 years, Cornelia Spencer Ives of Boca Grande, FL, his sons Kenneth, Michael, Christopher and George of NYC and Edward of Seattle, WA, a sister Edith Reynolds of Loudonville, NY, and six grandchildren. Memorial service August 3, 2 PM at St. Paul's School, Concord, NH. Donations may be made to the Advanced Medical Research Foundation, 333 Longwood Ave., Boston, MA 02115, National Rowing Foundation, 67 Mystic Road, N. Stonington, CT. 06359 or to St. Paul's School, Concord, NH 03301.
