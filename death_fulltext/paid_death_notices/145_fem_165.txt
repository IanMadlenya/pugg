
        GORLIN-Sylvia (nee Levy). Age 83. Beloved mother of Alexander and Rena. Wife of the late Philip. Sister of Benjamin Levy. Graduate of Columbia University School of Dental and Oral Surgery, 1938, and NYU School of Education, 1945. An inspiration to all through her love, determination, energy, independence, music and teaching. She will be greatly missed by the many lives she touched. Services were held February 18, at Old Montefiore Cemetery.
