
        EMER--Herman, died peacefully on February 14th at age 96. A lifelong resident of New York City, Mr. Emer had a long and distinguished career in public service. A graduate of Brooklyn Law School, he served as Chief Investigator of the Workmen's Compensation Board and as an Assistant Attorney General in the New York State Department of Law, Labor Bureau. Mr. Emer is survived by his beloved brother Jack (Ruth), nieces Elaine Trager (David), Judith Lickson, Nancy Schulman (Robert), Toby Emmer, Eileen Halfon and nephew Howard Emmer. A graveside service will be held on Friday February 18th at 2PM at Mount Moriah Cemetery, Fairview, NJ.
