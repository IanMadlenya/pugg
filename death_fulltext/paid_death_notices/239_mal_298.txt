
        PLANCK--Dr. Russell E., 87, of Greensboro, NC died on December 26th, 2006. Dr. Planck is survived by his companion and partner of many years, Anthony L. Dickerson, and by several cousins. At Dr. Planck's request no funeral service will be conducted. A mass of celebration and thanksgiving will be held at a later date at the church of St. Mary the Virgin, 145 West 45th Street, New York, NY.
