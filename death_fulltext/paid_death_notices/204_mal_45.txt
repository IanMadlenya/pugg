
        LONGCOPE -- Duncan, 83, died December 23, 2003, in Boston, MA. An avid reader, a writer and talented artist, he graduated from Milton Academy, 1937 and Harvard College, 1941. During WWII he served in the U.S. Army with the 5th Air Force. After the War, he joined the staff of the New Yorker. From 1960 to 1971 he lived abroad in Paris, and in Boston and Lee, MA. from 1971 until his death. His sister, Mary Lee Johansen of Lee, MA. survives.
