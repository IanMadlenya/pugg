
        DOBLIN--Dorothy (Dotty), of Hackensack, NJ, passed away peacefully at home March 8, 2007, 3 months short of her 93rd birthday. She was the daughter of the late Jacob and Rose Blum of East Orange, NJ, the widow of Philip Doblin, who predeceased her in 1994, and is survived by her daughters, Sara Dunn of Glen Rock, NJ, and Jane Kane of Gloucester, MA; her sons-in-law, Joe Dunn and Tom Sexton; her grandchildren Sally Dunn (Diane Waldo) of Lexington, MA, Elizabeth Gartenstein (David), of Brattleboro, VT, Nancy Dunn of Summit, NJ, Daniel Kane (Nanci), of Short Hills, NJ; and six great-grandchildren, Ben, Sam, Jake, Arianna, Alex and Sarah. Also surviving are her brother, Norman Blum and his wife Renee of Maplewood, NJ and many nieces and nephews. Dotty was as gentle, sweet and charming as she was beautiful to look at and an inspiration to all who knew her. Funeral services will be held at the Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Rt. 4 East, Paramus, NJ at 12:45 pm. Sunday, March 11, 2007. Donations may be made to the Hospice Program of Hackensack University Medical Center (HUMC), 30 Prospect Avenue, Hackensack, NJ 07601.
