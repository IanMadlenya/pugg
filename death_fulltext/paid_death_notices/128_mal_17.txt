
        DRUMMOND-Donald W., 93, of Vero Beach, died August 29, 1997 at his residence after a brief illness. He was born December 28, 1903 in Philadelphia, PA, and moved to Vero Beach 30 years ago from Morrisville, PA. He graduated from Wharton University of Pennsylvania. He had a long and distinguished career with Matheson Chemical, Squibb and Olin Industries and retired as an executive officer. He was a member of the First Presbyterian Church of Vero Beach; The Vero Beach Country Club and the Riomar Club. Surviving are his wife, ida, one daughter, Barbara Deibert of Asheville, NC, two grandsons and four great grandchildren. There will be no repose or visitation and no flowers. For information Strunk Funeral Home, Vero Beach, Florida.
