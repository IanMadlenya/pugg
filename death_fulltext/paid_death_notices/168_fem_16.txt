
        BARRY-Sister Mary David. The College of Mount Saint Vincent community mourns the loss of our past president, alumna and longtime friend, Sr. Mary David Barry. President of the College from 1963 through 1973, Sr. Mary David was an extraordinary person, with true vision and creative ideas for higher education. She was responsbile for many innovative programs including a unique cooperative agreement with Manhattan College that still exists today. She received a Doctor of Humane Letters degree from the College in 1973. Sr. Mary David's legacy will continue to be felt by all members of the Mount family and she will be remembered by all of us with great affection. Joseph P. DeCaminada, Chair, Board of Trustees Charles L. Flynn, President
