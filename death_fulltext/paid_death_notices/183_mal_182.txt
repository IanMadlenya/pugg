
        WOLF-James M. The Anti-Defamation League mourns the passing of James M. Wolf, a longtime ADL leader and supporter. An Honorary Life Member of the ADL National Commission, Jim served on our Omaha Regional Board who was committed to fighting hate and building bridges. We will miss him. We extend our condolences to his wife, Elaine, his children, and the entire family. Glen A. Tobias National Chairman Abraham H. Foxman National Director
