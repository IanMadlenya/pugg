
        FRIEDMAN-David, 94. Died peacefully on August 4. Educator, lawyer and social activist. Co-founder and director of The Rugby School, a school for handicapped children. Loving husband to his first wife, Henrietta. Equally devoted to his second wife, Jeannette. Father of Arnold (husband to Theresa) & Stella. Grandfather of Christopher, Mark, Cindy, Kelly, Helene and David. Greatgrandfather of Michael, Gabrielle and Jerry. Stepfather to Ruth and Joel. He was a brave and loving man.
