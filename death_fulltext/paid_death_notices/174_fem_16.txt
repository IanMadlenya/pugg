
        BRAUDE-Vera. Died peacefully in her home on July 1, 2001. Truly a person of unique generosity and strength, she enjoyed her independence and expressed herself through art and sculpture. Her hospitality, reciprocated by her friends, neighbors and caregivers, helped to make her last years at home possible. Loving mother of Kiki and Henry Etingin and Alex and Tu Braude. Beloved grandmother of Karen (Jin), Gregory (Milene) and Ani. Cherished great grandmother of Rebecca, Amanda, Guilian and Albert. Services Thursday, 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave.
