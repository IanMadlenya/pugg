
        BADALIAN--Sebou in Paris on July 22, 2006. At 15 his genius was clear, doing makeup and hair for the Royals of his city Tehran. In 1962 he resided in New York and worked for Revlon and soon opened his own salon, Sebou Salon. His finds lifelong loyal clientele among the International jetsets. Dali wanted to paint him, Cartier Paris presented him with gold scissors and his great pal Elaine gave him his own table. He was a one of a kind, brought beauty and passion to this world. Those who shared his life will never forget him. He is survived by his loving wife Vika. Your loving friends
