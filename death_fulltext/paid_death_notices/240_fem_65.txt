
        FARKAS--Elisabeth (Lily), nee Princess zu HohenloheOehringen, has died. She is survived by her husband Jnos, her daughter Alexandra, her brother Kraft Prince zu Hohenlohe-Oehringen, her sister Alexandra Hilz, and her sister Princess Dagmar zu Hohenlohe-Oehringen. A memorial service will be held in Vienna, Austria, on January 17th at St. Anna's Church.
