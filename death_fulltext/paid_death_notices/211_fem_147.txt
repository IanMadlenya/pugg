
        APPELBAUM--Barbara. Of New York, died on August 20, 2004. Wife of her late beloved Benjamin. She is survived by her son, Joseph, her sister, Joan B. Rosen, as well as a close-knit family of cousins, nephews, nieces, in-laws and life long friends. Services Monday August 23, 11:45 AM at ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in her memory may be made to Forbes Norris MDA/ALS Research Center, California Pacific Medical Center, P.O. Box 7999, San Francisco, CA 94120-7999 or Cabrini Medical Center Development Office, 227 E. 19 St., NY, NY 10003.
        APPELBAUM -- Barbara. What can I say about the most incredible woman I've ever known. She was my sister-in-law for 22 years. Always battling ALS, she displayed dignity and courage that knew no bounds. She was always there for me and everybody. I really learned a lot about how to value life from Barbara and her late husband Ben. My love and condolences to her son Joe and my darling wife Joan. You will never be forgotten. Bobby Rosen
        APPELBAUM--Barbara. The Ben Appelbaum Foundation mourns the loss of our inspirational leader. Barbara Appelbaum lived her life with courage, optimism, hope, character and love of family and friends. She will be terribly missed by everyone fortunate enough to have known her.
