
        GLIEDMAN - Richard. De parted suddenly on October 10, 2002. Our beloved friend, husband of Carole, father of Shari, Lesley - Anne and Pamela. Brother of Monroe. Adoring grandfather of Zachary, Ethan and Lindsay, Jack and Josephine, Maxwell, Benjamin, Zachary and Alexandra. Graduate of Bronx High School of Science and M.I.T. Graduate and professor for 47 years at Columbia School of Dental and Oral Surgery. Served as a Naval Lieutenant in World War II. Loved by all for his stories and jokes. Admired for his intellect and interest in collecting. He will be missed forever. Service Sunday 11:45AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Contributions in his memory may be made to Israel Emergency Campaign, UJA Federation.
