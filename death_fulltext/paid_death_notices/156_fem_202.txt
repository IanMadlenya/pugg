
        MORRISON-Eleanor Layton, Dr. Died on January 21. She was a Professor of Speech Pathology and Audiology at Teachers College, Columbia University for 31 years. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Walbert (Bill) Morrison, her children Dr. Leslie Faerstein and Robert (Mo) Morrison, her children-in-law Amy, Carlos, Sonia and Paul, and her grandchildren Alexander, Rebecca and Cody. Donations in her name may be sent to the Teachers College Fund, 525 W. 120 St., NYC 10027.
