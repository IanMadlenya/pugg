
        FRIEDAN--Carl. December 22 in his Sarasota home of cancer. Graduated Boston Latin School, served in the 69th Division in WWII. Magician, theater producer, inventor, his career was advertising, with Friedan-Allen-Celestino and then as a one-man agency until his death. Survived by ex-wives Betty, Noreen and Donatella, children Daniel, Jonathan, and Emily, step-son John Holzach, nine grandchildren, and many devoted friends. Memorial to be announced. Gifts in memory to Americans United for Separation of Church and State.
