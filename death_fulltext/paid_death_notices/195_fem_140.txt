
        RABIN-Rhoda Shapiro, died April 11th. She was 76. As described in her book, ''At the Beginning: Teaching Piano to the Very Young Child,'' she developed an innovative approach to teaching piano to 3 to 8 year-olds. She taught piano pedagogy and children at several universities. She was a graduate of NYC's Music and Art High School and the Eastman School of Music. In lieu of flowers, memorials may be made to the Rhoda Rabin Music Education Fund, 4 Sebring Court, Madison, WI, 53719. She was a loving wife, mother, grandmother, friend and teacher, and will be missed by the many people she touched and enriched.
