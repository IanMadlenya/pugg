
        SHERNOV-Samuel. Age 100, of Hallandale Beach, Florida, passed away on February 25, 2001. Loving father of Jan (Arthur) Leichtung, grandfather of Kathy Reichenbaum, Robert (Debbi) Leichtung, great-grandfather of Harris, Jamie, Stephanie and Aaron, brother of Edith Goldstein. He taught us to never give up trying to find the answer to any problem. He will be missed by all. Graveside services will be held at 1 PM today at New Montefiore Cemetery.
