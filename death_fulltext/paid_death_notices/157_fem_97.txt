
        GOODRICH-Virginia O.., 79, of West Hartford, CT, suddenly on Sunday, February 6, 2000, in her home at Stratton Mountain, Vermont. Beloved wife of Ernest C. Goodrich. Loving mother of Ernest C. Goodrich, Jr. and motherin-law of Kathleen Maher. Proud grandmother of Jasper, Nicholas and Alec. Memorial contributions may be made to St. Joseph College, 1678 Asylum Avenue, West Hartford, CT. 06117 or to the Wadsworth Atheneum, 600 Main Street, Hartford, CT. 06103.
