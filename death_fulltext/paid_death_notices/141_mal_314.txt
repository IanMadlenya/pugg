
        HORN-Morris, known as ''Mr. Local 627'' for founding the Provision Salesmen and Distributors Union, which he headed for over 50 years, died October 26th, two months shy of his 97th birthday. He is survived by his wife of over 72 years Yetta, daughters Muriel Sokol and Roslyn Bank (Alex), son Howard (Lois), five grandchildren and eleven great-grandchildren. The Marc Ballroom on Union Square is named for a grandchild who predeceased him. Services today at ''Sinai Chapel'', 162-05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, at 1 P.M.
