
        TAYLOR--Carol. Prize-winning reporter for World Telegram and Sun for 25 years, died August 28 in St. Petersburg, Florida at 89. She was among the journalism students expelled from Louisiana State in the 30's when the school newspaper criticized political boss Huey Long. In 1957, she was the only journalist to sail on maiden U.S. voyage of Mayflower II. Survivors included daughter, Susan Taylor Martin, foreign correspondent for St. Petersburg Times; grandson Steven and a great-granddaughter.
