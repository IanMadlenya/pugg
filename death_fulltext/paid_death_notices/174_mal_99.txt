
        DORF-Mitchell Lane. Age 42, of Freehold Township, died Tuesday, July 3, 2001, at Massachusetts General Hospital, Boston, after a long illness while awaiting transplant surgery. Mr. Dorf is survived by his father and law partner, Gerald L. Dorf, his mother Evelyn S. Dorf, an interior designer, and his brother Brian K. Dorf, a public relations consultant, all of Freehold. Funeral services were held Sunday. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Mitchell Lane Dorf Memorial Fund, c/o Cornell University School of Industrial and Labor Relations, Ithaca, NY 14850.
