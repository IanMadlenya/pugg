
        SEUBERT--Bernard, 67, died Saturday, June 17, 2006. Worked as a program officer for U.S. Department of State at the International Visitor Program office in NY from 1980 till 2006. Office liaison to NYPD. Ph.D in French from Columbia University. Loyal to the office, devoted to international exchanges and kind to his co-workers who miss him beyond all measure. Funeral Mass on Wednesday, June 21 at 10 AM at Mary Immaculate RC Church, 16 Browns Lane, Bellport, LI.
