
        VON STADE--Philip Richard. Of Locust Valley, LI on March 16, 2004 age 75. Beloved husband of Marjorie M. Father of Philip Jr. and MaryAnne, Robert and Liz Munson, Richard and Kimberly, Kathryne and Brett Lewis, F. Skiddy and Elizabeth. Brother of Frederick, John, Kathryne Krumpe and Nannie Ward. Also survived by nine grandchildren. Service St. John's of Lattingtown, Friday April 30th at 11AM. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Shelburne Museum, PO Box 10, Shelburne, VT 05482.
        VON STADE--Philip R. The Trustees and staff of the Shelburne Museum mourn the passing of Philip R. von Stade, our beloved Chairman of the Board. His steady leadership and unflagging devotion were an inspiration and have made the Museum an immeasurably stronger institution. We extend our deepest sympathies to Marjorie & the entire von Stade family.
