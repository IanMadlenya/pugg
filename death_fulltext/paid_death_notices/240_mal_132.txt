
        ROOSEVELT--Oliver W. Jr, 79, died on January 9th after a long illness. Son of Oliver W. and Verdery Akin Roosevelt, he was Fine Arts Editor and Music Critic for The Birmingham News. He is survived by his wife Ann; son Oliver W. III (Carol); daughters Verdery, Laura Patterson (Jim), Nicky (Judy Jackson), and Carolyn (Bill Gramby); grandsons Nicholas W. and Oliver W. IV; step-grandchildren Reed (Lisa) and Megan Patterson; and brother J. West.
