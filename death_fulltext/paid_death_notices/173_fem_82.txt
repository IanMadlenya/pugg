
        BENJAMIN-Ranana (nee Goldberg), on June 7th, 2001 at age 82. Born in NYC, formerly of Great Neck, NY and a resident of Lake Worth, FL. Beloved wife of Joseph for 60 years, dear sister of Natalie and Joseph. Devoted mother of David, Laura and Daniel, loving grandmother of Alexander, Nicholas, Geoffrey, Andrew and Joshua. A pioneer in Origami in America and author of several Origami books. Her love, kindness, friendship and enthusiasm for life are a tremendous loss. Funeral arrangements private.
