
        HEGGIE-Rose (nee Tenke), 91. Died peacefully September 20 at Britthaven of Chapel Hill, NC, where she had resided since 1994. The widow of John S. Heggie and mother of the late Dr. John F. Heggie. She is survived by her two loving daughters, Marilyn DeLalio of Fearrington Village, NC, formerly of Laurel Hollow, NY, and Anne Warnes of Durham, NC, formerly of Manhasset, NY. Other survivors include two sisters, Margaret Lisberg of Glen Head and Elizabeth Wilken of Bayville, and a brother, John Tenke of Bayville, ten grandchildren, six great-grandchildren and one great-great grandchild. A Mass of Christian Burial will be held Tuesday, September 23, 9:30 AM, at St. Dominic's Chapel, Oyster Bay, NY, with interment to follow at St. Charles Cemetery, Farmingdale. Visiting hours Monday, 3-5 and 7-9 PM, at the Francis P. Devine Funeral Home, Oyster Bay, NY. Donations to the National Endowment for the Arts, Washington, DC.
