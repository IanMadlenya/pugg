
        SPRINGER-Gustave, 89. On March 7th, 2002 at home after a long illness. Surviving his wife of 66 years, Florence; sons, Paul, Karl, Michael; granddaughter, Elizabeth. Served four years in army, W.W. II. Bronze Star for bravery. Attorney representing the Dutch Government for 45 years. Knighted by Queen Juliana of Holland. Visitors received by family at home: Sunday, March 10th, 12-4 PM, at 2500 Johnson Avenue, Riverdale, New York.
