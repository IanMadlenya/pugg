
        LEBER-Lucia (nee Schwadron). Age 83, of Whitestone, New York, died peacefully March 10, 2001. Beloved wife of Dr. Arthur, mother of Dr. Mark, mother-in-law of Vivian and grandmother of Jessica, Stephanie and Rebecca. She was a survivor of the horrors of World War II. She was a staunch supporter of the Technion and Jewish education and values. She will be missed and loved forever. Burial at Mount Hebron Cemetery. Shiva will be at son's home in Roslyn, Long Island through March 18. Donations may be given to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 710 West 168 Street, NY, NY 10032.
