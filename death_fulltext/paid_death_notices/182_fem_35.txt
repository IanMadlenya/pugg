
        NOLLSTADT-Gisela. Joined her beloved husband Fred in heaven on February 18, 2002. Born in Berlin, Germany, on October 4, 1919. She lived and worked in New York City from 1939 through 1962. She traveled the world for both business and pleasure, visiting Europe and the Middle East many times. She worked as a secretary for Dr. Cohn at The Salk Institute in La Jolla, CA, for over 20 years. She was a member of the Green Mountain Club in Vermont. Memorial Services will be held on Saturday, March 9th, at 2 PM, at Graham Memorial Presbyterian Church in Coronado, CA.
