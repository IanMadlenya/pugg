
        COGAN-David J. Died Thursday, February 7 at his home in Bedford, New York. Beloved husband of Arline, loving father of Sharon and Carol whose mother is Ferne, father-in-law of Joel Cogan-Beck and Burt Savitsky, devoted grandfather of Jessica, Nicole, Samuel, and Katrina, caring brother of Esther and family. Loving stepfather of Kim, Jill, Gary Snyder and his wife Kristin, and their children, Rachel, Daniel, and Elizabeth. Life-time New Yorker, producer ''A Raisin in the Sun'', owner of Biltmore Theater (Barefoot in the Park, Hair). Theatrical agent/accountant. President, Cogan, Bell & Co. Teacher, New School. Resided in New York and Palm Beach, Florida; member of Friars Club, Old Oaks and The Falls country clubs. Passionately devoted to his family, with a wonderful sense of humor, he will be greatly missed by all those whose lives he touched. Services will be held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue (91st Street), NYC, 212-769-4400 on Sunday, February 10 at 12 noon.
        COGAN-David. The Officers and Members of Old Oaks Country Club mourn the passing of their dear friend and fellow member, David Cogan. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife Arline, to his children and other members of his family. Paul J. Konigsberg, President
        COGAN-David J. The Board of Governors and members of the Falls Country Club, Lake Worth, FL, acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, David Cogan. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Arline and their family. Kenneth Mahler, President
