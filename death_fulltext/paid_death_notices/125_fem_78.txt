
        GARGIULO-Helen A., 79, died June 10 in Monterey, CA. Born February 4, 1918 in New York, NY, she was a legal secretary for the State of New York for 37 years. A graduate of Hunter College, she moved to Pacific Grove, CA. a year ago after living in New York all her life. Survived by son, Ted Gargiulo, Jr., Pacific Grove, CA; brother, Victor Grande, Long Island, NY; sister, Italia Mainland, Monterey, CA; 2 grandchildren. No services will be held. Contributions to Hospice of the Central Coast, P O Box 1798, Monterey, CA. 93942.
