
        KENWORTHY - Marthaonja Weyl/d on March 18th in Amherst, MA. Vivid, supportive wife of F. Joachim Weyl, former dean and acting president of Hunter College, she later married E.W. Kenworthy of The New York Times, and then on the brink of ninety, wed her college beau Harlan M. Thompson, formerly of General Foods. She leaves two daughters, two grandchildren, and a worldwide family of loving friends. Contributions to the 15th Street Monthly Meeting Society of Friends, 15 Rutherford Place, New York City 10003.
