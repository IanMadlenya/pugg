
        DEWHURST--Terrence. On December 10, 2004, of Long Island City. Beloved husband of Margaret. Loving brother of Frances Dewhurst and Dorothy Faulkner, adored uncle of Deborah Gluck and two grandnieces. He received a B.S. Degree from Columbia University School of General Studies after serving in the United States Navy during World War II as a Yeoman First Class. Retired in 1984 after 33 years at the Times Editor of School Weekly for 15 years. Visiting hours from 2-5 PM Tuesday, December 14 at Thomas Quinn, 35-20 Broadway, Long Island City. Memorial contributions to the New York Times Neediest Cases Fund.
        DEWHURST--Terrence. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Terrence Dewhurst, associated with The Times from June 11, 1951 until July 1, 1984.
