
        BOLOKER--Pauline. Loved by all that knew her. Admired by her children. A loving mentor to her grandchildren. She will be terribly missed byall her nieces & nephews who grew up at her knee. Her sister Honie and brothers Paul and Jerome Berko will treasure her memory forever.
        BOLOKER--Pauline. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Pauline Boloker, beloved sister of Jerome Berko and sister-in-law of Henrietta Berko, cherished leaders of the New York Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
