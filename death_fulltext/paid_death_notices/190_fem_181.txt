
        MAYLAND-Betty. 90. Died at home surrounded by her family on November 23, 2002. Beautiful and beloved wife of the late Irving W. Devoted mother of Andrea (Bachner), Peter and his wife Laurel. Adoring grandmother of Brandon, Randall, Katherine, Gabriel, Melissa and her husband David, and loving greatgrandmother to their children Zachary, Jonah and Isabelle. As the matriarch of our family she personified social and political conscience and culture. Her elegance and grace served as an inspiration to all who loved her. Contributions to Physicians for Social Responsibility. Services today 12:30 PM, at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, Amsterdam Ave, corner 91st Street in Manhattan.
