
        O'CONNOR-Robert K. Died on August 22, 2000, in Vero Beach, FL, after a long illness. Born January 13, 1922, in Holyoke, MA, he was a graduate of Amherst College and NYU School of Business. During WWII he was a Navy pilot. After a long career as a stockbroker and securities analyst he retired to Florida from Rowayton, CT. He is survived by his wife, Jean; a son, Douglas O'Connor; a daughter, Judy Gluckstern and four grandchildren. Donations may be made to Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 710 W. 168th St, NY, NY 10032. A memorial service will be held Saturday, September 23, at 3 PM, at The United Church of Rowayton, CT.
