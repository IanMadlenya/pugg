
        COLEMAN--Janet M., on May 5th, 2007 at age 89. Beloved wife of the late Martin S. Coleman. Survived by her children Ann and Fred Lynn, John and Linda Coleman, Nancy and Matthew Mintzis. Her grandsons, Jeff Lynn and Julian Mintzis, her nephews, James Coleman and William Perper. Her niece Victoria Lasdon Rose and her sisterin-law Dorie Coleman. Family will receive friends at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. on Wednesday, 7 to 9 pm. Funeral services Thursday, 11 am at Frank E. Campbell. Entombment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to New York University Medical Center, 550 First Ave., New York, NY 10016.
        $(6$)COLEMAN--Janet. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Janet Coleman, whose dedication, expressed through her family's generosity, sets an example of philanthropic leadership which impacts our community and enriches the lives of millions around the globe. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Janet's children Ann and Fred Lynn, John and Linda Coleman, and Nancy and Matthew Mintzis; her grandchildren Jeff and Julian; and the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
