
        PROTERO--Dodi, 76, died of heart and lung failure on April 22, 2007. Born in Toronto, Canada, she went to Italy at 17 and made her European debut with the San Carlo Opera, Naples, in 1956 in 'Die Zauberflote'. Other opera engagements followed in Salzburg, Cologne, Glyndebourne, The English National Opera. She returned to Canada in the mid 1960's and sang many roles with the Canadian Opera Company, Toronto. As a Voice Teacher she was on the faculties of the School of Fine Arts, Banff, Alberta, Canada, The University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign, The Academy of Vocal Arts, Philadelphia, Opera Music Theater Institute (OMTI), Newark, NJ, The Juilliard School, Evening Division. She is survived by her sister Shirley and six nieces and nephews. A Memorial Service will be held on May 23 at 5pm at The Ethical Culture Society, 2 West 64th St. at Central Park West. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The American Heart Association, 122 East 42nd St., NYC, NY 10017.
