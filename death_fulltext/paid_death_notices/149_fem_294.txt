
        BRAND-Evelyn. Passed away peacefully in her sleep on June 26, 1999. Wife of the late Fredrick Brand, loving cousin to many, and devoted friend. Philanthropical, vivacious and lover of the arts, she will be missed by all who knew her. Services will be held at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, 1 PM, Tuesday, June 29th. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
