
        SLAWTER-Paul B., 85. Retired vice president of Rosston, Kremer & Slawter, Inc., New York City, died August 12, 2001, in Bethlehem, Pennsylvania. During World War II, He was a 1st Lieutenant in the Army Air Corps. Mr. Slawter served as executive secretary for the SACI. He was an active member of the Chemist Club and Union League Club. He is survived by his wife, Audrey; three daughters, a brother, five grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren. There will be a private memorial service with military funeral honors at the graveside. For more information contact the Guerro & Ruggiero Funeral Home.
