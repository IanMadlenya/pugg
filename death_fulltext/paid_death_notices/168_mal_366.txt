
        EBIN-Milton, DDS. On January 23, 2001. Born and raised in New York, Dr. Ebin practiced dentistry for many years in NY. He was a Veteran of the Army during World War II, serving in the European campaign at Ardennes, the Rhineland and Central Europe. Dr. Ebin is survived by his daughter Linda (Ronald) Stutz, sister Natalie Bloch, brother Herbert Ebin and many nieces and nephews.
