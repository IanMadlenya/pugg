
        KOERNER--Naomi. Of Scarsdale, NY. Died peacefully in her sleep on February 19, 2006. She was 77 years old. Born June 26, 1928, to Harry and Ceal Blumberg. She was the beloved mother of Alissa and Joshua, and a devoted grandmother to Hannah. She is also survived by her brother Lee Blumberg. She was predeceased by her husband Howard. A graduate of Hunter College, Naomi was a talented and dedicated educator, who taught for over 20 years at Greenvale School in Eastchester, touching and enriching the lives of hundreds upon hundreds of Eastchester residents. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St, in Mount Vernon, on Tuesday, February 21, at 10 am. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Eastchester Volunteer Ambulance Corps, 914-337-3051.
