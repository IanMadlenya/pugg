
        CHESKIN--Joel Richard. Died June 21, 2006. A tenured professor in mathematics at Marymount College, Tarrytown, he also taught at Mercy College, Manhattanville College and was in private practice tutoring mathematics. He was very much beloved and will be truly missed by his family. Survived by his parents Irving and Mildred (Estrin) Cheskin, daughters Jessica (Mark) Cheskin Wilson and Julie Cheskin, granddaughter Kira Wilson. Funeral service private. Contributions to the Actor's Fund.
