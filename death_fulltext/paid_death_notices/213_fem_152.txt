
        WELS -- Marguerite Samet, 84, died peacefully at home in San Francisco on October 17, 2004. She lived most of her life in New York City, where she had a thriving career as an award-winning interior designer for 50 years. She was a former vice president of the New York Chapter of ASID, served on the board of the Margaret Chase Smith Library and was listed in Who's Who of American Women. Marge was the widow of Richard H. Wels of New York City and David Stern III of Palm Beach, FL. She will be lovingly remembered for her affection, energy, great cooking and honest, unconventional approach to life. She is survived by her sister Helen, daughters Susan and Amy, and grandchildren Emily, Casey, Ben and Laura.
