
        SCHLESINGER--Arthur M., Jr. The Academy of Political Science and Political Science Quarterly are deeply saddened by the death of the eminent political and social historian, Arthur M. Schlesinger, Jr. Arthur Schlesinger has been a valued member of the Academy for over 45 years, and an active member of the Quarterly's Editorial Board for over 30 years. His fellow scholars will miss him greatly as will the Academy. We express to his family our most sincere condolences. Peter J. Goulandris, Chairman of the Academy Board George B. Munroe, Chairman Emeritus Demetrios James Caraley, President and Editor of Political Science Quarterly
