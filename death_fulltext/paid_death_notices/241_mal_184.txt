
        KING--Robert John, of Mt. Crested Butte, CO, formerly of Mt. Kisco, NY, February 1, 2007, after a brief illness. Graduate of Amherst College, class of 1957, and Fordham University Business School (MBA). Bob was captain of Amherst's 1956 football team and earned Little All-American honors. Bob handled international real estate for several companies including Reader's Digest, J. Walter Thompson, Continental Insurance and Credit Suisse First Boston before finishing his career at Commonwealth Advisors. Survived by wife of 46 years, Anita Cunningham, daughter Robin of Atlanta, GA, son Robert Jr. of Denver, CO, and two grandchildren, Eliza and Vincent. Also survived by sister Carol Tanczos of Bayse, VA, and brother William of Old Greenwich, CT. A memorial service will be held in New York at a later date. Donations may be made to Amherst College Alumni Fund, Amherst College, PO Box 5000, Amherst, MA, 01002-5000.
