
        TURNER-Alice E. of Manhasset, NY passed away on March 19, 2001. Born Alice Frail on December 19, 1925 in Flushing, NY. As a young girl she was a fine athelete who excelled in track and swimming competition. Upon graduating Bayside High School in 1944 she became a stewardess for TWA. She later went on to United Airlines and became the first secretary for the Airline Pilots Association. She was married in June 1950 & is survived by her devoted husband James and four children; John, Jeanne, Claire & Andrew to whom she taught many valuable lessons and who will miss her dearly. She is also survived by her older sister Mary Hay of Norwalk CT, and ten grandchildren. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd, Manhasset, New York, Wednesday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass 10 AM Thursday at St. Marys Church in Manhasset. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory can be made to Maryknoll Fathers and Brothers C/O Father Peter LeJacq-Reference Alice Turner, P.O. Box 305 Maryknoll, NY 10545.
