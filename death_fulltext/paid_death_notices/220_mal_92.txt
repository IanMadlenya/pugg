        SAPERY--Sandy. The Board of Directors and members of the Quaker Ridge Golf Club record with sorrow the passing of our esteemed member of over 40 years, Sandy Sapery. His many years of friendship, kindness and enthusiastic spirit will be remembered. Steven M. Slaven, President Thomas W. Brock, Secretary
