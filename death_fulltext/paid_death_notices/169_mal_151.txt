
        FITZGERALD-Edward E., 81. Of Eastchester, died on February 11, 2001. Beloved husband of Libuse Ostruk Fitzgerald. Devoted father of Eileen Fitzgerald and her husband Paul Rosenblum, and Kevin and his wife Linda Fitzgerald. Cherished grandfather of Leona Rosenblum. He was a noted writer, editor and former president of the Book of the Month Club. The family will receive friends at the Westchester Funeral Home, Inc. Eastchester, Tuesday 2-4 & 7-9PM. Service at the funeral home, Wednesday, February 14 at 11 AM.
