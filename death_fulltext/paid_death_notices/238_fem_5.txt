
        WOOD--Julia Snell. Died on October 31, 2006 at home in Haworth, N.J. She was born on September 20, 1920 in Herkimer, N.Y. Daughter of Katharine Kinne and Cornelius Harry Snell. She was wife of John R. Wood, Jr. and is survived by two daughters Cornelia Ferrara and Margaret Wood and four sons Richard, David, Roger and John Wood and grandchildren Laura Ferrara and Emily Wood. Services will be private. Please omit flowers.
