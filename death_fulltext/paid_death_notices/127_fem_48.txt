
        GENAUER-Rabbi Sam. The Board of Directors, Faculty and Staff of CLAL -The National Jewish Center for Learning and Leadership extend deepest condolences to Blu Greenberg, our esteemed associate faculty member, her husband Rabbi Irving Greenberg, our president and founder, and their entire family, on the passing of Rabbi Sam Genauer, beloved father, grandfather and great-grandfather. Rabbi Genauer was a great scholar, a warm, generous and caring person who deeply touched the lives of everyone. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Radine Abramson Spier Chairman Rabbi Irwin Kula President-Elect Donna M. Rosenthal Executive Vice Chairman
