
        COREN-Rose L. Died Sunday March 24, 2002. Wife of Arthur, mother of Jonathan and Charlotte, mother-in-law of Pam and Hal. Grandmother of Justin, Perry, Benjamin and Lauren, sister of Esther Abramson. A graduate of Hunter College, former chairperson of the Department of Biological Sciences at NYC Technical College, Docent at the Museum of Jewish Heritage, active community member. A dynamic and intellectual participant in life, she was a sensitive and caring human being who will be missed by all who knew her. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Museum of Jewish Heritage, 212968-1800 x174, Lanie.
        COREN-Rose. The Trustees and Staff of the Museum of Jewish Heritage-A Living Memorial to the Holocaust mourn the loss of our beloved volunteer Gallery Educator Rose. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family. May her memory be for a blessing. Robert M. Morgenthau, Chairman
