
        MORRIS-Norman. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Medical Staff of The Hospital for Joint Diseases Orthopaedic Institute and The Officers of The HJD Research and Development Foundation are saddened to learn of the passing of Norman Morris, a Founder of The Foundation and a committed friend and loyal benefactor of the hospital. His lifetime of dedicated service and philanthropic support of our initiatives in pediatric orthopaedics will always be remembered. We extend our condolences to his daughter, Arline Lubin, his son, Robert and to the entire family. William A. Perlmuth Chairman of the Board George M. Jaffin Chairman of the Foundation Victor H. Frankel, MD President John N. Kastanis, FACHE Chief Executive Officer Joseph D. Zuckerman, MD Surgeon-In-Chief
