
        REICHMAN-Adele Ronson Nahm, actress. Died October 31, 2000 in New York City. Her career began in the 1920s in radio and on the stage and continued until her death. Predeceased by her husbands, Horace H. Nahm and Theodore Reichman, DDS. Mother of Dorothy Nahm Friedberg, M.D. and Nancy Nahm Sidhu, mother-in-law of Neal Friedberg, M.D. and Dip Singh Sidhu, M.D. Stepmother of Lee Reichman, M.D., Rose Reichman, David Reichman and Sheila Uniti, grandmother of Abbe and Michael Friedberg and Daniel and Deborah Gar Reichman, aunt of Pat Stewart. Died in her sleep one day after returning from a trip to Venice with her family. An unforgettable woman of great spirit who will live through the memory of all those who knew her. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, November 5, 3:30PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the charity of your choice.
