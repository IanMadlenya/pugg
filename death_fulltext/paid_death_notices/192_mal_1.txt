
        CURRAN-Patrick M.J. Died on Saturday, December 28, in his 57th year. Devoted son of the late Patrick and Bridget (nee Ginnelly). He will be deeply missed by his sisters Patricia Carroll and Catherine McTernan, and his brothersin-law Thomas and James. Beloved by his nieces Catherine Maguire and Jeanne Montross, his nephews James and Michael McTernan, and Matthew Carroll, 11 grand-nieces and nephews, and his godson Michael O'Brien. He is a retired member of the Central Office of the City University of New York where he held a number of administrative posts for over 25 years, the last one as Coordinator of OSHA for CUNY. He graduated from Cardinal Hayes High School and Fordham University. Pat loved his family, friends, music and The Times crossword puzzles. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, at 81st St, Thursday, January 2, 2-5, 7-9 PM. Requiem Mass will be held on Friday, 10 AM, St. Stephen's Church, 414 E. 82nd St, 212-861-8500; followed by interment at Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the Ursuline Convent, Attention: Sr. Catherine Marie Jordan, 2885 Marion Avenue, Bronx, New York 10458.
