
        JOHNSTON-Kenneth F. Sr., of Manhasset, New York (retired NYPD), suddenly on 13 May 1997. Beloved husband of the late Catherine M. (nee Crichton). Cherished father of the late Kenneth Jr. (Stephanie), Rev. Jeffrey, Beth Perchuk (Mark), Janice Abdenour (Michael), Donna Cardone (James) & Douglas (JeanMarie). Adored ''Gumpy'' of Kenneth III, Matthew, Kathleen, Heather, Nolan and Kaylyn Johnston, Kyle and Kendall Cardone, Michael and Keith Abdenour. Devoted brother of Audrey Reed, and brother-inlaw of Ivy Voelbel and Anne Crichton. Also survived by nieces, nephews, many friends and ''his kids,'' the hundreds of youngsters he coached during the past 30 years in St. Mary's CYO program. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, 1570 Northern Boulevard in Manhasset on Thursday from 7-9 PM and on Friday from 3-5 and 7-9 PM. There will be a Vigil Service at 8 PM on Friday. Funeral mass at 10:45 Saturday, 17 May at St. Mary R.C. Church in Manhasset. Interment at Holy Rood Cemetery in Westbury. Memorial offerings may be made to ''The Bear Foundation,'' a not-for-profit organization established by the Johnston family in support of childrens' athletic programs. P.O.
