
        GOLDSCHMIDT--Gustave W. Loving husband for 62 years of Ilse (nee Fluss), father of Eric (Pam), Sidney (Sandi), and Debbie (Mark Blechner). Grandfather of Doron (Shira), Lisa (Jonathan), Judah (Daphne), Elisha (Sara), Joshua (Sarah), Daniella (Yehuda), Michele (Doni), Jenna, Adam, Joey. Great-grandfather of Chaya, Akiva, Tzvi, Rina, Meir Simcha, Tova, Kayla, Yisrael, Aliza, Gabby, Ari, and Jadyn. Brother of Marta, Jack, and the late Eric. Brother-in-law of Herbert and the late Helga Lipow, Henry and Ruth Wertheimer, and loving uncle and granduncle to many, many, nephews and nieces. And an ever so special man who gave his time, love, effort, and charity to many Shules, especially Washington Heights Congregation, New York and Heichel Moshe, Netanya, Israel; many Yeshivot in New York, New Jersey, Connecticut, Massachusetts and Israel, and countless other charities around the world. A man who was respected in business for his honesty and fairness to everyone with whom he came in contact. Donations may be made in his memory to the Washington Heights Congregation, 815 West 179th Street, New York City 10033. The funeral was held on February 21, 2007, in New York City. Burial to take place February 22, 2007 in Netanya, Israel.
        GOLDSCHMIDT -- Gustav. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the loss of Gustav Goldschmidt, beloved father of Eric S. Goldschmidt, regional chair of Westchester, member of the Board of Directors and Executive Committee of UJA-Federation of New York, whose commitment and generosity enables us to renew and enrich Jewish life in New York, in Israel, and worldwide. We extend our heartfelt condolences to Gustav's wife, Ilse; to Pam and Eric; to their children, Lisa, Adam, and Jenna; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K.
