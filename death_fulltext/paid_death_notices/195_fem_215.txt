
        GALLAGHER-Sr. Elizabeth, RSHM. Marymount School of New York mourns the death of Sr. Elizabeth Gallagher, RSHM. A teacher and colleague who influenced generations of students and parents, she joined the faculty at Marymount in 1926, the School's inaugural year, and stayed at the School until 1940. After many years at other Marymount Schools worldwide, she returned to Fifth Avenue in 1966 as librarian and bursor. She retired in 1992. Sr. Elizabeth will be remembered for her warm and welcoming presence, sense of humor and commitment to our School. Sr. Kathleen Fagan, RSHM Headmistress and the Board of Trustees
