
        ARTEL-Jacques. Born January 18, 1914 in Rostov-on-Don, Russia. Died December 18, 1997. Beloved husband of Janet Ann Bissell Artel. Devoted father of Catherine Lee Pietrogallo, Marie Christine Wallace and Alexis Artel. Also survived by five grandchildren and five nieces and nephews. He fled Russia with his mother and brother to France where he served in the French Army during W.W. II. In 1946, he came to the United States, a country which he loved very much. Friend to many people, cared for all, especially loved children. He died peacefully after a full and interesting life. Memorial service will be held at the First Reformed Church, Hastingson-Hudson, New York at 11 A.M., on January 17th, 1998. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the First Reformed Church or to the Tolstoy Foundation of Valley Cottage, New York.
