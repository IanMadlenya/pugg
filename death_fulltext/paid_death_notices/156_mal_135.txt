
        STENERSON-Justin F., Sr. Died Thursday, January 6, 2000 at age 83. He was born December 4, 1916 to Harry and Pauline Stenerson of Bronx, New York. He was married to Anne Jennings on November 27, 1943. He was a graduate of Fordham Preparatory School and RCA Institute. Justin retired from CBS Records in 1981 as a studio engineer. He is survived by his wife, Anne Stenerson, of White Plains, New York, and his children, Vincent Stenerson of Santa Fe, NM, Justin Stenerson, Jr., Eileen Stenerson and Robert Stenerson, all of White Plains.
