
        EDELBAUM-Saul. Died May 4, 2003, at age 95. He was a graduate of Yale University School of Architecture '33, lived and practiced in New York City and also lived in Katonah, New York. In 2000 he moved to Meadow Lakes, in Hightstown, New Jersey. He is survived by his wife of 45 years, Hedda Hendrix; Jon and George, both of Easthampton, N.Y., sons by a previous marriage to the late Augusta Ross; five grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Interment private. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. Donations may be made to a charity of your choice.
        EDELBAUM-Saul. 94, on May 4. Beloved father of Jon and George, loving grandfather of Amanda, Kate, Susannah, Daniel and Joshua, great-grandfather of Jacob, Ethan and Cole. Dear husband of Hedda and of the late Augusta Ross. Architect, painter, sculptor. He lived life with a good heart. We love and miss you greatly.
