
        WEINRACH-Barbara Kaye. Age 63, with great sadness and a tremendous sense of loss we mourn her passing. A talented artist, teacher, beloved daughter to Rachel, loving mother to Maggie and Robert, Douglas and Jeanne, and Elizabeth and Keith, chesrished sister to Irwin and Mary, adoring grandmother to Jake, Matthew, Alex and Jacqueline and aunt to Jillian and Gregory. Services took place Monday at 11:00 AM from Temple Israel, 14 Coleytown Rd., Westport, with interment at Temple Israel Cemetery in Norwalk. A woman with a great passion for life-we will miss you forever. Memorial contributions may be made to the Yale University Oncology Dept.
