
        REDER-Lois C. Of Flushing, N.Y. On Saturday, September 18, 1999 at the age of 58. Beloved daughter of the late Andrew and Charlotte Reder. Dear sister of Joan Koechler and Anne Hoppe. Loving aunt of Alexis and Andrew. A teacher in special education, Lois was with the Dominican Sisters of Amityville for 15 years, and afterward devoted her life to working with the learning disabled. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated at Saint Aloysius Church, Wednesday, 9:45AM. Interment All Faiths Cemetery. Reposing at Morton Funeral Home/Ridgewood Chapels, 663 Grandview Ave, Ridgewood, N.Y. Tuesday, 7-9PM.
