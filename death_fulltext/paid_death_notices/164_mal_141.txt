
        BEAUDINE-Frank R. Born December 20, 1923 in New York, passed away Sept. 2, 2000 in Plano, Texas. Frank was Chairman of Eastman & Beaudine, a Major Int'l Executive Recruiting Firm he cofounded in 1967. He was a pioneer in the retained search industry. Frank was a Chaminade and Notre Dame grad, McKinsey & Co. alum, author of best selling book ''Ultimate Success''. He is survived by wife Martha, three sons and a daughter and 9 grandchildren. Service is Wed., Sept. 6 in Dallas.
