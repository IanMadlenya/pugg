
        CUSHING-ZINN--Jean (Dalzell). Died September 22, 2003, in Greenwich, Connecticut. She was 79. Born January 23, 1924 in Brooklyn, she was the daughter of the late Lloyd Hunter Dalzell and Frances Jones Redmond and the stepdaughter of the late Cyril James Redmond. She is predeceased by her husband, Charles M. Cushing and a sister Barbara Redmond Ryan. She is survived by two daughters Deborah Zinn Prouty and Kathryn Zinn Cerussi, both of Greenwich, and three grandsons. A service will be held on Wednesday, September 24, 2003, at 3 P.M. at St. Paul's Episcopal Church, 200 Riverside Avenue, Riverside, Conn.
