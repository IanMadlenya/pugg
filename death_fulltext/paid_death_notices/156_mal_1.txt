
        CAVALUZZO-Christopher A.. Age 46. On December 28, 1999. Long-time resident of Manhattan. Beloved son of Edith and the late John Cavaluzzo. Brother of the late Marcus. Yale University, class of 1975. He will be remembered for his keen intellect, quick wit and his generousity toward those in need. A service will be held 10AM Monday, January 3rd at the Sacred Heart Chapel, Bernards Ave., Bernardsville, New Jersey. Inquiries to Galloway & Crane Funeral Home, Basking Ridge, N.J. Memorial contributions may be made to the AIDS Benefit Committee of New Jersey.
