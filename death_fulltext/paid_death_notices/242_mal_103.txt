
        MATZ--Benjamin, 1913-2007, former Manager of Technical Information for the Bulova Watch Company, is author of ''The History and Development of the Quartz Watch''. A Certified Master Watchmaker, he was awarded the title of ''Fellow'' by the American Watchmakers - Clockmakers Institute in 2001. He is survived by brother-in-law Simpson Kalisher of New York City and Steven Kalisher of Coral Springs, FL. Additional information available at: www.benjaminmatz.com
