
        COOKE-Henrietta (nee Fellig). On May 8, 1999. A smart, complicated, wonderful person who lived life to the fullest. Will always be remembered by her children, Richard and Elizabeth, and their spouses Debbie and John, as well as the many young people she helped as a placement counselor. Preceded in death by George, her husband of 26 years. Last surviving of the Fellig children: Eli, Philip, Arthur (Weegee), Rachel, Jacob and Molly. Her wit will live on. Services private.
