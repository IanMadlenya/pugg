
        KOLKER--Leona Grobla, 87, of Brooklyn, NY and Phoenixville, PA. Died on May 24, 2005 after a lengthy illness. A pioneering woman executive in the floor covering industry, she created a popular 1950's Congoleum design embedded with flecks of gold and silver. Survived by her sister Cecelia (Tsirl) Waletzky and granddaughter Susan Lee Kolker. Services to be held at 2pm on Thursday, May 26, 2005 at B'nai Jacob Synagogue in Phoenixville, PA.
