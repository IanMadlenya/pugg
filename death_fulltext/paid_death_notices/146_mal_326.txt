
        BERG-David. Attorney and philanthropist, died at age 94, loving father of Betsy Berg. Service on Friday 9:45 AM at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment, Mt. Hebron Cemetery.
        BERG David. The Board of Directors of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale deeply mourns the passing of their esteemed, long standing member, David Berg. A philanthropic leader on behalf of many civic, cultural and humanitarian causes, Mr. Berg expressed his deep commitment to meeting the needs of older people through his wise counsel and support for the residents and the mission of The Hebrew Home. Our profound sympathies go to his beloved daughter Betsy, with a prayer that his name shall be forever a blessing in Israel. Milton A. Gilbert, President Bernard Goldberg, Chairman Daniel Reingold, Exec. V.P.
