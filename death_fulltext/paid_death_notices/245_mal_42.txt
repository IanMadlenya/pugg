
        WEINSTEIN--Albert A., of Stanfordville, New York, born June 4, 1930 in Brooklyn, died after a brief illness on May 27, 2007. Albee, engineer and inventor, is survived by his wife Roseanne, daughters Sharon, Laura, Mobee, Erin and Shauna Weinstein, granddaughters Amanda and Ariana Gonzalez, brother Herbert Weinstein and sister Sheila Fleischer. A tribute will be held on June 21, 2007 from 123pm at The New York Botanical Garden.
