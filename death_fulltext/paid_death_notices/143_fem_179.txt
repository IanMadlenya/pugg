
        VANDERPOOL-Ann West. On December 14th, 1998 Ann West Vanderpool, nee Ann West Wheeler from Providence, Rhode Island, who was the beloved wife to the late Wynant D. Vanderpool, Jr. She is survived by three children: Stuart H. Johnson III of New York City, Thomas W. Johnson of Baltimore, Maryland, and Willow Johnson of New York City, her sister Elizabeth Wheeler Belshaw of Princeton, New Jersey, and five grandchildren. The funeral service will be held at Christ Church Georgetown, 31st and O Streets, N.W. at 11:00 a.m. Monday, December 21, 1998. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent in Ann Vanderpool's name to The Corcoran Gallery of Art, Director's Office, 500 17th Street, N.W., Washington, DC 20006 or to the National Symphony Orchestra, John F. Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts, Washington, DC 20566 Attn: Development Office. Interment private.
