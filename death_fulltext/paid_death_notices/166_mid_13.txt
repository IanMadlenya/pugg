
        HOPKINS-Marylu. The principals and staff of Strategic Resources Corporation extend their heartfelt condolences to their partner, collegue & dear friend, Jim Hopkins, on the passing of his wife, Marylu. Strong until the end, Marylu was a beacon to all of us, and she will be remembered for her will, fortitude & class. Our warmest sympathies are extended to Jim, his sons and the entire Hopkins family. Contributions in Marylu's memory may be made to The Marylu Hopkins Charitable Fund, C/O Strategic Resources Corporation, Carnegie Hall Tower, 152 West 57 Street, 44 floor, New York, NY 10019, which will support charities endorsed by Marylu and her family. Paul Schack Christian Van Pelt Jacques DeLodder And the staff of Strategic Resources Corporation
