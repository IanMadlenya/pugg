
        MOORE--Dr. David B. Age 86 in Bryn Mawr, PA. Survived by his beloved wife, Barbara; loving children Eric, Melissa, and Amanda; and his six grandchildren. He was a WW II veteran of the Navy. He enjoyed a long career as a practicing obstetrician and gynecologist at Presbyterian Hospital and on the faculty at Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons, where he was revered by his patients, his students, and his colleagues. He was a member of numerous professional societies; a Professor of Obstetrics and Gynecology; a Life Trustee at Columbia; and a past President of the New York Obstetrical Society. He will be missed, and long remembered, by his devoted family, friends, and patients. Funeral will be at Brookside Cemetery Chapel, Englewood, New Jersey, Saturday November 26 at 1 PM.
