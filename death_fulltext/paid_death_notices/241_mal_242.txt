
        KANE--Martin. Died February 14, at age 89. He is survived by beloved wife Rhoda of 60 years. Loving father of Phyllis Sondes, Benjamin and Richard, and grandfather to Ryan. Dear brother of Syma Melamed and the late Sylvia Freifeld. He proudly served as a Lieutenant J. G. in the US Coast Guard in WWII. He became a real estate developer, and later President of ARCS, a publicly held engineering firm. He was a past President of Baldwin Jewish Center and Hewlett-East Rockaway Jewish Center. He will be missed.
