
        GOLOMB-Mira I. Died June 11, 1998. The Golomb Cousins Club records with sorrow the first loss of a charter member. Beloved sister of Barry and Adeline Golomb and Sarai and Sam Zitter, she is also survived by many nieces, nephews, grandnieces and a grandnephew. Funeral services will be at Mt. Sinai Memorial Chapel, 454 Cranbury Rd, E. Brunswick, NJ, at noon today.
