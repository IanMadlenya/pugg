
        BREWER-Louise H., died on October 19, 1997 in Hamden, CT. A longtime resident of New York City, she lived at 235 E. 22nd Street. Through her acquaintance with Eleanor Roosevelt in the late 1940's, she was inspired to a lifelong commitment to politics and activism. She worked for numerous Democratic party candidates on both local and national campaigns. She marched down Fifth Avenue in the front line of the National Organization of Women's first demonstration. She worked in the Peace Movement and was deeply concerned with issues of the elderly. She served on the Senior Citizen Council of New York City and was an early instigator of the Meals on Wheels Program. In 1986 she moved to the Whitney Center in Hamden.
