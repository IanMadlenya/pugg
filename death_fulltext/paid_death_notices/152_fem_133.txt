
        SPALDING-Jean U., September 13, 1999. Beloved wife of the late John C. Spalding. Loving aunt of Dr. Ann Simon Koppelman and great-aunt of Erika and Sabrina Simon. A past member of the Colony Club, she was a linens buyer at Lord & Taylor and later operated a linens store on Park Avenue. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Wednesday, September 15, 11:30-12:30 with a prayer service following. Interment, Sleepy Hollow Cemetery.
