
        GOLDRING-Edwin I. Of Longboat Key and Buffalo, died March 18, 2000 in Sarasota, FL. Former Senior Vice President for Jacobson Stores, Inc. Predeceased by his wife, Vera, He is survived by his four children, six grandchildren, a great grandchild and his second wife, Judith. His strength, generosity, extraordinary competence and love for his family will be much missed.
