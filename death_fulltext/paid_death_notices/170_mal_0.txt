
        AMATO-Fulvio ''Jack''. 75. Passed away Thursday, February 15, 2001 in Houston, TX. Born in Brooklyn, NY to John and Fanny Amato, Mr. Amato was Vice President at Chemical Bank for 41 years. In his spare time he was actively involved in the Little League of Long Island, NY. He is survived by his wife Irene, son Robert Amato, M.D. and wife Patti; daughter Lenore Dweck and husband Sean; grandchildren Kyle, Zoe and Quinton and sisters Louise and Ardie.
