
        WERMUTH-Shirley. The Jewish Center mourns the passing of its longtime member and wife of its former dedicated Ritual Director, Rabbi Joseph Wermuth. We fondly remember her gentleness, friendliness and love for her family. May they all be comforted among those who mourn for Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Jacob J. Schacter, Rabbi Samuel Goldman, President Arthur G. Degen, Chairman of the Board Elza Weinman, Sisterhood
