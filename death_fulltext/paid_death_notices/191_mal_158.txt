
        COHEN-Herbert S. Of Lake Worth, FL, died on December 15, 2002 at age 78. Cherished husband of Elayne. Loving father of Barbara (Michael) Greenblatt and Lawrence (Jane) Cohen. Adored grandfather of Robin, Jodi and Jeffrey Greenblatt and Joanna and Brandon Cohen. Beloved brother of Janice Gorsky. He will be deeply missed and always remembered by all who knew and loved him. Services will be private.
