
        GOLDIN-Abe R. Raconteur, oenophile, lover of life, guardian of family and religious traditions, World War II veteran, passed onto his next journey November 12, 1999 after a courageous 10 year battle with cancer. The loss of this loving and distinguished man will be felt by his wife Molly, daughters Holli and Jill, sons-in-law Bert and Sam. grandson Alex, sisters Ruth and Bea, nephews, nieces, friends and colleagues who knew and loved him.
