        STILLERMAN - Maxwell, M.D. The Department of Pediatrics at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset deeply mourns the passing of Max Stillerman, M.D., one of the hospital's Founding Physicians. A specialist in infectious disease in children who was nationally known for his research in streptococcus, he helped make great strides in the treatment of infections in children throughout his long and productive career. Our prayers and heartfelt sympathy go to all the members of his loving family. Mervin Silverberg, M.D. and Staff Department of Pediatrics North Shore University Hospital/ Manhasset
