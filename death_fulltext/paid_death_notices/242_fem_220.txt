
        BEHAR--Amy, (nee Feiman), born August 3, 1957. Amy was surrounded by her family and friends when she died on March 29, 2007, following a long and courageous battle with breast cancer. She was a Brown University graduate, and maintained dual American and Israeli citizenship. Amy excelled as office manager at Princeton Consultants, Inc., and was an active member of the National Council of Jewish Women and of Congregation B'nai Tikvah. Always a loving mother, caring friend, and beloved daughter and sister, Amy is survived by her husband, Arie, her daughters Maya and Leah, her parents Len and Donald Feiman, her sister Debra Feiman Sparks, and her brother Gary Feiman. Funeral services will be held on Friday, March 30, 11:00am, at Congregation B'nai Tikvah, 1001 Finnegans Lane, North Brunswick, NJ. Contributions in Amy's memory may be made to the Susan G. Komen Breast Cancer Foundation.
