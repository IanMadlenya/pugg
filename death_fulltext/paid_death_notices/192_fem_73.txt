
        BROOKS-Hazel M. Died on December 23, 2002 in Atlanta, GA. Formerly of N.Y.C. Daughter of the late Eugene Brooks and Lola Futrell Brooks. A graduate of Fisk University, she worked for Fisk in research and public relations. Later she joined the staff of the March of Dimes/ Birth Defects, retiring in 1982. Memorial services will be held on January 15th, 2003, 11:30 A.M. at the Community Church of New York, 40 East 35th Street, N.Y.C.
