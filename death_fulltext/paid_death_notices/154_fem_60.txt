
        KELSEY-Frances Voorhees. Age 83, died peacefully on November 6, 1999, at her residence in Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, NJ, as a result of a stroke which occurred on October 22. At her bedside were her loving husband of 58 years, H. Burr Kelsey, and her three devoted and loving children, Patricia Kelsey Schultz of Richmond, VA, Peter B. Kelsey of Fairfax, VA, and Marjorie Kelsey White of Annandale, VA. She is also survived by eight grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at the First Presbyterian Church of Hightstown, Thursday, November 11, 1999, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Garden Club of America, 14 East 60th St, New York, New York 10022, or to a charity of your choice would be appreciated.
