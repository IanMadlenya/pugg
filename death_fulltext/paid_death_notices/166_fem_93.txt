
        WEIDMAN-Zora. Lilith Magazine mourns the death of Zora Weidman in Winnipeg on November 10th. She is survived by her children, Dr. Martin Weidman and our editor-in-chief, Susan Weidman Schneider, and their families. At the request of the family, donations in Zora Weidman's memory may be made to Lilith Magazine in New York.
