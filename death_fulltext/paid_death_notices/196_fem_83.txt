
        TROTZKY - Helen. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services acknowledge with sadness the death of Helen Trotzky, beloved grandmother of Cathy Zises, our devoted trustee. Cathy and her husband Seymour have always been great and generous friends to children and families in need. Our condolences to Cathy and Seymour and their entire family. Jean L. Troubh, Pres. Joseph S. Kaplan Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Exec V.P. & CEO
