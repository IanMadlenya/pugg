Bradley Dwight, Susannah Dwight Prout; Jonathan Dwight, Jay Dwight, Leila Dwight, children of her deceased son Jerome; Andrew Emery and Nicolas Emery; and nine great - grandchildren. Memorial service on Thursday, August 2, 11 AM. All Saints Church, 230 E. 60th St., New York City. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to St. Timothy's Alumnae Assoc. of NY c/o St. Timothy's School, Stevenson, MD 21153.
