
        AHRENS--Peter. We mourn his passing on May 22,2006.Peter was a successful broker and money manager.Until his death he was a vice president at Ladenburg Thalmann.In the 60s Peter ran the Champagne Gallery in Greenwich Village. Performers including Richard Pryor,BarbraStreisand and Woody Allen appeared at the club during his tenure. During the 70s Peter entered the broadcast business representing many radio properties across the U.S.Peter was an avid skier and enjoyed his country home in Stanfordville. He will be greatly missed by family,friends and business associates. Donations can be made to, Calvary Fund Inc. Calvary Hospital 1740 Eastchester Rd,Bronx,NY 10461-9955
