
        ROTH--Dennis. The Board and Staff of The Workmen's Circle and Camp Kinder Ring/ Circle Lodge mourn the untimely death of Dennis Roth. Turning grief at the loss of his son Jeffrey into activism, Dennis worked unstintingly to advance brain cancer research and services. We grieve with his beloved wife Arlene, children Eric and Jen, and grandchildren. Peter Pepper, Pres. WC/AR Marty Krupnick, KR/CL Chair
