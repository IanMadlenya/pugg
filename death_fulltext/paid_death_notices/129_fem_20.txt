
        FRANKFURTER-Estelle S. On October 2, 1997, peacefully at home at 101 years of age. Beloved sister of the late Otto, Fred, Felix, Paul and Ella Frankfurter. Devoted aunt of Ruth Lehr and Dorothy Hart. Services 9:45 A.M., Monday, October 6th, ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers, a contribution in her memory to an appropriate charity would be appreciated.
