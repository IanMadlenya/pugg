
        AMES-Lawrence Dr., 94, formerly of Brooklyn, died December 12, 2001 at the Jewish Home of Central New York in Syracuse. A 1931 graduate of New York Medical College. He was a physician in private general practice in Brooklyn since 1932. A past President of the Kings County Medical Society, past President of the NY State chapter of the American Academy of Family Practice, the New York State Board of Professional Medical Conduct and the Jewish Community House of Bensonhurst. He was a Captain in the Medical Corps, World War II. Predeceased by his wife Blanche in 1989. Survived by a son, Dr. Ira of Fayetteville, NY, a sister Edna Braverman of NYC, two grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
