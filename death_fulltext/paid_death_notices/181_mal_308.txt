
        BOLAN-Sean. Age 48. Died peacefully in his sleep February 27, 2002 after a long battle with multiple sclerosis. His courage, unfailing good spirit and goodness of heart inspired all who came in contact with him. Prior to his illness he worked for National Review & was an active member of Young Americans for Freedom. He authored ''The Jaws of the Red Navy''. He is survived by his parents, Marie and Thomas, brothers Douglas and William, sisters Mary and Jacqueline Engelhart, and five nieces and nephews. Mass of Christian Burial on Saturday, 10:15AM, St. Andrew Avellino RC Church, Northern Blvd. and 158th St., Flushing, New York. Interment St. Charles Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to Human Life Foundation, 215 Lexington Ave, New York, NY 10016.
