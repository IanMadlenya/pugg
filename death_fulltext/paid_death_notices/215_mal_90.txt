
        BECK--Franklin B. The Board of Trustees, Associate Board and Administration of Parker Jewish Institute mourn the passing of our dear friend, Franklin Beck. Together with his wife, Shirley, and children, Lois and Lester Garfinkle, Franklin was a devoted advocate and supporter of the Institute's health care and rehabilitation programs and services for the elderly. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Jerry Landsberg, Chairman of the Board Michael N. Rosenblut, President and CEO
