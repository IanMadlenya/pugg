
        JOSELOW-Esther. Devoted wife of sixty-three years, wonderful mother and grandmother died on March 26th after a long illness. Formerly of Palm Beach, Florida, & New York City, New York, she is survived by her beloved husband, Samuel Joselow, her son, Robert Joselow, her daughters Froma Harrop and Marsha Joselow, her sister, Shirley Krasney, her brother Aaron Paderofsky, her five grandchildren and several nieces and nephews. She was loved by all who knew her. Donations may be made in her memory to the Dana Farber Cancer Institute-Richard Kessler Lymphoma Research Fund, 44 Binney Street, Boston, Massachusetts 02115.
