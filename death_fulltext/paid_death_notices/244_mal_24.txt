
        GERSCHWER--Honorable Seymour, known as Sy, died peacefully at his home in Brooklyn on May 2, 2007. He was 82. A graduate of Brooklyn College and St. John's University School of Law, he was elected as a Civil Court Judge in 1984 and served as a Criminal Court Judge and Acting Supreme Court Justice in Brooklyn for the entirety of his ten-year term. After his retirement in 1995 he continued to serve as a Judicial Hearing Officer; he worked in that capacity on April 24, his 82nd birthday, the week before his death. Prior to his election to the bench he had a successful career in the electronics industry and was a longtime civic leader. He is survived by his wife, Margaret, who was with him at the time of his death, sons Lawrence, Scott and David, daughters - in - law Barbara, Rorie and Mara, and grandchildren Stephanie, Jake, Adam, Steven, Benjamin and Caroline. There will be a service on Friday, May 4, 2007, at 11:30am at Parkside Memorial Chapels, 2576 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn.
