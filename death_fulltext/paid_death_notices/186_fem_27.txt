
        FORSBERG - Beatrice Moore. 86, of Old Greenwhich, CT, on June 16, 2002, of heart failure. Wife of the late Torsten Forsberg. Daughter of Charles and Josephine Engstrom Moore of Washington, DC, and Stockholm, Sweden. She was a founder of the Connecticut Branch of the Humane Society of the United States, and served on the Executive Board of the Woodycrest Children's Home and the Women's Bible Society of New York.
