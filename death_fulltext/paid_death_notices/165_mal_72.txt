
        AUSTIN-Maurice, on Wednesday, October 4, 2000, in his 94th year. Devoted husband and beloved companion of Jennie Austin for 71 years; loving father of Alice and John Brumbaugh, Alan and Irene Austin; doting grandfather of Sharon Moser, Sheila Sybrant, Jonathan Austin, and Kathleen Austin; and great-grandfather of five. Distinguished scholar, lecturer, lawyer, and accountant, Maurice Austin, in his seventy-five years of practice, conducted himself with impeccable integrity and dignity. He brought to his clients, family, and friends the benefit of his extraordinary intellect, kind and generous spirit, and sense of humor. He was a recipient of numerous honors and awards, the most recent being the endowment for Maurice Austin Scholars established in his honor by The J.M. Kaplan Fund, Inc., at City College of New York. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be sent to the Maurice Austin Research Fund at the National Jewish Medical and Research Center, 1400 Jackson Street, Denver, CO 80206.
