
        SIMON--Leo. Of Boca Raton, FL, passed away peacefully surrounded by his family on October 19. Formerly of the Lower East Side and Queens, NY. Honest Leo was 87 and began his career driving a truck when he was 13 years old. He was the owner of Simon Haulage, a self made man and entrepreneur. Leo was preceded in death by his beloved wife Frieda Simon. He was widely adored and was greatly loved by his family, Marilyn and Alan Rothstein of Avon, CT, Sandra and Kenny Klein of East Meadow, NY, Debra Simon and Jay Johnson of Durham, NC, as well as his wonderful companion Doris Barkan and his sister Rebecca Horbund. Honest Leo was the loving Poppy of Sharyn and Marisa Rothstein. Funeral services Tuesday, October 21, 11:30 am, at ''Sinai Chapels,'' 162 - 05 Horace Harding Expressway, Fresh Meadows, NY. Interment to follow at Mt. Moriah Cemetery in Fairview, NJ.
