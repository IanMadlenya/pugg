
        DOLKART--Irwin. 1914-2005. Beloved husband of Ellen, father of Jane and Andrew and grandfather of Jennifer. Irwin was an extraordinarily dedicated teacher and chairman inspiring generations of science students at Lafayette High School and the Yeshiva of Flatbush in Brooklyn. He also had a passion for travel which he fulfilled after retiring by becoming a travel agent so that he and Ellen could see the world. Donations to the City College Fund, The City College, 139th St & Convent Ave, NY 10031-9986.
