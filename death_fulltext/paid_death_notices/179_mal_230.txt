
        COLBATH-Edwin H. On December 17, 2001 of Ridge, L.I. Formerly of Forest Hills, Queens. Beloved uncle of Douglas Colbath and his wife, Rebecca. Also survived by many loving friends. Mr. Colbath was appointed to a teaching position with NYC Public School 101 in Forest Hills, Queens in 1939. He pursued his educational career and became principal of P.S. 97 in Woodhaven, Queens and eventually principal of P.S. 101 in 1962. He remained Principal of P.S. 101 until his retirement in 1971. Reposing at O.B. Davis Funeral Homes, 1001 Route 25A, Miller Place, L.I. Visitation Thursday 2-4 and 7-10 PM. Services Friday 11 AM. Interment Maple Grove Cemetery, Kew Gardens, NY.
