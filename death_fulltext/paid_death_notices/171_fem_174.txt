
        LEEDS-Sylvia Shaw, October 26, 1914-April 17, 2001. Devoted wife of the late Maxwell J. Leeds. Loving mother, grandmother and greatgrandmother. She was a Founding Director of the Maxwell Leeds Pediatric Tumor Foundation at Babies Hospital, Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, known today as the Pediatric Cancer Foundation. Services 2PM, April 19, at the Greenwich Reform Synagogue, 257 Stanwich Road, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Breast Center at Greenwich Hospital, 89 Lake Avenue, Greenwich, CT. 06830.
