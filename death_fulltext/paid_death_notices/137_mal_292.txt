
        ASHWORTH-Richard G. Husband of Amy; father of Everard, the late Tucker, and the late Eric; father-in-law of Brooke, and grandfather of Henry and Emma died at home June 24, 1998, from cancer. He was a graduate of Princeton University and Yale Law School, and a former senior partner at Haight, Gardner, Poor & Havens. Throughout his life, Dick devoted himself to public service, most notably Parents and Friends of Lesbians & Gays. He will be remembered for his vigor, convictions and counsel. He is survived by his mother, Leonore Goodspeed Ashworth, sister Joan Nichols, and nephews Terry and Dick Nichols. A memorial service will be held at All Souls Church, Lexington Avenue at 80th Street, at 6:00 pm on July 7, 1998. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations be made to Southern Poverty Law Center (400 Washington Ave., Montgomery, Alabama 36104) or to New York City Parents and Friends of Lesbians & Gays (P.O. Box 553, Lenox Hill Station, New York, New York 10021).
