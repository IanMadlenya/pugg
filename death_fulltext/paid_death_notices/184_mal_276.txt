
        WESTON-Dr. M. Moran. The Trustees, Faculty and Staff of the Mount Sinai School of Medicine and The Mount Sinai Hospital mourn the passing of Dr. M. Moran Weston, an outstanding and compassionate business and civic leader, who served on the Mount Sinai Board of Trustees with dedication for more than 30 years. Dr. Weston was deeply committed to health care at Mount Sinai and to the well being of all the members of our New York community. His thoughtful and steadfast generosity, which included support of programs in patient care, research and medical education, will long be remembered. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Miriam, his son Gregory, his daughter Katherine Weston, and the entire Weston family. Peter W. May, Chairman of the Board The Mount Sinai Medical Center Kenneth I. Berns, M.D., PhD., President and Chief Exec Officer The Mount Sinai Medical Center Nathan G. Kase, M.D., Interim Dean Mount Sinai School of Medicine Barry R. Freedman, President The Mount Sinai Hospital
        WESTON-M. Moran, Rev., PhD. The Board of Trustees and administration of the Sarah Neuman Center for Healthcare and Rehabilitation mourn the passing of the Rev. Dr. M. Moran Weston, a trustee of the Westchester campus of the Jewish Home and Hospital Lifecare System. Dr. Weston's deep commitment to the community was visible through his leadership of one of Harlem's most prominent churches, founding the nation's largest black-owned financial institution and building housing for thousands. He was a visionary and his wisdom and values will be missed. Fred M. Lowenfels, Chairman of the Board Sheldon L. Goldberg, President/CEO Amy Pollinger, Chairman of the Board/ Sarah Neuman Center
        WESTON-M. Moran. The members of The Lotos Club mourn the loss of their distinguished colleague and longtime friend, M. Moran Weston, and send condolences to his family.
