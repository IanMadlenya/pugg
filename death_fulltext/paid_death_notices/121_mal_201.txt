
        LEVY-Benjamin E. Officers and members of the Board of Directors of Medical Development for Israel, Inc. (MDI) and the Schneider Children's Medical Center of Israel are saddened by the passing of Benjamin E. Levy, brother of the Chairman of the Board of MDI, H. Irwin Levy. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife Lillian, his children, 5 grandchildren and other members of his family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Arnold Forster, President Shlomit Manson, National Executive Director
