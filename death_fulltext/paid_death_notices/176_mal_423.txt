
        CROTTY-Kevin R. Of Summit, NJ. Died on September 11, 2001 in the attack on 2 World Trade Center. He was a Managing Director of Sandler O'Neill & Partners. He is survived by his wife Lori, his three children Megan (7), Kyle (5) and Sean (2-1/2). He is also survived by his parents, Mary and Raymond of Frederick, MD, two brothers, Brian of Gaithersburg, MD, and David of Ijamsville MD. A memorial service will be held on October 6th at 2PM in the Presbyterian Church at New Providence, 1307 Springfield Avenue, New Providence, NJ. In lieu of flowers, please make donations to The Kevin R. Crotty Trust (Megan Kyle Sean), Box 1258, Summit, NJ 07901.
