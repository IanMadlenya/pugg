
        CLAYMORE-Sarah R. Died on November 14, 1999 at the age of 98. Daughter of the late Rose & Samuel Clayman. Beloved sister of Marjorie Cole and Jessie Rinsky and the late Anna Levine, Dr. Sigmund, Dr. Lawrence and Abraham Clayman. Devoted sister-in-law of Daniel Cole and Rita Clayman and the late Rosalind Clayman and Edna Clayman. She will be deeply missed. Services are at Riverside Funeral Home, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon on Thursday, November 18, 1999 at 1:30 P.M. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory can be made to the Spanish Portuguese Synagogue in New York or to a charity of your choice.
