
        BERG--Sylvia. Formerly of Jackson Heights, NY, died peacefully at Connecticut Hospice on February 12, 2004. She is survived by her children Dr. Gerald and Barbara Berg of Woodbridge, CT and Stanley Berg of Garnerville, NY. She was the wife of the late Alban (Abe) Berg. Our dear Grandma Sylvia will be greatly missed by grandchildren Adam of Hyde Park, VT, Jason and Laura Berg of North Hampton, MA, and Melissa and Dr. Andrew Lawson of Woodbridge, CT. She was the darling great-grandmother of Matt, Alex, Emily and Samantha. Beloved sister of Mildred and Sidney Spector and Lorraine and Harold Mechlin. Loving aunt and friend to many nieces and nephews and caring companion to Nathan Katz. Her enduring legacy is her family and friends who love her deeply. Services at Schwartz Bros.-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 114-03 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, NY at 12 Noon. Contributions may be made to the Jewish Center of Jackson Heights.
