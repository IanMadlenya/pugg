
        NADEL--Joel S. The Union for Reform Judaism mourns with profound sorrow the death of Joel S. Nadel of Boca Raton, FL, a member of its Board of Trustees since 2003. In a variety of roles, Joel served the Union with devotion. We extend deepest condolences to his wife, Renee, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and may Joel's memory be for a blessing. Robert M. Heller, Chairman Rabbi Eric H. Yoffie, President
