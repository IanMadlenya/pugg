
        VOLLERS-Joseph E., Sr. Age 86. Died on January 30, 2000 in Richmond, California. Retired from New York City Fire Department as Battalion Chief in 1967. Survived by his beloved wife Josephine E. Vollers of El Cerrito, CA; sisters Gertrude Leibel and Florence Miller; sons Joseph E. Vollers, Jr. and Charles S. Vollers; daughters Judith Martin and Maryanne Vollers Campbell; eleven grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
