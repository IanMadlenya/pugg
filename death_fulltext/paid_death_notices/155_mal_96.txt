
        BOWLING-Leslie. The NYU Postdoctoral Community mourns the loss of our dear colleague and friend, Les Bowling. He was a Psychologist and Psychoanalyst in private practice, supervising faculty at the NYU Postdoctoral program in Psychotherapy and Psychoanalysis, and faculty at the Suffolk Institute. A wonderful, caring, compassionate analyst and great outdoorsman. Services will be at the Fox Funeral Parlor, 98-07 Ascan Avenue, Forest Hills, Wednesday December 8, 7:00-9:00 PM, and Thursday December 9, 2:00-5:00 and 7:00-9:00 PM. Funeral arrangements will be announced.
