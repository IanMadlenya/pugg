
        REICH-Simon, age 92. Cherished husband of the late Anna. Beloved father of Drs. Walter & Raymond. Devoted father-in-law of Tova and Sue. Adored grandfather of Daniel, David and Rebecca, and of Yael (and David Hillel), Shlomo (and Adina), Yitzchak, Yosef and Avigail. Survivor of the Holocaust. His dignity, modesty and quiet strength has always been, and will forever be, a blessing for us all. Shiva will be begin Tuesday night, October 13, and end Monday morning October 19, at the home of Dr. Raymond Reich, 131 Dover Street, Brooklyn, New York. Phone (718-646-2642).
