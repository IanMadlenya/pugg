
        ALBERT-Roy E., M.D., Died March 25, 2002 at home in Cincinnati, OH. During his 50 year career in cancer research, he was from 1985-1994 Director of Environmental Medicine at the University of Cincinnati and was Vice Chair of The Department of Environmental Health at NYU. He was a graduate of Columbia College and NY School of Medicine. His wife of 56 years Abigail Lewin and children Daniel, Julie, and Elizabeth Albert and Lucy and Tom Bermingham, his brother Bernard and six grandchildren deeply mourn his loss. A memorial service will be held at Cedars of Lebanon, Spring Grove Cemetery, Cincinnati, April 20th at 11 AM.
