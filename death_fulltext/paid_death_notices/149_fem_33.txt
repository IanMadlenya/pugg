
        GOLDSTEIN-Helene. The New York Road Runners Club mourns the passing of Helene Goldstein, a dear friend and supporter of this Club and of the New York City Marathon for more than 20 years. She was an inspiration and a guide to her beloved husband, Joey, who served as our Public Relations Representative, and to her three sons, Robert, Adam and Jared. New York Road Runners Club Allan Steinfeld, President
