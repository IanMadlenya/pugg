
        FEINSTEIN--Mary Lilly. Of NYC and the Hamptons, passed away May 16, 2005. She was active in philanthropy all her life and was known as the ''Mitzah Lady''. For over 30 years, she was Executive Vice President of the Realty Foundation of NY, which is the philanthropic arm of the real estate industry in NY. She is survived by her husband Melvin Feinstein, her two sons Joseph and Robert Lilly, her grandsons Orion, Jamie and Jason and her brothers Joseph and Norman Paul. Services will be held Thursday, May 19th, 11:30 A.M. at Plaza Funeral Home, 630 Amsterdam Ave.
