
        RIDDER-Ethelette French Tucker. Of New York City and Southampton on Tuesday, March 16 at Lenox Hill Hospital after a short illness. Mother of Susan Lane Ridder and Eric Ridder, Jr. of Bedminster, NJ and grandmother of Catherine Tucker Ridder and Eric Ridder, III. Longtime companion and special friend of Nelson Montgomery Loud. Member of the Board of Trustees and former Chairman of the Womens Committee of Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St, Friday March 19 from 11AM to 5PM. Services will be held at St. James Church, Madison Ave. at 71st St., New York City, Monday, March 22 at 12 noon. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club, 1930 Randall Avenue, Bronx, New York 10473.
        RIDDER-Ethelette French Tucker. Mrs. Ridder, a loyal member of Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club serving as a trustee and chairman of the Women's Committee for more than thirty years. She was a wonderful friend and will be greatly missed. Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club
        RIDDER-Ethelette French Tucker. The Designer's Committee of Kips Bay Boys and Girls Club mourn the passing of Mrs. Ridder, a longtime supporter. Richard L. Ridge, Chairman
