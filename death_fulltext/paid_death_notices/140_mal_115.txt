
        ISAAC-Hyman. A prominent New Jersey lawyer & former president of the Union County Bar Association, died at age 88 on September 8, 1998. He was a loving gentleman who will be missed. He tried cases before the Federal District Courts in NJ and NY and handled appeals before the U.S. Supreme Court. He is survived by his wife, Frances, a sister, Cecilia Eisenberg of Elizabeth, N.J., two stepsons, Major Howard Holland of Durham, N.H., Richard Holland of Framingham, Massachusetts, five grandchildren, six great-grandchildren & two nieces. Services are today, 1 P.M., at Higgins and Bonner Funeral Home, Westfield, New Jersey.
