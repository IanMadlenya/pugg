
        KELLNER-Robert J. Suddenly on July 1, 2000. Beloved and devoted husband of Estelle. Wonderful, loving father of Nancy, Andrew and Jeffrey. Proud adoring grandfather to Sophie Jae. Caring father-in-law of Chris and Samantha. Devoted son of the late Sam and Stella, and step-son of the late Sadie. Beloved uncle, brother-in-law and brother. A 1946 graduate of Amherst College. The shining light of all of our lives. A kind, gentle and beautiful soul who brought so much joy to his family and friends. A true prince among men. Services Monday, 9:30 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Interment Cedar Park Cemetery.
