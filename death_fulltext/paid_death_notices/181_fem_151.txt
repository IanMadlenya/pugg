
        COOLIDGE-Martha (Muffy) Henderson. Of Cambridge, Massachusetts, 77. Died on February 11, after a long illness. The widow of Harold Jefferson Coolidge, she is survived by her sister-in-law, Maia Henderson, her cousin, Theodore Conant, many Coolidge relatives and dear friends. A memorial service will be held on March 23, at noon in Harvard's Memorial Church. Donations may be made to The Squam Lake Natural Science Center, P.O. Box 173, Holderness, N.H. 03145.
