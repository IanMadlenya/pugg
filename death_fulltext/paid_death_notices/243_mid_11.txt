
        STARR--Adele Louise Landau, 90, died peacefully on April 24, 2007. Survived by Lee, her loving husband of 64 years, brother Alfred, his wife Bobbie, and brother-in-law, Bill Teel. Beloved mother of Jonathan and Frederick, adored mother-in-law of Kelly Starr and Kitty Kiefer and loving granddaughter, Hazel Kiefer. Attended Radcliffe College. Community activist, elegant lady and Proust lover. Funeral private. Family will receive at home on April 26, from 3 to 6pm, and 7:30 to 9:30pm. In lieu of flowers, send contributions to Mamaroneck Library Building Fund, 136 Prospect St., Mamaroneck, NY 10543, care of Mary Soto.
