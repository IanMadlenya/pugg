
        COHN--Mae Kilpatrick, died peacefully on May 10, 2005 after a long and courageous battle with cancer. Loving and devoted mother of Margaret Cohn, beloved wife of the late Eugene Cohn. Family will receive friends on Thursday from 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Funeral services Friday, 10AM at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in Mae Cohn's name to the charity of your choice or to the Mary Manning Walsh Nursing Home, 1339 York Ave., New York, NY 10021.
        COHN--Mae. North Shore Country Club records with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Mae Cohn. We extend our deepest condolences to her family. Howard S. Jacobs, President Sandra J. Ash, Secretary
