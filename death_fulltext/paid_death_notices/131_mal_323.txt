
        PAPPAS - George N., M.D. 1918-1997, Tuesday, December 23rd. Dedicated neurosurgeon who served residents of Westchester County for more than forty years. He was the former Director of Surgery, Founding Chief of Neurosurgery and major patron of Phelps Memorial Hospital Center. He will be missed by his friends and colleagues at Phelps. Our deepest sympathy to his family. The Board, Staff and Administration at Phelps Memorial Hospital Center
