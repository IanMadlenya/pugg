Friends may call Sunday 7-9 and Monday 2-5 and 7-9 at Frank E. Campbell 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. Massive Christian burial Tuesday 10am St. Patricks Cathedral 5th Ave. at 50th St NYC. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Guiding Eyes for the Blind, Yorktown Heights, NY.
