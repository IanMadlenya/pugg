
        WOLFSON - Marjorie. Beloved wife of the late Paul Wolfson. Devoted mother of Steven Wolfson, M.D., and Sarah Spoerri. Adored grandmother of Ellen P. Wolfson, Roger S. Wolfson, Michael Spoerri, Karl Spoerri and Sasha Spoerri. She was a psychiatric social worker at the Maimonides Hospital. Contributions to the Shorefront Jewish Geriatric Center, West 29th Street at Boardwalk, Brooklyn, New York. Services were held at ''The Riverside.''
