
        ROBINSON - Reverend George A. III, (Hoppy), from Marion, MA., Minister of The Unitarian Universalist Church, Flushing. Died September 22 at age 51. He also served congregations in East Rochester, MA., and Ashby, MA. He was a passionate hiker and lover of wilderness. He will be deeply missed by his family and all those he touched in his life. His family asks that donations be made to Joslin Diabetes Center, 1 Joslin Place, Boston, MA. 02215. A memorial service will be held at The Flushing Church, Sept. 29, 11 AM, 14754 Ash Avenue, 718-353-3860.
