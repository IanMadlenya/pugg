
        CAPECI-Theodore, J. MD. The Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital were saddened to learn of the recent passing of Theodore J. Capeci, MD, Honorary Physician, who joined our Department of Surgery in 1955 and served as the Chief of Plastic Surgery from 1986 until 1988. Dr. Capeci was highly respected by his peers and patients alike for his outstanding skills as a cosmetic surgeon. A kind and compassionate physician, he was also a devoted and gifted teacher. Lenox Hill Hospital honors the memory of this exceptional physician who exemplified the highest standards of his profession. We extend our deepest sympathies to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman, Board of Trustees Gladys George, President & CEO Stephen A. Obstbaum, M.D., President, Medical Board Valavanur A. Subramanian, M.D., Director, Surgery
