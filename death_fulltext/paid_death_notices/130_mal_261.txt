
        SAPIENZA-Nicolo ''Pat.'' Founder of ''The Theatre Bar Restaurant'' of 45th St. passed away peacefully 11/22/97 at the age of 92. Friend of many young budding actors and actresses of the 40's and 50's, with an encouraging word, sympathetic ear and a free meal when needed. Loving father of Mr. Sam Sapienza, Mrs. Tina Grillo and Dr. Frank Sapienza. Grateful fatherin-law of Gloria, Joseph and May. An inspiration to his 13 grandchildren and 32 great-grandchildren. Reposing at The Branch Funeral Home of Nesconset, New York.
