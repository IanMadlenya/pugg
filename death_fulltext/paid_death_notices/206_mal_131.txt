
        RIVA--Frank. The Board of Directors, staff, membership and participants of the Association for the Help of Retarded Children, New York City Chapter, wish to convey their condolences to the family of Frank Riva, one of the founders of the Association, and longtime member. Frank, You remain in our memory for the depth of your commitment. Frank Riva is survived by his wife, Margaret, who was for many years a member of the original Board of Directors of the Association, and three children: Frank, Jr., Lorraine and Carol. Funeral Mass will be held Saturday, 11 AM, March 13, at Our Lady of Peace in Lynbrook, NY. Genevieve O'Neil, President Michael Goldfarb, Exec. Dir
