
        SPORN-Shirley A. The Light of My Life. The Joy of My Life. For 55 Glorious Years and Forever. ''Sugar'' Sporn, wife of Woodrow Sporn, died yesterday after a long illness in White Plains hospital. Courageous & brave, she endured many years of silent suffering. Shirley is survived by daughters Andrea and Susan; grandchildren, Alexandra, Daniel, Abbey and Douglas; extraordinary son-in-law, Dr. Jerome Haber; sister, Roslyn Reiffel; nephews and nieces, Dr. Max and Joan Sporn, Drs. Susanne and Robert Reiffel, Dr. James and Bonnie Reiffel, Linda and Jeffrey Halbreich; brother-inlaw Morty Halbreich. Shirley was a Scarsdale resident 38 years; active in the Parent School Association, a Girl Scout leader and involved in many other community activities; in the past twelve years, she was a resident of Purchase and a Board member of the Purchase Free Library. She was the first woman to serve on the Board of Westchester Cerebral Palsy. She was an avid bridge player and at one time was captain of the Fairview Country Club Women's Bridge Team. She will be missedmissed-missed. All my love, Sugar, forever and forever. ''Semper Fidelis'' Woody. Graveside services, Monday, October 23, at 1 PM, Mount Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, New York. Family and friends welcome. Visiting at family home in Purchase, NY after the service.
        SPORN-Shirley. Sugar was the best and the brightest. We have lost a dear friend. Our love and deepest sympathy to Woody, Andrea, Susan and their families. Arleen and Justin Miller
