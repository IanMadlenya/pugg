
        MERIN-Herbert L., passed away on June 15th, 2000 at his residence in Palm Beach after a long illness. Beloved husband of Rita. Loving father of Andrew (Violetta), Kenneth, Neil (Susanna). Cherished grandfather of David, Dara, David, Laura, Caroline, Madeline, Ian and Natalie. After serving as a General's Aide in the US Army, he was a prominent toy manufacturer. Chapel service 10 AM today at Riverside-Gordon Memorial Chapel, 4714 Okeechobee Blvd., West Palm Beach, FL with a graveside service Thursday at Beth Moses Cemetery in Farmingdale, NY under the direction of I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 631-499-6060. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that those who wish may send contributions to the Herbert Merin Lewy Body Disease Research Fund at Massachusetts General Hospital, Development Office, 100 Charles River Plaza, Suite 600, Boston, MA 02114-2792.
        MERIN-Herbert. June 15, 2000. The Board of Governors and the Members of Glen Oaks Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their esteemed member, Herbert, and extend their heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Rita and his family. Board of Governors Richard A. Horowitz, President
