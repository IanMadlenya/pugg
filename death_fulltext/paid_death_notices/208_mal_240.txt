        ARLOW--Jacob, MD. The NYU Psychoanalytic Institute (NYU Medical Center) and the Psychoanalytic Association of New York mourn the loss of our most highly esteemed teacher and colleague, Jacob Arlow, MD. Dr. Arlow, one of the foremost psychoanalytic scholars and practitioners, reshaped modern psychoanalytic theory and practice. His extraordinary contributions, which span over half a century, have been essential to the education of several generations of psychoanalysts. While his presence will be sorely missed, his wisdom will remain forever and will continue to enlighten future generations. Our condolences go out to his family. Robert Fischel, MD Director, NYUPI Samuel Herschkowitz, MD Chair, Education Committee, NYUPI Charles Goodstein, MD President, PANY Milagros Picon, MD Secretary, PANY
        ARLOW -- Jacob. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of our distinguished member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, Pres. Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
