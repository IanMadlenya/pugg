
        DAVIS-Dr. Hazel Grubbs. A memorial service for Dr. Hazel Grubbs Davis will be held on Saturday, November 13, at 2 PM, National Cemetery Chapel, Farmingdale, NY. Officiating at the Service will be Rev. Maggie Morrison. She was promoted to glory at the age of 85. She will long be remembered as a college professor at Slippery Rock University, PA; Oneonta, NY; University of Arizona; Hofstra University and CUNY. She served for over 50 years on the White House Committee for Early Child Development, International Early Childhood, etc. Reception will follow the service at Syosset, NY.
