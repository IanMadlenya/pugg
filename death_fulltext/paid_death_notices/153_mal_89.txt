
        WAGNER-Robert Dean, on October 6, 1999 at age 73. An esteemed and beloved professor of English Literature at Sarah Lawrence College from 1953 until his retirement in May of 1999. He served as Dean of the College from 1971-1976, and as head of the Sarah Lawrence College Summer School in London. He leaves his brother C. Roland Wagner; his wife Anneliese; his daughters Elise and Carrie; son-in-law Robin Stout; grandchildren Leah and Alice; and his companion Susan Silver. Funeral Service Friday, 11 A.M., Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY. Interment Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, New York. Memorial donations in his memory may be made to Sarah Lawrence College, Bronxville, New York.
