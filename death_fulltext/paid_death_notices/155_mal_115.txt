
        ZIMMER-Max J. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of our senior Trustee and Benefactor Max J. Zimmer, a visionary in the building of our day school. He headed the three generations of Zimmer family members connected with our congregation. To his devoted wife, Georgia; children, Joel and Karen, and Anita; grandchildren, Gary and Jill; and the entire family our deepest sympathy. Service today at Park East Synagogue, 163 E. 67 St., 9:30 AM. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
