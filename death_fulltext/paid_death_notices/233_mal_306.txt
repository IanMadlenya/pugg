
        SORIAN--Jack. 94, of New York City, NY. On June 26, 2006. Beloved uncle of Joseph, Janice A., David, Janice R., Estelle, Sidney, Stephen and Richard. Jack had been acting since 1945, and had been involved in theatre productions in Off-Broadway and Summer Stock. He was Alan King's understudy in the Broadway production of ''The Impossible Years.'' Throughout Jack's life he was dedicated and generous to his siblings--and all of their families. Jack served as a Medical Technician during WW II in the Army. Services 12 noon at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 12 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon, NY.
