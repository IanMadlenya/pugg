
        HACKETT-Bruce C. Age 61, died at his home in NYC, Friday evening, March 10, 2000. Mr. Hackett was formerly head of Global Equities and a member of the Executive Committee of Salomon Bros., Inc. He was most recently co-head of Global Equities at Wasserstein Perrela and Co. until early 1999 when he resigned for health reasons. Mr. Hackett was born and raised in Easton, PA, from which he attended the Lawrenceville School and Brown University. His career on Wall Street spanned nearly four decades, starting at Citibank, Bank of NY, Salomon Bros. and Wasserstein Perrela. He was also a member of the Board of Directors of the CBOE and the National Association of Securities Dealers (NASD). His wife Joann survives him with their two children Henry, age 11, and Daisy, age 9. In addition, he is survived by his brother Leeds Hackett of NYC and another daughter Ann Hackett of Pecos, NM. Mr. Hackett was a member and Governor of the Metropolitan Club of NY, a member of the Ardsley Country Club and the Southampton Club and the Meadow Club of Southampton, NY, and a Board Member of Project ALS.
