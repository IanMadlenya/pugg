
        TRIPPE--John Terry. Died peacefully on October 29 in Mechanicsville, PA after a long illness. He was 67. Husband of Jane Reis and proud father of Juan T. Trippe II. Beloved son of the late Betty and Juan Trippe. His sister Betsy T. DeVecchi and brothers Charles W. Trippe and Edward S. Trippe survive as well as 11 nieces and nephews. Private funeral services will be held at Greenwood Cemetery, Brooklyn, NY on November 17. Contributions in John's memory may be sent to the National Alliance for Research on Schizophrenia and Depression, 60 Cutter Mill Road, Great Neck, NY 11021.
