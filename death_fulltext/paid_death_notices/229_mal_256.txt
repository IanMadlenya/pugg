
        STERN--Robert J., 58. Of New City, NY, died Monday, February 20, 2006. Mr. Stern was a retired NYC School Principal. Mr. Stern is survived by his beloved wife Risa, the joys of his life, daughters Nicole and Robyn, his loving mother Edith, his sister Rima, and his brother-in-law, Stephen Julien. He was predeceased by his father Irving. A funeral service will be held Wednesday, February 22, 2006, 11:00 AM, at ''Temple Beth Sholom'', 228 New Hempstead Road, New City, NY. Entombment will immediately follow on the grounds of New Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. A period of mourning will be observed at the Stern residence through Monday, February 27, 2006.
