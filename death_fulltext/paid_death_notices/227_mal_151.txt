
        CUNNINGHAM--William T. 63, of Denville died on December 4, 2005 at Morristown Memorial Hospital. Husband of 32 years to Janice D. Cunningham nee Dolan, of Denville. Also surviving is his mother Nellie S. Cunningham of Chatham, a brother, Robert, of Newtown, PA. and three nieces and a nephew. A memorial service will be planned for a later date. He graduated from the University of Iowa. Mr. Cunningham retired in 2000 as the Vice President of the Bank of New York. Bill was a long-time member of the Northern New Jersey Region Porsche Club of America and a HAM radio operator with the call sign K2WTC.
