
        HALL-Josephine Dawes. Widow of Frederick Bailey Hall. Died September 29, 1997. She was a resident of Norfolk, CT and San Francisco, CA. She was born in Englewood, NJ, daughter of Mr. and Mrs. Henry Franklin Dawes. She was a graduate from Milton Academy and Sarah Lawrence College. She was active in Planned Parenthood of Bergin County, the Hospital Auxiliary in Englewood, NJ and Sheltering Arms in NYC. She was a member of the Colonial Dames and of the Colony Club in New York. Mrs. Hall is survived by a daughter Adele Zachrisson, two grandsons Carl and Christopher Zachrisson and a son-in-law Carl Zachrisson all of San Francisco, California.
