
        De MENIL-Dominique. The Trustees and staff of Dia Center for the Arts note with great sadness the passing of our beloved Trustee, Dominique De Menil. We'll miss her guiding hand, keen judgement and generous spirit. We extend our heartfelt sympathies and condolences to her children, grandchildren and greatgrandchildren. Dominique inspired us and we will all miss her. The world has lost a guiding light. Board of Trustees Charles Wright, Chairman Michael Govan, Director
