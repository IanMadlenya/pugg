
        BRYAN-Katherine. Passed 3/21/ 97, in Birmingham, AL. Resident in NYC area for 45 years, internationally acclaimed operatic singer, commercial real estate appraiser, former President of the Third Street Music School in Manhattan. She is so very much loved and missed by family members and friends. There will be a memorial service at the Liederkranz Club, 6 E. 87 St, Saturday, April 26 at 6PM.
