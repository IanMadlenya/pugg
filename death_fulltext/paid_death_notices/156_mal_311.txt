
        TUBERT-George. The friends and family of George and Sabine are profoundly saddened to announce George's death at age 42 on January 20, 2000. Their love, dignity and humanity remained intact even during the worst of times. George would want all of us to live life to the maximum of our capacities and desires. He shall live forever in our hearts. Contributions may be made in George's name to the Order of: ''Supportive Care Program-PPPS'' at the Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Ave, NY NY 10021.
