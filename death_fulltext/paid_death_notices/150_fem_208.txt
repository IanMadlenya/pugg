
        SHEERR-Lillian Jamison. 98, a resident of Palm Beach, FL for more than 50 years. Died in a car accident Friday, July 23, 1999. She was an active member of the Palm Beach Country Club and Philmont Country Club. She was the much loved matriarch of her large and extended family. She is survived by her daughters, Muriel Kaplan and Simone Goldblum of Palm Beach, FL; her brother Sidney Jamison of Philadelphia and 7 grandchidren and 13 great-grandchildren. Services will be at Beth Sholom Synagogue, Elkins Park, PA at 2PM Monday, July 26, 1999. Arrangements by Goldsteins' Funeral Home of Philadelphia, PA.
