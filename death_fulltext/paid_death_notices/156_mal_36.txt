
        CRANDALL-Roland D. Of Greenwich, Ct. on January 2, 2000. Survived by his wife Edna and his daughter Mary Ann. A graduate of Greenwich High School, he served in the Army Air Force 3 years stationed in India. Later graduated from the University of South Carolina in 1950 with a degree in Business/Economics. He joined Allen and Company Inc. as an investment banker in 1956. He served as a Director or Investment Banking Consultant to several publicly held companies. An avid sailor, he enjoyed cruising the northeast coast. He was a member of the New York and Old Greenwich Yacht Clubs. Memorial contributions may be made to the Association for Rescue at Sea, 231 W. Lockwood Ave., St. Louis, Mo. 63119. Services will be private.
