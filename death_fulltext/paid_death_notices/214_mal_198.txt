
        GRAY--Arthur, M.D. The physicians and staff of Queens-Long Island Medical Group are deeply saddened by the passing of our beloved friend, esteemed shareholder, and cherished member of our Board of Directors. Dr. Gray was a distinguished and dedicated Orthopedist with our Group for almost 30 years. He was admired and respected by his patients and colleagues. Those of us who knew him well loved him and will profoundly miss him. We extend our condolences to his beloved Sigrid, to his daughters, Nicole and Heather, and to all of his family and friends. Suman Reejsinghani, M.D., President and CEO
