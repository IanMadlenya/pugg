
        DUCKWORTH - Frank. On July 26, 2002, at his residence in Vienna, VA. He was the husband of Katherine Alexander Duckworth, father of John and Katherine R. Duckworth, and grandfather of Sara and Alex Duckworth and Rebecca and Austin Johntra. Mr. Duckworth was with the legal department of Pfizer, Inc, and retired in 1986 as General Counsel. Memorial services will be held at Money & King Funeral Home, 171 West Maple Ave., Vienna VA, on Monday, August 5, at 11 AM.
