The National Women's Division of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with profound sorrow the passing of Caryl Palin, a member of our New York Chapter Executive Board. The Palin family has been an integral part of Einstein's Family Day in the Hamptons, and Caryl's participation will be deeply missed. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to our very dear friends, Roxanne and Dean Palin, to Caryl's beloved husband Michael, and the entire family. Yeshiva University Richard M.
