
        GOLDSTEIN-Reuben P., 94. March 29, 1997. Remembered with great love by his children Judie and Howard Ganek; his grandchildren Tori, Danielle and David Ganek; and his great-grandchildren Harrison and Nicholas Ganek. Our lives are greatly enriched by the standards and love with which he lived his life. He will be sorely missed. Services private. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Educational Alliance, 197 East Broadway, New York, NY 10002.
