
        WALLACE-Julius. Born in the Bronx, New York in his 89th year. One of ten children. Survived by his brother, Louis Wallace, his nephew and companion Melvin Wallace, his sister-in-law and companion Rita Wallace & numerous nieces and nephews. Julius was an officer of Hall Jewelers, Inc., a retail jeweler and importing business where he worked with his brothers, Nat and David. Julius was a veteran of the U.S. Marine Corp. Services will be held Sunday, 1 P.M., Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, 76th Road and Queens Blvd. in Forest Hills.
