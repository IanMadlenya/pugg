
        ROSS-Morton S. Age 91, of Larchmont, NY, died February 28, 2003. Beloved husband of Alice (Plaut) for 62 years. Loving father of Stephen, Margaret Ross Tobey, James and Anne (Kaufman). Adored grandfather of Samantha, Stephanie, Gary, Scott and Jennifer (Moss). A caring and gentle man who will be remembered and missed by the many of all ages and backgrounds whose lives he touched. Funeral service Monday, 12:30 PM, at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 E. Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, NY. Donations in his memory may be made to the Restless Leg Syndrome Foundation, Rochester, MN 55902.
