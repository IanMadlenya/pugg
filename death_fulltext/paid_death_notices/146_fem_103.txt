
        EPSTEIN-Minnie, 103. Died peacefully at home on March 6. Her loss will be mourned by her loving children, Mary Jo Hamburger and William Easton, and their spouses, Robert Hamburger and Margarita Easton. Her grandchildren will miss Nana since she was important to all of them, Bobby and Peter Hamburger, their wives, Ilene and Joy; Elizabeth Traub, her husband, James; Tom Easton, his wife, Eliza. She thought her greatgrandchildren were great, Zoe and Max Hamburger, Alex Traub, David Hamburger and Isaac Easton. They in turn thought she was magic, 103 years young. Contributions in her memory may be sent to your favorite charity.
