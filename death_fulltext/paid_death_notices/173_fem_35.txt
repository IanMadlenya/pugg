Engel, Brian Allan Siegel, devoted grandmother of Liana Laura Engel, Kyra Jacqueline and Taryn Ruth Siegel. Loving mother-in-law of Arlene Siegel and Robert Engel. ''With her sunny disposition, she was never alone.''
