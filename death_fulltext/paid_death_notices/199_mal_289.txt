
        HALLIDAY -- Ernest M. Known to his friends as Hal. Died August 1, at home in Manhattan, at age 89. Editor of American Heritage Magazine for many years. Author of ''Understanding Thomas Jefferson'' and ''John Berryman and the Thirties'' and ''The Ignorant Armies''. Hal was one of the first important critics of Ernest Hemingway. Hal was a passionate lifelong advocate of civil liberties and the separation of church and state. Memorial service to be held in Graduate Lounge of Philosophy Hall, Columbia University, at 4 P.M. on Saturday, September 20.
