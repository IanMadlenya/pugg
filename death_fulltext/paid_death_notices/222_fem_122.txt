
        FRANKEL--Gloria. On July 21, 2005. She was 83. Gloria cofounded Trimtime Buttons and used both her fashion acumen and high quality standards to build a successful business. She helped others pursue their dreams. Beloved mother of Peter and Eric. Cherished grandmother of Elisabeth and Michael. Dear mother-in-law of Susan. She will be greatly missed by her family and friends. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Memorial Hospital, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
