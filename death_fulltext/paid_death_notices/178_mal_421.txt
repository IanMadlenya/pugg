
        SULLIVAN-Robert J., 84. Of Princeton, NJ, and Vero Beach, FL, died on November 21, 2001, at the Medical Center at Princeton. He was a proud member of the Class of 1939 at Princeton University. He is survived by his wife of 56 years, Janet, and children Barbara Sullivan of Wilmington, NC, Robert J. Sullivan Jr. of Sag harbor, NY, and Martha Sword of Princeton, NJ, as well as five grandchildren. His professional career spanned 45 years at Lenox, Inc. A memorial service was held in Princeton on November 26.
