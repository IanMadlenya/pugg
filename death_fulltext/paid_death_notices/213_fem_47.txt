
        LAND--Minna. 73. Died on October 6, 2004 at her home in NYC after a brief illness. Minna's unbridled optimism, intellectual curiosity and devotion to friends and family is the legacy she leaves behind. She is survived by her husband of 51 years, Arthur, their three sons, Jeffrey, Douglas and Peter, three grandchildren, Maya, Sara and Cole, daughters - in - law Wendee Wechsberg, Lynn Peebles and Kim Land. Services will be at Temple Israel Cemetery, Hastings-on-Hudson today Friday at 3PM. Contributions can be made to Smith College attn: Gift Accounting, 33 Elm St, Northampton, MA 01063.
