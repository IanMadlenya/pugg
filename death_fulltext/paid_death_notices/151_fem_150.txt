
        BECK-Joyce A. (Davis), 67, of Plymouth, Mass., formerly of Sandwich, Mass., died Tuesday. She was the wife of John W. Beck. Besides her husband, she is survived by her mother, Iva (Horner) Davis, of Bordentown, N.J. A memorial service will be held at 11 AM Friday, August 20, at St. John's Episcopal Church, Main Street, Sandwich. There are no visiting hours. The family suggests that in lieu of flowers donations be made in her memory to Medical Oncology Research & Education, c/o Jerry Younger, M.D., Massachusetts General Hospital, 100 Blossom St., Boston, MA 02114-2696.
