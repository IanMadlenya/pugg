
        BECK--Dr. Arthur. Age 90, on November 11, 2005 in Trumbull, CT. Born in Brooklyn. Devoted husband of the late Ruth Beck; beloved father of Dr. Lawrence & Alice Beck, Dr. Michael & Marcia Beck; adored grandfather of Sara, Andrew, Marissa, Morgen, and brother of Mildred Jonas. Dr. Beck was a graduate of Columbia University in 1936 and the Medical College of Glasgow and Edinborough in 1940. He served in the military as a troop ship physician until 1945, and practiced 37 years in Brooklyn until his retirement to Florida in 1984. He will be deeply missed by his family, friends and patients. Services Sunday Mt. Ararat Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. Shiva Monday, Tuesday and Wednesday at the home of Dr. Michael & Marcia Beck. Memorial contributions to Bridgeport Hospital Foundation, 267 Grant St., Bridgeport, CT. 06610.
