        HOPP--Lani. HADASSAH, the Women's Zionist Organization of America, Inc., expresses its deep sorrow at the passing of Lani Hopp, beloved wife of Robert, cherished mother of Dana and Jenna. Loving daughter of David, of blessed memory and Sylvia Salzberg; devoted sister of Barry and Jane Salzberg and caring aunt of Mark and Karen. As a Guardian of the Dream, Lani was a generous benefactor of HADASSAH. She was truly devoted to Israel and the Jewish people. Her generous legacy will live on through the visionary projects she supported at the HADASSAH Medical Organization. She will be deeply missed by all who knew her. Bonnie Lipton, National Pres
