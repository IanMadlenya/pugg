
        BROWN-Patrick J., Capt. FDNY, lost his life September 11, 2001. His soul, spirit and life were honored on November 9, 2001. His remains were recovered December 14, 2001. There will be a visiting on Saturday, December 29, 2001, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. 2-5 and 7-9 PM for family and close friends. Funeral service at 7:30 PM.
