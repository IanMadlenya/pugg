Her kindness and spirit will be greatly missed. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, Sacred Heart RC Church, Bronx, NY, 11 AM. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers a contribution may be made to Kateri Nursing Home or the charity of choice.
