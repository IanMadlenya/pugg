
        SCHINDLER-Hans. On March 27, 2002, in his 90th year. Beloved husband of the late Marianne. Loving brother of William Schindler and cherished uncle of Erica Neumann. He will be missed by all his family and friends. Funeral services Monday, 11:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. Interment to follow at Old Mount Carmel Cemetery.
        SCHINDLER-Hans. The Board of Directors and staff of Selfhelp Community Services and United Help mourn the loss of Hans Schindler, Sc.D., a dear friend and exemplary Board member for 25 years. His leadership and unwavering commitment to ensuring quality services for elderly New Yorkers, especially survivors of Nazi persecution, inspired all of us. Dr. Schindler's dedication, integrity and gentle warmth will be greatly missed. Our sympathy is with his family.
