
        GASTON-SHAPIRO-Gloria L. Retired foreign service officer, on Sunday March 24, 2002 at her home in Washington D.C. She had a distinguished career as a diplomat in the U.S. Department of State and other government agencies. Wife of the late Manheim S. Shapiro, stepmother of Ezra Shapiro, grandmother of Elinor Gaston Shapiro. Memorial service will be held at Temple Sinai, 3100 Military Rd, Northwest Washington D.C. Friday April 26th at 1:00p.
