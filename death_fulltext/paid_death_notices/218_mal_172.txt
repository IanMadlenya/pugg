
        RODGERS--James Beall. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center family joins with the physicians and staff of the Department of Surgery, in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, James Beall Rodgers, M.D., world-renowned surgeon and former senior attending physician at Roosevelt Hospital. To the entire Rodgers family and to all who loved him, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Richard F. Daines, M.D. President & CEO George J. Todd, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Surgery Carl W. Braun, M.D. President, Medical Board
