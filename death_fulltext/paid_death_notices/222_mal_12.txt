
        COBLE--David Leland, 6, on June 29, 2005. Artist, paleontologist. He loved Spider-man and Venom. Superheroes, reptiles, dinosaurs, cats big and little, and all things fierce. David inspired us to be our best. We love him. He loved flowers, but donations may also be made to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, the Valerie Fund; New York Restoration Project or The Metropolitan YMCA of the Oranges Scholarship Fund. Survived by his loving mother Alana, father Rick, sister Julia, aunt Julie, uncles, aunts, cousins and grandparents. A funeral service will be held on Wednesday July 6, 2005 at 11:00AM at St. John's Lutheran Church, 587 Springfield Ave, Summit, NJ. The family will be receiving relatives and friends on Tuesday July 5 from 4-7PM at the The William R. Dangler Funeral Home, 309 Springfield Avenue, Summit, New Jersey.
