
        LEBOW-Ruth. Feb. 22, 2003. Beloved wife of 68 years to the late Sylvan Lebow. Loving mother of Mark Lebow and Joy Lebow Cohen. Adoring grandmother to Michael, Lauren, Jeffrey and Alexandra. Proud mother-in-law of Dr. William Cohen and Patricia E. Harris. Burial private.
        LEBOW-Ruth. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Ruth Lebow. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, Pres Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
