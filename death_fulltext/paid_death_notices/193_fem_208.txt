
        WALT-Rose. February 15, 2003 in Los Angeles, CA after a lengthy illness. Survived by sons Ira and Mark, daughter Barbara, stepdaughters Barbara and Jo Beth, sister Ruth, seven grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Rose was an active member in Workman's Circle and loved her work as an education secretary at Temple Isaiah. Memorial contributions may be made to the Workman's Circle Cultural Center, 1525 So. Robertson Blvd, LA, CA 90034 or National Yiddish Book Ctr, Old East St. School, POB 969, Amherst, MA. 01004.
