
        FRIEDLANDER-Dr. Susan E. Died unexpectedly of complications related to pneumonia on December 4th, 2002. Beloved daughter of Shirley Friedlander and the late George Friedlander. Susan was treasured by her family, sister Merri and brother-inlaw Mitchell Gelbard, nephew Jonathan, niece Joanna Brynn, partner Mitchell Nagrowski and his son Paul. A gifted and concerned educator, she had a passion for helping handicapped and special needs Pre-K children. As an administrator of Special Education for the City of New York, her career focused on meeting the educational needs of these children. Cherished by family, friends and colleagues for her kind, generous and loving spirit, she will be sorely missed by all whose lives she touched. She was truly one of the ''Good People''. Funeral services Sunday, December 8th at 10AM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY. Donations in her memory should be made to Eden II Programs-School for Autism, 150 Granite Avenue, Staten Island, New York 11303, or contact at eden2si@aol.com.
