
        ZWERLING-Samuel, DDS. Died April 11 at the age of 94 in Deerfield Beach, FL. Beloved husband of the late Edith Zwerling, he was a graduate of Harvard University's School of Dental Medicine. Lovingly remembered by his nieces, Molly Zwerling O'Byrne of New Fairfield, CT and Mimi Zwerling of Alexandria, VA as well as his companion and caregiver, Linda Taylor of Delray Beach, FL. Services were held April 22 in Florida.
