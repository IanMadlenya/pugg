
        JOYCE-Timothy, Ph.D. The officers and employees of Roper Starch Worldwide mourn the passing of our Vice-Chairman, Dr. Timothy Joyce on Saturday, September 13. He was a true pioneer in the media/market research field and we will long remember his unparalleled wit, keen intelligence and loyal devotion to his friends and colleagues throughout the world. The research industry and we, in particular, will miss him dearly. Our deepest sympathies to his wife Helen, his son Nicholas, and daughter Rachel Frosh.
