
        LIPSIG-Dr. Florence Steinhaus, died peacefully at her New York City home on February 3, 1999. She is survived by her daughter, Dana Lipsig of New York City; a sister, Pearl Sullivan of Hackensack, New Jersey; her brother, Henry Steinhaus of Sarasota, FL; her nephews, Peter M. Steinhaus of Orlando, FL; and Richard Z. Steinhaus of New York City. Dr Lipsig, who received a Ph.D. from NYU in Pyschology, was a highly regarded therapist and will be warmly remembered by her family, many friends and colleagues.
