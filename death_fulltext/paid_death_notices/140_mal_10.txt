
        Van BRUNT-Robert Ernest. On August 29, at age 87 in Columbia, SC. Formerly of Roslyn Heights, N.Y. and Sarasota, FL. Beloved husband of the late Gladys Strongin, father of Vincent of Columbia, SC and Peter of Chevy Chase, MD. Dear father-in-law of Marjorie J.; devoted grandfather of Edward R. and Zachary J. of Columbia, SC; brother of Edwin D. of Somers, NY and Albert D. of NYC and East Hampton, NY. Loving companion of Janet Cohen of Longboat Key, FL. Retired VP of Consolidated Insurance Companies of Brooklyn, NY; taught at College of Insurance in NYC. Served in the US 7th Army in North Africa and France in WW II. Contributions in his memory can be made to the Alzheimer's Association, Development Division, 919 North Michigan Ave. Suite 1000, Chicago, IL 60611.
