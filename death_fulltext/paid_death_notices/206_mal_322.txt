
        STUPNICK--Louis, age 92, died at home in New York, on March 23, 2004. Beloved husband of 70 years to his ''Kid'' Florence Stupnick. Devoted father to Nancy and Jan Brumm, Lois and Albert Leon and Nina and Lawrence Epstein. Cherished grandfather of Daniel, Nicholas, Rebecca, Sarah, Isabel, Amy and Brian. Loving greatgrandfather of Brennen, Abigail, Isabelle, Sophie, Gabrielle, Alec and Ptolemy. Funeral at Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, March 26, 2004, 11am. Burial at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, Long Island.
