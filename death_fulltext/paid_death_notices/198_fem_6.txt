
        MAHER--Eileen M., 82, of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn, New York, died on June 27, 2003, at the home of her niece in Pennsylvania with whom she resided since January. She was the last surviving member of her immediate family. She was the daughter of John J. Maher and Mary Kelly. The family, including her late brothers John, James and Edward and her late sister Mary, lived in Bay Ridge since 1905. She had worked as an executive secretary for the American International Group, New York City, until her retirement. Funeral arrangements are in New York. Wake will be held from 2-9 P.M. on Sunday, July 6, at McLaughlin and Sons, 9620 3rd Avenue, Brooklyn. Funeral Mass at St. Patrick's R.C. Church, 9511 4th Avenue, Brooklyn. >>AD#
