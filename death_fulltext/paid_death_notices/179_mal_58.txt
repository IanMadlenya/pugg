
        LEWIS-Arthur L. Passed away on November 28, 2001 after a brief illness. He was 76 years old. Arthur is survived by his wife of 53 years, Joan Lewis, two daughters, Cathy Lewis of Larchmont, NY and Jane Horsfall of Freeport, Maine, and four grandchildren. He was a husband, father and grandfather extraordinaire, as well as an astute businessman and proud Marine. A memorial service will be held at the Westchester Funeral Home, 190 Main Street, Eastchester, New York (914-337-4585) on Saturday, December 8 at 10 A.M. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the East Yonkers Rotary Club Scholarship Fund, and to the First Church of Christ Scientist, White Plains-Scarsdale.
