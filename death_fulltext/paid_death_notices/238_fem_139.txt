
        WOLTER--Edith L. Born on January 13, 1914, in Berlin, she passed away on November 14. A beloved sister, mother, aunt, great-aunt and friend, she was a long time member of the Church of the Advent Hope, who opened her heart and her home to many. Memorial services will be held at the Church, 111 E. 87th Street, on January 13, 2007, at 4 p.m.
