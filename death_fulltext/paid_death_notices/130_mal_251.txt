
        SMITH-Ronald Stanton. Ronnie was a wonderful human being. He was a truly honorable man. His talent, sense of humor and kindness will be greatly missed. He was retired from Tiffany & Co, where he worked for 27 years. He is survived by his sister Carolyn, his brother-in-law Richard, nieces and nephews, and his companion Richard Currier. Donations can be sent to the National Association for the Visually Handicapped, 22 West 21st St, NY, NY 10010.
