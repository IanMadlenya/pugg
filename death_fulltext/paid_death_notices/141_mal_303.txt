
        STATEMAN-Daniel. 78. On October 25, of Cancer. He is survived by his wife of 52 years, the former Ruth Gladstone, sons, Ethan and Keith. A 1947 graduate of the Brooklyn College of Pharmacy, he owned and operated a pharmacy in Flushing. He retired as Alumni Director and Director of Special Services for the College of Pharmacy of Long Island University. On December 8, 1941 he enlisted in the Army Air Corps and was discharged 5 years later as a 1st Lt. An advocate for pharmacy, active member Temple Beth Sholom in Flushing.
