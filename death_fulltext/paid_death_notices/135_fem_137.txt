
        CORRADO-Carmella. Age 93, died Saturday, April 18, in Maimonides Medical Center in Brooklyn. Wife of the late Anthony J. Corrado, she is survived by a son Fred L. Corrado and 2 granddaughters Christine and Caroline Corrado. A Funeral Mass will be held on Wednesday, 10 AM, in St. Mary's R.C. Church, 178 Greenwich Avenue, Greenwich, CT. Friends may call at Fred D. Knapp & Son Funeral Home, 267 Greenwich Avenue, Greenwich, CT., from 4-8 PM, Monday and Tuesday.
