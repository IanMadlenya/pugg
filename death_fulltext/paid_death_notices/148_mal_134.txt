
        WETTACH-Michael F. The Board of Trustees and staff of The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation deeply mourn the loss of Michael F. Wettach. The son of Fred Wettach, Jr., and Barbara Guggenheim and grandson of Solomon R. Guggenheim, Michael joined the Board of Trustees of The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation in 1957. He was a member of the Executive and Finance Committee from 1958 to 1965, and a member of the Art and Museum Committee from 1958 to 1999. Michael was a generous donor to the Museum, and a trusted and valued friend. He is survived by his partner of 21 years, Robert C. Ludwig of Monkton, MD, his half-brother, Peter Lawson-Johnston of Princeton, NJ, three nieces, Wendy McNeil, Tania McCleery, and Mimi Howe of Princeton, Hopewell, and Tennington, NJ, respectively, and a nephew, Peter Lawson-Johnston II, of Greenwich, CT. A memorial service will be held on May 29 at St. James Church, Monkton, MD, at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to The Solomon R. Guggenheim Foundation, 1071 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10128. Peter Lawson-Johnston Honorary Chairman Peter B. Lewis, Chairman Ronald O. Perelman, President Thomas Krens, Director The Solomon R.
