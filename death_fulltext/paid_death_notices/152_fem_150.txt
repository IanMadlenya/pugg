
        PORTER-Jeanne. 86, of New York, NY, died September 13th at Mt. Sinai Hospital after a valiant fight against cancer. Beloved mother and aunt, widow of Benjamin Silverberg and Joseph Porter, survived by sons Stephen B. Silverberg (Maureen) and Andrew I. Porter, and many nieces and nephews. The last of six siblings, predeceased by sisters Ida, Rose, Eva and Betty, and brother Michael. A volunteer at Lenox Hill Hospital, she loved New York. Memorial on Friday, September 24, 2 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street. For further information, call 212-288-3500.
