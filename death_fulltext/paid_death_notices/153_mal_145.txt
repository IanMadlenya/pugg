
        GILDSTON-Dr. Harold. Beloved husband of Phyllis. Devoted father of Eva Rochelle Close, and the late Jessica Nan Gildston. Loving brother of Sarah Kohn. Naval Officer during WWII, and Professor at C.W. Post College of L.I.U. for 35 years. Services Sunday October 17, 1999 at 1 P.M. at ''RiversideNassau North'' Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, New York.
        GILDSTON-Dr. Harold. Temple Beth-El of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his bereaved family. Shelley M. Limmer, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
