
        ESS-Henry N. October 25, 2000. Born 1921 in Kansas City, MO. He graduated from The Hill School in Pottstown, PA, in 1938, Princeton University in 1942 and Harvard Law School in 1944. He was a Trust and Estates Partner at Sullivan and Cromwell. Memorial service will be held Tuesday, October 31, at 10 AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave, NYC. The family requests a contribution to the New York Public Library in lieu of flowers.
