
        GREEN - Herman. Delaire Country Club mourns the loss of their esteemed member and past president Herman Green. His continuing staunch support for the betterment of Delaire will not be forgotten. Our heartfelt sympathy goes to his cherished wife Arona and his beloved children and grandchildren. Mal Levenson, President
        GREEN-Herman. With a heavy heart we say goodbye to a loyal and faithful friend, Herman Green. We both had the privilege as serving as president of Delaire Country Club, together we were the founding sponsors of ''I Had A Dream Foundation'' of Delray Beach, FL, and had the extreme pleasure of sharing many a round of golf. We will miss you Herman, as will all of your many friends and associates. Shirley and Jay Felner
