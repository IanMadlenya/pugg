
        MITCHELL-Alex. Devoted widower, father and grandfather, died peacefully at home with his family on Tuesday, May 28, 2002. His loyalty, compassion and determination will be greatly missed. Mr. Mitchell was owner and CEO of Parkell Inc., a manufacturer of dental products. Services will be held Thursday, May 30 at 10:00 AM at Gutterman's, 8000 Jericho Turnpike, Woodbury, NY. Private burial has been arranged. Donations may be made in his memory to Cancer Research Institute, United Jewish Appeal and Baruch College.
