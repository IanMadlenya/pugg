
        MAILMAN-Norton W. On November 1, 2002, at his home in Manhattan, after a long illness. Beloved husband of Virginia Shevlin. Father of Christopher B. Mailman and Bruce W. Addison of NYC, and Matthew D. Addison of Los Angeles. Grandfather of four. Interment private. Memorial service to be announced. Kindly omit flowers.
        MAILMAN - Norton. The Board of Trustees, medical staff and administration of The Hospital for Special Surgery mourn with profound sorrow the death of Norton Mailman, a dedicated Board member and friend. He will be greatly missed and warmly remembered. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife Virginia and their family. Richard L.
