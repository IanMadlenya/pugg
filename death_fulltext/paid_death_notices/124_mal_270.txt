
        KORNFELD-Magda Ranschburg. On May 22, 1997, age 94. Of Medford, NJ. Wife of the late Dr. Werner Kornfeld, mother of Alice Buskin of Woodbridge, N.J., and Henry and his wife Sylvia of East Norwich, New York. Seven grandchildren, 7 great grandchildren. Numerous cousins, nieces and nephews. Services private. Memorial contributions to: Holocaust Memorial Museum, 100 Raoul Wallenberg Place S.W., Washington, D.C. 20024-2150.
