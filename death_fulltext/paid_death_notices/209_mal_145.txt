
        WALL--Sidney, 84, passed away peacefully on June 6th, 2004 at Bethesda Hospital in Boynton Beach, FL after a brief illness. He was the beloved and loving husband of Ann Wall (nee Plager), the devoted father of Roberta and Steve, beloved grandfather of Eryka, Kristen, Jill and Rebecca. He was comforted and attended to by his loving family and friends during his illness. He was an avid sportsman and lover of opera and all the arts. His many friends and relatives will remember him as a loving, caring and generous man.
