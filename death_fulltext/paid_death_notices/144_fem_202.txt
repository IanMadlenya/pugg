
        ANTHONY-Bettina Moran, 64 years old, of Palm Beach, FL, died on Thursday, January 14th, 1999, after an extended illness. Born in New York, New York, she attended the Convent of the Sacred Heart, New York, NY; Miss Hall's School, Pittsfield, MA; and Bennett Junior College, Millbrook, New York. She is survived by her beloved daughters, Lucy Webster & Laura Anthony, both of NY, NY; Her mother, Helen G. Morgan, also of NY, NY; & three brothers, Bernard A. Moran, Jr. of Fort Lauderdale, FL, John P. Moran of Ft. Collins, CO, and Michael Moran of Abescon, NJ. A Memorial Mass will be celebrated at 2:00 P.M. on Saturday, January 23, 1999 at St. Thomas More Catholic Church, New York, NY. For information Quattlebaum - Holleman - Burse Funeral Home, (561) 832-5171.
