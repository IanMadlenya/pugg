
        McDERMOTT - Catherine (nee Fox). On October 21, 2001. Last surviving child of the late John J. Fox, founder of John J. Fox and Sons Inc., funeral directors since 1893 and Elizabeth (nee Bracken). Loving wife of the late Thomas Delmour, Sr. Beloved mother of Catherine E., of Washington, DC. and Sarah A. of New York City, and the late Thomas Delmour, Jr. Also survived by her grandson, John Delmour McDermott of Omaha, Nebraska. and many loving nieces and nephews. Family will receive friends at John J. Fox Funeral Home Inc., 2080 Boston Post Road, Larchmont, NY, Friday 4-6PM and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday, 10AM, St. Mary Star of the Sea Church, City Island, New York. Interment St. Raymond's Cemetery.
