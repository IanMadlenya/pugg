
        TEMMER-Georges M. Born April 10, 1922, Vienna. Died January 12, 1997, Princeton, New Jersey. Beloved husband of Sylvia, and loving step-father to Julianna Mastroserio, Jenni Spencer and Edward Twerdahl IV, and grandfather to five. He was a professor of Nuclear Physics at Rutgers for 29 years. Memorial service Trinity Church, Princeton, New Jersey, Sunday, January 19, 1:30 P.M.
