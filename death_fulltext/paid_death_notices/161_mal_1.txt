
        BERSON-Paul Arthur, 68, passed away on Monday May 29, 2000. Loving husband of Selma, adoring father of Beth and Lois, devoted zaidy to his 18 grandchildren, Paul was the retired president and chief executive officer of B & B Motor & Control Corp., Long Island City, NY. He is also survived by his sisters Sarah, of Brooklyn, NY and Sylvia, of PA. In our hearts he will remain forever young. Donations in Paul's memory may be made to The Hebrew Institute of Riverdale, Chevra Kadisha.
