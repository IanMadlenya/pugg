
        STRAX-Philip, M.D. The faculty, administration and alumni of the New York University School of Medicine are profoundly saddened by the passing of Dr. Philip Strax, Class of 1931, on Tuesday, March 9, 1999. Dr. Strax was a loyal member of the faculty in the Department of Environmental Medicine, and diligently served on the Board of Governors for the School of Medicine Alumni Association for over 25 years culminating in his presidency for the 198182 academic year. We will always remember his warm sense of humor and wise counsel. To his children and grandchildren we express our most heartfelt sympathy. Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean NYU School of Medicine Beth W. Lieberman, M.D. '73 President, NYU School of Medicine Alumni Association
        STRAX-Philip, M.D. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family acknowledges with sadness the passing of our dear friend and highly respected member of our attending staff, Philip Strax, M.D. We are proud of our association with Dr. Strax, who worked closely with the late Louis Venet, M.D., of Beth Israel in the ground-breaking studies that led to the advancement of mammography for early detection and prevention of breast cancer. To his family and friends, our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew E. Fink, M.D. President and CEO, BIMC Deborah Axelrod, M.D. Physician-in-Charge Louis Venet, M.D. Comprehensive Breast Service, BIMC
