
        HADDAD-Mary Lou (nee Scorsone). On June 10, 2001, after a long illness. She has been a teacher at P.S. 142 in Manhattan since 1984. She is survived by her husband, Dr. Jamil R. Haddad, three sons, Ralph, John, and James, and five grandchildren. She is also survived by her mother, Mary, and her brother, John Scorsone. Friends may call at The Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue (at 20th Street), NYC, Thursday 3-5 and 7-9 P.M. Funeral Mass will be on Monday June 18, at 10 A.M. at Saint Anselm R.C. Church, Bay Ridge, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Mary Lou Haddad Memorial Fund at P.S. 142.
