
        GLUCK--Arline Margery (Oppenheim) of Brookline, MA. Died December 22 at the Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center in Boston. She was 82 years old. She was the wife of the late Larry Gluck. Arline was born in New York City, daughter of Jesse and Jean Oppenheim. She was a resident of Brookline. Arline was an Art Director and Fashion Coordinator at several department stores. She is survived by her daughter Andrea Norkus and her husband Michael of Brookline, by her brother Jack Oppenheim, and by her grandchildren Tyler and Vanessa Norkus. Funeral services are private. www.lawlerfuneralhome .com
