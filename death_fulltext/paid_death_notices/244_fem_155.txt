
        STARRS--Florence M. Of Hampton Bays, NY. Formerly of Jackson Heights, NY on May 17, 2007. Executive Secretary to the Rockefeller Family Officers at 30 Rockefeller Plaza in NY. Prior to that position she was Executive Secretary to the Vice President of American Airlines. She attended Fordham University at Lincoln Center. She loved acting and was in many Summer Stock plays. Florence was a member The Olde Towne Garden Club of Southhampton, Dominican Sisters Family Health Services and a volunteer at Southampton Hospital. She is cherished by many nieces, nephews and friends. Services entrusted to the R.J. O'Shea Funeral Home, 94 E. Montank Hwy., Hampton Bays, NY. A mass of Christian Burial will take place Wednesday at 10:30 am at St. Rosalie's R.C. Church. A private committal will follow. Donations to the Dominican Sisters F.H.S. Box 1028, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978. Family will receive friends Monday and Tuesday 1-4 and 7-9 pm.
