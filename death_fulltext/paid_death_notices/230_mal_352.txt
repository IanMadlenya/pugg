
        du MOULIN--Edward I., age 91, of Port Washington, on March 29, 2006. Beloved husband of the late Eleanor. Loving father of Richard (Ann), and the late Cathy Morea. Dear brother of Julia and Charles. Also survived by seven grandchildren, son-inlaw Jerry, and many nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be held Saturday, April 1 at 10 AM at the Knickerbocker Yacht Club in Port Washington. Contributions in his memory can be made to the Seamen's Church Institute, 241 Water St., NY, NY 10038 or the Herreshoff Marine Museum in Bristol, RI.
