
        WHITE--Stephen Bonsal Jr. On July 2, 2006. Beloved husband of Constance O'Neil White. Fond father of William, Louise, Lawrence, Stephen and Ellen. Born May 21, 1922. Mr. White attended the Gilman School and Princeton University, where he was Phi Beta Kappa and a member of the Ivy Club. Former Managing Partner of Alex Brown, he was also the President of the Baltimore Cemetery Co. Services will be private. Contributions to the Chesapeake Bay Foundation or the Penikese Island School, PO Box 161, Woods Hole, MA 02543.
