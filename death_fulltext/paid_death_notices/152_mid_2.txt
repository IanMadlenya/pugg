
        STEINBUGLER-Charles J. Jr. Of Garden City, NY on September 9, 1999. A man of intelligence and probity, loyally devoted to his family, his faith, the legal profession, Notre Dame and the Marine Corps. A loving husband to his wife Jane and a caring father to his daughters and their spouses: Sara & her husband David Cordon, M.D., Elizabeth and her husband Anthony Saltalamacchia, Kathryn and her husband Joseph Ronan, Mary and her husband David Ekeren, and Anne and Margaret; & a proud grandfather of eleven. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Ave. at 12th Street, Garden City, 2-5 Saturday, and 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. Sunday. Mass of Christian burial St. Joseph's Church, Garden City, 10 A.M. Monday. Interment Calverton National Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to The Brooklyn Prep Alumni Association, Loyola Hall, Fordham University, Bronx, NY 10458-5198.
