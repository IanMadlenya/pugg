
        WALSH-Gloria Monterubio. Died May 20, 1999. Survived by her son James, daughter Victoria, her brother Fred, and sister Rose. She was a Veteran of the U.S. Marine Corps in World War II and a graduate of Barnard College. Gathering of friends 7-9 PM, service at 7:30, Thursday May 27, Volk Leber Funeral Home, 789 Teaneck Rd, Teaneck, NJ. Donations in her name may be made to the charity of choice.
