
        STOMBLER-Bertha. The officers, staff and leadership of the Jewish Labor Committee share in the sorrow at the death of Bertha Stombler, mother of our dear friend and colleague, Natalie Cimbol. A devoted and loving mother to Natalie and her brothers, Eugene and Milton Stombler, she leaves a loving family of ten grandchildren and thirteen great-grandchildren with warm memories of happier times. We extend our deepest condolences to her relatives and all who knew and loved her. May her memory be a blessing. Morton Bahr President Avram B. Lyon Executive Director
