
        ABRANDT-Gustav S. Age 89. Formerly of Oyster Bay and Syosset NY. Beloved son of Sigrid Abrandt and August Abrandt, on July 17,2000. Beloved father of Jonathan C. Abrandt and Jeffrey G. Abrandt; he was one of the first lifeguards at Jones Beach State Park, serving as Lieutenant under Captain Johns, the creator of the Corps. A graduate of Syracuse University and Columbia University Law School, he retired in 1960 from the Federal Bureau of Investigation after working on the most prominent WWII Nazi spy cases, serving as a Resident Agent in Nassau County, and then as an Agent in the New York Office. He then worked with the New York State Thoroughbred Racing Protective Association. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Rosary Hill Home, hospice for the incurable sick with cancer, 600 Linda Av, Hawthorne,NY 10532
