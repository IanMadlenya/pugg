
        OSTRO--Pauline (nee Susserman). Died peacefully on July 28, 2005, at 97 years. Widow of Benjamin Ostro. Beloved mother of Susan Solomon (Dr. Lawrence Shaderowfsky), Robert (Karen). Cherished and loving grandmother of Alexander and Katherine Ostro and Jennifer Solomon. Mourned by her beloved younger brother and his wife, Dr. Alexander and Rosalie Surrey, and by sisterin-law Edith Surrey, widow of Harold. She will be missed by nieces and nephews in the Surrey and Ostro families, and by her special friend Henry Macon. A longtime Larchmont, NY, resident, and dedicated New York City schoolteacher, she will be remembered for her spirit, humor and love of family. Graveside burial was on Sunday.
