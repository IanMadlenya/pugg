
        CUSACK-Yvonne. Died peacefully in New York at her home on Tuesday, May 5, in the company of her beloved husband of 46-1/2 years, Maurice. The members of the Board of The Spanish Institute express their deepest condolence. Yvonne, a dear friend of the Institute and of all things Spanish, travelled frequently with her husband to Spain and Iberoamerica. She will be very much missed by all her many friends.
