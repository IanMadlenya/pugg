
        PEARCE-Mary C. Age 83, of Mahwah, NJ, formerly of Riverdale, New York, died September 10, 1997. She ran the Kingsbridge Answering Service in the Bronx for 45 years, retiring in 1996. Beloved mother of Deborah Pearce, Ph.D. Funeral Mass will be on Saturday 9:30 A.M., at Our Lady of Mount Carmel RC Church, Ridgewood, with interment in Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Visiting Friday 7-9 PM at the Feeney Funeral Home, 232 Franklin Ave, Ridgewood, N.J. (201-444-7650).
