
        LIFSHITZ--Moshe. The Officers, Members of the Board and Staff of the American and International Societies for Yad Vashem mourn the passing of Moshe Lifshitz, husband of Cheryl Skura Lifshitz, Member of the Board and our Executive Committee, son-in-law of Stella and Sam Skura. He is survived by his beloved wife Cheryl and their two daughters Iris and Ilana. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the entire Skura and Lifshitz families. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Eli Zborowski, Chairman
