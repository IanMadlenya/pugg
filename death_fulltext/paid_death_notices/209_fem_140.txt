
        RAPAPORT -- Pauline (nee Busky). Age 89, on June 17, 2004. Wife of the late Monroe Rapaport. Survived by her children, Joseph Rapaport of Manhattan and Suzy Naylor of Roberts Creek, British Columbia. Also survived by daughter-in-law Bonnie Rapaport and son-in-law Frank Henning. In addition, there are five grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. A memorial service is being planned.
