
        HIRSCHHORN-Gertrude Reitman, 88, peacefully in her sleep, on April 6, 2000 in Oakland, CA. Loving mother and mother-in-law of Eric Hirschhorn and Leah Wortham, Victoria and Kenneth Bochat, and Jessica Hirschhorn and Henry Howard. Adoring grandmother of Alexander, Samuel, Elizabeth, Anne, Joseph, Lucy, and Sophie. We will miss her joy and love of life. A memorial service will be held in Oakland on April 10. Contributions to the Employee Education Fund or the Claremont Endowment Fund, Claremont House, 4500 Gilbert St., Oakland, CA 94611, would be appreciated.
