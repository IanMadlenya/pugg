
        ACKERMAN-Bernat. On October 8, 2000. Devoted husband of Priscilla, beloved father of Vivian and Everett, cherished brother of Irving, Naftali and Hani. Loved and admired by many grandchildren, nieces, nephews and cousins. He was a past President of Congregation Sons of Israel, Yonkers and past President of the New York State Society of CPAs, Westchester Chapter, as well as past District Deputy Grand Master of the Masonic Order. Funeral and burial service was held Oct. 10. Family will receive friends at the Ackerman home, 96 Belvedere Drive, Yonkers until Friday at noon.
