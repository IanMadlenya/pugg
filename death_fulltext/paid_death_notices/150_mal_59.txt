
        BRUNO-Jay, 63. Of NYC and Wallkill NY on June 27 of cancer. Born in the Bronx, NY, Jay was a print salesman and served in the 82nd Airborne Division from 1954 to 1957. He is survived by his loving wife Susan, son Jay (Orlando Fl.), son Michael (Pine Bush, NY), mother Evelyn Kirkwood (Dania Beach, Fl.), brother Stephen (Ft. Lauderdale, Fl.) and sister Lynne (Hollywood Fl.) A gathering of family and friends will take place at the Church Center for the United Nations, 777 UN Plaza (44 St. and 1st Ave.) on Wednesday, July 7 from 2-4 and 6-8PM. A celebration of Jay's life will begin at 7PM.
