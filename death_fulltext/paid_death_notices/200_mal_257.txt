
        GARDNER--Herb. The Writers Guild of America, East mourns the passing of its distinguished member Herb Gardner. He received the WGA Award for Best Screenplay for ''A Thousand Clowns'' in 1966 and was the recipient of the Writers Guild of America, East's Lifetime Achievement Award in 2000. His wit and wisdom will be missed. Herb Sargent President, WGAE Mona Mangan Executive Director, WGAE
