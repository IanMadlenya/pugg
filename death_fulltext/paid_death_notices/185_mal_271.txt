
        SLANE-Steve. Born in Norfolk, VA, died at age 86, on June 2, in Sarasota, FL. He was Founding Director of N.S. Music-Theatre in Beverly, MA, from 1955 until he retired in 1983. Survived by wife of 38 years Eva, and nephews, Leonard and Louis Schlain. Memorial services will be held in the fall at NSMT in Beverly and Kripalu in Lenox, MA. He is greatly missed.
