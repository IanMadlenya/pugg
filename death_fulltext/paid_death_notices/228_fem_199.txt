
        MICHAELI--Adina. Devoted daughter of the late David and Uschi Michaeli. Loving sister of Jonathan, dear sister-in-law of Dorlee, and adored aunt of Daniel and Talia. Passed away at her home in Manhattan late January 21 at the age of 51. ''Doda Zsa Zsa'' was a special friend to many. She left behind a legacy of love, friendship, compassion and generosity. During the last few years of her life she was a real trooper, battling complications resulting from diabetes and Breast Cancer with great resolve and optimism. Donations may be made to the Joslin Diabetes Center in Boston. Funeral service is at 1:15PM Tuesday, January 24 at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel on 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
