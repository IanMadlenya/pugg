
        KENNEDY-Franklin Jr. At the age of 86. He died peacefully at his home in Bronxville after a brief illness. Beloved husband of Florence. Father of Franklin III, Joyce Heeter, Carolyn Hollingsworth and Kathleen Kennedy. Brother of Roderick. Also surviving are four grandchildren and one great grandson. Mr. Kennedy was president of Mayhew in NYC. His family and friends will love and miss him always. Services are private. Memorials may be made to Hospice of Westchester.
