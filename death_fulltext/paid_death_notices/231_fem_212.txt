
        EHRENFEST -- Susan (nee Wertheimer). Was born in Vienna, Austria, January 6th, 1919, and died on April 22, 2006. Susan received a degree in International Diplomacy from the Diplomatic Academy of Vienna, Austria. She immigrated to St. Louis in 1941, via Russia and Japan. She was married to Steven Ehrenfest until his death in 1969. She worked for Libson Shops as a sportswear buyer for 42 years, until their closure. She will be terribly missed by all of her family and friends. In lieu of flowers please send donations in her name to Logos School, 9137 Old Bonhomme Rd, St. Louis, MO 63132.
