
        TIEDER--William P., died May 23rd, 2007, age 98. Husband of the late Myra Tieder, loving father of Martha and Bob Plotnick, Susan and Sy Allis, devoted grandfather to William and Jacqueline Grae, Eric Grae, Beth and Victor Wittenberg, Marnina Allis, special great-grandfather to Talia, Amira and Nili Wittenberg and Lauren Grae. He will be missed by his friends and entire family.
