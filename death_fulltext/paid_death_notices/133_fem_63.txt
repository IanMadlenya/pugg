
        FANE-Agnes ''Dolly.'' February 6, 1998. Beloved wife of the late G. Wiliiam Fane. Devoted mother of Anne Cote Taylor and her husband Paul E. Taylor, Jr. Loving sister of Mary O'Brien. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, February 11, 1998, 10:30 AM, St. Joseph's Church, 404 E. 87th St. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Ethics Program of the NY and Presbyterian Hospitals, 525 E. 68th St, NYC 10021.
