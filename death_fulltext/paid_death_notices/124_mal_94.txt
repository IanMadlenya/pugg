
        KOSHLAND-William A. On May 7, 1997. Greatly loved uncle of Kathryn K. and Anthony D. Blue, great uncle of Caitlin, Toby, Jessica, and Amanda Blue, and Stephen A. Koshland, treasured cousin of Sally J. Kuhn, & devoted friend of Tibor S. Heredy. Memorial service to be announced at a later date. Donations in his memory may be made to Harvard University, Phillips Exeter Academy, & the UJA-Federation of Jewish Philanthropies.
