
        O'CALLAGHAN-Bridget T. (nee Murphy). Born in Cartron, Drumlish, County Longford, Ireland. Passed away on March 26, 2003, at the age of 98 in Queens, New York. Beloved wife of the late Patrick and mother of Thomas, now deceased. Survived by her sons, James and John; her niece, Mary Lennon; seven grandchildren; five greatgrandchildren and numerous nephews and nieces. Reposing at the Frederick Funeral Home of Flushing, Northern Blvd at 193rd Street. Visiting hours Friday 2-5 and 7-9PM. Mass of Christian Burial Saturday 9:45AM St. Kevin's Church. Interment Mt. St. Mary's Cemetery, Flushing, NY.
