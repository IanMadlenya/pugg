
        FOGELMAN-Sam (Simcha). In his 86th year. Loving husband of Leah. Brother of Malka and Bella. Father of Eva and Gila. Adoring grandfather of Adam. Father-inlaw of Jerome Chanes. Simcha was a partisan during World War II, a soldier in the Israel Defense Forces, and active in Holocaust remembrance in America. In New York, he was Vice President of GUF Knitting Mill. Shiva at 60 Riverside Dr, Apt. 11G, Wednesday, October 14 through Tuesday, AM, October 20. May his memory be for a blessing.
