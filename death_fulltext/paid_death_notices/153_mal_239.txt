
        TONKIN-Joseph. On October 15, at age 94. Beloved husband of the late Celina Tonkin. Loving son of the late Nathaniel Tonkin and Lily Freedman Tonkin and brother of the late A. Ted Tonkin. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81 St, Tuesday, 12 noon. Interment Mt. Neboh Cemetery.
        TONKIN-Joseph. The Yeshiva University family is saddened by the passing of our longtime friend and YU Guardian. Through his creative vision and concern for Jewish education, he established the Joseph Tonkin Scholarship Fund at our undergraduate schools. May his memory always remain a source of abiding blessing in our midst. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees
