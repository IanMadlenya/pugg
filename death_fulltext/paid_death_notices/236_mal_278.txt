
        OPATUT--Joel In Remembrance of Joel Opatut. The Boards of Trustees and physicians and staff of Monmouth Medical Center and Monmouth Medical Center Foundation are deeply saddened by the passing of Joel Opatut on September 23, 2006. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his family and friends and honor the memory of a true philanthropist, whose generous spirit will live on in the community programs he so proudly supported. While we will greatly miss this remarkable individual, his legacy of compassion, generosity and human kindness will forever remain with us and continue to touch the lives of future generations. Memorial donations may be made to Joel Opatut Cardiac Rehabilitation Center at Monmouth Medical Center or the Radiation Oncology Department at Monmouth Medical Center, Long Branch, NJ.
