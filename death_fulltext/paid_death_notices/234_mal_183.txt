
        EPSTEIN--Dr. Fred. The Neurofibromatosis community mourns the passing of Dr. Fred Epstein. His pioneering work and indomitable spirit brought hope to many with NF. His innovative surgical approaches were matched with warmth and respect for his patients. We are eternally grateful for his services to pediatric neurosurgery, as his legacy will continue to benefit patients. Our thoughts are with his wife, Kathy, and his family. John Risner, President, Children's Tumor Foundation
