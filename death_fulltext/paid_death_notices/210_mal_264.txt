
        PRESS--Michael S. of Oyster Bay, NY, died on July 27, 2004 at age 55 following a courageous six month battle with sinus cancer. Beloved husband of Priscilla Campo Press and devoted father of Michael S. Press Jr. and Priscilla D. (''Dodie'') Press. He is survived by his brother Thomas of Houston, Texas, and by numerous loving sisters-inlaw, brothers-in-law, and nieces and nephews. Michael, the quintessential gentleman and scholar, was thoroughly devoted to his family, and known for his wonderful wit. Born in Manhattan, Michael served as a Captain in the U.S. Air Force and was a graduate of Dartmouth College and the University of Miami School of Law, where he graduated with honors and was a member of the University of Miami Law Review. An accomplished trial lawyer and avid golfer, Michael served as Secretary of the Board of Trustees of the East Woods School in Oyster Bay and was a member of the Piping Rock Club in Locust Valley NY and the Hyannisport Club in Hyannis Port, MA.
