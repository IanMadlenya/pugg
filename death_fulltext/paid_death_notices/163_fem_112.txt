
        HEIDENBERG - Sylvia. Temple Emanu-El of Englewood, New Jersey extends its deepest sympathy to the families of Robert and Susan Heidenberg, Susan Laden, Richard Heidenberg and Laurence Lazar. She was an esteemed figure in our congregation and in the Jewish and civic communities. May her family be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Geoffrey Haber, Rabbi Arthur Hertzberg, Rabbi Emeritus Israel Singer, Cantor Kurt Silbermann, Cantor Emeritus Marlene Erlebacher, President Allen Brown, Executive Director
