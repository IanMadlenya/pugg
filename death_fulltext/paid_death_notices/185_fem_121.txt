
        STEBMAN-Betty. Beloved aunt and friend, passed away on May 24, 2002, in West Palm Beach, FL, at age 86. She helped organize the United Negro College Fund in NYC and was their first official fund raiser, retiring after serving as director of their New York campaign (1943-1969). She was Fund Raising Director for the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, and served on the Boards of the Children's Defense Fund and the Natural Resources Defense Council. Ms. Stebman served as Chairman of the Educational Committee of the Orchestra of St. Lukes. She was a Trustee of the Charles Evans Hughes Memorial Foundation. She also served on the Boards of both the National and the New York Young Audiences; she also served as President of the New York Group. She served as a Board member of the American Place Theatre. She is survived by numerous nieces, nephews, grandnieces and grandnephews. She was held in high esteem by her many colleagues, distinguished dignitaries and Presidents. Contributions in her memory may be made to any of the above organizations.
