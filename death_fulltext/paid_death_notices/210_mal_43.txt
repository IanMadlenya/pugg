
        ROTHENBERG--William H. Of Riverdale, NY on July 3, 2004, at the age of 85. Adored husband of Corinne. Beloved father of Ronald and Mace and father-in-law of Katherine and Joyce. Cherished grandfather of Stephanie and Bryce, and dear brother of Ernie. Bill's warmth, friendly smile and beautiful singing voice will be missed by all who had the privilege of knowing him. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, Monday, July 5, at 11:30 am.
