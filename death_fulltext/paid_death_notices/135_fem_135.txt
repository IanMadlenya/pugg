
        SHERMAN-Rebecca Levenson On April 18, 1998, 93, of Pearl River, NY. Darling wife of the late Irving; beloved mother of George and grandmother of Molly and Peter; blessed great grandmother of Cooper. Devoted aunt and greataunt; cherished friend. Many came to her table, all were fed. Services will be held at Temple Beth El of Spring Valley on Monday, April 20. Interment at Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ.
