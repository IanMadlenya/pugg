
        FLYNN-Phyllis. 60. Of Northfield, VT, formerly of Hudson View Gardens, New York City, died March 3, 1999. She began her library career as an archivist with the Jung Foundation in NYC. Later she was employed by the American Jewish Committee, in the Blaustein Library also in NYC. Survived by a sister, Ellin Lord of Northfield, VT; a niece, Elizabeth Lord of Waterbury, VT.
