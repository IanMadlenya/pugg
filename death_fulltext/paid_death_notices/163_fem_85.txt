
        LEAVITT-Edith S. ''Taffy.'' On August 9, 2000. Cherished wife of the Honorable Peter M. Leavitt. Beloved mother of Karen & Amy. Sister of Susan Mirsky and Jane Brown. Treasured niece of Marjorie Fine. Loving aunt and friend to all. Editor and Occupational Therapist. She will be profoundly missed. Services Sunday, 10:30 AM at Temple Beth-El, Chappaqua, NY. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions in her memory to: Cure for Lymphoma Foundation, 215 Lexington Avenue, New York, New York 10016-6023.
        LEAVITT-Edith (Taffy). The congregational family of Temple Beth El of Northern Westchester notes with profound sorrow the death of Edith Taffy Leavitt, beloved wife of Judge Peter Leavitt. We share their grief, along with their entire family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Chaim Stern, Rabbi David Thomas, Rabbi Dana Anesi, Cantor Stephen J.
