
        SWEENEY-John Patrick. Age 77, died on Thursday, February 28 at Crosslands, Kennett Square, PA; formerly of NY City, he was a resident of Crosslands since 1989. Born in Boston, MA, he was the son of the late Arthur and Mildred Louise Grimes Sweeney. Mr. Sweeney served as an ensign in the U.S. Navy during World War II. He was a 1948 graduate of Harvard College and a 1952 graduate of The University of Virginia Law School. He was a partner in the law office of Townley and Updike in NY City. Prior to that he was a partner in the law office of Gifford, Woody also in NY City. He is survived by one brother, Arthur Sweeney of S. Freeport, ME, six nieces and nephews. He was predeceased by two sisters, Martha Read and Joan Sweeney. A memorial service will be held at 2:00 PM on Saturday, April 6, 2002 at Crosslands, Route 926, Kennett Sq., PA. For information: Kuzo & Gofus Funeral Home, Ltd., Kennett Square, PA.
