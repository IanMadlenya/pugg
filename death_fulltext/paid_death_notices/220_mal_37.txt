
        WARBURG--Joseph H. Age 93, beloved husband of Ilse for sixty years, cherished father of Ronnie (Resa), Joan (Nathan), David (Chaye), and brother Ralph, very much loved by twelve grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. He will be deeply missed and will always be alive in our hearts.
        WARBURG--Joseph H. The Yeshiva University family is deeply grieved by the passing of a consummate gentleman, whose kindness and compassion endeared him to all who were privileged to know him and who, together with his beloved wife, Ilse, was a YU Guardian. Heartfelt condolences are extended to his wife, Ilse, to their cherished children Rabbi Dr. Ronald (and Risa) Warburg, Joan (and Dr. Nathan) Rothman, and David (and Chaye Lamm) Warburg, to their grandchildren and great grandchildren, and to his beloved brother, Ralph (and Anita) Warburg. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Morry J. Weiss, Chairman Board of Trustees
