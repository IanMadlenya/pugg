
        CALABRESE-Benjamin. Born on April 7th, 1918 in Brooklyn, died on March 25th, 1998 in Manhattan. Lover of justice and music, he enriched the lives of many, both in his personal life and as a long time proprietor of the Piano Gallery. Father of Philip (Sylvia), loving grandfather of Julia and Christopher, cherished brother of Rose Cassella, devoted uncle and great uncle to many. Services April 25, 11 AM, St. James' Church, 865 Madison Ave. Donations to St. Roses Home, 71 Jackson Street, NYC.
