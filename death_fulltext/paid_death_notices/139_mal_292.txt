
        LUNN-Harry H. Jr. The members of the Art Dealers Association of America mourn the passing of their colleague, friend and member of the Board of Directors. A pioneer photography dealer, he was a leader in the recognition of photography as an important visual art. A fine human being, he had the respect, admiration and affection of his colleagues. Our deep sympathy and condolences go to his wife, Myriam, and his children, Alexandra, Florence and Christophe. Richard Gray, President Gilbert S. Edelson, Admin V.P.
        LUNN-Harry H., Jr. Christie's Photographs Departments in New York and London mourn the loss of our friend, colleague, and mentor Harry H. Lunn, Jr.
