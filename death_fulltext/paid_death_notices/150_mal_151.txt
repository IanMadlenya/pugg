
        GILDEA-Thomas J. Of Saddle River, New Jersey, died on July 6, 1999 at his home. Beloved husband of Isabel Gaye Bulick Gildea, her children Susan Reynolds, Jeff and Bill Bulick, his stepsons, Drew and David Ferrari whose mother Jane Becton died in 1978. Loving uncle of Charles A. Gildea of Saddle River, Sharon Christ of Wyckoff and Mary Ann Kelleher of Bedford, New Hampshire. He was a specialist on the floor of the New York Stock Exchange for 39 years, retiring as a partner with MJ Meehan and Company in 1985. He was a long time volunteer at the Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, New Jersey. The family will receive relatives and friends Saturday from 9:00 to 9:45 AM in the Msgr. Kavin Center, located behind St. Gabriel the Archangel RC Church, 88 E. Saddle River Road, Saddle River, New Jersey. A funeral Mass will be celebrated at 10:00AM in the main church, with interment to follow in Maryrest Cemetery, Mahwah, NJ. As there are no visiting hours at the funeral home, donations instead of flowers to the Valley Hospital Auxiliary, c/o The Valley Hospital, Linwood and North Van Dien Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ 07450 would be appreciated by the family. Feeney Funeral Home, Ridgewood NJ 201-444-7650.
