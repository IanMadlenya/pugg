
        ARESTY-Julian J. The officers, members and staff of the American Jewish Committee send heartfelt condolences to our dear colleague and friend Jane Silverman on the death of her beloved father, Julian Aresty. Jane, a long time member of our AJC family and wife of our late Executive Director Ira Silverman, serves as AJC Secretary and as a member of our Executive Committee and Board of Governors. At this time of sorrow, our thoughts and prayers are with Jane, her children Jacob, Rachel and Sarah, and the entire grieving family. Bruce M. Ramer, President David A. Harris, Exec. Dir.
        ARESTY-Julian. The Board and staff of the National Foundation for Jewish Culture mourn the death of Julian J. Aresty. Our hearts go out to his daughter, Jane Sliverman, and to the entire family. May his memory be a blessing. Lynn Kroll, President Robert Frankel, Chairman Richard Siegel, Exec. Dir.
