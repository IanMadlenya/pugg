
        EDMONDS - Edward Dana. Passed away untimely on Sept. 20. A resident of Boca Raton, Florida, Edward was a Lt. (j.g.) in the U.S. Navy having retired from the service after an automobile accident in 1982. A graduate of the University of South Carolina, Edward was an active participant in the Paralyzed Veterans of America, and an avid supporter of continuing spinal chord research everywhere. His father L. Scott Edmonds, Captain U.S. Navy (Ret.) of Jacksonville, Florida, died four years ago. Edward is survived by his mother, Mary Elizabeth Edmonds, his brother Robert Scott Edmonds, and innumerable saddened friends. A Memorial Service will be held at St. Bart's Episcopal Church, 50th and Park Avenue on Friday, September 25 at 2PM. Contributions may be made in his name to the Miami Project to Cure Paralysis, P.O. Box 016960 (R. 48), Miami, Florida 33101.
