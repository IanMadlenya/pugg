
        BAROUCH-Herman N. Died May 1, 2003, age 86. Beloved husband to Gloria and beloved husband to the late Edna. Beloved son of Anna and Nissim. Beloved brother of Millie, Lucy and the late Manny. Proud father of Norman (Laurie), Dennis and Andi (Steve); step-father to Laurie (Jeff). Proud grandfather of eight. Hy graduated from CUNY Baruch College 1937; was Chief Warrant Officer during WWII; and president of Barouch Bros. He will be missed by all who knew him.
