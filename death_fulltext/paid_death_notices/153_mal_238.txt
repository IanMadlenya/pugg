
        SIVIGLIA-Alexander of Scarborough, NY, died on Sunday October 17, 1999 of heart failure. He was 95 years old. He is survived by his son Peter and Peter's wife Anne, his grandchildren Ellen and Judy and Ellen's husband Rich and a great-grandson Jacob. The son of immigrants, he illuminated the opportunities America provides and the success that diligence and hard work can achieve in our country. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers, donations to The Multiple Sclerosis Society or the Parkinson's Disease Assn. would be appreciated.
