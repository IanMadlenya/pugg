
        BEGLIN--Richard W. 73, of Searsport, ME and Nassau, the Bahamas, died July 26, 2006 after a long illness. He is survived by his life partner, David N. Berg, his brother, retired New Jersey Superior Court Judge, Edward W. Beglin, Jr., and his sister-in-law Rev. Helen M. Beglin of Westfield, NJ, his nephew Dr. Peter Beglin and his wife Elizabeth of Bellingham, WA, his nieceJulie Beglin and her husband Daniel Horan of Brooklyn and their three children. The funeral will be private. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Richard W. Beglin American Literature Collection of Carver Memorial Library, POBox 439, Searsport, ME 04974.
