
        CHAMBERLIN-W. Macy. On March 13, 1998. The entire family of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center mourns the passing of Honorary Trustee W. Macy Chamberlin. A member of our Board of Trustees for two decades, he was a beloved and revered leader of St. Luke's Hospital. His dedication and committment will be sorely missed. To his wife, Irma Morell, his daughters, Kate DeForest Johnson and Judith Macy Stroud, and his entire family, we offer our profound condolences. Lawrence S. Huntington Chairman of the Board Ronald C. Ablow, MD President Richard P. Fried, MD President of the Medical Board
