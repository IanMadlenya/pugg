
        GIBBON-John, Ph.D. The faculty and staff of the New York State Psychiatric Institute mourn the loss of Dr. John Gibbon, a distinguished scholar and admired colleague. He started his career at the Institute in 1963 and was director of the Biopsychology Department from 1990 until his death. Our deepest condolences are extended to his family. John Oldham, M.D., Director NY State Psychiatric Institute
