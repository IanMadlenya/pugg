
        WILLARD-Eugenia. The Board of Directors, Staff and neighbors of Hamilton Madison Settlement House, located in The Two Bridge/ Chinatown community in Manhattan, mourn the loss of Eugenia Willard, a Board Member of The Settlement House since 1941. She was a Past President, Chairperson, and a dedicated fund raiser. We will all truly miss her. Joseph P. Ritorto, President Frank T. Modica, Exec. Director
