
        RICH--Joy C. The Rich-Pure family mourns the passing of their beloved mother and grandmother. After struggling for years with Alzheimer's, Joy died peacefully in her bed at the age of 84, on October 2, 2004. Joy lived a full and meaningful life devoted to her family, friends and psychoanalytic profession. She will be greatly missed. A memorial will take place on Friday, October 8, 4 pm, in Riverside-Nassau Memorial Chapel, across from Great Neck train station.
