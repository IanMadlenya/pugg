
        SCHULHOF-Rudolph B. 1912-1999. After a short illness. Devoted husband of 60 years to Hannelore Buck, father of Michael, Ronald, Thomas, and Wendy. Devoted grandfather to Mark, Amy, Dean, David, Jonathan, Lauren, Scott, Todd, Toni, and Ronnie. Born Czechoslovakia, May 13, 1912. Czech Davis Cup tennis player, graduated from Charles University, Prague, moved to New York in 1940 creating Reproducta Company as a major force in the greeting card and publishing industry. Well known patron of the arts, business publisher, founding director of the Israel Museum, Jerusalem; Trustee, Solomon R. Guggenheim Museum New York; Trustee, Peggy Guggenheim Foundation, Venice. Survived by his wife, children, grandchildren, and brother, Joseph. Funeral service at Campbell Funeral Home, 1076 Madison Avenue, on Sunday, January 17, at 10:00 AM.
        SCHULHOF-Rudolph. The Board of Governors and we of North Shore Country Club record with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Rudolph. We extend our deepest condolences to his wife and family. Jerome Grossman, President Enid Rosenthal, Secretary
        SCHULHOF-Rudolph B. The Fifth Central Park Corp. extends their deepest sympathies to the family and friends of Mr. Rudolph B. Schulhof, long-term Board Member. He will be greatly missed and always remembered. Board of Directors
