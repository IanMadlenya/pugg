
        VARNEDOE -- Kirk. The members of The Contemporary Arts Council of The Museum of Modern Art deeply mourn the loss of their esteemed friend and colleague, Kirk Varnedoe. Kirk, through his extraordinary scholarship and irrepressible enthusiasm for art and artists, expanded all of our horizons immeasurably. We extend to his family our profound condolences.
