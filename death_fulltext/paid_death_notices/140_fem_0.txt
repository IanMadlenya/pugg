
        CARVER-Marie (nee Frega), died at Englewood Hospital and Medical Center, on August 30, 1998, after a brief illness. Mrs. Carver was predeceased by her beloved husband, William and her fond companion, Charles Dreitlein. She is survived by her dear cousin, Sophie Walsh and her devoted brother-in-law, Gino Bagnasco. Marie Carver was for many years Executive Secretary and Personal Assistant to Jack Wrather, Chairman of the Board of Wrather Corporation and Muzak Corporation. She also assisted Mr. Wrather's wife, the noted actress Ronita Granville. During her years with The Wrather's she worked on such shows as ''Lassie'' and ''Sgt. Preston of the Yukon''. In later years, she joined Worldvision Enterprises, Inc. as Executive Secretary and Special Assistant to the Chairman of the Board, Kevin O'Sullivan. During her tenure at Worldvision, Mrs. Carver was involved with the development of such Television Presentations as ''Holocaust'', ''Little House on the Prairie'', Freedom Road'', ''The Love Boat'', ''Mod Squad'', Barnaby Jones'', and many others. Kevin O'Sullivan stated, 'Marie Carver as a remarkable talented woman. She not only was of great professional assistance to me, but she was a true friend. My family and I will miss her deeply'.
