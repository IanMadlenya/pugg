
        FLEETWOOD--Harry. Died at 86, January 18, in New York City. A broadcast icon for 50 years, he was radio's first allnight classical music DJ for 30 years, first on NBC and then WNCN. He hosted, narrated, announced and sang on many other New York City radio/TV stations and, in French, broadcast on the Lively Arts for Radio Luxembourg and Radio Television Francaise. Interment will be private. There will be a memorial service at a date to be announced.
