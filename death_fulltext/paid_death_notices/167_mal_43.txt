
        KLEIN-Ruben. December 2. Late of Mt. Vernon, NY. Beloved husband of Helen (Kramer). Devoted father of Joan E. Cohen of Newton, MA, and Stephen F. Klein of Yardley, PA. Loving brother of the late Albert Klein and Clare Smithline. Dear grandfather of Seth and Heidi Cohen, Nicole and Alexander Klein. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY, on Monday, December 4, at 1 PM. Memorial Observance Monday and Tuesday at the home of Eugene and Carol Klein, 77 Greenridge Ave., White Plains, NY. Donations in his memory may be made to Sinai Free Synagogue, 550 North Columbus Ave., Mt. Vernon, NY 10550, or the YM & YWHA of Southern Westchester, 30 Oakley Ave., Mt. Vernon, NY 10552.
        KLEIN-Ruben. The Officers, Directors and Members of the Bronx Realty Advisory Board deeply mourn the passing of Ruben Klein, our President of 25 years and recently Chairman of the Board. A wonderful leader and an inspiration to us all. Our heartfelt sympathy to his wife Helen, his children, grandchildren and entire family. Michael Laub, President Carol Keenan, Executive Director
