
        FEILZER-Dorothy (nee Knight). Of East Meadow. Peacefully at home on July 11, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Devoted mother of Ellen O'Reilly, Christine Catharine and Paul Feilzer. Loving grandmother of Mary Ellen, Edward, Patrick, John and Maureen O'Reilly, Sharon, Douglas & Suzie Catharine. Also survived by her four loving great grandchildren. Family will receive friends at the Leo F. Kearns Funeral Home, 445 East Meadow Avenue, East Meadow, Wednesday from 2-4 & 7-9 P.M. Funeral Thursday. Requiem at Christ the King Episcopal Church, on Thursday at 10:00 A.M. Interment, Greenfield Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Christ the King Episcopal Church Memorial Garden would be appreciated.
