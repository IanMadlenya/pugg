
        JONES-Russell G. 54, Of Darien, CT, passed away on December 16, 1999. Devoted husband of Virginia Lolli Jones and loving father of Melissa, Douglas and Laura. Beloved brother of Margie Tremblay of Holden, MA, Audrey Muir of Brooks, ME, Shirley Gravemberg of Sulphur, LA, Larry Jones of Matthews, NC, Beverly Jones of Shrevenport, LA, and Barbara LeBouef of Manchester, NH. Mr. Jones was a Managing Director and Head of Telecom Project Finance at Citigroup. During his 30 year career at Citigroup, Mr. Jones held a variety of key assignments, including management and transactional responsibility relating to the Power, Telecommunications, and Metals and Mining industries. His many friends at Citigroup will always remember his intellectual prowess, generosity and integrity. He was a mentor and teacher to Citigroup colleagues and clients worldwide. He will awlays remain an inspiration to those fortunate to have known him.
