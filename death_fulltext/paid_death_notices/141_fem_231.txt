
        LIEF-Lila Ehrich, 72, of Chestnut Hill, MA, formerly of New York City, died of leukemia on October 26 at her home. Her husband, Dr. Philip A. Lief of the Brigham and Women's Hospital and the Harvard Medical School, died in 1992. She is survived by her four children, William Lief of Manalapan, NJ, Louise Lief of Washington, D.C., Gerald Lief of Brookline, MA, and Varda Lief of Chestnut Hill, MA, and three grandchildren, Erica Lief, Scott Lief and Zachary LiefPaul. She was the sister of Fredric Ehrich of Marblehead, MA and the late Zara Blechman. Services at the Levine Chapel, Brookline, MA on Wednesday, October 28.
