
        TESTA-Ettore. Passed away peacefully at home on February 15, at age 90, surrounded by his family: wife Constance, daughters Constance, Jeanette and Maria, son-in-law Robert Lutz, and grandson Eric. Also survived by his brother Guido and sisters, sistersin-law, nieces and nephews. Graduated from Brooklyn College of Pharmacy in 1927. Retired from the Upjohn Company after 30 years of service. Services are private. Donations may be made to the New York Times Neediest Cases Fund or to a charity of choice.
