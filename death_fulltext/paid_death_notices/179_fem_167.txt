
        POPPER-Helen Krass on December 24, 2001, at age 90. Beloved wife of the late Edward Popper. Loving mother of Jane & Warren Zorek and John and Abby Popper. Cherished grandmother of Jennifer and Mark Pressman, Michael Zorek and Shelly Friedland, Robert Arthur Popper and Marta Melvin & Juliette Popper. Adoring great-grandmother of Noah Duncan Popper, Ella Popper and Jessica Pressman. Loving daughter of the late Rabbi Nathan Krass, Senior Rabbi at Temple EmanuEl, and the late Eda Krass. Dear sister of Alice Loewenheim. Service and interment private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the 92nd Street Y or Temple Emanu-El.
        POPPER-Helen Krass. The Rabbis, Officers, Trustees and Members of Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York, record with deep sorrow the death of Helen Krass Popper, whose late father, Rabbi Dr. Nathan Krass, served as our spiritual leader more than half a century ago. Mrs. Popper, in her own right, brought abundant blessings to our community. With her children and loved ones, we mourn her passing, assured that her memory will endure as a precious blessing. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Senior Rabbi Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
