
        DUNEFSKY - Phyllis. The staff of the Anti-Defamation League mourns the untimely passing of our colleague, Phyllis Dunefsky, Assistant Director, New York Campaign. We extend our condolences to her brother, Jerry, sister-in-law, Joyce, nephews, Brian, Adam and Eric, and the entire family. Abraham H. Foxman, National Director Joel J. Levy, Director, New York Regional Office
