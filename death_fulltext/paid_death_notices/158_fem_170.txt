
        REINHARDT-Marguerite E. Educator and teacher of visually limited children for over forty years died Thursday, March 16, at Francis Schervier Home, Riverdale, NY at age 94. A graduate of The College of New Rochelle, a college she loved and never tired of singing its praises. Formerly of Bronxville, NY, and the Bronx, NY, she retired from the NYC public school system in 1971. Calling hours 2pm-4pm on Sunday, March 19 at the Fred Mc Grath Funeral Home. Mass of Christian Burial, St. Joseph's RC Church on Monday, March 20 at 10AM. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to The College of New Rochelle, C/O Brenna Mayer, 914-645-5000.
