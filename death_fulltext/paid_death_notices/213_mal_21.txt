
        GEFFEN--Steven B. The Boards of the Friends of the North Castle Public Library, Inc. and the Armonk Outdoor Art Show mourn the untimely passing of our President and Friend, Steven B. Geffen. Steve worked tirelessly as Chairman of the Art Show and President of the Friends for many years. Under his leadership, commitment and guidance, the show grew to become the highest rated show in the New York metropolitan area. His energy, enthusiasm and passion were invaluable to the Library, the Whippoorwill Association and the Armonk community. Steve will be remembered as a leader, citizen and a friend. We will mourn his loss. Executive Board and Directors of the Friends of the North Castle Public Libary, Inc.
