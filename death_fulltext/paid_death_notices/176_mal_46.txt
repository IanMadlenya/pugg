
        SO-Pablo, MD. The Medical Staff of St. Clare's Hospital and Health Center mourn the passing of Dr. Pablo So, a member of the Dept. of Anesthesiology from 1975 until his retirement in 1996. He will be remembered as a caring physician and a man devoted to this institution. We wish to extend our heartfelt condolences to his wife, Constanzia, and to his children, Katharina, Edward, Melena and Bernadette. David M. Burke, MD President, Medical Staff
