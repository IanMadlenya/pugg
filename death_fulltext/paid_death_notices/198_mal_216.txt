
        LEMOINE--Very Rev. Harold F. died July 7, 2003. Survived by his nephew, Frank Lemoine of Laurel, MD. Dean Emeritus of The Cathedral of the Incarnation, Garden City, New York from November 1, 1942 to January 31, 1957. A memorial service will be held at The Cathedral of the Incarnation (to be announced). Donations to his Memorial Fund may be made payable to the Cathedral of the Incarnation Dean Harold F. Lemoine Memorial Fund, addressed to his executor John K. Giacinto, 580 Mineola Avenue, Carle Place, New York 11514. >>AD#
