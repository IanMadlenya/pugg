
        SWORDS-Donald Joseph, 69, of Freehold Township, NJ on May 13, 2001. He was an attorney in New York City. Beloved husband of Marion Schaad Swords and cherished father of Alana and Liam Swords. Calling hours will be held at the Higgins Memorial Home, 20 Center Street, Freehold on Wednesday from 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. The Funeral Liturgy will be celebrated at St. Rose of Lima Church, Freehold on Thursday at 10 AM followed by interment at St. Rose of Lima Cemetery, Freehold. Memorial donations to the New York City Police Relief Fund, 3813 Northern Blvd, LIC, NY 11101 or Lung Cancer Screening Fund at New York University, c/o of Dr. Chachoua, NYU, 160 East 32 Street, New York, NY 10016 would be appreciated.
