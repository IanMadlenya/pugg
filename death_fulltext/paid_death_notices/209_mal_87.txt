
        TANZMAN--Norman, real estate executive, former NJ State Senator and philanthropist, 85, of West End, NJ, died on June 6, 2004. Funeral services will be on June 8, 12 noon, at Congregation Adath Israel, Amboy Ave., Woodbridge, NJ. Family requests donations to Marion Tanzman Diabetes Center at Raritan Bay Medical Center, c/o Raritan Bay Healthcare Foundation, 471 Lawrie St., Perth Amboy, NJ 08861. Shiva will be observed at the home of Rona and Jeffries Shein, Holmdel, today till 9 pm and Wednesday and Thursday, 19 PM. Crabiel Parkwest Funeral Chapel, 239 Livingston Ave., New Brunswick, NJ
        TANZMAN -- Norman. The Board of Directors and staff of The Oscar and Ella Wilf Campus for Jewish Life mourn the passing of Norman Tanzman. Mr. Tanzman was a founder and past president of the Central NJ Jewish Home for the Aged in Somerset, NJ. He also served as Chairman of the Board of the Central NJ Geriatric Services Corporation. Mr. Tanzman was an inspiration to all who care about the elderly in Central NJ. His legacy will live on in the programs and services on the Wilf Campus. He will be sorely missed. Central NJ Jewish Home for the Aged; The Lena & David T. Wilentz Senior Residence; SomerBrook, The Martin & Edith Stein Assisted Living Residence.
        TANZMAN--Norman. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Hollywood Golf Club note with sorrow the passing of their longtime friend and member, Norman Tanzman. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his family. Jerold L. Zaro, President Harvey L. York, Secretary
