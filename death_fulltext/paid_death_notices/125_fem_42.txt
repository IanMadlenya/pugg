
        FLEISHER-Estelle. The American Friends of Tel Aviv University mourns the passing of Estelle Fleisher, the loving and devoted wife of our esteemed board member Seymour Fleisher. Estelle together with Seymour, generously supported Tel Aviv University's Bridge to Peace Scholarship Fund and co-founded the Attia/Gordon Memorial Scholarship Fund. We will miss her warmth and humor. Our heartfelt condolences to the family. Melvin S. Taub, Chairman, Board of Directors Robert J. Topchik, President Lester Entin, Deputy Chairman, TAU Board of Govenors Jules Love, Executive V.P.
