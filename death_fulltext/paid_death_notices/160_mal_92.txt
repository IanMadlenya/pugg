
        COHEN-Rev. Gerald Philip CSC, of Middletown, Connecticut, May 7, 2000. Son of the late Louis and Mary (Donovan) Cohen; cousin of Alice DeFranco of Sudbury, Robert and Richard Brown of California; also survived by all his brothers of the Holy Cross Community. A Concelebrated funeral mass will be held in the Chapel of Mary Mother of the Church, Stonehill College, North Easton, Massachusetts on Wednesday at 10:00 A.M. Interment will be in the Holy Cross Community Cemetery, North Easton. Rev. Cohen CSC will lie in state in the Chapel of Mary Mother of the Church on Tuesday evening from 7-9 P.M. with a vigil service at 7:30 P.M. In lieu of flowers donations in Rev. Cohen CSC memory may be sent to the Rev. Cohen CSC Scholarship Fund c/o Fran Dillon, Office of College Relations, Stonehill College 320 Washington Street, North Easton, Massachusetts 02356.
