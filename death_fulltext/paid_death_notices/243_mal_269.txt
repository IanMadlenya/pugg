
        GILL--Captain Frank, 64, a native of Long Beach, N.Y., and a son of the late Arthur and Ruth Gill, on April 21, 2007, after a courageous battle with cancer. A Vietnam veteran who loved the sea, Frank was a great fisherman and pioneered surfing in Long Beach. He is survived by his sons Blake and Alex; daughter Erin; sisters Nancy McShea (Jerry), Kit Gill and Jane Gill; brother Roger Gill (Susan); nieces Colette, Susan, Marisa and Grace; nephews Justin, Jeremy and Robert; grandnephew and niece Quinn and Tatum; and his beloved Bichon, Lily. A memorial mass will be held at St. Ignatius Martyr Church in Long Beach.
