
        MARKS-Gerald, January 27, 1997. The Board of Directors of the New York Sheet Music Society mourns the loss of our gentle, devoted member. Gerald, one of the last Tin Pan Alley giants, composer of ''All of Me,'' ''Is It True What They Say About Dixie,'' and 400 other songs will be missed, admired and remembered. He is survived by a niece, Harriet Bloomberg, 400 songs and thousands of friends and admirers. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers a donation to the Gerald and Edna Marks Award Fund or the New York Sheet Music Society at P.O. Box 354, Hewlett, NY 11557 will be appreciated.
        MARKS-Gerald. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Gerald Marks, on January 27, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Private services were held. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
