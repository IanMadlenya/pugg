
        CORNELL-J. Martin. Our sincere condolences to Harriet, Valerie, Keith, Kendall and Allison, the family of J. Martin Cornell, a longtime friend of those suffering from mental illness and for 32 years a vital and contributing member of the Board of Directors of the Mental Health Association of Rockland County. In his quiet, soft-spoken manner he touched the lives of so many and was deeply respected and admired. This kind, loyal gentleman will be missed by all. Sandy Forman Chairman of the Board Dr. Karen A. Oates President/CEO MHA of RC
