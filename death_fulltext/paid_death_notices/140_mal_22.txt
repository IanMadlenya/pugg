
        LISMAN-Jack V. M.D. Age 81. Died on August 30, 1998. Survived by his wife of 54 years Sylvia; children Richard and Lee Lisman, Jane and Robert J. Katz; sister Leona Rothstein; four grandchildren, Hilary, Bogert, Cyrena L. Fink and James and Emily Katz, and great-granddaughter Brigitte J. Fink. Distinguished staff member and past Chief of the Division of Opthalmic Plastic Surgery at Manhattan Eye and Ear Hospital. Private services were held Tuesday, September 1 in Hastings-on-Hudson, NY. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to NYU School of Medicine or The Orbital Disease Foundation (635 Park Avenue NYC 10021)
