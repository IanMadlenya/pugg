
        VEITCH--Robert A. Of Commack, NY and Stuart, FL, on March 6, 2004. Beloved husband of Pearl (nee Pecorella). Loving father of Robert Alan Veitch and Joann Schedra Sterber. Robert served in the U.S. Army from 1943-1945. He also graduated from Massachusetts Institute of Technology with a degree in Aeronautic Engineering in 1946. Upon graduation, Robert began his 44 years of dedicated service to Grumman Aerospace until retirement in 1990. A private cremation was held. A memorial service will be held Friday, June 4th from 11am - 12pm at the A.L. Jacobsen Funeral Home, Inc., 1380 New York Ave., Huntington Station, NY 11746. Donations may be made in Robert's memory to MIT, 77 Massachusetts Ave., Cambridge, MA 02139.
