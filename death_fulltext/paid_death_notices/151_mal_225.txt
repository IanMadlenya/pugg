
        HOWARD-Harold, Paul. On August 19, 1999 after a valiant battle with cancer. Vice President of Bear Stearns. He was a sergeant in The U.S. Marine Corps. Beloved son of the late Rose and Harry Howard. Cherished brother of Roslyn Madsen and Loretta Buscarino. Devoted uncle of James Buscarino and Danielle Rebischung, MD. Also survived by loving cousins and many dear friends. Reposing at Edward D. Lynch & Son Funeral Home, 43-07 Queens Blvd., Sunnyside, NY, Saturday, 7-9:30PM and Sunday, 2-5 & 7-9:30PM. Funeral mass at St. Teresa R.C. Church, Woodside, NY, Monday, 10:15AM.
