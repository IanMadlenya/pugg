
        FISCHER - Margaret (Maggie). The Saltaire community is grieved beyond measure at the sudden and tragic passing of Maggie Fischer. Her brightness, compassion and gentle nature were a joy in all our lives. She leaves us with memories we shall always cherish. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Maggie's parents, Bob and Mary, her brother Chris, and all her family and friends. Mayor and Trustees Village of Saltaire
