
        ISEMAN-Marjorie Frankenthaler of Manhattan, died on May 10, 2003. Greatly loved & admired mother of Peter, Frederick and Ellen and devoted grandmother of Eugenie Iseman, Henry Iseman and Alexander O'Neill. Loving sister of Helen Frankenthaler and the late Gloria Bookman. A memorial gathering will be held at the Cosmopolitan Club, 122 East 66 Street on Thursday, June 5th. 4PM. Friends planning to attend should please call Ozen at 212-774-5809 for details. Contributions, in lieu of flowers, may be made in the name of Marjorie F. Iseman to The Writers Room, 740 Broadway, 12th floor, New York, New York 10003
        ISEMAN-Marjorie F. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Marjorie F. Iseman. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Rabbi David M. Posner Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y
