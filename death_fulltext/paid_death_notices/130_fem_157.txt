
        NELSON-Evelyn Baker, died on Friday, November 21, age 76. Mother of Callie Angell and Alice Angell Evangelista, grandmother of Laura and Lily, sister of Susan MacKenna, Mary Carr, and the late Peggy Stewart. Married to Clifford C. Nelson for 23 years and previously to Roger Angell for 19 years. Born in Boston, MA., she lived in Mystic, CT. for the last 28 years. Elegant and proudly independent, she loved her friends, family, and home. She was a champion knitter, and avid reader and painter, and a great gardener and cook. She will be missed by us all. Donations in her memory may be made to the American Diabetes Association, 1660 Duke Street, Alexandria, VA. 22314.
