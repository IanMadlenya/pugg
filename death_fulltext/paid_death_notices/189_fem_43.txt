
        SCHUMAN-Seymour. On October 5, 2002, at age 86. Born in NYC, attended City College, founder and Chairman of Propper Manufacturing Company, real estate developer in Long Island City and winner of the Tom DeLay Business Leadership Award. Beloved husband of Beatrice. Loving father of Ina (and her husband Daniel Ebenstein), Jane Schuman and Marian Schuman Brennan. Adoring grandfather of David, Julie, Andrew and Stacy Ebenstein, Joseph Schuman, Elizabeth, Alex and Carolyn Schuman Brennan. Services at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave at 65 St, Monday, October 7th, 10 AM. Interment to follow, Old Montefiore Cemetery.
