
        FAYERWEATHER--John, 82, formerly of Hartford, VT, and Hastings-on-Hudson, NY, February 3, after a long battle with Alzheimer's disease. Husband of the late Ruth (Selina) Fayerweather, survived by his sons and their families, Charles and Carol and their daughter, Lily, of Sharon, MA, and James and Susan and their children, Andrew and Sarah, of Simsbury, CT. Professor Fayerweather was a graduate of the Kent School, Princeton University, and Harvard Business School, and an Army veteran of World War II and the Korean War. He was a Professor Emeritus of Management and International Business at the Stern School of Business at New York University. Professor Fayerweather was the author of ten books about international business, the founder and Managing Editor of ''The International Executive'', a digest of articles of interest to the academic international business community, and a founder and Fellow of the Academy of International Business. He was a lifelong outdoorsman and supporter of conservation causes. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Nature Conservancy, 27 State St., Montpelier, VT 05602
