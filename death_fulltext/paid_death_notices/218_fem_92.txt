
        FLYER--Diana, 94, has joined her beloved husband, the late Joseph Paul Flyer. Devoted mother of Dennis, Gary and Ronald and mother-in-law of Susan and Susie. Loving grandmother of Vanessa, Jamie, Michael and Caroline. Dear sister of Francine and the late Sophie, Anne and Daniel. Loving daughter of the late Rabbi Chaim Witkin and Rachel Witkin. Also survived by many cousins, friends and admirers. Diana was always there for all of her family and friends. Her wit, wisdom and joy of life will be missed by all who were fortunate to have known and loved her. Services on Friday, 11:30 A.M. at ''The Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
