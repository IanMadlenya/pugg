
        SCHAPIRO-Shirley. Private services were held on Monday, December 21st, 1998 at Old Montefiore Cemetery, Queens, New York. Survived by her beloved children Caren Hilman and Joel Schapiro & loving grandchildren Laurence and Marra Hilman and Marielle Schapiro. Mrs. Schapiro was a lifelong resident of Manhattan before moving to Maplewood, NJ four years ago. Mrs. Schapiro managed the Village Realty office in Manhattan before retiring 12 years ago. For information Menorah Chapels at Millburn, 2950 Vauxhall Rd., Union, New Jersey.
