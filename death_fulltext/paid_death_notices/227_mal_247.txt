
        RENICK--Theodore M. ''Ted''. Age 79 of Centerville, and Boca Raton, FL. Entered into rest December 22, 2005. Beloved husband of Jacqueline ''Jackie'' (Zekind). Devoted father of Jamie Elias and her husband Howard, Bruce Renick and his companion Jodie Berro. Loving poppy of Caroline, Jordana Elias, and Sydney Renick. Dear brother of the late Charles Renick. Services at The Falmouth Jewish Congregation, 7 Hatchville Road, E. Falmouth, Monday, December 26 at 12 Noon. Memorial observance will be at his late residence following interment until Monday afternoon, and Wednesday and Thursday at Jamie and Howard's residence. In lieu of flowers, expressions of sympathy in his memory may be made to Patient to Patient, Heart to Heart c/o Beth Israel Deaconess Medical Center, Office of Development, 330 Brookline Avenue, GZ-216, Boston, MA. 02215.
