
        SCHERR--Albert E. III., on March 28, 2007 at his home in Williamstown, MA. Husband of the late Elizabeth Babcock Scherr, and father of Elizabeth, Albert, Alexander and Edward. Born November 26, 1921 in Mt. Vernon, NY, as the only son of Albert Edward Scherr, Jr. and Marguerite Christine Schwiers. A 1942 graduate of Yale University and a WWII Navy veteran, he married in 1949, and moved to Williamstown in 1950, where he worked for Sprague Electric Company of North Adams until his retirement as Vice President in 1986. He and his wife subscribed to the Metropolitan Opera, were members of numerous New York City museums, and visited the city regularly. He is survived by his four children and their spouses, and grandchildren Anna, Charles, William, Elizabeth, McKenzie and Isabella.
