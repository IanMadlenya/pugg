
        PATTASHNICK-Rabbi Abba. Congregation Orach Chaim expresses its profound sorrow on the passing of the father of our dedicated member, Bonnie Pollak, and expresses heartfelt condolences to her, her husband Isaac, and their children, Zamir, Ovadya and Elianna. A Torah scholar, a man of great dignity and charm, he graced our Congregation for many years. Shiva observed in New York at the Pollak residence, Wednesday evening through Friday noon. May they and the entire family be consoled among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Michael D. Shmidman, Rabbi Wolf Scheck, President
