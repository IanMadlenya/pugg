
        HAIGHT - Frances Hoge. Frances Haight, 90, died on January 27 in Fullerton, CA. Before moving to California in 1995, she lived in New York City, Stamford, CT, Bedford, NY and Loon Lake, NY in the summers. She dedicated her life to family and community service, working worldwide for child welfare and child adoption services. For many years she served as president of International Social Service, traveling to its affiliated agencies around the world. Among the many organizations she served in earlier years were the Spence-Chapin Adoption Agency in NY, the Family and Children's Center in Stamford, Planned Parenthood in NY and the Citizens Committee for Children. She is survived by two sons, Courtenay H. Haight of Blue Hill Falls, Maine, and Peter V. Haight of Los Angeles, eight grandchildren and stepgrandchildren, one greatgranddaughter, and two nieces and their families. Her husband of more than 50 years, Eric H. Haight, died in 1987. There will be a memorial service on Saturday, February 22, 10:30 AM at St. Matthews Episcopal Church, Bedford Village, NY. Donations in her memory may be made to International Social Service, 700 Light Street, Baltimore, MD 21230-3850.
