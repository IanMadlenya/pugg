
        AUERBACH-Sylvia ''Sally''. Beloved sister, aunt and friend. Lifelong resident of NYC died June 7, 2000 in Queens. She was a longtime employee of the New York Times. She was a graduate of New York School for the Deaf, Fanwood. She was very active in the Hebrew Association of the Deaf, The Fanwood Alumni and the National Congress of Jewish Deaf. She is survived by her sisters-inlaw, Hortense Auerbach, Helen Auerbach; Nieces, Deborah Giancoli, Susan Auerbach, Dorry Dimos; Nephew, Michael Auerbach; and great-niece, Nicole Dimos. A memorial service will be planned for September. Donations in her memory may be sent to Kids Fund, New York School for the Deaf-Fanwood, White Plains, NY, or the Hebrew Association of the Deaf, NYC.
