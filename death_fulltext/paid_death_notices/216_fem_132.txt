
        EISENHARDT -- Mildred F. May 28, 1935-January 6, 2005. Millie was a wife, mother and grandmother to Charles, Charles and Melissa, and her twin joys, Hunter and Jaden. She was an artist, composer, pianist and a member of Sigma Alpha Iota. A memorial service will be held January 23, 2005, 3-7PM at Martin A. Gleason Funeral Home, 10-25 150 St, Whitestone New York. 718-359-1122. Millie loved flowers, but a donation may be made at your discretion & in her memory to Calvary Hospital Fund, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461.
