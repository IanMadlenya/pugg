
        HIRTENSTEIN-Daniel. The Board of Directors of the Hebrew Hospital Home wish to express their condolences to the family of Daniel Hirtenstein on their profound loss. We were privileged to have Dan serve as a member of our Board of Directors for over 10 years and we were inspired by his courage during his final illness. Our thoughts are with his beloved wife Audrey, his son Andrew and daughter Traci at this difficult time. Arnold Goldstein, Chairman David A. Spector, Chairman Emeritus Mary Frances Barrett, Chief Executive Officer Hebrew Hospital Home, Inc.
