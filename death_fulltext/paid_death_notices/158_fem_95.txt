
        COOK FERRITER-Judith, accomplished educator, died March 1, 2000 in Palm Desert, California. She founded Edgemont High School's Alternative Education Program in Scarsdale, NY in 1978 and served as Director for seventeen years. She was a resident of Larchmont, NY for thirty-three years. Her interests included education, music, and English literature. She is survived by her husband Roger; four daughters: Ellen, Catherine, Andrea, and Courtney; and four grandchildren. Memorial services to be announced. No gifts or flowers, please.
