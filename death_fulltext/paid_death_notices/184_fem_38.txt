
        ROSWELL-Florence (Garfunkel). Beloved wife of the late David S. Roswell, cherished mother of Arthur and Elaine, devoted motherin-law of Betty, loving grandmother of Robert, Barbara, Marjorie, Judith and Kenneth and adoring great-grandmother of six greatgrandchildren. She was a pioneer professor in the field of remedial reading at CUNY. She will always be remembered for her warmth, concern for others, and her intelligence and love of life. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue and 81st Street Monday, May 6, 11:30 AM. Donations may be made to either Temple Beth-El, 67 Rt. 206, Hillsborough, NJ 08844 or the Florence G. Roswell Computer Laboratory at The Fisher Landau Center, 1165 Morris Park Ave, Bronx, NY 10461, or to Dorot, 171 West 85th St, NY, NY 10024.
