
        VOLLBEHR-Robert Jules, Colonel. A USAF Retired Colonel. Air commander expired from natural causes on April 16, 2003 at the age of 88. Colonel Robert Vollbehr was a native of Amsterdam in the Netherlands, proud of being a US citizen and served in the Air Force for 28 years. He was an assistant to General Patton, later served with General Walter Mueller, after the Second World War joined USAF Continental Air PatrolAir War College, Senior Officer. He is survived by his cousin Veronica P. Donne, New York City. Funeral private.
