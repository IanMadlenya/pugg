
        LEVY-Ceil (nee Novison). May 11, 1914 to November 7, 1999, surrounded by many loving people to ease her passage into the next phase of the journey. Her time on earth was only the beginning. Her spirit, courage, intelligence and love of learning leave a legacy to her family and friends now and forever. Among the survivors are brother Hyman; daughters Rodelle, Sheryl and Suelle; son-in-laws Irwin and David; grandchildren Patti and Scott, Robin, Stacey and Gregg; and great grandchildren Max, Lindsey and Mitchell.
