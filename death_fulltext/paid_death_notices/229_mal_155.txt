
        WISNER--Louis (Lukie). On February 10, 2006 of Forest Hills, NY. He is survived by his wife Lillian (nee Bernstein). Sister Belle Littenberg and nieces Charlotte Futterman, Abby and Nancy Bloom and their extended families. A graduate of Erasmus Hall High School and The City College of New York.
