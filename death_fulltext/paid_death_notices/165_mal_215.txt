
        SCHLACHET-Peter. The Staff, Faculty and Trustees of Postgraduate Center for Mental Health mourn the passing of Peter Schlachet, Ph.D., distinguished faculty member, senior supervisor, psychoanalyst and author. His contributions to our individual and group training programs and to the development of clinical theory will always be remembered. We express our heartfelt sympathy to family and friends. Dr. Jacob Barak President and CEO Dr. Mary Beth Cresci Dean of Training
