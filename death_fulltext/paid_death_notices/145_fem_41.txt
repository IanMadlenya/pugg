
        RAYMOND-Dorothy Ann. On February 5, 1999. Beloved mother of Monica, Denise, Katherine and Margaret. Wife of the late Boris Raymond. Devoted sister of Morton Silk. Proud mother-in-law of Charles Lewitz and Mark Sidel. Adoring grandmother of Rosemary, Theadora, and Daniel. Lifelong friend of Margaret Coyne. Services on Sunday, February 7, at 10:45 A.M., at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. We will miss her unique wit, spirit and steadfast presence.
