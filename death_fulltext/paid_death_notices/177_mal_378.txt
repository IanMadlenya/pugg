
        GOLOVIN-Willard Bayer. On Sunday, October 21, 2001, at age 93. Devoted husband of Isabelle Ripley Peck Golovin, beloved father of Ripley Hathaway and father-inlaw of Tyler Hathaway, and cherished ''Pop-Pop'' to Clement Willard Beecher Hathaway. Interment services were private. In lieu of flowers the family requests that donations be made in his name to The Masters School Community Service Program, 49 Clinton Avenue, Dobbs Ferry, N.Y. 10522, or Scenic Hudson, Inc., 9 Vassar Street, Poughkeepsie, N.Y 12601.
