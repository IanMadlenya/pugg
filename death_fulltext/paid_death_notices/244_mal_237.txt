
        DINEEN--Cornelius E. (Neil), of Hartsdale passed away on May 24, 2007. He was 75 years old. Mr. Dineen was a 37 year employee of NYCTA MABSTOA Division. He was a bus driver, driving the Lexington Ave. Line, Manhattan 100 Street Bus Depot. He is predeceased by his late wife Bridget M. Dineen.
