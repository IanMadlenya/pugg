
        CHEW-Robert Preston, formerly of Greenwich, Conn., died of cancer at his home in Charlottesville, VA., September 15, 1997. Bob Chew graduated from Princeton University in 1942, served as Field Artillery Captain in the Philippines where he earned the Silver Star for ''action behind the Japanese lines.'' He then spent 38 years in the chemical industry, retiring in 1984 from Essex Chemical Co. Bob Chew is survived by his wife Elena and by his sons John, Robert and Thomson.
