
        BITTKER-Evelyn Brown, 91. Died April 20, 1999 at her home in Las Vegas. Born in New York City, she spent many years as Head of Reservations at the Grossinger Hotel in Ferndale, N.Y. Several years after her marriage to Arthur Bittker, they bought property in Tucson and started the El Dorado Ranch which had a highly successful run of over twenty years. Arthur and Evelyn then moved to Las Vegas where she has lived for almost over 30 years. Adoring wife of the late Arthur, revered aunt of seven, great aunt of 14, great grandaunt of 29. She will be missed by family and numerous friends alike.
