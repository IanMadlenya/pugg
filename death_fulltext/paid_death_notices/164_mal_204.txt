
        LEPORE-Michael J., M. D. The Gastroenterology Dept. of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital was saddened to hear of the loss of Dr. Michael Lepore, the former Chief of Gastroenterology at Roosevelt Hospital (1962-1966). He was an outstanding educator and an inspiration to many of our trainees and attending physicians. Heartfelt condolences to members of the family and friends. Peter Holt, M.D. Albert Attia, M.D. Gastroenterology Section St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital
