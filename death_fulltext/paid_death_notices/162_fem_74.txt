
        TOFFLER-Karen. Died prematurely at age 46, July 9, 2000, in Los Angeles leaving many, many friends and a legacy of love, admiration, grief and laughter. She touched the hearts of people of all races, classes and cultures. She is survived by her parents, Heidi and Alvin Toffler. Donations may be made to the Guillain Barre Syndrome Foundation, PO Box 262, Wynnewood, PA 19096 or the Wildlife Waystation, 14831 Little Tujunga Canyon Rd, Angeles National Forest, CA 91342-5999. Memorial services will be held at Westwood Memorial Park, 1218 Glendon Ave., Los Angeles, 1:30 PM, July 12, 2000.
