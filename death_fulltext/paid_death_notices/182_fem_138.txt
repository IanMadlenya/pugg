
        ROTHSCHILD-Ida. On March 11, 2002 in her 102nd year. Beloved wife of the late Max. Adored mother of Carolyn and Norman Oshin, and William and Carol Rothschild. Receiving at the Rothschild residence in Great Neck Tuesday, Wednesday and Thursday evenings.
        ROTHSCHILD-Ida. Temple BethEl of Great Neck records with profound sorrow the passing of its longtime member. We extend heartfelt sympathy to her bereaved family. Franklin S. Abrams, President Jerome K. Davidson, Rabbi
