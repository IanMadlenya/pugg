
        PULLO-Rev. Francis S. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the retired parochial vicar of Corpus Christi Church, Woodside, August 10. Reposing at Maloney's Hauppauge Funeral Home, 840 Wheeler Road (Route 111), Hauppage, L.I., Wednesday and Thursday, August 12 and 13, from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9:00 P.M. Funeral Mass in the Immaculate Conception Center Chapel, 7200 Douglaston Parkway, Douglaston, Friday, August 14, at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
