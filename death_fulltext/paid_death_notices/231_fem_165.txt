
        ROSSITER--Lesley. After a courageous two year battle with cancer, Lesley passed peacefully at home with her family on April 21, 2006. Born Lesley Marlene Holtan, September 17, 1957, Lesley was a free, creative spirit who lived life uncompromisingly on her own terms. She is survived by her husband Robert Rossiter, daughter Alexandra Rossiter, mother Marlene Holtan, and her siblings, Brian Holtan, Gary Holtan, Kim Holtan Lang. She was deeply loved and will be dearly missed. Memorial service Saturday, April 29, Brooklyn's Greenwood Cemetery. In lieu of flowers please send donations in Lesley's name to Cabrini Hospice, NYC.
