
        AUCHINCLOSS-Hugh, MD of Westwood, MA and Manchester, MA, formerly of Ho-Ho-Kus, NJ, died on October 25, 1998 at the age of 83. His death resulted from complications of Alzheimer's Disease. Dr. Auchincloss was born in New York City, the son of Dr. Hugh Auchincloss and Frances C. (Newlands) Auchincloss. He attended Groton School, graduated from Yale in 1938 and from the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons in 1942. After service in the Navy during World War II, he finished his residency and joined the faculty at Columbia as Associate Clinical Professor of Surgery at Columbia. He was also Attending Surgeon at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, NJ for 39 years prior to his retirement in 1992. He is survived by his wife of 56 years, Katharine Lawrence (Bundy) Auchincloss, three daughters, Katharine A. Corbin of Phoenix, AZ, Margaret A. Strachan of London, Elizabeth L. Auchincloss, MD of New Rochelle, NY, his son, Hugh Auchincloss, Jr, MD of Newton, MA, two sisters, Maria A. Look of West Tisbury, MA and Barbara A. Thacher of New Canaan, CT, eleven grandchildren and one great grandson. Services and interment at Mount Auburn Cemetery in Cambridge will be private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Valley Hospital, N. Van Dien Ave, Ridgewood, NJ 07450. For information, The J.S. Waterman and Sons-Waring Funeral Home, Wellesley, MA.
