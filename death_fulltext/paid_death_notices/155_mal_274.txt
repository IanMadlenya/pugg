
        HALBFINGER - Abraham. The Board, staff and members of the Jewish Association for Services for the Aged (JASA), note with deep sorrow the passing of the father of our beloved and esteemed Trustee, Marty Halbfinger. Our hearts go out to Marty and Lynn, their daughters and his mother Sara in their grief. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Leonard Boxer, President Marilyn Friedman, Chair David J. Stern, Exec VP
        HALBFINGER-Abraham. On December 20, 1999, age 99. Beloved husband of Sara for 68 years. Proud grandfather of Marla and Scott Hyndman, Felicia Posner, Bonnie & Caroline Halbfinger. Great grandfather of Melissa and Justin Hyndman. Service on Wednesday at 12:45 P.M., at the Plaza Memorial Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Avenue at 91st St.
        HALBFINGER-Abraham. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Abraham Halbfinger, beloved father of our dedicated members, Martin and Lynn Halbfinger. Deepest sympathy to the entire family.
