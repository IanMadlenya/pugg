
        CHERNUCHIN-Paul, Ph.D. Of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. and New York, N.Y. died Saturday, July 27, 2002 at Palm Beach Gardens Medical Center. He was 80 years old. Paul leaves behind his wife Elayne Link, his children, Michael and Cindy and their spouses Janis Diamond and Dan Barsky, his grandchildren, Jennifer and Jordan Barsky, and his sisters Beatrice Schuman and Toby Scholl. Dr. Paul Chernuchin received his B.A. from the University of Michigan, his M.A. from George Washington University, and his Ph.D. from the University of London. Paul headed his own company, Chernuchin Associates, Inc., and was an adjunct associate professor at New York University. He was known for his development of the Chernuchin Pictographs, a psychological projective technique, his love for his family, his zest for life and his sense of humor. We will all miss him. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made in Paul's memory to the Juvenile Diabetes Research Foundation, 381 Park Avenue South, Suite 507, New York, NY 10016, 212-689-2860.
