
        SCOLEDES-Nancy Neidhardt. Beloved wife of Jim Scoledes and loving mother of Amanda Scoledes, on May 25, 2001. Born in Philadelphia, PA. She was a graduate of Temple University and the City College of New York. Nancy enjoyed a career that spanned 31 years with United Airlines. Her most recent position was as Manager of Marketing, Latin America and Miami. Nancy, Jim and their daughter Amanda relocated to Miami in 1998 after having resided in Franklin Lakes, NJ for 17 years. Nancy spear-headed United Airlines extensive community involvement in the New York and South Florida communities. Nancy will be remembered for her extraordinary courage and love of life, which enriched the lives of her family, colleagues and many friends. Her indomitable spirit and integrity will be missed by all. She was predeceased by her parents Earl and Connie Neidhardt and is survived by her sister Susan (Neidhardt) and brother-in-law Steve D'Orio as well as her brother David Neidhardt and his wife Debbie, along with nieces and nephews. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Oncology Dept. at Mt. Sinai Cancer Center in Miami Beach, FL.
