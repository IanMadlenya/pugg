
        HOROWITZ-Sidney. Of Astoria, Queens, on August 2, in his 80th year. Husband of 54 years to Myrtle (deceased). Father of Daniel (deceased), Gina and Mollie. Grandfather of Cori, Joseph, Gabriel and Aaron, and great-grandfather of Jacob. Sidney was a successful businessman-owner of Pawley Construction Corp. and a lifelong member, supporter and officer of the Astoria Center of Israel. We will all miss his wit and humor. Services will be conducted at 10 AM in the Astoria Center of Israel, at 27-35 Crescent St.
