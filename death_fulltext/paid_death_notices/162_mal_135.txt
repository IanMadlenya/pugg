
        POLLACK-Frank. Just shy of 100. Passed away in Florida leaving a devoted son Herbert. Beloved father to the late Alex and Albert. Loving husband to the late Lillian and Dora. Cherished grandfather of nine and great-grandfather to nine who will always remember him. He was the Founder of The Guaranteed Printing Company in New York City in 1931. Graveside services Mount Ararat Cemetery at 11 A.M. today.
