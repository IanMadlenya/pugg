
        WOLFF-Anne. 87, died September 30, 1999, in Scottsdale, AZ. She and her late husband, Joel J. Wolff, were antique dealers in NY. She moved to Arizona two years ago. She is survived by her daughter Arlene (Beryl) Dulsky, step-son Michael (Sara) Wolff, sister Sylvia Jonas, six grandchildren, and eleven great-grandchildren. Crypt side services will be Monday, October 4, at 1PM, at New Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, NJ. Contributions in her memory to a hospice or other charity of your choice.
