
        BUEHLER-Seymour J. On September 11, 1997. Devoted and beloved husband of Joan. Adoring and proud father of Robert and Susan Buehler. Cherished brother of Joan and Howard Eisenstein. Dear uncle of Susan and Jon Kerz, and Beth and Jim Eisenstein. Service Sunday, 11:30 AM. Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Sy fought a heroic battle. A special man of utmost integrity, he will be missed by his family and many friends. Contributions in his memory may be made to Cancer Research and Treatment Fund, 1440 York Ave, P4, NY NY 10021.
