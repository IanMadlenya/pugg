
        PFLUGFELDER-Frederick J. Of Bay Shore, NY. Peacefully on February 3, 2001. Mr. Pflugfelder proudly served in the U.S. Merchant Marines during WW II. He was a member of the New York Stock Exchange since 1945. Devoted husband of Margaret. Much loved father of Noel Oakes and brother of Alice Punger and the late Helen, Charles and William. Dear grandfather of Chris and Karen Oakes. Life celebration and visitation at the White-Voltz Funeral Home, 364 East Main Street, Bay Shore, NY, Monday, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 11 AM, at St. Patrick R.C. Church, Bay Shore, NY. Interment St. Patrick's Cemetery, Bay Shore, NY. Memorial contribution to the St. Patrick's Church Hospitality Center, 9 North Clinton Avenue, Bay Shore, NY 11706 would be appreciated.
