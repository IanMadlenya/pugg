
        CLEMENT--Gregory, III, Architect, died at home in Weston, CT, on Wednesday, April 11th, 2007, of melanoma, at 56. Managing Principal of Kohn Pedersen Fox and Fellow of the AIA. Graduate of St. George's School in Newport, RI, and The University of Pennsylvania. Oversaw numerous international and award-winning projects. Respected for his integrity and character. Survived by wife Elizabeth, daughter Lili, sister Tina, and nephews Anawa, Shane and Devin. Donations may be made to the Gregory and Elizabeth Clement Melanoma Research Fund, Abramson Cancer Center of The University of Pennsylvania, 3535 Market St., Suite 750, Philadelphia, PA 19104.
