
        BLUME-Eli. Retired Chairman of Language Department, Forest Hills High School of Hampton Bays, died on June 15, 2000 at the age of 87. He leaves his dear wife Gloria. He is survived by his daughter Alison Kaufman. He leaves his grandson Bruce, his nephews and neices Lawrence and Janet Mayer, Shelley and Tonie Blackler, his sister-in-law Corinne Melkin and his nephew Stephen Melkin. A graveside service will be held at 1PM, Sunday June 18, 2000 at Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus, New Jersey.
