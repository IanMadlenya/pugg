
        GOLDBERG-Richard. The Board of Directors and the entire congregational family of Beth El J.C. of Flatbush mourn the tragic loss of our beloved Board member and benefactor, Richard Goldberg. With deep sorrow, we express our condolences to his wife Joan, a longtime Trustee of our Board, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jersusalem. Robert Behm, President George Green, Chairman, Board of Directors Pinchas Pearl, Rabbi
