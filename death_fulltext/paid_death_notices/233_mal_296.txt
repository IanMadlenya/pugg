
        STUART--Lyle. As members of the Board of Trustees and staff of The Albert Ellis Institute, we wish to extend our deepest sympathy to the family of Lyle Stuart on the occasion of his untimely passing. Lyle lived a long and full life, championing truth, justice and freedom of expression at every turn. He will be remembered by his many friends and admirers as a kind and caring man. In recent months, Lyle had given generously of his time to The Albert Ellis Institute, serving tirelessly as president of our board. We are greatful for his extraodinary leadership, highly principaled insight and strong support. We will miss him greatly. The Board of Trustees and staff Albert Ellis Institute
