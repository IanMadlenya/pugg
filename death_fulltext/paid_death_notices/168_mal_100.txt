
        KLEINSINGER-Harold. Professor Emeritus at St. John's University who taught chemistry and physics from 1932 to 1973, passed away in NYC, on January 6, at the age of 93. He is survied by his wife Frieda; the two recently celebrated their 70th wedding anniversary. Other survivors include his son Jack; a brother, Bert; and sister-inlaw Mildred. Funeral services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam, on Monday afternoon.
