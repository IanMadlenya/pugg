
        GORDON-Geraldine P. Of Palm Beach Gardens, FL; August 21 after a long illness. Born October 13, 1928, in Huntington, NY, to the late William Poli and Marie Ferrara Poli. Devoted daughter, the beloved wife of the late Sol Gordon, and adoring mother and grandmother, a businesswoman, and a smart, fun and stylish lady. Survived by her devoted children, Hazel and William Dunnigan of Westchester County, NY; cherished grandchildren, Justin and Jacob; daughter in-law Allison Amiel; and son in-law Jack Lorenz. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Lung Association.
