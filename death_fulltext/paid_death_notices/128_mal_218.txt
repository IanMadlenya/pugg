
        SHAPIRO-Edward. On September 16th, 1997. Born in New York City on April 18th, 1911. Edward was President of Diane Button Co., Manhattan, which he founded over 65 years ago. Edward resided in Bronxville, New York and Boca Raton, FL. He is survived by his loving wife of 59 years, Irene and three children: Lester, Barry and Lori. Also surviving are daughtersin-law Samantha and Roz, son-inlaw Harold Gross, 9 grandchildren and three great grandchildren. Memorial service will be held on September 17th, 10 AM, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon.
        SHAPIRO-Edward. Temple Israel of New Rochelle records with sorrow the death of its cherished member and extends its heartfelt sympathy to the family. Amiel Wohl, Rabbi
