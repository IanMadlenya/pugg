
        GINGOLD-Esther. Died May 20, peacefully at home, a day after joyfully celebrating her 90th birthday at a party with family and friends. Devoted wife of the late George, adoring mother of Julian, Beverly Sanders, Eric Wally and Herb, mother-in-law of Dawn, Irene and the late Ronald Sanders, grandmother par excellence of Annelise, Thomas, Rebecca, Laurence and Elizabeth, and greatgrandmother of April, Karl, Sofia, Erik. We sorely miss her keen intellect, passion for music and history, her strength of character. Service Wednesday 11:30am, Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 N. Station Plaza, Great Neck NY
        GINGOLD-Esther. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Rabbi Mordecai Waxman Steven Markowitz, President
