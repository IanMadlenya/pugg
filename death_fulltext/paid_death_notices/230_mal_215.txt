
        IMPERATO--Louis G., 95. Formerly of Tenafly, NJ, died March 13, 2006 in Palmetto, FL. Born in Brooklyn, NY, Lou was a member of the New York State Bar for over 70 years and practiced law until shortly before his death. He is survived by loving son George, grandchildren Robert, Richard, Jack, David and Deborah, eight greatgrandchildren, three greatgreat-grandchildren, many nieces, nephews and friends. Visitation to be held Sunday, March 19 from 2-4 and 7-9PM at Barrett Funeral Home, 148 Dean Dr., Tenafly, NJ www.barrettfuneralhome.net A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Monday, March 20, 10AM, Mt. Carmel Church, 10 County Rd. Tenafly, NJ. In lieu of flowers please donate to Tidewell Hospice, 4151-37th St. Palmetto FL 34221; or Palma Sola Botanical Park, 9800 17th Ave. N., Bradenton, FL 34209.
