
        MAZZEO-Joseph A. July 6, 1998. Beloved husband of Lucy (Welch). Also survived by his sisters, Mary Rinaldi, Mae Scolaro and Lauretta Korothy, and uncle of many nieces and nephews. Avalon Foundation Professor Emeritus in the Humanities of the Department of English & Comparative Literature at Columbia University. A Memorial service to be held at Westend Collegiate Church, 368 West End Avenue, New York City, Friday, July 17th, at 11 AM.
