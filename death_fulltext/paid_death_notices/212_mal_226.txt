
        ROSENSTOCK--Robert. International lawyer, legal advisor to the U.S. Mission to the United Nations, member of the International Law Commission, died of natural causes on September 20. Mr. Rosenstock is survived by his wife of 34 years, Gerda Michorl Rosenstock, daughter Elizabeth Rosenstock-Siller, son-in-law Michael Siller, grandson Phillip Siller and sons Thomas and Nicholas. The memorial will be private. The family asks that in lieu of flowers donations be sent to the Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 710 West 168 Street, NYC 10032.
