
        MARCELLINO-Fred. July 12, 2001. Husband of Jean. Father of Nico and his wife Elizabeth. Brother of Marie Marcellino Allen. He was a noted illustrator and author of children's books after an illustrious career as both a book jacket and record album cover designer. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave, at 81 St, Monday, July 16, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Service on Tuesday, July 17, 9:30 AM, at Frank E. Campbell. Contributions in his memory may be made to the NYU Medical Center, Deptartment of Oncology, or the Cabrini Hospice. In tribute to Fred's love of gardening, flowers are welcomed and appreciated.
