
        HERZOG--Pnina. With a profound sense of loss and sadness Emunah of America mourns the passing of Pnina Herzog, past President of World Emunah. Pnina was an eloquent advocate for Israel, for human rights, and for civility. Our hearts go out to her children Shira, Eliezra, and Isaac. Heddy Klein Emunah National President Marcia Genuth Chairman of the Board Carol Sufian Executive Director
