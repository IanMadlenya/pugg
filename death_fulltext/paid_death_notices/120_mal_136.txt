
        CURTIS - Lt. Col. Cragin Croyle, OBE, of Tucker's Town, Bermuda, died suddenly on Jan. 3, 1997. He was 65. He is survived by his wife, Susan, and five children, Diana Price of Bellmore, NY; Harry Curtis of Summit, NJ; Laura Curtis of Boulder, CO; Cragin Curtis and Devon Curtis of Bermuda. Contributions may be made to: St. John's Ambulance Brigade, PO Box DV, 575 Devonshire, DV BX, Bermuda.
