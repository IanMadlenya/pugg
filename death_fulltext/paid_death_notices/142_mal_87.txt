
        STINNETT-Caskie. Travel editor, writer, humorist, nature and animal lover. On November 5, 1998 at home, of cancer. Survived by wife Joan, son Jim, daughters Karen (Aschi) and Savannah, sisters Happy and Thelma and grandsons Jonathan, David and Nathaniel. Private ceremony will take place on his beloved island in Maine. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his name to Fund For Animals, 200 West 57th Street, Suite 705, N.Y., N.Y. 10019.
