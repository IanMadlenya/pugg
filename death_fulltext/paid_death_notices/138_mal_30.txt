
        GOODYEAR-Stephen. Of Charlottesville, VA, died peacefully on July 2, 1998. Beloved father of Zachary, Jessica, Talbot and Abigail. Born and raised in Buffalo, he graduated from Harvard in 1938 and received his MD from the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia in 1943. After service as a medical officer in World War II and a three-year residency at Paine Whitney Clinic, he practiced psychiatry in New York from 19501974. Retired from his New York practice in 1974, he moved to Santa Fe, NM for eleven years, and in 1984 to Charlottesville. In addition to his children, he is survived by his brother George of Buffalo and six grandchildren. No service.
