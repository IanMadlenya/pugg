
        MICHALAK-Karl, lost consciousness on Monday, June 30 due to cardiac arrythmia. Writer, film critic, artist, cartoonist, costumer, musician, song writer, linguist, activist. Karl fought long & valiantly against all odds. He is survived by Michael his partner of ten years, his parents Katherine and William, his sisters Judith and Kristine, niece Carol, nephews Nick, David and Daniel and many, many people he touched and who loved him. A memorial meeting is being planned for late July. For more information: 212-227-6561.
