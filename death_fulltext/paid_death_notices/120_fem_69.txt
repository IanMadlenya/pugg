
        BLAUNER - Roxane m. January 9, 1997. Beloved wife of the late Altoa, loving mother of John Blauner and Jackie Bruni, devoted sister of the late Robert Mack, dear grandmother of Gregory, Christopher, and Alexandra, great-grandmother of Colin and Griffin. An extraordinary woman, whose love was as vast as eternity. Services Monday, January 13, 12 noon, at Temple Emany-El, Fifth Avenue of 65th Street.
        BLAUNER, Roxane M. Congregation Emanu-El of the city of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Roxane. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing.  David E. Marrus, President  Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
