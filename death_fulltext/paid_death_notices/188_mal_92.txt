
        ADLER-George B. Of Harrison, NY and Boca Raton, FL died on September 9, 2002 at 76. Beloved husband of Marion, cherished father of Gary, Andre' and Roger and his wife Vicki, proud grandfather of Jason and Spencer. A native of Switzerland, who graduated from the top Swiss business school with an MBA, George came to NY in 1952. He was first an analyst with Loeb Rhodes and later with Smith Barney, rising to managing director and member of the steering committee. He was voted a star analyst for an unprecedented sixteen consecutive years. Funeral services will be held at 10 a.m. on Thursday, September 12, at the Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, NY. 914381-1809. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to the Cardiovascular Institute of Mount Sinai Hospital or UJA.
