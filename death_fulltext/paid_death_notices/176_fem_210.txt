
        HAUSER-Carmen Angleton. Died August 9, in Rome, Italy. Born in Boise, Idaho, 1921, attended Bryn Mawr College. In 1939 she edited the 5th edition of the poetry magazine, ''Furioso'', along with her brother, James J. Angleton and E. Reed Whittemore, Married Ernest Hauser 1947. Lived in Rome 1947-2001, traveling extensively with her husband in eastern Europe, the middle east, and Mediterranean countries. She was fluent in Greek and Latin and several modern languages and greatly aided other writers in their understanding of Italian and other cultures. She leaves four nephews, two nieces and several grand nieces and nephews. To be buried in Boise, Idaho. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be sent to the American Academy, Rome, Italy.
