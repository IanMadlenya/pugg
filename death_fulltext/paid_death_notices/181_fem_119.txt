
        GABRINER-Lillian (nee Rosenbaum). At age 98. Of Palm Beach, FL. on February 11, 2002. She was the beloved wife of Arthur, for 72 years. Devoted mother of Linda Friedman and son-in-law Harold. Loving grandmother of Lianne, Jeffrey and Peter Friedman, and Laura Blum. Adoring great grandmother of Annabelle Friedman and loving aunt. She was born in New York City on September 6, 1903 and was among the first women to graduate Fordham Law School, earning her degree in 1925. She loved literature, travel and, most of all, her family. Funeral service Wednesday, February 13, 11:30AM at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, New York.
