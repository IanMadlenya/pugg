
        RICHKIN-Gerald (Jerry). It is with great sadness that we announce the passing of Gerald Richkin, 150 West 96th Street, New York, NY 10025. The funeral will be held on Wednesday Nov 11, 1998 at 12 noon at The Plaza Memorial Funeral Home, 91st Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Jerry was an actor and a teacher and he leaves behind his loving wife, Carmen, his three daughters, Jean, Valerie, and Beth, and his four grandchildren, Oren, Dana, Tali, and Jonah. We will miss him very much.
