
        McCLAVE-Wilkes II. Age 83, of Harding Township, NJ, on Tuesday, July 16, 2002. Father of Margaret Snowden of Voorheesville, NY, Wilkes McClave III of Bedford, NY, Pam Jackson of Washington, CT, and Madeline McClave of New Hartford, CT. He is also survived by seven grandchildren and his sister Nancy Lyman of Hilton Head, SC. A Memorial Service will be held at St. Peter's Church in Morristown, NJ, on Wednesday, July 31, at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers the family asks that contributions be made to Neighborhood House, 12 Flagler Street, Morristown, NJ 07960 or the Harding Land Trust, PO Box 576, New Vernon, NJ 07976.
