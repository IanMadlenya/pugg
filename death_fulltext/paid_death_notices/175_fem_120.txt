
        FELTMAN-Susan. On August 15, 2001. Loving daughter of the late Dr. James A. Feltman & Priscilla Savage Feltman. Ms. Feltman was an accomplished stage actress who also pursued a career as a nurse and healthcare consultant. She was respected and admired by her friends and colleagues for her warmth, loyalty and humor. Ms. Feltman lived and died with dignity and courage. She is survived by her brothers William Ross and Richard Andrews, her niece Julie and her nephew Wil Hopkins. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street today 5-9PM with funeral service on Saturday, August 18 at 9:30AM followed by burial at Flushing Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to ''The Actors Fund of America'', 729 7th Avenue, New York, New York 10019.
