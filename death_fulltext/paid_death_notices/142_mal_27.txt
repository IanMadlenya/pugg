
        FUS-Msgr. Edward A. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor emeritus of Holy Cross Church, 61-21 56th Rd., Maspeth, on Nov, 1. Reposing at Megiel Funeral Home, 64-18 Flushing Ave., Maspeth, Tuesday, Nov. 3, from 2-5 and 7-9 P.M., and Wednesday, Nov. 4, from 2-5 P.M. Mass of Transferral at Holy Cross Church at 7:30 P.M. Funeral Mass, Thursday, Nov. 5, at 10 A.M. All priests' are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society.
