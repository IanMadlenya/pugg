
        AXELROD -- Harvey. Died January 16th, 2004, in Clearwater, FL. Survived by his devoted wife, Shirley Axelrod, brother Stanley, children Linda Kastner, Amy Klein and Steven Lewis, and grandchildren Zohar, Megan and Gaby. A veteran of WWII, he was decorated with a Purple Heart. An active and successful part of the fashion industry in NYC for over 40 years, his sense of style and sharp wit will live inside of us forever. He will be remembered with love.
