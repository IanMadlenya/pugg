
        LANDAU--Alan Martin, age 62 died December 25, 2004. the author of Airborne Rangers; he was a self-employed writer and photographer; his works appeared in various national magazines. He is survived by his wife Frieda Landau; a brother Peter Landau; an aunt Miriam Wagner. Burial will take place Sunday January 2, 2005 at Mt. Hebron Cemetery, Queens, NY, 11:30 AM.
