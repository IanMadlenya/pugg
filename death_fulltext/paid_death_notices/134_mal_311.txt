
        HERBST-Daniel L. Age 69, of Larchmont, NY, died March 23, 1998, at his home of a heart attack. Born in Brooklyn, and a graduate of Brooklyn College, Mr. Herbst was a sales and marketing executive in the hospital supply business most of his career. He was a U.S. Army captain during the Korean War and was awarded a silver star, two bronze stars and two purple hearts. He was an avid swimmer and sailor, a certified scuba instructor, member of the University Club of Larchmont, and a volunteer for the Elissa Tall Ships Program in Galveston, TX.
