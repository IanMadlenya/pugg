
        BENJAMIN-Robert Alan. Beloved husband of Jean, of Raleigh, NC, formerly of Tuxedo Park, NY, passed away on Monday, August 12, after a long illness. He is survived by his wife Jean; daughters Penny Pesaturo and husband Michael, and Robin Durr and husband Bill; granddaughter Morgan Holly; brother Donald Benjamin and wife Anne; his nephew Scott and wife Cindy; and niece Cindy Benjamin. There will be a private service at the home on August 14 at 2 PM; guests will be received at the home at 4 PM. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation to the Duke University Medical Center, PO Box 90581, Durham, NC 27708, in memory of Robert Benjamin for Parkinson's Research.
