
        SMITH--Vernon Siems. Of New Canaan, CT died on October 26, 2003 at age 89. Predeceased by her husband, Crosby T. Smith in 1970. She is survived by her sister Dorothy Siems dePeyster of Palm Beach, and five sons, Crosby of New Canaan, Dudley of Grantham, NH, Christopher of New Canaan, Jeremy of Wellesley, MA and Geoffrey of San Francisco as well as eight grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. A graduate of the Nightingale-Bamford School in NYC, she was a member of the Colonial Dames of America and had been active in community affairs in New Canaan in her younger years. Funeral services will be private.
