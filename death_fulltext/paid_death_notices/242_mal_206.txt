
        HOUK--Bruce Victor, writer and traveler, 68, died January 11, 2007, in Queens, NY, due to a heart attack. Born March 16, 1938, to Rudy J. Houk and Sally Homer. Attended Bard College, Columbia Graduate School and graduated Hunter in 2000. He lived as he wanted; the last ten years he was an office assistant at Fountain House. He is survived by a former wife Lilly and son Jesse, brother John Hikory of Vermont, and nieces and nephews. Predeceased by his parents and his brother Rudy Houk, of New Hampshire.
