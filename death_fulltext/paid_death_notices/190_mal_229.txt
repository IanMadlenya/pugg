
        LUTZER-Martin. Of New York, NY, aged 85, on November 16, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Millicent Joy, beloved uncle of Dena and Harvey Solomon, and greatuncle of Andrew and Julie. He served his country as a dentist in the U.S. Army, and thereafter was engaged in the private practice of dentistry in Manhattan until retirement. A private memorial service will be held.
