
        GLASCOTT--John A. (Jack) Jr. on December 24, 2006 at the age of 80 of Briarcliff Manor, NY. Retired Senior Vice President National Sales Manager of MetroMail, a Metromedia Company. A veteran of World War II, he also served combat duty as a First Lieutenant in the United States Marine Corps First Marine Division during the Korean War. He was awarded the Silver Star and Purple Heart. He is survived by his wife of 56 years, Joan, three sons, John, Jeff and Jim, 10 grandchildren and two brothers. Reposing Waterbury & Kelly Funeral Home, 1300 Pleasantville Road, Briarcliff Manor on Wednesday, 2-4pm and 7-9pm. Funeral Mass, St. Theresa Church, Briarcliff Manor on Thursday at 10am. Interment Sleepy Hollow Cemetery. Contributions to Phelps Memorial Hospital, Sleepy Hollow or St. Theresa Church, Briarcliff Manor.
