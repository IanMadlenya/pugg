
        MINKIN--David. 99, of New York, and Palm Beach, FL. Passed away September 19, 2003. Adored uncle of Patricia and Howard Lester. Greatuncle of Peter and Prescott Lester and Pamela Ford. Great-great-uncle of Owen Ford and Scott Lester. He was a true renaissance man, a leader of the community and a second father to us all. He will be sorely missed. Services Wednesday, September 24, 10 AM, at Riverside Nassau North, 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, LI; 516487-9600. Contributions may be made to the Mayo Foundation, attn: Paul Harkess, Rochester, MN 55905.
        MINKIN--David. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of David Minkin, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. Dedicated to helping those in need, Mr. Minkin gave his full support to numerous Jewish and humanitarian causes. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
        MINKIN--David. JBI (formerly the Jewish Braille Institute) is deeply saddened by the passing of David Minkin, a devoted philanthropist in the Jewish community. We are particularly grateful for his caring generosity in helping enrich the cultural lives of the Jewish blind and visually impaired whom we serve. May his memory serve as a blessing. Barbara B. Friedman, Pres. Dr. Jane Evans, Honorary President Dr. Ellen Isler, Executive Vice President
        MINKIN--David. We mourn a leader of an empire. We have lost a great man and leader. He was generous to all mankind. He was a friend and business associate for over 45 years. We are forever indebted to him and his family. Ronald Richbell
