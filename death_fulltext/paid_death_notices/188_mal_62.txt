
        SMOLKA-Norman. 84 years, of Roslyn Hts, on September 5, 2002. Beloved husband of the late Rose. Devoted father of Karen Albucker, Judy Fink and Scott Smolka. Devoted father-in-law of Dr. Steven Fink. Loving grandfather of Melissa, Joshua, Jeffrey, Samantha and Zoe. Brother of the late Sidney Smolka and Mildred Furman. He was a Major in the US Army during W.W. II and a POW. He was President of Glauber Inc, a major plumbing supply house in NYC. Funeral services will be held on Monday, September 9, at 9:45 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels,'' 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions to Project Time-Out, WJCS, 845 North Broadway, White Plains, NY 10603; or the Sass Foundation for Medical Research, 1025 Northern Blvd, Roslyn, NY 11576.
