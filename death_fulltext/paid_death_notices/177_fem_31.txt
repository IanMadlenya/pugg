
        HENDERSON-Kathryn Silverthorne. Of NYC died peacefully on September 28, 2001 at the age of 84. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Edward Henderson, loving mother of Edward and Susan, sister of Frederick, grandmother of Samantha, Victoria, and Monica, and great-grandmother of Eleanor and Charles. Her life was full, and she left her ''footprints in the sands of time''. She will be missed.
