
        BOTTE-Frank. Of New York City and Easthampton. Died on November 8 at New York Hospital at the age of 56. The beloved son of Marie Amerosa, he is also survived by his devoted companion of many years, Stacy Charles. ''You are a child of the universe, no less than the trees and the stars...be at peace with God.'' Reposing at Prospero and Son Funeral Home, 2444 86th Street, Brooklyn, Tuesday and Wednesday. Visiting hours are 2-5 and 7-9:30 P.M. Funeral service will be held on Thursday at 10 A.M. at the Unitarian Church of All Souls, 1157 Lexington Avenue, between 79th and 80th Streets. Donations to God's Love We Deliver.
