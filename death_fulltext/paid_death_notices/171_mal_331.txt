
        DREW-Leon. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York are deeply saddened by the passing of Leon Drew, a longtime supporter of our mission. As charity chairman at the Woodmere Club, Leon worked tirelessly on behalf of people in need on Long Island and throughout the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife, Sheila, and to his children and grandchildren. May his memory be a blessing. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair Arthur M. Katz, LI Cabinet Chair John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO Simon Kaminetsky, LI Regional Director
