
        JONES--Alonzo. Died on January 12, 2005. He was an employee of the The New York Times for over 30 years. He is survived by his wife Doloris Jones, and two children Lois and Renee Jones, nine grandchildren & three great-grandchildren. He was rated number one truck driver for the New York Times and safest driver for two consecutive years prior to his retirement. He will be greatly missed.
        JONES--Alonzo. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Alonzo Jones, associated with The Times from October 1977, until retiring in January 2004.
