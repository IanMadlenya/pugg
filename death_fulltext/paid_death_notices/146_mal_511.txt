
        LIEBGOLD-Sidney, born 1901, in New York City, lately of Silver Springs, Md. A friend to all, beloved by his family, including the late Sara, Barbara and Harriet; cherished by Bruce, Susan, Randy and Dennis. A dedicated servant of the City of New York as an administrator at City College, he guided and encouraged generations of young people. Lifelong champion of civil and equal rights, with an unshakeable belief in his fellow human beings. Graveside service at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, L.I., Tuesday, 2:30 PM.
