
        ARMELLINO -- Vincent V. ''Jim''. Husband for 60 years of Anne Martone Armellino of Greenwich, CT died June 15 in Greenwich. Father of Tricia Solari and Jan Duffy of Greenwich, CT. He is also survived by his two grandsons, Joseph Solari III and Ray Duffy, Jr. Before moving to Greenwich the Armellino's were longtime residents of Little Neck, LI, where Mr. Armellino resided during his long career in NYC government. Family will receive friends Thursday, June 17 at the Leo P. Gallagher & Son Funeral Home, 31 Arch St, Greenwich, CT from 2 to 4 and 7 to 9 PM. A Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Friday, June 18, 11 AM at St. Michael Church, 469 North Street, Greenwich, CT. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Alzheimer Disease and Related Disorders Assn., Fairfield Co. Chapter, 607 Main Avenue, Norwalk, CT 06851.
