
        KAHN-Arthur H. Of New York City & Philadelphia, died Jan. 30, 2001. Survived by his wife Donna S., their sons Robert B., Walter A., Andrew W. & four grandchildren. Mr. Kahn was a former Chairman of the law firm Schnader Harrison Segal & Lewis. Memorial Services will be held Thursday, February 1 at 11 A.M., at Stephen Wise Free Synagogue, New York.
