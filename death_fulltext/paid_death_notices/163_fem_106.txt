
        SCOTCH-Frances (Gauchman). 82, of Long Meadow, MA; formerly of Brookline, died Saturday. Her husband Theodore died in May. She was the mother of Dr. David S. Scotch of Westport, CT, and Elaine Fialky of Long Meadow, MA; mother-in-law of Barbara Scotch and Gary Fialky; sister of Max Gauchman of Brookline and Nechama Grossman of Israel; grandmother of Jeffrey and Joshua Fialky, Jonathon and Peter Scotch; great-grandmother of Justin and Hannah Scotch. Funeral and burial are Monday. Contributions can be made to Jewish Geriatric Services, 770 Converse St, Long Meadow, MA 01106, or American Cancer Society, 53 Capital Ave, West Springfield, MA 01089.
