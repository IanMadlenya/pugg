
        SHIRRELL--Robert Donald, of Millbrook died of lung cancer on March 19, at age 86 with his family by his side. A WWII veteran, he graduated from the University of Michigan in 1948. Mr. Shirrell was with General Electric for 35 years. He is survived by daughters Ann (Walter) Rand of Pawling and Sarah (Earl) Carlin of Millbrook, and six grandchildren. His wife, Barbara, died in 2001.
