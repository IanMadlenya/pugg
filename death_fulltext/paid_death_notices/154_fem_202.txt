Devoted mother of Debra Samuelson of NYC and Cynthia Samuelson and her husband, Jonathan Samen of Lexington, MA. Dear grandmother of Joel, Hayley and Maggie. Services at the Rye Community Synagogue, 200 Forest Ave., Rye, NY on Monday, November 29, at 12 Noon. Memorial observance at her late residence until Friday, sundown. Remembrances to Women's American O.R.T., 315 Park Avenue South, NYC 10010.
