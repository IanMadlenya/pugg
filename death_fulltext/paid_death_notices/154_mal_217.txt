
        GALLAGHER-Thomas F.., 87. A retired advertising executive with Joseph E. Seagram and Sons died on November 16, 1999 at his home in New Canaan, CT. He leaves his beloved wife of 57 years, Mary Ann O'Shea and children, Paul, Joan, and Peter; and six grandchildren. A Mass will be held at 11AM, Saturday, November 20, 1999 at St. Thomas More R.C. Church, 374 Middlesex Rd., Darien, CT. Visiting hours will be 4-8pm, Friday November 19th at the Hoyt Funeral Home, 199 Main St., New Canaan, CT. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to The Thomas F. Gallagher, Sr. Scholarship Fund, University of Notre Dame, 1100 Grace Hall, Notre Dame, IN 46556 Attn: Carol Hennion.
