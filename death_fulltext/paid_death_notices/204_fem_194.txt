
        TURNER--Margaret Nimmo Rogers. 93, formerly of Darien, CT, and Sanibel, FL, died Wednesday, January 14, 2004, in Columbus, NC. Mrs. Turner was the widow of Robert L. Turner Sr. She is survived by a daughter, Louisa Turner of New York; a son and daughter-in-law, Robert L. Turner Jr. and Otile E. McManus of Milton, MA, and two grandchildren, Julia and Maggie. Remembrances may be made to Boy's Home, Covington, VA 24426.
