
        KURASCH-Martin. 84. On January 23, 1998 in Stamford, CT. Loving husband of the late Lillian. Brilliant U.S. government career, serving as lawyer for War Labor Board, NLRB, General Counsel for Wage Stabilization Board and Rural Electrification Union. In 1951, he was First General Counsel and helped create Development Corporation for Israel until retirement in 1984. But most importantly, he had the gentlest soul, the sharpest mind, warmest laugh. Survived by daughters Toni Elizabeth, Polly (Terry) Blank; grandchildren Marni, Willa; sister Gladys (Stanley) Segal.
