
        ADLER--Mordant (Mort) D.M.D., was 87 when he passed away at home on March 7, 2007. His beloved wife of 59 years, Sunny, his cherished daughter Kiva, and the incredible nurses of the Hospice of Palm Beach County were constantly by his side. He died in a peaceful way with courage, dignity and total comfort. Mort was predeceased by his beloved son Andrew. He leaves behind his sister Reina Adler, sister-in-law, Ethel Manheimer, son-in-law, Bil Alverson and many other treasured relatives, friends, and colleagues. Dr. Adler practiced dentistry in New York City for 45 years. A memorial will be held in the near future. Donations may be made to: Hospice of Palm Beach County, 5300 East Ave., West Palm Beach, FL 33407 or an orgaization of your choice.
