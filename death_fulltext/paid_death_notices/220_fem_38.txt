
        FARLEY--Irene Daly, 82, on Tuesday, May 3, 2005 at her home in Princeton, NJ. Beloved wife of the late Edward R. Farley, Jr., and mother of the late Thomas Joyce Farley. She is survived by two daughters, Nancy Farley Jarrell and Jane Farley von Oehsen, her son-in-law Thomas von Oehsen, a son Edward Raymond Farley III and daughter - in - law Carolyn Kurtz Farley. She is also survived by five grandchildren, Lacey Jane Jarrell, William Robert Jarrell IV and Thomas Weld von Oehsen, Jr., William McNamara von Oehsen and Alexander Campbell von Oehsen. Friends may call Sunday, May 8th, 2005, 6-8 PM, Mather-Hodge Funeral Home, 40 Vandeventer Ave, Princeton. Mass of Christian Burial will be celebrated Monday, May 9, 2005, 10 AM at St. Thomas Aquinas Institute in Princeton. Following the Mass, family and friends are invited to share their memories and love at the Present Day Club of Princeton. In lieu of flowers, please send donations in Irene's memory to the American Parkinson's Disease Foundation.
