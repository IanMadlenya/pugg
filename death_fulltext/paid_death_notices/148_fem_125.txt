
        GANNETT-Sarah Derby, 78. Died at home in Brattleboro, Vermont on Monday after a long illness. A granddaughter of President Theodore Roosevelt born in Oyster Bay. Survived by her husband, Robert T. Gannett; her daughter, Alden G. Taylor of Mequon, Wisconsin and William of New York City and nine grandchildren. A private memorial service will be held at the convenience of the family.
