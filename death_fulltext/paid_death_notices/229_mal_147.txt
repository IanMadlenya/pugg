
        MOFFETT--John ''Jack'' was born December 5, 1929 in New York City and passed away on January 23, 2006. Preceded in death by his wife, Elisa Valverde Moffett. He was a Seton Hall graduate and Korean War vet. Services will be February 17, 2006, at 10 AM at Calvary Hill Funeral Home in Dallas, TX.
