
        BASSETT--Genia Esther (nee Paltrowitz), 97. Beloved wife of the late Dr. David Bassett and the loving and adored mother of Irwin and Rita, Lawrence and Charlotte; and Ann and Fred Schwartz. She also leaves 10 grandchildren and 17 great grandchildren. A woman of high ideals and wisdom. Graveside services at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont, Long Island, Sunday, February 1, at 11 A.M.
