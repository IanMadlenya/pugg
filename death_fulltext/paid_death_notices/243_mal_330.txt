
        CAPORALE--Ralph Jr., March 12, 1917-April 24, 2007. WWII Veteran. Inspirational Educator and Civic Leader. Adjunct Professor, Hunter College; Principal, IS 51 Staten Island; Principal, PS 208 Manhattan; Assistant Principal Francis Scott Key JHS, Brooklyn; Math teacher Galvani JHS Manhattan; Advocate for increased funding for education; early organizer of teachers (HSTA ''picket''), leading to creation of UFT with Al Shanker and other pioneers; CSA; Exec. Board JHS Principals Association. Devoted, beloved, extraordinary husband to Stella, father to Lynn, Judy, Barbara, Jill; father-in-law to Jeff Lape; grandfather to Brooks, Michael, and Parker Lape and Rockella Caporale. Gentle, upbeat, warm, hard-working, generous, compassionate, determined, humanitarian. Loved by so many. Asked that his students would take with them ''an awareness... of the need for compassion towards others, and growth in the knowledge and skills needed for a full and rich life.'' Donations, to support education and other programs for children in NY and around the world, to the Ralph and Stella Caporale Fund of the NY Community Trust. Taken painfully from us due to medical misjudgments. Services Riverside Chapel 76th and Amsterdam Ave. Manhattan. 2pm Sunday April, 29.
