
        SCHACHTER--Oscar. Hamilton Fish Professor of International Law and Diplomacy at Columbia University, former Director of United Nations Institute of Training and Research, and Director General Legal Division United Nations. Beloved husband of Muriel Sackler, devoted father of Judith Schachter and Ellen Leventhal, loving grandfather of Jennifer McLoughlin, Matthew Modell, Philip Leventhal and Josh Leventhal. Caring brother of Marvin Schachter and Honey Zimmer, and father-in-law of Albrecht Funk and J. P. Leventhal. Widower of Mollie Miller Schachter. Service Monday, December 15, at 11:15 am, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th and Amsterdam Ave. His magnificent heart and mind shall live forever.
