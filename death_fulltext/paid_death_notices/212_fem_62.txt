
        KAPNICK--Sylvia (Syd). Died at age 84 on September 3, 2004. Beloved wife and dearest friend of Oscar Kapnick. Devoted and loving mother of Steven Kapnick, the late JoAnne Kapnick and the late Susan Kapnick Bart. Devoted and loving mother-in-law of Joan Kapnick and Jacob Bart. Adored and cherished grandmother of Joshua and Madelyn Bart. She was a strong and independent woman, deeply loved and respected by all those who knew her. Together with her husband, she contributed to many charitable activities including longtime support for the Sound Shore Medical Center, Albert Einstein College of Medicine, United Hebrew Geriatric Center, the Guidance Center and Temple Israel of New Rochelle. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 21 West Broad St., Mt. Vernon, NY, on Wednesday, September 8, at 10 AM. Shiva will be observed on Wednesday and Thursday at the Bart residence.
        KAPNICK -- Sylvia ''Syd''. Temple Israel of New Rochelle expresses condolences to the family of Sylvia ''Syd'' Kapnick. Patricia Lampl, President Michael Z. Cahana, Rabbi
