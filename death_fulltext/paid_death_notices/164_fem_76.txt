
        ROSENTHAL-Anne M., 93. Died September 1, 2000 at Waterside Retirement Estates in Sarasota, Florida. She was born November 11, 1906 in New York City and lived in Sarasota since 1972. A graduate of Hunter College, she worked as a teacher of children with impaired vision and later as an accountant at the Brooklyn Navy Yard. Her survivors include two nephews, Stephen and Eugene Rosenthal. She was predeceased by a brother, Joseph Rosenthal and a sister, Ruth Feiring.
