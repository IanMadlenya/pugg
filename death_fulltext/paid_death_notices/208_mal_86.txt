
        DOWLING--John L. 66, on May 4, 2004, died at his home in Boca Grande, Florida. Beloved husband of Hebe S. Dowling. Devoted father of Meaghan, John and Peter Dowling. Cherished father-inlaw of Michael Chorske and Rebecca Dowling. Loving grandfather of Jack Chorske. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Sunday, May 9, 7-9 PM. A funeral mass will be held at St. Patrick's Cathedral, Fifth Avenue at 51st Street on Monday, May 10 at 10:30 AM.
        DOWLING--John L. The Society of the Friendly Sons of St. Patrick in the City of New York, records with profound sorrow the death of John L. Dowling a Member of the Society, and extends sincere sympathy to his family. Robert J. Reilly, President Henry F. White, Jr. Historian
