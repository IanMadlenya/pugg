
        TEDESCO - Vittoria. 100 years and 6 months on November 4, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Flaviano. Loving and adored mother of Lucia and Anna Tedesco-Garone, and cherished mother-in-law of Vincent T. Garone. She was a loving aunt and greataunt to her family and her extended family: Robert, Jamie, Hanna, and Ryan Young. Her generosity and loving warmth touched the lives of many. She will be profoundly missed and forever remain in the hearts of her devoted family. Family will receive friends Saturday and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 P.M. at the Flinch & Bruns Funeral Home, Inc., 34 Hempstead Avenue, (corner Peninsula Blvd) Lynbrook. Funeral mass Monday 9:45 A.M. St Joseph's R.C. Church, Hewlett. Interment Holy Rood Cemetery, Westbury. Memorial contributions may be made to the Stecher and Horowitz Foundation, 250 W 57 St, Suite 1005, New York, New York 10107.
