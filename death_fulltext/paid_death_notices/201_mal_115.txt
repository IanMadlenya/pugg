
        HAHN--Prof. Dr. Fred. On Sunday, October 12, 2003. Educator, international lecturer, prominent member of the Czech Jewish Community, President of the Congregation of Jews from Austria, and the Joseph Popper Lodge. Beloved husband of the late Edith H. Devoted father of Susan and Jeanette. Caring father-in-law of Andy and Robert. Adored grandfather of Daniel, Elissa, David, Michael and Rachel. He will be greatly missed.
