
        DiLORENZO-Jeannette B. The United Federation of Teachers Jewish Heritage Committee/Jewish Labor Committee Educators Chapter, deeply mourns the passing of our friend, colleague, and founder. Jeannette was a very special person. She will always be remembered for her leadership and for her dedication to education, trade unionism, human rights, and the State of Israel. We send our sympathy to her sister Blanche. Joel H. Shiller, Chairperson
