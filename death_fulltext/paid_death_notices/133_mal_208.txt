
        LAUTERSTEIN-Lincoln W. On February 12, 1998, on his 81st birthday in Rockport, MA. Beloved husband of Ingeborg and father of Adrian. Also survived by his brother Henry and nephews and nieces. A Summa Cum Laude graduate of Princeton and graduate of Harvard Law School. Attorney for the Metropolitan Opera. A veteran and active as attorney for the American Veterans Association. A brilliant man who will be greatly missed. Services are private.
