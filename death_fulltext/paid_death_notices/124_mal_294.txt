
        GUTTENPLAN-Seymour, 73, of Weston, FL. Formerly of Valley Stream, LI. Passed away May 26, 1997. He was the Founder and President of Tele-Wire Corp. and the past President of Congregation Tree of Life, Valley Stream. A veteran of World War II serving in the Army, he is survived by his wife of 46 years, Sylvia; two children: Dr. Susan Manella (Ross), Dr. Michael Guttenplan (Mary); four loving grandchildren: David and Morgan Manella and Kevin and Katie Guttenplan; and brother Herbert Guttenplan. Service will be today at 9:15 AM, I. J. Morris Funeral Directors, Dix Hills, NY.
