
        TABAK-Abraham. On February 23, 1997, age 95. Beloved husband of Ethel Triebwasser, first wife Tesse Tabak (died in 1976). Survived by children, Frances Cooperman, Bella Feldman, Sylvia Hoffstein, and Harry Tabak, nine grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. He was a good father and a kind man.
