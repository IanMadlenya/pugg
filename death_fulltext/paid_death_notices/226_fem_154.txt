
        MEYERSON - Beatrice, 81. Rockleigh, NJ. Devoted wife of the late Frank, beloved mother of Barbara (Boe) and George, adored grandmother of Lesley and Jill, who were her greatest joy. She was a medical records technician at Jewish Memorial and Columbia Presbyterian hospitals, administrator for the Columbia University Chemistry Department, gardener, reader, cook, and active member of the Washington Township Jewish Y. Her family will miss her open hearted caring.
