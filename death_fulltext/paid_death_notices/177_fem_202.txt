
        CALLAGHAN - Kathryn Larkin. Died October 8, 2001. A native of New York City, she was 91. Survivors include her children, James Callaghan, Jacqueline Novellino, Patricia Hood, and Kathryn Dawson, her sister, Veronica Dwyer, 12 grandchildren, 9 great-grandchildren, and 18 nieces and nephews. Burial took place October 11.
