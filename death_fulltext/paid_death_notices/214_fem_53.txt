
        BIRNE--Taube. Wife of the late Dr. Ralph Birne, survived her daughters, Joan Gelber, and Carole H. Polsky, grandsons, David and David, and cousin Edith Mannis. Formerly of Glenwood Road, Brooklyn, Stonybrook, New York, and The Williams Residence, NYC. A founder of a Stonybrook Chapter of Hadassah. Contributions may be made to Hadassah.
