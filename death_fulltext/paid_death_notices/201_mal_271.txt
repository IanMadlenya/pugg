
        SCHWARTZ--Leo. Of Pearl River. Died October 9. He was born in New York to the late Minnie and Henry Schwartz. He was a New York City Fireman for over 20 years, then went to work for The New York Times. He worked at The Times for over 20 years until his retirement. He is survived by his wife Elaine, his daughter Roberta and her husband Jeff Dunn, son Dr. Martin Schwartz and wife Sally, and grandchildren Alisa and Lonnie Shupak, Sheryl Dunn and Eric, Alison and Jaclyn Schwartz, and a sister, Florence Urband.
