
        GUTTAG--Marjorie. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Marjorie Guttag, a former leader who together with her late husband Irwin, shared a historic commitment to our network of agencies and whose support enabled us to enrich millions of lives around the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to Marjorie's children: Addie; John, and his wife, Olga; her grandchildren: David, Andrea, Michael, and Mark, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
