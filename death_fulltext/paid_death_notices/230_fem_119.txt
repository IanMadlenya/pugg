
        MALVIN--Susan Yogman. 63, of Union, NJ, on March 11, after a long struggle with Crohn's disease. She was a devoted wife, sister, mother, daughter, teacher, dog lover and generous friend to all. Survived by her husband Dr. Philip Malvin of Union, her brother Dr. Michael Yogman of Cambridge, MA, her daughter Ann Michele Malvin and her cousin Shirley Strickler of DelRay Beach, FL. Services Monday, 2 pm, at Beth Israel Chapel, Woodbridge, NJ. Donations to Boston Children's Museum, 300 Congress St, Boston, MA 02210, attn: Trish Meyer.
