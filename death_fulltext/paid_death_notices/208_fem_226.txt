
        SYMES--Margaret Gill. On May 24, 2004, in her 96th year. Formerly of Bay Ridge, Brooklyn; Tarpon Springs, FL, and Manchester Village, VT. Wife of the late William (1994). Beloved mother of Mary Symes-Leith, Nancy Symes Hersom and Kathleen Symes Junsch. Also survived by seven grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Burial private in Arlington, VT, at a later date.
