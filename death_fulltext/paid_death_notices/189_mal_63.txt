
        ASTRACHAN-Dr. Boris M. Died on September 21 at the age of 70. A national leader in the field of administrative and community psychiatry. Former director of the Connecticut Mental Health Center and deputy chairman Yale Psychiatry Department. In 1990, he became professor and head of the Psychiatry Department at the University of Illinois at Chicago. Beloved husband of Batja, brother of Samuel. Devoted father of David (Karen), Joseph, Michael (Bethany), and Ellen (Tony). Adored by his ten grandchildren. Donations may be made to the Johns Hopkins Pancreatic Cancer Research, Bunting-Blaustein Cancer Research Building, care of Dr. E. Jaffee, Room 4M-07, 1650 Orleans Street, Baltimore, MD. 21231.
