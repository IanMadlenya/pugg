
        GLOTZER-Dr. Philip. After a long illness. On March 21, 1999. He was a veteran of the Korean War and medical officer for the New York City Fire Department. Most loved and loving husband of Joan. Father of Taya and David. Father-inlaw of Michael Sommer and Wendy Lazarus. Grandfather of Adam and Carolyne Sommer. Also survived by a brother, Dr. Donald Glotzer of Boston and many friends and colleagues. Service will be at Zion Memorial Chapel, 785 East Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck, on Tuesday at 1 PM.
