
        FASS-Robert J., M.D., Professor of Internal Medicine, Director of Division of Infectious Diseases at the Ohio State University, died at the age of 62, on January 1, 2002, from lung cancer. His distinguished career in the field of Infectious Diseases was world renowned and his impact on the training of hundreds of physicians will persist for generations. He loved his family, his friends and his job. He will be remembered for his independent spirit, his sense of fairness, and his generous nature. There will be a private service.
