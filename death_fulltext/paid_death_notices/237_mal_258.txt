
        O'MOORE--John J., of New Rochelle, died October 24, 2006, at age 83. Born in Ireland February 3, 1923 to John & Mae (Gabbett) O'Moore, he was a Licensed Real Estate Broker. Beloved husband of the late Gilda (Micera) O'Moore. Survived by his son Paul O'Moore (wife Margo), grandchildren, Marshall, Natalie & Madeline O'Moore and a sister Mary O'Moore. Predeceased by brother Michael O'Moore. Visitation Friday 6-9pm Coxe & Graziano Funeral Home, 767 E. Boston Post Road, Mamaroneck. 914-698-5968. Funeral Service Saturday 11:30 am Our Lady Queen of Peace Chapel at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
