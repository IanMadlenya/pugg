
        AUGENTI-Marion Wallace. Died at the Methodist Church Home in Riverdale, NY on November 5, 2001 at age 104. A graduate of Oberlin Conservatory of Music, she had resided in New York City since 1924. Her husband, Anthony, died in 1971. She is survived by a daughter, Martha Kershaw, a son, Thomas, four grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren.
