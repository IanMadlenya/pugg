
        BERG-Sidney. Formerly of Great Neck, NY, passed away at his home in San Antonio, Texas on October 31, 2002. Devoted husband of Ruth, loving father of Judith and the late Daniel, grandfather of Miriam. Family requests donations be sent to the National Yiddish Book Center, 1021 West St. Amherst, MA. 01002. Graveside services were held Friday, November 1, 2002 in Agudas Achim Cemetery and were officiated by Cantor David Silverstein. For info.
