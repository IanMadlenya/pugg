
        KOMITO--Carrie, on May 9, 2007, age 102 years. Mother of Avodah K. Offit, M.D., sister of Tessie Karper, grandmother of Michael Offit, M.B.A., and Kenneth Offit, M.D., M.P.H., great-grandmother of Anna, Caroline, Tristan, Wyatt and Lily. Wife of the late Abraham Komito, Deputy Chief Engineer, Borough of Brooklyn. For 70 years impresario of The Aladdin Hotel, Woodbourne, NY; sometimes investor in Manhattan real estate. During the last five years of her productive life, authored three books of reminiscences. Private burial.
