
        SHARCOFF-Corinne E. Died February 28, in Tucson, AZ. Much loved by her children Ted Goldstein (Eva Kjelberg) and Lynn Goldstein (Dennis McCarthy), her sister Ellen Jaffee (David), her grandchildren Erik Goldstein and Daniel and Matthew McCarthy, her niece Julie Lebo (Jim) and her nephew Michael Jaffee (Randi), and numerous other members of her family. Also much loved by her extended family of friends. Abundantly loving to so many, she leaves a legacy of love and kindness for all. At her request, no services were held. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory can be made to research organizations focusing on post-polio or to Odyssey House Hospice, 750 East Foothills Drive, Tucson, AZ. 85718.
