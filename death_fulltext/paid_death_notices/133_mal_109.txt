
        SHAPIRO-Richard S., 55, on February 6, of New City, NY. National Sales Manager for Metro Fabrics. Survived by his wife, Barbara; his children, Ira and Nadia Shapiro and Lori Shapiro; his sister, Norma Feldman; and his adored grandson, Noah. Services 12 noon, Monday, at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, Paramus, NJ.
        SHAPIRO - Richard. Dellwood Country Club expresses its profound sorrow on the passing of our loyal and cherished member of over 20 years. Richie was a devoted member of our Board of Governors and lead many of our committees. His friendship, leadership and kindness will be sorely missed by all. His life enriched ours. Our deepest sympathies to his devoted wife Barbara, son Ira, daughter Lori, grandson Noah and their entire family. Richard Schwartz, President
