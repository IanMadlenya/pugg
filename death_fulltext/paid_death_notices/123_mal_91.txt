
        COOPER - Sol. The Officers, Governors and Members of the New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed Member, Friar Sol Cooper, on April 7, 1997. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Private services were held in Florida on April 8. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec. Dir.
        COOPER-Sol. The Monday Nighters mourn the passing of their very dear and longtime friend and charter member, Sol Cooper. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Ethel and the entire family. F.S. Strauss, Chairman
        COOPER-Sol. The members of the City Athletic Club mourn the loss of its esteemed member, Sol Cooper, and wish to express heartfelt sympathy to his family. The Board of Governors
