
        ILCHMAN--Alice Stone. Director of the Jeannette K. Watson Fellowships and President Emerita of Sarah Lawrence College. Died of complications arising from pancreatic cancer on August 11th, in Bronxville, NY. A tireless advocate for international educational exchanges and an authority on philanthropy, she was admired for her inspiring and cheerful leadership of educational and charitable organizations. Alice was a pioneer in studying economic development in India. Her public service encompassed terms as Chairman of the Board of the Rockefeller Foundation and Assistant Secretary of State for Educational and Cultural Affairs. Her professional legacy resides in the hundreds of students she encouraged and mentored. Alice is survived by her husband of 46 years, Warren, her son Frederick, daughter Sarah, and a loving extended family. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Bronxville (17 Sagamore Road, Bronxville, and www.ccbny.org), at 4 pm, on Thursday, September 7th. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to some of her favorite causes: Chamber Music Society of Lincoln Center, Mount Holyoke College, PBS Foundation, Sarah Lawrence College, and Save the Children.
