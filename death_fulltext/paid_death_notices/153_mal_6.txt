
        RABINOVICH-Joseph. On September 30th, 1999. Beloved husband of Marcelle. Devoted father of Joyce Barkin. Adoring grandfather of Frances and greatgrandfather of Nicholas and Spencer. Also survived by children Matthew, Daniel, Jamie and Adrienne. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street, Friday and Saturday, 7-9 PM, with service on Sunday 11:30 AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Temple Emanu-El, 1 East 65 Street, New York, NY 10021.
        RABINOVICH-Joseph. The Officers, Governors and Members of The New York Friars Club mourn the loss of their esteemed member, Joseph Rabinovich, on September 29, 1999. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to his family. Services will be held at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave., on Sunday, October 3rd at 11:30AM. Alan King, Abbot Freddie Roman, Dean Jean-Pierre Trebot, Exec Director
