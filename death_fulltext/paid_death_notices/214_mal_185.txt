
        FACTOR--Sheldon ''Shelly,'' 75, of Brooklyn, NY and Las Vegas, NV, passed suddenly on Saturday, November 13th in Las Vegas. He was born Sheldon Fakterman on June 27, 1929 in Brooklyn. He retired as Director of Finance of the Rent Stabilization Association of NY and served in the U.S. Marine Corps during the Korean War.
