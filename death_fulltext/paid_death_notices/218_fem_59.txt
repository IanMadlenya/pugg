
        RATNER--Hilda, MD. The members of the Medical Society of the County of Queens express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their beloved colleague and dear friend Hilda Ratner, MD on March 6, 2005. A distinguished and dedicated physician she served The Society well for 68 years as President, Trustee, Secretary and Chair of various committees. Our deepest symapthies to her son, Robert Dressler; her daughter, Martha Dressler; her grandchildren and greatgrandchild. Cornelius J. Foley, MD President Fred S. Fensterer, MD Chairman Board of Trustees
