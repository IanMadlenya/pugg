
        LYNCH-Daniel Joseph. ''Joe Lynch'', 78 a retired Vice President of Donaldson, Lufkin & Jenrette, past Executive Vice President and Treasurer of Wood, Struthers & Winthrop, and a resident of North Merrick, on September 12, 1999. Survived by his wife Eleanore; his children, Patrick, Michael, Keelin, Daniel, Shannon, Meghan and William; and 7 grandchildren. Friends may call Tuesday 2-5 and 7-9 PM at Walker Funeral Home, 2039 Merrick Avenue, Merrick. Mass of Christian Burial, Wednesday, 10:45 AM, at Cure' of Ars Church, 2323 Merrick Avenue, Merrick. Interment in Arlington National Cemetery. Memorial donations may be made to the Society for Progressive Supranuclear Palsy, Inc., Suite 515, 1838 Greene Tree Road, Baltimore, MD. 21208.
