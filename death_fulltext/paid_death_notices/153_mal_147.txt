
        HOLMES-William C. Beloved son, brother, and friend of Emilie Holmes, Peter Holmes, and Frank D'Accone, at age 71 on October 11, 1999 at his home in Laguna Beach, Calif. He was Professor Emeritus of Music at the University of California, Irvine, and formerly taught at Cornell University. He published widely, specializing in Italian opera & was an accomplished musician. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
