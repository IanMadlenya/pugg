
        CLARK, Lilly Wahwer. In loving memory. Born in Riga, Latvia, June 18, 1898, died New York City, Jan. 8, 1997. Ballerina, equestrienne, multilingual, Diplomatic hostess in London, Berlin and Havana. She worked for the Near East Relief in Erivan, America, 1921-1923. She leaves daughter Doris and 4 grandsons, the Westbay brothers in Colorado, nieces, nephew, and counsins in Riga, Arizona, California, Rochester, New York and Canada and friends in NYC. A memorial service will be held in Rochester in the spring.
