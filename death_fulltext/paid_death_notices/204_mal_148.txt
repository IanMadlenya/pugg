
        LASALA--Anthony of Pelham Manor died January 9, 2004. He was 96. Husband of the late Eleanor Poletti LaSala. Father of Lenora Vinci and Kenneth A. LaSala. Brother of A. Stephen LaSala and the late Frank and Andrew LaSala, May Devirgilis and Louise Buonagurio. Grandfather of Audra McGuire, Mark and Kenneth LaSala Jr., Ellie and Bruce Vinci, Jr. Great-grandfather of Audrey, Mark and Charles McGuire III, Samantha and Kenneth LaSala III. Visiting hours at the Pelham Funeral Home, 64 Lincoln Ave, Pelham, NY on Monday and Tuesday from 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Funeral mass will be at Our Lady of Perpetual Help Church, Pelham Manor on Wednesday at 10 AM. Entombment at Woodlawn Mausoleum. In lieu of flowers donations in his memory may be made to Att: Dr. Petrillo, Mt Vernon Hospital, 12 North 7 Ave, Mt Vernon, NY 10550.
