
        FEDER--Maria, M.D. Died peacefully on Friday, April 2nd, 2004. Beloved wife of the (recently) late Ted, devoted mother of Gene, Ann and Joan, loving grandmother of Ellie, Ben, Josh, Jojo, Lizzie, Jake and Sammy and aunt of Beverly, June, Allan and Lami. Born on the shores of Lake Balaton, she survived the Holocaust, met the love of her life and lived in Austria, Germany, Iran, Israel, Switzerland, before retiring in New York. She combined raising her family with a distinguished career in geriatric psychiatry, setting up the first day hospital in Switzerland. We loved everything about her. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Burden Center/C.V. Starr, 1484 1st Avenue, New York NY 10021. Funeral will be held at Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 630 Amsterdam Ave, corner of 91st St on Sunday, April 4th, 2004 at 10AM.
