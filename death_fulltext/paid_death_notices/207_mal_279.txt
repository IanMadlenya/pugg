
        JASPER--Leonard. Beloved husband of Jane, adored father of Robin-Eve Jasper and Roger LaRouche and Francesca and Bruce Rudin. Darling (Boppo) of Maxwell and Lauren Wessel and dearest (Cops) of Samuel and Alexandra Rudin. Dear brother of Harvey Jasper and the late Sara Jane Jasper. Passed from us on April 24, 2004, at the age of 76. He is loved and missed.
