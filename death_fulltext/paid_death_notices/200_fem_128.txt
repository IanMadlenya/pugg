Grandmother of Elaine and Ken Sonenberg, Randy and Cindy Heller, Margie and Seth Rosenthal-Hanft and the late Barry Douglas, great-grandmother of Zoe, David, Marissa, Tyler and Noah. Services Thursday, at 11 AM, at ''Riverside-Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
        BREENBERG--Minnie. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to her family. Temple Israel of Great Neck. Myron Fenster, Int. Sr. Rabbi Sidney Freedman, President
