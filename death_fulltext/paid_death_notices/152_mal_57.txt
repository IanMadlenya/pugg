
        LEE-James Wideman II. Age 93, a resident of Dorset, VT., and husband of May Foote Lee died September 5. Following graduation from Princeton, he joined Ivy Lee and T.J. Ross as a public relations counselor. A memorial service will be held September 10 at 10 AM at the United Church of Dorset and East Rupert. Donations may be made to the Dorset Nursing Association or Manchester Rescue Squad in care of Brewster Funeral Service, P.O. Box 885, Manchester, Vermont 05255.
