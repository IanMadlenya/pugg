
        SHEIN-Joseph, M.D. The Department of Pediatrics at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset sadly acknowledges the death of one of its senior pediatricians, Joseph Shein, M.D. Dr. Shein passed away July 15, 1999 at the age of 83. Dr. Shein received his medical degree from Witwatersand University in Johannesburg, South Africa. He joined the staff at North Shore University Hospital in 1954 as a Founding Physician. Dr. Shein served the Great Neck community as a solo practitioner from 1950 and was well known for his dedication to his patients. His long association with our institution was an inspiration to all of us who knew him. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his family. Mervin Silverberg, M.D. Chairman, Dept. of Pediatrics
