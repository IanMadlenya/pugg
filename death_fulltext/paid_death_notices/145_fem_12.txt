
        ROBERTS-Bernice. A kind friend to all. Beloved wife of the late Philip. Devoted mother of Adam and Diane. Loving sister of Edith and Dr. Jerry Oberman. Services 12 noon Tuesday at ''Gutterman's'', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, N.Y.
        ROBERTS-Bernice. The Board of Trustees, Medical Staff and Employees of Long Beach Medical Center, mourn the sudden passing of our esteemed colleague Bernice Roberts. As Director of Community Relations, her energy, creativity, intellect and charm advanced the mission of the Medical Center and were instrumental in fostering its close ties to the community. The entire Medical Center family will miss her. We extend our deepest sympathies to her son Adam, daughter Diane and sister Edith. Alfred I. Osterland, Chairman Board of Trustees Robert Rubin, M.D., Chairman, Medical Staff Martin F. Nester, Jr., Chief Executive Officer
