
        LEIHER-Florence McCaffrey. On October 28, 1999, in New York City. She was a 1927 graduate of New Paltz SUNY University. Retired teacher, Our Lady of Assumption School, Bronx, NY. Active member for many years with the Ladies of Charity. Beloved wife of the late Frank M., who was the former Resident Manager of Peter Cooper Village and Stuyvesant Town, New York City. Loving mother of Gregory, James and Robert (Chip). Dear mother-in-law of Diane and Nancy. Devoted grandmother of Suzanne, Kathryn and Rebecca. Dear sister-in-law of Anna and Dorothy. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the Ladies of Charity, care of the Archdiocese of New York, 1011 First Avenue, New York, NY 10022. Friends may call at the Andrett Funeral Home, 353 Second Avenue, 20th Street, New York, NY, Monday, 2-5 and 79 PM. Mass of Christian Burial, Church of the Epiphany, Tuesday, 11 AM. Interment St. Mary's Cemetery, Port Jervis, NY.
