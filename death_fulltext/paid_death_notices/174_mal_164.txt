
        McCLANE-G. Warren. 81. Manager of the Booth Theatre died on June 25, 2001 at Lenox Hill Hospital from cancer. During four decades in the theater he was considered the consummate company manager (''Beyond The Fringe'', ''Irene'', ''Dancin''', ''Woman Of The Year'') before becoming a theater manager for the Shubert Organization. He is survived by four nieces, Farrell Gonsalves of MA, Barbara Jean McClane, Sharon McClane and Dana McClane of CA., and three nephews, Kenneth an English Professor at Cornell, Walter and Duncan of CA. A memorial service will be held after Labor Day.
