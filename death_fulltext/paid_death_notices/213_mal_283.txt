
        ROUSMANIERE--James A. 86, Democrat, fund raiser and sailor, October 22, 2004, in Southbury, CT, following a stroke. He ran campaigns for Harvard, United Negro College Fund, Museum of Modern Art, among others. He mounted civic betterment efforts in Oyster Bay, NY, and Southbury. He made a difference in countless ways. Survived by beloved wife of 61 years, Jessie; sister Frances Storrs and children John, James, Peter, David, Joseph, Edmund, Arthur and Kate; 15 grandchildren; one greatgrandchild. Services Saturday, October 30, at 2 pm, at St. Paul's Church, 294 Main St South, Woodbury, CT. In lieu of flowers contributions welcomed to St. Paul's Church and Southbury Public Library.
