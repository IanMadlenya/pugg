
        EARLE-William P.S. Jr. Of Palm City, Florida, and NYC. Died on October 1, 2001. He is survived by his children William, Julia, Valerie Miller and Doreen Mogavero, six grandchildren and two greatgrandchildren. His life was devoted to his family, his work and his country. He was a retired Army Captain who lead the first wave onto the Phillipines during WWII. He returned home and became a member and Governor of the American Stock Exchange. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the New York Stock Exchange Fallen Heroes Fund at Mogavero Lee and Co, Inc, 25 Broad St, Apt PHE, NYC 10004, attn: Doreen Mogavero. A memorial service will be held at St. Albans Church on Staten Island, 10 AM, October 25, 2001. He will be greatly missed and lovingly remembered.
