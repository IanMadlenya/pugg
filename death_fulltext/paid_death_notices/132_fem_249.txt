
        MARKS-Dr. Sema Joy (nee Fichtenbaum). Age 55. Of Manhattan. Died on January 19, 1998 after a year-long battle with brain cancer. Sema's unique personality and broad background with IBM made her a valuable member of the BBN/GTE Internet organization. Sema held a Doctorate in Information Systems and Education from Harvard. Sema is survived by an aunt, Francis Kanarek, a number of cousins and numerous friends around the world.
