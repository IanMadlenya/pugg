
        SHERIDAN--William J., Jr. 86, of New Canaan, CT. A retired Senior Executive for ESSO and Exxon, died of complications from a hip fracture on December 27, 2003. He is survived by his wife of 62 years, Rita, children William J. III, M.D. of Halifax, Nova Scotia, Laurie of Milton, MA. and Susan of Rhinebeck, N.Y., five grandchildren; brothers Robert J., M.D. of Rocky Mount, NC and Charles B. of Media, PA., and nieces and nephews. A private remembrance will be held later. Donations may be made to Nursing and Home Care of New Canaan (VNA) or AmeriCares.
