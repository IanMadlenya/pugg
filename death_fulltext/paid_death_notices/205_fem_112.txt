
        DeVOE--Nina Carr Bondurant. On February 8, in Gladwyne, PA, age 94. A graduate of Vassar College, she attended the Art Students League in New York and was an accomplished watercolorist. She is survived by her husband A.G. DeVoe, M.D., her daughter Linda Carr MilneTyte, her granddaughter Ashley and grandson Alistair. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. Donations may be sent to Frances Lehman Loeb Art Center, Vassar College, Poughkeepsie, NY.
