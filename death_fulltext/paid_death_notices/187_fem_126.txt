
        BARTLETT-Lysbet Lefferts. Died August 5, 2002, at age 95. Beloved wife of the late Philip Golden Bartlett. Devoted mother of Phyllis Bartlett and Sarah Bartlett Jones. Cherished grandmother of Christopher Walton Jones and Philip Bartlett Jones. Faithful great-grandmother to Carter Fenton Jones and Lysbet Lefferts Jones. A graduate of the Chapin School, she attended Bryn Mawr College and during WWII was active in the Women's Relief Efforts. She also volunteered at the Museum of the City of New York. She was a member of the National Society of Colonial Dames, the Colonial Lords of the Manor, and the Society of Daughters of the Holland Dames of America. Service Wednesday, August 21, at 3 PM St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave at 50th St. In lieu of flowers contributions would be appreciated to the Visiting Nurse Service of NY, Home Hospice Program, 1250 Bway, NYC 10001.
