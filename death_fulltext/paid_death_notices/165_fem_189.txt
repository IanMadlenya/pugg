
        HORN-Jacqueline L. Jacqueline L. (Jackie) Horn, 69, of Lee's Summit, MO died October 17, 2000 at home. Services will be 1 P.M. Saturday, October 21 at the Lee's Summit Community Church, Lee's Summitt, MO. Cremation, with interment at Longview Memorial Gardens. Visitation will be 7-8:30 P.M., Friday, October 20 at the Church. The family suggests contributions to the Lee's Summit Community Church or Wayside Waifs Animal Shelter. Jackie was born January 2, 1931 in New York, NY. She earned a B.S. degree from Skidmore College, and also attended Case Western Reserve. She was an art teacher, a realtor, a broker, former editor of The Bratenahl (Ohio) Lamplighter, a Sunday School teacher, vacation Bible School teacher, past president of the PTA, past president of the Bratenahl School Board, and a member of the American Contract Bridge League. She is survived by sons; Douglas G. Brown, Keith H. Brown, Chaz D. Horn, all of Lee's Summit, MO. A sister; Marion E. Kelsey of Miami, FL and grandchildren; Naomi R. Brown, Michael K. Brown, Stephanie R. Brown, Rachel J. Brown, Daniel W. Brown, Matthew D. Brown, Tyler R. Horn, Jayme N. Horn all of Lee's Summit, MO. D.W. Newcomer's Longview Funeral Home.
