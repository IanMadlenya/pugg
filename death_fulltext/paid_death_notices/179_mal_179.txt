
        WADLINGTON-Cuba, Jr. The Board of Trustees, staff and volunteers of the March of Dimes mourn the passing of our friend and esteemed Board of Advisors member Cuba Wadlington, Jr. Mr. Wadlington, executive vice-president of Williams (NYSE: WMB) of Tulsa, OK, and president and chief executive officer of its gas pipeline division, began his volunteer leadership for the March of Dimes in Texas, where he served as chairman of Houston's WalkAmerica. We will long remember his dedication to our mission to prevent prematurity and infant death. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Ann, and family. Jennifer L. Howse, Ph.D. President Gary D. Forsee, Chairman of the Board March of Dimes
