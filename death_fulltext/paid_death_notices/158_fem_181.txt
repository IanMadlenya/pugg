
        ELKIN-Rosalind. The American Jewish Congress expresses its deep sadness over the passing of Rosalind Elkin on March 6, the daughter of Lillian and Jack Elkin, longtime devoted leaders of this organization. We will join with others of her friends and family in recalling her life at a memorial service to be held on Thursday, March 23, at 3 PM, at Stanton House, 190 Stanton St., New York. Phil Baum, Executive Director American Jewish Congress
