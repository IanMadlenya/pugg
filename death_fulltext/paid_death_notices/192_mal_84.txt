
        KLINGENSTEIN - Paul. American Friends of The Hebrew University and The Hebrew University of Jerusalem note with profound sorrow the loss of Paul Klingenstein, a dedicated American Friend. Paul and his wife Selma have been long time supporters and Benefactors of the University. Through their generous support they established the Paul and Selma Klingenstein Fellowship Fund for BioMedical Research to provide research fellowships for postgraduate students. Their commitment has also benefited the Sarasota-Manatee Chapter through the Sarasota Scholarship Fund and where Paul served as the first president and most recently as co-chair. We offer our heartfelt condolences to his wife Selma and the entire family. AFHU Ira Lee Sorkin, President Keith L. Sachs, Chair Peter T. Willner, Exec V.P. The Hebrew University of Jerusalem Prof. Menachem Magidor, President Prof. Haim Rabinowitch Rector Yigal Arnon, Chair
