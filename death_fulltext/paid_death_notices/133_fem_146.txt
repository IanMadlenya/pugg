
        GERAGHTY-Mary (nee Tubridy). Of Riverdale, NY on February 17, in her 88th year. Native of Clohanmore, Cree, Co. Clare, Ireland. Loving wife of the late Matthew. Dear mother of John, Joseph and Edward and mother-in-law of Margaret and Barbara. Cherished grandmother of Kate and Elizabeth. Dear sister of Peggy Hamann, Michael and Thomas Tubridy and Paddy Tubridy (Ireland) and Sean Tubridy (England) and sister-in-law of Cecily Geraghty. Dear aunt to many. Reposing Thursday 2-4 and 7-9PM, at Riverdale-On-Hudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Avenue. Mass of Christian Burial Friday 10am at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to Fordham Prep School, East Fordham Road, Bronx, NY 10458 would be greatly appreciated.
