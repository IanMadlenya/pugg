
        DREW--Stanley. Baker and noble simpleton. Born April 26, 1912, Warsaw, Poland and died Erev Yom Kippur 2005, Englewood, NJ. Born a fighter and died a fighter after 93 years and his first lost bout. He was kind and humble and saw the good in everyone and helped everyone find the good in themselves. He leaves behind his beloved wife of 64 years, Liza, his dedicated daughter, Pauline, his son-in-law, Avy, his grandchildren Ronny, Danny, Tammy, and his great-grandchildren Rachel, Max and Miriam.
