
        STERLING--Charles Archer, Jr. Died peacefully 30 March 2006, at St. Andrews Village at the age of 91. He was born in Montclair, N.J. on 16 May 1914 to Charles A. Sterling & Susie Williams Sterling. He graduated from The Taft School class of 1934 and Williams College class of 1938. He spent his working career with IBM in New York City and White Plains, N.Y. retiring in 1972. He had been a resident of New Canaan, CT, Naples, FL. and New London, NH. before moving in 2000 to St. Andrews Village in Boothbay Harbor, Maine. He spent many long summers on Squirrel Island, Maine. He was dedicated to his wife, Terry, of 65 years, his family, and to the birds that fluttered to his well-stocked bird feeder. In addition to his wife, he is survived by Charles A. III and his wife Kathy of Berkeley, CA. Arlyn Doran and her husband Charles of North Attleboro, MA, James and his wife Elizabeth of South Freeport, ME, Carol Laro and her husband Steve of Concord, NH; nine grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
