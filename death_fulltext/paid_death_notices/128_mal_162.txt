
        BRODY-Dr. Stanley (Steve) J., on Sept. 11, 1997 in La Jolla, CA. He was 79. Dr. Brody was Prof. Emeritus of Rehabilitation Medicine in Psychiatry, Univ. of Pennsylvania and of the Wharton School of Business. He authored first texts on rehabilitation and the aged. Among his awards, Dr. Brody was recipient of the Key and Donald P. Kent Awards for Excellence in Gerontology. He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Elaine, daughter Laurel Karpman of Phoenix, son Peter of San Diego and four granddaughters. Donations: The Stanley J. Brody Memorial Fund The Gerontological Society of America, 1275 K St NW Suite 350, Washington, DC 20005-4006.
