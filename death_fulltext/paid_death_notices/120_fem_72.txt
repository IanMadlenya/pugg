
        LEWIS - Claire J. On Saturday, January 4, 1997, after a hardfought battle with cancer. Goddaughter and lifelong friend of Helen Keller. Made much publicized cross country drive of Hudson Motor Cars to demonstrate the car's reliability and ease with which women could travel Independently and confidently. Performed as ''Billie Dawn'' in ''Born Yesterday'' for regional theater. In the '50's pioneered womens' role in hosting radio interview shows with a show from Westport, CT, and marketed her food marinades as Claire's Flair. Son Robert Emory and granddaughter Jessica survive. Quaker memorial meeting will be held at future date.
