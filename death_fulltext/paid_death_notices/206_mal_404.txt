
        SCHULMAN--Dr. David. Died on Sunday, March 28th, 2004 of complications from Parkinson's disease at his home in Manhattan. Beloved husband of Gloria Yevish Schulman, cherished father of Sarah Schulman, Helen Schulman & Charlie Schulman. Loving father-in-law of Bruce Handy and Juliet Bellow, devoted grandfather of Zoe and Isaac Handy. A memorial service will be conducted at a later date. In lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Division of Child Psychiatry at Mt. Sinai Hospital.
