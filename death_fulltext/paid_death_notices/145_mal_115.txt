
        REINGOLD-Jacob. The Board of Trustees and Staff of the New York Presbyterian Hospital are deeply saddened by the passing of our friend, Jacob Reingold. We will miss his wisdom and his compassion, and join with all those who knew him in mourning his loss. Our deepest sympathies are extended to the Reingold family.
