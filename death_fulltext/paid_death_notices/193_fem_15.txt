
        KRANTZ - Bertha. Dearly loved by family and friends, died at home in Mt. Dora, FL where she retired in 1989 after a celebrated career in publishing, 34 years of which were spent at Random House. She is survived by lifelong friends, co-workers, colleagues, and her beloved family, and was known by many as the little lady whose presence was huge in so many lives. She will be truly missed. Donations in her memory to St. Jude's Children's Hospital in Memphis, TN.
