
        SHAPIRO--Dr. Miriam Klein. The Solomon Schechter School of Westchester joins the New York Jewish community in mourning the death of our friend and teacher Dr. Miriam Klein Shapiro. Miriam was the mother of five graduates, wife of Saul, a founding trustee, a past President of our PTA, and was influential in the development of our Judaic Studies program. May her memory be a blessing to her family and all who knew her. M. Mac Schwebel, Chairman James M. Dubin, Vice Chair. Dr. Elliott Spiegel, Headmstr.
