
        TIPLITZ-George Lewis. On September 9, 1997. Succumbed to death after a heroic battle with pneumonia and Alzheimers at White Plains Medical Center. He leaves his wife of 50 years Doris (nee Wiederschall), one son John Evan, and two grandchildren Lee Evan and Ian David; two cherished sisters Ruth Zelinka and Anita Kirsten, husband Marvin. He also leaves myriad nieces and nephews who adored and were devoted to him. Service Friday, September 12, 1:30 PM at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Those who may wish to remember him may send contributions to White Plains Medical Center, White Plains, New York or a charity of your choice.
