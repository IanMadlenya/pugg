
        KISSINGER-Paula Stern. Age 97, on November 15, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Louis Kissinger. She is survived by her two sons Henry A. and Walter B., six grandchildren and six great-grandchildren. Funeral services at 11 AM on Monday, November 16, at Hirsch & Sons, 1225 Jerome Ave. at 167th St., Bronx. The family requests in lieu of flowers that donations be sent in her memory to the Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital, Development Office, 622 West 168th St., New York, NY 10032-3784.
