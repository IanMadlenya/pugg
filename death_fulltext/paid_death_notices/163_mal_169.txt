
        FEIN-Mitchell. 84. Lost his battle with Parkinson's disease Saturday, August 12. Brilliant engineer, loving husband, father and grandfather. He was kind, political and very generous. He will be missed by his colleagues, his community and his family. He is survived by his wife of fifty-one years, Gertrude, a son, David of Hillsdale, NJ, a son Jeremy, of Denver, CO, a daughter, Lisa Gilford of NYC and two grandchildren, Max and Molly. In accordance with his wishes, Mr. Fein's body has been donated to Columbia University for Parkinson's research.
