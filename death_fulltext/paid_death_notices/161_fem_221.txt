
        MICHAELI-Ursula. The Michaeli family announces with the deepest sorrow the death of our mother and grandmother, Ursula (Muzzi) Michaeli on Sunday, June 25, 2000. The loving wife of the late David (Pops), sister of Inga Cavero Blumenfeld, Jean Fasman, Ruth Brass and Shirley Hirsch, wonderful mother of Adina, Jonathan and Dorlee and the adoring grandmuzzi of Daniel and Talia. A survivor of the Holocaust, Muzzi, along with Pops, devoted herself to a variety of causes including the Wantagh Jewish Center, US Holocaust Museum, Long Island Committee for Soviet Jewry and the Juvenile Diabetes Foundation. She was loved by everyone she touched and by everyone who touched her. You will live in all of our hearts forever. Donations may be made to any of the above organizations. Services will be held on Tuesday, June 27 at 10 AM at the Wantagh Jewish Center, Woodbine Ave, Wantagh, NY.
