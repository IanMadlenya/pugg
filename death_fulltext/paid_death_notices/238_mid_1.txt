
        FISCHMAN--Helen T., 85, died on November 3, 2006. Beloved mother of Meryl Ain and Arthur Fischman, mother-inlaw of Stewart Ain and Janet Margolies, loving grandmother of Jonathan, Daniel and Michael Ain, Anna and Molly Fischman, grandmother-inlaw to Beth Ain, great-grandmother of Grace Ain. WWII veteran who married her husband Herbert while the two were on furlough in 1944. They lived happily together until his death in 2005. Longtime manager at Long Island Jewish Hospital. A woman of dignity, kindness, wisdom and optimism. May her memory be for a blessing. Funeral Sunday, 11am, at IJ Morris, 21 E. Deer Park Rd., Dix Hills.
