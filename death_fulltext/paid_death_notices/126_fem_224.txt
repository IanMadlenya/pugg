
        HUTTON-Virginia W., environmentalist and pioneer in the recycling movement, died Thursday, July 23, 1997 at her home in New Hope, PA. Wife of the late Lefferts Hutton. Former member of the Hartwood Club. She is survived by two daughters, a brother, a sister and two grandchildren. Services private. Memorials to the Audubon Society of Doylestown Hospital Hospice, Doylestown, PA.
