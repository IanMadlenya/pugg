
        WISE-Blanche. On May 21, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Philip Wise. Devoted mother of Ellen Questel her husband Roger and the late Stephen Wise. Cherished grandmother of Niccolo Salvadori and adopted grandmother of Aline and Lucia Questel. Funeral services Thursday 11:30 am at Frank E. Campbell 1076 Madison Ave at 81 Street. Interment to follow at Beth David Cemetery, Elmont NY.
        WISE - Blanche. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Blanche Wise. To her family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
