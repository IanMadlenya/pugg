
        RANDELMAN-Milton, on Feb. 25 at Lenox Hill Hospital. Survived by his beloved wife Roslyn, his loving son and daughter-in-law Hal and Mary Randelman, two adored grandchildren Craig and Nicole Randelman, three devoted sisters: Jimmie Josephson, Beulah Glickman, and Evelyn Rifkin. Member of Congregation Emanu-El and the Friars Club. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Wednesday 7-9 PM, with service Thursday, 11:30 AM, Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        RANDELMAN-Milton. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Milton Randelman. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        RANDELMAN-Milton. Milton, we will always remember the happy times our families enjoyed together. We will miss you. Zelinka and Greenman Families
