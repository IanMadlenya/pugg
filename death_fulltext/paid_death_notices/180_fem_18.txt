
        BEDARD-Gertrude King. On December 31, 2001 at her home, The Manhattan House. She was 90 years of age. Mrs. Bedard was a member of the Colony Club and the Clam Bake Club in Newport, Rhode Island. Her many friends in New York City and Newport mourn the loss of this wonderful person. There are no living relatives. A memorial service will be announced at a later date.
