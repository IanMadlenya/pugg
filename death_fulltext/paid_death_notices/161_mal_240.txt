
        RAFTERY-William A. (formerly of Alpine, N.J.) died at his home in Pinehurst, North Carolina on June 19, 2000. Mr. Raftery is survived by Vivian, his wife of 46 years; daughters Donna of New York City and Linda of Durham, N.C.; granddaughters, Claire and Adriane; and brother John Jr. of Las Vegas, Nevada. His dry humor, exceptional intellect, strong integrity and love will be fondly remembered and greatly missed by his family, friends and business associates. Mr. Raftery is well known as a leader in building international trade with the automotive aftermarket as well as being instrumental in developing the domestic auto parts industry. He has served on Presidential Advisory Committees to each White House since the Kennedy Administration. Friends may call at the Barrett Funeral Home, 148 Dean Dr., Tenafly, N.J. on Thursday 79PM and Friday 2-4 & 7-9PM. Funeral Mass Saturday 9:45AM, St. Joseph's Church, Demarest, N.J. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery, Hawthorne, N.Y. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Duke Childrens Hospital, P.O. Box 2975, Durham, N.C. 27710 would be appreciated.
