
        SIEGEL -- Florence Jacobs. On Friday, October 22, 2004. She was 94 years old. Devoted wife of Meyer for 49 years. Loving mother of Lloyd and Herbert and daughters-inlaw, Roslyn and Martha. Cherished grandmother of Randolph, Janine, Noah and Miranda. Proud great-grandmother of Hallie, Willa, Elijah, Evan and Joshua. Devoted daughter of Reuben and Stella Jacobs and loving sister of recently deceased Marcia Horowitz and Evelyn Herman. --Mother, your intelligence, smile, energy, and love will always be remembered. You led an elegant life, full of compassion for others. We love you! Your children. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 St and Amsterdam Ave, Sunday, October 24 at 1:30 pm. Interment to follow. The family will receive friends and relatives on Sunday after 7 pm, at the home of Lloyd and Roslyn, and on Monday, October 25, after 7 pm, at the home of Herbert and Martha. Contributions in Florence's name may be made to the Ramaz School, 114 E. 85 St, NY, NY 10028; or to the Police Athletic League, 341/2 E. 12 St, NY, NY 10003.
