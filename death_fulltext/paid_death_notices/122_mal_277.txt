
        KLIGERMAN-Alan. Age 70, on March 22. Beloved husband of Miralyn. Devoted and adored father to Andrew, Cheryl and daughter-in-law June. Loving grandfather of Matthew. Dear friend to brother Howard and sister-in-law Carole. Cherished by his countless friends and loved by every person who was fortunate enough to have their lives touched by this extraordinary individual. As a Founding Partner of the insurance agency of Kligerman & Kalayjian, Inc., successor to A. Schoen Agency, he was a guiding force in the insurance industry for over 35 years. Alan, your caring, generous and joyous nature brightened and enriched our lives. You were always there for us. You will forever be in our thoughts and hearts and we will deeply miss you. Services will be held at 1 PM, Monday, at the ''Riverside Memorial Chapel,'' 21 West Broad Street, Mount Vernon. Interment will be in Cedar Park Cemetery.
        KLIGERMAN-Alan. The Board of Governors and members of Hampshire Country Club are deeply saddened by the passing of our esteemed member Alan Kligerman. Our heartfelt sympathy is extended to his wife, Miralyn, and to all his family. Hampshire Country Club Board of Governors Norman Zarkin, President
