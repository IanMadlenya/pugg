
        KRAMER-Ruth. Beloved mother of Paul, Bren, Michael, Mark, grandmother of David, Jeffrey, Nina, Brian, Elliot, Aurora, Arielle, Melinda, great grandmother of Kelsey, Lindsay, Michaela, mother-in-law of Jennifer, Helen, Steve, grandmotherin-law of Duncan and Sophia and wife of the late Harry Kramer, died on July 8, 2002 after a long illness. She was revered, adored & respected by her family, to whom she gave unconditional love all her years. She never lost her spark or spunk and was involved in all our lives in the most caring way. We will miss her always. In lieu of flowers donations can be made to the new York Times Neediest Cases Fund, 212-556-5851, ext. 7.
