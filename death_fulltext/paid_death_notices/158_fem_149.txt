
        ZION-Edith. SAR Academy notes with deep sorrow the sudden passing of Edith Zion. Beloved wife of Abe Zion, founder, long-time Benefactor and Trustee; adoring mother of our Trustee, Mark, Joshua, Adina, and their spouses, as well as cherished grandmother. May the entire family find consolation in her memory and comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jack C. Bendheim, Pres. Ludwig Bravmann, Vice Chmn. Board of Trustees Rabbi Joel Cohn, Principal
        ZION-Edythe. The Riverdale Jewish Center mourns the loss of our member Edythe Zion. We express profound condolences to her husband Abraham Zion, our benefactor and honorary trustee, and her three children, Mark, Adina and Joshua Zion, to her grandchildren, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jonathan I. Rosenblatt, Rabbi Tobias B. Schapiro, President Ludwig Bravmann, Chairman of the Board
