
        WEINSTEIN-Sylvia Linder. Beloved mother of three. cherished grandmother of four. Loving Aunt, wife, sister, cousin and devoted friend to many. Born on Lincoln's Birthday, 1912, teacher of English and French, graduated NYU Phi Beta Kappa, married to the late Dr. Mandel Weinstein for 41 years. She lived in Forest Hills, Atlantic Beach and Florida, travelled extensively, an amateur photographer who won many prizes, and achieved professional status with a walking book and a portrait of a Japanese warship in New York Harbor, which hangs in the Library of Congress. President of Hadassah and UJA in Queens and one of the original founders of the Forest Hills Jewish Center. Donations in her memory may be made to UJA, Hadassah, The Sierra Foundation and The Humane Society. Services will be held in the Schwartz Memorial Chapel, Thursday, May 4, 1PM, (718) 263-7600, 114-03 Queens Boulevard, Forest Hills. She is survived by daughters Marion Weinstein, Ruth Klein and Bette Kaplan, sister Charlotte Lebowitz and grandchildren David, Brian and Benjamin Klein and Manda Kaplan. She continues to be an inspiration to us all.
