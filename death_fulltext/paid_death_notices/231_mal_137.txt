
        WYATT--George R. Died April 9, 2006, age 85. New York Director, Trustee, President (1978-1993) and Senior Consultant of the Johnson O'Connor Research Foundation, a national aptitude testing institution. He is survived by his wife, Shirley, and their son, Jeffrey. Erudite and gracious, George was an inspiration to all who knew him. His family, work colleagues, and those who received career advice from him share in the loss, but live with the encouragement and wisdom he provided.
