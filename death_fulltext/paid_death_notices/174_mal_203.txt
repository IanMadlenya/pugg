
        SINGER-Norman. The Board and Staff of City Center are saddened by the death of Norman Singer, whose vision kept this theater alive during a critical period in its history. His commitment to the arts helped insure that City Center thrives today as a home for theater, music and dance. Judith E. Daykin, President and Executive Director City Center
        SINGER-Norman. The Board of Directors of the Dance Notation Bureau salutes with joy, sadness and affection the memory of Norman Singer and the many years of loyal service he devoted to our cause. A former member of the DNB board his wise counsel, good nature, generosity and wit shall long be remembered. He shall be greatly missed.
