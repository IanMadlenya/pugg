
        BOZIAN--Betty on November 5th 2005. Loving wife of 57 years to George Bozian of Chatham Borough. Devoted mother of Alan G. Bozian and his wife Alison Overseth; adoring grandmother of Hannah, Peter, and Emily Bozian, all of New York City. Dear sister of Dorothe Parsons of Chatham Township. Funeral services were private.
