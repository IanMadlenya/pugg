
        KRAUS-H. Robert, 76 of Kent, CT. Author, illustrator, publisher and cartoonist. Survived by his wife, Pamela (Evan Wong) Kraus, two sons, Bruce R. Kraus of New York, NY and C. Friends will be received at the Kane Funeral Home on Thursday after 1PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Art Students League of New York, 215 W. 57th St., NY, NY 10019.
