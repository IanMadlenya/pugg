
        BRUGGEMANN--Robert A. 56. Formerly of Weston and Norwalk, CT, died peacefully Saturday, August 13 at his home in Peru, VT surrounded by his family. Born in Teaneck NJ on April 24, 1949, the son of Arnold and Rita Bruggemann. Bob was a devoted father and the President of A.E. Bruggemann & Company, serving the oil industry for over 35 years. He was also a generous supporter of youth and children's charities. He is survived by his wife Barbara, daughters Carrie and Atty, his son Robert & granddaughter, Brooklyn. He is also survived by his mother Rita and sisters Barbara Boyer and Joanne Schubert and his brother Richard. A private service was held in Vermont Saturday. A memorial Mass will be held at St. Mary's Church, 669 West Ave, Norwalk, CT. on Monday, August 22 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations can be made to the St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, Tribute #
