
        ADELSON-Beatrice. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Beatrice Adelson, beloved mother of Warren Adelson, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to Warren, and his wife, Jan; to Beatrice's four grandchildren; and to the entire Adelson family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
