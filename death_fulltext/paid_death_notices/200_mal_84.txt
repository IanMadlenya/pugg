
        WEINSTOCK -- Mandel George. Born May 19th, 1909, in New York. Died August 25th, 2003, in Los Angeles. He is survived by Ida, his wife for 67 years, his daughter, Diana, Ph.D., and son, Robert, M.D. He was a devoted, wise, inspirational, loving, treasured husband and father, with a voracious appetite for knowledge, literature, music and politics that he passed on to all who knew him.
