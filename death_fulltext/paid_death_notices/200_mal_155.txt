
        DiGANGI--M. Richard, M.D. On September 11, 2003. Beloved husband of the late Marie for 60 years. Loving father of Linda, Richard, Peter and Roberta. Dear grandfather of Christi, Rebecca, Matthew and Kate. Companion of Barbara Friedman. Richard was a Lieutenant Colonel in the Army Air Corps during WWII, where he was a Flight Surgeon. After his honorable military service, he was a General Surgeon in various Brooklyn hospitals for 35 years. Reposing Monday, September 15, 2003, from 6-9 PM, at Cusimano & Russo Funeral Home, 2005 W. 6th Street, Brooklyn, NY. Funeral Mass Tuesday, 9:45 AM, at Saints Simon & Jude Roman Catholic Church. Entombment to follow in St. Michael's Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, family requests donations be made to the American Cancer Society. www.cusimanoandrusso.com
