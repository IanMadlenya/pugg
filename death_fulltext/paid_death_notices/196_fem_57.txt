
        SPEYER-Germaine M. Wife of the late Ernest A. Speyer. Died in her 90th year. Mother of Vivian and Donald Sonnenborn, Jerry Speyer and Katherine Farley. Grandmother of Valerie and Jeffrey, Robert, Holly and Jonathan, Laura, Jonah, Andrew and David. Great-grandmother of William, Charles and Arden. Special friend and ''Grandmother'' to many. Funeral services private. The family will receive friends on Thursday, May 8, 4-7PM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street. In lieu of flowers contributions can be made to Columbia University for the Germaine M. Speyer Scholarship or to the Shoah Foundation.
