
        MALOZEMOFF-Plato. A visionary leader of the American Mining Industry, Plato Malozemoff, passed away on Aug 8, 1997 at the age of 88 from congestive heart failure. For 30 years he lead Newmont Mining Corp as President and CEO and held many directorships. A man of high principles and steadfast dedication to his friends and family, he is survived by his loving wife Alexandra and children Alex and Irene, one granddaughter and 4 grandsons. The funeral service will be held at St. Sergius Russian Orthodox Church at the Tolstoy Foundation Center in Nyack, NY on Sunday Aug. 10 at 1PM. Driving directions can be obtained by calling 212986-7570. In lieu of flowers friends may make contributions to the Tolstoy Foundation Inc., 104 Lake Road, Valley Cottage, New York 10989 or the Boys' & Girls' Club of Greenwich, Inc., 4 Horseneck Lane, Greenwich, CT. 06830.
        MALOZEMOFF-Plato. The Board of Directors and staff of the Tolstoy Foundation, Inc., with deep sadness and regret announce the death of Plato Malozemoff, The Foundation's Chairman Emeritus and twice Chairman of the Board. The Tolstoy Foundation mourns the loss of a distinguished board member, loyal friend and generous benefactor whose steadfast devotion and service to the Foundation for over forty years will not be forgotten.
