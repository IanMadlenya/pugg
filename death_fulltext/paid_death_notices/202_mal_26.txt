
        ROMERO -- Jonathan Ed wards, 65, at home, on October 24, 2003 after a long courageous battle with Multiple System Atrophy. Descendant of early American theologian, Jonathan Edwards, he is survived by his wife, Elaine, sons Jonathan and Gregory, and granddaughter Juliana. Founder and CEO of Sugarland Oil Co. Former Chairman and board member of The Tuxedo Club and Trustee of Berkshire School. Served on the Vestry of St. Mary's in Tuxedo, Tuxedo Park, NY. Memorial service November 8 at 11 AM at St. Mary's. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the church in his memory.
