
        LAKE--Charles H., 81, died at home in Naples, FL on November 13. Loving husband, father, WWII PT Boat Commander, attorney, civic leader, athlete. CCNY, Cornell LLB, NYU LLM. Survived by his devoted wife of 49 years, Vivianne Orendain of Guadalajara, Mexico, and daughter, Vivianne of NYC. Memorial service in Naples, FL. Burial Arlington National Cemetery. Memorials: Mayo Foundation, Parkinson's Disease Research, 200 First St., SW, SI-9, Rochester, MN 55905.
