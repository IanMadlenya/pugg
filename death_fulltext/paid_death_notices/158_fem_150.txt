
        ABRAMS-Rose. The Officers, Management and Staff of Elderplan and Metropolitan Jewish Health System sadly mourn the passing of Rose Abrams, beloved mother and wife of esteemed Elderplan Board Member, Norman Abrams. May her legacy act as a source of inspiration to all those whom she touched. We extend our condolences and deepest sympathies to all the family and friends.
