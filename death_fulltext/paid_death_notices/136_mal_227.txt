
        ROSENTHAL-Morton. The Board of Trustees, Clergy and staff of Temple Beth El of Northern Westchester notes with profound sadness the death of Morton Rosenthal, a founder and past president of our congregation. We share in the grief of his wife Rosalind and his daughter Jane Goldman and their entire family. May they find comfort among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Chaim Stern, Sr. Rabbi Dana Anesi, Cantor David Thomas, Assistant Rabbi Gloria Meisel, President
