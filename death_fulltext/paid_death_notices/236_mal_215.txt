
        MARGOLIS--Seymour (Si). The Board of Governors and members of Montammy Golf Club express their deepest sorrow at the passing of their beloved member and extend their heartfelt sympathy to Janice and the entire family. Fred H. Weinshank, President
        $(6$)MARGOLIS--Seymour (Si). On September 20, 2006. Loving and beloved husband of Janice for sixty-two years, dearest father of Barbara (Peter) Brandenberg, and the late Wendy Margolis Smith. Devoted brother-in-law of Stephen Elstein, cherished grandfather, great grandfather, uncle, and dear friend to many. A gentle man of honor and dignity, with a delicious sense of humor. He will live in our hearts forever. Services private.
