Campbell, 1076 Madison at 81st St., Wednesday, May 28, 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to: Peritoneal Dialysis Dept., Mt. Sinai Hospital, 1 Gustave Levy Pl., NY, NY 10029.
