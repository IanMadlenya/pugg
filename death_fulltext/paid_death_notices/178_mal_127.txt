
        McCRACKEN-John Edward. July 28, 1914-November 3, 2001. Beloved husband of Jeanne M.S. McCracken. Devoted father of David J.B. McCracken. Princeton University '36, Yale Law School '39, The Union Club. Memorial service Fifth Ave. Presbyterian Church, 7 West 55 St. Thursday, November 15, 2001 at 11AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to his Church or Princeton UniversityClass of '36 Endowment Fund.
