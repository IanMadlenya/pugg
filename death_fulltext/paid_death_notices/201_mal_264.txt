
        BALL--John H. Died in his 83rd year. Son of the late Hattie and Nathan Ball. Cherished husband of the late Alice W. Ball. Adored father of Joan B. Joffe and Jean Ball Scheinert, father-in-law of Dean and loving grandfather of Andrew and Michael. Services will be private. In lieu of flowers contributions to Central Synagogue where he was honored to serve as president for 6 years or the Hebrew Home for the Aged, Riverdale would be appreciated.
        BALL--John. Central Synagogue mourns the loss of our respected and cherished past president, John Ball. He served our congregation as president with distinction from 1978 to 1985. We extend our condolences to his beloved daughters Joan Joffe, Jean A. Ball Scheinert and his entire family. May his memory be a blessing. Peter J. Rubinstein, Rabbi Alfred Youngwood, President
