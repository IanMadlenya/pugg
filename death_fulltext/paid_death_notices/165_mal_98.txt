
        LEVENTHAL-Adam J., 42. A sculptor, died September 29, 2000 in Los Angeles. He is survived by his wife, Cindy Kolodziejski, his daughter, Olivia Moon, his parents, David and Ann Z. Leventhal of Hartford, CT, a sister, Amy, two brothers, Max and Seth, and eight nieces and nephews. There was a memorial service in Los Angeles. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Las Familias del Pueblo, 307 E. 7 St., LA, CA 90014.
