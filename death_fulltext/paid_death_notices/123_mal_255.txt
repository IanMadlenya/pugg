
        BEYDA-Joseph. The American Institute for Psychoanalysis is deeply saddened by the passing of our friend and benefactor, Joseph Beyda. As a member of the Institute's Advisory Board, he touched and inspired all with his enthusiasm, generosity of spirit and mission to do good in the world. He will be greatly missed. Joann Gerardi, Ph.D., President Jeffrey Rubin, M.D., Dean
