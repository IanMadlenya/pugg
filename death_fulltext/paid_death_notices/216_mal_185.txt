
        ANNENBERG--Norman. Died January 8, 2005, aged 92. Harvard Law '35, Bronze Star hero, adored companion of Bernice Silverman, beloved friend, mentor and wise counselor to colleagues and clients alike. Till the end he was an active lawyer, world traveler, exceptional dancer and violinist in the Doctors' Orchestra. Great was his mind; greater was his heart. A man of unparalleled intelligence and generosity. We shall not see his like again. A memorial service will be held.
