
        GARCIA-Maria. Age 90. On May 1, 1999. She and her husband owned and operated El Charro Restaurant in Greenwich Village, N.Y. from 1948 until their retirement in 1967. She is survived by her husband, Menendo, son Tony, daughter Odulia Jacott, grandson Carlos Jacott, sister Loretta Trevino and brother Macario Garcia. Funeral private.
