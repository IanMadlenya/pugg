
        BERMAN--Herbert, 80. Died November 4. Publisher of the Brooklyn Graphic newspaper for 50 years. Husband to the late Phyllis. Adored by children Jonathan and Lisa, three stepchildren and seven grandchildren. He is survived by wife Joyce. His humor and personable nature made him legendary and much loved beyond the west Brooklyn community where he served innumerable charities and organizations. Memorial service Sunday, November 13, 3PM, at Weinstein, 1153 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn (718-377-4848).
