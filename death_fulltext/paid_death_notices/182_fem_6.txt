
        TRAINOR-Ellen Veronica, nee O'Donnell. On February 28, died peacefully in her sleep. Of Pompano Beach, FL. Born in Manhattan and resided in New York City and Hampton Bays, NY. Beloved wife of the late John J. Trainor, a Battalion Chief in the FDNY. Devoted mother of daughters Jane Ellen Gomes of Boynton Beach, FL., and Susan Mills of Delray Beach, FL. and son, John J. Trainor, Jr. of Glen Ridge, NJ. Adored grandmother of Matthew (Kim) and Alex Gomes; Brian Trainor; Leah and Samantha Mills. Much loved mother-in-law of Michael Gomes and Patricia Erhard Trainor. Dear sister of Anna Moloney. Her wit, style, sense of humor, determination, drive, compassion and unconditional love will be etched forever in the hearts of her family and friends. Wake and funeral services in Pompano Beach, FL. Reposing at Forest Lawn, 200 West Copans Road, Pompano Beach, FL. 33064, (954-784-4000). Mass on Monday, March 4 at 11:00AM at St. Gabriel's RC Church in Pompano Beach.
