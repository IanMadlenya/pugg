
        CHAPMAN-Edward Of Paterson, NJ on June 27, 1999. Beloved husband of Lillian (Slotkin). Father of Barbara Calderhead and her husband Richard, and Benson Chapman and his wife Marcia. Grandfather of Emily, Maxine DellaVecchia and her husband Robert, and Steven. Brother of Mildred and Anita. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Retired as proprietor of Chapman's Sporting Goods in Paterson. Former president of Lower Main Street Merchants Association. Member of Temple Emanuel, Paterson; Veritans Club; and YM-YWHA. Memorial donations may be made to the Veritans Club or Temple Emanuel.
