
        GUADAGNO--Rose, June 14, 2004. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Charles Guadagno. Loving mother of Therese and her son-in-law David Megley. Proud grandmother of Catherine Rose Megley. Rose was President of the Resident's Council at Mary Manning Walsh for several years. In her memory donations may be made to the Mary Manning Walsh Home, 1339 York Ave, New York 10021.
