
        FEIN--Gertrude. On August 4, 2006 at the age of 90. In accordance with her wishes, her body was donated to the Anatomical Program, New Jersey Medical School. Remembered for her wit and directness, a champion player of tennis, Duplicate Bridge and The New York Times crossword puzzles. She will be missed. Preceded in death by her husband of 47 years, Mitchell Fein on August 12, 2001 and survived by a son David of NJ, a son Jeremy and stepdaughter Lisa, of Denver & two grandchildren, Max and Molly of LA.
