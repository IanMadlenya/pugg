
        NICHOLAS-Frederick S. Died peacefully January 30, 2001 in Bryn Mawr, PA, aged 90. Born in Syosset, NY, the son of Dorothy and Harry Nicholas. Educated at St. Paul's School, Concord, NH and Harvard University. Mr. Nicholas's career was in the textile industry. In 1942, he joined the Navy and was a Lieutenant Commander. His wife of 62 years, Martha Milliken Nicholas, died in 1995. He is survived by four children: Frederick S. Nicholas, Jr., of Stonington, CT; Mrs. Douglas G. Lovell of Newtown Square, PA; Mrs. Charles J. Hatfield of Princeton, NJ; and Anthony P. Nicholas, also of Princeton, 11 grandchildren and 10 great-grandchildren. Mr. Nicholas, who was President and Master of Fox Hounds for the Radnor Hunt Club will be remembered for his storytelling and sense of humor. Memorial Service will be held at 12 noon on Wednesday, February 14 at St. Francis-in-theFields, Sugartown Road, Malvern, PA. Please send any contributions to The Animal Hospital of Chester Co., 1353 Pottstown Pike, West Chester, PA. 19382.
