
        GRIER-Laurabelle (nee Robinson). After an extended illness, met with great bravery and courage, had a peaceful death on August 23, 1997. Widow of Robert S. Grier, mother of Lucy Feller and Patricia Weinberg, sister of Constance Wineman of Bloomfield Hills, Mi., sister-in-law of Marjorie Wazer and grandmother of Amy and Wendy Feller, Lisa Tarbox and Neil Weinberg, great grandmother of Haley Elizabeth Tarbox. She was a great inspiriation to us all and will always be remembered for her kindness, warmth and graciousness. We will miss her. Memorial service is private.
