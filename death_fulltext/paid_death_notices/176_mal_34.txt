
        ACHACOSO-Iluminado, M.D., 79, on August 22, 2001 in San Jose, CA. Born in Botolan, Zambales, in the Philippines, he was a graduate of the University of the Philippines College of Medicine (1943). He and his wife Aurora (nee Valdez) raised their eight children in St. Catherine of Genoa R.C. parish in Brooklyn, New York. Services were held in Fremont, CA.
