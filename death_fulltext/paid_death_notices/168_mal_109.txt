
        CRUSO-Saul. Passed away on January 7th at the advanced age of 104. Blessed by good looks and exemplary talents, he was a Broadway actor in the early 20's, & subsequently a brilliant political cartoonist for Hearst and other newspapers. He is survived by stepdaughter Roberta Bresnick, nephews Lawrence and Norman Cruso, nieces Miriam Cruso and Cecelia Reich. Also nephew Miron Nacmanie. Saul was loved and will be greatly missed.
