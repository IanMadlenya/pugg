
        BRODIE--Sheldon, 77, died peacefully in FL after a long illness. He was a Professor of Clinical Dermatology at NYU Medical School and Chief of Service, Skin & Cancer Unit where he was an attending physician for 40 years. He had a private practice in Beth Page, LI. He loved his work and his patients loved him. He is survived by his wife of 48 years, Charlotte and his children Barnett and Martha and her husband Harvey Sepler, grandchildren Aubree and Danny and his sister Barbara Klebanow. He was a paradigm of bravery, tenacity and dignity. We love you and you are an inspiration to us.
