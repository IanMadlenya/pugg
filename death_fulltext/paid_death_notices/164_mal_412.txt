
        SNYDER-Edward Philip. Of Southport, CT, died September 23, 2000, in his home. Born in 1919, he graduated from the Taft School where he long served as a Trustee. He was in the Class of '42 at Yale and served with distinction during WW II in the U.S. Navy in both the North and South Pacific Theaters, attaining the rank of Lt. Commander. Mr. Snyder was a retired Senior Partner with Moore & Munger in New York City. He founded the Carlos Otis Medical Clinic at Stratton Mountain where he served as a Director. A lifelong sailor, he was a member of the New York Yacht Club and Cruising Club of America in addition to being a member and past Commodore of both the Storm Trysail and Pequot Yacht Clubs. Survivors include his wife, Frances, three children, seven grandchildren; his brothers Peter and Frank and their children. A memorial service will be held Tuesday, September 26, 2000, at 11 AM at the Southport Congregational Church, 524 Pequot Ave., Southport, CT. The family requests in lieu of flowers that contributions be made to the Southport Congregational Church, the Carlos Otis Medical Clinic, Stratton Mountain, VT, or Norwalk Hospital, Maple St., Norwalk, CT.
