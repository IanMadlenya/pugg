
        STERNBERG-Leo. Artist. On February 10, 1998 in Great Barrington, MA. Husband of Mary, father of Amy Sternberg Mann and Daniel Sternberg, father-in-law of Jeffrey Mann and Deborah Cooper Sternberg, grandfather of Jessica Mann, Rachel and Seth Rosan and Julia and Samuel Sternberg. Services at Finnerty and Stevens, 426 Main Street, Great Barrington, MA. on Sunday, February 15, 1998, at 1 P.M. Family will meet friends at the home of Amy and Jeffrey Mann, 75 East End Avenue, N.Y., evenings Monday-Wednesday. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the National Yiddish Book Center, 1021 West Street, Amherst, MA. 01002. We loved him dearly and we will miss him.
