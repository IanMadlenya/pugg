
        CAMPBELL--Dr. Robert G. Leading research doctor, former executive vice president and medical director of St. Luke's-Roosevelt, Trustee of the Mary Imogene Bassett Hospital, Cooperstown, NY, and Professor Emeritus of Medicine at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons, died Friday, August 18. He was Professor of Medicine at University of Rochester School of Medicine and Dentistry from 1981-1985, at Cornell University Medical College from 1985-1993, and at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons from 1993-2003. Dr. Campbell is remembered by family, patients, colleagues and students as a thoughtful, rigorous teacher, a talented researcher and a compassionate doctor. He is survived by his wife, Barbara Yoder Campbell of Essex, CT, his three children, Robert, Susan and William, four grandchildren, and three brothers, Frederick, Douglas and Colin. A memorial service will be held 1 pm, Friday, August 25, at The First Congregational Church, Old Lyme, CT.
