
        SILBERMINTZ-Seymour. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Seymour Silbermintz, a creative teacher of Jewish Music. As conductor of our Shirah Choir, he inspired our congregation with his liturgical arrangements and his sincerity in prayer. Deepest sympathy to his beloved wife, Elaine, children Shelley and Jeff Daube, Tobe Goldfinger, Naomi Silbermintz, and the entire family. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Canton Ari Klein Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
