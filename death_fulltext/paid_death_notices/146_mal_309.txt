
        AARONSON-Howard O. Beloved husband, father, grandfather and brother. Retired partner from Jaros, Baum and Bolles. Volunteer at the Metropolitan Museum. Died peacefully March 14, 1999, at Suburban Hospital in Bethesda, MD. He will be remembered by all for his kindness and gentleness. Services will be held at Riverside Chapel, 76 St and Amsterdam Avenue, Thursday, March 18th at 12:30PM.
