
        SILVERSTEIN--Peter Alan, died peacefully at home in the company of his devoted family on May 24. Beloved husband for 45 years of Rosalind. Loving father of Michele and her husband Richard Furman, Keith and his wife Shari. Grandfather of Matthew, Samantha and Gabriella. Cherished son of Libby and the late Paul C., devoted brother of Bette Marks and her husband George. A practicing attorney for 40 years, he was dedicated to his clients and friends. Loved by all of us, he will be cherished forever.
