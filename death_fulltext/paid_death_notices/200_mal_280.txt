
        HIRSCHBERG--Robert S. On September 25. Son of the late Samuel and Betty. Survived by his sister, Judith (Seymour) Liss. His niece and nephew Jan Liss (Jim Coddington) and Jonathan (Ann) Liss. Great-nephew Jack Wolfe Liss. Friend to so many. He is also fondly remembered by Marian Gibson, missed by his loyal pal Duffy.
