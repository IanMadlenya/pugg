
        SAMUELS-Kathryn Morris, 39. Of Atlantic Beach, NY, died Friday, Dec. 6, 2002 at Lenox Hill Hospital after a sudden illness. KT was a graduate of Tulane University. She was the founding partner of Morris & Partners, a NYbased marketing communications firm. She is survived by her husband Steven, their two year old son Aiden, stepdaughter Lisa, parents Susan and Richard Morris, sister and brother-in-law Leslie and Robert Godwin, brothers-inlaw, sister-in-law, mother-inlaw, aunts, uncles, cousins, nephews, nieces, a grandniece, and numerous friends and associates. ''Her quiet eyelids closed-she had another morn than ours.''
