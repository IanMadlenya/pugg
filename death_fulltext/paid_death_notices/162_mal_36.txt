
        COOPER-Dr. Mark Dale, passed away on Thursday, June 29, 2000. He was born in Tulsa, OK on August 23, 1959. Mark attended the University of Oklahoma, received his medical degree from Washington University Medical School at St. Louis and completed his residency in psychiatry at Mt. Sinai Medical College in NY. He is survived by longtime partner and loving families. Goodbye to most loving, kind, witty and generous soul we have had the honor of having known & been loved by. Memorial service will be held at Marble Collegiate, 29th and 5th Avenue, July 15, 11 AM, Poling Chapel.
