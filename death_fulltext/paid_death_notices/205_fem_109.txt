
        CARING--Lillian Cott. 85, Ph.D., N.Y.U.; Founders Day Award, PsyChi and Pi Lambda Theta Honor Societies; School Psychologist, East Meadow Public Schools; private practice in Rockville Centre and Asharoken, Long Island, NY; listed in Who's Who in American Women. Devoted wife of the late Alfred B. Caring, inspiring mother, mother-in-law, and grandmother to M'Lou Caring, Ph.D., Jan Roggeveen, P.E., Sofia - Jeanne, and Joanne Caring, M.D., Steven Lobel, Alex. Services at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Tuesday, February 17th at 9:30 AM. In lieu of flowers, donations in her memory may be made to NARAL.
