
        SPIER--Larry. 73, on June 30, 2003. Beloved husband of Dorothy (deceased). Devoted father of Mark and Roberta, father-in-law of Lori and Wayne, loving grandfather of Matthew, Benjamin, Danielle, and Jeffrey. Cherished brother and brother-in-law. Owner and president of the Memory Lane Music Group encompassing the publishing firm of Larry Spier, Inc., and Memory Lane Music. Mr. Spier had been at the helm of the company since 1967, and was a pioneer in catalogue administration, entering into one of the first administrative deals in 1975. Relatives and friends are invited to services Thursday, July 3, 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81st St. The family shall return to the home of the deceased. At the families request, gifts in his memory may be made to ARPKD/ CHF Alliance, PO Box 70, Kirkwood, PA 17536. >>AD#
