
        BALLANTYNE-Marion C. Died peacefully at home on February 15 in her 94th year. Beloved widow of the late Jacques C. Nordeman and the late John J. Ballantyne. Loving mother of Mary Ann and Jacques Nordeman, devoted grandmother of Landon, John and Eliza. Private services will be held for the immediate family. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue, Friday, February 25, 6-8PM. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory can be made to the Central Park Conservancy, 14 East 60 Street, 8th floor, NY, NY 10022, a park which Marion loved dearly.
