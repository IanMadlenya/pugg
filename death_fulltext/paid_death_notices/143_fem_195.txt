
        NYSWANDER-Dorothy. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with the leadership of its parent company, Continuum Health Partners, Inc., and with the staff of the hospital's Methadone Maintenance Treatment Program (MMTP) in acknowledging the passing of Dr. Dorothy Nyswander. Dr. Nyswander was one of the most highly respected health educators in the world and the beloved mother of our late colleague and friend, Dr. Marie Nyswander, a pioneer in the use of methadone for the treatment of heroin addiction. To her innumerable colleagues, students, friends and admirers around the world, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Robert G. Newman, M.D., Pres. Continuum Health Partners Addie Corradi, Admin. Dir., MMTP Beverly L. Richman, M.D., Chief of Medical Services, MMTP
