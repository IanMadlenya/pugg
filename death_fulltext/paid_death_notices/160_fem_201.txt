
        ULMER-Janet Lees, 74, died Easter Sunday following a fourmonth battle with brain cancer. Mrs. Ulmer of Vero Beach, Florida & Ocean Grove, New Jersey was predeceased by her husband of 50 years, Kenneth Marvin Ulmer in September 1998. Mrs. Ulmer graduated from East Orange High School and attended the University of Southern California. A long time resident of New Providence, New Jersey, Mrs. Ulmer taught at the Summit Speech School and was a volunteer at the Overlook Hospital. In her retirement, she was a volunteer in the nursery at Indian River Memorial Hospital in Vero Beach. Mrs Ulmer had been both a summer and year-round resident of Ocean Grove for 50 years, and had been an active volunteer with the Women's Auxiliary. Janet is survived by her three children, Kevin Ulmer of Cohasset, Massachusetts; Scott Ulmer of Atlanta, Georgia; and Kathy Stilin of Lewisburg, Pennsylvania, and five grandchildren: Justin, Ashley, Kimberly, Ethan and Wesley. A memorial service will be held at 11 A.M. on Saturday, May 27th at the New Providence United Methodist Church, 1441 Springfield Avenue, New Providence, N.J. In lieu of flowers, please make a donation to your favorite charity.
