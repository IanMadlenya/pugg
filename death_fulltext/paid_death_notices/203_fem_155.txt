
        BLANK--Dr. Blanche Davis. Yeshiva University mourns the passing of a respected educator, social scientist, and author. Dr. Blank served as vice president for academic affairs at YU from 1977 until 1981, the first woman in Yeshiva's history to occupy a major administrative post. She served with distinction as a lecturer, dean and acting president at Hunter College. We extend condolences to her husband, Joseph S., her daughters, Laura, Barbara and Alice, and her grandchildren. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Norman Lamm, Chancellor Ronald P. Stanton, Chairman Board of Trustees
        BLANK--Blanche Davis. The City University of New York deeply mourns the death of Dr. Blanche Davis Blank, an alumna of Hunter College who served with distinction as its acting president. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to her family. Benno C. Schmidt, Jr., Chairman, Board of Trustees Matthew Goldstein, Chancellor
