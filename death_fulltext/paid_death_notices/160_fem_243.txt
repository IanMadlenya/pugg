
        BRUCKMAN-Asta Lillian. Passed away quietly at home in Franklin Square, Long Island, New York in the morning of May 30, 2000. Loving wife of Morris Bruckman, who passed away on October 25, 1995. Born Asta Lilli Guth in Freistadt Danzig on January 8, 1914. Fled Europe in 1939 with her mother and sister; resided New York City; married Morris 1940; son Neal born 1943; daughter Hazel born 1946; moved to Franklin Square, L.I. in 1951; graduated Queens College Summa Cum Laude 1976; teacher in Valley Stream, Franklin Square and Garden City School Districts. Survived by sister Helga Kanarek (nee Guth), son Neal, daughter Hazel Fershleiser, grandchildren Christopher, Nicholas and Rachel. Funeral services at 1 PM, Wednesday May 31 at I.J. Morris Funeral Home, 46 Greenwich Street, Hempstead, New York.
