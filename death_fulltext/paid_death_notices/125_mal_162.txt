
        GROSSMAN-Neal Lawrence, died peacefully after a long illness on Friday, June 6, 1997, at Columbia Presbyterian Hospital, Manhattan. Born in Boston and a former resident of Marblehead, Mass., London and Los Angeles, he was 50 years old. He is mourned and missed by his many friends on both sides of the Atlantic, and by his brother, Lloyd; sister-in-law, Debbie, and beloved nieces, Florence and Constance, all resident in London.
