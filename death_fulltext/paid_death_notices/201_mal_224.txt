
        SCHWARTZBERG--Neil. On October 21, 2003 at the age of 61 our much loved Neil lost his heroic battle to overcome a prolonged illness. Beloved brother of Paul J. Sutton and Nancy Miller. Cherished uncle of Daniel and Lily Sutton. A gentle soul who had a great capacity to accept whatever life and nature dealt him, Neil devoted most of his professional life in the service of children and young adults with special needs. The family requests that contributions in Neil's memory be made to The Jewish Board of Family and Children Services, 120 West 57th St, NY NY 10019, Attn: Jane Berenbeim, whose work he admired and supported. A memorial service will take place at Noon, Sunday October 26, at Mt. Pleasant Cemetery, Hawthorne, NY.
