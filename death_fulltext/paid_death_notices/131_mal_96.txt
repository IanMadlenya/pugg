
        ISENBERG - Charles. Professor Charles Isenberg died of cancer at his home in Milford, CT on Dec. 4, at the age of 53. He is survived by his wife, Rhea Paul; children, William, Martin and Aviva; parents, Marcia and Arnold Isenberg of Pompano, FL; and brother, David of Fairfield, CT. In lieu of flowers, the family requests that donations be sent to the Charles Isenberg Memorial Fund at Reed College, Portland, OR 97202.
