
        BLACK-Gayle, Ph.D (nee Gayle Marks). Cherished mother, devoted wife, beloved sister and dear beautiful friend to many. She died as she lived-with courage, dignity and grace surrounded by loving family and friends. Gayle will be missed by her husband Shelly Marks, daughter Margot Black and brother Joel Black as well as by many other loving family members and friends. She was the vibrant daughter of the late Sylvia and Nathan Black. Gayle Black, Ph.D was author of the ''Sun Sign Diet'' book as well as a private practice nutritionist and president of the Cancer Survival Center in New York City. She was an active participant in the SHARE breast cancer network and a member of Temple Shaaray Tefila. Services will be held Thursday, February 26th at 9:30AM at Temple Shaaray Tefila, 250 East 79th Street, in New York City.
        BLACK-Gayle (Marks). Temple Shaaray Tefila is grieved to record the passing of its devoted member. To her husband Shelly, daughter Margot, and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. Rabbi Harvey M. Tattelbaum Rabbi Melinda Panken Ellen R. Saxl, President
