
        VAN PELT-Charles Bingham Penrose. Died on January 2, 2003, at Palm Beach, FL. Born May 2, 1922 in Philadelphia. He was predeceased in death by his father Andrew Van Pelt, his mother Sarah Hannah Boles Penrose Van Pelt and his sister Sarah Hannah Boles Penrose Van Pelt. He was graduated from Groton School, Harvard University and the University of Pennsylvania Law School. He was a veteran of World War II, serving in the U.S. Army Military Intelligence in the Pacific. Survived by his wife Jane Eger Van Pelt and his daughter Jullet Evelyn Eger Van Pelt and his children Peter Turner Van Pelt, Abby Ann Van Pelt and Guy Fairfax Cary Van Pelt. Also survived by two sisters Katharine Drexel Van Pelt Beaver and Abby Ann Van Pelt Griscom, niece Helen Cutting Pilkington and her children Charles Fulton Pilkington, Justine Catherine Pilkington and Elizabeth Cutting Pilkington. Funeral services will be conducted Saturday, January 11, at 11AM, at the Church of the Redeemer, Bryn Mawr, PA, by The Reverend Canon Gary Hall and The Reverend Mr. Timothy Pickering. In lieu of flowers memorial contributions may be sent to St. Christopher's Hospital for Children, Philadelphia, PA
