
        KELLY-Regina G. Age 95 of Brick, N.J. died on October 25. She was born in Brooklyn, NY. Lived in Staten Island, NY before moving to Brick two years ago. She was employed for 20 years as a Receptionist with the Holy Family Hospital in Brooklyn, retiring 14 years ago. Predeceased by her beloved husband Bernard in 1960. Survived by son Bernard L. Kelly, and daughters Regina Baumann of Delaware, Anne Dennehy of Cranbury, NJ and Maureen Janeski of Glen Ellen, IL & 18 grandchildren & 13 great grandchildren. Visiting on Sunday, October 28 1-5 PM at O'Brien Funeral Home, 505 Burnt Tavern Road at Route 70, Brick, NJ. Funeral Mass Monday at 9:30 AM at St. Martha's R.C. Church in Point Pleasant NJ. Burial at Holy Cross Cemetery, Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers, donations to United Cerebral Palsy, 1660 L St NW, Suite 700, Washington, DC 20036.
