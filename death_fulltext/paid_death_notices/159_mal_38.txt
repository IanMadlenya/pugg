
        WYKER-Joseph. On April 2, 2000. Beloved husband of Lynn. Also survived by many nieces, nephews, grand nieces and grand nephews. Mr. Wyker was cited for bravery and saving many lives during the landing at Normandy. He will be sorely missed by all. Services at Ferncliff Mausoleum, Ardsley, NY, Tuesday, 3:15 PM.
        WYKER-Joseph. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Joseph Wyker. To his family and loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
