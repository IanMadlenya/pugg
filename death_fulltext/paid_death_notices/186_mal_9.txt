
        VISCONTI-Martin. 61, of East Windsor, NJ died Friday June 28, 2002 at Morristown Memorial Hospital, Morristown, NJ. He was born in Bronx, NY and lived in South Brunswick, NJ for 10 years before moving to East Windsor 19 years ago. He was a Senior Vice President with Veronis, Suhler & Stevenson Partners LLC in New York City for the past 13 years. He is survived by his wife Linda (nee Fay) Visconti; one son, Andrew Turff of Atlanta, GA; three daughters, Hope Peters of Dayton, NJ, Robyn Bohall of East Windsor, NJ, Kelly Turff of Tucson, AZ; one sister, Judy Sumin of New York City; two brothers, Michael Visconti and Gary Visconti, both of Katonah, NY; five grandchildren, Brett, Samantha and Jonathan Peters & Sydney and Katelyn Bohall. Donations to Multiple Myeloma Foundation, local chapters. The family is sitting shiva at their home in East Windsor until Friday. For information, Bloomfield - Cooper Jewish Chapels, Manalapan Twp.
