
        McGOVERN-John H., MD. Retired Professor of Surgery, Department of Urology, New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center and past President of the American Urological Association, died on May 28, 2001, in Sarasota, FL. Born in Bayonne, NJ, Dr. McGovern built a successful urology practice in Manhattan before retiring to Long Boat Key, Fl in 2000. Dr. McGovern was a Merchant Marine veteran of WWII, a graduate of Columbia University and Downstate Medical College. He was also a member of the prestigious American Association of Genito-Urinary Surgeons. He is survived by his wife, Mary Alice; four sons, John H. Jr., Esq. of Brooklyn, NY; Dr. Robert P. of Vero Beach, FL; Ward M. of Old Orchard Beach, ME and Raymond F. of Little Ferry, NJ; two sisters, Anne Rumage of Ortley Beach, NJ; and Katherine Kalinak of Linden, NJ and four grandchildren. A memorial Mass will be held in Manhattan at St. Ignatius Loyola on Monday, June 11, 2001 at 11 AM. In lieu of flowers, memorial donations may be made to the James Buchanan Brady Foundation c/o Darracott Vaughn, MD, 525 East 68th St., New York, NY 10021.
