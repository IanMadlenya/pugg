
        WOODBURY--George Francis. Beloved father of George, Gail and Gary (Joan). Beloved grandfather of Eric, Christopher and Valerie. Beloved brother of Carl (Joan) and Britta (Rev. Harold Kuehne). George passed on March 13, 2006. Viewing services will be held at Andrew Torregrossa Funeral Home, 2265 Flatbush Avenue, Brooklyn, New York 11234 on Wednesday, March 15, 2-5 and 7-9 PM. Also Thursday, March 16, 11 AM-1 PM. George F. Woodbury was a faithful employee of The New York Times for 40 years. He was a decorated World War II veteran. He will be sorely missed by family and friends.
