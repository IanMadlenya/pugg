
        KROLL-Douglas. The Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services and its Youth Counseling League Division note with great sadness the passing of Douglas Kroll, who died after a long illness. Doug was a gifted social worker whose optimism, compassion and empathy were an inspiration to all who knew him. He touched many lives and will truly be missed. Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D. Executive Vice President and CEO Paul Levine, C.S.W. Associate Executive VP Joan Adams, Director, YCL
