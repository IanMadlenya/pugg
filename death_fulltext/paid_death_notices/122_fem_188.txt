
        PECHENIK-Mamie. Born in Brooklyn 1896, lived for the last 35 years in Greenwich Village where she gained recognition as an artist and dressmaker. Mother of six, Harold, Irene Weinberg, Harriet Miller, Sylvia Schulman and the late Albert and Martin. Grandmother of twenty-two, great grandmother of nineteen. Devoted her life to love and support of family. Cherished by everyone who knew her. Services today 12 Noon, Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76 Street and Amsterdam Ave. Burial, Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, N.Y. immediately after service.
