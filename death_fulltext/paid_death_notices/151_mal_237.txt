
        CHESTER-John. The Medical Staff, Board of Directors, and Administration of Brookhaven Memorial Hospital Medical Center mourn the passing of our dear and beloved colleague and friend, John Chester. A member of the Board of Directors, his wise counsel and concern for the hospital will be deeply missed. The Hospital and all affiliated with it extend their sincerest condolences to his wife and family. Marion Bergman, M.D. President, Medical Staff Fredric G. Rose Chairman, Board of Directors Thomas Ockers President/CEO
