
        OLIVER--John Lee. Died peacefully at home in East Hampton on February 28, 2005 at the age of 67 after a long battle with Parkinson's disease. Survived by Anne, his loving wife of 39 years, daughter Vicky Oliver, son-inlaw Gordon Cohen, and mother Janice Sullivan. A 1959 graduate of Brown University he spent 30 plus years in advertising at various components of the Interpublic Group of Companies. He was a longtime member of the Devon Yacht Club, Amagansett and the University Club, Manhattan. Memorial contributions to Brown University or Parkinson's Disease Foundation. Memorial gathering in East Hampton in the spring.
