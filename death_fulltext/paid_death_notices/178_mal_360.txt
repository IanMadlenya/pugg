
        CLEARY-Kevin F. Esq., 38 died tragically on September 11, 2001 as a result of the World Trade Center disaster. He was a Vice President at Euro Brokers Maxcor Inc. Kevin graduated from UW Madison and Fordham School of Law and studied acting with Lily Lodge at the Actor's Conservatory, Stella Adler and the Michael Howard Studios. Kevin is survived by his mother Elizabeth, sisters Cathleen, Maureen and Patricia, his brothers Thomas, Michael and Chris, his in-laws Gina, Christine and Paul and nine nieces and nephews. His father Thomas and brother Brian predeceased him. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Community Funds Inc. and sent to Mr. Owen Wincig, Esq. 574 Fifth Avenue, New York, NY 10036. Celebration of Life Mass was celebrated on September 27 at St. Ignatius Loyola, NY, NY. Private burial was held November 26 at Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
