
        FEATHERSTON-Anthony Godfrey, Jr. January 10th, 1998. Of Newton, Massachusetts, formerly of New York City. Beloved father of Anthony G., IV, Jan-Marc, and Carel Pieter. Grandfather of Riley. Brother of Elizabeth A. Featherston-Quick of Edgartown, Mass. Son of the late Dr. Anthony G. and Mabel (McKissack) Featherston. Also survived by his former wife and life long friend Cornelia J. van Kempen-Featherston.
