
        POWELL-Thomas R. On November 30, 1997. Former member, New York City Opera Company. Twenty-eight years, retired member Metropolitan Opera Company. Devoted husband of Idessa and father of Idessa, Jr., and Thomas, Jr. Ten year Met chorus delegate whose intelligent advisory role model inspired colleagues. A beloved, delightful person to his family, associates and friends. He will be greatly missed. He will always be with us.
