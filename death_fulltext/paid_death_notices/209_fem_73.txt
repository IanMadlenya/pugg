
        ROOT--Mary Bidwell (Molly). 86, of College Hill, Clinton died peacefully at home with her family on June 10, 2004. She was born in New Haven, Ct on December 13, 1917, the daughter of the late Anna Cabot Almy and Percy Wells Bidwell. Molly graduated from Radcliffe College and received a Masters Degree in Government from Columbia University. She was married to the late Elihu Root III. She is survived by a son David Root of Clinton; two daughters, Carolyn Root of Durham, NC and Alice Root of Vernon Center; four grandchildren, Hazel Bogan Nichols, Elihu Root Bogan, Rosalind Bogan and Sophie Root; a brother John Bidwell and two sisters Anne Minafra and Charlotte Ryder; and predeceased by a brother Samuel Bidwell. A Memorial Service will be held at 11am on June 14, 2004 at the Hamilton College Chapel, Clinton. Donations may be made to the Community Foundation of Herkimer and Oneida Counties Inc., 1222 State Street, Utica, New York 13502.
