
        MURDOCH--David A. Age 55, passed away October 15, 2005 at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital with his loving companion Jennifer Wortham by his side. A graduate of Pittsburgh Law School and former CFO of Caron Foundation. Children are Ben and Sarah; brother Chip Murdoch; sister-in-law Debbie. A memorial service will be held Saturday, October 22 at 2 PM at Caron Foundation, in Warnersville, PA. For information call 800-678-2332. Donations can be made to the Caron Foundation or to the Natural Resources Defense Council.
