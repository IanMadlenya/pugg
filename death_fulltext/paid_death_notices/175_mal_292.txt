
        KUNZ-Donald, Passed August 24, 2001. The Administration and Faculty of The Cooper Union for the Advancement of Science and Art mourn the death of Professor Donald Kunz. Serving the Cooper Union with distinction for 33 years, he enriched the lives of generations of talented students and contributed immensely to the teaching and learning of graphic design. A memorial Mass is scheduled for 10am, Thursday, August 30 at the Church of St. Francis Xavier in Manhattan. Faculty, Staff & Administration The Cooper Union
