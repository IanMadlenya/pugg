
        ZOULLAS-Louiza. On March 23, 1998 in Lausanne, Switzerland. Widow of Sophocles. Beloved mother of Catia Chapin and Nicholas Zoullas. Adored grandmother of Nicholas and Liza Mortimer, and Sophocles and Alexis Zoullas and great-grandmother of Sofia Zoullas. She was co-founder of St. Michael's Home for the Aged in New York and of the Entre-Aide Hellenique in Lausanne. Contributions in her memory may be made to St. Andrew's Dune Church, P.O Box 1245, Southampton, NY 11969.
