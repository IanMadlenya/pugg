
        JOSEPH--Jim. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Jim Joseph, a cherished friend and a leader in Jewish education. Working with us to create The Partnership for Excellence in Jewish Education, Jim will long be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathies to the entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        JOSEPH--Jim. Jewish Life Network/Steinhardt Foundation mourns the untimely death of our friend and partner, Jim Joseph. Jim was a pioneering philanthropist committed to helping the entire Jewish people (clal yisrael), and a fervent proponent of the primacy of Jewish education to assure the future. He will be missed. Michael Steinhardt, Chairman Rabbi Yitz Greenberg, President Rabbi David Gedzelman, Executive Director
