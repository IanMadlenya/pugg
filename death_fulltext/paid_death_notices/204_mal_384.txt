
        CONWAY--John Phillips, 19, of Greenwich, CT. Died on January 29, 2004 in Nashville, TN where he was a sophomore at the Vanderbilt University School of Engineering. He died from a sudden fatal heart arrhythmia while visiting a professor. Beloved son of John B. and Patricia Ranlet Conway. Adored brother of Benjamin B. and Patricia Laura Conway. Devoted grandson of Robert B. and Patricia P. Silleck. John was a graduate of Brunswick School in Greenwich., CT and Deerfield Academy in Deerfield, MA. A memorial service was held for his many friends and classmates at the Benton Chapel at Vanderbilt University on January 29, 2004. Another memorial service will be held at Deerfield Academy on Saturday, February 21, 2004 at 11:00 AM. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to Deerfield Academy.
