
        FREEZER-Jeanette. Park East Synagogue mourns the passing of Jeanette Freezer, devoted Member, past President of its Sisterhood and Women's Division Sponsor, who with her late husband, Irving, provided leadership to our Congregation. To her son Jacob, our dedicated Member, and the entire family, our condolences. Rabbi Arthur Schneier Michael Scharf, President Jerry Forst, Vice President Jack Rosen, Vice President
        FREEZER-Jeanette. Sisterhood of Park East Synagogue sadly mourns the passing of our beloved Honorary President and longtime Board Member, Jeanette Freezer. Her devotion, wisdom and elegance will be missed. Heartfelt condolences to her son Jacob and to all members of the bereaved family. Beverly Greenberg, President Polly Etkind-Hochberg, Chairman of the Board
        FREEZER-Jeanette. Park East Synagogue Men's Club records the passing of Jeanette Freezer, beloved mother of our Vice President, Jacob Freezer. Deepest sympathy to the bereaved family. Dr. Allan Goldberg, President Herman Hochberg, Chairman
