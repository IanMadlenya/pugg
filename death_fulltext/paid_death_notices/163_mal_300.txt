
        SUGARMAN-Morton. July 6, 1920August 17, 2000. World War II veteran. His wife, Lydia, children, Rob and his wife Christine, Nancy, John and grandson Keith will miss this unique, non-conforming, humorous bike rider, world traveler, nature lover and avid reader with a mind like a sponge. Memorial service at Oyster Bay Funeral Home, 261 South St, Oyster Bay, Sunday, August 27, at Noon.
