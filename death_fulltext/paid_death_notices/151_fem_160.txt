
        SAIDENBERG-Eleanore B. August 14, 1999, at age 88. Founder of Saidenberg Galleries. Beloved wife of the late Daniel, loving mother of Lawrence and Robert. Adored grandmother of Andrew, Leila, Steven, Richard, Jocelyn, Marc, Julie, Katherine, and Douglas. Cherished great grandmother of six. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St, Thursday, August 19th at 7:30 P.M. Interment private.
        SAIDENBERG - Eleanore. The Juilliard School community deeply mourns the death of Eleanore Saidenberg. A person of extraordinary intellect, grace and generosity, she and her husband Daniel greatly enhanced many programs at Juilliard through their vision and continuing support. We send our heartfelt condolences to her sons, Larry and Bob, and all the members of her family. Her presence at the School will be missed by us all. Mary Rodgers Guettel, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
