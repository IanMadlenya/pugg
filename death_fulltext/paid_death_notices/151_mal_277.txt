
        BRAVERMAN-Gabriel. Age 91. On August 24, 1999 in Philadelphia, PA. Violist with the Philadelphia Orchestra for 35 years. Survived by his wife Shirley, sister Bella Bookbinder, daughters and sonsin-law Bonny and Robert Israeloff, Stephanie and Michael Applebaum, four grandchildren and four great grandchildren. He will be sorely missed.
