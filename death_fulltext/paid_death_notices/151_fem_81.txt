
        LEMBERSKY - Ethel (Evelyn). Loving wife of the late Max Lembersky and the late Julius Horwitz. Loving mother of Sandra Horwitz Assael, mother-in-law of Isaac Assael and the late Leonard J. Grossman. Loving sister of Ed Tashman. After 92 years, she passed away peacefully . She will be missed by all who knew her.
