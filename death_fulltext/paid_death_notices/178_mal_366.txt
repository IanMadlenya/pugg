
        ROSS-James O. On November 24, 2001, of Thornton, PA, formerly of North Bergen, NJ. Beloved husband of Cindy. Devoted father of Dana and Meredith. Son of Agnes and the late Honorable Wilbur L. Ross, and brother of Karen Ross Tanzola, and of Wilbur L. Ross Jr. After graduating from Phillips Academy, Andover, MA, and receiving both AB and MBA degrees from the University of Rochester, he had a wonderful career in investments, and served as an Elder of his church. His funeral service will take place at Concord Liberty Presbyterian Church, 256 Bethel Rd, Concordville, PA, on Wednesday, November 28, at 10 AM.
