
        WHITMAN-The Honorable Charles S., Jr. The Board of Managers and the members of the St. Nicholas Society of the City of New York records with sorrow the death of their member and Past President, the Honorable Charles S. Whitman, Jr. We extend condolences to his wife Janet and their children. Samuel D. Williams, President
        WHITMAN-The Hon. Charles S. Jr. Sons of the Revolution in the State of New York notes with sorrow the death of Charles S. Whitman, Jr., a Life Member and Past President of this Society. He served with devotion on the Board of Managers and held many other vital committee positions. Our heartfelt condolences to his family. Laurence S. Simpson, D.D.S. President
        WHITMAN-Charles S., Jr. The Society of Colonial Wars in the State of New York regrets the passing of its former Governor. M. David Sherrill, Governor James B. Gardiner II, Sec'y
