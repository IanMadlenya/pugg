
        JONES-Richard N. On October 19, 1997, at age 92, in Hanover, N.H. Husband of Leila Elizabeth Jones (deceased). He is survived by two loving children, Elizabeth Thorne, of Manchester, Mass., and Gerard E. Jones of Greenwich, Conn., six loving grandchildren and two stepgrandchildren and one greatgrandchild. He devoted over 35 years of his professional life to his work in sales and as a publisher with Time Inc. In addition, he participated actively in community life in Cleveland, OH, Winetka, IL, and Greenwich, CT. We will all miss his character, intellect and devotion to his family and friends. A memorial service will be held at Christ Church, Greenwich, Conn., at 2 P.M., on December 20, 1997.
