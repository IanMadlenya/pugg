
        DIENER-Beatrice. The officers and staff of State of Israel Bonds deeply mourn the passing of Beatrice Diener of New Jersey. Mrs. Diener and her late husband Leonard were longstanding supporters and beloved leaders of Israel Bonds and the local community. Mrs. Diener's dedicated efforts on behalf of Israel and the Jewish people will long be remembered. Our heartfelt condolences to her children: Marjorie, Larry and Adele, and the entire family. Burton P.
