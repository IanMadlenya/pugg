
        SPIEGLER--Jeanne, 101 yrs old. Adoring aunt to Judith and Susan, loving great-aunt to Matthew, Sharon, Anna, Danielle, Rachel, April and Sara, sister-in-law to Beatrice. Passionate feminist, supporter of Jewish causes, conscientious manager at ''Davidsons'' for over 40 years. Her unconditional love will be sorely missed by those privileged to have been loved by Jeanne. Funeral Service Sunday November 12, 10am Zion Memorial Chapel, Mamaroneck, 914-381-1809. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery.
