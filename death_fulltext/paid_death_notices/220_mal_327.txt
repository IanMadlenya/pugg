
        KLEIN--Victor, 65, died of cancer in the morning of May 28, 2005 at Beth Israel Hospital. Devoted husband of Dorothy and loving father of Emily, caregiving son of his mother, Sadie, age 99. Brother of noted, iconoclastic author of novels for young people, Norma Klein. Himself a hospital social worker. Also well-versed in art history and connoisseur of classical music. Loyal to friends over decades. Donations in his memory may be made to the Sierra Club.
