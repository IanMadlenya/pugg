
        KARFUNKEL-Louis. The American Friends of Sanz Medical Center/Laniado Hospital express deep sorrow on the passing of Reb Yisroel Yehuda Karfunkel O.B.M., illustrious Talmudic lecturer and author, who personified the blessed combination of Torah and Gedula. Our heartfelt condolences to his wife Clara and sons Michael and George, long time benefactors of Netanya's only hospital. Leibis Morgenstern, Treasurer Henry Spitzer, Secretary
