
        TAUBER-Herman J. 91, of West Palm Beach. Widower of Ruth Tauber. He is survived by sons Arthur (Sandra) Tauber and Richard (Barbara) Tauber, daughter Judith (Jim) Gordon; grandsons Daniel, Jeffrey, Michael, Charles, and Matthew; great - grandchildren Maddie, Becky, Mickey, Justin and Jeremy. Mr. Tauber was President of Temple Beth El in Bellmore, NY, and a longtime member of Temple Beth Tikvah of Greenacres, FL. A graveside service will be held Sunday (today), 1:30 PM, at Wellwood in Pinelawn, NY. A memorial service will be held in Florida on Wednesday, May 28. For further information, please call Riverside-Gordon 1800-844-0980.
