
        TRIVISONNO-Harry J. Of Chatham, NJ (formerly of Brooklyn, NY), on August 9, 2002; husband of the late Vivian; father of Valerie Moreno and Joseph; also survived by five grandchildren and one great-grandchild. He was an architect for the NYC Housing Authority from 1938-1962, and served on the Board of Housing and Redevelopment for NYC. For funeral information contact Ippolito Berkeley Memorial, Berkeley Heights, NJ; 908277-6446.
