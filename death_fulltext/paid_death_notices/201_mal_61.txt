
        ROCKLIN--Ralph J. Died on September 13, 2003, in the hospital. We thank you for sharing with us your love of New York City, libraries and museums, painters of light, prints, music and poetry, kittens and puns. He is survived by his children, Edward Rocklin of California, Peter Rocklin of New Jersey, Arthur Rocklin of Maryland, Dorothy Rocklin of Massachusetts, and by his granddaughter Rachel Rocklin.
