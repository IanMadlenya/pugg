
        EVANS-James Lloyd. Loving father of James L., Jr., John, Kevin, and Michael, loving brother of Margaret Brower, and Joseph Evans. He was a former long-time employee of The New York Times from 1985 to 1992. Commander, American Legion and Korean War veteran.
        EVANS-James Lloyd. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of James Lloyd Evans, associated with The Times from October 5, 1985 until retiring on December 28, 1992.
