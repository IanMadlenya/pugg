
        GRANT-James Deneale. On July 13th, 2002, at age 70. Former Deputy Commissioner of the US Food and Drug Administration. Retired Chairman and CEO of T Cell Sciences. Director of several biotech boards. Beloved husband of Bonnie J. Grant. Loving father of Glenn, Bruce Scott and grandfather of four grandchildren and two step grandchildren. Interment private. Memorial service October 5th, 2 PM, at Fifth Ave. Presbyterian Church, 5th Ave. at 55th St., NY, NY 10019. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Church.
