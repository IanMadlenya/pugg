
        PECK-Harris B., M.D. The Board of Trustees & staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services mourn the passing of Harris B. Peck, M.D., an esteemed member of our senior staff and a consultant to the agency for many years. A leading psychiatrist, Dr. Peck will long be remembered as a pioneer in the fields of community mental health and group therapy. JBFCS extends its deepest sympathy to his wife, Ilka; his children, Ann, Yoav, Jonathan, Joshua and Joel; and other members of his family. Joseph S. Kaplan, President Seymour R. Askin, Jr., Chairman of the Board Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive Vice President Robert Abramovitz, M.D., Chief Psychiatrist
