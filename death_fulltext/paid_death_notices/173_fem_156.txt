
        MILLER-Roselyn. 82, of Coconut Creek, FL, formerly of Great Neck, NY, and Cranberry, NJ, died peacefully at home on June 16, 2001. She was the adored wife of Saul. Cherished mother of Emily Miller Budick and Lawrence D. Miller. Mother-in-law of Sanford Budick and Wendy Miller. Grandmother of Rachel Elmakeyes, Ayelet Sharon, Amanda and Laura Miller and the late Yochanan Budick and great-grandmother of Chananel and Michah Elmakeyes. She will be deeply missed by all.
