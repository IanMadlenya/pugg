
        ROCKEFELLER-Mary French. The Trustees and staff of the Wildlife Conservation Society are deeply saddened by the passing of Mary French Rockefeller, long time friend of the Society, its zoos and aquarium, and beloved wife of the Society's Honorary Trustee and past Chairman, Laurance S. Rockefeller. Her gentleness, compassion and kindness will long be remembered. We extend our deepest sympathy to her family. She will be missed by all. David T. Schiff, Chairman Howard Phipps, Jr., Chairman Emeritus William Conway, President
