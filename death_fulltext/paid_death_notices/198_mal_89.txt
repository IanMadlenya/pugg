
        BATSCHA--Robert M. The Board, the staff and the Friends of New Yorkers For Children mourn the passing of Bob Batscha, a devoted member of our Board of Directors. Bob was an exemplary member of the NYFC family, providing support and guidance and we will continue our work according to the high standards that he encouraged in us. Bob was a true friend to the children of New York City. We offer our condolences to his son Eric and all of his family. We join you in treasuring the memory of this remarkable man. Nicholas Scoppetta, Pres. New Yorkers For Children >>AD#
