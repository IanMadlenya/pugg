
        BACKAL-Lorraine, 75. Of Murrysville, PA, formerly of Long Island, died Tuesday February 19, 2002. She was born June 11, 1926, in Brooklyn, a daughter of Aziz and Mabel Backal. Prior to retirement in 1994, she had been a judge in Bronx County, NY. Mother of Sinde Snitger of Murrysville, PA, and Arthur (Trish) Backal of Brookville, NY. Grandmother of Jeremy; sister of Gloria (Ernie) Wunderman of Boca Raton, FL. Funeral services Monday, February 25, 10:30am, Frank E. CampbellThe Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St. Interment will follow at Woodlawn Cemetery. Friends may call Sunday from 7-9 P.M.
