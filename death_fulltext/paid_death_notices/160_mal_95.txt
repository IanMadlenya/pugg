
        FAIRBANKS-Douglas, Jr. We mourn the loss of our beloved member and dear friend, Douglas. A true gent in every sense of the word. He regaled us often with his wonderful stories and charming company. Our love and support to his beautiful Vera and the Fairbanks family. He will be truly missed. The Players
        FAIRBANKS-Douglas Jr. USNR (Ret), KBE, DSC. Henry Luce III, President Eben W. Pyne, Treasurer Mark Dixon, Secretary
