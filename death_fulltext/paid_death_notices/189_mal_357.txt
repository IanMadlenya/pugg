
        MENDLOWITZ-Jerome. Beloved husband of Ruth, passed away peacefully at 78. He is also survived by his devoted daughter Brenda and her husband Mark. He was a Navy Seabee during World War II, followed by working in the family leather business. He was a wonderful man liked by all and his kindness will never be forgotten. Services will be on Tuesday, October 29, at 1:15 PM at the Sinai Chapels (718) 445-0300.
