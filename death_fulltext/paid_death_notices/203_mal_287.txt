
        BERENTSON--John Franklin. On December 19th in New York City. After graduating from Lehigh University, he served in the U.S. Navy and became a successful cable TV executive, but will best be remembered as a selfless husband, father and friend. He was the cherished husband of Lynne Gorman Berentson and beloved father of and best friend to Ben Berentson. He is mourned by his three loving sisters, nieces, nephews, mother-in-law and many devoted friends.
