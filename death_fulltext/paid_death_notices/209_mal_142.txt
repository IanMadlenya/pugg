
        TRAUM--Samuel Benjamin, 90, of Great Neck, NY. Widower of Edith. Father of Sara, Jessica and Irene and grandfather of Marcel and David. A highly respected CPA, he was known and loved for his passions: work, family, garden, travel and humanitarian and political beliefs. Memorial service Friday, June 18, at 2 pm, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY.
