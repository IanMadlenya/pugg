
        RYAN--John B. III, 74. John's many friends and associates at Oppenheimer & Co. Inc. are saddened by his death on December 3, 2003. John was associated with the firm for almost thirty years. His personality, sense of humor, and distinguished voice will be greatly missed. John's loyalty, dedication and deep affection impacted many people. His memory will be cherished throughout the firm and by those he touched. Sincere condolences to his wife, Elana Ryan; sister, Virginia Airlie; sons, Beau and Drew; daughter - in - law, Susan; granddaughters, Jesse and Noa, and to his many family members and friends.
