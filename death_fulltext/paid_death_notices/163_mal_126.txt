
        WEISSLER-Ben, 92, originally from Ft. Lee, N.J, died Thursday evening, August 9, in Boca Raton, FL, where he resided. He is survived by his wife Natalie, son Barry, and stepchildren Carol Bleeker and Dr. Harvey Rosen. He was grandfather to Alex Bleeker, Steven Weller and Cindy Rosenberg. He was great grandfather to Kevin and Daryn Rosenberg, David and Michael Weller, and Andrew and Katherine Rosen. He was father-in-law to Fran Weissler and Dr. Letitia Rosen. Ben was a lover of life who will be truly missed. Services to be held at Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel Sunday, August 22 at 11am in Del Ray, FL
