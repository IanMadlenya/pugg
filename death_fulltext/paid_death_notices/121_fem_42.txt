
        LEWIS-Mary C. The family records with deep sorrow the passing of our loving mother and grandmother on February 2, 1997. Carol Matthew, Raphael O. Lewis
        LEWIS-Mary C. The board and staff of Spence Chapin Services to Families and Children mourn the passing of Mary C. Lewis, our devoted friend and the former president of our board of directors. We remember her with affection for her many years of dedication to the agency and for her work with families and children in the New York area. Our deepest sympathies go out to her family and friends.
