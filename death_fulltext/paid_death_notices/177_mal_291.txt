
        MINER-William R., Ph.D. Age 68. On October 18, 2001, unexpectedly. Loving husband of Anne, father of Reeve Washburn of Seattle, Washington, and Neal Miner of New York, New York, and doting grandfather of Faye Washburn. A lifelong Manhattan resident, he was a practicing psychoanalyst. Bill had a passion for jazz and was a noted collector and discographer. He graduated from Princeton University and later received his doctorate in Counseling Psychology from Teachers College at Columbia University. He also graduated from the Center for Modern Psychoanalytic Studies. Bill is survived by his brothers, Henry C. Miner, III of Brooklyn, N.Y. & Thomas H. Miner, of Conway, MA. A private memorial service is planned for a future date.
