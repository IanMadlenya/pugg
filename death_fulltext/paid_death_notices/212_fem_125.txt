
        GOFFEN--Rona, died September 8, 2004. Her family mourns the passing of Rona Goffen of Princeton, NJ, noted art historian and Board of Governors Professor at Rutgers University. Her enthusiasm and expertise in the Renaissance inspired all who knew her. Memorial service will be held November 20th at 10:30AM at Kirkpatrick Chapel at Rutgers University.
