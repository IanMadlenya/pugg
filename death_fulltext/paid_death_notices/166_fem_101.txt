
        SEEGERS-Edith A. Age 72, of Pleasantville, New York passed away November 12 while at home in Florida. Edith worked as a bookkeeper for the Legend Group for thirty years. She is survived by her beloved companion, Tony Ciulla, sons, Richard, Paul and David, and many cherished grandchildren, nieces, nephews and friends. Her generosity, warmth, humor and loving ways will be missed by all who knew her. In lieu of flowers contributions can be sent to the American Cancer Society or the National Audubon Society.
