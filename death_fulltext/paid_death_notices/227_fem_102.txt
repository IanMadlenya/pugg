
        SAMELSON - Ellen. Long time resident of Great Neck, NY, on December 14, 2005 at age 80. Beloved wife of the late Joseph. Devoted mother of Dr. Jeffrey (Amanda) Rapaport, Holly Samelson, and Marc (Sheri) Rapaport. Treasured grandmother of Will, Ali, Lauren, Robyn, Danny and Lanna. A member of the Vanderbilt Bridge Club. She will be sorely missed by all. Services Friday, December 16, 2005, 9:45 A.M. at the ''Riverside - Nassau North Chapels'', 55 North Station Plaza (opposite the LIRR), Great Neck, NY.
