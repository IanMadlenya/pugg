
        DAVIS--Ellsworth ''Binky'', 69, of Lyme, CT, died December 28, 2003. He was born in Hartford on September 4, 1934. A memorial service will be held at 1 PM, Saturday, January 3, at St. Ann's Episcopal Church, Old Lyme, CT. with a reception at 3:30 PM at the Graduate Club, 155 Elm Street, New Haven.
