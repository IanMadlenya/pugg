
        KELIHER-Agathe S. Died in Meadowbrook, Pennsylvania on February 18 at the age of 102. Born in Kansas City, MO in 1898, she lived 95 years in Brooklyn, N.Y. before moving to Meadowbrook. Widow of Francis T. Keliher. She is survived by a daughter, Agathe Crouter of Philadelphia and a son, Frank of Washington, D.C., 3 step-grandchildren and 6 step-great-grandchildren. Funeral services and burial are private.
