
        LAMULA-Claire, 81, widow of Assemblyman John J. Lamula, passed away April 20, 1999. An NYC native, she resided in Connecticut and later Maryland. She was an elegant and honorable lady who established her own insurance agency, assisted Mr. Lamula in his brokerage firm and volunteered at CASA. She is survived by 3 children, Dr. Pamela R. Hartley (Atlanta, GA), JoD Straub (Columbia, MD) and Harry B. Rosen (Merritt Island, FL), and 2 grandchildren, Ashleigh J. Snell and Christopher J. Snell (Columbia, MD). Remembrances may be sent to the Humane Society.
