
        WILDERS-Monsignor James G. The trustees and staff of the Seamen's Church Institute (SCI) note with sorrow the death on Tuesday, August 25, 1998 of former Trustee, Msgr. James G. Wilders. Msgr. Wilders served the Institute with distinction from 1981 to 1993. He was actively involved in ministry to seafarers, serving as the Roman Catholic Chaplain at Manhattan's Passenger Ship Terminal. As such, he was instrumental in developing a spirit of cooperation among various church organizations serving the port. His was a true friend to seafarers.
