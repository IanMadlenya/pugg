
        FLEMING-Frederic G. of Port Washington, NY, died November 13th. Born in Belgium, he served with the US Army during World War II. He retired from Air Products in 1970. He is survived by his loving wife, Mathe, daughters, Lynne Goss, Georgia Bill, and Barbara Fleming, and six grandchildren. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent to Camphill Special School, Glenmoore, PA, 19343.
