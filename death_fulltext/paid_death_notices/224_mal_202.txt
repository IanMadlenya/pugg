
        GREENE - Daniel. October 17, 1912 to September 17, 2005. Beloved father to Karin Grouf, Sherri Greene, Ron Greene and Ken Greene. Grandfather of Caitlin Greene and Ryan Greene. Brother to Syliva Piven, brother-in-law to Fred Hill and father-in-law to Diane Greene. Close friend to Phyllis Harnik and Jon Grouf, and uncle to Peter, Mark, Jacqueline and Ellen. Blessed with a quiet mind, he was always content and enjoyed everything he did and was liked wherever he went. He joins his wife Dorrit Greene after 61 years of marriage. Now they may both rest in peace together. Funeral services private.
        $(6$)GREENE -- Daniel. Park Avenue Synagogue mourns the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. May his memory remain for a blessing. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Amy A.B. Bressman, Chairman of the Board Menachem Z. Rosensaft, President
