
        MORRELLO - Charles J., 92, Knight of Malta, of Tudor City, passed away on January 27th. He was a Commissioner of the New York City Board of Assessors during the administrations of all mayors from Fiorello LaGuardia until John Lindsay, when he retired. He was a candidate for State Senator for The Democratic Party. Viewing Saturday 7-9PM and Sunday 25 and 7-9PM at Dimiceli & Sons Funeral Home, 182 Lexington Ave, NYC. Mass of Christian Burial, Monday 10AM, Saint Agnus Church (no flowers please).
