
        DENNIS-Sharon Ann. Beloved mother, daughter, grandmother and wife. Died November 30, 1999 in Tucson, Arizona. A beautiful & inspirational woman who devoted her life to literacy and education. She dearly loved her New York Times and Crossword. She is profoundly missed.
