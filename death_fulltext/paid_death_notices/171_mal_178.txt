
        BALDWIN-John M. Of New York City, suddenly on April 10, 2001 at age 64. Executive Vice President of Kenner and Co. Beloved husband of Ellen Brooks Baldwin. Father of Jonathan and Alan Baldwin. Son of Gwendolyn M. Baldwin and the late Charles E. Baldwin, Jr. Brother of Charles E. Baldwin III and Beverley B. McCoid. Interment private. Memorial services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday April 17th, 3PM. Contributions in his memory may be made to Middlesex School, Concord, Mass. 01742.
        BALDWIN-John M. Kenner & Company, Inc. expresses profound sorrow at the death of John M. Baldwin, our esteemed colleague and co-founder. His friendship and contribution to our firm will be greatly missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Ellen, sons Jonathan and Alan and to other members of his family
