
        WESTIN--Eliot, died May 29 at the age of 101. Beloved husband of Florence, father of Av, grandfather of Mark and adored uncle of many nieces and nephews and their children, and many friends. He was a life-loving, peopleloving citizen of New York City. His warmth and wit and generosity towards the world and towards people in particular will be remembered always. Services will be held at the Plaza Jewish Community Chapel, 90 St. and Amsterdam Avenue, Manhattan, 10AM Wednesday, June 1.
