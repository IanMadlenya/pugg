
        TIEBOUT-Alice Margaretta. At age 96, died peacefully on Sunday, October 11, in Huntington, L.I. A talented interior decorator-the Air Force Academy Chapel was one of her projects-she was also an accomplished amateur musician in the Tiebout family tradition. During World War II, she served with distinction with the American Red Cross in the ChinaBurma-India theater. Alice is survived by seventeen nieces and nephews. Interment was at Cypress Hill Cemetery, Brooklyn.
