
        HOCHBERG-Max. Died Nov. 11, 1999. He founded the Hochberg Bros. Painting Co. in 1929 and the JM Charles Painting Co. In 1967, and remained an active partner until his retirement at age 90. He was predeceased by his beloved wife Ida and daughters Alice and Beverly. He is survived by his daughter Janice Siegel and her husband Gerald in Irvington, New York, as well as 10 grandchildren, 19 great grandchildren and two great-great grandchildren. He was affectionately known to all who knew him for his indomitable spirit and devotion to family. He was lovingly cared for by Marion Benjamin throughout his long illness. Services will be held at 11 A.M., November 18, 1999 at Temple Beth Abraham, 25 Leroy Avenue, Tarrytown, NY. Donations may be made to Temple Beth Abraham or Hospice Care in Westchester and Putnam.
