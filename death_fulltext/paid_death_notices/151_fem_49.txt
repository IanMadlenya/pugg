
        GILMAN-Sylvia Phillips. The Board of Trustees and staff of the American Friends of the Tel Aviv Museum of Art mourn the passing of our respected and dear friend, Sylvia Phillips Gilman. She was a dedicated supporter of the Tel Aviv Museum of Art, and will long be remembered for her kindness and generosity. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her family and loved ones. Uzi Zucker, Chairman Tamar Rudich, Vice Chairman
