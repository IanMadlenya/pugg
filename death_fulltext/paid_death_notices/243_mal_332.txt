
        DARLINGTON--Thomas, longtime Short Hills resident, died Wednesday, March 14th, 2007 at the Brevard Hospice Care Center in Rockledge, FL. Born in Manhattan, he lived in Short Hills for 47 years and has been in Palm Bay, FL for the past year. Mr. Darlington was a retired stock broker. He served with the U.S. Navy during WWII and was a Past-President with the Sons of the Revolution. Mr. Darlington is survived by his son and daughter-in-law Thomas III and Rita Darlington, along with his grandchildren Dawn and Eric, all of Palm Bay, FL. He was predeceased by his wife of 51 years, Marcia, who died four years ago. A memorial service will be held at 9:30am on Saturday, May 5th, at Christ Church, 66 Highland Avenue, Short Hills.
