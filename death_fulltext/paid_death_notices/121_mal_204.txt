
        PRESSMAN-Jack. The Warsaw Ghetto Resistance Organization mourn the loss of its Secretary, Jack Pressman. A survivor of the Warsaw Ghetto, as well as the Oberlanzdorf and Gross Rosen concentration camps, Mr. Pressman devoted his life after the war to remembrance of the Holocaust. Born in Warsaw, he came to the United States in 1951, where he served as an engineer with the Otis Elevator Company. He is survived by his wife of 54 years, Alina Kaplan; daughters Barbara Eckstrom, Jennifer Pressman and Eva Zeidman and their families; and his brother and sister-in-law, Adek and Hansie Pressman. Funeral services on Monday, February 17, 10 AM at the Schwartz Brothers Funeral Home, Queens Blvd. & 76th Rd., Forest Hills, NY. Benjamin Meed, President Warsaw Ghetto Resistance Organization
