
        RUDEL-Anne Kiley. Died on November 28th, 2000, in Hightstown, New Jersey. Wife of the late William E. Rudel. Survived by her children Thomas K. Rudel (Susan Golbeck), Joan E. Rudel (Chris Stinson), Elizabeth Rudel Mayersohn (Arnold L.) and three grandchildren Daniel, Sarah and Veronica. She will be missed. Mrs. Rudel was a poet, painter and scholar. Memorials in her name will be accepted at Barnard College and the Waquoit Congregational Church, Waquoit, MA. 02536.
