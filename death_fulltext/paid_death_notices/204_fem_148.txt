
        FISHER--Elizabeth Kenowsky. The Boy Scouts of America, Greater New York Councils acknowledges the passing of E.K. Fisher and extends its sincere sympathy to her family and many friends. Mrs. Fisher and her late husband, Zachary, were strong supporters of scouting and other charitable programs throughout New York City. The Intrepid Foundation, founded by the Fishers, continues to do fine work, most notably through its ''Fallen Heros Fund'' and the refurbishing of the Intrepid as a floating museum. Mrs. Fisher will be greatly missed. John F. McGillicuddy, President Daniel R. Gasparo, CEO
        FISHER--Elizabeth K. The Alzheimer's Association, NYC Chapter extends deepest condolences to the family of Elizabeth K. Fisher who with her husband Zachary bravely led the fight against this terrible disease. Bill Kaye, Irvine Flinn, Co-Presidents Jed Levine, Acting Exec. Dir.
