
        KAY-Irene R. Quietly and with dignity on March 13. She left behind dear friends who cared for her during her brief illness. Born in England on March 26, 1918, she travelled widely before moving to Manhattan in 1954. After a long career in personnel and finance management, she became an active gardener. She was a member of the Ninth District Federated Garden Clubs of New York, and as a member of the Larchmont Garden Club held the position of National Council Landscape Design Critic Chairman and National Council Master Judge. Services will be held Friday, March 17th at 11:30 AM at Ferncliff Memorial Chapel in Hartsdale, N.Y.
