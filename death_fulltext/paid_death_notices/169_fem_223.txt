
        MILLER-Anita. 83, of Holmdel, on February 23, 2001. Born in NYC. Longtime resident of Brooklyn. A 1949 Hunter College Graduate. She was a Medical Librarian for the State University of NY Downstate Medical Center. Services private. For info contact the Holmdel Funeral Home, 26 South Holmdel Rd., Holmdel, NJ 07733.
