
        AUFHAUSER-Fred E., 81, of Boca Raton, FL., died January 24. He is survived by his loving family, wife Lore of 52 years, daughters Vicky Aufhauser Stein of Seattle, WA., and Susan Aufhauser Soifer of Teaneck, NJ; son-in-law Matthew Soifer and three grandchildren Shira, Eliana and Terri. Funeral services will be held on Sunday, January 28, in Florida. The family suggests donations to Hospice by the Sea, 1531 West Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, FL. 33486.
