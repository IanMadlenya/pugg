
        BANG-Frank J. Age 92, died peacefully on December 2, 2000. He lived in Grandview and Shelter Island, New York. He was a founding partner of Marboro Books, the largest mail order business of its era. A gardener, conservationist and sailor, he sailed his boat until age 91. ''Uncle Bud'' was born in Lynbrook, Long Island, September 20, 1908. His wife, Ruth Forman Bang, his children Peter, Paul, Francesca and five grandchildren survive him. In lieu of flowers please send donations to The Nature Conservancy, Shelter Island, New York, 11964.
