
        KLEBAN-Julian Milton. Died on March 19th, 1998 at the age of 92. Beloved father of Toby Levine of Potomac, MD and the late Edward Kleban, devoted husband of the late Sylvia Kandel Kleban. Loving father-in-law of Andrew Levine. Adoring grandfather of Caren Pelletier and Amy Levine and grandfather-in-law of Michael Pelletier. Cherished cousin and keeper of the Kleban family tree. Remembered by all who knew him as a sweet, kind and generous man who had a rare gift for friendship with people from all walks of life and a song for everyone. Graduate of DeWitt Clinton High School and New York Law School. Member of the Bronx County Bar Association, New York State Bar Association, and former president of the Jewish Lawyer's Guild. Services Friday, March 20, 1 PM, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to The Misler Jewish Senior Day Program, 1801 East Jefferson St, Rockville, Maryland 20852.
