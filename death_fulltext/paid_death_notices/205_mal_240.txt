
        BESSUNGER -- Hans -Joachim (John). The Board of Directors and employees of Haram - Christensen Corpora tion, Carlstadt, N.J., acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of its Chairman. Mr. Bessunger was a brilliant man with expert knowledge in the specialty food business. Mr. Bessunger leaves behind his loving wife, Kelen, and his sister, Ann. Services will take place Sunday, February 22, 24 and 7-9PM at O'Shea Funeral Home, Hampton Bays, L.I.
