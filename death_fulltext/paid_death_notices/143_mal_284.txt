
        LEVY-Samuel J. The Board of Overseers of the Albert Einstein College of Medicine of Yeshiva University records with deep sorrow the passing of Samuel J. Levy, a past chairman of our Society of Founders and a Guardian of our University. To his wife, Marie, and to the entire family, we offer our heartfelt condolences. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees Burton P. Resnick, Chairperson Board of Overseers Albert Einstein College of Medicine Dr. Dominick P. Purpura Marilyn and Stanley M. Katz Dean Albert Einstein College of Medicine
        LEVY-Samuel J. Temple EmanuEl of Palm Beach, FL offers its sincerest condolences to the family of Samuel J. Levy, his wife Marie and his brothers and sister Murray, Sidney and Frances. A true Jewish leader who will be forever remembered in all our hearts. May God comfort them among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Leonid Feldman Stephen A. Levin, President
