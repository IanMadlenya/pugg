
        HUDDLESTON-Alma Robinson. On February 28, 1999 in her 90th year in Gadsden, AL; formerly of NYC. Widow of William E. Huddleston, Jr., of Clanton and Tuskegee, AL. Loving mother of twin daughters, Patricia Byrd Huddleston, wife of the Hon. James Douglas Martin, of Gadsden, AL and the late Annette Knowles Huddleston, wife of Timothy Field Beard of Roxbury, CT. Grandmother of three, great-grandmother of two.
