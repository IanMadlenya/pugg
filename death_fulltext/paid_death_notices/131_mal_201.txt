
        ABBOTT-Forrest L. Barnard College notes with sorrow the passing of Forrest L. (''Duke'') Abbott on November 22, 1997, at the age of 90. For 23 years-between 1953 and 1976-he served as Barnard's Treasurer and Controller, overseeing the financial administration of the College as well as a signficant building program. He also served on the boards of numerous Morningside Heights community associations. He is survived by his wife Marian, his children Jeffrey T. of Denver, James P. of Baltimore, Jonathan C. of Washington, DC, and Penny of Connecticut. A memorial service will be held at Barnard College at a later date. Contributions in his memory may be made to Barnard College.
