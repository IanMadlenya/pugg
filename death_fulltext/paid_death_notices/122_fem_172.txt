
        PARKES-Mollie. Psychotherapist and clinical social worker, died at home March 21, 1997, after a short illness. Survived by daughters Nancy and Alison, five grandchildren and four great-grandchildren. She was always learning. Among her many loves were books, music, gardening, travel and her family, friends and patients. Those who knew her are invitied to visit with family at her home, Monday, March 24, 3-7 PM.
