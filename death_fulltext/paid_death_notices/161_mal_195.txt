
        JONES-Robert Trent of Fort Lauderdale, Florida, formerly of Montclair on June 14, 2000, husband of the late Ione David Jones, father of Robert Trent Jones, Jr. of Woodside, CA and Rees Lee Jones of Montclair, NJ, grandfather of Robert Trent III, Elizabeth Taliaferro, Alden Elizabeth and Amy Singleton Jones. A Memorial service will be held at All Saints Episcopal Church, 333 Tarpon Dr., Fort Lauderdale, FL on Tuesday, June 20 at 2PM. In Lieu of flowers, donations may be made in his memory to Robert Trent Jones Endowment Fund The Golf Course Superintendents Association of America, 1421 Research Park Dr., Lawrence, KS 66049 or Cornell University Care of Inga Reichenback Day Hall, Ithaca, NY 14853-8201. A private graveside service will be held at Mount Hebron Cemetery, in New Jersey on Wednesday, June 21 followed by a reception for family and friends at Montclair Golf Club, West Orange, New Jersey at 4PM. For information contact Arthur K. Brown, Inc., Bloomfield, New Jersey. JONES-Robert Trent. Sr. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the passing of Robert, father of our esteemed member and architect of our course, Rees Jones. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Rees, his wife Susan and their children, Alden and Amy. Robert E. Morrow, President Harvey L. Radler, Secretary
