
        JOYCE-Donald. On Tuesday, March 10, 1998, age 45, of cancer. Musician, organist, choir director, teacher. A service will be held on Saturday, March 28, at 1 P.M., at the Church of the Holy Apostles, 9th Ave at 28th St., NYC 10001. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made either to the Organ Fund at the above church or to the Donald Joyce Scholarship Fund at the Copland School of Music, Queens College, Flushing, N.Y. 11367.
        JOYCE-Donald. The Clergy, Wardens, Vestry, Choir, Congregation and Staff of the Church of the Holy Apostles mourn the passing of Donald Joyce, dedicated and esteemed organist and Director of Music, 1992-1998, and extend deepest sympathy to his family.
