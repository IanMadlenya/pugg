
        PAYSON-Rosalind S. Of Southport, CT, died February 21, 2001. She was a residential real estate agent with Nicholas H. Fingelly Real Estate. She is survived by her companion, Charles (Bud) Hamilton Sanford, three sons, Laurence, Blakeney and Eliot Payson, and five grandsons. A Memorial service will be held Friday, March 2, 2001 at 11:00AM at Trinity Episcopal Church, Southport. Donations in her memory, Trinity Episcopal Church Restoration Fund, 651 Pequot Avenue, Southport, CT 06490.
