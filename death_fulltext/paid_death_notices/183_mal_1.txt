
        DEUTSCH-Arnold. Of New York City, who passed away on March 30, was a pioneer in the field of advertising as Founder and President of Deutsch, Shea & Evans Advertising Agency. A member of the American Association of Advertising Agencies, he was held in high esteem by his business associates. A lover of music, he founded The Friends of Young Musicians, a foundation that gave many well known young musicians the opportunity to concertize. He was generous of spirit and philanthropic in support of many organizations. He began his career working for many newspapers and after he retired he donated his time to Jewish Week and The Forward. He also generously gave of his time as President of Metropolitan Synagogue. His support of family and friends was legendary and his indominable spirit, energy and follow through were to be admired. A loving son of the late Louis and Sadie Deutsch and loving brother of Emery and Frank Deutsch and Rose Frenkel, he is survived by his beloved partner in life Barbara Scharf, nephews Richard Frenkel, James Frenkel and Gregory Deutsch and their families. A memorial service in his honor is being planned.
        DEUTSCH-Arnold. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Arnold Deutsch. To his loved ones we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn his passing. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
