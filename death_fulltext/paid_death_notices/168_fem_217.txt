
        JAMES-Katherine Magner. Died at home on Sunday, December 31st. Daughter of Thomas F. and Isabella C. Magner, she was born on January 23rd, 1901 in Brooklyn, NY. She is the widow of Warner Rutherford James and was also predeceased by a daughter, Cynthia McDonald. She is survived by a daughter, Nancy Magner McDonald of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL and a step-daughter and her husband Clare and Jess Dall of Greenwich, CT; Jonathan Dall of Frankfort, MI; and David and Michelle Dall of Cos Cob, CT. A Memorial Service will be held on her 100th birthday on Tuesday, January 23rd 11:00 AM at the Church of St. Thomas More, 65 East 89th St., New York City. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Fresh Air Fund, 1040 Avenue of the Americas, New York City, NY 10018, the Salvation Army 120 W. 14th St., New York City, NY 10011 or the charity of one's choice.
