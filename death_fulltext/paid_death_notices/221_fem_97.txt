
        LEMING--Beatrice Willard. Age 78 at her home in Stony Brook, on June 8, 2005. She graduated in 1947 from Vassar College and taught for many years in the Three Village School District. She is survived by her husband Howell, her daughter Mary Claire, two step-sons, and her niece and nephew. Services will be held at the St. James Episcopal Church, Route 25A, St. James, NY on Monday June 20th at 11:30 AM. Donations may be made to Defenders of Wildlife, 1130 17th St. NW, Washington, DC 20036.
