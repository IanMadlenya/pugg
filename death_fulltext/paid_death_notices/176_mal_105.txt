
        SPENCER-William I. Of North Palm Beach, FL, died of cancer September 9, 2001, at his summer residence in Larchmont, NY. He fought a gallant battle. He was predeceased by his first wife Kathryn Cope. He was the cherished and loving husband of Susan Hammel-Namm. He will be sorely missed by his step-children, Adam Namm of Arlington, VA, and Leslie and Charles Coplin of Larchmont, NY. He was the adoring and adored grandfather of Rebecca Namm, Lucy, and Miles Spencer Coplin. Funeral services at 11:30 AM, on Thursday, September 13, at Frank E. Campbell, The Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Ave at 81st St. A private cremation to follow. Family will be receiving friends Wednesday, September 12, from 2-5 and 7-9 PM, at Frank E. Campbell. In lieu of flowers the family would appreciate donations to the New York University Medical Center to the attention of Theresa Bischoff, President, 550 First Ave, NY, NY 10016.
