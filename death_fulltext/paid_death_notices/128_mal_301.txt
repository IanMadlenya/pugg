
        RACKOW-Leon L., M.D., Died September 16, 1997 in Pompano Beach, Florida. Survived by his loving wife Doris, children Eric, Kenneth and Sari, grandchild Lauren and sister Norma Perlman. He was Director of FDR Hospital in Montrose, New York and Clinical Professor of Psychiatry at Cornell Medical School. A memorial fund has been established in his name at St. Vincent's Hospital, 153 West 11th Street, New York, New York 10011, C/O Dr. Eric Rackow.
