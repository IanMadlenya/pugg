
        WILSON-Walter Barron Wilton. Of NYC. His family mourns the loss on November 14 of a devoted father, doting grandfather, brother and uncle. Born in New York, educated at Trinity College and the Fletcher School of Foreign Diplomacy at Tufts, served in the U.S. Navy. Foreign Service aide, investment broker, prospector, publisher. He dedicated his keen intellect, instinctive good will, and warm sense of humor to his family and many friends. He was the gentlest of men. He loved to dance. For funeral information contact Frank E. Campbell at 212288-3500. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to House of the Redeemer, NYC, Trinity College, Hartford, CT, or the Fletcher School at Tufts.
