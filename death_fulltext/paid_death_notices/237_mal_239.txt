
        BROTMAN--Richard E. New York Medical College is saddened by the loss of our colleague, Dr. Richard E. Brotman, clinical professor of psychiatry and director of the Center For Comprehensive Health Practice in Manhattan. His dedication to teaching and patient care will be deeply missed. We extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Ronald F. Poe, Chairman Board of Trustees Ralph A. O'Connell, M.D. Acting President Provost and Dean School of Medicine
