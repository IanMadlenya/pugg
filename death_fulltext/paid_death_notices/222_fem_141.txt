
        BERDACH--Thea, died Friday, July 22, 2005, at Mather Hospital, Port Jefferson, NY at the age of 86. Beloved wife of Fred Berdach. Loving mother of James & Howard Berdach. Devoted daughter of the late Sarah and Jacob Koppelmann. Dear sister of Litza and Sam Schlanger. Fond grandmother of Gabriel Franklin Berdach. Thea was an accomplished painter whose works were much admired. Her gentle smile and effervescent spirit will be sadly missed and fondly remembered by all who knew her. Funeral 12:30 PM, on Tuesday, July 26th, at Boulevard - Riverside Chapel, Hewlett, New York. Family requests that donations be made in her memory to the Simon Wiesenthal Center.
