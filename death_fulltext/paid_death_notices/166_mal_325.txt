
        MELMED-David. 77. Died November 25. He graduated Yeshiva University in '45, received Rabbinical ordination in '47, and M.S. of Education in '60. He served as a Rabbi and educator and was President of Ben Ari Arts NYC. He was admired for his Talmudic scholarship and honesty in business, and was loved by all. He is survived by his devoted wife of 51 years, Shoshana Balaban Melmed, children Devora and Zelig Prag, Yona and Nesanel Kasnett, Yakey and Chayella Melmed, and many loving grandchildren. Shiva will be observed at his home, 1665 54th Street, Brooklyn.
