
        SCHWARTZ-Harold M. Died peacefully on May 2, 2002. Cherished husband of Yvette (Kass) for 53 years. Much loved father of Hillary and Dr. Joseph, respected father-in-law of C.B. Miles of Atlanta & Lisa Schwartz of Morganville, New Jersey. Proud grandfather of Heather & Daniel. Harold was devoted to his family including his ''kissin'' cousins from the Claman clan. He loved his profession (law), his college (Lehigh), his religious heritage, and his country, which he served with honor in World War II and in Korea. Graveside services May 5, 2002, Union Fields Cemetery. Direct all inquiries to Shermans Memorial Chapel, 718-377-7300.
