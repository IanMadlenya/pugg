
        COZINE-Kathryn, M.D. The Medical Board of NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital is deeply saddened by the sudden passing of our dear friend and colleague, Kathryn Cozine, M.D., Associate Clinical Professor of Anesthesiology at Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons. For the past several decades, Dr. Cozine served her patients with compassion, humility, and distinction. We speak for her colleagues, former residents, close friends, and grateful patients in expressing our sincere appreciation for her many contributions to the New York medical community and in extending our heartfelt condolences to her family. Lanny G. Close, M.D. President NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital Medical Board O. Wayne Isom, M.D. Vice President NewYork-Presbyterian Hospital Medical Board
