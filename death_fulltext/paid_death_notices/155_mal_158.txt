
        MIESMER-Charles W.. Age 81. Beloved and devoted husband of Virginia Zarou Miesmer, died peacefully, November 11, 1999 at New York Hospital. Born in Hoboken, NJ, December 13, 1917, he was the only son of the late Lauretta and William Miesmer. He served in the U.S. Navy during WWII. He spent his career in the construction industry in NYC and retired in 1991 as owner and president of Charles Miesmer, Inc. Survivors include his wife, Virginia, son, Charles R., daughters, Adrienne Miesmer Cosel and Susan Miesmer, six grandchildren and many, many devoted in-laws. Private funeral services were held November 13, 1999.
