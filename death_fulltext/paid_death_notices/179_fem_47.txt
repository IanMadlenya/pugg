
        RUBINSTEIN-Jeanne Sara. Of New York, NY died on December 5th from pancreatic cancer at 79 years of age. She was a painter, printmaker and poet whose work was widely admired. Her lively wit caused many a solemn face to turn bright with laughter. We mourn her loss and will miss her dearly. Ernie, Paul, and all Jeanne's family and friends.
