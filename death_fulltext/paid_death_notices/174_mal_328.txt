
        NESENOFF-Norman of Syosset on July 29, 2001. Loving husband of Goldie. Beloved brother of Bernie and Judy, and Debbie and Arnie. Devoted father of Dr. Mitchell and Marjorie Nesenoff, Rabbi David and Nancy Nesenoff, Mr. Marc and Linda Lawrence, and Mr. Perry Nesenoff. Morris, Inc., 21 East Deer Park Road, Dix Hills, L.I.
