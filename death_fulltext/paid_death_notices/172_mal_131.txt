
        KROGSTAD-John, a resident of Bayonet Point, FL, died recently at the Tulane Rehabilitation Center in New Orleans at the age of 95. He was born in Hovin, Norway, on January 10, 1906, and emigrated to Seattle in 1917. He graduated from Renton High School in Seattle in 1925 and from the University of Washington in 1930. From 1931 to 1971 he worked at Barnes & Noble, where he was vice president for 25 years, directed the wholesale department and helped develop the College Outline Series. In 1975 he and his wife Ruth (who died in 1998) moved from Long Island to Florida. He is survived by two sons, Donald J. Krogstad, M.D., of New Orleans, LA, and Robert T. Krogstad of Rochester, NY, and three grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at St. Mark's Presbyterian Church on State Road 52 in Hudson, FL, at 2PM on Monday, May 14. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Hernando-Pasco Hospice or the Alzheimer's Foundation.
