
        REYNOLDS--James Thompson. Died at his home in Bonita Springs, FL, on Wednesday, October 20th, 2004. He was 69. Reynolds graduated from Xenia (Ohio) High School and went on to Princeton University (1957). He was a member of Baltusrol Golf Club, Springfield, NJ. James, known as Jim, began his career at Bankers Trust Company in 1961, following three years of service in the US Navy, where he achieved the rank of lieutenant. He then went on to become a member of the New York Stock Exchange as an independent floor broker until his retirement in 1995. He is survived by his wife Carol and his sons, James Jr. and Philip, their wives, Laura and Kirsten, and six grandchildren. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be sent to ''The Princeton '57 Classmates Fund,'' c/o Shep Davis, 401 Atlantic Avenue, Suite 1002, Virginia Beach, VA 23451.
