
        FRANK-Helen S. On February 25, 2001, in Boston, MA. Age 88. Resident of Needham, MA., formerly of Westchester County, N.Y. Wife of the late Martin Frank. Mother of David Freund of New York City and Elizabeth Fideler of Framingham, MA., and the late Susan Lamb and John Freund. She is also survived by five grandchildren and five great grandchildren. She earned an Ed.D. from Teachers College and practiced psychotherapy for twenty years. Donations to Gillette Center for Women's Cancers, Massachusetts General Hospital, 100 Blossom St, Boston, MA 02114.
