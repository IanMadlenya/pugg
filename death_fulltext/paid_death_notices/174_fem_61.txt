
        SINGER-Anna. It is with deep regret that the Maimonides Medical Center family notes the passing of Anna Singer. Ms. Singer, a staunch member of Maimonides Voluteer Assembly, was also a member of Rivka Laufer Bikur Cholim. To her children, Dr. Israel Singer, a friend of our Medical Center, and Barbara Jacobowitz, and their families, we extend our sincere sympathy. Martin D. Payson, Chairman of the Board of Trustees Stanley Brezenoff, President
        SINGER-Anna. We mourn the loss of Anna Singer, beloved mother of Israel Singer, esteemed Vice Chairman, Yad Vashem Council. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to the entire Singer family. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Eli Zborowski, Chairman American and International Societies for Yad Vashem
        SINGER-Anna. The Foundation for Ethnic Understanding mourns the passing of the beloved mother of our distinguished colleague and tireless advocate for intergroup relations, Dr. Israel Singer. Heartfelt sympathy to the entire family. Rabbi Marc Schneier, President Lawrence D. Kopp, Exec Director
