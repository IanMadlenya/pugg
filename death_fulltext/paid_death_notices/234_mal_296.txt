
        HOBLER--Edward Windsor. 89, on July 23, 2006, in Evanston, Illinois. Won many National Senior Squash Championships. Devoted to Princeton, Class of '39. Son of Benton and Bowles Founder, Atherton Hobler. Brother of Herbert W. , '4 4 of Princeton, and the late Virginia Hobler Redpath and Wells A. Hobler, '41. At the end, his exeplary lifetime of fitness in many sports could not defeat pneumonia. Our loving dad as left us to soon. Dave, Windsor, Anne, Ruth
