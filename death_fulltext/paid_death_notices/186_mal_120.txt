
        NOTO-Lore. The League of Off-Broadway Theatres and Producers mourns the loss of Lore Noto, whose faith, vision and commitment helped to create ''The Fantasticks,'' an Off-Broadway masterpiece which has brought joy to audiences all over the world. We send our deepest sympathy to his wife, Mary, to children, Janice, Tony, Thad and Jody, and to his grandchildren. Board of Directors, League of Off-Broadway Theatres and Producers
