
        JANES-Frank. 54, died suddenly in Houston, TX, August 28, 1997 of a heart attack. Survivors include his two daughters Caryn and Marianne and his two sisters Delores Janes-Wheeler and Beverly Leazer. A memorial service will be held at 3 PM on Wednesday, September 3, 1997, in the chapel of SouthPark Funeral Home, 1310 N Main St., Pearland, TX. Revs. Roy Skeeter and Joseph Rohrer of Friendswood Friends Church will officiate. Inurnment will follow at SouthPark Cemetery.
