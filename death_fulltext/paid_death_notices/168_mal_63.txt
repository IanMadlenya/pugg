
        RIZKALLA-Sam, M.D. The entire New York Community Hospital family mourns the sudden passing of Sam Rizkalla, M.D. Dr. Rizkalla dedicated 33 years of service to our Hospital beginning as a young physician in 1967. He served as our Hospital's President of the Medical Board and remained active on the medical staff until his sudden passing. Our condolences to Dr. Rizkalla's family. George Weinberger, President, Board of Trustees Lin H. Mo, President and Chief Executive Officer
