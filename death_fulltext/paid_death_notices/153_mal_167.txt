
        ROSENBLUM-Sidney P. Beloved husband for 62 years of Sylvia Cantor. Dear father of Beth and Reuben Blumenthal, Pearl and Jerry Mann, Dr. Norman and Judith Rosenblum and Dr. Howard and Miriam Rosenblum. Brother of Birdie Silver, Jerome Rosenblum and the late Helen Winkler. Loving grandfather of twelve grandchildren and seven greatgrandchildren. He was a friend to everyone, recognized for his outgoing personality and a great sense of humor. Burial in Israel.
        ROSENBLUM-Sidney. Young Israel of Fifth Avenue records with sorrow the death of the father of our esteemed member Beth Blumenthal and extends to her and the family our heartfelt condolences.
