
        SHIPLEY-Carl Layton. Age 82, passed away at his home in Naples, Florida after a brief illness on August 25, 1998. He formerly had homes in Washington, D.C. and Charlottesville, Virginia. He is survived by his wife Barbara Baumann Shipley, and his two sons, Zachary Kane Shipley (Nicole) and Dr. Joshua Beale Shipley (Linda), and his six grandchildren Alexandra, Victoria, Genevieve, Nancy Kane, Anna, and Lillian Shipley. He leaves a stepdaughter, Melissa Baumann Siebert (Johannes) and stepson, Merrill John Baumann (Patricia) and their children Remsen, Peter, and Jared Baumann and Rafe Siebert. He was predeceased by his wife of forty-five years, Nancy Kane Shipley. We all loved him and will miss him terribly. There will be a memorial service in Washington D.C. in the early fall. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions in his name may be sent to Hospice of Naples, 1095 Whippoorwill Lane, Naples, Florida 34105. For further information Fuller Funeral Home 1625 Pine Ridge Road, Naples, Florida 34109.
