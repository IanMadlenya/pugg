
        LEVENTHAL--Jack, 94, of Delray Beach, FL, formerly of Roslyn, NY. Artist, naturalist, sportsman, thinker, seeker of the truth, and lover of the universe -A Renaissance Man. Handsome debonair and beloved husband of Rose (deceased), cherished and devoted father of Peter, Joan and Robin, kind and adored grandfather of Andrew (Nada) and Alexander Wynn, Gabriella Posner-Leventhal and great-granddaughter Alissar, honest and admired friend and father-in-law of Richard Wynn; honored and loved by cousins, nieces and nephews; respected and cared for by Lois Anderson and family. His compassion, resilience, energy, passion, and love lighted our way.
