
        THOMPSON-Mary Tyson. Age 92, died peacefully on November 27, 2001, at her home in New Jersey. A prolific and award-winning artist, she was a member of the American Watercolor Society and her work is in many collections. She was the wife of the late Kenneth W. Thompson, and is survived by her sons Kenneth S. Thompson and Loran T. Thompson and her granddaughter Kirsten D. Thompson. In keeping with her wishes, there will be no service.
