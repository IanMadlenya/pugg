
        GETZ-Harriette. On August 21, 2002. Beloved wife of the late Alden Getz. Loving mother of Deren and his wife Barbara. Cherished grandmother of Alec and Anders. Devoted sister of the late Harold Berger, the late Mildred Boltuch, and the late Emmanuel Berger. Loving aunt and great aunt. She was the co-founder of Waterman, Getz, Niedelman Advertising, and the Publicity Director for the American Booksellers Association. She will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved her. Service on Friday, August 23, 11:30AM at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street. NYC.
