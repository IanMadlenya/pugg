
        DAILEY--Evelyn H. Died June 11, 2003, peacefully at home in New York City; Five days short of a century of joy from her adoring family and friends, music, books and handcrafts. Predeceased by her husband Louis B. after a marriage of 63 years. She is survived by her twin sons Robert H., MD of Orinda, Ca. and Thomas H., MD of NYC; her daughters Sarah Diamond of Berkeley, Ca., and Susan Lohse of Portsmouth, NH. Contributions of remembrance may be directed to Concert Artists Guild, 850 7th Ave, NY, NY 10019. >>AD#
