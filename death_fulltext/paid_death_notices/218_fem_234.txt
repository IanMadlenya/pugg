
        GOING--Jeanne L. Of Garden City and Westhampton Beach, NY, on March 26, 2005, after a courageous battle with ovarian cancer. Beloved daughter of Irene and the late Edmund Connolly. Loving wife of James P. Going. Cherished mother of Garrett and his wife Jennifer, Drew and his wife Marilyn, Bryan and Devon. Also survived by her four grandchildren Garrett, Sean, Terrance and Paige, her sister Colleen Finegan, and many loving nieces and nephews. Jeanne was a talented interior designer and owner of Jeanne Leonard Interiors in Westhampton Beach. Her talent is exhibited in many homes and businesses throughout New York, the Hamptons and Palm Beach. She created beauty and harmony in everything she touched. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Avenue at 12th Street, Garden City, Monday, 7-9 pm, Tuesday, 2-4 and 7-9 pm. Mass of Christian Burial St. Joseph's Church, Garden City, Wednesday, 11 am. Interment to follow at Westhampton Cemetery. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Jeanne Going Memorial Fund for Ovarian Cancer Research, 22 Oneck Road, Westhampton Beach, NY 11978 appreciated.
