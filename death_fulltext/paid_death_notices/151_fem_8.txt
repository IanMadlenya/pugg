
        SMITH-Elisabeth Sperry. Died on July 20 in Washington, CT. after a long illness. Wife of the late Carleton Sprague Smith, she is survived by a daughter Damaris, her sonin-law John Rogers Horan, four grandchildren: Abigail Moore, Daniel Horan, Patrick Horan, Quincy Horan & two grandchildren. There will be a memorial service on Sunday, August 29, at Waldingfield Farm in Washington, CT. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made in her memory to the Washington Warren Visiting Nurse Association.
