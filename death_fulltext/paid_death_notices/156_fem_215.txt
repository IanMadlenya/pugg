
        GOMBOS-Maria Died January 22, 2000 at the age of 96. Mrs Gombos was born in Vienna, Austria and had an extended career in Europe, Australia and the USA with the world's famous cosmetics firm of Helena Rubenstein. She retired as President. She was married to Leslie Gombos who pre-deceased her. She had no children. In lieu of flowers donations to Zion Orphanage Inc., POB 11822, Cathedral Station, NY, NY 10025 would be appreciated. Kit and Michael Meves, Jacob, David, Esther and Anna from Kronberg, Germany
