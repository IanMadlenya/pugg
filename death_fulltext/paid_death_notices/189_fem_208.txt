
        OPALEK-Irene Lerner Solovay. Died peacefully on October 25 at her home in Yuma, AZ, at the age of 94. Beloved mother of Norman and Mark Solovay, mother-in-law of Eleanor and Kathryn Solovay. She was the very devoted and adored grandmother of Michael Solovay, Elizabeth Solovay Goldburg, Sondra Solovay and Matthew Solovay, and great-grandmother of Charlie, Samantha, Sarah, Robert, Harry and Jack. Services Monday, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Interment Mt. Judah Cemetery, Glendale, NY.
