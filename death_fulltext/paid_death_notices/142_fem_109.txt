
        ROMANOWITZ-Eva, 84. Beloved mother of Harry and wife, Sheila. Grandmother of Avra and Daniel, and devoted sister of Joseph Tenenbaum. Born in Tomaszow, Poland, she was a Holocaust survivor, a dear and cherished aunt, trusted friend and counsel to many. Services Sunday, November 15 at 10 A.M., Gutterman's/Parkside, 2576 Flatbush Avenue., Brooklyn, N.Y. Memorial contributions to United Jewish Federation, 1035 Newfield Ave., Stamford, CT 06905
