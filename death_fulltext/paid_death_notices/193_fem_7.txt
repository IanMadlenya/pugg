
        CHEIKEN-Betty. Of Rockland County, NY, formerly of Hollywood, FL, died January 28, 2003 at the age of 95. She was the wife of the late Louis Cheiken. She is survived by her daughter Carole Green; her grandchildren, Lisa and Michael Kossar, Donna and Michael Wolff; and five greatgrandchildren, Stuart, Kelly, Jordan, Alex and Zack.
