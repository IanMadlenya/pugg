
        DUBIN--Ruth. Beloved wife, mother and grandmother, passed away on November 7, 2005 at the age of 93 after a long illness. Her husband David died 14 years ago. She is survived by two children, Francine Lieberman, her husband Shelly, who in many ways was a real son to her, and a source of great comfort. Also her son Lawrence and his wife Susan and daughter-in-law Felice Dubin. Her first son, Paul Dubin died in 1999. She is also survived by nine grandchildren and two great grandchildren. The family requests that in lieu of flowers all donations be made to the Habitat For Humanity. Funeral November 9, Lakeside Memorial Cemetery, Miami, FL, 12:45 PM.
