
        VINCENT-Mitchell C. Retired Music Educator, Ardsley, NY died Thursday, April 11, 2002 at the Littleton Regional Hospital, Littleton, NH. Surviving are his wife, Jane Lazo Vincent, 3 children, Jacqueline Vincent, James Vincent, Virginia Foss, 3 stepdaughters, Sandra Shepard, Sarah Scott, Mary Shepard, and 11 grandchildren. A memorial service will be held Saturday, May 4, 2002, 2:00 PM, Town Meeting House, Sugar Hill, NH.
