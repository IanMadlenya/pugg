
        DUBIVSKY-Anne. At age 91, of Hartsdale NY, on December 5, after a long illness. Beloved sister of Barbara Dubivsky, devoted aunt to Carol Becker, and Jane Coene. Grand-aunt to Christopher, Susan, Michael, Stephen, Sally and Elizabeth. And great grand-aunt to ten. Services at St. Vladimir's Seminary, Crestwood, NY, Saturday, December 8. Interment Kensico Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. Predeceased by parents John Sr. and Barbara and brothers: Stephen, John, Peter, George, and Theodore. During her career she was associated with the Department of Justice, RCA, and Morgan Stanley.
