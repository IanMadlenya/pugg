
        MANDEL-Rose Blum, died October 1, 1998, age 95. Beloved mother of Mimi Frishman, Susan Prusky and Samuel Mandel. Sister of Irene Silver and Sophie Kronenberg. Loving grandmother and great-grandmother. An inspiration and force in all our lives. She leaves a legacy of charity and good deads. Memorial donations may be made to Hadassah and Brandeis University.
