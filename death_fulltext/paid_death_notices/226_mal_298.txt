
        HECTOR -- Louis J. Died peacefully on November 19th, one month before his 90th birthday. Predeceased by his first wife, Dorothy Anne Dooley, in 1973, he is survived by his wife, Nancy Bean Hector, his sons, Denis H. Hector (Joanna), William F. Hector, Louis J. Hector, Jr. (Hilde), and his daughter, Anne Hector; his stepchildren, Heyden White Rostow (C. Nicholas) and David F. White (Margaret), four grandchildren and three stepgrandchildren. A Memorial Mass will be held at St. Hugh Catholic Church, 3460 Royal Road, Coconut Grove, Florida, Friday, November 25 at 3:30 P.M. Contributions may be made to The Nancy B. and Louis J. Hector Fund at the Dade Community Foundation, 200 South Biscayne Boulevard, Suite 505, Miami, FL 33131 or Planned Parenthood of Greater Miami and the Florida Keys (PPGM/FK) Shehan Fund, C/O Northern Trust Bank, 700 Brickell Avenue, Miami, FL, 33153.
