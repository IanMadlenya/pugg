
        CHILCOTT-Ruth Hackney. Of New Vernon, New Jersey, at her residence on Monday, March 15, 1999. Wife of the late James Clemens Chilcott. Stepmother of Barbara C. Esten, Joan C. McKenna and Diane C. Carson; and aunt of Mary Goodrich Johnson and Lorraine G. Simonton. A memorial service will be held in the First Presbyterian Church, New Vernon, New Jersey on Thursday, March 18, 1999 at 2:00pm. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to Morristown Memorial Hospital, Morristown, NJ; First Presbyterian Church, New Vernon, NJ; The Dartmouth Medical School, Hanover, NH or The Seeing Eye, Morristown, NJ.
