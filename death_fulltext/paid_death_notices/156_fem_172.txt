
        ELLIOTT-Mary Angela Connaghan. Age 84, died on January 18, 2000 at Halifax General Hospital in Daytona Beach, FL. She was a resident of Florida since 1976 after living for many years in Ocean Township, NJ. Mrs. Elliott was born July 28, 1915 to Hannah (Sweeney) and Cornelius Connaghan in Motherwell, Scotland. She was raised in Falcarragh County Donagal, Ireland, and was a graduate of The Mercy Convent School in Strabane, Ireland. She emigrated to the United States in 1939 as a teacher and governess. During World War II, she was employed as a draftsman in the design of submarines. In 1943, she married John T. Elliott in New York City. In addition to her husband, she is survived by her six children, John C., of Fort Lauderdale, FL, James G., of Forest Hills, NY, Michael D., of Ormond By The Sea, FL, Brendan P., of New York City, NY, T.J., of Tarrytown, NY, and M. Irene Elliott-Hupe, of Eatontown, NJ. She is also survived by her thirteen grandchildren, three great grandchildren, and one brother, James Connaghan, of Lisburne County Antrim, Northern Ireland. Reposing at Walter B. Cooke-Kennedy Funeral Home, 117 West 72 Street, NYC, Thursday and Friday from 2-5 and 7-9 P.M. Mass of The Resurrection at Holy Trinty R.C. CHurch, Saturday 9:45 A.M. Interment Mary Rest Cemetery, Mahwah, NJ.
