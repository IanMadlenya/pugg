
        YARMON--Morton. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Morton Yarmon, beloved husband of Betty and a true philanthropic visionary. Morton's love of community and Jewish values were entwined in his support of such endeavors as hospitals and international Jewish organizations. We extend our deepest sympathies to Betty and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
        YARMON--Morton. My condolences to his family. He was a great guy and good neighbor. Stanley H. Birke
