
        NEWSUM-Fletcher A., 49. Of New York City on Monday, September 9, 2002, at his home. Survivors include his mother Barbara, brothers C.T. and Matt, sister Sherree, nephews Patrick, Daniel, Thomas and Nathan and nieces, Kaity and Lauren. His father, Thomas Newsum, preceded him in death. He was born August 17, 1953. He received his BA from Swarthmore College, a Master's Degree in Philosophy from Oxford University, and an MBA from Harvard. At the time of his death he was employed with Muzinich & Company as a portfolio manager. A memorial service will be held on Thursday, October 3, at 4:00 PM at The Church of St. Thomas More, 65 East 89th Street. In lieu of flowers, contributions in his memory may be made to the American Diabetes Association.
