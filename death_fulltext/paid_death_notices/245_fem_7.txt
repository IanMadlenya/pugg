
        BONNEVILLE--Nancy Schneider, 1953-2007. Beloved wife of David Bonneville, devoted mother of Rebecca and Noah Bonneville, cherished daughter of Leila and the late Joseph Schneider. Elder sister to Hallie, Emily and Adam. Nancy was a native New Yorker, and a graduate of SUNY Binghampton. Her pleasures included reading, gardening, traveling, fashion and interior design. She had a truly unique passion for teaching, mentoring and molding thousands of young minds during a long and distinguished career as an English teacher and Chair of the English department at Clayton Valley High School, in Concord, California. Nancy's dedication to her family, friends and students will always be remembered. Donations can be made to the Wellness Community, San Francisco East Bay, 3276 McNutt Avenue, Walnut Creek, California, 94957.
