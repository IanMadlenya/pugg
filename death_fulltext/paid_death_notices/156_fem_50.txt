
        WEINBAUM - Henrietta. Died peacefully on January 2. Born on July 4, 1893, she lived through three centuries. Funeral service at Knollwood Park Cemetery, Queens, Sunday, January 9 at 11 A.M. For information, call (212) 496-6682. Henrietta was a respected elocution, speech, and drama teacher, and she lives on in the hearts of three generations of students. To her family of devoted sisters, nieces, nephews, grandnieces, and grandnephews, Henrietta was their cherished ''Aunt Boopie,'' a warm, bubbly presence of affection and zest for life. She will be missed.
