
        SPITZ-Agatha Welsch Kessler. Beloved wife of Benjamin Spitz, mother of Lawrence Kessler, grandmother and great-grandmother. A teacher of English at Flushing High School for 30 years and a good companion of her many friends in her retirement. The funeral will be at 1 PM on Tuesday, June 19, at Parkside Funeral Home, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills, New York.
