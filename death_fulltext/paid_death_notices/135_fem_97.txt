
        GERSTELL-Joan Foley, 57. Passed away at her home in Beverly Hills on April 10, 1998. She is survived by her daughters Carolyn, Elizabeth and Alexandra; grandchildren Harrison and Daisy; and sisters Judith and Elizabeth. A memorial service will be held at 10:30AM, on Thursday, April 16, 1998 at St. Dominic's R.C. Church, Oyster Bay, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations to Memorial Sloan-Kettering, New York, N.Y.
