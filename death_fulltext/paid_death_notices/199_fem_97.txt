
        RUBEL--Tillie. It is with profound sadness that we mourn the passing of our much beloved Tillie Rubel, peacefully in her sleep at home on August 15, 2003, at the age of 103. She was a courageous and remarkable force in all of our lives. She was the dear wife of the late Louis Rubel; devoted mother and mother-inlaw of Estelle and Bert Hochman, Florence and the late Richard Koplow, and Marcia Nirenberg and the late Marvin Rubel; grandmother of Gail and Steven Hochman, Stacey Ben Menashe, Douglas and Deborah Koplow, and Meredith and Richard Rubel; and great-grandmother of Jacob and Elena Levi; Emily and Jonathan Hochman; Anna, Tal and Morad Ben Menashe; Eva, David and Savannah Koplow; and Skylar, Mia and Chloe Rubel. For over a century, Tillie enriched the lives of all who knew her, and she will be greatly missed. Funeral services Tuesday, August 19, 10 AM, at Weinstein-Garlick-Kirschenbaum, 1153 Coney Island Ave (at Ave H), Brklyn.
