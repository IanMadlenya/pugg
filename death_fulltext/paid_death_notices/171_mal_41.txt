
        KING-Lawrence P. The entire NYU Law School community mourns the loss of a beloved colleague, Larry King. A preeminent scholar and leader of the Bankruptcy bar, he gave tirelessly of himself to better the law. Never one to compromise quality in any dimension, he also was a gifted and inspiring teacher. And, most of all, he was a model of the best one expects of a colleague and friend. We will miss him. Our thoughts and prayers are with his family. Lester Pollack Chair of Board of Trustess NYU School of Law Foundation John Sexton, Dean
        KING-Hon. Lawrence P. The Mayor and Trustees of the Village of Saltaire are shocked and saddened by the passing of Larry King, former Mayor and Trustee and acting Village Justice. A man of great learning and even greater warmth, Larry honored us with his dedication and hard work, graced us with his warmth and wit. A steadfast friend, devoted public servant, kind and lovely gentleman. His spirit will forever enfuse the Village he loved so well and those of us who, in turn, loved and mourn him. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Martin M. Berger, Mayor
        KING-Prof. Lawrence. The Board of Trustees of the Community Synagogue Max D. Raiskin Center mourns the loss of our esteemed member and friend, Prof. Lawrence King. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Naftali Rotenstreich Jack Lebewohl, Chairman of the Board Brenda Pace, President
        KING-Lawrence. The teachers and students of the Hi-Tech Hebrew School mourn the passing of our dear friend and staunch supporter, Prof. Lawrence King. We extend our heartfelt condolences to the entire King family, his colleagues, his students and the many that will miss him. Rabbi Naftali Rotenstreich Executive Director
