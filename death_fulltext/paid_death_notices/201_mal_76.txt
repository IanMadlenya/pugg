
        ASPER--Israel. The Shalem Center of Jerusalem mourns the untimely loss of Israel Asper. A bold visionary and passionate defender of Israel, he worked tirelessly on behalf of the Jewish people around the world. We offer heartfelt condolences to Babs, Gail, David, Leonard, and the entire Asper family. May his memory serve as a blessing for his family and all of Israel.
