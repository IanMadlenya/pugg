
        COLTOFF-Kay. The Officers, Trustees and Staff of The Children's Aid Society join with the children of New York City and extend their heartfelt condolences and deepest sympathy to Philip Coltoff, the Society's Executive Director, and family upon the death of Kay Coltoff, beloved wife and mother, and cherished colleague. She was a friend of all children and families in the city. She touched each of us and tens of thousands in need with her courage, compassion and abiding humor. She is mourned, will be deeply missed and always remembered. Mrs. Stowe C. Phelps, Honorary Chairman John S. Griswold, Honarary Chairman Edward M. Lamont, Chairman Edgar R. Koerner, President
