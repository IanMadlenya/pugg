
        WEINBERG-Jay, born September 18, 1931, died July 8, 1997. Wonderful husband to Fay; devoted and loving father to Lorraine and Steve Goldsmith and Barbara Weinberg. Doting Zaydie to Jonathan, Zachary and Cara Goldsmith. Survivor of Auschwitz, a true Zionist who loved and believed in Israel. A good and honest friend with a beautiful and giving soul. We will miss his constant presence in our lives. He will live in our hearts always. All our love, Lorraine and Barbara
