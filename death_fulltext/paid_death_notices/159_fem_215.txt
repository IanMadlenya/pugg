
        LEBOWITZ-Shirley. The Executive Committee, Board and Staff of the Jewish Labor Committee note with sorrow the death of Shirley Lebowitz, mother of our dear friend and colleague, Lawrence (Arieh) Lebowitz. A devoted and loving wife to her husband, Sey, who passed away this past December, devoted mother to Arieh and Janet and Shaul Yamin, and loving grandmother of Daniella and Ilana, she leaves warm memories. We extend deepest condolences to all her relatives and friends. May her memory be a blessing. Shiva will be observed from Thursday evening, April 27, until Wednesday evening, May 3, but minyans only on Sunday, Monday and Tuesday at 7:30 PM. (Further details are available by calling (212) 477-0707.)
