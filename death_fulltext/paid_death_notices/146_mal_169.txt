
        LAURICELLA-John August. Of East Hampton, NY; formerly of Scotch Plains, NJ on February 16, 1999 in Altamonte, FL. Beloved husband of Lucille. Loving father of John, James and Kenneth. Adored grandfather of Katie, Michael, Daniel and Julia. A stock trader for 30 years, he was VP with Morgan Stanley for eight years before retiring in 1989. Earlier he had worked for the First Boston Corporation, Salomon Brothers and Merrill Lynch all in NYC. A funeral liturgy was held at 11AM at St. Helen's R.C. Church in Westfield, NJ on February 22, 1999. Entombment followed at Fairview Cemetery in Westfield. Memorial contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society or to the charity of your choice.
