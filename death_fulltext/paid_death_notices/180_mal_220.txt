
        SCHREIBER-Robert. The Board of Directors of United Jewish Federation of Greater Stamford, New Canaan and Darien, expresses its profound sorrow to the family of Robert Schreiber. Bob served as our second president and continued his dedication to Israel, the Jewish people and our community throughout his life. We will miss this fine and caring man. Ronald M. Gross, President Edith Samers, Exec. Vice Pres.
