
        ANATOLE--Lucille, 87, of Greenwich, CT, and formerly of Egremont, MA, and Lawrence, NY, died at her home on September 24th, 2006. She will always be remembered as Babs. Beloved wife of David (Tolia) for 66 years, loving mother of Amy Schwab and her husband Jim, and Mark Anatole and his wife Jodi. Devoted Grammy to Kate and Joanna Schwab, and Emily and Carter Anatole. Grammy, we will always remember the ''Happy Times''. Donations to the Alzheimer's Association would be appreciated.
