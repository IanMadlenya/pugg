
        WIENER-Lawrence A. Of New York City and beyond, February 17, 1916 to May 14, 2001. Artist, tennis player, bicyclist, baritone, real estate entrepreneur, philanthopist, master negotiator, and above all, family man. Beloved husband of Ann, brother of Helen, father of Larry, Consuelo, Elizabeth, Bobbin, stepfather of Tim, Jenifer, Chad, Gregg, Monique. Grandfather of Aran, Rachel, Matthew, Daniel, Tamara, Esma, and Max. Celebration of his life to be held this summer. We will miss him.
