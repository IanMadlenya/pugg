
        LIBERMAN-Rose C. Yeshiva University and its Women's Organization mourn the passing of a longtime, devoted friend and tireless worker, beloved wife of the late Norman Liberman and mother of Barnet (and Sue) Liberman and Gladys (and Dr. Robert) Richter. A stalwart advocate of Jewish education and the State of Israel, she strengthened YU through nearly seven decades of extraordinary service to the YUWO-as a founding member and vice president, Manhattan Chapter; national tablet chairman and, with her mother, the late Sara Rivka Cunin, founder of the YUWO Hall of Plaques in our Main Building; chairman, theater and opera benefits; and esteemed mentor of YUWO's national chairman. Sue Liberman and Gladys Richter carry on her noble efforts as YUWO vice presidents. Heartfelt condolences as well to grandchildren, great-grandchildren, and the entire family. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Dr. Norman Lamm, President Yeshiva University David S. Gottesman, Chairman Board of Trustees Dinah Pinczower Chairman, National Board, YUWO
        LIBERMAN-Rose. The Roshei Yeshivah, Staff and Students of the United Lubavitcher Yeshivoth extend their deep condolences to our benefactors and friends Barnet Liberman and Mrs. Gladys Richter on the passing of their righteous mother, a ''woman of valor.'' In the merit of her deeds, may the Almighty comfort the entire family among those who mourn Zion and Jerusalem. United Lubavitcher Yeshivoth
        LIBERMAN-Rose. The Mohegan Park Jewish Center notes with sorrow the passing of our former member and beloved mother of our devoted member, Gladys Richter. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Philip Gassel, President
        LIBERMAN - Rose. Yeshivat Sha'alvim sends heartfelt condolences to her son, member of our Board of Governors, Barnett and Sue Liberman. Hamakom Yenachemchem.
