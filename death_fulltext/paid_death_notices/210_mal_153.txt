
        KORNHAUSER--William, 79. Pioneering sociologist, professor at the University of California at Berkeley and advocate for democracy and social justice. Died in his sleep on July 3, 2004, at his home in Berkeley, CA. Much loved father of Anne Kornhauser of New York City and devoted brother of Ruth Zubrensky of Milwaukee, WI. A private memorial service will be held. Memorial donations may be made to the United Farm Workers or the National Asthma Center.
