
        WEISBARTH-Victoria on October 3rd. Adored wife of the late Morris Weisbarth, and twin sister of the late Lillian Boshnack. Our wholehearted sympathy to her cherished and devoted daughters and sons-in-law, Berissa and Jack Frame, Ellen and Michael Azaria, and her precious grandchildren, John Frame and Mathew Azaria. We could always count on her unflagging loyalty and sunny disposition to brighten our days. Her love lives on. Dorothy Lang, Murray and Adele Kleiman and Families
