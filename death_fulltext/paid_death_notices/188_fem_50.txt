
        HAWKINS-Sylvia. 90, longtime resident of Redding, CT, died on September 5, at the Wilton Meadows Nursing Home. Mrs. Hawkins was the daughter of Clifford and Beatrix Hawthorne Smyth. Her father was the founder and longtime editor of the New York Times Book Review. On her mother's side, she was a great-granddaughter of Nathaniel Hawthorne. Memorial contributions may be made to the Mark Twain Library in Redding, CT.
