
        BARTRAM--Mary ''Teddy'' Sheppard died at her home in Hobe Sound, Florida on Monday, June 13, 2005. Formerly of Greenwich, Connecticut and Edgartown, Massa chusetts, she was born Mary Sesso Sheppard on July 21, 1908 to Josephine and William Sheppard of New Jersey. She married the late J. Burr Bartram on April 16, 1929. An active volunteer, she devoted her time to raising her family and helping the WW II efforts by serving with the Red Cross. She also donated her time to the Greenwich Hospital and Christ Church in Greenwich, Connecticut. Mrs. Bartram was an honorary member of the Indian Harbor Yacht Club and a member of the New York Yacht Club, both of which her husband, J. Burr Bartram served as Commodore. ''Teddy'', as she was known, is survived by her son, J. Burr Bartram, Jr., (Joe) and her daughter, Nina Griswold. She is also survived by three grandchildren and seven great grandchildren. A kind and loving woman, she will be remembered fondly by all who knew her. Visitation will be held at her residence from 10-5 P.M., on Wednesday, June 15th. A memorial service in her honor will be held at a later date in Hobe Sound and Greenwich, Connecticut. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to Jupiter Island Clinic, 1 Estrada Rd., Hobe Sound, FL, 33455 or the Humane Society of the Treasure Coast, Inc., 4100 SW Leighton Farm Ave., Palm City, FL 34990.
