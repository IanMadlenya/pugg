
        NELKIN-Les. The Board of Columbia/Barnard Hillel mourns with deepest sorrow the untimely passing of Les Nelkin. As a Founder of the Kraft Center for Jewish Student Life, the Nelkin Family Foundation has been a devoted supporter. Our heartfelt condolences to Les's parents, Ruth and Harold Nelkin, and to his sister, Amy. May his memory be a blessing. Prof. Robert Pollack, President Earle W. Kazis, Chairman Arthur O. Sulzberger, Chairman, Executive Committee Rabbi Charles H. Sheer, Jewish Chaplain Joanne Ben Avi, Director of Development
        NELKIN-Leslie. The National Jewish Outreach Program (NJOP) mourns the untimely passing of its beloved friend and devoted supporter, Les Nelkin, a paradigm of faith and courage and the possessor of unquenchable hope and optimism in the face of his illness. At NJOP's Fifth Annual Dinner in February, 1999, Les was honored with his parents, Ruth and Harold Nelkin, and his sister, Amy, Yibadlu L'chaim, for playing a crucial role in NJOP's efforts to assure a bright Jewish future for American Jewry. Condolences to the entire family. Yehi Zichro Baruch. Rabbi Ephraim Z. Buchwald, Dir.
