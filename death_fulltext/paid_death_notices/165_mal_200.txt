
        WINBURN-Richard Gardner. 82, died on October 12, 2000 in Oklahoma City, OK. Born on July 31, 1918, he moved from NYC to Oklahoma City in September of 1998. Dick was the fourth generation of a family of photographers. He worked for Neiman Marcus, The New York Times, and Manufacturers Hanover Trust. A veteran of World War II, he served in the U.S. Army Air Force. Preceded in death by his mother Gertrude Gardner and his father Jay Te. Survived by his sons Richard Winburn Jr., of Bedford, TX, Charles D. Winburn and family of Oklahoma City; his former wife Mary Melton Winburn of Chickasha, OK; brothers Jay Te Winburn and family of Brandon, Manitoba, Canada, Walter Winburn and family of Sharon, CT; George Winburn and family of Corpus Christi, TX. He will be fondly remembered for his keen wit and ability to make people laugh from presidents to children.
