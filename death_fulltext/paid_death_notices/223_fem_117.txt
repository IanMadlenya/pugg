
        CUDDIHY--Joan G. On August 15, 2005, of Bay Shore, L.I., after a courageous battle with brain cancer. Beloved wife of the late Paul R. Cuddihy, Jr. Devoted mother of Paul R. Cuddihy III and the late Hope Cuddihy. Dear sister of Carole G. Baxter of Santa Monica, California, and the late Clare G. Frost. Cherished aunt of her many nieces and nephews. Joan was a highly accomplished Interior Designer of residential and commercial interiors. She coordinated and participated in numerous Designer Showcase Homes for the benefit of local charities. Joan's talent in design were shared with the additional interests in her life. She was a proud member of the Bay Shore Chamber of Commerce and an active contributing member of the Bay Shore Beautification Committee. Her devotion continued as an active parishioner of St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore. Reposing at the Fredrick J. Chapey & Sons West Islip Funeral Home, 1225 Montauk Highway (1/4 mile west of the Robert Moses Cswy.). Celebration of the Liturgy of Christian Burial St. Patrick's R.C. Church, Bay Shore, L.I., Thursday, 10:00 AM. Interment St. Patrick's Cemetery, Bay Shore, L.I. Visiting Wednesday, 2-4:30 and 7-9:30 PM. The family suggests donations be made in Joan's memory to Hospice Care Network, 99 Sunnyside Blvd., Woodbury, NY 11797.
