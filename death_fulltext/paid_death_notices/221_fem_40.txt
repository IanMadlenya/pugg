
        LAURENS--Grace. On June 2, 2005. Beloved wife of the late Stephen, devoted mother of Peter, dear sister of George, admired sister - in - law of Toshiko and loving aunt of William and Tony Elison. She had an eventful life that spanned a world war and two continents and will be sorely missed by her relatives, friends and colleagues. A memorial service will be held 2 PM, Sunday, June 12, 2005, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 St.
