
        GLOVER-Stuart B. (Bus). Attorney, at age 92. A resident of Scarsdale for over fifty years, died at his home Feb. 4, 1998. Beloved husband of the late Peggy Glover. Devoted father of James, Richard, Amy, Elaine and Kathryn. Beloved grandfather of Eric, Rachel, Lori and Peter. Funeral services will be held at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, Friday at 1:30PM. Interment to follow at Sharon Gardens, Valhalla, NY. Memorial contributions may be made to the Tourette's Syndrome Association, 42-40 Bell Blvd., Bayside, NY 11361, or WJCS, 141 N. Central Ave., Hartsdale, NY 10530, or Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester, 2125 Westchester Ave. East, Rye, New York 10580, or a charity of your choice.
        GLOVER-Stuart B. (Bus). The partners, associates and staff of Parker Chapin Flattau & Klimpl, LLP mourn the passing of their beloved friend and a founder of the firm. A distinguished practitioner in banking asset based lending and equipment leasing, he was counsel and advisor to a long list of valued firm clients. He was a thoughtful mentor, never too busy to listen and help. His concern and respect for others, his gentle manner and upbeat attitude were always evident. He was admired and respected by all of us and we will all miss him. We extend our deepest sympathy to the family. Parker Chapin Flattau & Klimpl, LLP
