
        HEINTSCHEL-Donald E., Msgr. The Diocese of Toledo, Ohio sadly announces the death of one of its long-time priests, internationally renowned, Canonist and lawyer, Msgr. Donald E. Heintschel. Heintschel also served as Associate General Secretary of the US Catholic Conference of Bishops, 1982-1989. His funeral will be 11 AM, August 28, Monday, at Queen of Peace Chapel, Sisters of Saint Francis Motherhouse, Sylvania, Ohio.
