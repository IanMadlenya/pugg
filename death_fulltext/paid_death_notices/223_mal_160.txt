
        POSNER--Donald. Ailsa Mellon Bruce Professor at the Institute of Fine Arts, NYU. He served on the faculty from 1962, and as Deputy Director 1983-2002. The Institute mourns the loss of a brilliant art historian, dedicated teacher, and dear friend, and extends heartfelt condolences to his daughter Anne Posner, sister Phyllis Levine, and companion Joan Leibovitz. Mariet Westermann, Dir Judy Steinhardt, Board Chair Institute of Fine Arts, NYU
