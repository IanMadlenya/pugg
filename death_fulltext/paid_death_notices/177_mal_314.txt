
        ANSCOMBE - Francis John. Professor (Emeritus) of Statistics, Yale University, died on October 17 in New Haven. He was 83. Born in Elstree, England, he studied Mathematics at Trinity College, Cambridge, taking First Class Honours in 1939. He taught at Cambridge, Princeton, and the University of Chicago before moving to Yale, where he founded the Department of Statistics in 1963. He was the beloved husband of Phyllis and loving father of daughter Elizabeth Valeika and sons Francis, Anthony, and Frederick. He also leaves his brother Anthony and five granddaughters. Funeral service will be at 10:00 Thursday at St. John's Church, 400 Humphrey Street, New Haven.
