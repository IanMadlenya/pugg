
        FERRANTI--Marie nee Giresi, age 96 of Port Washington on May 13, 2005. Former Director of Promotions and an employee of The NY Times for 48 years. Devoted wife of the late Joseph, loving sister of Mae, Josephine, Jane and the late James, Antionette and Frank. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Marie was greatly loved and will be missed by all who knew her. Donations in her memory to Good Shepherd Hospice, 528 Mineola Avenue, Carle Place, New York 11514, would be greatly appreciated.
        FERRANTI--Marie. The New York Times records with deep sorrow the passing of Marie Ferranti, associated with The Times from 1925 until retiring in 1974.
