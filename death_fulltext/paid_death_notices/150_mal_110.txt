
        KAPLAN-Solomon I. Of Hampton, NH, on July 2, 1999. Husband of Beatrice (Kantor), grandfather of Jody Smith and his wife Susan of Leominster, MA; brother of Robert Kaplan, his wife Evelyn, and of Ada Nogee, all of Pompano Beach, FL; and several nieces and nephews. Private services will be held at the Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. He worked all his life in the shoe business and was a member of the 210 Foundation and the Harvard Club. Donations may be made to the 210 Foundation, 56 Main Street, Watertown, MA. 02472.
