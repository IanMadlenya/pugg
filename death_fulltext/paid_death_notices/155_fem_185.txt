
        RUBIN-Fannie. Beloved wife of the late Arthur, and the late George Dubrow. Cherished mother of Helene Grossman, Leonard Dubrow, the late Irwin and Barbara Dubrow. Devoted grandmother of Gregory, Thomas, Elizabeth, Barbara, Joanne, Crystal, Bliss, Adam and Hope. Great-grandmother of Nathan, George and Rachel. Services were held December 19.
        RUBIN-Fannie. The residents and staff of The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale mourn with sorrow the passing of Fannie Rubin, the mother of our esteemed colleague, Helene D. Grossman. We extend condolences to Helene and her family, and hope that the many special memories of her mother will serve as a source of comfort. Daniel Reingold Executive Vice President The Hebrew Home for the Aged at Riverdale
