
        SHAPIRO-Lilly Langsam. Died on January 5th, 1998, leaving a void in the lives of all she touched. A Hunter College graduate, she was a brave, intelligent and generous woman. Predeceased by her sister, Helen Schleien. She is survived by a brother, Kenneth Langsam, devoted daughter, Susan Garfield, two sons, Robert and William Shapiro and 6 grandchildren.
