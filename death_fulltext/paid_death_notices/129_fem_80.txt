
        TANENBAUM-Mary Mayer, for 59 years beloved wife of Charles J. Tanenbaum, and loving mother of Ann Tanenbaum King. Services will be held at Frank E.         TANENBAUM-Mary. The Board of Governors and members of Sunningdale Country Club are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed member, Mary Tanenbaum. We extend our deepest sympathy to her husband, Charles and to the entire family. William Ettelson, President Michael Roth, Secretary
