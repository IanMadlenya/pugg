
        WOLFF-Lester, MD. Passed away on March 26th, 2001, peacefully at home. A graduate of the University of Chicago Medical School, specializing in ear, nose and throat surgery. A pioneer in the art of reconstructive nasal plastic surgery. He served with honor in the Korean War in the Army Medical Corp. Upon his return from Korea, he set up one ofthe most successful nasal plastic surgery practices in Manhattan. He was a dedicated surgeon and physician who loved his work. He is survived by his loving wife of 50 years, Marilyn (Dolly) and many other loving family relatives and friends. He will be missed and remembered by all whom he touched. Funeral services private by request of the family.
