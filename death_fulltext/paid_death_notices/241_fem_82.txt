
        LOBELL--Eleanor, on January 15. Beloved daughter of the late Bernard and Sarah, sister of Joseph and Lillian. A devoted aunt and cherished cousin, she was treasured by Edna, Eric, Alan, Jane, all her family, and her many friends who knew her for her kindness, generosity, quiet strength, fidelity and love. Graduate of Sarah Lawrence, playwright and advocate for the ill and unfortunate. Eleanor loved and was devoted to her family and friends, and we love her still. We miss her very much but she will live in our hearts. Memorial service on Saturday, March 3rd, 1PM at Redden's Funeral Home, 325 West 14th St., Manhattan, NY.
