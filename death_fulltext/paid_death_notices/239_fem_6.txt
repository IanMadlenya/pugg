
        YEAGER--Barbara, of Bronxville, NY, died on November 29th, after a long battle with cancer. She is survived by her husband, George, a son Scott of Rye, NY, a daughter Kerry Yeager Stevens of Darien, CT, her brother Robert Gow of Houston, Texas and seven grandchildren. A celebration of her life will be held at the Reformed Church of Bronxville on Saturday, December 9th at 2 pm In Lieu of flowers, contributions can be made to the Barbara G. Yeager Memorial Music Fund, The Reformed Church, 198 Pondfield Road, Bronxville, NY, 10708.
