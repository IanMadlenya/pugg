
        WILLIAMS-Gwendolyn M. Lutheran Medical Center mourns the loss of Gwendolyn Williams, a dedicated member of our Board of Trustees who cared deeply about our mission to serve our patients and our community. We offer our heartfelt condolences to her husband Claude Oliver and all of her family. Her forthright advocacy, willingness to serve and gregarious personality will be missed by all of us. Dominic J. Lodato, President Howard Smith, Chairman Board of Trustees
