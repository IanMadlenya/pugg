
        KEEGAN--James F. Died May 14th, 2005. NYPD (retired), 1952-1980. Member of the Board Patrolman's Benevolent Association, 1961-1980, and PBA Brooklyn South Trustee, 1964-1980. Affectionately known as ''Mr. PBA'' for his service and dedication to assisting police officers. He worked on behalf of police officers as the PBA Liaison to CCRB, 1980-1997. Served in the Army during WWII as a Master Sergeant. Husband of the late Rita McNulty. Survived by children James, Kathie, Robert, John and Thomas, and numerous grandchildren. Visitation on Monday and Tuesday, May 16th and 17th, between 2-5 and 7-9 pm, at McLaughlin Funeral Home, Third Avenue and 97th Street, Brooklyn, NY. Mass of Christian Burial Wednesday, May 18th, 10 am, at OLPH Church, Fifth Avenue at 59th Street, Brooklyn, NY.
