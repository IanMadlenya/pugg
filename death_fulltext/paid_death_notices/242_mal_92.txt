
        LIEBERBAUM--Sheldon, on March 7, 2007. Husband of Carol. Father of Suzie and Jonny. Pappa Shelly to Rachel, David, Kyle, Matthew and Eric. Brother of Michael. Father-in-law of Stephen and Kimberly. A true mensch who was loved by his friends, family and fine doggies and who loved them back in double doses. Master of the one-liner, author of epic comedic poems, victor over countless landlocked salmon, tennis player whose game was feared from Boro Park to Emerald Hills. He will be lovingly remembered.
