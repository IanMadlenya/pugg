
        STIEFEL-Edward Henry. On May 23, 1999. A retired partner of the law firm of Milbank, Tweed, Hadley & McCloy LLP. Two daughters survive, Barbara Anne Rebell of Seneca, S.C. and Margaret Jeanne Bergman of Ridge, N.Y., as well as five grandchildren and six great grandchildren. He was predeceased by his wife of fifty-nine years, Anna Lucille Tibbals Stiefel.
