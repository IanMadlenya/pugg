
        EILEN-Adele. Died Sunday, October 19, 1997. Adele valiantly fought Parkinson's Disease for 31 years and finally lost. She will be greatly missed by her husband, Arthur; daughter and son-in-law, Sherry and Henry Krostich, and grandchildren, Seth and Wendy, and sister, Evelyn Gold, and many, many friends and relatives who loved beautiful Adele. Services will be held at Riverside Nassau North Chapel at 55 North Station Plaza, Great Neck, NY, at 2 PM, Wednesday, October 22nd.
