
        COLEMAN-Marian B. On December 3, 1998. Wife of the late Harold S. Coleman; sister-in-law to Betty S. Buechler and the late Ruth C. Krause. Also survived by two nephews and several cousins. Mrs. Coleman was a long time member of the National Council of Jewish Women, the Arthritis Foundation, Temple Emanu-El and the Harmonie Club. Funeral services private. Contributions in her memory may be made to a charity of your choice.
        COLEMAN-Marian B. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Marian B. Coleman. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
