
        CLICK-Philip. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Queens Borough Public Library mourn the passing of a dear friend and colleague. He was a tireless advocate for literacy and strengthened Queens Library with his wisdom and leadership. He served as a library Trustee from 1973 until his death and was Board President in 1983 and 1984. He will be deeply missed. Our deepest condolences to his family. George Stamatiades, Pres. Queens Library Board Gary E. Strong, Library Dir. and the Board of Trustees
