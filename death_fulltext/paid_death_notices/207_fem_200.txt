
        TIFFANY -- Winifred (nee Teichgraeber). On April 23, 2004. Weetzie was a longtime resident of Pelham, NY, lately of Southbury, CT. Graduate of Vassar College in 1947. Survived by her husband Robert M. Tiffany. A beloved mother and grandmother, she is survived by Susan T. Pugh, Dr. Peter N. Tiffany, Molly T. Cross, and five grandchildren. Services Munson-Lovetere Funeral Home of Southbury.
