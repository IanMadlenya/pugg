
        CLARK-Lon. On October 2, 1998. Beloved husband of Michelle. Loving father of Lon Jr. and Stephen and step-father of Timothy and George Morse. Grandfather of Lon III. Dear brother of Gerald and Eduard. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday 2-5 and 7-9 PM, and Tuesday, 2-5 PM. Interment private. Memorial services to be announced. Contributions in his memory may be made to the Episcopal Actors' Guild, 1 East 29 St, NY NY 10016.
        CLARK-Lon C. The Episcopal Actors' Guild of America mourns the death of its esteemed Executive Secretary Emeritus, Lon C. Clark. Honored last year by the creation of the Lon C. Clark Scholarship Fund, his devoted 22-year service enabled the Guild to continue forever.
