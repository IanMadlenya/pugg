
        HEYMANN--Henry. Formerly of Henry Haymann Associates, a designer and consultant to the men's clothing industry for over 40 years, passed away on February 13. A WWII veteran, recipient of the Silver Star and Purple Heart. He was active in the German American Soccer League, the Boy Scouts and the American Arbitration Association. He is survived by his loving wife Shirley, his sons Mark and Ken, their wives Dawn and Susan, his grandchildren Madison, Harrison and Alexandra, and his extended family, Tim, Jane and Nicole. He will be missed.
