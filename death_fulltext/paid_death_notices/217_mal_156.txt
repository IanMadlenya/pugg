
        CHARKIN--Norton, age 93, died peacefully on February 6, 2005. Beloved husband for 70 years of Selma. Adored father of Eileen Weiler Judell (Robert) & the late Joy Ganney. Cherished grandfather of Pamela Weiler Grayson (Douglas), Andrew Weiler (Cecilia), and Ryan Ganney. Devoted great-grandfather of Deanna, Jamie, Erica and Marcus. He was the President of the Mayflower Wallpaper Company for 45 years. He was dedicated to Jewish philanthropies and was the recipient of many honors and awards. He was also President of the Lincoln Park Jewish Center. He will be deeply missed by his adoring family and friends. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Lincoln Park Jewish Center, 311 Central Park Avenue, Yonkers, New York 10704, or a charity of your choice.
