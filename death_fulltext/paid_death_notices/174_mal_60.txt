
        HUPKA-Robert. July 3, 2001. Robert was a renowned photographer known for his photo essays of Arturo Toscanini and Michaelango's Pieta. Robert was also a lifelong friend to Manhattanville College in Purchase, NY. He is survived by his sister Marie Parkinson of Devon, England, and nephews, cousins and many dear friends. Interment Manhattanville College Cemetery, Purchase, NY. In lieu of flowers donations to The Cabrini Medical Center, Cardiac Unit, 227 E. 19 St, NYC 10003; or The St Paul Apostle Church, Homeless Shelter Program, 405 W. 59 St, NYC 10019.
