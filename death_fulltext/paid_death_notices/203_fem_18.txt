
        STRAUS--Carol Herschel. 66, of Austin, TX died peacefully on Monday, December 1, 2003. She is survived by her husband, Tom Straus; children, James Straus and wife, Lisa, Phebe Brecher and husband, James, Stephen Straus and Douglas Straus and wife, Ann; six grandchildren; brother, James Andrew Herschel and wife, Susan; and sister, Elisabeth Gambler. A memorial service will be held at 2:00 P.M., Saturday, December 6, 2003 at Westlake United Methodist Church, 1460 Red Bud Trail, Austin, TX. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made in Carol's honor to Hospice Austin, 4107 Spicewood Springs Road, Suite 100, Austin, TX, 78759 or Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY, 10021, Attn: Joan Roseman, for pancreatic cancer research. For information contact Cook-Walden Funeral Home, 6100 N. Lamar Blvd., Austin, TX, 78752, (512)454-5611.
