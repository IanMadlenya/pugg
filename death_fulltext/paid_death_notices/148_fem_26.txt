
        BIRNBAUM-Mildred. 84. Of Boca Raton, FL, died Wednesday, May 5 at the Boca Raton Community Hospital. Born in New York City to Phillip & Beatrice Behrman. Mrs. Birnbaum wintered in Boca Raton from New York and Connecticut before permanently moving here 12 years ago. Mrs. Birnbaum is the daughter of world-famous doll maker Beatrice Alexander of the Alexander Doll Company in New York City. Mildred was a renowned artist exhibiting her paintings in Palm Beach, Connecticut, and New York and was featured in Who's Who in American Art. She was preceded in death by her husband Richard E. Birnbaum in 1992. She is survived by her loving son William and his wife Kathleen of Greenwich, CT and beloved granddaughter Stephanie Birnbaum. A Funeral Service will be held at 11:00 am on Friday, May 7 at the Boca Raton Mausoleum with entombment to follow. The family requests expressions of sympathy be made to the Make A Wish Foundation, P.O. Box 17377, Fort Lauderdale, FL 33318.
