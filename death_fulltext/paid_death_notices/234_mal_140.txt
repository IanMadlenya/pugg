
        BOGEN--Irving, 90, of Manhattan died Thursday July 6th, 2006 after a long illness. A free lance commercial artist and master calligrapher, his art work for Random House, Delacorte, Knopf, and many others graced the covers of many first editions, including Truman Capote's ''In Cold Blood''. He is survived by his wife, Evelyn; his sister and brother-in-law Pearl and Sol Parnell; three nephews, Peter Parnell, Bobby Sandler and John Sandler and his grandniece Lucy Sandler.
