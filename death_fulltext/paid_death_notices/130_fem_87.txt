
        SAYERS-Lillian. Beloved wife of Theodore, loving mother of Barbara and Mark, cherished grandmother of Lauren and Nicole Braun, surviving sister May Green, of Los Angeles. Lillian Sayers was a pioneer in the Intimate apparel industry and held over ten patents. She was the Chief Designer for Exquisite Form Industries, Bali Bras, and Lillyette. Mrs. Sayers worked for the Community AIDS Society and The 62nd Street Association. Lillian was a unique and vibrant woman and will be missed for her extraordinary inspiration, creativity, hospitality and generosity to her family and friends. Funeral services will be held on Thursday, Nov. 13 at 12:00 Temple Emanuel, 65th Street and Fifth Avenue in the Beth El Chapel. In lieu of flowers, donations may be sent to The Lung Cancer Research Fund at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Hospital, NYC.
