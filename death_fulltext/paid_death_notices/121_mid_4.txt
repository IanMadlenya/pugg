
        HELWIG-Florence M., on February 5, 1997 at her Brooklyn home, age 95 years, graduated from Wellesley College (1925) after attending Packer Collegiate Institute where she later taught. Survived by a brother, Edward T. Helwig, his wife, Christine and their 16 descendants. Services are private. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to the Wellesley College Scholarship Fund.
