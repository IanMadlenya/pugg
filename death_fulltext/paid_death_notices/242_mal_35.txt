
        RITTENBERG--Arthur. A WWII combat Marine and a dedicated Red Sox fan. Our dear witty friend, we'll miss you Art. Judi and Jack Howard and Gloria
        $(6$)RITTENBERG--Arthur M., 81, of Piermont, NY, passed away on Friday, March 2, 2007. He was born on May 3, 1925 in Boston, MA. He is survived by his loving wife of 58 years, Louise and, children, Cindy, Gerry and Margie and their spouses and, grandchildren Greg, Keith, Andrew James, Craig (Courtney) and Garrett, sister, Barbara Roth and husband Philip. He graduated from Boston University and started his career with Prentice - Hall Publishing Company lasting 35 years and then started his own consulting company. He proudly served in the U.S. Marine Corps on Iwo Jima WWII, member of Piermont V.F.W., he was a baseball coach with Piermont P.A.L. for 30 years, saw opening of New Piermont Library dream, mentor to countless young people, friend to all. He touched many lifes and leaves behind a spirit of joy and a love of life that will live on forever. Services will be 1:30 PM Monday, March 5, 2007 at Temple Sholom, 385 Howland Avenue, River Edge, NJ.
