
        COMFORT-George V. Philip Morris Companies Inc. notes with sorrow the passing on March 27, 2000, of retired Philip Morris Board member George V. ''Dan'' Comfort. Mr. Comfort served as a member of the Philip Morris Board of Directors from 1971 until his retirement in 1982. He was named Director Emeritus in April 1982. His colleagues on the Board remember him as a highly dedicated and effective Board member who made significant contributions to the Board, its membership and to the Company. The Philip Morris Board and employees extend their deepest sympathy to his devoted family.
