
        HALPER-Ann Barnes. Book lover, died at home in New Hartford, CT, of glioblastoma. Attended City & Country '46, New Lincoln '50, Radcliffe '54. Predeceased by her husband Joseph Halper. Survived by her daughters Margaret Bloodgood and Sarah Halper, their husbands Chip Bloodgood and Tor Krogius, sister Nancy Barnes and brother Andy Barnes, grandchildren Max and Emma, and Mao.
