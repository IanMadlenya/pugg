
        SARETSKY-Philip. His family mourns the loss of their beloved father, grandfather and greatgrandfather at the age of 99 years. Philip was a husband of 72 years to his late wife Estelle and is survived by his children Ruth Brooks, Muriel and Mel Brown, Erwin Raffle, 8 grandchildren and 16 great-grandchildren. Born in NYC in 1897. He was a Shriner and a Mason for over 70 years. Services will be held in Los Angeles.
