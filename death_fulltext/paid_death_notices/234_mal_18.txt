
        GITTELSON--Stanley, M.D. Peacefully on June 29, 2006. Beloved husband to Joan (nee Derris) for 52 years. Loving father to Harry, Glenn and Lori, Anne, Madeline and Gary. Adored grandpa to Joseph, Zachary, Conner, Caroline, Morgan, Paige and Heather. Devoted brother to Dr. Arthur and Genie, the late Dr. Roger and Fern and Dr. David Derris. Loving uncle, great-uncle, cousin and loyal friend to many. Dedicated pediatrician who for 40 years cared for the children of the Merrick-Bellmore communities. Accomplished physician, scientist, patented inventor, musician and golfer with a sense of humor to match. An inspiration to all who knew him, he will be missed and never forgotten. Services Sunday, 10 A.M. at Gutterman's Inc., 175 N. Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre.
