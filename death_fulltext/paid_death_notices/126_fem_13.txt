
        HOPE-Hope Douglas. Beloved wife of the late Norman L. Hope after a brief illness. Graduated from St. Mary's Academy, Peekskill, N.Y. Longtime volunteer at Roosevelt Hospital; member for 72 years at St. Bartholomew's Church. She loved her name, Hope Hope. Funeral services Wednesday, July 2, 11 AM, St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Ave at 50th St. Memorial contributions to St. Bartholomew's or Roosevelt Hospital, 1000 10th Ave, NY 10019.
