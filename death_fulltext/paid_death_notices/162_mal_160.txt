
        VALLES-James M., of Orange, CT, died July 13, 2000. Beloved husband of Renke; father of Anne, James Jr. and Andrew; and grandfather of Nikki, Matthew and William. His love of people, curious mind and passion for sports were apparent to all who knew himfrom his days at The Berkshire School ('50) and Yale University ('55) through his successful sales and marketing career in the textile industry. His wonderful smile, generous heart and endless supply of jokes will be greatly missed, but deeply cherished by his family and friends. A memorial service in the Celentano Funeral Home, 424 Elm St, New Haven, CT, Thursday July 20 at 3PM. Contributions may be made to Connecticut Hospice, 61 Burban Dr, Branford, CT 06405 or the charity of one's choice.
