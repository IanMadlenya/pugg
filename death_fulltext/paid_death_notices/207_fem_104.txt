
        RUBIN--Beatrice D. A major philanthropist of The Brookdale University Hospital and Medical Center, Beatrice continued the legacy of her late husband, Samuel, who was Treasurer of the Board of Trustees. Along with her son Lowell, a Brookdale Trustee, and her daughter-in-law, the Hon. Alice Fisher Rubin, she received Brookdale's distinguished honor, The Brookdale Medal, at the 2002 annual Tribute Ball. Our sincere condolences to the entire Rubin family. Alvin I. Kahn, M.D., FACP Chairman, Board of Trustees David P. Rosen, President and CEO Richard J. Fogler, M.D., FACS Sr. Vice President and Chief Medical Officer
