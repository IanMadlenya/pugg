
        KNOTHE--Stannard B., 82, died peacefully on June 27. Predeceased by his wife, Doreen Drexel Knothe. Survived by his sister, Nancy, his children, Peter, Wendy and Jeff, and his grandchildren, Christopher, Leah, Anna and Katherine. Served in the U.S. Naval Armed Guard in WW II from 1942 through 1946. He will be deeply missed.
