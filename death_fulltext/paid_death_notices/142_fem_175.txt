
        WAGNER-Victoria. The Board of Trustees and the Ethical Culture Fieldston School note with sadness the death of Victoria Wagner, devoted and vital member of our school community for almost 40 years. Teacher, principal of the Ethical Culture School, and director of the Ethical Culture Fieldston School, she is remembered as a beloved and legendary educator. We extend heartfelt condolences to her daughter Eve Barnett (Fieldston class of '52) and her entire family. The family has requested donations in Mrs. Wagner's memory be made to Ethical Culture Fieldston School, 33 Central Park West, New York, NY 10023-6099. Sara E. Nathan and S. Donald Sussman Co-Chairs, Board of Trustees Joseph P. Healey, Ph.D. Head of School
