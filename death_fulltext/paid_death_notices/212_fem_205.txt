
        LERNER--Lila Stein, 75, died peacefully September 26, 2004. Adored wife of the late Robert Lerner died January 28, 2004. She is survived by her cherished family-daughter Susan and partner Sandy; son David, daughter-in-law Marisa and granddaughter Donielle; daughter Lori; son Andrew; and her beloved dog Sweetie. Gravesite service will be held at Cedar Park Cemetery, Paramus, NJ on September 28 at 2pm.
