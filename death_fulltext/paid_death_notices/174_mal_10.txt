
        LUEDERS-Louie, Jr. Died June 26. He enjoyed a distinguished career with RCA, Lockheed Electronics and Dataproducts Corporation developing advanced data and voice communications systems. He graduated from the Citadel in 1955 and from UCLA Executive Program in 1967. He is survived by three children Maia Dixon of Chagrin Falls, OH, Ashley Lueders of Brooklyn Heights, NY & Matthew Lueders of Stamford, CT and three grandchildren Kenny and Grace Dixon & Tyler Lueders.
