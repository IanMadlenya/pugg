
        COAKLEY-Elizabeth Kelly. Of Harrison, NY and former 50 year Larchmont, NY resident, died peacefully at home on May 7, 2003. Also survived by a multitude of Kelly and Coakley relatives. She was predeceased by her husband Lt. Henry Beckman Coakley, her son-in-law Jed Dolce and sisters, Margaret Kelly, Catherine Lynch, Frances Shattuck, Mary Cornelia Hochschwender and Joan Pierce. Family will receive friends on Monday, May 12 from 4-7 PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers contributions to the Lighthouse for the Blind, 170 Hamilton Ave., White Plains, NY or the charity of your choice.
