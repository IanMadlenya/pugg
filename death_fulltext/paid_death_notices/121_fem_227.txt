
        PAIEWONSKY-Ethel Heller, on Thursday, February 27, 1997. Loving wife of the late Governor Ralph M. Paiewonsky. Daughter of the late Abraham and Anna Heller. Beloved mother of Dr. Bernard Paiewonsky and Joyce Adolfsen. Cherished grandmother of Adrienne, Betty, Eric and Lauren. Great grandmother of Nate, Sam and Anna. Loving mother-in-law of Mary Paiewonsky and Louis G. Adolfsen. Adored sister, aunt and friend. Services Friday 10AM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue. Burial in Saint Thomas, Virgin Islands. In lieu of flowers contributions in her memory made to a charity of your choice.
