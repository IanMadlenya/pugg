
        SINATRA--Phyllis Catherine, 79, on February 19, 2007 after enduring a long illness. Predeceased by beloved husband, Thomas. Dedicated Mother to Nina, Frank, Carla, and the late Kathryn. Adored grandmother, true friend. Graduate of Marymount College. Talented pianist and singer. We will forever honor her kindness, generosity, and selflessness and will miss her quick wit and reassuring love. Wake at Aievoli Funeral Home, 13th Ave at 65th St, Brooklyn, Thursday 2-5pm and 7-9:30pm. Funeral Mass Friday 10am at St. Anselm, 4th Ave at 82nd St, Brooklyn. In lieu of flowers please send donations in Phyllis' name to the Alzheimers Association, 225 North Michigan Ave, Ste 1700, Chicago, IL 60601 or 800272-3900.
