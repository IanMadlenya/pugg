
        ZUCKERBRAUN-Helen nee Rogowsky, peacefully March 20th, 2001 at age 94 in Lauderhill, FL. Beloved wife of the late Dr. Baer Zuckerbraun. Devoted mother of Dr. David and Mary of Pomona, NY, Dr. Matthew and Myra of NYC. Cherished grandmother of Sara of Pomona, Dr. Brian and Dr. Noel Zuckerbraun of Pittsburgh and of 12 step-grand and greatgrandchildren. Services ''The Riverside,'' Amsterdam Ave. and 76th St., NYC, Friday, 11:30 AM. She will be deeply missed.
