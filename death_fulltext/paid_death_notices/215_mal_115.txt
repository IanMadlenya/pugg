
        PECHTER--Morton, of NYC and Juno Beach, FL passed away on December 8, 2004. Mr. Pechter was the loving husband of Katherine Machis Pechter. He was the loving son of the late Tillie and Harry Pechter, cherished father of Steven Pechter, the late Susan Pechter, and Barbara and John Lichtman. He was the adoring grandfather of Maggie & Jason Lichtman, Colby and David Sirowich, and John Daniel Lichtman, and the proud great-grandfather of Julia Lichtman and Jacob Lichtman. He was the devoted brother of Katie and Karl Pechter, Adeline and Theodore Adler, and Herbert and the late Ann Charles. Funeral services will be Sunday, December 12th at 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell Funeral Chapel, 1076 Madison Avenue (81st Street). Family will be receiving friends Sunday evening, December 12th, and Monday, December 13th, in their New York home. In lieu of flowers, please send contributions to Beth Israel Hospital Oncology Nutrition Fund, 10 Union Square East, Suite 4C, New York, New York 10013, or the charity of one's choice.
        PECHTER--Morton.
