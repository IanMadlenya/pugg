
        BLUNT--James Philander, of Hadlyme and New York, NY, passed away December 1st. He is survived by two daughters Emily Victoria Nelson of Turners Falls, MA, and Susan Alexander of Cleveland, OH; a sister Phoebe Kurz of Sterling, VA; six grandchildren, seven great grandchildren. Memorial services will be at a later date at the convenience of the family. Donations may be made in his memory to Bowdoin College, 5 College St., Brunswick, ME. 04011.
