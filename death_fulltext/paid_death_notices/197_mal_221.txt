
        ROTHSCHILD--Walter N. Jr. ''Bill.'' 83 of Syosset, LI, peacefully, on June 20. Husband of Virginia, father of Trip Rothschild of New Millford, CT, Nina Rothschild-Utne of Minneapolis, Suzanne Rothschild of Arlington, MA, and Jane Rothschild of Sydney, Australia, brother of Carol Noyes of Syosset and Phyllis Farley of New York. Grandfather of ten. Services at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th St, 11:30 AM, Tuesday, June 24.
        ROTHSCHILD--Walter N., Jr. In sorrow, Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records the passing of our distinguished and dedicated member, Walter N. Rothschild Jr. With his family, we share the grief of loss and bereavement. The beautiful qualities of his life shall endure forever in the hearts of those who loved him. Dr. David M. Posner Senior Rabbi Dr. Ronald B. Sobel Senior Rabbi Emeritus Robert A. Bernhard, Pres. Herbert C. Bernard, Sec'y. >>AD#
