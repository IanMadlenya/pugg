
        ROTHWELL--Peter Falcon. Age 51, of Bloomfield, NJ and East Hampton, NY, died on February 7, 2005 after a brief battle against lymphoma. Beloved brother to Susan Rothwell, Anne Forlines, and James Rothwell, brother-inlaw to Susan Poliak Rothwell and John Forlines, III, and cherished uncle to Alex and Zack Rothwell, and Megan, Molly and Jack Forlines. Graduate of Montclair Academy, Swarthmore College and NYU's Stern School of Business. Musician and saltwater fly fisherman extraordinnaire, Peter worked at SunGard for 11 years. Special thanks to life-long friend Bob McMahon, cousin Stan Mortimer, fellow fisherman David Blinken, SunGard Manager Craig Dudsak, and numerous others for their support during his illness. Gathering Friday, February 11, 3:00 PM at ''the Riverside'', 76 St. and Amsterdam Ave. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to The Coastal Conservation Association of NY (CCA), PO Box 12451, Hauppauge, NY 11788.
