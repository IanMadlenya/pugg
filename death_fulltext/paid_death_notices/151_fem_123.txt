
        LANSBURGH-Ruth Lloyd. 86, died Friday, August 13, 1999. The adored wife of the late Robert Ranger Lansburgh for 47 years; devoted mother of Patricia L. Malcolm Alling and Cynthia L. McAdam; grandmother of Ashley, Peter, and Nikki Malcolm; and Barrett, Quincy and Kyrie McAdam; mother-in-law of David MacGregor Malcolm, Charles V. McAdam III and Wilson S. Alling. She was a lovely lady whose beauty, competence and strength will long be remembered. In lieu of flowers, gifts may be made to The Hospice Fund, Greenwich Hospital, 1 Perryridge Road, Greenwich, CT 06830.
