
        NAESS-Margaret Dixon. December 15, 1903-April 17, 1998. Beloved wife of the late Ragnar D. Naess. Devoted mother of sons, Ragnar and Robert, and mother-in-law of Margo, grandmother of Spenser and Cooper. Her death came quietly in great peace just before a spring dawn. Private services. Memorial to be announced. Memorial donations to the League of Women Voters Education Fund, 817 Broadway, 6th Floor, NYC 10003.
