
        LINDER--Cortland D., of Redding, CT and Pawling, NY, died October 11th, 2006. The entire Circumnavigators Club expresses profound sorrow on the passing of its International Past President (19661967), Foundation Founder, Past President (1978-1979) and Honorary President. Our deepest and sincerest sympathy to his wife, Barbara, and his entire family.
