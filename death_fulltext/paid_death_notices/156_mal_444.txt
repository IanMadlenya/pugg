
        NEHMER-Stanley. Born December 8, 1920, in NYC. Son of Alexander and Laura Nehmer. BSS degree from CCNY; MA from Columbia University. OSS 1942-45; State Department 1945-57; World Bank 1957-61; Commerce Department 1965 until retirement in 1973; President ECS, Inc., 1973-88. He died on January 30, 2000 due to complications from pancreatic cancer. Survivors include his wife of 53 years Phyllis Nehmer; two children, Sherry Nehmer of NYC and Jonathan Nehmer and wife Geri Nehmer of Rockville, MD and two grandchildren, Jason and Michael Nehmer, also of Rockville.
