
        HAUBEN-Oscar. Age 93. On November 18, 1998. Beloved husband, father and grandfather. A Holocaust survivor who managed successfully to bring his family to the U.S. and build a shoe business and a comfortable and loving home. He was adored by his family and he will be greatly missed. He is survived by his wife Frida Hauben, daughter Cyla Klein and son-inlaw Neal Klein, son Ben Hauben and daughter-in-law Lana Hauben and grandchildren Michael and Jeffrey Klein, Alissa Appel, Laura and Jill Hauben, and Jonathan and Alan Goodman.
