
        DIENNO-Christopher George., of Bala Cynwyd, PA. Died suddenly December 28, 1999. Husband of Michelle Lynne DiEnno, father of Abigail Claire DiEnno and Olivia Grace DiEnno, son of Nino and Marilyn (MacIndoe) DiEnno, grandson of Jack and Augustine MacIndoe and brother of Lisa Levy and Lauri Hogge. He was a graduate of the University of Alabama. He was Vice President Portfolio Manager at Bank of New York, Manhattan, New York City, and he was also Co-Manager of the Bank of New York Large Cap Growth Fund. Relatives and friends are invited to attend his Funeral Mass at 10 AM Friday, December 31, 1999, in St. Isaac Jogues Catholic Church, 50 Walker Road, Wayne, PA. Friends may call from 6:30 to 8:30 PM Thursday at the Devlin - Rosmos - Kepp Funeral Home, Inc., Main Street at Fourth Avenue, Phoenixville, PA, and from 9 AM to 10 AM Friday at the church. Burial will be private.
