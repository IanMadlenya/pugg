
        ADLER--Elaine (Lipton). Of Jenkintown, PA, formerly of New York, died September 29, 2003. Beloved wife of Edward B., mother of Tony and Joe, loving grandmother and sister. Elaine will be remembered as a master potter, for her love of family and an unparalleled approach to life. As she said in her final days, ''I fought a good fight.'' A memorial will be held in New York. Donations can be made in her name to the Educational Alliance, 197 East Broadway, NYC 10002.
