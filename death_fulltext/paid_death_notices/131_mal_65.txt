
        CRAWFORD - Michael Robert. Born December 5, 1943. Youngest ever Deputy Director of Indian Affairs in Maine. Co-Director of TRIBE, an experimental program in Indian education. Consultant to the New England Equal Education Center, the Boston Public Schools and the Ford Foundation. Worked with the American Indian Community House and many other community-based organizations in New York. A passionate advocate for Native Americans, he worked on the Land Claims Project and many other projects to improve housing, education and the standard of living for Native Americans. Husband, father, grandfather and loyal friend. Teacher, fighter, champion, and hero to many. Your flame continues to burn within us, Mike-and your inspiration will never die. Funeral on Saturday, December 6, 1997, Church of the Ascension, 5th Avenue and 10th Street, New York City, 2 PM.
