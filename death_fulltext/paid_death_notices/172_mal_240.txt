
        O'BRIEN-Gerard John. Died on May 21, 2001, of Somers, NY. Formerly of New York City. Beloved husband of Patricia Broderick O'Brien, father of Gerard J. O'Brien, Jr., of Arlington, VA, David C. O'Brien, of Alexandria, VA, Richard C. O'Brien, of Somers, NY, Patricia A. Peters, of Portsmouth, R.I., and Laura M. White, of South Salem, NY. Brother of Betty Forker, of Venice, FL, also survived by eight grandchildren. Son of the former Mayor of New York City, John P. O'Brien, and his wife, Helen. Graduate of Regis High School, Georgetown University, & Harvard Law School. Member of The Knights of The Holy Sepulchre, and The Friendly Sons of Saint Patrick. Calling hours at Clark Associates Funeral Home, Katonah, NY, Wednesday 2-4 & 7-9 P.M. Mass of Christian Burial at Saint Joseph's R.C. Church, Croton Falls, NY, Thurs. 10:30 A.M. Interment Gate of Heaven Cemetery.
