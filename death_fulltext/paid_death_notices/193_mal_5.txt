
        SULLIVAN-John G. The officers, employees and agents of Brown Harris Stevens are deeply saddened by the death of our colleague John G. In lieu of flowers, the family requests donations to Memorial Sloan - Kettering Cancer Center, Box E, 1275 York Avenue, NY, NY 10021.
