
        LAWNER--Murray W. On August 4, 2005, in Yonkers, NY. Federal safety engineer and teacher, prolific storyteller and humorist. Beloved husband of 68 years to Martha. Devoted father of Neil Lawner and Sharon Weinberg, devoted father-inlaw of Paula Lawner and Steven Weinberg and loving grandfather of Brian & Kevin Lawner, Allison and Carolyn Weinberg and Jason Barro. He will be greatly missed by his family and friends. Donations may be made to the American Heart Association in his memory.
