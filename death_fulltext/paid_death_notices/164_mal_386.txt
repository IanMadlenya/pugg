
        BINGHAM-Lewis E. The Committee of Methadone Program Administrators Inc. (COMPA) of New York State extends heartfelt condolences to the family and colleagues of Lewis E. Bingham, late Senior Executive Vice President of the Addiction Research and Treatment Corporation (ARTC) of Brooklyn, NY, where he worked for 27 years. Lewis was a longstanding member of the COMPA Executive Committee, a consummate professional and an extraordinary person. We will miss his wisdom and good nature. We share the sorrow of his passing with his wife and children, his friends and staff of ARTC. His spirit remains forever in our memories. Peter Coleman, Chairperson Martin Livenstein, Exec. Director
