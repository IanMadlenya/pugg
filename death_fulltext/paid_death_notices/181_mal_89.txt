
        CORNERETTO-Alan. February 3, 2002. Survived by his beloved wife & inseparable companion, Margot Lehman Corneretto, son Roger, sister Joan Silverstein, brothers-inlaw Albert Silverstein, Herbert and Edgar Lehman, and sisters-in-law Virginia and Elisabeth Lehman. Editor, public relations and publishing professional with an emphasis in the sciences. A World War II veteran, traveler, and accomplished gymnast, he was a serious student of wine, wine labeling, music, and language. He was devoted to a lifelong exploration and scholarly appreciation of the development and progress of New York City. Memorial gathering February 23, Riverdale Ethical Culture Society.
