
        GROSS-Melvin F. Age 79, on June 27. Beloved husband of Ina and father of Laura Gross. Also survived by two loving stepchildren, Michael Molnar and Myrna Maltz, four grandchildren: Dean and Gary Maltz, Jacqueline and Julia Molnar, and three greatgrandchildren: Daniel, Alexandra and Eric Maltz. A highly-decorated World War II veteran who directed decoding of enemy communications in the Philippines, he was interred with full military honors on June 29 at Cemetery Beth El in Westwood, New Jersey. Scholar of Jewish history and beliefs, and outspoken advocate for what he believed to be right. We bid you sweet farewell and thank you for your contribution to our lives. May you rest in peace.
