
        RUBINSTEIN-Irving. Of Lake Worth, FL, formerly from New York City, died Monday morning, March 18, 2002. He is survived by his wife Adele; his sons Bobby and Marc; his grandchildren Craig, Brett, Jordan, Jennifer and Nathaniel. The funeral will be held Thursday morning at 11 AM at Riverside Gordon Memorial Chapel, 7205 West Atlantic Avenue, Delray Beach, FL.
        RUBINSTEIN-Irving. The Board of Governors and members of the Falls Country Club, Lake Worth, FL, acknowledge with deep sorrow the passing of our esteemed member, Irving Rubinstein. We extend our deepest sympathy to his beloved wife Adele and their family. Dr. Elliott Solomon, President
