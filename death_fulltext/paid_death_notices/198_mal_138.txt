
        McLEMORE--Dr. George A., Jr., 78 of Manhattan died Sunday, July 13, 2003. He was a graduate of Harvard University and practiced medicine for 55 years. He specialized in internal medicine and cardiology. He was an Assistant Clinical Professor of Medicine at Cornell Medical College. He is survived by his wife Mary, three sisters, a brother, a daughter, a son-in-law and two grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at 4 p.m. on Tuesday, July 22, at Central Presbyterian Church, 64th and Park Avenue. Memorial contributions may be made to the Department of Medicine at Weill Medical College of Cornell University or to the Johnston County Heritage Center, P.O.
