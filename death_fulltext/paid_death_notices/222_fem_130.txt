
        LIDDELL -- Jane Hawley Hawkes, age 97, died peacefully at home July 20, 2005 of complications of a stroke. She had remained passionate and involved with her family and her causes until last week. The widow of Donald M. Liddell, Jr., with whom she had celebrated 59 years of marriage, she is survived by her daughter, Jane Liddell Bass, and her son and daughter-in-law, D. Roger Brooke and Florence Liddell, of New York City, and four grandchildren, Steedman L. Bass of Cambridge, MA, Margot S. Bass of Takoma Park, MD, Alice Liddell Rooke of Bloomfield, CT and Torrey B. Liddell of New York City. She was predeceased by one granddaughter, Catherine H. Bass. Burial will be in Baltimore on July 25. A Memorial Service will be held September 16 at noon at St. James' Episcopal Church, Madison Avenue at 71 St. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be sent to the Liddell Memorial Garden at National Society of Colonial Dames, 215 East 71 Street, NY, NY 10021.
