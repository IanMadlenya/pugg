
        ELLERS--Peter, 50. Much beloved husband of Pam Sommers, died at home on February 2nd. Peter is also survived by his parents Elaine & James Ellers, sister Penny Ellers-Britton, her husband Michael & daughter Emma, mother-in-law Carol Sommers, sister-in-law Florie Sommers Huppert and her husband Barry & son Nathan, and father-in-law Joseph Sommers. Peter died as he lived, with grace and with dignity. His spirit will live on in the hearts of those who loved him. Memorial service at Brotherhood Synagogue, 28 Gramercy Park South, Sunday, February 26, at 1 PM.
