
        FRIEDMAN-Blanche. 90, died peacefully in her home October 20, 2000. She is survived by Israel, her beloved husband of 63 years, her daughter Lois Friedman of New York City, her son Dr. Matthew Friedman of Longmeadow, MA, and her cherished grandchildren Jessica Hutchins of Manhattan Beach, CA, Carly Friedman of Washington, DC, and Daniel Friedman of Ann Arbor, MI. She earned a BA degree at New York University and a masters degree in Psychology at The University of North Carolina, Chapel Hill. She worked 20 years for the New York City Board of Education as a teacher of hospitalized children at Tisch Goldwater Hospitals.
