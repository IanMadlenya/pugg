
        LESLIE-George. Of Westwood, Massachusetts, and Fishers Island, New York, peacefully on April 5 at age 85, George Robert III. Beloved husband of Catherine (McIntire) Leslie. He attended Bovee and Pomfret Schools and New York University, and was a former Vice President of the Irving Trust Co of New York. In addition to his wife, he is survived by his loving family, Alexander G. Leslie, Peter D. Leslie, Rhoda MacCallum, H. Ashley Smith, Catherine S. Leonard, Patricia S. Perron, William T.M. Smith, 12 grandchildren and one great grandchild. Contributions to the American Field Service International, 313 East 43rd Street, NYC, New York, 10017 in his memory would be appreciated. Interment will be in Mount Auburn Cemetery, Cambridge, Massachusetts.
