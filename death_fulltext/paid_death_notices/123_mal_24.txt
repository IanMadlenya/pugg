
        O'BRIEN-Francis Joseph, 71, of Spring Lake, N.J., formerly of Bklyn, NY on April 1, 1997. Beloved husband of Ellin (nee Amorosi). Dear father of Dr. Francis J. of Manhattan, Paul E. of New Canaan, CT, Matthew L. and Christopher J. of Spring Lake and beloved grandfather of two. He was a graduate of Regis High School, Holy Cross College and Fordham Law School. He was a partner for many years at Hill, Rivkins, Loesberg, O'Brien, Mulroy & Hayden, an admiralty law firm in NY City. An internationally known lecturer and authority in maritime law, he was past-president of the Maritime Law Association of the U.S., a member of the Comite Maritime Int'l, and associate editor of the Journal, American Maritime Cases. He also served on the Board of Governors of the Downtown Athletic Club and India House in NY City and was a member of the Lake Placid Club and the Spring Lake Golf and Country Club. Funeral from the Meehan Funeral Home, 55 Warren Avenue, Spring Lake Heights, NJ on Thursday, April 3, 1997. Mass of Christian burial 9:30 AM, St. Mark's RC Church, Sea Girt, NJ. Interment St. Anne's Cemetery, Wall, NJ. Visitation Wednesday 2:00-5:00 PM, 7:00-10:00 PM. In lieu of flowers, donations to Regis High School, 55 East 84th Street, NYC would be appreciated.
