
        DAVIS--Dwight F. III. The grandson and namesake of the donor of the Davis Cup, died at age 68, on May 7, at Memorial Sloan-Kettering, in New York. Following graduation from Harvard College in 1959, Mr. Davis began a long career in advertising. He served as a Board member and director of the US Tennis Association's Education Foundation. Mr. Davis is survived by his wife Anne Marie, and three children, Daphne Bazer, Dwight F. Davis IV, Christopher Davis, and seven grandchildren. His wit and intellect will be missed by his family and friends. A funeral service will be held at St. James Episcopal Church, Madison Ave. and 71st St., at 1 PM, on Friday, May 14.
