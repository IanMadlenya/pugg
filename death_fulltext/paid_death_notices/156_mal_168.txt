
        GROSS-Joseph Stanley, September 8, 1905-January 7, 2000. He was a devoted, admiring husband to Dolly Gross, deceased, and concerned father to daughter Stephanie Gross, deceased. He was for many years a caring and ethical broker and then financial advisor to the elderly whose trust he honored. He enjoyed opera and patronized the musical arts. He is survived by nephews Sanford Gross of Larchmont, NY, Dr. William S. Gilbert of Chevy Chase, MD and Robert Gilbert of Glendora, CA and great-nephew Dr. Allen Goldberg of Chicago, IL. Contributions may be made in his memory to Marble Hill Senior Center, 5365 Broadway, NY, NY 10463 or to the New York Metropolitan Opera.
