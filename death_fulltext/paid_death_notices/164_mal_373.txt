
        NEUMAN-Sheldon M. Age 65. On September 21, 2000 in Miami, FL. after a brief illness. Devoted lifetime partner of Cami Green. Beloved father of Rachel & Jonah. Father-in-law of Richard. Proud grandfather of Olivia. Wonderful and caring brother of Herbert and Marvin and brother-in-law of Stephanie.         NEUMAN-Sheldon.
