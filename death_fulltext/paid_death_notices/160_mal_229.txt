
        PRATT-Lawrence Southwick. Retired partner of Wilkie Farr & Gallagher and amateur jazz musician. Died April 28 in Rye New York. He was ninety-three years old. He lived in New York City and Squirrel Island Maine. He leaves his wife, Emily, his son, Lawrence Spencer Pratt, and two grandsons, Sam Pratt of Hudson NY, and Robert W. Pratt of New York City.
