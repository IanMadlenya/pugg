
        McKENNA-James J. Rev. Msgr. On May 3, 2002. Beloved son of the late Thomas Joseph and Elizabeth Agnes McKenna. Dear brother of the late Thomas and Sr. Mary P. McKenna C.S.J. Brother-in-law of Setsuko McKenna and uncle of Lisa Romo and Tom McKenna. Msgr. McKenna was Pastor Emeritus of St. Mary Star of the Sea Church in Far Rockaway, NY. He rebuilt the current church after the original church was destroyed by fire in 1974. Co-founder of the Margert Community Corp. and recipient of several awards from the Jewish community for his ecumenical work. Reposing at Denis S. O'Connor Funeral Home, 91-05 Beach Channel Dr., Rockaway Beach, NY, until 5 PM, Monday. Mass of Transferral at St. Mary Star of the Sea Church, Monday at 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass on Tuesday at 10:30 AM. Interment Holy Cross Cemetery.
