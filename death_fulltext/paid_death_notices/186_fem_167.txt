
        LEBOR-Dorothy Patrick. Beloved wife of John F. Lebor. Passed away suddenly at New England Medical Center in Boston, MA. She was the daughter of Sadie Eardley Patrick and Joseph Patrick. Mrs. Lebor was born in Chelsea, MA. She graduated from Saint Mary's H.S. in Chelsea and received a Bachelor's degree from Saint Regis College, Boston, MA. She lived and worked in Boston for many years as an interior designer. After moving to Chestnut Hill, MA, and later to Sarasota, FL, she relocated to Rye, NY, to be near her daughter and grandchildren. She married John F. Lebor in September of 1984 and lived in both Rye and North Palm Beach, FL. She is survived by her husband John Lebor, daughter Diana and her husband John Plunkett of Rye, NY, granddaughter Lisa Wallace of Rye, NY, and Hong Kong, two grandsons, Christopher Pollack of Rye, NY, and Todd Pollack of New York City, stepson Andrew Lebor of Callawassi Island, SC, stepdaughter Catherine Scheu of Portsmith, NH, step grandsons Keith Lebor of New Providence, NJ, and Todd Lebor of Chicago, IL, four great-grandchildren and four step great-grandchildren. She was predeceased by her brother James Joseph Patrick. Calling hours will be on Sunday, July 28, from 4-7 PM at the Graham Funeral Home, 1036 Boston Post Rd., Rye, NY. A funeral service will be held on Monday, July 29, at 10 AM at the Church of the Resurrection, Rye, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Saint Vincent's Hospital, 275 North St., Harrison, NY 10528
