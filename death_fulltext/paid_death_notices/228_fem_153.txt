
        BERNSTEIN -- Ray (Aunt Ruthie), 88, of Boynton Beach, FL, formerly of Jericho, NY, passed away on December 11, 2005. She is survived by her loving husband of 58 years, Milton and adoring children, Dr. Jay Bernstein (Sandy), Dr. Larry Bernstein (Diane), Sandy Lenger (Dr. Steven) and loving grandchildren, Emily, Amy, Jane, Marisa, Benjamin, Samuel, Paul, Carly and nieces and nephews. She was both beautiful within and without and left a legacy of love and devotion to all who knew her. The Bernstein Family
