
        BLAUSTEIN-Max. The League Treatment Center extends our heartfelt sympathy to Tommye Feit, our dear colleague, and her family on the loss of her beloved father Max Blaustein. Mr. Blaustein is survived by his wife Lillian and his three daughters Tommye Feit, Ellen Safeer, Vicki Ehren, eight grandchildren and twelve great-grandchildren. Mr. Blaustein was a retired Deputy Chief Probation Officer for the New York City Court System.
