
        CONTE--Hope R., on August 17, 2005, of Garden City. Beloved wife of Paul F. Conte. Loving mother of Carolyn Sheehy, cherished grandmother of Michelle Button and husband, Allen Button. Loving mother of Brian Conte and his wife, Marianne, and cherished grandmother of Kristina M. Conte. Dr. Conte was a retired Emeritus Professor at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. She was a graduate of the Riverdale Country School, received a B.A. from Adelphi University, an M.A. from Hofstra University and a Ph.D. from New York University. She wrote and co-authored several books on Psychology, among them ''Ego Defenses: Theory and Measurement'' and ''Models of Personality and Emotions''. Visiting at the Fairchild Chapel, Franklin Avenue at 12th Street, Garden City, 2-4 and 7-9 PM, Saturday and Sunday. Religious service at Funeral Home 8 PM Sunday. Interment Monday at the Locust Valley Cemetery.
