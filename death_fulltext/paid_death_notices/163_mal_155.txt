
        GINNEL-Frank Brown, 87. Of Bedford Hills. Died peacefully at home on August 12 after a brief illness. Husband of the late Helen W. Ginnel. Father of Nancy G. Duncan, Helen G. O'Reilly and Daniel H. Ginnel. Also survived by eight grandchildren. Attended Columbia University. He served in the U.S. Navy during World War II. He founded Ginnel Real Estate in 1951, served as President of the Westchester Realty Board. Services will be held on Tuesday, August 15 at 11:30AM at St. Matthew's Church, Bedford, N.Y. Interment private. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Northern Westchester Hospital Foundation, 400 East Main Street, Mount Kisco, N.Y. 10549.
