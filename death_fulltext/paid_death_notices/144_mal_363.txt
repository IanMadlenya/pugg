
        BIRCH-Chester, a former Briarcliff Manor, NY, resident, died on January 17, 1999, in Palm Beach Gardens, Florida, at the age of 86. An advertising executive, Mr. Birch became President of Dancer, Fitzgerald & Sample, Inc. in the early 1950's. He is survived by a son, Bruce Birch of Blacksburg, Virginia; a daughter, Susan Duffy of Briarcliff Manor; his grandchildren, Laura Buchanan, Margaret Birch, Keara Buck and Mark Duffy, and two great-grandsons, Connor and Aidan Buck.
