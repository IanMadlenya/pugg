
        ROSENKRANZ -- Jules Joseph. Age 91, of New York City, died September 17, 2004. Mr. Rosenkranz was the beloved husband of Marjorie Sable Rosenkranz. He was former Vice President of Durst Manufacturing. Jules was loved by all whose lives he touched and will be remembered for his kindness, generosity and compassion. Jules is survived by his two loving daughters Ann and Jill Rosenkranz of Massa chusetts. Services will be held at Campbell Funeral Home, 81 St and Madison Ave, in New York, on September 19.
