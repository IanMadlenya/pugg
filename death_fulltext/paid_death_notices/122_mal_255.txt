
        ROGERS-Sydney. On March 21, 1997, born Jan. 13, 1907, London, England. Husband of 62 years to Beatrice, father of Teri Lehmann and Michael Rogers, grandfather to Bruce, David, Mark and Amy Lehmann and their spouses and Lisa and Jessica Rogers. Great grandfather to Dylan, Justin, Robert, Nicholas, Kate and Merideth Lehmann and Samantha and Joshua Sklare. Father-inlaw to Jack Lehmann and Gail Rogers. He affected all the lives he touched. In lieu of flowers a donation to the Pulmonary Fund, St. Luke's Roosevelt Hospital would be greatly appreciated.
