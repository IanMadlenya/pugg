
        MILLER-Gladys Colbert. Born 1919 in Far Rockaway, she was cherished as Mom, Nana, Glady, Aunt Glady and Cousin Glady. A pivotal member of her clan, she gathered family for occasions happy and sad. In her first life, she was the loving wife of Nathanial and co-partner in a radio business. 18 years after his untimely death, she married Lewis Miller with whom she shared 15 joyful years. Survived by her husband, children Rosanne of Cheshire, CT and Douglas and his wife Suzanne Sangree of Baltimore, MD, and adored grandchildren, Heather, Neil, Matthew, Abigail and Nathanial.
