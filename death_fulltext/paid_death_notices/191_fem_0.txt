
        DOLAN-Elizabeth M, 93, MIT graduate, November 28, of NYC and formerly of Cambridge, MA. Survived by nephews Harry Dolan III of Gloucester, MA, Peter Dolan of Princeton, NJ, nieces, Sarah Simmons of New Canaan, CT and Johanna Baldwin of Lynchburg, VA. She was the sister of the late Harry Dolan, Jr of Wilton, CT and Eleanor Dolan of Alexandria, VA. Funeral services by the Whittier-Porter Funeral Home of Ipswich, MA will be private.
