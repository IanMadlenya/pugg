
        CARR-R.. Bruce, 75, of Charlotte, N.C., died January 17, 2000 at his home. Mr. Carr, a native of Wilkes-Barre, Penn., had been a faculty member of the history department at Columbia University and was later a captain with TWA. Services were held in Charlotte. For more information, contact Hankins & Whittington Funeral Service in Charlotte, N.C.
