
        KREISBERG-Barrett. The entire School of American Ballet family notes with sadness the death of Barry Kreisberg.         KREISBERG-Barrett G. City Center 55th Street Theater Foundation extends its deepest condolences on the passing of Barry Kreisberg to the trustees of The Harkness Foundation and to his family.
