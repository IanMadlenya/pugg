
        BLOCK--Dorothy Lily (nee Kraft), at age 75, passed peacefully on November 7, 2004 in Norwalk, Connecticut after a prolonged illness. A longtime resident of Boca Raton and Delray Beach, Florida, she was predeceased by her loving husband, Alan J. Block. Survived by devoted daughter, Pamela Works-McCarthy and adoring granddaughter, Allison Noble Works of Rowayton, CT.
