
        NEWMAN-Norman, age 45. Of Lawrence, New York. Winston & Strawn remembers and remains saddened by the untimely passing of their partner & friend, Norman Newman. Mr. Newman served as chairperson of the firm's employee benefits and compensation practice, was a frequent lecturer and writer in his field, and was a highly respected member of the legal community. His devotion to the practice of law and steadfast dedication to clients will be remembered with great admiration. He is deeply missed personally and professionally by all who worked with him. We extend our sincere and heartfelt sympathy to his family.
