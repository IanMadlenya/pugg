
        WALKER-Natalie Barth. Costume Designer, died Sunday, Jan. 16, 2000 at the age of 77 at Evanston Hospital, Evanston, Ill. Beloved mother of Anne, Wanda and Clare Edward,Jr. Adored grandmother of Clare Edward III and Henry Woo, Jr. A member of the United Scenic Artists, Natalie's distinguished career as a costume designer spanned over 50 years and included Broadway, feature films and network television. Memorial Service to be held Saturday, Feb 19th, 4pm at the Unitarian Church of Evanston, 1330 Ridge Avenue. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made in her name to Animal Protective Association's, Harmony House for Cats, POB 18098, Chicago, Illinois 60618. For more information 847-864-8538
