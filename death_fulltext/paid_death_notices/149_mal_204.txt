
        GROBART-Doris Leah. The Officers, Board of Trustees, Congregation and Clergy of The Jewish Center of The Hamptons, mourn the passing of Doris Leah Grobart, beloved mother of Bernard Grobart, a devoted member of our Board of Trustees. We extend our deepest condolences to his family. Rabbi David J. Gelfand Cantor Debra Stein-Davidson Joseph Oppenheimer, President
