
        STRULLY--L. Vincent, MD, FACS, of Fair Lawn, NJ died April 10, 2004. He was 90. Son of Jacques M. Strully, DMD and Rebecca Waisman Strully. Dr. Strully was born on March 6, 1914 in Brooklyn, NY. He graduated from Hamilton College, Clinton, NY in 1934 and received his medical degree in 1938 from Long Island College of Medicine (now Downstate Medical School). During WWII, he was a Lieutenant in the Navy and served in the European Theater from 1934 to 1946. For 40 years, he maintained an active medical practice at St. Joseph's Hospital in Paterson, NJ. He is survived by his wife of 62 years, Claire Kleinfeld Strully, his two children, L. Vincent Strully, Jr. of Cambridge, MA and Maxine R. Strully, of Fair Lawn, NJ and his former daughter-in-law, Marylou Sudders of Cambridge, MA. Funeral Services will be held Tuesday, April 13, 2004 at 1:30 PM at the Louis Suburban Chapels, 13-01 Broadway, Fair Lawn, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to the New England Center for Children, 33 Turnpike Rd., Southborough, MA 01772.
