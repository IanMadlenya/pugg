
        DEVLIN-Vivienne K. Most beloved mother, retired teacher, Sacred Heart Academy and Lindenhurst Schools, formerly of Babylon Village, died April 4, 1999 at home surrounded by loved ones. She is survived by her six children, Philip Fergus, Cecily Anne, Akiva Zohar, Sheila Marie, Maureen Elise, and Ellen Louise. In lieu of flowers, she has requested donations to The University of Notre Dame in memory of her beloved brother, Fergus Ford Kelly, Class of 1937, a Navy pilot killed in World War II. The family will be receiving friends and loved ones at LaGrange Inn, Thursday from 4:308:30 P.M. A Memorial Service will be held at 7:30 P.M.
