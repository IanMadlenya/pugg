
        McGRAW--Donald C., Jr. The entire community of Mystic Seaport - the Museum of America and the Sea-notes with sorrow the death of Donald C. McGraw, Jr. and extends its sincere sympathy to his family. Don served as a charter member and first Chairman of the Museum's National Council of Advisors. All who visit Mystic Seaport will be touched by Don's love of and support for maritme art, ships, and nautical publications and education.
