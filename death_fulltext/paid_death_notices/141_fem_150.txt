
        MARGOLIS-Dr. Renee K. The administration, faculty, staff and students of SUNY Downstate Medical Center mourn the passing of our colleague, Dr. Renee K. Margolis, professor of pharmacology. Dr. Margolis was an esteemed scientist whose pioneering studies on the structure and function of brain glycoconjugates won international acclaim. Our deepest sympathy is extended to her husband, Dr. Richard Margolis, with whom she worked in close collaboration, her family, and friends. Eugene B. Feigelson, MD Interim President and Dean, College of Medicine
