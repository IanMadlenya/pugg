
        CYMBOL - Irwin P., 65. Passed away October 7, 2002. Loving father of Todd and Bradley Cymbol. Cherished father-in-law of Nina Cymbol. Devoted husband. He will be remembered in the early years for his dedication and sacrifice to his sons on and off the playing fields. In the latter years, for his courage, strength and determination. The Memorial Service will be held Wednesday, October 9th at 11:15AM at the Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 West 76th Street, New York, N.Y.
