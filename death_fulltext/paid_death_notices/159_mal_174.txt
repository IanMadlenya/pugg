
        VORENBERG-James. The Board of Directors and Staff of the NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, Inc. deeply mourn the loss of our dear and respected colleague James Vorenberg. A brilliant and selfless leader in the cause of civil and human rights, Mr. Vorenberg provided our organization with his knowledge and guidance for over 28 years as a member of our National Board of Directors and our New England Committee. We extend our sincere condolences to the Vorenberg family. Julius L. Chambers Martin D. Payson Board Co-Chairs Elaine R. Jones, President and Director-Counsel Theodore M. Shaw Associate Director Counsel NAACP Legal Defense and Educational Fund, Inc.
