
        WARNER-Jo. A painter, she died on October 25, 1999, from complications of bone marrow cancer. Raised in Idaho Falls, and a graduate of The University of Colorado, and The Art Students League, in New York, she had many one woman exhibitions during her career at galleries including the Camino and Phoenix, in New York. Two of her oil paintings were included in the Museum of Modern Art's Circulating Exhibition from 1961 to 1963 and were viewed throughout the U.S. and Europe. She was married for 29 years to Peter E. DeBlasio, and she was the very loving mother of Caralee DeBlasio, of Manhattan, and Alessandra DeBlasio, of Washington, D.C.
