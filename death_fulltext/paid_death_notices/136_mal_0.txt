
        CRALLE-Joseph B. 2nd. Resident for 28 years of Upper Montclair, NJ, since 1980 of the Tides Lodge area of Virgina, died April 28 after a long illness. Betty Robb Cralle, his wife of 43 years, died in 1989, second wife Marguerite Houle Walton survives him, along with sons Jay, David & granddaughter Katharine. The family prefers that memorial contributions by friends be directed to charities of their own choosing or to Historic Christ Church Foundation, Irvington, Virginia 22480.
