
        PETRUCCIANI - Michel. The House of Steinway & Sons notes with profound sorrow the untimely passing of our beloved friend and devoted Steinway Artist, Michel Petrucciani, on January 6, 1999 in New York City at the age of 36. Mr. Petrucciani was a dazzling pianist with a generous and an indefatigable spirit, and his artistry captivated and inspired audiences throughout the world. His significant discography will keep his legacy alive in the hearts of music lovers everywhere. To Isabelle, Alexandre and to his entire family, we extend our deepest sympathy. His loss is keenly felt. Bruce A. Stevens, President Peter B. Goodrich, Vice President, Worldwide Concert and Artist Activities
