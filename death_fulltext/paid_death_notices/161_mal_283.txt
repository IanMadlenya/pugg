
        CRILEY-Richard. We mourn the death of Richard Criley, June 18, 2000 and celebrate his lifelong fight for social justice and peace. An activist for more than six decades, Dick Criley was a leader of the ACLU, founder of the National Committee to Abolish HUAC, recipient of the 1985 Earl Warren Civil Liberities Award and an inspiration to generations of civil rights advocates. We send our deepest condolences to his wife Jan Penney and family. American Civil Liberties Union of Northern California
