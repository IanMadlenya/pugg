
        CREEL-Dana Shannon, 84, of Sharon, CT, died at his home on Haisdan Farm on May 15, 1997. Beloved husband of Jane Haislip Creel; brother of Virginia Creel Clark and uncle of David Clark. He graduated from Emory University, its Law School and the Harvard Business School, and served in the Counter Intelligence Corps of the U.S. Army in World War II. He served as John D. Rockefeller, Jr's principal philanthropic advisor and worked closely with other members of the Rockefeller Family for more than 50 years. He was president of the Rockefeller Brothers Fund from 1950-1975. After his retirement, he remained active with many conservation and preservation organizations, including the Hudson River Valley Association, Sleepy Hollow Restorations, Boscobel and the Young-Morse Restoration Site. He was a board member of the Berkshire Taconic Community Foundation. Haisdan Farm is noted for its scenic beauty and has won state and national awards for its corn production. The funeral service will be private. The date of a memorial service will be announced. Contributions, in lieu of flowers, may be made to the Dana S. Creel Fund of the Berkshire Taconic Community Foundation, Lakeville, CT 06039.
