
        KLINGER-Beulah. Age 81, of Hollis Hills, Queens. Esteemed Music Teacher at the High School of Music and Art in New York City. Predeceased by her beloved husband Emanuel. Survived by her daughters Beth Agin, Arlene Romoff and Carole Rothman. Sister of Maureen Lerner & William Rabinowitz. Grandmother of six loving grandchildren. Services are Sunday, 11:30 AM at Robert Schoem's Menorah Chapel, W-150 Route 4 East, Paramus, New Jersey.
        KLINGER - Beulah. The Board of Directors, Staff and Members of the League for the Hard of Hearing note with sadness the death of Mrs. Beulah Klinger, mother of our esteemed Board Member Arlene Romoff. We extend our sympathies to Arlene and to her entire family. Joseph R. LaBato, President
