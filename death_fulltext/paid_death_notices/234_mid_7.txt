
        SL0TSKY--Gordon J. On July 11, 2006 at age 72 of cardiac arrest. Husband of Alice Weisfeld Slotsky; father of Pamela J. Slotsky and her husband Richard L. Zinser, Jr. Distinguished mathematical statistician, avid punster and devout audiophile. Donations in his memory may be made to Parkinson's Disease Foundation, 1359 Broadway, Suite 1509, NY, NY 10018.
