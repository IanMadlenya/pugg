
        GREEN-Walter J. 62. Died February 24 after a heroic three year battle with lung cancer. Husband of Rona, father of Adam and Matthew, son of Emily, he worked as the Chief of Corporate Editorial Services for the Metropolitan Transportation Authority. Walter was a man whose love, integrity, fierce intelligence, passion for social justice, respect for language, fervent spirit, and wit will continue to inspire all those who were close to him. Contributions in his honor can be made to the Walter J. Green Fund for Clearwater Urban Outreach of the Hudson River Sloop Clearwater, to the New York Civil Liberties Union, or to the New Israel Fund.
