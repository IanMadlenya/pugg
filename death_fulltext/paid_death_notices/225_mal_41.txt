
        LAISERIN--Maurice. 87, of Floral Park, N.Y. and Boca Raton, FL., died peacefully on October 3, 2005 at home in New York. Beloved husband and sweetheart of Dorothy for 62 years. Sensational father of Jerry Laiserin (Joyce) and Susan James (Andy). Devoted grandfather of Rachel Laiserin (Toby) and Aaron Laiserin, and Elizabeth and Ian James. Doting great grandfather of Daniel and Joshua Kovacs. Loving brother of the late Anne Epstein. A graduate of St. John's University, he was a CPA who served in the US Army during WW II, attaining the rank of Major. He was a Mason, a member of the Knights of Pythias and of the Old Westbury Golf and Country Club and the Boca Woods Country Club. He was a very special person who was beloved by everyone who knew him. A devoted family man, he was gentle, soft spoken and generous. He will be sorely missed by his adoring family and wonderful friends. Memorial service Wednesday, October 5, 1PM at the Sunset Chapel, 1285 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, N.Y. He will live in our hearts forever.
        $(6$)LAISERIN--Maurice. It is with deep sorrow that we record the passing of our esteemed member, Mr. Maurice Laiserin, and extend our deepest sympathy to his wife, Dorothy, and family. Old Westbury Golf & Country Club David Simon, President Steven Tolman, Secretary
