
        OKIN--Louis. Passed away peacefully on May 4th, 2007. Born August 25th, 1912, he grew up in Chattanooga, TN and the Bronx after his family immigrated from Russia. He attended NYU on a football scholarship and graduated from its law school as a member of Its law review. In addition to a successful legal career, he served on the New York City Tax Commission, City Council, and Civil Court. He will be remembered with deep respect, love, and affection by his wife, Anne, children, Elizabeth and Steven, their spouses, Jak and Carolyn, five grandchildren, Adam, Jared, Michael, Rebecca and Rachel, brother, Harry, sister, Reba and many other family members and friends. Services will be held at the ''Riverside'' 76th St and Amsterdam Ave at 11:15am on Sunday May 6. Donations in his memory can be made to Congregation Shearith Israel, 8 West 70th St, NY, NY 10023. $(6$)OKIN--Louis. Congregation Shearith Israel mourns the loss of its devoted member and extends sympathy to his family. Peter Neustadter, Parnas
