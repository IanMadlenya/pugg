
        SEIDMON--Dorothy (nee Solomon), age 92, of NYC, formerly of Plainfield, NJ, on May 19, 2004. Widow of Dr. Edward Edgar Park Seidmon and former wife of the late Joseph L. Goldman. Mother of Beth Ann (Arthur) Nelkin and Dr. E. James (Nancy) Seidmon. Grandmother of David, Laura (Max), Eric and Emily. Great-grandmother of Bella. A graduate of the University of Chicago, she attended its Graduate School of Social Service Administration. Interment private. Cremation Society of PA
