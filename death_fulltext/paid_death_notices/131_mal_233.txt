
        BIGGS-George Pearson. Age 91, of Southport, CT., died on Monday, December 15th, 1997. He was a graduate of the Hill School and Yale University. Survivors include his wife, Jane Shaw Biggs of Southport; sons, G. Whitney Biggs and his wife, Pauline, of Southport; and James P. Biggs and his wife, Elinor, of Greens Farms; two stepsons, William Reiter of Riverton, Wyo., and Brian Reiter of Miami, FL.; a sister, Jessie P. Smith; and grandchildren, nieces and nephews. A memorial service will be Friday, December 19 at 11 AM, in Southport Congregational Church, Pequot Avenue, Southport. For more information, contact Spear Funeral Home, Fairfield, CT.
