
        TOONI--S. Neal. Of Plymouth and Newton, MA; originally from Montclair, NJ, on April 4, 2005, at Massachusetts General Hospital, after a long and courageous battle with esophageal cancer. Adored husband of Mettie Whipple, and beloved stepfather to Arthur Schiro and Stephanie Schiro. He is also survived by his brother Frank Tooni of Montclair, NJ and sisters Suzy Kane of Taos, NM and Sally Hendrick of Ventura, CA. Memorial contributions may be sent to the Eel River Watershed Ass'n, Ltd., 52 Clifford Rd, Plymouth, MA 02360.
