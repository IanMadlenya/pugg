
        BRODSKY--Grace Ann (nee Duffy) of River Edge, NJ formerly of Manhattan on November 26, 2003. Mrs. Brodsky was a Registered Nurse with a Master's Degree from Georgetown University. She was Chief Executive and Owner of Medical Arts Center Hospital in New York City before retiring. She was formerly a parishioner of St. Peter's RC Church in River Edge. Beloved wife of the late William Brodsky (1959), daughter of the late William and Elizabeth Duffy, she is survived by her children, Barbara Lynn Brodsky of San Francisco, CA, Deborah Ann Brodsky of Montville, NJ and Paul Frances Brodsky of River Edge, NJ. Dear sister of Romayne Fialk of Pittstown, NJ, Paul Duffy of Archbald, PA, Eugene ''Oney'' Duffy of Rockaway Township, NJ and the late Francis Duffy, Jerome Duffy, Ronald Duffy and William Duffy, Jr. She also leaves behind many beloved nieces and nephews and especially Arthur Fialk of Pittstown, NJ. Dearly beloved friend of Rebecca Capabianca and family of North Caldwell, NJ. The Funeral service will be held Monday, December 1, 2003 at 11:30 AM from Vander Plaat Vermeulen Memorial Home, 530 High Mountain Road, Franklin Lakes, NJ and Funeral Mass will be performed at 12:00 PM at Most Blessed Sacrament RC Church, Franklin Lakes. The interment will take place at George Washington Memorial Park, Paramus, NJ. Family will receive their friends Sunday 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Donations to Sacred Heart RC Church, 4 Richards Avenue, Dover, NJ 07801 or to St. Jude's Children's Research Hospital, 501 St. Jude Place, Memphis, TN 38105 would be appreciated, in lieu of flowers.
