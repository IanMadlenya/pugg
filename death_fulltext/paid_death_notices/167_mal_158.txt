
        BARNEBY-Rupert C. The Board and Staff of The New York Botanical Gardens are deeply saddened by the loss of their colleague, Rupert C. Barneby. Rupert was a distinguished scholar, an inspiring mentor and an enormous contributor to the botanical world. We shall miss his wit, kindness, and inspiration. Wilson Nolen, Chairman Thomas J. Hubbard, Chairman Emeritus Gregory Long, President
