
        LIBERMAN-Melville N., on December 3, 2000, in his 93rd year. Predeceased by his dear wife Hilda. He is survived by his loving son Stuart and his granddaughters Tracy and Cari-Anne. Loving brother of Adele Feingold, Marjorie Mottus, Frank Liberman and the late Bertram Liberman and several nieces and nephews. He will be deeply missed by all who knew and loved him.
