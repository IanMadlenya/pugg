
        CARHART--Wendy. Died on Monday, March 13, at home in New York. Beloved daughter of Lucile and Amory S. Carhart, she is survived by her brother Amory Jr., her aunt Nancy H. Biddle and many cousins. A love of animals marked her life and she served for many years on the board of the ASPCA. Memorial service to be announced. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the ASPCA.
