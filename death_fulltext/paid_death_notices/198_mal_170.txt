
        HONT--Michael. The members of the Hungarian Feszek Klub mourn with deep sadness the passing of their Managing Director. Mr. Hont was a founding member of the club and his contributions, financial and cultural, helped sustain the Feszek Klub and fulfill its artistic and cultural mission. He will be sorely missed and his memory will be guarded with respect, gratitude and deep sorrow. Marianne Krencsey, President Paul Kovesdy, Chairman of the Board
        HONT--Michael. The Officers and Directors of the American Hungarian Foundation mourn with deep sorrow the passing of Michael Hont, a dedicated and leading member of our Board of Directors.
