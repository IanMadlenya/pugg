
        ADLER-Theodore W. Died at home in Scarsdale, NY on October 22, age 95. Devoted husband of Martelle and loving father of Louise and the late Thomas. Senior partner of Wolf Haldenstein Adler Freeman and Herz, LLP. Born in Des Moines, IA, brought up in Mattoon, IL, he graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Cornell University in 1928 and from Harvard Law School in 1931. He was a captain in the U.S. Army Air Force in WW II. Services private. Friends may call at home Tuesday, October 23, and Wedneday, October 24, 5-7 PM. Contributions may be made to White Plains Hospital or Cornell University.
