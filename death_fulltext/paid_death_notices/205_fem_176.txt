
        COSTELLO--Vivian. The entire Long Island College Hospital family and the Guild of Long Island College Hospital 1897 acknowledges with deep sadness the passing of our good friend and Guild Board member, Vivian Costello. Vivian was a volunteer member of the LICH Guild for more than 50 years and Chairman of the Guild gift shop for 40. she will forever be remembered for her commitment to LICH. To family, friends and loved ones, we extend heartfelt condolences The Guild of Long Island College Hospital 1897 John Wren, Chairman of the Board, LICH Stanley Brezenoff, Interim President & CEO, LICH John Byrne, Interim Chief Operating Officer Daniel D. Ricciardi, M.D. President Medical Staff
