
        KATZ-Esther (nee Gottesman). Wife of the late and beloved Harry Katz, died peacefully in Boca Raton, Florida, August 10, 2000. A woman of remarkable strength and intelligence. She was the true embodiment of family values. Her love and caring presence will be missed greatly by all who knew her. Mother of Aron and Phyllis Katz, Albert and Virginia Katz, and the late Richard Katz; sister of Abe Gottesman; grandmother of Martin and Marcellina Katz, Margaret and Stephen Cann, Rebecca Katz and Rachael and Nick Carey; great-grandmother of Alejandro and Francisco Katz, and Harrison and Simon Cann. May God grant you peace. Services will be held at 9:45 AM on Tuesday, August 15, 2000, at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 180 W. 76, Manhattan, NY.
