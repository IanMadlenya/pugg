
        SCHWARZ-Erwin, 93. Of Tucson, Arizona formerly of New York City and of Scarsdale, N.Y. Died in Gaithersburg, Maryland on February 1, 1998. He was married to the late Louise Lasker Schwarz for over 40 years and leaves his wife of 23 years Julia B. (Judy) Schwarz, three children, Arthur of Scarsdale, N.Y., Peggy Akin of Phoenix, Arizona and John of Tucson, Arizona, eight grandchildren and ten great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be held at, The Harmonie Club, 4 East 60th St N.Y. N.Y., on Sunday, February 8, 2 PM. Contributions may be made to the Ethical Culture Fieldston Schools, 33 Central Park West, New York, New York 10023.
