
        WETZLER-Monte E. Of Essex, CT. and Manhattan, died March 27. He was the husband of Sally Elsas, father of Andrew, brother of Garrett and his wife Cynthia. His legacy shines through; his deeply held sense of integrity, his gentleness, the clarity of his mind and his natural grace in adversity. A graveside service will be Friday March 29 at 1:00 PM in Riverview Cemetery, N. Main St. Essex, CT. A reception will be Monday April 1 from 3-5 PM at the Essex Yacht Club, 13 Novelty La. Essex. Contributions in his memory to Vanderbilt Shy-Drager Research Fund, Vanderbilt University Medical Center., AA-3228 Medical Center North Nashville, TN. 37232-2195.
