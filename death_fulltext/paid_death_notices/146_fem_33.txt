
        LEONARD-Elizabeth Peach, 87. On February 22, in Albuquerque, N.M. She was preceded in death by her husband Richard Anthony Leonard. Born in Emporia, KS, a graduate of the University of Kansas, she lived for many years in New York City and Larchmont. She was a board member of the Riverdale Children's Association, Candlelight Concerts benefiting the Wilton Library Association, and Senior Warden of St. Matthew's Episcopal Church. She is survived by her sons Anthony Charles of Albuquerque, NM, Amb. John Peter of Falls Church, VA and Richard William of New York City, seven grandchildren and one great-grandson. Burial service will be at St. Matthew's, Wilton, CT on March 13, at 11AM.
