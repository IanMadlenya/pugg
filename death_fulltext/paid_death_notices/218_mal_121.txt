
        ROSENBAUM--Aaron. The Yeshiva University and Rabbi Isaac Elchanan Theological Seminary Family is saddened by the passing of a distinguished leader of the Jewish community. Our sincerest condolences to his wife, Rosalind, children Anne Rosenbaum (and Rabbi Harvey) Senter, Esther Rosenbaum (and Rabbi Dr. Sidney) Scharf, Rabbi Yitzchak (and Judith) Rosenbaum, and Rabbi Yehuda (and Tova) Rosenbaum. Heartfelt condolences are extended to his brother, a member of the RIETS Board of Trustees and YU Guardian, Cantor Jacob H. (and Dr. Belle) Rosenbaum. May the entire family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Yeshiva University Richard M. Joel, President Dr. Norman Lamm Rosh HaYeshiva, RIETS Morry J. Weiss, Chairman YU Board of Trustees Julius Berman, Chairman RIETS Board of Trustees
