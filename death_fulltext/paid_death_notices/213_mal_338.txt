
        WEISSLER--Irving. On October 30, 2004 at age 97. Beloved husband of the late Gladys. Loving brother of Frances and the late Manny. Devoted uncle of Elizabeth, Linda and Amy. Caring step dad of Rachel and Cyrus. Longtime active and influential member in the Jewish Community and great friend to many, especially Hy and Joe. He had such a zest for living with a wonderful sense of humor. Services today, at Congregation Mt. Sinai, 250 Cadman Plaza West, Brooklyn, NY 11201. In lieu of flowers donations may be made to either Congregation Mt. Sinai, Congregation B'nai Abraham, 117 Remsen St., Brooklyn, NY 11201 or Brooklyn Heights Synagogue, 131 Remsen St., Brooklyn, NY 11201.
