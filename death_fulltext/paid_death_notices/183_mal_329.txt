
        STERN-Gerald. 68, died on April 26, 2002, at his home in East Moriches. Beloved husband to the late Doris Stern. Devoted and loving father to sons Nelson and Andy, and daughter Teri Wilde. Doting and incredibly proud grandfather to Jeffrey, Matthew, Adam, Daniel, Jessica and Corey Stern, as well as Dylan Wilde. He is also survived by his adoring sister Rita Way and his loving partner Marian Saggese. He built his career at Paul Weiss, where he became partner and stayed more than 30 years. Admired for his loyalty, intelligence and many passions, he will live on through his family and friends, but will be greatly missed
