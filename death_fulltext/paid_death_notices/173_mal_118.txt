
        BOND-Simon (Sasha). The family of Simon Bond wishes to thank all of the people who attended his funeral and, in particular, to express our appreciation to Mr. and Mrs. Irving Schneider, who consistently and faithfully were in touch with Simon Bond and worried about him, to Dr. Norman Lamm, who was his moral and spiritual guide, to Rabbi Sol Roth, who was his religious advisor, to his friend, Rabbi Arthur Schneier and to his Doctor, Martin Fox.
