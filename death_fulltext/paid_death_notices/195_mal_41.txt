
        BOGAN-Carl. Our beloved, age 89, died on March 29, 2003. He is survived in marriage to the late Mary Fox by son Robert Bogan, daughter Andrea Haber, four grandchildren and four greatgrandchildren. He is survived in his second marriage by wife Mary Moss, her eight children and fifteen grandchildren. His career in law enforcement as a detective with the NYPD and criminal investigator for the U.S. Department of Justice was exemplary. For those whose lives he touched, please join us for the Memorial service April 12th, 10 AM at The Osborn in Rye, New York. Love,Mary Bogan
