
        YINKEY--Millicent Barham, born April 6th, 1915 in Los Angeles, CA, died peacefully at home June 11th, 2006 in Stonington, CT. Wife of the late Arden Yinkey, Jr. She is survived by her children Charles Barham Yinkey (Edith) of Hyattsville, MD, Anne Yinkey Helm (Harold) of Louisville, KY, Elizabeth Woods Yinkey Moore (Clement) of Chestertown, MD, Susan Goff Yinkey of New York City; four grandchildren & one great-grandchild. A memorial service will be announced at a later date. Contributions may be made to the Stonington Free Library, Stonington, CT or Hospice of SE Connecticut, P.O. Box 902, Uncasville, CT 06382.
