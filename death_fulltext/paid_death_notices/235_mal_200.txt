
        CAMPBELL--Robert G., M.D. The entire St. Luke's-Roosevelt (SLR) Hospital Center family acknowledges with deep sadness the passing of our dear friend and colleague, Robert G. Campbell, M.D. Dr. Campbell served for many years as Executive Vice President and Medical Director for SLR and played a key role in the hospital center's growth and development. He will be remembered by all who knew him and worked with him for his dedication to quality patient care, the advancement of medicine through research and the education of the next generation of physicians. To his wife Barbara, his children Robert, Susan and William, the entire Campbell family, and all who loved him, we extend heart felt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman, Board of Trustees Richard F. Daines, M.D. President and CEO Donna Mendes, M.D. President, Medical Board $(6$)CAMPBELL--Robert G., M.D. The New York Medical and Surgical Society mourns the death of our esteemed colleague and dear friend, Dr. Robert G. Campbell. Possessed of a gentle and unfailing sense of humor, kind, caring, sensible, and steady, he wonderfully epitomized all the best characteristics of a physician. We will miss his presence greatly. Our sincere condolences to his wife Barbara and the Campbell family.
