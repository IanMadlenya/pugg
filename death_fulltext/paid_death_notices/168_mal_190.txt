
        BERGREEN-John. Beloved husband of Natasha Cowger. Cherished son of Adele and Morris Bergreen. Dear brother of Laurence Bergreen and Betsy. Wonderful uncle of Nicholas, Sara, Hanan, Tim, Jeff, Robert and Nanoucka. Generous, kind, unique, talented, charming and unforgettable John. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St. Monday and Tuesday, January 15 and 16, 7-9 PM. Services at Temple Emanu-El, 5th Ave. at 65 St., Wednesday, January 17, 11:30 AM. Interment private.
        BERGREEN-John. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses profound sympathy to the family of John Bergreen. Taken from this life before the fullness of years, our hearts reach out with compassion and caring concern. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted in the preciousness of remembrance. Dr. Ronald B. Sobel, Sr. Rabbi Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
