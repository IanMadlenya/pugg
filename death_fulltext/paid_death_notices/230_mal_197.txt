
        MEYER--Brett J. Age 60, died on March 12, 2006, after a courageous fight against Lymphoma. Brett was an ethical and devoted attorney who served as a partner at the law firm of Pryor, Cashman, Sherman and Flynn. Brett is survived by his loving wife of 34 years Patricia, his two children Damon and Elizabeth, his mother Elaine, his brother Douglas, sister-in-law Jill, nieces Emily and Sara and an energetic Labrador Retriever, Maggie. Brett's integrity, his perpetual optimism and his never ending love of life will live on in his friends and family, and he will be deeply missed. Services on Thursday, at 11:30 AM at Frank E. Campbell (The Funeral Chapel), 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st St. In lieu of flowers please send donations in Brett's name to Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center.
