
        AERENSON -- Marjorie, 81, died October 24, 2003. Beloved wife of Bob. Cherished mother of Carol Lucy. Devoted sister to Helen Gurwitt Saltzman, Reis Gurwitt (Lenore) and the late Laurel Gurwitt Chaback. Also survived by Dorothy Rosenberg, Annette and Lee Aerenson, many nieces, nephews, great nieces, great nephews, cousins and friends. Dedicated volunteer and advocate for justice. She will be greatly missed.
