
        PFEFFER-Maxine S. It is with great sorrow that the United States Attorney's Office for the Southern District of New York and its employees mourn the passing of Maxine S. Pfeffer, our friend and colleague, of cancer on June 23, 1998. Ms. Pfeffer was 37. We will miss Maxine, an Assistant United Sates Attorney, whose talents, energy and commitment to public service made us proud to be her friend and colleague for these past seven years. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her beloved husband, Khalid, their precious son, Sami, and her loving mother, Shirley. Memorial service on Friday, June 26, 1998 at 10:00AM in Courtroom 110, United States Courthouse, 40 Centre Street, New York, New York. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to ''The Maxine Pfeffer Trust Fund for Sami Chahi,'' c/o Michael Carolan, Merrill Lynch Trust Company, 717 Fifth Avenue, 9th Floor, New York, NY 10022.
