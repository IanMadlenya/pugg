
        MINKOFF--Harry. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Harry Minkoff, beloved husband of Ruth Minkoff, a treasured friend whose dedication to our Long Island Campaign profoundly impacts millions of lives in New York, in Israel, and around the globe. We offer our heartfelt condolences to Ruth and her entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
