
        GOSSARD -- Sander ''Sam''. The Jazz at Lincoln Center family mourns the loss of Sander ''Sam'' Gossard, project director from 2000-2004 for the creation of Frederick P. Rose Hall. At a crucial time in the facilities development, Sam dedicated his extraordinary energy and talent to building this new home for jazz. We extend our condolences to Pamela, his children and the entire Gossard family.
