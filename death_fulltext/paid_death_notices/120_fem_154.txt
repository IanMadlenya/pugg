
        KERN-Ruth, 89, North Miami Beach, died Jan. 21, 1997. Beloved wife of Jimmy for 69 years. Dearest mother of Dorothe and Joseph Levy and the late Henry. Cherished grandmother of Susan and Allan Deutsch and Debbi and Jerry Waters. Adored greatgrandmother of Hayley and Joshua Waters. Loving sister of Gladys, Shelly, Ruben and the late Helen and David. She was a treasure to all who knew and loved her
