
        COBB-Dorothy Laura. Died June 16, 2001 at home, at the age of 99. Born in Staten Island, NY in 1901, the daughter of Merton Raleigh Cobb and Elsie Hathaway Cobb. Retired head librarian of the St. Agnes Branch of the New York Public Library. Dorothy was a loyal and active participant in the Protestant Episcopal Church in the Diocese of New York and in St. Michael's Parish, for many decades. She was an inspiration and friend to many members of the Clergy & Laity. A funeral service will be held at 2:00 PM on Friday, June 22 at St. Michael's Church, 225 West 99th Street. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to Music and Arts at St. Michael's Church.
