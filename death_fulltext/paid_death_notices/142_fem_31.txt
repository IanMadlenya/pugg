
        TILT-Victoria P. Devoted daughter of Albert and the late Mary C. Born 1939, Vicki graduated from Miss Porter's School in 1957 and completed her studies at Briarcliff College in 1959. Vicki spent over 20 years in the travel business with Fredric Helton in Manhattan, NY. She was a woman of great love and thoughtfulness to her friends and family, aunt of four, greataunt of five, and godmother of twelve. Vicki will be deeply missed. Memorial service at St. John's, Salisbury, CT, 11:30 A.M., on Saturday, November 14. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to Bide-A-Wee.
