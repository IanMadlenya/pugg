
        KOHLER-James E. Sr. On November 7, 2001, of Breezy Point, NY, formerly of Brooklyn, NY. Deputy District Attorney, Kings County, NY. Former Deputy Commissioner, NYFD. Beloved husband of Jeanne. Loving father of Patricia and Thomas McVeigh, James Jr. and Patricia, Theresa and William Estes, Michael, Andrew and Margaret, and Jeanne. Cherished grandfather of T.J., Kaitlin, Sean, William and Christopher. Friends may visit Thursday and Friday, 2-5 and 7-10, Marine Park Funeral Home, 3024 Quentin Road, Brooklyn, NY. Mass of Christian Burial, Saturday 10AM at St. Thomas Moore R.C. Church, Breezy Point, NY. Interment to follow at St. Charles Cemetery, East Farmingdale, NY.
        KOHLER-James E. The Kings County District Attorney and his staff mourn with deep sorrow the passing of James E. Kohler, beloved husband of Jeanne, wife of more than 40 years. Loving father of Patricia, James, Michael, Teresa, Andrew and Jeanne and beloved grandfather of T.J., Kaitlin, Sean, Christopher and William. James was a man of honor, goodness and decency, who served the people of Kings County with distinction as a Deputy District Attorney for 12 years. He was a cherished and loyal friend, fierce and fair litigator and mentor to many in his personal and professional life. His brilliant mind, twinkling eyes, and indomitable spirit will be missed but remembered by all those whose lives he touched. Charles J. Hynes, District Attorney and the Staff of the Kings County District Attorney's Office
