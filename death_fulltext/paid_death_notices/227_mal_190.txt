
        LIVINGSTONE--Curtis Stephen. Curtis Stephen Livingstone of Sleepy Hollow, NY died on December 16, 2005. He was 51 years old. Survived by his wife Laurie Ann; his children Dylan Thomas age 6 and Sean William age 4. He was Senior Vice President of Security Architecture for Citigroup, New York, NY. He is also survived by his parents Adam and Phyllis Livingstone; brother Christopher Livingstone and sister Patricia O'Leary. Visiting Sunday 7:009:00 and Monday 2:00-4:00 and 7:00-9:00 PM, Beecher Funeral Home, Pleasantville, NY. Funeral service Tuesday, 10:00 AM, St. John's Episcopal church. Interment Kensico Cemetery. In lieu of flowers donations to a College Fund for the children in care of Beecher Funeral Home would be appreciated.
