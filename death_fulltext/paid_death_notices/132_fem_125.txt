
        BUTLER-Kathleen. Died January 10, 1998, at The Meadow Lakes Retirement Community in Hightstown, NJ. Born January 4, 1914 in New York City and educated in NYC schools. She attended Hunter College High School and Drake Business College. Mrs. Butler resided in Little Silver, NJ and in Silver Spring, MD for many years. She leaves her husband of fiftyseven years Herbert Butler of Hightstown and two sons, David Butler, Audio Engineer of Newton, Mass and Alan Butler, Business Consulting Manager of Pittsfield, Mass. Also one sister Maureen Florio of North Haven, CT. Mrs. Butler was a past president of The Goddard Space Flight Center Wive's Club in Greenbelt, MD. She enjoyed playing the piano, ballroom dancing, reading, letter writing and flower arranging, particularly of the Dahlias grown by her husband. Mrs. Butler has belonged to The Women's Community Club of Eatontown, NJ and The Little Silver Historical Society. A memorial service will be held at Meadow Lakes, Hightstown, at 2 P.M., on Thursday, January 22, 1998. Kathleen donated her body for the advancement of medical science. Please make contributions to The American Parkinson's Disease Association, 1250 Highland Blvd, Staten Island, New York 10305-1946.
