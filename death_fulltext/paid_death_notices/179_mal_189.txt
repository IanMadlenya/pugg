
        MUSHER-Albert. On December 14, 2001, age 97. Beloved husband of the late Willma. Loving father of Betsy M. Boruchoff, Jonathan E. Musher and Leo Boruchoff. Dear grandfather of Nancy Orgel, Barbara Winer, Louis, Caroline, Noah, Lucas and Jeremy Musher. Great-grandfather of Jeffrey, Hilary and Alison Winer and Evan and Zoey Orgel. Cherished friend of Lorraine Albert. Service Sunday 1:00 PM at ''the Riverside'', 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
        MUSHER-Albert. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Albert Musher our deepest sympathies on the occasion of his passing. It is our hope and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to his life. May all who loved him be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
