
        STIEREN-Arthur Tewes Stieren, Jr. passed away on Sunday, February 4, 2001 at the age of 71. The cause of death was lung cancer. He was born in San Antonio, Texas on October 24, 1929 to Arthur Tewes Stieren and Elizabeth Huth. He attended Alamo Heights schools and graduated from Culver Military Academy in 1946 and from Yale University in 1950. He entered the Harvard Business School in 1950, but his education was delayed by serving in the U.S. Army Field Artillery for two years. He served in this country and in Korea. He was honorably discharged a First Lieutenant in 1953. After returning to the Harvard Business School, he was awarded a Masters of Business Administration in 1954. He is survived by his wife, Jane and daughters, Lisa Cox, Wendy Wirth, Kelly Daniell, Amy Burgess, by his son, George L. Stieren, and numerous grandchildren. He is survived also by a sister, Betty Kelso. He was a Trustee of Trinity University, San Antonio, and was active in the McNay Art Museum, the Santa Fe Opera and the Metropolitan Opera. A Memorial Service at Parker Chapel, Trinity University, is planned for Monday, February 12, 2001 at 4:00 P.M. Contributions are welcome to the institutions named above, as well as to the charity of choice. Services with Porter Loring Mortuary, 1101 McCullough, San Antonio, Texas, 210-227-8221.
