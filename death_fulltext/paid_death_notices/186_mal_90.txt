
        WALKER-Benjamin James. Of Airmont, died tragically and innocently at The WorldTrade Center on September 11, 2001. He was 41. Benjamin was born on August 2, 1960, in England, to David Walker and Mary Brown. Benjamin graduated Ashfolt Prep School and St. Edwards College in Oxford, England. On September 30, 1989, he married Laura Kenney in Queens, NY. At the time of his death, he was an insurance broker for Marsh & McLennan for over 20 years. He was a fan of many sports. His passion for sports led him to become a soccer coach for the Hal Block soccer league and the Ramapo little league. Survivors include his devoted wife Laura, of Airmont; three loving children Henry, Christopher, and Samantha; his beloved parents David Walker and Mary Brown of England; dear sisters Jane Chevis, Ruth Walker, and Anne Marie Mosley, all of England; cherished nieces Rebecca Chevis, and Rowena Mosley; and a nephew Mark Chevis. The family wil be present to receive friends on Friday, July 12, 2002, from 7-9 PM, only at Wanamaker & Carlough, 177 Rte 59 in Suffern, NY. Funeral services will be private at the family's request. In lieu of flowers, please send donations to the Walker Educational Fund, c/o Brian Praver, Ridgewood Financial Center, 1 E. Ridgewood Avenue, Ridgewood, NJ 07450.
