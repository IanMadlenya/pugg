
        ARCADI-Antony. Of Brooklyn on March 3, 2002. Beloved husband of Linda (nee Squiciarino). Loving father of Antony Philip and Jackie. Cherished son of Carmela Arcadipane. Fond brother of Mariann. Adored grandfather of Gabrielle. Antony was a C.P.A. and professor at Brooklyn College, where he was a department chairperson. He donated his time as an auditor for New York City under former Mayor Giuliani. At the request of the deceased, funeral services will be private. The family will receive visitors at their home on Tuesday and Wednesday, 4-9PM.
