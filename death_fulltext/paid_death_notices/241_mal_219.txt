
        RICHARD--Peter Lanman, of Sloatsburg, NY, beloved husband and father, died suddenly on February 14, 2007 at his winter home in Camden, South Carolina. He is survived by his wife of 35 years, Virginia, his sons Zachary and Travis and daughter Cecily, his parents Jane and Harold V.B. Richard and his sister Phyllis Fritts. He was a graduate of St. Mark's School and received a BA and JD from his the University of Denver. He was an active investor and venture capitalist, serving on the boards of many companies during his long career. He was Chairman of the Board of TDG Aerospace and a member of the board of directors of Microdose Technologies Inc, at the time of his death. He was the Chairman of the Board of the Tuxedo Park School in Tuxedo Park, New York from 1991 to 1995 and a long-time member of the board of the York Harbor Reading Room in York Harbor, Maine. He was a devoted husband and father, a kind and loyal friend, and a passionate New York Yankees fan.
