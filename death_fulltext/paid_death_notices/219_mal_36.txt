
        RABIN--Leon J. Of Charleston, SC, and New York City, died on March 31 at 94 years of age. He was the retired owner and operator of Leon's Men's Wear and President of Dawn's Inc. in Charleston, Chairman of Rale Stores in South Carolina, North Carolnia and Newport News, VA. The funeral will be held at 3 pm at Emanuel Synagogue's Cemetery in Maryville, Monday, April 4. Mr. Rabin was born on May 14, 1911, in New York City, a son of Hyman and Anna Rabin. He graduated from New York City Schools and attended New York University. He was married for 64 years to his late beloved wife, Ann Mazo Rabin.
