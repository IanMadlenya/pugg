
        EDGELL-Stephanie. On Oct. 17, 1999. Beloved daughter of Stephen Maurice Edgell and Elsie Aldrich Edgell Campbell, sister of Stephen M. Edgell Jr. and Helena Franklin, and aunt of Stephen M. Edgell III, Helena Rozier, George Franklin, Cynthia Franklin, & Sheila Lieber. Cherished by her many friends for her extraordinary kindness, dry wit, perceptiveness & generosity. A lover of literature and the arts, a skilled potter, ardent traveller and caring social worker, she will be deeply missed. Please call (203) 454-9847 about funeral services. Donations may be made to the Greenwich, CT. Public Library, (203) 622-7900.
