
        PARSONS-Gerald A. Died unexpectedly on July 5, 1999 at his home in the Salishan Hills, on the Oregon Coast. He was 70 years old. Many kind words have been shared with the family by those who proudly considered him their friend. He will be missed by us all. A memorial has been established through Marylhurst University. Contributions are being taken by the Development office, P.O. Box 261, Marylhurst, OR. 97036.
