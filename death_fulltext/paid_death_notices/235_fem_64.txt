
        ISRAEL--Carole Rose. American Friends of Midreshet B'erot Bat Ayin mourns the death of our founder, Chaya Tova bat Chananya Hacohen and Chana on 7 Adar (August 1). She was a true woman of valor and a beautiful soul. We extend our heartfelt condolences to her husband Jonas, her sons Shimon and Avner, her stepmother Eve and her brothers Bob and Dan. May they be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. May the charity we give in her memory elevate her soul to the highest levels in Heaven. www.berotbatayin.org
