
        MARCUS--Seymour. 91, died on March 16, 2004. Devoted husband of the late Elsie, loving father of Jay and Toby Hoffman, cherished grandfather to Jeffrey, Amy, Wendy, David, Michael and Elyssa, adoring great-grandfather of four. With his brothers he founded Halsey Drug Company, also a founder of Congregation Tree of Life in Valley Stream. Memorial services were held on March 18, 2004. This kind and gentle man was respected and admired by everyone he touched.
