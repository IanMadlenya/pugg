
        BARDSLEY-R. Geoffrey. Of Stamford, CT., died August 14, age 72, while recovering from injuries sustained when struck by a vehicle June 7th. while training for a half marathon. Beloved husband of Elda Shantz Bardsley and father of Kathryn, Alex and Alyson. Born and raised in England, educated at Cambridge, he immigrated to the U.S. in 1956. Retired senior corporate executive specializing in international finance, who worked first for the Bank of England and then several New York banks, as well as Xerox Corporation and its subsidiaries, including Industrial Indemnity Finance Corporation, in San Francisco, of which he was president and CEO 1985-92. For the last 7 years, active volunteer and executive recruiter with International Executive Service Corps, personally acting as consultant on financial and development projects in Africa, Asia, and the former Soviet Union, as well as working with volunteers world wide. Marathoner, linguist, musician, gardener; driven and generous, he still had much to give. The world is poorer for his absence and he is painfully missed. A memorial service is planned for mid September in Stamford.
