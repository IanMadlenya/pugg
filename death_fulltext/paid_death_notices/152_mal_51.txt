
        STEIN-Louis Henry, M.D., D.D.S. A retired surgeon of White Plains, NY, and Stratton Mountain, VT, died September 2, 1999. He attended Lehigh University and the University of Pennsylvania School of Dentistry, and Jefferson Medical College. Dr. Stein practiced surgery in Westchester and Rockland Counties. Dr. Stein was a direct descendant of Jack Loeb, the original owner and founder of Fox Films. He is survived by his wife Carol Morgan-Stein, his son Louis Henry Stein, his step-sons Curtis Michael Hart and Carl Christopher Hart, and three granddaughters Jessica, Taylor-Ann and Ashley Nicole. Gifts to honor his memory may be made to Mayo Clinic for Research in Cardiology, 200 First St. SW, Rochester, MN 55905. Funeral service Tuesday, 1:30 PM, at the Chapel in the Woods, at Congregation Kol Ami, Soundview Ave., White Plains, NY. Friends may call at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave., White Plains, NY, Sunday 7-9 PM, and Monday 2-4 and 7-9 PM.
