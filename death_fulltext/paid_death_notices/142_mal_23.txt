
        BREGMAN-Dr. Stanley M. Of South Orange, NJ, a devoted and beloved practitioner in optometry died Sunday in St. Barnabas Medical Center, Livingston, NJ. He was 75. Dr. Bregman was a world traveler, a gourmet cook, a gifted pianist and a patron of the arts. Dr. Bregman is survived by his wife of 52 years, Dorothy, four children: Peter, Dr.Sally, Daniel and Emily, a son-on-law Mark Nedostup, daughters-in-law Susan Zimmer and Valorie Petrak and four grandchildren: Nicholas and Matthew Nedostup and Andrew & Laura Bregman. Funeral services Wednesday, November 4, 12 noon Apter-Bernheim-Goldsticker, 1600 Springfield Avenue, Maplewood, New Jersey. In lieu of flowers, please make a contribution to the charity of your choice.
