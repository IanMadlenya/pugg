
        BROWNSTEIN--Dorothy. 88, of Hollywoood, FL. Beloved mother of Harriet Rothman and Dale Freedman, died on April 15, 2004, after a long illness. She will be greatly missed by all who knew her. A graveside service will be held Monday, April 19, 2004, at 10:30 am, in Mt. Neboh Cemetery in Queens, NY.
