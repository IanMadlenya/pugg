
        ENGELMAN-Gus. Former reporter for WABC Radio and the NY Journal American, adjunct professor at NYU, former president of the New York Press Club. The family of Gus Engelman deeply mourns his passing on May 7. His intelligence, wit, enthusiasm for life and love of knowledge will be greatly missed. He is survived by his wife Linda, mother Eva Engelman, brother Harold Engelman, nephew Mark Engleman, nieces Alice Engleman and Roni Bongiovianni, three grandnieces and two grandnephews. A memorial service will be held on Sunday, May 20, 2 PM, at Morrison Funeral Home, 86 Bartholdi Ave., Butler, NJ. For further information call 973-838-2290.
