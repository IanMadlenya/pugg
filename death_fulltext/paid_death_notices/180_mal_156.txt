
        EDEN-Robert Earl. Of New York City. Died on January 5, 2002. For 40 years, beloved husband of Susan Hendrickson Eden. Loving son of Judith Eden Nelson, Sun City, AZ; cherished brother of William J. Eden, St. Paul, MN; and a perpetual source of mirth and merriment to an extended family of true and devoted friends around the world. Memorials in his name may be made to Central Park Conservancy, Memorial Gift Fund, 14 East 60 St, 8th fl, NY, NY 10022.
