
        OGILVY -- Stephen Hunter, age 88, of Westport, CT, passed away on June 16, 2005. Mr. Ogilvy was born on November 9, 1916 in New Rochelle, NY. Mr. Ogilvy graduated from Princeton University in 1939. He founded the AA Hearing Aid Center in 1946. An avid tennis player, he was inducted in the New England Tennis Hall of Fame in 1997. He is survived by his wife of 64 years, Alicia, nine children, 30 grandchildren and 14 great grandchildren. A memorial service will be he held at the Saugatuck Congregational Church in Westport, CT at 10:30 AM on July 9, 2005.
