
        HERST-Elissa S., 32, died Monday May 22. at her office. She was a resident of New York City for the past 11 years. A graduate of Hood College, she was President of Zoom Associates Inc, a member of the Museum of Modern Art and the Children's Hearing Institute. Memorial contributions may be made to a charity of the donor's choice. Funeral services were held at Har Sinai Temple on May 24th.
