
        LASELL-Chester H. On September 30, 1998, in Greenwich, CT. His first wife Ruth D. Lasell died in 1973. In addition to his widow, Elizabeth G. Lasell, he is survived by two children-Virginia L. Westgaard, of Denver and Chester K. Lasell, of Manchester, VT and Vero Beach, FL; a stepdaughter, Elizabeth H. Cousineau, of Seattle; six grandchildren and two great grandchildren. Chester H. Lasell joined Morgan Stanley & Co. in 1935 at the Firm's inception. He was named a partner in 1951. He served as the second President of Morgan Stanley & Co. from 1971 until 1972 and continued to advise the Firm as an Advisory Director until recently. A memorial service will be held at 2:00 PM, Monday, October 5 at Christ Church Chapel in Greenwich. The interment will be private. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be directed to Williams College, P.O. Box 231, Williamstown, MA 01267, and Greenwich Hospital, C/O Development Office, 5 Perryridge, Greenwich, CT. 06830.
