        BECKER-Ted E. Sr. M D. The Child Analysis of The New York Psychoanalytic Institute mourn the loss of their esteeemed colleague, Dr. Ted E. Becker Sr. He had a unique ability to understand children, and was an outstanding and gifted child analyst. He was responsible for training a generation of child analysts during the years he chaired the Child Analysis Committee. His interest, enthusiasm and commitment to child analysis, his skills as a teacher and supervisor, his personal warmth, his directness and integrity, served as an inspiration and model for us all, and drew many to the study of child analysis. He was our friend and guiding spirit. Our heartfelt sympathy to his wife, Dr. Amy Becker, and to his family.
        BECKER-Ted E, Sr. The members of the New York Psychoanalytic Institute and Society mourn the loss of Dr. Ted Becker. A revered and honored clinician and educator, Dr. Becker headed the program in child analysis at the New York Psychoanalytic Institute for many years. He has profoundly influenced the professional development of many generations of child and adult psychoanalysts, and will be deeply missed. We extend our sincere condolences to his wife and family. Albert Sax, M.D. President/Society Eslee Samberg, M.D. President/Institute
        BECKER-Ted E. Sr., M.D. The Board of Directors and Staff of Freedom Institute extend their heartfelt condolences to our esteemed colleague, Dr. Ted E. Becker Jr., Medical Director of The Institute, and all his family at this time of his father's death.
