
        DYBWAD-Gunnar. YAI/National Institute for People with Disabilities is deeply saddened by the passing of Gunnar Dybwad. A distinguished member of YAI's Professional Advisory Council and a passionate speaker at our International Conference, he was a true champion of the rights of people with developmental disabilities as well as an inspiration to all of us. He will be greatly missed. Joel M. Levy, D.S.W. Chief Executive Officer Philip H. Levy, Ph.D. President YAI/National Institute for People with Disabilities
