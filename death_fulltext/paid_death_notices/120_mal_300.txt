
        DVORTZOFF-Dorothy Kaufman. Wife of the late Harry. Sister of Ruth Kaufman Berthold and sister-in-law of Arthur J. Berthold. Sister of the late Ethel G. Kaufman and Robert L. Kaufman. Aunt of David A. Berthold and his wife Nancy, and great-aunt of David, Anna, Michael and Madiline Berthold of Keller, Texas. Mrs. Dvortzoff was a graduate of Brooklyn Jewish Hospital School of Nursing, and served as a member of the Army Nurse Corps in World War II. In lieu of flowers, contributions to your favorite charity will be appreciated. Services, 11:45 A.M., Wednesday, ''The Riverside,'' 76 St. and Amsterdam.
