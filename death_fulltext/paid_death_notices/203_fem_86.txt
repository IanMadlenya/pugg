
        EPSTEIN--Janice (nee Kaufman). On December 14, 2003. Devoted wife of the late William. Beloved mother of Carla Silber and mother-in-law to Dan Silber. Treasured grandmother of Rebecca and Abigail. Cherished aunt. Sister of the late Louis Kaufman, Mary Kaufman, Florence Silverstein and Bess Fisher. Proud graduate of NYU Washington Square College. Her energy for life and love of her family will remain her most important legacies. She lived life to its fullest. Her absence will leave a void in the hearts of her family. Shiva will be at the home of Dan and Carla Silber. Services Tuesday 1:30 pm at ''The Riverside'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Avenue.
