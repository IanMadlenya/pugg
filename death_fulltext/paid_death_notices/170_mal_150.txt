
        MITCHELL-Lee D. The Sophie Davis School of Biomedical Education/CUNY Medical School mourns the death of Dr. Lee D. Mitchell on February 17, 2001, after a courageous battle against cancer. Dr. Mitchell, a faculty member of the Department of Physiology and Pharmacology for the past 16 years, will be greatly missed as a teacher, advisor, raconteur and professional colleague. We express our deepest condolences to his wife Nancy. A future memorial service will be announced. Stanford A. Roman, Dean The Sophie Davis School of Biomedical Education
