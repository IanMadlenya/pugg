
        WHEELER--Anne Carroll. On May 21, 2006, of Elizabeth, NJ, in her 102nd year. Wife of the late Henry Lamont Wheeler Jr.; mother of Henry L. Wheeler III, Anne Harrison Buchanan and Elizabeth Cromwell L'Hommedieu. Also survived by seven grandchildren and fifteen great-grandchildren. A memorial service will be at St. Johns Church, 61 Broad St, Elizabeth, on Friday, June 23, 2006, at 1:30 PM. Contributions in her memory to Jefferson Park Day Care Center, 485 Madison Ave., Elizabeth, NJ 07201; or Family Children's Service, 16 Jefferson Ave., Elizabeth, NJ 07201; or Visiting Nurse Service, 354 Union Ave., Elizabeth, NJ, 07208, will be appreciated.
