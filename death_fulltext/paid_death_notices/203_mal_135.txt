
        GOLDBERG--George, 78, of Great Neck and East Moriches, retired publisher of SCAN Newsletter and President of GGX Assoc., on December 10, 2003. Devoted husband of Teddy and beloved father of Jeff of Washington, DC; Robbi of East Moriches; and David and wife Nanci of Port Washington; grandfather of Jonathan; brother of Leon and wife Brana of Tamarac, FL; and many nieces and nephews. Services at the RiversideNassau North Chapels, Great Neck, Friday at 1:15 PM. In our hearts forever.
        GOLDBERG -- George. We mourn the loss of our esteemed member and extend heartfelt condolences to his family. Temple Israel of Great Neck Howard Stecker, Sr. Rabbi Sidney Freedman, President
