
        STEINBERG-Frederick Theodore ''Ted'' February 27, 2000, of Delray Beach, Fla., formerly of Scarsdale and Chappaqua, N.Y. He was a visionary in the formation of the shopping center industry as it is today. He started the first shopping center on Central Ave. in Scarsdale. He and his brothers were instrumental in the development of the ICSC. He was a WWII US Army Veteran. He attained the rank of First Lieutenant and served in the European theatre. Husband of Ethel, father of Lyle, Spencer and the late Glenn Steinberg. Stepfather of Leslie Platto, and Marc and Bruce Stoller. Grandfather of Rachelle, Jared, Jamie, Carey, Jodi, Terry, Jason, Corey, Andrew and Erick. Funeral service at Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester in Rye, N.Y. on Tuesday at 2pm. Interment Kensico Cemetery. In lieu of flowers contributions may be made to the Hospice By The Sea, Inc., 1531 W. Palmetto Park Road, Boca Raton, Fla., 33486 or the American Cancer Society.
