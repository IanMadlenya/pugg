
        KASSEL-Jerry. The Board of Trustees of Long Beach Medical Center records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed colleague, Jerry Kassel. For over thirty years, Jerry Kassel helped advance the mission of the Medical Center through his generous support and his commitment to excellence. His wisdom, charm and caring spirit will be sorely missed by all. We extend our heartfelt sympathies to his beloved wife, Norma, and their son Lewis, daughter Terry and grandchildren. Alfred I. Osterland, Chairman, Board of Trustees Bernard Kennedy, President Board of Trustees Douglas L. Melzer Chief Executive Officer
