
        TISCH--Laurence A. The Board of Trustees, Administration and Faculty at Suffield Academy are deeply saddened by the death of Laurence Tisch. Laurence brought vision, leadership and a kind heart to the world. We are proud that his four sons and one of his grandsons are Suffield Academy alumni: Andrew 1967, Daniel 1969 (a member of Suffield's Board of Trustees), James 1971, Thomas 1972 and Michael 2002. Suffield is forever grateful for Laurence's friendship and generosity. Our deepest sympathies go to his wife Billie and the entire Tisch family. The Suffield Academy Community
