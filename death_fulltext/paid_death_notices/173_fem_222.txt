
        MOREHOUSE-Barbara. On Sunday, June 24, at age 58, of cancer. Born on March 31, 1943, the daughter of Murray and Elizabeth Sinclaire in Short Hills, NJ, she was a resident of New York City since 1970. She graduated with honors from Fordham University, Lincoln Center. She is survived by her husband, Peter Morehouse, sisters, Mary Attig and Katharine Guyer, brother, Murray Sinclaire many nieces and nephews, and countless loving friends. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, June 30, at 11AM, at Christ Church, 66 Highland Ave., Short Hills, NJ.
