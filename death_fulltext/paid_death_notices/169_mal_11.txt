
        KRUENER-Rev. Harry H. Died January 23, 2001 at the age of 85 in Camden, Maine. Rev. Kruener was the Senior Minister at Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims from 1960-1984. He was a marvelous preacher who touched many with a spiritual message and taught many about faith. Rev. Kruener is survived by two sons, John and Philip along with his second wife Cheryl. Memorial Service will be held at Plymouth Church of the Pilgrims located in Brooklyn Heights, on Orange Street between Hicks and Henry at 5 PM on February 15, 2001.
