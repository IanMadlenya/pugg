
        NERKEN-Jean. The Board, Faculty and Staff of The Cooper Union and the Albert Nerken School of Engineering note with sorrow the passing of Jean Nerken. With her late husband Albert, a trustee and major benefactor, Jean Nerken was a great friend of the college and a generous and loyal supporter. We are grateful for their friendship and we extend our condolences to her daughters Ruth and Sara and her entire family. Robert A. Bernhard, Chairman Dr. George Campbell Jr., Pres. Dr. Eleanor Baum, Dean The Albert Nerken School of Engineering
