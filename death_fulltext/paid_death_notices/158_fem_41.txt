
        STANTON-Mildred (nee Gilman) Scher. 93, of Hollywood Beach, FL. Formerly New York. Passed away February 28, 2000. Wife of the late Daniel Scher and Seymour Stanton. Devoted mother of Robert and Evelyn, Harold, Sara-Jane and Peter. Adoring grandmother of Julie, Daniel, Laurie, Dylan and Danielle. Cherished great-grandmother of eight. Sister of Bea and Arnold Henochstein and Alvin and Selma Gilman. Her wisdom and love will always be with us. Donations may be made to Aquarius Cancer Unit, 2751 South Ocean Drive, Hollywood, Florida, 33019, Apt. 1208 South.
