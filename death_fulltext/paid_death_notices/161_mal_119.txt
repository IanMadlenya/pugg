
        BALL-Andrew D. Beloved husband of Judith and devoted father of Jeffrey and Jonathan, died Saturday, June 10, 2000, at his home in Potomac, MD. He was 60 years old. Mr. Ball graduated from Yale University in 1962 and Harvard Law School in 1965. He worked as an attorney in New York and in private practice in Rockville, MD. He is survived by his wife Judith, his sons Jeffrey and Jonathan, and his sister Joan B. Sanger, who lives in New York. Expressions of sympathy to Jewish Council for the Aging, Washington, and Wellfleet Public Library, Wellfleet, MA.
