        HIRSCHTRITT--Ethel. The Board of Trustees, management and staff of the Jewish Home & Hospital extends their deepest sympathies to trustee Joel Hirschtritt, his wife Nancy and the family on the passing of their beloved mother Ethel Hirschtritt. Joel has been a valued trustee since 2001 and is a strong advocate for providing compassionate care, dignity and respect to older adults. We hope the loving memories the family shares will comfort them at this time of loss. Gene D. Resnick, M.D. Chairman of the Board Audrey S. Weiner, D.S.W. President & CEO
