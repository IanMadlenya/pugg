
        McDOUGALL--Olga Louise. 86, peacefully in her sleep at home in Pasadena, CA, on Monday, October 6, 2003, after a full, rich life. She is lovingly remembered by her three children, two sons-inlaw, one daughter-in-law, ten grandchildren, ten greatgrandchildren, and a host of other family and friends. In lieu of flowers a donation to the American Heart Association would be appreciated.
