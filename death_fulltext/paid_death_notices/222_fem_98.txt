
        BENJAMIN--Joan Ellett, 87, widow of William H. Benjamin, of New York and Garrison, New York, died while swimming in her pool at home in Garrison Saturday, July 16, 2005. She attended the Chapin School, Madeira School, and Bennington College. She was an accomplished sculptor and gardener. She is survived by her children, Beverley Goodwin of New York, John Benjamin of Garrison and Hotchkiss, CO, Nathaniel Benjamin of Vineyard Haven, MA; six grandchildren and eight great-grandchildren. Funeral services will be held at St. Philip's Church, Garrison, on Saturday, July 23, 2005, at 11AM. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to Hudson Highlands Land Trust, PO Box 226, Garrison, NY 10524.
