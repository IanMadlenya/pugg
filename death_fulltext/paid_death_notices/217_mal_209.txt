
        KUPERSMITH--Irving. Age 95. On February 14, 2005. Beloved husband of Sarah. Loving father of Fredric and Phyllis Jacobs, stepfather of Phyllis Cohen and Joan Easton. His extended family included seven grandchildren and ten great grandchildren. Past Grand Master and longtime distinguished brother of the Independent Order of Odd Fellows, Greater City Marshall Lodge Number 387. Services today, 11AM, Sinai Chapel, 162-05 Horace Harding Expwy, Fresh Meadows, New York, 718-445-0300.
