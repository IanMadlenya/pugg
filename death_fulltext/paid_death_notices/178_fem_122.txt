
        AMILL-Nancy Jayne, 68. Of Weston, CT, died on November 11, 2001 at home. Graduate of Brown University, beloved wife for 46 years of Richard, mother of Andrew, Alexander, & Sally. Grandmother of Ryan, Jarrett, Sean, and Kennison. Interment will be private. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Alzheimers Association, Southern Connecticut Chapter, 607 Main Avenue, Norwalk, CT. 06851.
