
        PELL--William R., 55, July 26, 2003. A graduate of Manhattan School of Music and Indiana University, Bill enjoyed an illustrious opera career in Berlin, Bayreuth, Canada, around the world. An adoring and devoted father he is survived by son Christopher, exwife Andrea, mother Helen Pell, sister Glenda. Memorial service Saturday, August 16, 5 PM. Christ and St. Stephen's Church, 120 W. 69th St., NY
