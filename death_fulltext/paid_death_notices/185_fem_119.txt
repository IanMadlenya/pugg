
        RAY-Rosaline Muckenfuss. 99, died June 14, 2002, in Akron, Ohio. Formerly of New York City and Montrose, Pennsylvania. Beloved wife of the late Hasseltine Chafee Ray. She is survived by her daughters, Rosaline R. Mather of Akron, Ohio, and Adeline R. deButts of Upperville, Virginia, seven grandchildren and 11 great-grandchildren. A lady of great spirit, she will be sorely missed.
