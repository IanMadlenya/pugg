
        DEVENNEY-Raymond K. On May 17, 2001 at his Manhattan home at age 84. A valued member of The Olayan Group for 24 years, Mr. Devenney was Executive Vice President of the group's U.S. subsidiary, Olayan America Corporation, at the time of his passing. He joined the group's New York office in July 1977 following a career as a financial executive with the Arabian American Oil Company (Aramco) in Dhahran, Saudia Arabia, and Houston, TX, that spanned some three decades. Originally from Kansas, he held a B.S. in finance from the University of Kansas. Mr. Devenney is survived by his wife of 54 years, Kay; their daughter Bunny Devenney of New York City; nephew Scott Devenney of Hawaii; niece Pamela Devenney-Peppers of New Canaan, CT; her husband Donald Peppers; and their children, Tess, Margot, and Bryce. A memorial service will be held at 5 PM on May 30, 2001 at the Church of Our Lady of Peace on 62nd Street in Manhattan, NY between Second and Third Avenues. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to the Wolk Heart Foundation, 520 East 72nd St., NY NY 10021
