
        BAILEY--Albert Lee Jr., 68, passed away on June 28, 2003 in Orlando, FL. He is survived by his wife Sally Ann, and loving children: Albert Lee Bailey III, Robert Ford Bailey, John Jackson Bailey, and Shannon Bailey Jordon. Mr. Bailey was a faithful member of many of the nation's historic and patriotic organizations. There will be a memorial service in New York City at a later date. >>AD#
