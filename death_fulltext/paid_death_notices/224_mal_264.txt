
        MOORE--Roy, Author/Photographer of ''Reflections on Greece'' and Co-Author/Photographer with his wife Alma of ''Thomas Jefferson's Journey to the South of France,'' died September 23 in NYC. Born in Virginia, he practiced his profession of travel and fashion photography in America and Europe. He is survived by his wife, his son and daughter-in-law, Michael and Gretchen Moore of Veazie, ME, his grandson, Demian Moore of Washington, DC, and his granddaughter, Tanya Moore of Miami Beach, FL. He was predeceased by his daughter, Melinda Moore. Droll, funny and feisty, he was deeply loved and will be greatly missed by his family and friends. Remembrances may be contributed for Stem Cell Research.
