
        PINIES--Jaime de. The officers, members of the Board of Directors and staff of Queen Sofia Spanish Institute mourn the death of our dear friend and Gold Medal Awardee, Ambassador Jaime de Pinies, who died in Madrid on December 29. Our deepest condolences to his brother and sister-in-law, Felix and Carmen de Pinies, his sons Jaime and Pablo, his daughter-in-law Maria and his grandchildren, Jaime, Javier and Luis. Jaime will be greatly missed. He will, however, remain in our minds and hearts and will be remembered with warmth and admiration for his personal qualities and his stellar career as one of Spain's most prominent ambassadors.
