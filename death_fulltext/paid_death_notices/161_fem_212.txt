
        MOSELY-Ruth Bissell. Loving wife of the late Philip Mosely, former professor at Columbia University. Mother of Ann Lesch and the late Patricia M. Jackson. Grandmother of Todd Jackson and Laura J. Adams, and caring aunt and great-aunt. Died on June 24 in Washington, DC. A memorial service will be held at Thomas House, 1330 Mass Ave. Washington, DC, 11 AM, July 1. Her ashes will be buried in Westfield, MA.
