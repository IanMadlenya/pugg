
        MURPHY--Justin J., 96, of East Hampton, CT, formerly of Manhasset, NY, husband of the late Helen (Nolan) Murphy, passed away Tuesday (December 28, 2004) at home. Graduate of Regis High School, New York, and honored by the school with its ''Deo et Patriae'' award. Honored recipient of The College of the Holy Cross ''In Hoc Signo'' award and 1929 Class Agent for his entire life. Life member of Plandome Country Club, retired from the New York Telephone Company and A. T. & T. Two succeeding careers, first with the Brooklyn Academy of Arts and Sciences and then President of the Downtown-Lower Manhattan Association. He is survived by a son, Justin J. Murphy Jr. Colonel, USAF retired and his wife, Dayle of Palmdale, CA; three daughters and sons-in-law, Kathleen and Ted Toombs of Glen Head, NY, Eileen and George Connelly of Vero Beach, FL, Rosemary and Bob Kilpatrick of East Hampton, CT, eight grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Funeral Monday, January 3rd, 11AM, St. Patrick Church, East Hampton, CT. Memorial contributions, The Holy Cross Fund, C/O College of the Holy Cross, One College Square, Worcester, MA 01610-2395 or Regis High School Development Office, 55 East 84th St., New York, New York 10028-1221.
