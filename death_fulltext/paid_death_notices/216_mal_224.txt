
        ABRONS--Herbert L. The Metropolitan Opera mourns the death of Herbert L. Abrons, a Member of the Association from 1988 until 2003. Herbert was a generous, caring man who will be missed by all who knew him here in this house. He was a good friend to our company, and we extend our deepest condolences to his wife, Hyonja, his brother, Richard, his sister Rita Aranow, and to all his children and grandchildren. Beverly Sills, Chairman of the Board William C. Morris, President
