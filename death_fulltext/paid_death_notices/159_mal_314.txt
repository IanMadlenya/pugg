
        ALTMAN-Jerome, DDS, 79, of Aventura, FL, formerly of Woodmere, NY, on April 27th, 2000 after a long illness. Best friend and devoted husband of 56 years to Anita. Loving father of Linda (Michael) and Meryl (Ralph). Adored grandfather of Dana (Michael), Jordan, Jared and Amanda. He graduated NYU College of Dentistry and enjoyed a long and successful career specializing in Orthodontics. An Assistant Clinical Professor in Orthodontics, he taught at NYU College of Dentistry 1967-1986. All who knew this kind, compassionate and wise man will miss him.
