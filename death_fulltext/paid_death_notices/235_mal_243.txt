
        MITCHELL--Robert D. Theatre designer, died on August 18th of complications from epithelioid hemangio endothelioma. Mr. Mitchell's scenic designs were produced on and off Broadway and by, among others, the National Theatre of Greece, the Paris Opera, and the Oxford Union. He is survived by his brother and sister-in-law, Fred and Ruth Mitchell, his nephew Kenneth Mitchell, his nieces, Martha Kinsel, Julie Manlogan, and Jean Touw, and his partner of 45 years, Patrick Horrigan. Contributions may be made to the American Cancer Society.
