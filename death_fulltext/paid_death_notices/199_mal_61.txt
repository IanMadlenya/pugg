Cherished grandfather of David, Emily, Daniel, Emma, Adam, A.J., Samantha and Jacob. Dear brother of Joan and Joe Lehman. Services at Frank E. Campbell, Madison Ave., at 81st St Wednesday August 6th, 1:30 PM. Interment to follow at Sharon Gardens Cemetery. Contribution in his memory may be made to: Jacob's Cure, 83 North Street, Harrison, NY 10528.
