
        BROWN--Mary Duer. 74, landscape watercolorist died September 14, 2005 at her home in Bethesda, MD. after a long battle with breast cancer. Mrs. Brown was a native of New York City and Middleburg, VA. She was a graduate of the Foxcroft School, Middleburg, VA and The School of the Museum of Fine Arts, Boston. She summered in Bass Harbor, ME. She was a member of the Southern Watercolor Society, Midwest Watercolor Society and Kentucky Watercolor Society. She painted for The Coast Guard Arts Program. She was a member of the Colony Club, NY, the Salmagundi Club, NY, the Sulgrave Club, Washington, DC and a former member of the Blue Hill Troupe, NY. She is survived by her husband, Stanley N. Brown, Jr., two daughters, Catherine Stanton of NYC, Starr Sears of Washington, DC, a son, Henry Brown of NYC, a brother, Beverley Chew Duer of NYC and five grandchildren. Donations in lieu of flowers may be made to: Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center 1275 York Ave. NY, NY 10021.
