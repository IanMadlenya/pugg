
        MELLIN-John. The Board of Directors mourns the loss of Mr. John O. Mellin, one of the founders and presidents of the Association in Manhattan for Autistic Children. For over 20 years he was a mainstay of AMAC, fostering the growth of autistic children and their families. His pioneering work is a lasting legacy. The AMAC family extends its sympathy to his wife Gloria and the entire Mellin family. He will be missed. The Board of Directors
