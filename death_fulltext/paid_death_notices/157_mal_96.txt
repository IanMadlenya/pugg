
        GOLDENSON-Leonard H. Founder and Chairman of the Board of ABC, Inc., 1953-1986, died in Sarasota, Florida on December 27, 1999 at the age of 94. He is survived by his wife, Isabelle Weinstein Goldenson, his two daughters, Loreen Arbus and Maxine Goldenson, both of Los Angeles, and one grandson, Lucas. A Memorial Service will be held Friday, February 11, 10:30 AM, at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street, New York City. Contributions can be made in his memory to The Dr. William Berenberg Fund for Handicapped Children, C/O Children's Hospital, 300 Longwood Avenue, Boston, MA 02115.
