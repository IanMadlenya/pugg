
        ABELOFF-Abram J., M.D. The Officers, Board of Trustees, and staff of the American Jewish Joint Distribution Committee (JDC) record with sorrow the loss of its esteemed Honorary Board Member. A modest, gentle, caring person he gave unstintingly of his energy and professional knowledge in bringing healing to tens of thousands of people around the world. An outstanding surgeon, he played a major role in developing JDC's medical and health programs in Israel, North Africa and Europe. After World War II, he visited D.P. Camps and shaped JDC's life saving medical services for 250,000 Holocaust survivors in Germany, Austria and Italy. His abilities, concern and vision made an enormous impact. We extend condolences to his beloved wife, Gertrude and his son Toby. Jonathan W. Kolker, President Michael Schneider, Exec V.P. Amb. Milton A. Wolf, Chair of Brd Ralph I. Goldman, Exec V.P.
