
        CARONE-William J. Of Bayville. On June 30, 1997. Beloved husband of Mary (Nee DeNatale)). Devoted father of William J. Jr., Elizabeth Peters and Julienne. Loving son of Catherine. The family will receive friends Tuesday 7-9pm and Wednesday 3-5 and 7-9pm at the McLaughlin Kramer Megiel Funeral Home, 220 Glen Street, Glen Cove. Funeral Mass St. Gertrude's Church, Thursday 10am. Interment Locust Valley cemetery. Donations in his name may be made to North Shore Hospital, Development Dept., 300 Community Drive, Manhasset, NY 11030.
        CARONE-William. The Educational Foundation for the Fashion Industries records with great sadness the untimely passing of our long-time member, William Carone. His exemplary commitment to the Foundation and the Fashion Institute of Technology has touched us all. Richard Streiter Leonard Siegel Bernard Olsoff Stephen Elkin
