
        GREER-John K. Died December 5, 2000. Mr. Greer was a U.S. Olympian in two sports, field hockey and fron tennis. He participated in six Olympic Games, and four Pan American. He worked for IBM until retiring in 1998. Husband of Ethel Lewis Taylor Greer, father of W. Kirk Greer, John T. Greer, Robyn G. Hallock, brother of Sheila Duker, grandfather of seven. A memorial service will be held Tuesday at 11:30 AM in Christ Church, 254 East Putnam Avenue, Greenwich, CT. Memorial contributions may be made to Men's Field Hockey Foundation, 3160 Rustic Oak Drive, Westlake Village, CA. 91361.
