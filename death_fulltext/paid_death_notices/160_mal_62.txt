
        BELMONT-Gerard. Age 77. Died May 5. Beloved father of Carol de Gunzburg and Valerie Belmont of New York and Geraldine Belmont and Patrick Belmont of Geneva, Switzerland. Loving grandfather of Gregory and Alexia de Gunzburg. He also is survived by his brother Francis Belmont and sister Nadine Grelsamer, both of New York. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Monday, May 8 at 4:30 PM. Interment Paris, France.
