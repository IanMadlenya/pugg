
        MURPHY-John Joseph, 91. Born September. 28, 1905, in Ballinlassa, Castle Bar, County Mayo, Ireland, passed away July 1, 1997, in Newport Beach, CA. He is survived by his wife Mary Murphy, daughter Mary Monroe, both of Newport Beach, CA, grandchildren Maura T., William J., and John D. Monroe, and sister Anne Reilly of Bilcarra, County Mayo, Ireland, plus numerous nephews and nieces. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated 11 AM, Monday, July 7, at Our Lady of Mt. Carmel Church in Newport Beach, CA.
