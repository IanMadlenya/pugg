
        LiMARZI-Joseph. Artist and teacher, born September 15, 1907, in Chicago; died peacefully March 4, 2000. Husband of the late Mary LiMarzi; stepfather of the late Aurora Askinazy. He is survived by a sister, Virginia LiMarzi; stepson, Louis Cassotta of New York City; step-grandchildren, Laurie Askinazy and Amy Cassotta and her two children; and two nieces, Carole Scapelli and Roberta Weinsheimer. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
