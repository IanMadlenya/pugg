
        FUTTER--Victor. Columbia College and the Office of Alumni Affairs and Development mourn the passing of Victor Futter, College Class of 1939. Mr. Futter served as President of the College Alumni Association from 1972-74. He was a dedicated supporter of the College and received the President's Cup in 1999. We offer our sincere condolences to his wife, Joan; children, Jeffrey, Deborah and Ellen, former president of Barnard College, and his seven grandchildren. Derek A. Wittner, Dean, Collumbia College, Office of Alumni Affairs and Development
