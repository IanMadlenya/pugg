
        BERLINGER--Rhonie Hofheimer, peacefully at home on August 31, 2004 after 89 vigorous years. Loving and beloved wife of the late George F. Berlinger. Devoted mother of Nancy Klee Stone and Mary Ellen Klee. Admiring mother-in-law of Bob Stone. Dear sister of Marion H. Levy and the late Lt. Lester Hofheimer. Proud ''Noni'' of Peter and Betsy, Robert and Douglas Kempner; Jim and Janet, Ellen and Julia Kempner; Carrie and Matthew, Brian and Daniel Getz. Rhonie actively served as a VicePresident of the National Alzheimer's Association since her husband's death in 1986. Funeral private. A memorial service and celebration of her life will be held Sunday, September 12 at 4pm at the Community Unitarian Church, 468 Rosedale Ave, White Plains, NY, 914946-1660. In lieu of flowers the family would greatly appreciate contributions in Rhonie's memory to Alzheimer's Association, 225 N. Michigan Ave, Chicago, IL, 60601; Hospice and Palliative Care of Westchester, 95 South Broadway, White Plains, NY, 10601 or a charity of your choice.
        BERLINGER -- Rhonie. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Rhonie Berlinger, a devoted leader whose dedication to our Women's Campaign and network of agencies strengthened the Jewish community of New York. We extend our deepest sympathies to her daughters, Mary Ellen and Nancy, and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
