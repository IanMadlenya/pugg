
        RATNER-Elizabeth M. Suddenly on Friday, June 28, 2002, of Bethesda, MD, formerly of New York, NY, after a long battle with Anorexia Nervosa. Beloved daughter of Victor and Judy Ratner. Loving sister of Deborah Ratner. Cherished granddaughter of Manfred and Sybil Siodmak, Rosalind Ratner and the late Morris Ratner. Despite numerous hospitalizations for anorexia and related illnesses, she had counseled other young women with the disease and volunteered to help doctors-in-training learn how to diagnose and treat psychiatric disorders in young women. Graveside services were held at the Beth El Cemetery, Paramus, NJ on Monday, July 1. Shiva at the residence of Victor and Judy Ratner on Tuesday and Wednesday evenings, 7:308:30PM. Memorial contributions may be made to the Eating Disorders Coalition, 733 15 St NW, Washington, DC 20015 or Housing Unlimited, 4928 Wyaconda Rd, Suite 15, Rockville, MD 20852. For information: Torchinsky Hebrew Funeral Home, Washington, DC 202-541-1001.
