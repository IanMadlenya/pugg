
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. 86, died April 21, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Marian and devoted father of Barry, Loren and Lisa and loving grandfather of Marc, David, Sarah, Tal, Elan, Tova and Benjamin. He was CEO of Spellman High Voltage Electronics; past chair for North Shore Campaign UJA; member of the Boards of Trustees Long Island Jewish Medical Center and Worcester Polytechnic Institute and President of Gurwin Geriatric Foundation. Funeral services were held on April 22nd.
        SKEIST--S. Merrill. The North Shore-Long Island Jewish (LIJ) Health System is saddened by the passing Thursday of our esteemed trustee, S. Merrill Skeist of Syosset, NY, and Delray Beach, FL. Mr. Skeist joined the LIJ Medical Center Board of Trustees in 1976 and was a generous supporter of LIJ and later, the North Shore-LIJ Health System. He and his late wife, Marion, were generous contributors to the Ann & Jules Gottlieb Women's Comprehensive Health Center in Manhasset, NY. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family and friends. Daniel de Roulet, Chairman Michael Dowling, President & CEO North ShoreLIJ Health System
