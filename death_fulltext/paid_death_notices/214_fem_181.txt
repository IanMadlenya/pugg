
        GILLMAN--Alecia, 51, a resident of New York City died on November 24, 2004 afer a short illness. She was born December 1, 1952 to Marcelle Pastoret Gillman and the late William G. Gillman. She graduated from Rosemont College with a B.A. and received a Masters Degree in business from Fordham University. Alecia was a Marketing Consultant for Merril Lynch. Besides her mother, Alecia is survived by her sister Eileen and her husband Greg Choron, three nieces, Rachel, Deborah and Rebecca Choron, a Goddaughter Ariel Sachtjen and her dear friend Burt Gordon. A mass of Christian Burial Saturday at Immaculate Conception Church, Tuckahoe at 10:30AM. Burial to follow at Woodlawn Cemetery.
