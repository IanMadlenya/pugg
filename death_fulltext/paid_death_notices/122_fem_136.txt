
        WARDELL-Elsa, A. On March 14, 1997. Devoted wife for 60 years of Charles W.B., Jr. and loved mother of Wendy Chamberlin, Charles III, Christopher and David. Grandmother of Talbot Harris, Hunter, Charles IV, Diana, Cooper, Clayton, and Cale. Sister of Hector Adam and the late Helen Simon. Graduate of Vassar College. She was a writer and researcher for Time Inc. and before retiring, special assistant to Henry R. Luce. She was active in many local charities and a board member of East Woods School and The Cold Spring Harbor Beach Club. Memorial Service at St. John's Episcopal Church, Cold Spring Harbor to be announced.
