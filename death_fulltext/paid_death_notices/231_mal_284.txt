
        FASTENBERG--Norman. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Norman ''Danny'' Fastenberg, beloved brother of Dr. David Fastenberg and brother-in-law of Leslie Fastenberg, dedicated friends and leaders whose deep commitment to the New York Jewish community profoundly impacts millions of lives. We extend our deepest sympathies to David and Leslie; to Danny's wife, Barbara, and their children, Joel and Michelle; to his parents, Abner and Shirley; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
