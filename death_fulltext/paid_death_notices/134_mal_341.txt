
        NANNI-Louis F. March 29, 1998. Loving husband of the late Ethel. Owner and proprietor of Nanni's Restaurant and Nanni Il Valletto, Institutions of Italian Cuisine. He will be greatly missed by all who knew him, especially his longtime and loyal staff. Friends may call Wednesday and Thursday, 2-5 and 7-9, at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street.
