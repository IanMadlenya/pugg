
        MARIANO-Robert J. In Los Angeles, October 28th, 2002. Beloved son of Guy & Mary, cherished brother of Patti and JoAnn. From the Broadway Stage as a child to the cutting edge of the communications revolution, Bob was a visionary who was always ahead of the curve. His genius, insight and sheer joy of living will by sorely missed by his family and friends throughout the world.
