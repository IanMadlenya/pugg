
        ANDERBERG--Elinor Drews. 89. Died March 28, 2006. She was predeceased by her sisters Mary Ralston Kenney, Jannet Douglass and her brother Edward and is survived by three nieces, two great nephews and one great niece. She was born on Staten Island on January 21, 1916, educated in White Plains, N.Y. public schools and graduated from Skidmore College in 1938 with honors in English. She had a long career in publishing, for many decades with Book-of-the-Month Club in the Art Department. After retirement, her focus changed to social work directed through her Church. With two parishioners she founded the West Side Campaign for Hunger. She also founded the Christ & St. Stephen Brown Bag Program. Funeral Saturday, April 8, 11AM at Christ & St. Stephen, 122 W. 69 St, NYC 10003. Contributions appreciated to either of above agencies.
