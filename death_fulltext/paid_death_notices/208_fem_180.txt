
        LEVY--Kathryn. On May 22, 2004, at the age of 103. She was always ''Kitty'' to her loving family and legion of friends. Wife of the late Dr. Abraham Levy, Kitty was active in the community. She is survived by three children, nine grandchildren and seven great-grandchildren. Graveside service on Monday, May 24, at 12:30 pm, at Mt. Ararat Cemetery, East Farmingdale.
