
        SPROW-Howard T. 82, died on May 25, 2002, at his home. Mr. Sprow graduated from Colgate University and Columbia Law School. He was General Counsel, Vice President and Secretary of Merrill Lynch from 1970 to 1977. From 1977 to 1980, he was a senior partner at Rogers and Wells. He was adjunct professor at Fordham Law School from 1972 to 1980. Professor Sprow taught at Albany Law School until 1991. He was a trustee at St. Lawrence University. Survivors include his wife, Mildred J. Sprow; children, Howard H. Sprow and wife, Madeline, Emmy Wilson and husband, Gary, and Matthew T. Sprow and wife, Kate; six grandchildren. Calling hours are Tuesday, 2 to 4:30 PM, with funeral following at 4:30 PM at McVeigh Funeral Home, Inc., 208 North Allen St., Albany, NY (518489-0188). Graveside prayers will be on Thursday at 1 PM at Atlantic City Cemetery, Pleasantville, NJ. Family suggests memorials to Albany Law School, 80 New Scotland Ave., Albany, NY 12208 or St. Lawrence University, Canton, NY 13617.
