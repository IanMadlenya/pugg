
        YOUNG--Alice Klein, 94, Peacefully on February 15, 2006. Beloved matriarch of the Klein family, she was cherished as daughter, sister, cousin, wife, mother, grandmother, and as the most devoted aunt to three generations of nieces and nephews. She was the widow of Frank O. Young and is survived by her devoted son Robert, daughter-in-law Renee, and grandchildren Brett, and Alyson Clark.
