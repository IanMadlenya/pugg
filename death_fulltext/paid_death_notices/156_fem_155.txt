
        STARK-Ruth. Much loved and ever loving wife; confidante and life-long friend of Morton Stark, died peacefully on Sunday, January 16 after valiantly fighting a variety of ailments for years. Cherished sister of Paula Rabinow. Fond aunt of and role model for Hannah Scherer, Andrew Scherer, Claudia Slovinsky, Sara Whipple and Laura Whipple. Doting greataunt of Maya Rose Scherer and Eli Scherer. Co-creator, along with her dear friend Florence Valla, of Stark-Valla, their pioneering Greenwich Village shop. All are invited to share fond memories at a mememorial service to be held at 3:00 p.m., Thursday, January 20 at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave.
