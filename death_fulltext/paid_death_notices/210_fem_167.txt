
        SUFFERN--Cecil H. A life time resident of NYC at her home on July 27, 2004. She is survived by her son, Stephen J. Suffern, her brother Melville Harris and her sister, Pearl Hoag. Mrs. Suffern was for many years director of broadcasting for the NYC Board of Education radio and television network. In 1975 she received a NY area television Emmy for the tv series ''Dial M for Music'' and a Governor's Award recognizing her 31 years of outstanding service to the Board of Education. Services are to be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel at 11:30 AM, Friday, July 30, 2004.
