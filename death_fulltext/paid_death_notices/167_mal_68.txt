
        SMALLBERG - Seymour. Died peacefully on December 4, 2000. Beloved husband of the late Ruth. Devoted father of Ralph and Victor. Adored grandfather of Michael, Eric and Kate. We will all miss your warm heart and constant love. Services Wednesday, December 6, 1PM, at ''The Riverside'', 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave
        SMALLBERG - Seymour. The Mayor and Trustees of the Village of Saltaire are profoundly grieved and saddened by the passing of their esteemed former colleague, Seymour Smallberg, on December 3. Seymour served our village with exceptional ability for ten years and earned the love, admiration and respect of all who knew and worked with him. A man of grace and humor who will be greatly missed by his friends. Our deepest sympathies to his sons, Ralph and Victor, and their families. Martin M. Berger, Mayor
