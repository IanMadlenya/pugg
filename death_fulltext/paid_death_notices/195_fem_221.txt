
        STEINBECK-Elaine A. On April 27, 2003 at age 88. Beloved wife of the late Nobel Laureate and Pulitzer Prize Winning Author, John Steinbeck. Mother of Waverly Scott Kaffaga and stepmother of Thomas Steinbeck and the late John Steinbeck IV. Grandmother of David Farber, Anderson Farber, Jebel Kaffaga and Bahar Kaffaga. Also survived by her sisters Jean Boone and Frances Atkinson. Memorial services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81 Street, Thursday, May 1st, 3PM. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Center for Steinbeck Studies at San Jose State University, San Jose, Ca, Bay Street Theater, Sag Harbor, N.Y. or Zachary Scott Theater Center, Austin, TX.
        STEINBECK-Elaine. The directors and staff of The Library of America note with sadness the death of their friend and advisor, Elaine Steinbeck. Her admiration for American writing and her vibrant spirit were an inspiration to us all. Richard Poirier, Chairman Cheryl Hurley, President The Library of America
