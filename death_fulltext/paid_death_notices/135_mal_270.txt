
        FINLAY-Terence. The Rev. Dr. Terence J. Finlay died on April 8 at his home in Grimsby, Ontario, Canada. A memorial service will be held for him at St. Bartholomew's Church, Park Avenue at 51st Street, New York City, on Monday, April 27 at 11:00 AM. Dr. Finlay served as Rector of St. Bartholomew's from 1955 until his retirement in 1978.
