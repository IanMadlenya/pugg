
        SARAVAY-Bess J. (nee Levine), 94, at her home in Florida on December 26, 2002. Wife of the late George Saravay. Mother of Stephen, Richard and Susan Hahn; mother-in-law of Jill, Barbara and Michael. Grandmother of Adam, Matthew, Nancy, Mara, Leslie, Becky, David and Laurie. Great-grandmother of Zack, Scott, Emma, Daniel, Jacob, Eric and Henry. An exceptional teacher and a remarkable woman whose boundless love and devotion nurtured the extended family who now mourn her passing and honor her example in the joy we take in each other's company.
