
        INGBER-Anna Harris passed away on Wednesday, October 15, 1997 in Los Angeles, California. She was 93 years old and the wife of the late Nathan Ingber. She will be remembered by those she left behind for her unselfishness, her caring, her sense of humor, and her deep and passionate love for her children, grandchildren, and great grandchildren. She was loved by all who knew her, her family and friends from New York to Los Angeles. Her generous spirit will far outlive her life on this earth. We will miss her deeply and remember her for the way that she touched all of our hearts. Services and interment are private. Forever, Fran and Alfie, Jamie, Laurie and Paul, Sara and Ben
