
        GRUEN-Gerta (Weiniger). Died September 8, 1997. Devoted wife of William, beloved sister of Ron and Jaffa Vered, Tel-Aviv, and Harry and Lore Kirchheimer, Chicago; dear loving aunt of Opal, David, Jim, Guri and Lea, great aunt to 10 nieces and nephews and beloved by all. She was a geriatric researcher, poet, and author. Her devotion and active participation will be missed at the Jewish Community Center of Palisades, Englewood & Washington Heights Institute of Retired Persons. Services at 11 a.m. at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue, NYC. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
