
        KRUPNICK-Audree (Wigler). Of Palm Beach Gardens, FL. passed away on April 2nd after a long bout with lung cancer. She leaves her adoring husband, Irwin, after 51 years of a most wonderful marriage; three children, Gary, James and Jane; daughters-in-law, Tammy and Debra; as well as the lights of her life, six grandchildren, Buffy, Katie, Kayla, Phineas, Amy and Ari. Audree will be sorely missed. Funeral from the Bernheim-Apter-Goldsticker Suburban Funeral Chapel, 1600 Springfield Avenue, Maplewood, New Jersey, Sunday, April 5, at 11 A.M.
