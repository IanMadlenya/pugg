
        STEINBERG-Edythe. On September 16, 1999. Beloved mother of Richard and Rona, mother-in-law of Renee and Howard, grandmother of Sarah, Jonathan, Stephanie, and Michael, and companion of Murray Savel. She lived life as a continually engaged and loving spirit, full of opinions, passions, and advice. Her intellect was keen and her heart warm all the days of her life; she will remain eternally young in our hearts and prayers. Service to be held Sunday, 10AM at Temple Emanuel in Westfield, New Jersey. A period of mourning will be held at the home of Mr. and Mrs. Richard Steinberg at 7PM on Wednesday.
