
        McGOVERN - Marie V. (nee Gough). Of Irvington, New York on October 7, beloved wife of Joseph W., mother of Marie Therese Melillo, Raymond, Joseph, Philip & Lawrence. Sister of Virginia Kutner and the late Anne Madigan, Rev. Lawrence Gough, & Rev. Raymond Gough, S.J. Grandmother of 22, Great grandmother of 7. Mrs. McGovern was a trustee of Fordham Preparatory School, College of Mount Saint Vincent and Manhattan College & served as a member of the NYC Landmarks Commission. Visiting will be Sunday and Monday 2-4 & 7-9, Edwards-Dowdle Funeral Home, 64 Ashford Ave. (exit 17 Saw Mill), Dobbs Ferry, NY. Mass of the Resurrection, 10 AM, Tuesday at Church of the Immaculate Conception, Irvington, NY. Contributions in her memory to Catholic Charities of the NY Archdiocese or Catholic Relief Services.
