
        DERWIN-Jordan. On January 6, 2000 from cancer. Loving husband of Joan. Devoted father of Susan and Ellen (Jeff). Attorney, actor and longtime SAG officer. Service Sunday, 11:15, Plaza Memorial, 91st and Amsterdam Ave. Donations in his memory may be sent to Motion Picture Players Welfare Fund, C/O Screen Actors Guild, 1515 Broadway, New York 10036.
        DERWIN-Jordan. Screen Actors Guild is saddened by the loss of former SAG Board member and friend, Jordan Derwin. His untiring devotion to the needs of all actors was clear in the boardroom and on set. Our heartfelt sympathy goes out to Jordan's family and friends. SAG-New York Branch
