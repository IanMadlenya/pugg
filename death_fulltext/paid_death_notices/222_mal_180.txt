
        SHERR--William Charles. 80, beloved husband of Miriam Sherr for 51 years, and loving brother to Jessie Wallerich and Nettie Kitzes, died on July 15, 2005 in Phoenix, AZ. Mr. Sherr graduated from the University of Wisconsin and received his Law Degree from Fordham University in 1949. He also served honorably as an Ensign and later promoted to Lieutenant J.G. during WW II in the Pacific. During the 1950's in the US Navy through the 1980's, Mr. Sherr practiced law in NYC--helping hundreds of clients with his brilliant legal mind, and his great compassion. He also served as President of the Board of his Sutton Place condominium complex. Retiring in 1981, Mr. Sherr moved to Paradise Valley, AZ, where he was an active member of his community and President of the Board of the Marquesa Condominium. Other survivors include his five nieces and one nephew who live in Phoenix, New York, Washington, DC, and Israel, along with numerous grand nieces and grand nephews. Memorial services will be held at Riverside Chapel in Manhattan on Monday, July 18th, at 11:30 am, and burial will follow at Sharon Gardens in Valhalla, NY.
