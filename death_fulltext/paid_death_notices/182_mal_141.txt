
        TYRAS-Denis, M.D. The Officers and Members of The Staff Society of the Long Island Jewish Medical Center mourn the tragic passing of our friend and colleague, Denis Tyras, M.D. Dr. Tyras was the Chief of the Division of CardioThoracic Surgery at LIJ from 1984 to 1991. He devoted his life to helping others. We extend our heartfelt condolences and best wishes to his family. Ira N. Kalfus, M.D., President Eugene Friedman, M.D., V.P. Keith Cardell, M.D., Secretary Randall Feingold, M.D., Treasurer
