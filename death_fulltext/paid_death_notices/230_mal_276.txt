
        WILLIAMSON--James Geiger. Of Pt. Pleasant Beach, New Jersey was a former resident of Short HIlls, New Jersey. He was born in Palm Beach, Florida. He graduated from McCauley High School in Chattanooga, Tennessee and the University of Virginia in Charlottesville, Virginia. Mr. Williamson was married to Sheila Campbell Williamson. He is survived by his two daughters, Anne Holman of Short Hills and Christina Donnelly of Jersey City, and a granddaughter, Campbell Holman. He retired as a Portfolio Manager from Shearson Asset Management. He was a member of the Short Hills Club, Manasquan River Golf Club and the Bay Head Yacht Club. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Williamson Family Fund of the Community Foundation of New Jersey, P.O. Box 338, Morristown, NJ 07963-0338. A memorial service will be held on Saturday, March 25, 2006 at 1:30 P.M. at Community Congregational Church, 200 Hartshorn Dr., Short Hills, New Jersey.
