
        KAPLAN--Pearl Rebecca, stage name Sandra Shaw, died December 24th, 2006, three weeks before her 90th birthday. Eldest sister of Martin Kaplan, Naomi Schlessel and George Kaplan (deceased). A talented musician, she was a cocktail pianist in New York and Miami Beach, FL. During World War II she travelled with the USO entertaining the troops in Italy and Hawaii. She also toured with the Gene Autry troupe playing the accordion and singing different languages. Funeral is Wednesday, 11am, at Old Montifiore Cemetery.
