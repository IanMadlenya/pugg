
        COHEN--Doris (Maslan), of Oakland, CA. Formerly of Tom's River and Millburn, New Jersey on November 23, 2004. Beloved by all who knew her. Devoted wife of the late Benjamin A. Cohen. Adored mother of Marc (Lorraine) Cohen, Leslie (Doug) Hall, and Abby Cohen (Jeff Sandler) and proud grandmother. A nurse's nurse who brought honor to her profession and hope and comfort to innumerable patients. Avid reader, gracious hostess, fabulous cook, lover of music and scenic drives, she had a heart of gold which she gave of unstintingly. She will live on in the acts of goodness she performed and in the hearts of those who cherish her memory.
