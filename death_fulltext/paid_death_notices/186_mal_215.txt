
        LEIBMAN-Sidney. Age 92, on July 15, 2002, after a brief illness. Dearly beloved husband of Frances Moskin Leibman for 56 years and uncle of many. He was born in New York City, February 23, 1910, graduated from Syracuse University and the Harvard University Law School, and, as a captain in the United States Army, commanded a Signal Corps company attached to the Air Force in the European Theater of Operations during World War II.
