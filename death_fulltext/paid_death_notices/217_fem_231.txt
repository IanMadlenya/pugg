
        ECKMAN -- Adeline. 77, Greenwich, CT, on February 20, 2005. A graduate of CCNY. Loving and devoted wife of 52 years to the late Sidney Eckman. Her love, nurturing influence and zest for life will be missed by her children, Dr. Mark Eckman and Heidi Silverstein, brothers Bernard, Harold, and Norton Ansher, and her four grandchildren.
