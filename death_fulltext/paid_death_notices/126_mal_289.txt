
        KIPNIS-Sidney. A resident of Santa Monica and Palm Springs, CA. and formerly of Williambsburg, Brooklyn, died on July 23, at age 70. Survived by his beloved wife, Geri, his dear children, Neal Kipnis of Riverside, CA. and Robin Kipnis Kaufman, of Agoura, CA., their equally cherished mates, Dana Kipnis and Gregg Kaufman, his sweet grandchildren, Lindsay Kipnis and Jared and Brooke Kaufman and his dear brother, Sol, and his mate, Lily. A resident of California since his discharge from the US Navy at the end of World War II, he never forgot Brooklyn and the friends of his youth.
