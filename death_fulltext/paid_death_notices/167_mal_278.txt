
        KORNBERG-Eugene. Of Boynton Beach, Florida, formerly of Teaneck, N.J., on December 19, 2000. Loving husband of Shirley, devoted father of Steven and Janet, beloved granfather. Retired general manager of The Artkraft Strauss Sign Corporation of New York and respected leader in the sign industry, a model of integrity, friend and mentor, he will be sorely missed.
