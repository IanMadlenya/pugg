
        STEIN-Ruth Pedolsky. Died on Sunday, October 26, 1997 in Corning, New York. Services on Tuesday, October 28, at 10 AM, at Riverside-Nassau North Chapels in Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the American Cancer Society in her memory.
