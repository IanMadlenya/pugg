
        MUNCAN--Mihaela, M.D. The Department of Psychiatry of Maimonides Medical Center mourns the loss of our esteemed and beloved colleague, Mihaela Muncan, M.D. Dr. Muncan was the Director of the Inpatient Psychiatry Unit of our Community Mental Health Center. Dr. Muncan was an inspiration and a source of strength to her colleagues and her patients. She will be sorely missed. Marvin H. Lipkowitz, M.D., Chairman And Entire Staff of the Department of Psychiatry
