
        WHEELER-Ira B. The Board and staff of the National Executive Service Corps are deeply saddened by the death of our esteemed colleague, friend and Board member, Ira B. Wheeler. We extend our deepest sympathy to his wife Mary and his family. A. Wright Elliott, Chair Robert N. Sellar, President
        WHEELER-Ira B. Canterbury Choral Society mourns the loss of our good friend, fellow singer and past President, Ira Wheeler who gave so generously of his time, talents and loyalty to the Society over many years. We extend heartfelt sympathy to his wife, our fellow singer, Mary Wheeler and to the entire Wheeler Family.
