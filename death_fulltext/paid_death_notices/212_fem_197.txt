
        HALLWACHS -- Marianne Jenner Marta Ehrenborg. Of Philadelphia, PA, on September 21, 2004. Born April 20, 1914. in Stockholm, Sweden. Cherished wife of the late Robert G. Hallwachs, loving and beloved mother of Marianne J. Charny (Joel) and Winnie Hallwachs (Daniel H. Janzen), devoted grandmother of Matthew and Claire Charny. Interment was private. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Guanacaste Dry Forest Conservation Fund, Biology Department, University of Pennsylvania, Philadelphia, PA 19104.
