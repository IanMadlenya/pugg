
        NEBEL-Patricia Stewart. On Feb. 11, 1998, at Norwalk Hospital in Norwalk, CT. She is survived by daughter, Victoria of San Francisco, CA., son, Christopher of Gilford, CT., son, Dino of Westport, Ct., and four grandchildren. Born in London in 1928, she was awarded a scholarship to the University of London, studied with Jean Piaget in Geneva, and received her Master's Degree through a fellowship at Bank Street College of Education in New York. In 1956 she married Emil Nebel, son of prominent Westport sculptor Berthold Nebel, and moved to Westport, Ct. In 1963 she became Director of the Westport - Weston Cooperative Nursery School and retired 30 years later in 1993. Passionate advocate for child-centered preschool education, beloved mother and grandmother, cherished confidante, Mentor and friend, she will be missed by many. A Memorial Service will be held 1pm, Saturday, Feb. 21 at the Unitarian Church, 10 Lyons Plains Road, Westport, CT. In lieu of flowers contributions may be sent to The Pat Nebel Grandchildren Education Fund c/o the Westport-Weston Cooperative Nursery School, 10 Lyons Plains Road, Westport, CT. 06880.
