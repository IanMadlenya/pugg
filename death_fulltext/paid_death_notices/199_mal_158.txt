
        OLMSTED--Robert G. Adelphi University mourns the passing of Robert G. Olmsted, our former trustee, interim President, Trustee Emeritus and benefactor. He was a visionary leader who shared his knowledge and guidance with the entire Adelphi community. We will build upon his legacy of commitment to the highest academic standards and civic minded initiatives. Our deepest sympathies are with his family. Robert A. Scott, Pres. Steven N. Fischer, Chairman The Adelphi University Board of Trustees
