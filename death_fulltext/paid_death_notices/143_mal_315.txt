
        ELBIRT-Jacob. On Dec. 25, 1998. Devoted husband of Laura. Beloved father of Morton and Paula. Cherished grandfather of Adam, Benjamin, David & Shira. Loving brother of Rachael and Helen. His lifelong devotion to the state of Israel and the Jewish people was served by his many charitable activities and his tireless leadership in Federation of Wolynian Jews. In later years, he was instrumental in the establishment of the first Yiddish & Hebrew monument to the memory of those who perished in the Holocaust in his birthplace, Lutsk, Poland. He loved all mankind and was righteous in all his ways. Donations may be made to Aleh Foundation. Services are on Sunday, 11:00 AM at Nieberg's Midwood Chapel, 1625 Coney Island Avenue, Brooklyn (Corner Avenue M), 718-377-2700.
        ELBIRT-Jacob. Congregation Kehilath Jeshurun and its Sisterhood mourn the passing of the beloved father of their devoted member Dr. Paula Elbirt and extend heartfelt condolences to all members. Haskel Lookstein, Rabbi Joshua Lookstein, Assistant Rabbi Fred Distenfeld, President
