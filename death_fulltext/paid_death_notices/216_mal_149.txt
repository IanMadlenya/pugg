
        HAY--Nicholas Romeyn Taylor, 82, of Niantic, CT died on January 10, 2005. He was the widower of Ann Hay. He is survived by his son Andrew Wellington Hay and his wife Nina of New York, NY, his brother Wellington Hay and his wife Mia of New Canaan, CT, his grandchildren Wellington and Nicholas Hay, his niece Lucinda Hardzog and his nephew Stephen Taylor Hay. Funeral services will be private. For information: Fulton - Theroux Funeral Home, Niantic, CT.
