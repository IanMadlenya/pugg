
        AARON--Frieda Wakschlag. Holocaust survivor, educator and author of ''Bearing the Unbearable,'' a book on Holocaust poetry, died on May 31, 2005. Her loving core has inspired all who knew her. Her four grandchildren Noah, Adam, Michelle and Brian were her greatest joy. Dr. Aaron is survived by her husband Sol, her daughters Fern Zagor & Stephanie Glaser, & her sister Estelle Laughlin.
