
        RUSSO-Fortunee. Of New York City, died in Florida, February 11, 2000. Adored wife of the late Albert, loving mother of Katherine Russo Bernstein (Mel), Victor Russo (Norma), and the late Nicky Blumenthal (Bob). Doting grandmama of Kimberly Freed, Ariel Russo, Victoria Russo Bernstein, David Russo, and Elana Bernstein. Proud great grandmother of Derrick and Nikki Freed and Ria Russo. Fortunee Russo was president of the Ventura Gem & Pearl Compay for 23 years. She was a long time member of Congregation Shearith Israel, the Spanish and Portuguese Synagogue. She will be forever remembered by her family and friends.
