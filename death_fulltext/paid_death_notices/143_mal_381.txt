
        DEWEY-Robert F. Died in North Hollywood, CA, Friday, December 25, 1998 at age 67. Born January 29, 1931 to Charles and Marjorie Evans Dewey in Pleasantville, NY where he was raised & educated. He served in the army in the Korean War. Mr. Dewey is survived by a sister, Dorothy Goodding, of Friendswood, TX and a nephew, Charles Goodding.
