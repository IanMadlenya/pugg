
        GOLDMAN-Jacob M. Died peacefully at his home on March 3, 1999, at age 91. Beloved husband of 57 years of Dorothy (nee Goldrosen). Loving father of Leonard and Lesley Goldman, William Goldman and Jill Lesser, and Daniel Goldman and Sonja Mezic. Adored grandfather of Michael, David and Richard. Founding partner of the law firm Goldman, Horowitz and Cherno, founding president of Sterns Park Civic Association and former general counsel of the Pastel Society of America. Services Friday, 11:15AM at ''Gutterman's Inc. '', 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, LI.
