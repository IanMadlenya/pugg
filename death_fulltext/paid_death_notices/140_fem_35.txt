
        SCHURGIN-Audrey. The SAR Academy family notes with great sorrow the passing of our former Associate Principal. Condolences are extended to her dedicated and devoted husband Marvin, sons Jonathan and Paul, her father Simon Grishman, and to the entire family. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, September 8, at 12:30 PM at The Riverside Memorial Chapel, 76th and Amsterdam. May the family find solace in her memory and be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Jack C. Bendheim, President Rabbi Joel Cohn, Principal Michael Schreck, Exec. Director
