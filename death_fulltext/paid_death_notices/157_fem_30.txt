
        BOGRAD-Pauline Klemes. Age 91, of Hackensack, NJ. A supporter of Planned Parenthood for 50 years. The Paterson, NJ center is named in her honor. Founder of Womens' American Ort in Paterson, first female president of Jewish Family Services of North Jersey. Mother of Joseph and Marcia Bograd, Naomi and Edward Robbins, Harriet Bograd and Kenneth Klein. Grandmother of Louis, Mark, Stephanie, Joyce, Richard, Marjorie and Sarah. Great-grandmother of Evan and Benjamin. Service Schoem's Memorial Chapel, Paramus, NJ, Sunday, 10AM Contributions to Planned Parenthood of Metropolitan NJ, 151 Washington St, Newark, NJ 07102
