
        TAMAGNI-Raymond J., a New York investment banker died at his home in Summit, NJ, Sunday, after a long illness. Mr. Tamagni was born in Brooklyn, NY in 1910. He joined the investment banking firm of F.S. Moseley in 1928. Starting as a runner just a year before the stock market crashed in 1929, he rose through the ranks to become a General Partner of the Boston Firm. He was married for 52 years to Laura Searle Tamagni, who died in 1986. He is survived by a son, John S. Tamagni of Summit, a partner of Lazard Freres & Co., four grandchildren, Susan Kulick, Lisa Jordan Tamagni, John S. Tamagni, Jr. and Christina Fastert and one great grandchild, Natasha Kulick. A memorial service will be held at Calvary Episcopal Church in Summit, New Jersey, Thursday, October 16, 1997 at 11 A.M. In lieu of flowers, contributions to the Overlook Hospital Foundation or Calvary Episcopal Church would be appreciated by the family. For information, contact Brough Funeral Home, 535 Springfield Avenue, Summit, New Jersey.
