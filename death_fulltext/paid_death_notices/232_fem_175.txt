
        BLOOM--Leslie. June 19, 1943, to May 26, 2006. Beloved sister of Deborah and Michael, loving daughter of the late Bea and Sam. Leslie loved her family and friends, bird watching, books, and the New York Mets. She will live forever in our hearts. Contributions in Leslie's memory may be made to the Samuel and Beatrice Marks Bloom Memorial fund, c/o the Westchester Community Foundation.
