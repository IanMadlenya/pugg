
        STRAUSBERG-Mildred. On May 17, 2000. Of Palm Beach, FL, formerly of Great Neck, NY, at age 83. She was the devoted and beloved wife of the late Morris O. Strausberg, cherished twin sister of the late Selma Worth, and dear friend to many. She is survived by her daughter and son-in-law, Drs. Susan and Michael Brook, granddaughter Kira, and several nieces and nephews.
