
        SHULMAN-Max. The Board and Staff of The American Friends of The Israel Museum and The Israel Museum, Jerusalem, are deeply saddened by the death of Major Max Schulman, National Committee Member, and original founder of The Israel Museum, and a longtime patron and supporter. The Schulmans helped foster the growth of the Museum's Ruth Youth Wing. Our condolences to his wife, Sylvia, and to the entire family. Maureen Cogan, Chairman, International Council James S. Snyder, Anne & Jerome Fisher Dir, IM Nurit Shilo-Cohen, Chief Curator, Ruth Youth Wing, IM Carolyn Cohen, Exec Dir, AFIM
        SHULMAN-Major Max. Beloved husband of our Board member Sylvia Shulman, a founder of the Joe Weinstein Center for Research in Medical Textiles at Shenkar College of Engineering and Design. Our deepest condolences to the entire family. David Pernick, Chair, Governors Prof. A. Weinberg, Pres., Shenkar Nahum G. Shar, President, American Committee Charlotte A. Fainblatt, Exec. Dir.
        SHULMAN-Maj. Max L. The Offices, Trustees, Clergy and Members of Park Ave Synagogue mourn the passing of a devoted congregant. We extend to his wife Sylvia and the entire family our heartfelt sympathy. David H. Lincoln, Senior Rabbi Geoffrey J. Colvin, Ch. of the Bd. Paul S. Schreiber, President
