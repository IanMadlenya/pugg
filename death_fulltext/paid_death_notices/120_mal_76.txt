
        LAPIDUS - Joseph. The officers leadership, and staff of UJA-Federation mourn the passing of Joseph Lapidus, who taught the value of tzedakah to his family. His son, Sidney, carries on the tradition as an outstanding leader and long-time friend of UJA-Federation, serving ont he board of directors and chairing its Shared Services Cabinet. We extend our deepest sympathies to his wife Patty; sister Gertrude Leichter; children Sidney and Ruth Lapidus, and Martina nd Merry Lapidus; step-children Cookie and Bob Greenblott; grandhcildren Gail, Janet, Roy, HEather and Richard; and great-granchildren Sara and Eric. May you be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem.  Larry A. Silverstein, Board Chair  Louise B. Grelisheimer, President  Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V. P.
