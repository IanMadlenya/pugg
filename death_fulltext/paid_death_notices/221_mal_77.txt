
        DANON--Danny died on May 6, 2005, the day after his 34th birthday, at Mount Sinai Hospital in NYC from complications arising during emergency surgery on February 3rd. He is survived by his wife, the former Rachel Hossenlopp of Hoboken, NJ and Amagansett, NY and by his parents, Shai and Dalia Danon, and his brother Gil, all of Tel Aviv, Israel. After serving in the israeli Army, Mr. Danon attended the University of Sussex in Brighton, England where he met his wife to be. He graduated with honors in law in 1996, was admitted to the Israeli bar, and practiced law in Tel Aviv. Mr. and Mrs. Danon moved to the US in November 2002. At the time he fell ill, Mr. Danon had completed the formalities for admission to the New York Bar and had founded a NY-based real estate brokerage firm called Urban Sanctuary Realty. Mr. Danon was buried on May 10th at Einat Cemetery near Tel Aviv, following a service in the gardens at the Einat Kibbutz. Contributions in memory of Mr. Danon may be made to Mount Sinai School of Medicine, c/o Dr. Diane E. Meier, Director-Center to advance Palliative Care, Mount Sinai School of Medicine, One Gustave Levy Place, Box 1070, New York, NY 10029-6574.
