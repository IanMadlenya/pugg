
        BALSAM-Ruth Rosalie. On April 9, 1999. Beloved wife of the late Artur. President of the Artur Balsam Foundation for Chamber Music. She will be deeply missed. A gathering of friends will be held Tuesday, April 13, 11 AM, at ''The Riverside,'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions in her memory may be made to the Artur Balsam Foundation, 825 West End Ave., New York, NY 10025.
