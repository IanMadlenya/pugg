
        KETCHAM--J. Parker. Died January 17, 2005, age 83. He is survived by his wife Langdale Andrews Ketcham and his son William, both of Greenwich, CT. His daughter Sally Ketcham Thomsen predeceased him. He is also survived by five grandchildren, Stacy, Emily and Margaret Thomsen of Rumford, RI and Saunders and Elizabeth Ketcham of Greenwich, CT. He graduated from Cornell, Class of '43, and served as an officer in the Navy during WWII on both submarines and aboard the attack transport ''Audubon.'' He was a member of the American Yacht Club in Rye, NY and the New York Yacht Club. A memorial service will be held in February.
