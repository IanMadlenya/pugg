
        BARBALATA-Victor, MD. August 30, 2000. Beloved husband of Milly for 60 years. Father of Jeanne, grandfather of Stephanie, Alexandra and Isabelle and brother-inlaw to Sergiu Comissiona. Born in Romania and graduated from University of Bucharest Medical School in 1940. Arrived in the United States in 1959. Senior Clinical Assistant in Medicine, Mount Sinai Hospital. Attending Physician at Kateri Residence. He devoted himself to the care and treatment of his loyal patients in his private practice for over 45 years. His family will miss his intelligence, compassion and wisdom. He will be missed always and remembered forever. Please contact Riverside Memorial Chapel, West 76th & Amsterdam Avenue, NYC.
        BARBALATA-Victor, M.D. The Samuel Bronfman Department of Medicine of the Mount Sinai School of Medicine mourns the loss of our long time colleague. Dr. Barbalata devotedly served the Department of Medicine, Division of Hypertension from 1962 until 1997. During his years of service, Dr. Barbalata was highly regarded by his patients, his fellow clinicians and the nursing staff. We extend our sincere condolences to the Barbalata family. Barry S. Coller, M.D. Chairman
