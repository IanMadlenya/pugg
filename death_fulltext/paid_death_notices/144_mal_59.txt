
        DUCHIN-Morris G. Captain, USN Ret. On January 2, 1999, at home in his 92nd year. Beloved husband of the late Norma. Loving father of Leslie (Hugh) Heller and Paul. Cherished grandfather of Peter and Roger Heller, and uncle of Joel and Howard (Mattie) Hirschel and family. Services 11 AM, Tuesday, January 5 at ''The Riverside,'' 76th Street and Amsterdam Ave. Contributions may be made in his memory to UJA Federation or East End Temple, New York City.
