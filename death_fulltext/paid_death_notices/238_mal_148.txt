
        SLAY--Burton. Age 83, avid daily reader of The New York Times, died in the presence of his family in Delaware, OH, on October 11, 2006. Besides being a loving & caring husband and father, he was a true humanitarian, a lifetime advocate of social justice, and political honesty. In the 1960's he was President of The Columbus, Ohio Committee to Defend The Bill of Rights, and President of The Unitarian Fellowship for Social Justice, and also sponsored debate, direct action and panel discussions on the war in Viet Nam, abolition of the House Un-American Activities Committee, the war on poverty, social responsibility of the press, and school integration. His family and friends remember him lovingly.
