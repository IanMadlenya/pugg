
        ANYON-Gilbert. Of North Salem. Born June 29, 1921; passed away October 19, 2001 of heart failure. Beloved husband of Connie Fox & cherished father of Timi Hallem & Jill Anyon. The best grandfather Elissa and Seth Hallem could have had. The world is richer for his life and poorer for his loss. Contributions in lieu of flowers may be made to the Southern Poverty Law Center, MADD, or Greenpeace.
