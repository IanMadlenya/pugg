
        JAFFIN-Katheryn Renate. On November 27. Adoring mother of Madeleine R. Kania. Devoted grandmother of Jennifer Ann and Laura Jean Kania. Proud motherin-law of Robert J. Kania. Devoted wife of 52 years to the late Theodore S. Jaffin. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave at 81 St, Wednesday, December 1, 11:30 AM. Interment Ferncliff Cemetery, Ardsley, NY.
        JAFFIN-Katheryn R. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York records with deep sorrow the death of our cherished member, Katheryn R. Jaffin. To her family and loved ones, we express our profound sympathy. May beautiful memory bring comfort to those who mourn her passing. David E. Marrus, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
