
        PRYSTOWSKY-Florence. Died May 17, 1999 at age 90 after a heroic battle against cancer. Beloved wife of the late David. Devoted mother of Seymour (Cochava) and Steven (Gail). Loving grandmother of Elan (Galila), Donna, Aliza and Shana. Burial in Israel; Shiva in U.S. Dedicated her life to teaching Torah. Any donations may be made to Beth MiriamBeth Jacob School, 2126 Barnes Avenue, Bronx, New York.
