
        ROSS--John G. ''Jack'' died on November 14, 2006. He was 70. He was a partner in the specialist firm DeCordova Cooper & Co. in New York City, a position held until his retirement in 1990. Survivors include his wife of 48 years: Patricia (Edgar) Ross of Bernardsville his children: Jill Robinson of Suwanee, Ga., Louis Ross of Whitwell, TN., Caroline Fitzpatrick of Durham, NC; four grandchildren: John Aaron Robinson, John Craig Fitzpatrick, Michael Leland Fitzpatrick and Margaret Susan Fitzpatrick, brother: Hamilton Ross of Bonita Springs, FL. A Celebration of his life will be held on Monday, November 20, at 11 a.m. at The Basking Ridge Presbyterian Church, 1 East Oak St. , Basking Ridge, NJ 07920. For those planning an expression of sympathy contributions may be made to the Carol G. Simon Cancer Center, 100 Madison Ave., Morristown, NJ 07960 or to the Atlantic Home Care & Hospice, 33 Bleeker St. Millburn, NJ 07041. For information Gallaway & Crane Funeral Home, 101 S. Finley Ave., Basking Ridge 908-766-0250.
