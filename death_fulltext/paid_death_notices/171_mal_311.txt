
        SEVILLE-Leon. Of San Francisco, CA., and a former resident of Oceanside, N.Y. After a lifetime as a feisty and tireless volunteer for many causes, Lee died April 21 at age 86 following a courageous struggle with heart disease. He is survived by Ruth, his wife of 61 years, children Marci of San Francisco, CA., son Carl, daughter-inlaw Fran, and daughter Jane of Atlanta, GA. and 3 loving grandchildren Lisa, Paula and Alex. Donations to ACLU of Northern CA., 1663 Mission St., SF, CA. 94103, or the SF Ombudsman Program, 6221 Geary Blvd, SF, CA. 94121. The family plans a memorial service for early summer.
