
        HAMBURGER -- Tilly. Died peacefully at home at 97 years of age on Wednesday, October 22. Mother of two, grandmother of six, great grandmother of seven, widowed 40 years. Charming, formidable, witty, up-to-date, with something to say to each generation. She will be sorely missed. Services will be held at Riverside Memorial Chapel, 11AM, Friday, October 24.
