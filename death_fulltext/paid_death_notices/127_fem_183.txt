
        HERSCH-Anne (nee Carlin). On Tuesday, August 26, 1997. Raised in Dubuque, Iowa, over 90 years ago, Annie did the uncoventional by completing a college education and joining the WACs, rising to the rank of Sergeant. A NYC resident of 50 years, Annie was a walking ''metropolitan diary.'' Caring wife of the late Joe, she was a staunch friend to many, with a strong sense of family. Annie was the well-loved grandmother of Kathie and Daniel (Candace), step-mother of Irwin, and mother-in-law of Sherrin. A memorial gathering is being planned.
