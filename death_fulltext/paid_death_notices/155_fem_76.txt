
        ROSAN-Menorah J. Beloved mother of Alan, Shira, Naomi and Deborah; devoted wife of the late Howard; dear grandmother of Seth, Daniel, Hayim and Lior who will miss ''Nunu''. Died December 7th with family in attendance. Menorah was a parent and teacher at Spuyten Duyval Infantry. She was an active member of Riverdale Mental Health Association and Riverdale Neighborhood House. A stalwart and gracious friend to many in the Riverdale community. Shiva at the home. Contributions in her memory may be made to The Papa and Nunu Fund, C/O New York Community Trust, 2 Park Avenue, 24th Floor, New York City.
