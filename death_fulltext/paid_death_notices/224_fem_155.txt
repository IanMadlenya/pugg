
        FELDMAN--Sandra. We extend our heartfelt condolences to our sisters and brothers in the American Federation of Teachers over the death of their inspired leader Sandra Feldman. Sandy was a powerful advocate for teachers and students. In New York, and later in Washington, she was an effective champion of adequate school funding and decent standards for classroom teachers. She used her union's political power for the betterment of our nation's children, and we honor her for it. Dennis Rivera, President George Gresham, Secretary Treasurer 1199SEIU, New York's Health & Human Service Union
        FELDMAN -- Sandra. The New School is saddened by the passing of Sandra Feldman, beloved wife of our former Jazz board chair, Arthur Barnes. As a tireless and uncompromising advocate for better education and equal rights for children, Sandy has made contributions to the betterment of countless young lives. We extend heartfelt sympathy to Arthur and the family. Philip Scaturro, Chair, Board of Trustees and Jazz Board of Governors Bob Kerrey, President, The New School Martin Mueller, Executive Director, The New School for Jazz
