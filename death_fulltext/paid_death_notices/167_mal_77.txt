
        KURMAN-Martin, MD. The Board of Trustees and Medical Staff of Lenox Hill Hospital mourn the death of Martin Kurman, MD, a distinguished member of our Department of Obstetrics and Gynecology for 25 years. Dr. Kurman was a dedicated and caring physician who exemplified the highest standards of the medical profession. We honor his memory and extend our deepest sympathies to his family. James S. Marcus, Chairman Board of Trustees Gladys George, President and CEO Michael Y. Divon, Director Obstetrics and Gynecology
