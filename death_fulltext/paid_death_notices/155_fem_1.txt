
        DECHERT-Lela E. On November 26, at age 78. Beloved wife of the late William E. Dechert of Chas. Pfizer & Co. She is survived by her two sons Daniel H. Lavezzo, III, and W. Douglas Dechert; her grandson Christian E. Dechert; her great-grandson Booker Lewis Dechert; and by her brother John D. Ellis of Dripping Springs, TX. She was previously married to Daniel H. Lavezzo, Jr., owner of P.J. Clarke's. There will be a memorial service at the Brick Church Chapel, Park Avenue at 91 St., at 4 PM on Thursday, December 2, 1999.
