
        KLEIN-Lenore. On April 24, 2002. Devoted wife of the late Howard. Beloved sister of the late Judith Riggs and the late Mortimer Metzger. Loving aunt, grand aunt and great grand aunt. She graduated Woodmere Academy 1932 and Barnard College 1936. She was devoted to ballet and the arts and volunteered at The Jewish Museum of New York. Funeral services Sunday, 10:30 am at Schwartz Brothers-Jeffer Memorial Chapels, Queens Boulevard and 76th Rd, Forest Hills. Donations to the charity of your choice or the New York City Ballet.
