
        LOWENSTEIN -- Sherman. Age 88, of New York and Palm Beach, passed away on February 29th at home after a courageous battle with cancer. Sherman is survived by his wife Lucille, his son Hank, his brother Norman and three grandchildren Vanessa, Helen and David. Services are being held at Frank B. Campbell, on Tuesday, March 2nd at 11:30AM. In lieu of flowers, please make contributions to The Visiting Nurse Service of New York Hospice Program. We will miss you and always remember that your spirit lives on. Love, Lucille and Hank.
        LOWENSTEIN--Sherman. A distinguished, handsome gentleman. Thank you for being a loyal husband and companion for over twenty-eight years to Lucille, our mother and grandmother. We will remember you always. Janie and Robert, Trudi and Michael, Heather, Todd, Michelle
        LOWENSTEIN--Sherman. The Jewish Center of Atlantic Beach records with profound sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Sherman Lowenstein, husband of Lucille Lowenstein. May the family be comforted amongst the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem and know no further sorrow.
