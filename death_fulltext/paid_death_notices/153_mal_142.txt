
        deSIMONE-Paul. Beloved son of Jack and the late, Adele; brother of Diane deSimone Hamilton and James Hamilton, died suddenly October 11, 1999. He was blessed with a huge generous heart and an intelligence that was a beacon for nurturing all races and people. Paul was a renaissance man with a habit of quoting from many distinguished classic and modern works. He was a lifelong New Yorker having moved to Tucson, AZ recently. All family members and friends feel the loss of a deeply devoted human being. Private services will be held.
