
        BEER--Bernard, PhD. The entire Beth Israel Medical Center family joins with our colleagues in the Beth Israel Cancer Center in acknowledging with sadness the passing of our dear friend and beloved benefactor, Bernard Beer, PhD. Dr. Beer's generosity helped Beth Israel expand its scope of services in cancer care and outreach to cancer patients who turn to us for help. He will be greatly missed by all whose lives were touched by his charismatic personality and spirit. To his family and all his loved ones, we extend our heartfelt condolences. Morton P. Hyman, Chairman Board of Trustees David J. Shulkin, MD President and CEO Louis B. Harrison, MD Director, Continuum Cancer Centers of New York
