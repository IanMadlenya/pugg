
        POWERS-Joseph P., 62, of New York City, and husband of Jo Ann Langer Powers, died Wednesday, January 29 at his home. Family and friends of the family may express their condolences on Saturday, February 8, from 1 to 5 P.M. at the residence of Jo Ann Langer Powers. Memorial contributions may be made in his memory to Free Arts for Abused Children, 1431 Broadway, 7th Floor, New York, New York 10018. For information Brenna Funeral Home, Trenton, NJ.
