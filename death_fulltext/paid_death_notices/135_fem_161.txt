
        LUKOMNIK-Joanne. The Dept. of Community Health and Social Medicine, CUNY Medical School, mourns the loss of our colleague and dear friend. A fearless opponent of the poverty, injustice and racism in our community, she brought her passion for social justice to the classroom. Her activism inspired faculty and student alike. She will be lovingly remembered and profoundly missed.
