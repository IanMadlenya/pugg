
        ZIMMERMAN-Frances. The law firm of Newman Schwartz & Greenberg is deeply saddened by the death of its beloved and esteemed bookkeeper and friend, Frances Zimmerman, and expresses its deepest condolences and sympathies to her brother, Gustave H. Newman, the firm's Senior Partner, for his loss.
        ZIMMERMAN - Frances (nee Newman.) January 26, 1998. Beloved wife of Carl. Cherished sister of Edith, Gus and Winnie. She embraced her family and families by marriage and her many friends who will always remember her generosity and loving kindness.
