
        BASSECHES-Paula Treinis, 95. Passed away after an extended illness. She was a pioneer, being one of the earliest female registered patent agents. Beloved wife of the late Jack Basseches, she is survived by sons Mark and Robert, daughters-in-law Janet and Harriet, grandchildren Julie, Stuart, K.B., Joshua, Jeffrey and Jessica, and great-grandchildren Aaron, Jacob, Sophie and Ivy. Contributions may be made to the Jewish National Fund.
