
        VECCHIONE-Edward, Jr. On Nov. 22, 2001 at age 76. Loving brother of Dorothy Martin and cousin of Vincent Lombardi, Marie Pelusso, Lucretia Trisci and Michael Trisci. Companion of the late Lilo Marcus. Dear friend of Bernice & Arthur Weiss and Joan Linsky and Danny Vitanzo. Also survived by many nephews, cousins and dear friends. Mr. Vecchione was a member of The Hotel Management Association, a veteran of W.W. II, a prisoner of war and a graduate of Columbia University. Funeral Mass was held at Our Lady of Peace Church, 237 E. 62 St, Saturday 11:15AM. Interment followed at Christ the King Cemetery, Franklin Lakes, NJ. Contributions in his memory may be made to Calvary Hospital, 1740 Eastchester Road, Bronx, NY 10461.
