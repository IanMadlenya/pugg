
        ROSOFF--Thelma (Tiny). Of New York, NY. Wife of the late Morris Rosoff. Beloved mother of Paula Grossman of New York. Dear grandmother of Amy Breiger and Julie Grossman. Sister of the late Eve Winston. She and her husband were partners in their law firm, Rosoff and Rosoff. Tiny was a resident of New York and Vermont, and loved her home and many friends in Vermont. Funeral services in the Mishkan Israel Cemetery, Whalley Ave, New Haven, Tuesday, at 12 noon. Memorial contributions may be sent to Mt. Sinai Hospital, Development Office, 1425 Madison Ave, NYC 10029. For information, The Robert E. Shure Funeral Home, 543 George St, New Haven.
