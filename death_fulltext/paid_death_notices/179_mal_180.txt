
        WOLOSOFF-Milton. The Officers, Trustees and Members of the Forest Hills Jewish Center mourn the loss of Milton Wolosoff, past Vice President and sitting Trustee of our congregation. His devotion to his family and community was exemplary. May his family be comforted among the mourners of Zion and Jerusalem. Rabbi Gerald C. Skolnik Cantor Adam Frei Valerie K. Leibler, President Tina Berger, Executive Director
