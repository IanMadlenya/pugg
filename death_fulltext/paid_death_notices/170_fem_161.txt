        LURIE-Nettie. The Faculty and Staff of the Division of Social Work and Behavioral Science mourn the passing of Mrs. Nettie Lurie, wife of our distinguished colleague, Dr. Abraham Lurie. Nettie Lurie was an extraordinary woman and Abe's partner for 53 years. She will be sorely missed. We extend our deepest sympathy to her daughters and the extended family. Gary Rosenberg, Ph.D Edith J. Baerwald, Professor of Community Medicine Mount Sinai School of Medicine
