
        PASTER-Esther. On May 9, 2001. Widow of Salem Paster, who died on February 3, mother of the late Joyce Paster Foley, grandmother of Suzanne Foley. Esther was a teacher and guidance counselor in New York City schools for 37 years. Her love of literature, learning and young people lasted throughout her life. She was a generous, caring friend and mentor.
