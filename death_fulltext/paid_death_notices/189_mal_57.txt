
        PALTROW - Bruce. The Trustees, staff and artists of the Williamstown Theatre Festival are profoundly saddened by the loss of Bruce Paltrow. As a writer and director, Bruce demonstrated wit, grace and a passion to raise the stakes. Around the festival, he was a perpetual source of humor and warmth. He inspired us to greater achievement. Our heartfelt condolences to Blythe, Gwyneth and Jake. Ira S. Lapidus, President Michael Ritchie, Producer
        PALTROW-Bruce. Adored and adoring father, husband, son, brother, uncle and friend. The definition of a mensch. We will miss him. Meryl and Dan Levinson
