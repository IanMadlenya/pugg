
        CALLAHAN-Frederic L. Died on November 10, 1998 after a brief illness. Retired Associate Professor of Education and English, Lehman College, CUNY. Beloved husband of Louise (nee Samoyedna). Loving father of Siobhan, Alison and Evin. Dear brother of Lenore O'Shea and Paul, and the late Joan Fitzsimmons. Donations in his memory may be made to Boston College, Office of Development, More Hall, Room 220, Chestnut Hill, Massachusetts 02167.
