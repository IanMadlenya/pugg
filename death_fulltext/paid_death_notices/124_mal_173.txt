
        PORTER-George L., a long time resident of Greenwich Village, died May 15, 1997. Beloved husband of Doris Woodell Porter. Born in Vienna, Austria he became Vice President for Business and Finance, as well as controller, of the National Audubon Society, from which he retired in 1975. He was the author of ''The World of the Frog and the Toad'' a well reviewed book published by J.B. Lippincott and was author, as well, of the ''Reptile and Amphibian'' section of ''Hammond's Nature Atlas of America''. His amphibian photographs appear world-wide.
