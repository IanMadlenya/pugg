
        HOLLAND--Mrs. Estelle. Of Houston, Texas, died on October 2, 2003. The loving wife, for more than 65 years, of the late George Holland, mother of Ira and Maria, Peter and Merle, grandmother of Matthew, Lizza, John and Kate, great-grandmother of Harris, and a beloved friend to many. She will be missed by all of us who spent time with this truly special person.
