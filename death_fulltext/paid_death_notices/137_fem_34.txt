
        LUBASH-Miriam Scheff. Died June 3, 1998, quietly of complications of old age in her 95th year. Wife, mother, grandmother, sister and friend, she was a remarkable woman who enjoyed a full active life. She will be missed by all who were fortunate to know her and share her happiness. Service Thursday June 4th, 1 P.M. at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Avenue at 81st Street.
