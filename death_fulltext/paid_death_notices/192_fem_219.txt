
        KANNENSOHN-Edith. Age 90, died January 21 at Isabella House, NYC. Wife of the late Fred Kannensohn. Born in Germany, she was an artist, model and a very elegant woman. She studied painting at the Art Students League and the National Academy of Fine Arts. Services 10:30AM Thursday, January 23, Parkside Memorial Chapels, 98-60 Queens Blvd, Forest Hills.
