
        ROBBINS-Leonard John. In Manhattan at the age of 95. A graduate of Jesus College, Cambridge University, and New York Law School, he was a senior partner of Ladas & Parry. A member of the New York Bar Association, he was an internationally renowned patent attorney. Also an accomplished artist, and a proud member of the Century Association. Beloved husband of 66 years of Ann Roe Robbins. Loving father to Susan Morrison, Jennifer Manocherian and Victoria Cowal. Grandfather of eleven, great grandfather of eleven. He will be sorely missed.
