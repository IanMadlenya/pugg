
        LAZAR-Arthur. Age 87. Passed away peacefully on November 6, 1999 at his home. Beloved husband of the late Rosa Lazar for 57 years. Loving father and father-inlaw of Mattie and David Schlang and the late Laurence Lazar. Cherished grandfather of Lisa and Julie Schlang, loving brother of Lil Hollander and the late Jack and Marty Lazar. Beloved brother-inlaw, uncle and great uncle. Arthur had an insatiable zest for life. His kind heart, wisdom and generous nature will be deeply missed.
