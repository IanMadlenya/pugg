
        McBEAN-George H. On December 16, 2000. Retired NYC Police Officer and World War II Army veteran. Loving father of Holly Mcbean-Sheeger and Gina McBeanLinton. Patriarch of an extended family including seven grandchildren. He is also survived by his brother Roy McBean and will be remembered as a cherished uncle and friend to many. Reposing Monday, 4-8 PM, at Riverdale-onHudson Funeral Home, 6110 Riverdale Ave, Bronx. Mass of Christian Burial Tuesday, 10 AM, at St. Margaret of Cortona Church. Entombment Trinity Church Cemetery, Manhattan. In lieu of flowers, donations to the American Cancer Society would be deeply appreciated.
