
        SILVERMAN-Deborah Halpern. 55, of Great Neck, NY. Died peacefully on June 15, 2000 at Mt. Sinai Hospital, New York. She was the daughter of the late Rabbi Harry Halpern and the late Mollie Singer Halpern. Surviving are her beloved and loving husband Alan, daughter Meredith, step-mother Jean Rosenhaus Halpern, and ''sister'' in friendship Alice Katz Berglas. Funeral services will be held at noon at Temple Beth-El, Great Neck. In lieu of flowers, contributions may be made to the Gynecologic Cancer Research Fund, c/o Carmel Cohen, MD, Mt. Sinai Medical Center, 1176 5th Avenue, Box 1173, NY, NY 10029
