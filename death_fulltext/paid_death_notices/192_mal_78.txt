
        ROTH-Joseph Howard. 75, of Boynton Beach, FL. Joseph was a Navy veteran and worked many years as a furrier. The center of Joe's life was his family. He was a caring and generous man who made people laugh until the very end. He is survived by his wife Lila (nee Getz), sons Richard (Edith) and Michael (Kari), and six grandchildren. His memory is a blessing to those who knew him.
