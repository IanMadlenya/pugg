
        FRIEDMAN-Frances W. (October 9, 1931-June 23, 2000). Died Friday evening at Lenox Hill Hospital. She is survived by her devoted husband Jack and loving daughter Katie. Proud graduate of Radcliffe College, Class of 1952. Private memorial.
        FRIEDMAN-Frances W. Dearest Frannie: only death could have cheated me of the other 50 years you promised me-you would never have failed me. I don't know whether you believed me when I told you, but you were the best wife and best friend a man could have had. Love, Jack
        FRIEDMAN-Frances W. A longtime resident of 1016 Fifth Avenue. Our deepest sympathy and condolences are extended to her family. Board of Directors 1016 Fifth Avenue
