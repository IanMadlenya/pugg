
        SUNDERLAND -- J.B. Born Aug. 14, 1928, died at home on July 8, 2005. He was President of Aminoil and Transpacific Geothermal Co., a director of the Near East Foundation since 1966, and a founder and director of Americans for Middle East Understanding since 1967. He was also a Member of the Council on Foreign Relations since 1971. He is survived by his wife, four children, and their families. Services will be held at 11A.M. on July 18 at St. Bridget's Church, Cornwall Bridge, CT. Donations may be made to A.M.E.U. 475 Riverside Dr. NY, NY 10115-0245 or the Near East Foundation, 90 Broad St, 15th Fl, NY 10004.
