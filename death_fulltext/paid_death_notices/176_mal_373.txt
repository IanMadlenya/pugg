
        EHRENKRANTZ-Ezra, 69. Of Dobbs Ferry, on September 22, 2001. An architect for the firm Ehrenkrantz Eckstut & Kuhn, located in New York, NY. He was also the head of The Center for Architecture and Building Science Research at the New Jersey Institute of Technology in Newark, NJ. Beloved husband of Cynthia, loving father to three children Ruth, Dan, and Jonathan Ehrenkrantz. Cherished grandfather of Joe and Eliav Ehrenkrantz and Solomon and Micah Klein. Funeral service will be held on Monday September 24, 2001 at 11 AM at Bet Am Shalom Synagogue, 295 Soundview Ave, White Plains, New York. Interment will follow at Cedar Park Cemetery, Westwood, NJ.
