
        CLEMENTS--Richard K., MD, 90 years old, of NYC and Leominster, MA, died June 8th in Leominster. He was an Ophthalmologist with a private practice on Park Avenue in NYC for 50 years. Dr. Clements was a Major in the US Army during World War II and was an antique car enthusiast. Funeral services and burial will be private. There are no calling hours. Information: The Silas F. Richardson & Son Funeral Home, 106 West St., Leominster.
