
        BLUM-Robert P. The Members of the Board of Directors of the New York Society for the Deaf wish to express their condolences on the death of Robert P. Blum to his immediate family, his mother Ray, and his sister Judy. Robert P. Blum is the son of the late Joseph G. Blum who was the first Chairman and former President of the New York Society for the Deaf. Stephen E. Adler, Chairman Paul L. Kohnstamm, President Pearl Johnson, Exec. Dir.
