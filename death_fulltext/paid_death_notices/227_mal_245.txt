
        HOWELLS--William White. On December 20 at home in Kittery Point, ME. Predeceased in 2002 by his wife of 73 years, Muriel Gurdon Seabury, survived by a daughter, Gurdon Metz of New York City, a son, William Dean Howells of Kittery Point, ME, four grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren. A Memorial service will be held at First Congregational Church, 23 Pepperell Road in Kittery Point, ME on December 29 at 11:00AM. Contributions in his memory may be made to The Strabery Banke Museum, Box 300, Portsmouth, NH 03802-0300, or The Hooton Fund, C/O Harvard Development Office, Harvard University, Cambridge, MA. 02138.
