
        GOLDSTINE-Sydney A., MD. Ninety-one years. Passed on February 13 at Mt. Sinai Hospital. Cherished and loving husband of Tamie Laurance Goldstine. Loving father of Jonathan and Thea. Loving grandfather of Susan. Affiliated with Mt. Sinai Hospital forty years, he was a devoted physician and friend to many over a long, satisfying medical career. A true gentleman and scholar. A memorial service will be held at Temple Emanu-El, Fifth Ave. at 65th St., NYC on March 1st at 4 PM. Donations in his memory may be made to the Palliative Care Program, Attn: Dr. Diane Meier, Mt. Sinai Hospital School of Medicine, Box 1070, NY, NY 10029.
