
        SURKS--Sy. Long Island Jewish (LIJ) Medical Center is saddened by the loss of Sy Surks, M.D. Dr. Surks was one of LIJ's original medical staff members when it opened in 1954 and served as the hospital's Chairman of Anesthesiology for 25 years. His contributions to LIJ and to our patients will long be remembered. LIJ Medical Center Administration
