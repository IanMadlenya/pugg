
        ROSKEIN-Florence (Solomon), on February 1, 1999 at home. Beloved wife of the late David Roskein. Loving mother of Nancy Vines, Sheila Kronisch and Leonard J. Felzenberg. Beloved grandmother of seven and great-grandmother of ten. Friends may call at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison Ave. at 81 St., Tuesday, February 2, 2-4 and 7-9 PM. Interment private.
        ROSKEIN-Florence. The Board, staff and members of Fight for Sight Inc. and its Manhattan League mourns the loss of our beloved friend and longtime member. We extend our sympathy to her children and grandchildren.
        ROSKEIN-Florence. We sadly record the passing of Florence Roskein, our devoted member and Secretary. Our heartfelt sympathies are extended to her family. Manhattan Guild
