
        SINGER-Yitzchok. The offices, Board of Directors and staff of the United Jewish Council of the East Side, join the Lower East Side community and the entire Jewish community of New York in mourning the untimely death of Rabbi Yitzchok Singer (of blessed memory) spiritual leader of the Bialystoker Synagogue for over 40 years. Rabbi Singer served as President of the United Jewish Council since 1987. He is survived by his wife Bluma, five children and several grandchildren. The United Jewish Council of the East Side, Inc. The Board of Directors Joel Kaplan, Esq, Exec. Dir.
