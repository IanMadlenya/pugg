
        PERL--Simone (nee Levy), granddaughter of renowned Captain Alfred Dreyfus, has died in Paris. Mme Perl, Member of the Order of the French Family, an Officer of Arts and Letters, served in the Resistance during World War II. In a final message of farewell, she asked for all to retain the memory of her grandparents. Lorraine Beitler, Curator Dreyfus Exhibition
