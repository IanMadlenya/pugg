
        SPENCE-Elizabeth Barrett. Of Hendersonville, NC, passed away Wednesday, September 16, 1998. She was born in Bloomfield, NJ, the daughter of Josephine Cox Barrett and Randolph Coe Barrett. She was the beloved wife of the late Dr. Harold George Spence. She is survived by and the mother of Eleanor S. Bates of Punta Gorda, FL, Virginia S. LeRoy of Big Sky, MT and Douglas B. Spence of Somersworth, NH. She is also survived by seven grandchildren and one great-granddaughter.
