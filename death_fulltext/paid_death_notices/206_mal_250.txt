
        BORNMANN--William C., retired after 30 years as VP/ Head of Casualty at Johnson & Higgins, died March 12 in Westfield, N.J. AB Hamilton, MA Columbia, ABD Yale, he also taught math at Georgia Tech for five years. Survived by his wife Gladys, children Barbara and Bill, sisters Adelaide and Betty, grandchildren Amelia and William. Donations: First United Methodist Church, 1 E. Broad Street, Westfield, NJ where a memorial service will be held April 18, 2PM.
