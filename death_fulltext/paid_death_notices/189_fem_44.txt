
        WHITE-Ruth. Our darling sister died on Sunday, October 6, after a long illness. Ruth was an advertising executive but her first love was the theater. Known as Joniruth White in the theater she was an original member of the Roundabout Theater Company, toured France and Italy with Robert Wilson's production of ''Edison'' and appeared in John Waters' cult film ''Polyester.'' Ruth is survived by two sisters, Sylvia White Eisenberg of San Francisco, Janet White Loeb of New York, and a brother, Lawrence White. Services will be private
