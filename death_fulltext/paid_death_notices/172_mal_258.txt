
        WOODWARD - Ambassador Robert Forbes. On May 18, 2001 in Georgetown. Foreign Service Officer 1931-1968; Ambassador: Costa Rica, Chile, Uruguay, Spain. 1961 Assistant Secretary of State for Latin American Affairs. Survived by son, Robert Jr., Boyds, MD., daughter Mary Woodward Truland, Germantown, MD., six grandchildren. B.A. Degree and Distinguished Service Award from University of Minnesota. Hon. LL.D., The University of the Pacific. Graduate National War College. Member The Metropolitan Club, Chevy Chase Club, Sulgrave Club, The Washington Institute of Foreign Affairs, The Diplomatic and Consular Officers, Retired, and The American Academy of Diplomacy. Private graveside service, celebration of his life for friends 4 PM Saturday, May 26th at his home.
