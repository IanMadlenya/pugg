
        JOEL--Mathilde (nee Mady). 81, of Ft. Lee and Coconut Creek, FL, died Saturday, October 30, 2004. Born in Salonika, Greece, Mrs. Joel lived in the Bronx before moving to Ft. Lee 30 years ago and Coconut Creek, FL, for the past five years. She and her late husband, Manfred, were the founders of Shaare Zedek Hospital in Jerusalem, benefactors of an auditorium and many youth projects in Natanya, Israel, and active in the United Jewish Appeal of Bergen County. Mr. and Mrs. Joel were survivors of the Bergen Belsen concentration camp. She is survived by her son and daughter-in-law Ralph and Rosalie Joel of Watchung, NJ, and two grandchildren, Jennifer and Haley. Services were held Monday, November 1, 2004, and burial was at Cedar Park Cemetery in Paramus. Memorial contributions can be made to the Hebrew Immigrant Aid Society, 333 7th Avenue, New York, NY 10001.
