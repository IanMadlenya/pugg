
        GALANTER-Wynne L. The faculty and staff of NYU School of Medicine and the Department of Psychiatry extend our heartfelt condolences to our friend and colleague, Mark Galanter, M.D., on the death of his wife, Wynne Galanter, Ph.D. Since 1985 the late Dr. Galanter was a Clinical Instructor of Psychiatry and an outstanding member of the faculty. Our thoughts and prayers are with the Galanter family. Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean, NYU School of Medicine Robert Cancro, M.D., Chairman, Department of Psychiatry, NYU School of Medicine
