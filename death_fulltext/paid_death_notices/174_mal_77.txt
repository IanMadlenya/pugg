
        BUSE-Fred W., M.D. Formerly of Manhasset, NY, at age 93, on June 27, 2001. Veteran of WWII, graduate of Yale Medical School and known for his innovative treatment for catastrophic burn victims. Loving father of Isabel Abdirkin and Beverly Weisman. Grandfather of Jessica, Jared, Daniel, Carrie and Alexis. Survived by three nieces and one nephew. Companion of Feli and Suzi. Dr. Buse will be buried at sea. A celebration of his life will take place Saturday, July 14th, at 10 AM, at The First Presbyterian Church, Smithtown. In lieu of flowers memorials may be made to the Boston Museum of Fine Arts or Nassau Suffolk Law Services.
