
        SHATIN-Dr. Harriet Evelyn. 85, of Boca Raton, FL, on September 2, 2002. Beloved wife of Leo, adored mother of Deborah, Judith, Dianne and Lisa. Awarded Ph.D. in Microbiology by Boston University (1949), she researched malaria immunization at New York's Public Health Institute, taught at several New Jersey colleges, was Director of Microbiology at East Orange VA Hospital and was active in the National Council of Jewish Women and Women's American ORT. A joy to those around her, she will be deeply missed.
