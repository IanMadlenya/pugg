
        JOHNSEN-Charles Axel. Died at the age of 82, on October 26, 1999. Beloved husband of Julia. Loving father of Robert and Alfred. Cherished father-in-law of Joan and Christine. Loving grandfather of Katharine, Michael, Erika and Gregory. Fond brother of Mildred Cantwell. He served in the 82nd Airborne Division of the U.S. Army during World War II. An employee at The New York Times, he retired in 1979. Visitation at Hanley Funeral Home, 60 New Dorp Lane, Staten Island, New York, on Friday, October 29th at 9:30 A.M., with a funeral service following at 10:30 AM. Interment is to follow at Moravian Cemetery, Staten Island.
