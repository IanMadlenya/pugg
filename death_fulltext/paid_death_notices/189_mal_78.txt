
        WEITZ-John. The directors, staff and residents of Phoenix House mourn the loss of a great spirit and good friend. A celebrated author, historian, designer and entrepreneur, John Weitz was an early supporter and longtime board member of Phoenix House. Committed to our work, he was a generous and imaginative benefactor, who shared with us his wit, elegance and humor, sense of style, and spirit of adventure. We will miss him. Edward D. Miller, Chairman George A. Kellner, Vice Chairman Mitchell S. Rosenthal, M.D. President
        WEITZ-John. The AllenStevenson School mourns the passing of John Weitz, Trustee Emeritus, who served on the Board from 1977 to 1992 and was elected Trustee Emeritus in 1992. We express our heartfelt sympathy and condolences to his wife Susan, sons Chris '84 and Paul '80, and the entire Weitz family. An enthusiastic supporter of the School, John will be missed by the entire AllenStevenson community. David R. Trower, Headmaster Ronald S. Rolfe '60, President of the Board
        WEITZ-John. You were a very special person, unique, full of life and love. We shall miss you. With our deepest sympathy to Susan and to the family. Sue and Herman
