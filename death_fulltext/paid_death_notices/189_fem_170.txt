
        HALL-Evelyn J. Mrs. Evelyn J. Hall, the former Eve St. John, passed away very quietly on October 22 at her home in New Preston CT. She also lived in NYC and spent the winters on Hutchinson Island, FL. She was an accomplished singer and actress. She was born in Chicago and spent her youth in Evansville, Ind. She was a member of the Meade Johnson family and is survived by her loving husband, Ronald Hall, daughters Pamela Hellstrom, Stephanie Bailey - Britton, and four grandchildren and six great grandchildren. She will be buried on Johnson Island in Evansville.
