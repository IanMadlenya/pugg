
        BRADEN--William. Died May 22 at home in Lloyd Harbor. He was 85. A retired vice president in the investment department of Morgan Guaranty Trust Co. in New York, Mr. Braden had run the bank's European investment operations in Paris from 196772. Mr. Braden was a lieutenant in the U.S. Navy during World War II. He was a Knight of Malta, and had served on the board of the Society for the Propagation of the Faith and other institutions. He was married to the late Lachlan Porter Braden and Adelaide Weld Braden. He is survived by a son, William Braden III, of Providence, R.I; a daughter, Maria Braden Clark, of Lexington, Ky; a stepdaughter, Hope W. Lapsley; a stepson, Robert Whitney, both of Long Island, and five grandchildren. He also leaves two sisters, Maruja Meirelles of New York, Patricia Clark of Pinehurst, N.C., and a brother, Spruille Braden, of Bethel, Conn. A Funeral Mass will be celebrated 9:30 A.M. Saturday St. Patrick's Catholic Church, 630 Main St., Huntington. Donations may be made in lieu of flowers to Visiting Nurse Service Suffolk County.
