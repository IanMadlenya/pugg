
        GOLDMAN-Estelle, 89, of Long Beach, NY. Loving mother, wife & friend. Passed away Wednesday, May 13. She is survived by her three children, Michael Goldman, Jerrold Goldman & Rae Kudysch and her grandchildren Melissa Goldman, Kevin Goldman, Judy Kule, Pamela Kudysch, Andrew Kudysch, Seth Goldman and Matthew Goldman. Services private. In lieu of flowers, contributions to Gurwin Jewish Geriatric Center, Commack, New York.
