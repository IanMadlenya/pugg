
        ROSENZWEIG-Elias. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Elias Rosenzweig, a cherished friend of UJA-Federation, whose dedicated service as past president and longtime member of the Board of the Herman Goldman Foundation helped support numerous Jewish and humanitarian causes in New York & throughout the world. We extend our deepest sympathies to his entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
