
        COLFIN-Sylvia (Sally). February 13, 1919 to October 15, 2000. Civilian Employee for US Army during WWII with Artist Corps. Was first secretary to Willard Cummings' Skowhegan School of Painting and Sculpture. During a 52 year marriage to Abe Colfin she was a devoted wife and mother. She was an avid volunteer, Den Mother and employee at the East New York YM-YWHA. At the age of 50 she began a second career as a civil servant retiring at age 75 from the payroll office at Brooklyn Development Center in East New York. She leaves two sons Larry and Bruce, their wives and three grandchildren, a foster daughter, a sister and nieces and nephews whom she raised. Services to be held at Gutterman's Funeral Home, 175 Long Beach Road, Rockville Centre, NY at 10AM, Wednesday, October 18th.
