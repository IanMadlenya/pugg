
        JENTIS--Felice L. On November 2, 2005. She was born April 9, 1966 in New York City, educated at the University of Michigan, worked in advertising, dynamic, witty, involved in many community activities. Leaves behind her loving husband Stuart and two adorable children, Alexa and Matthew, beloved sister Kim, her dear parents Muriel and Michael, her adored grandfather Morris. She will be in our hearts forever.
