
        DEEGAN--Jane Lucey at home in Providence, Rhode Island 10 March 2006. Partner and muse in the jewelry firm Jeff Deegan Designs with her husband. Born 14 July 1951 in Perth Amboy, New Jersey to the late James J. and Winifred Lucey, Providence resident since 1978; member of the Handicraft Club. Graduate of Rosemont College. Survived by husband J.F. Deegan III and sons Seamus Brew Deegan of Long Beach, California and Liam Lucey Deegan of Providence. Sister of Denis J. Lucey III MD of Philadelphia and Deborah L. Kroop of Woodbury, New Jersey. Jane requests in lieu of flowers a donation to Dana Farber Cancer Institute or buy something nice for yourself.
