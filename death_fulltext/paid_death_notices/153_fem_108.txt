
        DEMBITZER-Robin C., M.D. The Mount Sinai Alumni record with sorrow the untimely death of Dr.Robin Dembitzer, School of Medicine graduate of the class of 1988. Freed, M.D., Vice Pres. Maria Padilla, M.D., Secretary William G. Stein, M.D., Treasurer
