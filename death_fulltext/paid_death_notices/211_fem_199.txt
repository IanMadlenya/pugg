
        FOSSETT--Janet Perloff. 50, of Delmar, NY, formerly of New York City, on August 29, 2004. Beloved wife of James W. Fossett, mother of Jeffrey Fossett, daughter of Sondra Perloff and the late Milton Perloff, sister of Linda (Perloff) Freundlich and Sheila (Perloff) Eddison. Born and raised in Queens, Janet earned a bachelor's degree from Kirkland College and master's and doctoral degrees from the University of Chicago. Since 1989, she held faculty and administrative appointments in the University at Albany's Schools of Social Welfare and Public Health. Her last position was as Professor and Associate Dean for Research in the School of Social Welfare and Professor in the School of Public Health. Funeral services will be held on Tuesday, August 31, 11 a.m. at B'Nai Sholom Reform Congregation, 420 Whitehall Road, Albany. Interment will follow in Elmwood Hills Cemetery in Troy. The family will sit shiva at their home for three days; family and friends are invited to call. In lieu of flowers, memorial contributions may be made to the Janet D. Perloff Lecture/Scholarship Fund at the University at Albany. Checks payable to the University at Albany Foundation with ''Janet D. Perloff Fund'' noted in the memo can be sent to Mary-Beth NelliganGoodman, School of Social Welfare, University at Albany, 135 Western Avenue, Albany, NY 12222.
