
        SMITHIES--Dolores Cynthia. Beloved mother, daughter, sister, grandmother, and godmother; patron of the arts, and friend to many, died peacefully at her home in Key Biscayne after a long illness. She was 65 years old, and surrounded by her children. Born in Bryn Mawr, PA, and raised in Havana, Cuba, Mrs. Smithies divided her time between Miami, New York and Gstaad, Switzerland, where she will be laid to rest. She will be rememberd for her devotion to helping others including underprivileged families, children with AIDS, and Cuban refugees. In her later years, she sponsored young artist from Cuba and other Latin American countries. Mrs. Smithies is survived by her four children, Andre, Sergei, Natasha, and Sasha; her mother Gladys, her brother John, her granddaughter Anastasia, her daughter-in-law Sandra and her godchildren. ''Dollsie'' will be missed and always in our hearts. Her generosity, joiede-vivre, beauty and elegance will stay with us forever. Funeral Mass will be held at St. Agnes Church in Key Biscayne, Thursday June 10 at 10 am. In lieu of flowers please send donations to the Miami Art Museum, the IRC, or the GHHC.
