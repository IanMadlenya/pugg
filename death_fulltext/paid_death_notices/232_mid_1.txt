
        DONENFELD--Arlene J. Actress, Administrator, Advocate--died on May 2. She performed with Charles Laughton and Jerry Stiller. Was a Supervisor of Speech for the New York City Public Schools. Wife of the late Philip P. Donenfeld, mother of Allison Nichols and her husband Malcolm, and Clifford Donenfeld, grandmother of Steffany and Justin and sister of Dr. Bernard Lieberman and his wife Cindi. Graveside service at Beth Moses Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY on Friday, May 5 at 12:30 PM. Memorial service at Stanetsky Chapels, Brookline, MA (800-842-4280) on Sunday, May 7 at 2:00 PM.
