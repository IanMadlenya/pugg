
        FINKEL-Lena S. Age 97, died December 24, 1999. She had an interesting and varied life. Was secretary to famous attorney/judge, Samuel Liebowitz. Later partnered with late husband, Harry, to run Shades Incorporated. Mother of Jerome (deceased) and Ruth, Elaine and Myron Adler, and Bruce and Barbara. Grandmother of ten and great grandmother of 18. She will be missed. Services Sunday, December 26, at 3:30 PM, Robert Schoem, Paramus, NJ. Contributions to Jewish Home in Rockleigh, NJ or the charity of your choice.
