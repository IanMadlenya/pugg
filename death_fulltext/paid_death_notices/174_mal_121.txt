
        TANIER-George I. October 17, 1917-July 6, 2001. Importer of Danish furniture for half a century and founder of Design Selections International, Inc. Survived by his wife Inger-Marie Tanier of CrotonOn-Hudson, New York, son Jeffrey Tanier Berg of Cambridge, MA, daughter Nanna Tanier of Malverne, NY, and his sisters Eva Hindus of Newton, MA. and Roma Konefsky of Brooklyn, New York.
