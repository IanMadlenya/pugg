
        YING-Dr. Sai Hou on May 7, 2003, at the age of 95. He devoted more than sixty years to his patients, students, and family. He came to the U.S. as a visiting scholar at P.& S. College, Columbia University. Later, he served as a physician at the Memorial Hospital for Cancer and Allied Disease, assistant professor at the Cornell University Medical School, and a founding member at the Strang Clinic of the New York Preventive Cancer Research Institute. He had a private practice in Manhattan for more than 15 years. He saved lives, made contributions to the medical science, and established the image of a man of professionalism and integrity. He is survived by his wife, four daughters, six grandchildren, and two great grandchildren. Memorial service, Friday, May 16, 5 PM at Chun Fook Funeral Home, 134-35 Northern Blvd., Flushing, NY, 11354.
