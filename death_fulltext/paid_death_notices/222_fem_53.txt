
        SMITH--Rita, 89, died peacefully at the Allerton House Assisted Living Facility, Duxbury, Mass., on Tuesday, July 5. A longtime resident of Forest Hills, NY, she moved there in January to be near her daughter and son-in-law, Barbara and Morgan Lamarche. Also survived by grandson, Gregory La marche, and his wife, Mikaela, of NYC; sister, Alyse Dettmers of Chevy Chase, MD, and sister-in-law, Pat Schoenfeld of Durham, NC. Contributions in her memory may be made to Cranberry Hospice, 36 Cordage Park Circle, Plymouth, MA 02360. A woman of great worldliness, charm and spirit, we will all miss her very much.
