
        BERKOWITZ-Bryant, 68, formerly of Flushing, NY, passed away at his residence in West Palm Beach, FL on Thursday, April 20, 2000. Beloved husband of Enid, devoted father of Andree, Matthew, Jack, cherished grandfather of Michael, dear brother of Joan, Daveda and the late Martin and loving uncle. Graveside service will be held 11:00am, Monday, April 24 at Beth EL Cemetery, Paramus, NJ. Memorial contributions may be made to Hospice of Palm Beach county, 5300 East Ave, West Palm Beach, FL 33407. Levitt-Weinstein Memorial Chapels 1-800-947-4256
        BERKOWITZ-Bryant. The Metronorth Region of B'nai B'rith and its 10,000 members express deepest sympathy to our President Jack Berkowitz on the loss of his father. The Jewish principals of caring for others that the deceased taught his son have been invaluable to the charitable endeavors of B'nai B'rith. Martin Levitin, Past President Harold Mitchell, Vice President Harold Miller, Vice President Arthur Jacobs, Regional Director
