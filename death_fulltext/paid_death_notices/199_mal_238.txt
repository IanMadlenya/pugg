
        SILVERMAN--Herbert R. The Boards of Trustees, faculty, and staff of NYU Medical Center mourn the passing of Herbert R. Silverman, a cherished friend, benefactor and Life Trustee of the Medical Center and New York University. Mr. Silverman's son, Henry R. Silverman, Chairman, President, and CEO of Cendant Corporation, also serves on our board. A distinguished attorney and pioneer in the commercial finance industry, Herbert Silverman was a graduate of NYU School of Commerce. He went on to serve the University as founder and first president of the NYU Club, president of the NYU Alumni Federation, professor of finance and honorary retired Vice Chairman of the NYU Board of Trustees. Mr. Silverman was a man who touched the lives of many people, and he will be deeply missed by all who knew him. We send our deepest condolences to our friend and colleague, Henry, and the Siverman family. Kenneth G. Langone, Chairman, Boards of Trustees NYU School of Medicine Foundation NYU Hospitals Center Robert M. Glickman, M.D., Dean, NYU School of Medicine CEO, NYU Hospitals Center Theresa A. Bischoff President, NYU Hospitals Center
        SILVERMAN--Herbert R. The partners and staff of Joseph Stern & Company sincerely mourn the death of Herbert R. Silverman, a client of our firm for over 50 years. His integrity, kindness and elegance will be deeply missed. We extend our heartfelt condolences to his family. Joseph Stern & Company
