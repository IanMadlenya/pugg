
        SUALL-Joan (nee Parnes). Of Manhattan, on Sunday, January 17, 1999. Wife of the late Bertram Suall. Died of complications relating to emphysema. Joan spent a lifetime fighting for social justice and economic democracy at home and abroad. She played significant roles in many organizations including Social Democrats, USA, the Jewish Labor Committee, the National Council of Jewish Women, the Amalgamated Clothing and Textile Workers Union, AFL-CIO, the Workmen's Circle, the N.Y. Coalition of Center Democrats, the Chelsea Midtown Democratic Club, the Village Reform Democratic Club, and the Tilden Democratic Club. She is mourned and will be missed by all who were touched by her passion for a world of peace, freedom and democracy. She is survived by many cousins, relatives and loving friends. A graveside ceremony will be held on Wednesday, January 20, 2 pm, at the New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY. A memorial service will be held in the near future. Friends are invited to her home, 330 Third Ave., NYC, on Wednesday, January 20, and Thursday, January 21, 6:30-8:30 PM.
