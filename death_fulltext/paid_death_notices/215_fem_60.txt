
        LICHTENSTEIN--Lillian L. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJAFederation of New York mourn the passing of Lillian L. Lichtenstein, beloved mother of Lynne Tarnopol; and mother-in-law of Mickey Tarnopol, cherished leaders of the New York Jewish community whose dedication makes a profound impact on our ability to care for those in need. We extend our deepest sympathies to Lynne and Mickey; to their children: Lori, and her husband David Moore; and Lisa Silverman; and to the entire family. Morris W. Offit, President Susan K. Stern, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
