
        SIMPSON-William Douglas. Former international vice president, Sterling Drug Company, died April 16, 1998, in Briarcliff Manor, New York at age 89. Devoted husband of Alayne, he was an avid sportsman with many trophies in golf, hunting and fly fishing. Born in Scotland and educated in Chile, Argentina and England, he spearheaded Sterling Drug's expansion throughout South America in the 1950s and '60s.
