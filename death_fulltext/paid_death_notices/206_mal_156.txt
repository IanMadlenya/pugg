
        SIEGEL--Joel E., age 63, died on March 11, 2004. Of Arlington, VA. Survived by his parents Miriam D. and Sherman H. Siegel of Washington, PA and his sister Judith E. Siegel-Baum of New York City. Lyricist, Author, film critic, Grammy winner, music producer and manager and retired professor of English at Georgetown University. He was a master of words and music and his unique brilliance and creativity were inspirational to all who were lucky enough to know him. A memorial to celebrate his life, in Washington, DC on June 25th. Memorial donations may be made to any organization benefitting dogs or Jazz musicians.
