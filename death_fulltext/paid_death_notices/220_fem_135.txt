
        AGNEW--Rebecca Tenney. Devoted wife of the late Donald Agnew. Born in 1910 and died March 18, 2005 at Martin Memorial Hospital, Stuart, FL. She leaves a sister, Mrs. Clifford Sutter of Osterville, MA., brothers, C. Thomas Tenney of Ponte Vedra Beach, FL., Harral Straat Tenney (deceased). She was a devoted aunt to a multitude of nieces and nephews. Mrs. Agnew was a resident of Hobe Sound, FL. for 40 years and a former resident of Locust Valley, N.Y. and New York City. She attended Rosemary Hall and Smith College. She was active in community affairs New York City. Her first husband was Francis X. Shields, deceased.
