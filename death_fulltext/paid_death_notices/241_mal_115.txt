
        BALTON--Charles (Ibo) D. Of Harlem, New York, Died February 6, 2007. He is survived by his mother Marion Van Allen, son Eddie, five grandchildren, one great-grandchild, two brothers, devoted cousins, and other relatives and friends. The memorial for Ibo is Saturday, the 10th at 3:30 pm at New Song Community Church, 2230 Fredrick Douglas Blvd., Harlem, NY.
