
        HARTH--Robert J. The Board of Directors and staff of the American Symphony Orchestra League extend our sincere condolences to the family, friends, and colleagues of our fellow board member, Robert J. Harth. We are all deeply indebted to his invaluable service to classical music and America's orchestras. He inspired us all and we will miss him. Lou Mason, Chair Henry Fogel President and CEO Board and Staff American Symphony Orchestra League
        HARTH--Robert. The Culture and Arts Programming Department of 13/WNET New York mourns the passing of Robert Harth, a colleague and collaborator for far too short a time. We had anticipated a long partnership of bringing his inspired vision for Carnegie Hall to fruition for a national audience. We will miss him and seek to carry his spirit with us as we move forward.
        HARTH--Robert. The Juilliard Community profoundly mourns the tragic death of our friend and colleague, Robert Harth. With vision, determination, and courage, he showed us all how the power of music can change the world. We extend our deepest condolences to his family and friends. Bruce Kovner, Chairman Joseph W. Polisi, President
        HARTH--Robert. New York City Opera is deeply saddened by the passing of Robert Harth. His vision, his commitment, and his personal warmth will be greatly missed.         HARTH--Robert J. Chris Roberts, Nancy Zannini, and Universal Classics Group mourn the untimely death of our treasured friend and colleague. We offer our sincere condolences to his family, friends, and colleagues.
