
        ADNEPOS-Selma R. Died peacefully on December 1 at the age of 88. Devoted wife of the late S. Bernard Adnepos. Survived by her daughter Marjorie Berkley and her son Lee Adnepos and five grandchildren and one great grandchild. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to The Family Centers, Inc., 40 Arch Street, Greenwich, Ct. 06830 and directed to the Early Childhood Education Book Fund.
