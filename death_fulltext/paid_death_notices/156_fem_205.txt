
        RIDDLE-Alma R. On January 19, 2000. Daughter of the late James O. Riddle and Adeline Swingle Riddle. Survived by one sister Sara Riddle Lowery of Chattanooga, TN. Graduate of the University of Tennessee at Chattanooga and the Fashion Institute of Technology of NYC. A resident of Manhattan since 1956, she was employed in the fashion industry as a designer and pattern maker for 35 years. Memorial service Sunday, 2 PM at ''The Riverside'' 76 Street and Amsterdam Avenue and at a later date in Chattanooga, TN.
