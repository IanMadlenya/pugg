
        BRUELL - Florence. Flossie Schweitzer, 85, of Hewlett Bay Park, N.Y., on September 27. Beloved wife for fifty-four years of the late Frederick. Adored mother of Chris, Margaret, Lucy & Emily. Loving grandmother of Harry and Jenny Bruell, Katie and Scott Watson, Ada Rubin and Jesse Ruigomez and great-grandmother of Leo Bruell. Devoted mother-in-law of Marjorie Bruell and Christopher Ruigomez. Also mourned by her loving sister Helen Brooke and many dear nieces, nephews and friends. Athlete, social worker and humanitarian. Lifelong friend of the community, Florence's irrepressible spirit touched all that knew her. The family will announce a memorial service at a later date. Contributions in her memory may be sent to the Fresh Air Fund.
