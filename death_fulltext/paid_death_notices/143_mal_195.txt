
        COLLERAN-Walter M. , 86, of Palm City, FL, formerly of Bay Shore, L.I. Retired attorney of Colleran, O'Hara & Mills, died Dec. 12, 1998. He leaves his wife Anne, three daughters, Glynn Donlon of Germantown, TN, Maureen Doyle of Pennington, NJ, Patricia C. Strong of St. David's, PA, one son Walter M. Colleran, Jr. of Shoreham, NY, two sisters Blanche Healey and Jean Cruthers of Southampton, NY, 12 grandchildren, one great grandchild. Memorials may be made to the St. Jude Children's Research Hospital, Memphis, TN.
