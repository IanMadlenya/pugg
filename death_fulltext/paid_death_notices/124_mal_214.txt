
        CREEL-Dana S. We note with sadness the passing of Dana S. Creel, a dear friend, wise colleague, and thoughtful advisor. As first president of the Rockefeller Brothers Fund, Dana practiced philanthropy with gracious style and visionary intellect, anticipating some of today's most pressing social issues and needs. He set a standard for giving that inspires us still. Abby M. O'Neill, Chairman Colin G. Campbell, President Russell A. Phillips, Jr. Executive Vice President Benjamin R. Shute, Jr. Secretary & Treasurer On behalf of the Rockefeller Brothers Fund
