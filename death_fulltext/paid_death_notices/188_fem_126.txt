
        HOLDSTEIN-Sophie G. On September 17 at 100 years of age. Beloved wife of the late Walter M. Holdstein and mother of Jill Edelson and Margot Harley. Her grandsons Geoffrey, David, Andrew and Joshua Edelson and great - grandchildren Charlotte, Annabel, Oliver, Benzi and Mia Edelson will all miss Raggy. Services Friday, September 20, at 1 P.M. at Temple Emanul-El, Fifth Avenue at 65th Street.
        HOLDSTEIN - Sophie. The Board of Trustees and staff of the Jewish Board of Family and Children's Services acknowledges with great sadness the passing of Sophie Holdstein in her 101st year. She was the beloved mother of Jill Edelson and Margot Harley. The Agency is grateful to the Edelson family for their longstanding devotion to our children and families. Jill is an active member of the Mishkon Committee and her late husband Kenneth was a Big Brother and member of the Scholarship and Westchester Committees. Grandson David is a dedicated JBFCS Trustee and grandson Andrew a former Chair of the JBFCS Friends. Our sincere condolences to the entire family. Jean L. Troubh, President Joseph S. Kaplan, Chairman of the Board Susan Tofel, Chair, Mishkon Divisional Committee Alan B. Siskind, Ph.D., Executive VP and CEO
