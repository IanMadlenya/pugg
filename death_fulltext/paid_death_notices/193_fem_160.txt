
        WILLEN-Janet Y. On February 15, 2003, at age 84. Beloved wife of the late Joseph Willen and Hans A. Sonnenthal. Loving mother of Peter Y. Sonnenthal and Valerie J.R. Sonnenthal. Adoring grandmother of Hudson and Sawyer Klebs. Services at Frank E. Campbell, 1076 Madison at 81st Street, Friday, February 21, 12 noon. Interment Beth Olem Cemetery. Contributions in her memory may be made to the UJA Federation, 130 East 59th Street, NY, NY 10022, or the American Cancer Society, 19 West 56th Street, NY, NY 10019.
        WILLEN-Janet Y. The officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of Janet Y. Willen, who with her late husband, Joseph Willen, former executive vice president of the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies, was committed to the Jewish community. We extend our deepest sympathies to her children, Peter Y. Sonnenthal and Valerie J.R. Sonnenthal, and to her entire family. Larry Zicklin, President Morris W. Offit, Chair of the Board John S. Ruskay, Exec. V.P. & C.E.O.
