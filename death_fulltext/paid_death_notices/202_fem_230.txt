
        GOLDSTEIN--Anita. A longtime New Rochelle resident, passed away Friday, November 28, 2003. She was 97. She was pre-deceased by her husband Harry Goldstein and survived by her daughter and son Marcia and Ted Streit, her grandchildren Susan & Howard Meltzer, Meredith & Steve Kleitzel and greatgrandchildren Jesse, Zoe, Nikki & Eppie. Graveside service is scheduled for today, at Sharon Gardens, 11:30 AM. Family will observe shiva at the home of Marcia and Ted Streit, November 30 and December 1. For information contact George T. Davis Funeral Home: 914-632-0324.
