
        SHAPIRO-Bernie, passed away Monday evening. He was a graduate of the United States Coast Guard Academy who served his nation well and thereafter entered the business world. He founded Polaris Plastics Corp. which was sold to Ashland Oil Company in 1970 and thereafter organized Rhodes Plastics Corp. which was sold to Dutch Boy (Artra) in the late 1970's. He is survived by his sister, Dorothy Garbus, his brother, Stanley Shapiro, four nephews and one niece. Services will be held Wednesday, May 7 at 11:00 A.M. at Riverside Memorial Chapel, between 75th and 76th Street on Amsterdam Avenue, New York. Persons wishing to make contributions in his memory should transmit these to Dr. Gavril Pasternak, Department of Neurology, Memorial Sloan Kettering Cancer Center, 1275 York Avenue, New York, NY 10021.
