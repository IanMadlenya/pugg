
        WALLACH-James. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of James Wallach: loving husband and father. Our sympathies are extended to his wife, Mary, and sons, Andrew and Scott, as well as his sisters, Sue Wachenheim and Kate Cassidy, and brother, Kenneth Wallach and parents, Ira and Miriam, who are dedicated supporters of UJA-Federation and many civic and educational organizations. We offer our deepest condolences to his entire family and all those whose lives he has touched. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair Louise B. Greilsheimer, President Stephen D. Solender, Exec. V.P.
