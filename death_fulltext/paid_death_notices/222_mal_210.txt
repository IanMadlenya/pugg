
        RANDISI--Hon. Martin A. Class of 1948, Saint John's University School of Law. A talented, distinguished jurist possessed of impeccable legal scholarship. Totally devoted to his ever loving wife Anne, who has been his life's partner. Marty was a mainstay at Saint John's School of Law and held every leadership position that the University could bestow. His energy, commitment and strength will forever be remembered. Jerome Belson, Class of 1948 Saint John's University School of Law
