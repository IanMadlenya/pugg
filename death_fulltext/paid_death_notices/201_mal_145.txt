
        GARABEDIAN -- Dennis P. The Board of Trustees of Long Island Educational TV Council, Inc. and the staff of WLIW21 mourn the untimely passing of our esteemed Board Member, Dennis Garabedian, on October 10, 2003. Dennis was a dedicated member of our Board and a generous contributor of both time and money and support of our mission of bringing the finest quality public television to our audience. His vision, energy and commitment will be sorely missed. Our sincere condolences to his family.
