
        HALPERN-Rhoda W. On April 29, 2001, at age 90. Beloved mother of Joel Halpern, Margot (David) Youngs and Stephen Halpern. Cherished grandmother of Peter, William, Zachary and Zoe. Devoted sister of Vivian Michelson and Mimi Rieger. Caring sister-in-law of Belle Klusk. Her beauty, intellect and charm enriched all who knew her. Funeral service Tuesday, 12 noon, at the Ballard-Durand Funeral Home, 2 Maple Ave, White Plains, NY.
