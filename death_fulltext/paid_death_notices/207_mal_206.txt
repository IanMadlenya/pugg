
        COLE--Robert L. (Bob). 85, of New York, NY, died on April 13th, of heart failure. Born in Chicago, IL on January 23rd, 1919. Graduated from the University of Chicago, Navy Veteran of WWII. He was proud of his role as a Radar Officer on the USS Alabama during the Marianas campaign. Surived by his children Peter Cole, Michael Schaff, Anabel Cole and Maggie Cole and his six grandchildren, his sister and brother, and his beloved partner, Ingrid Edwards. Enjoyed a long and happy career as an investment research analyst at Goldman, Sachs and Co., Wertheim Inc., and Nordberg Capital Inc. He will be remembered for his capacity for learning, joy in life and generosity. A memorial service will be held at a later date.
