
        KEYSER--Barbara Loncope. On December 23 at the age of 87 at Jones' Contrivance Farm, Reisterstown, MD. Wife of the late William Fenwick Keyser, she is survived by her sister, Mary Lee Johansen of Lee, MA; her son, Michael Keyser of Butler, MD; two daughters, Belinda Keyser Kaye and Susanna Keyser Borghese, both of New York City and four grandchildren. A memorial service will be held in Baltimore after the 10th of January. In lieu of flowers, donations may be made to the Women's Committee Contemporary Print Fund of the Baltimore Museum of Art.
