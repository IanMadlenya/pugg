
        WEBB-CLANCY--Lisa Anne. 38, of San Francisco, formerly of Hazlet, passed away on January 18, in San Francisco, CA. She was a resident of Hazlet before moving to San Francisco fourteen years ago. She attended St. John Vianney High School in Holmdel and graduated with honors in 1984. She attended Boston University and later transferred to Fordham University, Lincoln Center Campus of Performing Arts, where she graduated with a BA in Communications. She had an independent spirit and enjoyed hiking, camping and the outdoors. She is survived by her father, John P. Clancy of New York, NY, her mother, Carol Ann Clancy of Hazlet, three sisters, Laurie Clancy of Monmouth Junction, NJ, Janine Clancy of New York, NY, Kim Clancy of New York, NY, a stepmother, Maureen Clancy, her grandparents, Rita Clancy of Brooklyn, NY, Grace and Tony Fumari of Brooklyn, NY, two nephews, John Sarno and Vincent Sarno of Monmouth Junction. Visitation will be held on Tuesday, January 25, from 4-8 pm, at the Waitt-Nelms Funeral Home, 501 Hwy 79, Morganville. A funeral service will be held at the funeral home on Wednesday, January 26, at 11 am. Interment will follow in Old Tennent Cemetery, Tennent, NJ. In lieu of flowers donations may be sent in her name to Greenpeace, 75 Arkansas St., San Francisco, CA 94107. Condolences may be sent in her name via www.waittnelmsfh.com.
