
        WEISS--Mildred (Millie). The Board of Directors and members of Bocaire Country Club Inc in Boca Raton, Florida record with sorrow the passing of our esteemed member Millie Weiss on September 5, 2004 in Munster, Indiana. Our deepest sympathies go to her husband Marvin (Mickey) and all of her family. Services were held at 11 AM, Tuesday, September 7 at Burns-Kish FH in Munster, IN Kurt Merl, President
