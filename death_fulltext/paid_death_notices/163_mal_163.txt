
        TUCKER-Herman. The Board of Directors and staff of UJA-Federation of New York note with deep sorrow the death of Herman Tucker, beloved father of our distinguished board member Rabbi Gordon Tucker of Temple Israel Center, White Plains. Throughout his life, Mr. Tucker was dedicated to his family and the Jewish people throughout the world. We extend our sincere sympathy to his children, Rabbi Tucker and Amy Cohen, and Dr. Toby Tucker Hecht and Barry Hecht; his sister Sylvia Rosencranz; grandchildren Ethan, Ariela, Becky, Michal, Spencer, and Jonathan; and all the members of the family. May his memory endure as a blessing. James S. Tisch, President Larry Zicklin, Board Chair John S. Ruskay, Exec VP & CEO
