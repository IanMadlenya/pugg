
        MAONI--Antonio, 54. Born on the island of Corsica, he fulfilled his dream of coming to live in America and finding love, happiness and success here. He died in New York, Saturday, February 5, 2005 only 13 days after being diagnosed with small cell lung cancer. Beloved life-companion and husband of Elyse Simring. Dear son-in-law of Francine and Marvin Simring. Fond brother-in-law of Jim and Ruth Simring, Bob and Lise Simring, and June Simring-MacAuley. Loving uncle of Annabelle, Ella, Mia and Noah. Services Sunday, 3:45PM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Amsterdam Ave. between 76th & 77th St.
