
        LIEBERMAN-May. The Officers, Board of Directors, and staff of UJA-Federation of New York mourn the passing of May Lieberman, beloved mother of Lillian Eisman, a distinguished member of our Board of Directors and honorary chair of the Board of Jewish Education of Greater New York, a beneficiary agency. We extend our sincerest condolences to Lillian, her husband, Elliot, and the entire family. Judith Stern Peck, Board Chair James S. Tisch, President John S. Ruskay, Exec. VP & CEO
        LIEBERMAN-May. The Aleph Society extends its sincere condolences to its esteemed friend Lillian Eisman, and to the entire family, on the passing of its matriarch. May the family be comforted among the mourners of Zion. Rabbi Adin Steinsaltz
