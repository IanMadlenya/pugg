
        TREEGER--Clarence R. 101, on Sunday, March 20, 2005. Beloved husband of the late Helen Felstiner Treeger, dear father of Thomas Treeger (Madge) and Betsy Zander (Michael). Cherished grandfather of Jennifer Peters (Eric), Anne Treeger (Tom Huck), Nicola Zander, and Jonathan Zander; great grandfather of August and Charles Peters; brother of the late Irving Treeger, the late Syd Moskowitz, and the late Blanche Ginsburg. Dear uncle, great uncle and friend. Funeral Thursday, March 31 at 11:15 AM at ''The Riverside'' 76th St. and Amsterdam Avenue. In lieu of flowers contributions preferred to Visions, 500 Greenwich Street, New York, NY 10013.
        TREEGER -- Clarence. The Board of Directors and staff of VISIONS/Services for the Blind and Visually Impaired records with great sorrow the passing of Clarence Treeger. He was a past president of the Board of Directors and member of the Board and Advisory Council for 49 years. Mr. Treeger was a dedicated supporter of VISIONS and Vacation Camp for the Blind. He contributed to VISIONS his legal mind and sense of humor. Our heartfelt sympathy is expressed to his family. Nancy T. Jones, President Nancy D. Miller Executive Director
        TREEGER--Clarence R., President Emeritus, Solon E. Summerfield Foundation, New York City. The Board of Directors of the Solon E. Summerfield Foundation records with great sorrow the passing of Clarence R. Treeger, President of the Foundation from 1994 to 2002. He had served as an officer and director for 49 years, guiding the Foundation in its mission of supporting education, social service, the arts and medical research. William W. Prager, Jr. President
