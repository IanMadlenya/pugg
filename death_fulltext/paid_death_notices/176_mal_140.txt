
        SHEERIN - Raymond Thomas. Born February 18, 1934. Died August 13, 2001 after a long and brave battle with prostate cancer. Survivers include his beloved wife Barbara, children and step-children Laurie, Raymond Jr. (Pat), Matthew (Eileen), Mark, Lisa (Jussi) Melartin, Michael Waitzman, eight grandchildren, brotherin-law Dr. Robert (Barbara) Margolis. Niece Dr. Jennifer (Dr. Eric) Fryer, nephew Steven (Jenifer) Margolis. Donations to Fordham Prep in his memory would be appreciated.
