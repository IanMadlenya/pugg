
        ANOS - Margaret MacDonald (Maggie) nee Duffield, at age 94, on December 5, 1998. Born in Ayrshire, Scotland. Devoted wife of the late William. Beloved mother of the late John Boyd, Maria Govier and Theonie White. Loved mother-in-law of Robert Govier and Howard White. Sister of Isabel Milliken of Canada. Cherished grandmother of Glenn and Cliff Govier, John and W. Gordon Boyd of Scotland and Theonie Mackovinyi of South Africa. Great-grandmother to David (deceased) and Alison Boyd, Claire Mackovinyi and Joshua Govier. She was loved by all who knew her. Her sense of humor brought joy to those around her. She brought a ray of sunshine into every life she touched. She will be greatly missed. The family will receive friends at the Riverdale Funeral Home, Broadway and 214th Street, on Tuesday, December 8, 1998, 2 PM to 4 PM and 7 PM to 9 PM. Funeral service will be Wednesday, December 9, at 10 AM, at the Riverdale Funeral Home, followed by interment at Ferncliff Cemetery, Ardsley, New York. In lieu of flowers, contributions in her memory may be made to the American Heart Association.
