
        PLATT-Abe A. 83, of Bronxville, New York and formerly of Mount Vernon, NY, died February 14, 1998, in New York. Beloved husband of the late Ann. Devoted brother of Esther, Anna and the late Ruth and Philip. Loving father of Mitchell, William (Lynne), Diane and Gale. Dear companion of Dottie D'Augelli. Cherished uncle, adored grandfather of seven and great-grandfather of four. A graduate of New York University and Brooklyn Law School, Abe was devoted to the family business he helped build, from which he had not retired. Abe enjoyed golfing and spending time with his family and friends. He will be deeply missed and never forgotten. Funeral services Monday, February 16, at 10 AM at Riverside Memorial Chapel, Mount Vernon, NY. Interment Sharon Gardens Cemetery, Valhalla, NY. In lieu of flowers, donations to The Giving Tree Scholarship Fund, Boston University, Sargent Camp, 36 Sargent Camp Road, Hancock, NH 03449 would be appreciated.
