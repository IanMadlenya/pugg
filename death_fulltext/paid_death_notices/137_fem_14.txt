
        SACKS-Shirley, Ed.D. The Board of the National Council on Women's Health mourns the passing of its beloved and highly respected member. As editor-in-chief, she triumphed in turning the newsletter into a great source of information on women's health issues. Her enduring legacy of empowering women through education will continue with the establishment of an annual Shirley Sacks Memorial Lecture. For those who want to share her vision, commitment, and love for humankind, please call 212-746-6967. Clarita Herrera, M.D.
