
        PROBST-Michael L. On November 7, 1998. The New York University School of Continuing and Professional Studies is saddened by the passing of Michael Probst who died peacefully at home following a long illness. His devotion and exemplary performance as an employee of NYU since 1978, was recognized when he received the NYU Distinguished Administrator Award. Michael will leave a major void for his colleagues and friends. Sincere condolences are extended to his family. We will miss you. Gerald A. Heeger, Dean Frances Gottfried, Vice Dean
