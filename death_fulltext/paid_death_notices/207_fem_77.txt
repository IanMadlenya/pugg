
        SCOTT--Lorrayne ''Joni,'' age 85, of Ocean Ridge, FL, died Wednesday afternoon, April 7, 2004. She had lived in this area since 1978, coming from Bronxville, NY. Mrs. Scott had been a volunteer at Lawrence Hospital and the registrar for the Reformed Church Sunday School in Bronxville. She was predeceased by her husband of 61 years, Orland M. Scott, and is survived by a son, Roger (Linda) Scott of San Juan Bautista, CA; a daughter, Sherry Scott of Reno, NV; five grandchildren; and six great-grandchildren. A private service will be held in Bronxville at a later date.
