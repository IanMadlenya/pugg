
        BRAND-Ina Barishaw. June 11, 2002. Widow of the late Joseph Brand. Survivors: son Peter, daughter Rena Sue, and brother Lee Barishaw.
        BRAND-Ina. Congregation Emanu-El of the City of New York expresses to the family of our devoted member, Ina Brand our deepest sympathies on the occasion of her passing. It is our hope and and prayer that beautiful memories will linger as a legacy to her life. May all who loved her be comforted. Robert A. Bernhard, President Herbert C. Bernard, Secretary
        BRAND-Ina. The Women's Auxiliary of Congregation Emanu-El notes with deep sorrow the death of our esteemed Honorary Past President, Ina Brand. We send condolences to her family. Eve Haberman, President
