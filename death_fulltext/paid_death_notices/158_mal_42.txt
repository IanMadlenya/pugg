
        STEINHORN-Paul, M.D. Beloved and loved husband of Adele (nee Lyons). Loved and devoted father of Charles (Elizabeth) and Leonard (Sabine). Devoted and loving grandfather to Sarah, Maureen, Ariella and Max. He was retired Chief of Radiology at the La Guardia Medical Group (HIP), pastPresident of the Long Island Radiology Society, Naval Lieutenant in WW2 and the Korean War. Graveside service at 2PM on Sunday, March 5, 2000 at New Montefiore Cemetery, Pinelawn, NY.
