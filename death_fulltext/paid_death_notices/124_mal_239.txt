
        FOX-James M. It is with our deepest sorrow that the Directors, Officers and Employees of the Dime Savings Bank of Williamsburgh mourn the passing of our friend and Director, James M. Fox. We extend our sympathy to his wife, Elaine, and their entire family. We will miss him greatly. The Dime Savings Bank of Williamsburgh. Vincent F. Palagiano, Chairman
