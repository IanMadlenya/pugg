
        SCHIFFMACHER-Rita Devoe, on Feb. 2, 1997 of Great Neck, NY. Beloved wife of the late C. Ellis Schiffmacher. Survived by her son Craig of Manhasset, and her sister Marion Smith of Rockville Centre. Also survived by several nephews, grand nieces, and grand nephews. In repose at the Fairchild Funeral Chapel, 1570 Northern Blvd., Manhasset, Tuesday 3-5 and 7-9 PM. Religious service on Wednesday 11 AM at the Community Church, Great Neck. Interment Greenfield Cemetery, Hempstead. In lieu of flowers, donations to the Women's Club of Great Neck or the Community Church of Great Neck.
