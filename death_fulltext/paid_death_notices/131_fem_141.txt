
        ZUNDEL-Kathryn Kemp. Of Edison, New Jersey on December 12, 1997. Devoted wife of Richard R. Zundel. She was a member of the First Presbyterian Church of Metuchen and Metuchen Country Club, and a 1935 graduate of Smith College. Funeral private. For information Costello-Runyon Funeral Home, Metuchen, New Jersey.
