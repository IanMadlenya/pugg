
        BENDER-Lillian, July 7, 1997, nee Zebooker. Loving wife of Dr. Israel Boris Bender of Philadelphia. Devoted mother of Dr. Andrew Bender and Susan (Alan) Blumstein. Loyal sister of Dr. Eli Zebooker. Adoring grandmother of Rachel, Deborah, Eve and Zachary. Great-grandmother of Emily. Services and interment were held Wednesday in Philadelphia. The Blumstein family will return to their residence on Sunday and respectfully requests contributions in her memory be made to I.B. Bender Fund at Albert Einstein Medical Center-Philadelphia or the I.B. Bender Fund at the University of Pennsylvania. For further information, Joseph Levine & Son.
