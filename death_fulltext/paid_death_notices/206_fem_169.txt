
        Di GANGI--Linda, Ph.D., on March 17. Beloved daughter of the late M. Richard, MD and Marie. Loving sister of Richard, Peter and Roberta. Sister-in-law of Lana and Judy. Dear aunt of Christi, Becky, Kate and Matt. Linda was a psychologist for the Bellmore-Merrick School District as well as in private practice in NYC. Linda was an avid traveler and bicyclist. She was a member of the Nassau County Psychology Assn., NYS Psychology Assn., American Psychology Assn. and the Society for Psychoanalytic Training. Cremation services will be private. Cusimano & Russo Funeral Home., 2005 W. 6th St., Brooklyn, NY. Donations may be made to the American Cancer Society.
