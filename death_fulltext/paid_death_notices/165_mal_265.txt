
        McNELIS-Rev. Patrick J. The Diocese of Brooklyn announces the death of the pastor of Immaculate Heart of Mary Church, 2805 Ft. Hamilton Parkway, Brooklyn, on October 19. Reposing in Pitta Funeral Home, 300 McDonald Ave., Brooklyn, on Sunday October 22, from 25 PM. Mass of Transferral on Sunday, October 22 at 7:30 PM. Funeral Mass on Monday, October 23 at 10:30 AM. All priests are invited to concelebrate. He was a member of the Priests' Purgatorial Society. Most Rev. Thomas V. Daily Bishop of Brooklyn
