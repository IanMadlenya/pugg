
        WEISS--Carol, age 80, peacefully on April 19 at her home in East Williston after battling Alzheimers. Beloved wife of Robert Weiss. Carol graduated NYU School of Commerce in 1947. A skilled accountant and leader, she passionately loved and served her family. She is also survived by her loving children Larry (Wendy), and Susan (Ken). Brother Lewis Kaufman, and her adoring grandchildren Shennan, Sascha, Emily, and Daniel. She sought and found the good and beauty in everything she encountered.
