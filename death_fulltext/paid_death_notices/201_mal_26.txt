
        STAPP--Philip. Called ''one of the most orginal and creative animators ever to work in educational films,'' died on October 2, after a brief illness. He was 95 and lived in Stonington, CT. Over the decades, Stapp contributed finely crafted elements to many films (listed in the International Film Foundation catalog) that set the standard for creativity in animating the educational film. For the past 20 years he designed scrolls using geometrical and musical structures. He is survived by a sister, Martha List and several nieces and nephews.
