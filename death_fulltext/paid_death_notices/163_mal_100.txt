
        KOPPELMAN-Irving. In loving memory of Irving Koppelman. Father of Rosalind and Charles. Grandfather of Larry, Suzanne, Karyn, Brian, Stacy and Jennifer. Great - grandfather seventeen times. Honest and strong with a potent sense of humor, he was a man who chose his words carefully but he was never sparing in his expression of love and pride for his family. We loved and will miss him so much. Services Thursday 12:00 Noon at Riverside Nassau North Chapels, Great Neck, NY.
        KOPPELMAN-Irving. The Atlantic Golf Club mourns the passing of Irving, father of fellow member, Charles Koppelman. We extend our heartfelt sympathy to Charles and his family. Robert E. Morrow, President Harvey L. Radler, Secretary
