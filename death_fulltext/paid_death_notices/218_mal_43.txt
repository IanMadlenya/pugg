Fun-loving Papa to Matthew, Cassidy, Corrina, Alex, Joseph and Jeffrey. Dear brother of Jack, Mary O'Leary, Nancy Hartmann, his late twin Thomas, Robert, and Kathleen Olsen Wall. Also survived by many nieces and nephews. Visiting Saturday and Sunday 2-4 and 7-9PM, Clavin Funeral Home, 7722 Fourth Ave., Brooklyn. Mass of Christian burial on Monday, 9:30AM, Our Lady of Angels R.C. Church. Interment in Calverton National Cemetery.
