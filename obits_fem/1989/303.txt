        Jane Bronson Philips, an associate professor of English at Pace University, died of cancer Friday at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, N.Y. She was 69 years old and lived in Briarcliff, N.Y.
        Professor Philips joined the university as an adjunct faculty member in 1962 and was appointed to the full-time faculty in 1964.
        She is survived by her mother, Leota Bronson of Yorktown Heights, N.Y.; two daughters, Melody, of Detroit, and Jennifer Marks of Seattle; a brother, John Bronson of Brewster, N.Y., and two grandchildren.
