        Mary Stevens Baird, who for many years was active in overseeing New Jersey correctional institutions, died Sunday at her home in Bernardsville, N.J. She was 87 years old.
        She was a former chairwoman of the board of managers of the Clinton Correctional Institution for Women, an agency she joined in 1942. She was also president of the Morrow Association, now known as the New Jersey Association on Corrections, and from 1958 until her retirement in 1972 was a member of the New Jersey State Board of Control of Institutions and Agencies.
        There are no immediate survivors.
