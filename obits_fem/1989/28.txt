        Lee Goode Kingsley, who worked in the New York music world for many years, was killed on Jan. 30 by a hit-and-run motorist at 67th Street and Broadway. She was 80 years old.
        Mrs. Kingsley had been a publicity associate for the New York Philharmonic and had worked for the Musicians' Emergency Fund and the William Judd Concert Agency.
        She is survived by her sister, Cecilia Wilkenfeld, and her brother, Peter Goode, both of Manhattan.
