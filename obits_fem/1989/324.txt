        Dr. Virginia Connolly Mitty, a pediatrician in Manhattan for more than 40 years, died of a stroke on Thursday at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center. She was 70 years old.
        Dr. Mitty was an attending physician at St. Vincent's and at the New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center, where she was a professor of pediatrics.
        She graduated from the New York University College of Medicine in 1946.
        Surviving are a sister, Catherine Nolan of Ridge, N.Y., and a brother, Joseph G. Connolly of Manhasset, L.I.
