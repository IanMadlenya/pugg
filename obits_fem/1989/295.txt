        Dr. Beatrice Greenwald Kresky, a pediatrician and proponent of prenatal care for pregnant teen-agers, died of cancer Thursday at South Nassau Communities Hospital in Oceanside, L.I. She was 73 years old and lived in Rockville Center, L.I.
        Dr. Kresky, a 1945 graduate of New York University Medical School, was a pediatrician in Rockville Center. In 1973, she became chairwoman of Jamaica Hospital's department of ambulatory care. She was also project director of the comprehensive child care program at Brookdale Hospital Medical Center in Brooklyn. She served on many boards and commissions, including the Public Health Association of New York City.
        She is survived by a son, Harry, of Manhattan; a daughter, Caroline, of Atlanta, and one grandchild.
