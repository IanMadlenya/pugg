        Josephine O'Mahoney Riordan, a nurse with the British Expeditionary Forces in France during World War I, died Wednesday at the Powhatan Nursing Facility in Falls Church, Va. She was 100 years old and lived in Elmhurst, Queens.
        Mrs. Riordan was born in County Cork, Ireland. She came to the United States in 1919 and continued her nursing career until she retired in 1964.
        She is survived by a daughter, Patricia O'Reilly of Falls Church; a son, James, of Bridgehampton, L.I., and seven grandchildren.
