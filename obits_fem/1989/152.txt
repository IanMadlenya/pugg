        Marion Pierce Mack, vice chairwoman of the Westchester County Republican Committee for 33 years, died yesterday at a nursing home in Rockport, Mass. She was 94 years old and lived in Rockport.
        Mrs. Mack was a delegate to six national Republican conventions and the first woman elected to the State Republican Committee from the Third Assembly District.
        Surviving are a son, Harold, of Newburyport, Mass.; a daughter, Dorothy Russell of Rockport; five grandchildren, and seven great-grandchildren.
