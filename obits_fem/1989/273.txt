
       Anne S. Loftus, district manager for Community Board 12, which covers the area from 155th Street to the northern tip of Manhattan, died of a heart attack Thursday at her home in Manhattan. She was 64 years old.  Mrs. Loftus, a native New Yorker, had held the post since October 1980. She was in charge of monitoring the delivery of city services and conducted monthly meetings with the representatives of city agencies to discuss neighborhood needs.
