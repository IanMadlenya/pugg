        Margaret E. Boorman, who was awarded the Medal of Freedom in 1946 for her service in China, died on Wednesday at Mount Auburn Hospital in Cambridge, Mass. She was 78 years old and lived in Cambridge.
        Mrs. Boorman, the former Margaret Stelle, was a Presbyterian missionary. During World War II, she ran the Chinese mission of the United States Foreign Economic Adminstration's Air Transport Division, which airlifted war supplies over Tibet to India.
        She is survived by her son, Scott, of New Haven.
