        Ethel Danzig, a New York State Supreme Court Justice, died of colon cancer Sunday at New York Hospital. She was 58 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Justice Danzig was first elected to a 14-year term in 1979. She had been a civil court judge for six years and had maintained a private law practice.
        Justice Danzig, who was born in the Bronx, was a graduate of the University of Michigan and Brooklyn Law School, magna cum laude.
        She is survived by her husband, Stanley, a retired civil court judge, and two children, Elizabeth and Marc, both of Manhattan.
