        Marian Tyler Chase, co-author of several books with her husband, the economist Stuart Chase, died of pneumonia May 4 at a nursing home in Danbury, Conn. She was 92 years old and lived in Redding, Conn.
        Mrs. Chase, a native of Detroit, was a violinist who performed with the Danbury Symphony Orchestra. She had been a staff member of The Nation, where she met Mr. Chase, a contributing author. Mr. Chase died in 1985.
        She is survived by a stepdaughter, Sonia Chase Hodson of Weatherford, Tex.; three grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
