        Paula Melhado Gilpatric, a real-estate broker, died of cancer yesterday at her home in Manhattan. She was 55 years old.
        She was a senior vice president of Brown Harris Stevens Inc., a Manhattan real-estate firm, which she joined this year. She had been a vice president of Douglas Elliman Gibbons & Ives Inc.
        Mrs. Gilpatric, who was born in Manhattan, graduated from Vassar College. She was a trustee of the Foxcroft School and a member of the board of the Pleasant Street Foundation.
        Surviving are two daughters, Alexandra Washburn of Palm Beach, Fla., and Pamela Washburn of Manhattan; her mother, Louise Melhado of Palm Beach, and her brother, Frederick Melhado of Manhattan.
