        Dr. Jane V. Williams, a former director of education for the New York City Department of Health, died Sunday at Brooklyn Hospital after a long illness. She was 64 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Dr. Williams, who was born in New York City, joined the Department of Health in 1963 as a resident in public health administration. In 1965 she received a master's degree in public health from Columbia University. She retired in 1988.
        She is survived by her mother, Annie M. Williams of Yonkers; two brothers, George and Milton, both of Yonkers, and 13 nieces and nephews.
