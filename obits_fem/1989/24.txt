        Constance Chilton, a former director of the Elizabeth Morrow School in Englewood, N.J., died after a long illness on Dec. 28 at Englewood Hospital. She was 85 and lived in Englewood.
        She helped start The Little School in 1930 with a friend, Elizabeth Morrow, for whom the school was renamed. In Miss Chilton's 50 years as director, the elementary school grew to a campus of 11 acres and an enrollment of 485 pupils. She retired in 1981.
        Miss Chilton was a native of Brockton, Mass.
        She is survived by a sister, Jean Winchester, of New Canaan, Conn.
        A memorial will be held at 5 P.M. on Jan. 20 at the Elizabeth Morrow School.
