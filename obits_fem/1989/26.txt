        Dr. Marion McIlveen, a pediatrician and clinical professor of pediatrics, died of heart failure Saturday at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, N.J. She was 78 years old and lived in Ridgewood.
        Dr. McIlveen, a 1938 graduate of the Medical College of Pennsylvania, worked at New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center until her retirement in 1973 as an assistant clinical professor of pediatrics. She also practiced pediatrics in Ridgewood for more than 32 years and was the first head of the pediatrics department at Valley Hospital.
        There are no immediate survivors.
