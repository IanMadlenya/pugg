        Blanche Schwartz, a teacher and public school principal in Manhattan for 46 years, died of a heart attack Monday at the Cabrini Medical Center in Manhattan. She was 68 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Ms. Schwartz retired in February as the principal at Intermediate School 70 in Manhattan. She was a vice president of both the Council of Supervisors and Administrators and the American Federation of School Administrators.
        She is survived by a sister, Gladys Singer, of the Bronx.
