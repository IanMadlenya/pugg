        Justine Van Rensselaer Townsend Friberg, a member of one of New York's oldest families, died March 19 in Cottonwood, Ariz., where whe resided. She was 93 years old.
        Born in New York, where she participated in amateur theatricals in her youth, Mrs. Friberg was educated in New York and Paris. She settled in Arizona in 1936.
        Two of her great-great-great grandfathers, Gen. Philip Schuyler and Rufus King, were New York State's first United States Senators, seated in the first Congress in 1789.
        Her closest survivors are several nieces and nephews.
