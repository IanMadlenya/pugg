        Irene Mattone, a teacher and president of the Queens division of the American Cancer Society, died of breast cancer Tuesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. She was 56 years old and lived in Douglaston, Queens.
        Mrs. Mattone taught for nine years in the Island Park, L.I., schools.
        She was born in Brooklyn and received a bachelor's degree from St. John's University and a master's degree from New York University, Surviving are her husband, Joseph; four daughters, Julie Bellow, Irene LoBosco and Francesca and Teresa Mattone, and three sons, Carl, Joseph Jr. and Michael, all of Douglaston; a brother, Henry Ficarra of New Hyde Park., L.I.; a sister, Rose Ficarra of Little Neck, Queens, and four grandchildren.
