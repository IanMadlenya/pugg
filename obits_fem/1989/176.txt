        Margaret A. Burt, a partner in Buck Associates of Manhattan, an actuarial firm, died of congestive heart failure Wednesday at her home in the Brooklyn Heights area. She was 98 years old.
        Miss Burt joined the late George B. Buck in 1916 to establish the firm now known as Buck Consultants. It created pension plans and had New York City as an early client. Miss Burt retired in 1960.
        She was born in Easthampton, Mass. Miss Burt graduated Phi Beta Kappa from Smith College in 1912.
        Surviving is a sister, Katherine B. Crocker of Hinsdale, Mass.
