        Sister Helen Madeline Ingraham, who in 1919 founded Emmanuel College, the first Roman Catholic college for women in New England, died Tuesday. She was 101 years old.
        Sister Ingraham was dean at Emmanuel for 31 years. After retiring in 1950, she founded another college for her religous order in Okayama, Japan. She also wrote a series of books on meditation and translations from the French of Abbe Gaston Courtois.
