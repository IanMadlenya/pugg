        Mena W. Rosenthal, a member of the board of governors of the American Jewish Committee, died of liver cancer Friday at her home in Manhattan. She was 84 years old.
        Surviving are a daughter, Mary Lefkowitz of Wellesley, Mass; two sons, Peter, of South Londonderry, Vt., and John, of Manhattan; a sister, Janice W. Byfield of Manhattan; eight grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
