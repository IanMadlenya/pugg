        Cecilia Lester Sarasohn, a former deputy director of guidance for the New York City Board of Education who directed college preparatory programs, died of complications from leukemia on Wednesday at her home in Manhattan. She was 79 years old.
        Mrs. Sarasohn, who was born in Manhattan, was a graduate of New York University and held a master's degree from Columbia. She served as a guidance counselor at, among others, Haaren High School, Gompers High School and Wadleigh High School, which is now a junior high school.
        She is survived by a daughter, Ruth Reinhold of Tenafly, N.J.; a son, Albert, of Mount Kisco, N.Y.; a sister, Sylvia Robinson of Manhattan, and three grandchildren.
