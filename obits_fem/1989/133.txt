        Caroline Taylor White, a former president of the New York City Y.W.C.A., died yesterday at Cathedral Village, a retirement home in Philadelphia. She was 95 years old.
        Mrs. White, a native of Wilmette, Ill., was associated with the Y.W.C.A. in the 1940's. She was a former president of the Wellesley College Alumnae Association and a 1915 graduate of the college.
        Her husband, Dr. William Crawford White, died in 1962.
        She is survived by a son, Norval, of Brooklyn; a daughter, Mary Fearey of Chestnut Hill, Pa.; 10 grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
