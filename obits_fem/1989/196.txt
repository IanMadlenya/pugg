        Sister Evelyn McLaughlin, principal of St. Barnabas High School in the Bronx for the last 18 years, died of cardiac arrest on Tuesday at a Sisters of Charity residence in Ardsley, N.Y., where she lived. She was 63 years old.
        She was born in the Bronx and was an alumna of St. Barnabas. After taking her vows, she taught mathematics for several years before becoming principal.
        She is survived by a sister, Helen, of Manhattan.
