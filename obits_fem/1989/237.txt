
       Edythe Gilfond, a costume designer for dance companies, theater and television, died yesterday at her home in Southampton, L.I., after a long illness. She was 79 years old. Mrs. Gilfond, a native of Brooklyn, designed costumes for Martha Graham's dance company - including those for the world premiere of ''Serpent Heart'' (1946) and ''Deaths and Entrances'' (1953) - and for several television series, including ''As the World Turns'' and ''Edge of Night.

        She is survived by her husband, Henry; a daughter, Pamela, of Manhattan; a son, Michael, of Paris, and three grandchildren.
