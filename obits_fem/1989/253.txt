        Jan Goren Zuckerman, retired head of the audio-visual division of the City College of the City University of New York, died of cancer Monday at Memorial-Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. She was 67 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She was a founder of the CUNY film consortium and was associated with City College for 21 years.
        She is survived by her husband Rabbi Arthur J. Zuckerman; a daughter, Carla Friedman of Toms River, N.J.; a son, Stephen Crohn of Manhattan; a sister, Betty Goren of Washington; a brother, David Goren of Philadelphia; three stepchildren, and seven grandchildren.
