        Anne M. Murphy, director of libraries at Fordham University, died of cancer yesterday at the Westchester County Medical Center in Valhalla, N.Y. She was 62 years old and lived in Bronxville, N.Y.
        Miss Murphy joined Fordham in 1954 as an assistant librarian and was responsible for the circulation department, reserve desk, reference room and four science departmental libraries. She was named associate librarian in 1956 and was appointed director in 1970. Miss Murphy was responsible for the five major university libraries. In her tenure, the collections doubled.
        Previously, she had worked for the New York Public Library.
        Miss Murphy was an alumna of Mount St. Vincent College and earned a library science degree from Pratt Institute in 1950.
        Surviving are two brothers, John, of Bronxville, and Timothy, of Ridgewood, N.J.
