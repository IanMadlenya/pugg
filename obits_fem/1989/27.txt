        Elizabeth Chapman Little, chairwoman of the board of the Bruce Museum in Greenwich, Conn., from 1983 until last year, died of cancer on Saturday at Greenwich Hospital. She was 77 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        Mrs. Little was coordinator of the woman's program for the International Executive Service Corps and was director of the Interfaith Hunger Appeal. She was in the United Nations Association and the League of Women Voters for more than 20 years.
        She is survived by a daughter, Sandra L. Clark of Manhattan and Greenwich, and a grandson.
