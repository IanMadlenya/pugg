        Helen Robinson, a world-class angler who held several competitive fishing titles, died here Monday of complications from a fall. She was 89 years old. Mrs. Robinson set women's world records, among them the 1961 capture of a 796-pound black marlin and a 584-pound black marlin in 1962, both at Pinas Bay, Panama.
        Mrs. Robinson, a native of Colorado, moved to South Florida with her husband, Webster (Doc) Robinson, for health reasons.
