        Elizabeth Henry, a founder of the Meals on Wheels program in this country, died Sunday of pneumonia. She was 86 years old.
        In 1958 Ms. Henry and two other members of the Visiting Nurses Association started the program in Rochester, N.Y., after they had seen its success in England.
        Ms. Henry, a past president of the National Dietetic Association, retired from the Visiting Nurses Association as a nutritionist in 1968.
