        Nancy M. Yedlin, the secretary of the Fashion Institute of Technology and director of its college relations, died of cancer Tuesday at her home in Princeton, N.J. She was 55 years old.
        Mrs. Yedlin worked at the institute for 33 years. She was born in Manhattan and graduated from Michigan State University.
        Surviving are her husband, Benedict; two stepdaughters, Jane Yedlin of Providence, R.I., and Nancy Yedlin of New Haven; a stepson, Charles, of Princeton, and her parents, Jan and William McCartin of Manhattan.
