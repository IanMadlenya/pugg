        Daisy Fuller Young, mother of Mayor Andrew Young, died Friday night. She was 87 years old.
        A statement from the Mayor's office said Mrs. Young died of natural causes.
        Mrs. Young had recently moved from New Orleans, where she had been a schoolteacher, and led a fund-raising drive for Atlanta programs for the elderly.
        Mrs. Young was a graduate of Straight College, which later merged with New Orleans University to become Dillard University. She was a Dillard trustee.
        In addition to Mayor Young, survivors include another son, Dr. Walter Young, eight grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
