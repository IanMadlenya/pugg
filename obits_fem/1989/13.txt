        Sister Ann Christine Heintz, a Roman Catholic nun who founded a national student-written news service, died of cancer here Friday. She was 58 years old.
        Sister Ann Christine, a member of the Sisters of Charity of the Blessed Virgin Mary, was a teacher of journalism at high schools in Iowa and Chicago.
        In 1976 she founded Youth Communication, which offers a news service to 500 high school newspapers and commercial publications and broadcasters. The organization also publishes New Expression, a newspaper for high school students in the Chicago area that became Chicago's fourth-largest newspaper in circulation.
