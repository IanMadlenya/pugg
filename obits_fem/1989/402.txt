        Catherine M. Kelly, an executive secretary at The New York Times from 1953 to 1970, died of a stroke Sunday at Roosevelt Hospital in Manhattan. She was 66 years old and lived in Cliffside Park, N.J.
        Miss Kelly joined The Times in 1948 and from 1949 through 1953 worked in the newspaper's United Nations Bureau.
        She is survived by a niece, Virginia Kelly, and a nephew, Timothy Kelly, both of Cliffside Park.
