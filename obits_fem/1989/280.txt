        Margaret B. Parkinson, a book and magazine editor, died of pancreatic cancer Saturday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan. She was 62 years old and had homes in Manhattan and East Quogue, L.I.
        Miss Parkinson had been an editor at The Reader's Digest, Charm and Mademoiselle, a senior editor at Stein & Day, the managing editor of Parents Magazine, the editor in chief of Mason Charter Publishers and the executive editor of the Bobbs-Merrill Publishing Company. She was a graduate of the University of Colorado.
        Survivors include a brother, Lucius, of Danville, Calif; three nieces and a nephew.
