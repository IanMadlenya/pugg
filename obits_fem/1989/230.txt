        Mary Hunter Wallace, a former vice president of Ogilvy & Mather, a New York-based advertising concern, died Sunday at Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital after a long illness. She was 61 years old and lived in New Canaan, Conn.
        Mrs. Wallace, who retired in 1982, had worked for Erwin Wasey, N. W. Ayer and J. Walter Thompson before joining Ogilvy & Mather.
        She is survived by her husband, Robert; two children, Sarah M. and David, both of New Canaan, and two sisters, Eleanor M. Crowe of Granby, Conn., and Georgiana M. Baker of Cary, N.C.
