        Margaret Sidney Eaton Boyle, a retired reporter for The Reporter Dispatch in White Plains, died of heart failure Friday at St. Agnes Hospital in White Plains. She was 75 years old and lived in Katonah, N.Y.
        She worked for the paper from 1958 to 1973. Previously, she wrote for Collier's magazine. Born in Bridgeport, Conn., she graduated from Wellesley College. From 1973 to 1978 she was executive director of the Westchester Easter Seals Society.
        Surviving are two sons, William and Charles, both of Somerville, Mass.
