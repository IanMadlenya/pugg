        Mathilda Knecht, a retired professor of linguistics at Montclair State College, died of cancer Monday at her home in Rahway, N.J. She was 76 years old.
        Mrs. Knecht joined the staff of Montclair State after teaching in the Rahway public school system. She was chairwoman of the linguistics department at the college from 1969 to 1975. Linguistics was accepted as a major area of study during that time. She retired in 1976.
        She born in Manhattan and graduated from Montclair State, summa cum laude, in 1934, A widow, she is survived by a son, Thomas Knecht of Houston.
