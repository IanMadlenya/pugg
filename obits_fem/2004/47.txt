
        A memorial service for the actress and teacher Uta Hagen will be held on Thursday at 1 p.m. at the Majestic Theater, 247 West 44th Street, Manhattan. She died on Jan. 14 at 84.
        The service is open to the public and will include readings and reminiscences by friends and colleagues including Edward Albee, Mia Farrow, David Hyde Pierce, Lindsay Crouse and Austin Pendleton.
