
        Charlotte O'Keefe Pepe, head of Chemicolloid Laboratories of Garden City Park, L.I., died on Thursday while attending a business meeting in Paris. She was 83 and lived in Garden City.
        Her family said the cause was a heart attack.
        Chemicolloid Laboratories was founded by Mrs. Pepe with her first husband, David O'Keefe, who died before her. The company exports equipment for industrial and food processing.
        Mrs. Pepe is survived by her second husband, Anthony C. Pepe.
