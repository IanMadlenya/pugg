        Ruth Newman Schwarz, an early psychiatric social worker, died of cancer Dec. 28 at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was 81 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Schwarz was associated with the Community Service Society from 1948 to 1973, and was director of its West Side office for 12 years.
        A native of St. Louis, she held bachelor's and master's degrees from Washington University. In 1931, she founded an experimental nursery school in St. Louis, where she helped begin the use of play therapy.
        Mrs. Schwarz's husband, Leo, predeceased her.
