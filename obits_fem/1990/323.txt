        Dr. Vivian Allison Tenney, an internist and a gynecologist in Manhattan for 45 years, died on Monday at Rockingham Memorial Hospital in Harrisonburg, Va. She was 77 years old and lived in Bridgewater, Va.
        She died of a ruptured aneurysm, said her sister and only survivor, Gena Tenney Phenix of Manhattan.
        Dr. Tenney began her practice in Manhattan after her graduation from Cornell Medical College in 1939 and after completing her training at the Massachusetts General Hospital and Philadelphia Hospital for Women. She also served as a medical missionary in Tennessee while in training.
        For 36 years she was affiliated with Memorial Hospital, now part of Memorial-Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. She was also affiliated with Beekman Downtown Hospital, now New York Infirmary-Beekman Downtown Hospital.
