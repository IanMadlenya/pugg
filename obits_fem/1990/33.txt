
       Claire McGlinchee, emeritus professor of English at Hunter College, died on Tuesday at her home in Manhattan. She was 91 years old.  Dr. McGlinchee, a graduate of Radcliffe College and a Shakespearean scholar, came to the college as an assistant instructor of English in 1924 and retired in 1969.
