
        Anne Kallem, an editor at the Sterling Publishing Company in Manhattan for 26 years, died on Sunday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Medical Center. She was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She died of lung cancer, David Boehm, chairman of the publishing company, said.
        At her death, she was managing editor emeritus of the company, which publishes nonfiction and reference books. She was born in the Bronx and graduated from Barnard College.
        Her huband, Henry Kallem, an artist, died in 1985. She is survived by a son, Matthew Kallem of Rockport, Me.
