
        Rosalind Solon Buchman, an accounting professor at New York City Technical College, died on Wednesday while visiting friends in Washington. She was 60 years old and lived in Brooklyn Heights.
        Tom Carroll, dean of human resources and labor relations at the college, said that she died in her sleep.
        She graduated from Hunter College and received a master's degree from New York University. She also studied at Columbia Teachers College.
        She is survived by her husband, George; a daughter, Deborah, and a son, Barry, both of Brooklyn Heights, and a sister, Babette Hollister of Queens.
