        Helen Finn Ruder, a specialist in child psychotherapy in Westchester County, died on Sunday at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital. She was 64 years old and lived in Larchmont.
        A spokeswoman for the family said Mrs. Ruder died of cancer.
        Mrs. Ruder was affiliated with the center for Preventive Psychiatry in White Plains for 20 years. She was also on the board of directors of U.S./Israel Women to Women, the Harrison (N.Y.) Children's Center and Blythedale Children's Hospital in Westchester County.
        She is survived by three daughters, Robin Halpren-Ruder of Providence, R.I., Abby Ruder of Philadelphia and Michal Ellen Ruder of Houston; two sons, Brian, of Pittsburgh, and Eric, of Princeton, N.J., and five grandchildren.
