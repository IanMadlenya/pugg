
        Mila P. Conanan, the director of operating rooms at the Allen Pavilion of the Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center, died on Thursday at Holy Name Hospital in Teaneck, N.J. She was 43 years old and lived in New Milford, N.J.
        She died of a blood clot in the brain, a family spokesman said.
        Mrs. Conanan was a native of Manila, where she earned a Bachelor of Science degree in nursing at Concordia College. She had been on the medical center staff for 20 years and the operating rooms director at the Allen Pavilion for three years.
        Surviving are her husband, Virgil; two daughters, Denise and Rachel, both of New Milford; her parents, Miguel and Rosalinda Picazo of Manila; eight brothers, Frank, Jojo, Miguel Jr. and Rico Picazo of the Philippines, Eddie, Ferdie and Abel Picazo of New Milford and Ramon Picazo of San Francisco, and four sisters, Rica Picazo of New Milford, Rosalinda and Racquel Picazo of the Philippines and Margot Fazeli of Australia.
