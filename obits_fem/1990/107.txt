        Lorraine Lester, a producer who worked in theater, films and television, died after a heart attack on Wednesday in the Coronary Care Unit of the New Rochelle Medical Center. She was 88 years old and lived in New Rochelle, N.Y.
        Ms. Lester, who had worked in Hollywood at the Fox Studios in the 1930's and early 1940's, operated her own studios out of the Henry Hudson Hotel on West 57th Street until 1967. Her principal product was television commercials.
        Surviving are her husband, George Lessner; two sons, Alford, of Newburgh, N.Y., and Robert, of Wappingers Falls, N.Y.; five grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
