        Helen Markel, a magazine writer and editor whose articles appeared in The New York Times Magazine and The Readers Digest, among other publications, died of cancer of the spine on Sunday at her home in Manhattan. She was 71 years old.
        Ms. Markel was an editor of The Ladies Home Journal in the 60's and of McCall's in the 70's. She specialized in character portraits of prominent figures in the arts and literature.
        Ms. Markel was the daughter of Lester Markel, Sunday Editor of The New York Times for several decades. She was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of Smith College.
        Surviving are a daughter, Jennifer Stewart, and a son, Mark Stewart, both of Manhattan.
