        Ruth M. Shattuck, a former member of the Board of Selectmen for Darien, Conn., died Friday at her home in Darien. She was 69 years old.
        She died of cancer, her husband, H. Francis Shattuck Jr., said.
        Mrs. Shattuck, a graduate of Smith College, was elected three times to the Board of Selectmen, serving from 1983 to 1989. She also headed the Republican Town Committee from 1980 to 1983 and was a former president of the Darien Library.
        Besides her husband, she is survived by a daughter, Christina Stenhuis-Shattuck of Rowayton, Conn.; a son, John, of Cambridge, Mass.; a brother, Samuel Murphy Jr. of Boca Grande, Fla., and three grandchildren.
