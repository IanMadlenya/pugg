        Sylvia Deutscher Kushner, a bassoonist who played with the New York City Opera and other orchestras, died on Monday at St. Patrick's Hospital in Lake Charles, La. She was 65 years old and lived in Lake Charles. She died of lung cancer, her sister, Martha Deutscher, said. A graduate of the Eastman School of Music in Rochester, Mrs. Kushner joined the New Orleans Symphony in 1947, and later played with the Sadler's Wells Ballet orchestra.
        She is survived by a daughter, Lesley Kushner of Manhattan; two sons, Tony Kushner, also of Manhattan, and Eric Kushner of Vienna, and two sisters, Martha Deutscher and Lucy Manoff, both of Manhattan.
