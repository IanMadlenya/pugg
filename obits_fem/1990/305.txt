        Florence King Weichsel, co-founder and former director of the Children's Studio in Rye, N.Y., a private art school for children that closed in 1972, died yesterday at a convalescent home in Albuquerque, N.M. She was 82 years old and had moved to Albuquerque from White Plains five months ago.
        Mrs. Weichsel died of cancer, her daughter, Judith Carr, said.
        She was associated with the Children's Studio for 23 years, from 1949 until its closing. In 1978 she became an art therapist with the White Plains Hospital. She retired in 1985.
        Mrs. Weichsel was born in Poughkeepsie, N.Y., and graduated from Smith College in 1928.
        A widow, she is survived by two children, a son, John, and her daughter, both of Albuquerque; two brothers, Sidney King of Poughkeepsie, and Daniel King of Harrison, N.Y., and a grandson.
