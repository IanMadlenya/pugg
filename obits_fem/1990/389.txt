
        Mildred Riorden Blake, a former New York advertising writer who was a founder of the World Federalists, died on Tuesday at a nursing home in Williamstown, Mass. She was 93 years old and lived in Williamstown.
        Mrs. Blake was active in civic and political circles, in particular the World Federalists, founded in 1938. She represented Friends of the Earth, an organization concerned with the environment, at the United Nations in the 1960's.
        She was a writer for Young & Rubicam Inc., a New York advertising agency, for 18 years.
        Her second husband, William Paul Blake, died in 1962.
        She is survived by her son from a former marriage, Shane Edwin Riorden of Williamstown; a brother, Stanton Mighell of Aurora, Ill.; three grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
