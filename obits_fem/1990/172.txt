        Jessica James, an actress, died of breast cancer on Monday at her home in Los Angeles. She was 60 years old.
        Ms. James appeared in many Broadway and Off Broadway shows, including ''Little Me,'' ''Gemini,'' ''Company'' and ''42d Street.''
        Her credits during a 20-year film career included ''Diner,'' ''Illegally Yours,'' ''Alien Nation,'' ''Power'' and ''Hook, Line and Sinker.'' She was also a singer and comedian, and she appeared in many television series, summer-stock productions, and cabaret and comedy shows in Las Vegas and New York.
        Ms. James is survived by a daughter, Bambi Sue Camerman of Flemington, N.J.; a son, Donald Burke of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
