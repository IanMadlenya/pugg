
        Sherry Goldman Abel, a retired managing editor of Commentary magazine, died on May 28 at Beth Israel Medical Center in Manhattan. She was 88 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She had suffered from a stroke and respiratory illness, friends said.
        Mrs. Abel was born in Chicago and graduated from the University of Chicago. She held a variety of editorial jobs, including compiling a Yiddish encyclopedia, editing a book on cinema and working at Time magazine.
        She joined Commentary about 1950 as an editorial assistant and eventually rose to managing editor. She retired in the 1960's.
        Her marriages to William McKenzie and Lionel Abel, a playwright and author, ended in divorce. Her daughter, Merry, died in 1964.
        Mrs. Abel is survived by a brother, Jack Goldman of Chicago.
