        Dr. Carol Neilson Wilder, an adjunct associate professor of speech pathology at Teachers College of Columbia University, died of colon cancer Thursday at her home in Pound Ridge, N.Y. She was 58 years old. She sang professionally with the Robert Shaw Chorale and the American Bach Society. She was a board member of the Voice Foundation and associate editor of The Journal of Voice.
        Surviving are her husband, David; two sons, Andrew, of Manhattan, and Matthew, of Pound Ridge, and her mother, Vera Neilson, of Pound Ridge.
