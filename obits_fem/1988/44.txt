        Gabrielle Kaufman Koppell, former owner and director of the Riverdale School for Young Children and the mother of Assemblyman G. Oliver Koppell, died of heart failure Sunday at her son's home in the Riverdale section of the Bronx. She was 87 years old.
        Mrs. Koppell fled Germany in the 1930's, chaperoning a group of children and then helping them to find homes in the United States.
        In addition to her son, she is survived by a daughter, Olivia, of Manhattan; two sisters, Anny Hochschild of Chile, and Elsa Drucker of Riverdale, and three grandchildren.
