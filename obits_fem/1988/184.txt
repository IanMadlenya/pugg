        Clara Woolie Mayer, an educator associated with the New School for Social Research for nearly 30 years, died of pneumonia Saturday at a nursing home in Los Angeles. She was 93 years old and lived in Los Angeles.
        Ms. Mayer was the New School's vice president from 1950 to 1962. Earlier, she had been assistant director and associate director of the school. She was dean of the School of Philosophy and Liberal Arts at the institution from 1943 to 1960.
        There are no immediate survivors.
