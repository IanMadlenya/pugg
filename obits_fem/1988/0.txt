        Patricia Easterbrook Roberts, a landscape designer and author, died of cancer Sunday at Danbury (Conn.) Hospital. She was 77 years old and lived in Southbury, Conn.
        She is survived by two daughters, Eleanor Roberts Lewis of Washington and Diane Raymond Kolinsky of South Glastonbury, Conn.; a sister, Olive M. Reid of Southbury, and two grandsons.
