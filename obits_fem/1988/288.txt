        Josephine Bliss, retired director of the Weekday School at Riverside Church in Manhattan, died of cancer Nov. 2 at the home of a nephew in South Portland, Me. She was 77 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Born in Buffalo, Miss Bliss graduated from the National College of Education and earned a master's degree from Union Theological Seminary. She joined Riverside in 1955 and retired in 1976. She then supervised teachers in training at City College.
        Surviving is a sister, Frances Crosby of Carmel, Calif.
