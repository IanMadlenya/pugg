        Dr. Esther Oshiver Fisher, a retired marriage counselor, died of complications from Alzheimer's disease Feb. 13 at her home in Princeton, N.J. She was 77 years old.
        Dr. Fisher was director of training in marriage counseling at the Institutes of Religion and Health in the 1960's and 70's. She was founding editor of The Journal of Divorce.
        She is survived by her husband, Mitchell; two sons, Franklin, of Cambridge, Mass., and Wesley, of Princeton; a daughter, Joanne Peterson of Woodbridge, Conn.; a brother, Joseph Oshiver of Kansas City, Mo., and eight grandchildren.
