        Helen Biren, professor emeritus of geology at Hunter College and Brooklyn College, died of a stroke yesterday at Peninsula Hospital in Far Rockaway, Queens. She was 81 years old and lived in Belle Harbor, Queens.
        She was born in Brooklyn, graduated from Hunter College and received a doctorate from Columbia University. In 1928, she founded the geology department at the Hunter-City College extension in downtown Brooklyn, which later became Brooklyn College. She retired in 1975. Survivors include her husband, William; a daughter, Diane, and a sister, Eunice Simon, of Los Angeles.
