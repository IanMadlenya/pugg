        Mary Lee Fairbanks, a sponsor of charity benefits and the wife of the movie actor Douglas Fairbanks Jr., died of cancer yesterday at Good Samaritan Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla. She was 75 years old and lived in Palm Beach.
        Mrs. Fairbanks lived in Manhattan for many years and was a sponsor of charity balls to help sick and needy children around the world.
        Besides her husband, she is survived by three daughters, Melissa Morant of London, Daphne Fairbanks-Kay of Colchester, England, and Victoria Fairbanks of Tappan, N.Y., and eight grandchildren.
