        Marilyn R. Menge, vice president of the New York State Women's Bar Association, died of ovarian cancer Tuesday at the New York University Medical Center in Manhattan. She was 31 years old and lived in Albany.
        Born in New Hyde Park, L.I., Ms. Menge graduated from State University College at Oneonta and George Washington University Law School.
        Since 1982, she had worked for the state's Facilities Development Corporation, which builds and acquires buildings for state agencies.
        She is survived by her mother, Adelaide, of New Hyde Park, and a sister, Nancy Gordis of Ephrata, Pa.
