        Michael Gifford, a longtime press agent, died of a heart attack Saturday while driving to the East Hampton, L.I., airport to meet her husband, Edwin. She was 53 years old and lived on the Upper East Side of Manhattan and in East Hampton.
        Mrs. Gifford's agency, Gifford-Wallace Inc., represented the musical ''Hair,'' Westinghouse Broadcasting, Fox Television and Studio 54.
        In addition to her husband, Mrs. Gifford is survived by two daughters: Tierney Gifford, and Mary-Elizabeth Berliner of Santa Barbara, Calif.; a son, Edwin Jr., and a sister, Stephanie Stone of Fort Lauderdale, Fla.
