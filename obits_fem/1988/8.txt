        Katherine Guy Fenimore Cooper, a horticulturist and member of the boards of many charitable groups, died Friday at her home in Cooperstown, N.Y. She was 92 years old.
        Her garden was known for the rarity of many of its flowers. The charitable organizations on whose boards she served in New York City included the Grosvenor Neighborhood House and the Henry Street Settlement.
        She is survived by two sons, James, of Lafayette, La., and Henry Jr., of Manhattan; two daughters, Katherine Cary of Manhattan and Susan Weil of Mount Kisco, N.Y.; 15 grandchildren and five great-grandchildren.
