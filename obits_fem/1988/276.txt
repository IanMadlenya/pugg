
       Mozelle Wallace, mother of former Gov. George C. Wallace of Alabama, died at her home in Montgomery Thursday after an extended illness. She was 90 years old. Mrs. Wallace, a former county health worker, is survived by the former Governor and three other children, Gerald Wallace, Jack Wallace and Mary Ann Dauphin.
