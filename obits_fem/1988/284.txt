        Ruth Tendler, assistant director of the Lincoln Institute for Psychotherapy, died of lung cancer Wednesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. She was 62 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She is survived by her mother, Sadie Fink of the Bronx; a sister, Anita Berger of Providence, R.I.; a son, Matthew, of Manhattan; a daughter, Cindy, of Manhattan, and a granddaughter.
