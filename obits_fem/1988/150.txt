        Gladys Shultz Sanders, a public relations consultant and writer, died of cancer Sunday at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital. She was 69 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Sanders wrote books on fashion and magazine and newspaper articles. From 1970 to 1986, she worked for the public relations firm of Carl Byoir & Associates. Before that, she was a copywriter for Young & Rubicam.
        Mrs. Sanders was born in Manhattan. She graduated from the College of William and Mary.
        Her husband, Bernard, died in 1967. There are no survivors.
