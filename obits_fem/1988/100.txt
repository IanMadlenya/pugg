        Carol Collins Tofel, director of administration at the Ackerman Institute for Family Therapy in Manhattan, died yesterday after a long illness at her home in the Riverdale section of the Bronx. She was 54 years old.
        Mrs. Tofel, before joining the institute in 1980, was administrator of the Ethical Culture Schools.
        Mrs. Tofel, a graduate of Skidmore College, also served on the board of the Barnard School in New York from 1968 to 1977.
        She is survived by her husband, Robert; two sons, Richard and Lawrence, and a granddaughter, all of New York.
