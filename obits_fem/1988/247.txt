        Felice Jarecky Louria, long active in social issues in New York and California, died of congestive heart failure Monday at the Orange Park (Calif.) Nursing Home. She was 88 years old and lived in Santa Ana, Calif.
        Mrs. Louria worked on wage and migrant-worker issues in the late 1930's and later was the industrial-relations enforcement officer at the New York State Department of Labor.
        She is survived by a son, Dr. Henry, of Newport Beach, Calif.; two daughters, Margot, of Washington, and Dr. Ellin Lieberman of Los Angeles; five grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
