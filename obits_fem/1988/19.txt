        Mary Brosnan Kratschmer, a former designer and manufacturer of mannequins, died of cancer of the spine Wednesday at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was 81 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Kratschmer, who founded her own company, Mary Brosnan Inc., in 1940, won the Neiman-Marcus Fashion Award in recognition of her contributions to fashion.
        She retired in the mid-1970's, but continued as a consultant for D. G. Williams Inc., manufacturers of mannequins.
        She is survived by two nieces and five nephews.
