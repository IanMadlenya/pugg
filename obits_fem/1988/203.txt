        Elsa Mott Ives, a philanthropist and the daughter of a founder of General Motors Corporation, died yesterday at King Edward Hospital in Hamilton, Bermuda. She was 83 years old and lived in Manhattan and Southampton, Bermuda.
        Born in Flint, Mich., Mrs. Ives was a generous supporter of the Metropolitan Opera, the English-Speaking Union, Goodwill Industries and WNET/Channel 13, where she was the first woman board member.
        Surviving are two sisters, Aimee Mott Butler of St. Paul, Minn., and Maryanne Mott of Santa Barbara, Calif; two brothers, C.S. Harding Mott of Flint and Stewart Mott of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
