        Maureen Roach, a former teacher and guidance counselor, died of cancer Feb. 21 at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. She was 56 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        She taught in the New York City school system before becoming a member of the Presentation Sisters of Staten Island, where she worked as a teacher for the Roman Catholic Archdiocese of New York.
        At her death, she was director of Valley Lodge, a shelter for the elderly homeless on Manhattan's West Side.
        She is survived by a sister, Betty Ann Healey of Belmar, N.J.
