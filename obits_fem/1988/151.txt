        Monica Bergman, an art historian, died of cancer Wednesday at Mount Sinai Hospital in Manhattan. She was 40 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A graduate of Vassar College, Mrs. Bergman had a master's degree from Cambridge University. She was a Fulbright Scholar in 1975 and specialized in the art of India.
        She is survived by her husband, Nicholas; her mother and stepfather, Dale and Harold Kaufman of Manhattan, and her father, Eric Lawyer of Atlantic Beach, L.I.
