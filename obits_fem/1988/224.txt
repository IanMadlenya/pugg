        Erika Mumford, a prize-winning poet, died of breast cancer Saturday. She was 53 years old.
        Mrs. Mumford wrote three collections of poetry - ''The Door in the Forest,'' ''Willow Water'' and ''The Karma Bazaar.''
        She was born in Geneva and spent her childhood in Germany, coming to the United States in 1946. She was a Radcliffe graduate and had a doctorate in Celtic languages and literature from Harvard.
        She is survived by her husband, four children and two brothers.
