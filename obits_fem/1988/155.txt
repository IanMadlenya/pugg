        Catherine Reynolds Fennelly, an actress and the widow of the actor and playwright Parker W. Fennelly, died of heart failure last Friday at her home in Cortlandt, N.Y. She was 95 years old.
        Mrs. Fennelly performed in vaudeville and Chautauqua shows as well as in radio, regional theater and Broadway productions from 1910 to the 1940's. She met her husband in a theatrical stock company in 1918.
        Mrs. Fennelly is survived by two daughters, Jane R. Fennelly of Quaker Hill, Conn., and Mary Michaelsen of Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y.; four grandsons, and a great-grandson.
