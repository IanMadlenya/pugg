        Dorothy Cimberg Finkelhor, founder and first president of Point Park College in Pittsburgh, died Tuesday at her home in Ocean Ridge, Fla., the college announced. She was 86 years old.
        Mrs. Finkelhor and her husband, L. Herbert Finkelhor, founded the Business Training College in downtown Pittsburgh in 1933. It eventually became the four-year Point Park College. Mrs. Finkelhor, a native of New York City, was president of the college until 1971.
        She is survived by her husband, three children and six grandchildren.
