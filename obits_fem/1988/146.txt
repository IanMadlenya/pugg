        Florence Salomone, vice president and general manager of The Rockland Journal-News in West Nyack, N.Y., since 1982, died of a heart attack yesterday at Montefiore Medical Center in the Bronx. She was 43 years old and lived in Mount Vernon, N.Y.
        Ms. Salomone began her career as a reporter with the Westchester-Rockland chain of newspapers in 1966 after graduating from New York University. She held various positions at newspapers in the chain and began Westchester Weekend, a tabloid listing of weekend events.
        She is survived by a sister, Leticia Salomone of Mount Vernon.
