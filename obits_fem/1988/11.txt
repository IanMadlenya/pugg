        Dr. Clara Mae Taylor-Olson, professor emeritus of nutrition at Teachers College, Columbia University, and a leading figure in the field of nutrition education, died after a long illness Jan. 6 in a Pittsburgh hospital. Dr. Taylor-Olson was 89 years old and lived in Pittsburgh.
        A native of Vineland, N.J., Dr. Taylor-Olson co-wrote the 1944 and 1956 revisions of ''Foundations of Nutrition'' by Dr. Mary Swartz Rose, one of the leading textbooks in the field.
        She is survived by a brother, Louis R. Taylor, of Bethlehem, Pa.
