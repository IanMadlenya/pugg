        Frances Healy Geyelin, a former fashion editor and consultant, died of cardiac arrest yesterday at New York Hospital. She was 69 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mrs. Geyelin was fashion director of Franklin Simon, a now defunct department store, and a fashion editor at Mademoiselle magazine. In 1951, she joined the advertising firm of Young & Rubicam, and in 1969 she established her own company, Fran Healy Associates. She retired in 1973.
        Mrs. Geyelin is survived by her husband, Henry; a daughter, Antoinette Hoar of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
