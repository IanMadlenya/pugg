        Susan Steele Brown, who did volunteer work for the Junior League of Bronxville, N.Y., for more than 25 years and was the wife of Robert U. Brown, president and editor of Editor & Publisher magazine, died of cardiac arrest May 2 at the couple's Manhattan apartment. She was 68 years old and also lived in Greenwich, Conn.
        She attended the Academy of Mount Saint Vincent, Dana Hall and the Finch School.
        Also surviving are two daughters, Robin Woods of Darien, Conn., and Elizabeth Phillips of Houston; a brother, Edward Steele of North Palm Beach, Fla.; a sister, Libby Rathbun of Palm Beach, Fla., and five grandchildren.
