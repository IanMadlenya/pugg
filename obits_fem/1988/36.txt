        Laura Louise Foster, a botanical artist, died last Sunday at Sharon (Conn.) Hospital after a brief illness. She was 70 years old and lived in Falls Village, Conn.
        Many of Mrs. Foster's depictions of flowers are in the Hunt Botanical Library in Pittsburgh. With her husband, she received the Silver Medal of the Massachusetts Horticultural Society.
        She is survived by her husband, H. Lincoln; two sons, Ellery Sinclair and John Sheldon Sinclair, both of Falls Village, and two stepchildren, Benjamin Griswald Foster of Wallingford, Conn., and H. Rebecca Light of Guilford, Conn.
