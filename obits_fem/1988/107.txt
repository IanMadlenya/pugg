        Dorothy Cooper Knowles, a former arts educator with the Museum of Modern Art and City College, died at her home in Manhattan on Monday. She was 76 years old.
        Miss Knowles worked for the Museum of Modern Art for more than 30 years, serving as assistant to the director of education. She also was secretary to the National Committee on Art and Education. In 1970, she became curator of the slide collection in the Department of Fine Arts at City College.
        Miss Knowles is survived by a sister, Patricia K. Novak of Boston.
