        Agatha Singer, a retired New York City physician who for many years worked in child-health care in the city's Health Department, died Sunday at New York Hospital. She was 76 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Singer also worked as an administrator at the Goldwater Memorial Hospital and, most recently, as a general-practice physician with the Strang Clinic-Preventive Medicine Institute. She retired in 1981.  She is survived by her husband, Andrew; a daughter, Paulette Barrett of Manhattan, and three grandsons.
