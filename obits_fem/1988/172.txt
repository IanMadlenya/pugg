        Edna Boyne Campbell, known to generations of Morningside Heights residents through her association with the Morningside Junior Library, died of cancer Monday at her home in Manhattan. She was 80 years old.
        Mrs. Campbell was a co-founder in the 1950's of the private, nonprofit library. She served the library as a volunteer for more than 30 years.
        She was the widow of Kenneth A. Campbell, a foreign correspondent for The New York Times, who died in 1979.
        Mrs. Campbell is survived by a daughter, Elliott C. Arensmeyer and two grandchildren, all of Manhattan.
