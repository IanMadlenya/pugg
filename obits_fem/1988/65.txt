        Martha Love McCagg Snow, an architect, died of colon and stomach cancer Wednesday at her daughter's home in Edison, N.J. She was 83 years old and lived in Bronxville, N.Y.
        Mrs. Snow designed hospitals in Brooklyn, Georgia and North Carolina, as well as houses in the Northeast.
        She is survived by her husband, Richard; a daughter, Dr. Caroline O. McCagg of Edison; a son, Edward K. McCagg of Seattle; two sisters, Dorothy Love Saunders of Bryn Mawr, Pa., and Mary Love Glenn of Cold Spring Harbor, L.I., and three grandchildren.
