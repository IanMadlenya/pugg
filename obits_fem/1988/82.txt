        Pearl E. Joseph, a senior vice president at Young & Rubicam Inc., died of a heart attack Sunday at her home in Carmel, N.Y. She was 47 years old.
        She was born in Brooklyn and graduated from New York University. She became senior vice president of the advertising agency in 1985.
        She is survived by her husband, Richard.
