        Gertrude Cowan, a newspaper editor and illustrator who was known professionally as Trudi Cowan, died of cancer yesterday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. She was 63 years old and lived in Merrick, L.I.
        Mrs. Cowan had been editor of Bellmore Life, a weekly newspaper, since 1964. She worked from 1943 to 1950 at The New York Times, where she was among the first copy girls.
        She was the widow of George Cowan, who was managing art director of The Times.
        Surviving are a daughter, Jill, and a son, Stephen, both of Manhattan; her mother, Ida Berger, and a sister, Jean Chimsky, both of Cincinnati, and two grandchildren.
