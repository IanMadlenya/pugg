        Ida Erickson Hughes, a retired elementary school teacher and a charter member of the Larchmont (N.Y.) Shore Club, died of liver cancer Wednesday at the Manor Care Nursing Home in Tucson, Ariz. She was 84 years old and formerly lived in Larchmont.
        She is survived by a daughter, Jane Hughes Bergler of Danville, Calif.; a son, Dr. John, of Tucson; a sister, Lempi Pagnucco of Cincinnati, and four grandchildren.
