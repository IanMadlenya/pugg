        Laura Chapman, a former dean of the School of Nursing at Rutgers University, died of leukemia Jan. 1, at Princeton (N.J.) Medical Center. She was 78 years old and lived in Piscataway, N.J.
        Miss Chapman, a native of Wayne, Neb., was dean from 1964 to 1973 and taught nursing until her retirement in 1975.
        Earlier in her career, she was the founding dean of the College of Nursing at Brigham Young University in Provo, Utah, and was assistant professor of nursing at the University of Illinois in Chicago.
        She is survived by a nephew, Duane D. Chapman of Somis, Calif.
