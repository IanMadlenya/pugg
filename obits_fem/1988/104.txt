        Mara Lynn Conrad, a dancer in Broadway musicals, movies and television, died of liver cancer on April 6 in Freeport, the Bahamas. She was 59 years old.
        Mrs. Conrad's career as a dancer spanned nearly 40 years, including television appearances on the Ed Sullivan, Groucho Marx and Milton Berle shows during the 1950's and 1960's. She performed in ''Stop the World,'' ''New Girl in Town'' and about two dozen other Broadway musicals, road shows and revivals. Her film credits included roles in ''Let's Make Love'' and ''Last Train From Gun Hill.''
        Mrs. Conrad is survived by her husband, Harold, and a son, Casey, both of Manhattan, and a brother, Robert Mosier, of Sebring, Fla.
