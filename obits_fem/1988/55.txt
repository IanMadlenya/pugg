        Mildred Evans Williams, who practiced psychiatry for more than 30 years in Manhattan, died Saturday at the Candlewood Valley Nursing Home in New Milford, Conn. She was 81 years old.
        Working under the name B. Mildred Evans, she was a teaching analyst for the New York Psychoanalytic Institute, until her retirement in 1973.
        Mrs. Williams is survived by a son, Alexander, of Chicago; a daughter, Elizabeth Fox of Philadelphia; a brother, Charles Evans of Jacksonville, Fla., and six grandchildren.
