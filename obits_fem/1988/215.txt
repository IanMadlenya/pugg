        Rosalind M. Schwenk, a longtime power in Republican politics in Suffolk County, died Monday in Southampton Hospital on Long Island after suffering a stroke Sunday. She was 92 years old and lived in Southampton.
        Mrs. Schwenk was vice-chairwoman of the Suffolk Republican Committee from 1949 to 1967 and a member of the party's state committee from 1958 to 1970.
        She is survived by her son, Edwin, of Southampton, a former chairman of the Suffolk Republican Committee; two daughters, Audrey Aldridge and Beverly Flanagan, both of Delray Beach, Fla.; 8 grandchildren, and 10 great-grandchildren.
