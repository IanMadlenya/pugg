        Ella Royston Udall, the wife of Representative Morris Udall, Democrat of Arizona, was found dead in her garage Saturday, an apparent suicide. She was 59 years old.
        Mr. Udall found his wife's body about 10 A.M. in the front seat of her car, said Warren Carmichael, a spokesman for the Fairfax County Police.
        ''It's being investigated as a suicide from carbon monoxide poisoning pending a test by the medical examiner,'' Mr. Carmichael said. A spokesman for Mr. Udall, Robert Neumann, said no suicide note was found.
