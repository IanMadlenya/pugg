        Dr. Naomi M. Kanof, who edited The Journal of Investigative Dermatology for 50 years, died at George Washington University Hospital here Sunday after a heart attack. She was 76 years old.
        Dr. Kanof, who was born in Brooklyn and was a former head of the department of dermatology at the Children's Hospital National Medical Center, also taught dermatology at Georgetown and Howard universities' medical schools.
        She is survived a son, Robert Kanof Tendler of Boston, a daughter, Rebecca Jane Tendler of Rosemont, Pa., and a grandson. Her husband, Max Tendler, died in 1974.
