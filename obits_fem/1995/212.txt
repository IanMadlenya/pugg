
        Susie C. Bradley, the mother of Senator Bill Bradley, Democrat of New Jersey, died last Thursday in Palm Beach, Fla., where she had made her home since the early 1970's. Mrs. Bradley was 86.
        Her funeral was held yesterday at Grace Presbyterian Church in Crystal City,  Mo., where the senator grew up. Mrs. Bradley was active in the church, in Crystal City and in Palm Beach.
        Her husband, Warren, died in 1994. In addition to her son, who lives in Montclair, N.J., Mrs. Bradley is survived by a sister, Hardeman Bond of Crystal  City, and a granddaughter.
