        Bernice Latrobe Maguire Smith, wife of Gerard C. Smith, who led the United States delegation from 1969 to 1972 in the negotiations on limiting strategic arms, died of cancer at her home yesterday. She was 74 years old.
        After graduating from Smith College in 1934, and before her marriage to Mr. Smith, she worked for Time magazine and as director of public relations for Lord & Taylor.
        Besides her husband, Mrs. Smith is survived by a daughter, Mrs. Richard B. Griffin Jr. of Washington; three sons, John T. Smith 2d of Washington, Gerard L. Smith of New York City and Hugh M. Smith of Easton, Md.; three sisters, Mrs. Frederick H. Robinson of Fredericksburg, Va., Mrs. Elizabeth J. Maguire of Washington and Mrs. Jay Stetzer of Philadelphia, and by nine grandchildren.
        A memorial service will be held at St. Matthew's Cathedral on Friday at 11 A.M.
