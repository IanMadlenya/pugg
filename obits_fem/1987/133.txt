        Claude Hill, associate principal harpist with the Metropolitan Opera orchestra for the last 25 years, died of cancer Friday at Lenox Hill Hospital. She was 53 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Miss Hill was born in Toronto and grew up in Montreal. She was a graduate of the Montreal Conservatory of Music and the New England Conservatory of Music, and a former member of the Montreal Symphony Orchestra.
        She is survived by her mother, Delvia Hill, of Montreal.
