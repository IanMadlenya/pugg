        Elizabeth Harper Landon Gurlitz, a magazine editor for four decades, died last Friday at the Mediplex Home in Westport, Conn., where she had lived several years. She was 96 years old.
        Miss Gurlitz, a descendant of James Harper, Mayor of New York in 1844-45, and Joseph Wesley Harper, a founder of the Harper Brothers publishing house, worked at several concerns, including Street & Smith and MacFadden Publications.
        Surviving are three nephews.
