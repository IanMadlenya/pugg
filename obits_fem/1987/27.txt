        Anita Brielke Stafford, senior vice president of Hermes of Paris Inc., the North American subsidiary of the Paris-based fashion boutique, was killed Friday in a car accident near Newtown, Conn. She was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan and in Bethlehem, Conn.
        Mrs. Stafford, who was born in Forst, Germany, was named senior vice president and general merchandise manager of Hermes for the United States and Canada in 1984.
        She is survived by her husband, Joseph.
