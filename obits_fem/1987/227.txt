        Mary F. Corkey, the first woman to be president of the Brooklyn Board of Realtors, died Thursday at Brooklyn Hospital. She was 74 years old and lived in the Flatbush section.
        Mrs. Corkey was past president of the Flatbush Real Estate Board, former regional vice president of the Women's Council of Realtors and president of the Brooklyn chapter of the State Board of Realtors.
        Surviving are a son, Donald Jr., of Naperville, Ill.; a daughter, Delia A. Mulvaney of Southboro, Mass., and five grandchildren.
