        Elizabeth W. Lawrence, a member of the New York State Hospital Review and Planning Council from 1973 to 1982, died of cancer of the pancreas on Thursday at the Elizabethtown (N.Y.) Community Hospital. She was 78 years old and lived in Elizabethtown.
        She is survived by her husband, Richard W. Lawrence Jr.; three daughters, Ruth Wilson of Moab, Utah; Alida Currey of Grand Junction, Colo., and Elizabeth Lawrence of Burlington, Vt.; a brother, Richard Wadhams, also of Burlington, and two grandchildren.
