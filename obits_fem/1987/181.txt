        Delores Martin, an actress and singer, died Friday at Mount Sinai Hospital in Manhattan. She was 72 years old.
        Ms. Martin, a resident of the Bronx, appeared on Broadway in ''Finian's Rainbow,'' ''Shuffle Along'' and ''Kiss Me Kate'' in the 1940's and 1950's, and in many regional-theater productions. She also sang in jazz clubs and on television variety shows.
        Ms. Martin is survived by a sister, Vera Williams, of Schenectady, N.Y., and three brothers, James Williams and Johnson Williams, both of Schenectady, and Rufus Williams, of Buffalo, N.Y. A memorial service is planned on tomorrow in Schenectady.
