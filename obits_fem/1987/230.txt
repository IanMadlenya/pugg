        Aimee Walsh, vice president of merchandising and design for Calvin Klein Underwear and Sleepwear, died of an aneurysm of the brain July 31 in North Shore University Hospital, Manhasset, L.I. A resident of Manhattan and Westport, Conn., she was 38 years old.
        Before joining Calvin Klein, Ms. Walsh was an assistant buyer for Bloomingdale's, a coat buyer for Macy's and was on Macy's corporate staff in Hong Kong.
        She is survived by her mother, Ann, of Westport.
