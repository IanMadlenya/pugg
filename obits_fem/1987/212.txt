        Elinor Raas Heller, the first woman to head the University of California Board of Regents, died of cancer Saturday. She was 82 years old.
        She was named to the board in 1961 by Gov. Edmund G. Brown Sr. to serve out the term of her husband, Edward, who died that year. She continued to serve until 1976 and was named chairwoman in 1975.
        Mrs. Heller was a member of the Democratic National Committee from 1944 to 1952. She was a trustee of the Bancroft Library at University of California at Berkeley and a board member of Stanford University Hospital.
