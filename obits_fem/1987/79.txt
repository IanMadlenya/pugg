        Lois Yellin Abramson, who helped obtain many items in the permanent collection of the art gallery at Queensborough Community College, died of brain cancer last Wednesday at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. She was 54 years old and lived in Queens.
        Mrs. Abramson helped arrange donations from art galleries, including works by Roy Lichtenstein, LeRoy Neiman and others. The college presented her and her husband, Oscar, with a certificate of appreciation for their work.
        In addition to her husband, she is survived by two sons, Harry and David, and a daughter, Jennifer, all of Queens.
