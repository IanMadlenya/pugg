        Rosalind LaRoche, founder and director of Earth House, a residential treatment center for mental disorders in East Millstone, N.J., died last Monday at the Robert Wood-Johnson Memorial Hospital in New Brunswick, N.J., after a long illness. She was 47 years old and lived in Princeton, N.J.
        Ms. LaRoche established Earth House in 1971 to treat schizophrenics. The center used a holistic approach.  Ms. LaRoche is survived by her husband, the Rev. Greer Sharp Imbrie Jr., and two brothers, Sean LaRoche of Princeton, and Chester LaRoche of Manhattan.
