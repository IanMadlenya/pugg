        Olive E. Andrews, a former director of women's activities for the United Hospital Fund of New York, died Tuesday at the Bethel Nursing Home in Ossining, N.Y. She was 90 years old.
        Mrs. Andrews headed the fund's Brooklyn drive from 1936 to 1951, when she was named to head women's activities.
        She is survived by her husband, G. Arthur Andrews, of Ossining; a son, William E., of Northport, L.I.; a brother, Holland Estill, of Manhattan; a sister, Katharine Bouterse, of Atlanta; four grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
