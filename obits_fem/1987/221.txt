        Eleanor Poynter Jamison, a former Indiana newspaper publisher, died Tuesday. She was 86 years old.
        For more than 30 years, Mrs. Jamison was the publisher of The Sullivan Daily Times and three weekly newspapers. In 1959 she became the first woman to be elected president of the Indiana Democratic Editorial Association, and she was the first woman on the board of directors of the Hoosier State Press Association.
