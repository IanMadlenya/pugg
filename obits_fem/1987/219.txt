
       A memorial mass is to be said for Catharine R. Hughes tomorrow at 12:10 P.M. at St. Joseph's Roman Catholic Church, 371 Avenue of the Americas, between Waverly and Washington Places, in Greenwich Village. Ms. Hughes, who was a drama critic, theater consultant, editor and author, died July 12. She was 51 years old.
