        Elizabeth Morgan, a veteran actress and longtime officer and board member of the American Federation of Television and Radio Artists, died of a stroke on Sunday at her Manhattan home.
        She was 84 years old. Her career encompassed radio, television, theater, films and nightclubs.
        Her husband, Julius Seebach, died in 1982. She is survived by a stepdaughter, Mary Esther Whitelaw of Midlothian, Va., and three grandchildren.
