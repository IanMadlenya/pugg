        Agnes Walker, the widow of an investment banker, died at New London (N.H.) Hospital yesterday after a brief illness. She was 74 years old and lived in New London.
        Her husband, Joseph T. Walker Jr., was a limited partner in Hornblower & Weeks-Hemphill Noyes Inc., an investment house. He died in 1976. Her first husband, H. R. Knickerbocker, a Pulitzer Prize-winning war correspondent with the New York Evening Post and Philadelphia Public Ledger, was killed in an airplane crash in India in 1949.
        She is survived by three daughters, Julia Knickerbocker, Miranda de Kay and Suzanne Colt, all of Manhattan; a sister, Gerd Sherman, of Manhattan; a brother, Rolf Schjoldager of Los Angeles, and eight grandchildren.
