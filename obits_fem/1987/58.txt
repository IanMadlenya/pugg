        Elizabeth Moffat Drouilhet, dean of residence at Vassar College for 36 years, died of cancer Sunday at a retirement home in Concord, Mass. She was 78 years old.
        From 1940 to 1976, she was in charge of the dormitory life of Vassar students. Previously, she had been assistant to the president of Harcum Junior College in Bryn Mawr, Pa.
        Surviving are a son, Paul, of Lexington, Mass., and three grandchildren.
