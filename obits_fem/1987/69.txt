        Elizabeth Martin Sanford, mother of Senator Terry Sanford of North Carolina, died Saturday after a heart attack she suffered in a holiday visit to her son's home. She was 99.
        Mrs. Sanford was born in Salem, Va., on Nov. 30, 1888. She taught in the public schools of Laurinburg, Va., for more than 40 years.
        Survivors include three other children, nine grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.
