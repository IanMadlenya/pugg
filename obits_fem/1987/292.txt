        Jane C. Murray, director of communications and development for the Alan Guttmacher Institute, died of cancer of the pancreas and liver Monday at her home in Manhattan. She was 48 years old.
        She joined the institute in 1979. It conducts social research, policy analysis and public education in family planning, reproductive health and population matters.
        Previously Ms. Murray had worked for the American Ballet Theater and Phoenix Theater in New York and the Atlantic Monthly Press and Charles Playhouse in Boston.
        Her marriage to Michael Murray ended in divorce some years ago.
        She is survived by a son, Nathaniel, and a daughter, Caitlin, both of Manhattan; a brother, John Campbell of Chittenden, Vt., and her parents, Ford and Miriam Campbell of Martha's Vineyard, Mass.
