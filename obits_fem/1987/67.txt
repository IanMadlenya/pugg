        Gretchen Uppercu Hill died of a stroke Monday in Sharon (Conn.) Hospital. She was 76 years old and lived in Stamford, Conn.
        She was born in Rumson, N.J., and graduated from the Westover School in Middlebury, Conn.
        She was a fashion model for Abercrombie & Fitch and a member of the Anglers Club of America and the Colony Club in New York. She judged flower shows for the Garden Club of America.
        She is survived by three sisters, Ella Dinkens and Isabelle Read, both of Palm Beach, Fla., and Mary Lillian Haight of Manhattan; three daughters, Lucie Collins of Lakeville, Conn., Gretchen Kingsley of Stony Creek, Conn., and Nancy Berger of Baltimore, and nine grandchildren.
