        Anna T. Schweinler, founder of the Roman Catholic Carmelite Monastery in Morristown, N.J., died last Tuesday at the Inglemoor Nursing Home in Livingston, N.J. She was 88 years old and lived in West Orange, N.J.
        Mrs. Schweinler founded the monastery in the 1930's. Her family's house became an Augustinian Recollect monastery in 1970.
        Surviving are 3 daughters, Mary Jo Ward of Short Hills, N.J., Anne Cloney of Dennis, Mass., and Judith Moser of Glen Ridge, N.J.; a son, Carl, of New Caanan, Conn.; 13 grandchildren, and 5 great-grandchildren.
