        Mollie Kofman, mother of Senator Frank R. Lautenberg, Democrat of New Jersey, died Tuesday at Beth Israel Hospital in Passaic, N.J. The cause was complications of Parkinson's disease. She was 83 years old and lived in Clifton, N.J.
        Mrs. Kofman, who was born in Minsk, Russia, came here when she was 2 years old. She married Samuel Lautenberg in 1923. After his death, she married George Kofman.
        Surviving are Senator Lautenberg, of Montclair, N.J.; eight grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
