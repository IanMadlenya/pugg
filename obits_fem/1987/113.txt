        Martha Birmingham, vice president and director of visual presentation for Lord & Taylor, died of a stroke Tuesday at Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital. She was 62 years old and lived in Westport, Conn.
        Mrs. Birmingham was responsible for overseeing and guiding the Lord & Taylor display merchandising staffs since 1977. Previously she had been assistant art director of the L. S. Ayres & Company department stores in Indianapolis and on the display staff of Rike's department store in Dayton, Ohio.
        She was born in Westerville, Ohio, and was a graduate of the Cranbrook Academy of Art in Bloomfield Hills, Mich.
        She is survived by two daughters, Jule Bogle of Dayton, Ohio, and Sylvia Stehley of Norwalk, Conn.; a son, Jay Birmingham, of Jacksonville, Fla., and six grandchildren.
