An accomplished pianist, Miss Adonaylo gave recitals and appeared with orchestras in her native Uruguay before devoting herself to her singing career.
It was there that she was discovered in 1939 by Irene Kitchings, who was then married to the jazz pianist Teddy Wilson.
A two-week engagement in a club as a singing pianist expanded to 17 weeks, and she ended up staying in Chicago three and a half years.
