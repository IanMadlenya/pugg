In October 1979, six weeks after Earl Mountbatten was killed by Irish Republican Army terrorists, she angered many Irish-Americans while on a visit to Chicago, reportedly declaring in the presence of Mayor Jane Byrne that the Irish were ''pigs.
In the 1950's she championed civil rights in Virginia and was active in the Republican Party.
Later she took Behan's Irish Republican Army play, ''The Hostage,'' to the West End and on to New York.
