
        Betty Hudson Clark, retired executive director of the New York Building Council, died Wednesday after becoming ill in Jefferson City, Mo., where she had gone to visit her family. A resident of Old Greenwich, Conn., she was 67 years old.
        Mrs. Clark, a former account executive, joined the construction-industry trade association of over 1,000 member companies in 1959 as an executive secretary and retired as executive director in 1987. She was a native of Jefferson City.
        She is survived by a sister, Nancy Hudson of Jefferson City, and three stepdaughters, Nora Clark Bell of Allentown, Pa.; Phoebe Clark Kline of New Carrollton, Md., and Sara Clark Reeves of Alpine, N.J.
