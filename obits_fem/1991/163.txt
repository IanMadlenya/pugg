
        Gertrude Ziesing Kemper, chairman of the Kemper Educational and Charitable Foundation of Chicago, died Wednesday at her home in Chicago. She was 101 years old.
        Mrs. Kemper was a member of the Women's Board of the Lyric Opera of Chicago. She was the widow of James M. Kemper, founder of the Kemper Group insurance companies and former Ambassador to Brazil, who died in 1981.
        She is survived by a son from a previous marriage, Frank D. Stout of St. Helena, Calif.
