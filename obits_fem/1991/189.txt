
        Sadie Daniels, for many years a leader in the Ladies Auxiliary of the Jewish War Veterans and a former president of the organization's New York State chapter, died on Monday at Long Beach Memorial Hospital on Long Island. She was 90 years old and lived in Long Beach.
        She died of heart disease, her family said.
        Mrs. Daniels is survived by two daughters, Leatrice Spitzer and Gloria Gold, both of Oceanside, L.I., five grandchildren and a great-grandaughter.
