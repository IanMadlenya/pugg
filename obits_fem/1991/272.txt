
        Katheryn Hait Dorflinger Manchee, a historian and lecturer whose specialty was glass, died on Wednesday in North Naples Hospital in Naples, Fla. She was 86 years old.
        She died of cardiac arrest, said her daughter, June Dorflinger Hardy.
        Mrs. Manchee was a historian for the Steuben Glass Company in the 1940's and later was a representative for the Swedish glass concern Orrefors.
        In addition to her daughter, of Naples, she is survived by three stepdaughters, Marilyn M. Wortman of Montclair, N.J.; Cynthia D. Brown of Darien, Conn., and Sally M. Batchelder of Weston, Mass.
