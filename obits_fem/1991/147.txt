
        Emily McLaughlin, an actress who was a star in the television soap opera "General Hospital" for nearly three decades, died of cancer on Friday at her home here. She was 61 years old.
        Miss McLaughlin, who portrayed Jessie Brewer, had appeared on the ABC series since its first broadcast, on April 1, 1963. She last appeared Feb. 13.
        Miss McLaughlin was born in White Plains and earned a bachelor's degree at Middlebury College in Vermont. Her career included Broadway appearances in "The Frogs of Spring" and "The Lovers."
        Survivors include a daughter, Mary Ann Anderson, and a son, Robert Lansing Jr.
