
        Esther Fisher, a classical pianist, died here on Feb. 12. She was 90 years old.
        No cause of death was given.
        She was born in Christchurch, New Zealand, and went to London in 1919. Ferruccio Busoni, the Italian pianist and composer, encouraged her to study with Isidore Philipp in Paris.
        She later studied with Artur Schnabel in Berlin. She made her debut in London in 1923.
        She became a professor at the Royal College of Music in London and married Sir John Barran in 1946. He died in 1952.
