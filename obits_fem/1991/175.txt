
        Jean Harder Ryan Shields, a longtime hospital volunteer, died on Tuesday at her home, Panther Ledge Farm in Allamuchy, N.J. She was 76 years old.
        She was a past chairwoman and a member of the Assistant Board of Woman's Hospital since 1949. In 1965 she became a member of the board of St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan.
        Her first husband, Clendenin J. Ryan, a financier, died in 1957. In 1969, she married William Shields Jr., a senior partner of Coudert Brothers law firm. He died in 1979.
        Mrs. Shields is survived by two sons, Robert Ryan of Asbury, N.J., and Cyr Ryan of Oldwick, N.J.; two daughters, Caryn Tuckerman of Manhattan, and Jean Sadler of Manns Harbor, N.C., 17 grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
