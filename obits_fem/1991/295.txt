
        Rita Greenland, an advertising executive active in Jewish organizations, died on Saturday at New York University Hospital. She was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan and Bedford Hills, N.Y.
        The family said she died of cancer.
        Mrs. Greenland, a graduate of Brooklyn College, was the senior vice president for human relations at Smith/Greenland Advertising Inc. in New York. Her associations included the American Jewish Committee, the Anti-Defamation League, the American Cancer Society and the Library Volunteers of the Scarsdale P.T.A.
        Mrs. Greenland is survived by her husband, Leo; two sons, Seth and Andrew; a sister, Yvette Rosenzweig, and a granddaughter, all of New York City.
