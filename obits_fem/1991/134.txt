
        Madelle Hegeler Semerjian, who was active in charitable and cultural affairs in Southampton, L.I., died on Tuesday at Mount Sinai Hospital. She was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan and Southampton.
        She died of leukemia, her family said.
        Mrs. Semerjian studied at Vassar College and at the Sorbonne in Paris. She was with the Paris office of French & Company, the art dealer, for several years.
        She is survived by her husband, George; two daughters, Cornelia Grassi and Giovanna Grassi, both of London, and a brother, Julius Hegeler of Danville, Ill.
