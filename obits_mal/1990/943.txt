        Louis M. Weber, a former counsel for the Skouras Theaters Corporation, died Saturday while visiting relatives in Vienna, Va. He was 85 years old.
        A 1927 graduate of New York Law School, Mr. Weber joined the law department of the 20th Century Fox Film Corporation shortly after graduation and became corporate counsel for Skouras in 1936. He retired in 1977 but kept his own practice in corporate and real-estate law.
        Mr. Weber, whose wife, the former Rose Seegan, died in 1981, is survived by two daughters, Laura Davis of Millerton, N.Y., and Anne Sherwood of Sarasota, Fla., three grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
