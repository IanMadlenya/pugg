        Alexei Yudenich, a former principal ballet dancer with the National Ballet of Canada who was a dance teacher in the South Jersey-Philadelphia area, died of cancer Thursday. He was 46 years old.
        Mr. Yudenich, a native of Sarajevo, Yugoslavia, joined the Sarajevo Opera Ballet School in 1961. In 1964 he joined the Pennsylvania Ballet Company as principal male dancer, a position he held until leaving in 1972 to become principal dancer of the National Ballet of Canada.
