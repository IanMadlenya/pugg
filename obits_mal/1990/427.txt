        Harold S. Spielman, professor emeritus of education at City College, died of complications from Parkinson's disease on Friday at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Yonkers.
        Dr. Spielman taught at City College from 1950 until he retired in 1983 and was chairman of the department of secondary and continuing education from 1968 to 1974.
        A native of Philadelphia, he taught high-school science there after receiving bachelor's and master's degrees from City College.
        In 1950 he received a doctorate from Teachers College of Columbia University, where he taught science before joining the City College faculty.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Eleanor Maier; a daughter, Dr. Ellen S. Gitler of Mamaroneck, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
