        George W. May, founder of Firecom Inc. of Woodside, Queens, a manufacturer and installer of alarms, died on Sunday at Glen Cove Community Hospital on Long Island. He was 64 years old and lived in Locust Valley, L.I.
        He died after a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. May was born in Brooklyn and graduated from the Merchant Marine Academy and Syracuse University. After serving in World War II he had management positions with Johnson & Johnson, Litton Industries, Victor Comptometer and Norton Simon Inc. before becoming chairman and president of Firecom.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Helen Payn; a daughter, Derrice, of Locust Valley; two sons, Douglas, of Chicago, and Marshall, of Locust Valley, and a granddaughter.
