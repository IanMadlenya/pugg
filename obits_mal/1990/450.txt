        Morton Freund, a New York advertising executive for more than half a century, died of a heart attack on Tuesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 91 years old.
        Mr. Freund was born in Manhattan and attended Columbia College. He maintained his own agency specializing in men's wear advertising until 1952, when his agency merged with Lawrence C. Gumbinner Associates, of which he became executive vice president.
        His wife, Betty, died last year. There are no immediate survivors.
