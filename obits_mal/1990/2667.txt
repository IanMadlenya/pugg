
        Louis C. Schramm, former president of Budd Enterprises, a theatrical moving company, and a vice president of Chelsea Warehouses, died on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 64 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. Schramm, who was known as Bud, was a grandson of Louis Schramm Sr., a founder of Allied Van Lines. His father, Louis Jr., was president of Allied from 1949 to 1957.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Nadine Shelby; two daughters, Valerie, of Manhattan, and Vickie Sargent of Fairfield, Conn.; a stepdaughter, Mary Salb of Fairfield; his mother, Denise, of Sag Harbor, L.I.; a brother, Robert, of Sag Harbor, and four grandchildren.
