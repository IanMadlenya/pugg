        Dr. Philip S. Bergman, a retired neurologist, died on Monday at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 69 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        His family said he died of prostate cancer.
        Dr. Bergman, who practiced in New Rochelle and Manhattan, was associated with New Rochelle Hospital from 1954 until he retired last year.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Mimi Leff; two daughters, Laura and Nancy, both of Manhattan; a son, Thomas, of Charlestown, Mass.; a sister, Ruth Winik of San Jose, Calif., and two grandchildren.
