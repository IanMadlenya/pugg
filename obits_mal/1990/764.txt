        Nathaniel Frison, head chef at the Old Original Bookbinders restaurant in Philadelphia for four decades and creator of many of its soups, died Friday at the Hospital of the University of Pennsylvania. He was 79 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Alberta Briggs; three daughters, a son, seven grandchildren and a brother.
