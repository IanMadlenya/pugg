        Anthony G. Barbieri, a former security officer and attache for the State Department, died on Thursday at the Westchester County Medical Center in Valhalla, N.Y. He was 74 years old and lived in Pelham Manor, N.Y.
        After graduating from Manhattan College in 1938, Mr. Barbieri served in the Army in World War II and the Korean War, leaving the service with the rank of lieutenant colonel.
        In 1955, he joined the Foreign Service and held a variety of positions at United States embassies in the Netherlands, Germany, the Philippines, Mexico, Switzerland, Spain and Uruguay, including posts as a security officer.
        He is survived by a brother, Gerard, and a sister, Nell, both of Pelham Manor.
