        Dr. Sydney C. Davis, professor emeritus of education at Adelphi University in Garden City, L.I., died of a heart attack on Wednesday at the Hollis Branch of the Queens Borough Public Library. He was 77 years old.
        Dr. Davis joined the faculty of the Institute for Teaching and Education Studies at Adelphi in 1960 and taught there until he retired in 1980.
        Dr. Davis was born in Manhattan, graduated from Wesleyan University and received an M.A. and Ph.D. from Teachers College at Columbia University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Hazel Grubbs, and a sister, Elena Davis of Mallorca, Spain.
