        Thomas Ravasz, a New York psychoanalyst, died on Wednesday at his home in Brooklyn. He was 45 years old and also lived in North Palm Beach, Fla.
        Dr. Ravasz had been ill for a long time, said his mother, Margaret.
        He maintained a private practice in Brooklyn and Manhasset, L.I., for more than 10 years. Earlier he had been a staff psychiatrist at Long Island Jewish Medical Center in New Hyde Park, Queens.
        Dr. Ravasz was born in Milwaukee and graduated from Marquette University in 1967. He received a medical degree from the Wisconsin College of Medicine in 1971.
        In addition to his mother, he is survived by a daughter, Michelle, of Milwaukee; a brother, Robert, of Wood Dale, Ill., and a granddaughter.
