        Dr. John Adrian Onderdonk Jr., chief of cardiology at the Chestnut Hill Hospital in Philadelphia, died Tuesday, apparently of a heart attack, at his home in Radnor, Pa. He was 55 years old.
        Dr. Onderdonk joined the staff at Chestnut Hill about 10 years ago. He was also in private practice and had served previously at Monmouth Medical Center in Long Branch, N.J., where he was director of the Cardiac Catherization Laboratory.
        He received his medical degree at the University of Pennsylvania in 1964. Dr. Onderdonk was an officer aboard a destroyer escort from 1956 to 1959.
        He is survived by two children, Adrian 3d and Daphne, both of Radnor; his mother, Marion Jacobson, and two sisters, Patricia Pruett and Adrianne Dudden, all of Bryn Mawr, Pa.
