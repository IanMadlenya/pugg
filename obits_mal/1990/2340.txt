
        George A. Alford, a banker and civic leader, died on Tuesday at Valley Hospital in Ridgewood, N.J. He was 63 years old and lived in Saddle River, N.J.
        He died of cancer, a spokeswoman for the hospital said.
        Mr. Alford was chairman of the Citizens First National Bank of New Jersey and an honorary trustee of the Valley Hospital Foundation. He had been a trustee of Valley Hospital and was a trustee of Fairleigh Dickinson University.
        Mr. Alford graduated from Yale in 1950.
        He is survived by his wife, Ann; five daughters, Carolyn Arnold of Sparks, Md., Patricia President of Centreville, Va., Katherine Johnson of Manhattan, Maria Alford of Saddle River, and Elizabeth Alford of Hoboken, N.J., and one grandson.
