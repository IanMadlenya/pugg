        Ralph Gambaro, the owner of Les Prismatiques Inc., a designer showroom for home furnishings, died of AIDS on Thursday at New York Hospital. He was 51 years old and lived in Manhattan and Garrison, N.Y.
        Mr. Gambaro, who was born in Red Bank, N.J., and was a graduate of Georgetown University, spent several years with furniture manufacturing concerns before buying Les Prismatiques in Manhattan nearly two decades ago.
        He is survived by his mother, Mary Sharabba of Eatontown, N.J., and a sister, Rosalie Scaccia of Dallas.
