        Henry X. O'Brien, former Chief Justice of the Pennsylvania Supreme Court, died of cancer Saturday. He was 86 years old. , Justice O'Brien served on the State Supreme Court for 21 years after 14 years as a trial judge in Allegheny County and five years as an Allegheny County assistant district attorney. He was Chief Justice from 1980 to 1983, after which he served as a consultant for a law firm.
        He graduated from Duquesne University in 1926 and received a law degree there two years later.
        Survivors include his wife, Rosemary Hager O'Brien, a daughter and a son.
