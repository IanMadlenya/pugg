        Dr. Emmanuel Gordon, a retired Queens dentist and past president of the Queens County Dental Society, died of complications of Parkinson's disease on Monday at his home in Royal Palm Beach, Fla. He was 93 years old.
        Dr. Gordon was in private practice from 1922 to 1972, when he retired and moved to Florida. He was a Fellow of the International College of Dentists.
        He was born in Philadelphia and graduated from the College of Dental and Oral Surgery of Columbia University.
        Dr. Gordon is survived by his wife, the former Marion Siner.
