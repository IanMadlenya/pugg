        Dr. Mansho T. Khilnani, a radiologist and educator, died of a chronic heart ailment on Jan. 4 at Coral Reef Hospital in Miami. He was 70 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        After completing his medical training at Calcutta University, Dr. Khilnani worked in refugee camps on the India-Pakistan border in the partition years.
        He came here in 1951 and completed his residency in radiology at Mount Sinai Hospital, where he joined the staff and became a professor of radiology. He was also in private practice in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, Lia; a son, Neil, and a daughter, Gita, both of Manhattan, and a brother, Harkrishan, of Bombay, India.
