        Dr. Simon Grolnick, a psychoanalyst and a teacher, died on Sunday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset L.I. He was 60 years old and lived in Great Neck, L.I., and East Hampton, L.I.
        His family said he died of a melonoma.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Maxine Gellis; four daughters, Deborah Godwin of Memphis, Wendy, of Princeton, N.J., Judith, of Boston, and Elizabeth, of Great Neck; his father, Dr. Max Grolnick of Great Neck; a brother, Dr. Lawrence Grolnick of Briarcliff Manor, N.Y., and two grandchildren.
