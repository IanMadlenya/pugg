        J. Willcox Dunn, a retired newspaper editor and publisher who actively opposed gambling, died Friday at a nursing home here. He was 91 years old.
        In 1952 Mr. Dunn established the weekly Princess Anne Free Press, serving Virginia Beach and Princess Anne County. He was its editor, publisher and sole owner until he retired and ceased publishing the paper in 1962.
        In 1958, Mr. Dunn was awarded the national Elijah Parish Lovejoy Award for Courage in Journalism for his opposition to political corruption and racketeering in the region.
        Mr. Dunn, a widower, is survived by a daughter, Margaret T. Dunn of Charlottesville, Va.; a son, Joseph W. Dunn Jr. of Virginia Beach; two sisters, Mary D. Wallace of Richmond and Martha D. Lyon of Warren, Mich., and three grandchildren.
