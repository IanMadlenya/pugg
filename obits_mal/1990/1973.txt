        Howard M. Grossman, a lawyer and Democratic leader in Westchester County, died on Tuesday at White Plains Hospital. He was 68 years old and lived in Hartsdale, N.Y.
        He died of cancer, his son James said.
        Mr. Grossman went into private practice in Mount Vernon shortly after graduating from Columbia University Law School in 1948. He moved his practice to White Plains about 10 years ago. He was active on the Greenburg town council and planning board and was a president of the Greenburg Democratic Club.
        He is survived by his wife, Adele; two sons, Andrew, of Brooklyn and James, of Chicago; a daughter, Elizabeth Scroggin of Beaverton, Ore; a brother, David, of Hartsdale, and five grandchildren.
