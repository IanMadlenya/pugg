        Theodore J. Holmgren, a former senior executive of Curtice Burns Foods in Rochester, died on Sunday at Strong Memorial Hospital there. He was 63 years old and lived in Pittsford, N.Y.
        Mr. Holmgren died of complications from surgery for blood clots, the president of Curtice Burns, David McDonald, said yesterday.
        Mr. Holmgren came to the food-processing company in 1968 from the General Foods Corporation. He served as senior vice president, corporate secretary, director of public relations and a member of the board before retiring in 1989.
        He is survived by his wife, Miriam; a son, Theodore D., of Pittsford; two daughters, Miriam McCrea of Simsbury, Conn., and Barbara Fowler of Pittsford, and two grandchildren.
