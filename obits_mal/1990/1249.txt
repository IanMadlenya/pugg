        Narciso Puente Jr., a retired administrative law judge, died on Wednesday at Beth Israel Hospital. He was 70 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Judge Puente died of a heart attack, his sister-in-law, Phyllis Puente, said.
        Mr. Puente, before his appointment to the New York State post in 1971, had been in private practice in Manhattan and had been active in Democratic Party politics. He retired in 1985.
        He was born in Bridgeport, Conn., and graduated from New York University and the Fordham Law School.
        Surviving is a brother, Vincent, of Southampton, Pa.
