
        Anthony F. Russo, dean of students at Kingsborough Community College in Brooklyn, died on Thursday at Coney Island Hospital. He was 55 years old and lived in Roslyn, L.I.
        He died of heart failure, a funeral home representative said.
        Mr. Russo was named dean of students at Kingsborough in 1971 after serving as chairman of the health and physical education department at the college since 1965. A graduate of Hunter College, Mr. Russo had been a minor league pitcher with the Detroit Tigers organization and a baseball coach at Long Island University.
        He is survived by his wife, Joyce; a son, Anthony Jr., of Howard Beach, Queens; a daughter, Lori, of Miami; his father and two grandchildren.
