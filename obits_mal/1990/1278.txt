        Harry Crockett Grumpelt, a former partner in the accounting firm of Arthur Young, died on Monday at his home in Salisbury, Conn. He was 82 years old.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Ann Zitzelberger; three daughters, Shirley Spengler of Katonah, N.Y., Laura Cann of Rochester and Susan Deeks of New Vernon, N.J.; two sisters, Helen Oren of Leonia, N.J., and Dorothy Macalister of Tenafly, N.J., and seven grandchildren.
