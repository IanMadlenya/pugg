        John W. Jorgensen, who retired this month as president and chief executive of the Earle M. Jorgensen Company, a California-based steel and aluminum distributor and processor, died of cancer on Friday at his home in San Marino, Calif. He was 64 years old.
        Mr. Jorgensen served in the Navy in World War II and graduated from Pomona College in 1948. He then joined the company, founded by his father, Earle M. Jorgensen, who is chairman, and served in a series of executive capacities.
        He is survived by his wife, Jacquelyn; a son, Jay, of Mira Loma, Calif; two daughters, Maren, of Costa Mesa, Calif., and Jill Swann, of Oak View, Calif.; his father, of Bel Air, Calif.; a sister, Maren Long, of Ojai, Calif., and a brother, Earle M. Jorgensen Jr., of Turlock, Calif.
