        Irving A. Isaacs, a lawyer and former bank official, died on Monday at Mount Sinai Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Isaacs was a director of the Standard Financial Corporation for more than 40 years and for 20 years was a vice president and general counsel of its parent company, Standard Prudential Corporation, now known as Sterling Bancorp. He retired in 1987.
        He is survived by two daughters, Joanne Froelich of Manhattan and Diane Mandel of Beverly Hills, Calif.; a sister, Ethel Reisman of Miami Beach, and four grandchildren.
