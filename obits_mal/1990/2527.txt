
        Dr. Peter Asch, a professor of economics at Rutgers University, died on Wednesday at his home in Belle Mead, N.J. He was 52 years old.
        He died of heart failure, his family said.
        Dr. Asch, who was on the Rutgers faculty for 25 years, was born in Brooklyn. He was a graduate of Oberlin College and received a doctorate from Princeton.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Rita Zindler; his parents, Florence and Solomon, of Haverford, Pa., and two sons, Eric, of Chicago and David, of Silver Spring, Md.
