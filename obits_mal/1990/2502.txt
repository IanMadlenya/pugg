
        Kwok Tak-seng, the chairman of one of this colony's largest property groups, died Tuesday of heart failure. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Kwok headed Sun Hung Kai Property Ltd. and was reported to have a personal fortune of more than $1 billion, making him one of the colony's wealthiest individuals.
        Keith Wu, a senior analyst at Citicorp Scrimgeour Vickers Hong Kong Ltd., portrayed Mr. Kwok as a widely admired business executive.
