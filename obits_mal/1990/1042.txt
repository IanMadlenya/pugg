        Sim Son, who was in charge of civilian affairs for the Khmer Rouge, was killed April 25 when a tree fell on him near the Thai border in Cambodia during a storm, Khmer Rouge officials said today. His age was not disclosed.
        Mr. Sim Son, a former Ambassador to North Korea, reportedly controlled decisions regarding the estimated 150,000 civilians living in camps run by the Communist Khmer Rouge near the Thai frontier.
