
        Peter Gurtler, the owner of Vienna's most famous hotel and former husband of a former American Ambassador to Austria, Helene Von Damm, committed suicide on Wednesday, the police reported. He was 44 years old
        Mr. Gurtler shot himself to death in his hunting lodge in Pernitz, 30 miles south of Vienna, the police said.
        Mr. Gurtler owned two top-class Vienna hotels, including the Hotel Sacher, famous for the Sacher torte, the delicately flavored chocolate cake the hotel began producing about 100 years ago.
        Ms. Von Damm was Mr. Gurtler's second wife. They were divorced in 1987, and Mr. Gurtler remarried.
