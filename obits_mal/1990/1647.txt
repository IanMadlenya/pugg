        Harry B. Freeman Jr., an investment banker, died yesterday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhatan. He was 64 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of cancer, his brother Peter said.
        Before retiring in 1980, Mr. Freeman was a managing partner of Lord Abbett & Company, chief executive of Channing Management Company and a general partner at Robert Winthrop & Company, as well as its successor firm, Wood Struthers & Winthrop. He graduated from Yale University in 1948.
        In addition to his brother Peter, of Providence, R.I., he is survived by a daughter, Tracy C. Wells of Tenants Harbor, Me.; a son, Harry 3d, of Manhattan, and another brother, Willard, of Aiken, S.C.
