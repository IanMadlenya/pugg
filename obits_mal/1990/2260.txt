        Francis X. Houghton, an advertising executive, died on Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 63 years old.
        Mr. Houghton died of a heart attack, a spokeswoman for the family said.
        He was a graduate of Princeton University and was with the advertising agency Ogilvy, Benson & Mather, later Ogilvy & Mather, for 28 years. He was a senior vice president and a director and was executive director of Ogilvy & Mather/2.
        In 1986 he joined Biederman, Kelly & Schaffer as executive vice president.
        He is survived by three sons, Alan, of Providence, R.I., David, of Chatham, Mass., and Nicholas, of Manhattan, and a brother, William, of Ponte Vedra Beach, Fla.
