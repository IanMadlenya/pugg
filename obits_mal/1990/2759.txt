
        Joseph Giordano, an advertising executive, died yesterday at New York Hospital in Manhattan. He was 58 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of congestive heart failure, a niece said.
        Mr. Giordano was a senior vice president and creative director at Benton & Bowles from 1980 to 1985. Before joining that firm in 1970, he was with Jacqueline Brandwynne Associates, Young & Rubicam and Hockaday/DeWolfe/Giordano. Most recently, he worked as a freelance creative director in Los Angeles.
        He was a graduate of Columbia University and had served in the Navy.
        He is survived by his mother, Loretta, of Glen Cove, L.I., and a brother, Anthony, of Naples, Fla.
