        Edgar Conradson, the former manager of the Swedish Tourist Board in Manhattan, died on Thursday at Bellevue Hospital Center after a fall in March that left him paralyzed. He was 58 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Conradson was also a director of Scandinavian Tourist Boards and a travel writer. He was a member of the American Scandinavian Foundation and other Scandinavian organizations.
        He is survived by several nieces and nephews in Sweden.
