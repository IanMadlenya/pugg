        Michael Kraus, professor emeritus of history at City College, died Aug. 9 in a nursing home in Sun City, Ariz. He was 89 years old and lived in Sun City.
        Professor Kraus joined the college history department in 1926 and was a full professor when he retired in 1966.
        He graduated from City College in 1923 and received a Ph.D. from Columbia University.
        He was a Guggenheim Fellow and a prolific writer. Among his books were ''The Writing of American History'' (1953, The University of Oklahoma), and ''The Atlantic Civilization: 18th -Century Origins'' (1949, Cornell University).
        His wife, Vera Edelstadt, died in 1984.
        He is survived by a twin sister, Ruth Kraus, of Manhattan, and a brother, Harry, of Sun City.
