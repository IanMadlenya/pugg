        Fergus Reid Jr., a stockbroker in New York for more than 60 years, died yesterday at York (Me.) Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in York Harbor, Me.
        Mr. Reid was a senior partner of the firms of Roosevelt & Sons and Mitchel, Schrieber & Watts. In World War II he was president of Foster Engineering, which won an award for excellence as a military contractor.
        Mr. Reid was an alumnus of the St. Paul's School and Yale University.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Ethelreda Seabury; two sons, Fergus 3d, of New Canaan, Conn., and William, of York Harbor; a daughter, Winthrop Burns of Darien, Conn.; a sister, Baronne Helen de Lustrac of Paris; five grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
