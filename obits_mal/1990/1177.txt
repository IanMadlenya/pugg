        Dr. Adrian Brodey, a dermatologist for more than 40 years, died of prostate cancer yesterday at his house in Lawrence, L.I. He was 78 years old.
        Dr. Brodey had practiced in Lawrence since 1947 and had formerly been director of dermatology at St. John's Episcopal Hospital-South Shore, Peninsula Hospital and Long Island Jewish Hospital. He graduated in 1937 from Rusk Medical College at the University of Chicago and was a lieutenant colonel in the Army Medical Corps in World War II.
        Surviving are his wife, Sylvia; two sons, Dr. Mitchell, of Syracuse, and Lawrence, of West Orange, N.J.; a daughter, Nancy Koch of Fishkill, N.Y.; three brothers, Jesse, of White Plains, Stanley, of Woodmere, L.I., and Dr. Marvin, of Great Neck, L.I., a sister, Elaine Brodey of Manhattan, and three grandchildren.
