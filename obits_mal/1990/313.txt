        Eugene J. Anspach Jr., former president of Anspach Brothers, a chain of opticians with headquarters in East Orange, N.J., died on Jan. 25 at Grossmont Hospital in La Mesa, Calif. He lived in La Jolla, Calif., and was 74 years old.
        Mr. Anspach retired four years ago from the company that was founded by his family in 1895. He took over leadership of the business after World War II and developed it into a chain of stores with branches in New Jersey in Summit, Livingston, Deal and Sea Girt. The business was sold in 1985.
        He was a graduate of Dartmouth College and a former president of the New Jersey State Board of Opthalmic Dispensers.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Liv Ingeborg Jacobson; two daughters, Norma Schnedler of Palo Alto, Calif., and Barbara Rosenbaum of Los Angeles; a brother, Andrew, of Manhattan, and a grandson.
