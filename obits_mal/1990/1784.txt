        David J. Melamed, a lawyer and former secretary of the Communications Satellite Corporation, died on Saturday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan. He was 60 years old and lived in Hastings-on-Hudson, N.Y.
        He died of complications of cancer, his wife, Eleanor, said.
        Mr. Melamed, a graduate of Harvard Law School, was an associate with Cravath, Swaine & Moore in Manhattan before joining the Communications Satellite Corporation in Washington.
        In addition to his wife, he is survived by a son, Daniel, of Cambridge, Mass., and a daughter, Barbara, of Boston.
