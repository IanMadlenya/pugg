        Joseph Leberman, an actor who performed on Broadway and in television, died of cancer April 24 at the Cabrini Medical Center. He was 85 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        On Broadway, Mr. Leberman appeared in ''Emperor Jones,'' ''The Brass Ring'' and ''The Visit.'' At the Hedgerow Theater, a repertory company in Rose Valley, Pa., Mr. Leberman performed 130 roles and designed 21 productions in 21 years with the company.
        On television, he portrayed Mr. Hickstein in ''The Goldbergs'' and acted in ''Robert Montgomery Presents,'' ''Studio One'' and ''The U.S. Steel Hour.''
        Surviving are a sister, Nancy Lawrence of San Francisco, and a brother, Jack Leberman of Meadowbrook, Pa.
