        Marc Neuhof, an architectural photographer whose work appeared in publications in the United States and abroad, died on Thursday at White Plains Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Larchmont, N.Y. His family said his death followed a stroke.
        Mr. Neuhof's work appeared over four decades in Architectural Record, Architectural Forum, House & Home, Housing and other magazines in the building field.
        He was also an accomplished amateur athlete and won four gold medals in sprints, hurdles and the high jump at the National Masters Track and Field Championships in Indianapolis earlier this month.
        Mr. Neuhof is survived by his wife, the former Trudy Bach; a son, Dirk, of Woodstock, Vt.; two daughters, Carol Bruckner of Scottsdale, Ariz., and Lisa, of Salt Lake City; two sisters, Marion Bernstein of San Francisco and Joan Mueller of Paris, and three grandchildren.
