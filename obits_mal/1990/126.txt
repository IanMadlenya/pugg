        Donald A. Stoddard, a former senior vice president of the Morgan Guarantee Trust Company in Manhattan, died yesterday at his home in Bloomingdale, N.J., after a short illness. He was 79 years old and had lived in Nutley until moving to Bloomingdale last year.
        Mr. Stoddard,who was with Morgan Guarantee for 28 years, was born in Nutley and graduated from Darmouth College in 1931.
        His wife, the former Marylyles Smith, died in 1988.
        He is survived by three sons, James P., of Basking Ridge, N.J., Donald A. Jr. of Califon, N.J., Capt. H. Sanford of San Diego, Calif.; a brother, H. Vincent, of Vero Beach, Fla., and six grandchildren.
