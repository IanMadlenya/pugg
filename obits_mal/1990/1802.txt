        The Rev. Arthur H. Tildesley, an Episcopal priest, died on Saturday at his home in Fair Haven, N.J. He was 58 years old.
        He died of lung cancer, his wife, Joan, said.
        Mr. Tildesley had been rector of Christ Episcopal Church in Middletown, N.J., since 1981. Before that he was development officer of the Diocese of New Jersey and had worked at parish churches. He graduated from the General Theological Seminary and was a trustee of the institution from 1972 to 1982.
        Before being ordained in 1976, Mr. Tildesley had been a vice president of Citibank and the Summit Bancorporation in New Jersey. Mr. Tildesley had been president of the Fair Haven Board of Education and had been on the board of Family and Children's Services and the Red Bank Y.M.C.A.
        Also surviving are a son, Arthur Jr., of Manhattan; three daughters, Jill, of Manhattan, Lauren McDonough and Susan Strasser, both of Fair Haven; five sisters, Jean Moss of Oakhurst, N.J., Gwendolyn King of Tinton Falls, N.J., Doris Regan of Spring Lake Heights, N.J., Ann Bonney of Wayside, N.J., and Lois Landis of Elberon, N.J., and seven grandsons.
