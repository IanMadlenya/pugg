        Owen D. Cox, a retired Federal district judge who in the 1970's ordered the busing of thousands of Corpus Christi students in the interest of desegregation, died Saturday at a nursing home here. He was 80 years old.
        He was appointed to the Federal bench in 1970 by President Richard M. Nixon and retired 10 years later.
        He was a lawyer and prominent member of the Texas Republican Party.
