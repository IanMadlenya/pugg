        Federal District Judge George Carter Carr, the chief judge of the Middle District of Florida, died of a brain tumor on Friday. He was 60 years old.
        Judge Carr had a 20-year career as a private lawyer before becoming a judge.
        He was appointed to the Federal bench by President Jimmy Carter in 1977.
