        Emmet V. Murray, a lawyer who had retired from the Caltex Petroleum Company, died yesterday at his home in Quogue, L.I. He was 64 years old and also lived in Gulf Stream, Fla.
        Mr. Murray died of cancer of the pancreas, said his wife, Jeanne.
        He joined the company in 1958 and retired in 1985.
        He was born in Manhattan and graduated from Brown University and the University of Virginia Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jeanne Beatty; two sons, Van, of Darien, Conn., and Chester, of New Canaan, Conn., and four grandchildren.
