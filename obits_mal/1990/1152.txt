        Donald J. Bedford, chief of investigators for the Brooklyn District Attorney's office, died of cancer on Monday at Long Island College Hospital. He was 54 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Mr. Bedford joined the prosecutor's office in 1962 as a detective investigator. He was named a senior detective investigator in 1970, assistant chief investigator in 1973 and chief of the 70-member squad in 1974.
        Mr. Bedford had been a military police sergeant in the Army in Europe and a security guard.
        Surviving is his wife, JoAnne.
