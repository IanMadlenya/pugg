        Dr. Earl R. Zack, a psychologist and an educator, died yesterday in Sarasota Memorial Hospital in Florida. He was 77 years old and lived in Sarasota.
        He died of septicemia, his daughter Jane said.
        Dr. Zack joined the Pace University faculty in 1963 as a professor of psychology after teaching at C. W. Post College, Hofstra and the City University. In 1973, he developed a doctoral program in business at Pace and became its director. He retired in 1983. Dr. Zack was also in private practice and was a management consultant.
        He graduated from New York University with a Ph.D. in speech psychology and was in the Army Air Corps in World War II.
        Surviving are his wife, Nancy, and two daughters, Melissa, of Manhattan, and Jane, of Tampa. Fla.
