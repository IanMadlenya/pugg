
        Abelardo Paseiro, director of physical medicine and rehabilitation at the Queens Hospital Center in Jamaica, Queens, died on Dec. 16 at his home in Manhattan. He was 57 years old.
        He died of a brain stem tumor, his executor, David McMullen, said.
        He joined the staff of the Queens Hospital Center in 1987. He was a member of the faculty of the New York Medical College from 1975 to 1990.
        Dr. Paseiro was born in Buenos Aires and graduated from the University of Buenos Aires Medical School in 1961. He came to the United States in the 1970's and did his residency at the Rusk Institute of Rehabilitation Medicine at the New York University Medical School and at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine.
        He is survived by two sisters, Elisa Paseiro and Maria Paseiro de Ussher, both of Buenos Aires.
