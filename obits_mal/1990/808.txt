        Arthur N. Field, a lawyer for more than 60 years, died of congestive heart failure on Sunday at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 84 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Field, a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, was a founder in 1927 of DiFalco, Field & DiGiovanna, a Manhattan law firm now known as Field, Lomenzo, Turret & Blumberg.
        He is survived by his wife, Rose; two sons, David, of Woodmere, L.I., and Robert, of Madison, N.J.; a sister, Frances Trauman of Miami, and five grandchildren.
