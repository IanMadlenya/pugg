        Paul W. Kayser, senior vice president of Paul R. Ray & Company, an international executive-recruiting concern based in Fort Worth, died of a heart attack Saturday while vacationing in Puerto Vallarta, Mexico. He was 72 years old and lived in Rancho Mirage, Calif.
        Mr. Kayser, a native New Yorker, had retired in 1983 from American Standard Inc., the plumbing-fixtures manufacturer, where he was vice president for human resurces. Previously he had been vice president of human resources for several companies, including Pepsico Inc. and American Airlines.
        In 1969 and 1970, he was president of the National Alliance of Businessmen.
        He was a graduate of Wesleyan University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jane Satter; a son, Craig, of Irving, Tex.; a stepdaughter, Joan Edwards of Belevedere, Calif.; two stepsons, John Harper and William Harper Jr., both of Los Angeles;, a sister, Joyce Steele of Yardley, Pa., and four grandchildren.
