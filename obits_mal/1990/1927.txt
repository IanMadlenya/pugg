        Willliam G. Andrews, a former vice president of Grand Union Food Stores, died on Saturday at his home in Winter Haven, Fla., where he had lived since he retired in 1971. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Andrews was born in Phoenix, N.Y., and was with Armour Meat Packing before he joined Grand Union in the late 1930's.
        He is survived by his wife, Victoria; four sons, Col. William G. Andrews Jr., of Evans, Ga., Philip, of Athens, N.Y., Patrick, of Bergenfield, N.J., and James, of Melbourne, Fla.; three daughters, Victoria, of Auburndale, Fla., Elizabeth Gavin of Winter Haven, Fla., and Susan Reilly of Kissimmee, Fla.; two sisters, Margaret Halpin of Seminole, Fla., and Jane Parkinson of Phoenix, N.Y., 21 grandchildren, and nine great-grandchildren.
