Mr. Tropper was a member of Actors' Equity and a founding member and former chairman of the Stage Managers' Association.         Joel M. Tropper, a stage manager, died of AIDS on Friday in Orlando, Fla. He was 46 years old. Mr. Tropper was a member of Actors' Equity and a founding member and former chairman of the Stage Managers' Association. He managed many Broadway shows, including ''Woman of the Year,'' ''Ain't Misbehavin','' ''Beatlemania,'' ''Porgy and Bess,'' ''Rags'' and ''Elvis.''
        Surviving are his father, William, of Las Vegas, and two brothers, Irvin, of Los Angeles, and Mark, of Phoenix.
