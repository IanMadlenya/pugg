        Irving X. Fabrikant, the president of M. Fabrikant & Sons, a leading diamond importing and manufacturing concern, from 1930 to 1980, died on Saturday at United Hospital in Port Chester, N.Y. He was 86 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of a heart attack, a family spokesman said.
        Mr. Fabrikant, a native of Brooklyn, was a graduate of Cornell College and the Columbia University School of Journalism.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Rhea Rissman; a daughter, Geraldine, of Manhattan, and a sister, Dorothy Margolis of Springfield, Mass.
