        Richard Foth Ferris, a management consultant, died on Monday at his home in Darien, Conn. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Ferris died of a heart attack, a funeral-home representative said.
        Mr. Ferris headed his own firm, Ferris Associates in Darien, for the last four years. Before that, he had been director of marketing and business strategies for the ITT Technology Corporation. Aftergraduating from Princeton University in 1951, he served in the Navy for three years. He was also a member of the Darien Housing Authority.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Ann Todarelli; a daughter, Susan Martin of Morristown, N.J.; a son, Richard, of Philadelphia; a sister, Anne Morrison of Holmdel, N.J., and a granddaughter.
