        Henry Allen Lord Mali, a New York textile executive, died on Thursday at the Yale-New Haven Hospital. He was 58 years old and lived in Manhattan and Norfolk, Conn. He died of complications from leukemia, his family said.
        Mr. Mali was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of Groton and Yale. He was vice president of H. W. T. Mali & Co., a family textile company established in 1826.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Jane Lawrence; two daughters, Adair, of Burlington, Vt., and Kate Pingeon of Boston; two sons, Taylor, of San Francisco, and Peter, of New Haven, and two brothers, Frederick and Derek, both of Manhattan.
