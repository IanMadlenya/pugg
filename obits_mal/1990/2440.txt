
        Ludwig J. Cibelli, a retired obstetrician and gynecologist, died on Monday at Nassau County Medical Center. He was 82 years old and lived in Garden City, L.I.
        Dr. Cibelli was struck by an automobile as he crossed a street near his home, his wife said.
        In recent years, he had worked for the Nassau County Health Department as a consultant on sexually transmitted diseases. Until 1980, he was in private practice in Garden City and was affiliated with Brooklyn Hospital and Hempstead General Hospital.
        Besides his wife, the former Anastasia Linardos, he is survived by a daughter, Susan Cioni of Garden City; a son, Ludwig Jr., of Lake Arrowhead, Calif.; two brothers, Francis, of Brooklyn and Alfred, of Manhattan; two sisters, Louise Cibelli of Brooklyn and Rita Bullock of Forest Hills, Queens, and four grandchildren.
