        The Rev. Robert Aaron Mayo, a priest in the Episcopal Diocese of Newark, died on Tuesday at Beth Israel Medical Center in Newark after a long illness. He was 53 years old and lived in East Orange, N.J.
        Mr. Mayo, a 1961 graduate of Yale Divinity School, was ordained as a deacon in 1962 and as a priest in 1963. He served churches in Massachusetts and Missouri and was executive officer of United Ministries of Higher Education in San Francisco and New York before becoming rector of Christ Church in East Orange in 1981.
        Most recently, he was interim rector at St. Luke's Episcopal Church in Montclair and St. Paul's Episcopal Church in Paterson.
        He is survived by his wife, Diane; a daughter, Judith Hall of Newark; a son, Peter, of Chester, N.J.; two grandchildren; four sisters, and five brothers.
