        Philip Gross, a retired construction superintendent, died yesterday at the Universal Medical Center in Plantation, Fla. He was 86 years old and had homes in Bayside, Queens, and Pembroke Pines, Fla.
        Mr. Gross supervised construction at public schools and other sites in the New York metropolitan region, including Shea Stadium. He was a retired commander in the Navy Reserve and served with the Seabees in Iceland and the South Pacific in World War II.
        Mr. Gross graduated from the Stevens Institute of Technology in 1923.
        Surviving is his wife, Elizabeth.
