        Royal Thomas Guernsey 3d, a partner in Chez Panisse, the highly successful restaurant in Berkeley, Calif., died of AIDS last Friday at his home in Berkeley. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. Guernsey was born in Kansas City, Mo., attended Claremont College and was a Peace Corps volunteer in Panama before moving to Berkeley.
        He began work at Chez Panisse as a bartender and rose to president of the board of directors, a position he held until his death.
        Survivors include his mother, two brothers and two sisters.
