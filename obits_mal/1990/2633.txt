
        Samuel Ranhand, a professor of economics at Baruch College in Manhattan for nearly 40 years, died on Tuesday at North Shore Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 75 years old and lived in Jackson Heights, Queens.
        He died of heart failure, his wife said.
        Dr. Ranhand is survived by his wife, the former Ethel Sinowitz; a daughter, Jori Lyn of Queens; and two brothers, Max, of Queens, and Harry, of East Northport, L.I.
