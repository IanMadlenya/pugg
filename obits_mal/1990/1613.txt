        Dr. Joseph E. Wisan, professor emeritus of history at City College, died on Monday at New York Infirmary-Beekman Downtown Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Brooklyn. He died of complications after stomach surgery, the university said.
        Professor Wisan was chairman of the college's history department from 1949 to 1965. He joined City College in 1922, the year he graduated with honors as a member of Phi Beta Kappa. He received his doctorate from Columbia in 1934.
        From 1929 to 1936 he coached the City College tennis team.
        He was born in Brooklyn. His wife, the former Edith Fox, died in November.
