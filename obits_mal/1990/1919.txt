        Dr. Joseph Schwartzman, professor emeritus of pediatrics at New York Medical College, died on Wednesday at his home in Brooklyn. He was 84 years old.
        Dr. Schwartzman died of stomach cancer, said his son-in-law, Dr. Claude Brachfeld of Denver.
        He joined the New York Medical College faculty in the mid-1920's and retired in 1971. He also maintained a private practice in Brooklyn until two months ago.
        He was born in Bessarabia, now a part of the Soviet Union, in 1905, and came to the United States at the age of 3. He was a graduate of New York Medical College.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Evelyn Barsky; two daughters, Betsy Brachfeld of Denver, and Joni Finkel of Scottsdale, Ariz.; three brothers, Larry, of Turnersville, N.J., Jack, of Hampton, Va., and Max, of Brooklyn, and four grandchildren.
