
       Allen Barke, former chief executive of the British subsidiary of Ford, died on Monday. He was 86 years old. The cause of his death was not given. Mr. Barke became director of Ford's product division in 1959 when the company's plants were being increasingly hit by wildcat strikes. In 1965 he contracted hepatitis, and soon after his health forced him to resign.
