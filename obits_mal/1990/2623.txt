
        David H. Moses, a lawyer who served as general counsel to the Town of Ramapo in Rockland County, N.Y., died on Wednesday in Palm Beach Gardens, Fla., where he lived in retirement. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Moses was born in Stamford, Conn., and was a graduate of the New York University School of Law. He was senior partner in the firm of Lexow & Jenkins in Suffern, N.Y., before he retired in 1976.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ida Katz; a daughter, Dr. Lois Shofer of Owings Mills, Md., a son, Ken, of Madison, N.J.; four grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
