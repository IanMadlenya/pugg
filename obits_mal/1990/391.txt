        William Quirk Mooney, a former vice president of the Shell Chemical Company, a division of the Shell Oil Company, died on Jan. 25 at his home in Kent, Conn. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Mooney joined Shell Oil as a clerk in 1929. He was later responsible for marketing industrial chemicals. He was manager of trade relations for Shell Chemical from 1960 to 1966, when he was named a vice president. Mr. Mooney retired in 1969. He was born in Fall River, Mass.  Surviving are three sons, William, of New City, N.Y., David, of Glen Mills, Pa., and Richard, of Cleveland, Tenn.; a daughter, Ruth McCurry of Burnsville, N.C., and six grandchildren.
