        Frederick C. Abdelnour, former owner and president of Frederick's of Greenwich, a Connecticut clothing store for women, died of Parkinson's disease on Tuesday at a nursing home in Rye Brook, N.Y. He was 79 and lived in Mamaroneck, N.Y.
        Mr. Abdelnour, a native of White Plains, founded the store in 1935. He retired in 1981. The store has since gone out of business.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lila Haddad; a son, William, of Rocky Hill, Conn., two daughters, Beverly Landsiedel of Stamford, Conn., and Bonita Verbitsky of Manhattan; a brother, Edward, of Fort Lauderdale, Fla.; a sister, Helen Hadad of Greenwich, and four grandchildren.
