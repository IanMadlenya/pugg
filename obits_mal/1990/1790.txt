        John T. Kolody, retired president of St. Barnabas Hospital, died on Saturday at St. Agnes Hospital in White Plains. He was 70 years old and lived in Whitestone, Queens.
        He died of prostate cancer, his family said.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mildred Secky, and a son, John Jr. of Manhattan.
