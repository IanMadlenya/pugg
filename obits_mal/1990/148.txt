        Bernard J. Ferguson, a lawyer and former president of the Queens County Bar Association, died on Saturday at Mary Immaculate Hospital. He was 88 years old and lived in Bayside, Queens, and had previously been a resident of Flushing for more than 60 years.
        Mr. Ferguson was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of St. Francis College and Fordham Law School. He headed the Queens Bar Association in 1942 and 1943 and was vice president of the New York State Bar Association from 1967 to 1970.
        He is survived by a sister, Mary, of Flushing, and a brother, Eugene, of Douglaston.
        Correction:  January 30, 1990, Tuesday, Late Edition - Final
        An obituary headline yesterday about a former president of the Queens County Bar Association misidentified him in some copies. He was Bernard J. Ferguson. A corrected obituary appears today on page B6.
