        Patrick R. Fiorilli, the former mayor who organized annual dandelion and eggplant festivals in Vineland, died at his home here on Sunday. He was 59 years old.
        Mr. Fiorilli was mayor from 1976 to 1984. He suffered from diabetes and was hospitalized for two months after the amputation of his right leg in January 1989.
        There are no immediate survivors.
