        Richard E. Goodman, an advertising executive, died on Tuesday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 62 years old and lived in Chatham Township, N.J.
        He died of complications of asthma, a funeral home representative said.
        A graduate of Rutgers University, Mr. Goodman had his own advertising agency, Dick Goodman Concepts and Executions, in Chatham Township since 1983. Before that, he was executive vice president and creative director at Dancer Fitzgerald Sample, a division of Saachi & Saachi of Manhattan. Previosusly, he was with Ogilvy & Mather and Grey Advertising.
        He is survived by his wife, Susan; a son, Todd, and a daughter, Lee, all of Chatham Township; and a brother.
