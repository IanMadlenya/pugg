        Dr. Carl Goldmark Jr., an obstetrician and gynecologist and a former president of the New York State Medical Society, died Friday at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan. He was 80 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Goldmark had been associated with Lenox Hill since graduating from the Long Island College of Medicine in 1933. He was treasurer of the State Medical Society from 1968 to 1976 and became president in 1977. He was also president of the New York County Medical Society in 1965-66 and a member of the American College of Obstetrics and Gynecology for many years.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Hazel Seligman; two daughters, Virginia Koehler of St. David's, Pa., and Carolyn Goodman of Las Vegas; seven grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
