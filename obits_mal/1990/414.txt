        Dr. Donald J. Leahy, chief of obstetrics and gynecology at the Flushing Hospital and Medical Center in Queens from 1974 to 1985, died of pneumonia on Friday at St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn, L.I. He was 66 years old and lived in Douglaston, Queens.
        Dr. Leahy was a former president of the Queens Gynecological Society and a governor of the State University of New York campus at Stony Brook, L.I. He was a native of Astoria, Queens, a graduate of Fordham and Cornell universities and the New York Medical College and interned at St. Vincent's and Flushing hospitals. In World War II, he served in the Navy.
        Surviving are his wife, Carol; four sons, Donald J. Jr., Gregory, Paul and Patrick, and two daughters, Kelly and Cameron, all of Queens and Nassau counties; a brother, State Supreme Court Justice John J. Leahy of Astoria, and a grandson.
