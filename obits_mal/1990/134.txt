        Ned N. Fleming, former chairman and chief executive of the Fleming Companies Inc., a food distributing concern based in Oklahoma, died Wednesday at a hospital in Phoenix, Ariz. He was 90 years and had homes in Paradise Valley, Ariz., and Topeka.
        In 1921 Mr. Fleming joined the company, which his father and two partners started in Topeka in 1915. In 1964 Mr. Fleming became chairman and chief executive.
        He was a former president of the National American Wholesale Grocers' Association.
        He is survived by a daughter, Marilyn Harrison of Oklahoma City; two sons, James B. of Tucson, Ariz., and Stephen M. of Comanche, Tex., and 11 grandchildren.
