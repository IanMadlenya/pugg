        Robert L. Kaufman, former president of Mollie Parnis Inc. and a former president of the New York Fashion Council, died on Thursday of complications from lung cancer at his home in Manhattan. He was 63 years old.
        Mr. Kaufman was born in Haverstraw, N.Y., graduated from Middlebury College and served in the Navy in World War II. He was a sales manager for Jerry Silverman Inc., which became known in the 1960's for quality ready-to-wear outfits, before joining Miss Parnis's dress- and coat-making company in 1970. He headed the Fashion Council from 1970 to 1980.
        He is survived by two sisters, Dorothy Dvortzoff, of Manhattan, and Ruth Berthold, of Old Tappan, N.J., and a nephew, David Berthold.
