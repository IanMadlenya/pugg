        Edward Moor, an acting teacher and actor, died of cancer on Feb. 19 at his home in Manhattan. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Moor acted on Broadway with Alfred Lunt and Lynn Fontanne in ''The Visit'' and ''The Great Sebastians'' and with John Gielgud in ''Much Ado About Nothing.''
        He taught with Sanford Meisner at the Neighborhood Playhouse School of the Theater and established his own acting school in 1973. Among his Playhouse students were Diane Keaton and Karen Black.
        He is survived by a brother, Alvin, of New York.
