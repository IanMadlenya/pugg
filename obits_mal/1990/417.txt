        Henry D. Caruthers, president of Caruthers Consulting, a management, marketing and sales development concern, died of complications after surgery yesterday at St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn, L.I. He was 65 years old and lived in Sands Point, L.I.
        Mr. Caruthers founded the firm in 1972. He had been a vice president of sales and marketing of Pan American World Airways and a vice president of Golightly International, a management-consulting concern.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Marilyn Mayekawa; three children from a former marriage, a son, Gavin, of Manhattan, and two daughters, Laurie Vickerson of Bradenton, Fla., and Bonnie, of Sands Point; two children from his present marriage, a son, Bruce, and a daughter, Lisa, both of Sands Point, and a brother, Felix, of Three Rivers, Calif.
