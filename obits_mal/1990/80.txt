        Dominick Lamanna, a physics teacher at the Bronx High School of Science who worked with students in independent research, including helping them prepare entries for the Westinghouse Science Talent Search, was killed on Wednesday in an automobile accident on the Taconic State Parkway. He was 42 years old and lived in Poughkeepsie, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Patricia Gordon, and three daughters, Tina, Paula and Giulianna.
