        Dr. Terence C. Langetieg, a vice president in the research department at Salomon Brothers Inc., died of cancer yesterday at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 41 years old and lived in New Providence, N.J.
        Dr. Langetieg joined Salomon Brothers in 1985 after 10 years as an associate professor of finance at the University of Southern California, where he also directed the Ph.D. program. He was educated at the University of Wisconsin at Madison, where he earned degrees in finance, including a Ph.D. He was a member of the Western Finance Association and he wrote articles for professional publications.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara; two sons, Kristofor and Patrick; a daughter, Erica; his parents, Magdalen and Merlin, of Ponte Vedra Beach, Fla.; two sisters, Karen Reed of Fox Pointe, Wis., and Kendra, of Bloomington, Ind.
