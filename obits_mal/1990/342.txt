        William H. Nelson, president of the Dallas Gay Alliance from 1984 to 1987, died of complications from AIDS on Tuesday. He was 40 years old.
        He is credited with transforming the organization from a political group into a broad-based provider of community service with an annual budget of more than $750,000.
        The Texas Senate honored Mr. Nelson in 1989 with a resolution citing his accomplishments as a community leader. He also received the Texas Human Rights Foundation's Robert Schwab Award for community service and the United Our Way Humanitarian Award.
