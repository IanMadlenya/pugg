
        Charles Rubenstein, a retired lawyer, died on Dec. 19 at the Somerset Medical Center in Somerville, N.J. He was 95 years old and lived in Cranford, N.J.
        Mr. Rubenstein was senior partner in the Cranford firm of Rubenstein and Glick when he retired in 1978. He had practiced for 60 years in Cranford, Bayonne and Jersey City.
        He was a native New Yorker and graduated from New York University Law School in 1918.
        A widower, he is survived by a daughter, Muriel Blum of Cranford; three brothers, William, of Sunrise, Fla., and Herbert and Irving, both of Bayonne; four grandchildren, and seven great-grandchildren.
