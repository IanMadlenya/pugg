        Vincent T. Malone, a retired senior vice president of the Manhattan stockbrokerage firm of Pershing & Company, died of cancer on Friday at his home in Monmouth Beach, N.J. He was 64 years old.
        Mr. Malone was a native of Jersey City and was a partner and executive of the brokerage house, a division of Donaldson, Lufkin & Jennrette Inc., for 32 years. He was a former president and a longtime board member of the Channel Club Tower Association of Monmouth Beach condominiums.
        Surviving are his wife, Edna; a son, Thomas, of West Hampton, L.I.; two daughters, Kathleen Earle of Newburyport, Mass., and Joyce Salvatori of Middletown, N.J.; a brother, James, of Bogota, N.J.; a sister, Mary Schmitt of Maywood, N.J., and seven grandchildren.
