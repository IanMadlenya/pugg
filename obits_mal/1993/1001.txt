
        John Robert Castro, an electronic communications specialist and retired agent of the Central Intelligence Agency, died on Sunday at the HCA Reston Hospital in Reston, Va. He was 71 and lived in Reston.
        His son Frank said the cause was a heart attack.
        His marriage to Margarita A. Castro ended in divorce. Besides his son Frank, of Oakton, Va., Mr. Castro is survived by two other sons, John, of Lorton, Va., and Stephen, of Herndon, Va.; a daughter, Cristina Tercero of Raleigh, N.C.; two sisters, Anne Inglese and Rose Margalino, both of the Bronx, and two grandchildren.
