
        John A. Thomas, a former Manhattan advertising executive, died Nov. 23 in his home in San Jose, Calif. He was 85. The cause of death was a heart attack, said Judith Naughton, his daughter.
        Mr. Thomas retired in 1967 after spending 30 years on Madison Avenue with Batten, Barton, Durstin & Osborne, then Benton & Bowles, and finally Ogilvy, Benson & Mather.
        He is survived by his wife, Beatrice Thomas of San Jose, and Mrs. Naughton of Los Gatos, Calif.
