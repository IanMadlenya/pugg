
        Peter Henderson, owner of the Peter Henderson Oil Company of New York and Houston and an avid polo player, died on Saturday at his home in Locust Valley, L.I. He was 69.
        The cause was cancer, said his daughter, Melissa Henderson.
        Mr. Henderson founded the business in 1955 after working for several other oil companies. The Peter Henderson Oil Company is an oil and gas exploration and production company that operates predominantly in Texas and Louisiana. For the last 25 years Mr. Henderson had played polo with the Meadowbrook Club.
        Mr. Henderson is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; five daughters, Amanda Cannell of Bethesda, Md., Sarah Caviston of Essex, Mass., Elizabeth Coleman of Charlottesville, Va., Tink Mortimer and Melissa Henderson, both of Locust Valley; one son, Peter Henderson Jr. of Brookville, L.I.; four sisters, Suzanne Erickson of Norton, Mass., Cecily Pennoyer of Locust Valley, Deirdre Henderson of Cambridge, Mass., and Jane Maynard of Manhattan, and two brothers, John, of Essex, Mass., and Michael, of Houston, and 13 grandchildren.
