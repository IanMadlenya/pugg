
        Howard Osborne, a former sales executive in the fashion industry, died on Sunday at the Danbury Hospital in Danbury, Conn., where he lived. He was 47.
        His family said he had been ill for a long time.
        Mr. Osborne was the national sales director for the Carolyn Roehm company from 1990-92. Before that he worked in sales for Geoffrey Beene, Akira Fashions, Hanae Mori U.S.A. and Oscar de la Renta Neckwear.
        He was a native of Concord, Calif.
        Mr. Osborne's survivors include his companion, Rene Acosta of Manhattan; his parents, Jeanne and Robert, and a brother, Randy, all of Concord.
