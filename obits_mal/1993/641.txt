
        Dr. Guy Scull Hayes, an international public health specialist and former associate director of the health sciences division of the Rockefeller Foundation, died on May 19 at his home in North Brooklin, Me. He was 81.
        Dr. Hayes, a graduate of Harvard Medical School, joined the Rockefeller Foundation in 1943 and worked in Brazil, Iran, Iraq and Colombia. He returned to New York in 1968 to become the foundation's associate director of health sciences, and retired in 1977.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy Hitching Barbour; three daughters, Lucie Semler of Barcelona, Spain, Anna Connard of Manhattan, and Priscilla Taylor of Pacific Palisades, Calif.; three sons, Guy H., of Winsted, Conn., Edward B., of Atlanta, and Bartlett H. 2d, of San Antonio, and nine grandchildren.
