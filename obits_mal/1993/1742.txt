
        Dr. Jerome A. Marks, a retired gastroenterologist and associate professor of clinical medicine at New York University Medical Center, died at his home in Manhattan on Sunday. He was 94.
        Dr. Marks was born in New York City. He graduated from Columbia College in 1921 and from the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University in 1925. He was in private practice in New York City for nearly 50 years, during which he served as director of medicine at Gouverneur Hospital, associated attending physician at Bellevue Hospital and consulting physician at Beekman Downtown Hospital. He taught at N.Y.U. Medical Center before and after his retirement in 1976.
        He is survived by his wife, Cora.
