
        Alfred L. Pilson, retired treasurer and chief financial officer of the Simplicity Pattern Company Inc., died on Sunday in Somers, N.Y. He was 83.
        His family said he had been ill for a long time.
        Mr. Pilson was born in New York City. He graduated from New York University's School of Commerce. He was employed at Price Waterhouse and Company for 18 years. He joined Joseph E. Seagram & Sons in 1953 as assistant treasurer. In 1955, he joined Simplicity, which produces patterns for home sewing. He retired at the age of 67.
        He is survived by his wife, Hilda; a son, Neal, and a daughter, Susan; five grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
