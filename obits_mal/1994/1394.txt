
        George Moskowitz, a labor lawyer and arbitrator who served on a number of statewide panels in New York, died Nov. 2 in Beth Israel Hospital North in Manhattan. He was 87 and lived in Manhattan.
        The cause was heart failure, said his daughter, Victoria Moskowitz of Brookline, Mass.
        He is also survived by his wife, Nell; a son, Steven, of Portland, Ore.; a stepson, Michael Fleischer of Ann Arbor, Mich., and three grandchildren.
