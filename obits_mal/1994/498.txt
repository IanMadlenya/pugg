
        Robert E. Scannell, the corporate secretary of A.T. & T., died on Monday at Mountainside Hospital in Montclair, N.J. Mr. Scannell, who lived in Montclair, was 54.
        The cause was cancer, his family said.
        Mr. Scannell became corporate secretary of the communications company in 1987. He joined the New York Telephone Company as a lawyer in 1971 and the A.T. & T. law department in 1979.
        Mr. Scannell graduated from the College of Holy Cross and in 1964 received a law degree from Columbia University.
        He is survived by his wife, Joan, and two daughters, Kathleen and Nancy, all of Montclair; his father, Daniel T., of Jamaica Estates, Queens, the vice chairman of the New York Metropolitan Transportation Authority; his mother, Gertrude, also of Jamaica Estates; two sisters, Maureen Bateman of Manhattan and Joan McMurry of Short Hills, N.J., and a brother, Daniel T. Jr., of Jamaica Estates.
