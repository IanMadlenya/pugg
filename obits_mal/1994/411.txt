
        Joseph Watkins, a former director of New York State's Municipal Service Division, died on March 9 at his home in Albany. He was 84.
        The cause was cardiac arrest, according to the Levine Memorial Chapel in Albany.
        Born in New York City, he graduated from Cooper Union Institute of Technology with a degree in electrical engineering. He spent 37 years in the state government, many of them in the Department of Civil Service. He directed the Municipal Service Division from 1969 until his retirement in 1977.
        Mr. Watkins is survived by a sister, Mildred Erdheim of Royal Oak Beach, Fla.
