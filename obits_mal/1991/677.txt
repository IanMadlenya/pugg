
        Msgr. James V. Hart, pastor emeritus of the Blessed Sacrament Roman Catholic Church in the Bronx and a New York priest for 67 years, died on Wednesday at Our Lady of Mercy Medical Center in the Bronx. He was 93 years old and lived at St. Patrick's Nursing Home in the Bronx.
        Monsignor Hart was the pastor of Blessed Sacrament from 1959 to 1973, when he retired. Earlier he had been pastor of Our Lady of Lourdes Church and St. Paul's Church, both in Manhattan.
        There are no immediate survivors.
