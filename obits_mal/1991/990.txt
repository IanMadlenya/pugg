
        Robert A. Lowe, a lawyer who specialized in adoption cases, died on Thursday at Cabrini Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 43 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of a heart attack, said a colleague, Lucille Rosenstock.
        A 1970 graduate of Colgate University, Mr. Lowe was the stage manager at the Roundabout Theater and at the Vivian Beaumont Theater from 1971 to 1979. He graduated from New York Law School in 1983 and was admitted to the bar the next year. He was a partner in the firm of Rosenstock, Lowe & Nichols.
        He is survived by his mother, Lee, of Enfield, Conn., and a sister, Jane, of Northampton, Mass.
