
        Paul A. Roberts, a personnel consultant in Manhattan and past president of the Association of Personnel Consultants of New York State, died on Tuesday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 54 years old and lived in Manhattan and Stanfordville, N.Y.
        He died of cancer, said a colleague, Jules Beitler.
        Mr. Roberts was president of Roberts-Lund Personnel Ltd. of Manhattan, with which he had had a 27-year association. He was born in Manhattan and attended Wayne State University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Joyce Hutton; two daughters, Kelly, of Manhattan and Jennifer, of Chappaqua, N.Y., and a brother, Arnold Halper of Lakewood, N.J.
