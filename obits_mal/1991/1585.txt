
        Msgr. Raymond E. Blust, who taught at Cardinal Hayes High School in the Bronx for 30 years and who later was pastor of St. Joseph's Church in Wurtsboro, N.Y., died on Monday at the Pope John Paul II residence for priests in Manhattan, where he lived. He was 80 years old.
        Monsignor Blust was trained for the priesthood at St. Joseph's Seminary in Yonkers and spent his career in the New York archdiocese. He retired in 1982.
        There are no immediate survivors.
