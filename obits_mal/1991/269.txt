
        Joseph A. Guarino, a lawyer who had been a member of the Town Board in North Hempstead, L.I., since 1981, died on Wednesday at his home in Port Washington. He was 44 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, a spokeswoman for the Nassau County Medical Examiner said.
        Mr. Guarino was a partner in the law firm of Martin, Van de Walle, Guarino & Donahue in Great Neck. From 1978 through 1981, he was the North Hempstead town attorney, then he was elected to the Town Board. A Republican, he was named majority leader early in 1990, but resigned soon after because of poor health.
        Mr. Guarino is survived by a daughter, Carly, of Eastchester, N.Y.; a son, Joseph Jr., of West Islip, L.I.; his parents, Frank and Josephine Guarino of Mineola, L.I., and a brother, Thomas, of Huntington, L.I.
