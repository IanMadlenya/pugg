
        Thomas Duffy Zumbo, a public relations executive and former city editor for United Press International, was found dead yesterday in his apartment in Manhattan. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Zumbo joined the United Press in the early 1950's and left in 1974 to enter public relations. He was for a time an associate editor of The New York City Tribune.
        He was a former president of the New York City Press Club. His late brother, Paul, was a reporter for The Daily News.
        Surviving is a sister, Vera Kenworthy, of Pearl River, N.Y.
