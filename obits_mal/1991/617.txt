
        Robert M. Edelstein, a professor of marketing and public policy at Adelphi University, died yesterday at Beth Israel Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 48 years old and lived in Setauket, L.I.
        He died of cancer of the small intestine, said his brother Stewart, of Southport, Conn.
        A 1971 Ph.D. graduate of Columbia University, Dr. Edelstein joined the faculty at Adelphi in 1976 and was named a full professor in 1979. He also taught at Columbia University, Long Island University, Cornell University and Beersheva University in Israel.
        He is survived by his wife, Rivcka; a son, Roy, of Cambridge, Mass.; a daughter, Ruth, of Worcester, Mass.; his parents, Clinton and Mollye Edelstein of Rochester; and two other brothers, Edward, of Seattle, and Burton, of New London, Conn.
