
        Dave Morrah, known for his humorous articles and cartoons in The Saturday Evening Post from 1946 to 1962, died on Friday. He was 77 years old.
        Best known for his mock German style, he wrote for Collier's, Look and American Magazine as well as The Saturday Evening Post. Mr. Morrah's parody of "A Visit From St. Nicholas" was read annually on Arthur Godfrey's radio show.
        He also wrote nine books, fiction and nonfiction, including "Our Honor the Mayor."
        He is survived by his wife, Patricia, and a daughter, Elizabeth.
