
        Willard S. Elsbree, an educator, died on Tuesday at Kendall at Longwood, a retirement community in Kennett Square, Pa. He was 93 years old.
        Mr. Elsbree graduated from Swarthmore College in 1922. He first taught at the Pennsylvania Military College in Chester, Pa., before becoming the research director for the public school system in Montclair, N.J.
        He became a professor of education at Teachers College of Columbia University, where he received his Ph.D. He was affiliated with Teachers College until he retired in 1963.
        Mr. Elsbree was the author of "The American Teacher," a history of teaching in the United States.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth.
