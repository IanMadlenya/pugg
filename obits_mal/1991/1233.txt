
        Michael Randazzo, an advertising executive for about 20 years, died on June 4 at Benedictine Hospital in Kingston, N.Y. He was 49 years old and lived in Manhattan and in Woodstock, N.Y.
        He died of pancreatic cancer, a family spokesman said.
        Mr. Randazzo was president of Michael Randazzo Advertising, an agency he formed in 1988 with offices on Madison Avenue and in Woodstock.
        He is survived by his wife, Susan; two children from a previous marriage, Nicole Shaw of Queens and David, of Leipsic, Ohio; his mother, Sarah Randazzo of Queens; two brothers, Peter, of Queens, and Jay, of Atlanta, and one grandchild.
