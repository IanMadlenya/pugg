
        Charles Milton Preston, a retired associate general counsel with the Metropolitan Life Insurance Company, died on Thursday at the Veterans Administration Hospital in East Orange, N.J. He was 73 years old and lived in Middletown, N.J.
        He died of heart failure, his wife, Marjorie, said.
        A native of Brooklyn, Mr. Preston joined Metropolitan Life in 1948 and retired in 1981. In the mid-1960's he established the law department at the company's West Coast headquarters in San Francisco.
        Besides his widow, he is survived by two daughters, Dr. Allyson Preston of Marblehead, Mass., and Frederica, of Salem, Mass.; two brothers, William H., of Hopkinton, Mass., and Russell J., of Ogunquit, Me., and two granddaughters.
