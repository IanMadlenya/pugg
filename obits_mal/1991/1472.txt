
        Jeremiah Moynihan, a real-estate executive in New York and Dallas, died on Monday at his home in Stillwater, N.J. He was 52 years old.
        He died of malignant melanoma, according to the Smith-McCracken Funeral Home of Newton, N.J.
        Mr. Moynihan was born in Brooklyn and was a graduate of St. John's University. He was president of Henry S. Miller, Grubb & Ellis of Dallas and was a former senior vice president of the New York Land Company.
        His wife, Elizabeth, died in 1977. He is survived by a son, William, of Stillwater; two daughters, Colleen, of Dallas and Susan, of Stillwater, and his mother, Anne Clark of Brooklyn.
