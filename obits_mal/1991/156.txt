
        Albert Saley, a librarian who was president of several library associations, died Jan. 13 at Morristown (N.J.) Memorial Hospital. He was 63 years old and lived in Blairstown, N.J.
        He died of cancer, a family spokeswoman said.
        Mr. Saley was the librarian at Mountain Lakes High School for the last 30 years and director of the Manville Public Library for 26 years. He was a 1964 graduate of Columbia University.
        He was a past president of the Morris County School Librarian Association, the New Jersey Educational Media Association and the Society of School Librarians International. Last year, the New Jersey State Library Association honored him with its leadership award.
        He is survived by his mother, Latefa; and a sister, Julia, both of Livonia, Mich.
