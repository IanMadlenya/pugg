
        Arno W. Mueller, a former executive of Storer Communications who was a pioneer in cable television, died Sunday of complications from pneumonia. He was 64 years old.
        Mr. Mueller became president of Storer Cable Communications Division in 1979. The division had 1.5 million subscribers by the time he left the company in 1983. He became a consultant.
        He is survived by his wife, Beverly; three daughters, a son and two grandchidlren.
