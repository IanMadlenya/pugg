
        Marion J. Wise Jr., an investment adviser, died on Friday at the St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan. He was 82 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He died of heart failure, a family spokesman said.
        Until recently he was president of Marion J. Wise Associates, a concern he founded in 1957. He had previously been a vice president in the investment department of the United States Trust Company of New York and an economist with the National Association of Manufacturers.
        Mr. Wise graduated from Amherst College in 1931 and founded the Wise Fund for the Fine Arts at the college. He was a former board member of the New York Osteopathic Hospital.
        There are no immediate survivors.
