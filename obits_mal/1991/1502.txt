
        Henri Bourrelly, an actor who performed under the name Rellys in several films directed by Marcel Pagnol, died today in St. Marguerite Hospital here. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Bourelly was a member of the Pagnol stock company that included Raimu, Fernandel, Pierre Fresnay and others. His best-known role was that of Father Gaucher in Pagnol's "Lettres de Mon Moulin" ("Letters From My Windmill") in 1954.
        A native of Marseilles, he started his career in Provence as a dance-hall singer.
