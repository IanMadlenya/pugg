
        John B. Matthews, a retired professor of business administration at Harvard, died Sunday at Mount Auburn Hospital in Cambridge, Mass. He was 69 years old and lived in Belmont, Mass.
        His death was caused by progressive heart disease, his family said.
        Professor Matthews, an expert in business ethics, joined the school's faculty in 1949, rose to be its Joseph C. Wilson Professor of Business Administration and retired last summer.
        He is survived by his wife, Margaret; two daughters, Martha Matthews Reiger of Washington and Laurel Matthews of Stamford, Conn.; two stepdaughters, Gail Word of El Paso and Joan Burt of Ruidoso, N.M.; and a sister, Elizabeth Shaw of Oxford, Maine.
