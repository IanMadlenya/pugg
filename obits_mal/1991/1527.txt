
        Charles Jurrist, a dance critic for The Daily News, died on June 23 at his home in Greenwich Village. He was 46 years old.
        The cause of death was not disclosed.
        Mr. Jurrist, who was born in New York City, graduated from Columbia College, where he was awarded the Kellett Fellowship for two years of study at Oxford University.
        He worked as a lawyer for the firm of Dewey, Ballantine, Bushby, Palmer & Wood in New York, leaving in the early 1970's to write about the arts.
        He is survived by his mother, Blanche, and a brother Larry, both of North Miami Beach, Fla.
