
        Owen D. Nee, assistant general counsel for R. H. Macy & Company from 1961 to 1980, died on Monday at the Plantation Estates retirement community in Charlotte, N.C., where he lived. He was 78 years old and had lived in Bristol, Conn., and Rye, N.Y., for many years before moving to Seabrook Island, S.C., in 1980.
        He is survived by two sons, Owen Jr., of Hong Kong and John, of Charlotte, and four grandchildren.
