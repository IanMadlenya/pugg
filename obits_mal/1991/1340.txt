
        Louis Fares, a Syrian journalist who was a correspondent for The Associated Press in Damascus for 20 years, died of a heart attack today. He was 48 years old.
        Mr. Fares complained of severe chest pains and then collapsed in his office. He died as he was being taken to Al-Shami Hospital.
        In addition to his work for the A.P., Mr. Fares was a correspondent for the Japanese news agency Kyodo, for theParis-based Radio Monte Carlo and for several European news agencies and Arabic publications. He was also formerly a part-time reporter for The New York Times.
        He is survived by his wife, Helen Ajji, and twin sons, Elias and Antoine, aged 13.
