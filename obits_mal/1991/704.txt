
        Dr. Kendall Stearns, a physician and surgeon in Hudson, N.Y., for more than 40 years, died on Wednesday at Exeter Hospital in New Hampshire. He was 75 years old and had moved to Exeter about a year ago.
        He died after surgery for lung cancer, his son-in-law Patrick O'Donnell said.
        Dr. Stearns, a 1940 graduate of the College of Physicians and Surgeons at Columbia University, was affiliated with Columbia Memorial Hospital in Hudson and was a member of the Catamount Ski Patrol in Hillsdale, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, Kate; two daughters, Kendra Stearns O'Donnell, principal of Phillips Exeter Academy in Exeter, and Dr. Gretchen Stearns of Claverack, N.Y., a physician; a son, Peter, of Montvale, N.J., an executive; two brothers, John, of Hanover, N.H., a retired executive, and Malcolm, of Haddam, Conn., a dealer in rare silver, and seven grandchildren.
