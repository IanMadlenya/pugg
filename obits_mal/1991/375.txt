
        Charles G. Adsit, a doctor who practiced in Manhattan for more than four decades, died on Jan. 25 in South Orleans, Mass., where he lived in retirement. He was 75 years old.
        Dr. Adsit was born in Atlanta and was a graduate of Yale University. He received his medical degree from the College of Physicians and Surgeons of Columbia University. He joined the staff of St. Luke's Hospital in 1942 and later served as its chairman of pharmacy and formulary.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Priscilla Amberson; a daughter, Suzanne, of East Hardwick, Vt., and a granddaughter.
