
        George Elrick Cummings, a professional squash player and later a coach, died on Friday in Bennington, Vt. He was 92 years old.
        Mr. Cummings's daughter, Judy Cotter, said he had died after a short illness.
        From 1919 until he retired in 1965, Mr. Cummings was the squash professional at the University Club in New York. He won four national doubles titles and twice was a finalist in professional and open national singles tournaments.
        He is survived by his wife, Edna, his daughter and three grandchildren.
