
        Edward K. Bryant, a retired partner in the engineering firm of Tippetts-Abbett-McCarthy-Stratton, died on Monday in Washington. He was 89 years old and lived in Chevy Chase, Md.
        He died of a heart attack, his family said.
        Mr. Bryant joined Tippetts-Abbett-McCarthy-Stratton in 1946 after World War II service in the Navy's Civil Engineer Corps in the South Pacific. He became a partner in the engineering firm in 1956 and retired in 1972.
        He is survived by his wife, Tamara; two sons, Edward A. Bryant of New York City, and John, of White River Junction, Vt.; four grandchlldren and two great-grandchildren.
