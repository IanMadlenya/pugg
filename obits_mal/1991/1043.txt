
        John F. Phillips, a Manhattan restaurateur who owned Frankie and Johnny's steakhouse in the theater district for several decades, died on Tuesday at Winthrop University Hospital in Mineola, L.I. He was 76 years old and lived in Garden City, L.I.
        He died five days after suffering a stroke, his family said.
        Mr. Phillips is survived by his wife, the former Rosanne Herron; a son, John Jr., of Manhattan; five daughters, Madeline Blaufuss of Phoenix, Maria Bryan of Garden City, Joan Phillips-Sandy of Waterville, Me., Marguerite Phillips-Phillips of Austin, Tex., and Angela Cody of Leonardo, N.J., and 14 grandchildren.
