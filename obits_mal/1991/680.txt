
        Patrick J. Gilligan, president of Valley Industries Inc., a steel company based in St. Louis, died on Monday at his home in St. Louis Hills, Mo. He was 64 years old.
        He died of a heart attack, a spokesman for Valley Industries said.
        Mr. Gilligan, a native of Galway, Ireland, came to the United States in 1948 and earned a degree in accounting at St. Louis University. In 1951 he joined a subsidiary of Valley Industries. He became president of the parent concern in 1973.
        He is survived by his wife, Peggy; two daughters, Kathleen Gilligan and Ann Hancock, both of St. Louis; six sons, Francis, James and Shawn, all of St. Louis, Patrick Jr. of Pacific, Mo., Mark, of Oakville, Mo., and Michael, of Fort Collins, Colo.; two brothers, Michael and Francis, both of Galway; a sister, Mary Brennan, also of Galway, and 11 grandchildren.
