
        E. Everett Smith, a retired director of McKinsey & Company, a management consulting concern, died on Friday at his home in Southport, Conn. He was 79 years old and also lived in Manhattan.
        He died of arteriosclerosis, a family spokeswoman said.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Ethel Clark; a son, E. Everett 3d, of Southport; a daughter, Susan Large of Ashland, Mass.; a stepson, Robert H. Clark of Brooklyn; a sister, Theodora Bury of Cornwall, Conn., and four grandchildren.
