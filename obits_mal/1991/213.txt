
        Philip K. Hills Jr., a senior partner in the New York law firm of Satterlee Stephens Burke & Burke, died on Monday at his home in Oyster Bay, L.I. He was 58 years old.
        He died of liver cancer, a family spokesman said.
        Mr. Hill joined the law firm in 1956 and specialized in energy and corporate law.
        He was born in Brussels and graduated from Hamilton College and the Columbia University School of Law.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Catherine Baratoff; his mother, Helene W. Meyendorff of Oyster Bay; a sister, Daria Kerdek-Vegas of Caracas, Venezuela, and two sons, Philip K. 3d., and Peter K., both of Oyster Bay.
