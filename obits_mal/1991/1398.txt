
        Edward G. Bathon, a lawyer in Manhattan for more than 60 years, died on Tuesday at his home in Dobbs Ferry, N.Y. He was 84 years old.
        He died of cancer, a family spokeswoman said.
        Mr. Bathon, who graduated from Fordham Law School in 1931, was a partner in the firm of Regan & Barrett for his entire career.
        He is survived by three sons, Gregory, of Baltimore, Stephen, of Warrenton, Va., and Lawrence, of St. Croix, V.I.; two daughters, Judith Morris of Dobbs Ferry and Gabrielle Brown of Greensboro, N.C.; a brother, William, of Dobbs Ferry; 19 grandchildren, and 5 great-grandchildren.
