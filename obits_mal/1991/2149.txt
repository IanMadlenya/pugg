
        John J. Anglim, a retired Wall Street executive and a former member of the New York Stock Exchange's Board of Governors, died on Monday at Morristown Memorial Hospital in New Jersey. He was 82 years old and lived in Madison, N.J.
        Mr. Anglim, who was born in New York City, went directly into the securities business after graduating from high school. For 28 years he worked for W. E. Hutton & Company, and was a partner when he retired.
        He became a member of the stock exchange in 1942 and served on its board from 1964 to 1970.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Betty Smith; three sons: James, of Matawan, N.J.; John, of Scotch Plains, N.J., and William, of Roanoke, Va., and nine grandchildren.
