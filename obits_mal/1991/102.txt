
        Robert J. Wert, former president of Mills College, died at Marin General Hospital Tuesday of complications from heart disease. He was 69 years old.
        Mr. Wert became head of Mills, a women's college in Oakland, Calif., in 1967 and retired in 1976.
        He came to Mills from Stanford University, where he was vice provost and professor of education and the first dean of undergraduate education.
        He had been with the Carnegie Corporation of New York and was a former director of the Clorox Company.
        Survivors include his wife, the former Ann Beyer, two sons and a daughter.
