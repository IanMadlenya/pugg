
        Donald F. Barrow, a former executive in the oil and travel industries, died on Sunday at Huntington Hospital. He was 77 years old and lived in Cold Spring Harbor, L.I.
        He died of a stroke, his family said.
        Mr. Barrow was born in Schenectady, N.Y., and was a graduate of Bard College. He was a lieutenant commander in the Navy's air wing in World War II.
        After the war he joined Standard Oil of New Jersey, now Exxon, where he was in charge of travel arrangements. After his retirement in 1967 he was with Travel Bureau, Inc.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Constance Bruen, whom he married in 1942; two daughters, Constance Hurley of Huntington, L.I., and Elizabeth Doering of Oyster Bay, L.I., and three grandchildren.
