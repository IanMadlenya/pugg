
        Philip Kramer, a former president and chief executive of the Amerada Hess Corporation, died on Jan. 30 at Del Ray Medical Center in Del Ray Beach, Fla. He was 77.
        Mr. Kramer joined Hess, an oil refining company, in 1969 as an executive vice president. He was named president three years later and chief executive in 1982. Mr. Kramer became a company director in 1970. He retired in 1986, but remained on the board until 1993.
        He is survived by his wife, Sarah, of Boca Raton, Fla.; two daughters, Noelle Parket of Great Neck, N.Y., and Marilyn Weitzman of New York City; a son, Glenn, of Summit, N.J.; seven grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
