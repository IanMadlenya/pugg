
        Thomas H. Broadus Jr., a managing director of T. Rowe Price Associates, died Aug. 17 while vacationing in Europe. He was 60 years old and lived in Baltimore.
        Mr. Broadus joined T. Rowe Price, a leading mutual fund company, in 1966 and rose to the position of managing director.
        A graduate of Washington & Lee University and Harvard Business School, Mr. Broadus served as executive vice president of the T. Rowe Price Blue Chip Growth fund and vice president of the T. Rowe Price Equity Income Fund.
        Mr. Broadus was active in Baltimore-area civic and educational organizations.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; a son, Thomas H. Broadus 3d of Philadelphia; a daughter, Lucy Eastwood Broadus of Baltimore, and a brother, Arthur Broadus of New Haven.
