        Placido Domingo Ferrer, a baritone who was a world-famous zarzuela performer and the father of the tenor Placido Domingo, died of a heart attack on Sunday at Hospital Espanol in Mexico City. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Domingo was born in Zaragoza, Spain, on March 8, 1907, moved here in 1946 and later became a Mexican citizen. He and his wife, Pepita Embil, toured Mexico constantly with their Spanish zarzuela musical comedy troupe, the Domingo-Embil Company. He retired in the late 1960's. He is survived by his wife, his son and a daughter, Mari Pepa.
