        James B. Carsky, senior vice president of Scholastic Inc., the publishing company, died of lung cancer Saturday at his home in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 58 years old.
        Mr. Carsky, who spent 33 years in book publishing, joined Scholastic in 1978 as vice president of sales and became senior vice president last year.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Katherine Skinner; three daughters, Laura, Cecilia and Margaret; two sons, Joseph and James, and a brother, Dr. Edward Carsky.
