        Dr. Jesse Waring Mahoney, who was chief of surgery at Bellevue Hospital Center for 20 years, died of a heart attack Tuesday at University Hospital. He was 80 years old and lived in Oldfield, L.I.
        Dr. Mahoney held the post from 1948 to 1967, continuing as a consultant at University Hospital until his death. He was also director of surgery at Mather Memorial Hospital in Port Jefferson, L.I., from 1958 to 1975, and chief of surgery and trauma at St. Charles Hospital, in Port Jefferson, from 1961 to 1974.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Reeves; two daughters, Brook of Manhattan and Dr. Lisa of Port Jefferson, L.I.; two sons, Jesse Jr., of Bradenton, Fla., and Reeves, of Norfolk, Va.
