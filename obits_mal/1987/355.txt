        Jerome Seckler, a retired publishing company executive, died of cardiac arrest Sunday after undergoing bypass surgery at St. Luke's Hospital. He was 72 years old.
        Mr. Seckler, who was born in New York City, served in the United States Army during World War II. Afterward he worked for the American Book Company before joining Holt, Rinehart & Winston. He retired in the late 1970's as director of marketing services for Holt's college division.
        Mr. Seckler is survived by a son, Donald, of Lincoln, Mass.
