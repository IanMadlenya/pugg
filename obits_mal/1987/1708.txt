        Harold J. Drescher, who practiced law in New York York City ifor more than 60 years, died Oct. 5 at Beth Israel Hospital. He was 87 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A specialist in transportation law, Mr. Drescher represented the Greyhound Corporation, Carey Transportation and other transportation systems, principally bus lines. He is survived by his wife, Berta; a son, Dr. Alexander of Lanesborough, Mass., and three grandchildren.
