        Edward Willis Scripps 2d, a trustee of the Scripps Howard news organization founded by his grandfather and namesake, died Monday on a flight from Honolulu to Sydney, Australia. He was 57 years old and lived in Honolulu.
        Announcing the death, the company said Mr. Scripps had set out on a fact-finding tour of the Pacific region. There was no immediate word on the cause of death.
        A native of New York City, Mr. Scripps was the youngest of the six children of Robert Paine Scripps. He was one of three brothers serving as trustees of the E. W. Scripps Trust formed by their grandfather.
