        Ellis Khouri, a top official of the Orthodox Antiochian Church, died Saturday. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Khouri was second in command of the New York-based denomination, which has one million members and 200 churches in the United States and Canada. In the 1920's and 1930's he was a member of a group of Middle Eastern writers and poets that included Kahlil Gibran, author of ''The Prophet'' and other works.
