        Horace Adali Sawyer, a former president and chairman of the Lone Star Cement Company, now Lone Star Industries, based in Greenwich, Conn., died last Wednesday in New Orleans, where he had moved after his retirement. Mr. Sawyer was 92 years old and formerly lived in Bronxville, N.Y.
        A native of Texas, Mr. Sawyer joined Lone Star in Dallas in 1925, but moved to New York the next year. He served as president of Lone Star Cement from 1952 to 1961 and as chairman from 1959 until his retirement in 1964.
        Mr. Sawyer is survived by a son, Horace A. Jr.; a daughter, Mary Rozelle Middleton; a sister, Rozelle Hall; seven grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
