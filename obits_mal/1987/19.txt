        Dow O. Walling, a retired cartoonist who created the comic strip ''Skeets,'' died Friday at the New Rochelle Hospital Medical Center. He was 84 years old and lived in Pelham Manor.
        Mr. Walling's comic strip ran every Sunday from 1932 to 1952 in The New York Herald Tribune. The strip also was syndicated.
        After retiring, Mr. Walling created drawings for publications of businesses including the Union Carbide Corporation and the International Business Machines Corporation.
        Mr. Walling was a founding member of the National Cartoonists Society and did promotional cartoons to help sell United States War Bonds during World War II.
