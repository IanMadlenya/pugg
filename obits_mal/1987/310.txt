        Charles Shaw, a former newspaper and broadcast journalist, died of complications from emphysema and heart disease at Doylestown (Pa.) Hospital. He was 76 years old and lived in Doylestown.
        He worked for The Pittsburgh Press, The Philadelphia Bulletin and The New Hope (Pa.) Gazette. During World War II, he worked for CBS News with Edward R. Murrow in London. In the 1950's and 60's, he was news director of WCAU-TV in Philadelphia.
        Mr. Shaw is survived by his wife, Anna Mae Wallowitch of Doylestown, and a son, Charles C. Jr., of Delhi, N. Y.
