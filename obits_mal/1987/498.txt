        Dr. Martin R. Katz, a New York cardiologist, died of leiomyo sarcoma of the lung last Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 54 years old.
        Dr. Katz was associated with the Beth Israel Medical Center more than 25 years as an attending cardiologist. He was president of the New York chapter of Confrerie de la Chine des Rotisseurs, an international dining society.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Harriette Meyers Rose; a daughter, Melissa, of Manhattan, and a sister, Betty Cooperman of Roslyn, L.I.
