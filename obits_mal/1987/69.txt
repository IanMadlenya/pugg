        Don Sleet, a jazz musician who was Downbeat magazine's trumpeter of the year three times during the 1950's, died Dec. 31 of cancer. He was 48 years old.
        Mr. Sleet performed with Stan Kenton's band in 1953 and went on to appear with the Lighthouse All Stars and the Shelly Manne All Stars. The magazine recognized him as trumpeter of the year from 1956 through 1958.
