        I. William Fishkind, an executive with the National Association of Securities Dealers in Washington, the regulatory organization for the securities industry, died of cancer Friday at his home in Burke, Va. He was 53 years old.
        Mr. Fishkind, a 1959 graduate of Pace University, was the associate director of financial responsibilty for the assocaition when he died.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Terri Kincaid; a a daughter, Cathy Grey of Brooklyn; two sons, Andrew and David, both of Brooklyn, and his mother, Eve Corwin of Queens.
