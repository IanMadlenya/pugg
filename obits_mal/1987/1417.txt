        Jorge Samaniego, a dancer and choreographer, died of acquired immune deficiency syndrome last Wednesday at County-University of Southern California Medical Center here. He was 40 years old.
        Mr. Samaniego appeared with the New York City Opera Ballet, danced on and Off Broadway and later formed his own company, the Samaniego Dance Gallery. He also performed with the Western Ballet, Netherlands Dance Theater and the Milwaukee Ballet while choreographing for the Puerto Rican Dance Theater and the Des Moines Ballet in Iowa.
        In 1981 he choreographed a Public Broadcasting Service production of ''Romeo and Juliet,'' in which he played the role of Mercutio.
