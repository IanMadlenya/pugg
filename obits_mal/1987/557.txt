        Theodore M. Weis, a trumpet player with several classical ensembles in New York, died, apparently of a heart attack, at Englewood (N.J.) Hospital on Saturday. He was 66 years old and lived in Englewood.
        Mr. Weis performed with the New York Chamber Symphony of the 92d Street Y, the New York City Opera, the New York City Ballet, the Brass Quintet and the Mostly Mozart Festival, with which he performed as a soloist.
        Mr. Weis is survived by his wife, Marie, and his son, Theodore Jr.
