        Peter Birch, a dancer, choreographer and television director, died of a heart attack Sunday at White Plains Hospital, White Plains, N.Y. He was 65 years old.
        Mr. Birch began his career as a dancer, became a choreographer and finally a director. For 25 years he directed ''Captain Kangaroo,'' a popular children's television show on CBS.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Patricia Rogers; a son, Douglas, of Medford, Mass., and a daughter, Nancy Carpenter, of Huntington, L.I.
