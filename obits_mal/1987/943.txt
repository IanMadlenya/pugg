        Dr. Morris F. Goldberger, an obstetrician and gynecologist who retired from a medical practice in Manhattan in 1965, died Wednesday in St. Anthony's Hospital in St. Petersburg, Fla. A resident of New Rochelle, N.Y., who was vacationing in St. Petersburg Beach, Fla., when he became ill, he was 101 years old.
        Dr. Goldberger, who was born in Hungary, was a 1907 graduate of the New York University School of Medicine. He was at one time chief of obstetrics and gynecology of the old Beth David Hospital in Manhattan.
        He is survived by his wife, Martha; a sister, Carrie Glick of Miami Beach; two grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
