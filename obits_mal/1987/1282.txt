        Irving J. Rifkin, a chemist and former owner of chemical laboratories in Brooklyn, died Tuesday at the Maimonides Medical Center in Brooklyn. He was 83 years old and had resided in North Miami, Fla.
        Mr. Rifkin, a Brooklyn native who was active in the community before moving to Florida a dozen years ago, was a former chairman and president of the Ocean Parkway Jewish Center.
        A graduate of Columbia University's College of Pharmacy, Mr. Rifkin was a member of the American Chemical Society and the American Institute of Chemists.
        He is survived by his wife, Florence; a son, Bruce L., and a daughter, Barbara Gutin, both of New York City, and six grandchildren.
