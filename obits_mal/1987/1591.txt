        Daniel J. Tobin, a retired vice president of Douglas Elliman Gibbons & Ives, a real-estate firm, died Wednesday at Franklin General Hospital in Franklin Square, L.I. He was 63 years old and lived in New York City.
        Mr. Tobin was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of St. John's University and St. John's Law School.
        He is survived by three daughters: Susan Antinoro of New York; Lisa Kurtz of Muttontown, L.I., and Claire Bradman of Garden City, L.I.
