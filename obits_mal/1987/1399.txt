        Bard Thompson, dean emeritus of the Drew University graduate school, died of a heart attack Wednesday in Overlook Hospital, Summit, N.J. A resident of Harding, N.J., he was 62 years old.
        Dr. Thompson, who received his Ph.D. in church history from Columbia University in 1953, was an ordained minister of the United Church of Christ.
        He joined Drew University in 1965 as a professor of church history and served as dean of the graduate school from 1969 until May 1986. After retiring as dean he remained on the faculty as an adjunct professor of history.
        He is survived by his wife, Bertha; a son, Andrew, of Hoboken, N.J., and a daughter, Frances Riccardi of Bedminster, N.J.
