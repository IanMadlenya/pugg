        Gerald A. Robbie, a trial lawyer who specialized in aviation negligence cases, died Wednesday at his doctor's office in Manhattan. He had a heart attack two weeks ago. He was 55 years old and lived in New Rochelle, N.Y.
        Mr. Robbie was a graduate of New York University and the Columbia University Law School. He was a partner for 19 years in the law firm of Kreindler & Kreindler.
        Surviving are his wife, Jorene, and a daughter, Hilary.
