        Clayton W. Berry, a New York actor, died of AIDS Aug. 21 at his home in Manhattan. He was 45 years old.
        Among other theatrical productions, Mr. Berry appeared in ''Table Settings'' at Playwrights Horizons in 1979 and in an Equity Library Theater staging of the Cole Porter musical ''Anything Goes'' in 1981.
        Mr. Berry is survived by his father, Paul M. Berry, of Boulder, Colo., and a sister, Nella, of Manitou Springs, Colo.
