        Louis H. Powell, a New York City lawyer whose practice was mainly in music publishing, motion pictures and the performing arts, died of complications of diabetes at his home in Manhattan Wednesday. He was 64 years old.
        He was a graduate of Harvard College and Columbia University Law School. He is survived by two daughters, Meris of Manhattan and Ann of Los Angeles; a son, Richard, of Somerville, Mass., and three grandchildren.
