        Richard C. Miles, a former director of McKinsey & Company Management Consultants, died of leukemia Wednesday at Greenwich Hospital in Greenwich, Conn. He was 72 years old and lived in Ridgefield, Conn.
        Mr. Miles was born in New Haven and attended the Lawrenceville School and Yale University.
        He served as a major in the United States Army in World War II and later taught at the Lawrenceville School.
        In 1946, he joined McKinsey & Company, where for several years he served as director of associate development.
        He is survived by his wife, Nina; a son, Richard Jr., of Ridgefield; two stepchildren, Kathryn Kristofik and Charles Moseley, both of Manhattan, and two brothers, Egbert J., of Branford, Conn., and Philip G., of Buffalo.
        Correction:  October 9, 1987, Friday, Late City Final Edition
