        Harold M. Florsheim, former chairman of the Florsheim Shoe Company, died here Friday. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Florsheim was the son of Milton F. Florsheim, who founded the company in Chicago. The younger Florsheim started as a management trainee after graduating from Cornell University in 1920 and served 20 years as the company's president. He was named chairman in 1966, serving until his retirement in 1969.
        He is survived by his wife, 4 children, 13 grandchildren and six great-grandchildren.
