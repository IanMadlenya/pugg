        Dr. Louis Gray, a retired physician from New York City, died Wednesday at the National Orthopaedic Hospital in Arlington, Va. He was 98 years old and lived in Rockville, Md.
        Dr. Gray, who retired from general practice in 1970, was the last surviving member of his graduating class from the New York University Medical School.
        He is survived by three daughters, Pearl Poretz of Alexandria, Va., and Vita Berzak and Shirley Panzer, both of Delray Beach, Fla.; 11 grandchildren, and 22 great-grandchildren.
