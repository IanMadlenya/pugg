        Richard K. Sykes, president of Dresner-Sykes Associates Inc., political campaign consultants with offices in Manhattan and San Diego, drowned Jan. 6 while exploring a tidal pool on Molokai Island in Hawaii. He was 35 years old and lived in Manhattan and Molokai.
        He is survived by two sons, Jason and Benjamin of Molokai; his parents, Betty and John Sykes, of Oconomowoc, Wis., and three brothers, Dustin, David and John.
