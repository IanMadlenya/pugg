        Carroll D. Fearon, former vice president in charge of personnel of the Frank G. Shattuck Company, operator of the Schrafft's restaurant chain, died at the North Broward Medical Center, Pompano Beach, Fla., on Aug. 18, after a brief illness. Mr. Fearon, who was 90 years old, lived in Pompano Beach and Thousand Island Park, N.Y.
        Mr. Fearon joined the Shattuck organization after his discharge from the Army in World War I, advancing over the years from restaurant manager to personnel manager to vice president.
        His wife died in 1985. He is survived by two sons, C. Dana Jr., of Thousand Island Park, and Christopher of Pleasantville, N.Y.; two daughters, Barbara Aycock of Belhaven, N.C., and Claire Pulrang of Plattsburgh, N.Y.; three brothers, Robert, of Naples, Fla., and Thousand Island Park, Charles, of Hilton Head, S.C., and Thousand Island Park, and Henry of Washington, Conn.; six grandchildren, and nine great grandchildren.
