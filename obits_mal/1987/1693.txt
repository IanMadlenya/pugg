        George R. Swift Jr., a former sailboat racer for the Vanderbilts and the Morgans, died Wednesday at his home in Asheville, N.C., after a long illness. He was 68 years old.  Mr. Swift, a member of the New York Yacht Club for most of his adult life, was manager of Luders Marine Construction Co. in Stamford, Conn., from 1935 until 1952. He was involved in the construction and racing of many of the best-known yachts of that period.
        He is survived by a daughter, Dorothy Dale Swift of Asheville, and a brother, Josiah Bartlett Swift of Boca Raton, Fla.
