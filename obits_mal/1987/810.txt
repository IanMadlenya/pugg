        Henry E. Del Rosso, a Republican district leader on the Lower East Side of Manhattan, died of a heart attack Monday at the New York Infirmary-Beekman Downtown Hospital. He was 71 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        At his death Mr. Del Rosso, an insurance broker, had been the Republican leader in 62d Assembly District South for more than 30 years.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy McLaughlin; two daughters, Paula Del Rosso and Eileen Di Quarto; two sons, Kevin and Brian, and five grandchildren.
