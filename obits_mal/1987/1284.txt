
       Marian Scott, wife of Hugh Scott Jr., a former Senate Republican leader, died Tuesday of complications from a cerebral hemorrhage, officials said today. She was 87 years old. Mrs. Scott was admitted to Georgetown University Hospital about six weeks ago. Mr. Scott, 86, a Pennsylvnian who led his party in the Senate from 1969 to 1977, remained in stable condition at Bethesda Naval Hospital, where he was admitted Sunday after suffering a stroke.
