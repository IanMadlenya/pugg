        Alec Campbell Jr., the founder and president of Prime TV Films Inc. of Manhattan, television film distributors, died of cancer at St. Vincent's Hospital on Aug. 24. He was 79 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Campbell, a native of Brookline, Mass., attended the Gilman Preparatory School in Baltimore and Ohio State University.
        Surviving are his wife, Elsa Jane, and a daughter, Constance Alessandra Campbell of Manhattan.
