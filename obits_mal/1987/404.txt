        Alfred W. Roberts, a lawyer who specialized in real-property law, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Mountain Lakes, N.J. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Roberts was a partner in the Manhattan firm of Kelley Drye & Warren.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marion Mann; three children from a former marriage, Alfred 3d, of New Caanan, Conn., William, of San Diego, and Leslie Holland of Woodstock, Conn., and seven grandchildren.
