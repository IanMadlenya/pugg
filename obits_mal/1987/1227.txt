        Kurt Lamm, the secretary general of the United States Soccer Federation since 1971, died of a heart attack at Brookdale Hospital in Brooklyn Wednesday night. He was 68 years old.
        Mr. Lamm, who was born in Germany, played and coached in the original American Soccer League, of which he was president.
        He is survived by his wife, Doris; a son, Michael; two daughters, Shirley and Nancy, and four grandchildren.
