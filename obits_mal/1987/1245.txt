        Michael J. Eagen, a former Chief Justice of the Pennsylvania Supreme Court, died Tuesday. He was 80 years old.
        He won a 21-year term to the state high court in 1959 and became the its 38th Chief Justice in February 1977. He retired in 1980.
        At 26, two years after he began practicing law, Mr. Eagen became the youngest district attorney in Lackawanna County. In 1941, he was elected to the Lackawanna County bench.
