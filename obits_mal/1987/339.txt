        Weir C. Ketler, president of Grove City College for 40 years and trustee for 23 more years, died Tuesday. He was 98.
        Mr. Ketler became the third president of the college in 1916 and, at the age of 27, was the youngest president of a recognized college in the nation. He retired as president in 1956. He became a member of board in 1964 and served actively until early this year.
