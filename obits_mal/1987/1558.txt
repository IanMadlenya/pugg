        Andrew Stewart, former president of Denhard & Stewart Inc., a book advertising agency in Manhattan, died Wednesday at his Manhattan apartment. Mr. Stewart, who had been suffering from lung cancer, was 79 years old.
        Before he joined the advertising agency in 1935, he was a magazine editor and had worked in public relations. He was president of the agency from 1958 to 1973, when he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, Rose; two sons, Jeff, of Denville, N.J., and Douglas, of South Egremont, Mass., and seven grandchildren.
