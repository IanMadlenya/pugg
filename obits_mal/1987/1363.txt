        Former President Abu Sayeed Chowdhury died of a heart attack in London today, the state-run radio announced. He was 66 years old.
        Mr. Chowdhury, a former judge and politician, was born Jan. 31, 1921. He was leader of a delegation to the United Nations seeking recognition for Bangladesh during that nation's struggle to secede from Pakistan in 1971.
        Mr. Chowdhury, a former chairman of the United Nations Human Rights Commission, was the figurehead President of Bangladesh from January 1972 to December 1973. Bangladesh was under a British-type parliamentary system until 1975.
