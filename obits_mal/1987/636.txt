        Dr. Kevin Sullivan, a former associate dean of graduate studies at Columbia University, died of lung cancer yesterday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 68 years old and lived in Brooklyn.
        Dr. Sullivan, who was an instructor in English at Columbia from 1951 to 1957, was appointed associate dean in 1958. In 1970, he moved to Queens College. From 1973 until his retirement last year, he directed the Irish Studies Program at Queens College.
        He was an alumnus of Georgetown University and received a master's degree from Princeton University. Dr. Sullivan earned his doctorate from Columbia in 1957.
        Until last November, he was also editor of The Recorder, a journal of the American-Irish Historical Society.
        Surviving is his wife, Fran.
