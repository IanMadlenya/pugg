        Joseph M. White, a consultant to pharmaceutical companies for 30 years, died of congestive heart failure Thursday at his home in Alexandria, Va. He was 67 years old.
        Dr. White had also taught at Yale University Medical School and the University of Maryland School of Medicine in the 1940's and 50's.
        He is survived by his wife, Nancy Burns; a brother, John, and a sister, Janet Connor, both of Baltimore; a daughter, Peggy Robertson of Warrenton, N.C., two sons, Stanley, of Ledgewood, N.J., and Joseph M. 4th, of Greenwich, Conn., and a grandson.
