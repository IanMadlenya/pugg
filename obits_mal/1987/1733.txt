
       The Rev. Willis A. Baxter, pastor of Christ's First Presbyterian Church in Hempstead, L.I., from 1951 until his retirement in 1974, died of cancer of the liver Sunday at the Lehigh Valley Hospital Center in Allentown, Pa. He was 79 years old and lived in Orefield, Pa.
        The Rev. Willis A. Baxter, pastor of Christ's First Presbyterian Church in Hempstead, L.I., from 1951 until his retirement in 1974, died of cancer of the liver Sunday at the Lehigh Valley Hospital Center in Allentown, Pa. He was 79 years old and lived in Orefield, Pa.
        He is survived by his wife, Priscilla; a daughter, Patricia Frank of Wellsville, Pa., two granddaughters; a brother, Howard, of New London, N.H., and a sister, Katherine Redmond of Sterling, Ill.
