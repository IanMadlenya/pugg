        Joseph Goldstein, who taught accounting and mathematics for 36 years at Julia Richman High School in Manhattan, died of pneumonia Monday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 97 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, Sarah; a son, David Garner of Dix Hills, L.I.; a daughter, Arlene Lief of Manhattan, five grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
