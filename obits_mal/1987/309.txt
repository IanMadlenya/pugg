        Charles George Muller, a novelist and naval historian, died Monday at his home in Westport, Conn. He was 90 years old.
        Mr. Muller wrote five books about the War of 1812, including ''Hero of Two Seas'' and ''The Proudest Day.''
        Mr. Muller, who was born in New York City, served in the Ambulance Corps, attached to the French Army, in World War I, and as a pilot with the United States Navy.
        He is survived by two daughters, Barbara Ornstein, of Greenwich, Conn., and Jenny Coons of Sedona, Ariz.
