        John P. Doswell, a World War II combat pilot and the former director of operations for Flight Safety International, a pilot training school at La Guardia Airport, died of brain cancer Sunday at his home in Old Greenwich, Conn. He was 64 years old.
        He is survived by his wife, Jean; two sons, John, also of Old Greenwich, and Donald, of Morgan Hill, Calif.; a brother, Donald, of Decatur, Ill., and his mother, Adele Shinn of Pasadena, Calif.
