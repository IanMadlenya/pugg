        Frederick R. Kearns, a former president and chief executive of Canadair Ltd., died Saturday in London, Ontario, of complications from a liver transplant that he had in September. He was 63 years old and lived in Montreal.
        Mr. Kearns joined Canadair, an aircraft manufacturer, in 1949 after he graduated from McGill University in Montreal. He was named president of the company in 1965 and retired in 1983.
        He is survived by his wife, Beth; three sons, Michael of Ottawa, John of Toronto and Stephen of Montreal, and two daughters, Sandy Shaughnessy of Toronto and Jane LeBlanc of Montreal.
