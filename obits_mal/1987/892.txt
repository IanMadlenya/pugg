        Dr. Monroe Maximilian Broad, a general surgeon, died May 19 at Long Island Jewish Hospital after a long illness. He was 78 years old and lived in Queens.
        Dr. Broad had been in practice for 51 years, during which he had been on the staffs of Queens General Hospital, Jamaica Hospital and Hillcrest General Hospital. He retired from private practice in 1984.
        Dr. Broad was born in Brooklyn, graduated from New York University in 1929 and from the medical college of the University of St. Andrews, Dundee, Scotland, in 1933.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Isobel Rutenberg; a daughter, Anita Scott, of Houston; a son, David, of Buffalo; a sister, Barbara Hirschl of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
