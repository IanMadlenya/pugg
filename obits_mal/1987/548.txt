        Emanuel Heller, professor of marketing and dean of students at Pace Unversity's Manhattan campus, died Monday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 69 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Professor Heller, an alumnus of Pace, joined the Pace faculty in 1947 and was appointed dean in 1975. Earlier, he had been a sales executive with apparel concerns in Manhattan, Los Angeles and Milwaukee.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Norma Breslaw; two sons, Michael, of Holbrook, L.I., and Paul, of Great Neck, L.I., and two grandchildren.
