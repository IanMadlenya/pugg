        Edward B. D. Neuhauser, a former chief of radiology at the Children's Hospital Medical Center here, died in his sleep Sunday morning. He was 79 years old.
        Through his articles and teachings, Dr. Neuhauser helped to establish pediatric radiology as a scientific discipline. He was the first president of the Society for Pediatric Radiology, which he helped found in 1958.
        Dr. Neuhauser, a native of Philadelphia, attended Germantown Friends School and received his medical degree from the University of Pennsylvania.
        Dr. Neuhauser is survived by his wife, Gernda; two sons, Duncan, of Cleveland, and Hans, of Savannah, Ga, and four grandchildren.
