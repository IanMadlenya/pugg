        J. P. Strom, who headed the South Carolina Law Enforcement Division for more than 30 years, died of a heart attack Monday at Providence Hospital here. He was 69 years old.
        Joseph Preston Strom, who was born on July 31, 1918, in McCormick County, had been the state's top law-enforcement officer since 1956.
        Hugh Munn, a spokesman for the agency, said Mr. Strom was considered the driving force behind professionalism in South Carolina law enforcement.
