        Robert E. Hall, former director of development and alumni affairs at the Johns Hopkins University School of Advanced International Studies, died of cancer Aug. 16 at Georgetown University Hospital in Washington. He was 68 years old and lived in Georgetown.
        Mr. Hall was senior staff officer at the school from 1959 until he retired in 1984. He was responsible for coordinating the school's fund raising and alumni affairs and is credited with establishing a global organization of alumni volunteers.
        He was born in Milford, Mass., and was a graduate of the University of Massachusetts at Amherst in 1941.
        Mr. Hall is survived by a brother, Eben T. Hall of Bloomfield, Conn.; three sisters, Virginia Livernash of Arlington, Mass., Sylvia Bronson of Hartford, and Betsy Carrow of Taos, N.M.
