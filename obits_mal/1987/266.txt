        William N. Bannard, a New York investment counselor, died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Cold Spring Harbor, L.I. He was 69 years old.
        Mr. Bannard was senior investment adviser for Wilkinson & Hottinger when he retired in 1985. He had been associated with the American Securities Corporation for 33 years, serving as its president for the last 13 years.
        He was born in Hazelton, Pa., and graduated from Yale University.
        He is survived by his wife, Marjorie; two daughters, Marie Lockwood of Dover, Mass., and Barbara Haller of South Salem, Mass.; a son, David, of Groton, Mass.; his mother, Emily M. Bannard of Manhattan; a sister, Emily Thomson of Hartford, and eight grandchildren.
