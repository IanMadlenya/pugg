        C. Gordon Lamude, a lawyer, died of bronchial pneumonia Saturday at Community Hospital in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 84 years old and lived in Douglaston, Queens.
        Mr. Lamude was born in Manhattan and was a graduate of Fordham University and Fordham Law School. He practiced law in Bayside, Queens, and was a trustee of the Queens Borough Public Library.
        He is survived by his wife, Ethel; two sons, Kevin, of Carbondale, Ill., and Terence, of Manhattan; a daughter, Barbara Cappucci of Ithaca, N.Y.; a sister, Emily Barry of Charlotte, N.C., and one grandchild.
