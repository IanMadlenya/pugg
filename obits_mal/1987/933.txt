        M. Doug Wood, a co-founder of Evelyn Wood Reading Dynamics, died Saturday at the age of 83.
        Mr. Wood founded the Evelyn Wood Reading Dynamics, which teaches speed reading, with his wife, Evelyn, in 1959 and served as president of the company until he retired in 1974.
        Besides his wife, his survivors include his daughter, Carol Davis Evans of Tuscon, Ariz., and four grandchildren.
