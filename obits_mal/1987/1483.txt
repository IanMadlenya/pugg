        Edward L. Brady, a former associate director for international affairs of the National Bureau of Standards, died of cancer Sunday at his home in Chevy Chase, Md. He was 68 years old.
        A physical chemist involved for many years in nuclear energy research, Dr. Brady served at the Bureau of Standards from 1963 until his retirement last July. He was named associate director in 1978.
        Survivors include his wife, Evelyn, and two brothers, Augustus of Los Altos, Calif., and Jack of Gig Harbor, Wash.
