        Paul E. Levine, a partner in a Manhattan law firm, died Friday, apparently of a heart attack, at his home in Manhattan. He was 38 years old.
        Mr. Levine was a partner in Wachtell, Lipton, Rosen & Katz.
        He was born in Staten Island and graduated from Wesleyan University and Columbia Law School. He also had a master's degree from the London School of Economics.
        He is survived by his wife, Arleen; a daughter, Allison; his mother, Sonya, of Staten Island, and a brother, Ezra, of Chevy Chase, Md.
