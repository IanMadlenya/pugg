        Sidney Cohen, founder and chairman of Andover Togs Inc., a manufacturer of children's sportswear, died yesterday at his home in Manhattan after a long illness. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Cohen established Andover Togs, which has headquarters in Manhattan, in 1949.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Florence Matelsky; a daughter, Caroline Zelikovic of Manhattan; two sons, William of Sands Point, L.I., and Peter of Manhattan; a brother, Joseph Cagan of Brooklyn, and four grandchildren.
