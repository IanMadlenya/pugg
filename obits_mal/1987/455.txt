        Bishara Sirhan, whose son killed Robert F. Kennedy in 1968, died last week at his home village of Taibe in the Israeli-occupied West Bank.He was in his 80's.
        Mr. Sirhan emigrated to the United States in the late 1950's. He sent later for his wife and their children but later left them to return to the West Bank. He was in the West Bank in 1968 when his son, Sirhan Sirhan, shot and killed Mr. Kennedy as he campaigned for the Democratic nomination for president. Sirhan Sirhan is serving a life term for the assassination.
