        Dr. Frederick C. Gevalt Jr., a specialist in internal medicine, died of heart failure Wednesday at his home in Lakeville, Conn. He was 73 years old.
        Dr. Gevalt was one of the founders in 1947 of the Sharon Clinic, in Sharon, Conn., and served on the staff of Sharon (Conn.) Hospital. He was a fellow of the American College of Physicians.
        He was born in Boston, received a bachelor's degree from Harvard in 1936 and a medical degree from the College of Physicians and Surgeons at Columbia University in 1940.
        He is survived by his wife, Sally; three sons, Frederick C. Gevalt 3d of Cambridge, Mass., Peter Y. Gevalt of Manhattan, and Geoffrey W. Gevalt of Boston; a sister, Ruth Glazer of Kezar Falls, Me., and three grandchildren.
