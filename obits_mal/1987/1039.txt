        Edward P. Blanchard Jr., a former magazine executive, died of cancer Monday in Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 63 years old and lived in Greens Farms, Conn.
        Mr. Blanchard had served as advertising director of Aviation Week and Forbes magazines and publisher of the the Bulletin of the American Institute of Aeronautics and Astronautics. In recent years, he operated his own consulting business.  He is survived by his wife, Barbara; two sons, Donald, of Newport, R.I., and Edward Jr., of Los Angeles; a daughter, Anne Blanchard of Philadelphia; a stepson, Timothy Dinge of Bellingham, Mass., and a stepdaughter, Martha Keefe of Saratoga Springs, N.Y.
