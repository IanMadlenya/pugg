        Enzo Magnozzi, a promoter of international soccer games in the United States for the last three decades, died of a brain tumor Sunday at St. John's Hospital in Elmhurst, Queens. He was 67 years old. Funeral services are scheduled for Thursday morning at St. Leo's Church in Corona, Queens.
        Mr. Magnozzi, a member of the United States Soccer Hall of Fame, is survived by his wife, Angela; two sons, Enzo Jr. and Robert, and two grandchildren.
