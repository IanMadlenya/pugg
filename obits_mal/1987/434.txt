        Henry Jacobsohn, a retired athletic director, died of Alzheimer's disease Monday at the Veterans Hospital in Miami. He was 80 years old and lived in Pompano Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Jacobsohn, a Bronx native, coached basketball and football teams. He was athletic director at the New York Institute of Technology from 1964 to 1978, when he retired. Earlier he had coached at DeWitt Clinton High School in the Bronx.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Minna Agress; a daughter, Nina Silva of Fort Lauderdale, Fla.; a son, Bruce, of Charlotte, N.C., and five grandchildren.
