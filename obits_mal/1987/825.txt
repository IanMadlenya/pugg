        D. Edward Ricchiuto, vice chairman and chief executive officer of the Hicks & Greist Advertising Agency in Manhattan, died of cancer Friday at New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center. He was 57 years old and lived in Ridgefield, N.J.
        He is survived by his wife, Louise; his mother, Angelo Ricchiuto; two sons, Edward and Steven; a sister, Nora O'Malley, and a brother, Anthony Richards.
