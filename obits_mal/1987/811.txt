        Dr. Richard J. Smith, a prominent hand surgeon, died Monday after a long illness. He was 56 years old.
        Dr. Smith, chief of hand surgery at Massachusetts General Hospital since 1972, was also a professor of orthopedic surgery at the Harvard Medical School.
        He made more than 100 original contributions to the literature on orthopedics and hand surgery. He served on the boards of the American College of Surgeons and the Brown University Medical Association.
