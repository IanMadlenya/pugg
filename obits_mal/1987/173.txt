        William H. Callahan, a geologist and former manager of exploration for the New Jersey Zinc Company, died Thursday at Newton (N.J.) Memorial Hospital. He was 84 years old and lived in Franklin, N.J.
        Mr. Callahan, a native of Massachusetts, graduated from Massachusetts Institute of Technology in 1926. He spent 43 years with New Jersey Zinc, one of the country's oldest companies, now based in Palmerton, Pa.
        He is survived by two daughters, Lillian C., of Franklin, and Katherine Forrestal of Manhattan; a sister, Mary, of Lexington, Mass., and a granddaughter.
