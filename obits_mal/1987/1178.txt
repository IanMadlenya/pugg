        Endy A. Anderson, a manufacturer of plastics, died last Friday in an automobile accident on the Cross Westchester Expressway in Rye, N.Y. He was 72 years old and lived in Greenwich, Conn.
        Mr. Anderson, who was born in Sweden, was a consultant to the Anderson Tool and Die Corporation Inc. of Linden, N.J., manufacturer of plastic products for the medical industry. He had founded the company in Brooklyn in the late 1940's and was its president until last year.
        He is survived by his wife, Dolores; a daughter, Karin Wadkovsky of Manhattan, and a son, Karl, of Greenwich.
