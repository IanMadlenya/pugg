        Wilson J. Gibney, senior partner of the Manhattan law firm of Gibney, Anthony & Flaherty, died of cancer Wednesday at Roosevelt Hospital. He was 69 years old and lived in Rye, N.Y.
        Mr. Gibney, a specialist in Federal and international taxation, established his firm in 1938. Previously, he was a special agent of the Federal Bureau of Investigation and an investigator for the Internal Revenue Service.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen Hayes, two daughters, Pamela Gibney of Manhattan, and Paula Tennyson, of Birmingham, Mich., and two grandchildren.
