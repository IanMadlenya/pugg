        Msgr. Michael J. Fleming, director of the Catholic Youth Organization in Whitestone, Queens, died of cancer Thursday at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 71 years old and lived in Whitestone.
        Monsignor Fleming, a priest of the Diocese of Brooklyn who spent 45 years ministering to the needs of young people, recently was honored by Catholic Charities in Brooklyn with the Caritas Award for service to others.
        He is survived by three sisters, Sister Michaelita, of Hempstead, L.I.; Rita Haff of Bayside, Queens, and Lillian Fischer of Bellerose, Queens, and a brother, William, of Briarcliff, N.Y.
