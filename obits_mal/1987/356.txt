        John Shiel, an electrical engineer and historian, died of a heart attack Saturday at his home in Glen Head, L.I. He was 87 years old.
        Since retiring from the Bell Telephone Laboratories in 1963, Mr. Shiel pursued his interest in the history of Quaker settlements on Long Island, where he was a member of four historical societies.
        Surviving are his wife, Yvonne, and daughter, Joan, of New York.
