        Daisuke Yamauchi, president of the newspaper Mainichi Shimbun, died of liver failure today. He was 62 years old.
        Mainichi Shimbun, Japan's third largest newspaper, has a daily circulation of 6.3 million.
        Mr. Yamuchi, a native of Fukuoka prefecture in western Japan, joined the Mainichi in 1949 after graduation from the University of Tokyo and worked as chief of the newspaper's New York bureau for two years from 1962. He became company president in 1980.
        Mr. Yamauchi is survived by his wife, Yuriko, and two daughters.
