        Roy Plaut, a founding partner of the Manhattan law firm of Lipkowitz & Plaut, died Wednesday at his home in Chapel Hill, N.C. Mr. Plaut, who had retired to Chapel Hill so he could attend courses at the University of North Carolina, was 85 years old.
        He attended City College of New York for three years but left in 1925 to attend Columbia Law School, from which he graduated in 1928.
        He is survived by his wife, Helaine Kaplan Plaut; a daughter, Marge Rogatz of Roslyn, L.I.; a son, Roy Jr., of Atlanta; six grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
