        Paul E. Reinhold, a dairy-business executive who was president of the Reinhold Corporation, died Monday at his home in Jacksonville, Fla. He was 93 years old.
        Mr. Reinhold, who entered the dairy business in 1916, was a founder of Foremost Dairies Inc. in 1931 and was honorary chairman of the McKesson Corporation at the time of his death. He is survived by a daughter, June R. Myers of Ashland, Ohio; four grandchildren and nine great-grandchildren.
