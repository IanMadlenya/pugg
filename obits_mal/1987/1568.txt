        Philip I. Bowman, a retired executive vice president of the Bristol-Myers Company and chairman of Bristol Laboratories, died of bone cancer on Friday at his home in Syracuse. He was 78 years old and also had a home in Stuart, Fla.
        He is survived by his second wife, the former Michele Sultan; a daughter, Joan Gardner of Rochester; three sons, Richard, of Syracuse, Robert, of Hackensack, N.J., and James, a doctor, of Fairport, N.Y.; three sisters, Patricia and Julia Bowman of Harrisburg and Maryann Gilbert of Churchville, Pa., and eight grandchildren.
