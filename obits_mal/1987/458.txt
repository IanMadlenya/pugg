        Joseph S. Stout Jr., a lawyer, died, apparently of a stroke, on Sunday in Canada at Toronto Western Hospital. He was 45 years old and lived in Lloyd Harbor, L.I.
        Mr. Stout, who was in Toronto visiting clients, was a partner in the New York City law firm of Winthrop, Stimson, Putnam & Roberts, specializing in international corporate and banking transactions.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; two sons, Joseph S. 3d and William; his parents, Joseph S. and Barbara, of Lloyd Harbor, and a sister, Lydia Dane of Lincoln, Mass.
        A memorial service will be held tomorrow at 11:30 A.M. at St. John's Church in Cold Spring Harbor, L.I.
