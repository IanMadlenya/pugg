        William Anderson Sutherland, a founding partner of Sutherland, Asbill & Brennan, an Atlanta and Washington law firm, died Nov. 7 of a heart attack at his home in Washington. He was 91 years old.
        Mr. Sutherland, who also maintained a home in Atlanta, had practiced law since 1920. He helped found Sutherland, Asbill & Brennan in 1924.
        Mr. Sutherland's wife, Sarah, died in 1984. He is survived by three daughters, Mary Strong of Summit, N.J.; Margaret Coleman of Dallas and Sarah Stoner of Sewickley, Pa.; two sisters, Sara Tuttle of Atlanta and Jenny Asbill of Washington; eight grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.
