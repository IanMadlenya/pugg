        Mox Arthur Weber, retired chairman of the department of physical education at Hamilton College in Clinton, N.Y., died Dec. 5 at the North Hill retirement home in Needham, Mass. He was 85 years old and lived in Needham.
        Mr. Weber served on the Hamilton faculty for 45 years until his retirement in 1972, and coached baseball, basketball, tennis, swimming, golf and track.
        His wife, Sally, died in 1975. He is survived by a son, Eric, of Wellesley, Mass.; two sisters, Elsa Schroeder of Fremont, Calif., and Rose Hawk of Walnut Grove, Calif., and three grandchildren.
