        Murray Posner, a resort hotel owner and president of the Catskill Resort Association, died of cardiac arrest Wednesday at his home in South Fallsburg, N.Y. He was 75 years old.
        With his brother, Benjamin Posner, he owned the Brickman Hotel in South Fallsburg.
        He was born in Manhattan, attended St. John's University and received a law degree from St. John's in 1935.
        He is survived by his wife, Helen of South Fallsburg; his brother, Benjamin, of South Fallsburg, and a sister, Bebe Toor of Frieburg, Me.
