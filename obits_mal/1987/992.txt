        Christopher G. Lindsay, a former business journalist in New York for The Associated Press, was found dead in his apartment in Manhattan on Saturday, after apparently suffering a heart attack. He was 44 years old.
        Mr. Lindsay was an editor and reporter on the national business desk of the news agency from 1981 until last fall, helping to cover business, finance and the economy. He specialized in the airline and banking industries.
        Mr. Lindsay is survived by his mother, Irene, of Farmingdale, Me., and his brother, Howard, of Readfield, Me.
