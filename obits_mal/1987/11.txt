        Allen Porter, for many years a member of the staff of the Museum of Modern Art, died last Wednesday at a nursing home in Rhinebeck, N.Y. He was 84 years old and lived in Barrytown, N.Y.
        Mr. Porter was a graphic designer at the General Motors Corporation before joining the museum's staff in 1937. He was exhibition and circulation director of its film library and had edited its Bulletin.
        He was appointed assistant secretary in 1945. He retired in 1964 and during the 1970's was the author of several photography publications.
        There are no immediate survivors.
