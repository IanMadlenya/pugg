        Abel Just, a New York lawyer, died Monday at St. Francis Hospital in Port Washington, L.I., after a brief illness. He was 80 years old and lived in Sands Point, L.I.
        Mr. Just, who specialized in interstate commerce law, maintained an active practice until January.
        He was born in Manhattan, attended the City College of New York and was a graduate of Brooklyn Law School.
        Mr. Just is survived by his wife, the former Ada Bernstein; a sister, Ethel, of Manhattan; a son, Dr. Harold, of Westwood, Mass.; a daughter, Barbara E. Spark of Westwood, and seven grandchildren.
