        Adam F. McQuillan, a former warden at several New York City jails and a former president of the Correctional Association of New York, died of a heart attack Monday at the Massapequa (L.I.) General Hospital. He was 64 years old and lived in Levittown, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, Irene; a son, Gerald, of Lindenhurst, L.I.; a daughter, Susan, of Manhattan; a sister, Molly Monahan of Long Beach, L.I., and a grandchild.
