        Thomas Benson, a founder and executive director of the Newport Museum of Yachting, died of a heart attack today while working out at a local gymnasium. He was 50 years old.
        Mr. Benson, who also organized the museum's annual Classic Yacht Regatta, was a sculptor, a restorer of art and antiques and an authority on historic preservation.
        He is survived by his mother, Esther; his wife, Anne; two sons, Oliver and Samuel; two stepdaughters, Sarah and Rachel Sherman, and two brothers, John and Richard.
