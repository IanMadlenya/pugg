        David Lewis Seidel, a Washington physician and psychiatrist, died of leukemia Monday at Sibley Memorial Hospital in Washington. He was 69 years old.
        In his 40 years of practice, Dr. Seidel was psychiatric consultant to several Federal departments and to the Theological College of Catholic University.
        Dr. Seidel, who lived in Washington, is survived by a nephew, Harry E. Seidel 3d of St. Louis, and a niece, Barbara Ellen Seidel of Brooklyn.
