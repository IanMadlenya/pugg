        Dr. Jeffrey L. Bishop, a lecturer in astronomy at Columbia University, died Nov. 13 when a car struck him as he jogged near his home in Rockland County. He was 29 years old.
        Dr. Bishop joined Columbia in September after two years at the Canadian Institute for Theoretical Astrophysics in Toronto.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ellen Simon; a son, Andrew; his mother, Muriel Simmons; his father, Thomas Bishop; a sister, Kathy Bishop, and two stepsisters, Kari Kling and Linda McNeely, all of Manhattan.
