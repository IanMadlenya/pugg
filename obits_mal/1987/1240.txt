        Karl P. Wegener, owner for almost half a century of the Arbor Inn restaurant in Rockville Centre, L.I., died of lung cancer on Monday in Mercy Hospital there. He was 82 years old and lived in Rockville Centre.
        Mr. Wegener came to this country in 1927 from Germany. He bought the restaurant in 1929 and ran it until his death.
        He is survived by his wife, Heidi; a son, Karl G., of Rockville Centre; a sister, Maria Wegener, of Coblenz, West Germany; two stepsons, Timothy A. O'Brien, of Kensington, Md., and Dr. Peter C. O'Brien, of Rochester, Minn.; a stepdaughter, Susan H. Hoffman, of Larchmont, N.Y., and seven grandchildren.
