        Dr. Joseph H. Siris, a neurosurgeon and retired director of neurology at Creedmoor State Hospital in Queens Village, Queens, died Thursday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 78 years old and lived in Floral Park, Queens.
        Dr. Siris was neurology director from 1968 to 1983, when he retired. He had also been attending neurosurgeon at the Long Island Jewish-Hillside Medical Center in New Hyde Park, L.I., and on the staff of North Shore University.
        He was born in Brooklyn and was an alumnus of Columbia College and the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Muriel Lazerus; a daughter, Jane; a son, Michael; a brother, Dr. Irwin Siris, all of of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
