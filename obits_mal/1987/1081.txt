        Curt McDowell, a prominent underground film maker, died of AIDS Wednesday in his San Francisco home, according to The Associated Press. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. McDowell's avant-garde films included ''Sparkie's Tavern,'' ''Loads,'' ''A Visit to Indiana,'' ''Pornogra Follies,'' a musical described as a ''bisexual scatological revue,'' and ''Nudes,'' a collection of sexual sketches.
