
       Harold M. Barrett, retired president of the Uniroyal Tire Company, died Friday at his home in Newtown, Conn. He was 80 years old.  Mr. Barrett, a native of New Haven, was also president of the Uniroyal Consumer Products Company in the 1960's. He was a graduate of Northeastern University.
        Harold M. Barrett, retired president of the Uniroyal Tire Company, died Friday at his home in Newtown, Conn. He was 80 years old.  Mr. Barrett, a native of New Haven, was also president of the Uniroyal Consumer Products Company in the 1960's. He was a graduate of Northeastern University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Anne Frances Carroll; a son, Harold, of St. Petersburg, Fla.; a daughter, Judith Theroux of Naugatuck, Conn., and three grandsons.
