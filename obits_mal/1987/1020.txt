        Albert Badain, who coached varsity and junior varsity basketball at Erasmus Hall High School in Brooklyn for 40 years, died yesterday at Biscayne Hospital in Hallandale, Fla., after a heart attack. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Badain, a physical education teacher at Erasmus, coached both Doug Moe, the current coach of the Denver Nuggets, and Billy Cunningham, the former coach of the Philadelphia 76ers.
        Mr. Badain is survived by his wife, Shirley; a son, Charles, of Rochester, and a daughter, Nancy Hart of Eastchester, N.Y.
