        Bernard L. Hegeman, a Brooklyn realtor, died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Rockville Centre, L.I. He was 90 years old.
        Mr. Hegeman, a native of Brooklyn, had owned his own business there since 1936. He was a past president of the Brooklyn Board of Realtors, a past president of the Industrial Real Estate Brokers Association and a founding member of the National Society of Industrial and Office Realtors.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Grace Campbell Peckman; three sons, George, of Weston, Mass., Robert, of Garden City, L.I., and Geoffrey, of Manhattan, and six grandchildren.
