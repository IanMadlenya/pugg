
       Raymond S. Dinsmore, a real-estate developer and world traveler who, in the late 1950's, was president of the Circumnavigators Club, an organization of travelers who have circled the globe, died Monday at the John F. Kennedy Memorial Hospital in Atlantis, Fla., of complications from a stroke. He was 87 years old and a resident of Manhattan.
