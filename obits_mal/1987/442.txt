        Jonathon ChaseLaw School Dean, 48 NORWICH, Vt., Dec. 6 (AP) - Jonathon Chase, who was dean of the Vermont Law School in South Royalton from 1982 to July 1987, died of cancer Saturday at his home here. He was 48 years old.
        Mr. Chase, a graduate of Columbia Law School who later was a clerk for Federal District Judge William B. Herlands in New York, had been a legal scholar at Boston University Law School, the University of Colorado Law School and the University of Puget Sound Law School.
        Mr. Chase was co-founder of Colorado Rural Services, a program providing legal aid to the state's rural poor.
