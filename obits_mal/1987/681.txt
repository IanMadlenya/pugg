        Gen. Hunter Harris Jr., former commander of Pacific Air Forces, died last Thursday. He was 77 years old.
        General Harris, a graduate of the United States Military Academy, was Pacaf commander from 1964 until his retirement in 1967. He was previously vice commander of the Strategic Air Command at Offutt Air Force Base, Neb.
        General Harris, who was born in Fort Sam Houston, Tex., had been living here since his retirement after 35 years service.
