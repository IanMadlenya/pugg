        Chauncey Leeds Mitchell Jr., a former investment counselor and vice president of the Standard & Poor's Corporation, died of cancer Tuesday at the Riverview Medical Center in Red Bank, N.J. He was 83 years old and lived in Red Bank.
        Surviving are his wife, Ann Crowley; a son, Keith, of Long Valley, N.J.; three daughters, Alexandra Buchanen of Nesconset, L.I., Dr. Meredith Platt of Manhattan and Rita Virginia Clarke of Little Silver, N.J., and eight grandchildren.
