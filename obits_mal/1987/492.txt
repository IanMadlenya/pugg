        Dr. Robert J. Adrian, associate professor of educational and community programs in the department of education at Queens College, died of a heart attack Saturday at his home in Syosset, L.I. He was 66 years old.
        Dr. Adrian, on the Queens College faculty 21 years, also was a staff psychologist at Winthrop-University Hospital in Mineola, L.I.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Helen Lyons; a son, Dean, of Manhattan; two daughters, Monica and Katie, of Huntington, L.I.; and a brother, Howard, of Chatham, Mass.
