        Edwin C. Berry, a longtime civil rights leader and former president of the Chicago Urban League, died Wednesday after a long illness. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Berry was leader of the league for more than a decade, mostly in the 1960's. He was also instrumental in the 1983 campaign by Harold Washington, the city's first black Mayor.
