        Gottfried Neuburger, a trade-show organizer, died Dec. 2 at the Mount Sinai Medical Center. He was 80 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        In the 1950's, Mr. Neuburger founded International Trade Shows, which organized and promoted expositions. In 1958, he organized a trade fair in Moscow for American businesses.
        Surviving are his wife, Margaret; a son, Albert, and a daughter, Jeanne, all of Manhattan.
