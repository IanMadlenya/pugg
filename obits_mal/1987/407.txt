        F. Joseph Holleran, a retired vice president of the First National City Bank in New York, died at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital on Dec. 23 after a heart attack. He was 88 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        For 39 years, Mr. Holleran was with the bank, since renamed Citibank. He retired in 1966.
        He is survived by a daughter, Glenna Ottley of Sun Valley, Idaho; two sons, Richard, of Greenwich, and Romer, of Hanover, N.H., and seven grandchildren.
        A memorial service is to be held Jan. 9 at 11:30 A.M. at St. Michael the Archangel Roman Catholic Church in Greenwich.
