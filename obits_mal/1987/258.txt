        Nicholas L. Castellano, a Long Island criminal lawyer, died of a heart attack Tuesday at Long Beach (L.I.) Memorial Hospital. He was 72 years old and lived in Island Park, L.I.
        Mr. Castellano, who grew up in West New York, N.J., was a graduate of the John Marshall Law School. He became an assistant district attorney in Nassau County in the 1950's before entering private practice in Mineola, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, Maria; a daughter, Nicole Samson of Ridgewood, N.J., and two grandchildren.
