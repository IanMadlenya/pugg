        Emery L. Van Horn, the first manager of the Atomic Energy Commission's office at the Brookhaven National Laboratory on Long Island, died of cancer Saturday at his home in Severna Park, Md. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Van Horn, a civil engineer, served as manager from 1946 until he retired in 1970.
        He is survived by his wife, Pauline; a son, Robert, of Estes Park, Colo., and two sisters, Genevieve Roberts of Hammondsport, N.Y., and Louise Cook of Camp Hill, Pa.
