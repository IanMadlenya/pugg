        John Danielson, an administrator at hospitals and medical centers for more than 30 years, died Monday at the age of 62.
        Mr. Danielson served as administrator of Roosevelt Hospital in New York City from 1952 to 1954 and of North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, N.Y., from 1954 to 1957. He was president of Detroit Medical Center Corporation, now known as Detroit Medical Center, from 1979 to 1984. Mr. Danielson served as president of Howard Young Medical Center in Woodruff, Wis., from 1984 until his death.
