        Ralph Rice Boyer, retired chief of contracts finance for the Atomic Energy Commission, died of a heart attack Thursday at his home in Sandy Spring, Md. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Boyer, a native of Elkhart, Ind., was with the Atomic Energy Commission from 1947 to 1974, when he retired. From 1945 to 1947 he was director of finance for the United Nations Relief and Rehabilitation Administration.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Alice M. Reich; a son, Dr. James L. Boyer of New Haven, Conn., a daughter, M. Christine Boyer of Manhattan; and two granddaughters.
