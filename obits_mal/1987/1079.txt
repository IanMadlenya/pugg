        Joseph Licitra, managing partner of the Garden City, L.I., law firm of Shaw, Goldman, Licitra, Levine & Weinberg, died Wednesday at Winthrop-University Hospital in Mineola, L.I., after a long illness. He was 57 years old and lived in Garden City.
        Mr. Licitra, who was born in New Orleans and reared in Queens, received his bachelor's and law degrees from St. John's University.
        He is survived by his wife, Helen; three sons, Dr. Joseph, of Clifton, N.J., Michael, of Westbury, L.I., and John, of Garden City; four daughters, Mary Fajardo of New Hyde Park, L.I., Kate Baum of Manhattan, Terri Baumann of Merrick, L.I., and Patricia Licitra of Manhattan; his mother, Clara, of Manhasset, L.I.; three sisters, Marilyn Sferrazza and Nancy Cole, both of Manhasset, and Claire Raia of Garden City, and nine grandchildren.
