        Louis T. Hindenlang, a retired executive vice president of the American Bank Note Company, died of cancer Sunday at Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 72 years old and lived in New Canaan, Conn.
        Mr. Hindenlang joined American Bank Note, a bank note printing concern, as secretary of the company in 1954. He was named executive vice president in 1975 and retired in 1980.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ethel Rush; two daughters, Susan, of Ridgewood, N.J., and Jane, of New Haven; a son, Louis Jr., of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
