        John H. Rizzuto, former president of Givenchy Inc., the United States corporate office of the Paris fashion house, died Tuesday at his home in Manhattan after a long illness. He was 52 years old.
        Mr. Rizzuto joined the Givenchy organization in 1960 and became a vice president of Parfums Givenchy in 1973. In 1976, he was named president of the newly formed corporate office. He left the company last year because of illness.
        He is survived by his mother, Lillian, and his sister, Mary Ann Zali, both of Rome, N.Y., and a brother, Kenneth, of Harrisburg, Pa.
