        Walter Bothe, a former Secret Service agent who guarded Henry A. Kissinger died of cancer Sunday. He was 46 years old.
        During his 12 years with the Secret Service, Mr. Bothe traveled with Mr. Kissinger, then Secretary of State, to China, the Middle East, the Soviet Union and South America. Mr. Bothe left the Government in 1977 together with Mr. Kissinger and became his private bodyguard.
