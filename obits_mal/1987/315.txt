        Jerry H. Johnson, an AIDS coordinator for the Gay Men's Health Crisis, died Dec. 14 of lung cancer at his home in Manhattan. He was 53 years old.
        A native of Ames, Iowa, Mr. Johnson attended the University of Colorado and the Chouihard Art Institute.
        Mr. Johnson was coordinator of the organization's AIDS hotline.
        A memorial service is to be held at the Washington Square United Methodist Church, 135 West Fourth Street, on Jan. 14.
