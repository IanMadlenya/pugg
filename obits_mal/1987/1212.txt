        William J. Casey, the former Director of Central Intelligence, left an estate worth more than $500,000 to his wife, according to papers filed in Nassau County Surrogate Court.
        In a five-page will filed on June 24, Mr. Casey specified that his wife, Sophia, was to receive his entire estate, which will be administered by her and by the Long Island Trust Company of Garden City.
        Mr. Casey, who died on May 6, gave the bank the power to sell, divide and manage the property, along with other financial powers.
        The will did not detail his assets.
