        Lawrence L. Preiss, a former New York investment broker, died Friday at Mount Sinai Medical Center in Miami Beach, after a series of heart attacks. He was 83 years old and lived in Miami Beach.
        Mr. Preiss was an associate vice president for investments of Prudential-Bache Securities, which he had joined on moving to Miami Beach more than 20 years ago. He was the founder of the Pierce Swiss Watch Company in Manhattan and had been a partner in Sprayregan and Haft Investments.
        He was also associated with the A.L. Stamm Investment Brokers Company and had been a successful builder and developer in Yonkers.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ursula Rosenthal; two daughters, Marilyn T. Saturn and Adrienne K. Bolender, both of Queens; and a granddaughter.
