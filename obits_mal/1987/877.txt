
       Barrett Wendell, a former executive director of the Ellis Memorial and Eldredge House of Boston, a social services agency that was originally one of the oldest settlement houses in the country, died April 8 at Coral Reef Hospital in Miami after a short illness. He was 73 years old and lived in Key Largo, Fla., and Beverly Farms, Mass.
