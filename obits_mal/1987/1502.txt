        Gregory J. Oberst Sr., former vice president of the Amerace Esna Corporation, died of a heart attack Sept. 6 at Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J. He was 77 years old and lived in Summit.
        Mr. Oberst, who was born in Fremont, Ohio, earned a bachelor's degree in civil engineering from the University of Detroit.
        From 1948 to 1970, he was treasurer and controller of the Elastic Stop Nut Corporation in Union, N.J.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary; two daughters, Jeanne Feyedelem of Saginaw, Mich., and Mary Kay Weir of Geneva, Ill.; a son, Gregory Jr., of Apalachin, N.Y.; a brother, William, of Lansing, Mich.; two sisters, Loyola Weber of Sandusky, Ohio, and Dolores Tobin of Norfolk, Va., and 10 grandchildren.
