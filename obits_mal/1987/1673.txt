        A. Baird Hastings, a leading biochemist and physiologist, died of heart disease Sept. 24 at Scripps Memorial Hospital in La Jolla, Calif. He was 91 years old.
        Born in Dayton, Ky., in 1895, Dr. Hastings was graduated from the University of Michigan in 1917. He received his doctorate in physiology in 1921 from Columbia University.
        He is survived by his son, Baird Jr., of New York City.
