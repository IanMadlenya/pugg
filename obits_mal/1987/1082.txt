        Frank E. Hedrick, former president of the Beech Aircraft Corporation, died of cancer Thursday. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Hedrick began work at Beech Aircraft in 1940. He served as president 13 years, starting in 1968. When the Raytheon Company bought Beech in 1980, Mr. Hedrick was elected to Raytheon's board. He recently relinquished that seat.
        Mr. Hedrick retired from Beech Aircrat in 1982 but continued his business activities with Beech Hedrick Investments.
