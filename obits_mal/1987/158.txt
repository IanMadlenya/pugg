        John A. McCandless, a retired president of Vance, Sanders & Company, an investment management concern in Boston, Mass., died Wednesday in Delray Beach, Fla. He was 82 years old and a resident Delray Beach.
        Mr. McCandless worked for Vance, Sanders, which is now the Eaton Vance Corporation, from 1949 to 1971.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary E. Lapp; a daughter, Beatrix Wadhams of Glastonbury, Conn.; two sons, Thomas E. of West Newbury, Mass., and John F. of Redwood, Calif.; a brother, the Rev. Hugh McCandless of Hamden, Conn., and six grandchildren.
