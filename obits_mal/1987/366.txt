        Clinton Sundberg, a character actor in dozens of films in the 1940's and 50's, died of heart failure on Dec. 14. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Sundberg, who was born in St. Paul, also appeared on Broadway in ''The Diary of Anne Frank,'' ''Arsenic and Old Lace'' and ''The Rugged Path'' with Spencer Tracy. Among his films were ''Undercurrent'' (1946), ''Easter Parade'' (1948), ''Annie Get Your Gun'' (1950) and ''How the West was Won'' (1967).
