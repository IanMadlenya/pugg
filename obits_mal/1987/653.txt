        Justin S. Dunn, former president and chief executive officer of E. W. Axe & Company, now Axe Houghton Management, an investment company in Tarrytown, N.Y., died Tuesday at his home in Briarcliff Manor, N.Y., after a brief illness. He was 92 years old.
        He joined the company as a vice president in 1941 and was named its president in 1967, succeeding Ruth Houghton Axe, a co-founder of the company. He retired in 1981.
        Mr. Dunn, who was born in New Haven, graduated cum laude from Yale University in 1927.
        He was a member of the board of governors of the Investment Company Institute in Washington.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ann H. Waypa Knudsen; two stepsons, Earl Knudsen of Palm Harbor, Fla., and Eugene Knudsen of Somers, N.Y.; a sister, Helen Greaney of Stamford, Conn., and seven grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
