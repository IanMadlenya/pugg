        Bill Brown, assistant football coach at Hofstra University who played briefly in the American Football League, died of cancer tonight. He was 52 years old.
        Brown, a 1958 graduate of Syracuse and captain of the 1957 Cotton Bowl team, served as the offensive line coach with the Flying Dutchmen since 1986. He played linebacker with the Boston Patriots in 1960. He was a high school coach before joining the staffs at C. W. Post and Hofstra. His Berner High School teams in Massapequa, L.I., finished undefeated four times.
        Funeral arrangements are pending.
