        Bertram Lichtenstein, a clothing manufacturer, died of cancer yesterday at Montefiore Medical Center. He was 70 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Lichtenstein acquired the men's wear manufacturer Delton Ltd. in the early 1960's, and under his leadership the company's collections enjoyed wide acceptance among the nation's quality retailers. He retired in 1982.
        Surviving are his wife, Muriel; two sons, John, of Pittsburgh, and Robert, of Westbrook, Conn., and four grandchildren.
