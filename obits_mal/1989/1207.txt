        Frank J. McGrath, a retired senior financial vice president and treasurer of Uniroyal Inc., died of spinal cancer on Tuesday at White Plains Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. McGrath worked for Baldwin Locomotive, Warner Brothers Pictures and Price, Waterhouse before joining United States Rubber, the predecessor of Uniroyal, in 1940. He retired in 1966.
        He was born in Philadelphia. Mr. McGrath had an accounting degree from the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania.
        Surviving are a son, F. Gerard, of Darien, Conn.; a sister, Mae Dunn of Swarthmore, Pa.; two brothers, Edward, of Philadelphia, and John, of Buffalo, and three grandchildren.
