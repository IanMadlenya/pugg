        John E. McAniff, a lawyer and retired law professor, died of a stroke Thursday at his home in Spring Lake, N.J. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. McAniff, a 1931 graduate of Fordham University Law School, taught Latin and psychology there while attending law school. He was in private practice before joining the faculty at Fordham in 1946. He taught there for 36 years before retiring in 1982.
        He is survived by a son, Edward J., of Pasadena, Calif.; two daughters, Mary M. Kresky of Manhattan, and Anne M. Graham of Gladwyne, Pa., and eight grandchildren.
