        Joel Stephenson Mitchell, the former chief executive of Standard Brands Inc., died Monday at his home in Lake Wales, Fla. He was 90 years old.
        Mr. Mitchell began his career in the early 1920's as a salesman for the Kellogg Company of Battle Creek, Mich. He left in 1944 as a vice president and joined Standard Brands as vice president for foreign operations. The company, a subsidiary of RJR Nabisco Inc., is based in East Hanover, N.J. He was named president in 1947 and chairman and chief executive in 1960. He retired in 1963.
        Mr. Mitchell is survived by his wife, Isabelle; a daughter, Ruth Freeman, of New Canaan, Conn.; a son, Joel, of Rye Brook, N.Y.; a brother, Dr. Russell Mitchell, of San Antonio; two sisters, Edith Matthews, of Angleton, Tex., and Elizabeth Currie, of Dallas; three grandchildren and one great-grandson.
