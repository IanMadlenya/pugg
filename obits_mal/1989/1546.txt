        The Rev. A. W. Wilson, whose church became a rallying point for the 1955 bus boycott here that set off the civil rights protests, died Friday at his home after a long illness. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Wilson was the pastor of Holt Street Baptist Church for 50 years and continued preaching in recent months despite his failing health.
