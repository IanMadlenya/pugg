        Rabbi Herbert A. Seltzer, a retired administrator of a geriatrics center, died Friday at Good Samaritan Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 82 years old and lived in Palm Beach.
        Rabbi Seltzer was executive director of the Daughters of Jacob Geriatrics Center in the Bronx from 1940 to 1975. Previously, he had been assistant director of the Brooklyn Home and Hospital.
        Surviving are his wife, Anita; three brothers, Morris, of Delray Beach, Fla., Jack, of St. Petersburg, Fla., and Carl, of Newton, Mass., and a sister, Martha Gordon of New Rochelle, N.Y.
