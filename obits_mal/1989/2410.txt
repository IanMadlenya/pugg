        Carlos Barral, an editor and author, died of internal bleeding in a hospital on Tuesday. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Barral was a member of the group of authors and intellectuals known as the Barcelona School, which took shape in Catalonia during the Franco dictatorship. Mr. Barral's company, Seix Barral, became the leading publisher of serious Spanish writing and translations of important foreign authors.
        Mr. Barral, the author of poetry in Spanish and Catalan and several volumes of memoirs, served one term as a Socialist senator in Spain's Parliament and one term as a Socialist member of the European Parliament.
