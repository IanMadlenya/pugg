        Xiao Jingguang, former commander of the Chinese Navy and Deputy Defense Minister, died on Wednesday, the official New China News Agency said Thursday. He was 86 years old.
        The press agency said Mr. Xiao joined the Communist Party in 1922. He served as political commissar of the 11th Army and commander of the Fujian-Jiangxi Military Area.
