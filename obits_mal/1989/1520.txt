        Sam Shapolsky, a builder of apartment houses on the East Side of Manhattan, died of bladder cancer on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 77 years old.
        From the 1930's through the 1950's, Mr. Shapolsky built many residential properties and renovated other buildings from the Lower East Side to Harlem. After his retirement in 1960, he remained active in real estate with his sons, Arthur and Allan.
        Besides his sons, who live in Manhattan, he is survived by his wife, the former Adele Goldberg, and a grandson.
