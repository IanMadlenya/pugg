        Yorke Allen Jr., a former representative in the Connecticut legislature and a former associate with the Rockefeller Brothers Fund, died Thursday at Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital. He was 73 and lived in New Canaan.
        Mr. Allen was a member of the legislature from 1979 to 1983 and was a member of the New Canaan Town Council from 1970 to 1978. He was an associate with the Rockefeller Brothers Fund from 1951 until his retirement in 1980. Previously he was with the investment banking firm of Eberhardt & Company and had served in World War II as a captain in Army intelligence.
        Mr. Allen graduated from Princeton University in 1936.
        Surviving are his wife, Elizabeth; two daughters, Frances Yerkes of Salisbury, Conn., and Mary, of Columbus, Ind., and four sons, Frederick, of Washington, Yorke 3d, of McLean, Va., David, of Ypsilanti, Mich., and Roger, of Acton, Mass.
