        Anthony L. Gagliardi, an insurance executive, died on Tuesday at New Rochelle (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 73 years old and lived in Larchmont, N.Y.
        Mr. Gagliardi was a vice president of the Leonard Newman Agency, an insurance business in White Plains. He had been a director of the former East River Savings Bank and president of Knox, Lent & Tucker in White Plains, another insurance business. He was born in Larchmont.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Pauline Smart; three sons, James, of Larchmont, Anthony Jr., of New Rochelle, and Michael, of Chicago; a daughter, Mary Elisabeth, of Larchmont; two brothers, Joseph, of Larchmont, and Lee, of Manchester, Vt.; four sisters, Phinina Forbes of Shelton, Conn., Diana Collins of Carmel, N.Y., and Gloria Gagliardi and Mary Ann Ryan, both of Fairfield, Conn., and six grandchildren.
