        Theodore Batton, a children's clothing designer, died March 25 at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 39 years old and lived in Jamaica, Queens.
        Mr. Batton died of a heart attack, his mother, Betty McMillan, said.
        He formerly worked for Magic Years, a clothing manufacturer, designing children's clothing. Recently, he was associated with Holland & Sahr Inc., a designers' representative.
        Surviving are his mother and stepfather, Adonnie McMillan; his father, Harry; a daughter, Andrea; a brother, Michael, and a granddaughter, all of Queens.
