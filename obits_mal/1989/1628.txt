        John G. Main, managing partner of Management Practice Inc., a management-consulting business in Manhattan, died of a heart attack on Monday at his home in Pelham Manor, N.Y. He was 60 years old.
        Mr. Main was a founder of Management Practice in 1968. He had worked for McKinsey & Company.
        Surviving are his wife, Marie; two daughters, Ramona, of Rye, N.Y., and Alison, of Pelham Manor; a son, Cortlandt, of Pelham Manor, his mother, Alice, and a brother, Robert, both of Dunedin, New Zealand.
