        Kieran M. Bracken, an accountant and former controller of the Ford Foundation, died of pneumonia Friday in the Belleair Nursing Care center in Clearwater, Fla., where he lived. He was 65.
        Mr. Bracken was born in Yonkers and graduated from the Manhattan College Business School. He was the Ford Foundation's controller from 1970 to 1979, when he retired.
        Surviving are his wife, Virginia; four daughters, Alycia, of Redington Beach, Fla.; Maura Kane of Rochester, N.H.; Ellen, of Washington, and Kiera Siobhan, of Clearwater; a stepmother, Mary, of Throgs Neck, N.Y.; two sisters, Alice Peper of East Salem, N.Y., and Catherine Andre of Warwick, N.Y.; two stepsisters, Joyce Sampson of Warwick and Arlayne Grimes of Yonkers; a stepbrother, Kieran Both of Yaphank, N.Y., and one grandchild.
