        Albert L. Shore, a financier and supermarket developer, died of cancer on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 81 years old and also had a home in Palm Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Shore founded the Tremont Finance Company in Providence, R.I., in the 40's and developed and managed the United Public Market supermarkets and shopping centers in New England.
        He was born in Providence. Mr. Shore was an alumnus of Brown University.
        Surviving are his wife, Beatrice; a daughter, Marjorie Gilbert of Rye, N.Y.; a son, Barrie, of East Greenwich, R.I., and four grandchildren.
