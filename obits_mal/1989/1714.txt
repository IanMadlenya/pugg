        John H. Williams, retired president and chief executive officer of Buck Consultants Inc., an actuarial concern with headquarters in Secaucus, N.J., died Monday at his home in Boca Raton, Fla., after a short illness. He was 76 years old.
        Mr. Williams, a native of Washington, joined the company in Manhattan in 1936. He retired in 1978 but remained on the board until 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy Baker; a son, James, of Boca Raton; a daughter, Ellen DeBois of Stuart, Fla.; a sister, Sarah Frances Bolling of Beltsville, Md., and two grandchildren.
