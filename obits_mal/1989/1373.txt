        Christian E. Albert, a marketing executive at Schieffelin & Somerset Company, liquor importers of Manhattan, died of cancer of the stomach July 1 at his mother's home in Quogue, L.I. He was 46 years old and lived in Pelham Manor, N.Y.
        Mr. Albert was an alumnus of Yale College and received a graduate degree at Oxford University. An oarsman, he rowed for Kent School, Yale, Oxford, the Leander Club at Henley-on-Thames and the London Rowing Club.
        He is survived by his wife, Cynthia; two sons, Christian H. M. and Alexander C., and a daughter, Sarah D., all of Pelham Manor; a sister, Jeanie A. Robinson of Huntington, L.I., and his mother, Jean Winters of Quogue.
