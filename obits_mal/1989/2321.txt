        Dr. Milton J. Grand, a member of the senior pediatrics staff at Montefiore Medical Center, died yesterday at Lenox Hill Hospital after suffering a heart attack Monday night. He was 85 years old and lived in the Riverdale section of the Bronx.
        Dr. Grand was director of Montefiore's pediatrics service from 1955 to 1963. He was a specialist in collagen vascular diseases in children, which are caused by malfunctions of the immune system.
        Before joining Montefiore in 1954, Dr. Grand was associated with Morrisania Hospital and Mount Sinai Hospital. He was also an associate clinical professor at the Albert Einstein College of Medicine.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Pearl Greene; a son, Dr. Richard, of Boston; a daughter, Ellen, of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
