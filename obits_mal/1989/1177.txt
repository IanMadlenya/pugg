        Richard K. Doan, a writer on radio and television affairs, died of natural causes Wednesday at his home in Yarmouth Port, Mass. He was 78 years old.
        Mr. Doan joined TV Guide in 1966 and retired in 1975. Earlier he had been radio-television editor for The New York Herald Tribune, for which he wrote a nationally syndicated column. He was also administrative head of Martin Stone Associates, a television program-packaging concern and program director of WCBS-TV.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy Bertsch, and a sister, Betty J. Johnson, of Fort Myers, Fla.
