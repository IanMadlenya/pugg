        Harry A. Denehy, an executive of Joseph E. Seagram & Sons, died of a heart attack Friday at Huntington (L.I.) Hospital. He was 69 years old and lived in Huntington.
        Mr. Denehy was senior vice president of international special markets for the Seagram Overseas Sales Company, where he oversaw sales in duty-free retail outlets. He joined Seagram in 1955 as a sales trainee.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Anne Althisar, and four children, Maureen, Thomas, Mary and Neil, all of Huntington.
        Correction:  September 28, 1989, Thursday, Late Edition - Final
        An obituary yesterday about Harry A. Denehy, an executive of Joseph E. Seagram & Sons who died Friday, misstated his age. He was 59 years old, not 69.
