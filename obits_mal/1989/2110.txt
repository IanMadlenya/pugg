        Ernesto Cardinal Civardi of Italy died on Tuesday in Rome. He was 83 years old. The cause of death was not made public.
        Cardinal Civardi became a bishop in 1967 and was elevated to cardinal by Pope John Paul II in 1979. He was secretary of the conclaves that elected Popes John Paul I and John Paul II.
        The Pope will lead funeral services at the Vatican on Thursday. Cardinal Civardi's death brings the number of Roman Catholic cardinals to 149.
