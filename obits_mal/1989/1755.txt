        Robert V. Engle, graphics director for the state's Urban Development Corporation and the Department of Economic Development, died of a heart attack Wednesday at Coney Island Hospital. He was 65 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Engle joined the state agencies in 1988. Previously, he had been a senior editor at Newsweek.
        Born in Chicago, Mr. Engle graduated from the University of Wisconsin. He was the coach of the Brooklyn Kings, a semi-professional football team, at the time of his death.
        Surviving are his wife, Lenore; a daughter, Laurie, of Brooklyn, and two sons, Jonathan and Jeremy, both of Manhattan.
