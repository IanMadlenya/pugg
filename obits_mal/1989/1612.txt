        Joshua Ashkenazi, an exporter and a philanthropist, died of cancer on Thursday at his home in Hewlett, L.I. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Ashkenazi was the founder and president of Oriental Exporters Inc. in Manhattan, which operates American-flag merchant ships. He was a frequent contributor to the United Jewish Appeal-Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and a trustee of the Congregation Sons of Israel in Woodmere, L.I. He was born in Russia.  Surviving are his wife, Sonia; a daughter, Dinah Evan; a son, Elliott, and three grandchildren.
