        Norris H. Hoyt, retired editorial director of Houghton Mifflin's educational division, died of coronary artery disease Monday at his home in Hanover, N.H. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Hoyt, a native of Middleport, N.Y., was a graduate of Hamilton College and a member of Phi Beta Kappa. He joined the book publishing concern in 1929 and rose to head the educational division. He was also a director of the company at his retirement in 1971.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jean Archibald; a son, Norris Jr. of Norwich, Vt., and two grandsons.
