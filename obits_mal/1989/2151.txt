        James R. Albano, a partner in the accounting firm of Deloitte Haskins & Sells, died of lung cancer on Saturday at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 48 years old and lived in Woodbury, L.I.
        Mr. Albano joined the firm in Rochester in 1965. He transferred as a partner to the Woodbury office in 1981.
        He was an alumnus of Niagara University.
        Surviving are his wife, Clotilde; five children, John, James, Joseph, Theresa and Lisa, all of Woodbury; his mother, Ann, of Greece, N.Y.; a brother, Stephen, of Greece, and two sisters, Bertha Makay of Webster, N.Y., and Kathleen Guadliardo of Greece.
