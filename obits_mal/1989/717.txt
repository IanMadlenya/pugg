        Robert S. Moss, a manufacturing executive, died of heart disease Monday at his home in Bergenfield, N.J. He was 69 years old.
        Mr. Moss was a former vice president of Lightolier Inc., a lighting manufacturer, and a former president of Airequipt Inc., a manufacturer of slide projectors. He was treasurer of the New York State Urban Development Corporation from 1969 to 1972.
        Surviving are his wife, Doris; two daughters, Nicole Moss, of Princeton, N.J., and Andrea Goldensohn, of Sri Lanka; two sisters, Sylvia Cohen, of Millburn, N.J., and Marilyn Richards, of Creskill, N.J., and two grandchildren.
