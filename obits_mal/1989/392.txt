        Charles S. Gage, a retired treasurer of Yale University, died Sunday at Yale-New Haven Hospital. He was 85 years old and lived in Hamden, Conn.
        Before becoming treasurer in 1954, Mr. Gage had been a vice president of the Mathieson Chemical Corporation.
        Mr. Gage was a former president of the Yale Club of New York, a former trustee of the Phillips Academy in Andover, Mass., and a former president of the board of Yale-New Haven.
        Mr. Gage was born in Tyngsboro, Mass. He was a Yale alumnus.
        Surviving is his wife, Margaret.
