        Dr. Lowyd W. R. Ballantyne Jr., a retired plastic surgeon, died of a heart attack on Wednesday at North Carolina Memorial Hospital in Chapel Hill, N.C. He was 65 years old and lived in Chapel Hill.
        He was chief of the reconstructive and plastic surgery clinics at Roosevelt Hospital in Manhattan before his retirement in 1983. He was also an instructor at the Columbia College of Physicians and Surgeons and a visiting surgeon at Harlem Hospital.
        Dr. Ballantyne, who was born in Syracuse, received his medical degree from the College of Physicians and Surgeons.
        He is survived by a daughter, Katharine Goodspeed of Yonkers, and a sister, Jean Gerhardt, and a brother, Robert, both of McLean, Va.
