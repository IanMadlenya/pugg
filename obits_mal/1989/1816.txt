        Dr. Edgar Bacon, a dentist in Manhattan for 50 years, died of a heart attack Sept. 28 at the Munson Medical Center in Traverse City, Mich. He was 86 years old and lived in Old Mission, Mich.
        Dr. Bacon, a 1926 graduate of the University of Michigan, was in private practice in Manhattan until his retirement in the mid-1970's. He was a past president of the New York Academy of Dentistry and in his retirement wrote a history of the organization.
        He is survived by a son, George, of Lubbock, Tex.; a sister, Blossom Dick of Wellesley Hills, Mass., and three grandchildren.
