        Henry W. Otis, retired chairman of Marsh & Mclennan Companies, died on Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Otis worked for the insurance broker from 1936 to 1971. For many years he led the pension department in New York. He became president and chief operating officer in 1969 and was elected chairman in 1970. Before 1935, he had worked for the Equitable Assurance Society, which he joined in 1924. Mr. Otis was born in New York. He was a director of seven Putnam mutual funds, the U.S.O. and the American Cancer Society. He was chairman of the executive committee and a board member of the Metropolitan Club.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Constance Maxfield; two daughters, Joan Murphy and Lynn Orczyk, both of Huntington, L.I.; seven grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
