        Charles Richard West Jr., former editorial page editor of The Dallas Morning News and a Texas journalist for 50 years, died Saturday after a long illness. He was 76 years old.
        Before joining the Morning News in 1944, he worked at The Dallas Journal and The Associated Press as a desk editor and sportswriter.
        In 1946, 1949 and 1952, Mr. West won first place in a state writing contest sponsored by The Associated Press Managing Editors Association.
