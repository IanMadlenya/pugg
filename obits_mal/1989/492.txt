        Joseph Romer, a former account executive in the advertising department of The New York Times, died Thursday at the Veterans Administration Hospital in Manhattan from complications after suffering a stroke. He was 76 years old and lived in Forest Hills, Queens.
        Born in Manhattan, Mr. Romer worked at The Times from 1932 to 1982.
        Surviving are his wife, Charlotte; a daughter, Judith, of Forest Hills and a sister, Miriam Aaron of Truro, Mass.
