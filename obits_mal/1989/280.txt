        Dr. Raymond J. Boller, who spent his medical career at St. Vincent's Hospital and Medical Center in Manhattan, died Wednesday at the hospital after a short illness. He was 72 years old and lived in Flushing, Queens.
        Dr. Boller, a 1942 graduate of New York University College of Medicine, was an intern and resident at St. Vincent's before joining the staff in 1946. He was director of personnel health services when he retired in 1988.
        He is survived by his wife, Winifred; a son, Raymond, of Chicago; six daughters, Kathleen Nugent of Mahwah, N.J., Maureen Delaney of Bayside, Queens, Margaret Mary, of Jersey City, Marybeth, of Flushing, and Rosemary and Mary Alice, both of Manhattan, and six grandchildren.
