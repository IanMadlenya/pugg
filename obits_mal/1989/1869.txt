        David J. Jacobson, a marketing executive, died of a heart attack Monday at his home in Mamaroneck, N.Y. He was 72 years old.
        Mr. Jacobson, a graduate of the University of North Carolina, was president of the Marketing Services Corporation at his death. He was also the former president of the MGM Merchandising Corporation and a former director of public relations for CBS Television.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; a son, John, of East Northport, L.I.; three daughters, Joy, of Manhattan, Kim Greene of Miami, and Amy Zinaman of Rye, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
