        Royal Riley, a retired assistant district attorney in Queens and a former newspaper reporter, died of complications from emphysema on Friday at Booth Memorial Medical Center in Queens. He was 82 years old and lived in Queens.
        Mr. Riley, a graduate of St. John's University Law School, was a reporter for The Daily News for 25 years before joining the District Attorney's office in 1955. He retired in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marie McDonough; a daughter, Mary-Gail Reilly of Setauket, L.I., and six grandchildren.
