        John A. Wiethe, an athlete and coach who became a lawyer and political leader, died of a blood disease Wednesday at Jewish Hospital. He was 76 years old.
        Before he resigned last year Mr. Wiethe served as chairman or co-chairman of the Hamilton County Democratic Party in Ohio for 34 years.
        A football guard and linebacker, he played for Xavier University and was with the Detroit Lions in the National Football League from 1939 to 1942. He was basketball coach at the University of Cincinnati from 1946 to 1952.
        Survivors include three daughters, a son and eight grandchildren.
