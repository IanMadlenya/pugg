        Paul A. Salomone, a retired retail executive, died of cancer Tuesday at St. Barnabas Medical Center in Livingston, N.J. He was 67 years old and lived in South Orange, N.J.
        Mr. Salomone was a former president of Gimbel's New York division before he retired in 1975. Since then he had worked as a consultant to retail stores.
        Mr. Salomone, who was born in Newark, was graduated from Rutgers University.
        Surviving are his wife, Caroline, and two sisters, Lucy Scaiano of Union, N.J., and Fay Rizzio of West Orange, N.J.
