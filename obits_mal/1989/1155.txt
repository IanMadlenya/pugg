        Oliver Hazard Perry Baldwin, a retired banker and retired Air Force colonel, died yesterday at the Devon Manor Home in Pennsylvania after a long illness. He was 57 years old and had moved to the home from Villanova, Pa.
        Mr. Baldwin, a graduate of New York University, worked for Morgan Guaranty Trust Company of New York before moving to the Girard Bank, now known as Mellon Bank East. He retired for health reasons in 1979.
        He is survived by a daughter, Elizabeth Jutila, of Plano, Tex.; two sons, O. H. Perry 3d, of Manhattan and Matthew, of Bryn Mawr, Pa.; his mother, Elizabeth, of Newtown Square, Pa.; a brother, a sister, and two grandchildren. He was the great-great-great grandson of Commodore Oliver Hazard Perry, a hero of the War of 1812.
