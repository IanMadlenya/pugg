        Thomas A. Savage, a former president of William Iselin & Company, which provides financial services to the garment trade, died of pneumonia on Sunday at the Youville Hospital and Rehabilitation Center in Cambridge, Mass. He was 70 years old and lived in Sebago Lake, Me.
        Mr. Savage, a former resident of Brookfield, Conn., had also been the chief executive officer of CBT Factors, a New York subsidiary of the Connecticut Bank and Trust Company.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Jane Haley; two sons, Thomas F., of Stamford, Conn., and Donald J., of Natick, Mass.; two daughters, Deborah J. Weinshank of Brookfield, and Laura A. Savage of Brookline, Mass., and four grandchildren.
