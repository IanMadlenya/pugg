        Dr. Carlos Carrillo, a psychiatrist and physician, died of a heart attack on Wednesday at St. Vincent's Hospital in Manhattan. He was 64 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He was a former resident in psychiatry at Bellevue Hospital Center. In 1963, he headed an alcoholism clinic for the Westchester Community Mental Health Service. More recently, he was affiliated with Hillside Hospital in Queens and with St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan.
        He is survived by a sister, Elena, and a brother, Jorge, both of Santiago, Chile.
