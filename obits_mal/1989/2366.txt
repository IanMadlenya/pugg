        The Rev. William David Eddy, the rector of Christ Episcopal Church in Tarrytown, N.Y., died of a brain tumor Tuesday at the Rosary Hill Home in Hawthorne, N.Y. He was 65 years old and lived in Tarrytown.
        Mr. Eddy graduated from Antioch College in 1947 and from the Virginia Theological Seminary in 1950. He was ordained a priest in Osaka, Japan, the following year.
        Except for brief stays in the United States, during which he earned a master's degree in Far Eastern Studies from Harvard University in 1960, he was in Japan for 20 years. Mr. Eddy was director of the student center at the University of Hokkaido in Sapporo, Japan, until 1971, when he became rector of Christ Church.
        He is survived by his wife, Elizabeth; two sons, William, of the Bronx, and Nathaniel, of Westbrook, Conn.; two daughters, Eleanor Meldahl of Truro, Mass., and the Rev. Maria Eddy of Alexandria, Va., and three grandchildren.
