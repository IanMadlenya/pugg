        Eugene R. Sullivan , a retired Wall Street lawyer, died after a long illness Sunday at his home in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 85 years old. Mr. Sullivan, a graduate of Holy Cross College and Harvard Law School, was a partner in Fruehaff, Farrell, Sullivan & Bryan. He represented the Cities Service Company in the divestiture of its gas utilities, and then many of the utilities. He retired in 1974.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Patricia Lawler; a son, Eugene R. Jr., of Larchmont, N.Y.; three daughters, Anne of Manhattan, Jane Burke of Bronxville, and Mary Tracy of Waban, Mass.; a brother, Louis A. of New Haven, and nine grandchildren.
