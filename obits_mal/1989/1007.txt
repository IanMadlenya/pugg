        V. Hall Everson Jr., a former state representative from Greenwich, Conn., for 40 years, died, apparently of a heart attack, Saturday at the Hospital of St. Raphael in New Haven. He was 92 years old and lived in Branford.
        Mr. Everson was a native of Brooklyn. He was the former president and chairman of the board of V. Hall Everson Jr. Inc., insurance brokers in New York. He was a past president of the Greenwich Insurance Board.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Violet Hardie Mars; three sons, Valentine H. 3d, of Branford, Gary A., of Palm Beach, Fla., and Allan F., of Louden, N.H.; nine grandchildren, and five great-grandchildren.
