        Donald P. Wright, a vice president and general counsel at the Harris Corporation, the electronics and communciations company, died of a heart attack Thursday at Holmes Regional Hospital in Melbourne, Fla. He was 48 years old and lived in Indialantic, Fla.
        A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Law School, Mr. Wright joined Harris in 1971 as a corporate attorney. He became vice president and general counsel in 1985. Previously, he was an associate with the Manhattan firm of Dewey, Ballantine, Busby, Palmer & Wood.
        He is survived by his wife, Caryl, and four daughters, Courtney, Heather, Hillary and Shannon, all of Indiatlantic.
