        Keith Waggoner, a manager for Jujamcyn Theaters, died on Saturday at Roosevelt Hospital in Manhattan. He was 42 years old.
        He died of lymphoma, said his parents, E. F. (Sandy) and Betty Waggoner of Beloit, Kan.
        Since 1983, Mr. Waggoner managed the Eugene O'Neill Theater, one of the Jujamcyn chain's five Broadway theaters. He had previously managed the New Apollo Theater, and had been the company manager for shows including ''On Golden Pond'' and the London production of ''A Chorus Line.''
        Mr. Waggoner was also the stage manager for several touring shows, an administrator with the New York Philharmonic for seven years and a member of the Association of Theatrical Press Agents and Managers.
        His companion was Dave Rogers of New York.
        In addition to his parents, he is survived by his brother, Rex, also of Beloit.
