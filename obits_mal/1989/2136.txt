        Thomas R. C. Hood, a retired textile executive, died of a heart attack Friday at his home in Larchmont, N.Y. He was 71 years old.
        Born in Baltimore, Mr. Hood joined Cone Mills Corporation, of Greensboro, N.C., in 1939 and later became a vice president and a director. He retired in 1969 and worked as a consultant. From 1977 to 1981, he was president and chief operating officer of Burke Mills Inc., of Valdese, N.C. He was a retired captain in the Marine Corps.
        He is survived by his wife, Elsie; a daughter, Linda, of New York City, and four sons, Thomas, of Colts Neck, N.J., Jeffrey, of Glen Head, L.I., Clarke, of New York City and Robin, of Raleigh, N.Y., and seven grandchildren.
