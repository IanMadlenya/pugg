        Louis A. Credidio, the president and chief operating officer of the Trans-Lux Corporation, died of lung cancer Saturday at the Connecticut Hospice in Branford. He was 46 years old and lived in Fairfield, Conn.
        Mr. Credidio, a 1968 graduate of Iona College with a master's degree in business, started his career with the Reuben H. Donnelley Corporation in Manhattan as an account executive. He joined Trans-Lux in its New York office in 1968 as a sales representative. He was named executive vice president in 1980 and in 1987 became president of the Norwalk-based telecommunications company. He had been on the board of directors since 1983. He was a member of the Sales Executive club and the American Management Association.
        He is survived by his wife, Evelyn; a son, Louis; a daughter, Kim, and his parents, Dominic and Edith Velto Credidio of Daytona Beach, Fla.
