        Brother Abdon Lewis, a former dean of Manhattan College, died of a stroke Sunday at St. Joseph's Medical Center in Yonkers. He was 73 years old and lived in the Bronx.
        Brother Lewis, whose given name was Louis Garavaglia, graduated from Manhattan College. He became a member of the Brothers of the Christian Schools in 1937, taught at the elementary and high school levels, and joined the faculty at Manhattan College in 1950. He was dean of the College of Arts and Sciences from 1962 until 1970, when he became director of the graduate division. He retired in 1985.
        He is survived by three sisters, Sister Mary Amadeo, S.S.N.D., of Milwaukee, Florence Hoffer of Harper Woods, Mich., and Virginia Saroglia of Dearborn, Mich.
