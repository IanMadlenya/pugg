        George Mc Grath, a certified public accountant, died of a stroke on Friday at his home in Short Hills, N.J. He was 99 years old.
        Mr. Mc Grath founded Mc Grath, Doyle & Phair in Manhattan in 1928 and was a partner in the firm until last year.
        He was one of the founding directors of the Jessie Smith Noyes Foundation, an environmental-advocacy group in Manhattan, and was a director at his death.
        He was born in Orange, N.J., and was a 1912 graduate of New York University.
        A widower, he is survived by a sister, Beatrice M. Baldwin of South Orange, N.J., and a grandson.
