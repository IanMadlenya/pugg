        Thomas Cheeger, a retired structural engineer, died Sunday at a nursing home in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 87 years old and lived in West Palm Beach.
        Mr. Cheeger spent much of his career with Madigan-Hyland, the engineering business in Manhattan now called URS Consultants. He retired in 1968. He helped design the Gov. Thomas E. Dewey Thruway, the Gowanus Parkway and Riverside Church.
        He was born in Poughkeepsie, N.Y., and graduated from the Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in 1923.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Pauline Lynn; a brother, Samuel, of Manhattan, a son, Jeff, of Brooklyn, and a granddaughter.
