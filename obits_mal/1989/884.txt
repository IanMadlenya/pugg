        Dr. Ferdinand F. McAllister, a surgeon and an emeritus professor of clinical surgery at Columbia University's College of Physicians and Surgeons, died yesterday at Indian River Memorial Hospital in Vero Beach, Fla. He was 77 years old and had lived in Nyack, N.Y. until two years ago, when he moved to Vero Beach.
        Dr. McAllister was a graduate of Dartmouth College and the Harvard Medical School and for many years was an attending surgeon at Presbyterian Hospital in New York.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen Walker; two daughters, Ann Minton of Philadelphia and Mary Conway of Asheville, N.C., and a son, Dr. Thomas McAllister of Philadelphia.
