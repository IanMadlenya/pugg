        Richard W. Simmons, a stockbroker and former chairman of the Midwest Stock Exchange, died Tuesday at the Lake Forest (Ill.) Hospital. He was 84 years old and lived in Lake Bluff, Ill.
        Mr. Simmons, who was born in St. Louis and was a Yale graduate, was with the firm of Lee Higginson from 1929 to 1948. He left to become a founder of the investment firm of Blunt, Ellis & Simmons. He served as chairman of the Midwest Stock Exchange in 1969 and 1970.
