        The Rev. Julius Lafayette Tucker, a radio and television minister, died Tuesday. He was 92 years old.
        Mr. Tucker, an Adventist minister, developed his program, ''The Quiet Hour,'' on radio in Portland, Ore., in 1935. In 1949 he and his son, LaVerne, began televising it.
        Mr. Tucker retired from pastoral duties in 1959 and devoted himself to his program, now produced in Redland, Calif. It is heard throughout North America, Europe, Africa and Asia.
