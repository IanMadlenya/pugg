        Theodore C. Agins, a retired dentist and former mayor of Kensington, L.I., died of a heart attack Thursday at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 80 years old and lived in Great Neck, L.I.
        Born in Minsk, Russia, he came to the United States as an infant. He graduated from Alfred University and Tufts University School of Dentistry. He practiced in Manhattan for 50 years before retiring in 1979.
        Dr. Agins served three terms as mayor of the village of Kensington, from 1956 to 1962.
        Surviving are his wife, Sarah; two sons, Stephen and Richard, both of Manhattan, and two grandchildren.
