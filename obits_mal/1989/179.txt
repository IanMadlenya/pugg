        John Allston Sargent, a retired executive and former chief financial officer of the General Dynamics Corporation, died of a stroke Monday in Greenwich, Conn. He was 79 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        Mr. Sargent, who received a bachelor's degree from Yale University in 1933, was also an executive vice president of the General Foods Corporation and a president of the Diamond Alkalai Company. Previously he had worked for the Campbell Metal Window Corporation and the Truscon Steel Company.
        Surviving are his wife, Shirley, and two sons, John, of Cleveland, and David, of San Francisco.
