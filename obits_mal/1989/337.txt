
       William Forrest, a former star athlete who appeared in nearly 200 films and on the television series ''The Adventures of Rin Tin Tin,'' died Jan. 29. He was 86 years old. Mr. Forrest's films included ''The Fighting Seabees,'' ''I'll See You in My Dreams,'' ''Here Comes Mr. Jordan,'' ''Miracle on 34th Street'' and ''They Died With Their Boots On.
