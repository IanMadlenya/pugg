        Dr. Charles N. Barrett, a physician, radiologist and insurance executive, died of cancer Saturday at his home in Cincinnati. He was 76 years old.
        Dr. Barrett was chairman of Western-Southern Life Insurance Company, a firm he joined in 1942 as associate medical director. He was named president in 1973 and chairman and chief executive in 1984. He relinquished the title of chief executive in 1988.
        He was also a radiologist and researcher on cancer. The cancer research center at the University of Cincinnati, where he was a trustee for 13 years, was named in his honor.
        He is survived by his wife, May Belle, two daughters, Angela Eynon and Marian Leibold; four sons, C. Francis, John, Michael and William, and 16 grandchildren. All live in Cincinnati.
