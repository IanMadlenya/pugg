        Julius Rolnitzky, a retired assistant United States attorney, died of cancer Sunday at the Presbyterian Hospital in Manhattan. He was 77 years old and lived in Long Island City, Queens.
        Mr. Rolnitzky, a 1934 graduate of St. John's University School of Law, was with the United States Attorney's offices in Brooklyn and Manhattan for 36 years before joining the New York County Lawyers Association in 1969 as executive director. He retired in 1976.
        He is survived by his wife, Anne; a son, Neil, of Los Angeles; a daughter, Linda, of Manhattan, and a granddaughter.
