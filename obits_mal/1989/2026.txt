        Claude H. Nourtier, a travel marketing executive, died of cancer Tuesday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 55 years old and lived in Manhattan and Southampton, L.I.
        Mr. Nourtier was the executive vice president and co-founder of Marketing Ahead, a reservation service for hotels in France, Spain and Portugal. He was born in Boulogne-sur-Mer, France, and began his career as a caterer.
        There are no survivors.
