        Seward Brisbane, a real-estate executive in Manhattan, died of respiratory failure Thursday at his home in Upper Brookville, L.I. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Brisbane was president of the Brisbane Organizations, a family-owned real-estate holding company. He became president in 1936 after his father died.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Doris Fauser; five children, Arthur, of Washington, Charles, of Manhattan, Aleta, of Bonsall, Calif., Peter, of North Palm Beach, Fla., and Maria, of Washington, and a sister, Elinor Philbin of Manhattan.
