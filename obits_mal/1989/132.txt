        Joseph K. Miller, a lawyer and accountant active in Jewish concerns, died in the crash of Pan American World Airways Flight 103 Dec. 20 in Lockerbie, Scotland. He was 56 years old and lived in Woodmere, L.I.
        Mr. Miller was a senior partner in Miller, Ellin & Company, a Manhattan accounting firm. Among others, he served as treasurer of Yeshiva University and the Stern College for Women.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Rhoda Malett; three sons, Alan and Gary, both of Forest Hills, Queens, and Geoffrey, of Woodmere; a daughter, Sharon, of Woodmere; a sister, Rita Bregman of Brooklyn, and a granddaughter.
