        Harry Schneider, a lawyer in New York for 60 years, died Monday at a Mamaroneck, N.Y., nursing home, after a long illness. He was 81 years old and lived in Harrison, N.Y.
        Mr. Schneider, who specialized in corporate law, was a co-founder in 1929 of Jaffin, Schneider & Conrad, a law firm in Manhattan. He retired in 1988.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lillian Epstein; a son, Peter, of Manhattan; a daughter, Ellen, of Manhattan; a brother, Matty, of Washington, N.J., and two grandchildren.
