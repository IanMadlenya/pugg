        Stephen E. Rothbaum, a managing director of Morgan Stanley & Company, investment bankers, died of heart failure yesterday at Morristown (N.J.) Memorial Hospital. He was 47 years old and lived in Millburn, N.J.
        Mr. Rothbaum, a graduate of Pace College, joined Morgan Stanley in 1978 as a manager of new accounts. He was named a principal of the firm in 1985 and became a managing director in 1988.
        He is survived by his wife, Ellen, and three children, Gregg, Marissa and Randi.
