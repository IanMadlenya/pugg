        T. Herbert Shriver 2d, a retired investment banker, died Wednesday at his home in Towson, Md., after a long illness. He was 77 years old.
        A 1935 graduate of Yale University, Mr. Shriver was associated for many years with the Manhattan investment firm of Harrison & Ripley.
        He is survived by his wife, Willa, of Towson; a son, T. Herbert 3d, of Oconomowoc, Wis.; two daughters, Hilda, of Takoma Park, Md. and Willa S. Bakker, of Braddock Heights, Md.; a brother, Sargent Shriver of Potomac, Md., who headed the Peace Corps, and one grandchild.
