        Donald Appleton, a retired senior vice president of the Oxford Paper Company in Manhattan and a former executive of paper companies in New England, died Tuesday at the Beverly Hospital in Massachusetts. He was 94 years old and lived in Manchester, Mass.
        Mr. Appleton, a 1918 graduate of Harvard College, had been a director of Nashwalk Pulp and Paper Company, Ltd., and Rumford Falls Power Company and had managed the Champion International Paper Company.
        He is survived by two daughters, Alix Hasson, of Rockville, Md., and Lucy Potter, of Ipswich, Mass.; a son, John, of Manchester, Mass.; 19 grandchildren and 13 great-grandchildren.
