        The Rev. Joseph S. Breen, a vice president of St. John's University, died, apparently of a heart attack, Monday night at his home at the university in Jamaica, Queens. He was 70 years old.
        Father Breen, a Vincentian, had been associated with St. John's since 1974. At his death he was dean of St. John's College, an undergraduate unit. He was ordained in 1944 and received a master's degree at Columbia University and a doctorate at Catholic University.
        He is survived by two sisters, Marie Pearson of Winston-Salem, N.C., and Bernadette Kline of Philadelphia.
