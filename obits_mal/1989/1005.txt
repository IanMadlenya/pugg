        Moe Goldman, a standout basketball player at City College who later played professionally with the Philadelphia Sphas, died Sunday. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Goldman played center for City College in the early 1930's and was with the Philadelphia team from 1934-43. He served as commissioner of the New York City Public Schools Athletic League before retiring in 1977.
        He is survived by his wife, Mae; a son, Stephen; and two daughters, Marian and Ronnie.
