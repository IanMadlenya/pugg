        Daniel T. Adams, a former executive vice president of Marine Midland Bank, died of heart failure yesterday at Mediplex Nursing Home in Westport, Conn. He was 80 years old and lived Wilton, Conn., and Manhattan.
        Born in Buffalo, he graduated from Yale University and joined the bank in the 1930's. He retired in 1971.
        Surviving are his wife, Adelaide; two daughters, Nancy Downey of Manhattan and Marjorie Kooke of Watch Hill, R.I.; a brother, Roger, of Buffalo and four grandchildren.
