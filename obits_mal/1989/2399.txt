        Joseph L. Ferraro, a retired lawyer and former president of the Board of Education in Paterson, N.J., died of complications of heart surgery on Sunday at the St. Joseph's Medical Center in Paterson. He was 67 years old and lived in Oakhurst, N.J.
        Mr. Ferraro practiced law for many years in Paterson and was on the Board of Education in the 50's. He was also a former housing coordinator for Paterson and had been president of the Lazzara Baking Company for 10 years.
        Surviving are a son, Angelo, of Manhattan; a daughter, Natalie Orties of Elberon, N.J.; a brother, Dr. Stephen Ferraro of Hillsdale, N.J., and two grandchildren.
