        Bradford W. Stone, a retired automotive executive, died of heart failure Tuesday at Alexandria (Va.) Hospital. He was 83 years old and lived in Falls Church, Va.
        Mr. Stone, a 1927 graduate of Dartmouth College, joined the General Motors Coporation in 1929 and managed plants around the world. He retired in 1970 as regional manager for Pan American operations.
        He is survived by his wife, Edith; three daughters, Josephine Salmon of Arlington, Va., Mary Cover of Manhattan and Susan Ferrer of Miami; a brother, George, of Bethesda, Md., and six grandchildren.
