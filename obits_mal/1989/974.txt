        James W. Rodgers, a retired partner in the law firm of Townley, Updike, Carter & Rodgers, died of a heart attack on April 29 at Phelps Memorial Hospital in North Tarrytown, N.Y. He was 81 years old and lived in North Tarrytown.
        Mr. Rodgers was associated with the firm in Manhattan from 1941 to 1974. His specialty was libel law.  He was an alumnus of the Naval Academy and the Harvard Law School.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Margaret Holmes; a daughter, Diana Brown of Lubbock, Tex.; four grandchildren, and one great-grandson.
