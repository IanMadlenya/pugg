        Carl Frank Bayerschmidt, a former chairman of the Germanics Department at Columbia University, died of a stroke Sunday at St. John's Hospital in Yonkers. He was 84 years old and lived in Yonkers.
        Dr. Bayerschmidt, who earned a Ph.D. from Columbia University in 1934, taught at Rutgers University and at the University of Chicago before moving to Columbia University in 1941. He led the Germanics Department from 1948 to 1961 and retired in 1972.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; two daughters, Carol Daniel of Manhattan and Janet Koch of Forest, Va., and three grandchildren.
