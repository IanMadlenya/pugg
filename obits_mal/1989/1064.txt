        Stanley F. Gray, former president of the retail-store division of Warnaco Inc., died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Westport, Conn. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Gray had worked for Sears Roebuck in New England for 20 years before joining Warnaco, of Bridgeport Conn., in 1975. Previously, he had managed L. A. Schulman's department store in White Plains. He retired last year.
        Mr. Gray had been chairman of the United Fund of Northern Westchester and president of the United Way of Westchester. He was an alumnus of Columbia University.
        Surviving are his wife, Mimi; a son, Thomas, of Peekskill, N.Y.; two daughters, Jane Carroll and Patricia Braun, both of Hollywood, Fla., and six grandchildren.
