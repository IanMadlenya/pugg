        Joseph Charla Jr., executive vice president of the East River Savings Bank, a division of River Bank America of New Rochelle, N.Y., died of a heart attack Tuesday at the Bellevue Hospital Center. He was 65 years old and lived in Larchmont, N.Y.
        Mr. Charla became associated with the bank in 1981, when it merged with the People's Bank for Savings of New Rochelle, of which he was chairman, president and chief executive officer. He joined the merged company as administrative vice president and was named executive vice president in 1984.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Rita Bracken, and four children, Kathleen and Patricia, both of Manhattan, and Joseph and Thomas, both of Hoboken, N.J.
