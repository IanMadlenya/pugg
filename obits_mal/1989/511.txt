        Dr. Henry Mannix Jr., the acting director of surgery at St. Francis Hospital and Medical Center in Hartford, died of cancer there Sunday. He was 61 years old and lived in West Hartford.
        Dr. Mannix, a graduate of Cornell University Medical School, had also been a professor of surgery at Cornell and at the University of Connecticut. He had been director of surgery at St. Francis from 1971 to 1977, before going into private practice. He was recalled to the hospital in July.
        He is survived by his wife, Margaret; a son, Henry 3d, and a daughter, Margaret Mary, all of West Hartford.
