        Henry G. Hay, a former vice president of Eastern Airlines, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Hobe Sound, Fla. He was 68 years old.
        Mr. Hay worked for Eastern for almost 20 years, then became president of Cargomatic, an aircraft maintenance company on Long Island. He was an avid amateur golfer.
        He is survived by his wife, Joan; three sons, John, of Boulder, Colo.; David, of San Francisco, and Henry, of Laguna Beach, Calif.; three daughters, Sandra, Laura and Lisa, all of Santa Monica, Calif.; a sister, Edith Wyckoff of Locust Valley, L.I., and four grandchildren.
