        Edward Schuldner, a senior partner in the Redford Group, an executive placement concern, died, apparently of an aneurism, Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 49 years old.
        Mr. Schuldner, a former vice president of Drake Beam Morin Inc., market researchers, also was associated with Sun Chemical Corporation and Philip Morris Companies.
        He graduated from St. John's University and received a master's degree from New York University.
        He is survived by two sons, Stephen and Edward, of Port Washington, L.I., and his mother, Emilie Tkatschuk of Brooklyn,
