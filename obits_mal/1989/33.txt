        James Marshutz, an advertising executive and television producer, died of cancer Jan. 12 at his home in Manhattan. He was 66 years old.
        Mr. Marshutz had worked for the J. Walter Thompson and Campbell-Ewald agencies and had been a senior vice president of Time-Life Television.
        He was born in St. Louis and was an alumnus of the University of Missouri.
        Surviving are a daughter, Katherine McGuire of Chesterfield, Mo., and a son, Scott, of Huntington Beach, Calif.
