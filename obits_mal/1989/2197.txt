        Joseph Markle, a lawyer and retired colonel in the Army, died of heart failure Monday at New York Hospital. He was 89 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A veteran of both world wars, Mr. Markle earned the Legion of Merit and the Army Distinguished Civilian Service Award. He was a former president of the New York chapter of the Association of the United States Army. He is survived by several nieces and nephews.
