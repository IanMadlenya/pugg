        Ronald J. Watkins, a former vice president of human resources for Joseph E. Seagram & Sons, died of complications from a heart transplant Tuesday at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center. He was 58 years old and lived in New Canaan, Conn.
        Mr. Watkins was born in Pittsburgh and graduated from Westminister College. He joined Seagram's in 1979.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Gretchen Schumacher, and two sons, David, of London, and John, of Chatham, N.J.
