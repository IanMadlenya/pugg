        Richard L. Liebeskind, the founder and former president of Ann Taylor Inc., died of a heart attack Tuesday at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Born in New Haven, Mr. Liebeskind founded the women's specialty-shop chain in 1954, building it into a national fashion concern. He resigned in 1979, when he sold the company, which is now part of Allied Stores. Since then, he had been a real-estate investor.
        Surviving are his wife, Barbara; two sons, Richard Jr. and Hank; a daughter, Elizabeth; two stepdaughters, Nicole Elkon and Eliot Sloan, all of Manhattan, and a brother, Paul, of New Haven.
