        Edwin Clark Davis, a retired vice president and general counsel of Borden Inc., died June 6 at a nursing home in Keller, Tex. He was 84 years old and lived in Grapevine, Tex.
        Mr. Davis, a native of Chicago, graduated from the University of Michigan and its law school. He had his own firm from 1930 until 1937, when he joined Borden's legal department. He was named general counsel in 1966 and retired in 1969.
        He is survived by a daughter, Louie Mohr of Fort Lauderdale, Fla.; a son, Clark J., of Grapevine; a brother, John B., of Fort Myers, Fla.; four grandchildren, and one greatgrandchild.
