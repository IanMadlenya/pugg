        Sol E. Kalvert, a businessman and exporter, died July 19 at Nyack (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Kalvert was president of Kalglas International, a Manhattan-based exporter of toys, games and restaurant equipment, and had been president of Lionel-Essex International. Previously, he was assistant export manager at the Lionel Corporation, the manufacturer of toy electric trains.
        He is survived by a son, Michael, of New City and Manhattan; two sisters, Lena Chanin, of Hollis, Queens, and Jenny Faye Zamore, of Brooklyn, and two grandchildren.
