        Colin Carter, a former vice president of the Chase Manhattan Bank, died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Greenwich, Conn. He was 55 years old.
        Mr. Carter worked for the bank from 1977 to 1985. Previously, he worked for the Commonwealth Oil Refining Company.
        Surviving are his wife, Sally; three daughters, Jane and Joanne, both of Greenwich, and Wendy, of Oxford, England; a son, Paul, of Greenwich; his mother, Kathleen, of Bristol, England, and a brother, Derek, of Goring-on-Thames, England.
