        Thomas F. Burke, the chairman and chief executive of M/A-Com Inc. of Burlington, Mass., manufacturers of electronic equipment, was fatally injured Thursday in an automobile accident near Tewksbury, Mass. He was 60 years old and lived in North Andover, Mass.
        Mr. Burke, a graduate of Boston College, had been with M/A-Com since 1961, serving as vice president and chief operating officer before he was elected president and chief executive officer in 1987. He was also chairman of the board.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary; five sons, Thomas, of Andover, and Robert, Daniel, Paul and John, all of North Andover; two daughters, Maureen and Sheila, both of North Andover; a brother, Donald, of Newton; six sisters, Rita Aucoin, Gertrude Burke, Dorothy Jordan, Esther Codyer and Carolyn Burke, all of Newton, and Virginia Welch, of Needham; and one grandson.
