        Dr. Seymour Birnbach, a professor at New York University's School of Dentistry for nearly 30 years, died of chronic heart disease Tuesday at his home in Queens. He was 68 years old.
        Dr. Birnbach was associated with New York University from 1960 until a few months ago, when he became too ill to work. He also taught for many years at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Ruth Saal; a daughter, Ritasue Charlestein of Philadelphia; two sons, Dr. Steven, of Boca Raton, Fla., and Dr. David, of Manhattan, and seven grandchildren.
