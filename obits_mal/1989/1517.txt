        John H. Tiedemann, senior vice president and general sales manager at ABC-TV, died of a heart attack Tuesday at South Nassau Community Hospital in Oceanside, L.I., after major surgery. He was 55 years old and lived in Baldwin, L.I.
        Mr. Tiedemann joined ABC's research department in 1959. He moved to the network sales division in 1963, where he remained for 20 years. He became vice president and director of daytime sales in 1977. In 1984 he was named vice president, national sales manager and two years later was promoted to senior vice president, national sales manager.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Joan Kopf; a son, John, of Baldwin, and a daughter, Cathy, of Manhattan.
