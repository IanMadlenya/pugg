        Arthur J. Emma, chairman and chief executive officer of G. Fox & Company, the Hartford-based department store chain, from 1972 until his retirement in 1980, died Monday at Pardee Hospital in Hendersonville, N.C., after a long illness. He was 68 years old and lived in Hendersonville.
        Mr. Emma was born in Brooklyn and graduated from Cornell University.
        He is survived by his wife, Ruth; a daughter, Mrs. Peter Zieman of Vernon, Conn.; three sons, William, of Hendersonville, Edward, of Hingham, Mass., and David, of Simsbury, Conn.; a sister, Gilda Mantin of Sun Lake, Ariz., and four grandchildren.
