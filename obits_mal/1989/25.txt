        Julian L. Greifer, a former social worker who was a professor emeritus of sociology at Temple University, died of internal bleeding Saturday at Arlington Hospital in Arlington, Va. He was 87 years old and lived in Philadelphia.
        Mr. Greifer taught at Temple from 1974 to 1978. From 1942 to 1965 he was the executive director of Neighborhood Centers of Philadelphia. He was a former president of the National Association of Jewish Center Workers.
        Surviving are his wife, Adeline; a daughter, Judith Benjamin, of McClean, Va.; six grandchildren and four great-grandchildren.
