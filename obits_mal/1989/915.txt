        O. Rundle Gilbert, an auctioneer and appraiser, died of heart failure Sunday at his home in Waitsfield, Vt. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Gilbert was a self-employed auctioneer for almost 50 years, specializing in Americana of the 19th and early 20th centuries. He ran his business out of Garrison, N.Y., and held auctions in many cities along the East Coast. He retired in 1984.
        Mr. Gilbert was born in Manhattan and attended Wesleyan University.
        He is survived by his wife, Anne; four sons, Townsend, of Barre, Vt., Richards, of Garrison, and Lisle and Robert, both of Waitsfield; four grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
