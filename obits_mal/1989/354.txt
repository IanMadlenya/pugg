        Louis A. Couderc, a former executive of the Michelin Tire Corporation, died of pancreatic cancer Tuesday at the Falk Clinic in Toronto. He was 60 years old and had homes in Roslyn Estates, L.I., and Southampton, L.I.
        Mr. Couderc, a native of France, joined Michelin in 1948 and was a vice president from 1962 to 1972. He later sold and distributed other imported tires.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Virginia Barrett; three sons, Gary, of Burnsville, N.C., Greg, of Mastic Beach, L.I., and Louis, of West Chester, Pa.; a daughter, Denise Sandberg of Glen Cove, L.I., and a brother and a sister in France.
