        Leo H. Barry, a lawyer, died Sunday at New York University Medical Center as the result of a stroke early last month. He was 71 years old and lived in Astoria, Queens.
        Mr. Barry was in private practice at 1 Hunter Street in Long Island City, Queens, for 46 years.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Fitzgerald; three sons, Jack of Houston, William of Massapequa, L.I., and Michael of Brooklyn; three daughters, Deborah Tarulli of Glen Head, L.I., Susan Conaghan of Aberdeen, N.J., and Ellen Barry of Manhattan; two brothers, John of Carle Place, L.I., and Richard of Baton Rouge, La., and nine grandchildren.
