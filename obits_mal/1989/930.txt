        Armenak (Albert) Mardirossian, a real-estate developer and philanthropist, died of a stroke Monday at Passaic (N.J.) General Hospital. He was 83 years old and lived in Clifton, N.J.
        Mr. Mardirossian came to the United States from Turkey in 1921 and founded Mardirossian Real Estate of Clifton. He was active in philanthropic causes in the Armenian-American community and was president of the Home for Armenian Aged in Emerson, N.J., for the last 35 years.
        He is survived by a son, Albert, of Clifton; a daughter, Lucille Morba of West Milford, N.J.; three grandchildren, and a great-grandchild.
