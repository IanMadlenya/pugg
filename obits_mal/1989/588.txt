        Dr. Henry Thomas Burns, a retired obstetrician and gynecologist who practiced for many years in New York City, died Monday at his home in Dorset, Vt. He was 95 years old and had also lived in Ardsley-on-Hudson, N.Y.
        Dr. Burns, a native of Maysville, Ga., was a graduate of the University of Georgia Medical College and Emory University's Medical Department. He was a clinical professor of obstetrics and gynecology at the New York University School of Medicine. He is survived by three daughters, Janet St. Germain and Frances, both of Manhattan, and Sheridan Olsen of Rumson, N.J., and two granddaughters.
