        George M. Hart, a lawyer and a former three-term Republican County chairman on Staten Island, died of pancreatic cancer March 20 at the University of California Hospital at San Diego. He was 57 years old and lived on Staten Island.
        Mr. Hart, a 1963 graduate of New York Law School, was county chairman from 1981 to 1987. He was a close aide to John J. Marchi, the Republican State Senator from Staten Island, and most recently an assistant counsel to the Senate Finance Committee. He was also in private law practice on Staten Island.
        In 1971 he ran unsuccessfully for a seat on the City Council, and in 1985 he lost a race for the borough presidency.
        He is survived by his wife, Carolyn; four daughters, Cara Mia, Maria, Elena and Veronica; his father, Clarence, and a sister, Elizabeth Guastavino, all of Staten Island.
