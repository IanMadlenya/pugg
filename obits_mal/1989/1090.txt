        Dr. Herman Alofsin, a dentist who practiced in Manhattan for 67 years, died of heart disease Saturday at White Plains Hospital. He was 91 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        A graduate of the University of Pennsylvania Dental School, Dr. Alofsin began his practice in 1919 and retired three years ago. He was an accomplished musician and played in the viola section of the Westchester County Philharmonic for many years.
        He is survived by his wife, Charlotte; two daughters, Bette Lou, of Manhattan, and Joanne Wilson of Montvale, N.J., and one grandson.
