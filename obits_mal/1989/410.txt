        Dr. Charles A. Hoffman, a pediatrician, died Monday at Good Samaritan Hospital in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 73 years old and lived in Queens.
        Dr. Hoffman was an attending member of the staff of the Booth Memorial Medical Center in Queens. He was also associated with North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I., and Long Island Jewish Hospital.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Miriam Botta; three sons, Dr. Robert Hoffman of Longmeadow, Mass., Dr. Richard Hoffman of Tampa, Fla., and Dr. Mark Hoffman of Glens Falls, N.Y.; a stepdaughter, Lorraine Mandel of Manhattan; a stepson, Robert Mandel of Brentwood, Calif., and seven grandchildren.
