        L. Reyner Samet, a New York divorce lawyer for 50 years whose clients included Colleen Dewhurst, Lee Remick, Barbara Walters and Roy Scheider, died of heart failure Monday in Marina del Rey, Calif., while on vacation. He was 83 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Samet, a native of Baltimore, was counsel to Becker & London. He retired in 1980.
        He was an alumnus of the University of Virginia and of its law school.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Getrude Reyner Levy; a daughter, Jane Rogers of Silver Spring, Md.; two stepsons, Stephen Conn of Las Cruces, N.M., and Dr. Howard Conn of Los Angeles, and seven grandchildren.
