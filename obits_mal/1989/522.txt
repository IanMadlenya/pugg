        Arthur A. Charpentier, a retired law librarian, died of respiratory failure on March 19 at the Eastern Maine Medical Center in Bangor. He was 69 years old and lived in Mount Desert, Me.
        He was librarian and associate dean of the Yale University Law School from 1967 to 1981, when he retired. He had been librarian at the City Bar Association in New York and at Boston University Law School.
        Mr. Charpentier, who was born in Waterbury, Conn., graduated from Springfield College and Boston University Law School.
        Surviving are his wife, Phyllis; a son, Peter, of Durham, Conn.; three daughters, Meredith, of Manhattan, Nancy, of Seattle, and Susan, of Seacliff, N.Y.; two sisters, Isabel Knowles of Harwichport, Mass., and Elinor Hartshorn of Springfield, Mass., and four grandsons.
