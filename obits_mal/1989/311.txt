        James Franklin Darr, a leader in shifting wire service transmissions from teletype and telephone lines to computers and satellites, died of heart failure yesterday at St. Joseph's Hospital in Stamford, Conn. He was 72 years old and lived in Stamford.
        He is survived by his wife, Helen; a daughter, Judy Eaton, of Darien, Conn.; a son, James R., of Chino, Calif., and six grandchildren.
