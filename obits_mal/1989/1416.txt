        Thatcher C. Jones, a securities analyst and retired professor emeritus of finance at New York University, died Saturday in Salt Lake City, where he lived. He was 100 years old.
        Mr. Jones, who earned a Ph.D. in finance from Columbia University in 1931, taught at N.Y.U. for 35 years and retired in 1955. He was also an investment banker.
        He is survived by his wife, Elda; two sons, Rud, of Bountiful, Utah, and Kaye , of Naples, Fla.; two daughters, Dorothea Larsen of Burlingame, Calif., and Phyliss Kimball of Fairfield, Conn., fifteen grandchildren and sixty great-grandchildren.
