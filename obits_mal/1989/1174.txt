        Samuel Harris Cohen, a retired labor lawyer from Manhattan, died Saturday in his sleep at the Bethesda Care Center in Laramie, Wyo. He was 83 years old and had moved to Laramie last year.
        Mr. Cohen, a graduate of New York University Law School, was with the Boudin, Cohn & Glickstein law firm in Manhattan for almost 45 years, representing several labor unions. He retired in 1975.
        He is survived by his son, Roy H. Carlin, of Manhattan; a daughter, Martha Pheneger of Laramie; a brother, David Carlin, and a sister, Bertha Levine, both of Hallandale, Fla., and four grandchildren.
