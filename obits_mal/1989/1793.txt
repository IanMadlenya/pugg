        Ferdinand F. E. Kopecky, a retired patent lawyer, died of a heart attack on Aug. 29 at his home in the Bronx. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Kopecky worked for chemical companies, including Monsanto and Merck. He was born in New York and graduated from Rutgers University and the St. Louis Law School. He retired in 1971.
        Surviving are his wife, Edith; a son, Ferinand F. O., of Placentia, Calif.; a daughter, Maria Higgins, of Washington; three brothers, August, of Linden, N.J., William, of St. Petersburg, Fla., and Theodore, of Shreveport, La.; two granddaughters, and a great-granddaughter.
