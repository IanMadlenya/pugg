        Lawrence Keville Larson, a retired lumber company official, died May 25 at his home in Chapman, Ala. He was 90 years old and had been ill for several months.
        Mr. Larson, a 1922 graduate of Lawrence College, worked for several lumber companies before joining the Weyerhauser Company in 1933 at its Manhattan headquarters. He retired in 1964 as vice president of the pulp and paper division.
        He is survived by his wife, Estelle; a daughter, Theresa Scheetz of Pittsburgh; two sons, Laurens, of Mobile, Ala., and Stallworth, of Douglaston, Queens, and eight grandchildren.
