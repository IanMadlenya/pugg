        Anthony Gaubis, a securities analyst and investment adviser, died Sunday at John F. Kennedy Medical Center in Edison, N.J. He was 87 years old and lived in Sparta, N.J.
        Mr. Gaubis, a graduate of the University of Michigan, was in the stock market before the crash of 1929 with the American Telephone and Telegraph forecasting group. He was the author of an investment newsletter called Business and Investment Timing and for the last 23 years was president of Anthony Gaubis & Company, investment counselors.
        He is survived by his wife, Siegfriede.
