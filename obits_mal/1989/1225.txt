        Edwin Pugsley, a metallurgical engineer, died of a heart attack Thursday at Middlesex Hospital in Middletown, Conn. He was 66 years old and lived in Guilford, Conn.
        Mr. Pugsley worked for the Olin Mathieson Chemical Corporation from 1953 to 1971. He was a project engineer for Gulf United Nuclear Corporation from 1971 to 1974, when he retired.
        Mr. Pugsley was born in New Haven, Conn., and graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute. He was a former governor of the Society of Colonial Wars. He was a member of the Society of Cincinnati.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Nancy Redway; a daughter, Lucy, of Manhattan; three sons, Edwin 3d, of Barrington, R.I., David, of Old Lyme, Conn., and Christopher, of Washington; two sisters, Elizabeth Gaines and Dorothy Alley, both of Guilford, and four grandchildren.
