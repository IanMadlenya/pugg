        Irving Seligman, a retired men's clothing manufacturer, died yesterday at Mount Sinai Medical Center after suffering a stroke. He was 79 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Seligman was born in Manhattan. In the 1950's, he founded Sussex Clothes Ltd., a maker of men's suits. He retired 10 years ago.  Surviving are his wife, Edythe; three daughters, Carole, of San Francisco, Wendy Lewis of Boston and Terri, of Los Angeles, and five grandchildren.
