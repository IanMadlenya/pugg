        Leonard Sunshine, a clothing manufacturer, died of lung cancer yesterday at Memorial-Sloan Kettering Cancer Center. He was 69 years old and lived in Easton, Conn., and Waynesboro, Ga.
        Mr. Sunshine, a native New Yorker, was a partner of Anne Fogarty, the dress designer, from 1960 to 1970. He was president of Collector's Items, a ready-to-wear concern, when he retired in 1983.
        He is survived by a daughter, Iris Weir of High Point, N.C.; two sons, Bradford, of Los Angeles, and David, of Somers, N.Y., and a brother, Dr. Abraham, of Manhattan.
