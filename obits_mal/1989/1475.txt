        Benjamin F. Rogers, a medical publisher, died of cancer Friday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in Manhattan. He was 59 years old and lived in Huntington, L.I.
        Mr. Rogers graduated from Fordham University. For the last nine years he was president of Dominus Publishing Co. Inc., which published the medical trade journals ''Primary Care and Cancer'' and ''Oncology.''
        Surviving are his wife, Jule; four sons, Stephen, of Huntington, Brian, of Los Angeles, Douglas, of Randolph, N.J., and Gregg, of Brentwood, Calif.; a daughter, Claire, of Boston, and two grandchildren.
