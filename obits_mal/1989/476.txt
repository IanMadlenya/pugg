        Roy Bongartz, a writer, died of a heart attack Tuesday in Lubbock, Tex. He was 64 years old and lived in Foster, R.I.
        Mr. Bongartz was born in Providence, R.I., and graduated from Miami University. He wrote for The New Yorker, the Travel section of The New York Times and other publications. He was the author of a play, ''The Applicant.''
        He is survived by a son, Joe, of Foster; his mother, Emma, of Evanston, Ill., and a sister, Audrey Christian, of Kenilworth, Ill.
