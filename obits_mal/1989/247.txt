        Jerome Alper, a lawyer in Newark for more than 50 years, died Tuesday at the Van Dyk Manor in Montclair, N.J. He was 91 years old and lived in Montclair.
        Mr. Alper, a graduate of the New Jersey School of Law, passed the bar in 1922. He formed the firm Alper & Alper in 1930 with his wife, Anne Klughaupt Alper.
        He is survived by two daughters, Judith A. Greene of Montclair, and Adele, of Cedar Grove, N.J.; two sisters, Pearl A. Kaufman of Williamsville, N.Y., and Cecele A. Weitzman of Miami Beach, Fla.; a brother, Harold, of Springfield, N.J., and six grandchildren.
