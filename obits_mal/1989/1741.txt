        Bertram S. Primoff, a tax consultant and certified public accountant, died Tuesday at his daughter's home in Eastchester, N.Y., after a long illness. He was 68 years and lived in Ardsley, N.Y.
        Mr. Primoff, an alumnus of New York University, was with Primoff & Company, his family's accounting concern in Manhattan, for more than 40 years. He continued as a senior partner when it merged in 1987 to become Primoff, Tarshis, Weisen & Company.
        He is survived by his wife, Esther; a son, Walter, of Manhattan; a daughter, Marilyn Kohn of Eastchester; a brother, Ernest, of Forest Heights, Md., and one grandchild.
