        Prof. Henry H. Bolz, who worked at Fordham University for 30 years, died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Brookville, L.I. He was 75 years old.
        Professor Bolz joined the staff at Fordham in the Bronx in 1950 and became chairman of the accounting department. He retired in 1980.
        He was born in Brooklyn. Professor Bolz graduated from St. John's University in 1936 and earned a master's from New York University. He began teaching at St. John's. Professor Bolz was also a certified public accountant.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Dorothea Dalton; three children, John, of Lloyd Neck, L.I., Gail Reilly of Oyster Bay Cove, L.I., and Dorothea Zydel of Oyster Bay, L.I., and seven grandchildren.
