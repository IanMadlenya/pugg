        James Walter McGovern, an author, died of heart failure on Wednesday at New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center. He was 65 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He is survived by a son, James, of Manhattan; a daughter, Elizabeth Johnstone, of Darien, Conn., and a sister, Cecile Danches, of Miami Shores, Fla.
