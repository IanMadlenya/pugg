        Dr. Harvey R. Nova, a neurosurgeon at Englewood Hospital in New Jersey, died of a heart attack Sunday at his home in Tenafly, N.J. He was 53 years old.
        Dr. Nova was a member of the hospital's senior medical staff and a former chief of neurosurgery.
        He was born in Brooklyn and graduated from Brooklyn College and the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.
        He was an associate clinical professor at Columbia.
        Surviving are his wife, Lois Lehrman Nova; two sons, David, of Washington, and Kenneth, of Manhattan, and a daughter, Stephanie, of Philadelphia.
