        Robert Charles Wallis, an epidemiologist and professor at the Yale University School of Medicine, died last Wednesday at Yale-New Haven Hospital after a brief illness. He was 68 years old and lived in North Haven, Conn.
        He is survived by two sons, Thomas F. 3d. and Robert C. Jr., both of New Haven; a sister, Mae Ann Senior of Maple Shade, N.J.; a brother, Thomas, of Dallas, and a granddaughter.
