        John Tobin Cassidy Jr., a retired shipping executive, died of heart failure Thursday at St. Joseph's Hospital in Fort Worth. He was 76 years old and lived in Fort Worth.
        Mr. Cassidy, a graduate of Columbia University, was a vice president with the Belgian Line, headquartered in New York City, from 1947 to 1969. Until 1977 he was senior vice president of another New York-based concern, the Dart Container Line, a consortium of shipping companies.
        He is survived by his wife, Enriqueta; a daughter, Claudia Walter of Fort Worth; a brother, Charles, of Sarasota, Fla.; a sister, Sally, of Boston, and four grandchildren.
