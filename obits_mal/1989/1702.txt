        Irving Berlin was buried Tuesday afternoon at a private ceremony in Woodlawn Cemetery in the Bronx.
        The songwriter was buried next to the grave of his wife, Ellin Mackay Berlin, who died in July 1988.
        Mr. Berlin's three daughters, Mary Ellin Barrett, Linda Louise Emmet and Elizabeth Irving Peters. other members of his immediate family and a few longtime members of his staff attended the ceremony. Rabbi Daniel Wolk of Congregation Emanu-El of Westchester presided at the ceremony.
        A memorial service for Mr. Berlin, who died at his Manhattan home on Friday at the age of 101, is to be announced later.
