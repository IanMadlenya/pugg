        Louis John Puglies, once a senior partner of the now defunct Manhattan brokerage firm of Filor, Bullard & Smythe, died yesterday at his home in Saddle River, N.J. He was 79 years old.
        Mr. Puglies, who was born in Manhattan, was a governor of the American Stock Exchange and a member of the board of directors of several railroads. He retired in 1972.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy Newcomb; two daughters, Jean Wilkinson of Rochester and Diana Miceli of Upper Saddle River, N.J.; two sisters, Edith George of Queens and Theresa Nagel of Spring Hill, Fla., and five grandchildren.
