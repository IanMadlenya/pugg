        Fred J. Howard, executive vice president of Sterling National Bank and Trust Company, died of a heart attack Friday at Montefiore Hospital in the Bronx. He was 69 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Howard worked for the bank and its parent, Sterling Bank Corporation, for more than 25 years. Born in Vienna, he immigrated to the United States in 1938. He was a former president of the Park East Synagogue in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, Ilse; a daughter, Riva Brandt of New York City, and one granddaughter.
