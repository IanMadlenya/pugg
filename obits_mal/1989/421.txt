        Daniel Steinberg, a lawyer and businessman, died of a cerebral hemorrhage Sunday at Northwest Regional Hospital in Margate, Fla. He was 79 years old and lived in Woodmere, L.I.
        Mr. Steinberg, a graduate of Brooklyn Law School, was president of Liberty Freight Forwarding and Warehousing in Manhattan when he retired in 1973.
        He was recently an arbitrator for the courts and the securities industry.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; a daughter, Barbara Cohen of Rockville Centre, L.I.; a sister, Bea Barnett of Howard Beach, Queens, and two grandchildren.
