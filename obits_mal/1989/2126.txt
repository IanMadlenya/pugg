        Alfred Finkel, a retired steel-company executive and philanthropist, died of a heart attack on Thursday at Tisch Hospital-University Hospital of New York University. He was 68 years old and lived in Woodmere, L.I.
        Mr. Finkel was a general partner in the Williamsburg Steel Products Company in Brooklyn until he retired in 1979.
        He was chairman of the trustees of the Brookdale Hospital Medical Center. He also worked with the New York City Hospital Trustees Association and the United Hospital Fund.
        He graduated from City College in 1941.
        Surviving are his wife, Maidy; three sons, Theodore, of Manhattan, Andrew, of Newton Centre, Mass., and William, of Manhattan; his mother, Anna, of Lido Beach, L.I.; two sisters, Miriam Berkowitz of Lido Beach and Ethelyn Wallach of Malverne, L.I., and two grandchildren.
