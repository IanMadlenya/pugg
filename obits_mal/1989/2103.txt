        Leslie Combs Bruce, a retired financier, died Saturday at the Doylestown (Pa.) Nursing Facility. He was 91 years old and had lived in Greenwich, Conn., before moving to Doylestown in 1984.
        Mr. Bruce, who studied at Princeton University, managed his own accounts and for a time headed the brokerage firm of Bruce, Lohr & Stuart in Manhattan. He had been active in volunteer activities in Greenwich.
        He is survived by his wife, Ida; two sons, Leslie 3d, of San Diego, and Douglas, of Greenwich; a daughter, Julia Coyle of Greenwich; 11 grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
