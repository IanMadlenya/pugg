        Roger F. Nelson, retired vice president of the Morgan Guaranty Trust Company, died of lung cancer Saturday at his home in Stuart, Fla.. He was 59 years old and lived in Darien, Conn., until he retired.
        Mr. Nelson joined the company after graduating from the Harvard Business School. He became a vice president in the late 1960's and retired in 1986.
        He was a graduate of the University of Connecticut and was an Army Ranger in the Korean War.
        He is survived by wife, the former Sonia Smith; two sons, Thomas B., of Miami, and Bradford F., of Manhattan; a daughter, Stephanie Nelson of Stuart; his mother, Ruth F. Nelson of Juno Beach, Fla., and a brother, Richard G., of Suffield, Conn.
