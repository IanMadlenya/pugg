        Lester Granet, a founder in 1934 of the accounting firm of Granet & Granet, of Livingston, N.J., died yesterday at Beth Israel Hospital in Newark. He was 76 years old and lived in Verona, N.J.
        Mr. Granet, a graduate of New York University, was a certified public accountant and had been a director of the Ronson Corporation since 1969.
        He is survived by his wife, Lois; a son, Dr. Roger, of Madison, N.J.; two daughters, Vicky Semel of South Orange, N.J., and Gail Velez of Maplewood, N.J.; a brother, Murray, of South Orange, N.J., and six grandchildren.
