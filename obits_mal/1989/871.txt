        Dale Linx Orrin, a dancer, teacher and choreographer, died on May 5 at St. Joseph's Mercy Hospital in Ann Arbor, Mich. He was 36 years old.
        Mr. Orrin's brother, Doug, said the cause of death was complications from pneumonia.
        Mr. Orrin danced with the May O'Donnell Dance Company from 1975 to 1986. He also performed with the Joyce Trisler Danscompany, Rika Burnham, Baker's Half Dozen, Uris-Bahr Dancers and the Ananda Renaissance Dance Company. Mr. Orrin was also a popular modern dance teacher who taught in New York City at the High School of Performing Arts and Juilliard School, among other academies.
        In addition to his brother, Mr. Orrin is survived by his parents and three sisters, Donna, Lin Orrin-Brown and Sue Jackson, all of Michigan. There will be a memorial service on June 22 at 9 P.M. at Dance Space, 622 Broadway, between Houston and Bleecker Streets.
