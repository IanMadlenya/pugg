        Ralph B. Henderson, a retired execu-tive vice president of the J. C. Penney Company, died of cardiac arrest Wednesday at New York Hospital in Manhattan. He was 62 years old and had homes in Manhattan and Bedminster, N.J.
        A 1946 graduate of Yale University, Mr. Henderson worked at Montgomery Ward and Company before joining J. C. Penney in 1963 as customer service manager in its Milwaukee catalog distribution center. He became a vice president of the company in 1974, senior vice president in 1981 and executive vice president in 1982. He retired in 1988. Mr. Henderson was also a trustee and former president of the YMCA of Greater New York.
        He is survived by his wife, Grace; three daughters, Sue Cowan of Plano, Tex., Meryl Learnihan of Easton, Pa., and Beth Goettel of Bangor, Me.; a son, Ralph, of Waukesha, Wisc., and seven grandchildren.
