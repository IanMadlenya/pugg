        John Ford Clymer, a Western artist who painted scores of covers for The Saturday Evening Post, died Thursday after a long illness. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Clymer painted more than 80 covers for the Post between 1945 and 1963.
        In 1964, after 40 years as an illustrator, he discontinued commercial work and devoted all his time to painting North American game animals and scenes from Western history.
