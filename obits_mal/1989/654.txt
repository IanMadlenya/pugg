        Irving Wise, who served in executive posts with the New York City Housing Authority for 34 years until his retirement in 1973, died of complications from leukemia on Sunday at North Shore Community Hospital in Great Neck, L.I. He was 77 years old and lived in Great Neck.
        Mr. Wise was successively general counsel, director of management and general manager of the authority.
        He is survived by his wife, Etta; two daughters, Jane Shear of Binghamton, N.Y., and Alice Marshall of Lubbock, Tex., and six grandchildren.
