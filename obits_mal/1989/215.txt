        Stanley Benn, a lawyer, died of a heart attack Saturday at Community Hospital in Boca Raton, Fla., while on vacation. He was 49 years old and lived in West Deal, N.J.
        Mr. Benn was a partner in the New Jersey firm of Wilentz, Goldman & Spitzer, which has offices in Woodbridge and Oakhurst. He was also president of Triad Property Management, a Woodbridge real-estate company.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Judy Tenzer; two sons, Alex and Bradford, both of West Deal; his parents, Meyer and Rose Benn of Sunrise, Fla., formerly of North Plainfield, N.J.; and a brother, Bernard, of Hanover, N.H.
