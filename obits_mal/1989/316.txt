        Edward L. Mabry, retired chief executive of the Vick Chemical Company, died Wednesday at his house in Litchfield, Conn. He was 91 years old.
        Mr. Mabry joined Vick, now Richardson-Vicks Inc., in 1916, after graduating from high school in Greensboro, N.C. He worked in accounting, sales and advertising. Mr. Mabry was named president in 1948 and chairman in 1957. He retired in 1964 and later wrote 18 privately published books of poetry and sayings.
        Surviving are two sons, D. Shelton, of Lyme, Conn., and Edward Jr., of Greensboro; two granddaughters, and a great-granddaughter.
