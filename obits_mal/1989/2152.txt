        David A. Neuman, an advertising executive, died of lung cancer Saturday at St. Vincent's Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Neuman, who was born in New York City, graduated from Syracuse University. He worked at the Lever Brothers Company and became a vice president of Chesebrough-Pond's. He was a regional advertising manager for the McDonald's Corporation from 1983 to 1985, when he left to become a consultant.
        He is survived by his wife, Thea Kliros; a daughter, Katherine, of Manhattan, and a brother, Bert, of Passaic, N.J.
