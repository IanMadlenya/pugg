        Grenville Temple Emmet Jr., a retired partner in the Manhattan law firm of Emmet, Marvin & Martin, died of complications of emphysema on Sunday at the Georgetown Retirement Home in Washington. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Emmet was born in Manhattan and graduated from Harvard College and Columbia University Law School. In 1935 he joined the law firm, which was founded by his father with Franklin D. Roosevelt. He retired in 1971.
        Surviving are his wife, Elizabeth; a son, Grenville 3d, of Leesburg, Va.; a daughter, Anne McLucas of Washington, nine grandchildren and five step-grandchildren.
