        The Rev. Eric Dean, a former leader of several national religious groups, died Saturday after a long illness. He was 64 years old and lived in Crawfordsville.
        Mr. Dean, an ordained United Presbyterian minister, served as chairman of the American Theological Society and the General Assembly of the United Presbyterian Church, U.S.A.
        In more than three decades on the faculty at Wabash College, Mr. Dean was chairman of the philosophy and religion department, chairman of the humanities division, director of the Wabash Executive Program and the Evans Professor of Religion.
        In 1982 a collection of his Wabash College sermons was published under the title ''The Good News About Sin.''
        Mr. Dean, a native of England, served in the Royal Air Force in World War II.
