        Edward W. Lang, a chemist who developed the formula for Head & Shoulders shampoo for the Procter & Gamble Company, died Nov. 27 while visiting in Cincinnati. He was 65 years old and lived in retirement in Naples, Fla.
        Mr. Lang received a patent and $1 for formulating the anti-dandruff shampoo, which has been a Procter & Gamble mainstay since 1961. The company retains the rights to patents developed by its employees.
