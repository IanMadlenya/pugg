        Robert William Haymes, a composer and broadcast producer, died of Lou Gehrig's disease Saturday at his home in Hilton Head Island, S.C. He was 65 years old and also had a home in Greenville, S.C.
        Mr. Haymes, a younger brother of the singer Dick Haymes, was a native of White Plains, N.Y. He was the television director of Richard M. Nixon's Presidential campaign in 1968.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Dee Workman; a son, Robert W. Jr., of Dallas; four daughters, Candi Grassi, of Greenwich, Conn., Pennie Cox, of Wilmington, Del., Taylor Haymes and Eaddy Kirbow of Greenville, and seven grandchildren.
