        The Rev. Edward J. Hogan, who was associated with St. Ignatius Loyola Roman Catholic Church for 48 years, died of cardiac arrest on Friday at the Murray-Weigel Health Care Center of Fordham University in the Bronx. He was 83 years old.
        Father Hogan, a graduate of Fordham, taught at St. Peter's Preparatory School in Jersey City and at Loyola High School in Baltimore before being ordained a priest in 1937. He joined St. Ignatius Loyola in 1939 and remained on the staff until his retirement in 1987.
        There are no immediate survivors.
