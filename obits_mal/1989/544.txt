        Dan Silverman, president of Metro Seliger Industries, a direct-mail advertising concern, died Feb. 22 at North Shore University Hospital in Manhasset, L.I. He was 66 years old and lived in Great Neck, L.I.
        Mr. Silverman worked for Metro Seliger for more than four decades and was named president in 1980. Last year, the company was acquired by the KLP Group, based in London.
        Mr. Silverman was a native of the Bronx and attended the University of Delaware and New York University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Anne Peskin; a son, Martin, of Kew Gardens, Queens; two daughters, Marcia, of Boca Raton, Fla., and Fran Hoffman of Fort Lee, N.J.; a brother, Lester, of Boynton Beach, Fla., and a granddaughter.
