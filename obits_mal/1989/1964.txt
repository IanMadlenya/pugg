        Barry Andrew Kearsley, stage manager of the Broadway play ''M. Butterfly,'' died of a heart attack Saturday after collapsing backstage during a performance. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. Kearsley had had open-heart surgery within the last few years, said John Springer, a public relations spokesman for the play.
        Mr. Kearsley had been the stage manager on Broadway for ''Equus,'' ''Seesaw,'' ''Hair,'' ''42d Street'' and other productions.
        He is survived by his wife, Janet Friedman, his mother, and two brothers.
