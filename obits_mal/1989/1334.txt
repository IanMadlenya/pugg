        James A. Linen 4th, executive vice president of Media General Inc. and head of the Richmond-based company's broadcast operations, died Sunday at Westminster Hospital in London. He was 51 years old and died from injuries suffered in a jogging accident.
        Mr. Linen, who was in London for the Wimbledon tennis championships, was hit by a van in a pedestrian crosswalk as he was jogging Wednesday.
