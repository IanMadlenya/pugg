        David J. Hertz, president of D. J. Hertz & Associates Limited, a personnel placement agency in Manhattan, died of a heart attack Saturday at St. Barnabas Hospital in Livingston, N.J. He was 48 years old and lived in Short Hills, N.J., and Boynton Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Hertz was a founder and partner of Rasch & Hertz, which became D. J. Hertz & Associates in 1980.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Margo Ostrick; a son, Jeffrey, and a daughter, Lisa, both of Short Hills; his parents, Nathan and Jean, of West Palm Beach, Fla.; a brother, Gerald, of Fort Lee, N.J., and a sister, Barbara Lee Goldman of Dayton, Ohio.
