        David Monroe Collins, administrator and assistant secretary of the Frick Collection, died in his sleep Thursday at his home in Manhattan. He was 51 years old.
        Mr. Collins apparently died of a stroke, said a spokesman at the Frick.
        He was born in Erie, Pa., and graduated from Yale University and the University of Virginia Law School. He became a trust officer of the Morgan Guaranty Trust Company of New York. He joined the Frick in 1970.
        Mr. Collins was a former member of the council of the Phillips Collection in Washington.
        He is survived by a brother, William Jr. of Erie.
