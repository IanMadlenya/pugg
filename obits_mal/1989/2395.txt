        Dominick P. Felitti, a retired government official who worked at the city, state and Federal levels, died Wednesday at the St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan. He was 83 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        A graduate of Fordham University Law School, Mr. Felitti was deputy commissioner of the Department of Markets in the administration of Mayor Fiorello H. La Guardia. He also worked for New York State in its housing office in Staten Island and later with the Federal government, overseeing housing projects in Boston and Puerto Rico. He retired from the Department of Housing and Urban Development in 1978.
        He is survived by a sister, Adele DeSanctis of Manhattan.
