        Victor Jacobson, the former president of a New Jersey ceiling contracting company, died of a heart attack Tuesday at the Morton Plant Hospital in Clearwater, Fla.. He was 90 years old and lived in Clearwater.
        Mr. Jacobson was president of Jacobson & Company of Elizabeth, N.J., from the late 1920's to 1963, when he retired. He was born in Manhattan and graduated from the Pratt Institute. For many years, he lived in New Rochelle, N.Y.
        Surviving are his wife, Lucille; two sons, Walter, of Miami and John, of Westfield, N.J.; a daughter, Margaret Brown of Sacramento, Calif.; seven grandchildren, and seven great-grandchildren.
