        John Patrick Walsh, an insurance lawyer in Manhattan for almost 57 years, died Saturday at Morristown (N.J.) Memorial Hospital after a long illness. He was 84 years old and lived in Convent Station, N.J.
        Mr. Walsh graduated from the College of the Holy Cross, in Worcester, Mass., and from Fordham University Law School in 1930. He was associated with the firm of Donovan Maloof Walsh & Repetto, where he specialized in reinsurance. He retired from the firm in 1984.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary; four sons, John, James, Mark and Paul; a daughter, Loretta, all of Convent Station; and a sister, Virginia Von Bokkellen, of Remsenburg, L.I.
