        John P. Coakley, a retired president of the United Tanker Corporation, a shipping company, died Monday at Marcus Lawrence Hospital in Cottonwood, Ariz. He was 70 years old and lived in Sedona, Ariz.
        Mr. Coakley was born in Brooklyn, graduated from Columbia University and lived for many years in Rye, N.Y. He was president of United Tanker in Manhattan from 1963 to 1979, when he retired.
        He is survived by his wife, Shirley.
