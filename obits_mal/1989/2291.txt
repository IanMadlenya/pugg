        Donald P. Sheahan, executive vice president of the American subsidiary of a major Japanese securities-trading house, died of cancer yesterday at Morristown Memorial Hospital. He was 58 years old and lived in Bernardsville, N.J.
        Mr. Sheahan was executive vice president of Nikko Securities International Inc., responsible for fixed-income trading and sales. He had been a director of the New York Futures Exchange.
        Mr. Sheahan is survived by his wife, the former Lois Eldridge; a daughter, Jacqueline Ann Sheahan of New York City; and three sisters, Margaret Moore of Mahopac, N.Y., Joan Savage of Peekskill, N.Y., and Jane Sheahan McLaughlin of New York City.
