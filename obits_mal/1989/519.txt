        Stanley E. Dimond, a professor emeritus of education at the University of Michigan, died Saturday after an extended illness. He was 83 years old.
        From 1932 to 1950, Mr. Dimond worked in the Detroit public schools as a high school teacher, department chairman, supervisor and director of social studies.
        From 1950 until his retirement in 1970 he was professor of education at the University of Michigan. He was a past president of the National Council for Social Studies.
