        Frank Cardi, a former president of the Metropolitan Professional Golfers' Association, died on Thursday at his home in West Palm Beach, Fla. He was 56 years old.
        Mr. Cardi was the Ohio junior champion in 1950, state amateur champion in 1951 and Big Ten Conference champion at Ohio State University. He was head pro at the Rockaway Hunting Club in Cedarhurst, L.I., and the Apawamis Club in Rye, N.Y. Mr. Cardi was president of the Professional Golfers' Association of America in 1979-80.
        He is survived by his mother, Agnes Cardi, and two brothers.
