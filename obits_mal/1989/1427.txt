        Dr. Manfred Weichsel, a pediatrician and immunologist for more than 50 years, died of congestive heart failure Wednesday at Long Island Jewish Hospital in Queens. He was 85 years old.
        Dr. Weichsel had been chief of pediatrics at Long Island Jewish Hospital, at Elmhurst General Hospital in Queens, and at the Willard Parker Hospital in Manhattan, which closed. He retired in 1983.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Gertrude Gans; two sons, John, of Southington, Conn., and Peter, of Encinitas, Calif.; two brothers, Lothar, of Queens, and Ernest, of Tel Aviv, and four grandchildren.
