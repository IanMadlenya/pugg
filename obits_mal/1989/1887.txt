        Fred E. Katzner, a former president of the Forest Hills Jewish Center in Queens, died of a heart attack on Wednesday at his home in Forest Hills. He was 88 years old.  Mr. Katzner was president of the Comfy Manufacturing Company, which made slipcovers, until he retired in 1965. He was also a former chairman of the Jewish center's executive board.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Edith Jeffrey; two sons, Stephen, of Brooklyn, and Kenneth, of Washington; two sisters, Rose Schermer and Violet Andrews, both of Manhattan; five grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
