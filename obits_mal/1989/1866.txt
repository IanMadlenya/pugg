        Michael S. Cecere, a lawyer who specialized in employment litigation, died of cancer of the colon Thursday at Hackensack Medical Center in Hackensack, N.J. He was 45 years old and lived in Tenafly.
        Mr. Cecere joined Jackson, Lewis, Schnitzler & Krupman, a law firm in Manhattan that specializes in labor and employment, in 1978. He represented management in several employment cases.
        He was born in the Bronx and was a graduate of City College of the City University of New York and St. John's University Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Kristine Jones; a daughter, Jessica Anne; his parents, Mario and Helen Cecere of Monsey, N.Y., and two sisters, Theresa Hanlon of Suffern, N.Y., and Susan Costello of Stony Point, N.Y.
