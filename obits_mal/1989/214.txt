        Robie E. Palmer, a retired captain in the Navy, died of pneumonia Sunday at the Vermont Veterans' Home in Bennington, Vt. He was 84 years old and lived in Westminster, Vt.
        He was a 1927 graduate of the United States Naval Academy. In World War II he commanded the submarine Pollack. He retired in 1957.
        He is survived by his wife, Katherine Hooker Palmer; two daughters, Georgiana Cheney of Wilton, Conn., and Alexandra Palmer of Manhattan, a son, Robie M. H. Palmer, the United States Ambassador to Hungary, and two grandsons.
