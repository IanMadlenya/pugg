        Lawrence Davis Barney, former president of Hoffmann-La Roche Inc., died of cancer Sunday at Mountainside Hospital in Glen Ridge, N.J. He was 83 years old and lived in Montclair, N.J.
        Mr. Barney was born in Milwaukee and graduated from the University of Wisconsin. He became president of Hoffmann-La Roche, the pharmaceutical company, in 1944 and retired in 1964.
        Surviving are his wife, Dorrit; a son, Lawrence Jr., of Baileys Harbor, Wis., a daughter, Elizabeth Gill of Willow Grove, Pa., three grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
