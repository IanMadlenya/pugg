
        BILOXI, Miss., May 10 (AP) - Billy Joe Clegg, a minister who ran for President seven times, died of a heart attack on Monday. He was 69.
        Mr. Clegg ran for President every four years since 1972, advocating what he termed conservative spiritual convictions, and he recently announced that he would run in 2000.
        Surviving Mr. Clegg are his wife, Betty; a son, Joe Carrel of Arlington, Tex.; a daughter, Carment Ruth Ark of Austin, Tex,; a brother; a sister, and four grandchildren.
