
        J. Alfred Guest, who developed many programs in his 25 years as alumni secretary of Amherst College and was active in alumni affairs nationally, died on Oct. 6 at Baystate Medical Center in Springfield, Mass. He was 86 and lived in Amherst and Martha's Vineyard.
        A native of East Orange, N.J., and a 1933 Amherst graduate, Mr. Guest also earned a degree from Yale Law School in 1936.
        He was alumni secretary from 1946 until 1971 and was also secretary of the college's board. He was also active in supporting collaboration among New England's private colleges and in rallying their supporters in business.
        Mr. Guest is survived by his wife, Elizabeth Montignani Guest; a son, James A., of Baltimore; a daughter, Anne Hazelton of Windsor, Conn.; eight grandchildren and three great-grandchildren.
