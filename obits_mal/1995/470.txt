
        Daniel Kearns, a theatrical manager who worked on and off Broadway, died on Monday at the Hospice at Mission Hill, outside Boston. He was 39.
        The cause was complications from AIDS, said his press representatives, Boneau/Bryan-Brown.
        Mr. Kearns was company manager for "Penn and Teller," "The Night Hank Williams Died" and "Tango Argentino," among other shows.
        He is survived by three brothers, Mark, Stephen and Brian, and three sisters, Ellen and Louise Kearns and Rosemary Cheever, all of Boston.
