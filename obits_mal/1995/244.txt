
        Irwin Schnurmacher, a philanthropist and real estate executive, died on Saturday at Beth Israel Medical Center. He was 83 and lived in Woodmere, L.I.
        Through the Irwin and Sylvia Schnurmacher Foundation, he helped many organizations, including the Anti-Defamation League; the United Jewish Appeal Federation; St. John's Episcopal Hospital, South Shore Division; Beth Israel Medical Center, and the Long Island Jewish Medical Center, of which he was a board member for more than 40 years.
        He was chief executive officer of the real estate concerns Schnurmacher Brothers and the Schnurmacher Corporation.
        His wife of 58 years, Sylvia, died last year. He is survived by two sons, Stephen, of Manhattan, and Leonard, of Hollywood, Fla.; a daughter, Laurie Tomchin, also of Hollywood, 15 grandchildren and 6 great-grandchildren.
