
        Robert Penniman Hulburd, a former chairman of the German department and administrator at Phillips Academy in Andover, Mass., died on Saturday in the L. W. Blake Hospital in Bradenton, Fla. He was 74 and lived in Holmes Beach, Fla.
        Mr. Hulburd, whose father was chairman of the mathematics department at Phillips Exeter Academy in Exeter, N.H., graduated from Phillips Exeter and, in 1943, from Princeton University. He later received a master's degree from Middlebury College in Vermont.
        He headed the German department at Phillips Andover from 1961 to 1965, and then held a number of administrative posts until his retirement in 1986.
        He is survived by three children, Holly Hulburd of San Francisco, Lindsey H. Walker of Carmel, Calif., and Robert P. Jr., of Bradenton, and three grandchildren.
