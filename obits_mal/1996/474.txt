
        Barry L. Conrad is chairman and chief executive of the Barrington Group, a company he formed two years ago. He is 55 and lives in Coral Gables, Fla.
        An associate of Mr. Conrad's said that the executive was developing hotels in Central and Eastern Europe.
        In addition to Eastern Europe, Barrington, which is based in Miami, is developing hotels in Latin America. Mr. Conrad founded the company after spending four years as the head of worldwide operations and sales for the Burger King Corporation, where he was part of the team credited with turning around the once-troubled fast-food chain.
