
        Walter J. Murphy, a senior executive at the AT&T Corporation, is a vice president of global sales for AT&T's Submarine Systems division and has spent much of his career building up the company's international business. He is 52 and lives in Fanwood, N.J.
        Mr. Murphy joined AT&T in 1970 and has held a wide variety of jobs over his  career.  Executives with the company, based in Morristown, N.J., said Mr. Murphy had been asked to join Mr. Brown's mission because he had extensive experience with global trade and communications infrastructure issues.
