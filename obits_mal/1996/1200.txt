
        John R. Sargent, a management consultant who advised many large companies on strategy and marketing, died on Monday at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 85 and lived in Bronxville.
        The cause was pneumonia, said a son, Michael H., of Bronxville.
        In addition to his son Michael, Mr. Sargent is survived by his wife, Mary; two daughters, Betsy Grogan of South Salem, N.Y., and Susan Goldman of Chestnut Hill, Pa.; another son, John S., of Lake Bluff, Ill.; a brother, S. Stansfield Sargent of Solana Beach, Calif., and nine grandchildren.
