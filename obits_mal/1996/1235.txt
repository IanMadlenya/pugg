
        Raymond O. Prada, an acting co-director and chief psychologist of Adult Psychology Services at Metropolitan Hospital Center in Manhattan, died on Oct. 10 at Beth Israel Hospital. He was 60 and lived on the Lower East Side.
        The cause was a stroke, said Dr. Leonard Davidman, a colleague at Metropolitan.
        Mr. Prada received a master's degree in psychology at Western Reserve University in 1959. He was a senior psychologist at Coler Memorial Hospital and taught at Cleveland State University before joining the staff at Metropolitan Hospital Center in 1980.
