
        Woodruff Jones English, a retired senior partner in the large Newark-based law firm of McCarter & English and a volunteer with medical, social service and  rights organizations, died on March 3 at the Meadow Lakes retirement community in Hightstown, N.J. He was 86.
        Born into a family of prominent lawyers in Elizabeth, Mr. English graduated from Princeton University and Harvard Law School. The firm admitted him as a partner in 1947. He was senior partner from 1976 until his retirement in 1981.
        He was active in civic affairs as a past trustee and president of Overlook Hospital in Summit, N.J., and chairman of the merged United Hospitals of Newark.
        Mr. English is survived by his wife of 53 years, Carolyn Barton English; two  sons, Woodruff J. 2d of Portland, Ore., and Barton C. of Fredericksburg, Texas;  three daughters, Virginia E. Sprenkle of Snohomish, Wash., Elizabeth C. English  of Philadelphia and Sydney, Australia, and Carolyn W. English of Liberty Corner; a brother, Nicholas C. of Hights town, and nine grandchildren.
