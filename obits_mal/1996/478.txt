
        Leonard J. Pieroni is a chemical engineer who rose to become chairman and chief executive officer of the Parsons Corporation. He is 57 and a resident of La Canada, Calif.
        Mr. Pieroni has worked for two decades for Parsons, an international engineering and heavy construction company based in Pasadena, Calif. He had participated in Mr. Brown's economic development trip to promote his company's experience rebuilding countries devastated by war and natural disasters.
        As Parson's top officer for the last six years, he presided over the company's expansion into transportation and environmental services. Mr. Pieroni  started with the company in 1972 as a business development representative.
