
        Peter G. Mirto, a Brooklyn Democrat who was a member of the New York State Assembly from 1969 to 1978, died on Monday in Glen Cove, N.Y. He was 85 and lived in a retirement home in Glen Cove.
        A native New Yorker, he graduated from St. John's Law School and was an assistant district attorney for Kings County from 1950 to 1966.
        Surviving are his wife, Pauline; three daughters, Camille Hanson of Oyster Bay, N.Y., Jo Ann Karlebach of Atlanta, Valerie Rothschild of Garden City, N.Y.; a son, Dr. Joseph Mirto of Manhattan; and 11 grandchildren.
