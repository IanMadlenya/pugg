
        Henry Benach, a retired chairman of the Starrett Corporation, the New York-based development and construction concern, died last Sunday at his home in Palm Beach Gardens, Fla. He was 83.
        Mr. Benach, formerly of Scarsdale, N.Y., was an executive in the apparel industry when he joined the Starrett organization in 1970 as president and a director of what was then Starrett Brothers & Eken. He later served as chairman and chief executive officer and retired several years ago.
        He is survived by his wife, Shirlee; a son, Douglas; a daughter, Michele; a sister, Annette; and five grandchildren.
