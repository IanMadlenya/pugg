        George B. Dippy, a retired executive who helped build Young & Rubicam, the New York-based advertising agency, died of a heart attack Saturday at White Plains Hospital. He was 88 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Dippy worked at Young & Rubicam from 1943 to 1965. He was a senior vice president and a director of the company.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Helen Wallis; a daughter, Joan Duffield of Darien, Conn.; a son, George, of Greenwich, Conn.; six grandchildren, and six great-grandchildren.
