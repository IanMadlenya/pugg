        Abe Waters, a textile executive, died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Lauderhill, Fla. He was 77 years old.
        Mr. Waters, who was a major merchandiser of ultrasuede in the early 1970's, was a vice president of the Skinner Fabrics division of Springs Mills, the American distributor and owner of the registered trademark for the fabric, until his retirement in 1979.
        He is survived by his wife, Evelyn; two sons, Michael, of Novato, Calif., and Gerald, of Manhattan; a sister, Rose Samansky of Queens, and four grandchildren.
