        Former Prime Minister Takieddin Solh, a longtime conservative politician, died Sunday in a private Paris clinic, his family said. He was 80 years old.
        A Foreign Ministry statement said Mr. Solh died after a heart attack. It said his body was to be flown to Beirut.
        Mr. Solh was elected to Parliament in 1957 and 1964 from the Bekaa region of eastern Lebanon. He was appointed Interior Minister in 1964 and served as Prime Minister from 1973 to 1974.
        He is survived by his wife, Fadwa Barazi Solh.
