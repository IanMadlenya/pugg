        Morton Fearey, a retired partner in the law firm of Davis, Polk, Wardwell, died of heart failure Thursday at Columbia-Presbyterian Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Manhattan and Edgartown, Mass.
        Mr. Fearey, a native of New York, was a graduate of Yale and the Yale Law School. He served as a naval aviator during World War II.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Senior; two sons, Morton Jr. and John; a daughter, Mary Lollis, all of Manhattan; eight grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
