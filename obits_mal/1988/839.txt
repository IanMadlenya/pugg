        Dr. Morris Schreiber, a theoretical mathematician and professor at the Rockefeller University, died of congestive heart failure on April 30 at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan. He was 61 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Schreiber was working on the generalization of number theory to polynomials at the time of his death.
        He is survived by his mother, Dora Miller Schreiber of Washington, and two sisters, Hilda Schreiber of Washington and Anibel S. Holland, of Bethesda, Md.
