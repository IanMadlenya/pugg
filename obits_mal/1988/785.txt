        Dr. Maurice H. Cowen, an orthopedic surgeon who specialized in sports medicine, died of multiple myeloma Monday at the Dana Farber Cancer Institute in Boston. He was 49 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Dr. Cowen served as orthopedist for the New York Yankees in 1977 and 1978, and he practiced for many years at the Institute of Sports Medicine at Lenox Hill Hospital in Manhattan.
        He was a graduate of Rutgers University and the New York Medical College.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Carol Fried; a daughter, Jodi; a son, Christopher; his mother, Bella, of East Orange, N.J., and his brother, Stephen, of Tenafly, N.J.
