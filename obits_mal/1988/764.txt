        Peter V. Busatti, director of the Ladies Professional Golf Association's Chrysler-Plymouth Classic tournament, died of cancer yesterday at the Northern Westchester Hospital in Mount Kisco, N.Y. He was 57 years old and lived in Amawalk, N.Y.
        Mr. Busatti, a former public-relations executive, started the L.P.G.A. event in 1976 as a benefit for the Trenton Archdiocese of the Roman Catholic Church.
        Mr. Busatti is survived by his wife, Phyllis; three children, Peter Jr., Jane Kene and Virginia Hill, and three grandchildren. Viewing will be at the Clark Funeral Home in Yorktown Heights, N.Y., today and tomorrow, 2-4 P.M. and 7-9 P.M. A funeral Mass will be held at 10 A.M. Wednesday at St. Patrick's Church in Yorktown Heights.
