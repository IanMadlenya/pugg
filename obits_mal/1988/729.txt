        John H. Nally, curator of the garden at Wave Hill in the Riverdale section of the Bronx, died of pneumonia Thursday at New York University Medical Center, according to a spokesman for Wave Hill. He was 46 years old and lived in Riverdale.
        Born in Excelsior Springs, Mo., he earned bachelor's and master's degrees from the University of Kansas. Before joining Wave Hill - a public garden on a 28-acre estate overlooking the Hudson River - Mr. Nally worked at the Horticultural Society of New York.
        There are no survivors.
