        Dr. Reynold E. Church, a New York surgeon, died of a heart attack last Tuesday at his home in Delray Beach, Fla. He was 85 years old.
        Dr. Church was affiliated with New York University Hospital as assistant professor of surgery from 1934 until he retired in 1977. He was a graduate of Syracuse University and its medical school.
        Dr. Church was a major with the Third Auxiliary Surgical Group during World War II in England, where he set up field hospitals.
        His wife, the former Mary Elting, died last year.
        He is survived by a daughter, Mary Elting Church of London.
