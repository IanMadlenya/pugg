        Paul F. Switz, an economic development consultant to Puerto Rico, died after suffering a stroke Sunday at Duke University Medical Center in Durham, N.C. He was 82 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        He is survived by a son, Wrightson, of Lawrence, Kan.; a daughter, Maria Hartford Halman, of Durham, and two grandchildren.
