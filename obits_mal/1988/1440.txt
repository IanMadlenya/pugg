        Nathan Henry Gates, a lawyer, died after a stroke Sunday at Southampton (L.I.) Hospital. He was 78 years old and lived in Manhattan and Bridgehampton, L.I.
        Mr. Gates was a founding partner in the Gates & Laber law firm. He had been with Goldwater & Flynn and was later counsel to Cole & Dietz.
        He graduated from the Harvard University Law School in 1933.
        Surviving are his wife, Beatrice; three daughters, Alice Magdol-Conrad and Caroline Anderson, both of Manhattan, and Martha Hays, of San Francisco, and four grandchildren.
