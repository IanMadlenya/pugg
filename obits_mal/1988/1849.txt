        John Joseph Lindsay, the Senate reporter for Newsweek magazine, died of cancer Wednesday at his home here. He was 67 years old.
        Mr. Lindsay worked for The Washington Post for eight years before joining Newsweek's Washington bureau in 1962. He covered the campaigns of major candidates in every election since 1952.
        Survivors include his wife, Lorraine Lindsay of Washington, and two children.
