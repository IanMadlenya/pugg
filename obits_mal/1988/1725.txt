        Lucian C. Warren, a Washington journalist, died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Issue, Md. He was 75 years old.
        Mr. Warren, a former president of the Gridiron Club and the National Press Club, was a Washington correspondent for The Buffalo Courier-Express and The Buffalo Evening News from 1945 until 1978.
        Surviving are his wife, Katherine; three daughters, Sylvia of Manhattan, Katherine Sites of Athens, Me., and Nancy Warren-Stringos of Skowhegan, Me.; a son, Lucian of Agoura, Calif., and 12 grandchildren.
