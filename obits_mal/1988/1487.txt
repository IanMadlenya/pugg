        Leonard S. Brown, an educator and accountant, died of lung cancer Aug. 31 at his home in Wayne, N.J. He was 58 years old.
        Mr. Brown, a professor of accounting at Pace University, served as president of the New Jersey Society of Certified Public Accountants in 1975.
        He is survived by his wife, Elaine Brown; a daughter, Randi Bolno of Wayne; a son, Richard, of Fort Lee, N.J.; his father, Dr. Arthur, of Bayonne; a brother, Melvin, of Fort Lee, and two grandchildren.
