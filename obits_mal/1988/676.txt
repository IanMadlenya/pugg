        Thomas Boone Pickens, an oil-lease buyer and the father of the investor T. Boone Pickens Jr., died Monday. He was 90 years old.
        After practicing law in Holdenville, Okla., Mr. Pickens moved to Amarillo in 1943, buying oil leases nationwide for the Phillips Petroleum Company. After retiring in 1959, he formed his own lease-buying business.
