        Lloyd H. Burke, who served nearly 30 years as a Federal judge, died Tuesday. He was 71 years old.
        Judge Burke began his law career as a deputy district attorney in Alameda County in 1940. He was named United States Attorney for Northern California by President Eisenhower in 1953.
        Five years later he was appointed by Eisenhower to the Federal District Court in San Francisco, and remained a judge until his death.
