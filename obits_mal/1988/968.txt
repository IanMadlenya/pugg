        The Rev. John Francis McConnell, a Roman Catholic biblical scholar and a Maryknoll priest, died of heart failure on June 19 at the Maryknoll residence in Los Altos, Calif. He was 79 years old.
        Father McConnell, who was ordained in 1931, was on the editorial board of the Confraternity of Christian Doctrine, the religious education office of the United States Bishops Conference, for 15 years and had served as chairman of its New Testament translation committee.
        He is survived by three sisters, Jean Dingeman of Troy, Mich., Sister M. Janet McConnell of Monrovia, Calif., and Sister M. St. David McConnell of Windsor, Ontario.
