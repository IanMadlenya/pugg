        Martin Nielsen Rasmussen, an engineer and machinist who built the Junkyard Telescope on display at the Smithsonian Institution, died Saturday. He was 92 years old.
        Mr. Rasmussen, whose telescope was donated by his family to the Smithsonian's National Museum of American History in 1982, built it out of 30 automobile flywheels purchased for 25 cents each. It had a maximum magnificaton of 1,300 times.
