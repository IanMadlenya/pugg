        Dr. Melville A. Platt, a retired deputy director of New York Hospital, died of kidney cancer Monday at the hospital. He was 60 years old and lived in Elmsford, N.Y.
        Dr. Platt joined New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center as a resident in obstetrics and gynecology in 1955 and entered the hospital's administration in 1967 as associate director.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Nancy Kirk; two daughters, Nancy and Margaret; a son, Melville Jr., and a brother, John, of Toronto.
