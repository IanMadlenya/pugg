        John D. Tracy, the executive assistant to Representative Mario Biaggi, died of a heart attack Friday at the Veterans Administration Hospital in the Bronx. He was 88 years old and lived in the Bronx.
        A former police sergeant and detective, Mr. Tracy taught police officers at the Delehanty Institute of Civil Service for many years. One of his students was Mr. Biaggi.
        Mr. Tracy joined the first Congressional campaign of Mr. Biaggi, a Bronx Democrat, in 1968, and he stayed on as executive assistant.
        He is survived by a niece, Mary Helen Fitzgerald of the Bronx.
