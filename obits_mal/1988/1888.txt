        Prince Robert de Lobkowicz died of a brain tumor on Saturday in Beirut, Lebanon. He was 26 years old and lived in Paris.
        Born in Paris, Prince Robert was descended from Bohemian aristocracy on his father's side and French and Austrian dynasties on his mother's side.
        A graduate of the University of San Francisco, he was a lieutenant in the French Army reserve and a Knight of Malta.
        Surviving are his mother, Princess Francoise de Bourbon-Parme de Lobkowicz; his father, Prince Edouard de Lobkowicz; a brother, Prince Charles-Henri, and a sister, Princess Marie-Gabrielle, all of Paris.
