        John L. Cotsworth, retired president and chairman of the board of the Berry Steel Company of Edison, N.J., died of a heart attack March 7 while vacationing in Vero Beach, Fla. He was 73 years old and lived in Bay Head, N.J.
        Mr. Cotsworth was with the company for nearly 22 years, until his retirement in 1982.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorcas Berry; two daughters, Alice Goltra of Lake Forest, Ill., and Marguerite Sorum of Washington; a son, Frederick, of Clayton, Mo.; four stepchildren, and nine grandchildren.
