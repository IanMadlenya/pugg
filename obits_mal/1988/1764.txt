        Peter Hood Ballantine Cumming, a retired executive and former mayor of Rumson, N.J., died of cancer Wednesday at his home in Little Silver, N.J. He was 78 years old.
        Mr. Cumming, an alumnus of Princeton University, was former executive vice president and manager of marketing of the J. P. Maguire Company. Previously, he was president of the Textile Banking Company, a vice president and director of the Iselin-Jefferson Financial Company, and a general partner in H. A. Caesar & Company, a factoring business in Manhattan.
        He was mayor of Rumson in the 1950's and was the great-grandson of Peter Ballantine, the founder of the Ballantine Brewing Company of Newark.
        Surviving are his wife, Dorothy; three daughters, Dorothy Scott and Susan McAlpin, both of Little Silver, and Diane, of Manhattan, and four grandchildren.
