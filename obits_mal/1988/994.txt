        Dr. Samuel J. Fanning, a retired history professor, died Friday at Long Island Jewish Hospital. He was 76 years old and lived in Floral Park, L.I.
        Dr. Fanning was a professor at St. John's University from 1964 until 1977. He had worked for the Central Intelligence Agency.
        Surviving are his wife, Lucille, and eight children, Samuel, of Gaithersberg, Md., Sister Mary Margaret of Martin, Ky., Patrick, of Olympia, Wash., Kathleen, of Centereach, L.I., Adrian, of Amityville, L.I., and Deirdre, Martha and Rosemary, all of Baldwin, L.I.
