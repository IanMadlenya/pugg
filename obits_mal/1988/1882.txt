        Zambia's Ambassador to the United States and several other countries, Nalumino Mundia, died of a heart attack today after collapsing at a diplomatic function, the Bolivian Foreign Ministry said. He was 62 years old.
        Mr. Mundia, envoy to Brazil, Peru and Venezuela as well as to Washington, had presented his credentials to President Victor Paz Estenssoro of Bolivia here Friday and was attending a diplomatic function late Monday night when he collapsed.
        He is survived by his wife and six children. His body will be flown to Washington later this week.
