        Harold Lief, who taught business law and accounting for 45 years at the former Haaren High School in Manhattan, died of complications after surgery for an aneurism Thursday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 85 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Lief was born in Russia and came here as an infant. He graduated from City College and the New York University Law School.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Arlene Goldstein; two sons, Fred, of Manhattan, and Anthony, of Plano, Tex., and two grandchildren.
