        Theodore Ornstein, a former mayor of Long Beach, L.I., and a founder and former chairman of Central Federal Savings Bank, died yesterday at his home in Long Beach after a short illness. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Ornstein also served as Parks Commissioner in Long Beach, where he lived for 65 years. He founded Central Federal in 1937 and served as chairman until 1975.
        Surviving are his wife, Rose; his son, J. Alan, of Sag Harbor and Manhattan; two brothers, Albert, of Manhattan, and David, of Greensboro, N.C.; nine grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
