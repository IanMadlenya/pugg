        Louis Newman, a lawyer, died Thursday at Winthrop University Hospital in Mineola, L.I. He was 79 years old and lived in Roslyn Heights, L.I.
        Mr. Newman was a lawyer for the National Labor Relations Board in the 1940's and counsel for the Second Regional Wage Stabilization Board in the 1950's. He was associated with the Manhattan law firm of Guggenheimer & Untermyer until 1986.
        He is survived by his wife, Edith; a daughter, Elizabeth Pearl of Katonah, N.Y., and three grandchildren.
