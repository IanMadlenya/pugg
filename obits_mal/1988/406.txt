        John T. De Palma , former chairman of Republic National Bank of New York in the mid-1970's, died Saturday at Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center in Manhattan. He was 76 years old and lived in Cranford, N.J.
        Mr. De Palma worked for Chase Manhattan Bank for nearly 40 years before joining the Kings County Trust Company, which later merged with Republic National.
        Survivors are his wife, Miriam of Plainfield, N.J.; a son, Jack Jr. of Manhattan; three daughters, Sister Jude Miriam of Sparkill, N.Y., Mary E. Burke of Basking Ridge, N.J., and Miriam Heck of Sharon Hill, Pa., and two grandchildren.
