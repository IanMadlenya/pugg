        David T. Marvel Sr., a former corporate vice president of the Olin Corporation, died of cancer Sunday at Columbia Presbyterian Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 81 years old and lived in Lewes, Del.
        Born in Richmond, Ind., he joined Olin in 1949 and worked there for 22 years. He served on the War Production Board in World War II and was a former chairman of the Annual Assay Committee, which verifies weights of United States coins.
        Surviving are his wife, Marcia; a sister, Elizabeth Quigg, of Wilmington, Del., and three grandchildren.
