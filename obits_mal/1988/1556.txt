        Carl Typermass, a retired insurance executive, died of a blood disorder Tuesday at St. Francis Hospital in Roslyn, L.I. He was 82 years old and lived in Manhassett, L.I.
        Mr. Typermass retired as executive vice president of the Home Insurance Company in 1971 after a career there of about 20 years. Previously he had been with the New York State Department of Insurance.
        He is survived by his wife, June, of Manhassett, and two sons, Arthur, of Garden City, L.I., and Robert, of Manhassett.
