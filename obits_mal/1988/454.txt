        Kenneth W. Tucker, senior vice president and general manager of Bozell, Jacobs, Kenyon & Eckhardt Public Relations of New York, died of cancer Sunday at Beth Israel Medical Center. He was 41 years old and lived in Manhattan and Southampton, L.I.
        A specialist in translating legalese into understandable language, Mr. Tucker was a graduate of Long Island University and held a law degree from Syracuse University.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary; a son, Zachary; his parents, Walter and Lydia Tucker of Sayville, L.I., and a sister, Virginia Freeman of State College, Pa.
