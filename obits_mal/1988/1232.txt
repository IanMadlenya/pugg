        Richard Glenn Gettell, former president of Mount Holyoke College, died of lung cancer Sunday at his home in Menlo Park, Calif. He was 76 years old.
        President of the college in Massachusetts from 1957 to 1968, Dr. Gettell was previously an economist for Time Inc., where he was responsible for economic forecasting. He also was a consultant to the White House in the Office of Defense Management.
        Before World War II he was an instructor at Yale University. He earned a bachelor's degree from Amherst College and a doctorate from the University of California at Berkeley.
        Surviving are his wife, Landonia, and two nephews.
