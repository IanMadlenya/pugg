        Dr. Ralph Daniel Junker, a surgeon, died of a heart attack last Thursday at Southampton (L.I.) Hospital, where he had been a member of the staff from 1958 to 1978. He was 65 years old and lived in Tryon, N.C.
        He is survived by two daughters, Ellen, of Boston and Lynn, of Groton, Conn.; three sons, Curtis, of Manhattan, Valentine, of Princeton and Barry, of Groton; two sisters, Ruth Peabody of Baltimore and Marion Miller of Glendora, Calif., and a brother, Curtis, of Oklahoma City.
