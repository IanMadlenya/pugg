
       Walton Jones, the oldest person ever given a temporary artificial heart, died today of an infection, doctors said. He was 60 years old. Mr. Jones' heart was replaced by the Jarvik-7 pump on Dec. 4 by Dr. William DeVries, the surgeon who has done four of the five permanent mechanical heart operations.
