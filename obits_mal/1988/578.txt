        Fred Stark, a builder, died of heart failure April 15 at his home in Holliswood, Queens. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Stark was born in Vienna and arrived in New York at the age of 18. He formed a construction business and built apartment houses, offices and shopping centers.
        Before World War II, he rescued 24 members of his family from occupied Austria.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Else Adrian; a brother, Leo, of Manhattan; a daughter, Rita, of Holliswood, and a son, Harold, of Bayside, Queens.
