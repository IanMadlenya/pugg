        Dr. Russell W. Lavengood Jr., a Manhattan urologist whose patients included actors, writers and politicians, died of a heart attack Wednesday at the New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center. He was 63 years old and lived in Pelham, N.Y.
        Dr. Lavengood was a clinical professor at the Cornell Medical College and the Columbia University College of Physicians and Surgeons.
        Surviving are his wife, Roberta Bedell, and two sons, Martin and Jason, all of Pelham, and a daughter, Paige, of New Haven.
