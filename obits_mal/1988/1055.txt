        Dr. Jan Ehrenwald, a psychoanalyst and author, died of a heart attack Wednesday at his home in Somers, N.Y. He was 88 years old.
        Dr. Ehrenwald was the author of eight books dealing with psychiatric issues, including ''From Medicine Man to Freud'' in 1957 and ''The ESP Experience'' in 1978.
        He is survived by his wife, Anny; a daughter, Barbara Krohn of Manhattan, and two granddaughters.
