        The Rev. Juvenal Lalor, a former president of St. Bonaventure University, died of complications following surgery at St. Anthony's Hospital in Warwick, N.Y., on Sunday. He was 77 years old and had lived in retirement at the Holy Cross Friary in the Bronx.
        Father Lalor headed St. Bonaventure from 1949 to 1955. A native of Brooklyn, he was ordained in the Franciscan order in 1936 and pursued advanced studies at the University of Toronto and at Laval University in Quebec, where he received his Ph.D.
        In 1974 he was awarded a high Vatican decoration, the Pro Ecclesia et Pontifice medal, by Pope Paul VI.
        Father Lalor is survived by two sisters, Helen Keefe of Remsenburg, L.I., and Margaret Lalor of Hampton Bays, L.I.
