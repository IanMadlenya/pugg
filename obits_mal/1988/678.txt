        Francis E. Conrad, a high-level Veterans Administration official, was stabbed to death Thursday. He was 51 years old.
        Dr. Conrad, who was the agency's director of the office of quality assurance, was waiting in an outer office of a Chicago apartment complex, helping his daughter find an apartment, when a person being evicted stabbed him and two other people, the police said.
        Before joining the V.A. in 1973, Dr. Conrad was an associate professor of clinical psychiatry and associate dean of veterans affairs at the State University of New York at Stony Brook, L.I.
