        Leo S. Frenkel, a former honorary chairman of Frenkel & Company, an insurance brokerage firm in Manhattan, died of heart failure Sunday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 92 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Frenkel was associated with Frenkel & Company, a firm founded by his father in 1878, for more than 69 years. He was a member of the Harmonie Club, a Manhattan social club, and was a former director of the Musicians Foundation Inc. He was also a former president of the East Bronx Y.M.H.A. and a director of the Insurance Brokers Association of the State of New York.
        He is survived by a daughter, Janice F. Pachner of Scarsdale, N.Y.; a son, Robert of Corvallis, Ore., and three grandchildren.
