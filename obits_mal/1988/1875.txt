        Bishop Jan Pasztor, one of only four Roman Catholic prelates in Czechoslovakia, died today. He was 76 years old.
        Bishop Pasztor, who was consecrated bishop of the Nitra Diocese in western Slovakia in 1973, had been ill for some time, according to the official press agency.
