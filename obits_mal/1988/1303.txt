        Irving Honigsberg, a real-estate lawyer, died of complications after surgery Sunday at Lenox Hill Hospital. He was 80 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Honigsberg was born in Brooklyn and was a graduate of Boys High School. He received a bachelor's degree and a law degree from New York University.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Clarice Salsberg; a daughter, Amy Bernstein of Manhattan; two sisters, Gertrude Schweitzer of Colts Neck, N.J., and Harriet Bien of Hanover, N.H.; and two grandchildren.
