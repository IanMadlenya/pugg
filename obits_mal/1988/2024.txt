        James Patrick Garvey, a former corporate vice president of Burlington Industries, died of cancer Tuesday at Greenwich (Conn.) Hospital. He was 77 years old and lived in Greenwich.
        Mr. Garvey, who was born in Trenton, graduated from New York University. He joined Burlington in 1947.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; two daughters, Joyce Bray of New Britain, Conn., and Lois Garvey of Greenwich, and two grandsons.
