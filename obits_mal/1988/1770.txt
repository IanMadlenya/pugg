        Seymour W. Weiner, an executive and former head of school board associations, died of lung cancer Thursday at Community Hospital in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 66 years old and lived in Glen Cove.
        Mr. Weiner was president of the Gelatine Products Company, a theatrical-lighting company, and was a former president of the New York State School Boards Association. He was also president of the Nassau-Suffolk School Boards Association and a member of the Glen Cove Board of Education from 1963 to 1979.
        Surviving are his wife, Gloria; two daughters, Deborah, of Athens, Ga., and Susan Aidun of Manhattan; two stepsons, Mark, of Manhattan, and Bruce Hershenson of Philadelphia; a brother, Dr. Daniel Weiner of Manhattan, and a grandson.
