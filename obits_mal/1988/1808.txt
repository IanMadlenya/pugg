        Philip S. Heisler, a former managing editor of The Evening Sun in Baltimore, died of cancer Thursday at his home in Guilford, Md. He was 73 years old.
        Mr. Heisler was managing editor of the newspaper for 30 years, until he retired in 1979. He joined the newspaper in 1939, working at various jobs including war correspondent, covering such historic moments as the invasion of Iwo Jima.
        Born in Dallastown, Pa., Mr. Heisler graduated from Pennsylvania State University. He is survived by his wife, Helen, and a sister, Louella Miller of York, Pa.
