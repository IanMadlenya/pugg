        Dr. Pierre R. Bretey, a former Wall Street executive and a leading railroad analyst, died of heart failure Tuesday at his Manhattan home. He was 89 years old.
        Dr. Bretey, who received M.B.A. and Doctor of Commercial Science degrees from New York University, was a former president of the New York Society of Financial Analysts and the Financial Analysts Federation. He was also a partner and senior vice president of Hayden, Stone & Company He later joined Reynolds & Company. He was also an editor of The Analysts Journal and an author of books on financial matters.
        He is survived by his wife, Wilma, and a son, Brad King, both of Manhattan.
