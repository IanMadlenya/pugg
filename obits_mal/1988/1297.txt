        John W. Rindlaub, a retired executive vice president of Municipal Issuers, died of cancer Thursday at his home in Saddle River, N.J. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Rindlaub, spent more than 50 years in the insurance and financial guarantee fields. He retired in March from Municipal Issuers, the managing agency of the Municipal Bond Insurance Association, the predecessor to the Municipal Bond Investers Assurance Corporation.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Edythe Van Antwerp; a daughter, Diane Hipkins of Saddle River; two sons, John V., of Irvington, N.Y., and Neil B., of Glen Rock, N.J., and seven grandchildren.
