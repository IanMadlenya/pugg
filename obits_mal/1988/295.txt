        George Britt, a former newspaperman, author and teacher, died Thursday at the Meadow Lakes retirement complex in Hightstown, N.J., where he lived. He was 92 years old.
        Mr. Britt was a feature writer for 14 years with The New York World-Telegram and The New York Post. He also worked on newspapers in Kansas City, Mo., Chicago and Oklahoma City.
        He was the author of two books and co-authored a third with the New York newspaperman Heywood Broun. He also taught news writing at Columbia University for more than 15 years.
        There are no immediate survivors.
