        Albert J. Sica, former dean of the Fordham University School of Pharmacy, died of melanoma Monday at his home in Delmar, N.Y. He was 71 years old.
        Mr. Sica was a member of the faculty of the School of Pharmacy for 40 years and dean from 1956 to 1962.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lee Belloise; six sons, Albert J. Jr., of Pitman, N.J., Robert, of Miami, John, of Philadelphia, and Michael, William and Dr. Gregory, all of Manhattan; two brothers, Louis, of Miami, and Vincent, of Morristown, N.J.; two sisters, Navena Correale and Lucy Galderi, also of Morristown, and four grandchildren.
