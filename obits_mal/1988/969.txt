        Roy W. Anderson, a vice president of the American Newspaper Publishers Association, a publisher trade group, died of liver cancer Sunday at his home in Baltimore. He was 63 years old.
        Mr. Anderson, a native of Chicago, was vice president of human resources for the association, which he joined in 1979. Earlier he was general manager of The Baltimore News-American.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marie Hardy; a son, Roy William Anderson Jr. of Rogers Forge, Md.; two daughters, Cynthia Hart and Donna Sue Ryan, both of Severna Park, Md., and two grandchildren.
