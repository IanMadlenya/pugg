        Robert B. Stewart, a retired Purdue University executive who also coordinated veterans' benefits for the Government, died Friday in Vero Beach, Fla. He was 92 years old.
        In his 36-year career at Purdue, Mr. Stewart was controller, vice president and treasurer. He retired in 1961.
        After World War II, as administrator of the Veteran Affairs Advisory Committee on Vocational Rehabilitation, Education and Training, he coordinating $14.5 billion in G.I. Bill of Rights benefits for 8 million veterans.
