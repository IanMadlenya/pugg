        Edward van Westerborg, the founding publisher of Facts on File, died Saturday at his home in Walnut Creek, Calif., after suffering from cancer. He was 88 years old.
        Born in Rotterdam, Mr. van Westerborg emigrated to the United States in 1940, and founded the Manhattan-based publishing company. He retired in 1975.
        Facts on File publishes the World News Digest, a standard index record used by American libraries. It also publishes some 120 library and popular reference books annually.
        Mr. van Westerborg is survived by his wife, Dita, and two grandchildren.
