        Dr. Jonah D. Kosovsky, an assistant attending physician in Mount Sinai Medical Center's department of medicine, died of cancer last Sunday at his home in Manhattan. He was 61 years old.
        Dr. Kosovsky, a native of Brooklyn, was a graduate of Columbia College and New York University College of Medicine. He had been associated with Mount Sinai since 1950. He was also an assistant clinical professor of medicine.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Marietta Rauhala; a brother, Dr. Nathan Kase of Manhattan; a son, Robert, of Manhattan; two daughters, Jennifer and Jane, also of Manhattan.
