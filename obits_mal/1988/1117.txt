        George F. Butterick, a lecturer in English and curator of the Literary Archives at the University of Connecticut, died of cancer Monday at his home in Willimantic, Conn. He was 45 years old.
        Mr. Butterick, an authority on the poet Charles Olson, edited Olson's 600-page work, ''The Maximus Poem'' and at the time of his death was working on a biography of the poet. He received the American Book Award for his ''Collected Poems of Charles Olson,'' which was published last year.
        Mr. Butterick is survived by his wife, the former Colette Hetzel; his sons, George A. and Aaron A.; his mother, Kathleen, and his father, George W..
