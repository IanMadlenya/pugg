        Richard Wedge Morin, a former State Department official and later Dartmouth College's librarian, died Sunday at the age of 85.
        Mr. Morin, a native of Albert Lea, Minn., graduated from Dartmouth in 1924 and earned his law degree from Harvard in 1928.
        He was the United States vice consul in Paris from 1929 to 1933 and later served with the State Department in Washington.
        In 1948 joined Dartmouth and in 1950 became librarian, a position he held for 18 years.
        Mr. Morin is survived by his wife, Pauline, and three daughters.
