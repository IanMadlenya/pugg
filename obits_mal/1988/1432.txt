        Arthur E. Voss, a publishing executive and former owner of Financial World magazine, died Thursday in Naples, Fla., where he lived in retirement. He was 94 years old.
        Mr. Voss joined the Guenther Publishing Corporation in 1922 and became publisher of Financial World in 1943. He later bought it, with other executives. He retired in 1969.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Lola Sillery; two daughters, Carol Beers of Huntington, L.I., and Linda Spehr of East Setauket, L.I.; five grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
