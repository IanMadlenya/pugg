        David C. Sutherland, vice president of Home Line Cruises Inc., died of pneumonia Saturday at St. Barnabas Hospital in Livingston, N.J. He was 63 years old and lived in West Orange, N.J.
        Born in London, Mr. Sutherland graduated from the University of London. He emigrated to Canada and joined Home Lines in 1956. The Manhattan-based company operates cruises to Bermuda and the Caribbean.
        Mr. Sutherland is survived by his wife, Bjorg; two sons, Roy and Richard, both of San Jose, Calif., and a sister, Muriel Major of London.
