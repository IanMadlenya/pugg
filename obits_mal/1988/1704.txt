        Brother Thomas S. Farrell, founding president of Walsh College, died of a stroke Saturday. He was 76 years old.
        He was president of Walsh from November 1960, when the liberal arts college in Canton opened with 67 students, until June 1970, when the school's enrollment had grown to about 1,000. The college currently has an enrollment of about 1,400.
        In 1985, after serving eight years as special assistant to the president, he was named president emeritus.
