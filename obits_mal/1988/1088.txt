        James P. O'Neill, a former executive vice president of the Xerox Corporation who retired in 1981, died on Tuesday at Stamford (Conn.) Hospital. He was 67 years old and lived in New Canaan.
        Mr. O'Neill is survived by his wife, the former Alice Smith; a daughter, Maureen Hennessy of Wheaton, Ill.; three sons, Terrence, of Delmar, N.Y., James, of Canandaigua, N.Y., and Michael, of Pittsford, N.Y., and seven grandchildren.
