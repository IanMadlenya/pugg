        Dr. Heinz Joseph Wittig, a pediatrician and head of the allergy division at the Ochsner Clinic, died at his home here Sunday after a long illness. He was 67 years old.
        Dr. Wittig, who was born in Munich, Germany, served on state medical examiners' boards in Illinois, New Jersey, New York and Florida.
        Survivors include his wife, a daughter, four sons and a granddaughter.
