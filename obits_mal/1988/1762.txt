        G. Kent Frandsen, an associate professor of law and associate dean for student affairs at the Indiana University School of Law, died of a heart attack Wednesday. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Frandsen, who served 23 years with the university, headed the admissions office at the law school when Dan Quayle, the Indiana Senator who is Vice President-elect, was admitted. As a city judge in Lebanon, Ind., he performed Mr. Quayle's wedding ceremony to Marilyn Tucker in 1972.
