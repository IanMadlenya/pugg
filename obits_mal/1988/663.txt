        Albert A. Jewett, retired chairman of the Riverhead (L.I.) Savings Bank, died Wednesday at Central Suffolk Hospital. He was 78 years old and lived in Riverhead.
        Mr. Jewett, who joined the bank in 1932, became president in 1968 and retired as chairman in 1984.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mildred Conklin.
