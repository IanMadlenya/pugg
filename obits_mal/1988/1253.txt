        Chriss Gittens, who performed for 35 years in the British Broadcasting Corporation's long-running radio serial ''The Archers,'' died Sunday. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Gittens played the irascible Walter Gabriel in the series, which was set in a fictional town in the Midlands.
        David Hatch, the managing director of BBC network radio, said: ''Chriss was one of the great personalities. He created a character that will live in the minds and hearts of listeners for a long time to come.''
