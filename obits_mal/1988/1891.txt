        Alan S. Greditor, director of United States equity trading for Drexel Burnham Lambert Inc., died in an automobile accident yesterday in Tenafly, N.J., where he lived. He was 38 years old.
        As a research analyst, Mr. Greditor covered the food industry. For the last nine years, he was named to the All-America Research Team by Institutional Investor magazine. Mr. Greditor joined Drexel Burnham in 1979, after working as an analyst for F. Eberstadt & Company.
        Surviving are his wife, Flora; two daughters, Marla and Jenna; his father, Howard, of Fort Lee, N.J.; a sister, Susan Merritt of Stamford, Conn., and his grandparents, Anna and Mac Adler of Miami Beach.
