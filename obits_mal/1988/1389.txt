        Gilbert L. Thurston, former president of Thurston-McGuire of New York, a furniture company, died Sunday at Northern Westchester Hospital in Mt. Kisco, N.Y., after suffering a stroke. He was 72 years old and lived in Katonah, N.Y.
        Surviving are his wife, Mary Kay; a daughter, Susan Campbell of Brookline, Mass.; a son, Stephen, of Scituate, Mass.; two sisters, Wilda Green of Eads, Tenn., and Marjorie Graham of Irvington, Ala.; four brothers, Raymond, of Jackson, Mich., Gerald, of Cranford, N.J., Russell, of Long Beach, Miss., and Stanley, of Mobile, Ala., and four grandchildren.
