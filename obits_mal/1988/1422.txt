        The Rev. Charles B. Persell Jr., Suffragan Bishop of the Episcopal Diocese of Albany from 1963 until his retirement in 1977, died Friday at St. Peter's Hospital in Albany after a stroke. He was 79 years old and lived in Loudonville, N.Y.
        Bishop Persell, a native of Lakewood, N.Y., was a graduate of Hobart College and of the General Theological Seminary.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dorothy Lurenz; three sons, Charles 3d, of Manhattan, the Rev. William, of Brooklyn, and Peter, of Kigali, Rwanda; two daughters, Carolyn Burnworth of Cumberland, R.I., and Susan Bischoff of Cory, N.C.; 14 grandchildren, and two great-grandchildren.
