        Anton Morris Leader, a director and producer whose work in television included the popular ''Star Trek'' and ''Ironside'' series, died Friday. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Leader's 30-year career in television also included work on ''The Virginian,'' ''Daniel Boone,'' ''Get Smart,'' ''I Spy'' and ''Hawaii Five-O.''Among the movies he directed were ''It Happens Every Thursday'' and ''Children of the Damned.''
