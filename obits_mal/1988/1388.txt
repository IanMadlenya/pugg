        Elliott Graham, a publicist at E. P. Dutton, the publisher, died of colon cancer Saturday at Yonkers General Hospital. He was 62 years old and lived in Yonkers.
        Mr. Graham, who was born in Manhattan, graduated from New York University. He joined Dutton in 1929 as a junior clerk. He rose to publicity director, working with many authors, including A. A. Milne, Francoise Sagan, Mickey Spillane and Gore Vidal.
        He is survived by a sister, Madeline Scheele; two nieces and a nephew, all of Yonkers.
        Correction:  August 20, 1988, Saturday, Late City Final Edition
        He was 82.
