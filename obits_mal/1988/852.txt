        Albert H. O'Neil, former vice president of the Freeport Sulphur Company Inc., died of Alzheimer's disease on Friday at Anne Arundel Hospital in Annapolis, Md. He was 84 years old and lived in Annapolis.
        He was with Freeport for 35 years before he retired in 1972. Mr. O'Neil was born in West Roxbury, Mass., and graduated from Harvard College.
        He is survived by his wife, Helen McGrath; a daughter, Nyia Yannatos of Cambridge, Mass.; two stepdaughters, Jo-Ann Malkin of Manhattan and Catherine Winter of Washington, and a sister, Marian Roach of Baltimore.
