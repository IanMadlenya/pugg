        Alex Goldberger, a former executive director of the Council of Insurance Brokers of Greater New York, died Wednesday in Hollywood (Fla.) Memorial Hospital after a heart attack. He was 87 years old and lived in Hollywood.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Sophie Gratz; two daughters, Judith Greenberg of Manhasset, L.I., and Francine Rubenstein of East Williston, L.I.; a sister, Jean Lipp of Deerfield, Mass.; five grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
