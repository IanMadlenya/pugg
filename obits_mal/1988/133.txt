        Michael N. Taylor, director of Indian education programs for the Association of American Indian Affairs, died of pnuemonia Tuesday at the Washington Adventist Hospital in Tacoma, Md. He was 47 years old and lived on Staten Island.
        Mr. Taylor, an Oglala Sioux, had been associated with the Manhattan-based, nonprofit group for the last year.
        He is survived by his grandmother, Lucille White of Portland, Ore.; his parents, Alfred and Hazel Taylor, also of Portland, and three children, Maria Theresa, Maria Elena and Jason, all of Denver.
