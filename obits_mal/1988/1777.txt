        Howard J. Friedman, chairman of the architecture department at the University of California at Berkeley, died Friday. He was 69 years old.
        After graduating from the university, Mr. Friedman opened an architectural office in San Francisco. He joined the Berkeley faculty in 1966 and was elected department chairman in 1986. He also collaborated with colleagues at Berkeley and Stanford University on a research project to reduce earthquake hazards of masonry buildings in China.
