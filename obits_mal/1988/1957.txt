        Harry M. Bainbridge, international counsel with the law department of Pepsico Inc., was killed Wednesday in the crash of the Pan American World Airways plane in Scotland. He was 34 years old and lived in Montrose, N.Y.
        Mr. Bainbridge, a graduate of the University of Notre Dame and its law school, joined Pepsico in 1983 and was based at its office in Somers, N.Y.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Dona Bardelli; his parents, Lawrence and Olive Bainbridge, of Farmington, W. Va.; a brother, Denny, of Fairmont, Va., and a sister, Sue Shumate of Live Oak, Fla.
