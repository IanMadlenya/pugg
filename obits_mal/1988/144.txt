        Edward V. Hickey Jr., chairman of the Federal Maritime Commission since 1985, died unexpectedly of heart failure on Saturday at his home in Falls Church, Va. He was 52 years old.
        A former Secret Service agent, Mr. Hickey became executive director of the California State Police in 1969. He later was a security officer in the State Department and director of the White House military office.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara; seven sons, Edward V. 3d, Michael F., Joseph G., Paul V., John D., David T. and Daniel J., and three brothers, Frank, William and Gerald.
