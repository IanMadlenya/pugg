        Robert J. Townsend, a member of the National Ski Hall of Fame and ski coach at Williams College for 22 years, died of cancer Friday at North Adams Regional Hospital. He was 66 years old.
        Mr. Townsend competed at the 1948 Winter Olympics. He coached skiing at Williams from 1950 to 1972.
        As secretary to the United States Olympic Ski Committee, Mr. Townsend helped select the United States ski teams in 1960 and 1964.
        He is survived by his wife, Harriet; a daughter, and two sons.
