        Huxley Madeheim, an engineer and former professor of industrial management at Baruch College, died Jan. 25 at St. Vincent's Hospital. He was 82 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Madeheim joined the Baruch faculty as an evening instructor in 1930 and rose to full professor by 1960. He retired in 1972 to devote full time to his private engineering practice.
        He is survived by his wife, Rhoda; a daughter, Sandra Barrett of Stamford, Conn.; two sons, James, of Tucson, Ariz., and Huxley T., of Sacramento, Calif.; seven grandchildren, and three great-grandchildren.
