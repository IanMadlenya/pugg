        Walter B. D. Hickey, a men's clothing manufacturer, died of cancer Sunday at his home in Rochester. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Hickey, who graduated from Georgetown University in 1927, was the former president and chairman of the Hickey-Freeman Company, a manufacturer of men's clothing.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth J. Flint, of Rochester; a son, Walter Jr., of Pittsford, N.Y.; two daughters, Sister Mary Walter, of Elmira, N.Y., and Betsy Macken, of Mahopac, N.Y., and four grandchildren.
