        Joseph A. Brunton Jr., chief executive of the Boy Scouts of America from 1960 to 1967, died yesterday after a long illness at St. Barnabas Village, a retirement home in Gibsonia, Pa., where he lived. He was 86 years old.
        Surviving are his wife, Marienne; a sister, Ruth Brown of Sarasota, Fla. and a brother, Paul of Glenshaw, Pa.
