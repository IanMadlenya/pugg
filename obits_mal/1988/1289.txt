        Solomon A. Klein, the former chief counsel to the Judicial Inquiry on Professional Conduct of the New York State Supreme Court, died of cancer of the prostate Friday at his summer home in Shelter Island, L.I. He was 82 years old and lived in Long Boat Key, Fla.
        Mr. Klein is survived by his wife, Ruth Porter; two daughters, Susan, of Manhattan, and Laura Feldbaum of Bethesda, Md., and two grandchildren.
