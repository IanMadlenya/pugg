        Raphael L. Elias, a lawyer who specialized in probate cases and estate planning, died of cancer Tuesday at New York Hospital. He was 82 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Elias, whose career in the law spanned 58 years, was in private practice up to his death and was also of counsel to the law firm of Shapiro & Schwartz in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Doris Gilman; a son, Ralph, of Manhattan; two daughters, Margaret Lehman of Manhattan and Mary Ivanyi of Mamaroneck, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
