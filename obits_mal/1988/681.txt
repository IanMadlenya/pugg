        The Rev. Craig Walter Casey, executive vice president and manager of the Episcopal Church Pension Fund, died of lung cancer Saturday at his home in Greenwich, Conn. He was 52 years old.
        In addition to his work with the pension fund, which serves retired Episcopal ministers throughout the country, Mr. Casey was an associate pastor at Christ Episcopal Church in Greenwich. He was also the chaplain of the men and boys' choirs there.
        He graduated from the University of the South in 1958 and the General Theological Seminary in 1964. In 1971, he received a master's degree from the Harvard Business School.
        He is survived by his wife, Sally; a son, Craig Alexander; two daughters, Sarah and Ellen; his mother, Hazel, of Memphis; his father, Fredric, of Palm Springs, Calif.; three brothers, Patrick and Dennis, both of Memphis, and W. Brent, of Dallas, and two sisters, Judith Walters and Mary Ann Basurto, both of Memphis.
