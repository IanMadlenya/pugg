        Myron S. Nightingale, a California wine maker for four decades and former president of the American Society of Enology and Viticulture, died Friday at the age of 73.
        Since 1983, Mr. Nightingale had been wine master emeritus and consultant at Beringer Vineyards. He also worked at several other wineries, including Italian Swiss Colony, and won numerous awards for wine making. He was associated with dessert wines that became known as Nightingale and were produced at Beringer.
