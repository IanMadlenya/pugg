        J. Leslie Rollins, a college administrator, died Sunday at O'Bleness Memorial Hospital in Athens, Ohio. He was 84 years old and lived in Athens.
        A graduate of Buena Vista College in Iowa, Mr. Rollins was the assistant dean of men at Northwestern University. He served as the assistant dean of the Harvard University Graduate School of Business Administration from 1942 to 1966 and also worked as the director of college relations from 1962 to 1966. From 1966 to 1970, he was a research fellow at Ohio University.
        Survivors include his wife, Edith; a daughter, Meredith Hamer of Yarmouth, Me., and two sons, James of Lincoln, Mass., and Michael of Plymouth, Mass.
