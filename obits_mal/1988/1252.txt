        Dr. Henry Chase, the secretary of the National Horse Show, died Tuesday after apparently suffering a heart attack at his home in Manhattan. He was 64 years old.
        Dr. Chase was graduated from Yale University and the Harvard Medical School as a doctor of dental medicine. He practiced in New York for many years and was a member of the New York Dental Society, the New York Academy of Dentistry and the American Dental Association.
        Dr. Chase had a lifelong interest in equestrian sports and was a longtime member of the National Horse Show Association and a trustee of the National Horse Show Foundation.
        Services will be held at 11 A.M. today at St. Thomas More Church in New York. He is survived by four brothers.
