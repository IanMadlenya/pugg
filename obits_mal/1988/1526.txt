        Anthony A. Goodchild, retired chairman of Armatron International Inc., died in an automobile accident near Allentown, Pa., Thursday. He was 60 years old and lived in retirement in Alexandria, N.H.
        Mr. Goodchild joined Armatron, a diversified manufacturing concern based in Melrose, Mass., as president in 1979. Before that, he was a vice president of W. R. Grace.
        Mr. Goodchild, a native of London, was a graduate of Cambridge.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Anne Larson; a daughter, Christian Elizabeth, of the Riverdale section of the Bronx, and two sons, Matthew, of Los Angeles, and Peter, of Manhattan.
