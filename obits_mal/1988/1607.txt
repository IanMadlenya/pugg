        Drew Brinkerhoff, a retired executive of CBS, died of cancer Sunday at Memorial Medical Center in Savannah, Ga. He was 66 years old and had recently moved to Savannah from Fair Haven, N.J.
        Mr. Brinkerhoff was with CBS for 27 years. At the time of his retirement in November 1985, he was vice president of facilities.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara; a daughter, Barbara, of Beverly, Mass.; three sons, Peter, of Manhattan; William, of Denver, and John, of Lake Bluff, Ill., and nine grandchildren.
