        Paul J. Cupp, former chairman of Acme Markets Inc., died of heart failure Monday at Bryn Mawr (Pa.) Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Bryn Mawr.
        Born in Johnstown, Pa., Mr. Cupp joined American Stores Company, which later became Acme, in 1929 and rose to chairman, a job he held from 1961 to 1974.
        Surviving are his wife, Louise; three daughters, Marilyn Krumhansl of Ithaca, N.Y., Janice Wilcox of Baltimore and Louann Geiger of Media, Pa.; a sister, Frances Coventry of Cazenovia, N.Y.; 10 grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
