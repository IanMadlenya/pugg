        L. Maxell Peace, a former vice president at Marine Midland Bank, died of complications of AIDS Monday at New York University Medical Center. He was 35 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Peace was a national commercial retail loan officer who specialized in financing for limited partnership ventures. He was a native of Memphis.
        He is survived by his parents, Luther and Nadine Peace of Proctor, Ark.; two sisters, Donna McBryde of Memphis and Rita Underhill of Heth, Ark., and his paternal grandmother, Elva Peace of Proctor.
