        Robert Fink, a retired classics pro-fessor, died of pneumonia Saturday at a nursing home in Mount Vernon, Ohio. He was 83 years old and lived in Gambier, Ohio.
        Professor Fink was an internationally known papyrologist and author of ''Roman Military Records on Papyrus,'' considered a definitive work in the field. He taught at Kenyon College in Gambier, Ohio, from 1944 to 1966 and at the State University of New York at Albany from 1966 to 1976. Earlier he taught at Yale University, Russell Sage College and Beloit College.
        He is surivived by his wife, the former Ruth Kuersteiner.
