        Dr. Eugene D. Rosenfeld, a former executive director of Long Island Jewish Hospital, died of a heart attack Tuesday at the University of Arizona Medical Center in Tucson. He was 71 years old and had lived in Manhattan and Montauk, L.I., before retiring to Green Valley, Ariz., in 1985.
        Dr. Rosenfeld, whose training was in internal medicine, was also the head of E. D. Rosenfeld Associates, a health services consulting concern in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Laura Sipin; two sons, Steven B., of Manhattan and Michael E., of San Diego; a daughter, Erica Wilson of Chicago; a sister, Edith Sherman of Denver, and eight grandchildren.
