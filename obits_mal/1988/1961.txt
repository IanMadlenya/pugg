        Richard A. Cawley, founder and president of the Rain Hill Group, Inc., a multinational investment concern based in New York, died in the crash of Pan American World Airways Flight 103 on Wednesday. He was 43 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Cawley, a graduate of the University of Virginia, was with Dean Witter, Clark Dodge and the First Boston Company before starting the Rain Hill Group.
        He is survived by his mother, Genevieve Walters of Manalapan, Fla., and two brothers, Leo N., of Manhattan and John C., of San Francisco.
