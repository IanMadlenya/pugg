        Bernard Lubar, an advertising executive, died of a heart attack Monday at his home in Calpe, Spain. He was 66 years old.
        A graduate of Brown University, Mr. Lubar was a vice president and creative director of McCann-Ericson Worldwide. From 1967 to 1976 he worked at his own agencies, which included Lubar, Stewart Inc.
        Surviving are three daughters, Judith, of Seattle, Deborah, of Northampton, Mass., and Cynthia, of Los Angeles and a sister, Rea Lubar-Duncan of Manhattan.
