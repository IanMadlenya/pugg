        George Gilbert, co-owner of the Raleigh Hotel in South Fallsburgh, N.Y., died of liver cancer Sunday at New York Hospital. He was 66 years old and lived in South Nyack, N.Y.
        Born in Manhattan, Mr. Gilbert graduated from Rutgers University. He was a co-founder of the Musical Theater Works Inc. in Manhattan and an honorary trustee of The Chemotherapy Foundation.
        He is survived by his sister, Hilda Herscher, of Miami.
