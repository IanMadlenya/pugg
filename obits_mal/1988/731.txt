        Morris Crystal, a former editor in chief of The Jewish Daily Forward, died of cardiac arrest yesterday at the Montefiore Medical Center. He was 80 years old and lived in the Bronx.
        Mr. Crystal was born in the Ukraine and came here in 1922. He began working at The Forward, the Yiddish-language newspaper, in 1943 and became editor in chief in 1969. He retired in 1970. Mr. Crystal was known for writing on culture and political commentary.
        Surviving are a son, Raphael, of Manhattan, and a daughter, Rachel Ehrenthal of Boston.
