        Dr. George J. Ginandes, a pediatrician and professor emeritus at Mount Sinai Medical Center, died of a heart attack Sunday at Mount Sinai. He was 84 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        From 1934 to 1980, he was in private practice in Manhattan. He taught at Mount Sinai from 1933 to 1975.
        He is survived by his wife, Dr. Janet; a son, Peter, of Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y.; a daughter, Dr. Carol, of Boston, and three grandchildren.
