        Carl Ingram, vice president of Kamco Supply Corporation, a Brooklyn-based supplier of building materials, died of cancer Monday at New York University Medical Center. He was 60 years old and lived in Manhattan and Fire Island, L.I.
        Mr. Ingram, who was born in New York City, graduated from Long Island University.
        Survivors are his wife, Sally; two sons, Stephen and Peter, and two brothers, Arthur, of Roslyn, L.I., and Morty, of Bayside, Queens.
