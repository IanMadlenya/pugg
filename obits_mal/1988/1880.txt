        John T. Burke, a longtime leader of teamster union locals in New York, died Saturday at his home in Allendale, N.J. He was 80 years old.
        For many years until his retirement in 1975, Mr. Burke was president of Locals 868 and 917 of the International Brotherhood of Teamsters, representing, among others, parking lot and gasoline station attendants, automobile salesmen and race track stablehands.
        Survivors include his wife, the former Janet Wheeler; a daughter, Barbara Kinzie of Smithtown, L.I.; a son, John Jr., of Secaucus, N.J., and two grandchildren.
