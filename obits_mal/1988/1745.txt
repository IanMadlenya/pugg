        Willard Bissell Pope, a college professor and editor, died Thursday at his home in Burlington, Vt., after a long illness. He was 85 years old.
        Professor Pope, a native of Detroit, came to the University of Vermont in 1942 and served as chairman of the English department before retiring in 1968. He edited the three-volume work ''The Diary of Benjamin Robert Haydon 1808-1824'' (Harvard University Press; 1963).
        He is survived by a son, Willard R. of Brookline, Mass., a daughter, Jane Bertoni of Burlington and Manhattan, and six grandchildren.
