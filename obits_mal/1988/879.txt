        Harold Gordon, vice president of business operations for Simon & Schuster Inc., died of cancer Tuesday at Englewood (N.J.) Hospital. He was 61 years old and lived in Fort Lee, N.J.
        Mr. Gordon joined the publisher in 1951 and was named a vice president in 1971. He was born in Brooklyn. Mr. Gordon received bachelor's and master's degrees from the City University.
        Surviving are his wife, Madeline, and a son, Scott.
