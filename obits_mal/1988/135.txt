        Robert U. Redpath Jr., an advisor to corporations for pension and estate planning, died Dec. 28 at his home in South Orange, N.J., after a long illness. He was 81 years old. Mr. Redpath graduated from Yale College and was associated with several life-insurance companies.
        He is survived by his wife, Nancy Shaw Miller Redpath; two sons, Robert 3d, of Beaconsfield, England, and William, of Lexington, Mass.; two daughters, Nancy M., of Rome, and Jean Becton of Weston, Mass.; two brothers, John, of Summit, N.J., and Frederick, of Upper Montclair, N.J., and four grandchildren.
