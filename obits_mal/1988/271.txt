        Francis J. Sorg, former chairman of the Sorg Printing Company, died Saturday at the Imperial Point Medical Center in Fort Lauderdale, Fla. He was 88 years old and lived in Fort Lauderdale.
        After 10 years as president of the Manhattan financial printing concern now called Sorg Inc., Mr. Sorg was its chairman from 1957 to 1960, when he retired.
        He is survived by two sons, Francis Jr., of Glen Cove, L.I., and Walter, of Washington; two daughters, Mignon Flipse of Albertson, L.I., and Beverly King of Oyster Bay, L.I.; a sister, Hermance Evans of Fort Lauderdale; 17 grandchildren, and 8 great grandchildren.
