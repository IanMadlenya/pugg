        Bishop Frederick Donaghy, a Roman Catholic missionary in China and Taiwan, died of cancer Friday at Maryknoll, the Catholic Foreign Mission Society of America's center in Ossining, N.Y. He was 85 years old.
        Born in New Bedford, Mass., he graduated from Holy Cross College and was ordained at Maryknoll in 1929, when he departed for China. He served there until he was expelled by the Government in 1955. He then served in Taiwan until last year.
        Bishop Donaghy is survived by his brother, Edward, of Citrus Heights, Calif.
