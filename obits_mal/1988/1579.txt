        Edward A. Terres, a retired vice president of States Marine Isthmian Lines, a steamship company, died of liver cancer last Saturday at Alta Bates Hospital in Berkeley, Calif. He was 78 years old and lived in Oakland, Calif.
        Mr. Terres joined States Marin in 1945 and retired in 1971. Ten years later he resumed work, as an assistant to the president of the Wall Shipping Company in Washington. He retired again in 1987.
        Surviving are his wife, Kate; his son, Todd of Oxnard, Calif.; two stepdaughters, Shaun Mulhern and Heather Ferguson, both of San Francisco; a brother, John of New Canaan, Conn., and two grandchildren.
