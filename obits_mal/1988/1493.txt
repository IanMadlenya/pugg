        S. Herbert Riesner, a real-estate executive and hotel owner, died of heart failure Wednesday at the New York Hospital-Cornell Medical Center. He was 85 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Riesner was part owner of a number of hotels and motels in New York and Florida. He headed a real-estate business and was a former president of the West Side Hotel Association.
        Surviving are a daughter, Catherine Franklin of Centerville, Mass.; a son, Michael, of Los Angeles; two stepsons, Richard and David Franklin of Atlanta, and three grandchildren.
