        Joe Carcione, who offered millions of consumers advice about produce as television's ''Green Grocer,'' died of cardiac arrest Tuesday at Peninsula Hospital. He was 73 years old and had been suffering from intestinal cancer.
        Mr. Carcione, whose trademark was his pronunciation of ''VEG-ee-tibles,'' began his career in his father's produce market in 1933. His newspaper columns, and television and radio spots helped him earn $500,000 a year. He was syndicated on about 80 television stations nationwide, including WWOR in Secaucus, N.J.
