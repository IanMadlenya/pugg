        William C. Brennan, a former advertising executive, died of cancer of the colon and liver Tuesday at Fairview Hospital in Great Barrington, Mass. He was 75 years old and lived in Great Barrington.
        Mr. Brennan worked for several New York advertising agencies, including Lennen & Newell; Needham, Harper & Steers; McCann-Erickson; and Norman, Craig & Kummel, of which he was a vice president.
        Mr. Brennan is survived by his wife, the former Jean Ford; a son, William Jr., of Westwood, Mass.; a brother, Francis, of Queens, and two grandchildren.
