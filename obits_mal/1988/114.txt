        Bruce J. Bergman, a former library director at Pace University, died last Sunday at the Yale-New Haven Medical Center. He was 55 years old and lived in New Haven.
        Mr. Bergman joined the Pace library in Manhattan in 1962 and became director in 1980. He retired last October. Mr. Bergman was a member of the Special Libraries Association, the New York Library Club and the American Library Association.
        Surviving are his parents, Robert and Alice, of Waupaca, Wis.; a sister, Katharine Ann Parello of Chicago, and a brother, Mark, of Madison, Wis.
