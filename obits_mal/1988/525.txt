        Harrison D. Horblit, a former textile executive, died of a heart attack Tuesday at Danbury (Conn.) Hospital. He was 75 years old and lived in Ridgefield, Conn.
        For 30 years, Mr. Horblit was president of Colonial Fabrics, a textile manufacturer in Manhattan. The company was closed when he retired in 1966. He was a graduate of Harvard College and Harvard Business School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Jean Mermin; a son, Richard of Denver; a daughter, Victoria Beth Gulker of Pound Ridge, N.Y., and two grandchildren.
