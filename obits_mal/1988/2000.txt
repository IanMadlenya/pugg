        Johnny Paychek, who once fought Joe Louis for the world heavyweight championship, died Saturday. He was 74 years old.
        Mr. Paychek began his boxing career on Chicago's South Side and won the Golden Gloves heavyweight title in 1933.
        By 1940, he earned a title bout against Louis, who knocked him out in the second round on March 29 at Madison Square Garden.
        In recent years, Mr. Paychek held several coaching jobs in Lyons Township, Ill.
