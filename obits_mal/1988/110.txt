        Saday Courville, who played second fiddle on the first known Cajun record in 1928 and was one of the last links with the ''pure'' sounds of the Acadian heritage, died of heart failure Sunday. He was 82 years old.
        Mr. Courville is survived by his wife, three children, six grandchildren and one great-grandchild.
