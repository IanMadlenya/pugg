        The Rev. Paul E. McKeever, professor of moral theology at St. John's University and a former editor of The Long Island Catholic, died of cardiac arrest Sunday at the Seminary of the Immaculate Conception in Huntington, L.I., where he lived. He was 64 years old.
        Father McKeever, ordained in the Brooklyn Diocese in 1948, was chairman of the theology department at St. John's from 1976 to 1981. He edited The Long Island Catholic, the weekly of the Rockville Centre Diocese, from 1967 to 1975.
        He is survived by a sister, Sister Edna.
