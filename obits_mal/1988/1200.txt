        Boyd Crumrine Patterson, former president of Washington & Jefferson College of Washington, Pa., died of a stroke Tuesday at his home in Clinton, N.Y. He was 86 years old.
        Dr. Patterson served as president from 1950 to 1970, a period in which the college's endowment grew from $2.3 million to nearly $11 million and a new student center and other buildings were erected.
        Dr. Patterson is survived by his wife, the former Mary Eleanor Dennison.
