        Wallis Bleecker Dunckel, the president of Bankers Trust Company from 1960 to 1966, died Thursday at Danbury (Conn.) Hospital after a brief illness. He was 86 years old and lived in Easton, Conn.
        A 1922 graduate of Yale, Mr. Dunckel joined Bankers Trust the following year and rose to vice president in 1944. After retiring from the bank, he was president of its holding company, the Bankers Trust New York Corporation until 1968.
        Mr. Dunckel is survived by his wife, the former Margaret Lindsay Sutherland; two sons, Peter and Nicholas, both of California, and a daughter, Margot, of Florida.
