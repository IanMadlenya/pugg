        Henry G. Kier, retired owner of Kier's Celebrity Photos, a shop on West 40th Street in Manhattan specializing in movie stills, died Friday at a nursing home in Lakewood, N.J., after a brief illness. He was 87 years old and lived in Queens.
        Mr. Kier, a native of Paterson, N.J., started his business in 1940, supplying fans with glossy 8-by-10's of their favorite stars. He retired in 1975.
        His wife, the former Evelyn Dolobovsky, died in 1981.
        He is survived by a daughter, Francine Berkowitz of Princeton Junction, N.J., and two grandchildren.
