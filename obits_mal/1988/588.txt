        Jean Gascon, a French-Canadian actor and director who was artistic director of the Stratford Festival in Ontario from 1969 to 1974, died Wednesday of heart failure. He was 67 years old.
        Mr. Gascon studied in France and began his acting career at Stratford in 1956. He was a former director of theater at the National Arts Center in Ottawa.
