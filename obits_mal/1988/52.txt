        John E. Bassett Sr., retired chairman of Bassett Furniture Industries Inc., died Thursday. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Bassett's father, Charles C. Bassett, was one of the founders of the Bassett Furniture Company, now the largest manufacturer of wood furniture in the world.
        John Bassett retired in 1981.
