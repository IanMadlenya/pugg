        Martin J. Walsh, an actor, died of acquired immune deficiency syndrome on July 7 at New York University Medical Center in Manhattan. He was 41 years old.
        Mr. Walsh appeared on Broadway in ''The Pirates of Penzance'' and ''Shenandoah'' and in many Off Broadway productions. He was a member of the Prince Street Players, a children's-theater company, in the 1970's.
        He is survived by his father, Vincent Walsh, of Fort Pierce, Fla.; a brother, Vincent Walsh Jr., of Auburn, Mass.; and three sisters, Sheila Shrewsberry, of Bowie, Md., Eileen Walsh, of Wabasso, Fla., and Joann Carlson, of Manchester, Conn.
