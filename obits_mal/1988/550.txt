        Alex Kelso Reddin, a business executive and consultant, died of cancer Thursday at his home in Norwalk, Conn. He was 72 years old.
        Mr. Reddin, a native of Australia, founded an executive consulting company, Alex Reddin Associates, in 1977. He operated the company in Norwalk and New York until his retirement last year.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elaine Greene; three daughters, Eve Lennon of Issaquah, Wash., Gillean Nelson of Wilton, Conn., and Dorothy Tetlica of Summerville, S.C.; a son, Ian of Norwalk, and nine grandchildren.
