        Robert H. Lehman, a retired investor and philanthropist, died of complications after heart surgery Wednesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 79 years old.
        He was a member of the board of the Jewish Child Care Association, of Manhattan, and was a trustee of its Pleasantville (N.Y.) Cottage School.
        He is survived by a son, Adam; a daughter, Deborah L. Conover, and two grandchildren, all of Manhattan.
