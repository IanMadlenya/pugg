        Maurice Richter, a former chairman of the pathology department at New York University Medical Center, died of bladder cancer Saturday at Friendship Village, a retirement center in Tempe, Ariz. He was 91 years old.
        Dr. Richter was born in Chicago. He was chairman of the pathology department from 1949 to 1951 and was a professor of pathology until he retired in 1962.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Brina Kessel; a sister, Marion, of St. Paul; three sons, Maurice Jr., of Albany, Marcel, of St. Paul, and Wayne, of North Oaks, Minn., and four grandchildren.
