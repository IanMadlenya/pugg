        Frank L. Harrington, retired chairman and president of the Paul Revere Life Insurance Company of Worcester, Mass., died Thursday at Worcester Memorial Hospital. He was 86 years old and lived in Worcester.
        Mr. Harrington, who retired in 1968, was a graduate and a former trustee of Dartmouth College.
        Survivors are his wife, Louise; three sons, Frank Jr., of Boston, Thomas, of Wilton, Conn., and George, of Framingham, Mass.; a daughter, Roxanna Stevenson of Fort Worth; two sisters, Lillia Molder of Worcester and Anna Boardman of Amherst, N.H.; 12 grandchildren, and 7 great-grandchildren.
