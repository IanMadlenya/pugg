        Sigfred L. Solem, a banker and former senior vice president of the Dime Savings Bank of New York, died of a heart attack Saturday at his home in East Williston, L.I. He was 67 years old.
        Mr. Solem, a graduate of Hofstra University, was with Dime for 43 years, retiring in 1983.
        He is survived by his wife, Gladys; two daughters, Karen Forman of Manhattan, and Deborah, of East Williston; two sons, Robert, of Madagascar, and Steven, of East Williston; two sisters, Esther Martin of Toms River, N.J., and Violet Belland of Hershey, Pa.
