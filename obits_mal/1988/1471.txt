
       Herbert W. Dates, former president and chief operating officer of Szabo Food Services Inc., died of cancer Friday at his home in Florham Park, N.J. He was 66 years old.  Mr. Dates was associated with the company, of Oak Brook, Ill., from 1972 to 1979.
        Herbert W. Dates, former president and chief operating officer of Szabo Food Services Inc., died of cancer Friday at his home in Florham Park, N.J. He was 66 years old.  Mr. Dates was associated with the company, of Oak Brook, Ill., from 1972 to 1979.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Doris Ryan; five daughters, Pamela Weinberger of Hinsdale, Ill., Penelope Sardelli of Glen Ridge, N.J., Joanne Olsen of Lafayette, Calif., Melissa Schmidt of Rome, N.Y., and Amy, of Mamaroneck, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
