        John B. Bridgwood, a retired executive vice president of Chase Manhattan Bank, died of congestive heart failure Thursday at Physicians Hospital in Jackson Heights, Queens. He was 84 years old and lived in Jackson Heights.
        Mr. Bridgwood retired in 1968 after working for Chase for 47 years. He also served as a financial consultant to Columbia University.
        He is survived by his wife, Bertha; a daughter, Carol, of Chatham, N.J., and two grandchildren.
