        Vincent H. Rendich, an importer of men's clothing, died of a heart attack Sunday at the United Hospital Medical Center in Portchester, N.Y. He was 85 years old and lived in Rye, N.Y.
        Mr. Rendich was president of Classic Imports, which he operated from his home beginning in 1968. He had been vice president of Valmeline Imports and president of Dawson Imports, clothing importers in Manhattan.
        Surviving are his wife, Victoria; a son, Jerome, of London; two daughters, Rikki Samuels of Piermont, N.Y., and Virginia Hastings of Barre, Mass., and six grandchildren.
