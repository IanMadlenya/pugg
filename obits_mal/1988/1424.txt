        Douglas Huff Jr., who resigned his seat in the Illinois House of Representatives two weeks ago after being convicted of Federal tax evasion and admitting a cocaine habit, died Thursday. He was 57 years old.
        Mr. Huff suffered a stroke at his home Sept. 16, nine days after resigning, said his secretary, Elizabeth Mallard. He had been scheduled to begin his four-year prison term Oct. 18, she said.
