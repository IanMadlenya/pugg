        George R. Hickey, who retired as commodore of the Farrell Lines shipping company in 1968, died Tuesday at the Dell Manor Nursing Home in Rockland, Mass., where he lived. He was 84 years old.
        Commodore Hickey, a graduate of the Massachusetts Maritime Academy, began sailing for Farrell in 1924 as a third mate. He served in the Navy in World War II with the rank of commander.
        He is survivedby a brother, Franklin, of Rockland, and a sister, Kathryn Flint of Deer Isle, Me.
