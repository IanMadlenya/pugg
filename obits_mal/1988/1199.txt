        Abraham Pereira, a Wall Street stockbroker, died of cancer yesterday at Calvary Hospital in the Bronx. He was 76 years old and lived in the Bronx.
        Mr. Pereira was a member of the New York Stock Exchange and a partner in J. Streicher & Company.
        He was president of the Educational Jewish Center, which is in the Bronx, for 25 years and a member of many groups, including the Federation of Jewish Philanthropies and the American Jewish Congress.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Esther Clusky; a son, Dr. Benjamin, of Fort Lee, N.J.; a daughter, Sheila Charmak of Tappan, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
