        Andrew W. L. Brown, a retired official of the United Automobile Workers, died of heart failure Aug. 16 at St. John Hospital in Detroit. He was 73 years old and lived in Grosse Pointe Park.
        Mr. Brown, a former director of the union's Community Services Department, was an early advocate of affordable child-care services and substance-abuse programs for auto workers.
