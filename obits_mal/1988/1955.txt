        George A. Brooks, a retired executive of the General Motors Corporation, died of heart failure yesterday at Lawrence Hospital in Bronxville, N.Y. He was 88 years old and lived in Scarsdale.
        Mr. Brooks joined General Motors' legal department in 1934 and rose to become its head. He later became secretary of the corporation. He retired in 1965.
        Mr. Brooks was born in Manhattan and graduated from Fordham University and its law school. He earned a master's degree in law from New York University.
        He was a former president of the Westchester Legal Aid Society and Lavalle School for the Blind. Mr. Brooks was a former trustee of Fordham.
        Surviving are his brother, the Rev. Edwin Brooks, of Manhattan, and his sister, Dorothy Brooks, of Scarsdale.
