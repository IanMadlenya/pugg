        Dr. Frank Cifarelli, a thoracic and vascular surgeon, died of respiratory arrest Tuesday at Mary Immaculate Hospital in Queens. He was 70 years old and lived in Floral Park, L.I.
        Dr. Cifarelli was chief of vascular surgery at Methodist Hospital in Brooklyn and a clinical professor at Downstate Medical Center.
        He is survived by two brothers, Jerome, of East Williston, L.I., and Joseph, of Massapequa, L.I., and three sisters, Mary Brocco of Albrightsville, Pa., Margaret Cifarelli of Mineola, L.I., and Helen Inserra of New Hyde Park, L.I.
