        Donald K. Phillips, an investment broker, died of heart failure yesterday at his home in Dothan, Ala. He was 84 years old.
        Mr. Phillips spent his entire career with E. F. Hutton, the brokerage firm, starting as a telegrapher in 1925 and retiring 40 years later as an executive vice president and a member of the board of directors. He had served as the principal broker for John Paul Getty and helped in the structuring of Getty Oil Company.
        Mr. Phillips is survived by his wife, Jean Allen Phillips; a daughter, Donna Adams, and two grandchildren, all of Dothan.
