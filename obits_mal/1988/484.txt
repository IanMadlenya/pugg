        James Peter Davis, Archbishop of Santa Fe from 1964 to 1974, died Friday. He was 83 years old.
        Archbishop Davis headed the Roman Catholic archdiocese after serving in Puerto Rico, where in 1960 he was ordained the first Archbishop of San Juan in 1960. He retired in 1974.
