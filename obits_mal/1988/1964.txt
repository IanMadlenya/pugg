        Dr. Irving Sigal, senior director of molecular biology at the Merck Sharp & Dohme Research Laboratories in Rahway, N.J., was killed in the crash of Pan American World Airways Flight 103 on Wednesday. He was 35 years old and lived in Pennington, N.J.
        He is survived by his wife, Catherine Teague Sigal; three brothers, David and Harold, both of New York, and Elliott, of San Francisco, and a sister, Katherine Sigal Rhodes, of Ann Arbor, Mich.
