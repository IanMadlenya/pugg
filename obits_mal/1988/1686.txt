        David Finkle, a private investor, died Tuesday at his home in Palm Beach, Fla., after a long illness. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Finkle was associated with Bear, Stearns & Company, a New York investment bank, from 1939 to 1957. In 1957 he became a private investor. From 1955 to 1980, he was a director of Northwest Industries.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Nettie Spiro; a sister, Sylvia Bennenson of Silver Springs, Md.; a son, Marc, of Aspen, Colo.; two daughters, Ann Miller of Aspen, and Patricia Klein of Zionsville, Pa.; 14 grandchildren, and 8 great-grandchildren.
