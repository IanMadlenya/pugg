        Willis Grandy Lipscomb, a former airline executive, died of cardiac arrest Friday at his home in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Lipscomb was a senior vice president and a director of Pan American World Airways at his retirement in 1967. He had been general traffic manager for American Airlines, before joining Pan Am in 1942. He was appointed to the board of directors in 1960.
        He is survived by two daughters, Ann L. Nolte and Gene L. Gardner, both of Glen Cove; six grandchildren, and four great-grandchildren.
