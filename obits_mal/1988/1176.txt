        Dr. Anthony Lewis Davidson, a surgeon, researcher and educator, died of colon cancer Sunday at his home in Brooklyn. He was 43 years old.
        Dr. Davidson, who received his medical degree from Bologna Medical School in Italy, became affiliated with Maimonides Medical Center in Brooklyn in 1972 and was assistant director of surgery there since 1980. He was also a clinical instructor in surgery at Coney Island Hospital.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara Degenshein Davidson; three sons, Erik, Jeffrey and Gregory; his parents, Jack and Mildred Davidson, and a brother, Robert, all of Brooklyn.
