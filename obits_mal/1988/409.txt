        Carl Hildebrand, former president of Sandvik Steel Inc. of Fairlawn, N.J., died at his Manhattan home Monday after a long illness. He was 91 years old.
        Born in Sweden, he immigrated to the United States in 1922 and founded a steel company, which he merged with Sandvik in 1938. Until he retired in 1962 he was Sandvik's president. Then he headed the Swedish Chamber of Commerce of the United States for five years.
        Survivors are his wife, Frances; two daughters, Mary Ann Lewis of New Canaan, Conn., and Karin Mills of Oakville, Ontario; 12 grandchildren, and 8 great-grandchildren.
