        Harry Reichenbaum, a lawyer who specialized in negligence and medical malpractice cases, died of a heart attack Monday at Jacobi Hospital in the Bronx. He was 63 years old and lived in Roslyn, L.I.
        Mr. Reichenbaum, a native of the Bronx, was a partner in Reichenbaum & Silberstein, a law firm with offices in Great Neck, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Eleanor Heiden; his mother, Ida Krasko of the Bronx; three sons, Mark, of Manhattan, David, of Manhattan, Eric, of Queens, and a sister, Sylvia Conrad of Carle Place, L.I.
