        The Rev. Ralph M. Houston, who served on the judicial council of the United Methodist Church for two decades, died of a heart attack Saturday at his home in Mount Tremper, N.Y. He was 84 years old.
        Mr. Houston was elected to the judicial body of the church five times, between 1950 and 1970, when he retired. He was a minister in parishes including Manhattan and Hempstead, L.I.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Margaret Cloke; a sister, Ruth Joor of Houston; a daughter, Susan M., of Manhattan; a son, David C., of Woodstock, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
