        Alfred Harvey Wheeler, a social studies teacher in the New York City public school system for 42 years, died yesterday at his home in Huntington, L.I., after a long illness. He was 83 years old.
        Mr. Wheeler was the author of several history textbooks used in public high schools and also taught history for many years at Clark and New York Universities.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Eleanor Lisenbein; three daughters, Nancy Graham of Manhattan, Sara Brush of Port Jefferson, L.I., and Elizabeth De Lucia of Kendall Park, N.J.; six grandchildren, and one great-grandchild.
