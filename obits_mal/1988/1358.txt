        Richard F. Sparks, professor of criminology at Rutgers University in Newark, died of heart failure on July 30 at his home in Manhattan. He was 54 years old.
        Professor Sparks was born in Ironton, Ohio, and received a bachelor's degree from Northwestern University and a doctorate from Cambridge. He joined the Rutgers faculty in 1974. He conducted studies of violent crime, crimes against businesses and crime victims.
        He is survived by a daughter, Samantha, of Washington; a son, Richard Jr., of Manhattan, and a sister, Julia, of San Luis Obispo, Calif.
