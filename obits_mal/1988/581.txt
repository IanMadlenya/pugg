        Matthew Blei, a lawyer and former village justice in Great Neck, L.I., died of cancer Tuesday at his home in Hollywood, Fla. He was 81 years old.
        Mr. Blei practiced law in his firm, Muhlstock & Blei, until 1981, when he moved to Florida.
        He served on the New York State Quarterhorse Racing Commission from 1970 to 1974 and was a founder and past president of Temple Emanu-El in Great Neck.
        He is survived by his wife, Ruth; two daughters, Susan Solow of Aberdeen, N.J., and Joan Shires of North Miami Beach; a brother, Nat, of Wayne, N.J., and five grandchildren.
