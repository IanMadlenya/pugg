        Jerome B. Lurie, a Manhattan labor lawyer, died of cancer yesterday at St. Luke's-Roosevelt Hospital Center in Manhattan. He was 71 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Lurie, who was born in Manhattan, earned a bachelor's degree at City College of New York and a law degree at New York University. Among the unions he represented were Actors Equity and Writers Guild East.
        He is survived by a sister, Ruth Glickstein of Hollywood, Fla.; four nephews and one niece.
