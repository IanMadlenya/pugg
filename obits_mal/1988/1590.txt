        Seymour R. Askin, a former president of Askin Stores Inc., a 65-unit chain of clothing stores, mostly in the South and Southwest, died of heart failure Tuesday at his home in Manhattan. He was 90 years old.  Mr. Askin, whose hometown was Rochester, had been a vice president of the Central Synagogue, on Lexington Avenue at 55th Street.
        Surviving are his wife, the former Ruth Bachrach Oppenheim; a son, Seymour Jr., of Greenwich, Conn.; a daughter, Susan A. Wolman of Baltimore, and five grandchildren.
