        John F. Finn Jr., a retired lawyer and former judge, died of leukemia Monday at Community Hospital in Glen Cove, L.I. He was 83 years old and lived in Sea Cliff, L.I.
        Mr. Finn, who served briefly as a City Court judge in Glen Cove, was a retired partner of the Manhattan law firm of Fried, Frank, Harris, Shriver & Jacobson. He joined the firm in 1943 and retired in 1977.
        He is survived by his wife, Catherine; two sons, John, of Atlanta, and Terence, of Bethesda, Md.; a sister, Katherine, of Queens, and four grandchildren.
