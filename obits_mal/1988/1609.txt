        John M. Rochford, a corporate lawyer, died Sunday at his home in Garden City, L.I., after a long illness. He was 66 years old.
        Mr. Rochford was an executive with various companies in the Royal Dutch Petroleum Group for nearly 30 years. At his retirement in 1984, he was a consultant to Transworld Oil America Inc.
        He held degrees from Columbia University Graduate School of Business and Columbia University Law School.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Alyce Maloney; a son, Michael, of Garden City; a brother, Dr. Emeric J., of Syosset, L.I., and a sister, Kathleen Hempstead, of East Meadow, L.I.
