        Marion Holmes Hartshorne, the Harry Emerson Fosdick professor emeritus of philosophy and religion at Colgate University in Hamilton, N.Y., died of a malignant brain tumor Tuesday at Community Memorial Hospital in Hamilton. He was 78 years old and lived in Hamilton.
        Surviving are his wife, Ruth; four sons, Jonathan, of Albuquerque, N.M., Richard, of East Sullivan, N.H., Timothy, of Wichita, Kan., and William Becker, of Lewisburg, Pa.; a daughter, Sarah Sucre of Albuquerque, and 10 grandchildren.
