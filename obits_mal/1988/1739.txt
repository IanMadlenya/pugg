        James D. Madigan 3d, a lawyer who specialized in labor law and taught at the Brooklyn Law School, died of Hodgkin's disease yesterday at the Memorial Sloan-Kettering Cancer Center. He was 37 years old and lived in Garden City, L.I.
        Mr. Madigan, a graduate of Syracuse University and the Brooklyn Law School, was a partner at the firm of Morgan, Lewis & Bockius and had represented The New York Times Company in labor law matters.
        He is survived by his wife, Patricia, and three children, Marisa, Daniel and Kelly.
