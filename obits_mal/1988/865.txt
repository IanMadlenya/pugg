        B. Cobbey Crisler, a Biblical scholar, educator and author, died of a heart attack on April 25 at his home in Carmel Valley, Calif. He was 55 years old.
        Mr. Crisler lectured internationally on his ''Search the Scriptures'' series. He was also president of the Daycroft School in Greenwich, Conn.
        He is survived by his wife, Janet Vaughn Crisler, and his brother, Timothy E. R. Crisler, both of Carmel Valley.
