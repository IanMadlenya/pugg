        Albert Fink Milton, a former financial executive in New York City, died last Friday at his home in Washington, Conn. He was 90 years old.
        In the early 1930's, Mr. Milton joined the Equity Corporation, an early conglomerate with interests in manufacturing and financial services. He served as an officer and director until his retirement in 1962. His brother, David M. Milton, was the corporation's founder and chairman.
        Mr. Milton is survived by a son, A. Fenner, of Washington, a sister, Ellen Hunt Milton of Seal Harbor, Me., and a granddaughter.
