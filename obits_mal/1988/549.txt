        William A. Gerard, a senior vice president at the Bank of New York, died Monday in an accident at his home in Stamford, Conn. He was 47 years old.
        Before joining the Bank of New York, Mr. Gerard was an officer with Chemical Bank, with Citibank and with the Securities Industry Automation Corporation.
        Mr. Gerard graduated in 1962 from the University of Tulsa and from the Wharton School of Finance in 1964.
        He is survived by his wife, Janece; two sons, William and Brian, and a daughter Melissa, all of Stamford, and his mother, Beryl A. Gerard, of Tulsa, Okla.
