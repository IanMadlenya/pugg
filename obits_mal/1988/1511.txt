        Leonard B. Goldman, former chief of radiology at Queens General Hospital, died of colon cancer Sunday at his home in Queens. He was 81 years old.
        Dr. Goldman established a radiation therapy department at the hospital in 1950. He also established and directed the hospital's free cancer-detection and prevention clinic and was the author of ''There is An Answer to Cancer,'' a book published by Harper & Bros. in 1959.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Blanche Goldman; two daughters, Geri Reichgut of Sea Cliff, L.I., and Alice Goldman of Queens; a son, Daniel, also of Queens, and a grandson.
