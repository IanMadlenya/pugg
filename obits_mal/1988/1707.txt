        Arminius N. Hayne, a musician who was believed to have been the oldest member of Local 802 of the American Federation of Musicians, died of heart disease last Thursday in Brooklyn Hospital. He was 102 years old and lived in the Clinton Hill section of Brooklyn.
        John Glasel, the president of Local 802, said Monday that he believed the local had no member older than Mr. Hayne. Mr. Hayne was a trombonist with the Brooklyn-based Monarch Symphonic Band of the Benevolent and Protective Order of Elks.
        A funeral service is to be held tomorrow at 10 A.M. at the Church of St. Luke and St. Matthew, at 520 Clinton Avenue, between Fulton Street and Atlantic Avenue in the Bedford-Stuyvesant section of Brooklyn.
