        Salvatore Ruggiero, a retired New Jersey Superior Court judge, died of a stroke Sunday at Chilton Memorial Hospital in Pequannock, N.J. He was 74 years old and lived in Wayne, N.J.
        Judge Ruggiero, who handled matrimonial cases in Passaic County, was a strong advocate of the rights of children in divorce cases. He had served as town attorney in Wayne Township and counsel to the Board of Education there.
        He is survived by two sons, Laurence J., of Sarasota, Fla., and Leonard F., of South Londonderry, Vt., and a daughter, Marianne Farina of Manhattan.
