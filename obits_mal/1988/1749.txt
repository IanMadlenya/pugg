        Robert M. Maynard, a retired partner in Price Waterhouse, died of a heart attack on Monday at his home in Millburn, N.J. He was 65 years old.
        He had been with the accounting firm from 1948 until 1983, when he retired. He was born in Harris, Iowa, and graduated from Ohio State University.
        Mr. Maynard was chairman of Millburn's Citizens Budget Committee. He was also a trustee of the United Way of Millburn-Short Hills and a former president of the Floating Hospital in New York.
        He is survived by his wife, Jean.
