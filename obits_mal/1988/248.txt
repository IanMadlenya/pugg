        Laszlo G. Versenyi, a philosophy professor at Williams College, died Tuesday of cancer at his home in Williamstown. He was 59 years old.
        Professor Versenyi, who began teaching at Williams in 1958, received many academic awards, including a senior fellowship from the National Endowment for the Humanities in 1970 and grants from the American Council of Learned Societies.
        He wrote numerous articles and four books on philosophy and ancient Greek thought. In addition to Greek philosophy, he taught courses on the German existentialist Martin Heidegger.
