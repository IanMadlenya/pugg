        Irving A. Greene, a Wall Street executive who worked in the securities industry for 75 years, died Tuesday in Manhattan after a brief illness. He was 89 years old and lived in Manhattan.
        Mr. Greene was a partner and senior adviser of David J. Greene and Company, a member of the New York Stock Exchange. The firm was founded by Mr. Greene's late brother, David.
        Mr. Greene is survived by a daughter, D'Vera Topol, of Mamaroneck, N.Y.; three grandchildren and one great-grandson.
