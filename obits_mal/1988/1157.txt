        Dr. Sterling A. Callisen, an art historian and former president of the Parsons School of Design, died of pneumonia last Saturday at his home in Andover, Mass. He was 89 years old.
        Dr. Callisen was president of Parsons from 1959 to 1963. For 10 years before that, he was dean of education at the Metropolitan Museum of Art. He was also a professor of art at the University of Rochester and an associate dean at Wesleyan University and taught at the Riverdale Country School.
        Surviving are a daughter, Josephine Callisen Bresnahan of Cambridge, Mass., and two grandchildren.
