        Dr. Herbert P. MacNeal, a surgeon who was a founder of the Council of Scottish Clans Association, died of a heart attack Monday at Morristown (N.J.) Memorial Hospital. He was 79 years old and lived in Convent Station, N.J.
        Dr. MacNeal was a recipient of the Ellis Island Medal of Honor in 1986. He was president emeritus of the Clan Macneil Association of America.
        Surviving are his wife, Ethel; a brother, Dr. Perry MacNeal of Moorestown, N.J., and a sister, Ruth Gring of Northport, L.I.
