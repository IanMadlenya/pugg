        Isaac L. Asofsky, retired director of the Hebrew Immigrant Aid Society, died at his home in Manhattan Jan. 18 after a short illness. He was 98 years old.
        Mr. Asofsky began working for the Society, which helps Jews come to this country, in 1912.
        He became general manager in 1922 and director during World War II, when the organization helped thousands of European Jews come to this country. He retired in 1952.
        He is survived by a daughter, Helen Ashley of Manhattan.
