        Hermann Cardinal Volk, the former Roman Catholic Bishop of Mainz and an advocate of ecumenism, died today after a lengthy illness, his press office said. He was 84 years old.
        He served as Bishop of Mainz from 1962-82 and was known for his efforts to build ties between the Catholic and Protestant churches. Pope Paul VI named him a cardinal in 1973.
        Bishop Volk was born Dec. 27, 1903.
