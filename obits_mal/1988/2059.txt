        Lewis R. Stewart, a former dentist whose patients included President Herbert Hoover and Supreme Court Justice John Harlan, died of a heart attack Dec. 2 at his home in Pinehurst, N.C. He was 90 years old.
        Dr. Stewart was in practice for 47 years at 730 Fifth Avenue in Manhattan. He retired in 1971.
        He was born in Lyons, N.Y., and received his Doctor of Dental Science degree from the University of Buffalo in 1923.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Barbara Field.
