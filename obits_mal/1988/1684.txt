        William P. Connors, a fashion photographer, died of cancer Sept. 29 at his home in the Coconut Grove area of Miami. He was 59 years old.
        Mr. Connors worked in Europe and the United States for such fashion magazines as Vogue, Elle and Harper's Bazaar. He was born in Newark.  Surviving are his son, Christian, of Miami; his stepbrother, Edward Asmuth of Cincinatti, and his mother, Eleanor Connors-Asmuth of Spring Lake, N.J.
