        Jean Sareil, a professor of French at Columbia University for 30 years, died last Thursday at White Plains Hospital after a long illness. He was 71 years old and lived in Scarsdale, N.Y.
        Dr. Sareil was a leading scholar of Voltaire and wrote 27 books, ranging from scholarly works on French literature to mystery novels. He also wrote several textbooks with his wife, Jacqueline Sareil, a professor of French at Manhattanville College.
        He graduated from the University of Paris and held a Ph.D. from Columbia.
        In addition to his wife, he is survived by a son, Philippe, of Scarsdale.
