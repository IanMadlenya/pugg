        Al Holbert, whose 63 victories ranked him first among International Motor Sports Association drivers, was killed when his twin-engine airplane crashed shortly after takeoff from a Columbus airport Friday night, officials said. He was 41 years old.
        Mr. Holbert had just left Don Scott Field at Ohio State University when his four-seat Smith Arrowstar nosed into an open field and burst into flames, Sgt. Floyd Golden of the State Highway Patrol said.
