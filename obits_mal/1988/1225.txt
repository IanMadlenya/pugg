        John R. May, the executive director of the San Francisco Foundation from 1948 to 1973, died of a heart attack July 13 while vacationing near Ketchum, Idaho. He was 79 years old and lived in San Rafael, Calif, When Mr. May was appointed the first director of the San Francisco Foundation in 1948, the philanthropy had assets of less than $1 million. By the time he retired, it had $50 million in assets and was dispensing its grants to more than 300 organizations in the San Francisco area.
        He is survived by his wife,the former Jean Bolton of San Rafael, Calif., and a brother, Henry, of Berkeley.
