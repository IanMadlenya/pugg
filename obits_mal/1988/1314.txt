        Arthur Albert Alexander, a former executive with American President Lines, Ltd., worldwide shipping operators, died Wednesday at Eastern Long Island Hospital in Greenport, after a brief illness. He was 86 years old and lived on Shelter Island.
        Mr. Alexander was associated with the company for 45 years and was vice president for its East Coast operations after World War II until he retired in 1967. His career began with the Dollar Line.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Mary Scott; two sons, Albert Jr. of Lima, Peru, and Scott of Kingston, N.Y., and six grandchildren.
