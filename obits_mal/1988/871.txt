        T. Ward Cleary, a lawyer and educator, died of a heart attack on Tuesday at his home in Stamford, Conn. He was 71 years old.
        Mr. Cleary was a senior partner in the law firm Curtis, Brinkerhoff & Barrett and a retired senior officer of the Fidelity Trust Company. He was a graduate of Fordham University School of Law and was an associate professor there.
        Surviving are his wife, Margaret Barrett Cleary, three sons, Ward, of Wilton; Frank, of Fairfield and Denis, of Manhattan; and a granddaughter.
