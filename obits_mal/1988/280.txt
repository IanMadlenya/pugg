        Warren F. Savage, professor emeritus of materials engineering at Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute, died of cardiac arrest on Jan. 24 in Reston, Va. He was 65 years old and lived in Averill Park, N.Y.
        Dr. Savage, a native of Harvard, Mass., was on the Rensselaer faculty from 1948 until he retired in 1984. He served as a member of a joint United States-Soviet commission on welding and special electrometallurgy and was an adviser and consultant to several Government agencies.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Helen Lambert; a daughter, Sharon Mardon of Troy, N.Y.; a son, Donald, of West Sand Lake, N.Y., and four grandchildren.
