        Joseph P. Maggio, who owned a design firm bearing his name in Manhattan, died of a heart attack last Saturday at his home in South Egremont, Mass. He was 61 years old.
        Mr. Maggio, who also had a home in Brooklyn Heights, was a professor at the New York School of Interior Design for 17 years. His company specialized in interiors for international businesses. Earlier this year, he had opened a branch office in London.
        He is survived by his wife, Patricia, of South Egremont; a son, Joseph Jr., and a daughter, Christina Sperduto, both of Brooklyn Heights, and two granddaughters.
