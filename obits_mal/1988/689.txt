        Former Representative John H. Dent, a 10-term Democrat from western Pennsylvania, died Saturday. He was 80 years old.
        Mr. Dent was first elected in 1958 from the 21st Congressional District. He retired in 1978. He was a member of House Education and Labor Committee and chairman of the Select Subcommittee on Labor.
        Before being elected to Congress, Mr. Dent served in the state House and Senate.
