        Robert T. Wieringa, a business executive and former president of the Warner-Lambert Company, died, apparently of a heart attack, on Saturday at his home on Hilton Head Island, S.C. He was 65 years old.
        Mr. Wieringa held various positions at Warner-Lambert from 1968 until his retirement in 1979, including president and chief administrative officer. From 1948 to 1968, he was an executive with the Gillette Company.
        He is survived by his wife, Darlene, of Hilton Head Island; a son, Robert Jr., of Lebanon, Pa.; a daughter, Lisa Hartman, of Port Orange, Fla., and two grandchildren.
