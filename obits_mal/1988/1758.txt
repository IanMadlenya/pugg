        Milton P. Rasmussen, a retired vice president of Parade magazine, died of prostate cancer Oct. 17 at his home in Deerfield, Ill. He was 78 years old.
        He worked at Parade for 23 years until his retirement in 1976. After that he continued as a consultant. Before joining Parade, Mr. Rasmussen worked for newspaper advertising representative groups.
        Surviving are his wife, Hope; a son, John, of Chicago, and a daughter, Claire Frank, of Glenview, Ill., and six grandchildren.
