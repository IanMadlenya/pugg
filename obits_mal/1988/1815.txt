        Edmund J. McCormick, an executive and philanthropist, died of kidney failure Sunday at Dobbs Ferry (N.Y.) Hospital. He was 76 years old and lived in Dobbs Ferry.
        Mr. McCormick was chairman of McCormick & Company, consulting engineers in Tarrytown, N.Y., and the McCormick Organization, a real-estate investment and management company.
        He was chairman of the New York Business Advisory Committee and was a member of the Hudson River Valley Commission. He was also a director of the Westchester County Y.M.C.A.
        Surviving are his wife, Suzanne; three sons, Edmund, of Sparta, N.J., Dennis, of Wellesley, Mass., and David, of Wyckoff, N.J.; two daughters, Ann Ritter of Marston Mills, Mass., and Laurie McKeever of North Ridge, Calif.; a sister, Helen Leaver of Bogota, N.J.; a brother, William, of Boston, and four grandchildren.
