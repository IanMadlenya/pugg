        Murdock M. Rand, a retired vice president of the Union Carbide Corporation, died of cancer Tuesday in Norwalk (Conn.) Hospital. He was 63 years old and lived in New Canaan, Conn.
        Mr. Rand worked for Union Carbide for 39 years before retiring at the beginning of this year.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Shirley Belnap; two daughters, Barbara Dailey of Pittsford, N.Y., and Paula Cornwall of Woodbury, Conn.; a sister, Nancy Osgood of Alma, Mich., and three grandsons.
