        Ira L. Cahn, a newspaper publisher and former officer of the New York Press Association, died of complications of diabetes Oct. 15 at Martin Memorial Hospital in Stuart, Fla. He was 69 years old and lived in Palm City, Fla.
        For almost 40 years, Mr. Cahn published weekly newspapers on Long Island. He founded The Levittown Eagle, The Massapequa Post and The Amityville Record.
        He is survived by his wife, Barbara; two sons, David, of Upper Marlboro, Md., and Michael, of Cumberland, R.I.; a sister, Joan Seide of Croton-on-Hudson, N.Y., and five grandchildren.
