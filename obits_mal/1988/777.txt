        Emmanuel Paul, a jazz saxophonist who played with the Preservation Hall Band for years, died Monday. He was 84 years old.
        Mr. Paul originally played banjo with jazz bands, but gained his reputation playing tenor saxophone. Before joining the Preservation Hall Band, he played with Kid Thomas and the Algier Stompers and with Dejan's Olympia Brass Band.
        He is survived by his wife, a son, a daughter and five grandchildren.
