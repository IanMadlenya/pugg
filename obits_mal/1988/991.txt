        James H. Gildea, a Democrat who represented Pennsylvania in the House of Representatives from 1935 to 1939, died of cardiac arrest Sunday. He was 97 years old.
        Mr. Gildea, a native of Coaldale, Pa., founded The Coaldale Observer, a weekly newspaper, and The Schuylkill Legal Record, a law publication.
        Seeking a third term in the House, he was defeated by a Republican opponent in 1938, and lost House races in 1940 and 1950.
