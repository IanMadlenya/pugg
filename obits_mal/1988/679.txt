        J. Hunter Walton, chairman emeritus of Hunter Walton & Company, a dairy merchant in New York, died of an aneurysm Thursday at his home in Montclair, N.J. He was 72 years old.
        Born in Brooklyn Heights, he joined the family business, which was founded in 1827, and remained in it until he retired in 1981.
        Surviving are his wife, Jean Berry Walton; two sons, J. Hunter Jr. and Bruce, both of Boston; three sisters, Pauline Fairman of Princeton, N.J., Nancy, of Ellsworth, Me., and Elizabeth, of Brooklyn, Me., and seven grandchildren.
