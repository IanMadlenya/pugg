        Arthur J. Raporte, a former vice president in charge of real estate for the Loews Corporation, died of respiratory failure Wednesday at his home in Rye, N.Y. He was 70 years old.
        A 1941 graduate of Harvard Law School, Mr. Raporte was counsel and a director of the Welch Grape Fruit Company for 10 years, until 1958. He joined Loews two years later and retired in 1982.
        Mr. Raporte is survived by a daughter, Stephanie Lindquist of Rochester; a son, Arthur J. Jr., of Washington Crossing, Pa.; a brother, Robert Rapport of Lake Worth, Fla., and four grandchildren.
