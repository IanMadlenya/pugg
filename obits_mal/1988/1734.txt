        James W. Kynes, a lawyer, business executive and former Attorney General of Florida, died of stomach cancer Thursday at his home in Tampa. He was 60 years old.
        After serving a year as Attorney General of Florida in 1964 he became vice president of Jim Walter Corporation and was executive vice president, secretary and general counsel of its successor companies, Hillsborough Holding Corporation and Walter Industries, Inc.
        He is survived by his wife, Marjorie Hiatt Kynes, of Tampa,; three sons, James, of Tampa; William, of Annandale, Va.; and John, of Washington; a sister, Kay Copeland, of Atlanta; and six grandchildren.
