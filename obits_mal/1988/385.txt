        Roland Drew, an actor who quit Hollywood in the 1940's for a career in dressmaking, died Thursday at his home. He was 87 years old.
        Mr. Drew's roles included that of Prince Baron of Vulcan in the ''Flash Gordon'' serials of the 1930's. As a romantic leading man, he co-starred with Dolores del Rio in the silent film ''Ramona.''
        Mr. Drew married Dorothy Dearing, a former Busby Berkeley dancer, in 1946. She died in 1965.
        He is survived by a son, Damon, and two grandsons.
