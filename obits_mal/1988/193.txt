        Dr. Leon Menaker, a charter member of the Lincoln Institute for Psychotherapy in Manhattan, died of liver cancer Friday at his home in Manhattan. He was 72 years old.
        He worked as a supervising psychologist at the Lincoln Institute, a private clinic, until last fall, while maintaining a private practice.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Olga San Giuliano; a daughter, Drusilla Menaker of Jersey City; and two brothers, Harry Menaker of Roslyn, L.I., and Ruby Menaker of Arlington, Va.
