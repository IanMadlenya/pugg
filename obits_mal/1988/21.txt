        William H. H. Wilkinson, a former administrator of the New York State Department of Labor, died of prostate cancer Jan. 10 at Beth Abraham Hospital in the Bronx. He was 84 years old and lived in Hartsdale, N.Y.
        Mr. Wilkinson, a native of Washington, received an engineering degree from Syracuse University. After a long career with the state, he retired in 1959.
        Surviving are his wife, Dr. Rachel Diggs Wilkinson; a son, William Jr., of Mankato, Minn., and a sister, Ethel, of Forestville, Md.
