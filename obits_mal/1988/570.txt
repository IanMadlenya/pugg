        Haruo Shikanai, chairman of the Fuji Sankei Communications Group, died of liver and kidney failure on Saturday. He was 42 years old.
        Mr. Shikanai joined Nippon Broadcasting in 1970. He became vice president of the radio company and managing director of Sankei Shimbun, a nationally circulated daily, in 1977.
