        Irving Jonathan Estrin, a retired doctor who specialized in physical rehabilitation, died of a heart attack Thursday at his home in New Rochelle, N.Y. He was 72 years old.
        Dr. Estrin joined Montefiore Hospital in 1944, founding an arthritis clinic there in 1959. He was also associated with the Albert Einstein College of Medicine. He retired in 1985.
        He is survived by his wife, the former Elizabeth Glogau; two daughters, Jennifer, of New Rochelle, and Judith, of Redwood City, Calif.; two sons, Jonathan, of Venice, Calif., and James, of Yonkers, and a grandson.
