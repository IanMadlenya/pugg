        Jaromir Vejvoda, the songwriter of the ''Beer Barrel Polka,'' died at his home near Prague on Sunday, the official C.T.K. press agency reported today. He was 86 years old.
        Mr. Vejvoda, who published about 70 songs during his lifetime, wrote the ''Beer Barrel Polka'' in 1927 for his own band. Also known as ''Roll Out the Barrel,'' the song was known in Czechoslovak as ''Skoda Lasky,'' or ''Love in Vain.''
