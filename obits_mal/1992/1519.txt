
        Stewart A. Hegleman, a retired executive vice president of Grey Advertising Inc. in New York, died on Sunday at St. Rose's Home in Manhattan. He was 60 years old.
        He died of cancer, his family said.
        A native of Albany, Mr. Hegleman graduated from Siena College and began his career with Bloomingdale's. He joined Grey Advertising in 1963 and played a role in its growth from a small New York-based firm into a worldwide billion-dollar agency.
        Mr. Hegleman is survived by his wife, Kathryn, and two children, Joseph and Jane, all of Manhattan.
