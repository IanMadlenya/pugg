
        Henry C. Mills, an educator who held the second highest post at St. John's University, died on Sunday at the Baltimore County General Hospital in Ran dallstown, Md. He was 91 and lived in Sykesville, Md.
        He died of congestive heart failure, said his daughter, Elizabeth Koehn.
        Dr. Mills was appointed academic vice president and provost of St. John's, in Jamaica, Queens, in 1967 and retired in 1971.
        Besides his daughter Elizabeth, of Huntington, L.I., he is survived by his wife of 62 years, the former Harriett Matheson; another daughter, Sara Mazie of Chevy Chase, Md.; seven grandchildren and two great-grandchildren.
