
        Frank S. Napal 3d, director of alumni development for Emerson College in Boston, and a former public relations executive in New York, died May 4 in Cambridge, Mass. He was 44 years old and lived in Boston.
        Mr. Napal died of toxoplasmosis, said his brother, Ronald J. H. Napal.
        Mr. Napal worked with various public relations firms in New York City in the 1970's and 1980's. He was a director of public affairs for the New York regional office of the United States Environmental Protection Agency during the Carter Administration and was a consultant to several recent political campaigns, including the Presidential campaign of Paul E. Tsongas.
        Mr. Napal is survived by his mother, Madeline T. Napal, and his brother, Ronald, both of New York City.
