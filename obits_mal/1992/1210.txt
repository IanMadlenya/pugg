
        John A. Weed, a vice president of Salomon Brothers Asset Management, died yesterday at his home in New Canaan, Conn. He was 52 years old.
        He died of abdominal cancer, his wife, Elizabeth, said.
        Mr. Weed joined the Salomon Brothers Funds Company in 1990, when the Lehman Fund, of which he was an officer, was acquired by Salomon Inc., the parent of Salomon Brothers Funds. From 1980 to 1989, Mr. Weed had been a managing director of J. P. Morgan Investment Management Inc.
        He is survived by his wife of 27 years, the former Elizabeth Davitt; four daughters, Susan Meeus of Brussels, Wendy and Erika, both of Boston, and Heather, of New Canaan; a sister, Mary Ellen Olsen of Portland, Ore., and a brother, Charles, of Newark, Del.
