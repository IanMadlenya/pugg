
        John N. Regan, an attorney and former president of Martindale-Hubbell Inc., the publisher of the Martindale-Hubbell Law Directory, died on April 30 in Dublin. He was 93 years old.
        He died of natural causes, a family spokeswoman said.
        Mr. Regan graduated from the New York University Law School in 1920 and was briefly an associate with the law firm of Sherman & Sterling before forming his own firm, Regan & Barrett, in 1923. He remained associated with the firm through the early 1980's. He had been serving as a director of Martindale-Hubbell Inc. for a number of years when he was appointed its president in 1958. He retired from that position in 1976.
        He is survived by his wife of 46 years, the former Kathleen Maguire; four daughters, Joan McGivern, Virginia Coleman, Ellen McCluskey and Robin Regan; three sons, John, Winston and Andrew; and several grandchildren and great-grandchildren, all of New York City.
