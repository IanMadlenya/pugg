
        Tom Pritchard, executive editor of King Features Syndicate, died on Friday at his home in Norwalk, Conn. He was 64 years old.
        He died after a heart attack, the syndicate said.
        Mr. Pritchard oversaw day-to-day editorial operations and the development of syndicated columns, political cartoons and editorial services for King Features and North America Syndicate.
        Mr. Pritchard, who was was born in Bronxville, N.Y., was a reporter at The Record-Journal in Meriden, Conn., a feature writer with The Hartford Times, the editor and publisher of the weekly Wethersfield (Conn.) Post and executive editor of The Manchester (Conn.) Journal Inquirer.
        He joined King Features in 1973 and became executive editor in 1990.
        He is survived by his wife, Dorothy; a son, Stephen, of St. Petersburg, Fla.; and a grandson.
