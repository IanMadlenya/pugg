
        Walter T. Lucking, a former head of the Hackensack Water Company in New Jersey, died on Tuesday in the John C. Lincoln Memorial Hospital in Phoenix. He was 78 years old and lived in Phoenix.
        His death was reported by Martha Green, a spokeswoman for the Hackensack Water Company, who said Mr. Lucking had died of emphysema.
        Mr. Lucking served as president and chief executive officer, and then as chairman of the water company from 1973 to his retirement in 1981. He remained a director of the company and of its parent, United Water Resources, until 1987.
        He is survived by his wife, Mary Chase Lucking; two sons, John C. and Stephen W., both of Phoenix, and four grandchildren.
