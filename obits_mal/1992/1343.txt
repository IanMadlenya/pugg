
        Don R. Davis, a retired insurance executive, died on Monday at his home in Fairfield, Conn. He was 68 years old.
        Mr. Davis died of prostate cancer, said a son, Timothy J. Davis, of Fairfield.
        In 1968 he became president of the American Life Insurance Company of Wilmington, Del., which sold insurance only outside the United States. In 1976 he was named senior vice president for the international life and health insurance division of the Continental Corporation, where he served until his retirement in 1982.
        Most recently, he was head of his own insurance consulting firm, Atlantic Search, of Westport, Conn.
        A native of Washingtonville, Ohio, Mr. Davis entered Ohio Wesleyan University and graduated in 1948 after serving in the Marine Corps during World War II.
        In addition to his son Timothy, Mr. Davis is survived by his wife of 40 years, the former Louise Hall; another son, D. Scott Davis, of Fairfield, and a daughter, Wendy L. Beard, of Madrid.
